fighter

(ˈfaɪtə )
Definitions
noun1. a person who fights, esp a professional boxer2. a person who has determination3. military an armed aircraft designed for destroying other aircraft

fighter

Powered by COBUILD
(faɪtəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: fighters
Definitions
1. countable nounA fighter or a fighter plane is a fast military aircraft that is used for destroying other aircraft.2. countable nounIf you describe someone as a fighter, you approve of them because they continue trying to achieve things in spite of great difficulties or opposition. [approval] ⇒ From the start it was clear this tiny girl was a real fighter.3. countable nounA fighter is a person who physically fights another person, especially a professional boxer. ⇒ ...a tough little street fighter.4. → See also firefighter, freedom fighter, prize fighter

madame

(ˈmædəm , French madam)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural mesdames (ˈmeɪˌdæm , French medam)a married Frenchwoman: usually used as a title equivalent to Mrs, and sometimes extended to older unmarried women to show respect and to women of other nationalities

Word Origin

C17: from French. See madam

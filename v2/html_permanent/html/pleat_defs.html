pleat

(pliːt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of various types of fold formed by doubling back fabric and pressing, stitching, or steaming into place. See also box pleat, inverted pleat, kick pleat, knife pleat, sunburst pleatsverb2. (transitive) to arrange (material, part of a garment, etc) in pleats

Word Origin

C16: variant of plait

incensed

(ɪnˈsɛnst)
Definitions
adjectivegreatly enraged ⇒ the furious blows of the incensed mob., ⇒ Mum was incensed at his lack of compassion., ⇒ He followed her, incensed that she'd dared to leave him alone.

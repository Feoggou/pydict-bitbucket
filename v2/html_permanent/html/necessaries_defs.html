necessaries

(ˈnɛsɪsərɪz )
Definitions
plural noun1. (sometimes singular) what is needed; essential items ⇒ the necessaries of life2. law food, clothing, etc, essential for the maintenance of a dependant in the condition of life to which he or she is accustomed

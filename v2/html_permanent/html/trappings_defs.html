trappings

(ˈtræpɪŋz )
Definitions
plural noun1. the accessories and adornments that characterize or symbolize a condition, office, etc ⇒ the visible trappings of success2. a ceremonial harness for a horse or other animal, including bridles, saddles, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from trap2

Definitions
plural nounThe trappings of power, wealth, or a particular job are the extra things, such as decorations and luxury items, that go with it. [disapproval] ⇒ The family were in government for several generations and evidently loved the trappings of power.

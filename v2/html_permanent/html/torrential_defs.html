torrential

(tɒˈrɛnʃəl , tə-) or torrentuous (təˈrɛntjʊəs)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to a torrent2. pouring or flowing fast, violently, or heavily ⇒ torrential rain3. abundant, overwhelming, or irrepressible ⇒ torrential abuse

Derived Forms

torrentially (torˈrentially) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adverb [n ADV]Therein means contained in the place that has been mentioned. [formal] ⇒ By burning tree branches, pine needles, and pine cones, many not only warm their houses but improve the smell therein.2. adverb [n ADV]Therein means relating to something that has just been mentioned. [formal] ⇒ Afternoon groups relate to the specific addictions and problems therein.3. →  therein lies

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveIf someone's jacket or coat, for example, is belted, it has a belt fastened round it. ⇒ He wore a belted raincoat that accentuated his portly middle. ⇒ She wore a brown suede jacket, belted at the waist.

filly

(ˈfɪlɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -lies1. a female horse or pony under the age of four2. informal, rare a spirited girl or young woman
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old Norse fylja; related to Old High German fulihha; see foal

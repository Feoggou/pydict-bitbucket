racy

(ˈreɪsɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: racier or raciest1. (of a person's manner, literary style, etc) having a distinctively lively and spirited quality; fresh2. having a characteristic or distinctive flavour ⇒ a racy wine3. suggestive; slightly indecent; risqué ⇒ a racy comedy

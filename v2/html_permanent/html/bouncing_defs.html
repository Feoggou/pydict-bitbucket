Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [v-link ADJ with n, ADJ n]If you say that someone is bouncing with health, you mean that they are very healthy. You can also refer to a bouncing baby. ⇒ They are bouncing with health in the good weather. ⇒ Derek is now the proud father of a bouncing baby girl.2. → see also bounce

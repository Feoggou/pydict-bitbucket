whisky

(ˈwɪskɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -kiesa spirit made by distilling fermented cereals, which is matured and often blended
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: shortened from whiskybae, from Scottish Gaelic uisge beatha, literally: water of life; see usquebaugh

Definitions
variable nounWhisky is a strong alcoholic drink made, especially in Scotland, from grain such as barley or rye. ⇒ ...a bottle of whisky. ⇒ ...expensive whiskies and brandies.A whisky is a glass of whisky. ⇒ She handed him a whisky.

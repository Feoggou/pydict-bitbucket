hideous

(ˈhɪdɪəs )
Definitions
adjective1. extremely ugly; repulsive ⇒ a hideous person2. terrifying and horrific
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hideously (ˈhideously) adverbhideousness (ˈhideousness) or hideosity (ˌhɪdɪˈɒsɪtɪ ) noun

Word Origin

C13: from Old French hisdos, from hisde fear; of uncertain origin

hideous

Powered by COBUILD
(hɪdiəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that someone or something is hideous, you mean that they are very ugly or unattractive. ⇒ She saw a hideous face at the window and screamed. ⇒ ...hideous new Europe architecture, and horrible metal sculptures.2. graded adjective & adjectiveYou can describe an event, experience, or action as hideous when you mean that it is very unpleasant, painful, or difficult to bear. ⇒ His family was subjected to a hideous attack by the gang. ⇒ It's been a perfectly hideous day.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

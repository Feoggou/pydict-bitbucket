high water

Definitions
noun1. another name for high tide (sense 1)2. the state of any stretch of water at its highest level, as during a flood

high water

for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounHigh water is the time at which the water in a river or sea is at its highest level as a result of the tide. ⇒ Fishing is possible for a couple of hours either side of high water.2. → come hell or high water

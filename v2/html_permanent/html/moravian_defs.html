Moravian

(məˈreɪvɪən , mɒ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Moravia, its people, or their dialect of Czech2. of or relating to the Moravian Churchnoun3. the Moravian dialect4. a native or inhabitant of Moravia5. a member of the Moravian Church

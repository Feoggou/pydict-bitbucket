Definitions
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you say that a fear, accusation, or story is groundless, you mean that it is not based on evidence and is unlikely to be true or valid. OPPOSITE: well-founded ⇒ Fears that the world was about to run out of fuel proved groundless. ⇒ A ministry official described the report as groundless.

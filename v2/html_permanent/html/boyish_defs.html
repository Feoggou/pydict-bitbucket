Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a man as boyish, you mean that he is like a boy in his appearance or behaviour, and you find this characteristic quite attractive. [approval] ⇒ She was relieved to see his face light up with a boyish grin. ⇒ He loves to learn, and has a boyish enthusiasm for life.boyishly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ John grinned boyishly.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a girl or woman as boyish, you mean that she looks like a boy, for example because she has short hair or small breasts. ⇒ ...her tall, boyish figure.

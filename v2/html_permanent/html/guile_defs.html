guile

(ɡaɪl )
Definitions
nounclever or crafty character or behaviour
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

guileful (ˈguileful) adjectiveguilefully (ˈguilefully) adverbguilefulness (ˈguilefulness) noun

Word Origin

C18: from Old French guile, of Germanic origin; see wile

(gaɪl ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounGuile is the quality of being good at deceiving people in a clever way. ⇒ His cunning and guile were not attributes I would ever underestimate. ⇒ I love children's innocence and lack of guile.

fiddling

Powered by COBUILD
(fɪdəlɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounFiddling is the practice of getting money dishonestly by altering financial documents. [British, informal] ⇒ Salomon's fiddling is likely to bring big trouble for the firm.2. uncount nounViolin playing, especially in folk music, is sometimes referred to as fiddling.3. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]You can describe something as fiddling if it is small, unimportant, or difficult to do. ⇒ ...the daunting amount of fiddling technical detail.

cumber

(ˈkʌmbə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to obstruct or hinder2. obsolete to inconveniencenoun3. a hindrance or burden
Derived forms
cumberer (ˈcumberer) noun
Word origin
C13: probably from Old French combrer to impede, prevent, from combre barrier; see encumber

atrocity

(əˈtrɒsɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. behaviour or an action that is wicked or ruthless2. the fact or quality of being atrocious3. (usually plural) acts of extreme cruelty, esp against prisoners or civilians in wartime

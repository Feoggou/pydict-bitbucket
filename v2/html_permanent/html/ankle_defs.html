ankle

(ˈæŋkəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the joint connecting the leg and the foot. See talus12. the part of the leg just above the foot
Word origin
C14: from Old Norse; related to German, Dutch enkel, Latin angulus angle1

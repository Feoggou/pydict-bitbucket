belongings

(bɪˈlɒŋɪŋz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun(sometimes singular) the things that a person owns or has with him; possessions; effects

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
plural noun [usu poss N]Your belongings are the things that you own, especially things that are small enough to be carried. possessions ⇒ I collected my belongings and left. ⇒ He was identified only by his uniform and personal belongings.

Similar Words

possessions, goods, things, effects, property, stuff, gear, paraphernalia, personal property, accoutrements, chattels, goods and chattels

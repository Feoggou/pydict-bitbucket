nous

(naʊs )
Definitions
noun1. metaphysics mind or reason, esp when regarded as the principle governing all things2. British slang common sense; intelligence
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Greek, literally: mind

uncount nounNous is intelligence or common sense. [British] ⇒ Few ministers have the nous or the instinct required to understand the ramifications. ⇒ He is a man of extraordinary vitality, driving ambition and political nous.

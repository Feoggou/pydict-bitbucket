infection

(ɪnˈfɛkʃən )
Definitions
noun1. invasion of the body by pathogenic microorganisms2. the resulting condition in the tissues3. an infectious disease4. the act of infecting or state of being infected5. an agent or influence that infects6. persuasion or corruption, as by ideas, perverse influences, etc

aqua

(ˈækwə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural aquae (ˈækwiː ) or aquas1. water: used in compound names of certain liquid substances (as in aqua regia) or solutions of substances in water (as in aqua ammoniae), esp in the names of pharmacological solutionsadjective2. short for aquamarine (sense 2)
Word origin
Latin: water

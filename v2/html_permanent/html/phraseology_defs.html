phraseology

(ˌfreɪzɪˈɒlədʒɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -gies1. the manner in which words or phrases are used2. a set of phrases used by a particular group of people

Derived Forms

phraseological (ˌfreɪzɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ) adjectiveˌphraseologically (ˌphraseoˈlogically) adverb

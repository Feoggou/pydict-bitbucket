Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as miserly, you disapprove of them because they seem to hate spending money, and to spend as little as possible. [disapproval] ⇒ He is miserly with both his time and his money.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe an amount of something as miserly, you are critical of it because it is very small. [disapproval] ⇒ Being a student today with miserly grants and limited career prospects is difficult.

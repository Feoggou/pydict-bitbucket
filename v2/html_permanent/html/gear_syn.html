Synonyms of gear

gear

noun

  1. mechanism, works, action, gearing, machinery, cogs, cogwheels, gearwheels
    • The boat's steering gear failed.
  2. equipment, supplies, tackle, tools, instruments, outfit, rigging, rig, accessories, harness, apparatus, trappings, paraphernalia, accoutrements, appurtenances, equipage
    • fishing gear
  3. possessions, things, effects, stuff, kit, luggage, baggage, belongings, paraphernalia, personal property, chattels
    • They helped us put our gear in the van.
  4. clothing, wear, dress, clothes, habit, outfit, costume, threads (slang), array, garments, apparel, attire, garb, togs, schmutter (slang), rigout
    • I used to wear trendy gear but it just looked ridiculous.

verb

  1. (with to or towards) equip, fit, suit, adjust, adapt, rig, tailor
    • Colleges are not always geared towards the needs of mature students.

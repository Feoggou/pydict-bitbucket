tyrannical

Powered by COBUILD
(tɪrænɪkəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as tyrannical, you mean that they are severe or unfair towards the people that they have authority over. ⇒ He killed his tyrannical father with a blow to the head. ⇒ His behavior grew more unpredictable by the day, and increasingly tyrannical.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a government or organization as tyrannical, you mean that it acts without considering the wishes of its people and treats them cruelly or unfairly. ⇒ ...one of the world's most oppressive and tyrannical regimes.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

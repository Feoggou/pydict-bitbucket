tutu1

(ˈtuːtuː )
Definitions
nouna very short skirt worn by ballerinas, made of projecting layers of stiffened sheer material
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

from French, changed from the nursery word cucu backside, from cul, from Latin cūlus the buttocks

tutu2

Definitions
nouna shrub, Coriaria arborea, of New Zealand, having seeds that are poisonous to farm animals

Word Origin

Tutu

(ˈtuːtuː )
Definitions
noun Desmond. born 1931, South African clergyman, noted for his opposition to apartheid: Anglican Bishop of Johannesburg (1984–86) and Archbishop of Cape Town (1986–96); in 1995 he became leader of the Truth and Reconciliation Commission, established to investigate human rights violations during the apartheid era. Nobel peace prize 1984
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

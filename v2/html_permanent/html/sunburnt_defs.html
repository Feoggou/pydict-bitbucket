sunburnt

Powered by COBUILD
(sʌnbɜːʳnt ) also sunburned
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is sunburnt has sore bright pink skin because they have spent too much time in hot sunshine. ⇒ A badly sunburned face or back is extremely painful.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is sunburnt has very brown skin because they have spent a lot of time in the sunshine. ⇒ Mr Cooper looked fit and sunburnt.
Example sentences containing 'sunburnt'
His neck felt sunburnt and raw as he reached down to slip off his shoes. O'Connor, Joe DESPERADOESAbout forty, stocky of build, smooth sunburnt skin, an interesting face. Gagman, Maurice DOUBTFUL MOTIVESI was just worried she would be sunburnt , lying spread out like that. Barbara Erskine SANDS OF TIME

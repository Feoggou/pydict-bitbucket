Lazarus

(ˈlæzərəs )
Definitions
noun New Testament1. the brother of Mary and Martha, whom Jesus restored to life (John 11–12)2. the beggar who lay at the gate of the rich man Dives in Jesus' parable (Luke 16:19–31)

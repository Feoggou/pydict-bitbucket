Valentine's day

(ˈvæləntaɪnz deɪ)
Definitions
noun14 February, the day on which lovers give each other Valentine cards or gifts. See also Valentine

  • British English: Valentine's Day /ˈvæləntaɪnz deɪ/ NOUN
    On Valentine's Day, the 14th of February, people send a greetings card to someone that they are in love with or are attracted to, usually without signing their name.

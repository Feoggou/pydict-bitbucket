diction

(ˈdɪkʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the choice and use of words in writing or speech2. the manner of uttering or enunciating words and sounds; elocution
Word origin
C15: from Latin dictiō a saying, mode of expression, from dīcere to speak, say

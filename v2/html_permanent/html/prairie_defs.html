prairie

(ˈprɛərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(often plural) a treeless grassy plain of the central US and S Canada. Compare pampas, steppe, savanna

Word Origin

C18: from French, from Old French praierie, from Latin prātum meadow

Definitions

variable nounA prairie is a large area of flat, grassy land in North America. Prairies have very few trees.

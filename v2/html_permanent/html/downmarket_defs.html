downmarket

Powered by COBUILD
(daʊnmɑːʳkɪt ) also down-market
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a product or service as downmarket, you think that they are cheap and are not very good in quality. ⇒ ...downmarket television drama. ⇒ It is a downmarket eating house, seating about 60.Downmarket is also an adverb. ⇒ Why is the company going downmarket and developing smaller machines?

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
singular nounA sprinkling of something is a small quantity or amount of it, especially if it is spread over a large area. [+ of] ⇒ ...a light sprinkling of snow. ⇒ Norway has a fair sprinkling of women ministers.

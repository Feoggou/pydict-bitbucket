littoral

(ˈlɪtərəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to the shore of a sea, lake, or ocean2. biology inhabiting the shore of a sea or lake or the shallow waters near the shore ⇒ littoral faunanoun3. a coastal or shore region
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin littorālis, from lītorālis, from lītus shore

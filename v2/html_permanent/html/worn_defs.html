worn

(wɔːn )
Definitions
verb1. the past participle of wear1adjective2. affected, esp adversely, by long use or action ⇒ a worn suit3. haggard; drawn4. exhausted; spent

worn for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(wɔːʳn )
Definitions
1. Worn is the past participle of wear.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Worn is used to describe something that is damaged or thin because it is old and has been used a lot. ⇒ Worn rugs increase the danger of tripping. ⇒ Most of the trek is along worn paths.3. graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If someone looks worn, they look tired and old. ⇒ She was looking very haggard and worn.4. → See also well-worn

pontiff

(ˈpɒntɪf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna former title of the pagan high priest at Rome, later used of popes and occasionally of other bishops, and now confined exclusively to the pope

Word Origin

C17: from French pontife, from Latin pontifex

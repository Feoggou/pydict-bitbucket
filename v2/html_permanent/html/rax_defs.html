rax

(ræks ) Scottish
Definitions
verb1. (transitive) to stretch or extend2. (intransitive) to reach out3. (transitive) to pass or give (something to a person) with the outstretched hand; reach ⇒ rax me the salt4. (transitive) to strain or sprainnoun5. the act of stretching or straining

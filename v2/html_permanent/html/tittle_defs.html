tittle

(ˈtɪtəl )
Definitions
noun1. a small mark in printing or writing, esp a diacritic2. a jot; particle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Medieval Latin titulus label, from Latin: title

Similar Words

bit, drop, scrap, dash, grain, particle, atom, shred, mite, jot, speck, whit, iota

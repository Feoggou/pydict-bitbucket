particulars

(pəˈtɪkjʊləz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. details ⇒ You will find all the particulars in Chapter 9., ⇒ When asked about the break-up of her marriage, she refused to go into particulars.2. personal details, such as name, address and phone number ⇒ The policeman took down her particulars., ⇒ The policeman jotted down her particulars and said they would contact her again.

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
plural nounThe particulars of something or someone are facts or details about them which are written down and kept as a record. details ⇒ You will find all the particulars in Chapter 9. ⇒ The nurses at the admission desk asked her for particulars. ⇒ ...a written statement of particulars of employment.

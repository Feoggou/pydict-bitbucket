theogony

(θɪˈɒɡənɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -nies1. the origin and descent of the gods2. an account of this, often recited in epic poetry
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

theogonic (ˌθɪəˈɡɒnɪk ) adjectivetheogonist (theˈogonist) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Greek theogonia; see theo-, -gony

memento

(mɪˈmɛntəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -tos or -toes1. something that reminds one of past events; souvenir2. Roman Catholic Church either of two prayers occurring during the Mass

Word Origin

C15: from Latin, imperative of meminisse to remember

(mɪmentoʊ )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA memento is an object which you keep because it reminds you of a person or a special occasion. [+ of] ⇒ More anglers are taking cameras when they go fishing to provide a memento of catches.

sepulture

(ˈsɛpəltʃə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of placing in a sepulchre2. an archaic word for sepulchre

Word Origin

C13: via Old French from Latin sepultūra, from sepultus buried, from sepelīre to bury

deer

(dɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural deer or deers1. any ruminant artiodactyl mammal of the family Cervidae, including reindeer, elk, muntjacs, and roe deer, typically having antlers in the male ▶ Related adjective: cervine2. (in N Canada) another name for caribou
Word origin
Old English dēor beast; related to Old High German tior wild beast, Old Norse dӯr

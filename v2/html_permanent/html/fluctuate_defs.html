fluctuate

(ˈflʌktjʊˌeɪt )
Definitions
verb1. to change or cause to change position constantly; be or make unstable; waver or vary2. (intransitive) to rise and fall like a wave; undulate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin fluctuāre, from fluctus a wave, from fluere to flow

pros

(prəʊz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. arguments or votes in favour of a proposal or motion ⇒ On Sunday, this column listed all the 'pros' for the proposed new downtown YMCA., ⇒ She drew a line down the centre and wrote 'pros' on one side and 'cons' on the other.2. people who vote in favour of a proposal, motion, etc3. See pros and cons
Example sentences containing 'pros'
Was this going to turn out to be a case where the amateurs beat the pros ? Forbes, Colin COVER STORYHer husband to be, Graham, had opted not to have a stag night and we were onto the relative pros and cons of this. James Birrell THE MANANA MANBut I think in the end he decided the pros outweighed the cons as far as the local community's concerned. Stuart Harrison LOST SUMMERI would never let my college players or the touring pros who come to me for help hit 40 driver shots in a row. Leeson, Robert THE LAST GENIE

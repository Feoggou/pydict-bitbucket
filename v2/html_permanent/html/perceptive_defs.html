Definitions

adjective1. quick at perceiving; observant2. perceptual3. able to perceive

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a person or their remarks or thoughts as perceptive, you think that they are good at noticing or realizing things, especially things that are not obvious. [approval] ⇒ He was one of the most perceptive U.S. political commentators. ⇒ ...a very perceptive critique of Wordsworth.perceptively graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj] ⇒ The stages in her love affair with Harry are perceptively written.perceptiveness uncount noun ⇒ The task I have in mind requires little more than perceptiveness and a good memory.

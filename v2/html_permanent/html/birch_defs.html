birch

(bɜːtʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any betulaceous tree or shrub of the genus Betula, having thin peeling bark. See also silver birch2. the hard close-grained wood of any of these trees3. See the birchadjective4. of, relating to, or belonging to the birch5. consisting or made of birchverb6. (transitive) to flog with a birch
Derived forms
birchen (ˈbirchen) adjective
Word origin
Old English bierce; related to Old High German birihha, Sanskrit bhūrja

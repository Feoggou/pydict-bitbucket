Synonyms of renew

renew

verb

  1. recommence, continue, extend, repeat, resume, prolong, reopen, recreate, reaffirm, re-establish, rejuvenate, regenerate, restate, begin again, revitalize, bring up to date
    • He renewed his attack on government policy.
  2. reaffirm, confirm, resume, breathe new life into, recommence
    • They renewed their friendship.
  3. replace, refresh, top up, replenish, restock, resupply
    • Cells are constantly renewed.
  4. restore, repair, transform, rebuild, overhaul, mend, refurbish, renovate, refit, redevelop, fix up (informal, mainly US & & Canadian), modernize, recondition
    • the cost of renewing the buildings

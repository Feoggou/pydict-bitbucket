midwife

(ˈmɪdˌwaɪf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -wives (-ˌwaɪvz)a person qualified to deliver babies and to care for women before, during, and after childbirth

Word Origin

C14: from Old English mid with + wif woman

countable nounA midwife is a nurse who is trained to deliver babies and to advise pregnant women.

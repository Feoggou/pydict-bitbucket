shifting sands

(ˈʃɪftɪŋ sændz)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
plural nounpoetic used to emphasize that something changes frequently, making it difficult to deal with or make plans about ⇒ He had been a rock in the shifting sands of her existence., ⇒ The company was built on the hot and shifting sands of finance, not the rock of industry.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrase [oft PHR of n]If you refer to the shifting sands of a situation, you mean that it changes so often that it is difficult to deal with. ⇒ He had been a rock in the shifting sands of her existence. ⇒ ...his shrewd tactical skills in the shifting sands of Arab politics. →  sand

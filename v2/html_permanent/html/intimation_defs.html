Powered by COBUILD
(ɪntɪmeɪʃən ) for learners of English

Definitions
countable noun [N that]An intimation is an indirect suggestion or sign that something is likely to happen or be true. [formal] ⇒ ...intimations of mortality. ⇒ I did not have any intimation that he was going to resign.

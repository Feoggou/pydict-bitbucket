driving

(ˈdraɪvɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having or moving with force and violence ⇒ driving rain2. forceful or energetic3. relating to the controlling of a motor vehicle in motion ⇒ driving test

(draɪvɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]The driving force or idea behind something that happens or is done is the main thing that has a strong effect on it and makes it happen or be done in a particular way. ⇒ Consumer spending was the driving force behind the economic growth in the summer. ⇒ Bruce Rioch's driving ambition is the main reason behind their new-found success.2. → see also drive

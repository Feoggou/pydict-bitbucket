tomorrow

(təˈmɒrəʊ )
Definitions
noun1. the day after today2. the futureadverb3. on the day after today4. at some time in the future
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English tō morgenne, from to1 (at, on) + morgenne, dative of morgen morning; see morrow

(təmɒroʊ , US -mɔːr- ) for learners of English
Word forms: tomorrows
Definitions
1. adverbYou use tomorrow to refer to the day after today. ⇒ Bye, see you tomorrow. ⇒ The first official results will be announced tomorrow.Tomorrow is also a noun. ⇒ What's on your agenda for tomorrow? ⇒ Davies plays for the Barbarians in tomorrow's match against England. ⇒ Tomorrow is Christmas Day.2. adverbYou can refer to the future, especially the near future, as tomorrow. ⇒ What is education going to look like tomorrow?Tomorrow is also a noun. ⇒ ...tomorrow's computer industry. ⇒ Experiences in the past become a part of us, affecting our tomorrows.

canned

(kænd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. preserved and stored in airtight cans or tins ⇒ canned meat2. informal prepared or recorded in advance; artificial; not spontaneous ⇒ canned music3. a slang word for drunk (sense 1)

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Canned music, laughter, or applause on a television or radio programme has been recorded beforehand and is added to the programme to make it sound as if there is a live audience.2. → see also can2

sensibilities

(ˌsɛnsɪˈbɪlətɪz)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. the capacity for responding to emotion, impression, etc2. emotional or moral feelings ⇒ We have to be careful not to offend our readers' sensibilities., ⇒ The challenge offended their sensibilities.3. the capacity for responding to aesthetic stimuli
Example sentences containing 'sensibilities'
He heard their laughter, mocking such pitiful sensibilities. Clive Barker THE GREAT AND SECRET SHOWWe are born with our sensibilities ; perhaps we are conceived that way. Hilary Mantel LEARNING TO TALK: SHORT STORIESI do not offend your sensibilities , as I do your king's, yet he can find it in himself to be gracious. Jennifer Fallon TREASON KEEP

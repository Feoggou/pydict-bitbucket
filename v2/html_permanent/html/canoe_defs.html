canoe

(kəˈnuː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a light narrow open boat, propelled by one or more paddles2. New Zealand another word for waka (sense 1)3. See in the same canoeverb -noes, -noeing or -noed4. to go in a canoe or transport by canoe
Derived forms
canoeing (caˈnoeing) nouncanoeist (caˈnoeist) noun
Word origin
C16: from Spanish canoa, of Carib origin

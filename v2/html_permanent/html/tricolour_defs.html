tricolour

or US tricolor (ˈtrɪkələ , ˈtraɪˌkʌlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective also: tricoloured or US tricolored (ˈtraɪˌkʌləd)1. having or involving three coloursnoun2. (often capital) the French national flag, having three equal vertical stripes in blue, white, and red3. any flag, badge, ribbon, etc, with three colours

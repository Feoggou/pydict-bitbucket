long-suffering

Definitions
adjective1. enduring pain, unhappiness, etc, without complaintnoun also: long-sufferance2. long and patient endurance

Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Someone who is long-suffering patiently puts up with a lot of trouble or unhappiness, especially when it is caused by someone else. ⇒ He went back to Yorkshire to join his loyal, long-suffering wife.

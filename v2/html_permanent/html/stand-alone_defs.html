Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]A stand-alone business or organization is independent and does not receive financial support from another organization. [business] ⇒ They plan to relaunch it as a stand-alone company.2. adjective [ADJ n]A stand-alone computer is one that can operate on its own and does not have to be part of a network. [computing] ⇒ ...an operating system that can work on networks and stand-alone machines.

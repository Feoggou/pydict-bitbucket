piteous

(ˈpɪtɪəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. exciting or deserving pity2. archaic having or expressing pity

Derived Forms

piteously (ˈpiteously) adverbpiteousness (ˈpiteousness) noun

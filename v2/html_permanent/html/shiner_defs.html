shiner

(ˈʃaɪnə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. something that shines, such as a polishing device2. any of numerous small North American freshwater cyprinid fishes of the genus Notropis and related genera, such as N. cornutus (common shiner) and Notemigonus crysoleucas (golden shiner)3. a popular name for the mackerel4. informal a black eye5. New Zealand old-fashioned, informal a vagrant or tramp
Example sentences containing 'shiner'
If his dad was as angry as you say he sounded, then he might've given Lenny a shiner. Locke, Joseph THE TEACHER

racer

(ˈreɪsə )
Definitions
noun1. a person, animal, or machine that races2. a turntable used to traverse a heavy gun3. any of several long slender nonvenomous North American snakes of the colubrid genus Coluber and related genera, such as C. lateralis (striped racer)

Definitions
1. countable nounA racer is a person or animal that takes part in races. ⇒ Tim Powell is a former champion powerboat racer.2. countable nounA racer is a vehicle such as a car or bicycle that is designed to be used in races and therefore travels fast.

Synonyms of task

task

noun

  1. job, duty, assignment, work, business, charge, labour, exercise, mission, employment, enterprise, undertaking, occupation, chore, toil
    • He had the unenviable task of breaking the bad news.

verb

  1. charge, assign to, entrust
    • The minister was tasked with checking that aid was spent wisely.
  2. strain, test, tax, push, load, burden, exhaust, saddle, weary, lumber (British, informal), oppress, overload
  • See take someone to task

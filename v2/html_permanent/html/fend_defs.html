fend

(fɛnd )
Definitions
verb1. (intransitive; foll by for) to give support (to someone, esp oneself); provide (for)2. (transitive; usually foll by off) to ward off or turn aside (blows, questions, attackers, etc)3. (transitive) archaic to defend or resist4. (intransitive) Scottish and Northern England dialect to struggle; strivenoun5. Scottish and Northern England dialect a shift or effort
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13 fenden, shortened from defenden to defend

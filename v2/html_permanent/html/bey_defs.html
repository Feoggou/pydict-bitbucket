bey

(beɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (in the Ottoman Empire) a title given to senior officers, provincial governors, certain other officials or nobles, and (sometimes) Europeans2. (in modern Turkey) a title of address, corresponding to Mr
Word origin

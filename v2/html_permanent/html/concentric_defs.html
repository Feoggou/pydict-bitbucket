concentric

(kənˈsɛntrɪk ) or concentrical (kənˈsɛntrɪkəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivehaving a common centre ⇒ concentric circles. Compare eccentric (sense 3)
Derived forms
concentrically (conˈcentrically) adverbconcentricity (ˌkɒnsənˈtrɪsɪtɪ ) noun
Word origin
C14: from Medieval Latin concentricus, from Latin com- same + centrum centre

(kənsentrɪk )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]Concentric circles or rings have the same centre. ⇒ On a blackboard, he drew five concentric circles.

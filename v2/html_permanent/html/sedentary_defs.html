sedentary

(ˈsɛdəntərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. characterized by or requiring a sitting position ⇒ sedentary work2. tending to sit about without taking much exercise3. (of animals) moving about very little, usually because of attachment to a rock or other surface4. (of animals) not migratory

Derived Forms

sedentarily (ˈsedentarily) adverbsedentariness (ˈsedentariness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin sedentārius, from sedēre to sit

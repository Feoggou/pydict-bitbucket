clean sweep

(kliːn swiːp)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an overwhelming victory ⇒ a golden chance to complete a memorable clean sweep2. the winning of all prizes or contests in a competition ⇒ only missing out on a clean sweep by 7 points3. a thorough and sweeping change, inspection, etc ⇒ an order to make a clean sweep fore and aft every evening for quarters

Synonyms of heave

heave

verb

  1. lift, raise, pull (up), drag (up), haul (up), tug, lever, hoist, heft (informal)
    • He heaved Barney to his feet.
  2. throw, fling, toss, send, cast, pitch, hurl, sling
    • Heave a brick at the telly.
  3. expand, rise, swell, pant, throb, exhale, dilate, palpitate
    • His chest heaved, and he took a deep breath.
  4. surge, rise, swell, billow
    • The grey seas heaved.
  5. vomit, be sick, throw up (informal), chuck (up) (slang, mainly US), chuck (Australian & & New Zealand, informal), gag, spew, retch, barf (US, slang), chunder (slang, mainly Australian), upchuck (US, slang), do a technicolour yawn (slang), toss your cookies (US, slang)
    • He gasped and heaved and vomited.
  6. breathe, sigh, puff, groan, sob, breathe heavily, suspire (archaic), utter wearily
    • Mr Collier heaved a sigh and got to his feet.

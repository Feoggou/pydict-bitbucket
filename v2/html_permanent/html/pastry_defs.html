pastry

(ˈpeɪstrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -tries1. a dough of flour, water, shortening, and sometimes other ingredients2. baked foods, such as tarts, made with this dough3. an individual cake or pastry pie

Word Origin

C16: from paste1

pastry

Powered by COBUILD
(peɪstri )
Word forms: pastries
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounPastry is a food made from flour, fat, and water that is mixed together, rolled flat, and baked in the oven. It is used, for example, for making pies.2. countable nounA pastry is a small cake made with sweet pastry.

prurient

(ˈprʊərɪənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. unusually or morbidly interested in sexual thoughts or practices2. exciting or encouraging lustfulness; erotic

Derived Forms

prurience (ˈprurience) nounpruriently (ˈpruriently) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin prūrīre to itch, to lust after

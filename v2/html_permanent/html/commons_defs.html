commons

(ˈkɒmənz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (functioning as plural) people not of noble birth viewed as forming a political order2. (functioning as plural) the lower classes as contrasted to the ruling classes of society; the commonalty3. (functioning as singular) British a building or hall for dining, recreation, etc, usually attached to a college4. (usually functioning as plural) British food or rations (esp in the phrase short commons)

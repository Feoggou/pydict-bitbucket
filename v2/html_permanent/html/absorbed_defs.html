Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If you are absorbed in something or someone, you are very interested in them and they take up all your attention and energy. [+ in/by] ⇒ They were completely absorbed in each other. ⇒ He had discovered politics and was rapidly becoming absorbed by it. ⇒ I get so absorbed in doing something that I am unaware of things happening round me.

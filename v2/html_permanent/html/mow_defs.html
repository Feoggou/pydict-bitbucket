mow1

(məʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verbWord forms: mows, mowing, mowed, mowed or mown1. to cut down (grass, crops, etc) with a hand implement or machine2. (transitive) to cut the growing vegetation of (a field, lawn, etc)

Derived Forms

mower (ˈmower) noun

Word Origin

Old English māwan; related to Old High German māen, Middle Dutch maeyen to mow, Latin metere to reap, Welsh medi

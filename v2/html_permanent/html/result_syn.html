Synonyms of result

result

noun

  1. consequence, effect, outcome, end result, issue, event, development, product, reaction, fruit, sequel, upshot
    • This is the result of eating too much fatty food.
    OPPOSITES:  beginning, cause, source, root, origin, outset, germ
  2. outcome, conclusion, verdict, determination, end, decision, judgement, termination
    • They were surprised by the result of their trials.

plural noun

  1. marks, scores, grades, grading, percentage, evaluation, appraisal
    • Her exam results were excellent.

verb

  1. (often followed by from) arise, follow, issue, happen, appear, develop, spring, flow, turn out, stem, derive, ensue, emanate, eventuate
    • Many hair problems result from what you eat.
  • See result in something

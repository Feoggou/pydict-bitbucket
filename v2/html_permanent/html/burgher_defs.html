burgher

(ˈbɜːɡə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a member of the trading or mercantile class of a medieval city2. a respectable citizen; bourgeois3. archaic a citizen or inhabitant of a corporate town, esp on the Continent4. South Africa historya. a citizen of the Cape Colony or of one of the Transvaal and Free State republicsb. (as modifier) ⇒ burgher troops
Word origin
C16: from German Bürger, or Dutch burger freeman of a borough

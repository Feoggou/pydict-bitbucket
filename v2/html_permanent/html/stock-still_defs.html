Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveabsolutely still; motionless ⇒ He stood stock still, with the tropical sun beating on his skull, and endlessly watched the rise and fall of the ship., ⇒ the efforts of a group of people to remain stock still for a whole hour

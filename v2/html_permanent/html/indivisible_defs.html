indivisible

(ˌɪndɪˈvɪzəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. unable to be divided2. mathematics leaving a remainder when divided by a given number ⇒ 8 is indivisible by 3
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

indivisibility (ˌindiˌvisiˈbility) or indivisibleness (ˌindiˈvisibleness) nounindivisibly (ˌindiˈvisibly) adverb

indivisible

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndɪvɪzɪbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveIf you say that something is indivisible, you mean that it cannot be divided into different parts. ⇒ Far from being separate, the mind and body form an indivisible whole.

well-fixed

(ˌwɛlˈfɪkst)
Definitions
adjective(well fixed when postpositive) US informal having been provided with a generous amount of something, esp money ⇒ One well-fixed Larchmont widow offered twenty grand., ⇒ We're well fixed for food.

notebook

(ˈnəʊtˌbʊk )
Definitions
noun1. a book for recording notes or memoranda2. a book for registering promissory notes
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

notebook for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(noʊtbʊk )
Word forms: notebooks
Definitions
1. countable nounA notebook is a small book for writing notes in. ⇒ He brought out a notebook and pen from his pocket. ⇒ ...her reporter's notebook.2. countable noun [usu N n]A notebook computer is a small personal computer. ⇒ ...a range of notebook computers which allows all your important information to travel safely with you.

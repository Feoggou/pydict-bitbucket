cotton wool

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: purified cotton mainly British bleached and sterilized cotton from which the gross impurities, such as the seeds and waxy matter, have been removed: used for surgical dressings, tampons, etc. Usual US term: absorbent cotton2. cotton in the natural state3. British informala. a state of pampered comfort and protectionb. (as modifier) ⇒ a cotton-wool existence

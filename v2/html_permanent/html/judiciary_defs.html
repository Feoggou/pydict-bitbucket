judiciary

(dʒuːˈdɪʃɪərɪ , -ˈdɪʃərɪ)
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to courts of law, judgment, or judgesnoun plural -aries2. the branch of the central authority in a state concerned with the administration of justice. Compare executive (sense 2), legislature3. the system of courts in a country4. the judges collectively; bench

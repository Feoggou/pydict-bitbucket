sixth sense

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany supposed sense or means of perception, such as intuition or clairvoyance, other than the five senses of sight, hearing, touch, taste, and smell

sixth sense

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
singular nounIf you say that someone has a sixth sense, you mean that they seem to have a natural ability to know about things before other people, or to know things that other people do not know. [+ for] ⇒ The interesting thing about O'Reilly is his sixth sense for finding people who have good ideas.

The Old Servants had developed a sixth sense in these matters. Stuart Harrison LOST SUMMERThere was nothing out of place yet a sixth sense convinced him the house had been violated in his absence.

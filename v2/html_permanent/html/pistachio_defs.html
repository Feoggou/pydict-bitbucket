pistachio

(pɪˈstɑːʃɪˌəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -os1. an anacardiaceous tree, Pistacia vera, of the Mediterranean region and W Asia, with small hard-shelled nuts2. Also called: pistachio nut the nut of this tree, having an edible green kernel3. the sweet flavour of the pistachio nut, used esp in ice creamsadjective4. of a yellowish-green colour

Word Origin

C16: via Italian and Latin from Greek pistakion pistachio nut, from pistakē pistachio tree, from Persian pistah

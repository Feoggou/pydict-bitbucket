Guards

(ɡɑːdz )
Definitions
plural nouna. (esp in European armies) any of various regiments responsible for ceremonial duties and, formerly, the protection of the head of state ⇒ the Life Guards, ⇒ the Grenadier Guardsb. (as modifier) ⇒ a Guards regiment

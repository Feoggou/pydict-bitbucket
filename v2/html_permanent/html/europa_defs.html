Europa1

(jʊˈrəʊpə )
Definitions
nounGreek mythology a Phoenician princess who had three children by Zeus in Crete, where he had taken her after assuming the guise of a white bull. Their offspring were Rhadamanthus, Minos, and Sarpedon

Definitions
nounthe smallest of the four Galilean satellites of Jupiter. Diameter: 3138 km; orbital radius: 671 000 km

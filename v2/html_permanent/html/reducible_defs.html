reducible

(rɪdjuːsɪbəl , US -duːs- ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveIf you say that an idea, problem, or situation is not reducible to something simple, you mean that it is complicated and cannot be described in a simple way. [formal] [+ to] ⇒ The structure of the universe may not be reducible to a problem in physics.

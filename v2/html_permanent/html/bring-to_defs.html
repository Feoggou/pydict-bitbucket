bring to

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. (adverb) to restore (a person) to consciousness2. (adverb) to cause (a ship) to turn into the wind and reduce her headway3. (preposition) to make (something) equal to (an amount of money) ⇒ that brings your bill to £17

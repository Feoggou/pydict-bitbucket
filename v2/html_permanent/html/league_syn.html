Synonyms of league

league

noun

  1. association, union, alliance, coalition, group, order, band, corporation, combination, partnership, federation, compact, consortium, guild, confederation, fellowship, fraternity, confederacy
    • the League of Nations
  2. championship, competition, tournament, contest
    • The club are on the brink of promotion to the Premier League.
  3. class, group, level, category, ability group
    • Her success has taken her out of my league.

verb

  1. unite, band, ally, associate, combine, collaborate, join forces, confederate, amalgamate
  • See in league with someone

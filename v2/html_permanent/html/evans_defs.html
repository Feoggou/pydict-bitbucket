Evans

(ˈɛvənz )
Definitions
noun1.  Sir Arthur (John). 1851–1941, British archaeologist, whose excavations of the palace of Knossos in Crete provided evidence for the existence of the Minoan civilization2.  Dame Edith (Mary Booth). 1888–1976, British actress3.  Sir Geraint (Llewellyn). 1922–92, Welsh operatic baritone4.  Herbert McLean. 1882–1971, US anatomist and embryologist; discoverer of vitamin E (1922)5. Mary Ann. real name of (George) Eliot (sense 1)6.  Oliver. 1755–1819, US engineer: invented the continuous production line and a high-pressure steam engine7.  Walker. 1903–75, US photographer, noted esp for his studies of rural poverty in the Great Depression

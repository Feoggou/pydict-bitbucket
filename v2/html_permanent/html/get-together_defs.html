get-together

Definitions
noun1. informal a small informal meeting or social gatheringverb get together (adverb)2. (transitive) to gather or collect3. (intransitive) (of people) to meet socially4. (intransitive) to discuss, esp in order to reach an agreement5. See get it together

get together

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. phrasal verbWhen people get together, they meet in order to discuss something or to spend time together. [V P] ⇒ This is the only forum where East and West can get together.2. → See also get-together3. phrasal verbIf you get something together, you organize it. [V n P] ⇒ Paul and I were getting a band together, and we needed a new record deal.4. phrasal verbIf you get an amount of money together, you succeed in getting all the money that you need in order to pay for something. [V n P] ⇒ Now you've finally got enough money together to put down a deposit on your dream home. [Also V P n (not pron)] →  get
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

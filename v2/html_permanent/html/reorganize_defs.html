Definitions
regional note:   in BRIT, also use reorganiseergative verb & verbTo reorganize something or to reorganize means to change the way in which something is organized, arranged, or done. [V n] ⇒ It is the mother who is expected to reorganize her busy schedule. [be V-ed + into] ⇒ Four thousand troops have been reorganized into a fighting force. [V] ⇒ They'll have to reorganise and that might cause them problems. [V-ed] ⇒ ...the newly reorganized local health service.reorganization (riɔːʳgənaɪzeɪʃən )Word forms: reorganizations variable noun [+ of] ⇒ ...the reorganization of the legal system. ⇒ David was worried about major reorganisations taking place at work.

Synonyms of rip

rip

verb

  1. tear, cut, score, split, burst, rend, slash, hack, claw, slit, gash, lacerate
    • I tried not to rip the paper.
  2. be torn, tear, split, burst, be rent
    • I felt the banner rip as we were pushed in opposite directions.

noun

  1. tear, cut, hole, split, rent, slash, slit, cleavage, gash, laceration
    • She looked at the rip in her new dress.

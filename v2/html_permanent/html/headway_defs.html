headway

(ˈhɛdˌweɪ )
Definitions
noun1. motion in a forward direction ⇒ the vessel made no headway2. progress or rate of progress ⇒ he made no headway with the problem3. another name for headroom4. the distance or time between consecutive trains, buses, etc, on the same route

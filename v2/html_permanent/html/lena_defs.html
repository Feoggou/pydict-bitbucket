Lena

(ˈliːnə , Russian ˈljɛnə)
Definitions
nouna river in Russia, rising in S Siberia and flowing generally north through the Sakha Republic to the Laptev Sea by an extensive delta: the longest river in Russia. Length: 4271 km (2653 miles)

flagellate

Definitions
verb (ˈflædʒɪˌleɪt )1. (transitive) to whip; scourge; flogadjective (ˈflædʒɪlɪt , -ˌleɪt) also: flagellated2. possessing one or more flagella3. resembling a flagellum; whiplikenoun (ˈflædʒɪlɪt , -ˌleɪt)4. a flagellate organism, esp any protozoan of the phylum Zoomastigina

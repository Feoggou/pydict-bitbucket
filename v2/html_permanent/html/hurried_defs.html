hurried

Powered by COBUILD
(hʌrid , US hɜːr- ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A hurried action is done quickly, because you do not have much time to do it in. ⇒ ...a hurried breakfast.hurriedly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ ...students hurriedly taking notes.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A hurried action is done suddenly, in reaction to something that has just happened. ⇒ Downing Street denied there had been a hurried overnight redrafting of the text.hurriedly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ The moment she saw it, she blushed and hurriedly left the room.3. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]Someone who is hurried does things more quickly than they should because they do not have much time to do them. ⇒ Parisians on the street often looked worried, hurried and unfriendly.

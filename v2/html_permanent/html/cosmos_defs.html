cosmos

(ˈkɒzmɒs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the world or universe considered as an ordered system2. any ordered system3. harmony; order4. plural -mos or -moses any tropical American plant of the genus Cosmos, cultivated as garden plants for their brightly coloured flowers: family Asteraceae (composites)
Word origin
C17: from Greek kosmos order, world, universe

dawning

(ˈdɔːnɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. gradually beginning to become light ⇒ the dawning day2. gradually taking shape in a person's consciousness ⇒ a dawning awarenessnoun3. another word for dawn
Example sentences containing 'dawning'
Then either understanding or memory filtered through, and with it a dawning horror. Fraser, Anthea PRETTY MAIDS ALL IN A ROWUntil the watcher in the dawning cried, `Ah God, ah God, the dawn!

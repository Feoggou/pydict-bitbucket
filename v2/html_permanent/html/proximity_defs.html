proximity

(prɒkˈsɪmɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. nearness in space or time2. nearness or closeness in a series

Word Origin

C15: from Latin proximitās closeness; see proximate

proximity

Powered by COBUILD
(prɒksɪmɪti )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft in N]Proximity to a place or person is nearness to that place or person. [formal] [+ to] ⇒ Part of the attraction is Darwin's proximity to Asia. ⇒ He became aware of the proximity of the Afghans. [+ to] ⇒ Families are no longer in close proximity to each other. [Also + of]

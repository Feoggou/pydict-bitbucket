steam engine

or steam-engine (stiːm ˈɛndʒɪn)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. mechanical engineering an engine that uses the thermal energy of steam to produce mechanical work, esp one in which steam from a boiler is expanded in a cylinder to drive a reciprocating piston ⇒ the invention of the steam engine2. railways a steam locomotive ⇒ Everything was put in order for the arrival of the first hooting steam engine with its cowcatcher out front.

dedicated

(ˈdɛdɪˌkeɪtɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. devoted to a particular purpose or cause ⇒ a dedicated man2. assigned or allocated to a particular project, function, etc ⇒ a dedicated transmission line, ⇒ dedicated parking space3. computing designed to fulfil one function ⇒ a dedicated microprocessor

(dedɪkeɪtɪd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]You use dedicated to describe someone who enjoys a particular activity very much and spends a lot of time doing it. ⇒ Her great-grandfather had clearly been a dedicated and stoical traveller. ⇒ ...dedicated followers of classical music.2. adjectiveYou use dedicated to describe something that is made, built, or designed for one particular purpose or thing. ⇒ Such areas should also be served by dedicated cycle routes. [+ to] ⇒ ...the world's first museum dedicated to ecology. ⇒ ...a microcomputer dedicated to playing chess.3. → see also dedicate

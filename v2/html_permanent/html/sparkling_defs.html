sparkling

(ˈspɑːklɪŋ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. clear and bright and shining with a lot of very small points of light ⇒ the sparkling blue waters of the ocean, ⇒ She wore sparkling diamond earrings., ⇒ His eyes were sparkling.2. (of drinks) slightly fizzy ⇒ a glass of sparkling wine, ⇒ a new lightly sparkling drink3. performing very well ⇒ another set of sparkling results, ⇒ Top retailer Marks & Spencer has romped in with another set of sparkling results.4. lively, intelligent, and witty ⇒ He is sparkling and versatile in front of the camera., ⇒ He is known for his sparkling wit.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Sparkling drinks are slightly fizzy. ⇒ ...a glass of sparkling wine. ⇒ ...a new lightly sparkling drink.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]If a company is described as having sparkling figures or sparkling results, it has performed very well and made a lot of money.

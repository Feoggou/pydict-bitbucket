coexist

(ˌkəʊɪɡˈzɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (intransitive)1. to exist together at the same time or in the same place2. to exist together in peace
Derived forms
ˌcoexistence (ˌcoexˈistence) nounˌcoexistent (ˌcoexˈistent) adjective

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf one thing coexists with another, they exist together at the same time or in the same place. You can also say that two things coexist. [V + with] ⇒ Pockets of affluence coexist with poverty. [V] ⇒ Bankers and clockmakers have coexisted in the City for hundreds of years.

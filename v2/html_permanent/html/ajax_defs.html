Ajax

(ˈeɪdʒæks )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Greek mythology1. the son of Telamon; a Greek hero of the Trojan War who killed himself in vexation when Achilles' armour was given to Odysseus2. called Ajax the Lesser, a Locrian king, a swift-footed Greek hero of the Trojan War

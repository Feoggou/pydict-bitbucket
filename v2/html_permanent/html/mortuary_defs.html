mortuary

(ˈmɔːtʃʊərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -aries1. Also called: morgue a building where dead bodies are kept before cremation or burialadjective2. of or relating to death or burial

Word Origin

C14 (as n, a funeral gift to a parish priest): via Medieval Latin mortuārium (n) from Latin mortuārius of the dead

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA mortuary is a building or a room in a hospital where dead bodies are kept before they are buried or cremated, or before they are identified or examined. 2. countable nounA mortuary is the same as a funeral home. [US]

furnished

(ˈfɜːnɪʃt)
Definitions
adjective(of a house, room, etc) provided with furniture, carpets, etc ⇒ Eleanor moved into a small furnished apartment., ⇒ All 300 elegantly furnished rooms have private bath., ⇒ We took tea by lamplight in his sparsely furnished house.

furnished

Powered by COBUILD
(fɜːʳnɪʃt ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveA furnished room or house is available to be rented together with the furniture in it. ⇒ Eleanor moved into a small furnished apartment.2. adjective [adv ADJ]When you say that a room or house is furnished in a particular way, you are describing the kind or amount of furniture that it has in it. ⇒ We took tea by lamplight in his sparsely furnished house. ⇒ All 300 elegantly furnished rooms have private bath.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

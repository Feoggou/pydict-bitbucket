kid gloves

(kɪd ɡlʌvz)
Definitions
plural noun1. gentle tact and diplomacy ⇒ I think he had the balance of authority, discipline, and kid gloves just about right., ⇒ We've left our kid gloves at home this week., ⇒ We should point out that we've left our kid gloves at home this week, so any reader tempted to write in pointing out that avocados are actually fruits will have to take their chances.2. See to handle sb with kid gloves

kid gloves for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
plural noun [oft with N]If you treat someone or something with kid gloves, or if you give them the kid glove treatment, you are very careful in the way you deal with them. ⇒ In presidential campaigns, foreign policy is treated with kid gloves. ⇒ Some artists have to be handled with kid gloves. ⇒ We must take off the kid gloves and smash these evil monsters once and for all.

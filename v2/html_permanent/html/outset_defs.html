Translations for 'outset'

  • British English: outset /ˈaʊtˌsɛt/ NOUN
    If something happens at the outset of an event, process, or period of time, it happens at the beginning of it. If something happens from the outset, it happens from the beginning and continues to happen.It was clear from the outset that the project would fail.

kennels

(ˈkɛnəlz)
Definitions
noun1. an establishment where dogs are bred, trained, boarded, etc ⇒ Pauline runs a kennels.plural noun2. an establishment where dogs are bred, trained, boarded, etc ⇒ The guard dog was now in kennels as it was not aggressive.

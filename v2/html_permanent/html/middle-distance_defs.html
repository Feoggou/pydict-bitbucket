middle-distance

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. athletics relating to or denoting races of a length between the sprints and the distance events, esp the 800 metres and the 1500 metresnoun middle distance2. Also called: middle ground part of a painting, esp a landscape between the foreground and far distance

middle distance

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. singular noun [usu into/in the N]If you are looking into the middle distance, you are looking at a place that is neither near nor far away. ⇒ He stares detachedly into the middle distance, towards nothing in particular.2. adjective [ADJ n]A middle-distance runner is someone who takes part in races of medium length, for example 800 metres.

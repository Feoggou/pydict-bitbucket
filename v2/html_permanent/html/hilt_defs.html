hilt

(hɪlt )
Definitions
noun1. the handle or shaft of a sword, dagger, etc2. See to the hiltverb3. (transitive) to supply with a hilt
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English; related to Old Norse hjalt, Old Saxon helta oar handle, Old High German helza

palmate

(ˈpælmeɪt , -mɪt) or palmated
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. shaped like an open hand ⇒ palmate antlers2. botany having more than three lobes or segments that spread out from a common point ⇒ palmate leaves3. (of the feet of most water birds) having three toes connected by a web

Word Origin

C18: from Latin palmatus, from palma palm; see palm2
Example sentences containing 'palmate'

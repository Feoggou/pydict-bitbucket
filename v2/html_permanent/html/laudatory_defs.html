Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A laudatory piece of writing or speech expresses praise or admiration for someone. [formal] ⇒ The New York Times has this very laudatory article about your retirement. ⇒ Beth spoke of Dr. Hammer in laudatory terms.

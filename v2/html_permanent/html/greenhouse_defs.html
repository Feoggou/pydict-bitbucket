greenhouse

(ˈɡriːnˌhaʊs )
Definitions
nouna building with transparent walls and roof, usually of glass, for the cultivation and exhibition of plants under controlled conditions

greenhouse

Powered by COBUILD
(griːnhaʊs ) for learners of English
Word forms: greenhouses
Definitions
1. countable nounA greenhouse is a glass building in which you grow plants that need to be protected from bad weather.2. adjective [ADJ n]Greenhouse means relating to or causing the greenhouse effect.

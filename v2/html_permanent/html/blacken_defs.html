blacken

(ˈblækən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to make or become black or dirty2. (transitive) to defame; slander (esp in the phrase blacken someone's name)

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. ergative verb & verbTo blacken something means to make it black or very dark in colour. Something that blackens becomes black or very dark in colour. [V n] ⇒ The married women of Shitamachi maintained the custom of blackening their teeth. [V] ⇒ You need to grill the tomatoes until the skins blacken.2. verbIf someone blackens your character, they make other people believe that you are a bad person. [V n] ⇒ He accused him of knowingly spreading falsehoods in an effort to blacken my character. [V n] ⇒ They're trying to blacken our name.

Synonyms of luxury

luxury

noun

  1. opulence, splendour, richness, extravagance, affluence, hedonism, a bed of roses, voluptuousness, the life of Riley, sumptuousness
    • She was brought up in an atmosphere of luxury and wealth.
    OPPOSITES:  want, poverty, deprivation, austerity, privation, destitution
  2. extravagance, treat, extra, indulgence, frill, nonessential
    • We never had money for little luxuries.
    OPPOSITES:  necessity, need
  3. pleasure, delight, comfort, satisfaction, enjoyment, bliss, indulgence, gratification, wellbeing
    • Relax in the luxury of a Roman-style bath.
    OPPOSITES:  difficulty, burden, misery, discomfort, hardship, uphill (South Africa), infliction

QUOTATION:
Give us the luxuries of life, and we will dispense with its necessitiesJohn Lothrop Motley

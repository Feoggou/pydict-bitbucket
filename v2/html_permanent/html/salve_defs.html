salve1

(sælv , sɑːv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an ointment for wounds, sores, etc2. anything that heals or soothesverb (transitive)3. to apply salve to (a wound, sore, etc)4. to soothe, comfort, or appease

Word Origin

Old English sealf; related to Old High German salba, Greek elpos oil, Sanskrit sarpis lard

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

[formal] [V n] ⇒ I give myself treats and justify them to salve my conscience.2. variable nounSalve is an oily substance that is put on sore skin or a wound to help it heal. ⇒ ...a soothing salve for sore, dry lips.

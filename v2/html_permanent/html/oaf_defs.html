(əʊf )
Definitions

Word Origin

oaf for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(oʊf )
Word forms: oafs
Definitions
countable noun [oft adj N]If you refer to someone, especially a man or boy, as an oaf, you think that they are impolite, clumsy, or aggressive. [disapproval] ⇒ Leave the lady alone, you drunken oaf.

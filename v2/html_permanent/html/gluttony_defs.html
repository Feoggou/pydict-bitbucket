gluttony / gluttonousness n. "(ˈɡlʌtənɪ). 1. habitual eating to excess (personified as one of the deadly sins). 2. the act or habit of eating too much and being greedy. ⇒ Gluttony is a deadly sin. 3. over-indulgence and over-consumption of food, drink, or wealth items to the point of extravagance or waste; •greed, rapacity, voracity, greediness, •gula, overeating " بِطْنَة؛ شَرَه؛ نَهَم

abreast of

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
preposition1. level with or in line with someone or something ⇒ He continued to watch her till she was nearly abreast of him., ⇒ As he drew abreast of the man he pretended to stumble.2. up to date with; fully conversant with ⇒ Supposedly middle management is abreast of the very latest business-ethics compliance practices., ⇒ He will be keeping abreast of the news., ⇒ We'll keep you abreast of developments.

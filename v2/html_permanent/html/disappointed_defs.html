Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ that] [ADJ to-inf]If you are disappointed, you are rather sad because something has not happened or because something is not as good as you had hoped. [+ with] ⇒ Castle-hunters won't be disappointed with the Isle of Man. ⇒ I was disappointed that Kluge was not there. ⇒ I was disappointed to see the lack of coverage afforded to this event.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are disappointed in someone, you are rather sad because they have not behaved as well as you expected them to. [+ in] ⇒ You should have accepted that. I'm disappointed in you.

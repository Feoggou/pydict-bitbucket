resinous

Powered by COBUILD
(rezɪnəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is resinous is like resin or contains resin. ⇒ Propolis is a hard resinous substance made by bees from the juices of plants. ⇒ ...great resinous logs burning in the wide hearth.

militia

(mɪˈlɪʃə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a body of citizen (as opposed to professional) soldiers2. an organization containing men enlisted for service in emergency only

Word Origin

C16: from Latin: soldiery, from mīles soldier

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA militia is an organization that operates like an army but whose members are not professional soldiers. ⇒ The troops will not attempt to disarm the warring militias.

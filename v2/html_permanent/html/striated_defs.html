striated

(straɪˈeɪtɪd)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. marked with striae ⇒ New imaging techniques have allowed us to see more clearly than ever before that some hominid bones are striated with deliberate cut marks., ⇒ We primed, painted and overglazed the light wood with a striated walnut finish.2. geology marked with striae ⇒ Imagine a surface, grey and striated, like the moon., ⇒ striated limestone cliffs, ⇒ He had once examined an emerald striated with a strange intrusive rivulet of azure.3. biology, anatomy marked with striae ⇒ The first and most obvious difference is that smooth muscle cells lack the striated banding pattern exhibited by skeletal muscle cells.4. architecture having striae5. having a pattern of scratches or grooves

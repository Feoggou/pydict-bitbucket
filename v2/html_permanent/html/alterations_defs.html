alterations

(ˌɔːltəˈreɪʃənz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. adjustments ⇒ last-minute alterations to the dress2. changes or modifications ⇒ The structural alterations made to the house were planned with Gail's help., ⇒ The alterations to the house cost a fortune.
Example sentences containing 'alterations'
Observe however, how by subtle alterations she preserves similarity to the original sounds while guiding our attitudes to the subject. Ian St James Awards AT THE STROKE OF TWELVEHe had made no notes, and no written alterations to her previous statements.

Word Origin

intact

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪntækt ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]Something that is intact is complete and has not been damaged or changed. in one piece ⇒ Most of the cargo was left intact after the explosion. ⇒ If the family unit is still intact, the patient frequently does very well.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

last-gasp

Definitions
noun(modifier) done in desperation at the last minute ⇒ a last-gasp attempt to save the talks

last gasp

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
phraseYou describe something as the last gasp to emphasize that it is the final part of something or happens at the last possible moment. [emphasis] ⇒ ...the last gasp of a dying system of censorship. ⇒ He snatched a last gasp winner. →  gasp

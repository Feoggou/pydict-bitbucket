foible

(ˈfɔɪbəl )
Definitions
noun1. a slight peculiarity or minor weakness; idiosyncrasy2. the most vulnerable part of a sword's blade, from the middle to the tip. Compare forte1 (sense 2)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from obsolete French, from obsolete adj: feeble

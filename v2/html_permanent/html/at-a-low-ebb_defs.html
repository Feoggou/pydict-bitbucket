at a low ebb

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
in a state or period of weakness, lack of vigour, or decline▷ See ebb

With his own tolerance at a low ebb , he found it difficult not to tell Agnes to keep her mouth shut. Ferrars, Elizabeth UNREASONABLE DOUBTNot surprisingly, her creativity was at a low ebb , so she was doggedly blocking out basic shapes to use later.

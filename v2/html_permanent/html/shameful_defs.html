Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a person's action or attitude as shameful, you think that it is so bad that the person ought to be ashamed. [disapproval] ⇒ ...the most shameful episode in U.S. naval history.shamefully graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj] ⇒ At times they have been shamefully neglected.

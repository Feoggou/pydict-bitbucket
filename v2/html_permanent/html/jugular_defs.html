jugular

(ˈdʒʌɡjʊlə )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or situated near the throat or neck2. of, having, or denoting pelvic fins situated in front of the pectoral fins ⇒ a jugular fishnoun3. short for jugular vein4. See go for the jugular
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Late Latin jugulāris, from Latin jugulum throat

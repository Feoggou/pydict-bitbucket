costa

(ˈkɒstə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -tae (-tiː)1. the technical name for rib1 (sense 1)2. a riblike part, such as the midrib of a plant leaf
Derived forms
costal (ˈcostal) adjectivecostally (ˈcostally) adverb
Word origin
C19: from Latin: rib, side, wall

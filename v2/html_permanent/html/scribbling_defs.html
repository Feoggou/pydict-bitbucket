scribbling

(ˈskrɪblɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a piece of writing or a drawing done in a hasty or illegible manner ⇒ The papers are random scribblings and nothing more.2. pejorative, humorous a piece of creative writing ⇒ He filled his bureau with his impressive poetic scribblings., ⇒ The collection is a set of banal scribblings and little else.
Example sentences containing 'scribbling'
True to the letter, if not the spirit, Armstrong reminded his queasy conscience, scribbling down Baxter's statement. Moore, Margaret FORESTS OF THE NIGHTThey waited, not moving, until Tony was speaking again and then scribbling something in his notebook. Thomas, Rosie THE WHITE DOVEShe could hear the rain on the windows, scribbling through the dust on the panes. Sue Welfare FALLEN WOMEN

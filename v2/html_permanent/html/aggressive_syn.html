aggressive

adjective

  1. hostile, offensive, destructive, belligerent, unkind, unfriendly, malevolent, contrary, antagonistic, pugnacious, bellicose, two-fisted, quarrelsome, aggers (Australian, slang), biffo (Australian, slang), inimical, rancorous, ill-disposed, arsey (British & Australian & & New Zealand, slang) • Some children are much more aggressive than others. OPPOSITES:  friendly, peaceful
  2. forceful, powerful, convincing, effective, enterprising, dynamic, bold, militant, pushing, vigorous, energetic, persuasive, assertive, zealous, pushy (informal), in-your-face (slang), carnivorous (informal) • He is respected as a very competitive and aggressive executive. OPPOSITES:  retiring, quiet, mild, submissive

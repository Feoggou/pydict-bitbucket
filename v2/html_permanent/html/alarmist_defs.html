alarmist

(əˈlɑːmɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who alarms or attempts to alarm others needlessly or without due grounds2. a person who is easily alarmedadjective3. characteristic of an alarmist
Derived forms
aˈlarmism noun

alarmist

Powered by COBUILD
(əlɑːʳmɪst )
Word forms: alarmists
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone or something that is alarmist causes unnecessary fear or anxiety that something unpleasant or dangerous is going to happen. ⇒ Contrary to the more alarmist reports, he is not going to die.2. countable nounAn alarmist is someone who causes unnecessary fear or anxiety that something unpleasant or dangerous might happen.

thrush1

(θrʌʃ )
Definitions
nounany songbird of the subfamily Turdinae, esp those having a brown plumage with a spotted breast, such as the mistle thrush and song thrush: family Muscicapidae. Compare water thrush ▶ Related adjective: turdine
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English thrӯsce; related to Old High German drōsca; see throstle, throat

thrush2

(θrʌʃ )
Definitions
noun1. a. a fungal disease of the mouth, esp of infants, and the genitals, characterized by the formation of whitish spots and caused by infection with the fungus Candida albicansb. another word for sprue12. a softening of the frog of a horse's hoof characterized by degeneration and a thick foul discharge
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: related to Old Danish törsk, Danish troske

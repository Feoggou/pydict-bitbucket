Definitions

also

Powered by COBUILD
(ɔːlsoʊ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adverb [ADV before v]You can use also to give more information about a person or thing, or to add another relevant fact. ⇒ It is the work of Ivor Roberts-Jones, who also produced the statue of Churchill in Parliament Square. ⇒ He is an asthmatic who was also anaemic. ⇒ She has a reputation for brilliance. Also, she is gorgeous.2. adverb [ADV before v]You can use also to indicate that something you have just said about one person or thing is true of another person or thing. ⇒ His father, also a top-ranking officer, had perished during the war. ⇒ We have been working very hard, and our families have also worked hard. ⇒ Not only cancer, but also heart and lung disease are influenced by smoking.

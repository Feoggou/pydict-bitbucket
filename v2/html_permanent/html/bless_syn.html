bless

verb

  1. sanctify, dedicate, ordain, exalt, anoint, consecrate, hallow, invoke happiness on • Bless this couple and their loving commitment to one another. OPPOSITES:  damn, curse, excommunicate, execrate, imprecate, anathematize
  2. endow, give to, provide for, grant, favour, grace, bestow to • If God has seen fit to bless you with this gift, you should use it. OPPOSITES:  trouble, destroy, burden, doom, plague, curse, torment, afflict, blight, scourge, vex
  3. praise, thank, worship, glorify, magnify, exalt, extol, pay homage to, give thanks to • Let us bless God for so uniting our hearts.

honeysuckle

(ˈhʌnɪˌsʌkəl )
Definitions
noun1. any temperate caprifoliaceous shrub or vine of the genus Lonicera: cultivated for their fragrant white, yellow, or pink tubular flowers2. any of several similar plants3. any of various Australian trees or shrubs of the genus Banksia, having flowers in dense spikes: family Proteaceae
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

honeysuckled (ˈhoneyˌsuckled) adjective

Word Origin

Old English hunigsūce, from honey + suck; see suckle

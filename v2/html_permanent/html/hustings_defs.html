hustings

(ˈhʌstɪŋz )
Definitions
noun (functioning as plural or singular)1. British (before 1872) the platform on which candidates were nominated for Parliament and from which they addressed the electors2. the proceedings at a parliamentary election3. political campaigning
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C11: from Old Norse hūsthing, from hūs house + thing assembly

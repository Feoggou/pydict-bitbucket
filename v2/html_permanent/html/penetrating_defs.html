Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA penetrating sound is loud and usually high-pitched. ⇒ Mary heard the penetrating siren of an ambulance. ⇒ Her voice was nasal and penetrating.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If someone gives you a penetrating look, it makes you think that they know what you are thinking. ⇒ He gazed at me with a sharp, penetrating look that made my heart pound. ⇒ ...dark penetrating eyes.3. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Someone who has a penetrating mind understands and recognizes things quickly and thoroughly. ⇒ ...a thoughtful, penetrating mind. ⇒ He never stopped asking penetrating questions.

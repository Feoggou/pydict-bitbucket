infinitude

(ɪnˈfɪnɪˌtjuːd )
Definitions
noun1. the state or quality of being infinite2. an infinite extent, quantity, degree, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'infinitude'
Because, she was beginning to see, there was between people an infinitude of possibilities which might be realised. Salley Vickers INSTANCES OF THE NUMBER 3`Now shall we all, together, reflect upon the infinitude of existence," Leo began with an earnest quaver in his voice. Wilcox, James SORT OF RICH

brimful

(brɪmfʊl )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveSomeone who is brimful of an emotion or quality feels or seems full of it. An object or place that is brimful of something is full of it. [+ of] ⇒ She was brimful of energy and enthusiasm. [+ with] ⇒ The United States is brimful with highly paid doctors.

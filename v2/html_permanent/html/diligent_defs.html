diligent

(ˈdɪlɪdʒənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. careful and persevering in carrying out tasks or duties2. carried out with care and perseverance ⇒ diligent work
Derived forms
diligently (ˈdiligently) adverb
Word origin
C14: from Old French, from Latin dīligere to value, from dis- apart + legere to read

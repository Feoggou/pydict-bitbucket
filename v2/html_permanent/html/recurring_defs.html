Powered by COBUILD
(rɪkɜrɪŋ) in Accounting
Definitions
adjective(Accounting: Basic) If something is recurring, it happens at regularly occurring intervals. ⇒ More than 60 percent of recurring costs were attributable directly to materials, products, and services purchased from external suppliers. ⇒ Include in the expenses accrued for the period all recurring fees that are charged to all shareholder accounts in proportion to the length of the base period. ⇒ If something is recurring, it happens at regularly occurring intervals.

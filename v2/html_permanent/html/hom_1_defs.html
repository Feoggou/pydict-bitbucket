hom

(hɒm ), homa (ˈhɒmə ) or haoma (ˈhəʊmə)
Definitions
noun1. a sacred plant of the Parsees and ancient Persians2. a drink made from this plant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

from Persian, from Avestan haoma

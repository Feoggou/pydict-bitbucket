irreducible

(ˌɪrɪˈdjuːsɪbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. not able to be reduced or lessened2. not able to be brought to a simpler or reduced form3. mathematicsa. (of a polynomial) unable to be factorized into polynomials of lower degree, as (x2 + 1)b. (of a radical) incapable of being reduced to a rational expression, as √(x + 1)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

irreducibility (ˌirreˌduciˈbility) or irreducibleness (ˌirreˈducibleness) nounirreducibly (ˌirreˈducibly) adverb

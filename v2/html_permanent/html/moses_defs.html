Moses

(ˈməʊzɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Old Testament the Hebrew prophet who led the Israelites out of Egypt to the Promised Land and gave them divinely revealed laws2.  Ed. born 1956, US hurdler; winner of the 400 m hurdles in the 1976 and 1984 Olympic Games3.  Grandma, real name Anna Mary Robertson Moses. 1860–1961, US painter of primitives, who began to paint at the age of 75

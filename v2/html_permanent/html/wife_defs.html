wife

(waɪf )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural wives (waɪvz )1. one's (female) partner in marriage; a married woman ▶ Related adjective: uxorial2. an archaic or dialect word for woman3. See take to wife
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

wifehood (ˈwifehood) nounwifeless (ˈwifeless) adjectivewifelike (ˈwifeˌlike) adjectivewifeliness (ˈwifeliness) nounwifely (ˈwifely) adjective

Word Origin

Old English wīf; related to Old Norse vīf (perhaps from vīfathr veiled), Old High German wīb (German Weib)

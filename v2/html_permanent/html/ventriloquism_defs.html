ventriloquism

(vɛnˈtrɪləˌkwɪzəm ) or ventriloquy
Definitions
nounthe art of producing vocal sounds that appear to come from another source
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ventriloquial (ˌvɛntrɪˈləʊkwɪəl ) or ventriloqual (vɛnˈtrɪləkwəl ) adjectiveventriloquially (ˌventriˈloquially) adverbventriloquist (venˈtriloquist) nounventriloquistic (venˌtriloˈquistic) adjective

Word Origin

C18: from Latin venter belly + loquī to speak

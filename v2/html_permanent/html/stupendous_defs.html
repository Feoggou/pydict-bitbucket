stupendous

(stjuːˈpɛndəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveastounding, wonderful, huge, etc

Derived Forms

stupendously (stuˈpendously) adverbstupendousness (stuˈpendousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin stupēre to be amazed

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Something that is stupendous is surprisingly impressive or large. ⇒ He was a man of stupendous stamina and energy. ⇒ This stupendous novel keeps you gripped to the end. ⇒ ...a stupendous amount of money.stupendously adverb ⇒ He is a stupendously swift writer.

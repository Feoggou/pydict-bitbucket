hubcap

Powered by COBUILD
(hʌbkæp) in Automotive Engineering
Word forms: (regular plural) hubcaps
Definitions
noun(Automotive engineering: Vehicle components, Brakes, steering, wheels, tires, and suspension) A hubcap is a metal or plastic disk that covers and protects the center of a wheel on a vehicle. ⇒ The hubcaps were missing from nearside wheels. ⇒ He got a tire iron out of the trunk and took the hubcaps off the wheels of his car. ⇒ The hubcap came off one of the wheels on the car and went rolling and clattering down the main street.

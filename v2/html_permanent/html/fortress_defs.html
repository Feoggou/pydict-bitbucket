fortress

(ˈfɔːtrɪs )
Definitions
noun1. a large fort or fortified town2. a place or source of refuge or supportverb3. (transitive) to protect with or as if with a fortress
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old French forteresse, from Medieval Latin fortalitia, from Latin fortis strong

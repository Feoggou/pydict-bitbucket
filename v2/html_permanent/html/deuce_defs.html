deuce1

(djuːs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a playing card or dice with two pips or spots; twob. a throw of two in dice2. tennis a tied score (in tennis 40-all) that requires one player to gain two successive points to win the game
Word origin
C15: from Old French deus two, from Latin duos, accusative masculine of duo two

deuce2 - informal

(djuːs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
exclamation1. an expression of annoyance or frustrationnoun2. See the deuce
Word origin
C17: probably special use of deuce1 (in the sense: lowest throw at dice)

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounDeuce is the score in a game of tennis when both players have forty points. One player has to win two points one after the other to win the game.

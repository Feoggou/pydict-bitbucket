cleaner

(ˈkliːnə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person, device, chemical agent, etc, that removes dirt, as from clothes or carpets2. (usually plural) a shop, etc, that provides a dry-cleaning service3. See take a person to the cleaners

(kliːnəʳ )
Word forms: cleaners
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA cleaner is someone who is employed to clean the rooms and furniture inside a building.2. countable noun [n N]A cleaner is someone whose job is to clean a particular type of thing. ⇒ He was a window cleaner.3. variable noun [usu n N]A cleaner is a substance used for cleaning things. ⇒ ...oven cleaner. ⇒ ...abrasive cleaners.4. countable noun [usu n N]A cleaner is a device used for cleaning things. ⇒ ...an air cleaner.5. → see also pipe cleaner, vacuum cleaner6. countable nounA cleaner or a cleaner's is a shop where things such as clothes are dry-cleaned.7. →  to take sb to the cleaners

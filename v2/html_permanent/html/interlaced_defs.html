interlaced

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪntəʳleɪst ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveIf things are interlaced, parts of one thing go over, under, or between parts of another. [written] ⇒ During my whole report, he sat with his eyes closed and his fingers interlaced. [+ with] ⇒ ...languid women, their flowing locks interlaced with flowers and vines.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

verdure

(ˈvɜːdʒə )
Definitions
noun1. flourishing green vegetation or its colour2. a condition of freshness or healthy growth
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

verdured (ˈverdured) adjectiveverdurous (ˈverdurous) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Old French verd green, from Latin viridis
Example sentences containing 'verdure'
The Zangre's cliff wall, naked of verdure in the winter, rose high and stark across the dark, rapid water of the river. Lois McMaster Bujold THE CURSE OF CHALION

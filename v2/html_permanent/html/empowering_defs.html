empowering

(ɪmˈpaʊərɪŋ)
Definitions
adjectiveapproving having qualities that give a person or a group of people the means to take more control of their lives and become stronger and more independent ⇒ Such experiences can be empowering., ⇒ Some of us find it enormously empowering and liberating not to have any sexual contact at all.

bougie

(ˈbuːʒiː , buːˈʒiː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounmedicine a long slender semiflexible cylindrical instrument for inserting into body passages, such as the rectum or urethra, to dilate structures, introduce medication, etc
Word origin
C18: from French, originally a wax candle from Bougie (Bujiya), Algeria

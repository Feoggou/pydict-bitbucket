castor1

(ˈkɑːstə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the brownish aromatic secretion of the anal glands of a beaver, used in perfumery and medicine2. the fur of the beaver3. a hat made of beaver or similar fur4. a less common name for beaver1 (sense 1)
Word origin
C14: from Latin, from Greek kastōr beaver

Castor

(ˈkɑːstə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the second brightest star, Alpha Geminorum, in the constellation Gemini: a multiple star consisting of six components lying close to the star Pollux. Distance: 52 light years2. classical mythology See Castor and Pollux

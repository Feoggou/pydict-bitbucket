heading

(ˈhɛdɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. a title for a page, paragraph, chapter, etc2. a main division, as of a lecture, speech, essay, etc3. mininga. a horizontal tunnelb. the end of such a tunnel4. the angle between the direction of an aircraft and a specified meridian, often due north5. the compass direction parallel to the keel of a vessel6. the act of heading7. anything that serves as a head

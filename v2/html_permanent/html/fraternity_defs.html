fraternity

(frəˈtɜːnɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. a body of people united in interests, aims, etc ⇒ the teaching fraternity. Gender-neutral form: community2. brotherhood3. US and Canadian a secret society joined by male students, usually functioning as a social club

fraternity

Powered by COBUILD
(frətɜːʳnɪti ) for learners of English
Word forms: fraternities
Definitions
1. uncount nounFraternity refers to friendship and support between people who feel they are closely linked to each other. [formal] comradeship ⇒ Bob needs the fraternity of others who share his mission.2. countable nounYou can refer to people who have the same profession or the same interests as a particular fraternity. ⇒ ...the spread of stolen guns among the criminal fraternity. ⇒ ...the sailing fraternity.3. countable nounIn the United States, a fraternity is a society of male university or college students.

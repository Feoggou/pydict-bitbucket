asparagus

(əˈspærəɡəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any Eurasian liliaceous plant of the genus Asparagus, esp the widely cultivated A. officinalis, having small scaly or needle-like leaves2. the succulent young shoots of A. officinalis, which may be cooked and eaten3. See asparagus fern
Word origin
C15: from Latin, from Greek asparagos, of obscure origin

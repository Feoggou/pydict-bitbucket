hankering

Powered by COBUILD
(hæŋkərɪŋ ) for learners of English
Word forms: hankerings
Definitions
countable noun [N to-inf]A hankering for something is a desire or longing for it. [+ for/after] ⇒ From time to time we all get a hankering for something a little different. ⇒ Have you always had a hankering to be an actress?

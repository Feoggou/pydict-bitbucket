mouldy

or US moldy (ˈməʊldɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: mouldier, mouldiestWord forms: US moldier or moldiest1. covered with mould2. stale or musty, esp from age or lack of use3. slang boring; dull

Derived Forms

mouldiness (ˈmouldiness) or US moldiness (ˈmoldiness) noun

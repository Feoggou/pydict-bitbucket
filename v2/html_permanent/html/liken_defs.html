liken

(ˈlaɪkən )
Definitions
verb(transitive) to see or represent as the same or similar; compare
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from like1 (adj)

liken

Powered by COBUILD
(laɪkən ) for learners of English
Word forms: likens , likening , likened
Definitions
verbIf you liken one thing or person to another thing or person, you say that they are similar. [V n to n/-ing] ⇒ She likens marriage to slavery. [be V-ed + to] ⇒ The pain is often likened to being drilled through the side of the head.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

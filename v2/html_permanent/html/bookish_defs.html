bookish

(ˈbʊkɪʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. fond of reading; studious2. consisting of or forming opinions or attitudes through reading rather than direct personal experience; academic ⇒ a bookish view of life3. of or relating to books ⇒ a bookish career in publishing
Derived forms
bookishly (ˈbookishly) adverbbookishness (ˈbookishness) noun

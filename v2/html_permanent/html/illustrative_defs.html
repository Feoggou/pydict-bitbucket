Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you use something as an illustrative example, or for illustrative purposes, you use it to show that what you are saying is true or to make your meaning clearer. [formal] ⇒ A second illustrative example was taken from The Observer newspaper. ⇒ The following excerpt is illustrative of her interaction with students. [Also + of]

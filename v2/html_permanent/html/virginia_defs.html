(vəˈdʒɪnɪə )
Definitions

Virginia2

(vəˈdʒɪnɪə )
Definitions
nouna state of the eastern US, on the Atlantic: site of the first permanent English settlement in North America; consists of a low-lying deeply indented coast rising inland to the Piedmont plateau and the Blue Ridge Mountains. Capital: Richmond. Pop: 7 386 330 (2003 est). Area: 103 030 sq km (39 780 sq miles). Abbreviation: Va. or (with zip code) VA

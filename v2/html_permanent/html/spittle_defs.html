spittle

(ˈspɪtəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the fluid secreted in the mouth; saliva or spit2. Also called: cuckoo spit, frog spit the frothy substance secreted on plants by the larvae of certain froghoppers

Word Origin

Old English spǣtl saliva; see spit1

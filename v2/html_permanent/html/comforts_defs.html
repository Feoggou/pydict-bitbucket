comforts

(ˈkʌmfəts)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounthings that make life easier and more pleasant ⇒ she enjoys the material comforts her career has bought her, ⇒ Electricity provides us with warmth and light and all our modern home comforts., ⇒ I do like my comforts.

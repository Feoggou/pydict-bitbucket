floral

(ˈflɔːrəl )
Definitions
adjective1. decorated with or consisting of flowers or patterns of flowers2. of, relating to, or associated with flowers ⇒ floral leaves
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

florally (ˈflorally) adverb

floral

Powered by COBUILD
(flɔːrəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]A floral fabric or design has flowers on it. ⇒ ...a bright yellow floral fabric.2. adjective [ADJ n]You can use floral to describe something that contains flowers or is made of flowers. ⇒ ...eye-catching floral arrangements.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

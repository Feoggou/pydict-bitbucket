two-thirds

or two thirds (ˌtuːˈθɜːdz)
Definitions
pronoun1. two out of three equal parts of something ⇒ two-thirds of householders, ⇒ Stir the chocolate, fruit and nuts into two-thirds of the cheese mixture.adverb2. to the extent of being two out of three equal parts of something ⇒ A second book has already been commissioned and is two-thirds finished., ⇒ Do not fill the container more than two-thirds full.adjective3. of, relating to, or consisting of two out of three equal parts of something ⇒ The two thirds majority in parliament needed to make constitutional changes.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

two-thirds

Powered by COBUILD
also two thirds for learners of English
Definitions
quantifierTwo-thirds of something is an amount that is two out of three equal parts of it. [+ of] ⇒ Two-thirds of householders in this country live in a mortgaged home. ⇒ Stir the chopped chocolate, fruit and nuts into two-thirds of the cheese mixture.Two-thirds is also a pronoun. ⇒ The United States and Russia hope to conclude a treaty to cut their nuclear arsenals by two-thirds.Two-thirds is also an adverb. ⇒ Do not fill the container more than two-thirds full. ⇒ A second book has already been commissioned and is two-thirds finished.Two-thirds is also an adjective. ⇒ ...the two thirds majority in parliament needed to make constitutional changes.

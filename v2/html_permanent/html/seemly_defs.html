seemly

(ˈsiːmlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -lier or -liest1. proper or fitting2. obsolete pleasing or handsome in appearanceadverb3. archaic properly or decorously

Derived Forms

seemliness (ˈseemliness) noun

Word Origin

C13: from Old Norse sœomiligr, from sœmr befitting

hiking

(ˈhaɪkɪŋ)
Definitions
nounthe sporting or leisure activity of going for long, often strenuous, walks in the country ⇒ This is the perfect base for hiking, white-water rafting and fly-fishing., ⇒ Also included in the price of the holiday are two days hiking in the High Atlas mountains., ⇒ some harder, more strenuous hiking on cliff pathways

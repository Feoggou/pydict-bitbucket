mangy

or mangey (ˈmeɪndʒɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -gier or -giest1. having or caused by mange ⇒ a mangy dog2. scruffy or shabby ⇒ a mangy carpet3. Irish informal stingy or miserly ⇒ a mangy reward

Derived Forms

mangily (ˈmangily) adverbmanginess (ˈmanginess) noun

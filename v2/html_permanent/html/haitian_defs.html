Haitian

(ˈheɪʃɪən , hɑːˈiːʃən )
Definitions
adjective1. relating to or characteristic of Haiti, its inhabitants, or their languagenoun2. a native, citizen, or inhabitant of Haiti3. the creolized French spoken in Haiti

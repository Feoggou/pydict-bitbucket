enliven

(ɪnˈlaɪvən )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to make active, vivacious, or spirited; invigorate2. to make cheerful or bright; gladden or brighten
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

enlivener (enˈlivener) nounenlivening (enˈlivening) adjectiveenlivenment (enˈlivenment) noun

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnlaɪvən ) for learners of English
Word forms: enlivens , enlivening , enlivened
Definitions
verbTo enliven events, situations, or people means to make them more lively or cheerful. [V n] ⇒ I love the way a good flirtation can enliven the most mundane situation. [be V-ed] ⇒ Even the most boring meeting was enlivened by Dan's presence.

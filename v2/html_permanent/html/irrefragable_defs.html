irrefragable

(ɪˈrɛfrəɡəbəl )
Definitions
adjectivenot able to be denied or refuted; indisputable
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

irrefragability (irˌrefragaˈbility) or irrefragableness (irˈrefragableness) nounirrefragably (irˈrefragably) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Late Latin irrefrāgābilis, from Latin ir- + refrāgārī to resist, thwart

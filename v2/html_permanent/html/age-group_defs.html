age group

(eɪdʒ ɡruːp)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe group of people born at a particular time ⇒ a shortage of jobs in this age group, ⇒ a style that would appeal to all age groups, ⇒ the 40 to 50 age group

Word forms: age groups
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounAn age group is the people in a place or organization who were born during a particular period of time, for example all the people aged between 18 and 25. ⇒ ...a style that would appeal to all age groups.

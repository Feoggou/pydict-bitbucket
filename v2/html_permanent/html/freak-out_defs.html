Definitions
nouninformal an episode of bizarre experience or behaviour ⇒ When I had a freak-out in a Council session, we decided to try to share the pressures we felt in order to avoid things getting on top of us.

ergative phrasal verb & phrasal verbIf someone freaks out, or if something freaks them out, they suddenly feel extremely surprised, upset, angry, or confused.

real estate

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounReal estate is property in the form of land and buildings, rather than personal possessions. [mainly US] ⇒ By investing in real estate, he was one of the richest men in the United States. ⇒ ...the most spectacular piece of real estate he had ever imagined.2. uncount noun [usu N n]Real estate businesses or real estate agents sell houses, buildings, and land. [US] ⇒ ...the real estate agent who sold you your house. ⇒ ...the real estate industry. ⇒ He had a long career in real estate.regional note:   in BRIT, use estate agency, estate agents

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as methodical, you mean that they do things carefully, thoroughly, and in order. ⇒ Da Vinci was methodical in his research, carefully recording his observations and theories. ⇒ It seemed a sensible and methodical way of proceeding.methodically (məθɒdɪkli ) graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ She methodically put the things into her suitcase.

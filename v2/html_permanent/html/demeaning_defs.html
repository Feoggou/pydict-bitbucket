(dɪˈmiːnɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveintended to lower a person's dignity, status, or character ⇒ demeaning sexist comments, ⇒ the demeaning treatment of prisoners, ⇒ Aid, however it is obtained, is demeaning to the recipients.

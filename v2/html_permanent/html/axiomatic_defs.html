axiomatic

(ˌæksɪəˈmætɪk ) or axiomatical
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. relating to or resembling an axiom; self-evident2. containing maxims; aphoristic3. (of a logical system) consisting of a set of axioms from which theorems are derived by transformation rules. Compare natural deduction
Derived forms
ˌaxiomatically (ˌaxioˈmatically) adverb

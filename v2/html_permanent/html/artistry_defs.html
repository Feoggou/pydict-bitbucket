Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount noun [oft with poss]Artistry is the creative skill of an artist, writer, actor, or musician. ⇒ ...his artistry as a cellist. ⇒ ...portrait sculptors of considerable skill and artistry.2. uncount nounYou can use artistry to refer to a high level of skill in a sport or activity. ⇒ John McEnroe beat Jimmy Connors with the most complete display of artistry the game had ever seen. ⇒ Tea processing requires both technique and artistry.

turning

(ˈtɜːnɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: turn a road, river, or path that turns off the main way ⇒ the fourth turning on the right2. the point where such a way turns off3. a bend in a straight course4. an object made on a lathe5. another name for turnery (sense 2)6. (plural) the waste produced in turning on a lathe

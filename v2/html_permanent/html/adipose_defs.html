adipose

(ˈædɪˌpəʊs , -ˌpəʊz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, resembling, or containing fat; fattynoun2. animal fat
Word origin
C18: from New Latin adiposus, from Latin adeps fat

Similar Words

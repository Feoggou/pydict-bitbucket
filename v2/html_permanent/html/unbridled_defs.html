unbridled

(ʌnˈbraɪdəld )
Definitions
adjective1. with all restraints removed2. (of a horse, etc) wearing no bridle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unbridledly (unˈbridledly) adverbunbridledness (unˈbridledness) noun

unbridled for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnbraɪdəld )
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe behaviour or feelings as unbridled, you mean that they are not controlled or limited in any way. ⇒ ...the unbridled greed of the 1980s. ⇒ ...a tale of lust and unbridled passion.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

public servant

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an elected or appointed holder of a public office2. Australian and New Zealand a member of the public service. British equivalent: civil servant

countable nounA public servant is a person who is appointed or elected to a public office, for example working for a local or state government.

religiosity

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪlɪdʒiɒsɪti ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIf you refer to a person's religiosity, you are referring to the fact that they are religious in a way which seems exaggerated and insincere. [formal] ⇒ ...their hypocritical religiosity.

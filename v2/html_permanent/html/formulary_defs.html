formulary

(ˈfɔːmjʊlərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -laries1. a book or system of prescribed formulas, esp relating to religious procedure or doctrine2. a formula3. pharmacology a book containing a list of pharmaceutical products, with their formulas and means of preparationadjective4. of, relating to, or of the nature of a formula

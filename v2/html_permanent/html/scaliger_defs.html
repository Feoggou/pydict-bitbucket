Scaliger

(ˈskælɪdʒə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Joseph Justus (ˈdʒʌstəs). 1540–1609, French scholar, who revolutionized the study of ancient chronology by his work De Emendatione temporum (1583)2.  his father, Julius Caesar. 1484–1558, Italian classical scholar, and writer on biology and medicine

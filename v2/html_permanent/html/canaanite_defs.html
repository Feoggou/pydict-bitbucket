Canaanite

(ˈkeɪnəˌnaɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a member of an ancient Semitic people who occupied the land of Canaan before the Israelite conquest2. the extinct language of this people, belonging to the Canaanitic branch of the Semitic subfamily of the Afro-Asiatic family3. (in later books of the Old Testament) a merchant or trader (Job 40:30; Proverbs 31:24)

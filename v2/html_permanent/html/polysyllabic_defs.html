polysyllabic

(ˌpɒlɪsɪˈlæbɪk ) or polysyllabical (ˌpɒlɪsɪˈlæbɪkəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveconsisting of more than two syllables

Derived Forms

ˌpolysyllabically (ˌpolysylˈlabically) adverb

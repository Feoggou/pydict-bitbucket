haven

(ˈheɪvən )
Definitions
noun1. a port, harbour, or other sheltered place for shipping2. a place of safety or sanctuary; shelterverb3. (transitive) to secure or shelter in or as if in a haven
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

havenless (ˈhavenless) adjective

Word Origin

Old English hæfen, from Old Norse höfn; related to Middle Dutch havene, Old Irish cuan to bend

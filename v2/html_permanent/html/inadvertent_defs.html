inadvertent

(ˌɪnədˈvɜːtənt )
Definitions
adjective1. failing to act carefully or considerately; inattentive2. resulting from heedless action; unintentional
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inadvertently (ˌinadˈvertently) adverb

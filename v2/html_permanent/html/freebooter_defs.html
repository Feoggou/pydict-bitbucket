freebooter

(ˈfriːˌbuːtə )
Definitions
noun1. a person, such as a pirate, living from plunder2. informal a person, esp an itinerant, who seeks pleasure, wealth, etc, without responsibility
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Dutch vrijbuiter, from vrijbuit booty; see filibuster
Example sentences containing 'freebooter'
He could feel a deep dread building that he might be reduced to a penniless freebooter as quickly as he had risen to prominence. Raymond E. Feist RISE OF A MERCHANT PRINCE: BOOK TWO OF THE SERPENTWAR SAGA

dimmer

(ˈdɪmə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a device, such as a rheostat, for varying the current through an electric light and thus changing the illumination2. (often plural) USa. a dipped headlight on a road vehicleb. a parking light on a car

dimmer

Powered by COBUILD
(dɪməʳ )
Word forms: dimmers
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA dimmer or a dimmer switch is a switch that allows you to gradually change the brightness of an electric light.

mousse

(muːs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a light creamy dessert made with eggs, cream, fruit, etc, set with gelatine2. a similar dish made from fish or meat3. the layer of small bubbles on the top of a glass of champagne or other sparkling wine4. short for styling mousse

Word Origin

C19: from French: froth

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounMousse is a sweet light food made from eggs and cream. It is often flavoured with fruit or chocolate.2. variable nounMousse is a soft substance containing a lot of tiny bubbles, for example one that you can put in your hair to make it easier to shape into a particular style.

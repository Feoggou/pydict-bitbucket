unnoticed for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnnoʊtɪst )
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ after v, oft v-link ADJ, ADJ n]If something happens or passes unnoticed, it is not seen or noticed by anyone. ⇒ I tried to slip up the stairs unnoticed. [+ by] ⇒ Her forty-fourth birthday had just passed, unnoticed by all but herself.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

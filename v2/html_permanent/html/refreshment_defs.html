refreshment

(rɪˈfrɛʃmənt )
Definitions
noun1. the act of refreshing or the state of being refreshed2. (plural) snacks and drinks served as a light meal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

refreshment

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪfreʃmənt ) for learners of English
Word forms: refreshments
Definitions
1. plural nounRefreshments are drinks and small amounts of food that are provided, for example, during a meeting or a journey.2. uncount nounYou can refer to food and drink as refreshment. [formal] ⇒ May I offer you some refreshment?

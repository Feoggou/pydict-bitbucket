Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverbIf you say that something is doubtless the case, you mean that you think it is probably or almost certainly the case. no doubt ⇒ He will doubtless try and persuade his colleagues to change their minds. ⇒ Doubtless he was justified in some of his criticism of the media. ⇒ She took off her shoes, doubtless because her feet hurt.

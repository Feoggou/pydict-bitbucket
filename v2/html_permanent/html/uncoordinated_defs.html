uncoordinated

(ˌʌnkəʊˈɔːdɪˌneɪtɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. lacking order, system, or organization2. (of a person, action, etc) lacking muscular or emotional coordination
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

uncoordinated for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnkoʊɔːʳdɪneɪtɪd ) also unco-ordinated
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as uncoordinated you mean that their movements are not smooth or controlled. ⇒ They were unsteady on their feet and rather uncoordinated. ⇒ ...an uncoordinated toddler.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe actions or plans as uncoordinated, you mean they are not well-organized. ⇒ Government action has been half-hearted and uncoordinated. ⇒ ...late, uncoordinated and piecemeal enemy responses.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

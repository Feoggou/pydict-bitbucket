Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnmɜːʳsɪfʊli )
Definitions
adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj]If you do something unmercifully, you do it a lot, showing no pity. ⇒ Uncle Sebastian used to tease Mother and Daddy unmercifully that all they could produce was girls.

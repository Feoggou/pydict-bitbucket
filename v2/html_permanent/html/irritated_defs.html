irritated

(ˈɪrɪteɪtɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. annoyed or angered ⇒ Perhaps they were irritated by the sound of crying., ⇒ Not surprisingly, her teacher is getting irritated with her.2. (of a bodily organ or part) excessively stimulated, resulting in inflammation, tenderness, etc

pas

(pɑː , French pɑ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural pas (pɑːz , French pɑ)1. a dance step or movement, esp in ballet2. rare the right to precede; precedence

Word Origin

C18: French, literally: step

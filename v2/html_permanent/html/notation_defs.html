notation

(nəʊˈteɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. any series of signs or symbols used to represent quantities or elements in a specialized system, such as music or mathematics2. the act or process of notating3. a. the act of noting downb. a note or record
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

notational (noˈtational) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Latin notātiō a marking, from notāre to note

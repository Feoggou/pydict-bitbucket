furore

(fjʊˈrɔːrɪ ) or especially US furor (ˈfjʊərɔː )
Definitions
noun1. a public outburst, esp of protest; uproar2. a sudden widespread enthusiasm for something; craze3. frenzy; rage; madness
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Latin: frenzy, rage, from furere to rave

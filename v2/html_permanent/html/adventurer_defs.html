adventurer

(ədˈvɛntʃərə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who seeks adventure, esp one who seeks success or money through daring exploits2. a person who seeks money or power by unscrupulous means3. a speculator

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounAn adventurer is a person who enjoys going to new, unusual, and exciting places.2. countable nounIf you describe someone as an adventurer, you disapprove of them because they are using dishonest or immoral methods to gain money or power. [disapproval] ⇒ ...unprincipled opportunists and ambitious political adventurers.

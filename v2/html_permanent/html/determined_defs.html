Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [oft ADJ to-inf]If you are determined to do something, you have made a firm decision to do it and will not let anything stop you. ⇒ His enemies are determined to ruin him. ⇒ She is a remarkably adroit and determined politician. ⇒ He made determined efforts to overcome the scandal.determinedly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ She shook her head, determinedly.

Daniel1

(ˈdænjəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Old Testamenta. a youth who was taken into the household of Nebuchadnezzar, received guidance and apocalyptic visions from God, and was given divine protection when thrown into the lions' denb. the book that recounts these experiences and visions (in full The Book of the Prophet Daniel)2. (often preceded by a) a wise upright person
Word origin
sense 2: referring to Daniel in the Apocryphal Book of Susanna

broth

(brɒθ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a soup made by boiling meat, fish, vegetables, etc, in water2. another name for stock (sense 19)
Word origin
Old English broth; related to Old Norse broth, Old High German brod, German brodeln to boil; see brew

aquatic

(əˈkwætɪk , əˈkwɒt-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. growing, living, or found in water2. sport performed in or on waternoun3. a marine or freshwater animal or plant
Word origin
C15: from Latin aquāticus, from aqua water

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]An aquatic animal or plant lives or grows on or in water. ⇒ The pond is quite small but can support many aquatic plants and fish. ⇒ ...aquatic birds.2. adjectiveAquatic means relating to water. ⇒ ...aquatic consultant Ben Tucker. ⇒ ...our aquatic resources.

aliment

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (ˈælɪmənt )1. something that nourishes or sustains the body or mind2. Scots law another term for alimonyverb (ˈælɪˌmɛnt )3. (transitive) obsolete to support or sustain
Derived forms
ˌalimental (ˌaliˈmental) adjective
Word origin
C15: from Latin alimentum food, from alere to nourish

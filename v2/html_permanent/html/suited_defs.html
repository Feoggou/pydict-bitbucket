suited

Powered by COBUILD
(suːtɪd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ] [ADJ to-inf]If something is well suited to a particular purpose, it is right or appropriate for that purpose. If someone is well suited to a particular job, they are right or appropriate for that job. [+ to] ⇒ The area is well suited to road cycling as well as off-road riding. [+ to] ⇒ Satellites are uniquely suited to provide this information.2. graded adjective [v-link ADJ, usu adv ADJ, oft ADJ to n]If two people, especially a man and a woman, are well suited, they are likely to have a successful relationship because they have similar personalities or interests. ⇒ They were well suited to each other.

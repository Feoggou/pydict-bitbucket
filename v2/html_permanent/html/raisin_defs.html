raisin

(ˈreɪzən )
Definitions
nouna dried grape
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

raisiny (ˈraisiny) adjective

Word Origin

C13: from Old French: grape, ultimately from Latin racēmus cluster of grapes; compare Greek rhax berry, grape

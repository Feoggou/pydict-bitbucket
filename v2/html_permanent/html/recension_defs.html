recension

(rɪˈsɛnʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a critical revision of a literary work2. a text revised in this way
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin recēnsiō, from recēnsēre to survey, from re- + cēnsēre to assess

skilful

or US skillful (ˈskɪlfʊl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. possessing or displaying accomplishment or skill2. involving or requiring accomplishment or skill

Derived Forms

regional note:   in AM, use skillfulgraded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is skilful at something does it very well. ⇒ He is widely regarded as Hungary's most skilful politician. ⇒ ...Rembrandt's skilful use of light and shade.skilfully graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ He had a clear idea of his company's strengths and skilfully exploited them.

beauty contest

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a competition in which the participants, usually women, are judged on their attractiveness, with a prize, and often a title, awarded to the winner2. informal any contest decided on the basis of superficial attractiveness, popularity, etc ⇒ the referendum might turn into a party political beauty contest

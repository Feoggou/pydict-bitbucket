attire

(əˈtaɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (transitive) to dress, esp in fine elegant clothes; arraynoun2. clothes or garments, esp if fine or decorative3. the antlers of a mature male deer
Word origin
C13: from Old French atirier to put in order, from tire row; see tier1

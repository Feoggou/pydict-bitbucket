buxom

(ˈbʌksəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (esp of a woman) healthily plump, attractive, and vigorous2. (of a woman) full-bosomed
Derived forms
buxomly (ˈbuxomly) adverbbuxomness (ˈbuxomness) noun
Word origin
C12: buhsum compliant, pliant, from Old English būgan to bend, bow1; related to Middle Dutch būchsam pliant, German biegsam

graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a woman as buxom, you mean that she looks healthy and attractive and has a rounded body and big breasts. ⇒ Melissa was a tall, buxom blonde.

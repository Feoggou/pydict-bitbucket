intentional

(ɪnˈtɛnʃənəl )
Definitions
adjective1. performed by or expressing intention; deliberate2. of or relating to intention or purpose3. philosophya. of or relating to the capacity of the mind to refer to different kinds of objectsb. (of an object) existing only as the object of some mental attitude rather than in reality, as a unicorn in she hopes to meet a unicorn. See also intensional

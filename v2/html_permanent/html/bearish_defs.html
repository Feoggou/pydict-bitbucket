bearish

(ˈbɛərɪʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. like a bear; rough; clumsy; churlish2. stock exchange causing, expecting, or characterized by a fall in prices ⇒ a bearish market
Derived forms
bearishly (ˈbearishly) adverbbearishness (ˈbearishness) noun

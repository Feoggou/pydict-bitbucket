floury

Powered by COBUILD
(flaʊəri ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is floury is covered with flour or tastes of flour. ⇒ She wiped her floury hands on her apron. ⇒ ...floury scones.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Floury potatoes go soft round the edges and break up when they are cooked.

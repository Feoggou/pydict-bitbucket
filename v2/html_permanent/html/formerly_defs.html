formerly

(ˈfɔːməlɪ )
Definitions
adverb1. at or in a former time; in the past2. obsolete in the immediate past; just now
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

formerly

Powered by COBUILD
(fɔːʳməʳli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [ADV before v]If something happened or was true formerly, it happened or was true in the past. ⇒ He had formerly been in the Navy. ⇒ ...east Germany's formerly state-controlled companies.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

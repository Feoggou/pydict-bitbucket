Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If you are fed up, you are unhappy, bored, or tired of something, especially something that you have been experiencing for a long time. [informal] cheesed off ⇒ I am fed up with reading how women should dress to please men. [+ with] ⇒ He had become fed up with city life. ⇒ I'm just fed up and I don't know what to do.

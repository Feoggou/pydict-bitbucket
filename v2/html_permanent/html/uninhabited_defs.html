uninhabited

(ˌʌnɪnˈhæbɪtɪd )
Definitions
adjective(of a place) not having inhabitants ⇒ an uninhabited island

Definitions
adjectiveAn uninhabited place is one where nobody lives. ⇒ ...an uninhabited island in the North Pacific. ⇒ The area is largely uninhabited. ⇒ ...a charred, uninhabited farmhouse.

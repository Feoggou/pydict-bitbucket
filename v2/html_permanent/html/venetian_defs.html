Venetian

(vɪˈniːʃən )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Venice or its inhabitantsnoun2. a native or inhabitant of Venice3. See Venetian blind4. (sometimes not capital) one of the tapes that join the slats of a Venetian blind5. a cotton or woollen cloth used for linings

mountaineer

(ˌmaʊntɪˈnɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who climbs mountains2. a person living in a mountainous areaverb3. (intransitive) to climb mountains

Derived Forms

ˌmountaineering (ˌmountainˈeering) noun

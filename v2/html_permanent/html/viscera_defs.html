viscera

(ˈvɪsərə )
Definitions
plural nounWord forms: singular viscus (ˈvɪskəs )1. anatomy the large internal organs of the body collectively, esp those in the abdominal cavity ▶ Related adjective: splanchnic2. (less formally) the intestines; guts
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin: entrails, pl of viscus internal organ

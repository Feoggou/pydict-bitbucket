full dress

Definitions
nouna. a formal or ceremonial style of dress, such as white tie and tails for a man and a full-length evening dress for a womanb. (as modifier) ⇒ full-dress uniform

full-dress

(ˈfʊlˌdrɛs)
Definitions
adjective1. clothing appropriate to a public ceremony, a formal dinner, or evening party ⇒ full-dress uniform2. complete; exhaustive; thorough ⇒ They had a full-dress discussion on what should be done.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

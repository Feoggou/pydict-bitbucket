unsullied for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnsʌlid )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is unsullied, it has not been spoiled or made less pure by the addition of something unpleasant or unacceptable. [literary] ⇒ She had the combined talents of toughness, intellect, experience and unsullied reputation. [+ by] ⇒ He smiled, unsullied by doubt.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

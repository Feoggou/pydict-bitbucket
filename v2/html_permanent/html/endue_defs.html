endue

or indue (ɪnˈdjuː )
Definitions
verb -dues, -duing or -dued (transitive)1. (usually foll by with) to invest or provide, as with some quality or trait2. rare (foll by with) to clothe or dress (in)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French enduire, from Latin indūcere, from dūcere to lead

Similar Words

provide, supply, fill, invest, furnish, endow

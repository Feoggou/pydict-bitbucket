lig

(lɪɡ ) British slang
Definitions
noun1. (esp in the entertainment industry and the media) a function at which free entertainment and refreshments are availableverbWord forms: ligs, ligging or ligged2. (intransitive) to attend such a function in order to take advantage of free entertainment and refreshments; freeload

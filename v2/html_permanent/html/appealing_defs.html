Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone or something that is appealing is pleasing and attractive. OPPOSITE: unappealing ⇒ There was a sense of humour to what he did that I found very appealing. ⇒ That's a very appealing idea.appealingly graded adverb [ADV adj/-ed, ADV after v] ⇒ Irish whiskeys, rather like the Irish themselves, have an appealingly direct charm about them.2. adjectiveAn appealing expression or tone of voice indicates to someone that you want help, advice, or approval. ⇒ She gave him a soft appealing look that would have melted solid ice.appealingly adverb [ADV after v] ⇒ Dena looked appealingly at Blair, hoping to hear a contrary opinion.3. → see also appeal

thoughts

(θɔːts)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. (someone's) opinions or ideas ⇒ Rothermere disclosed his thoughts on Britain., ⇒ his thoughts on love and fatherhood, ⇒ What are your thoughts on that?2. the things that someone thinks about ⇒ His thoughts turned to Somerset, where his mother had moved when she left the family., ⇒ His thoughts were interrupted by the sound of an approaching engine., ⇒ When I picked him up, my first thoughts were how much fitter and happier he looked., ⇒ Try questioning your negative thoughts on a daily basis., ⇒ Our thoughts were on Christmas., ⇒ to be in someone's thoughts, ⇒ If he wasn't there physically, he was always in her thoughts., ⇒ My thoughts started to wander.3. See someone's thoughts are with someone

removable

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪmuːvəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]A removable part of something is a part that can easily be moved from its place or position. ⇒ ...a cake tin with a removable base.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

sceptre

or US scepter (ˈsɛptə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a ceremonial staff held by a monarch as the symbol of authority2. imperial authority; sovereigntyverb3. (transitive) to invest with authority

Derived Forms

sceptred (ˈsceptred) or US sceptered (ˈsceptered) adjective

Word Origin

C13: from Old French sceptre, from Latin scēptrum, from Greek skeptron staff

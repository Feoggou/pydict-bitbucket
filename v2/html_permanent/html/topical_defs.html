topical

(ˈtɒpɪkəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or constituting current affairs2. relating to a particular place; local3. of or relating to a topic or topics4. (of a drug, ointment, etc) for application to the body surface; local
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

topicality (ˌtɒpɪˈkælɪtɪ ) nountopically (ˈtopically) adverb

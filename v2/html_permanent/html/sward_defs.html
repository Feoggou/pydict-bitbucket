sward

(swɔːd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. turf or grass or a stretch of turf or grassverb2. to cover or become covered with grass

Word Origin

Old English sweard skin; related to Old Frisian swarde scalp, Middle High German swart hide

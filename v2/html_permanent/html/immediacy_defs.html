immediacy

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmiːdiəsi ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounThe immediacy of an event or situation is the quality that it has which makes it seem important or exciting because it is happening at the present time. [+ of] ⇒ Do they understand the immediacy of the crisis? ⇒ The books that result have intensity and a fascinating immediacy, far beyond that of ordinary narrative.

yore

(jɔː )
Definitions
noun1. time long past (now only in the phrase of yore)adverb2. obsolete in the past; long ago
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English geāra, genitive plural of gēar year; see hour

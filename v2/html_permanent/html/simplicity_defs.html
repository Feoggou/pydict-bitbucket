Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounThe simplicity of something is the fact that it is not complicated and can be understood or done easily. [+ of] ⇒ The apparent simplicity of his plot is deceptive. ⇒ Because of its simplicity, this test could be carried out easily by a family doctor.2. uncount nounWhen you talk about something's simplicity, you approve of it because it has no unnecessary parts or complicated details. [approval] [+ of] ⇒ ...fussy details that ruin the simplicity of the design. ⇒ A pair of jewelled earrings will liven up this dress without detracting from its simplicity.3. →  simplicity itself

cestus1

(ˈsɛstəs ) or cestos (ˈsɛstɒs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounclassical mythology the girdle of Aphrodite (Venus) decorated to cause amorousness
Word origin
C16: from Latin, from Greek kestos belt, from kentein to stitch

cestus2

or caestus (ˈsɛstəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -tus or -tuses(in classical Roman boxing) a pugilist's gauntlet of bull's hide loaded or studded with metal
Word origin
C18: from Latin caestus, probably from caedere to strike, slay

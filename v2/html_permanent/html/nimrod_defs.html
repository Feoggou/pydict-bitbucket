Nimrod

(ˈnɪmrɒd )
Definitions
noun1. Old Testament a hunter, who was famous for his prowess (Genesis 10:8–9). Douay spelling: Nemrod2. a person who is dedicated to or skilled in hunting
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Nimrodian (Nimˈrodian) or Nimrodic (Nimˈrodic) adjective

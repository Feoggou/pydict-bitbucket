morning prayers

(ˈmɔːnɪŋ prɛəz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. theology an act of worship held in the morning2. Anglican Church the formal act of prayer of matins in the Anglican Church. See also matins
Example sentences containing 'morning prayers'
He hurriedly showers and says his own missed morning prayers and goes back to look at his father. Sara MacDonald SEA MUSICAll I can say in my own defence is that I've kept up with my early morning prayers.

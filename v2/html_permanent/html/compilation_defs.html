(kɒmpɪleɪʃən )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA compilation is a book, CD, or programme that contains many different items that have been gathered together, usually ones which have already appeared in other places. [+ of] ⇒ His latest album release is a compilation of his jazz works over the past decade.2. → see also compile

The results of this sifting through old manuscripts, field reports and collections, was the compilation of so-called Red Data Books.

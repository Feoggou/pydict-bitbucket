quoth

(kwəʊθ )
Definitions
verbarchaic (used with all pronouns except thou and you, and with nouns) another word for said1 (sense 2)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English cwæth, third person singular of cwethan to say; related to Old Frisian quetha to say, Old Saxon, Old High German quethan; see bequeath

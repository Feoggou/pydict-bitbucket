inviolate

(ɪnˈvaɪəlɪt , -ˌleɪt)
Definitions
adjective1. free from violation, injury, disturbance, etc2. a less common word for inviolable
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inviolacy (inˈviolacy) or inviolateness (inˈviolateness) nouninviolately (inˈviolately) adverb

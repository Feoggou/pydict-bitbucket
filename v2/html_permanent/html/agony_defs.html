agony

(ˈæɡənɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -nies1. acute physical or mental pain; anguish2. the suffering or struggle preceding death3. See pile on the agony4. (modifier) relating to or advising on personal problems about which people have written to the media ⇒ agony column, ⇒ agony writer
Word origin
C14: via Late Latin from Greek agōnia struggle, from agōn contest

Synonyms of enough

enough

adjective

  1. sufficient, adequate, ample, abundant, as much as you need, as much as is necessary, Enuf (text messaging)
    • They had enough money for a one-way ticket.

pronoun

  1. sufficiency, plenty, sufficient, abundance, adequacy, right amount, ample supply, Enuf (text messaging)
    • I hope you brought enough for everyone.

adverb

  1. sufficiently, amply, fairly, moderately, reasonably, adequately, satisfactorily, abundantly, tolerably, passably, Enuf (text messaging)
    • Do you think sentences for criminals are tough enough already?

PROVERB:
Enough is as good as a feast

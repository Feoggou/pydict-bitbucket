puce

(pjuːs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna. a colour varying from deep red to dark purplish-brownb. (as adjective) ⇒ a puce carpet

Word Origin

C18: shortened from French couleur puce flea colour, from Latin pūlex flea

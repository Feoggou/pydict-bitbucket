regurgitation

(rɪˌgɜrdʒəˈteɪʃən ) in American English
Definitions
noun a regurgitating; specif.,a.  the return of partly digested food from the stomach to the mouth, as in a ruminant animalb.  a backward flow of blood due to the imperfect closure of a heart valve

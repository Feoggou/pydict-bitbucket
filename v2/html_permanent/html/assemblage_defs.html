assemblage

(əˈsɛmblɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a number of things or persons assembled together; collection; assembly2. a list of dishes served at a meal or the dishes themselves3. the act or process of assembling or the state of being assembled4. (ˌæsəmˈblɑːʒ) a three-dimensional work of art that combines various objects into an integrated whole

Synonyms of lanky

lanky

adjective

  1. gangling, thin, tall, spare, angular, gaunt, bony, weedy (informal), scrawny, rangy, scraggy, rawboned, loose-jointed
    • He was six feet four, all lanky and leggy.
    OPPOSITES:  rounded, short, fat, muscular, plump, stout, burly, chubby, stocky, portly, rotund, sinewy, brawny

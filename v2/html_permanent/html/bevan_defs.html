Bevan1

(ˈbɛvən ) or Bev (bɛv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Australian informal, derogatory1. a stupid or unfashionable male2. an aggressive and surly youth
Word origin
from the boy's name Bevan

noun Aneurin (əˈnaɪərɪn), known as Nye. (1897–1960), British Labour statesman, born in Wales: noted for his oratory. As minister of health (1945–51) he introduced the National Health Service (1948)

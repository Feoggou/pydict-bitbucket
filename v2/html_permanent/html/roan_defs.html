roan

(rəʊn )
Definitions
adjective1. (of a horse) having a bay (red roan), chestnut (strawberry roan), or black (blue roan) coat sprinkled with white hairsnoun2. a horse having such a coat3. a soft unsplit sheepskin leather with a close tough grain, used in bookbinding, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Old French, from Spanish roano, probably from Gothic rauths red

furor

(ˈfjʊrˌɔr ; ˈfjʊrər ) in American English
Definitions
noun1.  fury; rage; frenzy2. a.  a great, widespread outburst of admiration or enthusiasm; craze; rageb.  a state of excitement or confusion; commotion or uproar
Webster’s New World College Dictionary, Fourth Edition, compiled by the editors of Webster’s New World Dictionaries. Copyright © 2010 by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. Published by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. All rights reserved.

Word Origin

ME furour < OFr fureur < L furor, rage, madness < furere: see fury

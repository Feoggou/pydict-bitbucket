Aquila1

(ˈækwɪlə , əˈkwɪlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: Latin genitive Aquilae (ˈækwɪˌliː )a constellation lying in the Milky Way close to Cygnus and situated on the celestial equator. The brightest star is Altair
Word origin

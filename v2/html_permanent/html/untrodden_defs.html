untrodden

(ʌnˈtrɒdən)
Definitions
adjectiveliterary not trodden or walked across ⇒ an untrodden path across fields, ⇒ the hills and mountains of a relatively untrodden Europe, ⇒ There was a perfect surface of untrodden snow.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

The furniture was too perfectly arranged, the rugs untrodden upon, everything clean, immaculate, untouched.

trine

(traɪn )
Definitions
noun1. astrology an aspect of 120° between two planets, an orb of 8° being allowed. Compare conjunction (sense 5), opposition (sense 9), square (sense 10)2. anything comprising three partsadjective3. of or relating to a trine4. threefold; triple
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

trinal (ˈtrinal) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Old French trin, from Latin trīnus triple, from trēs three

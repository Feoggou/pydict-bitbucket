darts

(dɑːts )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(functioning as singular) any of various competitive games in which darts are thrown at a dartboard

Having beaten the local youths at darts by doctoring the flights, Neddy Pink was now rattling his Guinness glass against the beer pumps. Christina Jones TICKLED PINKAnd there were colors in the brightness, brilliant darts of turquoise and tangerine.

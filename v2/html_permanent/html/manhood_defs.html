manhood

(ˈmænhʊd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the state or quality of being a man or being manly2. men collectively3. archaic the state of being human4. informal, humorous a euphemism for a man's genitalia

(mænhʊd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounManhood is the state of being a man rather than a boy. ⇒ They were failing lamentably to help their sons grow from boyhood to manhood.2. uncount nounManhood is the period of a man's life during which he is a man rather than a boy. ⇒ His handwriting, from earliest young manhood, was flowing and graceful.3. uncount noun [supp N]If you refer to American manhood or French manhood for example, you are referring to American men or French men considered as a group. ⇒ Italian manhood was appalled.

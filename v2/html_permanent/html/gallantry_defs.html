gallantry

(ˈɡæləntrɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ries1. conspicuous courage, esp in war ⇒ the gallantry of the troops2. polite attentiveness to women3. a gallant action, speech, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

gallantry

Powered by COBUILD
(gæləntri ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounGallantry is bravery shown by someone who is in danger, for example when they are fighting in a war. [formal] ⇒ For his gallantry he was awarded a Victoria Cross.2. uncount nounGallantry is kind, polite, and considerate behaviour towards other people, especially women. [formal] ⇒ It's that time of year again, when thoughts turn to romance and gallantry.

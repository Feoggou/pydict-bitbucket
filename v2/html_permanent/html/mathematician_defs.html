Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA mathematician is a person who is trained in the study of numbers and calculations. ⇒ The risks can be so complex that banks hire mathematicians to puzzle them out.2. countable nounA mathematician is a person who is good at doing calculations and using numbers. ⇒ I'm not a very good mathematician.

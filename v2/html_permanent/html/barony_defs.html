barony

(ˈbærənɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -nies1. a. the domain of a baronb. (in Ireland) a division of a countyc. (in Scotland) a large estate or manor2. the rank or dignity of a baron3. a sphere of influence dominated by an industrial magnate or other powerful individual

undeniable

(ˌʌndɪˈnaɪəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. unquestionably or obviously true2. of unquestionable excellence ⇒ a man of undeniable character3. unable to be resisted or denied
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
adjectiveIf you say that something is undeniable, you mean that it is definitely true. incontrovertible ⇒ Her charm is undeniable. ⇒ ...the undeniable fact that she was driving with almost twice the legal limit of alcohol in her blood.undeniably (ʌndɪnaɪəbli ) adverb ⇒ Bringing up a baby is undeniably hard work.

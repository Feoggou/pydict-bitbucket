forewarn

(fɔːˈwɔːn )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to warn beforehand2. See forewarned is forearmed

Powered by COBUILD
(fɔːʳwɔːʳn ) for learners of English
Word forms: forewarns , forewarning , forewarned
Definitions
1. verbIf you forewarn someone about something, you warn them in advance that it is going to happen. [V n + of/about] ⇒ The Macmillan Guide had forewarned me of what to expect. [Also V n that, V n]2. → forewarned is forearmed

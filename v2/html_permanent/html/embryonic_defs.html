embryonic

(ˌɛmbrɪˈɒnɪk ) or embryonal (ˈɛmbrɪənəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to an embryo2. in an early stage; rudimentary; undeveloped
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

embryonically (ˌembryˈonically) adverb

embryonic

Powered by COBUILD
(embriɒnɪk ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]An embryonic process, idea, organization, or organism is one at a very early stage in its development. [formal] ⇒ ...Romania's embryonic democracy. ⇒ ...the embryonic European central bank. ⇒ ...embryonic plant cells.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'embryonic'
My hair fell over my face and my body trembled with an embryonic sob.

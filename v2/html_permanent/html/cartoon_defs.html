cartoon

(kɑːˈtuːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a humorous or satirical drawing, esp one in a newspaper or magazine, concerning a topical event2. Also called: comic strip a sequence of drawings in a newspaper, magazine, etc, relating a comic or adventurous situation3. See animated cartoon4. a full-size preparatory sketch for a fresco, tapestry, mosaic, etc, from which the final work is traced or copied
Derived forms
cartoonist (carˈtoonist) noun
Word origin
C17: from Italian cartone pasteboard, sketch on stiff paper; see carton

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA cartoon is a humorous drawing or series of drawings in a newspaper or magazine. ⇒ ...one of Britain's best-loved cartoon characters, Rupert the Bear.2. → see also strip cartoon3. countable nounA cartoon is a film in which all the characters and scenes are drawn rather than being real people or objects. ⇒ ...the Saturday morning cartoons.

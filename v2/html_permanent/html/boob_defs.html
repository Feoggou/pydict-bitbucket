boob1

(buːb ) slang
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an ignorant or foolish person; booby2. British an embarrassing mistake; blunder3. a female breastverb4. (intransitive) British to make a blunder
Word origin
C20: back formation from booby

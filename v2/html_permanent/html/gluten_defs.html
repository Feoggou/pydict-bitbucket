gluten

(ˈɡluːtən )
Definitions
nouna protein consisting of a mixture of glutelin and gliadin, present in cereal grains, esp wheat. A gluten-free diet is necessary in cases of coeliac disease
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

glutenous (ˈglutenous) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Latin: glue

used to

(juːst )
Definitions
adjective1. made familiar with; accustomed to ⇒ I am used to hitchhikingverb (transitive)2. (takes an infinitive or implied infinitive) used as an auxiliary to express habitual or accustomed actions, states, etc, taking place in the past but not continuing into the present ⇒ I don't drink these days, but I used to, ⇒ I used to fish here every day▶ USAGE The most common negative form of used to is didn't used to (or didn't use to), but in formal contexts used not to is preferred

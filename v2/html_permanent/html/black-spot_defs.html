black spot

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a place on a road where accidents frequently occur2. any dangerous or difficult place3. a disease of roses, Diplocarpon rosae, that causes circular black blotches on the leaves

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounIf you describe a place, time, or part of a situation as a black spot, you mean that it is particularly bad or likely to cause problems. [British] ⇒ There are recognised black spots in marriages which can lead to trouble. ⇒ Gainsborough is known as an unemployment blackspot.2. countable nounA black spot is a place on a road where accidents often happen. [British] ⇒ The accident happened on a notorious black spot on the A43.

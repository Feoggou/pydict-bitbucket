fertilize

or fertilise (ˈfɜːtɪˌlaɪz )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to provide (an animal, plant, or egg cell) with sperm or pollen to bring about fertilization2. to supply (soil or water) with mineral and organic nutrients to aid the growth of plants3. to make fertile or productive

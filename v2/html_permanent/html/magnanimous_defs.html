Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are magnanimous, you behave kindly and generously towards someone, especially after defeating them or being treated badly by them. ⇒ I was prepared to be magnanimous, prepared to feel compassion for him. ⇒ He was a man capable of magnanimous gestures.magnanimously graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ 'You were right, and we were wrong,' he said magnanimously.

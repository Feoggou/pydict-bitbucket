villainy

(ˈvɪlənɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -lainies1. conduct befitting a villain; vicious behaviour or action2. an evil, abhorrent, or criminal act or deed3. the fact or condition of being villainous4. English history a rare word for villeinage
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

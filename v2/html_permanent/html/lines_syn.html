  9. (military) formation, front, position, front line, trenches, firing line

  10. approach, policy, position, way, course, practice, scheme, method, technique, procedure, tactic, avenue, ideology, course of action

  11. course, way, direction, tendency, drift, tack

  12. occupation, work, calling, interest, business, job, area, trade, department, field, career, activity, bag (slang), employment, province, profession, pursuit, forte, vocation, specialization

  18. clue, lead, hint, indication

rebut

(rɪˈbʌt )
Definitions
verb -buts, -butting or -butted(transitive) to refute or disprove, esp by offering a contrary contention or argument
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

rebuttable (reˈbuttable) adjectiverebuttal (reˈbuttal) noun

Word Origin

C13: from Old French reboter, from re- + boter to thrust, butt3

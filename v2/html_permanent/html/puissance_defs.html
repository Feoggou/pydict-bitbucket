puissance

(ˈpjuːɪsəns , ˈpwiːsɑːns ) or puissaunce (ˈpjuːɪsəns)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a competition in showjumping that tests a horse's ability to jump a limited number of large obstacles2. archaic or poetic power

Word Origin

C15: from Old French; see puissant
Example sentences containing 'puissance'
The glass bears her image, so she cannot be a witch; however, she saps my puissance. Various LOOKING FOR THE SPARKBurnell sketched the exterior of the church, enjoying its increasing puissance as evening drew near. Aldiss, Brian SOMEWHERE EAST OF LIFE

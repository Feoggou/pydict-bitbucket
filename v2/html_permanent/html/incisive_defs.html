incisive

(ɪnˈsaɪsɪv )
Definitions
adjective1. keen, penetrating, or acute2. biting or sarcastic; mordant ⇒ an incisive remark3. having a sharp cutting edge ⇒ incisive teeth
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

incisively (inˈcisively) adverbincisiveness (inˈcisiveness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveYou use incisive to describe a person, their thoughts, or their speech when you approve of their ability to think and express their ideas clearly, briefly, and forcefully. [approval] ⇒ ...a shrewd operator with an incisive mind. ⇒ She's incredibly incisive, incredibly intelligent.

urgent

(ˈɜːdʒənt )
Definitions
adjective1. requiring or compelling speedy action or attention ⇒ the matter is urgent, ⇒ an urgent message2. earnest and persistent
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

urgency (ˈɜːdʒənsɪ ) nounurgently (ˈurgently) adverb

Word Origin

C15: via French from Latin urgent-, urgens, present participle of urgēre to urge

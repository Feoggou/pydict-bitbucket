Caroline

(ˈkærəˌlaɪn ) or Carolean (ˌkærəˈliːən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. Also called: Carolinian characteristic of or relating to Charles I or Charles II, kings of England, Scotland, and Ireland, the society over which they ruled, or their government2. of or relating to any other king called Charles

Immanuel

or Emmanuel (ɪˈmænjʊəl )
Definitions
nounBible the child whose birth was foretold by Isaiah (Isaiah 7:14) and who in Christian tradition is identified with Jesus
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

from Hebrew `immānū'el, literally: God with us

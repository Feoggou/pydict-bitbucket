impalpable

(ɪmˈpælpəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. imperceptible, esp to the touch ⇒ impalpable shadows2. difficult to understand; abstruse
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun slang1. British a fellow or person2. See clever dick3. a slang word for penis▶ USAGE The third sense of this word was formerly considered to be taboo and it was labelled as such in older editions of Collins English Dictionary. Word origin
C16 (meaning: fellow): from the name Dick, familiar form of Richard, applied generally (like Jack) to any fellow, lad, etc; hence, C19: penis

The slang term dick as used for a penis likely comes from the German word dick which means thick. There is an obvious connection when the other English slang expression for a penis is considered, namely "a fat". So it is not a stretch to see how the German word fell into use.

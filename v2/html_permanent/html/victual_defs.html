victual

(ˈvɪtəl )
Definitions
verb -uals, -ualling, -ualled or US -uals, -ualing or -ualed1. to supply with or obtain victuals2. (intransitive) rare (esp of animals) to partake of victuals
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

victual-less (ˈvictual-less) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Old French vitaille, from Late Latin victuālia provisions, from Latin victuālis concerning food, from victus sustenance, from vīvere to live

solubility

(ˌsɒljʊˈbɪlɪtɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the ability of a substance to dissolve; quality of being soluble2. a measure of this ability for a particular substance in a particular solvent, equal to the quantity of substance dissolving in a fixed quantity of solvent to form a saturated solution under specified temperature and pressure. It is expressed in grams per cubic decametre, grams per hundred grams of solvent, moles per mole, etc

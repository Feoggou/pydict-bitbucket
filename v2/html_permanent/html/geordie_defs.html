Geordie

(ˈdʒɔːdɪ ) British
Definitions
noun1. a person who comes from or lives in Tyneside2. the dialect spoken by these peopleadjective3. of or relating to these people or their dialect
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: a diminutive of George

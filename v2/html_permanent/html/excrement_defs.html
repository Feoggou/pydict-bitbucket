excrement

(ˈɛkskrɪmənt )
Definitions
nounwaste matter discharged from the body, esp faeces; excreta
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

excremental (ˌɛkskrɪˈmɛntəl ), excrementitial (ˌɛkskrəmɛnˈtɪʃəl) or excrementitious (ˌɛkskrɪmɛnˈtɪʃəs ) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Latin excrēmentum, from excernere to sift, excrete

(sɪˈlɛbrɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. a famous person ⇒ a show-business celebrity2. fame or notoriety

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA celebrity is someone who is famous, especially in areas of entertainment such as films, music, writing, or sport. ⇒ In 1944, at the age of 30, Hersey suddenly became a celebrity. ⇒ ...a host of celebrities.2. uncount nounIf a person or thing achieves celebrity, they become famous, especially in areas of entertainment such as films, music, writing, or sport. ⇒ Joanna has finally made it to the first rank of celebrity after 25 years as an actress.

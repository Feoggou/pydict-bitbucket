sodden

(ˈsɒdən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. completely saturated2. a. dulled, esp by excessive drinkingb. (in combination) ⇒ a drink-sodden mind3. heavy or doughy, as bread is when improperly cookedverb4. to make or become sodden

Derived Forms

soddenly (ˈsoddenly) adverbsoddenness (ˈsoddenness) noun

Word Origin

C13 soden, past participle of seethe

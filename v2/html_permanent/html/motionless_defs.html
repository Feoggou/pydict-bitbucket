Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]Someone or something that is motionless is not moving at all. ⇒ He has this ability of being able to remain as motionless as a statue, for hours on end. ⇒ Her hands were motionless. ⇒ He stood there motionless.
Example sentences containing 'motionless'
He stood quite motionless , hands clasped behind his back, his head stooped. Forbes, Colin COVER STORYShe sat motionless in the front seat, seeming neither to know or any longer to care what was happening to her. Dobbs, Michael WALL GAMESJan was still standing by the door watching me, her hand raised in a motionless wave. Tapply, William G FOLLOW THE SHARKS

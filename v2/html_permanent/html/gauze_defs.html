gauze

(ɡɔːz )
Definitions
noun1. a. a transparent cloth of loose plain or leno weaveb. (as modifier) ⇒ a gauze veil2. a surgical dressing of muslin or similar material3. any thin openwork material, such as wire4. a fine mist or haze
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from French gaze, perhaps from Gaza, where it was believed to originate

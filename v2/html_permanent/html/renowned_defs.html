Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveA person or place that is renowned for something, usually something good, is well known because of it. [+ for] ⇒ The area is renowned for its Romanesque churches. ⇒ James was renowned as a forward thinker. ⇒ ...Sir William Crookes, the renowned chemist. [Also + as]

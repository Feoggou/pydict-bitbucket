repine

(rɪˈpaɪn )
Definitions
verb(intransitive) to be fretful or low-spirited through discontent
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from re- + pine2
Example sentences containing 'repine'
Sadly, it did not compare with my No. 30 bus ride up the Old Brompton Road to Knightsbridge and Park Lane but it was idle to repine. Malcolm, John THE GWEN JOHN SCULPTUREHowever, I shall win the Dressage, so if I don't win the Hunting I shan't repine. Colleen McCullough THE THORN BIRDS

Similar Words

complain, moan, grieve, murmur, lament, grumble, brood, fret, languish, sulk, mope, eat your heart out

student

(ˈstjuːdənt )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a person following a course of study, as in a school, college, university, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ student teacher2. a person who makes a thorough study of a subject

Word Origin

C15: from Latin studēns diligent, from studēre to be zealous; see study

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA student is a person who is studying at a university or college. ⇒ Warren's eldest son is an art student, at St Martin's. ⇒ ...a 23-year-old medical student.2. → see also mature student3. countable nounA student is a child who is studying at a secondary school. 4. countable nounSomeone who is a student of a particular subject is interested in the subject and spends time learning about it. [+ of] ⇒ ...a passionate student of nineteenth century history.

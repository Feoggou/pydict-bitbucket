accident and emergency

(ˈæksɪdənt ənd ɪˈmɜːdʒənsɪ), accident and emergency department, accident and emergency unit or accident unit
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounBritish the room or department in a hospital where people who have severe injuries or sudden illness are taken for emergency treatment ⇒ people waiting in accident and emergency

countable nounThe accident and emergency is the room or department in a hospital where people who have severe injuries or sudden illness are taken for emergency treatment. The abbreviation A & E is also used. [British] regional note:   in AM, use emergency room

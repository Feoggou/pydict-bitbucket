get across

Definitions
verb1. to cross or cause or help to cross2. (adverb) to be or cause to be readily understood3. (intr, preposition) informal to annoy ⇒ her constant interference really got across him

Definitions
ergative phrasal verb & phrasal verbWhen an idea gets across or when you get it across, you succeed in making other people understand it. [V P + to] ⇒ Officers felt their point of view was not getting across to ministers. [V n P] ⇒ I had created a way to get my message across while using as few words as possible. [Also V P] →  get

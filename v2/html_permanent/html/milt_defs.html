milt

(mɪlt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the testis of a fish2. the spermatozoa and seminal fluid produced by a fish3. rare the spleen of certain animals, esp fowls and pigsverb4. to fertilize (the roe of a female fish) with milt, esp artificially

Word Origin

Old English milte spleen; in the sense: fish sperm, probably from Middle Dutch milte

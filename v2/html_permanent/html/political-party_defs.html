political party

(pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈpɑːtɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounpolitics an organization of people who share the same views about the way power should be used in a country or society (through government, policy-making, etc) ⇒ All other political parties there have been completely banned.

Hereford

(ˈhɛrɪfəd )
Definitions
noun1. a city in W England, in Herefordshire on the River Wye: trading centre for agricultural produce; cathedral (begun 1079). Pop: 56 373 (2001)2. a hardy breed of beef cattle characterized by a red body, red and white head, and white markings

cunt

(kʌnt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun taboo1. the female genitals2. offensive, slang a woman considered sexually3. offensive, slang a mean or obnoxious person▶ USAGE Although there has been some relaxation of the taboo against using words such as fuck in conversation and print, the use of cunt is still not considered acceptable by most people outside very limited social contexts. Though originally a racily descriptive word in Middle English, it has been taboo for many centuries and continues to be so
Word origin
C13: of Germanic origin; related to Old Norse kunta, Middle Low German kunte

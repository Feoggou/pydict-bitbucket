wasteful

(ˈweɪstfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. tending to waste or squander; extravagant2. causing waste, destruction, or devastation
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

wastefully (ˈwastefully) adverbwastefulness (ˈwastefulness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveAction that is wasteful uses too much of something valuable such as time, money, or energy.

grubby

(ˈɡrʌbɪ )
Definitions
adjective -bier or -biest1. dirty; slovenly2. mean; beggarly3. infested with grubs
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

grubbily (ˈgrubbily) adverbgrubbiness (ˈgrubbiness) noun

grubby

Powered by COBUILD
(grʌbi ) for learners of English
Word forms: grubbier , grubbiest
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA grubby person or object is rather dirty. ⇒ His white coat was grubby and stained. ⇒ ...kids with grubby faces.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you call an activity or someone's behaviour grubby, you mean that it is not completely honest or respectable. [disapproval] ⇒ ...the grubby business of politics.

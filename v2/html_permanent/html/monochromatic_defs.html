monochromatic

(ˌmɒnəʊkrəʊˈmætɪk ) or monochroic (ˌmɒnəʊˈkrəʊɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. Also: homochromatic (of light or other electromagnetic radiation) having only one wavelength2. physics (of moving particles) having only one kinetic energy3. of or relating to monochromatismnoun4. a person who is totally colour-blind

Derived Forms

ˌmonochromatically (ˌmonochroˈmatically) adverb

confederacy

(kənˈfɛdərəsɪ , -ˈfɛdrəsɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cies1. a union or combination of peoples, states, etc; alliance; league2. a combination of groups or individuals for unlawful purposes
Derived forms
confederal (conˈfederal) adjective
Word origin
C14: from Anglo-French confederacie, from Late Latin confoederātiō agreement, confederation

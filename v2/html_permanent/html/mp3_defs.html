MP3

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
abbreviation for1. MPEG-1 Audio Layer-3: tradename for software created by the Motion Picture Experts Group that enables files to be compressed quickly to 10% or less of their original size for storage on disk or hard drive or esp for transfer across the internet2. an audio or video file created in this way

Definitions
noun(Pharmaceutical: Physiology) Irrigation is bathing or washing out a body part, cavity, or wound with water or a medicated fluid. ⇒ Nasal irrigation uses salt water in the nasal cavity to wash out the sinuses. ⇒ Colonic irrigation flushes the colon with water to remove toxins. ⇒ Irrigation is bathing or washing out a body part, cavity, or wound with water or a medicated fluid.

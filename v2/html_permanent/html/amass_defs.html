amass

(əˈmæs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (transitive) to accumulate or collect (esp riches, etc)2. to gather in a heap; bring together
Derived forms
aˈmasser noun
Word origin
C15: from Old French amasser, from masse mass

ala

(ˈeɪlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural alae (ˈeɪliː )1. zoology a wing or flat winglike process or structure, such as a part of some bones and cartilages2. botany a winglike part, such as one of the wings of a sycamore seed or one of the flat petals of a sweet pea flower
Word origin
C18: from Latin āla a wing

largo

(ˈlɑːɡəʊ ) music
Definitions
adjective, adverb1. to be performed slowly and broadlynoun plural -gos2. a piece or passage to be performed in this way
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Italian, from Latin largus large

largo

Powered by COBUILD
(lɑːʳgoʊ ) for learners of English
Word forms: largos
Definitions
1. graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v]Largo written above a piece of music means that it should be played slowly.2. countable nounA largo is a piece of music, especially part of a longer piece, that is played slowly.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Synonyms of table

table

noun

  1. counter, bench, stand, board, surface, slab, work surface
    • I placed his drink on the small table.
  2. list, chart, tabulation, record, roll, index, register, digest, diagram, inventory, graph, synopsis, itemization
    • Consult the table on page 104.
  3. (formal) food, spread (informal), board, diet, fare, kai (New Zealand, informal), victuals
    • She always sets a marvellous table.
  4. plateau, flat, plain, mesa, tableland, flatland

verb

  1. (British) submit, propose, put forward, move, suggest, enter, file, lodge, moot
    • They've tabled a motion criticizing the government for inaction.
related adjective:
mensal

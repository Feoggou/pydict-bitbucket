device

noun

  1. gadget, machine, tool, instrument, implement, invention, appliance, apparatus, gimmick, utensil, contraption, widget (informal), contrivance, waldo, gizmo or gismo (slang, mainly US & & Canadian) • This device can measure minute quantities of matter.
  2. smartphone, tablet, laptop, iPad®, iPhone®, Kindle®, BlackBerry® • You can use the same login across all your devices.
  3. ploy, scheme, strategy, plan, design, project, shift, trick, manoeuvre, stunt, dodge, expedient, ruse, artifice, gambit, stratagem, wile • His actions are obviously a device to buy time.
  4. emblem, figure, design, symbol, token, logo, badge, crest, motif, motto, insignia, colophon

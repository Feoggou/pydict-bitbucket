ban

verb

  1. prohibit, black, bar, block, restrict, veto, forbid, boycott, suppress, outlaw, banish, disallow, proscribe, debar, blackball, interdict, criminalize • Last year arms sales were banned. OPPOSITES:  let, allow, approve, enable, permit, sanction, authorize
  2. bar, prohibit, prevent, exclude, forbid, disqualify, preclude, debar, declare ineligible • He was banned from driving for three years.

noun

  1. prohibition, block, restriction, veto, boycott, embargo, injunction, censorship, taboo, suppression, stoppage, disqualification, interdiction, interdict, proscription, disallowance, rahui (New Zealand), restraining order (US, law) • The General also lifted a ban on political parties. OPPOSITES:  sanction, approval, permission, allowance

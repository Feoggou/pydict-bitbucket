Frankfurt1

or Frankfurt am Main (German ˈfraŋkfʊrt (am ˈmain))
Definitions
nouna city in central Germany, in Hesse on the Main River: a Roman settlement in the 1st century; a free imperial city (1372–1806); seat of the federal assembly (1815–66); university (1914); trade fairs since the 13th century. Pop: 643 432 (2003 est)

Frankfurt2 - German

or Frankfurt an der Oder ( ˈfraŋkfʊrt (an der ˈoːdər))
Definitions
nouna city in E Germany on the Polish border: member of the Hanseatic League (1368–1450). Pop: 67 014 (2003 est)

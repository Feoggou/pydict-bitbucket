burgh

(ˈbʌrə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (in Scotland) a town, esp one incorporated by charter, that enjoyed a degree of self-government until the local-government reorganization of 19752. an archaic form of borough (sense 1)
Derived forms
burghal (ˈbɜːɡəl ) adjective
Word origin

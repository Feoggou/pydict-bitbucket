mickle

(ˈmɪkəl ) or Scottish and Northern England dialect muckle (ˈmʌkəl ) or Northern England dialect meikle (ˈmaɪkəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. great or abundantadverb2. much; greatlynoun3. a great amount, esp in the proverb, mony a little makes a mickle4. Scottish a small amount, esp in the proverb, many a mickle maks a muckle

Word Origin

C13 mikel, from Old Norse mikell, replacing Old English micel much

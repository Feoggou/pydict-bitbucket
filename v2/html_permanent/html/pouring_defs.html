pouring

(ˈpɔːrɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of rain) heavy ⇒ in the pouring rain, ⇒ Don't go out into the pouring rain.2. characterized by heavy rainfall ⇒ in a pouring thunderstorm, ⇒ a pouring wet day, ⇒ It was pouring wet at the stadium.3. capable of being poured ⇒ Add a little juice to the puree to make a pouring sauce., ⇒ a potato and celeriac puree of almost pouring consistency, ⇒ a pouring consistency, ⇒ Ensure sauce is of a pouring consistency.

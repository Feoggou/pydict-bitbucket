Aristotelian

(ˌærɪstəˈtiːlɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Aristotle or his philosophy2. (of a philosophical position) derived from that of Aristotle, or incorporating such of his major doctrines as the distinctions between matter and form, and substance and accident, or the primacy of individuals over universalsnoun3. a follower of Aristotle

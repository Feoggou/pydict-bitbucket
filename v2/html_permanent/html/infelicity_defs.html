infelicity

(ˌɪnfɪˈlɪsɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. the state or quality of being unhappy or unfortunate2. an instance of bad luck or mischance; misfortune3. something, esp a remark or expression, that is inapt or inappropriate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'infelicity'
I cleared my throat to distract from the infelicity of the phrase. Edward Docx THE CALLIGRAPHER

Similar Words

misfortune, misery, sadness, woe, bad luck, unhappiness, wretchedness

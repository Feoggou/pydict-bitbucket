Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a physical attack, accident, or injury as horrific, you mean that it is very bad, so that people are shocked when they see it or think about it. ⇒ I have never seen such horrific injuries. ⇒ The slaughter was horrific.horrifically graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ He had been horrifically assaulted before he died.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as horrific, you mean that it is so big that it is extremely unpleasant. ⇒ ...piling up horrific extra amounts of money on top of your original debt.horrifically graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj] ⇒ Opera productions are horrifically expensive.

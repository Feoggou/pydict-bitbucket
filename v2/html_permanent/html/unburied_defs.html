unburied

(ʌnˈbɛrɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. not buried or placed in a grave ⇒ The unburied bodies were discovered at ten different sites., ⇒ His body lay still unburied in the castle chapel.2. not dismissed from mind ⇒ For five years he had lain unburied in her mind.

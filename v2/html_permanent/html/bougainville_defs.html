Bougainville1

(ˈbuːɡənˌvɪl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan island in the W Pacific, in Papua New Guinea: the largest of the Solomon Islands: unilaterally declared independence in 1990; occupied by government troops in 1992, and granted autonomy in 2001. Chief town: Kieta. Area: 10 049 sq km (3880 sq miles)

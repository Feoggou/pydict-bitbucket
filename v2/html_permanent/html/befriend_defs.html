Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf you befriend someone, especially someone who is lonely or far from home, you make friends with them. [V n] ⇒ The film's about an elderly woman and a young nurse who befriends her. [V n] ⇒ On the aeroplane I was befriended by a delightful German woman.

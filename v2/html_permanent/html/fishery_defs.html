fishery

(ˈfɪʃərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -eries1. a. the industry of catching, processing, and selling fishb. a place where this is carried on2. a place where fish are reared3. a fishing ground4. another word for piscary (sense 2)

(fɪʃəri ) for learners of English
Word forms: fisheries
Definitions
1. countable noun [usu pl]Fisheries are areas of the sea where fish are caught in large quantities for commercial purposes. fishing ground ⇒ ...the fisheries off Newfoundland.2. countable nounA fishery is a place where fish are bred and reared. fish farm

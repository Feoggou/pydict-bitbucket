guaranteed

(ˌɡærənˈtiːd)
Definitions
adjective1. bound to happen; certain ⇒ At this point in the game it looks like a guaranteed victory, ⇒ Their new product is a guaranteed success., ⇒ Morocco offers excitement and guaranteed sunshine.2. mandated by law or contract ⇒ a guaranteed income of £3.6 million, ⇒ They want a guaranteed minimum wage., ⇒ The savings scheme offers a guaranteed income of 8 per cent over four years., ⇒ The farmers demand guaranteed minimum prices for their produce., ⇒ parts of guaranteed quality

guaranteed

Powered by COBUILD
(gærəntiːd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [v-link ADJ] [usu ADJ to-inf]If you say that something is guaranteed to happen, you mean that you are certain that it will happen. ⇒ Reports of this kind are guaranteed to cause anxiety. ⇒ It's guaranteed that my colleagues think I'm deranged. ⇒ Success is not guaranteed.2. → See also guarantee

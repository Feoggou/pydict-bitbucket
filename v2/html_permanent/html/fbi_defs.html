FBI

Powered by COBUILD
(ef biː aɪ ) for learners of English
Definitions
proper nounThe FBI is a government agency in the United States that investigates crimes in which a national law is broken or in which the country's security is threatened. FBI is an abbreviation for 'Federal Bureau of Investigation'.

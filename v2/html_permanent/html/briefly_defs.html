Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]Something that happens or is done briefly happens or is done for a very short period of time. ⇒ He smiled briefly. ⇒ Guerillas captured and briefly held an important provincial capital.2. graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]If you say or write something briefly, you use very few words or give very few details. in brief ⇒ There are four basic alternatives; they are described briefly below.3. graded adverb & adverbYou can say briefly to indicate that you are about to say something in as few words as possible. ⇒ Briefly, no less than nine of our agents have passed information to us.

subcutaneous

(ˌsʌbkjuːˈteɪnɪəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivemedicine situated, used, or introduced beneath the skin ⇒ a subcutaneous injection

Derived Forms

ˌsubcutaneously (ˌsubcuˈtaneously) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin subcutāneus, from sub- + Latin cutis skin + -eous

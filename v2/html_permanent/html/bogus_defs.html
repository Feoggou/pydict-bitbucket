bogus

(ˈbəʊɡəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivespurious or counterfeit; not genuine ⇒ a bogus note
Derived forms
bogusly (ˈbogusly) adverbbogusness (ˈbogusness) noun
Word origin
C19: from bogus apparatus for making counterfeit money; perhaps related to bogey1

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as bogus, you mean that it is not genuine. OPPOSITE: bona fide ⇒ ...their bogus insurance claim. ⇒ He said these figures were bogus and totally inaccurate.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [oft in names]A barracks is a building or group of buildings where soldiers or other members of the armed forces live and work. 'Barracks' is the singular and plural form. ⇒ ...an army barracks in the north of the city. ⇒ ...the parade grounds of Wellington Barracks.2. verbIf people in an audience barrack public speakers or performers, they interrupt them, for example by making rude remarks. [British] [V n] ⇒ Fans gained more enjoyment barracking him than cheering on the team. [V n] ⇒ President Alan Garcia was barracked by the right wing opposition.barracking uncount noun ⇒ He was affected badly by the barracking that he got from the crowd.

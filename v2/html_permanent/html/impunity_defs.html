impunity

(ɪmˈpjuːnɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. exemption or immunity from punishment or recrimination2. exemption or immunity from unpleasant consequences ⇒ a successful career marked by impunity from early mistakes3. See with impunity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin impūnitās freedom from punishment, from impūnis unpunished, from im- (not) + poena punishment

(soʊkɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveIf something is soaking or soaking wet, it is very wet. sopping ⇒ My face and raincoat were soaking wet.
Example sentences containing 'soaking'
The effect of the cold rainwater soaking his collar from the inspector's awkwardly held umbrella had diminished. Thomas, Craig THE LAST RAVENThe redhead still sat motionless on the deck, oblivious of her soaking clothes, mechanically steering with one hand. Maclean, Alistair THE LONELY SEAShe set out her purchases on the little round table of her balcony and sat soaking in the sunshine. Curzon, Clare THE QUEST FOR K

perfumed

(ˈpɜːfjuːmd, pəˈfjuːmd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having a natural pleasant smell ⇒ Champenois wines can be particularly fragrant and perfumed., ⇒ the sweetly perfumed, creamy yellow flowers, ⇒ some perfumed roses2. having an added pleasant smell ⇒ She opened the perfumed envelope.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething such as fruit or wine that is perfumed has a sweet pleasant smell. ⇒ Champenois wines can be particularly fragrant and perfumed. ⇒ ...sweetly perfumed, creamy yellow flowers.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Perfumed things have a sweet pleasant smell, either naturally or because perfume has been added to them. ⇒ She opened the perfumed envelope. ⇒ ...perfumed roses.

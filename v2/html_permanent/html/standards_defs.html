standards

(ˈstændədz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. principles of propriety, honesty, and integrity ⇒ My father has always had high moral standards., ⇒ There has been a corruption of moral standards., ⇒ She's got high standards.2. criteria; a level of quality used to judge or compare something else ⇒ These families would be classified as rich by Asian standards., ⇒ systems that were by later standards absurdly primitive

Albany

(ˈɔːlbənɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a city in E New York State, on the Hudson River: the state capital. Pop: 93 919 (2003 est)2. a river in central Canada, flowing east and northeast to James Bay. Length: 982 km (610 miles)3. a port in southwest Western Australia: founded as a penal colony. Pop: 22 415 (2001)

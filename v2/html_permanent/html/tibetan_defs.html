Tibetan

(tɪˈbɛtən )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Tibet, its people, or their languagenoun2. a native or inhabitant of Tibet3. the language of Tibet, belonging to the Sino-Tibetan family
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Aldiss, Brian SOMEWHERE EAST OF LIFEThe Sea of Sand and the pampas, the outback and the Tibetan plateau, mountain ranges as tall as God and abandoned cities as old as time.

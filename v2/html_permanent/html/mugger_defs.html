mugger1

(ˈmʌɡə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. informal a person who commits robbery with violence, esp in the street2. mainly US and Canadian a person who overacts

mugger2

nouna large freshwater crocodile, Crocodylus niloticus, inhabiting marshes and pools of India and Sri Lanka. Also called: marsh crocodile

Word Origin

demonstrative

(dɪˈmɒnstrətɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. tending to manifest or express one's feelings easily or unreservedly2. (postpositive; foll by of) serving as proof; indicative3. involving or characterized by demonstration ⇒ a demonstrative lecture4. conclusive; indubitable ⇒ demonstrative arguments5. grammar denoting or belonging to a class of determiners used to point out the individual referent or referents intended, such as this, that, these, and those. Compare interrogative, relativenoun6. grammar a demonstrative word or construction
Derived forms
demonstratively (deˈmonstratively) adverbdemonstrativeness (deˈmonstrativeness) noun

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a rotating shaft in a machine or power tool on which a milling cutter or grinding wheel is fitted2. a rotating shaft or mandrel on which a workpiece is fitted for machining3. metallurgy a part, piece, or structure used to reinforce the core of a mould
Word origin
C17: from Latin: tree, mast

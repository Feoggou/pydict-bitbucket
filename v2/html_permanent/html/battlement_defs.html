battlement

(ˈbætəlmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna parapet or wall with indentations or embrasures, originally for shooting through
Derived forms
battlemented (ˈbattlemented) adjective
Word origin
C14: from Old French batailles, plural of bataille battle

And yet what does the ghost on the battlement want but a repayment of what he believes he is owed?

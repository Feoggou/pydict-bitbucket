Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an apparatus, usually of metal and heated by gas, electricity, oil, or solid fuel, for cooking food; stove2. British any large sour apple used in cooking

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA cooker is a large metal device for cooking food using gas or electricity. A cooker usually consists of a grill, an oven, and some gas or electric rings. [British] ⇒ ...a gas cooker.2. → see also pressure cookerregional note:   in AM, usually use stove

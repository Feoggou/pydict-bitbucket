unenviable for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnenviəbəl )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a situation or task as unenviable, you mean that nobody would enjoy dealing with it because it is very difficult, dangerous, or unpleasant. ⇒ She had the unenviable task of making the first few phone calls. ⇒ It put me in the unenviable position of having to lie.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

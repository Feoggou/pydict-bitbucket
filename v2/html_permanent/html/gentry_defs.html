gentry

(ˈdʒɛntrɪ )
Definitions
noun1. persons of high birth or social standing; aristocracy2. British persons just below the nobility in social rank3. informal, often derogatory people, esp of a particular group or kind
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French genterie, from gentil gentle

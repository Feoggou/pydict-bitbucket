rootless

Powered by COBUILD
(ruːtləs ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If someone has no permanent home or job and is not settled in any community, you can describe them as rootless. ⇒ These rootless young people have nowhere else to go. ⇒ ...people who refused to integrate within society and instead lived rootless, jobless lives.rootlessness uncount noun ⇒ ...a social mobility that threatens to become rootlessness.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverb [ADV with v]If people or things are dealt with separately or do something separately, they are dealt with or do something at different times or places, rather than together. ⇒ Cook each vegetable separately until just tender. ⇒ Chris had insisted that we went separately to the club. [Also + from]
Example sentences containing 'separately'
They spoke little, as if words could only spoil what each had separately felt. Appiganesi, Lisa DREAMS OF INNOCENCEWe'll arrange for each separately to have a session with Freddie. Forbes, Colin COVER STORYNor did the mature modern woman eject her husband and his clothes separately. Stewart, Michael COMPULSION

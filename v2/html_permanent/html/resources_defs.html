resources

(rɪˈzɔːsɪz, rɪˈsɔːsɪz)
Definitions
plural noun1. sources of economic wealth, esp of a country (mineral, land, labour, etc) or business enterprise (capital, equipment, personnel, etc) ⇒ scarce oil resources, ⇒ resources like coal, tungsten, oil and copper, ⇒ Today we are overpopulated, straining the earth's resources., ⇒ Oil is one of their greatest resources., ⇒ Britain's energy resources. See also natural resources2. financial means; money ⇒ Julius invested all his resources in an unsuccessful restaurant., ⇒ Some families don't have enough resources to eat properly., ⇒ They didn't have the resources to send him to private school.3. formal personal qualities such as intelligence, imagination, etc ⇒ We support and mobilise inner resources and work on the inner child.4. See leave someone to their own resources

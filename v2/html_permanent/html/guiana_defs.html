Guiana

(ɡaɪˈænə , ɡɪˈɑːnə ) or The Guianas
Definitions
nouna region of NE South America, including Guyana, Surinam, French Guiana, and the Guiana Highlands (largely in SE Venezuela and partly in N Brazil). Area: about 1 787 000 sq km (690 000 sq miles)

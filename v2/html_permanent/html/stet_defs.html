stet

(stɛt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a word or mark indicating that certain deleted typeset or written matter is to be retained. Compare deleverbWord forms: stets, stetting or stetted2. (transitive) to mark (matter to be retained) with a stet

Word Origin

Latin, literally: let it stand

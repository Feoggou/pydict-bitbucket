offal

(ˈɒfəl )
Definitions
noun1. the edible internal parts of an animal, such as the heart, liver, and tongue2. dead or decomposing organic matter3. refuse; rubbish
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from off + fall, referring to parts fallen or cut off; compare German Abfall rubbish

lief

(liːf )
Definitions
adverb1. rare gladly; willingly ⇒ I'd as lief go today as tomorrowadjective2. archaica. ready; gladb. dear; beloved
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English leof; related to lufu love
Example sentences containing 'lief'
I'd as lief have you stay, if you would, but I'm not such a fool as to think you can hide here in the valley without being found eventually. Tepper, Sheri S.

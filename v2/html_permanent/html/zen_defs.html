Zen

(zɛn ) Buddhism
Definitions
noun1. a Japanese school, of 12th-century Chinese origin, teaching that contemplation of one's essential nature to the exclusion of all else is the only way of achieving pure enlightenment2. (modifier) of or relating to this school ⇒ Zen Buddhism
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Zenic (ˈZenic) adjectiveZenist (ˈZenist) noun

Word Origin

from Japanese, from Chinese ch'an religious meditation, from Pali jhāna, from Sanskrit dhyāna

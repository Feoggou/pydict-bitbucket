nourishing in American English

(ˈnɜrɪʃɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective contributing to health or growth; nutritious

Derived Forms

nourishingly (ˈnourishingly) adverb

Yes, there were stones at their heart ---hard, bitter stones---but the meat around those stones was moist and nourishing. Clive Barker GALILEEUnder Chade's strict directions, I ate a nourishing meal of bland and healthful food every noon.

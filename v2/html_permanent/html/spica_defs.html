spica

(ˈspaɪkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cae (-siː) or -cas1. medicine a spiral bandage formed by a series of overlapping figure-of-eight turns2. botany another word for spike2 (sense 1)

Word Origin

C15: from Latin: ear of corn

aniline

or anilin (ˈænɪlɪn , -ˌliːn)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna colourless oily pungent poisonous liquid used in the manufacture of dyes, plastics, pharmaceuticals, and explosives. Formula: C6H5NH2. Also called: phenylamine

short account

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the aggregate of short sales on an open market, esp a stock market2. the account of a stock-market speculator who sells short
Example sentences containing 'short account'
I'll tell Pravda to run the obituary and a short account , sanitised -- and Isvestya can do the same. Thomas, Craig THE LAST RAVEN

Sigismund

(ˈsɪɡɪsmənd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun 1368–1437, king of Hungary (1387–1437) and of Bohemia (1419–37); Holy Roman Emperor (1411–37). He helped to end the Great Schism in the Church; implicated in the death of Huss

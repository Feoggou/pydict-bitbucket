recollection

(ˌrɛkəˈlɛkʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of recalling something from memory; the ability to remember2. something remembered; a memory

Powered by COBUILD
(rekəlekʃən ) for learners of English
Word forms: recollections
Definitions
variable nounIf you have a recollection of something, you remember it. [+ of] ⇒ Pat has vivid recollections of the trip. [+ of] ⇒ He had no recollection of the crash. ⇒ Suddenly his mind filled with the recollection of a song she used to sing. ⇒ She had a sudden burst of recollection and had to share it with me.

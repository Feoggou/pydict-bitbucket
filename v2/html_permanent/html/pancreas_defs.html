pancreas

(ˈpæŋkrɪəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna large elongated glandular organ, situated behind the stomach, that secretes insulin and pancreatic juice

Derived Forms

pancreatic (ˌpæŋkrɪˈætɪk ) adjective

Word Origin

C16: via New Latin from Greek pankreas, from pan- + kreas flesh

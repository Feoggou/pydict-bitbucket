Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [oft N n]A trade union is an organization that has been formed by workers in order to represent their rights and interests to their employers, for example in order to improve working conditions or wages. [mainly British]regional note:   in AM, usually use labor union

services

(ˈsɜːvɪsɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. work performed for remuneration2. See the services3. (sometimes singular) economics commodities, such as banking, that are mainly intangible and usually consumed concurrently with their production. Compare goods (sense 2)4. a system of providing the public with gas, water, etc

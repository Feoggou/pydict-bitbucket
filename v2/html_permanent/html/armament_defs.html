armament

(ˈɑːməmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (often plural) the weapon equipment of a military vehicle, ship, or aircraft2. a military force raised and armed ready for war3. preparation for war involving the production of equipment and arms
Word origin
C17: from Latin armāmenta utensils, from armāre to equip

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounArmament is used to refer to weapons and bombs carried by an aircraft or other military vehicle. [military] ⇒ ...a bomber with twin engines and heavy defensive armament.2. uncount nounA country's increase in the number and effectiveness of its weapons is its armament. OPPOSITE: disarmament ⇒ ...the pursuit of national security through national armament. ⇒ The fears have prompted several countries to embark on massive armament plans.

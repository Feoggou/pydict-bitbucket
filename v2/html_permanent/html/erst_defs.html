erst

or earst (ɜːst )
Definitions
adverb archaic1. long ago; formerly2. at first
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English ǣrest earliest, superlative of ǣr early; see ere; related to Old High German ērist, Dutch eerst

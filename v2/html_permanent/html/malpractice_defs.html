malpractice

(mælˈpræktɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. immoral, illegal, or unethical professional conduct or neglect of professional duty2. any instance of improper professional conduct

Derived Forms

malpractitioner (ˌmælprækˈtɪʃənə ) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable noun [oft N n]If you accuse someone of malpractice, you are accusing them of breaking the law or the rules of their profession in order to gain some advantage for themselves. [formal] ⇒ There were only one or two serious allegations of malpractice. ⇒ ...alleged financial malpractices. ⇒ ...medical malpractice suits.

unscathed for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnskeɪðd )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [ADJ after v, v-link ADJ]If you are unscathed after a dangerous experience, you have not been injured or harmed by it. ⇒ Tony emerged unscathed apart from a severely bruised finger. [+ by] ⇒ East Los Angeles was left relatively unscathed by the riots. ⇒ The tobacco industry escaped unscathed from its toughest legal challenge.

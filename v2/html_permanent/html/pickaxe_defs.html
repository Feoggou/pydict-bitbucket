pickaxe

or US pickax (ˈpɪkˌæks )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a large pick or mattockverb2. to use a pickaxe on (earth, rocks, etc)

Word Origin

C15: from earlier pikois (but influenced also by axe), from Old French picois, from pic pick2; compare also pique1

regional note:   in AM, use pickaxcountable nounA pickaxe is a large tool consisting of a curved, pointed piece of metal with a long handle joined to the middle. Pickaxes are used for breaking up rocks or the ground.

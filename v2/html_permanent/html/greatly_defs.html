Definitions
graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj]You use greatly to emphasize the degree or extent of something. [formal, emphasis] ⇒ People would benefit greatly from a pollution-free vehicle. ⇒ We were greatly honoured that Sheik Hasina took the trouble to visit us.

genitals

(ˈdʒɛnɪtəlz ) or genitalia (ˌdʒɛnɪˈteɪlɪə , -ˈteɪljə)
Definitions
plural nounthe sexual organs; the testicles and penis of a male or the labia, clitoris, and vagina of a female ▶ Related adjective: venereal

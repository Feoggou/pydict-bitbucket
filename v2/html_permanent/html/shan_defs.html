Shan

(ʃɑːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Word forms: plural Shans or Shan a member of a Mongoloid people living in Myanmar, Thailand, and SW China2. the language or group of dialects spoken by the Shan, belonging to the Sino-Tibetan family and closely related to Thai

arson

(ˈɑːsən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouncriminal law the act of intentionally or recklessly setting fire to another's property or to one's own property for some improper reason
Derived forms
ˈarsonist noun
Word origin
C17: from Old French, from Medieval Latin ārsiō, from Latin ārdēre to burn; see ardent

Kingston

(ˈkɪŋstən )
Definitions
noun1. the capital and chief port of Jamaica, on the SE coast: University of the West Indies. Pop: 574 000 (2005 est)2. a port in SE Canada, in SE Ontario: the chief naval base of Lake Ontario and a large industrial centre; university (1841). Pop: 108 158 (2001)3. the capital of Norfolk Island, in the S Pacific Ocean4. short for Kingston upon Thames

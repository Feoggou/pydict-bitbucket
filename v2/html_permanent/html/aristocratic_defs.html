aristocratic

(ˌærɪstəˈkrætɪk ) or aristocratical (ˌærɪstəˈkrætɪkəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. relating to or characteristic of aristocracy or an aristocrat2. elegant or stylish in appearance and behaviour
Derived forms
ˌaristoˈcratically adverb

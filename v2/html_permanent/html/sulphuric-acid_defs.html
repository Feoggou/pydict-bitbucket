sulphuric acid

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna colourless dense oily corrosive liquid produced by the reaction of sulphur trioxide with water and used in accumulators and in the manufacture of fertilizers, dyes, and explosives. Formula: H2SO4. Systematic name: sulphuric(VI) acid

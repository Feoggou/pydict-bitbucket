Scythian

(ˈsɪðɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to ancient Scythia, its inhabitants, or their languagenoun2. a member of an ancient nomadic people of Scythia3. the extinct language of this people, belonging to the East Iranian branch of the Indo-European family

Savoyard1

(səˈvɔɪɑːd , French savwajar)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a native of Savoy2. the dialect of French spoken in Savoyadjective3. of or relating to Savoy, its inhabitants, or their dialect

Savoyard2

(səˈvɔɪɑːd )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person keenly interested in the operettas of Gilbert and Sullivan2. a person who takes part in these operettas

Word Origin

C20: from the Savoy Theatre, built in London in 1881 by Richard D'Oyly Carte for the presentation of operettas by Gilbert and Sullivan

Synonyms of peace

peace

noun

  1. truce, ceasefire, treaty, armistice, pacification, conciliation, cessation of hostilities
    • They hope the treaty will bring peace to Southeast Asia.
    OPPOSITES:  war, warfare, hostilities, fighting, fight, battle, conflict, hostility, strife, bloodshed, armed conflict
  2. stillness, rest, quiet, silence, calm, hush, tranquillity, seclusion, repose, calmness, peacefulness, quietude, restfulness
    • All I want is a bit of peace and quiet.
  3. serenity, calm, relaxation, composure, contentment, repose, equanimity, peacefulness, placidity, harmoniousness
    • People always felt a sense of peace in her company.
  4. harmony, accord, agreement, concord, amity
    • a period of relative peace in the country's industrial relations
related adjective:
irenic
eirenic

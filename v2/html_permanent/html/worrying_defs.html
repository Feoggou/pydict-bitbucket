Definitions
regional note:   in AM, usually use worrisomegraded adjective & adjectiveIf something is worrying, it causes people to worry. [mainly British] ⇒ It's worrying that they're doing things without training. ⇒ ...a new and worrying report about smoking. → worriedworryingly graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj] ⇒ The rate of assaults was worryingly high. ⇒ Worryingly for those in favour of competition, the Minister has been resistant to this argument.

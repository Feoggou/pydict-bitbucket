suture

(ˈsuːtʃə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. surgerya. catgut, silk thread, or wire used to stitch together two bodily surfacesb. the surgical seam formed after joining two surfaces. Also called: seam2. anatomy a type of immovable joint, esp between the bones of the skull (cranial suture)3. a seam or joining, as in sewing4. zoology a line of junction in a mollusc shell, esp the line between adjacent chambers of a nautiloid shell5. botany a line marking the point of dehiscence in a seed pod or capsuleverb6. (transitive) surgery to join (the edges of a wound, etc) by means of sutures

Derived Forms

sutural (ˈsutural) adjectivesuturally (ˈsuturally) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Latin sūtūra, from suere to sew

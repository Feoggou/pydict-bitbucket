starving

(ˈstɑːvɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. dying of hunger ⇒ Getting food to starving people does nothing to stop the war.2. informal very hungry; ravenous ⇒ Apart from anything else I was starving., ⇒ I'm absolutely starving!

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [v-link ADJ]If you say that you are starving, you mean that you are very hungry. [informal] ⇒ Apart from anything else I was starving.

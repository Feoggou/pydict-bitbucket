Punjab

(pʌnˈdʒɑːb , ˈpʌndʒɑːb )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (formerly) a province in NW British India: divided between India and Pakistan in 19472. a state of NW India: reorganized in 1966 as a Punjabi-speaking state, a large part forming the new state of Haryana; mainly agricultural. Capital: Chandigarh. Pop: 24 289 296 (2001). Area: 50 255 sq km (19 403 sq miles)3. a province of W Pakistan: created in 1947. Capital: Lahore. Pop: 82 710 000 (2003 est). Area: 205 344 sq km (127 595 sq miles)

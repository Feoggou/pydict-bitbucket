shivery

(ˈʃɪvərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. inclined to shiver or tremble2. causing shivering, esp through cold or fear

graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are shivery, you cannot stop shivering because you feel cold, frightened, or ill. ⇒ She felt shivery and a little sick.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are affectionate, you show your love or fondness for another person in the way that you behave towards them. ⇒ They seemed devoted to each other and were openly affectionate. ⇒ She gave me a very long and affectionate hug.affectionately graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ He looked affectionately at his niece.

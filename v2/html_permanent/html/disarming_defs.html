Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone or something is disarming, they make you feel less angry or hostile. ⇒ Leonard approached with a disarming smile. ⇒ When you meet him, he is disarming as he talks about himself.disarmingly adverb [usu ADV adj, oft ADV with v] ⇒ He is, as ever, business-like, and disarmingly honest. ⇒ She looked at him directly and occasionally smiled disarmingly at him.

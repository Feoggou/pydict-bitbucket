eyewitness

(ˈaɪˌwɪtnɪs )
Definitions
nouna. a person present at an event who can describe what happenedb. (as modifier) ⇒ an eyewitness account

eyewitness

Powered by COBUILD
(aɪwɪtnəs ) for learners of English
Word forms: eyewitnesses
Definitions
countable nounAn eyewitness is a person who was present at an event and can therefore describe it, for example in a law court. ⇒ Eyewitnesses say the police then opened fire on the crowd. ⇒ ...dramatic eye-witness accounts of the fighting.

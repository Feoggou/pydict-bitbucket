ass1

(æs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. either of two perissodactyl mammals of the horse family (Equidae), Equus asinus (African wild ass) or E. hemionus (Asiatic wild ass). They are hardy and sure-footed, having longer ears than the horse ▶ Related adjective: asinine2. (not in technical use) the domesticated variety of the African wild ass; donkey3. a foolish or ridiculously pompous person4. See not within an ass's roar of
Word origin
Old English assa, probably from Old Irish asan, from Latin asinus; related to Greek onos ass

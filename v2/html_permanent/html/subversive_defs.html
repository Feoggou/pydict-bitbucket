subversive

(səbˈvɜːsɪv ) or subversionary (səbˈvɜːʃənərɪ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. liable to subvert or overthrow a government, legally constituted institution, etcnoun2. a person engaged in subversive activities, etc

Derived Forms

subversively (subˈversively) adverbsubversiveness (subˈversiveness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is subversive is intended to weaken or destroy a political system or government. ⇒ This courageous and subversive movie has attracted widespread critical support.2. countable nounSubversives are people who attempt to weaken or destroy a political system or government. ⇒ Agents regularly rounded up suspected subversives.

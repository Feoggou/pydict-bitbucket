bracelet

(ˈbreɪslɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an ornamental chain worn around the arm or wrist2. an expanding metal band for a wristwatcharmillary
Word origin
C15: from Old French, from bracel, literally: a little arm, from Latin bracchium arm; see brace

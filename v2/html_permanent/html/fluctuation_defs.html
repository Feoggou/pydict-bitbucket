fluctuation

(ˌflʌktjʊˈeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. constant change; vacillation; instability2. undulation3. a variation in an animal or plant that is determined by environment rather than heredity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Similar Words

change, shift, swing, variation, instability, alteration, wavering, oscillation, alternation, vacillation, unsteadiness, inconstancy

angler

(ˈæŋɡlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who fishes with a rod and line2. informal a person who schemes or uses devious methods to secure an advantage3. Also called: anglerfish any spiny-finned fish of the order Pediculati (or Lophiiformes). They live at the bottom of the sea and typically have a long spiny movable dorsal fin with which they lure their prey

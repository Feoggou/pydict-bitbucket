thereby

(ˌðɛəˈbaɪ , ˈðɛəˌbaɪ )
Definitions
adverb1. by that means; because of that2. archaic by or near that place; thereabouts

thereby

Powered by COBUILD
(ðeəʳbaɪ ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverbYou use thereby to introduce an important result or consequence of the event or action you have just mentioned. [formal] ⇒ Our bodies can sweat, thereby losing heat by evaporation. ⇒ A firm might sometimes sell at a loss to drive a competitor out of business, and thereby increase its market power.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

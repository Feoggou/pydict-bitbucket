electrified

(ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. charged with electricity ⇒ an electrified fence2. archaic subjected to electricity ⇒ He was electrified as part of the experiment.3. intensely startled or excited; shocked or thrilled ⇒ Readers were electrified by the paper's audacity., ⇒ The world was electrified by his courage.

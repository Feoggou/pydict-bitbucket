lost-and-found

(ˌlɒstənˈfaʊnd)
Definitions
adjectiverelating to or consisting of lost property ⇒ lost-and-found items, ⇒ the shelf where they stored lost-and-found articles

Definitions
1. singular nounLost and found is the place where lost property is kept. [US]regional note:   in BRIT, use lost property2. adjectiveLost and found things are things which someone has lost and which someone else has found. ⇒ ...the shelf where they stored lost-and-found articles. ⇒ ...the local paper's lost-and-found column.

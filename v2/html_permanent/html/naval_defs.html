naval

(ˈneɪvəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, characteristic of, or having a navy2. of or relating to ships; nautical
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin nāvālis, from nāvis ship; related to Greek naus, Old Norse nōr ship, Sanskrit nau

naval for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(neɪvəl )
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]Naval means belonging to, relating to, or involving a country's navy. ⇒ He was the senior serving naval officer. ⇒ ...the U.S. naval base at Guantanamo Bay.

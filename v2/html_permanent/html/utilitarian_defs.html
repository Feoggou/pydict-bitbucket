utilitarian

(juːˌtɪlɪˈtɛərɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to utilitarianism2. designed for use rather than beautynoun3. a person who believes in utilitarianism

utilitarian for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(juːtɪlɪteəriən )
Word forms: utilitarians
Definitions
1. adjectiveUtilitarian means based on the idea that the morally correct course of action is the one that produces benefit for the greatest number of people. [technical] ⇒ It was James Mill who was the best publicist for utilitarian ideas on government.A utilitarian is someone with utilitarian views. ⇒ One of the greatest utilitarians was Claude Helvetius.2. graded adjective & adjectiveUtilitarian objects and buildings are designed to be useful rather than attractive. ⇒ Bruce's office is a corner one, utilitarian and unglamorous.

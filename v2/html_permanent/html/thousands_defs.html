thousands

(ˈθaʊzəndz)
Definitions
plural noun1. a very large but unspecified number, amount, or quantity ⇒ Thousands of refugees are packed into over-crowded towns and villages., ⇒ Hundreds have been killed in the fighting and thousands made homeless., ⇒ I must have driven past that place thousands of times.2. the numbers 2000–9999

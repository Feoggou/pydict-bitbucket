receivable

(rɪˈsiːvəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. suitable for or capable of being received, esp as payment or legal tender2. (of a bill, etc) awaiting payment ⇒ accounts receivablenoun3. (usually plural) the part of the assets of a business represented by accounts due for payment

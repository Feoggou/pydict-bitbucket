inconsiderable

(ˌɪnkənˈsɪdərəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. relatively small2. not worthy of consideration; insignificant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inconsiderableness (ˌinconˈsiderableness) nouninconsiderably (ˌinconˈsiderably) adverb

inconsiderable

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnkənsɪdərəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [with neg, usu ADJ n]If you describe an amount or quality as not inconsiderable, you are emphasizing that it is, in fact, large or present to a large degree. [emphasis] ⇒ The production costs are a not inconsiderable £8 million. ⇒ He was a man of great charm and not inconsiderable wit.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

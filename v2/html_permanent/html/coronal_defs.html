coronal

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (ˈkɒrənəl )1. poetic a circlet for the head; crown2. a wreath or garland3. anatomy short for coronal sutureadjective (kəˈrəʊnəl )4. of or relating to a corona or coronal5. phonetics a less common word for retroflex
Word origin
C16: from Late Latin corōnālis belonging to a crown
Example sentences containing 'coronal'
The eyes were hollow and the carven beard was broken, but about the high stern forehead there was a coronal of silver and gold. J.R.R. Tolkien THE LORD OF THE RINGS

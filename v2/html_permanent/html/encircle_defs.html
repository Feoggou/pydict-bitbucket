encircle

(ɪnˈsɜːkəl )
Definitions
verb(transitive) to form a circle around; enclose within a circle; surround
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

encirclement (enˈcirclement) nounencircling (enˈcircling) adjective

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnsɜːʳkəl ) for learners of English
Word forms: encircles , encircling , encircled
Definitions
verbTo encircle something or someone means to surround or enclose them, or to go round them. [V n] ⇒ A forty-foot-high concrete wall encircles the jail. [V n] ⇒ By 22nd November the Sixth Army was encircled.

undone

(ʌnˈdʌn )
Definitions
adjective1. not done or completed; unfinished2. ruined; destroyed3. unfastened; untied

undone for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌndʌn )
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ after v]Work that is undone has not yet been done. ⇒ He left nothing undone that needed attention.2. → See also undo

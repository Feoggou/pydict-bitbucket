High Church

Definitions
noun1. the party or movement within the Church of England stressing continuity with Catholic Christendom, the authority of bishops, and the importance of sacraments, rituals, and ceremonies. Compare Broad Church, Low Churchadjective High-Church2. of or relating to this party or movement

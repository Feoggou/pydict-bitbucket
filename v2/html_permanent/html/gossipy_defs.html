gossipy

Powered by COBUILD
(gɒsɪpi ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a book or account as gossipy, you mean it is informal and full of interesting but often unimportant news or information about people. ⇒ ...a chatty, gossipy account of Forster's life.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone as gossipy, you are critical of them because they talk about other people's private lives a great deal. [disapproval] ⇒ ...gossipy old women.

Bodleian

(bɒdˈliːən , ˈbɒdlɪ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe principal library of Oxford University: a copyright deposit library
Word origin
C17: named after Sir Thomas Bodley (1545–1613), English scholar who founded it in 1602

Bethesda

(bəˈθɛzdə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. New Testament a pool in Jerusalem reputed to have healing powers, where a paralysed man was healed by Jesus (John 5:2)2. a chapel of any of certain Nonconformist Christian sects

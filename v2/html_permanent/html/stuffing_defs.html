stuffing

(ˈstʌfɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the material with which something is stuffed2. a mixture of chopped and seasoned ingredients with which poultry, meat, etc, is stuffed before cooking3. See knock the stuffing out of someone

stuffing

(stʌfɪŋ )
Word forms: stuffings
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounStuffing is a mixture of food that is put inside a bird such as a chicken, or a vegetable such as a pepper, before it is cooked. ⇒ Chestnuts can be used at Christmas time, as a stuffing for turkey, guinea fowl or chicken.2. uncount nounStuffing is material that is used to fill things such as cushions or toys in order to make them firm or solid.3. →  knock the stuffing out of sb

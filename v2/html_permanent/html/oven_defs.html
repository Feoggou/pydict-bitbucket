oven

(ˈʌvən )
Definitions
noun1. an enclosed heated compartment or receptacle for baking or roasting food2. a similar device, usually lined with a refractory material, used for drying substances, firing ceramics, heat-treating, etcverb3. (transitive) to cook in an oven
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

oven-like (ˈoven-ˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

Old English ofen; related to Old High German ofan, Old Norse ofn

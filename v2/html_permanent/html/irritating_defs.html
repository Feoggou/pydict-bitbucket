irritating

(ˈɪrɪteɪtɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. causing annoyance or anger ⇒ They have the irritating habit of constantly interrupting.2. (of a substance, chemical, etc) causing inflammation or tenderness of a bodily organ or part ⇒ The gas is highly irritating to the mucous membranes., ⇒ oils may be slightly irritating to the skin, ⇒ In heavy concentrations ozone is irritating to the eyes, nose, and throat.

(blɪzəʳd )
Word forms: blizzards
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA blizzard is a very heavy snowstorm with strong winds.2. countable noun [usu sing, usu N of n]You can refer to a large number of things that you do not like or which annoy you as a blizzard of those things. [written, disapproval] ⇒ Retailers were hit with a blizzard of new products and brands in all shapes, sizes and colours. ⇒ ...the annual blizzard of bills and amendments.

Edam

(ˈiːdæm , Dutch ˈeːdam)
Definitions
noun1. a town in the NW Netherlands, in North Holland province, on the IJsselmeer: cheese, light manufacturing. Pop: 28 000 (2003 est; includes Volendam)2. a hard round mild-tasting Dutch cheese, yellow in colour with a red outside covering

hors d'oeuvre - French

(ɔː ˈdɜːvr , ɔr dœvrə)
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural hors d'oeuvre or hors d'oeuvres (ˈdɜːvr , French dœvrə)an additional dish served as an appetizer, usually before the main meal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from French, literally: outside the work, not part of the main course

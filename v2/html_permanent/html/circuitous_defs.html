Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A circuitous route is long and complicated rather than simple and direct. [formal] ⇒ The cabdriver took them on a circuitous route to the police station. ⇒ Stuart came into film-making via a circuitous route.

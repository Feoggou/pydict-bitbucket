kicking

(ˈkɪkɪn)
Definitions
adjective1. old-fashioned, informal lively and exciting ⇒ a really kicking club, ⇒ a kicking beatnoun2. an act of kicking someone ⇒ Giving someone a severe kicking is clearly against the law., ⇒ A Labour official said that people who wanted to give the Government a kicking had stayed at home rather than vote.

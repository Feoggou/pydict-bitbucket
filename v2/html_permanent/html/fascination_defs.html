fascination

Powered by COBUILD
(fæsɪneɪʃən ) for learners of English
Word forms: fascinations
Definitions
1. uncount nounFascination is the state of being greatly interested in or delighted by something. [+ with/of/for] ⇒ I've had a lifelong fascination with the sea and with small boats.2. countable nounA fascination is something that fascinates people. ⇒ ...a series focusing on the fascinations of the British Museum.

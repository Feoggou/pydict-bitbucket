Synonyms of socialist

socialist

adjective

  1. left-wing, communist, Marxist, Labour, red, progressive, social democratic, leftist, Fabian, Leninist, Trotskyist, syndicalist
    • members of the ruling Socialist party

noun

  1. left-winger, communist, Marxist, red, social democrat, leftist, Fabian, Leninist, Trotskyist, syndicalist, Labourite, progressivist
    • His views have always been popular among socialists.

sturdy1

(ˈstɜːdɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -dier or -diest1. healthy, strong, and vigorous2. strongly built; stalwart

Derived Forms

sturdily (ˈsturdily) adverbsturdiness (ˈsturdiness) noun

Word Origin

C13 (in the sense: rash, harsh): from Old French estordi dazed, from estordir to stun, perhaps ultimately related to Latin turdus a thrush (taken as representing drunkenness)

egregious

(ɪˈɡriːdʒəs , -dʒɪəs)
Definitions
adjective1. outstandingly bad; flagrant ⇒ an egregious lie2. archaic distinguished; eminent
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

egregiously (eˈgregiously) adverbegregiousness (eˈgregiousness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin ēgregius outstanding (literally: standing out from the herd), from ē- out + grex flock, herd

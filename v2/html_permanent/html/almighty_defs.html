Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. proper nounThe Almighty is another name for God. You can also refer to Almighty God. ⇒ Adam sought guidance from the Almighty. ⇒ Let us now confess our sins to Almighty God.2. exclamationPeople sometimes say God Almighty or Christ Almighty to express their surprise, anger, or horror. These expressions could cause offence. [feelings]3. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]Almighty means very serious or great in extent. [informal, emphasis] ⇒ I had the most almighty row with the waitress. ⇒ I heard an almighty bang.

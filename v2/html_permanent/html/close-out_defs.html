close out

(kləʊz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (adverb) to terminate (a client's or other account) on which the margin is inadequate or exhausted, usually by sale of securities to realize cashnoun closeout2. the termination of an account on which the margin is exhausted

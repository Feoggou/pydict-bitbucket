blood flow

(blʌd fləʊ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe flow of blood through blood vessels around the body ⇒ a drug that improves blood flow, ⇒ the valve that controls the blood flow between the two chambers of the heart, ⇒ Exercise can improve blood flow to the feet and legs.

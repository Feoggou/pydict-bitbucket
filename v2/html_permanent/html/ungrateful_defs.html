ungrateful

(ʌnˈɡreɪtfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. not grateful or thankful2. unrewarding or unpleasant; thankless3. (of land) failing to increase fertility in response to cultivation
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ungratefully (unˈgratefully) adverbungratefulness (unˈgratefulness) noun

ungrateful for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌngreɪtfʊl )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as ungrateful, you are criticizing them for not showing thanks or for being unkind to someone who has helped them or done them a favour. [disapproval] ⇒ I thought it was rather ungrateful. ⇒ You ungrateful brat.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

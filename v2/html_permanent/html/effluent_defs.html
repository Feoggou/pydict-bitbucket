effluent

(ˈɛflʊənt )
Definitions
noun1. liquid discharged as waste, as from an industrial plant or sewage works2. radioactive waste released from a nuclear power station3. a stream that flows out of another body of water4. something that flows out or forthadjective5. flowing out or forth
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Latin effluere to run forth, from fluere to flow

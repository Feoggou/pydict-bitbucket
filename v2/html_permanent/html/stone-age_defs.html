Stone Age

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounarchaeologya. a period in human culture identified by the use of stone implements and usually divided into the Palaeolithic, Mesolithic, and Neolithic stagesb. (as modifier) ⇒ Stone-Age man

Stone-Age

(stəʊn eɪdʒ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
modifier(sometimes not capitals) of or relating to this period ⇒ Stone Age man hunted the woolly mammoth to extinction 13,000 years ago., ⇒ This has contributed to the perception that they are a strange, Stone Age people with undeveloped sensibilities., ⇒ Her daughter was put on a stone-age diet with no dairy products, additives, beef, pork, wheat, cereals, colouring and certain fruits., ⇒ He found some late Stone Age tools., ⇒ Some evidence indicates that Stone Age hunter-gatherers were healthier than early farmers., ⇒ Stone Age culture

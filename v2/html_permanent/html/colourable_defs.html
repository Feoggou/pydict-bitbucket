colourable

or US colorable (ˈkʌlərəbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. capable of being coloured2. appearing to be true; plausible ⇒ a colourable excuse3. pretended; feigned ⇒ colourable affection
Derived forms
ˌcolourability (ˌcolouraˈbility) or US ˌcolorability (ˌcoloraˈbility), colourableness (ˈcolourableness) or US colorableness (ˈcolorableness) nouncolourably (ˈcolourably) or US colorably (ˈcolorably) adverb

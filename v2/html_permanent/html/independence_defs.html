independence

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndɪpendəns ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounIf a country has or gains independence, it has its own government and is not ruled by any other country. [+ from] ⇒ In 1816, Argentina declared its independence from Spain. ⇒ ...the country's first elections since independence in 1962.2. uncount noun [oft poss N]Someone's independence is the fact that they do not rely on other people. ⇒ He was afraid of losing his independence. ⇒ ...a woman's independence, capability, and power.

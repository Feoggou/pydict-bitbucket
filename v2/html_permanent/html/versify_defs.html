versify

(ˈvɜːsɪˌfaɪ )
Definitions
verb -fies, -fying or -fied1. (transitive) to render (something) into metrical form or verse2. (intransitive) to write in verse
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

versifier (ˈversiˌfier) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Old French versifier, from Latin versificāre, from versus verse + facere to make

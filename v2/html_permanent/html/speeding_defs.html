speeding

(ˈspiːdɪŋ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna. the act of driving a vehicle at high speed, esp in excess of the speed limit ⇒ His driver's licence was suspended for speeding., ⇒ He was fined for speeding.b. (as modifier) ⇒ a speeding offence
Example sentences containing 'speeding'
The air was damp now; quiet yet weighty: full-bellied with the snarl of speeding cars in the distance. Nicola Barker BEHINDLINGSThe winged shapes wheeled, and stooped swiftly down, speeding back to Mordor. J.R.R. Tolkien THE LORD OF THE RINGSIn the Daimler speeding westwards, Heather Lawler looked across at her husband.

cumulus

(ˈkjuːmjʊləs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -li (-ˌlaɪ)1. a bulbous or billowing white or dark grey cloud associated with rising air currents. Compare cirrus (sense 1), stratus2. histology the mass of cells surrounding a recently ovulated egg cell in a Graafian follicle
Word origin
C17: from Latin: mass

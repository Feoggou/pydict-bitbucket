fad

(fæd )
Definitions
noun informal1. an intense but short-lived fashion; craze2. a personal idiosyncrasy or whim
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

faddish (ˈfaddish) adjectivefaddishness (ˈfaddishness) nounfaddism (ˈfaddism) nounfaddist (ˈfaddist) noun

Word Origin

C19: of uncertain origin

FAD

Definitions
nounbiochemistry flavin adenine dinucleotide: an ester of riboflavin with ADP that acts as the prosthetic group for many flavoproteins. See also FMN

Definitions
uncount noun [oft N n]Road rage is anger or violent behaviour caused by someone else's bad driving or the stress of being in heavy traffic. ⇒ ...a road rage attack on a male motorist. ⇒ A Times reporter yesterday became another victim of road rage.

thither

(ˈðɪðə ), thitherward (ˈðɪðəwəd ) or thitherwards (ˈðɪðəwədz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverbobsolete or formal to or towards that place; in that direction ⇒ the flowers and music which attract people thither

Word Origin

Old English thider, variant of thæder, influenced by hider hither; related to Old Norse thathra there

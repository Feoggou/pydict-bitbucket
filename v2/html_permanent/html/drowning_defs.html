drowning

(ˈdraʊnɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. on the verge of being drowned ⇒ Dolphins have sometimes been known to save drowning swimmers.2. See a drowning man will clutch at a strawnoun3. an instance of someone's dying when submerged in water and therefore unable to breathe ⇒ There were three drownings here last year., ⇒ Her life unravels after the accidental drowning of a child in her care.4. an instance of someone being deliberately submerged in water until they die ⇒ the drowning of toddlers by their mother, ⇒ the drowning of dogs

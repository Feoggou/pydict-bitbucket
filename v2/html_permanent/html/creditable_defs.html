Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA creditable performance or achievement is of a reasonably high standard. ⇒ They turned out a quite creditable performance. ⇒ Gazza finished a creditable third.creditably (kredɪtəbli ) graded adverb [usu ADV with v, also ADV adj/adv] ⇒ British riders performed creditably. ⇒ She puts in a creditably spirited performance.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone's actions or aims as creditable, you mean that they are morally good. ⇒ Not a very creditable attitude, I'm afraid.

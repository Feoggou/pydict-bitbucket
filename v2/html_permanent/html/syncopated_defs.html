syncopated

(ˈsɪŋkəˌpeɪtɪd)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. music (of a beat, rhythm, note, etc) modified or treated by syncopation ⇒ Some spirituals are based on syncopated rhythms.2. linguistics (of a word) shortened by the omission of sounds or letters from the middle

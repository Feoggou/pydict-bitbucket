spinal

(ˈspaɪnəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to the spine or the spinal cord2. denoting a laboratory animal in which the spinal cord has been severed ⇒ a spinal ratnoun3. short for spinal anaesthesia

Derived Forms

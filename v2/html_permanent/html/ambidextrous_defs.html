ambidextrous

(ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəs ) or ambidexterous (ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəs, æmbɪˈdɛkstərəs)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. equally expert with each hand2. informal highly skilled or adept3. underhanded; deceitful
Derived forms
ambidexterity (ˌæmbɪdɛkˈstɛrɪtɪ ) or ˌambiˈdextrousness nounˌambiˈdextrously adverb

Definitions

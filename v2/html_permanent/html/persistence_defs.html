persistence

(pəˈsɪstəns ) or persistency
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the quality of persisting; tenacity2. the act of persisting; continued effort or existence3. the continuance of an effect after the cause of it has stopped ⇒ persistence of vision

persistence

Powered by COBUILD
(pəʳsɪstəns )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounIf you have persistence, you continue to do something even though it is difficult or other people are against it. ⇒ Skill comes only with practice, patience and persistence. ⇒ Chandra was determined to become a doctor and her persistence paid off.2. uncount nounThe persistence of something, especially something bad, is the fact of its continuing to exist for a long time. [+ of] ⇒ ...an expression of concern at the persistence of inflation and high interest rates.

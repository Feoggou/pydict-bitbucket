stair

(stɛə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. one of a flight of stairs2. a series of steps ⇒ a narrow stair

Word Origin

Old English stæger; related to stīg narrow path, stīgan to ascend, descend, Old Norse steigurligr upright, Middle Dutch steiger ladder

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. plural nounStairs are a set of steps inside a building which go from one floor to another. ⇒ Nancy began to climb the stairs. ⇒ We walked up a flight of stairs. ⇒ He learned to walk safely up and down stairs. ⇒ He stopped at the top of the stairs. ⇒ ...a stair carpet.2. singular nounA stair is a flight of stairs. [literary] staircase ⇒ I followed her down the stair.3. countable nounA stair is one of the steps in a flight of stairs. ⇒ Terry was sitting on the bottom stair.

hardware

(ˈhɑːdˌwɛə )
Definitions
noun1. metal tools, implements, etc, esp cutlery or cooking utensils2. computing the physical equipment used in a computer system, such as the central processing unit, peripheral devices, and memory. Compare software3. mechanical equipment, components, etc4. heavy military equipment, such as tanks and missiles or their parts5. informal a gun or guns collectively

Definitions
1. uncount nounIn computer systems, hardware refers to the machines themselves as opposed to the programs which tell the machines what to do. Compare software.2. uncount noun [usu adj N]Military hardware is the machinery and equipment that is used by the armed forces, such as tanks, aircraft, and missiles.3. uncount nounHardware refers to tools and equipment that are used in the home and garden, for example saucepans, screwdrivers, and lawnmowers.

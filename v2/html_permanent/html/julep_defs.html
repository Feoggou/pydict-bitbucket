julep

(ˈdʒuːlɪp )
Definitions
noun1. a sweet drink, variously prepared and sometimes medicated2. mainly US short for mint julep
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French, from Arabic julāb, from Persian gulāb rose water, from gul rose + āb water
Example sentences containing 'julep'
"And a mint julep ," the waitress told him as she slid back to the ground. Melinda Metz REVELATIONS

Puerto Rico

(ˈpwɜːtəʊ ˈriːkəʊ , ˈpwɛə-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan autonomous commonwealth (in association with the US) occupying the smallest and easternmost of the Greater Antilles in the Caribbean: one of the most densely populated areas in the world; ceded by Spain to the US in 1899. Currency: US dollar. Capital: San Juan. Pop: 3 674 209 (2013 est). Area: 9104 sq km (3515 sq miles). Former name (until 1932): Porto Rico. Abbreviation: PR

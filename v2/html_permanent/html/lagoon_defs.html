lagoon

(ləˈɡuːn )
Definitions
noun1. a body of water cut off from the open sea by coral reefs or sand bars2. any small body of water, esp one adjoining a larger one
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Italian laguna, from Latin lacūna pool; see lacuna

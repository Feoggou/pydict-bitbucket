Walloon

(wɒˈluːn )
Definitions
noun1. a member of a French-speaking people living chiefly in S Belgium and adjacent parts of France. Compare Fleming12. the French dialect of Belgiumadjective3. of, relating to, or characteristic of the Walloons or their dialect
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Old French Wallon, from Medieval Latin: foreigner, of Germanic origin; compare Old English wealh foreign, Welsh1

Synonyms of marriage

marriage

noun

  1. wedding, match, nuptials, wedlock, wedding ceremony, matrimony, espousal, nuptial rites
    • When did the marriage take place?
  2. union, coupling, link, association, alliance, merger, confederation, amalgamation
    • The merger is an audacious marriage between old and new.
related adjectives:
conjugal
connubial
hymeneal
marital
nuptial
related mania:

conjugal

(ˈkɒndʒʊɡəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveof or relating to marriage or a married couple's relationship ⇒ conjugal rights
Derived forms
conjugality (ˌkɒndʒʊˈɡælɪtɪ ) nounconjugally (ˈconjugally) adverb
Word origin
C16: from Latin conjugālis, from conjunx wife or husband, from conjungere to unite; see conjoin

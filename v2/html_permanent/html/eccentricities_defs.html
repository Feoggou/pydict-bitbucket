eccentricities

(ˌɛksɛnˈtrɪsɪtɪz)
Definitions
plural noun1. See eccentricity2. ways of behaving that people think are strange, or habits or opinions that are different from those of most people ⇒ the eccentricities of British life, ⇒ We all have our eccentricities.

cowl

(kaʊl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a hood, esp a loose one2. the hooded habit of a monk3. a cover fitted to a chimney to increase ventilation and prevent draughts4. the part of a car body that supports the windscreen and the bonnet5. aeronautics another word for cowlingverb (transitive)6. to cover or provide with a cowl7. to make a monk of
Word origin
Old English cugele, from Late Latin cuculla cowl, from Latin cucullus covering, cap, hood

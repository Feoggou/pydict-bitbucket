Cronus

(ˈkrəʊnəs ), Cronos or Kronos (ˈkrəʊnɒs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounGreek mythology a Titan, son of Uranus (sky) and Gaea (earth), who ruled the world until his son Zeus dethroned him. Roman counterpart: Saturn

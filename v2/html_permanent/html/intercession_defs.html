intercession

(ˌɪntəˈsɛʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act or an instance of interceding2. the act of interceding or offering petitionary prayer to God on behalf of others3. such petitionary prayer4. Roman history the interposing of a veto by a tribune or other magistrate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

intercessional (ˌinterˈcessional) or intercessory (ˌinterˈcessory) adjectiveintercessor (ˌinterˈcessor) nounintercessorial (ˌintercesˈsorial) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Latin intercessio; see intercede

Definitions
adjectiveIf you are heedless of someone or something, you do not take any notice of them. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ Heedless of time or any other consideration, they began to search the underwater cave. ⇒ She was rummaging through the letters, scattering them about the table in her heedless haste.

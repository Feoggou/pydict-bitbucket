sentry

(ˈsɛntrɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -tries1. a soldier who guards or prevents unauthorized access to a place, keeps watch for danger, etc2. the watch kept by a sentry

Word Origin

C17: perhaps shortened from obsolete centrinel, C16 variant of sentinel

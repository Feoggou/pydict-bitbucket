poulterer

(ˈpəʊltərə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounBritish another word for a poultryman

Word Origin

C17: from obsolete poulter, from Old French pouletier, from poulet pullet

poulterer

(ˈpoʊltərər )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun British a dealer in poultry and game: also ; Archaic ˈpoulter

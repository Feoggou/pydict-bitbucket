croquet

(ˈkrəʊkeɪ , -kɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a game for two to four players who hit a wooden ball through iron hoops with mallets in order to hit a peg2. the act of croquetingverb -quets (-keɪz, -kɪz), -queting (-keɪɪŋ, -kɪɪŋ) or -queted (-keɪd, -kɪd)3. to drive away (another player's ball) by hitting one's own ball when the two are in contact
Word origin
C19: perhaps from French dialect, variant of crochet (little hook)

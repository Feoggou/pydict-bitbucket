(keɪɒtɪk )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is chaotic is in a state of complete disorder and confusion. ⇒ My own house feels as filthy and chaotic as a bus terminal. ⇒ Mullins began to rummage among the chaotic mess of papers on his desk.chaotically adverb [ADV after v, ADV -ed/adj] ⇒ Everything breakable had been broken and scattered chaotically about the room.

toleration

(ˌtɒləˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or practice of tolerating2. freedom to hold religious opinions that differ from the established or prescribed religion of a country

Derived Forms

ˌtolerationism (ˌtolerˈationism) nounˌtolerationist (ˌtolerˈationist) noun
Example sentences containing 'toleration'
I've had almost every office in my local, and sergeant-at-arms twice because the guys say I have no toleration for BS. Kingsolver, Barbara HOMELAND AND OTHER STORIES

lunar

(ˈluːnə )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to the moon2. occurring on, used on, or designed to land on the surface of the moon ⇒ lunar module3. relating to, caused by, or measured by the position or orbital motion of the moon4. of or containing silver
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin lūnāris, from lūna the moon

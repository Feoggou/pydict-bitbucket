Definitions

Definitions
pronunciation note:   The verb is pronounced (riːjuːz ). The noun is pronounced (riːjuːs ).verbWhen you reuse something, you use it again instead of throwing it away. [V n] ⇒ Try where possible to reuse paper.Reuse is also a noun. ⇒ Copper, brass and aluminium are separated and remelted for reuse.

breakneck

Powered by COBUILD
(breɪknek )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]If you say that something happens or travels at breakneck speed, you mean that it happens or travels very fast. ⇒ Jack drove to Mayfair at breakneck speed.
Example sentences containing 'breakneck'
Reggie spoke rapidly, gradually speeding up even more, to race through the explanation at breakneck pace.

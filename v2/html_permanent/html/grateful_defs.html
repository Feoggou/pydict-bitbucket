grateful

(ˈɡreɪtfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. thankful for gifts, favours, etc; appreciative2. showing gratitude ⇒ a grateful letter3. favourable or pleasant ⇒ a grateful rest
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gratefully (ˈgratefully) adverbgratefulness (ˈgratefulness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from obsolete grate, from Latin grātus + -ful

grateful

Powered by COBUILD
(greɪtfʊl ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are grateful for something that someone has given you or done for you, you have warm, friendly feelings towards them and wish to thank them. [+ to] ⇒ She was grateful to him for being so good to her. ⇒ I should like to extend my grateful thanks to all the volunteers. [Also + for]gratefully graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ 'That's kind of you, Sally,' Claire said gratefully.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

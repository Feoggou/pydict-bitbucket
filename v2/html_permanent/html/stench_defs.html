stench

(stɛntʃ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna strong and extremely offensive odour; stink

Word Origin

Old English stenc; related to Old Saxon, Old High German stank; see stink

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA stench is a strong and very unpleasant smell. [+ of] ⇒ The stench of burning rubber was overpowering.
Example sentences containing 'stench'
She is Iying down now, her head on the boards, and the stench she can smell is flesh.

retreating

(rɪˈtriːtɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. moving away ⇒ The young nurse pulled a face at the Matron's retreating figure., ⇒ He heard the sound of retreating footsteps.2. receding ⇒ Mr Allsop with his grey complexion and retreating hairline, ⇒ His retreating hair didn't stand a chance against John F. Kennedy's youthful mane.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectivePeople who are daring are willing to do or say things which are new or which might shock or anger other people. ⇒ Bergit was probably more daring than I was. ⇒ He realized this to be a very daring thing to ask. ⇒ ...one of the most daring political theatres in Prague.daringly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj] ⇒ 'Cheers,' he said and winked daringly at her. ⇒ ...a daringly low-cut dress.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A daring person is willing to do things that might be dangerous. ⇒ ...the heroic and daring Charlie Pierce. ⇒ His daring rescue saved the lives of the youngsters.3. uncount nounDaring is the courage to do things which might be dangerous or which might shock or anger other people. ⇒ His daring may have cost him his life.

tsetse fly

or tzetze fly (ˈtsɛtsɪ )
Definitions
nounany of various bloodsucking African dipterous flies of the genus Glossina, which transmit the pathogens of various diseases: family Muscidae
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: via Afrikaans from Tswana

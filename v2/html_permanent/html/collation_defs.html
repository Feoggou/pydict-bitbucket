collation

(kɒˈleɪʃən , kə-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or process of collating2. a description of the technical features of a book3. Roman Catholic Church a light meal permitted on fast days4. any light informal meal5. the appointment of a clergyman to a benefice
Example sentences containing 'collation'

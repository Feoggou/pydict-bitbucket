woodwork

(ˈwʊdˌwɜːk )
Definitions
noun1. the art, craft, or skill of making things in wood; carpentry2. components made of wood, such as doors, staircases, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

woodwork for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(wʊdwɜːʳk )
Definitions
1. uncount nounYou can refer to the doors and other wooden parts of a house as the woodwork. ⇒ I love the living room, with its dark woodwork, oriental rugs, and chunky furniture. ⇒ He could see the glimmer of fresh paint on the woodwork.2. uncount nounWoodwork is the activity or skill of making things out of wood. carpentry ⇒ I have done woodwork for many years. ⇒ Joseph instructs a class in woodwork.3. →  come out of the woodwork

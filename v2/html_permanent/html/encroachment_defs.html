(ɪnkroʊtʃmənt ) for learners of English

Definitions
variable nounYou can describe the action or process of encroaching on something as encroachment. [formal, disapproval] [+ of] ⇒ It's a sign of the encroachment of commercialism in medicine. ⇒ The problem was to safeguard sites from encroachment by property development.

full-faced

Definitions
adjective1. having a round full face2. Also: full face facing towards the viewer, with the entire face visible3. another name for bold face
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fullface (ˈfullˈface) noun, adverb

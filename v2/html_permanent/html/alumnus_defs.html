alumnus

(əˈlʌmnəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ni (-naɪ)mainly US and Canadian a graduate of a school, college, etc
Word origin
C17: from Latin: nursling, pupil, foster son, from alere to nourish

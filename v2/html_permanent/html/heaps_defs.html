heaps

(hiːps)
Definitions
plural noun1. informal a great deal; an enormous amount ⇒ I've got heaps to tell you., ⇒ Of course, we spent heaps and heaps while we were in New York., ⇒ Was there more misery for Scotland? Heaps., ⇒ You have heaps of time., ⇒ a job that might suit someone with heaps of experience, ⇒ I've got heaps of work to do.2. See give it heaps3. See give them heapsadverb4. informal used as intensifier; very much; a great deal ⇒ He said he was feeling heaps better., ⇒ There's heaps more cake if you want it.

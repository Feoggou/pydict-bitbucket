cobbler2

(ˈkɒblə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a sweetened iced drink, usually made from fruit and wine or liqueur2. mainly US a hot dessert made of fruit covered with a rich cakelike crust
Word origin
C19: (for sense 1) perhaps shortened from cobbler's punch; (for both senses) compare cobble (vb)

cobbler

Powered by COBUILD
(kɒbləʳ )
Word forms: cobblers
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA cobbler is a person whose job is to make or mend shoes. [old-fashioned]2. uncount nounIf you describe something that someone has just said as cobblers, you mean that you think it is nonsense. [British, informal] ⇒ The idea that greens are good for you is a load of cobblers. ⇒ These guys talk an awful load of old cobblers.

asperity

(æˈspɛrɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. roughness or sharpness of temper2. roughness or harshness of a surface, sound, taste, etc3. a condition hard to endure; affliction4. physics the elastically compressed region of contact between two surfaces caused by the normal force
Word origin
C16: from Latin asperitās, from asper rough

cession

(ˈsɛʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of ceding, esp of ceding rights, property, or territory2. something that is ceded, esp land or territory
Word origin
C14: from Latin cessiō, from cēdere to yield

Similar Words

ceding, grant, yielding, conceding, surrender, concession, abandonment, capitulation, renunciation, conveyance, abnegation, relinquishment

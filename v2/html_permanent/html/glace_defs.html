glace

(ɡlæs)
Definitions
noun obsolete1. ice2. a wound3. a swift blow
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

glacé

(ˈɡlæsɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. crystallized or candied ⇒ glacé cherries2. covered in icing3. (of leather, silk, etc) having a glossy finish4. mainly US frozen or icedverb -cés, -céing or -céed5. (transitive) to ice or candy (cakes, fruits, etc)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from French glacé, literally: iced, from glacer to freeze, from glace ice, from Latin glaciēs

diaper

(ˈdaɪəpə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. US and Canadian a piece of soft material, esp towelling or a disposable material, wrapped around a baby in order to absorb its excrement. Also called (in Britain and certain other countries): nappy2. a. a woven pattern on fabric consisting of a small repeating design, esp diamondsb. fabric having such a patternc. such a pattern, used as decorationverb3. (transitive) to decorate with such a pattern
Word origin
C14: from Old French diaspre, from Medieval Latin diasprus made of diaper, from Medieval Greek diaspros pure white, from dia- + aspros white, shining

clarinet

(ˌklærɪˈnɛt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun music1. a keyed woodwind instrument with a cylindrical bore and a single reed. It is a transposing instrument, most commonly pitched in A or B flat. Obsolete name: clarionet (ˌklærɪəˈnɛt )2. an orchestral musician who plays the clarinet
Derived forms
ˌclarinettist (ˌclariˈnettist) or ˌclarinetist (ˌclariˈnetist) noun
Word origin
C18: from French clarinette, probably from Italian clarinetto, from clarino trumpet

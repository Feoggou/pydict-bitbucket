heft

(hɛft )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to assess the weight of (something) by lifting2. to liftnoun3. US weight4. US the main part
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hefter (ˈhefter) noun

Word Origin

C19: probably from heave, by analogy with thieve, theft, cleave, cleft

She was absolutely still, except for her right hand, which was idly judging the heft of the knife.

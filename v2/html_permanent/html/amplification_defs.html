amplification

(ˌæmplɪfɪˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or result of amplifying2. material added to a statement, story, etc, in order to expand or clarify it3. a statement, story, etc, with such additional material4. electronicsa. the increase in strength of an electrical signal by means of an amplifierb. another word for gain1 (sense 13)5. Also called: gene amplification genetics the production of multiple copies of a particular gene or DNA sequence. It can occur naturally or artificially, by genetic engineering techniques

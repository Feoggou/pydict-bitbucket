mascot

(ˈmæskət )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna person, animal, or thing considered to bring good luck

Word Origin

C19: from French mascotte, from Provençal mascotto charm, from masco witch

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA mascot is an animal, toy, or symbol which is associated with a particular organization or event, and which is thought to bring good luck. ⇒ Our school had a mascot known as Freddy Bird. [+ of] ⇒ ...the official mascot of the Barcelona Games.

(smɒɡ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna mixture of smoke, fog, and chemical fumes

Derived Forms

smoggy (ˈsmoggy) adjective

Word Origin

C20: from sm(oke + f)og1

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounSmog is a mixture of fog and smoke which occurs in some busy industrial cities. ⇒ Cars cause pollution, both smog and acid rain.

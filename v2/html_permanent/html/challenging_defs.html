Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA challenging task or job requires great effort and determination. OPPOSITE: undemanding ⇒ Mike found a challenging job as a computer programmer. ⇒ I'm ready to do all those things which are more challenging.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you do something in a challenging way, you seem to be inviting people to argue with you or compete against you in some way. ⇒ Mona gave him a challenging look.

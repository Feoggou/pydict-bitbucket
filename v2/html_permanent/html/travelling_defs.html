travelling

or US traveling (ˈtrævəlɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. a. the activity of moving or journeying from one place to another, esp to places far from home ⇒ Getting to and from school involves two hours' travelling a day., ⇒ I love travelling.b. (as modifier) ⇒ a travelling bag, ⇒ He was a charming travelling companion.adjective2. journeying around an area or country to perform, work, or be seen or used in different places ⇒ troupes of travelling actors, ⇒ travelling entertainers

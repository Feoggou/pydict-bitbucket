wicker

(ˈwɪkə )
Definitions
noun1. a slender flexible twig or shoot, esp of willow2. short for wickerworkadjective3. made, consisting of, or constructed from wicker
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Scandinavian; compare Swedish viker, Danish viger willow, Swedish vika to bend

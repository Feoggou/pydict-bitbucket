erratum

(ɪˈrɑːtəm )
Definitions
noun plural -ta (-tə)1. an error in writing or printing2. another name for corrigendum
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin: mistake, from errāre to err
Example sentences containing 'erratum'
In tiny letters at the foot of the page were the words ` erratum : page 49'. Fidelis Morgan THE RIVAL QUEENS: A COUNTESS ASHBY DE LA ZOUCHE MYSTERY

Similar Words

misprint, error, omission, literal, typo (informal), corrigendum

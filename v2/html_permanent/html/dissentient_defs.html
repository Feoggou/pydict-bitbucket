dissentient

(dɪˈsɛnʃənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. dissenting, esp from the opinion of the majoritynoun2. a dissenter
Derived forms
dissentience (disˈsentience) or dissentiency (disˈsentiency) noundissentiently (disˈsentiently) adverb
Example sentences containing 'dissentient'
There were no loyal, dissentient voices: none, at least, that could be relied upon to stay firm. Townsend, Eileen IN LOVE AND WAR

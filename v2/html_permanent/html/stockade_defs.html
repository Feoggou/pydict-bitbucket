stockade

(stɒˈkeɪd )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an enclosure or barrier of stakes and timbers2. US a military prison or detention areaverb3. (transitive) to surround with a stockade

Word Origin

C17: from Spanish estacada, from estaca a stake, post, of Germanic origin; see stake1

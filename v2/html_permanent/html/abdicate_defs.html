Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. verbIf a king or queen abdicates, he or she gives up being king or queen. [V] ⇒ The last French king was Louis Philippe, who abdicated in 1848. [Also V n]abdication (æbdɪkeɪʃən ) uncount noun [usu with poss] [+ of] ⇒ ...the most serious royal crisis since the abdication of Edward VIII.2. verbIf you say that someone has abdicated responsibility for something, you disapprove of them because they have refused to accept responsibility for it any longer. [formal, disapproval] [V n] ⇒ Many parents simply abdicate all responsibility for their children.abdication uncount noun [+ of] ⇒ There had been a complete abdication of responsibility.

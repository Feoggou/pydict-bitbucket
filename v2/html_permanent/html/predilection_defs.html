predilection

(ˌpriːdɪˈlɛkʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna predisposition, preference, or bias

Word Origin

C18: from French prédilection, from Medieval Latin praedīligere to prefer, from Latin prae before + dīligere to love

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf you have a predilection for something, you have a strong liking for it. [formal] [+ for] ⇒ ...his predilection for fast cars and fast horses.

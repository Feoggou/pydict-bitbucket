Thalia

(θəˈlaɪə )
Definitions
noun Greek mythology1. the Muse of comedy and pastoral poetry2. one of the three Graces
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: via Latin from Greek, from thaleia blooming

marmot

(ˈmɑːmət )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any burrowing sciurine rodent of the genus Marmota, of Europe, Asia, and North America. They are heavily built, having short legs, a short furry tail, and coarse fur2. See prairie marmot

Word Origin

C17: from French marmotte, perhaps ultimately from Latin mūr- (stem of mūs) mouse + montis of the mountain

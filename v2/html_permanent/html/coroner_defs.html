coroner

(ˈkɒrənə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna public official responsible for the investigation of violent, sudden, or suspicious deaths and inquiries into treasure trove. The investigation (coroner's inquest) is held in the presence of a jury (coroner's jury). See also procurator fiscal. Compare medical examiner
Derived forms
coronerˌship (ˈcoronerˌship) noun
Word origin
C14: from Anglo-French corouner officer in charge of the pleas of the Crown, from Old French corone crown

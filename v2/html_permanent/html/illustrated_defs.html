illustrated

(ˈɪləstreɪtɪd)
Definitions
adjective(of a book, text, etc) decorated with or making use of pictures ⇒ The book is beautifully illustrated throughout., ⇒ a beautifully illustrated prayer book, ⇒ lavishly illustrated with colour photographs▶ USAGE The abbreviation for illustrated is illust. or illus.

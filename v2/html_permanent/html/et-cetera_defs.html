Word Frequency

et cetera

or etcetera (ɪt ˈsɛtrə )
Definitions
unknown1. and the rest; and others; and so forth: used at the end of a list to indicate that other items of the same class or type should be considered or included2. or the like; or something else similar▶ USAGE It is unnecessary to use and before etc as etc (et cetera) already means and other things. The repetition of etc, as in he brought paper, ink, notebooks, etc, etc, is avoided except in informal contexts
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

from Latin, from et and + cetera the other (things)

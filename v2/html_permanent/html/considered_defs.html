considered

(kənˈsɪdəd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. presented or thought out with care ⇒ a considered opinion2. (qualified by a preceding adverb) esteemed ⇒ highly considered

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]A considered opinion or act is the result of careful thought. ⇒ Obviously it was Anne's considered opinion that Mavis was a bold-faced liar. ⇒ We would hope to be able to give a considered response to the unions' proposals by the end of the year.2. → see also consider

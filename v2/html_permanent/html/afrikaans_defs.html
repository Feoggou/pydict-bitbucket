Afrikaans

(ˌæfrɪˈkɑːns , -ˈkɑːnz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounone of the official languages of the Republic of South Africa, closely related to Dutch. Sometimes called: South African Dutch
Word origin
C20: from Dutch: African

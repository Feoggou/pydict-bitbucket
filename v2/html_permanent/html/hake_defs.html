hake1

(heɪk )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural hake or hakes1. any gadoid food fish of the genus Merluccius, such as M. merluccius (European hake), of the N hemisphere, having an elongated body with a large head and two dorsal fins2. any North American fish of the genus Urophycis, similar and related to Merluccius species3. Australian another name for barracouta
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: perhaps from Old Norse haki hook; compare Old English hacod pike; see hook

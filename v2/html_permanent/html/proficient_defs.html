proficient

(prəˈfɪʃənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having great facility (in an art, occupation, etc); skillednoun2. an archaic word for an expert

Derived Forms

proficiency (proˈficiency) or proficience (proˈficience) nounproficiently (proˈficiently) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Latin prōficere to make progress, from pro-1 + facere to make

elegiac

(ˌɛlɪˈdʒaɪək )
Definitions
adjective1. resembling, characteristic of, relating to, or appropriate to an elegy2. lamenting; mournful; plaintive3. denoting or written in elegiac couplets or elegiac stanzas. Also (archaic): elegiacalnoun4. (often plural) an elegiac couplet or stanza
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

elegiacally (ˌeleˈgiacally) adverb

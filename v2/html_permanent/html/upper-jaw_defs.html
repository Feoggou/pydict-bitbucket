upper jaw

(ˈʌpə dʒɔː)
Definitions
nounthe maxilla or upper part of the skull of a vertebrate that frames the mouth and holds the teeth. In higher vertebrates the upper jaw is fused to the cranium and the lower jaw (mandible) ⇒ She has only two teeth in her upper jaw.

humanly

(ˈhjuːmənlɪ )
Definitions
adverb1. by human powers or means2. in a human or humane manner
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

humanly

Powered by COBUILD
(hjuːmənli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adverb [ADV adj/-ed, ADV after v]Humanly means relating to human beings. ⇒ A mother is not allowed to be humanly flawed; she has to be perfect.2. →  humanly possible
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

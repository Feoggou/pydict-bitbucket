creeping

(ˈkriːpɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of a plant) having a stem that grows horizontally along the ground and throws out roots at intervals2. pejorative (of a process or change) happening slowly and stealthily, without being noticed
Example sentences containing 'creeping'
In the darkness, she could hear the curtains creeping on the draught. Mark Burnell CHAMELEONBeneath the stern of the ship a waterman was watching the tide creeping up the concrete slope.

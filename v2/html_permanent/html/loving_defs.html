Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is loving feels or shows love to other people. ⇒ Jim was a most loving husband and father. ⇒ The children there were very loving to me.lovingly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ Brian gazed lovingly at Mary Ann.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Loving actions are done with great enjoyment and care. ⇒ The house has been restored with loving care.lovingly graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v, oft ADV -ed] ⇒ I lifted the box and ran my fingers lovingly over the top.3. → See also peace-loving

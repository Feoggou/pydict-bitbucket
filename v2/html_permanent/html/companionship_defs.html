(kəmpænjənʃɪp )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounCompanionship is having someone you know and like with you, rather than being on your own. ⇒ I depended on his companionship and on his judgment. ⇒ The majority of people own a dog for companionship.

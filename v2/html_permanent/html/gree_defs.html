gree1

(ɡriː )
Definitions
noun Scottish archaic1. superiority or victory2. the prize for a victory
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French gré, from Latin gradus step

(ɡriː )
Definitions

rappel

(ræˈpɛl )
Definitions
verb -pels, -pelling or -pelled1. another word (esp US) for abseilnoun2. another word (esp US) for abseil3. (formerly) a drumbeat to call soldiers to arms
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from French, from rappeler to call back, from Latin appellāre to summon

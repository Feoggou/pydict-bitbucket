(ɡjuːlz )
Definitions
adjective, noun (usually postpositive)heraldry red
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French gueules red fur worn around the neck, from gole throat, from Latin gula gullet

off key

Definitions
adjective, adverb (off-key when prenominal)1. musica. not in the correct keyb. out of tune2. out of keeping; discordant

off-key - music

(ˌɒfˈkiː)
Definitions
adjective1. out of tune ⇒ wailing, off-key vocals and strangled guitars, ⇒ No, no, no. This won't do. Someone is off-key.adverb2. in an out of tune manner ⇒ Moe was having fun banging the drums and singing off-key.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

gammon1

(ˈɡæmən )
Definitions
noun1. a cured or smoked ham2. the hindquarter of a side of bacon, cooked either whole or cut into large rashers
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old Northern French gambon, from gambe leg; see gambrel

gammon2

(ˈɡæmən )
Definitions
noun1. a double victory in backgammon in which one player throws off all his or her pieces before his or her opponent throws any2. archaic the game of backgammonverb3. (transitive) to score a gammon over
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: probably special use of Middle English gamen game1

gammon3 - British informal

(ˈɡæmən )
Definitions
noun1. deceitful nonsense; humbugverb2. to deceive (a person)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gammoner (ˈgammoner) noun

Word Origin

C18: perhaps special use of gammon2

gammon4

(ˈɡæmən )
Definitions
verb(transitive) nautical to fix (a bowsprit) to the stemhead of a vessel
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: perhaps related to gammon1, with reference to the tying up of a ham

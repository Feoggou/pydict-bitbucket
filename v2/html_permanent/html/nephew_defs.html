nephew

(ˈnɛvjuː , ˈnɛf-)
Definitions
nouna son of one's sister or brother
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old French neveu, from Latin nepōs; related to Old English nefa, Old High German nevo relative

nephew for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(nefjuː , nev-)
Word forms: nephews
Definitions
countable noun [oft poss N]Someone's nephew is the son of their sister or brother. ⇒ I am planning a 25th birthday party for my nephew.

equilateral

(ˌiːkwɪˈlætərəl )
Definitions
adjective1. having all sides of equal length ⇒ an equilateral trianglenoun2. a geometric figure having all its sides of equal length3. a side that is equal in length to other sides
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

equilaterally (ˌequiˈlaterally) adverb

sloth

(sləʊθ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of several shaggy-coated arboreal edentate mammals of the family Bradypodidae, esp Bradypus tridactylus (three-toed sloth or ai) or Choloepus didactylus (two-toed sloth or unau), of Central and South America. They are slow-moving, hanging upside down by their long arms and feeding on vegetation2. reluctance to work or exert oneself

Word Origin

Old English slǣwth; from slǣw, variant of slāw slow

(tʃælɪndʒəʳ )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA challenger is someone who competes with you for a position or title that you already have, for example being a sports champion or a political leader. [+ to] ⇒ Draskovic has emerged as the strongest challenger to the leader of the Serbian government. ⇒ The One Australia syndicate is to become the sixth challenger for the 1995 Americas Cup. [Also + for]

spina bifida

(ˈspaɪnə ˈbɪfɪdə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna congenital condition in which the meninges of the spinal cord protrude through a gap in the backbone, sometimes causing enlargement of the skull (due to accumulation of cerebrospinal fluid) and paralysis

Word Origin

New Latin; see spine, bifid

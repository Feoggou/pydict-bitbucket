Parian

(ˈpɛərɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. denoting or relating to a fine white marble mined in classical times in Paros2. denoting or relating to a fine biscuit porcelain used mainly for statuary3. of or relating to Parosnoun4. a native or inhabitant of Paros5. Parian marble6. Parian porcelain

wrapper

(ˈræpə )
Definitions
noun1. the cover, usually of paper or cellophane, in which something is wrapped2. a dust jacket of a book3. the ripe firm tobacco leaf forming the outermost portion of a cigar and wound around its body4. a loose negligee or dressing gown, esp in the 19th century

Definitions
countable nounA wrapper is a piece of paper, plastic, or thin metal which covers and protects something that you buy, especially food. ⇒ I emptied the sweet wrappers from the ashtray. ⇒ ...an unsmoked cigar in its cellophane wrapper.

Definitions
phraseIf you strike or find a happy medium between two extreme and opposite courses of action, you find a sensible way of behaving that is somewhere between the two extremes. ⇒ I still aim to strike a happy medium between producing football that's worth watching and getting results. ⇒ It's very difficult to strike a happy medium and make it right for everybody. →  medium

arming

(ˈɑːmɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of taking arms or providing with arms2. nautical a greasy substance, such as tallow, packed into the recess at the bottom of a sounding lead to pick up samples of sand, gravel, etc, from the bottom
Example sentences containing 'arming'
Behind Ferris there was the metal scrape of the escorts arming their rifles. Seymour, Gerald FIELD OF BLOOD

chaperon

or chaperone (ˈʃæpəˌrəʊn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (esp formerly) an older or married woman who accompanies or supervises a young unmarried woman on social occasions2. someone who accompanies and supervises a group, esp of young people, usually when in public placesverb3. to act as a chaperon to
Derived forms
chaperonage (ˈʃæpərənɪdʒ ) noun
Word origin
C14: from Old French, from chape hood, protective covering; see cap

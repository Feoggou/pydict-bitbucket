Troy

(trɔɪ )
Definitions
nounany of nine ancient cities in NW Asia Minor, each of which was built on the ruins of its predecessor. The seventh was the site of the Trojan War (mid-13th century bc). Also called: Ilion, Ilium ▶ Related adjective: Trojan

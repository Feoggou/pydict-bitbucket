strays

(streɪz )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. Also called: stray capacitance electronics undesired capacitance in equipment, occurring between the wiring, between the wiring and the chassis, or between components and the chassis2. telecommunications another word for static (sense 9)

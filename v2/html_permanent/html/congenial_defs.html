congenial

(kənˈdʒiːnjəl , -nɪəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. friendly, pleasant, or agreeable ⇒ a congenial atmosphere to work in2. having a similar disposition, tastes, etc; compatible; sympathetic
Derived forms
congeniality (kənˌdʒiːnɪˈælɪtɪ ) or congenialness (conˈgenialness) nouncongenially (conˈgenially) adverb
Word origin
C17: from con- (same) + genial1

peerage

(ˈpɪərɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the whole body of peers; aristocracy2. the position, rank, or title of a peer3. (esp in the British Isles) a book listing the peers and giving genealogical and other information about them

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounIf someone has a peerage, they have the rank of a peer. ⇒ The Prime Minister offered him a peerage. ⇒ It's thought she may eventually accept a peerage and move to the House of Lords.2. singular nounThe peers of a particular country are referred to as the peerage.

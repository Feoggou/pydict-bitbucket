betting

(ˈbɛtɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the laying of wagers ⇒ There was some illegal betting going on., ⇒ his thousand-pound fine for illegal betting2. likelihood; probability (esp in the phrases the betting is and what's the betting that) ⇒ The betting is that the experience will make Japan more competitive still., ⇒ And what's the betting he's in Manchester to collect yet another handout?

iced

(aɪst )
Definitions
adjective1. covered, coated, or chilled with ice2. covered with icing ⇒ iced cakes

iced

Powered by COBUILD
(aɪst ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]An iced drink has been made very cold, often by putting ice in it. ⇒ ...iced tea.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]An iced cake is covered with a layer of icing. frosted ⇒ We were all given little iced cakes.

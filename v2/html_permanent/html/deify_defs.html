deify

(ˈdiːɪˌfaɪ , ˈdeɪɪ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -fies, -fying or -fied (transitive)1. to exalt to the position of a god or personify as a god2. to accord divine honour or worship to3. to exalt in an extreme way; idealize
Derived forms
deiˌfier (ˈdeiˌfier) noun
Word origin
C14: from Old French deifier, from Late Latin deificāre, from Latin deus god + facere to make

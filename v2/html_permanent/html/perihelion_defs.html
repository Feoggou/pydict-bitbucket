perihelion

(ˌpɛrɪˈhiːlɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -lia (-lɪə) astronomythe point in its orbit when a planet or comet is nearest the sun. Compare aphelion

Word Origin

C17: from New Latin perihēlium, from peri- + Greek hēlios sun

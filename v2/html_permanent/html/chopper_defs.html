chopper

(ˈtʃɒpə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. mainly British a small hand axe2. a butcher's cleaver3. a person or thing that cuts or chops4. an informal name for a helicopter5. mainly British a slang name for penis6. a device for periodically interrupting an electric current or beam of radiation to produce a pulsed current or beam. See also vibrator (sense 2)7. a type of bicycle or motorcycle with very high handlebars and an elongated saddle8. New Zealand a child's bicycle9. obsolete, slang, mainly US a sub-machine-gun

frontlet

(ˈfrʌntlɪt )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: frontal a small decorative loop worn on a woman's forehead, projecting from under her headdress, in the 15th century2. the forehead of an animal, esp of a bird when it is a different colour from the rest of the head3. the decorated border of an altar frontal4. Judaism a phylactery worn on the forehead. See also tefillah
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French frontelet a little frontal

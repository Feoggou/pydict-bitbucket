ramblings

(ˈræmblɪŋz)
Definitions
plural nounspeech or writing that is meaningless because the person who said or wrote it was very confused or mentally ill ⇒ The official dismissed the speech as the ramblings of a desperate lunatic.

ramblings

Powered by COBUILD
(ræmblɪŋz ) for learners of English
Definitions
plural noun [usu with poss]If you describe a speech or piece of writing as someone's ramblings, you are saying that it is meaningless because the person who said or wrote it was very confused or insane. [disapproval] ⇒ The official dismissed the speech as the ramblings of a desperate lunatic.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

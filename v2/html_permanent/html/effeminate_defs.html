effeminate

(ɪˈfɛmɪnɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. (of a man or boy) displaying characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; not manly2. lacking firmness or vigour ⇒ an effeminate piece of writing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

effeminacy (efˈfeminacy) or effeminateness (efˈfeminateness) nouneffeminately (efˈfeminately) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Latin effēmināre to make into a woman, from fēmina woman

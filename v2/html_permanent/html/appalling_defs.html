Definitions

adjective1. causing extreme dismay, horror, or revulsion2. very bad

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is appalling is so bad or unpleasant that it shocks you. ⇒ They have been living under the most appalling conditions for two months.appallingly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ He says that he understands why they behaved so appallingly. ⇒ The detection rate for racial crimes is appallingly low.2. graded adjective & adjectiveYou can use appalling to emphasize that something is very great or severe. [emphasis] ⇒ I developed an appalling headache.appallingly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ It's been an appallingly busy morning.3. → see also appal

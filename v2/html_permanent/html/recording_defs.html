recording

(rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. a. the act or process of making a record, esp of sound on a gramophone record, magnetic tape, compact disc, computer, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ recording studio, ⇒ recording head2. the record, tape, disc, etc so produced3. something that has been recorded, esp a radio or television programme

Definitions

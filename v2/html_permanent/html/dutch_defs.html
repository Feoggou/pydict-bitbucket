Dutch

(dʌtʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the language of the Netherlands, belonging to the West Germanic branch of the Indo-European family and quite closely related to German and English. See also Flemish, Afrikaans2. See the Dutch3. See Pennsylvania Dutch4. See double Dutch5. See in Dutchadjective6. of, relating to, or characteristic of the Netherlands, its inhabitants, or their languageadverb7. See go Dutch

Dutch

Powered by COBUILD
(dʌtʃ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveDutch means relating to or belonging to the Netherlands, or to its people, language, or culture. ⇒ ...the Dutch prime minister.2. plural nounThe Dutch are the people of the Netherlands. ⇒ The Dutch developed a custom by which children put out shoes which Saint Nicholas would fill with gifts when he came visiting.3. uncount nounDutch is the language spoken in the Netherlands.4. →  go Dutch

anterior

(ænˈtɪərɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. situated at or towards the front2. earlier in time3. zoology of or near the head end4. botany (of part of a flower or leaf) situated farthest away from the main stem▷ Compare posterior
Derived forms
anteriority (ænˌtɪərɪˈɒrɪtɪ ) noun
Word origin
C17: from Latin, comparative of ante before

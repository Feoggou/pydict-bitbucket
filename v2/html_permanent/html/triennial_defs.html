triennial

(traɪˈɛnɪəl )
Definitions
adjective1. relating to, lasting for, or occurring every three yearsnoun2. a third anniversary3. a triennial period, thing, or occurrence
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

triennially (triˈennially) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin triennium

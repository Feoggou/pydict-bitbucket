partisanship

Powered by COBUILD
(pɑːʳtɪzænʃɪp , US -zən- )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounPartisanship is support for a person or group without fair consideration of the facts and circumstances. ⇒ His politics were based on loyal partisanship.

Amy smiled sadly at Bethan's partisanship , as if her happiness were the only consideration.

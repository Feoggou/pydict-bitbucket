Achaean

(əˈkiːən ) or Achaian (əˈkaɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a member of a principal Greek tribe in the Mycenaean era2. a native or inhabitant of the later Greek province of Achaeaadjective3. of or relating to Achaea or the Achaeans

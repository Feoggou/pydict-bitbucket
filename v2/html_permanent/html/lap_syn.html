Synonyms of lap 1

lap1

noun

  1. circuit, course, round, tour, leg, distance, stretch, circle, orbit, loop
    • the last lap of the race

verb

  1. overtake, pass, leave behind, go past, get ahead of, outdistance
    • He was caught out while lapping a slower rider.
  2. wrap, turn, cover, twist, fold, swathe, envelop, swaddle, enfold

lap2

verb

  1. ripple, wash, splash, slap, swish, gurgle, slosh, purl, plash
    • the water that lapped against the pillars of the pier
  2. drink, sip, lick, swallow, gulp, sup
    • The kitten lapped milk from a dish.
  • See lap something up

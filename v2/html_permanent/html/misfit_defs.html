misfit

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (ˈmɪsˌfɪt )1. a person not suited in behaviour or attitude to a particular social environment2. something that does not fit or fits badlyverb (ˌmɪsˈfɪt ) -fits, -fitting or -fitted (intransitive)3. to fail to fit or be fitted

(mɪsfɪt )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA misfit is a person who is not easily accepted by other people, often because their behaviour is very different from that of everyone else. ⇒ I have been made to feel a social and psychological misfit for not wanting children.

doer

(ˈduːə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that does something or acts in a specified manner ⇒ a doer of good2. an active or energetic person3. a thriving animal, esp a horse

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf you refer to someone as a doer, you mean that they do jobs promptly and efficiently, without spending a lot of time thinking about them. ⇒ Robertson was a doer, not a thinker.

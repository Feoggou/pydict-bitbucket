Homeric

(həʊˈmɛrɪk ) or Homerian (həʊˈmɪərɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or resembling Homer or his poems2. imposing or heroic3. of or relating to the archaic form of Greek used by Homer. See epic
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Homerically (Hoˈmerically) adverb

resurrect

(ˌrɛzəˈrɛkt )
Definitions
verb1. to rise or raise from the dead; bring or be brought back to life2. (transitive) to bring back into use or activity; revive ⇒ to resurrect an ancient law3. (transitive) to renew (one's hopes, etc)4. (transitive) facetious (formerly) to exhume and steal (a body) from its grave, esp in order to sell it
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

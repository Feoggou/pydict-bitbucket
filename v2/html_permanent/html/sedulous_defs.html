sedulous

(ˈsɛdjʊləs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveconstant or persistent in use or attention; assiduous; diligent

Derived Forms

sedulity (sɪˈdjuːlɪtɪ ) or sedulousness (ˈsedulousness) nounsedulously (ˈsedulously) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Latin sēdulus, of uncertain origin

Similar Words

diligent, busy, constant, persistent, conscientious, persevering, tireless, painstaking, laborious, industrious, unremitting, assiduous, unflagging

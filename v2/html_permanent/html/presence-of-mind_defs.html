Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrase [usu PHR after v]If you say that someone had the presence of mind to do something, you approve of them because they were able to think and act calmly in a difficult situation. [approval] ⇒ He had the presence of mind to put his emergency oxygen tube in his mouth. →  presence

Synonyms of graft 1

graft1

noun

  1. transplant, implant
    • I am having a skin graft on my arm soon.
  2. shoot, bud, implant, sprout, splice, scion
    • These plants are propagated by grafts, buds or cuttings.

verb

  1. transplant, implant
    • The top layer of skin has to be grafted onto the burns.
  2. join, insert, transplant, implant, splice, affix, ingraft
    • Pear trees are grafted on quince root-stocks.

graft2 (informal)

noun

  1. labour, work, industry, effort, struggle, sweat, toil, slog, exertion, travail, blood, sweat, and tears (informal)
    • His career has been one of hard graft.

verb

  1. work, labour, struggle, sweat (informal), grind (informal), slave, strive, toil, drudge, travail
    • I really don't enjoy grafting away in a stuffy office all day.

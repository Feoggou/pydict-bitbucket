forbidding

(fəˈbɪdɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. hostile or unfriendly2. dangerous or ominous

(fəʳbɪdɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a person, place, or thing as forbidding, you mean they have a severe, unfriendly, or threatening appearance. ⇒ There was something a little severe and forbidding about her face. ⇒ ...a huge, forbidding building.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

incognito

(ˌɪnkɒɡˈniːtəʊ , ɪnˈkɒɡnɪtəʊ )
Definitions
adverb, adjective (postpositive)1. under an assumed name or appearance; in disguisenoun plural -tos2. a person who is incognito3. the assumed name or disguise of such a person
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Italian, from Latin incognitus unknown, from in-1 + cognitus known

Definitions
adjective [v-link ADJ, ADJ after v]Someone who is incognito is using a false name or wearing a disguise, in order not to be recognized or identified. ⇒ Hotel inspectors have to travel incognito. ⇒ He preferred to remain incognito.

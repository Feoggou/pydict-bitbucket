ness

(nɛs )
Definitions
nouna. archaic a promontory or headlandb. (capital as part of a name) ⇒ Orford Ness
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English næs headland; related to Old Norse nes, Old English nasu nose

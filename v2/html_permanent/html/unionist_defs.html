unionist

(ˈjuːnjənɪst )
Definitions
noun1. a supporter or advocate of unionism or union2. a member of a trade unionadjective3. mainly British of or relating to union or unionism, esp trade unionism
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unionistic (ˌunionˈistic) adjective

Unionist

(ˈjuːnjənɪst )
Definitions
noun1. (sometimes not capital)a. (before 1920) a supporter of the union of all Ireland and Great Britainb. (since 1920) a supporter of union between Britain and Northern Ireland2. a supporter of the US federal Union, esp during the Civil Waradjective3. of, resembling, or relating to Unionists
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

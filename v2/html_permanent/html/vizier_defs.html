vizier

(vɪˈzɪə )
Definitions
nouna high official in certain Muslim countries, esp in the former Ottoman Empire. Viziers served in various capacities, such as that of provincial governor or chief minister to the sultan
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vizierial (viˈzierial) or vizirial (viˈzirial) adjectiveviziership (viˈziership) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Turkish vezīr, from Arabic wazīr porter, from wazara to bear a burden

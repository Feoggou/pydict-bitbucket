dene1

or dean (diːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounBritish a valley, esp one that is narrow and wooded
Word origin
Old English denu valley; see den

dene2

or dean (diːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noundialect, mainly Southern England a sandy stretch of land or dune near the sea
Word origin
C13: probably related to Old English dūn hill; see down3

Dene

(ˈdɛnɪ , ˈdɛneɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounthe North American Indian peoples of Nunavut and the Northwest Territories in Canada. The official body representing them is called the Dene Nation
Word origin
via French déné, from Athapascan dene people

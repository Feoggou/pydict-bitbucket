cash1

(kæʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. banknotes and coins, esp in hand or readily available; money or ready money2. immediate payment, in full or part, for goods or services (esp in the phrase cash down)3. (modifier) of, for, or paid by cash ⇒ a cash transaction4. See the cashverb5. (transitive) to obtain or pay ready money for ⇒ to cash a cheque
Derived forms
cashable (ˈcashable) adjective
Word origin
C16: from Old Italian cassa money box, from Latin capsa case2

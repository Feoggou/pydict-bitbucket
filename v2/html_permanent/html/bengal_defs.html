Bengal

(bɛnˈɡɔːl , bɛŋ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a former province of NE India, in the great deltas of the Ganges and Brahmaputra Rivers: in 1947 divided into West Bengal (belonging to India) and East Bengal (Bangladesh)2. See Bay of Bengal3. a breed of medium-large cat with a spotted or marbled coat

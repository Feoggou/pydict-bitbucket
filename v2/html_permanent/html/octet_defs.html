octet

(ɒkˈtɛt )
Definitions
noun1. any group of eight, esp eight singers or musicians2. a piece of music composed for such a group3. prosody another word for octave (sense 2)4. chemistry a group of eight electrons forming a stable shell in an atom
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from Latin octo eight, on the model of duet

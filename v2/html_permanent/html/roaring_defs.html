roaring

(ˈrɔːrɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. informal very brisk and profitable (esp in the phrase a roaring trade)2. See the roaring days3. Irish derogatory, informal (intensifier) ⇒ a roaring communistadverb4. noisily or boisterously (esp in the phrase roaring drunk)noun5. a loud prolonged cry6. a debilitating breathing defect of horses characterized by rasping sounds with each breath: caused by inflammation of the respiratory tract or obstruction of the larynx. Compare whistling
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

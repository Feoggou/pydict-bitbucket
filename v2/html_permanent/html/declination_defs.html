declination

(ˌdɛklɪˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. astronomy the angular distance, esp in degrees, of a star, planet, etc, from the celestial equator measured north (positive) or south (negative) along the great circle passing through the celestial poles and the body. Symbol: δ. Compare: right ascension2. See magnetic declination3. a refusal, esp a courteous or formal one
Derived forms
ˌdeclinational (ˌdecliˈnational) adjective

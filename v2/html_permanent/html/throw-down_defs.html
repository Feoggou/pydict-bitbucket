Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrasal verbIf you throw down a challenge to someone, you do something new or unexpected in a bold or forceful manner that will probably cause them to reply or react equally strongly. [V P n] ⇒ The regional parliament threw down a new challenge to the central authorities by passing a law allowing private ownership of businesses. [V-ed P] ⇒ Government ministers have been responding to the challenge thrown down by their former colleague. →  throw

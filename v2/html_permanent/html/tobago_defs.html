Tobago

(təˈbeɪɡəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan island in the SE Caribbean, northeast of Trinidad: ceded to Britain in 1814; joined with Trinidad in 1888 as a British colony; part of the independent republic of Trinidad and Tobago. Pop: 54 084 (2000)

arrogant

(ˈærəɡənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivehaving or showing an exaggerated opinion of one's own importance, merit, ability, etc; conceited; overbearingly proud ⇒ an arrogant teacher, ⇒ an arrogant assumption
Derived forms
arrogance (ˈarrogance) nounarrogantly (ˈarrogantly) adverb
Word origin
C14: from Latin arrogāre to claim as one's own; see arrogate

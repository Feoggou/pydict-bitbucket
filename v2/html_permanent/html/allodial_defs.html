allodial

(əˈləʊdɪəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of land) held as an allodium2. (of tenure) characterized by or relating to the system of holding land in absolute ownership ⇒ the allodial system3. (of people) holding an allodium

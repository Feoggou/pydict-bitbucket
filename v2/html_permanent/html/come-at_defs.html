come at

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (intr, preposition)1. to discover or reach (facts, the truth, etc)2. to attack (a person) ⇒ he came at me with an axe3. Australian slang to agree to do (something)4. (usually used with a negative) Australian slang to stomach, tolerate ⇒ I couldn't come at it5. Australian slang to presume; impose ⇒ what are you coming at?

chloride

(ˈklɔːraɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any salt of hydrochloric acid, containing the chloride ion Cl–2. any compound containing a chlorine atom, such as methyl chloride (chloromethane), CH3Cl
Derived forms
chloridic (kləˈrɪdɪk ) adjective

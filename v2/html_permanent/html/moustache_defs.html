moustache

or US mustache (məˈstɑːʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the unshaved growth of hair on the upper lip, and sometimes down the sides of the mouth2. a similar growth of hair or bristles (in animals) or feathers (in birds)3. a mark like a moustache

Derived Forms

moustached (mousˈtached) or US mustached (musˈtached) adjective

Word Origin

C16: via French from Italian mostaccio, ultimately from Doric Greek mustax upper lip

countable nounA man's moustache is the hair that grows on his upper lip. If it is very long, it is sometimes referred to as his moustaches. ⇒ He was short and bald and had a moustache. ⇒ ...a heavy man with drooping moustaches.moustached adjective ⇒ ...three burly, moustached middle-aged men.

precision

(prɪˈsɪʒən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the quality of being precise; accuracy2. (modifier) characterized by or having a high degree of exactness ⇒ precision grinding, ⇒ a precision instrument

Derived Forms

precisionism (preˈcisionism) nounprecisionist (preˈcisionist) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin praecīsiō a cutting off; see precise

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft with N]If you do something with precision, you do it exactly as it should be done. ⇒ The choir sang with precision. ⇒ The interior is planned with a precision the military would be proud of.

out-of-the-way

Definitions
adjective (prenominal)1. distant from more populous areas2. uncommon or unusual
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

out of the way

Definitions
a. removed or dealt with so as to be no longer a hindranceb. remotec. unusual and sometimes improper▷ See way
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

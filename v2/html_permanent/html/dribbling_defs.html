dribbling

(ˈdrɪblɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounsporta. the act of propelling the ball with repeated kicks, taps, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ dribbling practice, ⇒ Her dribbling skills look second to none.

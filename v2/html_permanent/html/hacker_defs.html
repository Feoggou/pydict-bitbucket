hacker

(ˈhækə )
Definitions
noun1. a person that hacks2. slang a computer fanatic, esp one who through a personal computer breaks into the computer system of a company, government, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

hacker

Powered by COBUILD
(hækəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: hackers
Definitions
1. countable nounA computer hacker is someone who tries to break into computer systems, especially in order to get secret information.2. countable nounA computer hacker is someone who uses a computer a lot, especially so much that they have no time to do anything else.

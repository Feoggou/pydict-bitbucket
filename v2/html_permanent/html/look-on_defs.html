look on

Definitions
verb (intransitive)1. (adverb) to be a spectator at an event or incident2. Also: look upon (preposition) to consider or regard ⇒ she looked on the whole affair as a joke, ⇒ he looks on his mother-in-law with disapproval

Definitions
phrasal verbIf you look on or look upon someone or something in a particular way, you think of them in that way. [V P n + as] ⇒ A lot of people looked on him as a healer.

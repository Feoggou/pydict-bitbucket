Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that someone is domineering, you disapprove of them because you feel that they try to control other people without any consideration for their feelings or opinions. [disapproval] ⇒ Mick was stubborn and domineering with a very bad temper. ⇒ She is not a domineering mother.

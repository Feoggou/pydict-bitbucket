placard

(ˈplækɑːd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a printed or written notice for public display; poster2. a small plaque or cardverb (transitive)3. to post placards on or in4. to publicize or advertise by placards5. to display as a placard

Word Origin

C15: from Old French plaquart, from plaquier to plate, lay flat; see plaque

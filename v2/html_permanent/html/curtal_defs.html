curtal

(ˈkɜːtəl ) obsolete
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. cut short2. (of friars) wearing a short frocknoun3. an animal whose tail has been docked4. something that is cut short
Word origin
C16: from Old French courtault animal whose tail has been docked, from court short, from Latin curtus; see curt

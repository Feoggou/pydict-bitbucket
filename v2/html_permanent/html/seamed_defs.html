seamed

(siːmd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. literary wrinkled; indented ⇒ his leathery seamed face, ⇒ There were scruffy coal barges with wheelhouses seamed with rust and blistered with old paint., ⇒ a face seamed with wrinkles, ⇒ grey rock seamed with white, ⇒ The cave was seamed with crevices.2. having a seam

expanse

(ɪkˈspæns )
Definitions
noun1. an uninterrupted surface of something that spreads or extends, esp over a wide area; stretch ⇒ an expanse of water2. expansion or extension
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from New Latin expansum the heavens, from Latin expansus spread out, from expandere to expand

anima

(ˈænɪmə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (in Jungian psychology)a. the feminine principle as present in the male unconsciousb. the inner personality, which is in communication with the unconscious. See also animus
Word origin
Latin: air, breath, spirit, feminine of animus

In death, with his anima passed from his lips, Haidar seemed smaller and diminished.

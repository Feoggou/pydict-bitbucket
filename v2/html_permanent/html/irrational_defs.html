irrational

(ɪˈræʃənəl )
Definitions
adjective1. inconsistent with reason or logic; illogical; absurd2. incapable of reasoning3. mathematicsa. not rationalb. (as noun) ⇒ an irrational4. prosody (in Greek or Latin verse)a. of or relating to a metrical irregularity, usually the occurrence of a long syllable instead of a short oneb. denoting a metrical foot where such an irregularity occurs
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

purvey

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (pəˈveɪ ) (transitive)1. to sell or provide (commodities, esp foodstuffs) on a large scale2. to publish or make available (lies, scandal, etc)noun (ˈpɜːvɪ )3. Scottish the food and drink laid on at a wedding reception, etc

Word Origin

C13: from Old French porveeir, from Latin prōvidēre to provide

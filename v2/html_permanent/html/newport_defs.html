Newport

(ˈnjuːˌpɔːt )
Definitions
noun1. a city and port in SE Wales, in Newport county borough on the River Usk: electronics. Pop: 116 143 (2001)2. a county borough in SE Wales, created from part of Gwent in 1996. Pop: 139 300 (2003 est). Area: 190 sq km (73 sq miles)3. a port in SE Rhode Island: founded in 1639, it became one of the richest towns of colonial America; centre of a large number of US naval establishments. Pop: 26 136 (2003 est)4. a town in S England, administrative centre of the Isle of Wight. Pop: 22 957 (2001)

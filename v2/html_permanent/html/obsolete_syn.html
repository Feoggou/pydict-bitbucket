obsolete

adjective

  1. outdated, old, passé, ancient, antique, old-fashioned, dated, discarded, extinct, past it, out of date, archaic, disused, out of fashion, out, antiquated, anachronistic, outmoded, musty, old hat, behind the times, superannuated, antediluvian, outworn, démodé (French), out of the ark (informal), vieux jeu (French)
    • The company says the plant is obsolete and does not merit further investment.
    OPPOSITES:  new, the new, current, modern, contemporary, fashionable, trendy (British, informal), up-to-date, present day, in vogue, in, du jour (French), à la mode, culty

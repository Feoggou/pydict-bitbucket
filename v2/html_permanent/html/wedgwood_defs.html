Wedgwood1

(ˈwɛdʒwʊd )
Definitions
noun1. trademarka. pottery produced, esp during the late 18th and early 19th centuries, at the Wedgwood factoriesb. such pottery having applied classical decoration in white on a blue or other coloured groundadjective2. relating to or characteristic of such pottery ⇒ Wedgwood blue

Wedgwood2

(ˈwɛdʒwʊd )
Definitions
noun Josiah. 1730–95, British potter and industrialist, who founded several pottery works near Stoke-on-Trent in Staffordshire

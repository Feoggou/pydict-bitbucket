Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Searing is used to indicate that something such as pain or heat is very intense. ⇒ She woke to feel a searing pain in her feet. ⇒ ...the searing heat of the Saudi Arabian desert.2. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]A searing speech or piece of writing is very critical. ⇒ ...searing criticism.

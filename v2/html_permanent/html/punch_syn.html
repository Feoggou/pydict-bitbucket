Synonyms of punch 1

punch1

verb

  1. hit, strike, box, smash, belt (informal), slam, plug (slang), bash (informal), sock (slang), clout (informal), slug, swipe (informal), biff (slang), bop (informal), wallop (informal), pummel
    • After punching him on the chin, she hit him over the head.

noun

  1. blow, hit, knock, bash (informal), plug (slang), sock (slang), thump, clout (informal), jab, swipe (informal), biff (slang), bop (informal), wallop (informal)
    • He's asking for a punch on the nose.
  2. (informal) effectiveness, force, bite, impact, point, drive, vigour, verve, forcefulness
    • The film lacks punch and pace.

punch2

verb

  1. pierce, cut, bore, drill, pink, stamp, puncture, prick, perforate
    • I took a pen and punched holes in the carton.

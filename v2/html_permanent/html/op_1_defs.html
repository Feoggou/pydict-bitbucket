op for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ɒp )
Word forms: ops
Definitions
1. countable nounAn op is a medical operation. [mainly British, informal] ⇒ ...breast cancer ops.2. countable noun [usu pl]Ops are military operations. ⇒ Flt Lt Beamont had completed a 200 hour tour of ops in December 1941. ⇒ Fusilier Jones ran towards the Ops Room to find his lieutenant.3. countable nounAn op is an opportunity. For example, a photo op is an opportunity for someone to take photographs, or to have photographs taken of them. ⇒ Many network TV reporters began using photo-ops as a jumping-off point to examine issues more in depth.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

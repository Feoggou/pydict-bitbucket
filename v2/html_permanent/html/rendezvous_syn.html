Synonyms of rendezvous

rendezvous

noun

  1. appointment, meeting, date, engagement, tryst (archaic), assignation
    • I had decided to keep my rendezvous with him.
  2. meeting place, venue, gathering point, place of assignation, trysting-place (archaic)
    • Their rendezvous would be the hotel at the airport.

verb

  1. meet, assemble, get together, come together, collect, gather, rally, muster, converge, join up, be reunited
    • The plan was to rendezvous on Sunday afternoon.

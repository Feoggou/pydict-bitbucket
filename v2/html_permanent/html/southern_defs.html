southern

(ˈsʌðən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. situated in or towards the south2. (of a wind, etc) coming from the south3. native to, inhabiting, or growing in the south4. (sometimes capital) astronomy south of the celestial equator

adjective [ADJ n]Southern means in or from the south of a region, state, or country. ⇒ The Everglades National Park stretches across the southern tip of Florida. ⇒ ...a place where you can sample southern cuisine.

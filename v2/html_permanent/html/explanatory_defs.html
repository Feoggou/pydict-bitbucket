Powered by COBUILD
(ɪksplænətəri , US -tɔːri ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Explanatory statements or theories are intended to make people understand something by describing it or giving the reasons for it. [formal] ⇒ These statements are accompanied by a series of explanatory notes. ⇒ The concept was later seen to have explanatory power.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

champagne

(ʃæmˈpeɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (sometimes capital) a white sparkling wine produced around Reims and Epernay, France2. (loosely) any effervescent white wine3. a. a colour varying from a pale orange-yellow to a greyish-yellowb. (as adjective) ⇒ a champagne carpet4. (modifier) denoting a luxurious lifestyle ⇒ a champagne capitalist
Word origin
from Champagne, a region of NE France

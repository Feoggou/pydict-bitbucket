Bonaparte

(ˈbəʊnəˌpɑːt , French bɔnapart)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. See Napoleon I2.  Jérôme (ʒerom), brother of Napoleon I. 1784–1860, king of Westphalia (1807–13)3.  Joseph (ʒozɛf), brother of Napoleon I. 1768–1844, king of Naples (1806–08) and of Spain (1808–13)4.  Louis (lwi), brother of Napoleon I. 1778–1846, king of Holland (1806–10)5.  Lucien (lysjɛ̃), brother of Napoleon I. 1775–1840, prince of Canino

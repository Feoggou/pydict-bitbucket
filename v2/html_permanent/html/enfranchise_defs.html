enfranchise

(ɪnˈfræntʃaɪz )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to grant the power of voting to, esp as a right of citizenship2. to liberate, as from servitude3. (in England) to invest (a town, city, etc) with the right to be represented in Parliament4. English law to convert (leasehold) to freehold

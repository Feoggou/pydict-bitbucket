siding

(ˈsaɪdɪŋ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a short stretch of railway track connected to a main line, used for storing rolling stock or to enable trains on the same line to pass2. a short railway line giving access to the main line for freight from a factory, mine, quarry, etc3. US and Canadian material attached to the outside of a building to make it weatherproof

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

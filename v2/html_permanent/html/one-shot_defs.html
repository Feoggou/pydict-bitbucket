one-shot

(ˈwʌnˌʃɒt)
Definitions
adjective1. US informal another word for one-off ⇒ I sincerely hope that it isn't a one-shot thing., ⇒ City Hall was looking for potentially hundreds of millions of dollars in a one-shot payment from them.2. golf consisting of a single shot ⇒ Colin Montgomerie has a one-shot lead after 36 holes., ⇒ He claimed a one-shot victory at the Classic yesterday., ⇒ He started the final round with a one-shot advantage.

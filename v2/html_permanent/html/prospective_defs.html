prospective

(prəˈspɛktɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. looking towards the future2. (prenominal) anticipated or likely

Derived Forms

prospectively (proˈspectively) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]You use prospective to describe someone who wants to be the thing mentioned or who is likely to be the thing mentioned. ⇒ The story should act as a warning to other prospective buyers. ⇒ When his prospective employers learned that he smoked, they said they wouldn't hire him.2. adjective [ADJ n]You use prospective to describe something that is likely to happen soon. ⇒ ...the terms of the prospective deal.

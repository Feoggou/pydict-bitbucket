Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]Matching is used to describe things which are of the same colour or design. ⇒ She bought more fabric in the same design so she could make matching curtains and cushions. ⇒ ...a coat and a matching handbag.
Example sentences containing 'matching'
Roy's pale skin turned grey, matching his unkempt hair, which had been mousy brown the last time she'd seen him. Mark Burnell CHAMELEONAlong the bottom was a shelf of shoes and matching handbags.

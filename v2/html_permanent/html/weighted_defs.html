weighted

(ˈweɪtɪd)
Definitions
adjectivebiased ⇒ a law weighted towards landlords, ⇒ The current electoral law is heavily weighted in favour of the ruling party., ⇒ They say the peace process is so heavily weighted against them that it will never achieve results.

deflection

or deflexion (dɪˈflɛkʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of deflecting or the state of being deflected2. the amount of deviation caused by a deflection3. the change in direction of a light beam as it crosses a boundary between two media with different refractive indexes4. a deviation of the indicator of a measuring instrument from its zero position5. the movement of a structure or structural member when subjected to a load
Derived forms
deflective (deˈflective) adjective

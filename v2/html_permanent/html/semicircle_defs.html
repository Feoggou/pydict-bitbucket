semicircle

(ˈsɛmɪˌsɜːkəl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. one half of a circleb. half the circumference of a circle2. anything having the shape or form of half a circle

Derived Forms

semicircular (ˌsɛmɪˈsɜːkjʊlə ) adjectiveˌsemicircularly (ˌsemiˈcircularly) adverb

facet

(ˈfæsɪt )
Definitions
noun1. any of the surfaces of a cut gemstone2. an aspect or phase, as of a subject or personality3. architecture the raised surface between the flutes of a column4. any of the lenses that make up the compound eye of an insect or other arthropod5. anatomy any small smooth area on a hard surface, as on a boneverb -ets, -eting, -eted, -ets, -etting or -etted6. (transitive) to cut facets in (a gemstone)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from French facette a little face

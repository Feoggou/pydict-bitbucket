acknowledged

(əkˈnɒlɪdʒd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. recognized as being true or existing ⇒ We are acknowledged leaders in information technology., ⇒ There is an acknowledged risk of lung cancer from radon., ⇒ an acknowledged decline in academic standards2. recognized, esp in legal form, as having authority, rights, or claims ⇒ Ptolemy had six acknowledged children.3. stated as being received

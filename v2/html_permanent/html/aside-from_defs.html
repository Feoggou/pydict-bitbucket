aside from

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
(preposition) mainly US and Canadiana. besides ⇒ he has money aside from his possessionsb. except for ⇒ he has nothing aside from the clothes he stands in. Compare apart (sense 7)▷ See aside

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseAside from means the same as apart from. This form is more usual in American English.

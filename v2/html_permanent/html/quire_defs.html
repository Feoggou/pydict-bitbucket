quire1

(kwaɪə )
Definitions
noun1. a set of 24 or 25 sheets of paper; a twentieth of a ream2. a. four sheets of paper folded once to form a section of 16 pagesb. a section or gathering3. a set of all the sheets in a book
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15 quayer, from Old French quaier, from Latin quaternī four at a time, from quater four times

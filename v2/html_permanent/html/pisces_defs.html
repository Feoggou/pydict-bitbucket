Pisces

(ˈpaɪsiːz , ˈpɪ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: Latin genitive Piscium (ˈpaɪsɪəm )1. astronomy a faint extensive zodiacal constellation lying between Aquarius and Aries on the ecliptic2. astrologya. Also called: the Fishes the twelfth sign of the zodiac, symbol ♓, having a mutable water classification and ruled by the planets Jupiter and Neptune. The sun is in this sign between about Feb 19 and March 20b. a person born when the sun is in this sign3. a. a taxonomic group that comprises all fishes. See fish (sense 1)b. a taxonomic group that comprises the bony fishes only. See teleostadjective4. astrology born under or characteristic of Pisces

Word Origin

C14: Latin: the fishes

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounPisces is one of the twelve signs of the zodiac. Its symbol is two fish. People who are born approximately between the 19th of February and the 20th of March come under this sign.2. singular nounA Pisces is a person whose sign of the zodiac is Pisces.

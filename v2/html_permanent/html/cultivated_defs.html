cultivated

(ˈkʌltɪˌveɪtɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. cultured, refined, or educated2. ((of land or soil))a. subjected to tillage or cultivationb. tilled and broken up3. (of plants) specially bred or improved by cultivation

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as cultivated, you mean they are well educated and have good manners. [formal] ⇒ His mother was an elegant, cultivated woman.2. adjective [ADJ n]Cultivated plants have been developed for growing on farms or in gardens. ⇒ ...a mixture of wild and cultivated varieties.

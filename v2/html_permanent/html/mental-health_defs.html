mental health

(ˈmɛntəl hɛlθ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the general condition of a person's mind ⇒ This sort of lifestyle can damage your mental health., ⇒ He feared for her mental health., ⇒ Don't overlook mental health., ⇒ Attending a church service can boost spirits and improve mental health., ⇒ All of a sudden their careers and mental health start to crumble., ⇒ poor mental health, ⇒ Poor mental health was related to longer working hours outside university and difficulty in paying bills.2. the branch of health care concerned with the treatment of mental illnesses and the improvement of mental wellbeing ⇒ She worked her way up, qualifying in midwifery, then completing a diploma in mental health.modifier3. of or relating to the condition of the mind or to the branch of health care concerned with the treatment of mental illnesses and the improvement of mental wellbeing ⇒ Treatment can reduce the prevalence of mental health problems., ⇒ director of mental health services at Bournewood, ⇒ There is a mental health unit in the hospital., ⇒ Community mental health centres were to be established., ⇒ the leading mental health charity, ⇒ a training ground for future mental health professionals, ⇒ Some mental health experts already predict problems., ⇒ new laws to protect the rights of mental health patients. See also mental health care

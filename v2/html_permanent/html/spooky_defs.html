spooky

(ˈspuːkɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: spookier or spookiest informal1. ghostly or eerie ⇒ a spooky house2. resembling or appropriate to a ghost3. US easily frightened; highly strung

Derived Forms

spookily (ˈspookily) adverbspookiness (ˈspookiness) noun

Definitions
adjective [v-link ADJ]If an activity is under way, it has already started. If an activity gets under way, it starts. ⇒ An investigation is underway to find out how the disaster happened. ⇒ It was a cold evening, winter well underway. ⇒ The conference gets under way later today with a debate on the family.

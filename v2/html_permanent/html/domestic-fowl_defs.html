domestic fowl

or barnyard fowl (ˈbɑːnˌjɑːd faʊl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna domesticated gallinaceous bird thought to be descended from the red jungle fowl (Gallus gallus) and occurring in many varieties. Often shortened to: fowl

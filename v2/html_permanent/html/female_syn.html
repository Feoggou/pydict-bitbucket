female

noun

  1. woman, girl, lady, lass, sheila (Australian & & New Zealand, slang), charlie (Australian, slang), chook (Australian, slang), wahine (New Zealand)
    • The average young female is stylish and confident.

adjective

  1. womanlike, woman, lady
    • Their aim is equal numbers of male and female MPs.

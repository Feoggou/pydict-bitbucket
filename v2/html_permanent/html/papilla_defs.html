papilla

(pəˈpɪlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -lae (-liː)1. the small projection of tissue at the base of a hair, tooth, or feather2. any other similar protuberance3. any minute blunt hair or process occurring in plants

Derived Forms

papillary (paˈpillary), papillar (paˈpillar), papillate (ˈpapillate) or papillose (ˈpapillose) adjective

Word Origin

C18: from Latin: nipple; related to Latin papula pimple

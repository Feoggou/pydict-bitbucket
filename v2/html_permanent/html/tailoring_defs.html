tailoring

(ˈteɪlərɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the way something is tailored; the cut or fit of something ⇒ the excellent tailoring of his jacket2. the work or profession of a tailor ⇒ All of us had done some tailoring for our fathers or mothers at home.

communist

noun

  1. (often cap.) socialist, Red (informal), Marxist, Bolshevik, collectivist • Its leader is a former Communist who joined the dissident movement in 1989.

QUOTATION:
 What is a communist? One who hath yearnings
For equal division of unequal earnings Ebenezer Elliot – Epigram

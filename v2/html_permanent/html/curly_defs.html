curly

(ˈkɜːlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: curlier or curliest1. tending to curl; curling2. having curls3. (of timber) having irregular curves or waves in the grain4. Australian and New Zealand difficult to counter or answer ⇒ a curly question
Derived forms
curliness (ˈcurliness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveCurly hair is full of curls. ⇒ I've got naturally curly hair. ⇒ Her hair was dark and curly.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Curly is sometimes used to describe things that are curved or spiral in shape. ⇒ ...cauliflowers with extra-long curly leaves. ⇒ ...dragons with curly tails. ⇒ ...spectacular curly water slides.

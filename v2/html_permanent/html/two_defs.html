two

(tuː )
Definitions
noun1. the cardinal number that is the sum of one and one. It is a prime number. See also number (sense 1)2. a numeral, 2, II, (ii), etc, representing this number3. music the numeral 2 used as the lower figure in a time signature, indicating that the beat is measured in minims4. something representing, represented by, or consisting of two units, such as a playing card with two symbols on it5. Also called: two o'clock two hours after noon or midnight6. See in two7. See put two and two together8. See that makes two of usdeterminer9. a. amounting to two ⇒ two nailsb. (as pronoun) ⇒ he bought twobinarydoubledualdi-bi-
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English twā (feminine); related to Old High German zwā, Old Norse tvau, Latin, Greek duo

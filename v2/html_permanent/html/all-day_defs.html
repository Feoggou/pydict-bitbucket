all-day

(ˈɔːlˌdeɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective (prenominal)1. available throughout the day ⇒ They serve an all-day breakfast at the motorway café.2. happening or lasting all day ⇒ There will be free all-day parking., ⇒ The festival is an all-day event.

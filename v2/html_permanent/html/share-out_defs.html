share-out

(ˈʃɛəraʊt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe act of giving several people equal or fair parts of something ⇒ the share-out of seats in the transitional government

share out

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. phrasal verbIf you share out an amount of something, you give each person in a group an equal or fair part of it. [V n P] ⇒ I drain the pasta, then I share it out between two plates. [V P n] ⇒ The company will share out $1.3 billion among 500,000 policyholders. [V P n among/between pl-n] ⇒ ...a formula for sharing out power among the various clans.2. → see also share-out →  share

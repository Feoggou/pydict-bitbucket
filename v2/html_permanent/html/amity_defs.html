(ˈæmɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -tiesfriendship; cordiality
Word origin
C15: from Old French amité, from Medieval Latin amīcitās friendship, from Latin amīcus friend

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounAmity is peaceful, friendly relations between people or countries. [formal] ⇒ He wished to live in amity with his neighbour.

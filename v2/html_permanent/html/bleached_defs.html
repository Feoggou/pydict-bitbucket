bleached

(bliːtʃt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivemade lighter in colour ⇒ a young woman with bleached hair
Example sentences containing 'bleached'
Her long pale yellow hair was bleached in highlights by the summer sun, her arms brushed with a light tan. Stuart Harrison LOST SUMMEROutside the cottage door the bleached brightness startled them. Thomas, Rosie THE WHITE DOVE

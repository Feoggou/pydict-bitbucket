freshen

(ˈfrɛʃən )
Definitions
verb1. to make or become fresh or fresher2. (often foll by up) to refresh (oneself), esp by washing3. (intransitive) (of the wind) to increase4. to lose or cause to lose saltiness5. (intransitive) mainly USa. (of farm animals) to give birthb. (of cows) to commence giving milk after calving

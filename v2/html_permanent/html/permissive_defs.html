permissive

(pəˈmɪsɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. tolerant; lenient ⇒ permissive parents2. indulgent in matters of sex ⇒ a permissive society3. granting permission4. archaic not obligatory

Derived Forms

permissively (perˈmissively) adverbpermissiveness (perˈmissiveness) noun

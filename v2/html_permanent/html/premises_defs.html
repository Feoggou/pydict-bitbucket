premises

(ˈprɛmɪsɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. a piece of land together with its buildings, esp considered as a place of business2. lawa. (in a deed, etc) the matters referred to previously; the aforesaid; the foregoingb. the introductory part of a grant, conveyance, etc3. law (in the US) the part of a bill in equity that states the names of the parties, details of the plaintiff's claims, etc

tiptoe

(ˈtɪpˌtəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -toes, -toeing or -toed (intransitive)1. to walk with the heels off the ground and the weight supported by the ball of the foot and the toes2. to walk silently or stealthilynoun3. See on tiptoeadverb4. on tiptoeadjective5. walking or standing on tiptoe6. stealthy or silent

tiptoe

Powered by COBUILD
(tɪptoʊ )
Word forms: tiptoes , tiptoeing , tiptoed
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. verbIf you tiptoe somewhere, you walk there very quietly without putting your heels on the floor when you walk. [V prep/adv] ⇒ She slipped out of bed and tiptoed to the window. [Also V]2. →  on tiptoe/on tiptoes

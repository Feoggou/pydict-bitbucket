carve

verb

  1. sculpt, form, cut, chip, sculpture, whittle, chisel, hew, fashion • One of the prisoners has carved a beautiful chess set.
  2. etch, engrave, inscribe, fashion, grave (archaic), incise, slash • He carved his name on his desk.
  3. slice, hack • Carve the beef into slices.

'carve' in Other Languages

    If you carve an object, you cut it out of stone or wood. You carve wood or stone in order to make the object.He carved a beautiful wooden chess set.I picked up a piece of wood and started carving.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA pro is a professional. [informal] ⇒ In the professional theater, there is a tremendous need to prove that you're a pro. ⇒ I have enjoyed playing with some of the top pros from Europe and America.2. adjective [ADJ n]A pro player is a professional sportsman or woman. You can also use pro to refer to sports that are played by professional sportsmen or women. [US] ⇒ ...a former college and pro basketball player. ⇒ Corsin played pro football for nine years.3. prepositionIf you are pro a particular course of action or belief, you agree with it or support it. [mainly British] ⇒ I'm one of the few that's very pro performance-related pay.4. →  pros and cons

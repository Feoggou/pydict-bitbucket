variable nounVeg are plants such as cabbages, potatoes, and onions which you can cook and eat. Veg is an abbreviation for 'vegetables'. [mainly British, informal] ⇒ ...fruit and veg. ⇒ ...youngsters who prefer burgers to meat and two veg.regional note:   in AM, usually use veggies

European

(ˌjʊərəˈpɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to Europe or its inhabitants2. native to or derived from Europenoun3. a native or inhabitant of Europe4. a person of European descent5. a supporter of the European Union or of political union of the countries of Europe or a part of it
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

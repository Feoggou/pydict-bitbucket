uncomplicated for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnkɒmplɪkeɪtɪd )
Definitions
adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as uncomplicated, you approve of them because they are easy to deal with or understand. [approval] ⇒ She is a beautiful, uncomplicated girl. ⇒ ...good, fresh British cooking with its uncomplicated, direct flavours.

Word forms: pincers
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. plural noun [oft a pair of N]Pincers consist of two pieces of metal that are hinged in the middle. They are used as a tool for gripping things or for pulling things out. ⇒ His surgical instruments were a knife and a pair of pincers.2. countable noun [usu pl]The pincers of an animal such as a crab or a lobster are its front claws.

impermeable

(ɪmˈpɜːmɪəbəl )
Definitions
adjective(of a substance) not allowing the passage of a fluid through interstices; not permeable
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

impermeability (imˌpermeaˈbility) or impermeableness (imˈpermeableness) nounimpermeably (imˈpermeably) adverb

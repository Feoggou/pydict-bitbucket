regional note:   in AM, use discolorergative verb & verbIf something discolours or if it is discoloured by something else, its original colour changes, so that it looks unattractive. [V] ⇒ A tooth which has been hit hard may discolour. [V n] ⇒ Some oil had seeped out, discolouring the grass.discoloured graded adjective & adjective ⇒ Some of the prints were badly discoloured.discoloration (dɪskʌləreɪʃən ) uncount noun ⇒ ...the discoloration of the soil from acid spills.

youthful

(ˈjuːθfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, possessing, or characteristic of youth2. fresh, vigorous, or active ⇒ he's surprisingly youthful for his age3. in an early stage of development ⇒ a youthful culture4. Also: young (of a river, valley, or land surface) in the early stage of the cycle of erosion, characterized by steep slopes, lack of flood plains, and V-shaped valleys. Compare mature (sense 6), old (sense 18)

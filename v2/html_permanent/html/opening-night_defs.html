opening night

(ˈəʊpənɪŋ naɪt)
Definitions
noun1. theatre the first performance (of a concert, opera, play, etc) ⇒ the opening night of the play2. the first night a new establishment (such as a restaurant, club, etc) is open for business ⇒ the club's opening night

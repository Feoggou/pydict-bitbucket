pestilent

(ˈpɛstɪlənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. annoying; irritating2. highly destructive morally or physically; pernicious3. infected with or likely to cause epidemic or infectious disease

Derived Forms

pestilently (ˈpestilently) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Latin pestilens unwholesome, from pestis plague
Example sentences containing 'pestilent'
And the vourhain---is that what that pestilent bloated one is called? Robert Silverberg LORD PRESTIMION

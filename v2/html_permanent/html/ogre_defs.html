ogre

(ˈəʊɡə )
Definitions
noun1. (in folklore) a giant, usually given to eating human flesh2. any monstrous or cruel person
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ogreish (ˈogreish) adjectiveogress (ˈogress) feminine noun

Word Origin

C18: from French, perhaps from Latin Orcus god of the infernal regions

phosphorescence

(ˌfɒsfəˈrɛsəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. physicsa. a fluorescence that persists after the bombarding radiation producing it has stoppedb. a fluorescence for which the average lifetime of the excited atoms is greater than 10–8 seconds2. the light emitted in phosphorescence3. the emission of light during a chemical reaction, such as bioluminescence, in which insufficient heat is evolved to cause fluorescence. Compare fluorescence

athletic

adjective

  1. fit, strong, powerful, healthy, active, trim, strapping, robust, vigorous, energetic, muscular, two-fisted, sturdy, husky (informal), lusty, herculean, sinewy, brawny, able-bodied, well-proportioned • He was tall, with an athletic build. OPPOSITES:  delicate, frail, feeble, sickly, puny, weedy (informal)
  2. sporting, games, sports, competitive • They have been given scholarships purely on athletic ability.

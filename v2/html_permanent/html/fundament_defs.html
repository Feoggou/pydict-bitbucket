fundament

(ˈfʌndəmənt )
Definitions
noun1. euphemistic or facetious the buttocks2. the natural features of the earth's surface, unaltered by man3. a base or foundation, esp of a building4. a theory, principle, or underlying basis
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Latin fundāmentum foundation, from fundāre to found2

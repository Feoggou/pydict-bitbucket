uninhabitable for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnɪnhæbɪtəbəl )
Definitions
adjectiveIf a place is uninhabitable, it is impossible for people to live there, for example because it is dangerous or unhealthy. ⇒ About 90 percent of the city's single-family homes are uninhabitable. ⇒ As parts of the world become uninhabitable, millions of people will try to migrate to more hospitable areas. ⇒ ...a young couple turning an uninhabitable wreck into their first home.

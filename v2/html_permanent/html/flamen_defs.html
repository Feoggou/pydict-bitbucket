flamen

(ˈfleɪmɛn )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural flamens or flamines (ˈflæmɪˌniːz )(in ancient Rome) any of 15 priests who each served a particular deity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin; probably related to Old English blōtan to sacrifice, Gothic blotan to worship

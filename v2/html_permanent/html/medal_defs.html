medal

(ˈmɛdəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small flat piece of metal bearing an inscription or image, given as an award or commemoration of some outstanding action, event, etcverb -als, -alling, -alled or US -als, -aling or -aled2. (transitive) to honour with a medal3. (intransitive) informal (in sport) to win a medal

Derived Forms

medallic (mɪˈdælɪk ) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from French médaille, probably from Italian medaglia, ultimately from Latin metallum metal

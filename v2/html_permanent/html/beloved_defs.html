Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. dearly lovednoun2. a person who is dearly loved, such as a wife or husband

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
pronunciation note:   When the adjective is not followed by a noun it is pronounced (bɪlʌvd ) and is hyphenated be+loved.1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n, Also v-link ADJ of/by n]A beloved person, thing, or place is one that you feel great affection for. cherished ⇒ He lost his beloved wife last year. ⇒ The rose is the most romantic of flowers, beloved of poets, singers, and artists.2. singular noun [usu poss N]Your beloved is the person that you love. [old-fashioned] ⇒ He takes his beloved into his arms.

Korean

(kəˈriːən )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to Korea, its people, or their languagenoun2. a native or inhabitant of Korea3. the official language of North and South Korea, considered by some scholars to be part of the Altaic family of languages
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Korean for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(kɔːriːən )
Word forms: Koreans
Definitions
1. adjectiveKorean means belonging or relating to North or South Korea, or to their people, language, or culture.2. countable nounA Korean is a North or South Korean citizen, or a person of North or South Korean origin.3. uncount nounKorean is the language spoken by people who live in North and South Korea.

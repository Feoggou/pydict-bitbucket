unconquerable

(ʌnˈkɒŋkərəbəl)
Definitions
adjective1. not able to be overcome or defeated ⇒ unconquerable strength, ⇒ the unconquerable soldiers, ⇒ De Gaulle projected the myth of an unconquerable France., ⇒ the unconquerable mountains, ⇒ his unconquerable soul, ⇒ an unconquerable desire to win2. not able to be overcome or surmounted ⇒ an unconquerable difficulty, ⇒ an unconquerable tendency3. not able to gain possession or control of ⇒ unconquerable fate

goo

(ɡuː )
Definitions
noun informal1. a sticky or viscous substance2. coy or sentimental language or ideas
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: of uncertain origin

uncount nounYou can use goo to refer to any thick, sticky substance, for example mud or paste. [informal] ⇒ ...a sticky goo of pineapple and coconut.

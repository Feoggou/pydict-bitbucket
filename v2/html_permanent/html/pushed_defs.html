COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If you are pushed for something such as time or money, you do not have enough of it. [British, informal] [+ for] ⇒ He's going to be a bit pushed for money.regional note:   in AM, use pressed for2. →  be hard pushed to do

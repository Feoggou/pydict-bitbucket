veiled

(veɪld )
Definitions
adjective1. disguised ⇒ a veiled insult2. (of sound, tone, the voice, etc) not distinct; muffled
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

veiledly (ˈveɪlɪdlɪ ) adverb

veiled

Powered by COBUILD
(veɪld ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]A veiled comment is expressed in a disguised form rather than directly and openly. ⇒ He made only a veiled reference to international concerns over human rights issues. ⇒ This last clause is a thinly-veiled threat to those who might choose to ignore the decree.2. adjectiveA woman or girl who is veiled is wearing a veil. ⇒ A veiled woman gave me a kindly smile.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

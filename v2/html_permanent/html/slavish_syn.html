slavish

adjective

  1. imitative, unimaginative, unoriginal, conventional, second-hand, uninspired • a slavish follower of fashion OPPOSITES:  independent, original, imaginative, radical, creative, revolutionary, inventive
  2. servile, cringing, abject, submissive, grovelling, mean, low, base, fawning, despicable, menial, sycophantic, obsequious • slavish devotion OPPOSITES:  assertive, rebellious, wilful, masterful, domineering, self-willed

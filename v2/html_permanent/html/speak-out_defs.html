Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
verb (intr, adverb)1. to state one's beliefs, objections, etc, bravely and firmly2. to speak more loudly and clearly

phrasal verbIf you speak out against something or in favour of something, you say publicly that you think it is bad or good.

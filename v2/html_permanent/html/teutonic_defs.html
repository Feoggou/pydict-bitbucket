Teutonic

(tjuːˈtɒnɪk )
Definitions
adjective1. characteristic of or relating to the German people ⇒ Teutonic thoroughness2. of or relating to the ancient Teutons3. (not used in linguistics) of or relating to the Germanic languagesnoun4. an obsolete name for Germanic
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Teutonically (Teuˈtonically) adverb

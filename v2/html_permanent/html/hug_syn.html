Synonyms of hug

hug

verb

  1. embrace, hold (onto), cuddle, squeeze, cling, clasp, enfold, hold close, take in your arms
    • They hugged each other like a couple of lost children.
  2. clasp, hold (onto), grip, nurse, retain
    • He trudged towards them, hugging a large box.
  3. follow closely, keep close, stay near, cling to, follow the course of
    • The road hugs the coast for hundreds of miles.

noun

  1. embrace, squeeze, bear hug, clinch (slang), clasp
    • She leapt out of the seat, and gave him a hug.

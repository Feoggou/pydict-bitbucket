Synonyms of slosh

slosh

verb

  1. splash, wash, slop, break, plash
    • The water sloshed around the bridge.
  2. wade, splash, flounder, paddle, dabble, wallow, swash
    • We sloshed through the mud together.
  3. (informal) pour, shower, spray, slap
  4. (British slang) hit, strike, punch, belt (informal), bash (informal), sock (slang), slug, swipe (informal), slog, biff (slang), wallop (informal), thwack

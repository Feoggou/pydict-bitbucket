providing

(prəˈvaɪdɪŋ ) or provided
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
conjunction(subordinating; sometimes foll by that) on the condition or understanding (that) ⇒ I'll play, providing you pay me

providing

Powered by COBUILD
(prəvaɪdɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
subordinating conjunction & conjunctionIf you say that something will happen providing or providing that something else happens, you mean that the first thing will happen only if the second thing also happens. ⇒ I do believe in people being able to do what they want to do, providing they're not hurting someone else. ⇒ Providing they do not panic, I believe that their chances of survival will be beyond 95 per cent.

greyish

Powered by COBUILD
(greɪɪʃ ) for learners of English
Definitions
regional note:   in AM, use grayishadjectiveGreyish means slightly grey in colour. ⇒ The building was of greyish plaster and looked old.Greyish is also a combining form. ⇒ ...greyish-green leaves.

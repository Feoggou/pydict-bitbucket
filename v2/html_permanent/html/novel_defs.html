novel1

(ˈnɒvəl )
Definitions
noun1. an extended work in prose, either fictitious or partly so, dealing with character, action, thought, etc, esp in the form of a story2. See the novel3. (usually plural) obsolete a short story or novella, as one of those in the Decameron of Boccaccio
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French novelle, from Latin novella (narrātiō) new (story); see novel2

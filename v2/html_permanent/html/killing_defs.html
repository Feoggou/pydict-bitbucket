killing

(ˈkɪlɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. informal very tiring; exhausting ⇒ a killing pace2. informal extremely funny; hilarious3. causing death; fatalnoun4. the act of causing death; slaying5. informal a sudden stroke of success, usually financial, as in speculations on the stock market (esp in the phrase make a killing)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

killingly (ˈkillingly) adverb

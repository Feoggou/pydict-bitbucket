Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something as intriguing, you mean that it is interesting or strange. ⇒ This intriguing book is both thoughtful and informative.intriguingly graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj, ADV with v] ⇒ ...the intriguingly-named newspaper Le Canard Enchainé (The Chained Duck).

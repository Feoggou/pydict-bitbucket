hammock1

(ˈhæmək )
Definitions
nouna length of canvas, net, etc, suspended at the ends and used as a bed
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hammock-like (ˈhammock-ˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Spanish hamaca, of Taino origin

fulminate

(ˈfʌlmɪˌneɪt , ˈfʊl-)
Definitions
verb1. (intransitive; often foll by against) to make criticisms or denunciations; rail2. to explode with noise and violence3. (intransitive) archaic to thunder and lightennoun4. any salt or ester of fulminic acid, esp the mercury salt, which is used as a detonator
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fulmination (ˌfulmiˈnation) nounfulminator (ˈfulmiˌnator) nounfulminatory (ˈfulmiˌnatory) adjective

Word Origin

C15: from Medieval Latin fulmināre; see fulminant

coin

noun

  1. money, change, cash, silver, copper, dosh (British & & Australian, slang), specie, wonga (slang), kembla (Australian, slang) • His pocket was full of coins.

verb

  1. invent, create, make up, frame, forge, conceive, originate, formulate, fabricate, think up • The phrase `cosmic ray' was coined by R. A. Millikan in 1925.
  2. mint, mould
related adjective:
nummary
related enthusiast:
numismatist

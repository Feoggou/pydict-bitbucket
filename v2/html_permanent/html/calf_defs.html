calf1

(kɑːf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural calves1. the young of cattle, esp domestic cattle ▶ Related adjective: vituline2. the young of certain other mammals, such as the buffalo, elephant, giraffe, and whale3. a large piece of floating ice detached from an iceberg, etc4. See kill the fatted calf5. another name for calfskin
Word origin
Old English cealf; related to Old Norse kālfr, Gothic kalbō, Old High German kalba

glorify

(ˈɡlɔːrɪˌfaɪ )
Definitions
verb -fies, -fying or -fied (transitive)1. to make glorious2. to make more splendid; adorn3. to worship, exalt, or adore4. to extol5. to cause to seem more splendid or imposing than reality
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
1. verbTo glorify something means to praise it or make it seem good or special, usually when it is not. [V n] ⇒ This magazine in no way glorifies gangs. [V n] ⇒ ...the banning of songs glorifying war and racism.glorification (glɔːrɪfɪkeɪʃən ) uncount noun ⇒ ...the glorification of violence. ⇒ ...a glorification of the past.2. verbIf you glorify God, you praise Him. praise [V n] ⇒ We are all committed to serving the Lord and glorifying His name in the best way we know.

audible

(ˈɔːdɪbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. perceptible to the hearing; loud enough to be heardnoun2. American football a change of playing tactics called by the quarterback when the offense is lined up at the line of scrimmage
Derived forms
ˌaudiˈbility or ˈaudibleness nounˈaudibly adverb
Word origin
C16: from Late Latin audibilis, from Latin audīre to hear

melancholic

(ˌmɛlənˈkɒlɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. relating to or suffering from melancholy or melancholianoun2. a person who suffers from melancholia

Derived Forms

ˌmelancholically (ˌmelanˈcholically) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as melancholic, you mean that they are very sad. [literary] ⇒ The night was as melancholic as his mood. ⇒ ...his gentle, melancholic songs.A melancholic is someone who is melancholic. ⇒ ...a self-made man, energetic and obsessively orderly, a histrionic exhibitionist and a melancholic.

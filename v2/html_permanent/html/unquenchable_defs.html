unquenchable

(ʌnˈkwɛntʃəbəl)
Definitions
adjective formal1. (of thirst) not able to be satisfied by drinking ⇒ He had a headache and an unquenchable thirst.2. not able to be satisfied ⇒ He had an unquenchable thirst for danger and excitement., ⇒ an unquenchable appetite for easy money, ⇒ his unquenchable desire to win

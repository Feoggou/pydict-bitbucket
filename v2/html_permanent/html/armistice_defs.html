armistice

(ˈɑːmɪstɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan agreement between opposing armies to suspend hostilities in order to discuss peace terms; truce
Word origin
C18: from New Latin armistitium, from Latin arma arms + sistere to stop, stand still

questionable

(ˈkwɛstʃənəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. (esp of a person's morality or honesty) admitting of some doubt; dubious2. of disputable value or authority ⇒ a questionable text
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

questionableness (ˈquestionableness) or questionability (ˌquestionaˈbility) nounquestionably (ˈquestionably) adverb

(kwestʃənəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that something is questionable, you mean that it is not completely honest, reasonable, or acceptable. [formal] ⇒ He has been dogged by allegations of questionable business practices. ⇒ It is questionable whether the expenditure on this project is really justified. ⇒ The film is a comedy in highly questionable taste.

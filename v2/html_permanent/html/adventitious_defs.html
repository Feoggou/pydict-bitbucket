adventitious

(ˌædvɛnˈtɪʃəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. added or appearing accidentally or unexpectedly2. (of a plant or animal part) developing in an abnormal position, as a root that grows from a stem
Derived forms
ˌadvenˈtitiously adverb
Word origin
C17: from Latin adventīcius coming from outside, from adventus a coming

Similar Words

accidental, chance, foreign, unexpected, casual, incidental, extraneous, fortuitous, nonessential

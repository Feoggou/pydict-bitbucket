larder

(ˈlɑːdə )
Definitions
nouna room or cupboard, used as a store for food
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French lardier, from lard

(lɑːʳdəʳ ) for learners of English

Definitions
countable nounA larder is a room or large cupboard in a house, usually near the kitchen, in which food is kept. [mainly British] regional note:   in AM, use pantry

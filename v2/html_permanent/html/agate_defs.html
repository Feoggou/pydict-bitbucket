agate1

(ˈæɡɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an impure microcrystalline form of quartz consisting of a variegated, usually banded chalcedony, used as a gemstone and in making pestles and mortars, burnishers, and polishers. Formula: SiO22. a playing marble of this quartz or resembling it3. Also called: ruby printing, US and Canadian (formerly) a size of printer's type approximately equal to 51⁄2 point
Word origin
C16: via French from Latin achātēs, from Greek akhatēs

scalpel

(ˈskælpəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna surgical knife with a short thin blade

Derived Forms

scalpellic (skælˈpɛlɪk ) adjective

Word Origin

C18: from Latin scalpellum, from scalper a knife, from scalpere to scrape

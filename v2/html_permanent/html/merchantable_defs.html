Definitions
COBUILD Key Words for Retail
adjective(Retail: General) If something is of merchantable quality, it is of high enough quality to sell or trade in. ⇒ The customer was entitled to claim damages as the product was not of merchantable quality. ⇒ Something is of merchantable quality if it complies with all the appropriate standards applicable for products of that type. ⇒ If something is of merchantable quality, it is of high enough quality to sell or trade in. ▶  merchant

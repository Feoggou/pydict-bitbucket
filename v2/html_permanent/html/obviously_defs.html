obviously

(ˈɒbvɪəslɪ )
Definitions
adverb1. in a way that is easy to see or understand; evidently2. without subtlety3. (sentence modifier) it is obvious that; clearly ⇒ obviously not everyone wants a bank account
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

obviously for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ɒbviəsli )
Definitions
1. adverbYou use obviously when you are stating something that you expect the person who is listening to know already. [emphasis] ⇒ Obviously, they've had sponsorship from some big companies. ⇒ There are obviously exceptions to this.2. graded adverb & adverbYou use obviously to indicate that something is easily noticed, seen, or recognized. ⇒ They obviously appreciate you very much. ⇒ She's so obviously cleverer and prettier than I am.

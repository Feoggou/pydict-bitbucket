confirmed

(kənˈfɜːmd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (prenominal) long-established in a habit, way of life, etc ⇒ a confirmed bachelor2. having received the rite of confirmation3. (of a disease) another word for chronic
Derived forms

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]You use confirmed to describe someone who has a particular habit or belief that they are very unlikely to change. ⇒ I'm a confirmed bachelor. ⇒ Leonard, a confirmed atheist, simply could not understand.

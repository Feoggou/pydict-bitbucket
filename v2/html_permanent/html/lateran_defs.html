Lateran

(ˈlætərən )
Definitions
noun the Lateran1. Also called: Lateran palace a palace in Rome, formerly the official residence of the popes2. any of five ecumenical councils held in this palace between 1123 and 15123. the basilica of Saint John Lateran, the cathedral church of Rome
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

from Latin: the district is named after the ancient Roman family Plautii Laterani

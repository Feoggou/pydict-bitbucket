Icarus

(ˈɪkərəs , ˈaɪ-)
Definitions
nounGreek mythology the son of Daedalus, with whom he escaped from Crete, flying with wings made of wax and feathers. Heedless of his father's warning he flew too near the sun, causing the wax to melt, and fell into the Aegean and drowned

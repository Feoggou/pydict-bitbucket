Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as dour, you mean that they are very serious and unfriendly. ⇒ ...a dour, taciturn man. ⇒ No wonder he looked so dour.dourly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj] [disapproval] ⇒ The old man stared dourly at them.

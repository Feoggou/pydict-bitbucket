serviceable

(ˈsɜːvɪsəbəl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. capable of or ready for service; usable2. capable of giving good service; durable3. archaic diligent in service

Derived Forms

ˌserviceability (ˌserviceaˈbility) or serviceableness (ˈserviceableness) nounserviceably (ˈserviceably) adverb

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

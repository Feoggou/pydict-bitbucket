invigorate

(ɪnˈvɪɡəˌreɪt )
Definitions
verb(transitive) to give vitality and vigour to; animate; brace; refresh ⇒ to be invigorated by fresh air
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

invigorating (inˈvigorˌating) adjectiveinvigoratingly (inˈvigorˌatingly) adverbinvigoration (inˌvigorˈation) nouninvigorative (inˈvigorative) adjectiveinvigoratively (inˈvigoratively) adverbinvigorator (inˈvigorˌator) noun

Word Origin

C17: from in-2 + Latin vigor vigour

conduce

(kənˈdjuːs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(intransitive; foll by to) to lead or contribute (to a result)
Derived forms
conducer (conˈducer) nounconducible (conˈducible) adjectiveconducingly (conˈducingly) adverb
Word origin
C15: from Latin condūcere to lead together, from com- together + dūcere to lead
Example sentences containing 'conduce'
A nun or monk is expected not only to avoid wrongdoing, but also the situations that conduce to it. Newbery, Linda RIDDLE ME THIS

Similar Words

lead, aid, advance, tend, promote, contribute, avail

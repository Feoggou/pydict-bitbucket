Moroccan

(məˈrɒkən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Morocco or its inhabitantsnoun2. a native or inhabitant of Morocco

Moroccan

Powered by COBUILD
(mərɒkən )
Word forms: Moroccans
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveMoroccan means belonging or relating to Morocco or to its people or culture.2. countable nounA Moroccan is a person who comes from Morocco.

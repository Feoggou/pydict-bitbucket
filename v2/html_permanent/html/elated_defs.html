Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are elated, you are extremely happy and excited because of something that has happened. ⇒ I was elated that my second heart bypass had been successful. ⇒ 'That was one of the best races of my life,' said an elated Hakkinen.

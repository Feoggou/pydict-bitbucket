hoarding

(ˈhɔːdɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. a large board used for displaying advertising posters, as by a road. Also called (esp US and Canadian): billboard2. a temporary wooden fence erected round a building or demolition site
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from C15 hoard fence, from Old French hourd palisade, of Germanic origin, related to Gothic haurds, Old Norse hurth door

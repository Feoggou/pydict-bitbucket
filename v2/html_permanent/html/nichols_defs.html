Nichols

(ˈnɪkəlz )
Definitions
noun1.  Peter (Richard). born 1927, British dramatist, whose works include A Day in the Death of Joe Egg (1967), the musical Privates on Parade (1977), and Blue Murder (1995)2.  Vincent (Gerard). born 1945, English clergyman: Archbishop of Westminster from 2009

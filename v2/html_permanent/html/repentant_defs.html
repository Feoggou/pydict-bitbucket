repentant

(rɪˈpɛntənt )
Definitions
adjective1. reproaching oneself for one's past actions or sins; contrite2. characterized by or proceeding from a sense of contrition ⇒ a repentant heart, ⇒ his repentant words
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

repentantly (reˈpentantly) adverb

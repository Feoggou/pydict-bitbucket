spacing

(ˈspeɪsɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the arrangement of letters, words, etc, on a page in order to achieve legibility or aesthetic appeal2. the arrangement of objects in a space

spacing

Powered by COBUILD
(speɪsɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounSpacing refers to the way that typing or printing is arranged on a page, especially in relation to the amount of space that is left between words or lines. ⇒ Please type or write clearly in double spacing on one side of A4 paper only.2. → see also space

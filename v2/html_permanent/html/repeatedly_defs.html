repeatedly

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪpiːtɪdli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [ADV with v]If you do something repeatedly, you do it many times. ⇒ Both men have repeatedly denied the allegations. ⇒ The rebel soldiers tried repeatedly to storm the building.

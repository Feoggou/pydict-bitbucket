thimble

(ˈθɪmbəl )
Definitions
noun1. a cap of metal, plastic, etc, used to protect the end of the finger when sewing2. any small metal cap resembling this3. nautical a loop of metal having a groove at its outer edge for a rope or cable, for lining the inside of an eye4. short for thimbleful
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English thӯmel thumbstall, from thūma thumb

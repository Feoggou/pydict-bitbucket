repentance

(rɪˈpɛntəns )
Definitions
noun1. remorse or contrition for one's past actions or sins2. an act or the process of being repentant; penitence
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

repentance

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪpentəns ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIf you show repentance for something wrong that you have done, you make it clear that you are sorry for doing it. ⇒ They showed no repentance during their trial.

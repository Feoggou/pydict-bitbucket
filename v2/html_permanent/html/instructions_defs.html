instructions

(ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz )
Definitions
plural noun1. directions, orders, or recommended rules for guidance, use, etc2. law the facts and details relating to a case given by a client to his solicitor or by a solicitor to a barrister with directions to conduct the case ⇒ to take instructions

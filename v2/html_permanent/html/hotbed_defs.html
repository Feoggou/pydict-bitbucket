hotbed

(ˈhɒtˌbɛd )
Definitions
noun1. a glass-covered bed of soil, usually heated by fermenting material, used for propagating plants, forcing early vegetables, etc2. a place offering ideal conditions for the growth of an idea, activity, etc, esp one considered bad ⇒ a hotbed of insurrection

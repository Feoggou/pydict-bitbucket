chuff1

(tʃʌf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a puffing sound of or as if of a steam engineverb2. (intransitive) to move while emitting such sounds ⇒ the train chuffed on its way
Word origin
C20: of imitative origin

chuff2

(tʃʌf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noundialect a boor; churl; sullen fellow
Word origin
C17: from obsolete chuff (n) fat cheek, of obscure origin

chuff3

(tʃʌf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(tr; usually passive) British slang to please or delight ⇒ he was chuffed by his pay rise
Word origin
probably from chuff (adj) pleased, happy (earlier: chubby), from C16 chuff (obsolete n) a fat cheek, of unknown origin

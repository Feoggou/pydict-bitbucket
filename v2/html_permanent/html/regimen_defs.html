regimen

(ˈrɛdʒɪˌmɛn )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: regime a systematic way of life or course of therapy, often including exercise and a recommended diet2. administration or rule
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin: guidance

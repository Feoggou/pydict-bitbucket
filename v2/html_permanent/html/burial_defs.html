burial

(ˈbɛrɪəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe act of burying, esp the interment of a dead body
Word origin
Old English byrgels burial place, tomb; see bury, -al2

variable nounA burial is the act or ceremony of putting a dead body into a grave in the ground. ⇒ The priest prepared the body for burial. ⇒ He can have a decent burial.

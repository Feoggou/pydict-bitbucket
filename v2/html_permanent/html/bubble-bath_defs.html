bubble bath

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a powder, liquid, or crystals used to scent, soften, and foam in bath water2. a bath to which such a substance has been added

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounBubble bath is a liquid that smells nice and makes a lot of bubbles when you add it to your bath water.2. countable nounWhen you have a bubble bath, you lie in a bath of water with bubble bath in it. ⇒ ...a long, relaxing bubble bath.

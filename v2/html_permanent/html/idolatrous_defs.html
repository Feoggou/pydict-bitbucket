idolatrous

(aɪˈdɑlətrəs ) in American English
Definitions
adjective1.  of, or having the nature of, idolatry2.  worshiping an idol or idols3.  having or showing excessive admiration or devotion
Webster’s New World College Dictionary, Fourth Edition, compiled by the editors of Webster’s New World Dictionaries. Copyright © 2010 by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. Published by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. All rights reserved.

Derived Forms

idolatrously (iˈdolatrously) adverb idolatrousness (iˈdolatrousness) noun

Similar Words

adoring, uncritical, reverential, worshipful, adulatory

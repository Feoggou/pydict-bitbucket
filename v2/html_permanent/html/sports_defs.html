sports

(spɔːts )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (modifier) relating to, concerned with, or used in sports ⇒ sports equipment2. (modifier) relating to or similar to a sports car ⇒ sports seats3. Also called: sports day British a meeting held at a school or college for competitions in various athletic events

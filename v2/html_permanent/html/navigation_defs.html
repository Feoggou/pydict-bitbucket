navigation

(ˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the skill or process of plotting a route and directing a ship, aircraft, etc, along it2. the act or practice of navigating ⇒ dredging made navigation of the river possible3. US rare ship traffic; shipping4. English Midlands dialect an inland waterway; canal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

incessant

(ɪnˈsɛsənt )
Definitions
adjectivenot ceasing; continual
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

incessancy (inˈcessancy) or incessantness (inˈcessantness) nounincessantly (inˈcessantly) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Late Latin incessāns, from Latin in-1 + cessāre to cease

(ɪnsesənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]An incessant process or activity is one that continues without stopping. ⇒ ...incessant rain. ⇒ ...his incessant demands for affection.incessantly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ Dee talked incessantly.

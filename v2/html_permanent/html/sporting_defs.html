sporting

(ˈspɔːtɪŋ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (prenominal) of, relating to, or used or engaged in a sport or sports ⇒ several sporting interests2. relating or conforming to sportsmanship; fair3. of, relating to, or characterized by an interest in gambling4. willing to take a risk

Derived Forms

sportingly (ˈsportingly) adverb

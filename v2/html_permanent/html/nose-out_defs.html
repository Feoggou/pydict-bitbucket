Word Frequency

nose out

Definitions
verb (tr, adverb)1. to discover by smelling2. to discover by cunning or persistence ⇒ the reporter managed to nose out a few facts3. informal to beat by a narrow margin ⇒ he was nosed out of first place by the champion

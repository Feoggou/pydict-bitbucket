Definitions

versed

Powered by COBUILD
(vɜːʳst ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [adv ADJ]If you are versed in or well versed in something, you know a lot about it. [+ in] ⇒ Page is well versed in many styles of jazz. ⇒ ...experts more versed in the economics of taxes than the politics of taxes.

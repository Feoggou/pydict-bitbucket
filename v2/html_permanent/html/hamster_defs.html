hamster

(ˈhæmstə )
Definitions
nounany Eurasian burrowing rodent of the tribe Cricetini, such as Mesocricetus auratus (golden hamster), having a stocky body, short tail, and cheek pouches: family Cricetidae. They are popular pets
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from German, from Old High German hamustro, of Slavic origin

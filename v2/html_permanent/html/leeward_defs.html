leeward

(ˈliːwəd , nautical ˈluːəd ) mainly nautical
Definitions
adjective1. of, in, or moving to the quarter towards which the wind blowsnoun2. the point or quarter towards which the wind blows3. the side towards the leeadverb4. towards the lee▷ Compare windward

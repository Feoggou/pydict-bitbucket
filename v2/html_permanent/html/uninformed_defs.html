uninformed for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnɪnfɔːʳmd )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as uninformed, you mean that they have very little knowledge or information about a particular situation or subject. [+ about] ⇒ He could not complain that he was uninformed about the true nature of the regime. ⇒ Cases of child abuse often go unreported or ignored by uninformed citizens.

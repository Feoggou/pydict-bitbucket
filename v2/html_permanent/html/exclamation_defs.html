exclamation

(ˌɛkskləˈmeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. an abrupt, emphatic, or excited cry or utterance; interjection; ejaculation2. the act of exclaiming
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

exclamational (ˌexclaˈmational) adjective

Definitions
countable nounAn exclamation is a sound, word, or sentence that is spoken suddenly, loudly, or emphatically and that expresses excitement, admiration, shock, or anger. ⇒ Sue gave an exclamation as we got a clear sight of the house.

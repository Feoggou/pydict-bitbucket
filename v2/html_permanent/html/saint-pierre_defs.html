English definition: Saint-Pierre1, Saint-Pierre2

Saint-Pierre1

(French sɛ̃ pjɛr)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun usually abbreviated to: St-Pierrea town on the coast of the French island of Martinique, destroyed by the eruption of Mont Pelée in 1902 with the loss of about 30 000 lives; later partly rebuilt

Saint-Pierre2 - French

( sɛ̃pjɛr)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun Jacques Henri Bernardin de (ʒak ɑ̃ri bɛrnardɛ̃ də). 1737–1814, French author; his work, which was greatly influenced by the writings of Rousseau, includes Voyage à l'Île de France (1773), Études de la nature (1784, 1788), and La chaumière indienne (1791)

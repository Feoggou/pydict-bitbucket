sandstone

(ˈsændˌstəʊn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany of a group of common sedimentary rocks consisting of sand grains consolidated with such materials as quartz, haematite, and clay minerals: used widely in building

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounSandstone is a type of rock which contains a lot of sand. It is often used for building houses and walls. ⇒ ...the reddish sandstone walls. ⇒ ...sandstone cliffs.

congruity

(kɑnˈgruəti ; kənˈgruəti )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural conˈgruities1.  the condition or fact of being congruous or congruent; specif.,a.  agreement; harmonyb.  fitness; appropriatenessc.  Geometry exact coincidence (of two or more figures)2.  an instance of agreement

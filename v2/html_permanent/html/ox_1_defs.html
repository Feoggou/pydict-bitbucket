ox

(ɒks )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural oxen (ˈɒksən )1. an adult castrated male of any domesticated species of cattle, esp Bos taurus, used for draught work and meat2. any bovine mammal, esp any of the domestic cattle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English oxa; related to Old Saxon, Old High German ohso, Old Norse oxi

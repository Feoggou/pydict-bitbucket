anemometer

(ˌænɪˈmɒmɪtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: wind gauge an instrument for recording the speed and often the direction of winds2. any instrument that measures the rate of movement of a fluid
Derived forms
anemometric (ˌænɪməʊˈmɛtrɪk ) or ˌanemoˈmetrical adjective

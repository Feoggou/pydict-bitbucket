(stɒk kjuːb)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna small cube-shaped block composed of dehydrated vegetable or meat juices, flavourings and other ingredients that you add to soups and casseroles to provide flavour

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA stock cube is a solid cube made from dried meat or vegetable juices and other flavourings. Stock cubes are used to add flavour to dishes such as stews and soups.

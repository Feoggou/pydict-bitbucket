invincible

(ɪnˈvɪnsəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. incapable of being defeated; unconquerable2. unable to be overcome; insuperable ⇒ invincible prejudices
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

invincibility (inˌvinciˈbility) or invincibleness (inˈvincibleness) nouninvincibly (inˈvincibly) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Late Latin invincibilis, from Latin in-1 + vincere to conquer

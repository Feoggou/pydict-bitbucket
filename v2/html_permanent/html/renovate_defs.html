renovate

(ˈrɛnəˌveɪt )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to restore (something) to good condition ⇒ to renovate paintings2. to revive or refresh (one's spirits, health, etc)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

renovation (ˌrenoˈvation) nounrenovative (ˈrenoˌvative) adjectiverenovator (ˈrenoˌvator) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin renovāre, from re- + novāre to make new, from novus new

amendment

(əˈmɛndmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of amending; correction2. an addition, alteration, or improvement to a motion, document, etc

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounAn amendment is a section that is added to a law or rule in order to change it. ⇒ ...an amendment to the defense bill. ⇒ ...a constitutional amendment. ⇒ Parliament gained certain rights of amendment.2. countable nounAn amendment is a change that is made to a piece of writing.

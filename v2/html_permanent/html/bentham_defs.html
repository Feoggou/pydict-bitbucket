Bentham

(ˈbɛnθəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Jeremy. 1748–1832, British philosopher and jurist: a founder of utilitarianism. His works include A Fragment on Government (1776) and Introduction to the Principles of Morals and Legislation (1789)

lidded

Powered by COBUILD
(lɪdɪd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Lidded is used to describe a container that has a lid. ⇒ ...a lidded saucepan.2. adjectiveWhen someone has lidded eyes, their eyelids are partly or fully closed. [literary] ⇒ Julie squinted at her through lidded eyes. ⇒ My own eyes had felt heavy-lidded, but now I was wide awake.

first floor

Definitions
noun1. British the floor or storey of a building immediately above the ground floor. US and Canadian term: second floor2. US and Canadian another term for ground floor

first-floor

(ˌfɜːstˈflɔː)
Definitions
modifier1. British on the floor above the ground floor ⇒ We live in a first-floor flat.2. US on the floor which is at ground level ⇒ He broke into her first-floor apartment at 4:15.

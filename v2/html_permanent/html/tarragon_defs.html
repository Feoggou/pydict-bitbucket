tarragon

(ˈtærəɡən )
Definitions
noun1. an aromatic perennial plant, Artemisia dracunculus, of the Old World, having whitish flowers and small toothed leaves, which are used as seasoning: family Asteraceae (composites)2. the leaves of this plant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Old French targon, from Medieval Latin tarcon, from Arabic tarkhūn, perhaps from Greek drakontion adderwort

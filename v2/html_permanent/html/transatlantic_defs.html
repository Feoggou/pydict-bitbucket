Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Transatlantic flights or signals go across the Atlantic Ocean, usually between the United States and Britain. ⇒ Many transatlantic flights land there. ⇒ ...the first transatlantic radio signal.2. adjective [ADJ n]Transatlantic is used to refer to something that happens, exists, or begins in the United States. [British] ⇒ ...transatlantic fashions.

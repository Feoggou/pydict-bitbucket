Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveIf you are self-taught, you have learned a skill by yourself rather than being taught it by someone else such as a teacher at school. ⇒ Paul is self-taught and became interested in photography just four years ago. ⇒ ...a self-taught musician.

baronet

(ˈbærənɪt , -ˌnɛt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(in Britain) a commoner who holds the lowest hereditary title of honour, ranking below a baron. Abbreviation: Bart., Bt
Word origin
C15: order instituted 1611, from baron + -et

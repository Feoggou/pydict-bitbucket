Dutchman

(ˈdʌtʃmən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -men1. a native, citizen, or inhabitant of the Netherlands2. a piece of wood, metal, etc, used to repair or patch faulty workmanship3. South Africa often derogatory an Afrikaner

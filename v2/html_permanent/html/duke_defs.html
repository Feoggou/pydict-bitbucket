duke

(djuːk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a nobleman of high rank: in the British Isles standing above the other grades of the nobility2. the prince or ruler of a small principality or duchyducal
Word origin
C12: from Old French duc, from Latin dux leader

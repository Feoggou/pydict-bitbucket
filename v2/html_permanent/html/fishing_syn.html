Synonyms of fishing

fishing

noun

  1. angling, trawling
    • Despite the weather, the fishing has been good.

piscatorial

fish

verb

  1. angle, net, cast, trawl
    • He learnt to fish in the River Cam.
  2. look (for), search, delve, ferret, rummage, fossick (Australian & & New Zealand)
    • He fished in his pocket for the key.

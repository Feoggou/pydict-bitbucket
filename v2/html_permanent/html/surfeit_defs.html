surfeit

(ˈsɜːfɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (usually foll by of) an excessive or immoderate amount2. overindulgence, esp in eating or drinking3. disgust, nausea, etc, caused by such overindulgenceverb4. (transitive) to supply or feed excessively; satiate5. (intransitive) archaic to eat, drink, or be supplied to excess6. (intransitive) obsolete to feel uncomfortable as a consequence of overindulgence
Derived forms
surfeiter (ˈsurfeiter) noun
Word origin
C13: from French surfait, from surfaire to overdo, from sur-1 + faire, from Latin facere to do

invigorated

(ɪnˈvɪɡəˌreɪtɪd)
Definitions
adjectivehaving been given vitality and vigour; animated; refreshed ⇒ She seemed invigorated, full of life and energy., ⇒ an invigorated UN with much less bureaucracy

ruction

(ˈrʌkʃən )
Definitions
noun informal1. an uproar; noisy or quarrelsome disturbance2. (plural) a violent and unpleasant row; trouble ⇒ there'll be ructions when she finds out
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: perhaps changed from insurrection

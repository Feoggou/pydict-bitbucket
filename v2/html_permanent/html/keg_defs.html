keg

(kɛɡ )
Definitions
noun1. a small barrel with a capacity of between five and ten gallons2. Britisha. an aluminium container in which beer is transported and storedb. Also called: keg beer beer kept in a keg: it is infused with gas and served under pressure
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: variant of Middle English kag, of Scandinavian origin; related to Old Norse kaggi cask

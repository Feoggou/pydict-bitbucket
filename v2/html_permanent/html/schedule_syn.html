Synonyms of schedule

schedule

noun

  1. plan, programme, agenda, calendar, timetable, itinerary, list of appointments
    • He has been forced to adjust his schedule.
  2. list, catalogue, inventory, syllabus
    • a detailed written schedule

verb

  1. plan, set up, book, programme, arrange, organize, timetable
    • No new talks are scheduled.

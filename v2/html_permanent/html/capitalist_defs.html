capitalist

(ˈkæpɪtəlɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who owns capital, esp capital invested in a business2. politics a supporter of capitalism3. informal, usually derogatory a rich personadjective4. of or relating to capital, capitalists, or capitalism
Derived forms
ˌcapitalistic (ˌcapitalˈistic) adjective

Word forms: capitalists
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveA capitalist country or system supports or is based on the principles of capitalism. ⇒ I'm a strong believer in the capitalist system. ⇒ ...capitalist economic theory.2. countable nounA capitalist is someone who believes in and supports the principles of capitalism. ⇒ ...relations between capitalists and workers.3. countable nounA capitalist is someone who owns a business which they run in order to make a profit for themselves. ⇒ They argue that only private capitalists can remake Poland's economy.

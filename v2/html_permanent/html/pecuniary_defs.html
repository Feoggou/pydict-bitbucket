pecuniary

(pɪˈkjuːnɪərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. consisting of or relating to money2. law (of an offence) involving a monetary penalty

Derived Forms

pecuniarily (peˈcuniarily) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Latin pecūniāris, from pecūnia money

pottle

(ˈpɒtəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. archaic a liquid measure equal to half a gallon2. New Zealand a plastic or cardboard container for foods such as yoghurt, fruit salad, or cottage cheese

Word Origin

C14: potel, from Old French: a small pot1

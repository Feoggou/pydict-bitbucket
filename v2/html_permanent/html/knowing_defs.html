knowing

(ˈnəʊɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. suggesting secret information or knowledge2. wise, shrewd, or clever3. deliberate; intentionalnoun4. See there is no knowing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

knowingly (ˈknowingly) adverbknowingness (ˈknowingness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A knowing gesture or remark is one that shows that you understand something, for example the way that someone is feeling or what they really mean, even though it has not been mentioned directly. ⇒ Ron gave her a knowing smile. ⇒ Dan exchanged a knowing look with Harry.knowingly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ He smiled knowingly.

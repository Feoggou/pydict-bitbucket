Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (intr, adverb) informal1. to become popular or fashionable2. to grasp mentally; understand

catch on

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. phrasal verbIf you catch on to something, you understand it, or realize that it is happening. cotton on [V P + to] ⇒ He got what he could out of me before I caught on to the kind of person he'd turned into. [V P] ⇒ Wait a minute! I'm beginning to catch on.2. phrasal verbIf something catches on, it becomes popular. [V P] ⇒ The idea has been around for ages without catching on. →  catch

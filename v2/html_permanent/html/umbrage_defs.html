umbrage

(ˈʌmbrɪdʒ )
Definitions
noun1. displeasure or resentment; offence (in the phrase give or take umbrage)2. the foliage of trees, considered as providing shade3. rare shadow or shade4. archaic a shadow or semblance
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French umbrage, from Latin umbrāticus relating to shade, from umbra shade, shadow

(læˈtiːnəʊ )
Definitions
noun plural -nosUS an inhabitant of the US who is of Latin American origin

Latina (Laˈtina) (læˈtiːnə) feminine noun

Latino

Powered by COBUILD
(lætiːnoʊ ) also latino for learners of English
Word forms: Latinos
Definitions
1. countable noun [oft N n]A Latino is a citizen of the United States who originally came from Latin America, or whose family originally came from Latin America. [mainly US] ⇒ He was a champion for Latinos and blacks within the educational system. ⇒ ...the city's office of Latino Affairs.2. adjectiveLatino means belonging or relating to Latino people or their culture. ⇒ ...an exhibition of artwork by local Latino artists.

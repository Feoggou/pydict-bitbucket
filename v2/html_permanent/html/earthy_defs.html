earthy

(ˈɜːθɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: earthier or earthiest1. of, composed of, or characteristic of earth2. robust, lusty, or uninhibited3. unrefined, coarse, or crude4. an archaic word for worldly (sense 1)5. electrical engineering on the earthed side of an electrical circuit, but not necessarily with a direct current connection to earth

earthy

Powered by COBUILD
(ɜːʳθi ) for learners of English
Word forms: earthier , earthiest
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as earthy, you mean that they are open and direct, and talk about subjects which other people avoid or feel ashamed about. [approval] ⇒ She is a very physical young woman, earthy, and very intense. ⇒ ...his extremely earthy humour.earthiness uncount noun ⇒ He loved Gerard's peasant earthiness.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something as earthy, you mean it looks, smells, or feels like earth. ⇒ I'm attracted to warm, earthy colours. ⇒ The rooms had the clean earthy smell of wet clay.

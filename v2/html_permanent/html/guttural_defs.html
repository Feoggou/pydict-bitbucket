guttural

(ˈɡʌtərəl )
Definitions
adjective1. anatomy of or relating to the throat2. phonetics pronounced in the throat or the back of the mouth; velar or uvular3. raucousnoun4. phonetics a guttural consonant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gutturally (ˈgutturally) adverbgutturalness (ˈgutturalness), gutturality (ˌgutturˈality) or gutturalism (ˈgutturalism) noun

Word Origin

C16: from New Latin gutturālis concerning the throat, from Latin guttur gullet

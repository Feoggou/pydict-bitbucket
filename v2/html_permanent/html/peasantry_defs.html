peasantry

(ˈpɛzəntrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. peasants as a class2. conduct characteristic of peasants3. the status of a peasant

singular noun [with sing or pl verb]You can refer to all the peasants in a particular country as the peasantry. ⇒ The Russian peasantry stood on the brink of disappearance.

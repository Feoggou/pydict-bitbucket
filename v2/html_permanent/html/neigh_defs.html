neigh

(neɪ )
Definitions
noun1. the high-pitched cry of a horse; whinnyverb2. (intransitive) to make a neigh or a similar noise3. (transitive) to utter with a sound like a neigh
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English hnǣgan; related to Old Saxon hnēgian

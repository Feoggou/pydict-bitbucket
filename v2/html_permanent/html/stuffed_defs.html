stuffed

(stʌft )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. filled with something, esp (of poultry and other food) filled with stuffing2. (foll by up) (of the nasal passages) blocked with mucus3. See get stuffed!
Example sentences containing 'stuffed'
The girl had money screwed up in balls and stuffed in her mouth which was taped. Robert Wilson INSTRUMENTS OF DARKNESSThe sharp edges of the jacket stuffed with casino chips cut into her ribs. Robert Wilson THE COMPANY OF STRANGERS

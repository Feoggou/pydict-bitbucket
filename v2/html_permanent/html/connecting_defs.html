connecting

(kəˈnɛktɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivejoined or linked; linking two things ⇒ Your father and I had connecting rooms., ⇒ a bedroom with connecting bathroom, ⇒ She didn't look back as she walked through the connecting door to her own room.

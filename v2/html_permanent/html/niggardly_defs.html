niggardly

(ˈnɪɡədlɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. stingy or ungenerous2. meagre ⇒ a niggardly salaryadverb3. stingily; grudgingly
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as niggardly, you are criticizing them because they do not give or provide much of something. [disapproval] ⇒ Officials say the E.U., which is supposed to provide most of the food needs, is being particularly niggardly. ⇒ ...a niggardly supply of hot water.

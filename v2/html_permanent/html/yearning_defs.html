yearning

(ˈjɜːnɪŋ )
Definitions
nounan intense or overpowering longing, desire, or need; craving
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

yearningly (ˈyearningly) adverb

Definitions
variable noun [N to-inf]A yearning for something is a very strong desire for it. [+ for] ⇒ He spoke of his yearning for another child. ⇒ He always had a yearning to be a schoolteacher.

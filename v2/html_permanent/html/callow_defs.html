callow

(ˈkæləʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. lacking experience of life; immature2. rare (of a young bird) unfledged and usually lacking feathers
Derived forms
callowness (ˈcallowness) noun
Word origin
Old English calu; related to Old High German kalo, Old Slavonic golú bare, naked, Lithuanian galva head, Latin calvus bald

temptation

(tɛmpˈteɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of tempting or the state of being tempted2. a person or thing that tempts

temptation

Powered by COBUILD
(tempteɪʃən ) for learners of English
Word forms: temptations
Definitions
variable nounIf you feel you want to do something or have something, even though you know you really should avoid it, you can refer to this feeling as temptation. You can also refer to the thing you want to do or have as a temptation. ⇒ Will they be able to resist the temptation to buy? ⇒ ...the many temptations to which you will be exposed.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

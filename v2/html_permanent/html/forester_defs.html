forester

(ˈfɒrɪstə )
Definitions
noun1. a person skilled in forestry or in charge of a forest2. any of various Old World moths of the genus Ino, characterized by brilliant metallic green wings: family Zygaenidae3. a person or animal that lives in a forest4. (capital) a member of the Ancient Order of Foresters, a friendly society
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

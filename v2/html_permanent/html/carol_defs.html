carol

(ˈkærəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a joyful hymn or religious song, esp one (a Christmas carol) celebrating the birth of Christ2. archaic an old English circular danceverb -ols, -olling, -olled or US -ols, -oling or -oled3. (intransitive) to sing carols at Christmas4. to sing (something) in a joyful manner
Derived forms
caroler (ˈcaroler) or caroller (ˈcaroller) nouncaroling (ˈcaroling) or carolling (ˈcarolling) noun
Word origin
C13: from Old French, of uncertain origin

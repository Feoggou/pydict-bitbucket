projected

(prəˈdʒɛktɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. proposed or planned ⇒ There were demonstrations against the projected visit by the Foreign Minister.2. predicted or forecast ⇒ the projected rate of economic growth, ⇒ a projected deficit of $1.5 million

dandy

Powered by COBUILD
(dændi )
Word forms: dandies
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA dandy is a man who thinks a great deal about his appearance and always dresses in smart clothes. ⇒ He was handsome and a dandy.2. adjectiveIf you say that something is dandy, you mean it is good or just right. [US, informal, old-fashioned] ⇒ There's a zoo round here? That's dandy for my kids.
Example sentences containing 'dandy'
`Oh sure, fine and dandy ," Pete grumbled, wriggling to get the stiffness out of his back. Arthur, Robert THREE IN ONEIf the thought of the Geary women returning here comforted him, then that was fine and dandy. Clive Barker GALILEE

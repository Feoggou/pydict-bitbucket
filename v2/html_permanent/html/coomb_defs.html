coomb

, combe, coombe or comb (kuːm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. mainly Southern England a short valley or deep hollow, esp in chalk areas2. mainly Northern England another name for cirque
Word origin
Old English cumb (in place names), probably of Celtic origin; compare Old French combe small valley and Welsh cwm valley

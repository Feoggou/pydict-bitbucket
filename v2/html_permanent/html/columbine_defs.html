columbine1

(ˈkɒləmˌbaɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany plant of the ranunculaceous genus Aquilegia, having purple, blue, yellow, or red flowers with five spurred petals. Also called: aquilegia
Word origin
C13: from Medieval Latin columbīna herba dovelike plant, from Latin columbīnus dovelike, from the resemblance of the flower to a group of doves

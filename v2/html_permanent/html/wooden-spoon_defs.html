Word forms: wooden spoons
Definitions
1. countable nounA wooden spoon is a spoon that is used for stirring sauces and for mixing ingredients in cooking. It is made of wood and has a long handle.2. countable nounIf someone gets the wooden spoon, they come last in a race or competition. [British] [+ in] ⇒ Jarvis took the wooden spoon in the first tournament.

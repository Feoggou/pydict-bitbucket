Definitions
1. countable nounA fishmonger is a shopkeeper who sells fish. [mainly British]2. countable nounThe fishmonger or the fishmonger's is a shop where fish is sold. [mainly British] ⇒ Purchase your oysters from a reputable fishmonger.

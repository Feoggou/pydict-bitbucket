Iranian

(ɪˈreɪnɪən )
Definitions
noun1. a native, citizen, or inhabitant of Iran2. a branch of the Indo-European family of languages, divided into West Iranian (including Old Persian, Pahlavi, modern Persian, Kurdish, Baluchi, and Tajik) and East Iranian (including Avestan, Sogdian, Pashto, and Ossetic)3. Also: Persian, Farsi the modern Persian languageadjective4. relating to, denoting, or characteristic of Iran, its inhabitants, or their language; Persian5. belonging to or relating to the Iranian branch of Indo-European
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

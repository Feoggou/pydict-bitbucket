banquet

(ˈbæŋkwɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a lavish and sumptuous meal; feast2. a ceremonial meal for many people, often followed by speechesverb -quets, -queting or -queted3. (intransitive) to hold or take part in a banquet4. (transitive) to entertain or honour (a person) with a banquet
Derived forms
banqueter (ˈbanqueter) noun
Word origin
C15: from Old French, from Italian banchetto, from banco a table, of Germanic origin; see bank1

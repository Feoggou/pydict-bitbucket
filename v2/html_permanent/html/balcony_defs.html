balcony

(ˈbælkənɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -nies1. a platform projecting from the wall of a building with a balustrade or railing along its outer edge, often with access from a door or window2. a gallery in a theatre or auditorium, above the dress circle3. US and Canadian any circle or gallery in a theatre or auditorium including the dress circle
Derived forms
balconied (ˈbalconied) adjective
Word origin
C17: from Italian balcone, probably from Old High German balko beam; see balk

roundelay

(ˈraʊndɪˌleɪ )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: roundel a slow medieval dance performed in a circle2. a song in which a line or phrase is repeated as a refrain
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Old French rondelet a little rondel, from rondel; also influenced by lay4

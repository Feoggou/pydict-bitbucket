verbatim

Powered by COBUILD
(vəʳbeɪtɪm ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [ADV after v]If you repeat something verbatim, you use exactly the same words as were used originally. ⇒ The President's speeches are regularly reproduced verbatim in the state-run newspapers.Verbatim is also an adjective. ⇒ I was treated to a verbatim report of every conversation she's taken part in over the past week.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

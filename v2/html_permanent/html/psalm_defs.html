psalm

(sɑːm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (often capital) any of the 150 sacred songs, lyric poems, and prayers that together constitute a book (Psalms) of the Old Testament2. a musical setting of one of these poems3. any sacred song or hymn

Derived Forms

psalmic (ˈpsalmic) adjective

Word Origin

Old English, from Late Latin psalmus, from Greek psalmos song accompanied on the harp, from psallein to play (the harp)

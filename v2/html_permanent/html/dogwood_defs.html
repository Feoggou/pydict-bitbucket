dogwood

(ˈdɒɡˌwʊd ) or dagwood (ˈdæɡˌwʊd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany of various cornaceous trees or shrubs of the genus Cornus, esp C. sanguinea, a European shrub with clusters of small white flowers and black berries: the shoots are red in winter

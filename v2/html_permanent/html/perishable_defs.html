perishable

(ˈpɛrɪʃəbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. liable to rot or withernoun2. (often plural) a perishable article, esp food

Derived Forms

ˌperishability (ˌperishaˈbility) or perishableness (ˈperishableness) nounperishably (ˈperishably) adverb

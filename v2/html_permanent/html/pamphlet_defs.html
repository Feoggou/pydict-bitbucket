pamphlet

(ˈpæmflɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a brief publication generally having a paper cover; booklet2. a brief treatise, often on a subject of current interest, published in pamphlet form

Word Origin

C14 pamflet, from Anglo-Latin panfletus, from Medieval Latin Pamphilus title of a popular 12th-century amatory poem from Greek Pamphilos masculine proper name

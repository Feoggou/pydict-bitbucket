oscillation

(ˌɒsɪˈleɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. physics, statisticsa. regular fluctuation in value, position, or state about a mean value, such as the variation in an alternating current or the regular swinging of a pendulumb. a single cycle of such a fluctuation2. the act or process of oscillating

Derived Forms

oscillatory (ˈɒsɪlətərɪ , -trɪ) adjective

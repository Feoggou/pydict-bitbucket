unfledged

(ʌnˈflɛdʒd )
Definitions
adjective1. (of a young bird) not having developed adult feathers2. immature and undeveloped
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'unfledged'
A magpie flew into sunlit foliage and started to drag out and eat the unfledged babies in a blackbird's nest. Hilton, John Buxton DISPLACED PERSON

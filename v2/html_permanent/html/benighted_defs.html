(bɪˈnaɪtɪd )
Definitions

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]If you describe people or the place where they live as benighted, you think they are unfortunate or do not know anything. [literary, disapproval] ⇒ Famine hit that benighted country once more.

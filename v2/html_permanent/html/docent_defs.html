docent

(ˈdəʊsənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a voluntary worker who acts as a guide in a museum, art gallery, etc2. (dəʊˈsɛnt, German doˈtsɛnt) (in the US) a lecturer in some colleges or universities
Derived forms
docentˌship (ˈdocentˌship) noun
Word origin
C19: from German Dozent, from Latin docēns from docēre to teach
Example sentences containing 'docent'
"You have to spark it up with Tabasco, otherwise it just tastes like lawn," my docent instructed me. Katherine Mosb THE SEASON OF LILLIAN DAWES

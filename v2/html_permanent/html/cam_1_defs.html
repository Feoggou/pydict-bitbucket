Definitions
COBUILD Key Words for Automotive Engineering
noun(Automotive engineering: Vehicle components, Engine, transmission, and exhaust) A cam is a shaped part in machinery such as an engine that rotates in order to cause another part to move or lift. ⇒ A circular cam is fitted off-center on the driving shaft and produces an oscillating movement in the follower. ⇒ Cams are used to change rotary motion into either reciprocating motion or oscillating motion. ⇒ Displacing 3.6 liters, the engine has twin overhead cams and four valves per cylinder.

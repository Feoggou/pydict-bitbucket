lawless

(ˈlɔːlɪs )
Definitions
adjective1. without law2. disobedient to the law3. contrary to or heedless of the law4. uncontrolled; unbridled ⇒ lawless rage
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

lawless

Powered by COBUILD
(lɔːləs ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Lawless actions break the law, especially in a wild and violent way. ⇒ The government recognised there were problems in urban areas but these could never be an excuse for lawless behaviour.lawlessness uncount noun ⇒ Lawlessness is a major problem.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A lawless place or time is one where people do not respect the law. ⇒ ...lawless inner-city streets plagued by muggings, thefts, assaults and even murder. ⇒ ...people struggling to restore moral values in an increasingly lawless and godless age.

akin

(əˈkɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective (postpositive)1. related by blood; of the same kin2. (often foll by to) having similar characteristics, properties, etc

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf one thing is akin to another, it is similar to it in some way. [formal] [+ to] ⇒ Listening to his life story is akin to reading a good adventure novel.

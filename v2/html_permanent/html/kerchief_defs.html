kerchief

(ˈkɜːtʃɪf )
Definitions
nouna piece of cloth worn tied over the head or around the neck
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

kerchiefed (ˈkerchiefed) adjective

Word Origin

C13: from Old French cuevrechef, from covrir to cover + chef head; see chief

conclude

verb

  1. decide, judge, establish, suppose, determine, assume, gather, reckon (informal), work out, infer, deduce, surmise • We concluded that he was telling the truth.
  2. come to an end, end, close, finish, wind up, draw to a close • The evening concluded with dinner and speeches. OPPOSITES:  start, open, begin, extend, commence
  3. bring to an end, end, close, finish, complete, wind up, terminate, round off • They concluded their annual summit meeting today. OPPOSITES:  start, open, begin, extend, initiate, commence, protract
  4. accomplish, effect, settle, bring about, fix, carry out, resolve, clinch, pull off, bring off (informal) • If the clubs cannot conclude a deal, an independent tribunal will decide.

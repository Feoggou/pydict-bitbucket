naturalist

(ˈnætʃrəlɪst , -tʃərəl-)
Definitions
noun1. a person who is expert or interested in botany or zoology, esp in the field2. a person who advocates or practises naturalism, esp in art or literature
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Ramadan

, Rhamadhan (ˌræməˈdɑːn )Ramazan (ˌræməˈzɑːn )
Definitions
noun1. the ninth month of the Muslim year, lasting 30 days, during which strict fasting is observed from sunrise to sunset2. the fast itself
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Arabic, literally: the hot month, from ramad dryness

Definitions
uncount nounRamadan is the ninth month of the Muslim year, when Muslims do not eat between the rising and setting of the sun. During Ramadan, Muslims celebrate the fact that it was in this month that God first revealed the words of the Quran to Mohammed.

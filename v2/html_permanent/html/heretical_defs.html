heretical

Powered by COBUILD
(hɪretɪkəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveA belief or action that is heretical is one that most people think is wrong because it disagrees with beliefs that are generally accepted. ⇒ I made the then heretical suggestion that it might be cheaper to design new machines.2. adjectiveA belief or action that is heretical is one that seriously disagrees with the principles of a particular religion. ⇒ The Church regards spirit mediums and people claiming to speak to the dead as heretical.

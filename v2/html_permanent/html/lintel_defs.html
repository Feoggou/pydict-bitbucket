lintel

(ˈlɪntəl )
Definitions
nouna horizontal beam, as over a door or window
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: via Old French probably from Late Latin līmitāris (unattested) of the boundary, influenced in meaning by līminaris of the threshold

Definitions
countable nounA lintel is a piece of stone or wood over a door or window which supports the bricks above the door or window.

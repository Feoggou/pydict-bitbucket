parted

(ˈpɑːtɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. botany divided almost to the base ⇒ parted leaves2. heraldry showing two coats of arms divided by a vertical central line

Still crouching with her head down, the Countess squinted towards it through parted fingers.

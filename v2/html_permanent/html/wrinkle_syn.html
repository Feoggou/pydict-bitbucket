Synonyms of wrinkle 1

wrinkle1

noun

  1. line, fold, crease, furrow, pucker, crow's-foot, corrugation
    • His face was covered with wrinkles.
  2. crease, gather, fold, crumple, furrow, rumple, pucker, crinkle, corrugation
    • He noticed a wrinkle in the material.

verb

  1. crease, line, gather, fold, crumple, ruck, furrow, rumple, pucker, crinkle, corrugate
    • I wrinkled the velvet.
    • The skin around her eyes had begun to wrinkle.
    OPPOSITES:  level, press, iron, smooth, unfold, flatten, straighten, even out

wrinkle2

noun

  1. trick, plan, idea, scheme, tip, device, stunt, dodge, ploy, gimmick, wheeze (British, slang), ruse

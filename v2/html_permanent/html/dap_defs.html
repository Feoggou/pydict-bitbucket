dap1

(dæp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verbWord forms: daps, dapping or dapped (intransitive)1. angling to fish with a natural or artificial fly on a floss silk line so that the wind makes the fly bob on and off the surface of the water2. (as of a bird) to dip lightly into water3. to bounce or cause to bounce
Word origin
C17: of imitative origin

lengths

(lɛŋθ)
Definitions
plural noun1. the extremes or limits of action ⇒ Had she known the lengths he was prepared to go to, she would have been appalled., ⇒ I'll go to any lengths to protect her from the truth., ⇒ To what lengths would she go to achieve her ends?2. See to go to great lengths to do sth

She hadn't gone to such lengths to stop a development she was philosophically opposed to, only to roll over at the end. Stuart Harrison LOST SUMMERBut then the car reappeared, now only a few car lengths back. Tapply, William G FOLLOW THE SHARKSTo protect the security of what they'd done, they would go to any lengths.

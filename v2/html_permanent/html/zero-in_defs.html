Word Frequency

zero in

Definitions
verb (adverb)1. (often foll by on) to bring (a weapon) to bear (on a target), as while firing repeatedly2. (intransitive; foll by on) informal to bring one's attention to bear (on a problem, etc)3. (intransitive; foll by on) informal to converge (upon) ⇒ the police zeroed in on the site of the crime

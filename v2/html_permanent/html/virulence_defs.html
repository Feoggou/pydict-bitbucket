virulence

(ˈvɪrʊləns ) or virulency
Definitions
noun1. the quality of being virulent2. the capacity of a microorganism for causing disease

virulence

Powered by COBUILD
(vɪrjʊləns ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounVirulence is great bitterness and hostility. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ The virulence of the café owner's anger had appalled her.2. uncount nounThe virulence of a disease or poison is its ability to harm or kill people or animals. ⇒ Medical authorities were baffled, both as to its causes and its virulence.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

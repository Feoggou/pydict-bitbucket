Word forms: readjustments
Definitions
1. variable nounReadjustment is the process of adapting to a new situation, usually one that you have been in before. ⇒ The next few weeks will be a period of readjustment. ⇒ The readjustment to peace and home is slow and painful.2. variable nounA readjustment of something is a change that you make to it so that it is more effective or appropriate. [+ of] ⇒ The organization denies that it is seeking any readjustment of state borders. ⇒ ...the effects of economic readjustment.

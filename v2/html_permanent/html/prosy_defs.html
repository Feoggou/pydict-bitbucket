prosy

(ˈprəʊzɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: prosier or prosiest1. of the nature of or similar to prose2. dull, tedious, or long-winded

Derived Forms

prosily (ˈprosily) adverbprosiness (ˈprosiness) noun

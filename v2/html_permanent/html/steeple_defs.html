steeple

(ˈstiːpəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a tall ornamental tower that forms the superstructure of a church, temple, etc2. such a tower with the spire above it3. any spire or pointed structure

Derived Forms

steepled (ˈsteepled) adjective

Word Origin

Old English stēpel; see steep1

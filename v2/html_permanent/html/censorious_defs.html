Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as censorious, you do not like the way they strongly disapprove of and criticize someone else's behaviour. [formal, disapproval] ⇒ Despite strong principles he was never censorious.

(ʌndʒʌst )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe an action, system, or law as unjust, you think that it treats a person or group badly in a way that they do not deserve. ⇒ The attack on Charles was deeply unjust. ⇒ He spent 25 years campaigning against racist and unjust immigration laws.unjustly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ She was unjustly accused of stealing money and then given the sack.

Schaffhausen

(German ʃaːfˈhauzən)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small canton of N Switzerland. Pop: 73 900 (2002 est). Area: 298 sq km (115 sq miles)2. a town in N Switzerland, capital of Schaffhausen canton, on the Rhine. Pop: 33 628 (2000)

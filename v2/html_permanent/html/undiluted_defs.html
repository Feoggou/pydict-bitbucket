undiluted

(ˌʌndaɪˈluːtɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. not diluted with water or any other liquid ⇒ undiluted fruit juice2. not moderated or qualified in any way ⇒ expressing undiluted pleasure

undiluted for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌndaɪluːtɪd )
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone's feelings or characteristics as undiluted, you are emphasizing that they are very strong and not mixed with any other feeling or quality. ⇒ I will look back at this one with undiluted pleasure. ⇒ The report had not been received with undiluted enthusiasm. ⇒ Her Irish accent, after thirty-odd years in London, is undiluted.2. adjectiveA liquid that is undiluted has not been made weak by mixing it with water.

deliberative

(dɪˈlɪbərətɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. involved in, organized for, or having the function of deliberating ⇒ a deliberative assembly2. characterized by or resulting from deliberation ⇒ a deliberative conclusion
Derived forms
deliberatively (deˈliberatively) adverbdeliberativeness (deˈliberativeness) noun

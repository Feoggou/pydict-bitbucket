grindstone

(ˈɡraɪndˌstəʊn )
Definitions
noun1. a. a machine having a circular block of stone or composite abrasive rotated for sharpening tools or grinding metalb. the stone used in this machinec. any stone used for sharpening; whetstone2. another name for millstone3. See keep one's nose to the grindstone

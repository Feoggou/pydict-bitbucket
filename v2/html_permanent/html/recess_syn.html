Synonyms of recess

recess

noun

  1. break, rest, holiday, closure, interval, vacation, respite, intermission, cessation of business, schoolie (Australian)
    • Parliament returns to work today after its summer recess.
  2. alcove, corner, bay, depression, hollow, niche, cavity, nook, oriel, indentation
    • a discreet recess next to a fireplace
  3. (often plural) depths, reaches, heart, retreats, bowels, innards (informal), secret places, innermost parts, penetralia
    • He emerged from the dark recesses of the garage.
  4. (often plural) depths
    • the inner recesses of the soul

verb

  1. adjourn, break, stop, take a break, take a recess, suspend proceedings
    • The hearings have now recessed for dinner.

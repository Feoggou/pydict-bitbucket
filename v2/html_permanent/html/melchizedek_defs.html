Melchizedek

(mɛlˈkɪzəˌdɛk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounOld Testament the priest-king of Salem who blessed Abraham (Genesis 14:18–19) and was taken as a prototype of Christ's priesthood (Hebrews 7). Douay spelling: Melchisedech

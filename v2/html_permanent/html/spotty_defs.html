spotty

(ˈspɒtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -tier or -tiest1. abounding in or characterized by spots or marks, esp on the skin ⇒ a spotty face2. not consistent or uniform; irregular or uneven, often in quality

Derived Forms

spottily (ˈspottily) adverbspottiness (ˈspottiness) noun

(spɒti )
Word forms: spottier , spottiest
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is spotty has spots on their face. ⇒ She was rather fat, and her complexion was muddy and spotty.2. graded adjectiveSomething that is spotty does not stay the same but is sometimes good and sometimes bad. [US] patchy ⇒ He quit in 1981–had a spotty political career. ⇒ His attendance record was spotty.

Westminster

(ˈwɛstˌmɪnstə )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: City of Westminster a borough of Greater London, on the River Thames: contains the Houses of Parliament, Westminster Abbey, and Buckingham Palace. Pop: 222 000 (2003 est). Area: 22 sq km (8 sq miles)2. the Houses of Parliament at Westminster

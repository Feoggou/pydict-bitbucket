chine1

(tʃaɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the backbone2. the backbone of an animal with adjoining meat, cut for cooking3. a ridge or crest of land4. (in some boats) a corner-like intersection where the bottom meets the sideverb5. (transitive) to cut (meat) along or across the backbone
Word origin
C14: from Old French eschine, of Germanic origin; compare Old High German scina needle, shinbone; see shin1

concord

(ˈkɒnkɔːd , ˈkɒŋ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. agreement or harmony between people or nations; amity2. a treaty establishing peaceful relations between nations3. agreement or harmony between things, ideas, etc4. music a combination of musical notes, esp one containing a series of consonant intervals. Compare discord (sense 3)5. grammar another word for agreement (sense 6)
Word origin
C13: from Old French concorde, from Latin concordia, from concors of the same mind, harmonious, from com- same + cor heart

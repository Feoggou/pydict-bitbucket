temporary

(ˈtɛmpərərɪ , ˈtɛmprərɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. not permanent; provisional ⇒ temporary accommodation2. lasting only a short time; transitory ⇒ temporary relief from painnoun plural -raries3. a person, esp a secretary or other office worker, employed on a temporary basis. Often shortened to: temp
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

temporarily (ˈtemporarily) adverbtemporariness (ˈtemporariness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin temporārius, from tempus time

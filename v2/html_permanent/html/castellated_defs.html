castellated

(ˈkæstɪˌleɪtɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having turrets and battlements, like a castle2. having indentations similar to battlements ⇒ a castellated nut, ⇒ a castellated filament
Derived forms
ˌcastellation (ˌcastelˈlation) noun
Word origin
C17: from Medieval Latin castellātus, from castellāre to fortify as a castle

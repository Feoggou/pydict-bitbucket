Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is wrapped up in a particular person or thing, they spend nearly all their time thinking about them, so that they forget about other things which may be important. [+ in] ⇒ He's too serious and dedicated, wrapped up in his career. ⇒ New mothers can get very wrapped up in their baby without realising it. [Also + with]

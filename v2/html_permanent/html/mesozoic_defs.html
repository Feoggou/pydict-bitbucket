Mesozoic

(ˌmɛsəʊˈzəʊɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, denoting, or relating to an era of geological time that began 250 000 000 years ago with the Triassic period and lasted about 185 000 000 years until the end of the Cretaceous periodnoun2. See the Mesozoic

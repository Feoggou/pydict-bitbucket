(taɪmɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounTiming is the skill or action of judging the right moment in a situation or activity at which to do something. ⇒ His photo is a wonderful happy moment caught with perfect timing. ⇒ If your timing is right, you may be fortunate enough to stumble across a village fiesta.2. uncount nounTiming is used to refer to the time at which something happens or is planned to happen, or to the length of time that something takes. [+ of] ⇒ The timing of the minister's visit, however, could somewhat detract from the goodwill it's supposed to generate.3. → see also time

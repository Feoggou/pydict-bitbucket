drying-up

(ˌdraɪɪŋˈʌp)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. See to do the drying-up2. the loss of water (in a lake, river, sea, etc) ⇒ the drying up of the Aral Sea3. the stopping of something such as a supply of something ⇒ the drying up of housing subsidies, ⇒ They have also been hurt by the drying-up of foreign credit.

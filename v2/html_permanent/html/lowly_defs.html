lowly

(ˈləʊlɪ ) rare
Definitions
adjective -lier or -liest1. humble or low in position, rank, status, etc2. full of humility; meek3. simple, unpretentious, or plainadverb4. in a low or lowly manner
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

lowly

Powered by COBUILD
(loʊli ) for learners of English
Word forms: lowlier , lowliest
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as lowly, you mean that they are low in rank, status, or importance. ⇒ ...lowly bureaucrats pretending to be senators. ⇒ He was irked by his lowly status.

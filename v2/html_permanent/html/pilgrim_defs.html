pilgrim

(ˈpɪlɡrɪm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who undertakes a journey to a sacred place as an act of religious devotion2. any wayfarer

Word Origin

C12: from Provençal pelegrin, from Latin peregrīnus foreign, from per through + ager field, land; see peregrine

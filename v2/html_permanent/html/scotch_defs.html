scotch1

(skɒtʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to put an end to; crush ⇒ bad weather scotched our plans2. archaic to injure so as to render harmless3. obsolete to cut or scorenoun4. archaic a gash; scratch5. a line marked down, as for hopscotch

Word Origin

C15: of obscure origin

Bode plot

Powered by COBUILD
(boʊd plɒt)
Word forms: (regular plural) Bode plots
Definitions
COBUILD Key Words for Electrical Engineering
noun(Electrical engineering: Instrumentation, measuring, and testing) A Bode plot is the graph of amplitude (in decibels) and phase against frequency (in logarithmic format). The Bode plot is named for US engineer Hendrik Bode (1905-1982). ⇒ The straight line approximation of a Bode plot is easy to draw and gives reasonably accurate results. ⇒ The Bode plot shows that, in this case, high frequencies will not pass through the filter. ⇒ A Bode plot is the graph of amplitude (in decibels) and phase against frequency (in logarithmic format).

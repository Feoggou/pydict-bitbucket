toddle

(ˈtɒdəl )
Definitions
verb (intransitive)1. to walk with short unsteady steps, as a child does when learning to walk2. (foll by off) humorous to depart3. (foll by round, over, etc) humorous to stroll; amblenoun4. the act or an instance of toddling
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16 (Scottish and northern English): of obscure origin

goodly

(ˈɡʊdlɪ )
Definitions
adjective -lier or -liest1. considerable ⇒ a goodly amount of money2. obsolete attractive, pleasing, or fine ⇒ a goodly man
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

goodliness (ˈgoodliness) noun

goodly

Powered by COBUILD
(gʊdli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]A goodly amount or part of something is a fairly large amount or part of it, often more than was expected. [formal] ⇒ The Central Intelligence Agency employed a goodly number of expert professionals in this particular field. ⇒ Laski spent a goodly part of his lecturing life in American universities.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

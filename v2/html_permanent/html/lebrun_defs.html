Lebrun

(French ləbrœ̃)
Definitions
noun1.  Albert (albɛr). 1871–1950, French statesman; president (1932–40)2.  Also spelled Le Brun. Charles (ʃarl). 1619–90, French historical painter. He was court painter to Louis XIV and executed much of the decoration of the palace of Versailles

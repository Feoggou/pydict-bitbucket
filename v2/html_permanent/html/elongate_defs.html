elongate

(ˈiːlɒŋɡeɪt )
Definitions
verb1. to make or become longer; stretchadjective2. long and narrow; slender ⇒ elongate leaves3. lengthened or tapered
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Late Latin ēlongāre to keep at a distance, from ē- away + Latin longē (adv) far, but also later: to lengthen, as if from ē- + Latin longus (adj) long

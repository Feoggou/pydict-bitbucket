defuse

or sometimes US defuze (diːˈfjuːz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to remove the triggering device of (a bomb, etc)2. to remove the cause of tension from (a crisis, etc)Avoid confusion with diffuse

defuse

Powered by COBUILD
(diːfjuːz )
Word forms: defuses , defusing , defused
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. verbIf you defuse a dangerous or tense situation, you calm it. [V n] ⇒ The organization helped defuse potentially violent situations. [V n] ⇒ Officials will hold four days of talks aimed at defusing tensions over trade.2. verbIf someone defuses a bomb, they remove the fuse so that it cannot explode. [V n] ⇒ Police have defused a bomb found in a building in London.

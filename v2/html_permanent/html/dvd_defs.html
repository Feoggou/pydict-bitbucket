Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA DVD is a disc on which a film or music is recorded. DVD discs are similar to compact discs but hold a lot more information. DVD is an abbreviation for 'digital video disc' or 'digital versatile disc'. ⇒ ...a DVD player.

series

noun

  1. sequence, course, chain, succession, run, set, line, order, train, arrangement, string, progression • a series of explosions
  2. drama, serial, soap (informal), sitcom (informal), soap opera, soapie or soapy (Australian, slang), situation comedy, set of programmes • Channel 4's `GBH' won best drama series.

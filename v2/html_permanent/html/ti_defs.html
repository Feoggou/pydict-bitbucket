nounWord forms: plural tis1. a woody palmlike agave plant, Cordyline terminalis, of the East Indies, having white, mauve, or reddish flowers. The sword-shaped leaves are used for garments, fodder, thatch, etc, and the root for food and liquor2. a similar and related plant, Cordyline australis, of New Zealand

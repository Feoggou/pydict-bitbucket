(dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃənz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounformal discussion and debate, as of a committee, jury, etc ⇒ The deliberations at Versailles led to a peace agreement., ⇒  … the outcome of the deliberations., ⇒ Their deliberations were rather inconclusive.

chanter

(ˈtʃɑːntə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who chants2. the pipe on a set of bagpipes that is provided with finger holes and on which the melody is played
Example sentences containing 'chanter'
Then the solemn face of the priest filled their sight: steadily he married them, while the chanter beside him sang the responses. Harvey, John COUP D'ETAT

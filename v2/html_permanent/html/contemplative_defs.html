contemplative

(ˈkɒntɛmˌpleɪtɪv , -təm-, kənˈtɛmplə-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. denoting, concerned with, or inclined to contemplation; meditativenoun2. a person dedicated to religious contemplation or to a way of life conducive to this
Derived forms

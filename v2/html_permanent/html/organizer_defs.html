organizer

or organiser (ˈɔːɡəˌnaɪzə )
Definitions
noun1. a person who organizes or is capable of organizing2. a container with a number of compartments for storage ⇒ hanging organizers to keep your clothes smart3. embryology any part of an embryo or any substance produced by it that induces specialization of undifferentiated cells

exports

(ˈɛkspɔːts)
Definitions
plural noungoods ( visible exports) or services ( invisible exports) sold to a foreign country or countries ⇒ He did this to promote American exports., ⇒ Coffee is Brazil's best-known export., ⇒ Ghana's main export is cocoa.

ingenue

or ingénue (ˌænʒeɪˈnjuː)
Definitions
nounformal an innocent and unsophisticated young woman ⇒ This was no fragile ingenue. Caroline knew exactly what she was doing.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

ingénue - French

(ˌænʒeɪˈnjuː , ɛ̃ʒeny)
Definitions
nounan artless, innocent, or inexperienced girl or young woman
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from French, feminine of ingénu ingenuous

ingenue

Powered by COBUILD
(ænʒeɪnjuː ) also ingénue for learners of English
Word forms: ingenues
Definitions
countable noun [usu sing]An ingenue is a young, innocent girl in a play or film, or an actress who plays the part of young, innocent girls. [formal] ⇒ I don't want any more ingenue roles.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

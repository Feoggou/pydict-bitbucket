moron

(ˈmɔːrɒn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. slang a foolish or stupid person2. old-fashioned, offensive a person having an intelligence quotient of between 50 and 70, able to work under supervision

Derived Forms

moronic (mɒˈrɒnɪk ) adjectivemoronically (moˈronically) adverbmoronism (ˈmoronism) or moronity (moˈronity) noun

Word Origin

C20: from Greek mōros foolish

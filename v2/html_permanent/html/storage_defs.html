storage

(ˈstɔːrɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of storing or the state of being stored2. space or area reserved for storing3. a charge made for storing4. computinga. the act or process of storing information in a computer memory or on a magnetic tape, disk, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ a storage device, ⇒ storage capacity

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounIf you refer to the storage of something, you mean that it is kept in a special place until it is needed. [+ of] ⇒ ...the storage of toxic waste. ⇒ Some of the space will at first be used for storage. ⇒ The collection has been in storage for decades.2. uncount nounStorage is the process of storing data in a computer. [+ of] ⇒ His task is to ensure the fair use and storage of personal information held on computer. ⇒ ...data-storage devices.3. → see also cold storage

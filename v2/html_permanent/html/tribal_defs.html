tribal

(ˈtraɪbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of or denoting a tribe or tribes ⇒ tribal chiefs in northern Yemen2. displaying loyalty to a tribe, group, or tribal values ⇒ the tribal loyalties of Labour MPs
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tribally (ˈtribally) adverb

Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]Tribal is used to describe things relating to or belonging to tribes and the way that they are organized. ⇒ They would go back to their tribal lands. ⇒ ...their rich heritage of ancient tribal customs.

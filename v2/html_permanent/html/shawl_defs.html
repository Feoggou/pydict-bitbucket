shawl

(ʃɔːl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna piece of fabric or knitted or crocheted material worn around the shoulders by women or wrapped around a baby

Word Origin

C17: from Persian shāl

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA shawl is a large piece of woollen cloth which a woman wears over her shoulders or head, or which is wrapped around a baby to keep it warm.

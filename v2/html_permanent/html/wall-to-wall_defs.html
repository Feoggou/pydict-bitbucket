wall-to-wall

Definitions
adjective1. (of carpeting) completely covering a floor2. informal as far as the eye can see; widespread ⇒ wall-to-wall sales in the high-street shops
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

wall-to-wall for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]A wall-to-wall carpet covers the floor of a room completely.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]You can use wall-to-wall to describe something that fills or seems to fill all the available space. ⇒ ...television's wall-to-wall soccer coverage. ⇒ There were wall-to-wall people, all invited guests and celebrities.

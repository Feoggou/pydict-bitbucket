chaise

(ʃeɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a light open horse-drawn carriage, esp one with two wheels designed for two passengers2. short for post chaise, chaise longue3. a gold coin first issued in France in the 14th century, depicting the king seated on a throne
Word origin
C18: from French, variant of Old French chaiere chair

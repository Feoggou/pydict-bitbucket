malapert

(ˈmæləˌpɜːt ) archaic or literary
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. saucy or impudentnoun2. a saucy or impudent person

Derived Forms

malaˌpertly (ˈmalaˌpertly) adverbmalaˌpertness (ˈmalaˌpertness) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Old French: unskilful (see mal-, expert); meaning in English influenced by apert frank, from Latin apertus open

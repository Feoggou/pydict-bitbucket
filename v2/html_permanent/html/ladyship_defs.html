Ladyship

(ˈleɪdɪʃɪp )
Definitions
noun(preceded by your or her) a title used to address or refer to any peeress except a duchess

Ladyship

Powered by COBUILD
(leɪdiʃɪp ) for learners of English
Word forms: Ladyships
Definitions
countable noun & proper nounIn Britain, you use the expressions Your Ladyship, Her Ladyship, or Their Ladyships when you are addressing or referring to female members of the nobility or the wives of knights. [politeness] ⇒ Her Ladyship's expecting you, sir.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

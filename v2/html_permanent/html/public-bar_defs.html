public bar

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounBritish a bar in a public house usually serving drinks at a cheaper price than in the saloon bar. Also called: the public Compare private bar

Definitions

countable nounIn a British pub, a public bar is a room where the furniture is plain and the drinks are cheaper than in the pub's other bars.

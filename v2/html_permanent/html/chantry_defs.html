chantry

(ˈtʃɑːntrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -tries Christianity1. an endowment for the singing of Masses for the soul of the founder or others designated by him or her2. a chapel or altar so endowed3. (as modifier) ⇒ a chantry priest
Word origin
C14: from Old French chanterie, from chanter to sing; see chant

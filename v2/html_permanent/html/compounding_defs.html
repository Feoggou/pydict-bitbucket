compounding

(ˈkɒmpaʊndɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. banking the addition of interest on interest already earned or charged2. the combining of elements or substances to make something, esp a drug

I sat at our old compounding table, surrounded by a welter of Skill-scrolls.

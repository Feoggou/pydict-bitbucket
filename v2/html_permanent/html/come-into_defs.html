come into

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. phrasal verb [no passive]If someone comes into some money, some property, or a title, they inherit it. [V P n] ⇒ My father has just come into a fortune in diamonds.2. phrasal verb [no passive]If someone or something comes into a situation, they have a role in it. [V P n] ⇒ We don't really know where Hortense comes into all this. [V P n] ⇒ It's an unrestricted journey, and rules of any sort don't come into it. →  come

expedite

(ˈɛkspɪˌdaɪt )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to hasten the progress of; hasten or assist2. to do or process (something, such as business matters) with speed and efficiency3. rare to dispatch (documents, messages, etc)adjective obsolete4. unimpeded or prompt; expeditious5. alert or prepared
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin expedīre, literally: to free the feet (as from a snare), hence, liberate, from ex-1 + pēs foot

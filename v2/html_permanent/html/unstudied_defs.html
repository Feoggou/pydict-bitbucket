unstudied

(ʌnˈstʌdɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. natural; unaffected2. (foll by in) without knowledge or training
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'unstudied'
Every movement he made was effortless and unstudied , yet suggested consciousness of power. Wright, L R SLEEP WHILE I SINGIn one fluid, unstudied gesture she scrabbled about on the banquette for her discarded clothing. Bringle, Mary DEATH OF AN UNKNOWN MAN

audit

(ˈɔːdɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. an inspection, correction, and verification of business accounts, conducted by an independent qualified accountantb. (as modifier) ⇒ audit report2. US an audited account3. any thoroughgoing check or examination4. archaic a hearingverb5. to inspect, correct, and certify (accounts, etc)6. US and Canadian to attend (classes, etc) as an auditor
Word origin
C15: from Latin audītus a hearing, from audīre to hear

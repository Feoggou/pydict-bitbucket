unbending

(ʌnˈbɛndɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. rigid or inflexible2. characterized by sternness or severity ⇒ an unbending rule
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unbendingly (unˈbendingly) adverbunbendingness (unˈbendingness) noun

unbending for learners of English

(ʌnbendɪŋ )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a person or their behaviour as unbending, you mean that they have very strict beliefs and attitudes, which they are unwilling to change. ⇒ He was rigid and unbending. ⇒ ...her unbending opposition to the old regime.

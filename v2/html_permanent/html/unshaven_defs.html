unshaven for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnʃeɪvən )
Definitions
adjectiveIf a man is unshaven, he has not shaved recently and there are short hairs on his face or chin. ⇒ His hair was disheveled, and his face was unshaven and gray.

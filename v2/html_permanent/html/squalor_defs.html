squalor

(ˈskwɒlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe condition or quality of being squalid; disgusting dirt and filth

Word Origin

C17: from Latin

Definitions

uncount nounYou can refer to very dirty, unpleasant conditions as squalor. ⇒ He was out of work and living in squalor.

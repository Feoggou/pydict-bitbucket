Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounAn absentee is a person who is expected to be in a particular place but who is not there.2. adjective [ADJ n]Absentee is used to describe someone who is not there to do a particular job in person. ⇒ Absentee fathers will be forced to pay child maintenance. ⇒ ...an absentee landlord.3. adjective [ADJ n]In elections in the United States, if you vote by absentee ballot or if you are an absentee voter, you vote in advance because you will be away. [US] ⇒ He has already voted by absentee ballot. ⇒ The deadline for absentee voting in Louisiana was Saturday.

Synonyms of vote

vote

noun

  1. poll, election, ballot, referendum, popular vote, plebiscite, straw poll, show of hands
    • They took a vote and decided not to do it.
  2. right to vote, franchise, voting rights, suffrage, say, voice, enfranchisement
    • Before that, women did not even have the vote.

verb

  1. cast your vote, ballot, go to the polls, mark your ballot paper
    • Over half of the electorate did not vote in the last general election.
  2. judge, declare, pronounce, decree, adjudge
    • They voted him Player of the Year.
  3. (informal) suggest, propose, recommend, move, table, advocate, submit
    • I vote that we ask him to come with us.
  • See vote someone in
  • See vote someone out

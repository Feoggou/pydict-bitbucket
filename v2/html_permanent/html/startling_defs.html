graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is startling is so different, unexpected, or remarkable that people react to it with surprise. ⇒ Sometimes the results may be rather startling. ⇒ ...startling new evidence. ⇒ His hair was dyed a startling black.startlingly graded adverb [usu ADV adj, also ADV after v, ADV with cl] ⇒ He was startlingly handsome. ⇒ Then, startlingly, the back door was jerked open by Binton.

lists

(lɪsts )
Definitions
plural noun1. historya. the enclosed field of combat at a tournamentb. the barriers enclosing the field at a tournament2. any arena or scene of conflict, controversy, etc3. See enter the lists
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: plural of list2 (border, boundary)

eczema

(ˈɛksɪmə , ɪɡˈziːmə )
Definitions
nounpathology a skin inflammation with lesions that scale, crust, or ooze a serous fluid, often accompanied by intense itching or burning
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

eczematous (ɛkˈsɛmətəs ) adjective

Word Origin

C18: from New Latin, from Greek ekzema, from ek- out + zein to boil; see yeast

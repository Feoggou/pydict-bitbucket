downs

(daʊnz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. Also called: downland rolling upland, esp in the chalk areas of S Britain, characterized by lack of trees and used mainly as pasture2. Australian and New Zealand a flat grassy area, not necessarily of uplands

Downs

(daʊnz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun the Downs1. any of various ranges of low chalk hills in S England, esp the South Downs in Sussex2. a roadstead off the SE coast of Kent, protected by the Goodwin Sands

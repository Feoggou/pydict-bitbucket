(bɪdekt )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [adv ADJ]If a place is bedecked with flags or other ornaments, these things have been hung up to decorate it. [literary] [+ with/in] ⇒ The palace was bedecked with flags.Bedecked is also a combining form. ⇒ ...a flower-bedecked stage.

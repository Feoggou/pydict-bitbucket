railway line

(ˈreɪlˌweɪ laɪn)
Definitions
noun railways, British1. a route between two points on a railway ⇒ Many farms are separated from their fields by the Leiden-Utrecht railway line.2. a railway track, including the roadbed, sleepers, etc ⇒ a disused railway line

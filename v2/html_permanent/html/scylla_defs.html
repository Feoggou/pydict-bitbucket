Scylla

(ˈsɪlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Greek mythology a sea nymph transformed into a sea monster believed to drown sailors navigating the Strait of Messina. She was identified with a rock off the Italian coast. Compare Charybdis2. See between Scylla and Charybdis

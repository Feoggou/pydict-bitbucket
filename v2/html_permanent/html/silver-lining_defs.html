Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. →  every cloud has a silver lining2. singular nounIf you talk about a silver lining, you are talking about something positive that comes out of a sad or unpleasant situation. [+ of] ⇒ The fall in inflation is the silver lining of the prolonged recession.

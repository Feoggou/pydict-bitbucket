Hormuz

(hɔːˈmuːz , ˈhɔːmʌz ) or Ormuz
Definitions
nounan island off the SE coast of Iran, in the Strait of Hormuz: ruins of the ancient city of Hormuz, a major trading centre in the Middle Ages. Area: about 41 sq km (16 sq miles)

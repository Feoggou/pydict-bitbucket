hoof

(huːf )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural hooves (huːvz ) or hoofs1. a. the horny covering of the end of the foot in the horse, deer, and all other ungulate mammalsb. (in combination) ⇒ a hoofbeat ▶ Related adjective: ungular2. the foot of an ungulate mammal3. a hoofed animal4. facetious a person's foot5. See on the hoofverb6. (transitive) to kick or trample with the hoofs7. See hoof it
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hoofless (ˈhoofless) adjectivehooflike (ˈhoofˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

Old English hōf; related to Old Norse hōfr, Old High German huof (German Huf), Sanskrit saphás

Definitions
countable noun [usu pl]The hooves of an animal such as a horse are the hard lower parts of its feet. ⇒ The horses' hooves often could not get a proper grip.

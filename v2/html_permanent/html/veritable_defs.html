veritable

(ˈvɛrɪtəbəl )
Definitions
adjective (prenominal)1. (intensifier; usually qualifying a word used metaphorically) ⇒ he's a veritable swine!2. rare genuine or true; proper ⇒ I require veritable proof
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

veritableness (ˈveritableness) nounveritably (ˈveritably) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Old French, from vérité truth; see verity

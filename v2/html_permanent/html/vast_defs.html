vast

(vɑːst )
Definitions
adjective1. unusually large in size, extent, degree, or number; immense2. (prenominal) (intensifier) ⇒ in vast hastenoun3. See the vast4. British dialect a very great amount or number
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vastity (ˈvastity) nounvastly (ˈvastly) adverbvastness (ˈvastness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin vastus deserted

dictatorial

(ˌdɪktəˈtɔːrɪəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or characteristic of a dictator2. tending to dictate; tyrannical; overbearing
Derived forms
ˌdictatorially (ˌdictaˈtorially) adverbˌdictatorialness (ˌdictaˈtorialness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveDictatorial means controlled or used by a dictator. ⇒ He suspended the constitution and assumed dictatorial powers.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone's behaviour as dictatorial, you do not like the fact that they tell people what to do in a forceful and unfair way. [disapproval] domineering, overbearing ⇒ If you are too strict with them, your children will see you as dictatorial. ⇒ ...his dictatorial management style.

barbarity

(bɑːˈbærɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the state or condition of being barbaric or barbarous2. a brutal or vicious act3. a crude or unsophisticated quality, style, expression, etc

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounIf you refer to someone's behaviour as barbarity, you strongly disapprove of it because you think that it is extremely cruel. [disapproval] ⇒ Rebellions were put down with appalling barbarity. ⇒ ...the barbarity of war.

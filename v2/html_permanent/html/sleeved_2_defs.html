-sleeved

(-sliːvd)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective(in combination) having sleeves of a particular length or type ⇒ He was wearing a short-sleeved blue shirt., ⇒ a long-sleeved dress

-sleeved

Powered by COBUILD
(-sliːvd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
combining form [usu ADJ n]-sleeved is added to adjectives such as 'long' and 'short' to form adjectives which indicate that an item of clothing has long or short sleeves. ⇒ ...a short-sleeved blue shirt.

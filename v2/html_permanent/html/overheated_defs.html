overheated

(ˌəʊvəˈhiːtɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. excessively hot ⇒ that stuffy overheated apartment2. economics inflated or tending towards inflation ⇒ the disastrous consequences of an overheated market3. agitated or irritated ⇒ In America, overheated drivers have been known to shoot each other.

dowdy

(ˈdaʊdɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -dier or -diest1. (esp of a woman's dress) drab, unflattering, and old-fashionednoun plural -dies2. a dowdy woman
Derived forms
dowdily (ˈdowdily) adverbdowdiness (ˈdowdiness) noundowdyish (ˈdowdyish) adjective
Word origin
C14: dowd slut, of unknown origin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or their clothes as dowdy, you mean their clothes are dull and unfashionable. [disapproval] ⇒ ...playing hostess to a lot of officials and their dowdy wives. ⇒ Her clothes were clean but dowdy.

wain

(weɪn )
Definitions
nounmainly poetic a farm wagon or cart

Word Origin

Old English wægn; related to Old Frisian wein, Old Norse vagn

Wain

(weɪn )
Definitions
noun John (Barrington). 1925–94, British novelist, poet, and critic. His novels include Hurry on Down (1953), Strike the Father Dead (1962), and Young Shoulders (1982)

nigella

(naɪˈdʒɛlə )
Definitions
nounany plant of the ranunculaceous genus Nigella, from the Mediterranean and W Asia, esp N. damascena. See love-in-a-mist
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

New Latin, diminutive of Latin niger black, from the colour of the seeds

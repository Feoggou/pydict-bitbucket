efficient

(ɪˈfɪʃənt )
Definitions
adjective1. functioning or producing effectively and with the least waste of effort; competent2. philosophy producing a direct effect; causative
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

efficiently (efˈficiently) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Latin efficiēns effecting

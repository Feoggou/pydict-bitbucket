salubrious

(səˈluːbrɪəs )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveconducive or favourable to health; wholesome

Derived Forms

salubriously (saˈlubriously) adverbsalubriousness (saˈlubriousness) or salubrity (səˈluːbrɪtɪ ) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin salūbris, from salūs health

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA place that is salubrious is pleasant and healthy. [formal] ⇒ ...your salubrious lochside hotel.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is described as salubrious is respectable or socially desirable. [formal] ⇒ ...London's less salubrious quarters.

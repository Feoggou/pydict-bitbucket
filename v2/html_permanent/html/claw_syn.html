claw

noun

  1. nail, talon, unguis (technical) • The cat's claws got caught in my clothes.
  2. pincer, nipper • The lobster has two large claws.

verb

  1. scratch, tear, dig, rip, scrape, graze, maul, scrabble, mangle, mangulate (Australian, slang), lacerate • She struck back at him and clawed his arm with her hand.

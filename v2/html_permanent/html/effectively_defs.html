effectively

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪfektɪvli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [oft ADV adj/-ed]You use effectively with a statement or opinion to indicate that it is not accurate in every detail, but that you feel it is a reasonable description or summary of a particular situation. in effect ⇒ This effectively means that the government does not agree with the proposals. ⇒ The region was effectively independent.

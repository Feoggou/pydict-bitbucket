yearn

(jɜːn )
Definitions
verb (intransitive)1. (usually foll by for or after or an infinitive) to have an intense desire or longing (for); pine (for)2. to feel tenderness or affection
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

yearner (ˈyearner) noun

Word Origin

Old English giernan; related to Old Saxon girnian, Old Norse girna, Gothic gairnjan, Old High German gerōn to long for, Sanskrit haryati he likes

illicit

(ɪˈlɪsɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. another word for illegal2. not allowed or approved by common custom, rule, or standard ⇒ illicit sexual relations
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

illicitly (ilˈlicitly) adverbillicitness (ilˈlicitness) noun

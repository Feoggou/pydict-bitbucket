ribald

(ˈrɪbəld )
Definitions
adjective1. coarse, obscene, or licentious, usually in a humorous or mocking waynoun2. a ribald person
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ribaldly (ˈribaldly) adverb

Word Origin

C13: from Old French ribauld, from riber to live licentiously, of Germanic origin

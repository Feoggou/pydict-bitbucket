witching

(ˈwɪtʃɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. relating to or appropriate for witchcraft2. rare bewitchingnoun3. witchcraft; magic
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

witchingly (ˈwitchingly) adverb

Word Frequency

lymph

(lɪmf )
Definitions
nounthe almost colourless fluid, containing chiefly white blood cells, that is collected from the tissues of the body and transported in the lymphatic system
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin lympha water, from earlier limpa influenced in form by Greek numphē nymph

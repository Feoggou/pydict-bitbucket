punctum

(ˈpʌŋktəm)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -tums or -ta (-tə)1. anatomy a tip or small point2. obsolete a mathematical point3. obsolete a small unit of time4. obsolete the essence of something5. zoology, botany, medicine a small dot or rounded mark6. grammar obsolete a full stop7. (in early music) an inflection used at the end of a chant8. (in early music) a neume

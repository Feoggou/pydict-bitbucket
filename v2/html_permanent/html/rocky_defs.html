rocky1

(ˈrɒkɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: rockier or rockiest1. consisting of or abounding in rocks ⇒ a rocky shore2. hard or unyielding ⇒ rocky determination3. hard like rock ⇒ rocky muscles

rocky

Powered by COBUILD
(rɒki ) for learners of English
Word forms: rockier , rockiest
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA rocky place is covered with rocks or consists of large areas of rock and has nothing growing on it. ⇒ The paths are often very rocky so strong boots are advisable. ⇒ ...a rocky headland.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA rocky situation or relationship is unstable and full of difficulties. ⇒ They had gone through some rocky times together when Ann was first married. ⇒ Their relationship had gotten off to a rocky start.

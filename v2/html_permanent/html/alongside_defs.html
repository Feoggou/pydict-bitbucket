Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. prepositionIf one thing is alongside another thing, the first thing is next to the second. ⇒ He crossed the street and walked alongside Central Park. ⇒ Much of the industry was located alongside rivers.Alongside is also an adverb. ⇒ He waited several minutes for a car to pull up alongside.2. prepositionIf you work alongside other people, you all work together in the same place. together with ⇒ He had worked alongside Frank and Mark and they had become friends. ⇒ Men aged 60 are fighting alongside young boys.3. prepositionIf one thing exists or develops alongside another, the two things exist or develop together at the same time. ⇒ Her self-confidence will develop alongside her technique. ⇒ Alongside the Job Creation Programme they launched a Work Experience Programme.

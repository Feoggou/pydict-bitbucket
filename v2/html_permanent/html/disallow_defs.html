(dɪsəlaʊ )
Word forms: disallows , disallowing , disallowed
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf something is disallowed, it is not allowed or accepted officially, because it has not been done correctly. [be V-ed] ⇒ England scored again, but the whistle had gone and the goal was disallowed. [V n] ⇒ The Internal Revenue Service sought to disallow the payments. [V n] ⇒ It was a shock to hear him rule that my testimony would be disallowed.

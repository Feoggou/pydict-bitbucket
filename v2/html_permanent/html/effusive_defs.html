effusive

(ɪˈfjuːsɪv )
Definitions
adjective1. extravagantly demonstrative of emotion; gushing2. (of rock) formed by the solidification of magma

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as effusive, you mean that they express pleasure, gratitude, or approval in a very enthusiastic way. ⇒ He was effusive in his praise for the general. ⇒ She was very gushing and very effusive.effusively graded adverb & adverb ⇒ She greeted them effusively.

garret

(ˈɡærɪt )
Definitions
nounanother word for attic (sense 1)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French garite watchtower, from garir to protect, of Germanic origin; see wary

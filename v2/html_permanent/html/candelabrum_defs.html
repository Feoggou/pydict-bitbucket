candelabrum

(ˌkændɪˈlɑːbrəm ) or candelabra
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -bra (-brə), -brums or -brasa large branched candleholder or holder for overhead lights
Word origin
C19: from Latin, from candēla candle

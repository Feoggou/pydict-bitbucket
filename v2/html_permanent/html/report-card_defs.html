report card

(rɪˈpɔːt kɑːd)
Definitions
noun1. a report for each school student which evaluates their academic performance and behaviour and is written by their teachers ⇒ The only time I got their attention was when I brought home straight A's on my report card.2. a report evaluating or appraising something ⇒ The President today issued his final report card on the state of the economy.

Word forms: report cards
Definitions
1. countable nounA report card is an official written account of how well or how badly a pupil has done during the term or year that has just finished. [US] ⇒ The only time I got their attention was when I brought home straight A's on my report card.regional note:   in BRIT, use report2. countable nounA report card is a report on how well a person, organization, or country has been doing recently. [US, journalism] ⇒ The President today issued his final report card on the state of the economy.

entertaining

(ˌɛntəˈteɪnɪŋ )
Definitions
adjectiveserving to entertain or give pleasure; diverting; amusing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

entertainingly (ˌenterˈtainingly) adverb

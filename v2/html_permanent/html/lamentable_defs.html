lamentable

(ˈlæməntəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. wretched, deplorable, or distressing2. an archaic word for mournful

lamentable

Powered by COBUILD
(læməntəbəl , ləment- ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as lamentable, you mean that it is very unfortunate or disappointing. [literary, feelings] ⇒ This lamentable state of affairs lasted until 1947. ⇒ His command of English was lamentable.lamentably (læməntəbli ) adverb [usu ADV adj] [oft ADV with v] ⇒ There are still lamentably few women surgeons. ⇒ They have failed lamentably. ⇒ There are, lamentably, no set rules.

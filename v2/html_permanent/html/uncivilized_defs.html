uncivilized

or uncivilised (ʌnˈsɪvɪˌlaɪzd )
Definitions
adjective1. (of a tribe or people) not yet civilized, esp preliterate2. lacking culture or sophistication
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

uncivilizedly or uncivilisedly (ʌnˈsɪvɪˌlaɪzɪdlɪ ) adverbuncivilizedness (unˈciviˌlizedness) or uncivilisedness (unˈciviˌlisedness) noun

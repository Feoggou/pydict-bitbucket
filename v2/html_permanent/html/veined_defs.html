veined

(veɪnd)
Definitions
adjectivea. filled, furnished, or marked with or as if with veins ⇒ Helen's hands were thin and veined., ⇒ a veined leafb. (in combination) ⇒ blue-veined, ⇒ a man who had blue-veined cheeks, ⇒ this distinctive blue-veined cheese

veined

Powered by COBUILD
(veɪnd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveVeined skin has a lot of veins showing through it. ⇒ Helen's hands were thin and veined.Veined is also a combining form. ⇒ ...a man who had blue-veined cheeks.2. adjectiveSomething that is veined has a pattern or colouring like that of veins showing through skin. ⇒ ...a bronze ashtray shaped like a veined leaf.Veined is also a combining form. ⇒ ...this distinctive blue-veined cheese.

Definitions
1. phrasal verbIf something such as an activity wakes you up, it makes you more alert and ready to do things after you have been lazy or inactive. [V P n] ⇒ A cool shower wakes up the body and boosts circulation. [Also V n P]2. → See also wake [sense 1] →  wake

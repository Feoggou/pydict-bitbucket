(ədmaɪərəʳ )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounIf you are an admirer of someone, you like and respect them or their work very much. [+ of] ⇒ He was an admirer of her grandfather's paintings.2. countable nounA woman's admirers are the men who are attracted to her. suitor ⇒ Johnny was the most persistent and most harmless of her admirers.

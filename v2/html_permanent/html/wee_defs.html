wee1

(wiː )
Definitions
adjective1. very small; tiny; minutenoun2. mainly Scottish a short time (esp in the phrase bide a wee.)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old English wǣg weight

wee for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(wiː )
Word forms: wees , weeing , weed
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Wee means small in size or extent. [Scottish, informal] ⇒ I've got a wee kitten in the flat. ⇒ He just needs to calm down a wee bit.2. verbTo wee means to urinate. Wee is an informal word used especially by children. [British] [V] ⇒ He said he wanted to wee.Wee is also a noun. ⇒ The baby has done a wee in his potty. ⇒ I put him on his pot, he did some wee.

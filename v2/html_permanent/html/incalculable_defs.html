incalculable

(ɪnˈkælkjʊləbəl )
Definitions
adjectivebeyond calculation; unable to be predicted or determined

Derived Forms

incalculability (inˌcalculaˈbility) or incalculableness (inˈcalculableness) nounincalculably (inˈcalculably) adverb

Definitions
adjectiveSomething that is incalculable cannot be calculated or estimated because it is so great. ⇒ He warned that the effects of any war would be incalculable. ⇒ This has done incalculable damage to his reputation.

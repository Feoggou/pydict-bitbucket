noodle1

(ˈnuːdəl )
Definitions
noun(often plural) a ribbon-like strip of pasta: noodles are often served in soup or with a sauce
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from German Nudel, origin obscure

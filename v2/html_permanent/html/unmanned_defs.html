unmanned

(ʌnˈmænd )
Definitions
adjective1. lacking personnel or crew ⇒ an unmanned ship2. (of aircraft, spacecraft, etc) operated by automatic or remote control3. uninhabited4. falconry (of a hawk or falcon) not yet trained to accept humans

unmanned for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnmænd )
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Unmanned vehicles such as spacecraft do not have any people in them and operate automatically or are controlled from a distance. ⇒ ...a special unmanned spacecraft. ⇒ ...unmanned rockets.2. adjectiveIf a place is unmanned, there is nobody working there. ⇒ The fare from the last unmanned station is probably less than a pound. ⇒ Unmanned post offices meant millions of letters went unsorted.

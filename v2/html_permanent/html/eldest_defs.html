Definitions
superlative adjective & adjectiveThe eldest person in a group is the one who was born before all the others. ⇒ The eldest child was a daughter called Fiona. [+of] ⇒ David was the eldest of three boys. ⇒ The two eldest are already doing well at Kings Wood.

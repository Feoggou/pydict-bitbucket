confederation

(kənˌfɛdəˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or process of confederating or the state of being confederated2. a loose alliance of political units. The union of the Swiss cantons is the oldest surviving confederation. Compare federation3. (esp in Canada) another name for federation
Derived forms
conˌfederationˌism (conˌfederˈationˌism) nounconˌfederationist (conˌfederˈationist) nounconfederative (conˈfederative) adjective

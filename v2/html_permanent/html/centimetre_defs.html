Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
regional note:   in AM, use centimetercountable nounA centimetre is a unit of length in the metric system equal to ten millimetres or one-hundredth of a metre. ⇒ ...a tiny fossil plant, only a few centimetres high.

unmindful

(ʌnˈmaɪndfʊl )
Definitions
adjective(usually postpositive; and foll by of) careless, heedless, or forgetful
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unmindfully (unˈmindfully) adverbunmindfulness (unˈmindfulness) noun

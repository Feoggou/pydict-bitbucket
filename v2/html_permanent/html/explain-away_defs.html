Definitions
phrasal verbIf someone explains away a mistake or a bad situation they are responsible for, they try to indicate that it is unimportant or that it is not really their fault. [V P n] ⇒ He evaded her questions about the war and tried to explain away the atrocities. [V n P] ⇒ I had noticed blood on my husband's clothing but he explained it away. →  explain

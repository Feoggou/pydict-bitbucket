Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]If you do something busily, you do it in a very active way. ⇒ The two saleswomen were busily trying to keep up with the demand.
Example sentences containing 'busily'
But Robert was busily consulting his map, which he then folded very carefully and put back in his pocket. Lowry, Lois ANASTASIA KRUPNIK (3-IN-1)Nicola glanced away from the screen on her desk, her fingers still clicking busily over the keyboard.

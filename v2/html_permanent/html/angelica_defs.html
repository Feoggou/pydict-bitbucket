angelica

(ænˈdʒɛlɪkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: archangel any tall umbelliferous plant of the genus Angelica, having compound leaves and clusters of small white or greenish flowers, esp A. archangelica, the aromatic seeds, leaves, and stems of which are used in medicine and cookery2. the candied stems of this plant, used for decorating and flavouring sweet dishes
Word origin
C16: from Medieval Latin (herba) angelica angelic (herb)

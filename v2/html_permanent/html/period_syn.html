Synonyms of period

period

noun

  1. time, term, season, space, run, stretch, spell, phase, patch (British, informal), interval, span
    • a period of a few months
  2. age, generation, years, time, days, term, stage, date, cycle, era, epoch, aeon
    • the Victorian period
  3. class, session, lesson
    • a Biology period

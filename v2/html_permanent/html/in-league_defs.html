in league

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
phraseIf you say that someone is in league with another person to do something bad, you mean that they are working together to do that thing. [+ with] ⇒ There is no evidence that the broker was in league with the fraudulent vendor. ⇒ Williams operated the smuggling scheme in league with his brother. →  league

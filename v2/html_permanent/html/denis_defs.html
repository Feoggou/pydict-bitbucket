Denis

(ˈdɛnɪs , French dəni)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Maurice (mɔris). 1870–1943, French painter and writer on art. One of the leading Nabis, he defined a picture as "essentially a flat surface covered with colours assembled in a certain order"2. See Saint Denis

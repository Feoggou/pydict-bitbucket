bereave

(bɪˈriːv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. (usually foll by of) to deprive (of) something or someone valued, esp through death2. obsolete to remove by force▷ See also bereft
Word origin
Old English bereafian; see reave1

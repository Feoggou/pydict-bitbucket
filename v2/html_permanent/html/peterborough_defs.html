Peterborough

(ˈpiːtəbərə , -brə)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a city in central England, in Peterborough unitary authority, N Cambridgeshire on the River Nene: industrial centre; under development as a new town since 1968. Pop: 136 292 (2001)2. a unitary authority in central England, in Cambridgeshire. Pop: 158 800 (2003 est). Area: 402 sq km (155 sq miles)3. See Soke of Peterborough4. a city in SE Canada, in SE Ontario: manufacturing centre. Pop: 73 303 (2001)5. a traditional type of wooden canoe formerly made in Peterborough, SE Ontario

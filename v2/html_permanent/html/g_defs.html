Definitions
1. variable nounG is the seventh letter of the English alphabet.2. variable nounIn music, G is the fifth note in the scale of C major.3. countable nounA G is a unit of measurement of the force resulting from acceleration. One G is equivalent to the force of the earth's gravitational pull. [technical]4. countable noun [usu a/num N]A G is a thousand pounds or dollars. [mainly US, informal] grand5. G or g is used as an abbreviation for words beginning with g, such as 'gram', 'gallon', and 'German'. ⇒ Oranges contain only 35 calories per 100g.

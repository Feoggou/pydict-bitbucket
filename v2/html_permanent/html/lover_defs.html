lover

(ˈlʌvə )
Definitions
noun1. a person, now esp a man, who has an extramarital or premarital sexual relationship with another person2. (often plural) either of the two people involved in a love affair3. a. someone who loves a specified person or thing ⇒ a lover of musicb. (in combination) ⇒ a music-lover, ⇒ a cat-lover

lover

Powered by COBUILD
(lʌvəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: lovers
Definitions
1. countable noun [oft poss N]Someone's lover is someone who they are having a sexual relationship with but are not married to. ⇒ Every Thursday she would meet her lover Leon. ⇒ He and Liz became lovers soon after they first met.2. countable nounIf you are a lover of something such as animals or the arts, you enjoy them very much and take great pleasure in them. [+ of] ⇒ She is a great lover of horses and horse racing. ⇒ Are you an opera lover?

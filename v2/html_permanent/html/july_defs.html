July

(dʒuːˈlaɪ , dʒə-, dʒʊ-)
Definitions
noun plural -liesthe seventh month of the year, consisting of 31 days
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Anglo-French julie, from Latin Jūlius, after Gaius Julius Caesar, in whose honour it was named

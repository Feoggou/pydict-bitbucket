amusement

(əˈmjuːzmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. something that amuses, such as a game or other pastime2. a mechanical device used for entertainment, as at a fair3. the act of amusing or the state or quality of being amused

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounAmusement is the feeling that you have when you think that something is funny or amusing. ⇒ He stopped and watched with amusement to see the child so absorbed. ⇒ Steamers tooted at us as sailors on deck waved in amusement.2. uncount nounAmusement is the pleasure that you get from being entertained or from doing something interesting. ⇒ I stumbled sideways before landing flat on my back, much to the amusement of the rest of the lads. ⇒ She excelled at impersonations, which provided great amusement for him and his friends.3. countable noun [usu pl]Amusements are ways of passing the time pleasantly. ⇒ People had very few amusements to choose from. There was no radio, or television.4. plural nounAmusements are games, rides, and other things that you can enjoy, for example at a fairground or at the seaside. ⇒ ...a place full of swings and amusements.

in the making

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
phraseIf you describe a person or thing as something in the making, you mean that they are going to become known or recognized as that thing. ⇒ Her drama teacher is confident Julie is a star in the making. ⇒ I think it's a disaster in the making. →  making

faceless

(ˈfeɪslɪs )
Definitions
adjective1. without a face2. without identity; anonymous

facelessness (ˈfacelessness) noun

faceless

Powered by COBUILD
(feɪsləs ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone or something as faceless, you dislike them because they are uninteresting and have no character. [disapproval] ⇒ Ordinary people are at the mercy of faceless bureaucrats.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

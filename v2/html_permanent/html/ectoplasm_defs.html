ectoplasm

(ˈɛktəʊˌplæzəm )
Definitions
noun1. cytology the outer layer of cytoplasm in some cells, esp protozoa, which differs from the inner cytoplasm in being a clear gel. See also endoplasm2. spiritualism the substance supposedly emanating from the body of a medium during trances
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ectoplasmic (ˌectoˈplasmic) or ectoplastic (ˌɛktəʊˈplæstɪk) adjective

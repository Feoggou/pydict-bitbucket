aftermath

(ˈɑːftəˌmɑːθ , -ˌmæθ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. signs or results of an event or occurrence considered collectively, esp of a catastrophe or disaster ⇒ the aftermath of war2. agriculture a second mowing or crop of grass from land that has already yielded one crop earlier in the same year
Word origin
C16: after + math a mowing, from Old English mæth

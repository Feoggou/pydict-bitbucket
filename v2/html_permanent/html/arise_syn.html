arise

verb

  1. happen, start, begin, follow, issue, result, appear, develop, emerge, occur, spring, set in, stem, originate, ensue, come about, commence, come to light, emanate, crop up (informal), come into being, materialize • if a problem arises later in pregnancy
  2. (old-fashioned) get to your feet, get up, rise, stand up, spring up, leap up • I arose from the chair and left.
  3. get up, wake up, awaken, get out of bed • He arose at 6:30 a.m. as usual.
  4. ascend, rise, lift, mount, climb, tower, soar, move upward • the flat terrace, from which arises the volume of the house

washer

(ˈwɒʃə )
Definitions
noun1. a person or thing that washes2. a flat ring or drilled disc of metal used under the head of a bolt or nut to spread the load when tightened3. any flat ring of rubber, felt, metal, etc, used to provide a seal under a nut or in a tap or valve seat4. See washing machine5. chemical engineering a device for cleaning or washing gases or vapours; scrubber6. Australian a face cloth; flannel

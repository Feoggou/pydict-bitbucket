Anglian

(ˈæŋɡlɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to the Angles or to the Anglian dialects of Old Englishnoun2. the group of Old and Middle English dialects spoken in the Midlands and the north of England, divided into Mercian and Northumbrian. See also Kentish, West Saxon▷ See also East Anglia

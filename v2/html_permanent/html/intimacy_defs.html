Definitions

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪntɪməsi ) for learners of English
Word forms: intimacies
Definitions
1. uncount nounIntimacy between two people is a very close personal relationship between them. [+ with] ⇒ ...a means of achieving intimacy with another person. [Also + between]2. uncount nounYou sometimes use intimacy to refer to sex or a sexual relationship. ⇒ Her sunburn made intimacy too painful. [+ with] ⇒ The truth was he did not feel like intimacy with any woman.3. countable noun [usu pl]Intimacies are things that you say or do to someone you have a very close personal relationship with. ⇒ ...the intimacies of a love scene.

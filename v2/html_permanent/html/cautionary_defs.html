Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A cautionary story or a cautionary note to a story is one that is intended to give a warning to people. ⇒ Barely fifteen months later, it has become a cautionary tale of the pitfalls of international mergers and acquisitions. ⇒ An editorial in The Times sounds a cautionary note.

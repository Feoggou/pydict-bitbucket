Sellotape

(ˈsɛləˌteɪp )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. trademark a type of transparent adhesive tape made of cellulose or a similar substanceverb2. (transitive) to seal or stick using adhesive tape

Sellotape

Powered by COBUILD
(seləteɪp )
Word forms: Sellotapes , Sellotaping , Sellotaped
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounSellotape is a clear sticky tape that you use to stick paper or card together or onto a wall. [British, trademark]regional note:   in AM, use Scotch tape2. verbIf you Sellotape one thing to another, you stick them together using Sellotape. [British, trademark] [V n adv/prep] ⇒ I sellotaped the note to his door.regional note:   in AM, use tape

endowments

(ɪnˈdaʊmənts)
Definitions
plural noun1. a person's natural gifts, talents and qualities ⇒ Man can be equal whatever his social rank, whatever his intellectual endowments.2. euphemistic, slang the male genitals3. euphemistic, slang a woman's breasts

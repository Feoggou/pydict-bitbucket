gendarme

(ˈʒɒndɑːm , French ʒɑ̃darm)
Definitions
noun1. a member of the police force in France or in countries formerly influenced or controlled by France2. a slang word for a policeman3. a sharp pinnacle of rock on a mountain ridge, esp in the Alps
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from French, from gens d'armes people of arms

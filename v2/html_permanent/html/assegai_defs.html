assegai

or assagai (ˈæsəˌɡaɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -gais1. a southern African cornaceous tree, Curtisia faginea, the wood of which is used for making spears2. a sharp light spear, esp one made of this wood
Word origin
C17: from Portuguese azagaia, from Arabic az zaghāyah, from al the + zaghāyah assegai, from Berber

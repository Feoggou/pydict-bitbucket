taxable

(ˈtæksəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. capable of being taxed; able to bear tax2. subject to taxnoun3. (often plural) US a person, income, property, etc, that is subject to tax
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

taxability (ˌtaxaˈbility) or taxableness (ˈtaxableness) nountaxably (ˈtaxably) adverb

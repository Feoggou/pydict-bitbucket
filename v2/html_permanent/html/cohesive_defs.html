cohesive

(kəʊˈhiːsɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. characterized by or causing cohesion2. tending to cohere or stick together
Derived forms
cohesively (coˈhesively) adverbcohesiveness (coˈhesiveness) noun

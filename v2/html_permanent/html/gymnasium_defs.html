gymnasium

(dʒɪmˈneɪzɪəm )
Definitions
noun plural -siums or -sia (-zɪə)1. a large room or hall equipped with bars, weights, ropes, etc, for games or physical training2. (in various European countries) a secondary school that prepares pupils for university
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin: school for gymnastics, from Greek gumnasion, from gumnazein to exercise naked, from gumnos naked

undefiled

(ˌʌndɪˈfaɪld)
Definitions
adjective literary1. not damaged or sullied ⇒ Juries were judges undefiled by practice.2. not made morally impure ⇒ She is a pale, blonde, undefiled, geeky innocent., ⇒ She is undefiled by any contact with him.3. not polluted or made dirty ⇒ an undefiled river4. not desecrated ⇒ God was assured that his Church was undefiled.

disfigurement

(dɪsˈfɪɡəmənt ) or disfiguration (ˌdɪsfɪɡəˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. something that disfigures2. the act of disfiguring or the state of being disfigured

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounA disfigurement is something, for example a scar, that spoils a person's appearance. ⇒ He had surgery to correct a facial disfigurement.

shagreen

(ʃæˈɡriːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the rough skin of certain sharks and rays, used as an abrasive2. a rough grainy leather made from certain animal hides

Word Origin

C17: from French chagrin, from Turkish çagri rump; also associated through folk etymology with shag1, green

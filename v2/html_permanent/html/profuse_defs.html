profuse

(prəˈfjuːs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. plentiful, copious, or abundant ⇒ profuse compliments2. (often foll by in) free or generous in the giving (of) ⇒ profuse in thanks

Derived Forms

profusely (proˈfusely) adverbprofuseness (proˈfuseness) or profusion (proˈfusion) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin profundere to pour lavishly

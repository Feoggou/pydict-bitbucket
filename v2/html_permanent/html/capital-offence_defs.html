capital offence

(ˈkæpɪtəl əˈfɛns)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a crime for which the death penalty is used ⇒ In some Islamic countries, homosexual activity is still a capital offence.2. humorous something that is considered a very bad thing to do ⇒ My employers were so strict that even a lunchtime stroll was a capital offence.
Example sentences containing 'capital offence'
She was alone on a wharf where it was a capital offence to be. Hilton, John Buxton MOONDROP TO MURDERAssembling with arms is a crime under the Constitution of Afghanistan, and revolt against the State is a capital offence. Shah, Idries KARA KUSH

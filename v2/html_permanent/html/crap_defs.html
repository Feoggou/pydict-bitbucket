crap

Powered by COBUILD
(kræp )
Word forms: craps , crapping , crapped
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as crap, you think that it is wrong or of very poor quality. [informal, rude, disapproval]Crap is also a noun. ⇒ It is a tedious, humourless load of crap.2. uncount nounCrap is sometimes used to refer to faeces. [informal, rude]3. verbTo crap means to get rid of faeces from your body. [informal, rude]4. uncount nounCraps or crap is a gambling game, played mainly in North America, in which you throw two dice and bet what the total will be. ⇒ I'll shoot some craps or play some blackjack.

breast

noun

  1. heart, feelings, thoughts, soul, being, emotions, core, sentiments, seat of the affections • Happiness flowered in her breast.
  2. bosom, boob (slang), tit (slang), jug (slang), booby (slang), mammary gland, bristol (slang), titty (slang) • a skimpy top which barely covered her breasts

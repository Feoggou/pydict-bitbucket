hawthorn

(ˈhɔːˌθɔːn )
Definitions
nounany of various thorny trees or shrubs of the N temperate rosaceous genus Crataegus, esp C. oxyacantha, having white or pink flowers and reddish fruits (haws). Also called (in Britain): may, may tree, mayflower
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English haguthorn from haga hedge + thorn thorn; related to Old Norse hagthorn, Middle High German hagendorn, Dutch haagdoorn

tetter

(ˈtɛtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a blister or pimple2. informal any of various skin eruptions, such as eczema

Word Origin

Old English teter; related to Old High German zitaroh, Sanskrit dadru, Late Latin derbita

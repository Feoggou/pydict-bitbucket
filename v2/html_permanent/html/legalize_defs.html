legalize

or legalise (ˈliːɡəˌlaɪz )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to make lawful or legal2. to confirm or validate (something previously unlawful)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

legalization (ˌlegaliˈzation) or legalisation (ˌlegaliˈsation) noun

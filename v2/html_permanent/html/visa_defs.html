visa

(ˈviːzə )
Definitions
noun plural -sas1. an endorsement in a passport or similar document, signifying that the document is in order and permitting its bearer to travel into or through the country of the government issuing it2. any sign or signature of approvalverb -sas, -saing or -saed (transitive)3. to enter a visa into (a passport)4. to endorse or ratify
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: via French from Latin vīsa things seen, from vīsus, past participle of vidēre to see

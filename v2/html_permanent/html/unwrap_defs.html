unwrap for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnræp )
Word forms: unwraps , unwrapping , unwrapped
Definitions
verbWhen you unwrap something, you take off the paper, plastic, or other covering that is around it. [V n] ⇒ I untied the bow and unwrapped the small box. [V n] ⇒ Vacuum-packed ham slices should be unwrapped 30 minutes before serving.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounYou use carpeting to refer to a carpet, or to the type of material that is used to make carpets. ⇒ ...a bedroom with wall-to-wall carpeting. ⇒ Carpeting is a reasonably cheap floor-covering.2. → see also carpet

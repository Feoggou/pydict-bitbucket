fickle

Powered by COBUILD
(fɪkəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as fickle, you disapprove of them because they keep changing their mind about what they like or want. [disapproval] capricious ⇒ The group has been notoriously fickle in the past.fickleness uncount noun [+ of] ⇒ ...the fickleness of businessmen and politicians.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that something is fickle, you mean that it often changes and is unreliable. ⇒ Orta's weather can be fickle.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

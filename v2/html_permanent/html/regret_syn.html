Synonyms of regret

regret

verb

  1. be or feel sorry about, feel remorse about, be upset about, rue, deplore, bemoan, repent (of), weep over, bewail, cry over spilt milk
    • She regrets having given up her home.
    OPPOSITES: be happy about, be satisfied with, rejoice over, have not looked back, feel satisfaction with
  2. mourn, miss, lament, weep over, sigh over, grieve for or over, pine for or over
    • I regret the passing of the old era.

noun

  1. remorse, compunction, self-reproach, pang of conscience, bitterness, repentance, contrition, penitence, ruefulness
    • He has no regrets about retiring.
  2. sorrow, disappointment, grief, sadness, unhappiness, lamentation, dejection
    • He expressed great regret.
    OPPOSITES:  pleasure, satisfaction, contentment, callousness, lack of compassion, impenitence

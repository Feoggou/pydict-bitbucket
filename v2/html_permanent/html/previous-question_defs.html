Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (in the House of Commons) a motion to drop the present topic under debate, put in order to prevent a vote2. (in the House of Lords and US legislative bodies) a motion to vote on a bill or other question without delay▷ See also closure (sense 4)

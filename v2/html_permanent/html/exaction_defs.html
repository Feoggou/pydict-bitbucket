exaction

(ɪɡˈzækʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act or an instance of exacting, esp money2. an excessive or harsh demand, esp for money; extortion3. a sum or payment exacted
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

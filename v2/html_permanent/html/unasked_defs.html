unasked for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnɑːskt , -æskt)
Definitions
1. adjectiveAn unasked question is one that has not been asked, although people are wondering what the answer is. ⇒ She was undernourished, an observation that prompted yet another unasked question. ⇒ Significant questions will go unasked.2. adjective [ADJ after v]If someone says or does something unasked, they say or do it without being asked to do it. ⇒ His advice, offered to her unasked, was to stay home and make the best of things.

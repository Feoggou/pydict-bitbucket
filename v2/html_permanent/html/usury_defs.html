usury

(ˈjuːʒərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ries1. the act or practice of loaning money at an exorbitant rate of interest2. an exorbitant or unlawfully high amount or rate of interest3. obsolete moneylending
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

usurious (juːˈʒʊərɪəs ) adjectiveusuriously (uˈsuriously) adverbusuriousness (uˈsuriousness) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Medieval Latin ūsūria, from Latin ūsūra usage, from ūsus use

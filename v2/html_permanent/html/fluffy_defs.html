fluffy

(ˈflʌfɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: fluffier or fluffiest1. of, resembling, or covered with fluff2. soft and light ⇒ fluffy hair3. a. sentimental or overromantic; not very intelligentb. characterized by nonviolent methods ⇒ fluffy environmentalist protestors
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fluffily (ˈfluffily) adverbfluffiness (ˈfluffiness) noun

fluffy

Powered by COBUILD
(flʌfi ) for learners of English
Word forms: fluffier , fluffiest
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something such as a towel or a toy animal as fluffy, you mean that it is very soft. ⇒ ...fluffy white towels. ⇒ It's a very fluffy kind of wool.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA cake or other food that is fluffy is very light because it has a lot of air in it. ⇒ Cream together the margarine and sugar with a wooden spoon until light and fluffy.

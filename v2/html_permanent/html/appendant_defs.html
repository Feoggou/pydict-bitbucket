appendant

(əˈpɛndənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. attached, affixed, or added2. attendant or associated as an accompaniment or result3. a less common word for pendent4. law relating to another rightnoun5. a person or thing attached or added6. property law a subordinate right or interest, esp in or over land, attached to a greater interest and automatically passing with the sale of the latter

public affairs

(ˈpʌblɪk əˈfɛəz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. politics; the business of governing ⇒ it is in everybody's interest that this outstanding minority should exercise more influence over public affairs than the untalented majority, ⇒ The civil service dominated public affairs.2. radio, television, journalism current affairs ⇒ news and public affairs3. (functioning as singular) business PR; lobbying ⇒ The only corporate area where the company does not operate is public affairs., ⇒ He is chief of public affairs.
Example sentences containing 'public affairs'
He has, it turned out, a nephew who is chief of public affairs at the base. Kallen, Lucille C B GREENFIELD - A LITTLE MADNESS

noxious

(ˈnɒkʃəs )
Definitions
adjective1. poisonous or harmful2. harmful to the mind or morals; corrupting
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

noxiously (ˈnoxiously) adverbnoxiousness (ˈnoxiousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin noxius harmful, from noxa injury

noxious for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(nɒkʃəs )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A noxious gas or substance is poisonous or very harmful. ⇒ Many household products give off noxious fumes. ⇒ ...carbon monoxide and other noxious gases.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you refer to someone or something as noxious, you mean that they are extremely unpleasant. [formal] ⇒ ...the heavy, noxious smell of burning sugar, butter, fats, and flour. ⇒ Their behaviour was noxious.

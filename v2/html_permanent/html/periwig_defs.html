periwig

(ˈpɛrɪˌwɪɡ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna wig, such as a peruke

Word Origin

C16 perwyke, changed from French perruque wig, peruke
Example sentences containing 'periwig'
Once he was down, his sword clattering to the ground, then Red leapt on the prostrate man, snarling and snapping at his periwig. Fidelis Morgan THE RIVAL QUEENS: A COUNTESS ASHBY DE LA ZOUCHE MYSTERY

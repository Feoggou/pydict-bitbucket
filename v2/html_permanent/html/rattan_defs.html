rattan

or ratan (ræˈtæn )
Definitions
noun1. any of the climbing palms of the genus Calamus and related genera, having tough stems used for wickerwork and canes2. the stems of such plants collectively3. a stick made from one of these stems
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Malay rōtan

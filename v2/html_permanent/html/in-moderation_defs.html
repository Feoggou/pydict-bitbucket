Definitions
phraseIf you say that someone does something such as eat, drink, or smoke in moderation, you mean that they do not eat, smoke, or drink too much or more than is reasonable. OPPOSITE: to excess ⇒ Many adults are able to drink in moderation, but others become dependent on alcohol. ⇒ Fats and oils can be used in moderation. →  moderation

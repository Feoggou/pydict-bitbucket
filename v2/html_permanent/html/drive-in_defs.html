drive-in

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. denoting a public facility or service designed to be used by patrons seated in their cars ⇒ a drive-in banknoun2. mainly US and Canadian a cinema designed to be used in such a manner

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA drive-in is a restaurant, cinema, or other commercial place which is specially designed so that customers can use the services provided while staying in their cars. ⇒ ...fast food drive-ins.Drive-in is also an adjective. ⇒ ...a drive-in movie theater.

Synonyms of harvest

harvest

noun

  1. harvesting, picking, gathering, collecting, reaping, harvest-time
    • 300 million tons of grain in the fields at the start of the harvest
  2. crop, yield, year's growth, produce
    • a bumper potato harvest
  3. product, return, result, effect, consequence, fruition

verb

  1. gather, pick, collect, bring in, pluck, reap
    • Many farmers are refusing to harvest the sugar cane.
  2. collect, get, gain, earn, obtain, acquire, accumulate, garner, amass
    • In his new career he has blossomed and harvested many awards.

halfpenny

or ha'penny (ˈheɪpnɪ , for sense 1 ˈhɑːfˌpɛnɪ )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: half plural -pennies a small British coin worth half a new penny, withdrawn from circulation in 19852. plural -pennies an old British coin worth half an old penny3. plural -pence the sum represented by half a penny4. plural -pence something of negligible value5. (modifier) having the value or price of a halfpenny6. (modifier) of negligible value

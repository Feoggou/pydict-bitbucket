Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf you achieve a result by dint of something, you achieve it by means of that thing. [written] ⇒ He succeeds by dint of sheer hard work. ⇒ He has acquired, by dint of threatening to resign, a directorate-general with about 150 officials. →  dint

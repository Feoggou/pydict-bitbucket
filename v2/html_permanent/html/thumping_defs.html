Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Thumping is used to emphasize that something is very great or severe. [British, informal, emphasis] ⇒ The Right has a thumping majority. ⇒ The gloom deepened after a thumping £145m loss at British Rail.Thumping is also an adverb. ⇒ A thumping good time was had by all.2. → see also thump

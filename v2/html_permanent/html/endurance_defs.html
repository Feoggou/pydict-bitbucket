endurance

(ɪnˈdjʊərəns )
Definitions
noun1. the capacity, state, or an instance of enduring2. something endured; a hardship, strain, or privation

endurance

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndjʊərəns , US -dʊr- ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounEndurance is the ability to continue with an unpleasant or difficult situation, experience, or activity over a long period of time. ⇒ The exercise obviously will improve strength and endurance. ⇒ ...his powers of endurance.

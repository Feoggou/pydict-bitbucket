pinion1

(ˈpɪnjən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. mainly poetic a bird's wing2. the part of a bird's wing including the flight feathersverb (transitive)3. to hold or bind (the arms) of (a person) so as to restrain or immobilize him4. to confine or shackle5. to make (a bird) incapable of flight by removing that part of (the wing) from which the flight feathers grow

Word Origin

C15: from Old French pignon wing, from Latin pinna wing

pinion2

(ˈpɪnjən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna cogwheel that engages with a larger wheel or rack, which it drives or by which it is driven

Word Origin

C17: from French pignon cogwheel, from Old French peigne comb, from Latin pecten comb; see pecten

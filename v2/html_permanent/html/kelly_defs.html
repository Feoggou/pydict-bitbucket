Kelly

(ˈkɛlɪ )
Definitions
noun1.  Gene, full name Eugene Curran Kelly. 1912–96, US dancer, choreographer, film actor, and director. His many films include An American in Paris (1951) and Singin' in the Rain (1952)2.  Grace. 1929–82, US film actress. Her films included High Noon (1952) and High Society (1956). She married Prince Rainier III of Monaco in 1956 and died following a car crash3.  Ned. 1855–80, Australian horse and cattle thief and bushranger, active in Victoria: captured by the police and hanged4. See game as Ned Kelly

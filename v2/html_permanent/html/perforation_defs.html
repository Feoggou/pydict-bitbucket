perforation

(ˌpɜːfəˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of perforating or the state of being perforated2. a hole or holes made in something3. a. a method of making individual stamps, coupons, etc, easily separable by punching holes along their marginsb. the holes punched in this way. Abbreviation: perf

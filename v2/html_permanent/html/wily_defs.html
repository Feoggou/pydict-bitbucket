wily for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(waɪli )
Word forms: wilier , wiliest
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or their behaviour as wily, you mean that they are clever at achieving what they want, especially by tricking people. OPPOSITE: artless ⇒ His appointment as prime minister owed much to the wily manoeuvring of the President.

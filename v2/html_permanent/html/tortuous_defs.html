tortuous

(ˈtɔːtjʊəs )
Definitions
adjective1. twisted or winding ⇒ a tortuous road2. devious or cunning ⇒ a tortuous mind3. intricate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tortuously (ˈtortuously) adverbtortuousness (ˈtortuousness) noun

tortuous

Powered by COBUILD
(tɔːʳtʃuəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A tortuous road is full of bends and twists. winding ⇒ The only road access is a tortuous mountain route.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A tortuous process or piece of writing is very long and complicated. convoluted ⇒ ...these long and tortuous negotiations aimed at ending the conflict. ⇒ The parties must now go through the tortuous process of picking their candidates.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

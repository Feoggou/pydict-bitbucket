Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
ergative reciprocal verb & verbIf two or more things conjoin or if you conjoin them, they are united and joined together. [formal] [V] ⇒ The wisdom of the retired generals and backbench MPs conjoins. [be V-ed + with] ⇒ America's rise in rates was conjoined with higher rates elsewhere. [V n] ⇒ ...if we conjoin the two responses. [Also V n + with, V + with]

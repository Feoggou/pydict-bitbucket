lusty

(ˈlʌstɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: lustier or lustiest1. having or characterized by robust health2. strong or invigorating ⇒ a lusty brew3. lustful

lusty

Powered by COBUILD
(lʌsti ) for learners of English
Word forms: lustier , lustiest
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you say that something is lusty, you mean that it is healthy and full of strength and energy. ⇒ ...plants with large, lusty roots. ⇒ ...remembering his lusty singing in the open park.lustily graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Andrew asserted himself from the moment of his birth, crying lustily when he was hungry. ⇒ Bob ate lustily.

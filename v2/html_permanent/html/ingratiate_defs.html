ingratiate

(ɪnˈɡreɪʃɪˌeɪt )
Definitions
verb(transitive; often foll by with) to place (oneself) purposely in the favour (of another)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ingratiating (inˈgratiˌating) or ingratiatory (inˈgratiatory) adjectiveingratiatingly (inˈgratiˌatingly) adverbingratiation (inˌgratiˈation) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin, from in-2 + grātia grace, favour

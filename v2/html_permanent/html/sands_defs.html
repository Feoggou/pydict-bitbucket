sands

(ˈsændz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. an extensive area of sand, esp at a seashore or in a desert ⇒ miles of golden sands, ⇒ lonely figures in the surrounding desert sands2. See shifting sands3. See the sands are running out

lubber

(ˈlʌbə )
Definitions
noun1. a big, awkward, or stupid person2. short for landlubber
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lubberly (ˈlubberly) adjective, adverblubberliness (ˈlubberliness) noun

Word Origin

C14 lobre, probably from Scandinavian. See lob1

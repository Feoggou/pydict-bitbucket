nightly

(ˈnaɪtlɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. happening or relating to each night2. happening at nightadverb3. at night or each night
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

nightly for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(naɪtli )
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]A nightly event happens every night. ⇒ I'm sure we watched the nightly news, and then we turned on the movie. ⇒ For months at a time, air raids were a nightly occurrence.Nightly is also an adverb. ⇒ She appears nightly on the television news. ⇒ A new and younger crowd filled the bar nightly.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

biographical

Powered by COBUILD
(baɪəgræfɪkəl )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Biographical facts, notes, or details are concerned with the events in someone's life. ⇒ The book contains few biographical details.

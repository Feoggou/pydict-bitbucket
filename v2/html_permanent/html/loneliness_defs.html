loneliness

Powered by COBUILD
(loʊnlinəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounLoneliness is the unhappiness that is felt by someone because they do not have any friends or do not have anyone to talk to. ⇒ I have so many friends, but deep down, underneath, I have a fear of loneliness.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'loneliness'
He suddenly felt a desperate loneliness , a sense of loss, a feeling that nothing in his life had meaning any longer. Forbes, Bryan THE ENDLESS GAMEFor the real torment lay inside him: the torment of remorse, of loss and of loneliness. Stewart, Michael COMPULSIONOnly in the hospital, her heart stopping and restarting, did she feel the weight of her loneliness.

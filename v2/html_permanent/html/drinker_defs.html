Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounIf someone is a tea drinker or a beer drinker, for example, they regularly drink tea or beer. ⇒ Are you a coffee drinker?2. countable nounIf you describe someone as a drinker, you mean that they drink alcohol, especially in large quantities. ⇒ You don't need to be a smoker or a drinker to risk heart disease. ⇒ I'm not a heavy drinker.

inflow

(ˈɪnˌfləʊ )
Definitions
noun1. something, such as a liquid or gas, that flows in2. the amount or rate of flowing in3. Also called: inflowing the act of flowing in; influx
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
countable nounIf there is an inflow of money or people into a place, a large amount of money or people move into a place. [+ of] ⇒ The Swiss wanted to discourage an inflow of foreign money. ⇒ ...the inflow of immigrants from Eastern Europe

Definitions
adjectiveSomething that is fitful happens for irregular periods of time or occurs at irregular times, rather than being continuous. ⇒ Colin drifted off into a fitful sleep. ⇒ The government is making slow and fitful progress in these negotiations.fitfully adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ The sun shone fitfully and then light snow blew into our faces.

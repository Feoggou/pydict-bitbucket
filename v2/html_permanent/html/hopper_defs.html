hopper

(ˈhɒpə )
Definitions
noun1. a person or thing that hops2. a funnel-shaped chamber or reservoir from which solid materials can be discharged under gravity into a receptacle below, esp for feeding fuel to a furnace, loading a railway truck with grain, etc3. a machine used for picking hops4. any of various long-legged hopping insects, esp the grasshopper, leaf hopper, and immature locust5. Also called: hoppercar an open-topped railway truck for bulk transport of loose minerals, etc, unloaded through doors on the underside6. South Africa another name for cocopan7. computing a device formerly used for holding punched cards and feeding them to a card punch or card reader

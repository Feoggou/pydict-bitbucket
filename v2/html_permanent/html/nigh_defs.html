nigh

(naɪ )
Definitions
adjective, adverb, prepositionan archaic, poetic, or dialect word for near
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English nēah, nēh; related to German nah, Old Frisian nei. Compare near, next

nigh for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(naɪ )
Definitions
1. adverb [be ADV]If an event is nigh, it will happen very soon. [old-fashioned] ⇒ The end of the world may be nigh, but do we really care? ⇒ The storm must still be nigh, she thought.2. → See also well-nigh3. →  nigh on
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

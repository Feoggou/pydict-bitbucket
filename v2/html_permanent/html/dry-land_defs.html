dry land

(draɪ lænd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounland as opposed to sea; terra firma ⇒ We take a 30-minute trip up the Thames before returning to dry land., ⇒ We're glad to be on dry land again.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft on N]If you talk about dry land, you are referring to land, in contrast to the sea or the air. ⇒ We were glad to be on dry land again.

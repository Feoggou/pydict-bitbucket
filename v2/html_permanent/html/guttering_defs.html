guttering

(ˈɡʌtərɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. the gutters, downpipes, etc, that make up the rainwater disposal system on the outside of a building2. the materials used in this system

(gʌtərɪŋ) in Automotive Engineering
Definitions
noun(Automotive engineering: Vehicle components, Bodywork, controls, and accessories) Guttering is the curved edge at the side of the roof panel of a vehicle, along which rain water is channeled away. ⇒ External guttering had been replaced by rubber drainage channels inside the tops of doors. ⇒ Drip rails extend from the roof guttering of the car. ⇒ On the coupe, the roof guttering and rear window surround are chromed.

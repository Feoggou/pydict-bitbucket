bombastic

Powered by COBUILD
(bɒmbæstɪk )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as bombastic, you are criticizing them for trying to impress other people by saying things that sound impressive but have little meaning. [disapproval] ⇒ He was vain and bombastic. ⇒ ...the bombastic style adopted by his predecessor.

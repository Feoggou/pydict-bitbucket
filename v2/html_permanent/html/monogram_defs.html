monogram

(ˈmɒnəˌɡræm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a design of one or more letters, esp initials, embroidered on clothing, printed on stationery, etcverbWord forms: monograms, monogramming or monogrammed2. (tr; usually passive) to decorate (clothing, stationery, etc) with a monogram

Derived Forms

monogrammatic (ˌmɒnəɡrəˈmætɪk ) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin monogramma, from Greek; see mono-, -gram

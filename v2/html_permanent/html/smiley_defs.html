smiley

(ˈsmaɪlɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. given to smiling; cheerful2. depicting a smile ⇒ a smiley badgenoun3. any of a group of symbols depicting a smile, or other facial expression, used in electronic mail

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

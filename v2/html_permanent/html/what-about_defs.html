Definitions
phraseYou use what about at the beginning of a question when you make a suggestion, offer, or request. how about ⇒ What about going out with me tomorrow? ⇒ 'What about Sunday evening at Frank's?'—'Sure. What time?'. ⇒ What about you? Would you like to come and live in Paris and work for me? →  what

Definitions
phraseYou say what about a particular person or thing when you ask someone to explain why they have asked you about that person or thing. ⇒ 'This thing with the Corbett woman.'—'Oh, yeah. What about her?' →  what

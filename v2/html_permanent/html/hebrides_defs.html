Hebrides

(ˈhɛbrəˌdiz ) in American English
Definitions
group of islands off the W coast of Scotland: they are divided into the Inner Hebrides, nearer the mainland, & the Outer Hebrides: c. 2,800 sq mi (7,252 sq km); pop. c. 80,000

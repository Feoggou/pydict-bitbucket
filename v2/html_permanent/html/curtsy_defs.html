curtsy

or curtsey (ˈkɜːtsɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -sies or -seys1. a formal gesture of greeting and respect made by women in which the knees are bent, the head slightly bowed, and the skirt held outwardsverb -sies, -sying, -sied, -seys, -seying or -seyed2. (intransitive) to make a curtsy
Word origin
C16: variant of courtesy

Word forms: curtsies , curtsying , curtsied
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf a woman or a girl curtsies, she lowers her body briefly, bending her knees and sometimes holding her skirt with both hands, as a way of showing respect for an important person. [V + to] ⇒ We were taught how to curtsy to the Queen. [V] ⇒ Ingrid shook the Duchess's hand and curtsied.Curtsy is also a noun. ⇒ She gave a curtsy.

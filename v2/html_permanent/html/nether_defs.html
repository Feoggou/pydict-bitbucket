nether

(ˈnɛðə )
Definitions
adjectiveplaced or situated below, beneath, or underground ⇒ nether regions, ⇒ a nether lip
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English niothera, nithera, literally: further down, from nither down. Related to Old Irish nitaram, German nieder

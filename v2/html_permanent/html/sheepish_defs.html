sheepish

(ˈʃiːpɪʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. abashed or embarrassed, esp through looking foolish or being in the wrong2. resembling a sheep in timidity or lack of initiative

Derived Forms

sheepishly (ˈsheepishly) adverbsheepishness (ˈsheepishness) noun

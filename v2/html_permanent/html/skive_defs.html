skive1

(skaɪv )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
verb(transitive) to shave or remove the surface of (leather)

Word Origin

C19: from Old Norse skifa; related to English dialect shive a slice of bread

skive2

(skaɪv )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
verb(when intr, often foll by off) British informal to evade (work or responsibility)

Word Origin

C20: of unknown origin

shipping

(ˈʃɪpɪŋ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. the business of transporting freight, esp by shipb. (as modifier) ⇒ a shipping magnate, ⇒ shipping line2. a. ships collectively ⇒ there is a lot of shipping in the Channelb. the tonnage of a number of ships ⇒ shipping for this year exceeded that of last

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounShipping is the transport of cargo as a business, especially on ships. ⇒ ...the international shipping industry. ⇒ The Greeks are still powerful players in world shipping.2. uncount nounYou can refer to the amount of money that you pay to a company to transport cargo as shipping. ⇒ It is $39.95 plus $3 shipping.3. uncount nounYou can refer to ships as shipping when considering them as a group. ⇒ They sent naval forces to protect merchant shipping.

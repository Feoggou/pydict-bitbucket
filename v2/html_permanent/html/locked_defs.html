locked

(lɒkt)
Definitions
adjective1. fastened with a lock ⇒ He moved along the corridor towards the locked door at the end., ⇒ I tried to open the suitcase but it was locked.2. engaged in an activity in a way that suggests an inability to stop ⇒ locked in battle, ⇒ locked in an embrace
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

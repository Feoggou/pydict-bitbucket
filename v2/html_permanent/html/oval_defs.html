oval

(ˈəʊvəl )
Definitions
adjective1. having the shape of an ellipse or ellipsoidnoun2. anything that is oval in shape, such as a sports ground
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ovally (ˈovally) adverbovalness (ˈovalness) or ovality (əʊˈvælɪtɪ ) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Medieval Latin ōvālis, from Latin ōvum egg

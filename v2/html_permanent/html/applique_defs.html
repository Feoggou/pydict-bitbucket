applique

or appliqué (əˈpliːkeɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. needlework textile art using pieces of material sewn one on top of another ⇒ Beadwork, applique and embroidery add a uniquely feminine touch.2. piece of material sewn on top of anothermodifier3. needlework using appliqué ⇒ a design of green applique holly leaves, ⇒ applique cotton pillowcases

appliqué

or applique
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a decoration or trimming of one material sewn or otherwise fixed onto another2. the practice of decorating in this wayverb -qués, -quéing or -quéd3. (transitive) to sew or fix (a decoration) on as an appliqué
Word origin
C18: from French, literally: applied

marksman

(ˈmɑːksmən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -men1. a person skilled in shooting2. a serviceman selected for his skill in shooting, esp for a minor engagement3. a qualification awarded in certain armed services for skill in shooting

Derived Forms

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA marksman is a person who can shoot very accurately. ⇒ Police marksmen opened fire.2. countable nounIn sports journalism, a marksman is a football player who scores a lot of goals. [British] ⇒ City's principal marksman was Joe Harvey who scored seventeen goals.

retirement

(rɪˈtaɪəmənt )
Definitions
noun1. a. the act of retiring from one's work, office, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ retirement age2. the period of being retired from work ⇒ she had many plans for her retirement3. seclusion from the world; privacy4. the act of going away or retreating

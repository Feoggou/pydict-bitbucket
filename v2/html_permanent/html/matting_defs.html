matting1

(ˈmætɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a coarsely woven fabric, usually made of a natural fibre such as straw or hemp and used as a floor covering, packing material, etc2. the act or process of making mats3. material for mats

(ˈmætɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. another word for mat2 (sense 1)2. the process of producing a mat finish

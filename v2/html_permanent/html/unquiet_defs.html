unquiet

(ʌnˈkwaɪət ) mainly literary
Definitions
adjective1. characterized by disorder, unrest, or tumult ⇒ unquiet times2. anxious; uneasynoun3. a state of unrest
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unquietly (unˈquietly) adverbunquietness (unˈquietness) noun

sunset

(ˈsʌnˌsɛt )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the daily disappearance of the sun below the horizon2. the atmospheric phenomena accompanying this disappearance3. Also called: sundown the time at which the sun sets at a particular locality4. the final stage or closing period, as of a person's life

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounSunset is the time in the evening when the sun disappears out of sight from the sky. sundown ⇒ The dance ends at sunset.2. countable nounA sunset is the colours and light that you see in the western part of the sky when the sun disappears in the evening. ⇒ There was a red sunset over Paris.

indefinitely

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndefɪnɪtli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [ADV with v]If a situation will continue indefinitely, it will continue for ever or until someone decides to change it or end it. ⇒ The visit has now been postponed indefinitely. ⇒ I couldn't stay there indefinitely.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

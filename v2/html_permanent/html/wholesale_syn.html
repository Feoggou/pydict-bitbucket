Synonyms of wholesale

wholesale

adjective

  1. extensive, total, mass, sweeping, broad, comprehensive, wide-ranging, blanket, outright, far-reaching, indiscriminate, all-inclusive
    • the wholesale destruction of life on this planet
    OPPOSITES:  limited, restricted, confined, partial, discriminate, selective

adverb

  1. extensively, comprehensively, across the board, all at once, indiscriminately, without exception, on a large scale
    • The army was burning down houses and killing villagers wholesale.

facies

(ˈfeɪʃɪˌiːz )
Definitions
noun plural -cies1. the general form and appearance of an individual or a group of plants or animals2. the characteristics of a rock or series of rocks reflecting their appearance, composition, and conditions of formation3. medicine the general facial expression of a patient, esp when typical of a specific disease or disorder. See Hippocratic facies
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin: appearance, face

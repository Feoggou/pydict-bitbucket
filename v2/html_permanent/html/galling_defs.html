galling

(ˈɡɔːlɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. irritating, exasperating, or bitterly humiliating2. obsolete rubbing painfully; chafing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gallingly (ˈgallingly) adverb

It was so galling having to smilingly serve food to someone you'd rather chuck it all over.

geometric

(ˌdʒɪəˈmɛtrɪk ) or geometrical
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or following the methods and principles of geometry2. consisting of, formed by, or characterized by points, lines, curves, or surfaces ⇒ a geometric figure3. (of design or ornamentation) composed predominantly of simple geometric forms, such as circles, rectangles, triangles, etc

Definitions
language note:   The form geometrical (dʒiːəmetrɪkəl ) is also used.1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Geometric or geometrical patterns or shapes consist of regular shapes or lines. ⇒ Geometric designs were popular wall decorations in the 14th century.geometrically (dʒiːəmetrɪkli ) adverb ⇒ ...a few geometrically planted trees.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Geometric or geometrical means relating to or involving the principles of geometry. ⇒ Euclid was trying to convey his idea of a geometrical point.

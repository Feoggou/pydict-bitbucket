laryngeal

(ˌlærɪnˈdʒiːəl , ləˈrɪndʒɪəl ) or laryngal (ləˈrɪŋɡəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to the larynx2. phonetics articulated at the larynx; glottal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

laryngeally (larynˈgeally) adverb

Word Origin

C18: from New Latin laryngeus of the larynx

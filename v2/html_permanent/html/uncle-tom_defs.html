Uncle Tom

Definitions
nouninformal, derogatory a Black person whose behaviour towards White people is regarded as obsequious and servile
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Uncle Tomism noun

Word Origin

C20: after the slave who is the main character of H.B. Stowe's novel Uncle Tom's Cabin (1852)

Word forms: Uncle Toms
Definitions
countable nounIn the past, some black people used Uncle Tom to refer to a black man when they disapproved of him because he was too respectful or friendly towards white people. [offensive, disapproval] ⇒ To the radical blacks of the Sixties, he was an Uncle Tom.

glaring

(ˈɡlɛərɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. conspicuous ⇒ a glaring omission2. dazzling or garish
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

glaring

Powered by COBUILD
(gleərɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something bad as glaring, you are emphasizing that it is very obvious and easily seen or noticed. [emphasis] ⇒ I never saw such a glaring example of misrepresentation.glaringly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ It was glaringly obvious. ⇒ He told a glaringly different story.2. → See also glare

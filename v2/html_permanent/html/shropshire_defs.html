Shropshire

(ˈʃrɒpˌʃɪə , -ʃə)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a county of W central England: Telford and Wrekin became an independent unitary authority in 1998, and the remaining county of Shropshire became a unitary authority in 2009; mainly agricultural. Administrative centre: Shrewsbury. Pop (excluding Telford and Wrekin): 286 700 (2003 est). Area (excluding Telford and Wrekin): 3201 sq km (1236 sq miles)2. a breed of medium-sized sheep having a dense fleece, originating from Shropshire and Staffordshire, England

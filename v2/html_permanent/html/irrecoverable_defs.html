irrecoverable

(ˌɪrɪˈkʌvərəbəl , -ˈkʌvrə-)
Definitions
adjective1. not able to be recovered or regained2. not able to be remedied or rectified
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

irrecoverableness (ˌirreˈcoverableness) nounirrecoverably (ˌirreˈcoverably) adverb

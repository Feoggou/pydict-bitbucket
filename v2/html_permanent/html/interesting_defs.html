Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you find something interesting, it attracts your attention, for example because you think it is exciting or unusual. ⇒ It was interesting to be in a different environment. ⇒ The two halves of the town face each other, and both have interesting churches. ⇒ His third album is by far his most interesting.

perversity

(pəˈvɜːsɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the quality or state of being perverse2. a perverse action, comment, etc
Example sentences containing 'perversity'
It was like the perversity of fate that death could not leave her alone once it had begun. Murray, Stephen DEATH AND TRANSFIGURATIONArthur took a second sip out of sheer perversity , swallowed. Nicola Barker BEHINDLINGSNo one really knew the depths of perversity in MacDougal's heart. Smiley, Jane BARN BLIND

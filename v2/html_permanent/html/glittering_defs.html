glittering

(ˈɡlɪtərɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. successful, impressive ⇒ a glittering academic career, ⇒ His school coach predicted a glittering career for the youngster.2. glamorous ⇒ a glittering occasion3. sparkling ⇒ His glittering eyes opened wide with alarm., ⇒ glittering Christmas trees

Derived Forms

glitteringly (ˈglitteringly) adverb

terse

(tɜːs )
Definitions
adjective1. neatly brief and concise2. curt; abrupt
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tersely (ˈtersely) adverbterseness (ˈterseness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin tersus precise, from tergēre to polish

decretal

(dɪˈkriːtəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Roman Catholic Church a papal edict on doctrine or church lawadjective2. of or relating to a decretal or a decree
Derived forms
decretalist (deˈcretalist) or decretist (deˈcretist) noun
Word origin
C15: from Old French, from Late Latin dēcrētālis; see decree

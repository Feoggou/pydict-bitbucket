Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseYou can use as hell after adjectives or some adverbs to emphasize the adjective or adverb. [informal, emphasis] ⇒ The men might be armed, but they sure as hell weren't trained. ⇒ I am angry as hell. →  hell

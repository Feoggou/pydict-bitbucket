pairing

(ˈpɛərɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. two people, esp sports players, actors, or musicians, who are working together as a pair ⇒ Tottenham's new midfield pairing are confident of a win., ⇒ In first place we now find the Belgian pairing of Nancy Feber and Laurence Courtois., ⇒ The mixed doubles pairing of Russell Hogg and Kirsteen McEwan.2. the action of putting two people together ⇒ Moviegoers were intrigued by the pairing of Hanks and Newman., ⇒ Her dessert was a luscious pairing of apricot and nutty flavours., ⇒ Is it the pairing of the lamb and beans, or the beans and mint?

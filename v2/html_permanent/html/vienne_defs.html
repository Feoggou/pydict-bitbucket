Vienne

(French vjɛn)
Definitions
noun1. a department of W central France, in Poitou-Charentes region. Capital: Poitiers. Pop: 402 555 (2003 est). Area: 7044 sq km (2747 sq miles)2. a town in SE France, on the River Rhône: extensive Roman remains. Ancient name: Vienna3. a river in SW central France, flowing west and north to the Loire below Chinon. Length: over 350 km (200 miles)

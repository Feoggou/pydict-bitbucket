dazed

(deɪzd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivein a state of stunned confusion or shock ⇒ He himself had been assaulted nearby and left dazed., ⇒ At the end of the interview I was dazed and exhausted., ⇒ Dazed survivors were being helped from the wreck.

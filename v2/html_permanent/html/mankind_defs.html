mankind

(ˌmænˈkaɪnd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. human beings collectively; humanity2. men collectively, as opposed to womankind▶ USAGE Nowadays many people object to the use of mankind to refer to all human beings and use the term humankind instead

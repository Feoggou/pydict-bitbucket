dividing line

(dɪˈvaɪdɪŋ laɪn)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a quality that distinguishes one thing from another ⇒ What is the dividing line between 'pretty' and 'beautiful'??, ⇒ Often the dividing line is blurred., ⇒ There's a very thin dividing line between joviality and hysteria.2. a boundary ⇒ People on both sides of the dividing line between Israel and the occupied territories.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu sing]A dividing line is a distinction or set of distinctions which marks the difference between two types of thing or two groups. [+ between] ⇒ There's a very thin dividing line between joviality and hysteria.2. singular nounThe dividing line between two areas is the boundary between them. [+ between] ⇒ ...people on both sides of the dividing line between Israel and the occupied territories.

exigencies

(ˈɛksɪdʒənsɪz)
Definitions
plural noun1. See exigency2. formal the exigencies of a situation or a job are the demands or difficulties that you have to deal with as part of it ⇒ His book suggests that men have been enslaved by social exigencies since hunter-gatherer days., ⇒ the exigencies of a wartime economy.

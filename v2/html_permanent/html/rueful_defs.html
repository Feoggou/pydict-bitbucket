rueful

(ˈruːfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. feeling or expressing sorrow or repentance ⇒ a rueful face2. inspiring sorrow or pity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is rueful, they feel or express regret or sorrow in a quiet and gentle way. [literary] ⇒ He shook his head and gave me a rueful smile. ⇒ 'Our marriage was a mistake,' she said, looking rueful.ruefully graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ He grinned at her ruefully.

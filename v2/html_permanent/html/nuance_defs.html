nuance

(njuːˈɑːns , ˈnjuːɑːns )
Definitions
noun1. a subtle difference in colour, meaning, tone, etc; a shade or graduationverb (tr; passive)2. to give subtle differences to ⇒ carefully nuanced words
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from French, from nuer to show light and shade, ultimately from Latin nūbēs a cloud

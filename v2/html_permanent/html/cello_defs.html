cello

(ˈtʃɛləʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -losmusic a bowed stringed instrument of the violin family. Range: more than four octaves upwards from C below the bass staff. It has four strings, is held between the knees, and has an extendible metal spike at the lower end, which acts as a support. Full name: violoncello
Derived forms

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounA cello is a musical instrument with four strings that looks like a large violin. You play the cello with a bow while sitting down and holding it upright between your legs.

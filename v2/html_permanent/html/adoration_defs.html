adoration

(ædɔːreɪʃən )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounAdoration is a feeling of great admiration and love for someone or something. ⇒ She needs and wants to be loved with overwhelming passion and adoration. ⇒ He had been used to female adoration all his life.

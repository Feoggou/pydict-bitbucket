(lɪsnəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: listeners
Definitions
1. countable nounA listener is a person who listens to the radio or to a particular radio programme. [+ to] ⇒ I'm a regular listener to her show. ⇒ Each week Dr Buczacki chooses a listener's question and gives a detailed answer.2. countable noun [adj N]If you describe someone as a good listener, you mean that they listen carefully and sympathetically to you when you talk, for example about your problems. ⇒ Dr Brian was a good listener. ⇒ If you can be a sympathetic listener, it may put your own problems in perspective.3. → See also listen

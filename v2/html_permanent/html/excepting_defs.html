excepting

(ɪkˈsɛptɪŋ )
Definitions
preposition1. excluding; except; except for (esp in the phrase not excepting)conjunction2. an archaic word for unless▶ USAGE The use of excepting is considered by many people to be acceptable only after not, only, or without. Elsewhere except is preferred: every country agreed to the proposal except (not excepting) Spain; he was well again except for (not excepting) a slight pain in his chest

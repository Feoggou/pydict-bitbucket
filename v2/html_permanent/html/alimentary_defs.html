alimentary

(ˌælɪˈmɛntərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to nutrition2. providing sustenance or nourishment3. Scots law free from the claims of creditors ⇒ an alimentary trust
Example sentences containing 'alimentary'

cherished

(ˈtʃɛrɪʃt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. clung to, esp when fulfilment is unlikely ⇒ the cherished dream of a world without wars2. treasured ⇒ He described the picture as his most cherished possession.3. highly valued, honoured, and respected ⇒ a weekend at home, a cherished privilege that presents itself only every so often, ⇒ UN membership would destroy his country's cherished neutrality, ⇒ Freud called into question some deeply cherished beliefs.

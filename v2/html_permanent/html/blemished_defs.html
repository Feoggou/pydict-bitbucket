Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivespoilt by physical or moral blemishes ⇒ a skin tonic for oily, blemished complexions, ⇒ She hoped the book would restore her blemished reputation.

blemished

(blemɪʃt )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]You use blemished to describe something such as someone's skin or a piece of fruit when its appearance is spoiled by small marks. ⇒ ...a skin tonic for oily, blemished complexions.
Example sentences containing 'blemished'

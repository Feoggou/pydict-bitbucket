(ˌmɒdəˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the state or an instance of being moderate; mildness; balance2. the act of moderating3. See in moderation

moderation

Powered by COBUILD
(mɒdəreɪʃən )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounIf you say that someone's behaviour shows moderation, you approve of them because they act in a way that you think is reasonable and not extreme. [approval] ⇒ The United Nations Secretary General called on all parties to show moderation. ⇒ ...the moderation and deep sense of responsibility which characterized their thinking.→  in moderation2. → see also moderate

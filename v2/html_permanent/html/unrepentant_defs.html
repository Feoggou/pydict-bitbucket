unrepentant for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnrɪpentənt )
Definitions
adjectiveIf you are unrepentant, you are not ashamed of your beliefs or actions. unabashed [+ about] ⇒ Pamela was unrepentant about her strong language and abrasive remarks. ⇒ ...unrepentant defenders of the death penalty.

blackness

Powered by COBUILD
(blæknəs )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounBlackness is the state of being very dark. [literary] ⇒ The twilight had turned to a deep blackness.

She felt her mind, her spirit, her flesh dissolving into a nebula of pure ecstasy burning across the blackness of outer space.

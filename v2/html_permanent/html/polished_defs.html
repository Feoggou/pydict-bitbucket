polished

(ˈpɒlɪʃt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. accomplished ⇒ a polished actor2. impeccably or professionally done ⇒ a polished performance3. (of rice) having had the outer husk removed by milling

polished

Powered by COBUILD
(pɒlɪʃt )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]Someone who is polished shows confidence and knows how to behave socially. [approval] ⇒ He is polished, charming, articulate and an excellent negotiator.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a performance, ability, or skill as polished, you mean that it is of a very high standard. [approval] ⇒ It was simply a very polished performance. ⇒ ...polished promotional skills.3. → see also polish

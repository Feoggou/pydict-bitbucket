naught

(nɔːt )
Definitions
noun1. archaic or literary nothing or nothingness; ruin or failure2. a variant spelling (esp US) of nought3. See set at naughtadverb4. archaic or literary not at all ⇒ it matters naughtadjective5. obsolete worthless, ruined, or wicked
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English nāwiht, from nā no1 + wiht thing, person; see wight1, whit

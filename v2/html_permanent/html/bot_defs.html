bot1

or bott (bɒt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the larva of a botfly, which typically develops inside the body of a horse, sheep, or human2. any similar larva3. New Zealand informal a mild illness in humans
Word origin
C15: probably from Low German; related to Dutch bot, of obscure origin

bot2 - Australian informal

(bɒt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to scrounge or borrow2. (intransitive; often foll by on) to scrounge (from); impose (on)noun3. a scrounger4. See on the bot
Word origin
C20: perhaps from botfly, alluding to the creature's bite; see bite (sense 12)

procession

(prəˈsɛʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of proceeding in a regular formation2. a group of people or things moving forwards in an orderly, regular, or ceremonial manner3. a hymn, litany, etc, sung in a procession4. Christianity the emanation of the Holy Spiritverb5. (intransitive) rare to go in procession

Word Origin

C12: via Old French from Latin prōcessiō a marching forwards

Synonyms of thump

thump

noun

  1. blow, knock, punch, rap, smack, clout (informal), whack, swipe, wallop (informal)
    • He felt a thump on his shoulder.
  2. thud, crash, bang, clunk, thwack
    • There was a loud thump as the horse crashed into the van.

verb

  1. strike, hit, punch, pound, beat, knock, deck (slang), batter, rap, chin (slang), smack, thrash, clout (informal), whack, swipe, clobber (slang), wallop (informal), lambast(e), belabour, lay one on (slang), beat or knock seven bells out of (informal)
    • He thumped me, nearly knocking me over.
  2. thud, crash, bang, thwack
    • She thumped her hand on the witness box.
  3. throb, pound, beat, pulse, pulsate, palpitate
    • My heart was thumping wildly.

Mother Shipton

(ˈʃɪptən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna day-flying noctuid moth, Callistege mi, mottled brown in colour and named from a fancied resemblance between its darker marking and a haggish profile

Word Origin

named after Mother Shipton, a legendary prophetess in 15th-century Yorkshire

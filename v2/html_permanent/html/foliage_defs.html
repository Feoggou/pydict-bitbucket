foliage

(ˈfəʊlɪɪdʒ )
Definitions
noun1. the green leaves of a plant2. sprays of leaves used for decoration3. an ornamental leaflike design
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

foliaged (ˈfoliaged) adjective

Word Origin

C15: from Old French fuellage, from fuelle leaf; influenced in form by Latin folium

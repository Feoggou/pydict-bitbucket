Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. verbIf you prep something, you prepare it. [mainly US, informal] [V n] ⇒ After prepping the boat, they sailed it down to Carloforte.2. verbIf a doctor or nurse preps a patient, they get them ready for surgery or another procedure. [mainly US, informal] [be V-ed] ⇒ I was already prepped for surgery so I went straight into theatre. [Also V n]3. uncount nounIn some British private schools, prep is the name given to school work that children do in the evening after school has finished. [British] homework ⇒ Anne struggled to help Peter and Zara with their prep.

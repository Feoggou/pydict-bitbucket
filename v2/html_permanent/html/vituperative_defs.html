vituperative

Powered by COBUILD
(vɪtjuːpərətɪv , US vaɪtuːp- ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]Vituperative remarks are full of hate, anger, or insults. [formal] ⇒ He is often the victim of vituperative remarks concerning his wealth. ⇒ ...one of journalism's most vituperative critics.

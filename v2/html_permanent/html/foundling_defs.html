(ˈfaʊndlɪŋ )
Definitions
nounan abandoned infant whose parents are not known

Word Origin

(faʊndlɪŋ ) for learners of English

Definitions
countable nounA foundling is a baby that has been abandoned by its parents, often in a public place, and that has then been found by someone. [old-fashioned]

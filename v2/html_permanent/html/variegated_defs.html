variegated

(ˈvɛərɪˌɡeɪtɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. displaying differently coloured spots, patches, streaks, etc2. (of foliage or flowers) having pale patches, usually as a result of mutation, infection, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

variegated

Powered by COBUILD
(veəriəgeɪtɪd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]A variegated leaf or plant has different colours on it. [technical] ⇒ The leaves are a variegated red.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is variegated consists of many different parts or types. [formal] ⇒ ...our variegated dialects.

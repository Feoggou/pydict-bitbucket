quartern

(ˈkwɔːtən )
Definitions
noun1. a fourth part of certain weights or measures, such as a peck or a pound2. Also called: quartern loaf Britisha. a type of loaf 4 inches square, used esp for making sandwichesb. any loaf weighing 1600 g when baked
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old French quarteron, from quart a quarter

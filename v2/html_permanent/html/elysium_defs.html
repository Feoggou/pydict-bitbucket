Elysium

(ɪˈlɪzɪəm )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: Elysian fields Greek mythology the dwelling place of the blessed after death. See also Islands of the Blessed2. a state or place of perfect bliss
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin, from Greek Ēlusion pedion Elysian (that is, blessed) fields
Example sentences containing 'Elysium'
He looked at me with a kind of impatient, tourist sympathy, a man passing quickly through problem country on his way to Elysium. Kallen, Lucille C B GREENFIELD - A LITTLE MADNESS

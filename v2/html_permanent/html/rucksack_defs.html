rucksack

(ˈrʌkˌsæk )
Definitions
nouna large bag, usually having two straps and a supporting frame, carried on the back and often used by climbers, campers, etc. US and Canadian name: backpack
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from German, literally: back sack

Definitions
countable nounA rucksack is a bag with straps that go over your shoulders, so that you can carry things on your back, for example when you are walking or climbing. [British] backpackregional note:   in AM, usually use knapsack, pack, backpack

(ɪˈrɛfjʊtəbəl , ˌɪrɪˈfjuːtəbəl )
Definitions
adjectiveimpossible to deny or disprove; incontrovertible

adjectiveIrrefutable evidence, statements, or arguments cannot be shown to be incorrect or unsatisfactory. [formal] ⇒ The pictures provide irrefutable evidence of the incident. ⇒ Her logic was irrefutable.

thrifty

(ˈθrɪftɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: thriftier or thriftiest1. showing thrift; economical or frugal2. rare thriving or prospering

Derived Forms

thriftily (ˈthriftily) adverbthriftiness (ˈthriftiness) noun

thrifty

Powered by COBUILD
(θrɪfti )
Word forms: thriftier , thriftiest
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that someone is thrifty, you are praising them for saving money, not buying unnecessary things, and not wasting things. [approval] ⇒ My mother taught me to be thrifty. ⇒ ...thrifty shoppers.
Example sentences containing 'thrifty'
"He'd noted the chill in her house, but had said nothing, understanding her desire to be thrifty with the wood. S. M. Stirling T2®: THE FUTURE WAR

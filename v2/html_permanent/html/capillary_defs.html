capillary

(kəˈpɪlərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. resembling a hair; slender2. (of tubes) having a fine bore3. anatomy of or relating to any of the delicate thin-walled blood vessels that form an interconnecting network between the arterioles and the venules4. physics of or relating to capillaritynoun plural -laries5. anatomy any of the capillary blood vessels6. a fine hole or narrow passage in any substance
Word origin
C17: from Latin capillāris, from capillus hair

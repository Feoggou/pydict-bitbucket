pent-up

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective (pent up when postpositive)1. not released; repressed ⇒ pent-up emotions2. kept unwillingly ⇒ I've been pent up in this office for over a year

pent-up

Powered by COBUILD
(pent ʌp )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Pent-up emotions, energies, or forces have been held back and not expressed, used, or released. ⇒ He still had a lot of pent-up anger to release.

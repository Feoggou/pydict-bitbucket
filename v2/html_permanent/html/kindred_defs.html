kindred

(ˈkɪndrɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. having similar or common qualities, origin, etc2. related by blood or marriage3. See kindred spiritnoun4. relationship by blood5. similarity in character6. a person's relatives collectively
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

kindredness (ˈkindredness) or kindredship (ˈkindredˌship) noun

Word Origin

C12 kinred, from kin + -red, from Old English rǣden rule, from rǣdan to rule

[dialect, or old-fashioned] ⇒ The offender made proper restitution to the victim's kindred.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Kindred things are similar to each other.

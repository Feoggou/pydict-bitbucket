pant

(pænt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to breathe with noisy deep gasps, as when out of breath from exertion or excitement2. to say (something) while breathing thus3. (intransitive; often foll by for) to have a frantic desire (for); yearn4. (intransitive) to pulsate; throb rapidlynoun5. the act or an instance of panting6. a short deep gasping noise; puff

Word Origin

C15: from Old French pantaisier, from Greek phantasioun to have visions, from phantasia fantasy

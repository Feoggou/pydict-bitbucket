impoverished

(ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃt)
Definitions
adjective1. made poor or with diminished quality of life ⇒ one of the most impoverished suburbs of the city, ⇒ an attempt to lure businesses into impoverished areas2. (of soil) deprived of fertility ⇒ plants that impoverish the soil quickly

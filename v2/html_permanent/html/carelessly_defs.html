Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]If someone does something carelessly, they do it without much thought or effort. [written] ⇒ He carelessly left the garage door unlocked. ⇒ 'Oh,' he said carelessly. 'I'm in no hurry to get back.'2. → see also careless

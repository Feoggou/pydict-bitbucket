doff

(dɒf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to take off or lift (one's hat) in salutation2. to remove (clothing)
Derived forms
doffer (ˈdoffer) noun
Word origin
Old English dōn of; see do1, off; compare don1

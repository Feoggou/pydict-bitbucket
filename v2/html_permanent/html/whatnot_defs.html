whatnot

(ˈwɒtˌnɒt )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: what-d'you-call-it informal a person or thing the name of which is unknown, temporarily forgotten, or deliberately overlooked2. informal unspecified assorted material3. a portable stand with shelves, used for displaying ornaments, etc

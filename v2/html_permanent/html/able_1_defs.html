able

(ˈeɪbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (postpositive) having the necessary power, resources, skill, time, opportunity, etc, to do something ⇒ able to swim2. capable; competent; talented ⇒ an able teacher3. law qualified, competent, or authorized to do some specific act
Word origin
C14: ultimately from Latin habilis easy to hold, manageable, apt, from habēre to have, hold + -ilis -ile

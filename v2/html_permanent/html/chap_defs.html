chap1

(tʃæp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verbWord forms: chaps, chapping or chapped1. (of the skin) to make or become raw and cracked, esp by exposure to cold2. Scottish (of a clock) to strike (the hour)3. Scottish to knock (at a door, window, etc)noun4. (usually plural) a cracked or sore patch on the skin caused by chapping5. Scottish a knock
Word origin
C14: probably of Germanic origin; compare Middle Dutch, German kappen to chop off

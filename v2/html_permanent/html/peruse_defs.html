peruse

(pəˈruːz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to read or examine with care; study2. to browse or read through in a leisurely way

Derived Forms

perusal (peˈrusal) nounperuser (peˈruser) noun

Word Origin

C15 (meaning: to use up): from per- (intensive) + use

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf you peruse something such as a letter, article, or document, you read it. [formal] [V n] ⇒ We perused the company's financial statements for the past five years. [V n] ⇒ She found the information while she was perusing a copy of Life magazine.

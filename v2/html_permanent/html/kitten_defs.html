kitten

(ˈkɪtən )
Definitions
noun1. a young cat2. See have kittensverb3. (of cats) to give birth to (young)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

kitten-like (ˈkitten-ˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Old Northern French caton, from cat1; probably influenced by Middle English kiteling

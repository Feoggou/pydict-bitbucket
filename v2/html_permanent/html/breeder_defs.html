breeder

(ˈbriːdə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who breeds plants or animals2. something that reproduces, esp to excess ⇒ rabbits are persistent breeders3. an animal kept for breeding purposes4. a source or cause ⇒ a breeder of discontent5. short for breeder reactor

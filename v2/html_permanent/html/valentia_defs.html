Valentia

(vəˈlɛntɪə)
Definitions
nouna port in E Spain, capital of Valencia province, on the Mediterranean: the third largest city in Spain; capital of the Moorish kingdom of Valencia (1021–1238); university (1501). Pop: 780 653 (2003 est). the Latin name for Valencia (sense 1)

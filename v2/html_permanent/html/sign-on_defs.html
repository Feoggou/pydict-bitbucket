sign on

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (adverb)1. (transitive) to hire or employ2. (intransitive) to commit oneself to a job, activity, etc3. (intransitive) British to register as unemployed with the Department of Social Security

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrasal verbWhen an unemployed person signs on, they officially inform the authorities that they are unemployed, so that they can receive money from the government in order to live. [British] [V P prep] ⇒ He has signed on at the job centre. [V P n] ⇒ I had to sign on the dole on Monday. →  sign

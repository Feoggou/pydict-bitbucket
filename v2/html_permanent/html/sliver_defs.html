sliver

(ˈslɪvə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a thin piece that is cut or broken off lengthwise; splinter2. a loose strand or fibre obtained by cardingverb3. to divide or be divided into splinters; split4. (transitive) to form (wool, etc) into slivers

Derived Forms

sliver-ˌlike (ˈsliver-ˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from sliven to split

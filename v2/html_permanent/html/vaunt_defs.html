Word Frequency

vaunt

(vɔːnt )
Definitions
verb1. (transitive) to describe, praise, or display (one's success, possessions, etc) boastfully2. (intransitive) rare or literary to use boastful language; bragnoun3. a boast4. archaic ostentatious display
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vaunter (ˈvaunter) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Old French vanter, from Late Latin vānitāre to brag, from Latin vānus vain

instigate

(ˈɪnstɪˌɡeɪt )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to bring about, as by incitement or urging ⇒ to instigate rebellion2. to urge on to some drastic or inadvisable action
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

instigatingly (ˈinstiˌgatingly) adverbinstigation (ˌinstiˈgation) nouninstigative (ˈinstiˌgative) adjectiveinstigator (ˈinstiˌgator) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin instīgāre to stimulate, incite; compare Greek stizein to prick

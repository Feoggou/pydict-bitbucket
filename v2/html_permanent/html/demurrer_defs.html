demurrer

(dɪˈmʌrə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. law a pleading that admits an opponent's point but denies that it is a relevant or valid argument2. any objection raised
Example sentences containing 'demurrer'
She repeated her demurrer : "Mr. Jefferson is unavailable at the present instant. William Lashner FATAL FLAW

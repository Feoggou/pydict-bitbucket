pectoral

(ˈpɛktərəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to the chest, breast, or thorax ⇒ pectoral fins2. worn on the breast or chest ⇒ a pectoral medallion3. rare heartfelt or sincerenoun4. a pectoral organ or part, esp a muscle or fin5. a medicine or remedy for disorders of the chest or lungs6. anything worn on the chest or breast for decoration or protection

Derived Forms

pectorally (ˈpectorally) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Latin pectorālis, from pectus breast

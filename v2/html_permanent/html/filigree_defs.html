filigree

(ˈfɪlɪˌɡriː ), filagree, fillagree or filigrain (ˈfɪlɪˌɡreɪn)
Definitions
noun1. delicate ornamental work of twisted gold, silver, or other wire2. any fanciful delicate ornamentationadjective3. made of or as if with filigreeverb -grees, -greeing or -greed4. (transitive) to decorate with or as if with filigree
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

filigreed (ˈfiliˌgreed) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from earlier filigreen, from French filigrane, from Latin fīlum thread + grānum grain

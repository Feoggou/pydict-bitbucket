transcript

(ˈtrænskrɪpt )
Definitions
noun1. a written, typed, or printed copy or manuscript made by transcribing2. education, mainly US and Canadian an official record of a student's school progress and achievements3. any reproduction or copy
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Latin transcriptum, from transcrībere to transcribe

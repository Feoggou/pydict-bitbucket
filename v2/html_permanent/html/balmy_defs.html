balmy

(ˈbɑːmɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: balmier or balmiest1. (of weather) mild and pleasant2. having the qualities of balm; fragrant or soothing3. a variant spelling of barmy
Derived forms
balmily (ˈbalmily) adverbbalminess (ˈbalminess) noun

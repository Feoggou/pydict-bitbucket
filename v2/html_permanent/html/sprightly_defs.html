sprightly

(ˈspraɪtlɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective -lier or -liest1. full of vitality; livelyadverb2. obsolete in a lively manner

Derived Forms

sprightliness (ˈsprightliness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from spright, variant of sprite + -ly1

Synonyms of plane

plane

noun

  1. aeroplane, aircraft, jet, airliner, jumbo jet
    • He had plenty of time to catch his plane.
  2. flat surface, the flat, horizontal, level surface
    • a building with angled planes
  3. level, position, stage, footing, condition, standard, degree, rung, stratum, echelon
    • life on a higher plane of existence

adjective

  1. level, even, flat, regular, plain, smooth, uniform, flush, horizontal
    • a plane surface

verb

  1. skim, sail, skate, glide, volplane
    • The boats planed across the lake with the greatest of ease.

Jove in American English

(dʒoʊv )
Definitions
noun Jupiter, the Roman god by Jove! an exclamation used to express astonishment, emphasis, etc.
Webster’s New World College Dictionary, Fourth Edition, compiled by the editors of Webster’s New World Dictionaries. Copyright © 2010 by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. Published by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. All rights reserved.

Word Origin

< L Jovis (used as gen. of Juppiter, Jupiter) < OL Jovis (gen. Jovis) < IE *diwes, gen. of *dyēus (> Gr Zeus) < base *dei-, to gleam, shine > deity

Jove

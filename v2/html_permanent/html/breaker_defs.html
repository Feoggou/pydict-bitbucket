breaker1

(ˈbreɪkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that breaks something, such as a person or firm that breaks up old cars, etc2. a large wave with a white crest on the open sea or one that breaks into foam on the shore3. electronics short for circuit breaker4. a machine or plant for crushing rocks or coal5. Also called: breaking plough a plough with a long shallow mouldboard for turning virgin land or sod land6. textiles a machine for extracting fibre preparatory to carding7. an operator on citizens' band radio

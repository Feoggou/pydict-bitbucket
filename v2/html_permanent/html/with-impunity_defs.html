Definitions
phraseIf you say that someone does something with impunity, you disapprove of the fact that they are not punished for doing something bad. [disapproval] ⇒ Mr Cook said future aggressors would be able to act with impunity if the objectives of the U.N. weren't met. ⇒ These gangs operate with apparent impunity. →  impunity

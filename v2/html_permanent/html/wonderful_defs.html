wonderful

(ˈwʌndəfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. exciting a feeling of wonder; marvellous or strange2. extremely fine; excellent
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something or someone as wonderful, you think they are extremely good. ⇒ The cold, misty air felt wonderful on his face. ⇒ It's wonderful to see you. ⇒ I've always thought he was a wonderful actor.wonderfully graded adverb & adverb ⇒ It's a system that works wonderfully well. ⇒ The weather was wonderfully warm.

poetic

(pəʊˈɛtɪk ) or poetical
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to poetry2. characteristic of poetry, as in being elevated, sublime, etc3. characteristic of a poet4. recounted in verse

Derived Forms

poetically (poˈetically) adverb

poetic

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is poetic is very beautiful and expresses emotions in a sensitive or moving way. ⇒ Nikolai Demidenko gave an exciting yet poetic performance.poetically graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj] ⇒ The speech was as poetically written as any he'd ever heard.2. adjectivePoetic means relating to poetry. ⇒ There's a very rich poetic tradition in Gaelic.

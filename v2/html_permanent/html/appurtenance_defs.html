appurtenance

(əˈpɜːtɪnəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a secondary or less significant thing or part2. (plural) accessories or equipment3. property law a minor right, interest, or privilege which passes when the title to the principal property is transferred
Word origin
C14: from Anglo-French apurtenance, from Old French apartenance, from apartenir to appertain

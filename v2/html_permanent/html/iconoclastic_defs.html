Powered by COBUILD
(aɪkɒnəklæstɪk ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or their words or ideas as iconoclastic, you mean that they contradict established beliefs. [formal] ⇒ Is it utopian to hope that such iconoclastic ideas will gain ground? ⇒ His iconoclastic tendencies can get him into trouble.

undemocratic for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌndeməkrætɪk )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveA system, process, or decision that is undemocratic is one that is controlled or made by one person or a small number of people, rather than by all the people involved. ⇒ ...the undemocratic rule of the former political establishment. ⇒ Opponents denounced the decree as undemocratic and unconstitutional. ⇒ ...the undemocratic seizure of power by the military.

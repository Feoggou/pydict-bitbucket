human nature

Definitions
noun1. the qualities common to humanity2. ordinary human behaviour, esp considered as less than perfect3. sociology the unique elements that form a basic part of human life and distinguish it from other animal life
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

protector

(prəˈtɛktə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that protects2. history a person who exercised royal authority during the minority, absence, or incapacity of the monarch

Derived Forms

protectoral (proˈtectoral) adjectiveprotectress (proˈtectress) or protectrix (proˈtectrix) feminine noun

Protector

(prəˈtɛktə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounshort for Lord Protector, the title borne by Oliver Cromwell (1653–58) and by Richard Cromwell (1658–59) as heads of state during the period known as the Protectorate

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounIf you refer to someone as your protector, you mean that they protect you from being harmed. ⇒ Many mothers see their son as a potential protector and provider.2. countable noun [usu n N]A protector is a device that protects someone or something from physical harm. ⇒ He was the only National League umpire to wear an outside chest protector.

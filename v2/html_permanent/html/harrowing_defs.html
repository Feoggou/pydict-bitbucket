harrowing

Powered by COBUILD
(hæroʊɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A harrowing experience is extremely upsetting or disturbing. ⇒ You've had a harrowing time this past month. ⇒ ...harrowing pictures of the children who had been murdered.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

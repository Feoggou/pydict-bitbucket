rathe

(reɪð ) or rath (rɑːθ )
Definitions
adjective archaic or literary1. blossoming or ripening early in the season2. eager or prompt
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English hrathe; related to Old High German hrado, Old Norse hrathr
Example sentences containing 'rathe'
said Rye with enough frost in her voice to blast a rathe primrose. Anthony Masters CASCADES - THE DAY OF THE DEAD

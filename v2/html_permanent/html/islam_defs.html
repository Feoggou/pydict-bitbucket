Islam

(ˈɪzlɑːm )
Definitions
noun1. the religion of Muslims, having the Koran as its sacred scripture and teaching that there is only one God and that Mohammed is his prophet; Mohammedanism2. a. Muslims collectively and their civilizationb. the countries where the Muslim religion is predominant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Islamic (Isˈlamic) adjective

Word Origin

C19: from Arabic: surrender (to God), from aslama to surrender

Powered by COBUILD
(dʒiːəlɒdʒɪkəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]Geological means relating to geology. ⇒ With geological maps, books and atlases you can find out all the proven sites of precious minerals. ⇒ ...a lengthy geological survey.geologically (dʒiːəlɒdʒɪkli ) adverb ⇒ At least 10,000 of these hectares are geologically unsuitable for housing.

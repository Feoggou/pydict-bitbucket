Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA protuberance is a rounded part that sticks out from the surface of something. [formal] ⇒ ...a protuberance on the upper jawbone.
Example sentences containing 'protuberance'
She put a sand-coated hand to her head and pressed the fleshy protuberance on her temple. Robert Wilson THE COMPANY OF STRANGERSWe could see it then, a slight protuberance and the growth rings not quite matching.

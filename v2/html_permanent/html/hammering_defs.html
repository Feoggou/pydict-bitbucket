hammering

(ˈhæmərɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the action of striking with a hammer ⇒ The noise of hammering was dulled by the secondary glazing.2. British the act of criticizing ⇒ Parents have taken a terrible hammering.3. sport informal an easy defeat ⇒ Our cricketers are suffering their ritual hammering at the hands of the Aussies.4. the action of hitting a surface several times to make a noise, or to emphasize something being said when angry ⇒ As he said it, there was a hammering outside.

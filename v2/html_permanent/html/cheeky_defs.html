Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a person or their behaviour as cheeky, you think that they are slightly rude or disrespectful but in a charming or amusing way. [mainly British] saucy ⇒ The boy was cheeky and casual. ⇒ Martin gave her a cheeky grin.cheekily (tʃiːkɪli ) graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ He strolled cheekily past the commissionaires for a free wash in the gentlemen's cloakroom.

tragic

(ˈtrædʒɪk ) or less commonly tragical (ˈtrædʒɪkəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of tragedy2. mournful or pitiable ⇒ a tragic face
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tragically (ˈtragically) adverb

tragic

Powered by COBUILD
(trædʒɪk ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA tragic event or situation is extremely sad, usually because it involves death or suffering. ⇒ It was just a tragic accident. ⇒ ...the tragic loss of so many lives. ⇒ The circumstances are tragic but we have to act within the law.tragically (trædʒɪkli ) graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj/adv] ⇒ Tragically, she never saw the completed building because she died before it was finished. ⇒ My father died very suddenly and very tragically.2. adjective [ADJ n]Tragic is used to refer to tragedy as a type of literature. ⇒ ...Michael Henchard, the tragic hero of 'The Mayor of Casterbridge'.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

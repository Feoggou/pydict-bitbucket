expend

(ɪkˈspɛnd )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to spend; disburse2. to consume or use up
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

expender (exˈpender) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin expendere, from pendere to weigh

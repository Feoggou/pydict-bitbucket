loon1

(luːn )
Definitions
nounthe US and Canadian name for diver (sense 3)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: of Scandinavian origin; related to Old Norse lōmr

loon2

(luːn )
Definitions
noun1. informal a simple-minded or stupid person2. Northeast Scotland dialect a lad3. archaic a person of low rank or occupation (esp in the phrase lord and loon)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: origin obscure

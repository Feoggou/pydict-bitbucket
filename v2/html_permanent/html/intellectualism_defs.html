intellectualism

(ˌɪntɪˈlɛktʃʊəˌlɪzəm )
Definitions
noun1. development and exercise of the intellect2. the placing of excessive value on the intellect, esp with disregard for the emotions3. philosophya. the doctrine that reason is the ultimate criterion of knowledgeb. the doctrine that deliberate action is consequent on a process of conscious or subconscious reasoning

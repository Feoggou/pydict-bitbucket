fatty

(ˈfætɪ )
Definitions
adjective -tier or -tiest1. containing, consisting of, or derived from fat2. having the properties of fat; greasy; oily3. (esp of tissues, organs, etc) characterized by the excessive accumulation of fatnoun plural -ties4. informal a fat person

fatty

Powered by COBUILD
(fæti ) for learners of English
Word forms: fattier , fattiest , fatties
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Fatty food contains a lot of fat. ⇒ Don't eat fatty food or chocolates. ⇒ The report dispels the myth that Northerners have a fattier diet than people in the south.2. adjective [ADJ n]Fatty acids or fatty tissues, for example, contain or consist of fat. ⇒ ...fatty acids. ⇒ The woman lost about 1.8kg of fatty tissue during the week's fast.3. countable nounIf you call someone a fatty, you are criticizing or insulting them for being fat. [informal, disapproval] ⇒ Consuming this amount of food could turn these fit players into fatties. ⇒ 'Get another one, Fatty,' said Jerry.

stony

or stoney (ˈstəʊnɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: stonier or stoniest1. of or resembling stone2. abounding in stone or stones3. unfeeling, heartless, or obdurate4. short for stony-broke
Derived forms
stonily (ˈstonily) adverbstoniness (ˈstoniness) noun

(stoʊni )
Word forms: stonier , stoniest
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveStony ground is rough and contains a lot of stones. ⇒ The steep, stony ground is well drained. ⇒ ...a stony track.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA stony expression or attitude does not show any sympathy or friendliness. ⇒ She gave me the stoniest look I ever got. ⇒ He drove us home in stony silence.

urbanity

(ɜːˈbænɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. the quality of being urbane2. (usually plural) civilities or courtesies
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'urbanity'
He lacked Wayne's urbanity , but he was more restful than Wayne, more reminiscent of her own kind. Hilton, John Buxton PASSION IN THE PEAKIt invaded the mind, plucked at the nerve-endings; and in spite of its polished urbanity , skewered the heart. West, Charles STAGE FRIGHTAmy and Richard had to lean heavily, for the moment, on Jack Roper's urbanity. Thomas, Rosie THE WHITE DOVEThe Village was growing on me, its casualness and urbanity , its diversity and tolerance, its filth and elegance, its quaintness and cool. Susan Isaacs AFTER ALL THESE YEARS

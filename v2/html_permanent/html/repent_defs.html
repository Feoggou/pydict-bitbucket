repent1

(rɪˈpɛnt )
Definitions
verbto feel remorse (for); be contrite (about); show penitence (for) ⇒ he repents of his extravagance, ⇒ he repented his words
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

repenter (reˈpenter) noun

Word Origin

C13: from Old French repentir from re- + pentir to be contrite, from Latin paenitēre to repent

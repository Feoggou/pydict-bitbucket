orchard

(ˈɔːtʃəd )
Definitions
noun1. an area of land devoted to the cultivation of fruit trees2. a collection of fruit trees especially cultivated
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English orceard, ortigeard, from ort-, from Latin hortus garden + geard yard2

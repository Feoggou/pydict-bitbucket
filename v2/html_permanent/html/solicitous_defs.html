solicitous

(səˈlɪsɪtəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. showing consideration, concern, attention, etc2. keenly anxious or willing; eager

Derived Forms

solicitously (soˈlicitously) adverbsolicitousness (soˈlicitousness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin sollicitus anxious; see solicit

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveA person who is solicitous shows anxious concern for someone or something. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ He was so solicitous of his guests.solicitously graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ He took her hand in greeting and asked solicitously how everything was.

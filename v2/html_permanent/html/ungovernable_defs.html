ungovernable for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌngʌvəʳnəbəl )
Definitions
1. adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you describe a country or region as ungovernable, you mean that it seems impossible to control or govern it effectively, for example because of violence or conflict among the population. ⇒ Beset by ethnic strife, the province remains ungovernable. ⇒ The country has become virtually ungovernable.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe feelings as ungovernable, you mean that they are so strong that they cannot be controlled. ⇒ He was filled with an ungovernable rage. ⇒ ...ungovernable passions.

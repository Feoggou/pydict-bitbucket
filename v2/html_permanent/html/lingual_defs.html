lingual

(ˈlɪŋɡwəl )
Definitions
adjective1. anatomy of or relating to the tongue or a part or structure resembling a tongue2. a. rare of or relating to language or languagesb. (in combination) ⇒ polylingual3. articulated with the tonguenoun4. a lingual consonant, such as Scots (r)

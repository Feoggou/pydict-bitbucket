communist

(ˈkɒmjʊnɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a supporter of any form of communism2. (often capital) a supporter of Communism or a Communist movement or state3. (often capital) a member of a Communist party4. (often capital) US any person holding left-wing views, esp when considered subversive5. a person who practises communal living; communalist6. another name for Communardadjective7. of, characterized by, favouring, or relating to communism; communistic

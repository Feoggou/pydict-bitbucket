involvement

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnvɒlvmənt ) for learners of English
Word forms: involvements
Definitions
1. uncount nounYour involvement in something is the fact that you are taking part in it. ⇒ You have no proof of my involvement in anything. [+ with] ⇒ She disliked his involvement with the group and disliked his friends.2. uncount nounInvolvement is the enthusiasm that you feel when you care deeply about something. [+ with] ⇒ Ben has always felt a deep involvement with animals.3. variable nounAn involvement is a close relationship between two people, especially if they are not married to each other. ⇒ They were very good friends but there was no romantic involvement. ⇒ Mahler was also known for his passionate involvements outside marriage.

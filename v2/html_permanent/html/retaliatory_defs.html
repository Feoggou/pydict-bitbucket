(rɪtæliətəri , US -tɔːri ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you take retaliatory action, you try to harm or annoy someone who has harmed or annoyed you. [formal] ⇒ There's been talk of a retaliatory blockade to prevent supplies getting through.

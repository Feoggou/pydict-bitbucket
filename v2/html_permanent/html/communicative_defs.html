communicative

(kəˈmjuːnɪkətɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. inclined or able to communicate readily; talkative2. of or relating to communication
Derived forms

(kəmjuːnɪkətɪv )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is communicative talks to people, for example about their feelings, and tells people things. ⇒ She has become a lot more tolerant and communicative.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Communicative means relating to the ability to communicate. ⇒ ...the notion of communicative competence. ⇒ We have a very communicative approach to teaching languages.

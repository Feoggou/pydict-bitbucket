tang

(tæŋ )
Definitions
noun1. a strong taste or flavour ⇒ the tang of the sea2. a pungent or characteristic smell ⇒ the tang of peat fires3. a trace, touch, or hint of something ⇒ a tang of cloves in the apple pie4. the pointed end of a tool, such as a chisel, file, knife, etc, which is fitted into a handle, shaft, or stock
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old Norse tangi point; related to Danish tange point, spit

influenza

(ˌɪnflʊˈɛnzə )
Definitions
nouna highly contagious and often epidemic viral disease characterized by fever, prostration, muscular aches and pains, and inflammation of the respiratory passages. Also called: grippe or informal flu
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

influenzal (ˌinfluˈenzal) adjective

Word Origin

C18: from Italian, literally: influence, hence, incursion, epidemic (first applied to influenza in 1743)

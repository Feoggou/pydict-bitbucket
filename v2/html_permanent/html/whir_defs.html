whir

or whirr (wɜː )
Definitions
noun1. a prolonged soft swish or buzz, as of a motor working or wings flapping2. a bustle or rushverbWord forms: whirs, whirrs, whirring or whirred3. to make or cause to make a whir
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: probably from Scandinavian; compare Norwegian kvirra, Danish hvirre; see whirl

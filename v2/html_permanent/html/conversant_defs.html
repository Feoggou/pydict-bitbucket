Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If you are conversant with something, you are familiar with it and able to deal with it. [formal] [+ with] ⇒ Those in business are not, on the whole, conversant with basic scientific principles.

beneficence

(bɪˈnɛfɪsəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of doing good; kindness2. a charitable act or gift
Example sentences containing 'beneficence'
He tucked it under his wiry forearm and smiled at me with what I took to be an underhanded beneficence.

latticework

(ˈlætɪsˌwɜːk)
Definitions
nouna. open frameworks of strips of wood, metal, etc, arranged to form an ornamental pattern on structures, furniture, etc ⇒ balconies shaded with latticework which lets in air but not lightb. (as modifier) ⇒ latticework chairs

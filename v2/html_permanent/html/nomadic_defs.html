nomadic for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(noʊmædɪk )
Definitions
1. adjectiveNomadic people travel from place to place rather than living in one place all the time. ⇒ ...the great nomadic tribes of the Western Sahara.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone has a nomadic way of life, they travel from place to place and do not have a settled home. ⇒ The daughter of a railway engineer, she at first had a somewhat nomadic childhood.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

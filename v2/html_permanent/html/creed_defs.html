creed

(kriːd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a concise, formal statement of the essential articles of Christian belief, such as the Apostles' Creed or the Nicene Creed2. any statement or system of beliefs or principles
Derived forms
creedal (ˈcreedal) or credal (ˈcredal) adjective
Word origin
Old English crēda, from Latin crēdo I believe

Noel

or Noël (nəʊˈɛl )
Definitions
noun1. (esp in carols) another word for Christmas2. (often not capital) rare a Christmas carol
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from French, from Latin nātālis a birthday; see natal1

morning dress

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounMorning dress is a suit that is worn by men for very formal occasions such as weddings. It consists of a grey or black coat that is longer at the back than the front, grey trousers, a white shirt, a grey tie, and often a top hat.

Hughie

(ˈhjuːɪ )
Definitions
nounAustralian and New Zealand informal the god of rain and of surf (esp in the phrases send her down, Hughie! and send 'em up, Hughie!)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: of uncertain origin

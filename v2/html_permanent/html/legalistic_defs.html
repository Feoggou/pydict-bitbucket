Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you say that someone's language or ideas are legalistic, you are criticizing them for paying too much attention to legal details. [disapproval] ⇒ ...complicated legalistic language. ⇒ ...his fussily legalistic mind.

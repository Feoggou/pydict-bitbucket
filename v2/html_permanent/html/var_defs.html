var

(vɑː )
Definitions
nouna unit of reactive power of an alternating current, equal to the product of the current measured in amperes and the voltage measured in volts
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

from v(olt-)a(mperes) r(eactive)

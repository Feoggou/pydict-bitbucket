miliary

(ˈmɪljərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. resembling or relating to millet seeds2. (of a disease or skin eruption) characterized by small lesions resembling millet seeds ⇒ miliary tuberculosis

Word Origin

C17: from Latin miliārius, from milium millet

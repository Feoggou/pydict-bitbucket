feigned

(feɪnd)
Definitions
adjective(of a gesture, statement or emotion) not real ⇒ 'Giles phoned this morning,' Mirella said with feigned indifference., ⇒ India's feigned surprise at local violence will not carry any credibility.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Flynn

(flɪn )
Definitions
noun1.  Errol. 1909–59, Australian-born Hollywood actor, who was noted for his swashbuckling roles; his films included Captain Blood (1935), The Adventures of Robin Hood (1938), and Too Much Too Soon (1958)2.  Rev. John. 1880–1951, founder of the Australian flying doctor service

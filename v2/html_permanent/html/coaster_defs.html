coaster

(ˈkəʊstə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. British a vessel or trader engaged in coastal commerce2. a small tray, sometimes on wheels, for holding a decanter, wine bottle, etc3. a person or thing that coasts4. a protective disc or mat on which to place a glass or bottle5. US short for roller-coaster6. West Africa a European resident on the coast

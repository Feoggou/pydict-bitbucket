Methodist

(ˈmɛθədɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a member of any of the Nonconformist denominations that derive from the system of faith and practice initiated by John Wesley and his followersadjective also: Methodistic, Methodistical (also without capital)2. of or relating to Methodism or the Church embodying it (the Methodist Church)

Derived Forms

ˌMethodistically (ˌMethodˈistically) adverb

dry goods

(draɪ ɡʊdz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. US textiles, clothing, threads, elastic and so on2. types of food such as tea, flour and sugar that do not contain liquid ⇒ a range of smoked meats and fish, and dry goods, ⇒ he carried the sugar, tea, rice, flour, and other dry goods home in reusable cotton bags., ⇒ Keep your dry goods fresh in these airtight, see-through dispensers with flip top lids.

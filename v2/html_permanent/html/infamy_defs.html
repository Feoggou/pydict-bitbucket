infamy

(ˈɪnfəmɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -mies1. the state or condition of being infamous2. an infamous act or event
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Latin infāmis of evil repute, from in-1 + fāma fame

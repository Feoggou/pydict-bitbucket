rub off

Definitions
verb1. to remove or be removed by rubbing2. (intr; often foll by on or onto) to have an effect through close association or contact, esp so as to make similar ⇒ her crude manners have rubbed off on younoun rub-off3. a resulting effect on something else; consequences ⇒ a positive rub-off

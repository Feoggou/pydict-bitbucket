arrangement

noun

  1. (often plural) plan, planning, provision, preparation • I am in charge of all the travel arrangements.
  2. agreement, contract, settlement, appointment, compromise, deal (informal), pact, compact, covenant • The caves can be visited only by prior arrangement.
  3. display, grouping, system, order, ordering, design, ranging, structure, rank, organization, exhibition, line-up, presentation, array, marshalling, classification, disposition, alignment, setup (informal) • an imaginative flower arrangement
  4. adaptation, score, version, interpretation, instrumentation, orchestration, harmonization • an arrangement of a well-known piece by Mozart

gridiron

(ˈɡrɪdˌaɪən )
Definitions
noun1. a utensil of parallel metal bars, used to grill meat, fish, etc2. any framework resembling this utensil3. a framework above the stage in a theatre from which suspended scenery, lights, etc, are manipulated4. a. the field of play in American footballb. an informal name for American footballc. (as modifier) ⇒ a gridiron hero
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13 gredire, perhaps variant (through influence of ire iron) of gredile griddle

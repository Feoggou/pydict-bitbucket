aground

(əˈɡraʊnd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb, adjective(postpositive) on or onto the ground or bottom, as in shallow water

aground

Powered by COBUILD
(əgraʊnd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverb [ADV after v]If a ship runs aground, it touches the ground in a shallow part of a river, lake, or the sea, and gets stuck. ⇒ The ship ran aground where there should have been a depth of 35ft.

fancy dress

Definitions
nouna. costume worn at masquerades, etc, usually representing a particular role, historical figure, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ a fancy-dress ball

fancy dress

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [oft N n]Fancy dress is clothing that you wear for a party at which everyone tries to look like a famous person or a person from a story, from history, or from a particular profession. ⇒ Guests were told to come in fancy dress. ⇒ He turned up at the fancy dress party as a footballer.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'fancy dress'
Her waist narrowed above small hips, modestly concealed by the flare of a fancy dress. Lisa Scottoline THE VENDETTA DEFENCE

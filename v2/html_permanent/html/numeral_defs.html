numeral

(ˈnjuːmərəl )
Definitions
noun1. a symbol or group of symbols used to express a number: for example, 6 (Arabic), VI (Roman), 110 (binary)adjective2. of, consisting of, or denoting a number
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Late Latin numerālis belonging to number, from Latin numerus number

atheist

(ˈeɪθɪˌɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who does not believe in God or godsadjective2. of or relating to atheists or atheism
Derived forms
ˌatheistic (ˌatheˈistic) or ˌatheistical (ˌatheˈistical) adjectiveˌatheistically (ˌatheˈistically) adverb

Northamptonshire

(nɔːˈθæmptənˌʃɪə , -ʃə, nɔːθˈhæmp-)
Definitions
nouna county of central England: agriculture, food processing, engineering, and footwear industries. Administrative centre: Northampton. Pop: 642 700 (2003 est). Area: 2367 sq km (914 sq miles). Abbreviation: Northants

musing

(ˈmjuːzɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. reflection ⇒ Carrie interrupted his musing., ⇒ He was startled out of his angry musings.adjective literary2. tending to muse; contemplative ⇒ His long, shapely, musing sentences spread themselves over page after page.3. dreamy ⇒ in a soft, musing voice
Example sentences containing 'musing'
She took the books up to her studio, leaving him musing at her slightly awkward secretiveness. Appiganesi, Lisa DREAMS OF INNOCENCEAn intensity emanated from them, a sense of private musing. Appiganesi, Lisa DREAMS OF INNOCENCEAt this moment a loud banging at the back door and a raised voice penetrated his musing. Salley Vickers MR GOLIGHTLY'S HOLIDAYThe younger man's brow had clouded and now as Bolsgar ended his tale, he rode in silence, musing on Gwyneth's treachery. Kathleen E. Woodiwiss THE WOLF AND THE DOVE

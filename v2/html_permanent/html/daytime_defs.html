(deɪtaɪm )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. singular nounThe daytime is the part of a day between the time when it gets light and the time when it gets dark. OPPOSITE: night-time ⇒ In the daytime he stayed up in his room, sleeping, or listening to music. ⇒ Please give a daytime telephone number. ⇒ Even at the end of the war, German factories mainly operated only in daytime.2. adjective [ADJ n]Daytime television and radio is broadcast during the morning and afternoon on weekdays. ⇒ ...ITV's new package of daytime programmes.

foggy

(ˈfɒɡɪ )
Definitions
adjective -gier or -giest1. thick with fog2. obscure or confused3. another word for fogged4. See not the foggiest
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

foggily (ˈfoggily) adverbfogginess (ˈfogginess) noun

Definitions

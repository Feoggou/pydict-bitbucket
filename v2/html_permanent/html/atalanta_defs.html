Atalanta

(ˌætəˈlæntə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounGreek mythology a maiden who agreed to marry any man who could defeat her in a running race. She lost to Hippomenes when she paused to pick up three golden apples that he had deliberately dropped

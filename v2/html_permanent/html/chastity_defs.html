chastity

(ˈtʃæstɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the state of being chaste; purity2. abstention from sexual intercourse; virginity or celibacy ⇒ a vow of chastity
Word origin
C13: from Old French chasteté, from Latin castitās, from castus chaste

Definitions

uncount nounChastity is the state of not having sex with anyone, or of only having sex with your husband or wife. [old-fashioned] ⇒ He took a vow of chastity and celibacy.

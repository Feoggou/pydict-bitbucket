nauseate

(ˈnɔːzɪˌeɪt , -sɪ-)
Definitions
verb1. (transitive) to arouse feelings of disgust or revulsion in2. to feel or cause to feel sick
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

nauseating (ˈnauseˌating) adjectivenauseation (ˌnauseˈation) nounnauseatingly (ˈnauseˌatingly) adverb

Definitions
verbIf something nauseates you, it makes you feel as if you are going to vomit. [V n] ⇒ The smell of frying nauseated her. [V-ed] ⇒ She could not eat anything without feeling nauseated.

recrimination

(rɪˌkrɪmɪˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act or an instance of recriminating2. law a charge made by an accused against his accuser; countercharge

Definitions
variable nounRecriminations are accusations that two people or groups make about each other. ⇒ The bitter rows and recriminations have finally ended the relationship. ⇒ The war sweeps up everyone in hatred and recrimination.

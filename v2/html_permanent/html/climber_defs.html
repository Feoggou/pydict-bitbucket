climber

(ˈklaɪmə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that climbs2. a plant that lacks rigidity and grows upwards by twining, scrambling, or clinging with tendrils and suckers3. mainly British short for social climber

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA climber is someone who climbs rocks or mountains as a sport or a hobby.2. countable nounA climber is a plant that grows upwards by attaching itself to other plants or objects.

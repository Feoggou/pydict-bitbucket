Powered by COBUILD
(wɜːʳθhwaɪl )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is worthwhile, it is enjoyable or useful, and worth the time, money, or effort that is spent on it. ⇒ The President's trip to Washington this week seems to have been worthwhile. ⇒ ...a worthwhile movie that was compelling enough to watch again. ⇒ It might be worthwhile to consider your attitude to an insurance policy.

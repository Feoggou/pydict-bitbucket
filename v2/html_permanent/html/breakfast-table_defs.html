breakfast table

(ˈbrɛkfəst ˈteɪbəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna table, usually the table in a home where a family has most of its meals, laid for breakfast ⇒ reading the morning papers at the breakfast table, ⇒ You were not allowed to come to the breakfast table in a state of undress., ⇒ They usually had their first row of the day at the breakfast table.

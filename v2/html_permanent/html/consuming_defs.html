consuming

Powered by COBUILD
(kənsjuːmɪŋ , US -suː- )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]A consuming passion or interest is more important to you than anything else. ⇒ He has developed a consuming passion for chess.2. → see also consume, time-consuming
Example sentences containing 'consuming'
He had discovered a way of consuming inconceivable quantities of energy. Asimov, Isaac THE COMPLETE STORIES VOLUME 2The flames leapt and died and leapt again, consuming the years of Anita's young womanhood, and she watched them hypnotized. Murray, Stephen DEATH AND TRANSFIGURATIONHe was still there, consuming her with the full intensity of his gaze. Stewart, Michael COMPULSION

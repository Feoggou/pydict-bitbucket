kilt

(kɪlt )
Definitions
noun1. a knee-length pleated skirt-like garment, esp one in tartan, as worn by men in Scottish Highland dressverb (transitive)2. to tuck (a skirt) up around one's body3. to put pleats in (cloth, a skirt, etc)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

kilted (ˈkilted) adjectivekiltlike (ˈkiltˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C18: of Scandinavian origin; compare Danish kilte to tuck up, Old Swedish kilta lap

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Brittany, its people, or their languagenoun2. a native or inhabitant of Brittany, esp one who speaks the Breton language3. the indigenous language of Brittany, belonging to the Brythonic subgroup of the Celtic family of languages

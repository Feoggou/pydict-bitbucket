pantry

(ˈpæntrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -triesa small room or cupboard in which provisions, cooking utensils, etc, are kept; larder

Word Origin

C13: via Anglo-Norman, from Old French paneterie store for bread, ultimately from Latin pānis bread

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA pantry is a small room or large cupboard in a house, usually near the kitchen, where food is kept.

carnivore

(ˈkɑːnɪˌvɔː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any placental mammal of the order Carnivora, typically having large pointed canine teeth and sharp molars and premolars, specialized for eating flesh. The order includes cats, dogs, bears, raccoons, hyenas, civets, and weasels2. any other animal or any plant that feeds on animals3. informal an aggressively ambitious person
Word origin
C19: probably back formation from carnivorous

facetiae

(fəˈsiːʃɪˌiː )
Definitions
plural noun1. humorous or witty sayings2. obscene or coarsely witty books
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin: jests, plural of facētia witticism, from facētus elegant

incivility

(ɪnsɪˈvɪlɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. lack of civility or courtesy; rudeness2. an impolite or uncivil act or remark
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Similar Words

rudeness, disrespect, bad manners, discourtesy, boorishness, ill-breeding, impoliteness, unmannerliness, discourteousness

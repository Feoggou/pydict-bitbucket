wiper

(ˈwaɪpə )
Definitions
noun1. any piece of cloth, such as a handkerchief, towel, etc, used for wiping2. a cam rotated to ease a part and allow it to fall under its own weight, as used in stamping machines, etc3. See windscreen wiper4. electrical engineering a movable conducting arm, esp one in a switching or selecting device, that makes contact with a row or ring of contacts

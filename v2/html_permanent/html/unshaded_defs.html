unshaded

(ʌnˈʃeɪdɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. having no shade ⇒ an unshaded light bulb2. having no shade from the sun ⇒ They suffered from heat stroke on unshaded Spanish roads.3. art not hatched or shaded ⇒ Illustrations are best when they are simple, unshaded line drawings.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

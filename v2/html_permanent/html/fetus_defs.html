fetus

or foetus (ˈfiːtəs )
Definitions
noun plural -tusesthe embryo of a mammal in the later stages of development, when it shows all the main recognizable features of the mature animal, esp a human embryo from the end of the second month of pregnancy until birth. Compare embryo (sense 2)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin: offspring, brood

fedora

(fɪˈdɔːrə )
Definitions
nouna soft felt or velvet medium-brimmed hat, usually with a band
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: allegedly named after Fédora (1882), play by French dramatist Victorien Sardou (1831–1908)

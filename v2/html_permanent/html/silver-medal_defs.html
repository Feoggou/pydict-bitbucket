Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf you win a silver medal, you come second in a competition, especially a sports contest, and are given a medal made of silver as a prize. ⇒ Gillingham won the silver medal in the 200 metres at Seoul.
Example sentences containing 'silver medal'

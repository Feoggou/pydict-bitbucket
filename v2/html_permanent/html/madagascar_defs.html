Madagascar

(ˌmædəˈɡæskə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan island republic in the Indian Ocean, off the E coast of Africa: made a French protectorate in 1895; became autonomous in 1958 and fully independent in 1960; contains unique flora and fauna. Languages: Malagasy and French. Religions: animist and Christian. Currency: franc. Capital: Antananarivo. Pop: 22 599 098 (2013 est). Area: 587 041 sq km (266 657 sq miles). Official name (since 1975): Democratic Republic of Madagascar. Former name (1958–75): Malagasy Republic

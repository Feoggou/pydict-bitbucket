mournful

(ˈmɔːnfʊl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. evoking grief; sorrowful2. gloomy; sad

Derived Forms

mournfully (ˈmournfully) adverbmournfulness (ˈmournfulness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are mournful, you are very sad. ⇒ He looked mournful, even near to tears. ⇒ Miss Dickerman was tall and somewhat mournful-looking.mournfully graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ He stood mournfully at the gate waving bye bye.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA mournful sound seems very sad. ⇒ ...the mournful wail of bagpipes.

readable

(ˈriːdəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. (of handwriting, etc) able to be read or deciphered; legible2. (of style of writing) interesting, easy, or pleasant to read
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

readability (ˌreadaˈbility) or readableness (ˈreadableness) nounreadably (ˈreadably) adverb

(riːdəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that a book or article is readable, you mean that it is enjoyable and easy to read. ⇒ This is an impeccably researched and very readable book.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA piece of writing that is readable is written or printed clearly and can be read easily. legible ⇒ My secretary worked long hours translating my almost illegible writing into a typewritten and readable script.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Synonyms of opposite

opposite

adjective

  1. facing, other, opposing
    • the opposite side of the room
  2. different, conflicting, opposed, contrasted, contrasting, unlike, differing, contrary, diverse, adverse, at odds, contradictory, inconsistent, dissimilar, divergent, irreconcilable, at variance, poles apart, diametrically opposed, antithetical, streets apart
    • Everything he does is opposite to what is considered normal behaviour.
    OPPOSITES:  like, same, similar, matching, uniform, consistent, identical, alike, corresponding
  3. rival, conflicting, opposed, opposing, competing, hostile, antagonistic, inimical
    • They fought on opposite sides during the War of Independence.

preposition

  1. (often with to) facing, fronting, face to face with, across from, eyeball to eyeball with (informal)
    • She sat opposite her at breakfast.

noun

  1. reverse, contrary, converse, antithesis, the other extreme, contradiction, inverse, the other side of the coin (informal), obverse
    • She's very shy, but her sister is quite the opposite.

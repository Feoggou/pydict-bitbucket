Berber

(ˈbɜːbə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a member of a Caucasoid Muslim people of N Africa2. the language of this people, forming a subfamily of the Afro-Asiatic family of languages. There are extensive differences between dialectsadjective3. of or relating to this people or their language

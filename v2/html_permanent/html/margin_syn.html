Synonyms of margin

margin

noun

  1. gap, amount, difference, majority
    • They could end up with a 50-point winning margin.
  2. room, space, surplus, allowance, scope, play, compass, latitude, leeway, extra room, elbowroom
    • There is very little margin for error in the way the money is collected.
  3. edge, side, limit, border, bound, boundary, confine, verge, brink, rim, brim, perimeter, periphery
    • These islands are on the margins of human habitation.

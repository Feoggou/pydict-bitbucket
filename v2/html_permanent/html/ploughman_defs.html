ploughman

or especially US plowman (ˈplaʊmən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -men1. a man who ploughs, esp using horses2. any farm labourer

Derived Forms

ploughmanship (ˈploughmanship) or especially US plowmanship (ˈplowmanship) noun

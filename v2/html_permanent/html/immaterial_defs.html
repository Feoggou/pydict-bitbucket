immaterial

(ˌɪməˈtɪərɪəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of no real importance; inconsequential2. not formed of matter; incorporeal; spiritual
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

immateriality (ˌimmaˌteriˈality) or immaterialness (ˌimmaˈterialness) nounimmaterially (ˌimmaˈterially) adverb

immaterial

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmətɪəriəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [v-link ADJ]If you say that something is immaterial, you mean that it is not important or not relevant. ⇒ Whether we like him or not is immaterial.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

(trɔːmætɪk , US traʊ- ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveA traumatic experience is very shocking and upsetting, and may cause psychological damage. ⇒ I suffered a nervous breakdown. It was a traumatic experience. ⇒ For a child the death of a pet can be traumatic.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

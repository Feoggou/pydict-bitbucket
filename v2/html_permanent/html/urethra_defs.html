urethra

(jʊˈriːθrə )
Definitions
noun plural -thrae (-θriː) or -thrasthe canal that in most mammals conveys urine from the bladder out of the body. In human males it also conveys semen
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

urethral (uˈrethral) adjective

Word Origin

C17: via Late Latin from Greek ourēthra, from ourein to urinate

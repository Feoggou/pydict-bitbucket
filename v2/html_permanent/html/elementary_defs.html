elementary

(ˌɛlɪˈmɛntərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
adjective1. not difficult; simple; rudimentary2. of or concerned with the first principles of a subject; introductory or fundamental3. mathematics (of a function) having the form of an algebraic, exponential, trigonometric, or a logarithmic function, or any combination of these4. chemistry another word for elemental (sense 5)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

elementarily (ˌeleˈmentarily) adverbelementariness (ˌeleˈmentariness) noun

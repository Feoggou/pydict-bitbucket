Jacobi

Definitions
noun1. (ˈdʒækəʊbɪ) Sir Derek (George). born 1938, British actor2. (German jaˈkoːbi) Karl Gustav Jacob (karl ˈɡʊstaf ˈjaːkɔp). 1804–51, German mathematician. Independently of N. H. Abel, he discovered elliptic functions (1829). He also made important contributions to the study of determinants and differential equations

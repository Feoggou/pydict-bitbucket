yolk

(jəʊk )
Definitions
noun1. the substance in an animal ovum consisting of protein and fat that nourishes the developing embryo ▶ Related adjective: vitelline2. a greasy substance secreted by the skin of a sheep and present in the fleece
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

yolkless (ˈyolkless) adjectiveyolky (ˈyolky) adjective

Word Origin

Old English geoloca, from geolu yellow

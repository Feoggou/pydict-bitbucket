livid

(ˈlɪvɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. (of the skin) discoloured, as from a bruise or contusion2. of a greyish tinge or colour ⇒ livid pink3. informal angry or furious
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lividly (ˈlividly) adverblividness (ˈlividness) or lividity (liˈvidity) noun

Word Origin

C17: via French from Latin līvidus, from līvēre to be black and blue

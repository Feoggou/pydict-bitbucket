Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Fleeting is used to describe something which lasts only for a very short time. ⇒ The girls caught only a fleeting glimpse of the driver. ⇒ She wondered for a fleeting moment if he would put his arm around her.fleetingly adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj] ⇒ A smile passed fleetingly across his face. ⇒ I think I saw her, fleetingly, at a football match.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

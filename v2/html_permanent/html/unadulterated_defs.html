unadulterated for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnədʌltəreɪtɪd )
Definitions
1. adjectiveSomething that is unadulterated is completely pure and has had nothing added to it. ⇒ Organic food is unadulterated food produced without artificial chemicals or pesticides.2. adjective [ADJ n]You can also use unadulterated to emphasize a particular quality, often a bad quality. [emphasis] ⇒ It was pure, unadulterated hell.

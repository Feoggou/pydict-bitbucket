Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Scattered things are spread over an area in an untidy or irregular way. ⇒ He picked up the scattered toys. ⇒ Tomorrow there will be a few scattered showers. [+ across/on/over] ⇒ The fridge door was open and food was scattered across the floor.2. adjective [v-link ADJ with n]If something is scattered with a lot of small things, they are spread all over it. [+ with] ⇒ Every surface is scattered with photographs.

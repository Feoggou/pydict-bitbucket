menial

(ˈmiːnɪəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. consisting of or occupied with work requiring little skill, esp domestic duties such as cleaning2. of, involving, or befitting servants3. servilenoun4. a domestic servant5. a servile person

Derived Forms

menially (ˈmenially) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Anglo-Norman meignial, from Old French meinie household. See meiny

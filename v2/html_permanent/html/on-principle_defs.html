on principle for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
phraseIf you refuse to do something on principle, you refuse to do it because of a particular belief that you have. ⇒ He would vote against it on principle. ⇒ His father, on principle, did not like to hear requests for money. →  principle

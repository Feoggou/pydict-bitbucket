Synonyms of highlight

highlight

verb

  1. emphasize, stress, accent, feature, set off, show up, underline, spotlight, play up, accentuate, flag, foreground, focus attention on, call attention to, give prominence to, bring to the fore
    • Two events have highlighted the tensions in recent days.
    OPPOSITES: play down, gloss over, overlook, neglect, airbrush, de-emphasize

noun

  1. high point, peak, climax, feature, focus, best part, focal point, main feature, high spot, memorable part
    • one of the highlights of the tournament
    OPPOSITES: low point, disappointment, lowlight

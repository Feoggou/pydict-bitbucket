vole1

(vəʊl )
Definitions
nounany of numerous small rodents of the genus Microtus and related genera, mostly of Eurasia and North America and having a stocky body, short tail, and inconspicuous ears: family Cricetidae. See also water vole
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: short for volemouse, from Old Norse vollr field + mus mouse; related to Icelandic vollarmus

vole2

(vəʊl )
Definitions
noun(in some card games, such as écarté) the taking of all the tricks in a deal, thus scoring extra points
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from French, from voler to fly, from Latin volāre

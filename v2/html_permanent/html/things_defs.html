things

(θɪŋz)
Definitions
plural noun1. possessions, articles of clothing, etc ⇒ Sara told him to take all his things and not to return., ⇒ Is there anything you'd like to borrow, before your own things are unpacked?, ⇒ I like my own things around me.2. the current situation; life ⇒ How are things?, ⇒ A change of ownership might improve things., ⇒ Things are getting better.3. concerns; matters ⇒ I think I'm more aware now of some spiritual things and I do believe in good and evil., ⇒ the things of this world, ⇒ a movement away from the things of this world to the things of the spirit4. See to be seeing things

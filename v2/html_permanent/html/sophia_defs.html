Sophia

(səʊˈfaɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun 1630–1714, electress of Hanover (1658–1714), in whom the Act of Settlement (1701) vested the English Crown. She was a granddaughter of James I of England and her son became George I of Great Britain and Ireland

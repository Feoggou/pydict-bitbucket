clothe

(kləʊð )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verbWord forms: clothes, clothing, clothed or clad (transitive)1. to dress or attire (a person)2. to provide with clothing or covering3. to conceal or disguise4. to endow or invest
Word origin
Old English clāthian, from clāth cloth; related to Old Norse klætha

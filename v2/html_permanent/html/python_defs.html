python

(ˈpaɪθən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany large nonvenomous snake of the family Pythonidae of Africa, S Asia, and Australia, such as Python reticulatus (reticulated python). They can reach a length of more than 20 feet and kill their prey by constriction

Derived Forms

pythonic (paɪˈθɒnɪk ) adjective

Word Origin

C16: New Latin, after Python

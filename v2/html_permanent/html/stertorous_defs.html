stertorous

(ˈstɜːtərəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. marked or accompanied by heavy snoring2. breathing in this way

Derived Forms

stertorously (ˈstertorously) adverbstertorousness (ˈstertorousness) noun

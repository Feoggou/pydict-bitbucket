bold face

or bold type (bəʊld taɪp)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. printing a weight of type characterized by thick heavy lines, as the entry words in this dictionary. Compare light faceadjective boldface2. (of type) having this weight
Example sentences containing 'bold face'
She lowered her head, forcing a few tears into her eyes and looking as abject as a bold face would allow. Low, Ona MURKY SHALLOWSMaria in the photograph albums that first summer of 1915, a bold face , hiding heaven knew what depths of bewilderment and loss. Haines, Pamela THE GOLDEN LION

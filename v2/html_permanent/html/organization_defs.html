organization

or organisation (ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of organizing or the state of being organized2. an organized structure or whole3. a business or administrative concern united and constructed for a particular end4. a body of administrative officials, as of a political party, a government department, etc5. order or system; method
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

organizational (ˌorganiˈzational) or organisational (ˌorganiˈsational) adjectiveorganizationally (ˌorganiˈzationally) or organisationally (ˌorganiˈsationally) adverb

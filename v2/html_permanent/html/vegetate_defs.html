vegetate

(ˈvɛdʒɪˌteɪt )
Definitions
verb (intransitive)1. to grow like a plant; sprout2. to lead a life characterized by monotony, passivity, or mental inactivity3. pathology (of a wart, polyp, etc) to develop fleshy outgrowths
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin vegetāre to invigorate

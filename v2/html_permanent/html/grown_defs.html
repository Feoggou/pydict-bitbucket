grown

Powered by COBUILD
(groʊn ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]A grown man or woman is one who is fully developed and mature, both physically and mentally. ⇒ Few women can understand a grown man's love of sport. ⇒ Dad, I'm a grown woman. I know what I'm doing.2. → See also full-grown
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

movie

(ˈmuːvɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna. an informal word for film (sense 1)b. (as modifier) ⇒ movie ticket

Word Origin

C20: from mov(ing picture) + -ie

movie

Powered by COBUILD
(muːvi )
Word forms: movies
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA movie is a film. [US, also British, informal] ⇒ In the first movie Tony Curtis ever made he played a grocery clerk. ⇒ ...a horror movie.2. plural nounYou can talk about the movies when you are talking about seeing a movie in a movie theater. [mainly US] ⇒ He took her to the movies.regional note:   in BRIT, usually use the cinema

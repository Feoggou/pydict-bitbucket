oats

(əʊts)
Definitions
plural noun1. the seeds or fruits of the grass Avena sativa ⇒ Oats can be effective in reducing cholesterol., ⇒ Oats provide good, nutritious food for horses.. See also rolled oats, wild oats2. See to feel your oats3. See to get your oats4. See to sow your oats

oats for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(oʊts )
Definitions
language note:   The form oat is used as a modifier.1. plural nounOats are a cereal crop or its grains, used for making biscuits or a food called porridge, or for feeding animals. ⇒ Oats provide good, nutritious food for horses. ⇒ ...oat bran.2. →  to sow your wild oats

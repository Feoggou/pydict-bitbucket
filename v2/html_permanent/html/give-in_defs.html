give in

Definitions
verb (adverb)1. (intransitive) to yield; admit defeat2. (transitive) to submit or deliver (a document)

give in

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. phrasal verbIf you give in, you admit that you are defeated or that you cannot do something. [V P] ⇒ All right. I give in. What did you do with the ship?2. phrasal verbIf you give in, you agree to do something that you do not want to do. [V P] ⇒ I pressed my parents until they finally gave in and registered me for skating classes. [V P + to] ⇒ Officials say they won't give in to the workers' demands. →  give

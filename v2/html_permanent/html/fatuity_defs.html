fatuity

(fəˈtjuːɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. complacent foolishness; inanity2. a fatuous remark, act, sentiment, etc3. archaic idiocy
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fatuitous (faˈtuitous) adjective
Example sentences containing 'fatuity'
Perhaps I just realized the sheer fatuity of what I was trying to do. Richard Wright 52449_CLARA

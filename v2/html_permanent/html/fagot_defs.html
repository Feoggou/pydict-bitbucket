fagot

(ˈfægət ) in American English
Definitions
noun1.  a bundle of sticks, twigs, or branches, esp. for use as fuel2.  Metallurgy a bundle or heap of iron or steel pieces to be worked into bars by hammering or rolling at welding temperature verb transitive3.  to make into a fagot; form fagots of4.  Sewing to decorate with fagoting

Word Origin

ME < OFr, with change of suffix < VL *facellum < Gr phakelos, a bundle

hostess

(ˈhəʊstɪs )
Definitions
noun1. a woman acting as host2. a woman who receives and entertains patrons of a club, restaurant, etc3. See air hostess

hostess

Powered by COBUILD
(hoʊstɪs ) for learners of English
Word forms: hostesses
Definitions
1. countable nounThe hostess at a party is the woman who has invited the guests and provides the food, drink, or entertainment. ⇒ The hostess introduced them. ⇒ She was a superb hostess to us all.2. countable nounA hostess at a night club or dance hall is a woman who is paid by a man to be with him for the evening.

Definitions

crepe

or crape (kreɪp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a light cotton, silk, or other fabric with a fine ridged or crinkled surfaceb. (as modifier) ⇒ a crepe dress2. a black armband originally made of this, worn as a sign of mourning3. a very thin pancake, often rolled or folded around a filling4. short for crepe paper, crepe rubberverb5. (transitive) to cover or drape with crepe
Word origin
C19: from French crêpe, from Latin crispus curled, uneven, wrinkled

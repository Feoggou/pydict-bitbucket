Brahmin

(ˈbrɑːmɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -min or -mins1. the older spelling of Brahman (sense 1)2. (in the US) a highly intelligent or socially exclusive person, esp a member of one of the older New England families3. an intellectual or social snob
Derived forms
Brahminic (Brahˈminic) or Brahminical (Brahˈminical) adjective

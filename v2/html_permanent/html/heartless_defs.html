Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as heartless, you mean that they are cruel and unkind, and have no sympathy for anyone or anything. ⇒ I couldn't believe they were so heartless. ⇒ It was a heartless thing to do.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

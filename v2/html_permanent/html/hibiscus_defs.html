hibiscus

(haɪˈbɪskəs )
Definitions
noun plural -cusesany plant of the chiefly tropical and subtropical malvaceous genus Hibiscus, esp H. rosa-sinensis, cultivated for its large brightly coloured flowers
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Latin, from Greek hibiskos marsh mallow

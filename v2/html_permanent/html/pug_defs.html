pug1

(pʌɡ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: carlin a small compact breed of dog with a smooth coat, lightly curled tail, and a short wrinkled nose2. any of several small geometrid moths, mostly of the genus Eupithecia, with slim forewings held outstretched at rest

Derived Forms

puggish (ˈpuggish) adjective

Word Origin

C16: of uncertain origin

pug2

(pʌɡ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verbWord forms: pugs, pugging or pugged (transitive)1. to mix or knead (clay) with water to form a malleable mass or paste, often in a pug mill2. to fill or stop with clay or a similar substance3. (of cattle) to trample (the ground) into consolidated mud

Word Origin

C19: of uncertain origin

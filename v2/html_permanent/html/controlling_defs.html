controlling

(kənˈtrəʊlɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. finance having or attempting to exert control ⇒ It plans to sell a controlling stake in CSOB.2. trying to control others' behaviour in an inappropriate way ⇒ We found a clear relation between experiencing controlling behaviour and violence.
Example sentences containing 'controlling'
Her grandfather had left her his controlling interest of fifty-five per cent of the shares in the private company.

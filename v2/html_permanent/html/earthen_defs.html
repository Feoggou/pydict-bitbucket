earthen

(ˈɜːθən )
Definitions
adjective (prenominal)1. made of baked clay ⇒ an earthen pot2. made of earth

earthen

Powered by COBUILD
(ɜːʳðən ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Earthen containers and objects are made of clay that is baked so that it becomes hard. ⇒ ...an earthen jar.2. adjective [ADJ n]An earthen floor, bank, or mound is made of hard earth. ⇒ Despite the mud outside, the earthen floor was clean.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

sinful

(ˈsɪnfʊl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having committed or tending to commit sin ⇒ a sinful person2. characterized by or being a sin ⇒ a sinful act

Derived Forms

sinfully (ˈsinfully) adverbsinfulness (ˈsinfulness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as sinful, you mean that they are wicked or immoral. ⇒ 'I am a sinful man,' he said. ⇒ ...this is a sinful world. ⇒ He reminded us that smoking was sinful.sinfulness uncount noun [+ of] ⇒ ...the sinfulness of apartheid.

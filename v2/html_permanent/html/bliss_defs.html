bliss

(blɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. perfect happiness; serene joy2. the ecstatic joy of heaven
Derived forms
blissless (ˈblissless) adjective
Word origin
Old English blīths; related to blīthe blithe, Old Saxon blīdsea bliss

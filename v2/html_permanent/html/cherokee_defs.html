Cherokee

(ˈtʃɛrəˌkiː , ˌtʃɛrəˈkiː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. plural -kees or -kee a member of a Native American people formerly living in and around the Appalachian Mountains, now chiefly in Oklahoma; one of the Iroquois peoples2. the language of this people, belonging to the Iroquoian family

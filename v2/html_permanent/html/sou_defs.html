sou

(suː )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a former French coin of low denomination2. a very small amount of money ⇒ I haven't a sou to my name

Word Origin

C19: from French, from Old French sol, from Latin: solidus
Example sentences containing 'sou'
If she ever had a sou in her pocket, it was a tip from some villager to whom she had sold eggs or given straw. Hilton, John Buxton DISPLACED PERSONSo if it happens too often, you haven't a sou to your name by the end of the week. Brent-Dyer, Elinor ADRIENNE AND THE CHALET SCHOOL

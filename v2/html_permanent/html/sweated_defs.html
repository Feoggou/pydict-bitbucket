sweated

(ˈswɛtɪd )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. made by exploited labour ⇒ sweated goods2. (of workers, etc) forced to work in poor conditions for low pay
Example sentences containing 'sweated'
The part of him that would have been revolted had been sweated out of him. Clive Barker COLDHEART CANYONAt worst that could mean ejection from the postgraduate social work course he'd sweated blood to get on to.

tubercular

(tjʊˈbɜːkjʊlə )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or symptomatic of tuberculosis2. of or relating to a tubercle or tubercles3. characterized by the presence of tuberclesnoun4. a person with tuberculosis
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tubercularly (tuˈbercularly) adverb

gory

(ˈɡɔːrɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: gorier or goriest1. horrific or bloodthirsty ⇒ a gory story2. involving bloodshed and killing ⇒ a gory battle3. covered in gore
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gorily (ˈgorily) adverbgoriness (ˈgoriness) noun

wildfowl

(ˈwaɪldˌfaʊl )
Definitions
noun1. any bird that is hunted by man, esp any duck or similar aquatic bird2. such birds collectively
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

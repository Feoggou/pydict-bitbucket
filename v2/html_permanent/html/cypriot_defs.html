Cypriot

(ˈsɪprɪət ) or Cypriote (ˈsɪprɪˌəʊt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a native, citizen, or inhabitant of Cyprus2. the dialect of Ancient or Modern Greek spoken in Cyprusadjective3. denoting or relating to Cyprus, its inhabitants, or dialects

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveCypriot means belonging or relating to Cyprus, or to its people or culture.2. countable nounA Cypriot is a Cypriot citizen, or a person of Cypriot origin.

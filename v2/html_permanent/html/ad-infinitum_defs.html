Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverb [ADV after v]If something happens ad infinitum, it is repeated again and again in the same way. ⇒ This cycle repeats itself ad infinitum.
Example sentences containing 'ad infinitum'
Emerald could see in years to come she would be repeating herself ad infinitum. Pacter, Trudi YELLOW BIRDIt was a classic case of large fleas, smaller fleas, and so on ad infinitum. Babson, Marian DEATH IN FASHIONOn and on went the coverage, ad nauseam and ad infinitum --- I wanted to throw. Isabel Wolff RESCUING ROSE"If you hadn't done this, I wouldn't have done that, " ad infinitum. Miller, Sue FAMILY PICTURES

(enviəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]You describe something such as a quality as enviable when someone else has it and you wish that you had it too. ⇒ Japan is in the enviable position of having a budget surplus. ⇒ They have enviable reputations as athletes.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

forestry

(ˈfɒrɪstrɪ )
Definitions
noun1. the science of planting and caring for trees2. the planting and management of forests3. rare forest land
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

forestry

Powered by COBUILD
(fɒrɪstri , US fɔːr- ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounForestry is the science or skill of growing and taking care of trees in forests, especially in order to obtain wood.

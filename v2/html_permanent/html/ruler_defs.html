ruler

(ˈruːlə )
Definitions
noun1. a person who rules or commands2. Also called: rule a strip of wood, metal, or other material, having straight edges graduated usually in millimetres or inches, used for measuring and drawing straight lines

ruler

Powered by COBUILD
(ruːləʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: rulers
Definitions
1. countable noun [oft with poss]The ruler of a country is the person who rules the country. [+ of] ⇒ ...the former military ruler of Lesotho. ⇒ He was a weak-willed and indecisive ruler.2. countable nounA ruler is a long flat piece of wood, metal, or plastic with straight edges marked in centimetres or inches. Rulers are used to measure things and to draw straight lines.

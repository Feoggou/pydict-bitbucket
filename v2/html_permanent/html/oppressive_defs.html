oppressive

(əˈprɛsɪv )
Definitions
adjective1. cruel, harsh, or tyrannical2. heavy, constricting, or depressing

oppressively (opˈpressively) adverboppressiveness (opˈpressiveness) noun

oppressive for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(əpresɪv )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a society, its laws, or customs as oppressive, you think they treat people cruelly and unfairly. ⇒ The new laws will be just as oppressive as those they replace. ⇒ ...refugees from the oppressive regime.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe the weather or the atmosphere in a room as oppressive, you mean that it is unpleasantly hot and damp. ⇒ The oppressive afternoon heat had quite tired him out. ⇒ The little room was windowless and oppressive.3. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]An oppressive situation makes you feel depressed and uncomfortable. ⇒ ...the oppressive sadness that weighed upon him like a physical pain.

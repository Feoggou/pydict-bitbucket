incubus

(ˈɪnkjʊbəs )
Definitions
noun plural -bi (-ˌbaɪ) or -buses1. a demon believed in folklore to lie upon sleeping persons, esp to have sexual intercourse with sleeping women. Compare succubus2. something that oppresses, worries, or disturbs greatly, esp a nightmare or obsession
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Late Latin, from incubāre to lie upon; see incubate

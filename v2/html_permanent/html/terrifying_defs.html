Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [oft ADJ to-inf]If something is terrifying, it makes you very frightened. ⇒ I still find it terrifying to find myself surrounded by large numbers of horses. ⇒ Rabies has been described as one of the most terrifying diseases known to man.terrifyingly graded adverb ⇒ Below was a terrifyingly deep crevasse. ⇒ Costs can escalate terrifyingly.

Milan

(mɪˈlæn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna city in N Italy, in central Lombardy: Italy's second largest city and chief financial and industrial centre; a centre of the Renaissance under the Visconti and Sforza families. Pop: 1 256 211 (2001). Italian name: Milano (miˈlaːno). Latin name: Mediolanum (ˌmeɪdɪəʊˈlɑːnəm )

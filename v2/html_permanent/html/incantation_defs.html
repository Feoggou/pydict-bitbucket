incantation

(ˌɪnkænˈteɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. ritual recitation of magic words or sounds2. the formulaic words or sounds used; a magic spell
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

incantational (ˌincanˈtational) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Late Latin incantātiō an enchanting, from incantāre to repeat magic formulas, from Latin, from in-2 + cantāre to sing; see enchant

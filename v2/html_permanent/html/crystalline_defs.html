crystalline

(ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having the characteristics or structure of crystals2. consisting of or containing crystals3. made of or like crystal; transparent; clear
Derived forms
crystallinity (ˌkrɪstəˈlɪnɪtɪ ) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]A crystalline substance is in the form of crystals or contains crystals. ⇒ Diamond is the crystalline form of the element carbon. ⇒ ...hard, crystalline rock.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Crystalline means clear or bright. [literary] ⇒ ...a huge plain dotted with crystalline lakes.

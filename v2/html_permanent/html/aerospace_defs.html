aerospace

(ˈɛərəˌspeɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the atmosphere and space beyond2. (modifier) of or relating to rockets, missiles, space vehicles, etc, that fly or operate in aerospace ⇒ the aerospace industry

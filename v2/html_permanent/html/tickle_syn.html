Synonyms of tickle

tickle

verb

  1. stroke, pet, chuck, touch lightly
    • I was tickling him, and he was laughing and giggling.
  2. amuse, delight, entertain, please, divert, gratify, titillate
    • The story really tickled me.
    OPPOSITES:  bore, annoy, irritate, trouble, bother, weary
  3. stimulate, interest, excite, appeal to, arouse, captivate
    • Interesting words tickle the imagination.

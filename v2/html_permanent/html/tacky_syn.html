Synonyms of tacky 1

tacky1

adjective

  1. sticky, wet, adhesive, gummy, icky (informal), gluey
    • If the finish is still tacky, leave to harden.

tacky2 (informal)

adjective

  1. vulgar, cheap, tasteless, nasty, sleazy, naff (British, slang)
    • tacky red sunglasses
  2. seedy, shabby, shoddy
    • The whole thing is dreadfully tacky.

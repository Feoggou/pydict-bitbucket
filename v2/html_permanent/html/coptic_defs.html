Coptic

(ˈkɒptɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an Afro-Asiatic language, written in the Greek alphabet but descended from ancient Egyptian. It was extinct as a spoken language by about 1600 ad but survives in the Coptic Churchadjective2. of or relating to this language3. of or relating to the Copts

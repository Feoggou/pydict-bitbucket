status

noun

  1. position, rank, grade, degree, ranking • promoted to the status of foreman
  2. prestige, standing, authority, note, influence, weight, reputation, honour, importance, consequence, fame, distinction, eminence, renown, mana (New Zealand) • She cheated banks to satisfy her desire for money and status.
  3. state of play, development, progress, condition, evolution, progression • Please keep us informed of the status of this project.

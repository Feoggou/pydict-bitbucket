Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveIf something is confined to a particular place, it exists only in that place. If it is confined to a particular group, only members of that group have it. [+ to] ⇒ The problem is not confined to Germany. [+ to] ⇒ These dangers are not confined to smokers.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A confined space or area is small and enclosed by walls. ⇒ His long legs bent up in the confined space.3. adjectiveIf someone is confined to a wheelchair, bed, or house, they have to stay there, because they have a disability or are ill. This use could cause offence. [+ to] ⇒ He had been confined to a wheelchair since childhood.

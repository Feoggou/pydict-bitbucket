trainer

(ˈtreɪnə )
Definitions
noun1. a person who trains athletes in a sport2. a piece of equipment employed in training, such as a simulated aircraft cockpit3. horse racing a person who schools racehorses and prepares them for racing4. (plural) an informal name for training shoes
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

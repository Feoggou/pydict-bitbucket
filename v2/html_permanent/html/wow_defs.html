wow1

(waʊ )
Definitions
exclamation1. an exclamation of admiration, amazement, etcnoun2. slang a person or thing that is amazingly successful, attractive, etcverb3. (transitive) slang to arouse great enthusiasm in
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: originally Scottish, expressive of surprise, amazement, etc

wow2

(waʊ , wəʊ )
Definitions
nouna slow variation or distortion in pitch that occurs at very low audio frequencies in sound-reproducing systems, such as a record player, usually due to variation in speed of the turntable, etc. See also flutter (sense 14)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: of imitative origin

wow for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(waʊ )
Word forms: wows , wowing , wowed
Definitions
1. exclamationYou can say 'wow' when you are very impressed, surprised, or pleased. [informal, feelings] ⇒ I thought, 'Wow, what a good idea'.2. verbYou say that someone wows people when they give an impressive performance and fill people with enthusiasm and admiration. [informal] [V n] ⇒ Ben Tankard wowed the crowd with his jazz.

claimant

(ˈkleɪmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who makes a claim2. a person who brings a civil action in a court of law. Formerly called: plaintiff Compare defendant (sense 1)

[British] ⇒ ...benefit claimants.2. countable nounA claimant is someone who asks to be given something which they think they are entitled to. ⇒ The compensation will be split between 140 claimants. ⇒ ...Louis-Alphonso, the rival claimant to the French throne.

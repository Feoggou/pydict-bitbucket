habitat

(ˈhæbɪˌtæt )
Definitions
noun1. the environment in which an animal or plant normally lives or grows2. the place in which a person, group, class, etc, is normally found
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Latin: it inhabits, from habitāre to dwell, from habēre to have

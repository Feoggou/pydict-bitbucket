segmental

(sɛɡˈmɛntəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, like, or having the form of a segment2. divided into segments3. linguistics of, relating to, or constituting an isolable speech sound

Derived Forms

segmentally (segˈmentally) adverb

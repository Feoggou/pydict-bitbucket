Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A painstaking search, examination, or investigation is done extremely carefully and thoroughly. ⇒ Forensic experts carried out a painstaking search of the debris.painstakingly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ Broken bones were painstakingly pieced together and reshaped.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that someone is sanctimonious, you disapprove of them because you think that they are trying to appear morally better than other people. [disapproval] ⇒ He writes smug, sanctimonious rubbish. ⇒ You sanctimonious little hypocrite!sanctimoniousness uncount noun ⇒ She displays none of the sanctimoniousness often associated with spirituality.

sumptuous

(ˈsʌmptjʊəs )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. expensive or extravagant ⇒ sumptuous costumes2. magnificent; splendid ⇒ a sumptuous scene

Derived Forms

sumptuously (ˈsumptuously) adverbsumptuousness (ˈsumptuousness) or sumptuosity (ˌsʌmptjʊˈɒsɪtɪ ) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Old French somptueux, from Latin sumptuōsus costly, from sumptus; see sumptuary

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verb [usu passive]If you are debarred from doing something, you are prevented from doing it by a law or regulation. [formal] [be V-ed + from] ⇒ If found guilty, she could be debarred from politics for seven years. [be V-ed from n/-ing] ⇒ Convictions for criminal offences might lead to the persons concerned being debarred from entering the teaching profession. [Also be V-ed from -ing]

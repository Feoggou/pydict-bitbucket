croon

verb

  1. sing, warble • a nightclub singer who crooned romantic songs
  2. say softly, breathe, hum, purr • The man was crooning soft words of encouragement to his wife.

'croon' in Other Languages

  • British English: croon VERB
    If you croon, you sing or hum quietly and gently.He would much rather have been crooning in a smoky bar.

Liberian

(laɪˈbɪərɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to Liberia or its inhabitantsnoun2. a native or inhabitant of Liberia

Powered by COBUILD
(laɪbɪəriən ) for learners of English
Word forms: Liberians
Definitions
1. adjectiveLiberian means belonging or relating to Liberia, its people, or its culture.2. countable nounA Liberian is a person who comes from Liberia, or a person of Liberian origin.

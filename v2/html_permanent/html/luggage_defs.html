luggage

(ˈlʌɡɪdʒ )
Definitions
nounsuitcases, trunks, etc, containing personal belongings for a journey; baggage
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: perhaps from lug1, influenced in form by baggage

luggage

Powered by COBUILD
(lʌgɪdʒ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounLuggage is the suitcases and bags that you take with you when travel. ⇒ Leave your luggage in the hotel. ⇒ Each passenger was allowed two 30-kg pieces of luggage.2. → See also left luggage
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

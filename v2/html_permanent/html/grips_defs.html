grips

(ˈɡrɪps)
Definitions
plural nounthe act of dealing with or confronting something ⇒ The government's first task is to get to grips with the economy., ⇒ unable to come to grips with his current problems.

What I can't get to grips with is the fact that there's no discernible pattern. Forbes, Bryan THE ENDLESS GAMEHe had an hour or so alone to get to grips with anything that was troubling him.

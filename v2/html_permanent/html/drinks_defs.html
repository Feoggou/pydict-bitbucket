drinks

(drɪŋks)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun(modifier) of or relating to alcoholic beverages ⇒ Remy Cointreau, the French drinks group, ⇒ Allied Domecq, the drinks company, ⇒ a drinks party, ⇒ the drinks industry, ⇒ the chain, owned by drinks giant Diageo, ⇒ the latest commercial for drinks firm Schweppes

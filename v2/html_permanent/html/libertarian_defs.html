libertarian

(ˌlɪbəˈtɛərɪən )
Definitions
noun1. a believer in freedom of thought, expression, etc2. philosophy a believer in the doctrine of free will. Compare determinismadjective3. of, relating to, or characteristic of a libertarian
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

libertarianism (ˌliberˈtarianism) noun

Word Origin

C18: from liberty

Definitions
adjectiveIf someone is libertarian or has libertarian attitudes, they believe in or support the idea that people should be free to think and behave in the way that they want. [formal] ⇒ ...the libertarian argument that people should be allowed to choose. ⇒ The town's political climate was libertarian.A libertarian is someone who with libertarian views. ⇒ Libertarians argue that nothing should be censored.

navigator

(ˈnævɪˌɡeɪtə )
Definitions
noun1. a person who is skilled in or performs navigation, esp on a ship or aircraft2. (esp formerly) a person who explores by ship3. an instrument or device for assisting a pilot to navigate an aircraft
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
1. countable nounThe navigator on an aircraft or ship is the person whose job is to work out the direction in which the aircraft or ship should be travelling. ⇒ He became an RAF navigator during the war.2. countable nounIn the past, a navigator was an explorer who travelled by sea.

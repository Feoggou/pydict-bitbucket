injury

(ˈɪndʒərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ries1. physical damage or hurt2. a specific instance of this ⇒ a leg injury3. harm done to a reputation4. law a violation or infringement of another person's rights that causes him harm and is actionable at law5. an obsolete word for insult
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin injūria injustice, wrong, from injūriōsus acting unfairly, wrongful, from in-1 + jūs right

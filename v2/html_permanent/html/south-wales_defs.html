South Wales

(saʊθ weɪlz)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nounan area of Wales bordered by England and the Bristol Channel to the east and south, and Mid Wales and West Wales to the north and west. Includes the capital city, Cardiff, as well as Swansea and Newport. Welsh name: De Cymru

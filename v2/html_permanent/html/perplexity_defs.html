perplexity

(pəˈplɛksɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the state of being perplexed2. the state of being intricate or complicated3. something that perplexes

perplexity

Powered by COBUILD
(pəʳpleksɪti )
Word forms: perplexities
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounPerplexity is a feeling of being confused and frustrated because you do not understand something. ⇒ He began counting them and then, with growing perplexity, counted them a second time.2. countable noun [usu pl]The perplexities of something are those things about it which are difficult to understand because they are complicated. [+ of] ⇒ ...the perplexities of quantum mechanics.

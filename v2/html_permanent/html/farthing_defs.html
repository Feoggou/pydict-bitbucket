farthing

(ˈfɑːðɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. a former British bronze coin, worth a quarter of an old penny, that ceased to be legal tender in 19612. something of negligible value; jot
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English fēorthing from fēortha fourth + -ing1

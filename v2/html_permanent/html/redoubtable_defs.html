redoubtable

(rɪˈdaʊtəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. to be feared; formidable2. worthy of respect
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

redoubtableness (reˈdoubtableness) nounredoubtably (reˈdoubtably) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Old French, from redouter to dread, from re- + douter to be afraid, doubt

redoubtable

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪdaʊtəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone as redoubtable, you respect them because they have a very strong character, even though you are slightly afraid of them. ⇒ ...the redoubtable Mr Brooks. ⇒ He is a redoubtable fighter.

waist

(weɪst )
Definitions
noun1. anatomy the constricted part of the trunk between the ribs and hips2. the part of a garment covering the waist3. the middle part of an object that resembles the waist in narrowness or position4. the middle part of a ship5. Also called: centre section the middle section of an aircraft fuselage6. the constriction between the thorax and abdomen in wasps and similar insects
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

waistless (ˈwaistless) adjective

Word Origin

C14: origin uncertain; related to Old English wæstm wax2

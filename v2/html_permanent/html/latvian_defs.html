Latvian

(ˈlætvɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to Latvia, its people, or their languagenoun2. Also called: Lettish the official language of Latvia: closely related to Lithuanian and belonging to the Baltic branch of the Indo-European family3. a native or inhabitant of Latvia

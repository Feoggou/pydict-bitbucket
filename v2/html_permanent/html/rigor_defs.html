rigor

(ˈraɪɡɔː , ˈrɪɡə )
Definitions
noun1. medicine a sudden feeling of chilliness, often accompanied by shivering: it sometimes precedes a fever2. (ˈrɪɡə) pathology rigidity of a muscle; muscular cramp3. a state of rigidity assumed by some animals in reaction to sudden shock4. the inertia assumed by some plants in conditions unfavourable to growth

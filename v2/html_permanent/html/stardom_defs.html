(ˈstɑːdəm )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the fame and prestige of being a star in films, sport, etc2. the world of celebrities

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounStardom is the state of being very famous, usually as an actor, musician, or sports player. ⇒ In 1929 she shot to stardom on Broadway in a Noel Coward play.

inertia

(ɪnˈɜːʃə , -ʃɪə)
Definitions
noun1. the state of being inert; disinclination to move or act2. physicsa. the tendency of a body to preserve its state of rest or uniform motion unless acted upon by an external forceb. an analogous property of other physical quantities that resist change ⇒ thermal inertia

Definitions

Definitions

manicure

(ˈmænɪˌkjʊə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. care of the hands and fingernails, involving shaping the nails, removing cuticles, etc2. another word for manicuristverb3. to care for (the hands and fingernails) in this way4. (transitive) to trim neatly

Word Origin

C19: from French, from Latin manus hand + cūra care

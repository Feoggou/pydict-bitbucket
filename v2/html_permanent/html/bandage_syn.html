bandage

noun

  1. dressing, plaster, sling, compress, gauze, lint, tourniquet • His chest was swathed in bandages.

verb

  1. dress, cover, bind, wrap, swathe, strap up, put a bandage on • Apply a dressing to the wound and bandage it.

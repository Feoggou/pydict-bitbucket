Augusta

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a city in the US, in Georgia. Pop: 193 316 (2003 est) (including Richmond)2. a port in S Italy, in E Sicily. Pop: 33 820 (2001)3. a town in the US, in Maine: the state capital; founded (1628) as a trading post; timber industry. Pop: 18 618 (2003 est)

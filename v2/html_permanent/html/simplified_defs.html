simplified

(ˈsɪmplɪfaɪd)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. made less complicated, clearer, or easier ⇒ Last night, the president presented a shorter, simplified version of his speech.2. mathematics reduced to a simpler form by cancellation of common factors, regrouping of terms in the same variable, etc ⇒ The algebraic expression can be simplified.
Example sentences containing 'simplified'
The Dialogue version has no colour of course and is greatly simplified , but basically they have much in common. Anthony Masters CASCADES - THE DAY OF THE DEADWe have here a miniaturized and vastly simplified version of the Chunnel. Terman, Douglas CORMORANT

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are respectful, you show respect for someone. ⇒ He was always so polite and respectful. ⇒ The children in our family are always respectful to their elders. ⇒ The patient has the right to considerate and respectful care.respectfully graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ 'You are an artist,' she said respectfully.

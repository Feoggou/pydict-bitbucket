trusty

(ˈtrʌstɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: trustier or trustiest1. faithful or reliable2. archaic trustingnounWord forms: plural trusties3. someone who is trusted, esp a convict to whom special privileges are granted
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

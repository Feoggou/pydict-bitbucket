bloodless

(ˈblʌdlɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. without blood2. conducted without violence (esp in the phrase bloodless revolution)3. anaemic-looking; pale4. lacking vitality; lifeless5. lacking in emotion; cold; unfeeling
Derived forms
bloodlessly (ˈbloodlessly) adverbbloodlessness (ˈbloodlessness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA bloodless coup or victory is one in which nobody is killed. ⇒ Reports from the area indicate that it was a bloodless coup. ⇒ The campaign would be short and relatively bloodless.bloodlessly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ This war had to be fought fast and relatively bloodlessly.2. adjectiveIf you describe someone's face or skin as bloodless, you mean that it is very pale. ⇒ ...her face grey and bloodless.

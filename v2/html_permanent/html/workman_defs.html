workman

(ˈwɜːkmən )
Definitions
noun plural -men1. a man who is employed in manual labour or who works an industrial machine2. a craftsman of skill as specified ⇒ a bad workman
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
countable nounA workman is a man who works with his hands, for example building or repairing houses or roads. ⇒ In University Square workmen are building a steel fence. ⇒ Millson saw the workman, Terry, descending the ladder.

dramatis personae

(ˈdrɑːmətɪs pəˈsəʊnaɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun (often functioning as singular)1. the characters or a list of characters in a play or story2. the main personalities in any situation or event
Word origin
C18: from New Latin

gravy

(ˈɡreɪvɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -vies1. a. the juices that exude from meat during cookingb. the sauce made by thickening and flavouring such juices2. slang money or gain acquired with little effort, esp above that needed for ordinary living3. slang wonderful; excellent ⇒ it's all gravy
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French gravé, of uncertain origin

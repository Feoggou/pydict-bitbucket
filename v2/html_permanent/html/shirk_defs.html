shirk

Powered by COBUILD
(ʃɜːʳk )
Word forms: shirks , shirking , shirked
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verb [usu with neg]If someone does not shirk their responsibility or duty, they do what they have a responsibility to do. [V n] ⇒ We in the Congress can't shirk our responsibility. [V + from] ⇒ The Government will not shirk from considering the need for further action. [Also V]shirker Word forms: shirkers countable noun ⇒ They know I'm not a shirker.

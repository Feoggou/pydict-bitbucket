none the for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
phraseYou use none the to say that someone or something does not have any more of a particular quality than they did before. ⇒ You could end up committed to yet another savings scheme and none the wiser about managing your finances. ⇒ He became convinced that his illness was purely imaginary: that made it none the better. →  none

death mask

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna cast of a person's face taken shortly after death. Compare life mask

death mask

Powered by COBUILD

Word forms: death masks
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA death mask is a model of someone's face, which is made from a mould that was taken of their face soon after they died.

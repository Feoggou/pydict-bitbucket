spongy

(ˈspʌndʒɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective -gier or -giest1. of or resembling a sponge, esp in texture, porosity, elasticity, or compressibility ⇒ spongy bread, ⇒ spongy bone2. of or like a sponge in respect of its capacity to absorb fluid and yield it when compressed

Derived Forms

spongily (ˈspongily) adverbsponginess (ˈsponginess) noun

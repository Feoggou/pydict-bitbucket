Thai

(taɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Thailand, its people, or their languagenoun2. Word forms: plural Thais or Thai a native or inhabitant of Thailand3. the language of Thailand, sometimes classified as belonging to the Sino-Tibetan family

Thai

Powered by COBUILD
(taɪ ) for learners of English
Word forms: Thais
Definitions
1. adjectiveThai means belonging or relating to Thailand, or to its people, language, or culture. ⇒ Thai food is terrific. ⇒ ...Thai businessmen.2. countable nounA Thai is a person who comes from Thailand.3. uncount nounThai is the language spoken in Thailand.

vixen

(ˈvɪksən )
Definitions
noun1. a female fox2. a quarrelsome or spiteful woman
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vixenish (ˈvixenish) adjectivevixenishly (ˈvixenishly) adverbvixenishness (ˈvixenishness) nounvixenly (ˈvixenly) adverb, adjective

Word Origin

C15: fixen; related to Old English fyxe, feminine of fox; compare Old High German fuhsīn

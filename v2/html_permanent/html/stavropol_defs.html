Stavropol

(Russian ˈstavrəpəlj)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a city in SW Russia: founded as a fortress in 1777. Pop: 362 000 (2005 est). Former name (1940–44): Voroshilovsk2. the former name (until 1964) of Togliatti1

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Southbound roads or vehicles lead or are travelling towards the south. ⇒ ...the southbound train from the Scottish Highlands. ⇒ ...the southbound carriageway of the M61.

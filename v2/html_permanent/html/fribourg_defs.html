Definitions
noun1. a canton in W Switzerland. Capital: Fribourg. Pop: 242 700 (2002 est). Area: 1676 sq km (645 sq miles)2. a town in W Switzerland, capital of Fribourg canton: university (1889). Pop: 35 547 (2000)

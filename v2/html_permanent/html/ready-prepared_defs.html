ready-prepared

(ˌrɛdɪprɪˈpɛəd)
Definitions
adjectiveprepared in advance of use ⇒ ready-prepared snacks/food/meals, ⇒ an injection with a ready-prepared syringe, ⇒ ready-prepared survival kits

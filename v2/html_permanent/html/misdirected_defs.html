misdirected

(ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛktɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. used for a wrong or bad purpose ⇒ a misdirected effort to mollify the bishop2. (of mail) wrongly addressed ⇒ the confusion caused by frequently misdirected mail, ⇒ Be assured that your prize will not be delayed or misdirected in the mail.

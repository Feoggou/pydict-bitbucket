Orientalia in American English

(ˌɔriɛnˈteɪliə ; ˌ oʊriɛnˈteɪliə )
Definitions
plural noun art objects, artifacts, documents, books, etc. having their origin in, or written about, the nations and cultures of Asia, specif. the Far East

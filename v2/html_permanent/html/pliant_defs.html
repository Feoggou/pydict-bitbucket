pliant

(ˈplaɪənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. easily bent; supple ⇒ a pliant young tree2. easily modified; adaptable; flexible ⇒ a pliant system3. yielding readily to influence; compliant

Derived Forms

pliancy (ˈpliancy) or pliantness (ˈpliantness) nounpliantly (ˈpliantly) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Old French, from plier to fold, bend; see ply2

runny

(ˈrʌnɪ )
Definitions
adjective -nier or -niest1. tending to flow; liquid2. (of the nose or nasal passages) exuding mucus

runny

Powered by COBUILD
(rʌni ) for learners of English
Word forms: runnier , runniest
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is runny is more liquid than usual or than was intended. ⇒ Warm the honey until it becomes runny. ⇒ ...a runny soft cheese.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If someone has a runny nose or runny eyes, liquid is flowing from their nose or eyes. ⇒ Symptoms are streaming eyes, a runny nose, headache and a cough.

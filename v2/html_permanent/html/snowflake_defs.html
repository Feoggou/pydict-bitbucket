snowflake

(ˈsnəʊˌfleɪk )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. one of the mass of small thin delicate arrangements of ice crystals that fall as snow2. any of various European amaryllidaceous plants of the genus Leucojum, such as L. vernum (spring snowflake), that have white nodding bell-shaped flowers

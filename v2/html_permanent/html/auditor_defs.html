auditor

(ˈɔːdɪtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person qualified to audit accounts2. a person who hears or listens3. Australian, US and Canadian a registered student who attends a class that is not an official part of his course of study, without actively participating it
Derived forms
ˌaudiˈtorial adjective
Word origin
C14: from Old French auditeur, from Latin audītor a hearer

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [n N]A sufferer from an illness or some other bad condition is a person who is affected by the illness or condition. [+ of] ⇒ Frequently sufferers of this kind of allergy are also sufferers of asthma. ⇒ ...hay-fever sufferers.

Gallic

(ˈɡælɪk )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to France2. of or relating to ancient Gaul or the Gauls

Gallic

Powered by COBUILD
(gælɪk ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Gallic means the same as French. You sometimes use Gallic to describe ideas, feelings, or actions that you think are very typical of France and French people. ⇒ The proposal has provoked howls of Gallic indignation. ⇒ Mme Arlette gave a Gallic shrug.

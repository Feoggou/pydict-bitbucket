Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf you say that something happens thanks to a particular person or thing, you mean that they are responsible for it happening or caused it to happen. [+ to] ⇒ It is thanks to this committee that many new sponsors have come forward. ⇒ Thanks to recent research, effective treatments are available. →  thank

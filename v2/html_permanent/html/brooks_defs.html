Brooks

(brʊks )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Geraldine. born 1955, Australian writer. Her novels include March (2005), which won the Pulitzer prize2.  Mel, real name Melvyn Kaminsky. born 1926, US comedy writer, actor, and film director. His films include The Producers (1968), Blazing Saddles (1974), High Anxiety (1977), and Dracula: Dead and Loving It (1996)3.  (Troyal) Garth. born 1962, US country singer and songwriter; his bestselling records include Ropin' the Wind (1991) and Scarecrow (2001)

sensual

(ˈsɛnsjʊəl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to any of the senses or sense organs; bodily2. strongly or unduly inclined to gratification of the senses3. tending to arouse the bodily appetites, esp the sexual appetite4. of or relating to sensualism

Derived Forms

sensually (ˈsensually) adverbsensualness (ˈsensualness) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Late Latin sensuālis, from Latin sēnsus sense. Compare French sensuel, Italian sensuale

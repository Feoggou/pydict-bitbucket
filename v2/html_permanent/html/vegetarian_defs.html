vegetarian

(ˌvɛdʒɪˈtɛərɪən )
Definitions
noun1. a person who advocates or practises vegetarianismadjective2. relating to, advocating, or practising vegetarianism3. cookery strictly, consisting of vegetables and fruit only, but usually including milk, cheese, eggs, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

mallard

(ˈmælɑːd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -lard or -lardsa duck, Anas platyrhynchos, common over most of the N hemisphere, the male of which has a dark green head and reddish-brown breast: the ancestor of all domestic breeds of duck

Word Origin

C14: from Old French mallart, perhaps from maslart (unattested); see male, -ard

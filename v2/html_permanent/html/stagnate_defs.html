stagnate

(stæɡˈneɪt , ˈstæɡˌneɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(intransitive) to be or to become stagnant

Derived Forms

stagnation (stagˈnation) noun

Powered by COBUILD
(stægneɪt , US stægneɪt )
Word forms: stagnates , stagnating , stagnated
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf something such as a business or society stagnates, it stops changing or progressing. [disapproval] [V] ⇒ Industrial production is stagnating. [V] ⇒ His career had stagnated.stagnation (stægneɪʃən ) uncount noun [+ of] ⇒ ...the stagnation of the steel industry.

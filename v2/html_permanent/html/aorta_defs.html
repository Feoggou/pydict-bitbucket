aorta

(eɪˈɔːtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -tas or -tae (-tiː)the main vessel in the arterial network, which conveys oxygen-rich blood from the heart to all parts of the body except the lungs
Derived forms
aˈortic or aˈortal adjective
Word origin
C16: from New Latin, from Greek aortē, literally: something lifted, from aeirein to raise

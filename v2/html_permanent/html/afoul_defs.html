afoul

(əˈfaʊl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb, adjective (postpositive)1. (usually foll by of) in or into a state of difficulty, confusion, or conflict (with)2. (often foll by of) in or into an entanglement or collision (with) (often in the phrase run afoul of) ⇒ a yacht with its sails afoul, ⇒ the boat ran afoul of a steamer

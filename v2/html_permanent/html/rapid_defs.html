rapid

(ˈræpɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. (of an action or movement) performed or occurring during a short interval of time; quick ⇒ a rapid transformation2. characterized by high speed ⇒ rapid movement3. acting or moving quickly; fast ⇒ a rapid worker
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

rapidly (ˈrapidly) adverbrapidity (rəˈpɪdɪtɪ ) or rapidness (ˈrapidness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin rapidus tearing away, from rapere to seize; see rape1

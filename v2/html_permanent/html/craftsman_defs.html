craftsman

(ˈkrɑːftsmən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -men1. a member of a skilled trade; someone who practises a craft; artisan2. Also called: (fem) craftswoman an artist skilled in the techniques of an art or craft
Derived forms
craftsmanˌlike (ˈcraftsmanˌlike) adjectivecraftsmanly (ˈcraftsmanly) adjectivecraftsmanˌship (ˈcraftsmanˌship) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf your behaviour is proprietorial, you are behaving in a proud way because you are, or feel like you are, the owner of something. [formal] ⇒ The longer I live alone the more proprietorial I become about my home.

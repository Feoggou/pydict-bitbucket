fitted sheet

(ˈfɪtɪd ʃiːt)
Definitions
nouna sheet with ends that are elasticated and shaped to fit tightly over a mattress
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Translations for 'fitted sheet'

  • British English: fitted sheet /ˈfɪtɪd ʃiːt/ NOUN
    A fitted sheet is a bedsheet with the corners sewn so that they fit over the corners of the mattress and do not have to be folded.

eucalyptus

(ˌjuːkəˈlɪptəs ) or eucalypt (ˈjuːkəˌlɪpt )
Definitions
noun plural -lyptuses, -lypti (-ˈlɪptaɪ) or -lyptsany myrtaceous tree of the mostly Australian genus Eucalyptus, such as the blue gum and ironbark, widely cultivated for the medicinal oil in their leaves (eucalyptus oil), timber, and ornament
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: New Latin, from eu- + Greek kaluptos covered, from kaluptein to cover, hide

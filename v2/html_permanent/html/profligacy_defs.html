profligacy

Powered by COBUILD
(prɒflɪgəsi )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounProfligacy is the spending of too much money or the using of too much of something. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ ...the continuing profligacy of certain states.

withstand

(wɪðˈstænd )
Definitions
verb -stands, -standing or -stood1. (transitive) to stand up to forcefully; resist2. (intransitive) to remain firm in endurance or opposition
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
verbIf something or someone withstands a force or action, they survive it or do not give in to it. [formal] [V n] ⇒ ...armoured vehicles designed to withstand chemical attack. [V n] ⇒ Exercise really can help you withstand stresses and strains more easily.

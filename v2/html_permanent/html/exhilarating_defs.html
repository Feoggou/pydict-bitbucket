Definitions

(ɪgzɪləreɪtɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe an experience or feeling as exhilarating, you mean that it makes you feel very happy and excited. ⇒ It was exhilarating to be on the road again and his spirits rose. ⇒ ...in the exhilarating days of German unification.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

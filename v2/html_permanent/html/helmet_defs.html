helmet

(ˈhɛlmɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a piece of protective or defensive armour for the head worn by soldiers, policemen, firemen, divers, etc2. biology a part or structure resembling a helmet, esp the upper part of the calyx of certain flowers
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

helmeted (ˈhelmeted) adjective

Word Origin

C15: from Old French, diminutive of helme, of Germanic origin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [usu in N of n]If you do something in pursuance of a particular activity, you do it as part of carrying out that activity. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ He ordered disclosure of a medical report to the Metropolitan Police in pursuance of an investigation of murder.

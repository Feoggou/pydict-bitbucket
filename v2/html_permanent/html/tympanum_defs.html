tympanum

(ˈtɪmpənəm )
Definitions
noun plural -nums or -na (-nə)1. anatomya. the cavity of the middle earb. another name for tympanic membrane2. any diaphragm resembling that in the middle ear in function3. Also called: tympan architecturea. the recessed space bounded by the cornices of a pediment, esp one that is triangular in shape and ornamentedb. the recessed space bounded by an arch and the lintel of a doorway or window below it4. music a tympan or drum5. a scoop wheel for raising water
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin, from Greek tumpanon drum; related to Greek tuptein to beat

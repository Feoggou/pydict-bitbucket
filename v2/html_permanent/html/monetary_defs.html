monetary

(ˈmʌnɪtərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to money or currency2. of or relating to monetarism ⇒ a monetary policy

Derived Forms

monetarily (ˈmonetarily) adverb

Word Origin

C19: from Late Latin monētārius, from Latin monēta money

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]Monetary means relating to money, especially the total amount of money in a country. [business] ⇒ Some countries tighten monetary policy to avoid inflation. ⇒ The courts will be asked to place a monetary value on his unfinished career.

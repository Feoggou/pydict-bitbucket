Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is pliable, you can bend it easily without cracking or breaking it. ⇒ As your baby grows bigger, his bones become less pliable. ⇒ The finely twined baskets are made with young, pliable spruce roots.2. graded adjectiveSomeone who is pliable can be easily influenced and controlled by other people. pliant, compliant ⇒ If we go on expecting our daughters to be decorative and pliable and empty-headed, they'll be inadequately prepared for the future.

prosperous

(ˈprɒspərəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. flourishing; prospering2. rich; affluent; wealthy3. favourable or promising

Derived Forms

prosperously (ˈprosperously) adverbprosperousness (ˈprosperousness) noun

fishing

(ˈfɪʃɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. a. the occupation of catching fishb. (as modifier) ⇒ a fishing match2. another word for piscary (sense 2)piscatorial

(fɪʃɪŋ) in the Oil and Gas Industry
Definitions
noun(Extractive engineering: Field development, Drilling) Fishing is the act of removing something from the borehole which should not be there. ⇒ In fishing, specially designed tools are used to grab equipment which has been accidentally lost down the borehole. ⇒ The process of removing unwanted objects from the borehole is called fishing. ⇒ Fishing is the act of removing something from the borehole which should not be there.

grenadier

(ˌɡrɛnəˈdɪə )
Definitions
noun1. militarya. (in the British Army) a member of the senior regiment of infantry in the Household Brigadeb. (formerly) a member of a special formation, usually selected for strength and heightc. (formerly) a soldier trained to throw grenades2. Also called: rat-tail any deep-sea gadoid fish of the family Macrouridae, typically having a large head and trunk and a long tapering tail3. any of various African weaverbirds of the genus Estrilda. See waxbill
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from French

superstition

(ˌsuːpəˈstɪʃən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. irrational belief usually founded on ignorance or fear and characterized by obsessive reverence for omens, charms, etc2. a notion, act or ritual that derives from such belief3. any irrational belief, esp with regard to the unknown

Word Origin

C15: from Latin superstitiō dread of the supernatural, from superstāre to stand still by something (as in amazement)

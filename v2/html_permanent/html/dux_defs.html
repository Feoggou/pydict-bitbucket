Comment by ALRifai on 9 Aug 2016
dux (duces) n. (dʌks). 1. In schools in the UK, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa and Iceland, dux is a modern title given to the highest-ranking student in academic or sporting achievement; the top pupil in a class or school. 2.

descendant

Powered by COBUILD
(dɪsendənt )
Word forms: descendants
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu pl, usu with poss]Someone's descendants are the people in later generations who are related to them. [+ of] ⇒ They are descendants of the original English and Scottish settlers. ⇒ ...Lord Cochrane and his descendants.2. countable nounSomething modern which developed from an older thing can be called a descendant of it. [+ of] ⇒ His design was a descendant of a 1956 device. ⇒ They are the descendants of plants imported by the early settlers.

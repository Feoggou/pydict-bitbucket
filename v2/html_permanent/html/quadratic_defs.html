quadratic

(kwɒˈdrætɪk ) mathematics
Definitions
noun1. Also called: quadratic equation an equation containing one or more terms in which the variable is raised to the power of two, but no terms in which it is raised to a higher poweradjective2. Also: quadratical of or relating to the second power

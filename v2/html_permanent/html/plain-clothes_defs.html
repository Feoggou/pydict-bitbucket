plain clothes

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nouna. ordinary clothes, as distinguished from uniform, as worn by a police detective on dutyb. (as modifier) ⇒ a plain-clothes policeman

plain-clothes

(ˌpleɪnˈkləʊðz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(of a police officer) wearing ordinary clothes when working, rather than a uniform ⇒ Two plain-clothes detectives lingered by the doorways., ⇒ The plain-clothes policemen tracked the visitors to the pub., ⇒ There were both uniformed and plain-clothes officers inside.

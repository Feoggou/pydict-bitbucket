punctual

(ˈpʌŋktjʊəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. arriving or taking place at an arranged time; prompt2. (of a person) having the characteristic of always keeping to arranged times, as for appointments, meetings, etc3. obsolete precise; exact; apposite4. mathematics consisting of or confined to a point in space

Derived Forms

ˌpunctuality (ˌpunctuˈality) nounpunctually (ˈpunctually) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Medieval Latin punctuālis concerning detail, from Latin punctum point

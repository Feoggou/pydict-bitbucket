Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]Someone who is apprehensive is afraid that something bad may happen. [+ about] ⇒ People are still terribly apprehensive about the future.apprehensively graded adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ I waited apprehensively for him to comment.
Example sentences containing 'apprehensive'
Now that the first shock was wearing off, Crook began to feel more irritated than apprehensive.

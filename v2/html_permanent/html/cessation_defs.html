cessation

(sɛˈseɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna ceasing or stopping; discontinuance; pause ⇒ temporary cessation of hostilities
Word origin
C14: from Latin cessātiō a delaying, inactivity, from cessāre to be idle, desist from, from cēdere to yield, cede

uncount noun [oft a N]The cessation of something is the stopping of it. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ He would not agree to a cessation of hostilities.

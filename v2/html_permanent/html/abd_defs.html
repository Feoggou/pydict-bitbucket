Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
abbreviation for1. all but dissertation: the stage at which a postgraduate doctoral student has completed the preparatory coursework and examinations for their doctorate, but has not yet completed a PhD dissertationnoun2. a student who is at this stage of their studies ⇒ She was still ABD after four years.

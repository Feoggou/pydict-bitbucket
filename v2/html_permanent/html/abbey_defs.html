abbey

(ˈæbɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a building inhabited by a community of monks or nuns governed by an abbot or abbess2. a church built in conjunction with such a building3. such a community of monks or nuns
Word origin
C13: via Old French abeie from Church Latin abbātia abbacy

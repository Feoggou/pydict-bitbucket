fastness

(ˈfɑːstnɪs )
Definitions
noun1. a stronghold; fortress2. the state or quality of being firm or secure3. the ability of a dye to remain permanent and not run or fade4. archaic swiftness
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English fæstnes; see fast1

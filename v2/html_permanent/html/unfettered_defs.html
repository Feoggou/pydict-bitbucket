unfettered for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnfetəʳd )
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n, v-link ADJ, ADJ after v]If you describe something as unfettered, you mean that it is not controlled or limited by anyone or anything. [formal] ⇒ ...unfettered free trade. [+ by] ⇒ Unfettered by the bounds of reality, my imagination flourished. ⇒ ...city slums, where drug traffickers reign virtually unfettered.

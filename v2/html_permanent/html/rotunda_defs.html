rotunda

(rəʊˈtʌndə )
Definitions
nouna building or room having a circular plan, esp one that has a dome
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Italian rotonda, from Latin rotundus round, from rota a wheel

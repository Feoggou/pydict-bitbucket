sojourn

(ˈsɒdʒɜːn , ˈsʌdʒ-)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a temporary stayverb2. (intransitive) to stay or reside temporarily

Derived Forms

sojourner (ˈsojourner) noun

Word Origin

C13: from Old French sojorner, from Vulgar Latin subdiurnāre (unattested) to spend a day, from Latin sub- during + Late Latin diurnum day

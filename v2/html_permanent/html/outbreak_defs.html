outbreak for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(aʊtbreɪk )
Word forms: outbreaks
Definitions
countable noun [usu sing]If there is an outbreak of something unpleasant, such as violence or a disease, it suddenly starts to happen. ⇒ The four-day festival ended a day early after an outbreak of violence involving hundreds of youths. [+ of] ⇒ ...the outbreak of war in the Middle East. ⇒ In Peru, a cholera outbreak continues to spread.

tourist

(ˈtʊərɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a person who travels for pleasure, usually sightseeing and staying in hotelsb. (as modifier) ⇒ tourist attractions2. a person on an excursion or sightseeing tour3. a person travelling abroad as a member of a sports team that is playing a series of usually international matches4. Also called: tourist class the lowest class of accommodation on a passenger shipadjective5. of or relating to tourist accommodation

Derived Forms

touristic (tourˈistic) adjective

acknowledgments

(ækˈnɒlɪdʒmənts) or acknowledgements
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nouna section of text containing an author’s statement acknowledging his or her use of the works of other authors and thanking the people who have helped him or her, usually printed at the front of a book ⇒ a long list of acknowledgments, ⇒ Neither are there any acknowledgments, footnotes or bibliography - no indication at all of the sources used.

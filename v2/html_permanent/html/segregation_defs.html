segregation

(ˌsɛɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of segregating or state of being segregated2. sociology the practice or policy of creating separate facilities within the same society for the use of a minority group3. genetics the separation at meiosis of the two members of any pair of alleles into separate gametes. See also Mendel's laws4. metallurgy the process in which a component of an alloy or solid solution separates in small regions within the solid or on the solid's surface

ˌsegregational (ˌsegreˈgational) adjective

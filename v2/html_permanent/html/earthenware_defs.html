earthenware

(ˈɜːθənˌwɛə )
Definitions
nouna. vessels, etc, made of baked clayb. (as adjective) ⇒ an earthenware pot

earthenware

Powered by COBUILD
(ɜːʳðənweəʳ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Earthenware bowls, pots, or other objects are made of clay that is baked so that it becomes hard. ⇒ ...earthenware pots.2. uncount nounEarthenware objects are referred to as earthenware. ⇒ ...colourful Italian china and earthenware.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

mixer

(ˈmɪksə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that mixes2. informala. a person considered in relation to his or her ability to mix sociallyb. a person who creates trouble for others3. a kitchen appliance, usually electrical, used for mixing foods, etc4. a drink such as ginger ale, fruit juice, etc, used in preparing cocktails5. electronics a device in which two or more input signals are combined to give a single output signal6. short for sound mixer, vision mixer

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA mixer is a machine used for mixing things together. ⇒ ...an electric mixer.2. → see also cement mixer, food mixer3. countable nounA mixer is a non-alcoholic drink such as fruit juice that you mix with strong alcohol such as gin.4. countable noun [adj N]If you say that someone is a good mixer, you mean that they are good at talking to people and making friends. ⇒ Cooper was a good mixer, he was popular.5. countable nounA mixer is a piece of equipment that is used to make changes to recorded music or film. ⇒ ...a three channel audio mixer.

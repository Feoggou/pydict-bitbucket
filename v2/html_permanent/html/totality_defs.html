totality

(təʊˈtælɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. the whole amount2. the state of being total3. the state or period of an eclipse when light from the eclipsed body is totally obscured
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions

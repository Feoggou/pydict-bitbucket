kindness

(ˈkaɪndnɪs )
Definitions
noun1. the practice or quality of being kind2. a kind, considerate, or helpful act
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

kindness for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(kaɪndnəs )
Word forms: kindnesses
Definitions
1. uncount nounKindness is the quality of being gentle, caring, and helpful. ⇒ We have been treated with such kindness by everybody.2. countable nounA kindness is a helpful or considerate act.

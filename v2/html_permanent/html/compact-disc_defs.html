compact disc

(ˈkɒmpækt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna small digital audio disc on which sound is recorded as a series of metallic pits enclosed in PVC; the disc is spun by the compact disc player and read by an optical laser system. Also called: compact audio disc. Abbreviation: CD, CAD

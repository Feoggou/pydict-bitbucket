Definitions

Definitions
verbIf you are enraged by something, it makes you extremely angry. [be V-ed + by] ⇒ He was enraged by news of plans to demolish the pub. [V n] ⇒ He enraged the government by renouncing the agreement.enraged graded adjective & adjective [+ at] ⇒ I began getting more and more enraged at my father. ⇒ The enraged crowd stoned the car, then set it on fire.

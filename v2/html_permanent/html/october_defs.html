October

(ɒkˈtəʊbə )
Definitions
nounthe tenth month of the year, consisting of 31 days
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English, from Latin, from octo eight, since it was the eighth month in Roman reckoning

October for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD

Definitions
variable nounOctober is the tenth month of the year in the Western calendar. ⇒ Most seasonal hiring is done in early October. ⇒ The first plane is due to leave on 2 October. ⇒ My grandson has been away since last October.

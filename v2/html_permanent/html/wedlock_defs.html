wedlock

(ˈwɛdlɒk )
Definitions
noun1. the state of being married2. See born out of wedlock
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English wedlāc, from wedd pledge + -lāc, suffix denoting activity, perhaps from lāc game, battle (related to Gothic laiks dance, Old Norse leikr)

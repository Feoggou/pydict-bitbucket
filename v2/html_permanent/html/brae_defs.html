brae

(breɪ , Scottish bre)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Scottish1. a hill or hillside; slope2. (plural) an upland area ⇒ the Gleniffer Braes
Word origin
C14 bra; related to Old Norse brā eyelash, Old High German brāwa eyelid, eyebrow; compare brow

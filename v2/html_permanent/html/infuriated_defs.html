infuriated

(ɪnˈfjʊərɪˌeɪtɪd)
Definitions
adjectivefurious ⇒ 'That's absolute rubbish!' said the infuriated Colin., ⇒ I was absolutely infuriated with him., ⇒ The champion was infuriated by the decision.

low tide

Definitions
noun1. the tide when it is at its lowest level or the time at which it reaches this2. a lowest point

Word forms: low tides
Definitions
variable noun [oft at N]At the coast, low tide is the time when the sea is at its lowest level because the tide is out. ⇒ The causeway to the island is only accessible at low tide.

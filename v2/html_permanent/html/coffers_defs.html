coffers

(ˈkɒfəz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounliterary a store of money ⇒ The proceeds from the lottery go towards sports and recreation, as well as swelling the coffers of the government., ⇒ Large public and private companies have long been contributors to Tory party coffers.

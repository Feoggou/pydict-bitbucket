outgoing

(ˈaʊtˌɡəʊɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. departing; leaving2. leaving or retiring from office ⇒ the outgoing chairman3. friendly and sociablenoun4. the act of going out
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

outgoing for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(aʊtgoʊɪŋ )
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]You use outgoing to describe a person in charge of something who is soon going to leave that position. retiring ⇒ ...the outgoing director of the Edinburgh International Festival.2. adjective [ADJ n]Outgoing things such as planes, mail, and passengers are leaving or being sent somewhere. ⇒ All outgoing flights were grounded.3. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is outgoing is very friendly and likes meeting and talking to people. ⇒ She's very outgoing. ⇒ ...his outgoing behaviour.

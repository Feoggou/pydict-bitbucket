stank2

(stæŋk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small cofferdam, esp one of timber made watertight with clay2. Scottish and Northern England dialect a pond or poolverb3. (transitive) to make (a stream, cofferdam, etc) watertight, esp with clay

Word Origin

C13: from Old French estanc, probably from estancher to stanch

stank3

(stæŋk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun dialect1. a drain, as in a roadway2. a draining board adjacent to a sink unit

Word Origin

special use of stank2 (in the sense: pool, pond)

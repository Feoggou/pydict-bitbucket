perjured

(ˈpɜːdʒəd ) or perjurous (ˈpɜːdʒərəs)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective criminal law1. a. having sworn falselyb. having committed perjury2. involving or characterized by perjury ⇒ perjured evidence

adjective [usu ADJ n]In a court of law, perjured evidence or perjured testimony is a false statement of events. ⇒ ...information that was based on perjured testimony.

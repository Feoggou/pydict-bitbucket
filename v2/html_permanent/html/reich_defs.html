Reich1

(raɪk , German raiç)
Definitions
noun1. the Holy Roman Empire (First Reich)2. the Hohenzollern empire from 1871 to 1919 (Second Reich)3. the Weimar Republic from 1919 to 19334. the Nazi dictatorship from 1933 to 1945 (Third Reich)

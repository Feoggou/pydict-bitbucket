counterpoise

(ˈkaʊntəˌpɔɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a force, influence, etc, that counterbalances another2. a state of balance; equilibrium3. a weight that balances another4. a radial array of metallic wires, rods, or tubes arranged horizontally around the base of a vertical aerial to increase its transmitting efficiencyverb (transitive)5. to oppose with something of equal effect, weight, or force; offset6. to bring into equilibrium7. archaic to consider (one thing) carefully in relation to another
Example sentences containing 'counterpoise'
Its mighty counterpoise , bright Thorius, lay not far to the left of it. Robert Silverberg KING OF DREAMS

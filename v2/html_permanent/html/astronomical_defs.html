astronomical

(ˌæstrəˈnɒmɪkəl ) or astronomic
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. enormously large; immense2. of or relating to astronomy
Derived forms
ˌastronomically (ˌastroˈnomically) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveIf you describe an amount, especially the cost of something as astronomical, you are emphasizing that it is very large indeed. [emphasis] ⇒ Houses in the village are selling for astronomical prices. ⇒ The cost will be astronomical.astronomically (æstrənɒmɪkli ) adverb [ADV adj, ADV after v] ⇒ He was astronomically wealthy. ⇒ House prices had risen astronomically.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Astronomical means relating to astronomy. ⇒ ...the British Astronomical Association.

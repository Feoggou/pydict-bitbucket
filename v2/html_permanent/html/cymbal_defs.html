cymbal

(ˈsɪmbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna percussion instrument of indefinite pitch consisting of a thin circular piece of brass, which vibrates when clashed together with another cymbal or struck with a stick
Derived forms
cymbaler (ˈcymbaler), ˌcymbaleer (ˌcymbalˈeer) or cymbalist (ˈcymbalist) nouncymbal-ˌlike (ˈcymbal-ˌlike) adjective
Word origin
Old English cymbala, from Medieval Latin, from Latin cymbalum, from Greek kumbalon, from kumbē something hollow

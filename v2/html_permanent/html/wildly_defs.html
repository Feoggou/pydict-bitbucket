wildly for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(waɪldli )
Definitions
1. graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj, oft ADV after v]You use wildly to emphasize the degree, amount, or intensity of something. [emphasis] ⇒ Reports of his drinking have been wildly exaggerated. ⇒ The island's hotels vary wildly.2. → See also wild

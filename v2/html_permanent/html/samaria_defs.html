Samaria

(səˈmɛərɪə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the region of ancient Palestine that extended from Judaea to Galilee and from the Mediterranean to the River Jordan; the N kingdom of Israel2. the capital of this kingdom; constructed northwest of Shechem in the 9th century bc

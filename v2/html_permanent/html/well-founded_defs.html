Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that a report, opinion, or feeling is well-founded, you mean that it is based on facts and can therefore be justified. ⇒ If the reports are well-founded, the incident could seriously aggravate relations between the two nations. ⇒ We must respond to well-founded criticism with a willingness to change.

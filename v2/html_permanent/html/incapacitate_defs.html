incapacitate

(ˌɪnkəˈpæsɪˌteɪt )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to deprive of power, strength, or capacity; disable2. to deprive of legal capacity or eligibility
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

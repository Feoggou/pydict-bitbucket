(ʌnˈɪntrɪstɪŋ , ʌnˈɪntərɪs-)
Definitions
adjectivenot interesting; boring ⇒ lifeless and uninteresting

uninteresting for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnɪntrəstɪŋ )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something or someone as uninteresting, you mean they have no special or exciting qualities. ⇒ Their media has earned the reputation for being rather dull and uninteresting. ⇒ Why did he choose these pale, nerveless, uninteresting people?

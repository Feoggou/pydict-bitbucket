decisive

adjective

  1. crucial, significant, critical, final, positive, absolute, influential, definite, definitive, momentous, conclusive, fateful • his decisive victory in the elections OPPOSITES:  uncertain, doubtful, undecided, indecisive
  2. resolute, decided, firm, determined, forceful, uncompromising, incisive, trenchant, strong-minded • Firm decisive action will be taken to end the incident. OPPOSITES:  uncertain, hesitating, hesitant, undecided, indecisive, vacillating, in two minds (informal), irresolute, pussy-footing (informal)

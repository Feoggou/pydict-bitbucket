Synonyms of television

television

noun

  1. TV, telly (British, informal), small screen (informal), the box (British, informal), receiver, the tube (slang), TV set, gogglebox (British, slang), idiot box (slang)
    • She turned the television on and flicked around the channels.

QUOTATIONS:
Television tells a story in a way that requires no imaginationWitold Rybczynski
I find television very educational. Every time someone switches it on I go into another room and read a good bookGroucho Marx
Television has brought back murder into the home - where it belongsAlfred Hitchcock

(ˈsɪtʃʊeɪtɪd)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjectivelocated; placed ⇒ the building in which Morris's office was situated, ⇒ The village is situated on the side of a hill., ⇒ His hotel is situated in one of the loveliest places on the Loire., ⇒ The pain was situated above and around the eyes., ⇒ The new store is better situated to attract customers.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [adv ADJ]If something is situated in a particular place or position, it is in that place or position. [+ in] ⇒ His hotel is situated in one of the loveliest places on the Loire. ⇒ The pain was situated above and around the eyes. ⇒ The new store is better situated to attract customers.

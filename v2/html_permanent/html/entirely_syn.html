Synonyms of entirely

entirely

adverb

  1. completely, totally, perfectly, absolutely, fully, altogether, thoroughly, wholly, utterly, every inch, without exception, unreservedly, in every respect, without reservation, lock, stock and barrel
    • The two cases are entirely different.
    OPPOSITES:  partly, somewhat, slightly, moderately, partially, incompletely, to a certain extent or degree
  2. only, exclusively, solely
    • The whole episode was entirely my fault.

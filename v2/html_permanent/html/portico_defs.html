portico

(ˈpɔːtɪkəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -coes or -cos1. a covered entrance to a building; porch2. a covered walkway in the form of a roof supported by columns or pillars, esp one built on to the exterior of a building

Word Origin

C17: via Italian from Latin porticus porch

verification

(ˌvɛrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. establishment of the correctness of a theory, fact, etc2. evidence that provides proof of an assertion, theory, etc3. lawa. (formerly) a short affidavit at the end of a pleading stating the pleader's readiness to prove his assertionsb. confirmatory evidence
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

verificative (ˈverifiˌcative) or verificatory (ˈverifiˌcatory) adjective

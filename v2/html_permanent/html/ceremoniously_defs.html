Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverb [ADV with v]If someone does something ceremoniously, they do it in an extremely formal way. [written] ⇒ They ceremoniously cut a piece of ribbon, declaring the exhibition open. ⇒ He thanked her ceremoniously.

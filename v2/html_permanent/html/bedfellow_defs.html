bedfellow

(ˈbɛdˌfɛləʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person with whom one shares a bed2. a temporary ally or associate

Word forms: bedfellows
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu pl]You refer to two things or people as bedfellows when they have become associated or related in some way. ⇒ It's possible to be bedfellows with someone on one issue, and at odds with them on another. ⇒ Sex and death are strange bedfellows.
Example sentences containing 'bedfellow'
They included him, welcomed him, invited him as bedfellow and companion. Ursula K. Le Guin THE DISPOSSESSED

adamantine

(ˌædəˈmæntaɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. very hard; unbreakable or unyielding2. having the lustre of a diamond
Example sentences containing 'adamantine'
Abasio rose, unable to resist the adamantine will in the old man's voice, unwilling to resist any request Olly had made. Tepper, Sheri S. A PLAGUE OF ANGELSDanlo's natural nobility and his adamantine resolve not to hate seemed only to frustrate Pedar.

perverted

(pəˈvɜːtɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. deviating greatly from what is regarded as normal or right; distorted2. of or practising sexual perversion3. incorrectly interpreted

Derived Forms

pervertedly (perˈvertedly) adverbpervertedness (perˈvertedness) noun

Powered by COBUILD
(pəʳvɜːʳtɪd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that someone is perverted, you mean that you consider their behaviour, especially their sexual behaviour, to be immoral or unacceptable. [disapproval] ⇒ You've been protecting sick and perverted men. ⇒ His actions, to his small perverted mind, were surely forgivable.2. graded adjective & adjectiveYou can use perverted to describe actions or ideas which you think are wrong, unnatural, or harmful. [disapproval] ⇒ ...a perverted form of knowledge. ⇒ ...the assertion of a perverted patriotism.

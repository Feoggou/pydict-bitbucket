Ave Maria

(ˌɑveɪ məˈriə ; ˌɑvi )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
1.  “Hail, Mary,” the first words of the Latin version of a prayer to the Virgin Mary used in the Roman Catholic Church2.  this prayer3.  a musical setting of this prayer

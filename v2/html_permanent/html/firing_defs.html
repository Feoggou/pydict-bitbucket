firing

(ˈfaɪərɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. the process of baking ceramics, etc, in a kiln or furnace ⇒ a second firing2. the act of stoking a fire or furnace3. a discharge of a firearm4. something used as fuel, such as coal or wood5. US a scorching of plants, as a result of disease, drought, or heat

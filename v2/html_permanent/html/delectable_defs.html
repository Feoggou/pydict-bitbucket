Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something, especially food or drink, as delectable, you mean that it is very pleasant. ⇒ ...delectable wine.2. graded adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone as delectable, you think that they are very attractive. [literary] ⇒ He didn't seem to notice the delectable Miss Campbell.

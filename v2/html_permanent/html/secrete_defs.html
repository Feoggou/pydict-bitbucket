secrete

Powered by COBUILD
(sɪkriːt )
Word forms: secretes , secreting , secreted
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. verbIf part of a plant, animal, or human secretes a liquid, it produces it. [V n] ⇒ The sweat glands secrete water.2. verbIf you secrete something somewhere, you hide it there so that nobody will find it. [literary] [V n prep/adv] ⇒ She secreted the gun in the kitchen cabinet.

assiduous

(əˈsɪdjʊəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. hard-working; persevering ⇒ an assiduous researcher2. undertaken with perseverance and care ⇒ assiduous editing
Derived forms
asˈsiduously adverbasˈsiduousness noun
Word origin
C16: from Latin assiduus sitting down to (something), from assidēre to sit beside, from sedēre to sit

salaam

(səˈlɑːm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a Muslim form of salutation consisting of a deep bow with the right palm on the forehead2. a salutation signifying peace, used chiefly by Muslimsverb3. to make a salaam or salute (someone) with a salaam

Word Origin

C17: from Arabic salām peace, from the phrase assalām 'alaikum peace be to you

Word forms: salaams , salaaming , salaamed
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. verbWhen someone salaams, they bow with their right hand on their forehead. This is used as a formal and respectful way of greeting someone in India and Muslim countries. [V] ⇒ He looked from one to the other of them, then salaamed and left.2. conventionSome Muslims greet people by saying 'Salaam'.

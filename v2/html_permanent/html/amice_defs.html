Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounChristianity a rectangular piece of white linen worn by priests around the neck and shoulders under the alb or, formerly, on the head
Word origin
C15: from Old French amis, plural of amit, or from Medieval Latin amicia, both from Latin amictus cloak, from amicīre to clothe, from am- ambi- + iacere to throw

ex libris

(ɛks ˈliːbrɪs )
Definitions
adjective1. from the collection or library of: frequently printed on bookplatesnoun ex-libris2. a bookplate bearing the owner's name, coat of arms, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from Latin, literally: from the books (of)

demarcation

or demarkation (ˌdiːmɑːˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of establishing limits or boundaries2. a limit or boundary3. a. a strict separation of the kinds of work performed by members of different trade unionsb. (as modifier) ⇒ demarcation dispute4. separation or distinction (often in the phrase line of demarcation)
Word origin
C18: Latinized version of Spanish demarcación, from demarcar to appoint the boundaries of, from marcar to mark, from Italian marcare, of Germanic origin; see mark1

curry1

(ˈkʌrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ries1. a spicy dish of oriental, esp Indian, origin that is made in many ways but usually consists of meat or fish prepared in a hot piquant sauce2. curry seasoning or sauce3. See give someone curryverb -ries, -rying or -ried4. (transitive) to prepare (food) with curry powder or sauce
Word origin
C16: from Tamil kari sauce, relish

curry2

(ˈkʌrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -ries, -rying or -ried (transitive)1. to beat vigorously, as in order to clean2. to dress and finish (leather) after it has been tanned to make it strong, flexible, and waterproof3. to groom (a horse)4. See curry favour
Word origin
C13: from Old French correer to make ready, from Vulgar Latin conrēdāre (unattested), from rēdāre (unattested) to provide, of Germanic origin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

overpowering

(ˌəʊvəˈpaʊərɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. so strong or intense as to be unbearable2. so powerful as to crush or conquer
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

overpoweringly (ˌoverˈpoweringly) adverb

overpowering for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(oʊvəʳpaʊərɪŋ )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveAn overpowering feeling is so strong that you cannot resist it. ⇒ ...hard, cold, overpowering anger. ⇒ The desire for revenge can be overpowering.2. graded adjective & adjectiveAn overpowering smell or sound is so strong that you cannot smell or hear anything else. ⇒ There was an overpowering smell of alcohol. ⇒ The noise was overpowering.3. graded adjective & adjectiveAn overpowering person makes other people feel uncomfortable because they have such a strong personality. ⇒ Mrs Winter was large and somewhat overpowering. ⇒ ...an overpowering manner.

recitation

(ˌrɛsɪˈteɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of reciting from memory, or a formal reading of verse before an audience2. something recited

recitation

Powered by COBUILD
(resɪteɪʃən ) for learners of English
Word forms: recitations
Definitions
1. variable nounWhen someone does a recitation, they say aloud a piece of poetry or other writing that they have learned. ⇒ The transmission began with a recitation from the Koran.2. countable noun [oft N of n]A recitation of something is a statement of it. [written] ⇒ The letter was short–a simple recitation of their problem.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

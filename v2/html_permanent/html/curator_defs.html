curator

(kjʊəˈreɪtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the administrative head of a museum, art gallery, or similar institution2. law, mainly Scottish a guardian of a minor, mentally ill person, etc
Derived forms
curatorial (ˌkjʊərəˈtɔːrɪəl ) adjectivecuratorˌship (cuˈratorˌship) noun
Word origin
C14: from Latin: one who cares, from cūrāre to care for, from cūra care

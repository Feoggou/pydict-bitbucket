Word forms: overlords
Definitions
1. countable nounIf you refer to someone as an overlord, you mean that they have great power and are likely to use it in a bad way. [written] [+ of] ⇒ We really don't want to be the overlords of the Palestinian population. ⇒ The running of Welsh rugby was left in chaos yesterday after a vote of no confidence in the game's overlords.2. countable nounIn former times, an overlord was someone who had power over many people. ⇒ Henry II was the first king to be recognized as overlord of Ireland.

desolated

(ˈdɛsəˌleɪtɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. wretched or forlorn ⇒ I saw them walk away and felt absolutely desolated.2. deserted and laid waste ⇒ the misery of the desolated countryside

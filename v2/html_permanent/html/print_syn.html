Synonyms of print

print

verb

  1. run off, publish, copy, reproduce, issue, engrave, go to press, put to bed (informal)
    • It costs far less to press a CD than to print a book.
  2. publish, release, circulate, issue, disseminate
    • a questionnaire printed in the magazine
  3. mark, impress, stamp, imprint
    • printed with a paisley pattern
  4. record, impress, stamp, imprint, etch, engrave
    • indelibly printed on his memory

noun

  1. photograph, photo, snap
    • a black and white print of the children
  2. picture, plate, etching, engraving, lithograph, woodcut, linocut
    • Hogarth's famous series of prints
  3. copy, photo (informal), picture, reproduction, replica
    • There was a huge print of `Le Déjeuner Sur l'Herbe' on the wall.
  4. type, lettering, letters, characters, face, font, fount, typeface
    • columns of tiny print
  5. mark, impression, fingerprint, footprint
    • the unmistakable print of a bare foot in the mud
  6. publication, book, newspaper, magazine, periodical, newsprint, typescript, zine (informal), printed matter
  • See in print
  • See out of print

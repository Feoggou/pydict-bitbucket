fink

(fɪŋk ) slang, mainly US and Canadian
Definitions
noun1. a strikebreaker; blackleg2. an informer, such as one working for the police; spy3. an unpleasant, disappointing, or contemptible personverb4. (intransitive; often foll by on) to inform (on someone), as to the police
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: of uncertain origin

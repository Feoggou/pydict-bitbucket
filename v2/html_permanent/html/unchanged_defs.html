unchanged for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌntʃeɪndʒd )
Definitions
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If something is unchanged, it has stayed the same for a particular period of time. ⇒ For many years prices have remained virtually unchanged. ⇒ In July Belgium's jobless rate was unchanged at 8.2% of the workforce.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

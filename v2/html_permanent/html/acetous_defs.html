acetous

(ˈæsɪtəs , əˈsiː-) or acetose (ˈæsɪˌtəʊs , -ˌtəʊz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. containing, producing, or resembling acetic acid or vinegar2. tasting like vinegar
Word origin
C18: from Late Latin acētōsus vinegary, from acētum vinegar

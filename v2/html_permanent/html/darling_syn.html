darling

noun

  1. beloved, love, dear, dearest, angel, treasure, precious, loved one, sweetheart, sweetie, truelove, dear one • Hello, darling!
  2. favourite, pet, spoilt child, apple of your eye, blue-eyed boy, fair-haired boy (US) • He was the darling of the family.

adjective

  1. beloved, dear, dearest, sweet, treasured, precious, adored, cherished, revered • my darling baby boy
  2. adorable, sweet, attractive, lovely, charming, cute, enchanting, captivating, cutesy (informal, mainly US) • a perfectly darling little house

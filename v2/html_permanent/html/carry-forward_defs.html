carry forward

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (tr, adverb)1. accounting to transfer (a balance) to the next page, column, etc2. Also called (in Britain and certain other countries): carry over tax accounting to apply (a legally permitted credit, esp an operating loss) to the taxable income of following years to ease the overall tax burdennoun carry-forward3. Also called: carry-over tax accounting an amount carried forward

eighteenth

(ˈeɪˈtiːnθ )
Definitions
adjective1. (usually prenominal)a. coming after the seventeenth in numbering or counting order, position, time, etc; being the ordinal number of eighteen: often written 18thb. (as noun) ⇒ come on the eighteenthnoun2. a. one of 18 approximately equal parts of somethingb. (as modifier) ⇒ an eighteenth part3. the fraction that is equal to one divided by 18 (1⁄18)

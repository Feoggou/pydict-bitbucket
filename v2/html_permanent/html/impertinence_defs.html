impertinence

(ɪmˈpɜːtɪnəns ) or impertinency
Definitions
noun1. disrespectful behaviour or language; rudeness; insolence2. an impertinent act, gesture, etc3. rare lack of pertinence; irrelevance; inappropriateness
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

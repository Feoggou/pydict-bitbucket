horseback

Powered by COBUILD
(hɔːʳsbæk ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount noun [usu on/by N]If you do something on horseback, you do it while riding a horse. ⇒ In remote mountain areas, voters arrived on horseback.2. adjective [ADJ n]A horseback ride is a ride on a horse. ⇒ ...a horseback ride into the mountains.Horseback is also an adverb. ⇒ Many people in this area ride horseback.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

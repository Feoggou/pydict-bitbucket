Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something such as a story or a piece of music as affecting, you think it is good because it makes you feel a strong emotion, especially sadness or pity. [literary, approval] ⇒ ...an affecting drama about a woman with a terminal illness.

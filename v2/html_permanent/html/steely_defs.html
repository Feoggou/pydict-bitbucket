Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Steely is used to emphasize that a person is hard, strong, and determined. [emphasis] ⇒ Their indecision has been replaced by confidence and steely determination. ⇒ Captain Grenville stared at him steely-eyed.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]You use steely to describe something that has the grey colour of steel. ⇒ ...steely grey hair.

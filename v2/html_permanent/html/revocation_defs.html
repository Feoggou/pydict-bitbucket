revocation

(ˌrɛvəˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of revoking or state of being revoked; cancellation2. a. the cancellation or annulment of a legal instrument, esp a willb. the withdrawal of an offer, power of attorney, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

revocatory (ˈrɛvəkətərɪ , -trɪ) adjective

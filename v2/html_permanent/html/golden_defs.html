golden

(ˈɡəʊldən )
Definitions
adjective1. of the yellowish or brownish-yellow metallic colour of gold ⇒ golden hair2. made from or largely consisting of gold ⇒ a golden statue3. happy or prosperous ⇒ golden days4. (sometimes capital) (of anniversaries) the 50th in a series ⇒ Golden Jubilee, ⇒ golden wedding5. informal very successful or destined for success ⇒ the golden girl of tennis6. extremely valuable or advantageous ⇒ a golden opportunity

golden

Powered by COBUILD
(goʊldən ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveSomething that is golden is bright yellow in colour. ⇒ She combed and arranged her golden hair. ⇒ ...an endless golden beach.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Golden things are made of gold. ⇒ ...a golden chain with a golden locket.3. adjective [ADJ n]If you describe something as golden, you mean it is wonderful because it is likely to be successful and rewarding, or because it is the best of its kind. ⇒ He says there's a golden opportunity for peace which must be seized.4. →  golden boy/girl

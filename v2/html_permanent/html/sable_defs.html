sable

(ˈseɪbəl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -bles or -ble1. a marten, Martes zibellina, of N Asian forests, with dark brown luxuriant fur ▶ Related adjective: zibeline2. a. the highly valued fur of this animalb. (as modifier) ⇒ a sable coat3. See American sable4. the colour of sable fur: a dark brown to yellowish-brown colouradjective5. of the colour of sable fur6. black; dark; gloomy7. (usually postpositive) heraldry of the colour black

Word Origin

C15: from Old French, from Old High German zobel, of Slavic origin; related to Russian sobol', Polish sobol

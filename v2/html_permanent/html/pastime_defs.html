pastime

(ˈpɑːsˌtaɪm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan activity or entertainment which makes time pass pleasantly ⇒ golf is my favourite pastime

Word Origin

C15: from pass + time, on the model of French passe-temps

Powered by COBUILD
(pɑːstaɪm , pæs-)

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA pastime is something that you do in your spare time because you enjoy it or are interested in it. ⇒ His favourite pastime is golf.

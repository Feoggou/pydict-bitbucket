wheeler

(ˈwiːlə )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: wheelhorse a horse or other draught animal nearest the wheel2. (in combination) something equipped with a specified sort or number of wheels ⇒ a three-wheeler3. a person or thing that wheels

Wheeler

(ˈwiːlə )
Definitions
noun1.  John Archibald. 1911–2008, US physicist, noted for his work on nuclear fission and the development (1949–51) of the hydrogen bomb, also for his work on unified field theory2.  Sir (Robert Eric) Mortimer. 1890–1976, Scottish archaeologist, who did much to increase public interest in archaeology. He is noted esp for his excavations at Mohenjo-Daro and Harappa in the Indus Valley and at Maiden Castle in Dorset

sinew

(ˈsɪnjuː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. anatomy another name for tendon2. (often plural)a. a source of strength or powerb. a literary word for muscle

Derived Forms

sinewless (ˈsinewless) adjective

Word Origin

Old English sionu; related to Old Norse sin, Old Saxon sinewa, Old High German senawa sinew, Lettish pasainis string

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is exciting, it makes you feel very happy or enthusiastic. ⇒ The race itself is very exciting. ⇒ This voyage was the most exciting adventure of their lives. ⇒ Jackie was an exciting player to watch.excitingly graded adverb [usu ADV adj] ⇒ He is an excitingly original writer.

REM

Powered by COBUILD
(ɑːr iː em ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]REM sleep is a period of sleep that is very deep, during which your eyes and muscles make many small movements. It is the period during which most of your dreams occur. REM is an abbreviation for 'rapid eye movement'.

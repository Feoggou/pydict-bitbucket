such as

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
a. for example ⇒ animals, such as elephants and tigersb. of a similar kind as; like ⇒ people such as your friend John make me angryc. of the (usually small) amount, etc ⇒ the food, such as there was, was excellent▷ See such

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as evocative, you mean that it is good or interesting because it produces pleasant memories, ideas, emotions, and responses in people. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ Her story is sharply evocative of Italian provincial life. ⇒ ...the evocative power of cinema.evocatively graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj] ⇒ ...the collection of islands evocatively known as the South Seas.

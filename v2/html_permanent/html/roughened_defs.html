roughened

(ˈrʌfənd)
Definitions
adjectivemade rough ⇒ the bristle of his roughened face, ⇒ Common warts have a roughened surface., ⇒ roughened surfaces such as Anaglypta and woodchip papers, ⇒ a pair of roughened feet, ⇒ She lifted her big, roughened hands., ⇒ the caressing-melting sound of Abigail in Adam Berendt's roughened voice

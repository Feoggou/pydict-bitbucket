(mɒd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounBritisha. a member of a group of teenagers in the mid-1960s, noted for their clothes-consciousness and opposition to the rockersb. a member of a revived group of this type in the late 1970s and early 1980s, noted for their clothes-consciousness and opposition to the skinheadsc. (as modifier) ⇒ a mod haircut

Word Origin

C20: from modernist

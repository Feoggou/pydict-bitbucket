whinny

(ˈwɪnɪ )
Definitions
verb -nies, -nying or -nied (intransitive)1. (of a horse) to neigh softly or gently2. to make a sound resembling a neigh, such as a laughnoun plural -nies3. a gentle or low-pitched neigh
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: of imitative origin

Definitions
verbWhen a horse whinnies, it makes a series of high-pitched sounds, usually not very loudly. [V] ⇒ The girl's horse whinnied.Whinny is also a noun. ⇒ With a terrified whinny the horse shied.

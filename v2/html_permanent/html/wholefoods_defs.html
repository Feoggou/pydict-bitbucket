wholefoods

(ˈhəʊlˌfuːdz)
Definitions
plural nounespecially British food that has been refined or processed as little as possible and is eaten in its natural state, such as brown rice, wholemeal flour, etc; unprocessed foods ⇒ Try and eat only wholefoods.

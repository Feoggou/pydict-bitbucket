Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA concoction is something that has been made out of several things mixed together. [+ of] ⇒ ...a concoction of honey, yogurt, oats, and apples.

Diane made an infusion of raw tree peony bark, with cinnamon and walnuts: the most soothing concoction she knew.

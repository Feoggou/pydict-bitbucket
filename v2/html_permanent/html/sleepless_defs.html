sleepless

(ˈsliːplɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. without sleep or rest ⇒ a sleepless journey2. unable to sleep3. always watchful or alert4. mainly poetic always active or moving ⇒ the sleepless tides

Derived Forms

sleeplessly (ˈsleeplessly) adverbsleeplessness (ˈsleeplessness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A sleepless night is one during which you do not sleep. ⇒ I have sleepless nights worrying about her.2. adjectiveSomeone who is sleepless is unable to sleep. ⇒ A sleepless baby can seem to bring little reward.sleeplessness uncount noun ⇒ Sleeplessness is sometimes the side effect of certain medications.

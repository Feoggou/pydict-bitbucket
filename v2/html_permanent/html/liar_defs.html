liar

Powered by COBUILD
(laɪəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: liars
Definitions
countable nounIf you say that someone is a liar, you mean that they tell lies. ⇒ He was a liar and a cheat. ⇒ 'She seems at times an accomplished liar,' he said.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

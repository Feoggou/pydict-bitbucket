tubercle

(ˈtjuːbəkəl ) or tuberculum (tjʊˈbɜːkjʊləm)
Definitions
noun1. any small rounded nodule or elevation, esp on the skin, on a bone, or on a plant2. any small rounded pathological lesion of the tissues, esp one characteristic of tuberculosis
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin tūberculum a little swelling, diminutive of tuber

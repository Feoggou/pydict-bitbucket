kern1

or kerne (kɜːn )
Definitions
noun1. the part of the character on a piece of printer's type that projects beyond the bodyverb2. (transitive) to furnish (a typeface) with a kern
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from French carne corner of type, projecting angle, ultimately from Latin cardō hinge

kern2

(kɜːn )
Definitions
noun1. (in medieval Ireland or Scotland) a lightly armed foot soldier2. a troop of such soldiers3. archaic a loutish peasant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Middle Irish cethern band of foot soldiers, from cath battle

kern3

(kɜːn )
Definitions
nounengineering the central area of a wall, column, etc, through which all compressive forces pass
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

from German Kern core, heart

Kern

Definitions
noun Jerome (David). 1885–1945, US composer of musical comedies, most notably Show Boat (1927)

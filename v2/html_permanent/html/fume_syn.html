Synonyms of fume

fume

verb

  1. rage, boil, seethe, see red (informal), storm, rave, rant, smoulder, crack up (informal), go ballistic (slang, mainly US), champ at the bit (informal), blow a fuse (slang, mainly US), fly off the handle (informal), get hot under the collar (informal), go off the deep end (informal), wig out (slang), go up the wall (slang), get steamed up about (slang)
    • I fumed when these women did not respond.

noun

  1. (often plural) smoke, gas, exhaust, pollution, haze, vapour, smog, miasma, exhalation, effluvium
    • car exhaust fumes
  2. stench, stink, whiff (British, slang), reek, pong (British, informal), foul smell, niff (British, slang), malodour, mephitis, fetor, noisomeness
    • stale alcohol fumes

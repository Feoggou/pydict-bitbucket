sideways

(ˈsaɪdˌweɪz ) or sidewise (ˈsaɪdˌwaɪz)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adverb1. moving, facing, or inclining towards one side2. from one side; obliquely3. with one side forwardadjective (prenominal)4. moving or directed to or from one side5. towards or from one side

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adverb [ADV after v]Sideways means from or towards the side of something or someone. ⇒ Piercey glanced sideways at her. ⇒ The ladder blew sideways. ⇒ He was facing sideways.Sideways is also an adjective. ⇒ Alfred shot him a sideways glance.2. adverb [ADV after v]If you are moved sideways at work, you move to another job at the same level as your old job. ⇒ He would be moved sideways, rather than demoted.Sideways is also an adjective. ⇒ ...her recent sideways move.3. →  to be knocked sideways

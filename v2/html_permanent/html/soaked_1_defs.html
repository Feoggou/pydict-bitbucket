Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If someone or something gets soaked or soaked through, water or some other liquid makes them extremely wet. ⇒ I have to check my tent–it got soaked last night in the storm. ⇒ My goodness, you're soaked through. Where's your car? ⇒ We got soaked to the skin. ⇒ His torn clothes were soaked in blood.

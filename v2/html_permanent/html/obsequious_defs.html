obsequious

(əbˈsiːkwɪəs )
Definitions
adjective1. obedient or attentive in an ingratiating or servile manner2. rare submissive or compliant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

obsequiously (obˈsequiously) adverbobsequiousness (obˈsequiousness) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin obsequiōsus compliant, from obsequium compliance, from obsequi to follow, from ob- to + sequi to follow

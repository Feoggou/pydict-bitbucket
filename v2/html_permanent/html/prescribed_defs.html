prescribed

(prɪˈskraɪbd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. recommended by a doctor ⇒ if you need to take prescribed medicines whilst you are abroad, ⇒ She took twice the prescribed dose of sleeping tablets.2. laid down or recommended formally ⇒ You can cancel it during the prescribed period, without charge.
Example sentences containing 'prescribed'
We used counselling mainly, but I prescribed methadone for a few cases. Moore, Margaret FORESTS OF THE NIGHTI meant medically prescribed pills which might have reacted sharply with something you ate or drank last night. Howatch, Susan ABSOLUTE TRUTHSIn the morning she would want to know exactly what had transpired, whether the cure she had prescribed had proved effective. Martin, Joy THE IMAGE OF LAURA

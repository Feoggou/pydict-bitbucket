purveyor

(pəˈveɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (often plural) a person, organization, etc, that supplies food and provisions2. a person who spreads, repeats, or sells (information, lies, etc)3. a person or thing that habitually provides or supplies a particular thing or quality ⇒ a purveyor of humour4. history an officer providing or exacting provisions, lodging, etc, for a sovereign

Butterworth

(ˈbʌtəwəθ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  George. 1885–1916, British composer, noted for his interest in folk song and his settings of Housman's poems2.  Nick. born 1946, English writer and illustrator of children's books, many of which feature Percy, the animal-loving park keeper

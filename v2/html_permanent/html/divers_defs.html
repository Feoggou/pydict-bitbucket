divers

(ˈdaɪvəz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
determinerarchaic or literarya. various; sundry; someb. (as pronoun; functioning as plural) ⇒ divers of them
Word origin
C13: from Old French, from Latin dīversus turned in different directions; see divert

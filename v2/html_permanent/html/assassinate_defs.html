assassinate

(əˈsæsɪˌneɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to murder (a person, esp a public or political figure), usually by a surprise attack2. to ruin or harm (a person's reputation, etc) by slander
Derived forms

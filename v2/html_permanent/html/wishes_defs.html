wishes

(ˈwɪʃɪz)
Definitions
plural noun1. expressed hopes or desire, esp for someone's welfare, health, etc ⇒ Western leaders sent good wishes to the new American president., ⇒ 'With best wishes, Kathy', ⇒ Give her my best wishes., ⇒ Please give George my best wishes., ⇒ My parents send their best wishes.2. what one desires or wants to happen ⇒ You will command them according to my wishes., ⇒ Holding me against my wishes in my own home is a crime in itself., ⇒ The decision was made against the wishes of the party leader.

Sackville

(ˈsækvɪl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Thomas, 1st Earl of Dorset. 1536–1608, English poet, dramatist, and statesman. He collaborated with Thomas Norton on the early blank-verse tragedy Gorboduc (1561)

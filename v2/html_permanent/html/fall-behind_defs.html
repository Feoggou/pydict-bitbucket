fall behind

Definitions
verb (intr, adverb)1. to drop back; fail to keep up2. to be in arrears, as with a payment

fall behind

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. phrasal verbIf you fall behind, you do not make progress or move forward as fast as other people. [V P] ⇒ Evans had rheumatic fever, missed school and fell behind. [V P n] ⇒ Boris is falling behind all the top players.2. phrasal verbIf you fall behind with something or let it fall behind, you do not do it or produce it when you should, according to an agreement or schedule. [V P + with] ⇒ He faces losing his home after falling behind with the payments. [V P] ⇒ Thousands of people could die because the relief effort has fallen so far behind. [V P n] ⇒ Construction work fell behind schedule. →  fall

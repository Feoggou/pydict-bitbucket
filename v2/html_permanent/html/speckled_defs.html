Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]A speckled surface is covered with small marks, spots, or shapes. ⇒ ...a large brown speckled egg. ⇒ The sky was speckled with stars.
Example sentences containing 'speckled'
His view encompassed the harbor and the faint lights of villas that speckled the hillside beyond. Terman, Douglas CORMORANTYou floated incredibly, weightlessly, in solid, speckled black. Asimov, Isaac THE COMPLETE STORIES VOLUME 2Even in the speckled sky there were forms hidden, curled up like the sleeping children of the stars. Clive Barker SACRAMENT

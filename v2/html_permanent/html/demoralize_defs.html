demoralize

or demoralise (dɪˈmɒrəˌlaɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to undermine the morale of; dishearten ⇒ he was demoralized by his defeat2. to debase morally; corrupt3. to throw into confusion
Derived forms
deˌmoralization (deˌmoraliˈzation) or deˌmoralisation (deˌmoraliˈsation) noundemoralˌizer (deˈmoralˌizer) or demoralˌiser (deˈmoralˌiser) noun

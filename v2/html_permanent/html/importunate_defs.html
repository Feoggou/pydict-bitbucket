importunate

(ɪmˈpɔːtjʊnɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. persistent or demanding; insistent2. rare troublesome; annoying
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

importunately (imˈportunately) adverbimportunateness (imˈportunateness) noun

importunate

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmpɔːʳtʃʊnət ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveIf you describe someone as importunate, you think they are annoying because they keep trying to get something from you. [formal, disapproval] ⇒ His secretary shielded him from importunate visitors.

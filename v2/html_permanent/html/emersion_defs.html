emersion

(ɪˈmɜːʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act or an instance of emerging2. Also called: egress astronomy the reappearance of a celestial body after an eclipse or occultation
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin ēmersus, from ēmergere; see emerge

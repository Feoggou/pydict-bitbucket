rigging

(ˈrɪɡɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. the shrouds, stays, halyards, etc, of a vessel2. the bracing wires, struts, and lines of a biplane, balloon, etc3. any form of lifting gear, tackle, etc

Definitions
1. uncount nounVote or ballot rigging is the act of dishonestly organizing an election to get a particular result. ⇒ She was accused of corruption, of vote rigging on a massive scale.2. uncount nounOn a ship, the rigging is the ropes which support the ship's masts and sails.

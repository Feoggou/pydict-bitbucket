High Court

Definitions
noun1. a. (in England and Wales) a shortened form of High Court of Justiceb. (in Scotland) a shortened form of High Court of Justiciary2. (in Australia) the highest court of appeal, deciding esp constitutional issues3. (in New Zealand) a court of law inferior to the Court of Appeal. Formerly called: Supreme Court

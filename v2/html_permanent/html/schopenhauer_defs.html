Schopenhauer

(German ˈʃoːpənhauər)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun Arthur (ˈartʊr). 1788–1860, German pessimist philosopher. In his chief work, The World as Will and Idea (1819), he expounded the view that will is the creative primary factor and idea the secondary receptive factor

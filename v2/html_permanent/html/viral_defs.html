viral

(ˈvaɪrəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or caused by a virus2. (of a video, image, story, etc) spread quickly and widely among internet users via social networking sites, e-mail, etc3. See go viralnoun4. a video, image, story, etc that is spread quickly and widely via the internet
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

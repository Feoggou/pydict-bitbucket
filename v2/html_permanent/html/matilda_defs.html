Matilda1

(məˈtɪldə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Australian informal1. a bushman's swag2. See waltz Matilda

Word Origin

C20: from the Christian name

Matilda2

(məˈtɪldə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun known as the Empress Maud. 1102–67, only daughter of Henry I of England and wife of Geoffrey of Anjou. After her father's death (1135) she unsuccessfully waged a civil war with Stephen for the English throne; her son succeeded as Henry II

Charles IV

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  known as Charles the Fair. 1294–1328, king of France (1322–28): brother of Isabella of France, with whom he intrigued against her husband, Edward II of England2.  1316–78, king of Bohemia (1346–78) and Holy Roman Emperor (1355–78)3.  1748–1819, king of Spain (1788–1808), whose reign saw the domination of Spain by Napoleonic France: abdicated4. title as king of Hungary of Charles I. See Charles I (sense 5)

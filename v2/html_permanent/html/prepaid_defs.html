prepaid

(ˌpriːˈpeɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. paid for in advance ⇒ prepaid funerals2. denoting an envelope on which the postage has already been paid ⇒ Return the enclosed Donation Form today in the prepaid envelope provided.▶ USAGE The abbreviation for prepaid is pp or PP or ppd

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Prepaid items are paid for in advance, before the time when you would normally pay for them. ⇒ Return the enclosed Donation Form today in the prepaid envelope provided. ⇒ ...prepaid funerals.

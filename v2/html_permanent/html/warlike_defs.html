warlike

(ˈwɔːˌlaɪk )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or used in war2. hostile or belligerent3. fit or ready for war
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

warlike for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(wɔːʳlaɪk )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Warlike people seem aggressive and eager to start a war. ⇒ The Scythians were a fiercely warlike people. ⇒ ...hopes of a peaceful solution despite increased warlike rhetoric from both sides.2. adjective [ADJ n]A warlike object or activity relates to fighting a war. ⇒ They were armed with spears and other warlike implements. ⇒ ...the warlike preparations of Mr Churchill.

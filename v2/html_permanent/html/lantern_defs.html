lantern

(ˈlæntən )
Definitions
noun1. a light with a transparent or translucent protective case2. a structure on top of a dome or roof having openings or windows to admit light or air3. the upper part of a lighthouse that houses the light4. photography short for magic lantern
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Latin lanterna, from Greek lamptēr lamp, from lampein to shine

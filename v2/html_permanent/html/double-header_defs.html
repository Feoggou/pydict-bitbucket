double-header

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a train drawn by two locomotives coupled together to provide extra power2. Also called: twin bill sport, US and Canadian two games played consecutively by the same teams or by two different teams3. Australian and New Zealand informal a coin with the impression of a head on each side4. Australian informal a double ice-cream cone

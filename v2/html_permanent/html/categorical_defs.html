categorical

(ˌkætɪˈɡɒrɪkəl ) or categoric
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. unqualified; positive; unconditional ⇒ a categorical statement2. relating to or included in a category3. logic another word for categorial
Derived forms

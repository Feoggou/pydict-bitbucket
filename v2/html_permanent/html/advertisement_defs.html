Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounAn advertisement is an announcement in a newspaper, on television, or on a poster about something such as a product, event, or job. [written] ⇒ Miss Parrish recently placed an advertisement in the local newspaper. [Also + for]2. countable nounIf you say that an example of something is an advertisement for that thing in general, you mean that it shows how good that thing is. [mainly British] [+ for] ⇒ The England team were a poor advertisement for European football tonight. ⇒ That would not be a good advertisement for Hungary's emerging democracy.
Example sentences containing 'advertisement'
They stopped talking in order to watch a television advertisement.

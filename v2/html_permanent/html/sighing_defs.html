sighing

(ˈsaɪɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of drawing in and exhaling audibly a deep breath as an expression of weariness, despair, relief, etc2. the gentle noise made by the wind
Example sentences containing 'sighing'
Maybe we shall soon be sighing for good hard rock under foot again. J.R.R. Tolkien THE LORD OF THE RINGSFrodo thought he could hear the voice of Gandalf above, muttering words that ran down the sloping roof with a sighing echo. J.R.R.

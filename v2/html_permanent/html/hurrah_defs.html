Word Frequency

hurrah

(hʊˈrɑː ), hooray (huːˈreɪ ), hurray (hʊˈreɪ ) or hooroo (huːˈruː) Australian and New Zealand
Definitions
exclamation, noun1. a cheer of joy, victory, etcverb2. to shout "hurrah"
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: probably from German hurra; compare huzzah
Example sentences containing 'hurrah'
It spelled W I N, and Jenny jumped up and shouted hurrah , as she had bought a lottery ticket. Anita Anderson SUMMER OF SECRETS

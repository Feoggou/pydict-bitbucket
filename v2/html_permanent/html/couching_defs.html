couching

(ˈkaʊtʃɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna. a method of embroidery in which the thread is caught down at intervals by another thread passed through the material from beneathb. a pattern or work done by this method
Example sentences containing 'couching'
He pointed to a low building which Mather had not noticed, couching low against the bank of the loch to his left. Lyall, Francis A DEATH IN TIME

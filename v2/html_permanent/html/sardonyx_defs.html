sardonyx

(ˈsɑːdənɪks )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna variety of chalcedony with alternating reddish-brown and white parallel bands, used as a gemstone. Formula: SiO2

Word Origin

C14: via Latin from Greek sardonux, perhaps from sardion sardine2 + onux nail
Example sentences containing 'sardonyx'

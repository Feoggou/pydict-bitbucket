perjure

(ˈpɜːdʒə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(transitive) criminal law to render (oneself) guilty of perjury

Derived Forms

perjurer (ˈperjurer) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Old French parjurer, from Latin perjūrāre, from per- + jūrāre to make an oath, from jūs law

jacks

(dʒæks )
Definitions
noun(functioning as singular) a game in which bone, metal, or plastic pieces (jackstones) are thrown and then picked up in various groups between bounces or throws of a small ball. Sometimes called: knucklebones
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: shortened from jackstones, variant of checkstones pebbles

psyche

(ˈsaɪkɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe human mind or soul

Word Origin

C17: from Latin, from Greek psukhē breath, soul; related to Greek psukhein to breathe

nounGreek mythology a beautiful girl loved by Eros (Cupid), who became the personification of the soul

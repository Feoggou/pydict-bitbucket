teeming1

(ˈtiːmɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. abounding in people, fish, insects, etc ⇒ The city's normally teeming streets were virtually deserted., ⇒ the streets of Cairo, a teeming city of 13 million people, ⇒ Boys pulled salmon from a teeming river.2. dense or bustling ⇒ They pushed their way through the teeming crowd.

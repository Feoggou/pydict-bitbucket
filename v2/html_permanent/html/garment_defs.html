garment

(ˈɡɑːmənt )
Definitions
noun1. (often plural) an article of clothing2. outer coveringverb3. (tr; usually passive) to cover or clothe
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

garmentless (ˈgarmentless) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Old French garniment, from garnir to equip; see garnish

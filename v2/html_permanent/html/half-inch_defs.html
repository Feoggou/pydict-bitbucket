Word Frequency

half-inch

Definitions
noun1. a measure of length approximately equivalent to 13 millimetresverb2. slang, old-fashioned to steal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

sense 2: from rhyming slang for pinch to steal

vacancy

(ˈveɪkənsɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -cies1. the state or condition of being vacant or unoccupied; emptiness2. an unoccupied post or office ⇒ we have a vacancy in the accounts department3. an unoccupied room in a boarding house, hotel, etc ⇒ put the "No Vacancies" sign in the window4. lack of thought or intelligent awareness; inanity ⇒ an expression of vacancy on one's face5. physics a defect in a crystalline solid caused by the absence of an atom, ion, or molecule from its position in the crystal lattice6. obsolete idleness or a period spent in idleness

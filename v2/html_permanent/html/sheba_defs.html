Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: Saba the ancient kingdom of the Sabeans: a rich trading nation dealing in gold, spices, and precious stones (I Kings 10)2. the region inhabited by this nation, located in the SW corner of the Arabian peninsula: modern Yemen

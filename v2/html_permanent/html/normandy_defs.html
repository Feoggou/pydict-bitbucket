Normandy

(ˈnɔːməndɪ )
Definitions
nouna former province of N France, on the English Channel: settled by Vikings under Rollo in the 10th century; scene of the Allied landings in 1944. Chief town: Rouen. French name: Normandie (nɔrmɑ̃di )

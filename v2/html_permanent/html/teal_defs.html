teal

(tiːl )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural teals or teal1. any of various small ducks, such as the Eurasian Anas crecca (common teal) that are related to the mallard and frequent ponds, lakes, and marshes2. a greenish-blue colour
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: related to Middle Low German tēlink, Middle Dutch tēling

Synonyms of massacre

massacre

noun

  1. slaughter, killing, murder, holocaust, carnage, extermination, annihilation, butchery, mass slaughter, blood bath
    • She lost her mother in the massacre.

verb

  1. slaughter, kill, murder, butcher, take out (slang), wipe out, slay, blow away (slang, mainly US), annihilate, exterminate, mow down, cut to pieces
    • Troops indiscriminately massacred the defenceless population.

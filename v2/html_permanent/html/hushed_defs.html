hushed

Powered by COBUILD
(hʌʃt ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA hushed place is peaceful and much quieter and calmer than usual. ⇒ The house seemed muted, hushed as if it had been deserted. ⇒ He liked a hushed and dignified atmosphere.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A hushed voice or hushed conversation is very quiet. ⇒ At first we spoke in hushed voices and crept about in order not to alarm them. ⇒ We discussed the situation in hushed whispers.

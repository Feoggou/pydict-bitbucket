legate

(ˈlɛɡɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a messenger, envoy, or delegate2. Roman Catholic Church an emissary to a foreign state representing the Pope
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

legateship (ˈlegateˌship) nounlegatine (ˈlɛɡəˌtaɪn ) adjective

Word Origin

Old English, via Old French from Latin lēgātus deputy, from lēgāre to delegate; related to lēx law

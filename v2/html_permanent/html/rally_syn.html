Synonyms of rally 1

rally1

noun

  1. gathering, mass meeting, convention, convocation, meeting, conference, congress, assembly, congregation, muster, hui (New Zealand)
    • They held a rally to mark international human rights day.
  2. recovery, improvement, comeback (informal), revival, renewal, resurgence, recuperation, turn for the better
    • After a brief rally, shares returned to 126p.
    OPPOSITES:  collapse, deterioration, relapse, turn for the worse
  3. regrouping, stand, reunion, reorganization

verb

  1. gather together, unite, bring together, regroup, reorganize, reassemble, re-form
    • He rallied his own supporters for a fight.
    OPPOSITES:  separate, disperse, split up, disband
  2. recover, improve, pick up, revive, get better, come round, perk up, recuperate, turn the corner, pull through, take a turn for the better, regain your strength, get your second wind, be on the mend
    • He rallied enough to thank his doctor.
    OPPOSITES:  fail, deteriorate, worsen, get worse, relapse, take a turn for the worse
  • See rally around

rally2

verb

  1. tease, mock, ridicule, taunt, send up (British, informal), make fun of, poke fun at, twit, chaff, take the mickey out of (informal)

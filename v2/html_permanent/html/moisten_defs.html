Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbTo moisten something means to make it slightly wet. [V n] ⇒ She took a sip of water to moisten her dry throat. [V-ed] ⇒ ...a moistened flannel.
Example sentences containing 'moisten'
He tried to lick his lips to moisten them, but his tongue felt cracked and swollen.

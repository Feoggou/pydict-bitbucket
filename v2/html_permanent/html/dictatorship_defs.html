dictatorship

(dɪkˈteɪtəˌʃɪp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the rank, office, or period of rule of a dictator2. government by a dictator or dictators3. a country ruled by a dictator or dictators4. absolute or supreme power or authority

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounDictatorship is government by a dictator. ⇒ ...a new era of democracy after a long period of military dictatorship in the country. ⇒ ...countries which are ruled by dictatorships.2. countable nounA dictatorship is a country which is ruled by a dictator or by a very strict and harsh government. despotism ⇒ Every country in the region was a military dictatorship.

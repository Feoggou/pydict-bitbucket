collagen

(ˈkɒlədʒən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna fibrous scleroprotein of connective tissue and bones that is rich in glycine and proline and yields gelatine on boiling
Derived forms
collagenic (ˌkɒləˈdʒɛnɪk ) or collagenous (kəˈlædʒənəs ) adjective
Word origin
C19: from Greek kolla glue + -gen

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounCollagen is a protein that is found in the bodies of people and animals. It is often used as an ingredient in cosmetics or is injected into the face in cosmetic surgery, in order to make the skin look younger. ⇒ The collagen that is included in face creams comes from animal skin. ⇒ ...collagen injections.

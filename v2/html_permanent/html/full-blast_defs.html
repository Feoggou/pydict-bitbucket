Definitions
phraseIf something such as a radio or a heater is on full blast, or on at full blast, it is producing as much sound or power as it is able to. ⇒ In many of those homes the television is on full blast 24 hours a day. ⇒ You are unlikely to run the heater at full blast for long periods. →  blast

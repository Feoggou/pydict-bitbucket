(oʊldən )
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]If you refer to a period in the past as the olden days, you feel affection for it. [literary] ⇒ We had a delightful time talking about the olden days on his farm. ⇒ ...the nicely painted railways of olden times.→  in the olden days/in olden days

rascal

(ˈrɑːskəl )
Definitions
noun1. a disreputable person; villain2. a mischievous or impish rogue3. an affectionate or mildly reproving term for a child or old man ⇒ you little rascal, ⇒ the wicked old rascal kissed her4. obsolete a person of lowly birthadjective5. (prenominal) obsoletea. belonging to the mob or rabbleb. dishonest; knavish
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French rascaille rabble, perhaps from Old Norman French rasque mud, filth

drinkable

Powered by COBUILD
(drɪŋkəbəl )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveWater that is drinkable is clean and safe for drinking.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that a particular wine, beer, or other drink is drinkable, you mean that it tastes quite pleasant. ⇒ The food was good and the wine drinkable. ⇒ ...a very drinkable plonk.

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as idealistic, you mean that they have ideals, and base their behaviour on these ideals, even though this may be impractical. ⇒ Idealistic young people died for the cause. ⇒ Older mothers tend to be too idealistic about the pleasures of motherhood.

mailer

(ˈmeɪlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who addresses or mails letters, etc2. US and Canadian a machine used for stamping and addressing mail3. US and Canadian a container for mailing things

Mailer

(ˈmeɪlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Norman. 1923–2007, US author. His works, which are frequently critical of modern American society, include the war novel The Naked and the Dead (1948), An American Dream (1965), his account of the 1967 peace march on Washington The Armies of the Night (1968), The Executioner's Song (1979), and Barbary Shore (1998)

solve

(sɒlv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to find the explanation for or solution to (a mystery, problem, etc)2. mathematicsa. to work out the answer to (a problem)b. to obtain the roots of (an equation)

Derived Forms

solver (ˈsolver) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin solvere to loosen, release, free from debt

stingy1

(ˈstɪndʒɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -gier or -giest1. unwilling to spend or give2. insufficient or scanty

Derived Forms

stingily (ˈstingily) adverbstinginess (ˈstinginess) noun

Word Origin

C17 (perhaps in the sense: ill-tempered): perhaps from stinge, dialect variant of sting

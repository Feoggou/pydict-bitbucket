decade

(ˈdɛkeɪd , dɪˈkeɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a period of ten consecutive years2. a group or series of ten
Derived forms
decadal (deˈcadal) adjective
Word origin
C15: from Old French, from Late Latin decad-, decas, from Greek dekas, from deka ten

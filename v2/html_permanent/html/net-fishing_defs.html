net fishing

(nɛt ˈfɪʃɪŋ)
Definitions
nounfishing Fishing using a net, esp a large commercial drift net ⇒ the switch from net fishing to line fishing for tuna, ⇒ Environmentalists argue that drift net fishing should be banned because it destroys marine life indiscriminately.

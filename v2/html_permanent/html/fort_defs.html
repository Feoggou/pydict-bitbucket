fort

(fɔːt )
Definitions
noun1. a fortified enclosure, building, or position able to be defended against an enemy2. See hold the fort
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French, from fort (adj) strong, from Latin fortis

Definitions
1. countable nounA fort is a strong building or a place with a wall or fence around it where soldiers can stay and be safe from the enemy.2. →  to hold the fort

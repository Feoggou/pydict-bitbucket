transitory

(ˈtrænsɪtərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
adjectiveof short duration; transient or ephemeral
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

transitorily (ˈtransitorily) adverbtransitoriness (ˈtransitoriness) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Church Latin transitōrius passing, from Latin transitus a crossing over; see transient

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that something is transitory, you mean that it lasts only for a short time. ⇒ Most teenage romances are transitory. ⇒ ...a sad reminder of the transitory nature of political success.

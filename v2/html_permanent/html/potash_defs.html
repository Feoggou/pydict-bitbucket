potash

(ˈpɒtˌæʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. another name for potassium carbonate, esp the form obtained by leaching wood ash2. another name for potassium hydroxide3. potassium chemically combined in certain compounds ⇒ chloride of potash

Word Origin

C17 pot ashes, translation of obsolete Dutch potaschen; so called because originally obtained by evaporating the lye of wood ashes in pots

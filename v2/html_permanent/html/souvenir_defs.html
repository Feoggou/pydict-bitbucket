souvenir

(ˌsuːvəˈnɪə , ˈsuːvəˌnɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an object that recalls a certain place, occasion, or person; mementoverb (transitive)2. Australian and New Zealand euphemistic, slang to steal or keep (something, esp a small article) for one's own use; purloin

Word Origin

C18: from French, from (se) souvenir to remember, from Latin subvenīre to come to mind, from sub- up to + venīre to come

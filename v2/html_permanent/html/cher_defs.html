Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a department of central France, in E Centre region. Capital: Bourges. Pop: 312 277 (2003 est). Area: 7304 sq km (2849 sq miles)2. a river in central France, rising in the Massif Central and flowing northwest to the Loire. Length: 354 km (220 miles)

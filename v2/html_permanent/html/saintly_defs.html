Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveA saintly person behaves in a very good or very holy way. [approval] ⇒ ...his saintly mother. ⇒ She has been saintly in her self-restraint.saintliness (seɪntlɪnəs ) uncount noun ⇒ They were praised for their courage and saintliness.

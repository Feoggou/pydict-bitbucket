Pentateuch

(ˈpɛntəˌtjuːk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe first five books of the Old Testament regarded as a unity

Derived Forms

ˌPentateuchal (ˌPentaˈteuchal) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Church Latin pentateuchus, from Greek penta- + teukhos tool (in Late Greek: scroll)

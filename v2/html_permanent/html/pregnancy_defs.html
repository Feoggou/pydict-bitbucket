pregnancy

(ˈprɛɡnənsɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cies1. the state or condition of being pregnant2. the period from conception to childbirth

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounPregnancy is the condition of being pregnant or the period of time during which a female is pregnant. ⇒ It would be wiser to cut out all alcohol during pregnancy. ⇒ She was exhausted by eight pregnancies in 13 years.

Stanley1

(ˈstænlɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the capital of the Falkland Islands, in NE East Falkland Island: scene of fighting in the Falklands War of 1982. Pop: 1989 (2001)2. a town in NE England, in N Durham. Pop: 19 072 (2001)3. See Mount Stanley

Stanley2

(ˈstænlɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun Sir Henry Morton. 1841–1904, British explorer and journalist, who led an expedition to Africa in search of Livingstone, whom he found on Nov 10, 1871. He led three further expeditions in Africa (1874–77; 1879–84; 1887–89) and was instrumental in securing Belgian sovereignty over the Congo Free State

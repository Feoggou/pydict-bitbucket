indite

(ɪnˈdaɪt )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. archaic to write2. obsolete to dictate▶ USAGE Indite and inditement are sometimes wrongly used where indict and indictment are meant: he was indicted (not indited) for fraud
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inditement (inˈditement) nouninditer (inˈditer) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Old French enditer, from Latin indīcere to declare, from in-2 + dīcere to say

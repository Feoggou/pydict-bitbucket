ventilation

(ˌvɛntɪˈleɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act or process of ventilating or the state of being ventilated2. an installation in a building that provides a supply of fresh air
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ventilative (ˈventiˌlative) adjectiveventilatory (ˈventiˌlatory) adjective

(vɛntəleɪʃən) in Automotive Engineering
Definitions
noun(Automotive engineering: Vehicle components, Bodywork, controls, and accessories) Ventilation is a system that provides a draft of air into and out of a vehicle passenger compartment. ⇒ The car's ventilation system demists the windscreen more quickly when you turn on the air cooling. ⇒ The only ventilation comes from quarterlights and tiny sliding windows in the doors, all the rear windows being fixed. ⇒ Use the car ventilation system for fresh air circulation rather than opening the windows.

getter

(ˈɡɛtə )
Definitions
noun1. a person or thing that gets2. a substance, usually a metal such as titanium, evaporated onto the walls of a vacuum tube, vessel, etc, to adsorb the residual gas and lower the pressureverb3. (transitive) to remove (a gas) by the action of a getter

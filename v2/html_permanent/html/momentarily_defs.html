(ˈməʊməntərəlɪ , -trɪlɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb1. for an instant; temporarily2. from moment to moment; every instant3. US and Canadian very soon

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adverb [usu ADV with v]Momentarily means for a short time. [mainly British, written] ⇒ She paused momentarily when she saw them.2. adverb [usu ADV after v]Momentarily means very soon. [US] imminently ⇒ The Senate Judiciary Committee is expected to vote momentarily on his nomination to the Supreme Court.

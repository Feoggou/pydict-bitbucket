apocalypse

(əˈpɒkəlɪps )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a prophetic disclosure or revelation2. an event of great importance, violence, etc, like the events described in the Apocalypse
Word origin
C13: from Late Latin apocalypsis, from Greek apokalupsis, from apokaluptein to disclose, from apo- + kaluptein to hide

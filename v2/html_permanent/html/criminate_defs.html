criminate

(ˈkrɪmɪˌneɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive) rare1. to charge with a crime; accuse2. to condemn or censure (an action, event, etc)3. short for incriminate
Derived forms
ˌcrimination (ˌcrimiˈnation) nouncriminative (ˈcriminative) or criminatory (ˈkrɪmɪnətərɪ , -trɪ) adjectivecrimiˌnator (ˈcrimiˌnator) noun
Word origin

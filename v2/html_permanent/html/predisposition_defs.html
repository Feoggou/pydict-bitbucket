Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the condition of being predisposed2. medicine susceptibility to a specific disease. See diathesis

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [oft N to-inf]If you have a predisposition to behave in a particular way, you tend to behave like that because of the kind of person that you are or the attitudes that you have. [formal] ⇒ ...a woman's predisposition to use the right side of her brain. [+ to] ⇒ There is a thin dividing line between educating the public and creating a predisposition to panic.2. countable nounIf you have a predisposition to a disease or illness, it is likely that you will suffer from that disease or illness. [formal] [+ to/towards] ⇒ ...a genetic predisposition to lung cancer.

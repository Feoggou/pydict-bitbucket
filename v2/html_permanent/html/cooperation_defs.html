cooperation

or co-operation (kəʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. joint operation or action2. assistance or willingness to assist3. economics the combination of consumers, workers, farmers, etc, in activities usually embracing production, distribution, or trade4. ecology beneficial but inessential interaction between two species in a community
Derived forms
coˌoperationist (coˌoperˈationist) or co-ˌoperationist (co-ˌoperˈationist) noun
Example sentences containing 'cooperation'
You seek aid; we can give none without your full cooperation. Goldsborough, Robert MURDER IN E MINORHe made a very interesting offer for my wholehearted cooperation. Brierley, David SNOWLINEFull cooperation was given by an unwitting Mexican government misled by corrupt bureaucrats. Cussler, Clive INCA GOLD

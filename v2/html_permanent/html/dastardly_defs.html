Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]If you describe an action as dastardly, you mean it is wicked and intended to hurt someone. [old-fashioned] ⇒ He described the killing as a dastardly act. ⇒ ...a dastardly attack on the queen.2. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]If you describe a person as dastardly, you mean they are wicked. [old-fashioned] ⇒ ...the heiress who is badly treated by her dastardly uncle.

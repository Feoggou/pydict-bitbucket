lineament

(ˈlɪnɪəmənt )
Definitions
noun (often plural)1. a facial outline or feature2. a distinctive characteristic or feature3. geology any long natural feature on the surface of the earth, such as a fault, esp as revealed by aerial photography
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lineamental (ˌlɪnɪəˈmɛntəl ) adjective

Word Origin

C15: from Latin: line, from līneāre to draw a line

warfare

(ˈwɔːˌfɛə )
Definitions
noun1. the act, process, or an instance of waging war2. conflict, struggle, or strife
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
1. uncount nounWarfare is the activity of fighting a war. ⇒ ...the threat of chemical warfare.2. uncount nounWarfare is sometimes used to refer to any violent struggle or conflict. ⇒ Much of the violence is related to drugs and gang warfare. ⇒ At times party rivalries have broken out into open warfare.

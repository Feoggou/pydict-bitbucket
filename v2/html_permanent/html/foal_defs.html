foal

(fəʊl )
Definitions
noun1. the young of a horse or related animalverb2. to give birth to (a foal)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English fola; related to Old Frisian fola, Old High German folo foal, Latin pullus young creature, Greek pōlos foal

Definitions

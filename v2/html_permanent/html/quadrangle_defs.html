quadrangle

(ˈkwɒdˌræŋɡəl )
Definitions
noun1. geometry a plane figure consisting of four points connected by four lines. In a complete quadrangle, six lines connect all pairs of points2. a rectangular courtyard, esp one having buildings on all four sides. Often shortened to: quad3. the building surrounding such a courtyard
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

quadrangular (kwɒˈdræŋɡjʊlə ) adjective

Word Origin

C15: from Late Latin quadrangulum figure having four corners

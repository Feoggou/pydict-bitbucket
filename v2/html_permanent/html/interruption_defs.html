interruption

(ˌɪntəˈrʌpʃən )
Definitions
noun1. something that interrupts, such as a comment, question, or action2. an interval or intermission3. the act of interrupting or the state of being interrupted
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Synonyms of hold up

hold up

  1. last, survive, endure, bear up, wear
    • Children's wear is holding up well in the recession.
  2. hold water (informal), be valid, be logical, be credible, be consistent
    • I'm not sure if the argument holds up, but it's stimulating.

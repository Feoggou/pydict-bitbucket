burnish

(ˈbɜːnɪʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to make or become shiny or smooth by friction; polishnoun2. a shiny finish; lustre
Derived forms
burnishable (ˈburnishable) adjectiveburnisher (ˈburnisher) noun
Word origin
C14 burnischen, from Old French brunir to make brown, from brun brown

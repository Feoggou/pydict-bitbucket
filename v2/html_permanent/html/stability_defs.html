stability

(stəˈbɪlɪtɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the quality of being stable2. the ability of an aircraft to resume its original flight path after inadvertent displacement3. meteorologya. the condition of an air or water mass characterized by no upward movementb. the degree of susceptibility of an air mass to disturbance by convection currents4. ecology the ability of an ecosystem to resist change5. electrical engineering the ability of an electrical circuit to cope with changes in the operational conditions6. a vow taken by every Benedictine monk attaching him perpetually to the monastery where he is professed

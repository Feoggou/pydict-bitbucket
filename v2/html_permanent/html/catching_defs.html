(kætʃɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If an illness or a disease is catching, it is easily passed on or given to someone else. [informal] ⇒ There are those who think eczema is catching.2. graded adjective [v-link ADJ]If a feeling or emotion is catching, it has a strong influence on other people and spreads quickly, for example through a crowd. infectious ⇒ Enthusiasm is very catching.

epithet

(ˈɛpɪˌθɛt )
Definitions
nouna descriptive word or phrase added to or substituted for a person's name ⇒ "Lackland" is an epithet for King John
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

epithetic (ˌepiˈthetic) or epithetical (ˌepiˈthetical) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Latin epitheton, from Greek, from epitithenai to add, from tithenai to put

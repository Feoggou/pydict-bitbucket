social disease

(ˈsəʊʃəl dɪˈziːz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. euphemistic a sexually transmitted disease ⇒ Eighteenth-century England was a world of loose sexual morals with accompanying social disease.2. a disease that is prevalent among certain social groups due to a predisposition caused by adverse conditions ⇒ Dental decay is largely a social disease, namely, the culture of high sugar consumption.

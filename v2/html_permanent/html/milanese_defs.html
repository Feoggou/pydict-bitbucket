Milanese

(ˌmɪləˈniːz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Milan, its people, culture, etc2. of a fine lightweight knitted fabric of silk, rayon, etcnoun3. the Italian dialect spoken in Milan4. plural -ese a native or inhabitant of Milan

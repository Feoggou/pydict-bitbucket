puffy

(ˈpʌfɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: puffier or puffiest1. short of breath2. swollen or bloated ⇒ a puffy face3. pompous or conceited4. blowing in gusts

Derived Forms

puffily (ˈpuffily) adverbpuffiness (ˈpuffiness) noun

(pʌfi )
Word forms: puffier
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf a part of someone's body, especially their face, is puffy, it has a round, swollen appearance. ⇒ Her cheeks were puffy with crying. ⇒ ...dark-ringed puffy eyes.puffiness uncount noun ⇒ He noticed some slight puffiness beneath her eyes.2. graded adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a cloud as puffy, you mean that it looks like a large ball of white cotton wool. Puffy clouds appear during fine weather. [literary] fluffy ⇒ The sea was blue, and white puffy clouds sailed by.

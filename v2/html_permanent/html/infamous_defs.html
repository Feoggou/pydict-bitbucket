infamous

(ˈɪnfəməs )
Definitions
adjective1. having a bad reputation; notorious2. causing or deserving a bad reputation; shocking ⇒ infamous conduct3. criminal law (formerly)a. (of a person) deprived of certain rights of citizenship on conviction of certain offencesb. (of a crime or punishment) entailing such deprivation
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

infamously (ˈinfamously) adverbinfamousness (ˈinfamousness) noun

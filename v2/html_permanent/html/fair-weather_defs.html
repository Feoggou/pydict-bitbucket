fair-weather

Definitions
adjective1. suitable for use in fair weather only2. not reliable or present in situations of hardship or difficulty (esp in the phrase fair-weather friend)

Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]You use fair-weather to refer to someone who offers help to someone, or who takes part in a particular activity, only when it is easy or pleasant for them to do so. [disapproval] ⇒ ...a fair-weather friend.

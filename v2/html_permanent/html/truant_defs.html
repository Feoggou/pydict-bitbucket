truant

(ˈtruːənt )
Definitions
noun1. a person who is absent without leave, esp from schooladjective2. being or relating to a truantverb3. (intransitive) to play truant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

truancy (ˈtruancy) or truanting (ˈtruanting) noun

Word Origin

C13: from Old French: vagabond, probably of Celtic origin; compare Welsh truan miserable, Old Irish trōg wretched

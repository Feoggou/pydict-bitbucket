trinket

(ˈtrɪŋkɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small or worthless ornament or piece of jewellery2. a trivial object; trifle

Derived Forms

trinketry (ˈtrinketry) noun

Word Origin

C16: perhaps from earlier trenket little knife, via Old Northern French, from Latin truncāre to lop

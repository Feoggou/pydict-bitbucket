irregularity

(ɪˌrɛɡjʊˈlærɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. the state or quality of being irregular2. something irregular, such as a bump in a smooth surface3. a breach of a convention or normal procedure
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

`If I proved some irregularity here, I certainly would have to, sir. Gagman, Maurice DOUBTFUL MOTIVESSome irregularity in application of duty roster, and unable to make contact.

bridal

(ˈbraɪdəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to a bride or a wedding; nuptialnoun2. obsolete a wedding or wedding feast
Word origin
Old English brӯdealu, literally: "bride ale", that is, wedding feast

bridal

Powered by COBUILD
(braɪdəl )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]Bridal is used to describe something that belongs or relates to a bride, or to both a bride and her bridegroom. ⇒ She wore a floor length bridal gown. ⇒ ...the bridal party.

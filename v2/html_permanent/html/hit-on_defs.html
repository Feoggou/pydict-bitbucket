hit on

Definitions
verb (tr, preposition)1. to strike2. Also: hit upon to discover unexpectedly or guess correctly3. US and Canadian slang to make sexual advances to (a person)

Definitions
1. phrasal verbIf you hit on an idea or a solution to a problem, or hit upon it, you think of it. [V P n] ⇒ After running through the numbers in every possible combination, we finally hit on a solution.2. phrasal verbIf someone hits on you, they speak or behave in a way that shows they want to have a sexual relationship with you. [informal] [V P n] ⇒ She was hitting on me and I was surprised and flattered. →  hit

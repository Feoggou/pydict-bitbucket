unchaste

(ʌnˈtʃeɪst)
Definitions
adjective1. not virginal; having experienced sexual intercourse ⇒ an unchaste woman2. impure or indecent ⇒ an unchaste book, ⇒ She was of unchaste character.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

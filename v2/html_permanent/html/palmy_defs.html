palmy

(ˈpɑːmɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: palmier or palmiest1. prosperous, flourishing, or luxurious ⇒ a palmy life2. covered with, relating to, or resembling palms ⇒ a palmy beach
Example sentences containing 'palmy'

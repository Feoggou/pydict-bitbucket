nave1

(neɪv )
Definitions
nounthe central space in a church, extending from the narthex to the chancel and often flanked by aisles
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: via Medieval Latin from Latin nāvis ship, from the similarity of shape

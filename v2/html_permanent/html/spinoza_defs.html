Spinoza

(spɪˈnəʊzə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun Baruch (bəˈruːk). 1632–77, Dutch philosopher who constructed a holistic metaphysical system derived from a series of hypotheses that he judged self-evident. His chief work is Ethics (1677)

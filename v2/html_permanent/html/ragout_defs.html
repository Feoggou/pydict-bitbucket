ragout

(ræˈɡuː )
Definitions
noun1. a richly seasoned stew of meat or poultry and vegetablesverb -gouts (-ˈɡuːz), -gouting (-ˈɡuːɪŋ) or -gouted (-ˈɡuːd)2. (transitive) to make into a ragout
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from French, from ragoûter to stimulate the appetite again, from ra- re- + goûter from Latin gustāre to taste

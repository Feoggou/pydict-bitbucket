slob

(slɒb )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. informal a slovenly, unattractive, and lazy person2. Irish mireverb3. (intr; often foll by around or about) to behave like a slob

Derived Forms

slobbish (ˈslobbish) adjective

Word Origin

C19: from Irish slab mud; compare slab

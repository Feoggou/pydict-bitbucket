restrictive

(rɪˈstrɪktɪv )
Definitions
adjective1. restricting or tending to restrict2. grammar denoting a relative clause or phrase that restricts the number of possible referents of its antecedent. The relative clause in Americans who live in New York is restrictive; the relative clause in Americans, who are generally extrovert, is nonrestrictive

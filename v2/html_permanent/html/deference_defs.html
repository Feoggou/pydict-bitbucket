deference

(ˈdɛfərəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. submission to or compliance with the will, wishes, etc, of another2. courteous regard; respect
Word origin
C17: from French déférence; see defer2

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounDeference is a polite and respectful attitude towards someone, especially because they have an important position. ⇒ The old sense of deference and restraint in royal reporting has vanished. ⇒ Out of deference to him, I lowered my head as he prayed. [Also + to]

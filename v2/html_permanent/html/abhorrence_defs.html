abhorrence

(əbˈhɒrəns ) or abhorrency (əbˈhɒrənsɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a feeling of extreme loathing or aversion2. a person or thing that is loathsome

abhorrence

Powered by COBUILD
(æbhɒrəns , US -hɔːr- )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft poss N]Someone's abhorrence of something is their strong hatred of it. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ They are anxious to show their abhorrence of racism.

Suddenly I perceived him as Dutiful did, and realized that the Prince's abhorrence went past the man's physical deformity and mental limits.

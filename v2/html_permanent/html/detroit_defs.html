Detroit

(dɪˈtrɔɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a city in SE Michigan, on the Detroit River: a major Great Lakes port; once the largest car-manufacturing centre in the world. Pop: 911 402 (2003 est)2. a river in central North America, flowing along the US-Canadian border from Lake St Clair to Lake Erie

lagging

(ˈlæɡɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. insulating material wrapped around pipes, boilers, etc, or laid in a roof loft, to prevent loss of heat2. the act or process of applying lagging3. a wooden frame used to support an arch during construction

lagging

Powered by COBUILD
(lægɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounLagging is special material which is used to cover pipes, water tanks, or the inside of a roof so that heat does not escape from them or so they do not freeze. [mainly British]

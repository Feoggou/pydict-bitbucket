financing

(ˈfaɪnænsɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. a. the provision of funds for an operation, project, etc ⇒ the financing of long-term care for the elderly, ⇒ The demand for flexible financing is higher than it was last year., ⇒ The financing of this deal needs thorough investigation.b. (as modifier) ⇒ the company's financing decisions, ⇒ a financing mechanism for the project2. money received or provided to finance something ⇒ Long-term financing more than covers the capital requirement., ⇒ The total financing amounts to just over $161 million.

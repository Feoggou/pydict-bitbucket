blooded

(ˈblʌdɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of horses, cattle, etc) of good breeding2. (in combination) having blood or temperament as specified ⇒ hot-blooded, cold-blooded, warm-blooded, red-blooded, blue-blooded

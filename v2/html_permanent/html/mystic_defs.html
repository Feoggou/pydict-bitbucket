mystic

(ˈmɪstɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who achieves mystical experience or an apprehension of divine mysteriesadjective2. another word for mystical

Word Origin

C14: via Latin from Greek mustikos, from mustēs mystery initiate; related to muein to initiate into sacred rites

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA mystic is a person who practises or believes in religious mysticism. ⇒ ...an Indian mystic known as Bhagwan Shree Rajneesh.2. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]Mystic means the same as mystical. ⇒ ...mystic union with God.

chic

(ʃiːk , ʃɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (esp of fashionable clothes, women, etc) stylish or elegantnoun2. stylishness, esp in dress; modishness; fashionable good taste3. any of various fashion movements based on a particular lifestyle ⇒ radical chic, ⇒ geek chic
Derived forms
chicly (ˈchicly) adverb
Word origin
C19: from French, of uncertain origin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething or someone that is chic is fashionable and sophisticated. ⇒ Her gown was very French and very chic.2. uncount nounChic is used to refer to a particular style or to the quality of being chic. ⇒ ...French designer chic. ⇒ He radiates charm and chic.

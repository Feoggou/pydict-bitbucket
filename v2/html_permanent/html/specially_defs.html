Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adverb [ADV with v]If something has been done specially for a particular person or purpose, it has been done only for that person or purpose. ⇒ ...a soap specially designed for those with sensitive skins. ⇒ Patrick needs to use specially adapted computer equipment. [+ for] ⇒ The school is specially for children whose schooling has been disrupted by illness.2. adverb [ADV adj]Specially is used to mean more than usually or more than other things. [informal] ⇒ Stay in bed extra late or get up specially early. ⇒ What was specially enjoyable about that job? ⇒ ...something which you specially want. ⇒ It can become extremely hot and dry, specially in a small glasshouse. ⇒ Dryness at the roots can occur very easily specially when plants are grown in containers.

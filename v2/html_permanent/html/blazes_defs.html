blazes

(ˈbleɪzɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. slang a euphemistic word for hell2. informal (intensifier) ⇒ to run like blazes, ⇒ what the blazes are you doing?3. See go to blazes!
Example sentences containing 'blazes'
My father, my sister, myself - all waiting, watching for some explosion, for one of those red-hot blazes of temper. Barnard, Robert POLITICAL SUICIDEAnd why the blazes couldn't you have warned me before we got there? Malcolm, John THE GWEN JOHN SCULPTUREStone wondered how in blazes Brunner had arranged that so easily. Terman, Douglas CORMORANT

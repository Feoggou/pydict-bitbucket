Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseYou use much less after a statement, often a negative one, to indicate that the statement is more true of the person, thing, or situation that you are going to mention next. ⇒ They are always short of water to drink, much less to bathe in. ⇒ But we must not think of Chekhov as a leftist, much less a revolutionary. →  much

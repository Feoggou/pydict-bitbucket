in tow

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
phraseIf you have someone in tow, they are following you closely because you are looking after them or you are leading them somewhere. [informal] ⇒ There she was on my doorstep with child in tow. ⇒ She had a reporter and a photographer in tow. →  tow

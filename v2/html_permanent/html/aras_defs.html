Aras

(æˈræs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna river rising in mountains in E Turkey and flowing east to the Caspian Sea: forms part of the E border of Turkey and the N border of Iran. Length: about 1100 km (660 miles). Ancient name: Araxes. Russian name: Araks

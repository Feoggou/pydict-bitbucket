yer

(jɜː)
Definitions
adjective1. an informal form of your ⇒ Mister, can we 'elp to carry yer stuff in?pronoun2. an informal form of you ⇒ I bloody told yer it would sell.

yer

Powered by COBUILD
(jɜːʳ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. Yer is used in written English to represent the word 'your' when it is pronounced informally. [British] ⇒ Mister, can we 'elp to carry yer stuff in?2. Yer is used in written English to represent the word 'you' when it is pronounced informally. [British] ⇒ I bloody told yer it would sell.

ascendant

or ascendent (əˈsɛndənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. proceeding upwards; rising2. dominant, superior, or influential3. botany another term for ascendingnoun4. rare an ancestor5. a position or condition of dominance, superiority or control6. astrology (sometimes capital)a. a point on the ecliptic that rises on the eastern horizon at a particular moment and changes as the earth rotates on its axisb. the sign of the zodiac containing this point7. See in the ascendant

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A demanding job or task requires a lot of your time, energy, or attention. ⇒ He found he could no longer cope with his demanding job. ⇒ It is a demanding role and she needs to work hard at it.2. graded adjective & adjectivePeople who are demanding are not easily satisfied or pleased. ⇒ Ricky was a very demanding child. ⇒ Her boss was very demanding but appreciative of Christina's talents.

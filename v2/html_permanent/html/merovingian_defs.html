Merovingian

(ˌmɛrəʊˈvɪndʒɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to a Frankish dynasty founded by Clovis I, which ruled Gaul and W Germany from about 500 to 751 adnoun2. a member or supporter of this dynasty

Word Origin

C17: from French, from Medieval Latin Merovingi offspring of Merovaeus, Latin form of Merowig, traditional founder of the line

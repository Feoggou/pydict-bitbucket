dishevelled

or US disheveled (dɪˈʃɛvəld )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (esp of hair) hanging loosely2. (of general appearance) unkempt; untidy
Word origin
C15 dischevelee, from Old French deschevelé, from des- dis-1 + chevel hair, from Latin capillus

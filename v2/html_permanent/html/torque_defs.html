torque

(tɔːk )
Definitions
noun1. Also: torc a necklace or armband made of twisted metal, worn esp by the ancient Britons and Gauls2. any force or system of forces that causes or tends to cause rotation3. the ability of a shaft to cause rotation
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from Latin torquēs necklace, and torquēre to twist

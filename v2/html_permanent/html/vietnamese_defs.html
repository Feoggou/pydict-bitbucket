Vietnamese

(ˌvjɛtnəˈmiːz )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Vietnam, its people, or their languagenoun2. plural -ese a native or inhabitant of Vietnam3. the language of Vietnam, probably related to the Mon-Khmer languages

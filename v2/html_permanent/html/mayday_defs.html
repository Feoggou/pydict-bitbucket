Word Origin

Word forms: Maydays
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounIf someone in a plane or ship sends out a Mayday or a Mayday message, they send out a radio message calling for help because they are in serious difficulty. ⇒ He raced to pick up the lifejackets while his stepmother sent out a Mayday call.2. convention'Mayday! Mayday!' is the phrase that is used in Mayday messages to indicate that help is needed.

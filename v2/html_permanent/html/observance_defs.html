observance

(əbˈzɜːvəns )
Definitions
noun1. recognition of or compliance with a law, custom, practice, etc2. the act of such recognition3. a ritual, ceremony, or practice, esp of a religion4. observation or attention5. the degree of strictness of a religious order or community in following its rule6. archaic respectful or deferential attention. Also called (obsolete): observancy

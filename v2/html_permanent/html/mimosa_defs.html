mimosa

(mɪˈməʊsə , -zə)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any tropical shrub or tree of the leguminous genus Mimosa, having ball-like clusters of yellow or pink flowers and compound leaves that are often sensitive to touch or light. See also sensitive plant2. any similar or related tree

Word Origin

C18: from New Latin, probably from Latin mīmus mime, because the plant's sensitivity to touch imitates the similar reaction of animals

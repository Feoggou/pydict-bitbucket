scammony

(ˈskæmənɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -nies1. a twining Asian convolvulus plant, Convolvulus scammonia, having arrow-shaped leaves, white or purple flowers, and tuberous roots2. a resinous juice obtained from the roots of this plant and having purgative properties3. any of various similar medicinal resins or the plants that yield them

Derived Forms

scammoniate (skæˈməʊnɪɪt ) adjective

Word Origin

Old English, via Latin from Greek skammōnia, of obscure origin

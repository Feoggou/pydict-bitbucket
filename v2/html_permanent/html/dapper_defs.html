dapper

(ˈdæpə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. neat and spruce in dress and bearing; trim2. small and nimble
Derived forms
dapperly (ˈdapperly) adverbdapperness (ˈdapperness) noun
Word origin
C15: from Middle Dutch: active, nimble

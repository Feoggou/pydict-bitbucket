barium

(ˈbɛərɪəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna soft silvery-white metallic element of the alkaline earth group. It is used in bearing alloys and compounds used as pigments. Symbol: Ba; atomic no: 56; atomic wt: 137.327; valency: 2; relative density: 3.5; melting pt: 729°C; boiling pt: 1805°C
Word origin
C19: from bar(yta) + -ium

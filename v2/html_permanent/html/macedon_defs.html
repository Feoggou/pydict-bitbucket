Macedon

(ˈmæsɪˌdɒn ) or Macedonia
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna region of the S Balkans, now divided among Greece, Bulgaria, and Macedonia (Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia). As a kingdom in the ancient world it achieved prominence under Philip II (359–336 bc) and his son Alexander the Great

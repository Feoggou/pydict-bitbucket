cellar

(ˈsɛlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an underground room, rooms, or storey of a building, usually used for storage. Compare basement2. a place where wine is stored3. a stock of bottled winesverb4. (transitive) to store in a cellar
Word origin
C13: from Anglo-French, from Latin cellārium food store, from cella cella

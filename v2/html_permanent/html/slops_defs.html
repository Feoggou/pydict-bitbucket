slops

(slɑps )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
plural noun1.  loosefitting outer garments; specif.,a.  [sing.] a smock, coveralls, or the likeb.  Archaic baggy trousers or breeches2.  clothing, bedding, etc. issued or sold to sailors3.  cheap, ready-made clothing
Webster’s New World College Dictionary, Fourth Edition, compiled by the editors of Webster’s New World Dictionaries. Copyright © 2010 by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. Published by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. All rights reserved.

Word Origin

ME sloppes, pl. of slop, sloppe < OE -slop (as in oferslop, loose outer garment); akin to sliefe, sleeve

winder

(ˈwaɪndə )
Definitions
noun1. a person or device that winds, as an engine for hoisting the cages in a mine shaft or a device for winding the yarn in textile manufacture2. an object, such as a bobbin, around which something is wound3. a knob or key used to wind up a clock, watch, or similar mechanism4. any plant that twists itself around a support5. a step of a spiral staircase

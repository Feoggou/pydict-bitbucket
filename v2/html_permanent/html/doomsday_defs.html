doomsday

or domesday (ˈduːmzˌdeɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (sometimes capital) the day on which the Last Judgment will occur2. any day of reckoning3. (modifier) characterized by predictions of disaster ⇒ doomsday scenario
Word origin
Old English dōmes dæg Judgment Day; related to Old Norse domsdagr

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounDoomsday is a day or time when you expect something terrible or unpleasant is going to happen. ⇒ ...the doomsday scenario of civil war between the two factions.2. proper nounIn the Christian religion, Doomsday is the last day of the world, on which God will judge everyone.

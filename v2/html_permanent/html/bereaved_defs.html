Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]A bereaved person is one who has a relative or close friend who has recently died. ⇒ Mr Dinkins visited the bereaved family to offer comfort.The bereaved are people who are bereaved. ⇒ He wanted to show his sympathy for the bereaved.

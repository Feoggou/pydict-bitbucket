look over

Definitions
verb1. (intr, preposition) to inspect by making a tour of (a factory, house, etc) ⇒ we looked over the country house2. (tr, adverb) to examine (a document, letter, etc) ⇒ please look the papers over quicklynoun lookover3. an inspection: often, specifically, a brief or cursory one

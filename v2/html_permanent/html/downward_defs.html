(daʊnwəʳd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]A downward movement or look is directed towards a lower place or a lower level. ⇒ ...a firm downward movement of the hands.2. → see also downwards3. adjective [ADJ n]If you refer to a downward trend, you mean that something is decreasing or that a situation is getting worse. ⇒ The downward trend in home ownership is likely to continue. ⇒ ...a decline in the economy, resulting in a general downward spiral.

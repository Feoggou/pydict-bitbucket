poster

(ˈpəʊstə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a large printed picture, used for decoration2. a placard or bill posted in a public place as an advertisement

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA poster is a large notice or picture that you stick on a wall or board, often in order to advertise something.

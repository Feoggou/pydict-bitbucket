(ɪndɪərɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If you describe someone's behaviour as endearing, you mean that it causes you to feel very fond of them. ⇒ She has such an endearing personality. ⇒ Henry's lisp is so endearing.endearingly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj] ⇒ He admits endearingly to doubts and hesitations. ⇒ She is endearingly free of pretensions.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Margaret

(ˈmɑːɡrət )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  called the Maid of Norway. ?1282–90, queen of Scotland (1286–90); daughter of Eric II of Norway. Her death while sailing to England to marry the future Edward II led Edward I to declare dominion over Scotland2.  1353–1412, queen of Sweden (1388–1412) and regent of Norway and Denmark (1380–1412), who united the three countries under her rule3.  Princess. 1930–2002, younger sister of Queen Elizabeth II of Great Britain and Northern Ireland

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveSomething that is diseased is affected by a disease. ⇒ The arteries are diseased and a transplant is the only hope. ⇒ Clear away dead or diseased plants.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]If you say that someone's mind is diseased, you are emphasizing that you think it is not normal or balanced. [emphasis] ⇒ Gardner describes the book as 'the product of a diseased and evil mind'.

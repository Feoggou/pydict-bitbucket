pitted1

(ˈpɪtɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveUS and Canadian (of fruit) having had the stones extracted ⇒ green and black pitted olives

pitted2

(ˈpɪtɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(of a surface) covered with indentations ⇒ the pitted surface of the moon, ⇒ Everywhere building facades are pitted with shell and bullet holes., ⇒ Their iron helmets were pitted with rust., ⇒ the man with the pitted face and the long hair

pitted

Powered by COBUILD
(pɪtɪd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Pitted fruits have had their stones removed. ⇒ ...green and black pitted olives.2. adjectiveIf the surface of something is pitted, it is covered with a lot of small, shallow holes. [+ with] ⇒ Everywhere building facades are pitted with shell and bullet holes. ⇒ ...the pitted surface of the moon.

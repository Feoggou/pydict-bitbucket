commemorative

Powered by COBUILD
(kəmemərətɪv )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]A commemorative object or event is intended to make people remember a particular event or person. ⇒ The Queen unveiled a commemorative plaque.
Example sentences containing 'commemorative'
`Antique mugs, commemorative mugs, advertising mugs... the house is full of them. Forsythe, Malcolm THE BOOK LADYThey went to flea markets and collected cartoon animal salt and pepper shakers, plastic cuckoo clocks, and commemorative plates. Metz, Don KING OF THE MOUNTAIN

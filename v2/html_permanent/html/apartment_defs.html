apartment

(əˈpɑːtmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (often plural) any room in a building, usually one of several forming a suite, esp one that is spacious and well furnished and used as living accommodation, offices, etc2. a. another name (esp US and Canadian) for flat2 (sense 1)b. (as modifier) ⇒ apartment building, ⇒ apartment house
Word origin
C17: from French appartement, from Italian appartamento, from appartare to set on one side, separate

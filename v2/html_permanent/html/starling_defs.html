starling1

(ˈstɑːlɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany gregarious passerine songbird of the Old World family Sturnidae, esp Sturnus vulgaris, which has a blackish plumage and a short tail

Word Origin

Old English stærlinc, from stær starling (related to Icelandic stari) + -line -ling1

navigable

(ˈnævɪɡəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. wide, deep, or safe enough to be sailed on or through ⇒ a navigable channel2. capable of being steered or controlled ⇒ a navigable raft
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

navigability (ˌnavigaˈbility) or navigableness (ˈnavigableness) nounnavigably (ˈnavigably) adverb

surplus

noun

  1. excess, surfeit, superabundance, superfluity • Germany suffers from a surplus of teachers. OPPOSITES:  lack, deficit, shortage, deficiency, shortfall, dearth, paucity, insufficiency

adjective

  1. extra, spare, excess, remaining, odd, in excess, left over, unused, superfluous • Few people have large sums of surplus cash. OPPOSITES:  lacking, inadequate, insufficient, scarce, limited, falling short, scant, deficient, scanty

pavement

(ˈpeɪvmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a hard-surfaced path for pedestrians alongside and a little higher than a road. US and Canadian word: sidewalk2. a paved surface, esp one that is a thoroughfare3. the material used in paving4. civil engineering the hard layered structure that forms a road carriageway, airfield runway, vehicle park, or other paved areas5. geology a level area of exposed rock resembling a paved road. See limestone pavement

Word Origin

C13: from Latin pavīmentum a hard floor, from pavīre to beat hard

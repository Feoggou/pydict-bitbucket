geranium

(dʒɪˈreɪnɪəm )
Definitions
noun1. any cultivated geraniaceous plant of the genus Pelargonium, having scarlet, pink, or white showy flowers. See also pelargonium, rose geranium, lemon geranium2. any geraniaceous plant of the genus Geranium, such as cranesbill and herb Robert, having divided leaves and pink or purplish flowers3. a strong red to a moderate or strong pink colour
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin: cranesbill, from Greek geranion, from geranos crane

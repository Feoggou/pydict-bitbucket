(dʒɜːʳnəlɪstɪk )
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]Journalistic means relating to journalism, or produced by or typical of a journalist. ⇒ He began his journalistic career in the early eighties in Australia. ⇒ ...journalistic descriptions of countries she visited.

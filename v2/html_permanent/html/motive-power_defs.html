motive power

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any source of energy used to produce motion2. the means of supplying power to an engine, vehicle, etc3. any driving force
Example sentences containing 'motive power'
The barge slewed violently as its motive power was lost, and began to drift downstream toward the eddy. Julian May IRONCROWN MOON: PART TWO OF THE BOREAL MOON TALE

album

(ˈælbəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a book or binder consisting of blank pages, pockets, or envelopes for keeping photographs, stamps, autographs, drawings, poems, etc2. a collection of recordings released as a single item3. a booklike holder containing sleeves for gramophone records4. mainly British an anthology, usually large and illustrated
Word origin
C17: from Latin: blank tablet, from albus white

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounAn album is a record with about 25 minutes of music on each side. You can also refer to a collection of songs that is available on a record or CD as an album. LP ⇒ Chris likes music and has a large collection of albums.2. countable noun [oft n N]An album is a book in which you keep things such as photographs or stamps that you have collected. ⇒ Theresa showed me her photo album.

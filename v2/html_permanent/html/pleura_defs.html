pleura

(ˈplʊərə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural pleurae (ˈplʊəriː )1. the thin transparent serous membrane enveloping the lungs and lining the walls of the thoracic cavity2. the plural of pleuron

Derived Forms

pleural (ˈpleural) adjective

Word Origin

C17: via Medieval Latin from Greek: side, rib

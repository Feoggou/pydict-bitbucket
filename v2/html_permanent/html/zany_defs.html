zany

(ˈzeɪnɪ )
Definitions
adjective -nier or -niest1. comical in an endearing way; imaginatively funny or comical, esp in behaviournoun plural -nies2. a clown or buffoon, esp one in old comedies who imitated other performers with ludicrous effect3. a ludicrous or foolish person
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

zanily (ˈzanily) adverbzaniness (ˈzaniness) nounzanyism (ˈzanyism) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Italian zanni, from dialect (Venice and Lombardy) Zanni, nickname for Giovanni John; one of the traditional names for a clown

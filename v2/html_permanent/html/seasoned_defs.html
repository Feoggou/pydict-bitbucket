Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]You can use seasoned to describe a person who has a lot of experience of something. For example, a seasoned traveller is a person who has travelled a lot. ⇒ The author is a seasoned academic. ⇒ He began acting with the confidence of a seasoned performer.

Marlette watched as she dodged the flailing arms and feet with the experience of a seasoned running back to reach Michelle. MacNeill, Alastair THE DEVIL'S DOORKomarov and Vatukin drank like seasoned veterans, letting it take off the edge, but nothing more. Mark Burnell CHAMELEON

alien

noun

  1. foreigner, incomer, immigrant, stranger, outsider, newcomer, asylum seeker, outlander • The woman had hired an illegal alien for child care. OPPOSITES:  national, resident, citizen, inhabitant, countryman, dweller
  2. extraterrestrial, visitor, ET, little green man (informal) • His sister was abducted by aliens.

adjective

  1. foreign, outside, strange, imported, overseas, unknown, exotic, unfamiliar, not native, not naturalized • They were afraid of the presence of alien troops in the region.
  2. strange, new, foreign, novel, remote, unknown, exotic, unfamiliar, estranged, outlandish, untried, unexplored, beyond your ken • His work offers an insight into an alien culture. OPPOSITES:  like, similar, related, allied, connected, parallel, alike, corresponding, affiliated, akin, analogous, kindred, cognate
  3. extraterrestrial, otherworldly • He wants to rule out the existence of alien beings.
  • See alien to

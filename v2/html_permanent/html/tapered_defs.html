tapered

(ˈteɪpəd)
Definitions
adjectivebecoming narrower towards one end ⇒ a tapered blade, ⇒ The scientists were not clear as yet as to the weapon itself, but they could say it had a sharp point, a tapered blade sharp on both sides., ⇒ perfectly tapered fingers, ⇒ He had the perfectly tapered fingers, the hands of a pianist or a surgeon.

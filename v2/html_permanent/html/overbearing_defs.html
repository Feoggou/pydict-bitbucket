overbearing

(ˌəʊvəˈbɛərɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. domineering or dictatorial in manner or action2. of particular or overriding importance or significance
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

overbearingly (ˌoverˈbearingly) adverb

survival kit

(səˈvaɪvəl kɪt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna set of equipment that will enable you to survive in dangerous conditions ⇒ a jungle survival kit, ⇒ Prepare a survival kit to carry in the car with you: meals and drinks for the day, kettle, spoons, towels, soap, loo paper, candles, matches, can opener and first-aid kit.
Example sentences containing 'survival kit'
There is every chance that even my survival kit won't go ahead at all. Scarlett Thomas POPCOMine's like a survival kit , with everything from eye pencil to Swiss Army knife via pocket camera and tape recorder. Val McDermid DEAD BEAT

educational

(ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃənəl )
Definitions
adjective1. providing knowledge; instructive or informative ⇒ an educational toy2. of or relating to education
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

educationally (ˌeduˈcationally) adverb

educational

Powered by COBUILD
(edʒʊkeɪʃənəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Educational matters or institutions are concerned with or relate to education. ⇒ ...the British educational system. ⇒ ...pupils with special educational needs.educationally adverb ⇒ ...educationally sound ideas for managing classrooms.2. graded adjective & adjectiveAn educational experience teaches you something. ⇒ The staff should make sure the kids have an enjoyable and educational day.

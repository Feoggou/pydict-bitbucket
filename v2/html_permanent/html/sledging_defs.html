sledging1

(ˈslɛdʒɪŋ) or sledding (ˈslɛdɪŋ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nounthe activity of travelling across snow on a sledge ⇒ Our hill is marvellous for sledging and we always have snow in January., ⇒ Let's go sledging.

sledging2

(ˈslɛdʒɪŋ)

Definitions

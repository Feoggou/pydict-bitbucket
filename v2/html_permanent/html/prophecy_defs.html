prophecy

(ˈprɒfɪsɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cies1. a. a message of divine truth revealing God's willb. the act of uttering such a message2. a prediction or guess3. the function, activity, or charismatic endowment of a prophet or prophets

Word Origin

C13: ultimately from Greek prophētēs prophet

subterranean

(ˌsʌbtəˈreɪnɪən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. Also: subterraneous, subterrestrial situated, living, or operating below the surface of the earth2. existing or operating in concealment

Derived Forms

ˌsubterraneanly (ˌsubterˈraneanly) or ˌsubterraneously (ˌsubterˈraneously) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin subterrāneus, from sub- + terra earth

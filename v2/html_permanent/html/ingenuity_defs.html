ingenuity

(ˌɪndʒɪˈnjuːɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. inventive talent; cleverness2. an ingenious device, act, etc3. archaic frankness; candour
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin ingenuitās a freeborn condition, outlook consistent with such a condition, from ingenuus native, freeborn (see ingenuous); meaning influenced by ingenious

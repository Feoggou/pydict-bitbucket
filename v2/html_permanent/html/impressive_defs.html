Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmpresɪv ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is impressive impresses you, for example because it is great in size or degree, or is done with a great deal of skill. OPPOSITE: unimpressive ⇒ It is an impressive achievement. ⇒ The film's special effects are particularly impressive.impressively graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj, ADV with v] ⇒ ...an impressively bright and energetic American woman called Cathie Gould. ⇒ The socialists performed impressively in the legislative elections.

coincident

(kəʊˈɪnsɪdənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having the same position in space or time2. (usually postpositive and foll by with) in exact agreement; consonant

coincident

Powered by COBUILD
(koʊɪnsɪdənt )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveCoincident events happen at the same time. [formal] ⇒ ...coincident birth times. [+ with] ⇒ Coincident with the talks, the bank was permitted to open a New York branch.2. graded adjective & adjectiveCoincident opinions, ideas, or policies are the same or are very similar to each other. [formal] [+ with] ⇒ Their aims are coincident with ours. ⇒ Coincident interests with the corporate rich and political directorate are pointed out.

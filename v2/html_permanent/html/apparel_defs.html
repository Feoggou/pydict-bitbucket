apparel

(əˈpærəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. something that covers or adorns, esp outer garments or clothing2. nautical a vessel's gear and equipmentverb -els, -elling, -elled or US -els, -eling or -eled3. archaic (transitive) to clothe, adorn, etc
Word origin
C13: from Old French apareillier to make ready, from Vulgar Latin appariculāre (unattested), from Latin apparāre, from parāre to prepare

Synonyms of ooze 1

ooze1

verb

  1. seep, well, drop, escape, strain, leak, drain, sweat, filter, bleed, weep, drip, trickle, leach, dribble, percolate
    • Blood was still oozing from the wound.
  2. emit, release, leak, sweat, bleed, discharge, drip, leach, give out, dribble, exude, give off, excrete, overflow with, pour forth
    • The cut was oozing a clear liquid.
  3. exude, emit, radiate, display, exhibit, manifest, gush, emanate, overflow with
    • Graham positively oozed confidence.

noun

  1. seepage, leak, leaking, discharge, drip, dripping, trickle, oozing, dribble, leakage, exudation
    • His shirt was soaked through with the slow ooze of blood.

ooze2

noun

  1. mud, clay, dirt, muck, silt, sludge, mire, slime, slob (Irish), gloop (informal), alluvium
    • He thrust his hand into the ooze and brought out a large toad.

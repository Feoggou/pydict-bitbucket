surf

(sɜːf )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. waves breaking on the shore or on a reef2. foam caused by the breaking of wavesverb3. (intransitive) to take part in surfing4. a. computing (on the internet) to move freely from website to website (esp in the phrase surf the net)b. to move freely between (TV channels or radio stations)5. a. informal to be carried on top of something ⇒ that guy's surfing the audienceb. (in combination) ⇒ trainsurfing

Derived Forms

surfable (ˈsurfable) adjectivesurfˌlike (ˈsurfˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C17: probably variant of sough1

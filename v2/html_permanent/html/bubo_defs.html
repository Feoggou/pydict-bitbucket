bubo

(ˈbjuːbəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -boespathology inflammation and swelling of a lymph node, often with the formation of pus, esp in the region of the armpit or groin
Derived forms
bubonic (bjuːˈbɒnɪk ) adjective
Word origin
C14: from Medieval Latin bubō swelling, from Greek boubōn groin, glandular swelling

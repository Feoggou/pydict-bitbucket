ling1

(lɪŋ )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural ling or lings1. any of several gadoid food fishes of the northern coastal genus Molva, esp M. molva, having an elongated body with long fins2. another name for burbot
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: probably from Low German; related to long1

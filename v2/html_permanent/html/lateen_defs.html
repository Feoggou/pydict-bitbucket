lateen

(ləˈtiːn )
Definitions
adjectivenautical denoting a rig with a triangular sail (lateen sail) bent to a yard hoisted to the head of a low mast, used esp in the Mediterranean
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from French voile latine Latin sail

surreptitious

(ˌsʌrəpˈtɪʃəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. done, acquired, etc, in secret or by improper means2. operating by stealth3. characterized by fraud or misrepresentation of the truth

Derived Forms

ˌsurreptitiously (ˌsurrepˈtitiously) adverbˌsurreptitiousness (ˌsurrepˈtitiousness) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin surreptīcius furtive, from surripere to steal, from sub- secretly + rapere to snatch

triumphal

Powered by COBUILD
(traɪʌmfəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Triumphal is used to describe things that are done or made to celebrate a victory or great success. ⇒ He made a triumphal entry into the city. ⇒ The triumphal arch commemorates Caesar's victory over Pompey.

lie down

Definitions
verb (intr, adverb)1. to place oneself or be in a prostrate position in order to rest or sleep2. to accept without protest or opposition (esp in the phrases lie down under, take something lying down)noun lie-down3. a rest

lie-down

(ˌlaɪˈdaʊn)
Definitions
nounBritish informal a brief rest lying down; a nap ⇒ She'd gone upstairs to have a lie-down.

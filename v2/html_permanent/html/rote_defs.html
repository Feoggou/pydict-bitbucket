rote

Powered by COBUILD
(roʊt ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [N n, by N]Rote learning or learning by rote is learning things by repeating them without thinking about them or trying to understand them. ⇒ He is very sceptical about the value of rote learning. ⇒ You are merely reciting facts that you have learned by rote.

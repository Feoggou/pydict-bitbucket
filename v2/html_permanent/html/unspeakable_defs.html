unspeakable

(ʌnˈspiːkəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. incapable of expression in words ⇒ unspeakable ecstasy2. indescribably bad or evil3. not to be uttered ⇒ unspeakable thoughts
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unspeakableness (unˈspeakableness) noununspeakably (unˈspeakably) adverb

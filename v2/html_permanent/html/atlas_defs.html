atlas

(ˈætləs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a collection of maps, usually in book form2. a book of charts, graphs, etc, illustrating aspects of a subject ⇒ an anatomical atlas3. anatomy the first cervical vertebra, attached to and supporting the skull in man. Compare axis14. Word forms: plural atlantes architecture another name for telamon5. a standard size of drawing paper, 26 × 17 inches
Word origin
C16: via Latin from Greek; first applied to maps, from depictions of Atlas supporting the heavens in 16th-century collections of maps

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Greek mythology a Titan compelled to support the sky on his shoulders as punishment for rebelling against Zeus2. a US intercontinental ballistic missile, also used in launching spacecraft3. astronomy a small satellite of Saturn, discovered in 1980

(fʌndz) in Accounting
Definitions
noun(Accounting: Basic) Funds are money that is readily available. ⇒ The government should not be using public funds to pay for these services. ⇒ If the investor loans are not provided and adequate funds from other sources are not available by April 30, the company will be required to terminate its operations. ⇒ Funds are money that is readily available.

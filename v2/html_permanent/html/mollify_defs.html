mollify

(ˈmɒlɪˌfaɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -fies, -fying or -fied (transitive)1. to pacify; soothe2. to lessen the harshness or severity of

Derived Forms

molliˌfiable (ˈmolliˌfiable) adjectiveˌmollification (ˌmollifiˈcation) nounmolliˌfier (ˈmolliˌfier) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Old French mollifier, via Late Latin, from Latin mollis soft + facere to make

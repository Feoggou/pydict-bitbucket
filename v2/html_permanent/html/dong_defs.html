dong

(dɒŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the deep reverberating sound of a large bell2. Australian and New Zealand informal a heavy blow3. a slang word for penisverb4. (intransitive) (of a bell) to make a deep reverberating sound5. (transitive) Australian and New Zealand informal to strike or punch
Word origin
C16: of imitative origin

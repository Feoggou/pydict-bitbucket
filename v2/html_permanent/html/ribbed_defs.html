ribbed

(rɪbd)
Definitions
adjective1. furnished or supported with a rib or ribs ⇒ It was an aluminium boat with a flat ribbed bottom., ⇒ special ribbed tyres2. marked with or formed into ribs or ridges ⇒ the curved, gently ribbed surface of clam shells3. knitted with plain and purl stitches alternately, forming raised rows ⇒ a ribbed polo shirt, ⇒ ribbed cashmere sweaters
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

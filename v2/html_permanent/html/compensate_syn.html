compensate

verb

  1. recompense, repay, refund, reimburse, indemnify, make restitution, requite, remunerate, satisfy, make good • To ease financial difficulties, farmers could be compensated for their loss of subsidies.
  2. make amends for, make up for, atone for, pay for, do penance for, cancel out, make reparation for, make redress for • She compensated for her burst of anger by doing even more for the children.
  3. balance, cancel (out), offset, make up for, redress, counteract, neutralize, counterbalance, countervail • The rewards more than compensated for the inconveniences involved in making the trip.

PROVERB:
 What you lose on the swings you gain on the roundabouts

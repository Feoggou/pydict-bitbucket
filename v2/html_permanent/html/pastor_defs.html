pastor

(ˈpɑːstə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a clergyman or priest in charge of a congregation2. a person who exercises spiritual guidance over a number of people3. an archaic word for shepherd (sense 1)4. Also called: rosy pastor a S Asian starling, Sturnus roseus, having glossy black head and wings and a pale pink body

Derived Forms

pastorˌship (ˈpastorˌship) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Latin: shepherd, from pascere to feed

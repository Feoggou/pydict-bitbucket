Synonyms of recompense

recompense

noun

  1. compensation, pay, payment, satisfaction, amends, repayment, remuneration, reparation, indemnity, restitution, damages, emolument, indemnification, requital
    • He demands no financial recompense for his troubles.

verb

  1. compensate, reimburse, redress, repay, pay for, satisfy, make good, make up for, make amends for, indemnify, requite, make restitution for
    • If they succeed in court, they will be fully recompensed for their loss.
  2. reward, pay, remunerate

noun

  1. reward, pay, return, payment, wages, meed (archaic)

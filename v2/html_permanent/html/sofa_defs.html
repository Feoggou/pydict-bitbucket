sofa

(ˈsəʊfə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nounan upholstered seat with back and arms for two or more people

Word Origin

C17 (in the sense: dais upholstered as a seat): from Arabic suffah

sofa

Powered by COBUILD

Word forms: sofas
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA sofa is a long, comfortable seat with a back and usually with arms, which two or three people can sit on.

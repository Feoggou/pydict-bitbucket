bodily

(ˈbɒdɪlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. relating to or being a part of the human bodyadverb2. by taking hold of the body ⇒ he threw him bodily from the platform3. in person; in the flesh

bodily

Powered by COBUILD
(bɒdɪli )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Your bodily needs and functions are the needs and functions of your body. ⇒ ...descriptions of natural bodily functions. ⇒ There's more to eating than just bodily needs.2. → see also grievous bodily harm3. adverb [ADV with v]You use bodily to indicate that an action involves the whole of someone's body. ⇒ I was hurled bodily to the deck.

habitus

(ˈhæbɪtəs )
Definitions
noun plural -tus1. medicine general physical state, esp with regard to susceptibility to disease2. tendency or inclination, esp of plant or animal growth; habit
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from Latin: state, habit

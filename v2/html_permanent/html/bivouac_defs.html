bivouac

(ˈbɪvʊˌæk , ˈbɪvwæk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a temporary encampment with few facilities, as used by soldiers, mountaineers, etcverb -acs, -acking or -acked2. (intransitive) to make such an encampment
Word origin
C18: from French bivuac, probably from Swiss German Beiwacht, literally: by + watch

Definitions

(ɪnəbɪlɪti ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [usu N to-inf, usu with poss]If you refer to someone's inability to do something, you are referring to the fact that they are unable to do it. incapacity ⇒ Her inability to concentrate could cause an accident. ⇒ ...the government's inability to provide basic services.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

contiguous

(kənˈtɪɡjʊəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. touching along the side or boundary; in contact2. physically adjacent; neighbouring3. preceding or following in time
Derived forms
contiguity (ˌkɒntɪˈɡjuːɪtɪ ) or contiguousness (conˈtiguousness) nouncontiguously (conˈtiguously) adverb
Word origin
C17: from Latin contiguus, from contingere to touch; see contact

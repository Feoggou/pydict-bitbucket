Word forms: free agents
Definitions
1. countable nounIf you say that someone is a free agent, you are emphasizing that they can do whatever they want to do, because they are not responsible to anyone or for anyone. ⇒ We are not free agents; we abide by the decisions of our president.2. countable nounIf a sports player is a free agent, he or she is free to sign a contract with any team. [US]

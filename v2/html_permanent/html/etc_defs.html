Definitions
etc is used at the end of a list to indicate that you have mentioned only some of the items involved and have not given a full list. etc is a written abbreviation for 'et cetera'. ⇒ She knew all about my schoolwork, my hospital work etc. ⇒ ...a packed programme of events–shows, dances, coach tours, sports, etc.

(dɪˈsɛpʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of deceiving or the state of being deceived2. something that deceives; trick

variable nounDeception is the act of deceiving someone or the state of being deceived by someone. ⇒ He admitted conspiring to obtain property by deception. ⇒ You've been the victim of a rather cruel deception.

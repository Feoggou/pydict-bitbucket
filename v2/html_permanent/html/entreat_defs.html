entreat

or intreat (ɪnˈtriːt )
Definitions
verb1. to ask (a person) earnestly; beg or plead with; implore2. to make an earnest request or petition for (something)3. an archaic word for treat (sense 4)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

entreatingly (enˈtreatingly) or intreatingly (inˈtreatingly) adverbentreatment (enˈtreatment) or intreatment (inˈtreatment) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Old French entraiter, from en-1 + traiter to treat

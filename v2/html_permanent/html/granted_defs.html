Definitions

granted

Powered by COBUILD
(grɑːntɪd , græntɪd) for learners of English
Definitions
subordinating conjunction & conjunctionYou use granted or granted that at the beginning of a clause to say that something is true, before you make a comment on it. ⇒ Granted that the firm has not broken the law, is the law what it should be?Granted is also an adverb. ⇒ Granted, he doesn't look too bad for his age, but I don't fancy him.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

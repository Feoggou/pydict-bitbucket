Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adverbConfidentially is used to say that what you are telling someone is a secret and should not be discussed with anyone else. between us ⇒ Confidentially, I am not sure that it wasn't above their heads.2. → see also confidential
Example sentences containing 'confidentially'
Nash hadn't raised his voice, still spoke rather softly, confidentially. Smith, Mitchell STONE CITY`She was actually telling me confidentially how much better my predecessor was at everything. Barbara Erskine SANDS OF TIME`He's an old man, tall, white-haired, speaks English," Helena lowered her voice confidentially , `my father, in fact. Appiganesi, Lisa DREAMS OF INNOCENCE

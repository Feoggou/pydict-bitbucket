resilience

(rɪˈzɪlɪəns )
Definitions
noun1. Also: resiliency the state or quality of being resilient2. ecology the ability of an ecosystem to return to its original state after being disturbed3. physics the amount of potential energy stored in an elastic material when deformed
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

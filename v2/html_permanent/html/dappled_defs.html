Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]You use dappled to describe something that has dark or light patches on it, or that is made up of patches of light and shade. ⇒ ...a dappled horse. ⇒ Sometimes I carry a book out under the pear trees and sit in the dappled shade. [+ with/by/in] ⇒ The path was dappled with sunlight.
Example sentences containing 'dappled'

Robert Wilson INSTRUMENTS OF DARKNESSIt was not the classical silver ribbon, but a dappled silver as it moved. Lyall, Francis A DEATH IN TIME

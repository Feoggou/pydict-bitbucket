Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveSomething that is studded is decorated with studs or things that look like studs. ⇒ ...studded leather jackets. ⇒ ...a beautiful gold bracelet studded with diamonds. [Also + with]Studded is also a combining form. ⇒ ...a gold and diamond-studded trophy.2. adjective [v-link ADJ, ADJ with n]If you say that something is studded with another thing, you mean that there are a lot of the second thing in or on the first thing. ⇒ His collection was studded with Flemish masterpieces. ⇒ ...a metal panel studded with small microphones.3. → see also star-studded

Bilbao

(bɪlˈbɑːəʊ , Spanish bilˈβau)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna port in N Spain, on the Bay of Biscay: the largest city in the Basque Provinces: famous since medieval times for the production of iron and steel goods: modern buildings include the Guggenheim Art Museum (1997). Pop: 353 567 (2003 est). Basque name: Bilbo

pointless

(ˈpɔɪntlɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. without a point2. without meaning, relevance, or force3. sport without a point scored

Derived Forms

pointlessly (ˈpointlessly) adverbpointlessness (ˈpointlessness) noun

Powered by COBUILD
(pɔɪntləs )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you say that something is pointless, you are criticizing it because it has no sense or purpose. [disapproval] ⇒ Violence is always pointless. ⇒ Without an audience the performance is pointless. ⇒ ...pointless arguments.pointlessly adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ Chemicals were pointlessly poisoning the soil. ⇒ ...lying awake pointlessly going over and over again something we need to do.pointlessness uncount noun [+ of] ⇒ You cannot help wondering about the pointlessness of it all.

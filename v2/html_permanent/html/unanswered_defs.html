unanswered for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnɑːnsəʳd , -æns-)
Definitions
adjective [v-link ADJ, ADJ n, ADJ after v]Something such as a question or letter that is unanswered has not been answered. ⇒ Some of the most important questions remain unanswered. ⇒ Readers should send a copy of unanswered letters to their MP. ⇒ He had always had difficulty leaving questions unanswered.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an inferior alternative ⇒ Don't settle for second best.2. See a good second bestadjective3. not as good as the best thing of its kind but better than all the other things of that kind ⇒ We polished and wore our second-best boots., ⇒ It's his second-best novel., ⇒ He put on his second best suit.4. not ideal; not meeting all your wishes; inferior ⇒ a second-best choice, ⇒ a second-best solution, ⇒ a messy, second-best solution, ⇒ He refused to settle for anything that was second best.adverb5. See to come off second best

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Second best is used to describe something that is not as good as the best thing of its kind but is better than all the other things of that kind. ⇒ He put on his second-best suit. ⇒ We polished and wore our second-best boots.2. adjectiveYou can use second best to describe something that you have to accept even though you would have preferred something else. ⇒ ...a messy, second-best solution. ⇒ He refused to settle for anything that was second best.Second best is also a noun. ⇒ Oatmeal is a good second best.

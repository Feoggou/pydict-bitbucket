three quarters

(θriː ˈkwɔːtəz)
Definitions
noun1. three of four equal parts or divisions ⇒ Three-quarters of the world's surface is covered by water., ⇒ Road deaths have increased by three-quarters., ⇒ I'm 29 and three-quarters., ⇒ It took him about three-quarters of an hour.adverb2. three-fourths; by or to three out of four equal parts or divisions ⇒ The money is three-quarters gone., ⇒ an open bottle of champagne three-quarters full

three-quarters

Powered by COBUILD
also three quarters for learners of English
Definitions
quantifierThree-quarters is an amount that is three out of four equal parts of something. [+ of] ⇒ Three-quarters of the country's workers took part in the strike. [+ of] ⇒ It took him about three-quarters of an hour.Three-quarters is also a pronoun. ⇒ Road deaths have increased by three-quarters. ⇒ I'm 29 and three-quarters.Three-quarters is also an adverb. ⇒ We were left with an open bottle of champagne three-quarters full. ⇒ Remove the vegetables from the steamer when they are three-quarters cooked.

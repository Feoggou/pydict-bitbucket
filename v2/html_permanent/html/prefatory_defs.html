prefatory

(ˈprɛfətərɪ , -trɪ) or prefatorial (ˌprɛfəˈtɔːrɪəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveof, involving, or serving as a preface; introductory

Derived Forms

prefatorily (ˈprefatorily) or ˌprefatorially (ˌprefaˈtorially) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin praefārī to say in advance; see preface

Similar Words

introductory, opening, preliminary, preparatory, antecedent, precursory, prefatorial, prelusive, prelusory, proemial, prolegomenal

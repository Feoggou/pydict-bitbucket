overset

Definitions
verb (ˌəʊvəˈsɛt ) -sets, -setting or -set (transitive)1. to disturb or upset2. printing to set (type or copy) in excess of the space availablenoun (ˈəʊvəˌsɛt )3. another name for overmatter

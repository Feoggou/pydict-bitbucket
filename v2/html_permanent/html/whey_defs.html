whey

(weɪ )
Definitions
nounthe watery liquid that separates from the curd when the milk is clotted, as in making cheese
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

wheyey (ˈwheyey), wheyish (ˈwheyish) or wheylike (ˈwheyˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

Old English hwǣg; related to Middle Low German wei, heie, Dutch hui

whey for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD

Definitions
uncount nounWhey is the watery part of sour milk that is separated from the thick part called curds, for example when you are making cheese.

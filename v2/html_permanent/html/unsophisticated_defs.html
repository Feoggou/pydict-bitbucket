unsophisticated

(ˌʌnsəˈfɪstɪˌkeɪtɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. lacking experience or worldly wisdom2. marked by a lack of refinement or complexity ⇒ an unsophisticated machine3. unadulterated or genuine
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unsophisticatedly (ˌunsoˈphistiˌcatedly) adverbunsophisticatedness (ˌunsoˈphistiˌcatedness) or unsophistication (ˌunsoˌphistiˈcation) noun

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveUnsophisticated people do not have a wide range of experience or knowledge and have simple tastes. ⇒ It was music of a rather crude kind which unsophisticated audiences enjoyed listening to. [+ in] ⇒ She was quite unsophisticated in the ways of the world.2. graded adjective & adjectiveAn unsophisticated method or device is very simple and often not very effective. ⇒ ...an unsophisticated alarm system.

beef

(biːf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the flesh of various bovine animals, esp the cow, when killed for eating2. Word forms: plural beeves (biːvz ) an adult ox, bull, cow, etc, reared for its meat3. informal human flesh, esp when muscular4. Word forms: plural beefs a complaintverb5. (intransitive) slang to complain, esp repeatedly ⇒ he was beefing about his tax6. (transitive; often foll by up) informal to strengthen; reinforce
Word origin
C13: from Old French boef, from Latin bōs ox; see cow1

fondle

(ˈfɒndəl )
Definitions
verb1. (transitive) to touch or stroke tenderly; caress2. (intransitive) archaic to act in a loving manner
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fondler (ˈfondler) nounfondlingly (ˈfondlingly) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from (obsolete) vb fond to fondle; see fond1

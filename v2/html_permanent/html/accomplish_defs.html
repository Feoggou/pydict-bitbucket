accomplish

(əˈkɒmplɪʃ , əˈkʌm-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to manage to do; achieve2. to conclude successfully; complete
Derived forms
acˈcomplishable adjectiveacˈcomplisher noun
Word origin
C14: from Old French acomplir to complete, ultimately from Latin complēre to fill up. See complete

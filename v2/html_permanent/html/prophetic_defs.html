prophetic

(prəˈfɛtɪk ) or prophetical (prəˈfɛtɪkəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to a prophet or prophecy2. containing or of the nature of a prophecy; predictive

Derived Forms

prophetically (proˈphetically) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf something was prophetic, it described or suggested something that did actually happen later. ⇒ This ominous warning soon proved prophetic. ⇒ Friends recalled Elisabeth's prophetic words of several years ago.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Prophetic means related to a prophecy or a prophet. ⇒ ...a charming romance intermingled with scientific fact and prophetic vision.

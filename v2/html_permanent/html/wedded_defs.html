wedded

(ˈwɛdɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. of marriage ⇒ wedded bliss2. firmly in support of an idea or institution ⇒ wedded to the virtues of capitalism
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

wedded for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(wedɪd )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are wedded to something such as an idea, you support it so strongly or like it so much that you are unable to give it up. [formal] [+ to] ⇒ Conservationists are mostly wedded to preserving diversity in nature.2. adjective [ADJ n]Wedded means the same as married. [formal] ⇒ He proposed she become his lawfully wedded wife. ⇒ She clings to a romantic fantasy of wedded bliss.

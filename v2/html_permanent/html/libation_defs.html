libation

(laɪˈbeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a. the pouring out of wine, etc, in honour of a deityb. the liquid so poured out2. usually facetious an alcoholic drink
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

libational (liˈbational) or libationary (liˈbationary) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Latin lībātiō, from lībāre to pour an offering of drink

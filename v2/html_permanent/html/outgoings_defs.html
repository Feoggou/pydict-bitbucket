Powered by COBUILD
(aʊtgoʊɪŋz )
Definitions
plural nounYour outgoings are the regular amounts of money which you have to spend every week or every month, for example in order to pay your rent or bills. [British] expenses ⇒ She suggests you first assess your income and outgoings. ⇒ ...monthly outgoings.regional note:   in AM, usually use outlay, expenses

open air

Definitions
nouna. the place or space where the air is unenclosed; the outdoorsb. (as modifier) ⇒ an open-air concert

open-air for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
also open air
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]An open-air place or event is outside rather than in a building. ⇒ ...the Open Air Theatre in Regents Park. ⇒ ...an open air concert in brilliant sunshine.2. singular nounIf you are in the open air, you are outside rather than in a building. ⇒ We sleep out under the stars, and eat our meals in the open air.

ineluctable

(ˌɪnɪˈlʌktəbəl )
Definitions
adjective(esp of fate) incapable of being avoided; inescapable
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ineluctability (ˌineˌluctaˈbility) nounineluctably (ˌineˈluctably) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin inēluctābilis, from in-1 + ēluctārī to escape, from luctārī to struggle

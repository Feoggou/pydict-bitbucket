v

or V (viː )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural v's, V's or Vs1. the 22nd letter and 17th consonant of the modern English alphabet2. a speech sound represented by this letter, in English usually a voiced labio-dental fricative, as in vote3. a. something shaped like a Vb. (in combination) ⇒ a V neck. See also V-sign

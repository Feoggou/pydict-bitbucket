virile

(ˈvɪraɪl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or having the characteristics of an adult male2. (of a male) possessing high sexual drive and capacity for sexual intercourse3. of or capable of copulation or procreation4. strong, forceful, or vigorous
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

virility (vɪˈrɪlɪtɪ ) nounvirilely (ˈvirilely) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Latin virīlis manly, from vir a man; related to Old English wer man and probably to Latin vis strength

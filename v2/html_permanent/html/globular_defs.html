globular

(ˈɡlɒbjʊlə ) or globulous
Definitions
adjective1. shaped like a globe or globule2. having or consisting of globules
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

globularity (ˌɡlɒbjʊˈlærɪtɪ ) or globularness (ˈglobularness) nounglobularly (ˈglobularly) adverb

per capita

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]The per capita amount of something is the total amount of it in a country or area divided by the number of people in that country or area. ⇒ They have the world's largest per capita income. ⇒ The per capita consumption of alcohol has dropped over the past two years.Per capita is also an adverb. per head ⇒ Ethiopia has almost the lowest oil consumption per capita in the world. ⇒ This year Americans will eat about 40% more fresh apples per capita than the Japanese.

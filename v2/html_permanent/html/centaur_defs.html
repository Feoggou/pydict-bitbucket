centaur

(ˈsɛntɔː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounGreek mythology one of a race of creatures with the head, arms, and torso of a man, and the lower body and legs of a horse. Also called: hippocentaur
Word origin
C14: from Latin, from Greek kentauros, of unknown origin

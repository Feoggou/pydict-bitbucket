refracting

(rɪˈfræktɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective physics1. undergoing or causing refraction ⇒ a bright refracting crystal2. the change in direction of a propagating wave, such as light or sound ⇒ 
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

This became an enjoyable occupation, and after a while he was able, as Newton had, to find soft rainbows refracting through the glass.

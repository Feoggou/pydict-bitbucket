enthusiastic

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnθjuːziæstɪk , US -θuː- ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are enthusiastic about something, you show how much you like or enjoy it by the way that you behave and talk. [+ about] ⇒ Tom was very enthusiastic about the place. ⇒ Bob Dole seemed less than enthusiastic about the proposed move. ⇒ He knew much about pictures and fine furniture, and was an enthusiastic gardener.enthusiastically (ɪnθjuːziæstɪkli , US -θuː-) graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj] ⇒ The announcement was greeted enthusiastically.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

nuts

(nʌts )
Definitions
adjective1. a slang word for insane2. (foll by about or on) slang extremely fond (of) or enthusiastic (about)exclamation3. slang an expression of disappointment, contempt, refusal, or defianceplural noun4. a slang word for testicles. See testicle

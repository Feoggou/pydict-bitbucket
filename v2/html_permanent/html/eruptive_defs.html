eruptive

(ɪˈrʌptɪv )
Definitions
adjective1. erupting or tending to erupt2. resembling or of the nature of an eruption3. (of rocks) formed from such products as ash and lava resulting from volcanic eruptions4. (of a disease) characterized by skin eruptions

Derived Forms

eruptively (eˈruptively) adverberuptivity (eˌrupˈtivity) or eruptiveness (eˈruptiveness) noun

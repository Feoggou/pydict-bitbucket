immortalize

or immortalise (ɪˈmɔːtəˌlaɪz )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to give everlasting fame to, as by treating in a literary work ⇒ Macbeth was immortalized by Shakespeare2. to give immortality to3. biology to cause (cells) to reproduce indefinitely
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

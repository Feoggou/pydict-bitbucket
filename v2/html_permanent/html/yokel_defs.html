yokel

(ˈjəʊkəl )
Definitions
nounderogatory (used chiefly by townspeople) a person who lives in the country, esp one who appears to be simple and old-fashioned
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

yokelish (ˈyokelish) adjective

Word Origin

C19: perhaps from dialect yokel green woodpecker, yellowhammer

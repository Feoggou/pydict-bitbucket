mortality

(mɔːˈtælɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the condition of being mortal2. great loss of life, as in war or disaster3. the number of deaths in a given period4. mankind; humanity5. an obsolete word for death

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft N n]The mortality in a particular place or situation is the number of people who die. ⇒ ...the relationships between mortality and unemployment. ⇒ The nation's infant mortality rate has reached a record low.

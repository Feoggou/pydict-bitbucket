surly

(ˈsɜːlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -lier or -liest1. sullenly ill-tempered or rude2. (of an animal) ill-tempered or refractory3. dismal4. obsolete arrogant

Derived Forms

surlily (ˈsurlily) adverbsurliness (ˈsurliness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from obsolete sirly haughty; see sir

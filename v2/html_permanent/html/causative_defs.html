causative

(ˈkɔːzətɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. grammar relating to a form or class of verbs, such as persuade, that express causation2. (often postpositive; and foll by of) producing an effectnoun3. the causative form or class of verbs
Derived forms
causatively (ˈcausatively) adverbcausativeness (ˈcausativeness) noun

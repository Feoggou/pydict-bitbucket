Definitions
adjectivequiet, inactive, or dormant

Derived Forms

Word Origin

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone or something that is quiescent is quiet and inactive. [literary] ⇒ ...a society which was politically quiescent and above all deferential. ⇒ ...a quiescent Southern seaside town.quiescence uncount noun ⇒ ...a long period of quiescence.

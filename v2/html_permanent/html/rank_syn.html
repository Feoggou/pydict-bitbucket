Synonyms of rank 1

rank1

noun

  1. status, level, position, grade, order, standing, sort, quality, type, station, division, degree, classification, echelon
    • He eventually rose to the rank of captain.
  2. class, dignity, caste, nobility, stratum
    • Each rank of the peerage was respected.
  3. row, line, file, column, group, range, series, formation, tier
    • Ranks of police in riot gear stood nervously by.

verb

  1. be graded, belong, be placed, be classified, be positioned, be classed, be categorized, have a status
    • He does not even rank in the world's top ten.
  2. order, class, grade, catalogue, classify, dispose, categorize
    • Universities were ranked according to marks scored in seven areas.
  3. arrange, sort, position, range, line up, locate, sequence, array, marshal, align
    • Daffodils were ranked along a crazy paving path.
  • See rank and file

rank2

adjective

  1. absolute, complete, total, gross, sheer, excessive, utter, glaring, thorough, extravagant, rampant, blatant, downright, flagrant, egregious, unmitigated, undisguised, arrant
    • He accused his rival of rank hypocrisy.
  2. foul, off, bad, offensive, disgusting, revolting, stinking, stale, pungent, noxious, disagreeable, musty, rancid, fetid, putrid, fusty, strong-smelling, gamey, noisome, mephitic, olid, yucky or yukky (slang), festy (Australian, slang)
    • the rank smell of unwashed clothes
  3. abundant, flourishing, lush, luxuriant, productive, vigorous, dense, exuberant, profuse, strong-growing
    • brambles and rank grass
  4. vulgar, shocking, gross, nasty, foul, outrageous, obscene, abusive, coarse, filthy, indecent, crass, atrocious, scurrilous

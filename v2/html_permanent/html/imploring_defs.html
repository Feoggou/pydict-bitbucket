imploring

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmplɔːrɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]An imploring look, cry, or letter shows that you very much want someone to do something and are afraid they may not do it. ⇒ Frank looked at Jim with imploring eyes.imploringly adverb [ADV after v] ⇒ Michael looked at him imploringly, eyes brimming with tears.

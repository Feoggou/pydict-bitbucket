hap1

(hæp )
Definitions
noun archaic1. luck; chance2. an occurrenceverbWord forms: haps, happing or happed3. (intransitive) an archaic word for happen
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old Norse happ good luck; related to Old English gehæplic convenient, Old Slavonic kobǔ fate

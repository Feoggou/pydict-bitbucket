(sɪmplɪfɪkeɪʃən )
Word forms: simplifications
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounYou can use simplification to refer to the thing that is produced when you make something simpler or when you reduce it to its basic elements. ⇒ Like any such diagram, it is a simplification.2. uncount nounSimplification is the act or process of making something simpler. [+ of] ⇒ Everyone favours the simplification of court procedures.
Example sentences containing 'simplification'
Sloan let this simplification of what must have been a very complicated chemical process pass. Aird, Catherine A DEAD LIBERTY

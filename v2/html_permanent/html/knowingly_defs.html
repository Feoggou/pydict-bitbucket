knowingly for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(noʊɪŋli )
Definitions
adverb [ADV before v]If you knowingly do something wrong, you do it even though you know it is wrong. ⇒ He repeated that he had never knowingly taken illegal drugs.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
plural noun [oft with poss]You can refer to the members of a particular organization or group, especially a religious group, as brethren. [old-fashioned] ⇒ We must help our brethren, it is our duty. ⇒ Sri Lankans share a common ancestry with their Indian brethren.

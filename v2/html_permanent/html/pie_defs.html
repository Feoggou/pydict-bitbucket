Definitions

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounA pie consists of meat, vegetables, or fruit baked in pastry. ⇒ ...a pork pie. ⇒ ...apple pie and custard.2. → see also cottage pie, shepherd's pie3. →  pie in the sky4. → to eat humble pie

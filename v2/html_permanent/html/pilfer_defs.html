Definitions

verbto steal (minor items), esp in small quantities

Derived Forms

pilferer (ˈpilferer) nounpilfering (ˈpilfering) noun

Word Origin

C14 pylfre (n) from Old French pelfre booty; see pelf

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf someone pilfers, they steal things, usually small cheap things. [V] ⇒ Staff were pilfering behind the bar. [V n] ⇒ When food stores close, they go to work, pilfering food for resale on the black market.pilfering uncount noun ⇒ Precautions had to be taken to prevent pilfering.

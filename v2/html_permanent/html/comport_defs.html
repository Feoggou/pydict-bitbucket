comport

(kəmˈpɔːt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (transitive) to conduct or bear (oneself) in a specified way2. (intransitive; foll by with) to agree (with); correspond (to)
Word origin
C16: from Latin comportāre to bear, collect, from com- together + portāre to carry

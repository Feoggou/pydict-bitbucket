America

(əˈmɛrɪkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. short for the United States of America2. Also called: the Americas the American continent, including North, South, and Central America
Word origin
C16: from Americus, Latin form of Amerigo; after Amerigo Vespucci

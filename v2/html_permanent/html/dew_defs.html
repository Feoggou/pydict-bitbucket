dew

(djuː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. drops of water condensed on a cool surface, esp at night, from vapour in the airb. (in combination) ⇒ dewdrop2. something like or suggestive of this, esp in freshness ⇒ the dew of youth3. small drops of moisture, such as tearsverb4. (transitive) poetic to moisten with or as with dew
Word origin
Old English dēaw; related to Old High German tou dew, Old Norse dögg

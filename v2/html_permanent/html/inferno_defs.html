inferno

(ɪnˈfɜːnəʊ )
Definitions
noun plural -nos1. See the inferno2. any place or state resembling hell, esp a conflagration
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from Italian, from Late Latin infernus hell

inferno

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnfɜːʳnoʊ ) for learners of English
Word forms: infernos
Definitions
countable noun [usu sing]If you refer to a fire as an inferno, you mean that it is burning fiercely and causing great destruction. [journalism] ⇒ Rescue workers fought to get to victims inside the inferno.

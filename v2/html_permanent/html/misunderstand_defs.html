Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. verbIf you misunderstand someone or something, you do not understand them properly. [V wh] ⇒ They have simply misunderstood what rock and roll is. [V n] ⇒ Maybe I misunderstood you.2. → see also misunderstood3. →  don't misunderstand me

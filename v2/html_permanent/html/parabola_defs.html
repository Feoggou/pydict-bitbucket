parabola

(pəˈræbələ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna conic section formed by the intersection of a cone by a plane parallel to its side. Standard equation: y2 = 4ax, where 2a is the distance between focus and directrix

Word Origin

C16: via New Latin from Greek parabolē a setting alongside; see parable

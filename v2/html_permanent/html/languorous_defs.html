Definitions

languorous

Powered by COBUILD
(læŋgərəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe an activity as languorous, you mean that it is lazy, relaxed, and not energetic, usually in a pleasant way. [literary] ⇒ ...languorous morning coffees on the terrace.

bucolic

(bjuːˈkɒlɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective also: bucolical1. of or characteristic of the countryside or country life; rustic2. of or relating to shepherds; pastoralnoun3. (sometimes plural) a pastoral poem, often in the form of a dialogue4. a rustic; farmer or shepherd
Derived forms
bucolically (buˈcolically) adverb
Word origin
C16: from Latin būcolicus, from Greek boukolikos, from boukolos cowherd, from bous ox

viola1

(vɪˈəʊlə )
Definitions
noun1. a bowed stringed instrument, the alto of the violin family; held beneath the chin when played. It is pitched and tuned an octave above the cello2. any of various instruments of the viol family, such as the viola da gamba
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Italian viola, probably from Old Provençal viola, of uncertain origin; perhaps related to Latin vītulārī to rejoice

viola2

(ˈvaɪələ , vaɪˈəʊ-)
Definitions
nounany temperate perennial herbaceous plant of the violaceous genus Viola, the flowers of which have showy irregular petals, white, yellow, blue, or mauve in colour. See also violet (sense 1), pansy (sense 1)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Latin: violet

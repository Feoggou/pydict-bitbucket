mortification

(ˌmɔːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a feeling of loss of prestige or self-respect; humiliation2. something causing this3. Christianity the practice of mortifying the senses4. another word for gangrene

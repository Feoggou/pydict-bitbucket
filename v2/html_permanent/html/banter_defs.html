Powered by COBUILD
(bæntəʳ )
Word forms: banters , bantering , bantered
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounBanter is teasing or joking talk that is amusing and friendly. badinage ⇒ She heard Tom exchanging good-natured banter with Jane.2. verbIf you banter with someone, you tease them or joke with them in an amusing, friendly way. [V + with] ⇒ The soldiers bantered with him as though he was a kid brother. [V] ⇒ We bantered a bit while I tried to get the car started. [V-ing] ⇒ All this was said in a bantering tone.

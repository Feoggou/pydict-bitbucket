learning

(ˈlɜːnɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. knowledge gained by study; instruction or scholarship2. the act of gaining knowledge3. psychology any relatively permanent change in behaviour that occurs as a direct result of experience
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions

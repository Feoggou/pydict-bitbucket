Definitions

nounshort for bon mot

Word Origin

MOT

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
abbreviation for1. (in New Zealand and formerly in Britain) Ministry of Transport (in Britain now part of the DTLR). See DTLR2. (in Britain) MOT test: a compulsory annual test for all road vehicles over a certain age, which require a valid MOT certificate

(em oʊ tiː )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIn Britain, an MOT is a test which, by law, must be made each year on all road vehicles that are more than 3 years old, in order to check that they are safe to drive. ⇒ My car is due for its MOT in two days' time.

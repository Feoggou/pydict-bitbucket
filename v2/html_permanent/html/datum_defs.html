datum

(ˈdeɪtəm , ˈdɑːtəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ta (-tə)1. a single piece of information; fact2. a proposition taken for granted, often in order to construct some theoretical framework upon it; a given. See also sense datum
Word origin
C17: from Latin: something given; see data

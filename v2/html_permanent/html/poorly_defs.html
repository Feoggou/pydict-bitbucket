poorly

(ˈpʊəlɪ , ˈpɔː-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb1. in a poor way or manner; badlyadjective2. (usually postpositive) informal in poor health; rather ill ⇒ she's poorly today

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If someone is poorly, they are ill. [mainly British, informal] ⇒ I've just phoned Julie and she's still poorly.regional note:   in AM, use sick2. → see also poor

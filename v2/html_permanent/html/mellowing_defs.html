mellowing

(ˈmɛləʊɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. softening and maturing ⇒ Long slow cooking has a mellowing effect., ⇒ It has a mellowing, softening effect on sharper flavours., ⇒ It is this that produces the mellowing and oxidative characters of the maturing process., ⇒ Jack Daniel's special charcoal mellowing process is what makes it a Tennessee Whiskey instead of a bourbon.2. having a softening effect in terms of colour ⇒ If you can't wait for the brickwork to age naturally, there are ways of speeding up the mellowing process.3. having a softening effect on a person's character or behaviour ⇒ the mellowing effects of age, ⇒ I suspect that it has had some mellowing effect on my behaviour., ⇒ She has a mellowing influence on me.

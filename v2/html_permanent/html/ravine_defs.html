ravine

(rəˈviːn )
Definitions
nouna deep narrow steep-sided valley, esp one formed by the action of running water
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French: torrent, from Latin rapīna robbery, influenced by Latin rapidus rapid, both from rapere to snatch

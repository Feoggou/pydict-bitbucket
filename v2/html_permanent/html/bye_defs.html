bye1

(baɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. sport the situation in which a player or team in an eliminatory contest wins a preliminary round by virtue of having no opponent2. golf one or more holes of a stipulated course that are left unplayed after the match has been decided3. cricket a run scored off a ball not struck by the batsman: allotted to the team as an extra and not to the individual batsman. See also leg bye4. something incidental or secondary5. See by the bye
Word origin
C16: a variant of by

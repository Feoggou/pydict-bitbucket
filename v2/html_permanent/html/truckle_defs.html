truckle1

(ˈtrʌkəl )
Definitions
verb(intransitive; usually foll by to) to yield weakly; give in
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

truckler (ˈtruckler) noun

Word Origin

C17: from obsolete truckle to sleep in a truckle bed; see truckle2

truckle2

(ˈtrʌkəl )
Definitions
noun1. a small wheel; caster2. a small barrel-shaped cheeseverb3. (intransitive) to roll on truckles4. (transitive) to push (a piece of furniture) along on truckles
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15 trokel, from Anglo-Norman trocle, from Latin trochlea sheave of a pulley; see trochlea
Example sentences containing 'truckle'
There were three blankets upon three raised beds---one large and old, the other two little more than truckle beds. Neil Gaiman STARDUST

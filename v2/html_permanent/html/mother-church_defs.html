Mother Church

(ˈmʌðə tʃɜːtʃ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the church viewed in its protective and nurturing role ⇒ before we go running back into the arms of mother church2. a the main church of a city, region, or country ⇒ The cathedral is the mother church of the Diocese of Clifton.
Example sentences containing 'Mother Church'
`So no courtier or King," he brayed, `will ever again lay their hands on a Bishop or the riches of Holy Mother Church. Grace, C.L A SHRINE OF MURDERS

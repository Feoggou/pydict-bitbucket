an1

(æn , unstressed ən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
determinera form of the indefinite article used before an initial vowel sound ⇒ an old car, ⇒ an elf, ⇒ an honour▶ USAGE An was formerly often used before words that begin with h and are unstressed on the first syllable: an hotel; an historic meeting. Sometimes the initial h was not pronounced. This usage is now becoming obsolete

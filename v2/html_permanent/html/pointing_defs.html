pointing

(ˈpɔɪntɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or process of repairing or finishing joints in brickwork, masonry, etc, with mortar2. a. the insertion of marks to indicate the chanting of a psalm or the vowels in a Hebrew textb. the sequence of marks so inserted

pointing

(pɔɪntɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounPointing is a way of filling in the gaps between the bricks or stones on the outside of a building so that the surface becomes sealed. ⇒ He did the pointing in the stonework himself.2. uncount nounPointing is the cement between the bricks or stones in a wall. ⇒ Cracks, bulges, crumbling pointing and damp patches mean trouble.

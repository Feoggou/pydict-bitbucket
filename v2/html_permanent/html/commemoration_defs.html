commemoration

(kəˌmɛməˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or an instance of commemorating2. a ceremony or service in memory of a person or event
Derived forms
comˌmemorational (comˌmemoˈrational) adjective
Example sentences containing 'commemoration'
There was Nazi graffiti everywhere, though the official commemoration was designed to display quite the opposite. Mosco, Maisie OUT OF THE ASHESIn the New Reckoning the year began on March 25 old style, in commemoration of the fall of Sauron and the deeds of the Ring-bearers. J.R.R.

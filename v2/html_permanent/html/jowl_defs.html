jowl1

(dʒaʊl )
Definitions
noun1. the jaw, esp the lower one2. (often plural) a cheek, esp a prominent one3. See cheek by jowl
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

jowled adjective

Word Origin

Old English ceafl jaw; related to Middle High German kivel, Old Norse kjaptr

jowl2

(dʒaʊl )
Definitions
noun1. fatty flesh hanging from the lower jaw2. a similar fleshy part in animals, such as the wattle of a fowl or the dewlap of a bull
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English ceole throat; compare Old High German kela

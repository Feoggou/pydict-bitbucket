pleasantry

(ˈplɛzəntrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ries1. (often plural) an agreeable or amusing remark, often one made in order to be polite ⇒ they exchanged pleasantries2. an agreeably humorous manner or style3. rare enjoyment; pleasantness ⇒ a pleasantry of life

Word Origin

C17: from French plaisanterie, from plaisant pleasant

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu pl]Pleasantries are casual, friendly remarks which you make in order to be polite. [+ about] ⇒ He exchanged pleasantries about his hotel and the weather.

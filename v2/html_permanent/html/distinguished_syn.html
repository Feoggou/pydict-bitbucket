distinguished

adjective

  1. eminent, great, important, noted, famous, celebrated, well-known, prominent, esteemed, acclaimed, notable, renowned, prestigious, elevated, big-time (informal), famed, conspicuous, illustrious, major league (informal) • a distinguished academic family OPPOSITES:  common, unknown, inferior, undistinguished
  2. marked, striking, signal, extraordinary, outstanding, conspicuous • a distinguished gentleman

distinguish

verb

  1. differentiate, determine, separate, discriminate, decide, judge, discern, ascertain, tell the difference, make a distinction, tell apart, tell between • Could he distinguish right from wrong?
  2. characterize, mark, separate, single out, individualize, set apart, make different, make distinctive • one of the things that distinguishes artists from other people
  3. make out, identify, recognize, perceive, know, see, tell, spot, glimpse, pick out, discern • He could distinguish voices.
  4. make famous, celebrate, honour, dignify, immortalize, signalize
  • See distinguish yourself

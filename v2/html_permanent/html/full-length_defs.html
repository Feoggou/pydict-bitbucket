full-length

Definitions
noun (modifier)1. extending to or showing the complete length ⇒ a full-length mirror2. of the original length; not abridged

full-length

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]A full-length book, record, or film is the normal length, rather than being shorter than normal. ⇒ ...his first full-length recording in well over a decade.2. adjective [ADJ n]A full-length coat or skirt is long enough to reach the lower part of a person's leg, almost to the ankles. A full-length sleeve reaches a person's wrist.3. adjective [ADJ n]Full-length curtains or other furnishings reach to the floor.4. adjective [ADJ n]A full-length mirror or painting shows the whole of a person.5. adverb [ADV after v]Someone who is lying full-length, is lying down flat and stretched out. ⇒ She stretched herself out full-length.

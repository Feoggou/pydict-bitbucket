curd

(kɜːd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (often plural) a substance formed from the coagulation of milk by acid or rennet, used in making cheese or eaten as a food2. something similar in consistencyverb3. to turn into or become curd
Derived forms
curdy (ˈcurdy) adjectivecurdiness (ˈcurdiness) noun
Word origin
C15: from earlier crud, of unknown origin

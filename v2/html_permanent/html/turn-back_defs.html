turn back

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. ergative phrasal verb & phrasal verbIf you turn back or if someone turns you back when you are going somewhere, you change direction and go towards where you started from. [V P prep/adv] ⇒ She turned back towards the crossroads. [V P] ⇒ They were very nearly forced to turn back. [V P n] ⇒ Police attempted to turn back protesters marching towards the offices of President Ershad. [Also V n P]2. phrasal verbIf you cannot turn back, you cannot change your plans and decide not to do something, because the action you have already taken makes it impossible. [V P] ⇒ The administration has now endorsed the bill and can't turn back. →  turn

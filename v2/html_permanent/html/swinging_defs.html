swinging

(ˈswɪŋɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. moving rhythmically to and fro2. slang modern and livelynoun3. slang the practice of swapping sexual partners in a group, esp habitually
Derived forms

(swɪŋɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something or someone as swinging, you mean that they are lively and fashionable. [informal, old-fashioned] ⇒ The stuffy '50s gave way to the swinging '60s. ⇒ ...a young student in mid-1960s 'swinging London'.

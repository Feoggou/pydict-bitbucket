emergency exit

(ɪˈmɜːdʒənsɪ ˈɛksɪt )
Definitions
nounan doorway out of a building that is designated for use in an emergency ⇒ The steward showed us the emergency exits., ⇒ They escaped through an emergency exit and called the police.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

wroth

(rəʊθ , rɒθ )
Definitions
adjectivearchaic or literary angry; irate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English wrāth; related to Old Saxon wrēth, Old Norse reithr, Old High German reid curly haired

hissing

(ˈhɪsɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. another word for hiss (sense 1), hiss (sense 2) ⇒ a silence broken only by a steady hissing from above my headadjective2. producing, uttering or accompanied by a hiss ⇒ Caporelli made a small hissing sound of irritation., ⇒ a hissing noise of escaping gas

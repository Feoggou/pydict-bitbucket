tribulation

(ˌtrɪbjʊˈleɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a cause of distress2. a state of suffering or distress
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old French, from Church Latin trībulātiō, from Latin trībulāre to afflict, from trībulum a threshing board, from terere to rub

hairy

Powered by COBUILD
(heəri ) for learners of English
Word forms: hairier , hairiest
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone or something that is hairy is covered with hairs. OPPOSITE: hairless ⇒ He was wearing shorts which showed his long, muscular, hairy legs. ⇒ The plant has serrated and hairy leaves.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a situation as hairy, you mean that it is exciting, worrying, and rather frightening. [informal] hair-raising ⇒ His driving was a bit hairy.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

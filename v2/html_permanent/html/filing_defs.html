filing

(ˈfaɪlɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the work of filing documents2. US something that is filed3. US a submission to a court or other authority ⇒ In court filings, they argued that the settlement was inadequate., ⇒ The warning was contained in a filing to the Securities and Exchange Commission.

enlighten

(ɪnˈlaɪtən )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to give information or understanding to; instruct; edify2. to free from ignorance, prejudice, or superstition3. to give spiritual or religious revelation to4. poetic to shed light on
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

enlightener (enˈlightener) nounenlightening (enˈlightening) adjective

Definitions
verb [no cont]To enlighten someone means to give them more knowledge and greater understanding about something. [formal] [V n] ⇒ A few dedicated doctors have fought for years to enlighten the profession. [V n] ⇒ If you know what is wrong with her, please enlighten me.enlightening graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n] ⇒ ...an enlightening talk on the work done at the animal park. ⇒ It can be an enormously enlightening and exciting experience.

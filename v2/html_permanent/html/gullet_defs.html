gullet

(ˈɡʌlɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a less formal name for the oesophagus ▶ Related adjective: oesophageal2. the throat or pharynx3. mining, quarrying a preliminary cut in excavating, wide enough to take the vehicle that removes the earth
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French goulet, diminutive of goule throat, from Latin gula throat

male

(meɪl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or designating the sex producing gametes (spermatozoa) that can fertilize female gametes (ova)2. of, relating to, or characteristic of a man; masculine3. for or composed of men or boys ⇒ a male choir4. (of gametes) capable of fertilizing an egg cell in sexual reproduction5. (of reproductive organs, such as a testis or stamen) capable of producing male gametes6. (of flowers) bearing stamens but lacking a functional pistil7. electronics, mechanical engineering having a projecting part or parts that fit into a female counterpart ⇒ a male plugnoun8. a male person, animal, or plant

Derived Forms

maleness (ˈmaleness) noun

Word Origin

C14: via Old French from Latin masculus masculine

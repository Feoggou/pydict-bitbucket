checkered

(ˈtʃɛkərd )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
adjective1.  having a pattern of squares2.  varied by the use of color and shading3.  marked by diversified features or by varied events, some unpleasant ⇒ a checkered career

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced American English Dictionary
regional note:   in BRIT, usually use chequered1. adjective [usu ADJ n]If a person or organization has had a checkered career or history, they have had a varied past with both good and bad periods. ⇒ Zenker, for his part, regarded Raeder's checkered political career as a distinct liability for a future chief of the navy.2. adjective [ADJ n]Something that is checkered has a pattern with squares of two or more different colors. ⇒ ...red checkered tablecloths.

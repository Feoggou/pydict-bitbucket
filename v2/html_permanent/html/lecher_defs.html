lecher

(ˈlɛtʃə )
Definitions
nouna promiscuous or lewd man
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C12: from Old French lecheor lecher, from lechier to lick, of Germanic origin; compare Old High German leccōn to lick

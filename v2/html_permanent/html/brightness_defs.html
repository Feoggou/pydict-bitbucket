brightness

(ˈbraɪtnɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the condition of being bright2. physics a former name for luminosity (sense 4)3. psychology the experienced intensity of light
Example sentences containing 'brightness'
There were no wall paintings, no food, no tiger brightness of light. Curzon, Clare THE QUEST FOR KKenworthy looked at him with exaggerated brightness , waiting for him to start talking. Hilton, John Buxton MOONDROP TO MURDERThe brightness of the sun smote her eyes; her head throbbed unbearably. Gaskin, Catherine THE AMBASSADOR'S WOMENHe was boarded out at a variety of schools where his natural brightness overwhelmed all around him. Armstrong, Campbell MAMBO

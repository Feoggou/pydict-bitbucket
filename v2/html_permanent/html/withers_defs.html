withers

(ˈwɪðəz )
Definitions
plural nounthe highest part of the back of a horse, behind the neck between the shoulders
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: short for widersones, from wider with + -sones, perhaps variant of sinew; related to German Widerrist, Old English withre resistance
Example sentences containing 'withers'
She says she withers if she doesn't get the light -- like a rose. Val McDermid DEAD BEATThe eyes of the cavalry soldiers bent over their withers showed grim determination. Terry Goodkind NAKED EMPIRE

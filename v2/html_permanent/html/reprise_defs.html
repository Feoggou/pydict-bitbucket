reprise

(rɪˈpriːz ) music
Definitions
noun1. the repeating of an earlier themeverb2. to repeat (an earlier theme)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French, from reprendre to take back, from Latin reprehendere; see reprehend

reprise

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪpriːz ) for learners of English
Word forms: reprises , reprising , reprised
Definitions
1. countable nounIn music, if there is a reprise, an earlier section of music is repeated.2. verbIf someone reprises a role or a song, they play or sing it again. [written] [V n] ⇒ He then proceeded to play for more than two hours, reprising every one of his hits.

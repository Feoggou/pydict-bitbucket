new face

(njuː feɪs)
Definitions
noun1. a newcomer, a person who has recently joined an organization, a person whom you have not seen before ⇒ The most exciting of the new faces is probably Matt Haize., ⇒ It's nice to see a few new faces in the cast., ⇒ Every year the town grew and new faces appeared., ⇒ There are six new faces in the cabinet.2. advertising a new person presenting an established product in an advertising campaign, especially for a cosmetic, hair product etc ⇒ I'm going to be the new face of Stay Sleek., ⇒ She has recently signed up to be the new face of fur giant Grizliglama

behove

(bɪˈhəʊv ) or US behoove (bɪˈhuːv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(tr; impersonal) archaic to be necessary or fitting for ⇒ it behoves me to arrest you
Word origin
Old English behōfian; related to Middle Low German behōven

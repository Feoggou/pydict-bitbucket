barberry

(ˈbɑːbərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ries1. any spiny berberidaceous shrub of the widely distributed genus Berberis, esp B. vulgaris, having clusters of yellow flowers and orange or red berries: widely cultivated as hedge plants2. the fruit of any of these plants
Word origin
C15: from Old French berberis, from Arabic barbāris

twofold

(ˈtuːˌfəʊld )
Definitions
adjective1. equal to twice as many or twice as much; double ⇒ a twofold increase2. made of two parts; dual ⇒ a twofold reasonadverb3. doubly
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

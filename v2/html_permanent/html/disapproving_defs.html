disapproving

Powered by COBUILD
(dɪsəpruːvɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveA disapproving action or expression shows that you do not approve of something or someone. ⇒ Janet gave him a disapproving look.disapprovingly graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v] ⇒ Antonio looked at him disapprovingly.

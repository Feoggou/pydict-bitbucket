Definitions
Collins English Dictionary

(əpiːs )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adverbIf people have a particular number of things apiece, they have that number each. ⇒ He and I had two fish apiece. ⇒ The World Series between the Atlanta Braves and Toronto Blue Jays is tied at one game apiece.2. adverbIf a number of similar things are for sale at a certain price apiece, that is the price for each one of them. ⇒ Entire roast chickens were sixty cents apiece.

nugatory

(ˈnjuːɡətərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
adjective1. of little value; trifling2. not valid ⇒ a nugatory law
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin nūgātōrius, from nūgārī to jest, from nūgae trifles

Similar Words

invalid, ineffectual, null and void, inoperative, useless, vain, futile, unavailing, bootless
trivial, worthless, trifling, insignificant, valueless

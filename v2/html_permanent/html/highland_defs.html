Highland

(ˈhaɪlənd )
Definitions
noun1. a council area in N Scotland, formed in 1975 (as Highland Region) from Caithness, Sutherland, Nairnshire, most of Inverness-shire, and Ross and Cromarty except for the Outer Hebrides. Administrative centre: Inverness. Pop: 209 080 (2003 est). Area: 25 149 sq km (9710 sq miles)2. (modifier) of, relating to, or denoting the Highlands of Scotland
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

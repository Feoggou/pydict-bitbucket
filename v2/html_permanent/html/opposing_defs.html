opposing for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(əpoʊzɪŋ )
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Opposing ideas or tendencies are totally different from each other. ⇒ I have a friend who has the opposing view and felt that the war was immoral. ⇒ Water is the opposing force to fire.2. adjective [ADJ n]Opposing groups of people disagree about something or are in competition with one another. ⇒ The Georgian leader said in a radio address that he still favoured dialogue between the opposing sides. ⇒ The opposing team must in turn try to keep the ball in the air before hitting it back over the net.

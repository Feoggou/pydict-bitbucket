probate

(ˈprəʊbɪt , -beɪt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or process of officially proving the authenticity and validity of a will2. a. the official certificate stating a will to be genuine and conferring on the executors power to administer the estateb. the probate copy of a will3. (in the US) all matters within the jurisdiction of a probate court4. (modifier) of, relating to, or concerned with probate ⇒ probate value, ⇒ a probate courtverb5. (transitive) mainly US and Canadian to establish officially the authenticity and validity of (a will)

Word Origin

C15: from Latin probāre to inspect

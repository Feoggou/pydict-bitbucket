(ædmɪnɪstrətɪv , US -streɪt- )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Administrative work involves organizing and supervising an organization or institution. ⇒ Other industries have had to sack managers to reduce administrative costs. ⇒ The project will have an administrative staff of 12.administratively adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj, ADV with cl] ⇒ Cuba is politically and administratively divided into 14 provinces.

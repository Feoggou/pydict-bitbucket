denominate

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (dɪˈnɒmɪˌneɪt )1. (transitive) to give a specific name to; designateadjective (dɪˈnɒmɪnɪt , -ˌneɪt)2. mathematics (of a number) representing a multiple of a unit of measurement ⇒ 4 is the denominate number in 4 miles
Derived forms
denominable (deˈnominable) adjective
Word origin
C16: from de- + Latin nōmināre to call by name; see nominate

Similar Words

name, call, term, style, entitle, phrase, dub, designate, christen

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If something is chargeable, you have to pay a sum of money for it. [formal] ⇒ The day of departure is not chargeable if rooms are vacated by 12.00 noon.2. adjectiveIf something is chargeable, you have to pay tax on it. [formal] ⇒ ...the taxpayer's chargeable gain.

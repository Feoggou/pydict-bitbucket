cleansing

(ˈklɛnzɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. serving or intended to cleanse ⇒ Harsh cleansing products can irritate your face., ⇒ They had to perform the cleansing rite on the bodiesnoun2. an instance of cleaning something, esp its surface. See also ethnic cleansing

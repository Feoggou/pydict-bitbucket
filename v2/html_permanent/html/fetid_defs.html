fetid

or foetid (ˈfɛtɪd , ˈfiː-)
Definitions
adjectivehaving a stale nauseating smell, as of decay
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fetidly (ˈfetidly) or foetidly (ˈfoetidly) adverbfetidness (ˈfetidness) or foetidness (ˈfoetidness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin fētidus, from fētēre to stink; related to fūmus smoke

meretricious

(ˌmɛrɪˈtrɪʃəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. superficially or garishly attractive2. insincere ⇒ meretricious praise3. archaic of, like, or relating to a prostitute

Derived Forms

ˌmeretriciously (ˌmereˈtriciously) adverbˌmeretriciousness (ˌmereˈtriciousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin merētrīcius, from merētrix prostitute, from merēre to earn money

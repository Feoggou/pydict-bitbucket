inflammable

(ɪnˈflæməbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. liable to catch fire; flammable2. readily aroused to anger or passionnoun3. something that is liable to catch fireSee note at flammable
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inflammability (inˌflammaˈbility) or inflammableness (inˈflammableness) nouninflammably (inˈflammably) adverb

Anatolia

(ˌænəˈtəʊlɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe Asian part of Turkey, occupying the peninsula between the Black Sea, the Mediterranean, and the Aegean: consists of a plateau, largely mountainous, with salt lakes in the interior. Historical name: Asia Minor

infected

(ɪnˈfɛktɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. having a communicable disease ⇒ objects used by an infected person, ⇒ a system to track infected animals, ⇒ Scientists believe she became infected in Africa., ⇒ As many as 15 million Americans are now infected with the tuberculosis bacterium., ⇒ people who are infected with HIV2. contaminated with pathogenic microorganisms ⇒ Wounds were infected and amputations were common., ⇒ a virus which is spread mainly by infected blood, ⇒ In tropical climates any small cut can quickly become infected.3. used to describe a place where pathogenic microorganisms are causing a disease to spread among people, animals, or plants ⇒ Pending tests, thousands more chickens within a 16-kilometre radius of the infected farm were quarantined., ⇒ In heavily infected areas, half the population become blind.

abrasive

(əˈbreɪsɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a substance or material such as sandpaper, pumice, or emery, used for cleaning, grinding, smoothing, or polishingadjective2. causing abrasion; grating; rough3. irritating in manner or personality; causing tension or annoyance
Derived forms
aˈbrasiveness noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

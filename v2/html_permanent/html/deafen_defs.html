Word forms: deafens , deafening , deafened
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. verbIf a noise deafens you, it is so loud that you cannot hear anything else at the same time. [V n] ⇒ The noise of the typewriters deafened her.2. verb [usu passive]If you are deafened by something, you are made deaf by it, or are unable to hear for some time. [be V-ed] ⇒ He was deafened by the noise from the gun.3. → see also deafening

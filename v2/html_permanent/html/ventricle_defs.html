ventricle

(ˈvɛntrɪkəl ) or ventricule (ˈvɛntrɪkjuːl)
Definitions
noun anatomy1. a chamber of the heart, having thick muscular walls, that receives blood from the atrium and pumps it to the arteries2. any one of the four main cavities of the vertebrate brain, which contain cerebrospinal fluid3. any of various other small cavities in the body
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin ventriculus, diminutive of venter belly

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable noun [oft at N]If something is sold at cost price, it is sold for the same price as it cost the seller to buy it. [British] ⇒ ...a factory shop where you can buy very fashionable shoes at cost price. ⇒ The shop claims to have sold computers below the manufacturer's cost price for three years.

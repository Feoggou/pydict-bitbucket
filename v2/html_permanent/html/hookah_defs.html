hookah

or hooka (ˈhʊkə )
Definitions
nounan oriental pipe for smoking marijuana, tobacco, etc, consisting of one or more long flexible stems connected to a container of water or other liquid through which smoke is drawn and cooled. Also called: hubble-bubble, kalian, narghile, water pipe
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Arabic huqqah

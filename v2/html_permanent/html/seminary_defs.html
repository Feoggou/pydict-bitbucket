seminary

(ˈsɛmɪnərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -naries1. an academy for the training of priests, rabbis, etc2. US another word for seminar (sense 1)3. a place where something is grown

Derived Forms

ˌseminarial (ˌsemiˈnarial) adjective

Word Origin

C15: from Latin sēminārium a nursery garden, from sēmen seed

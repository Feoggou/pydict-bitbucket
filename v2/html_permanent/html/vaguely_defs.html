vaguely

Powered by COBUILD
(veɪgli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj]Vaguely means to some degree but not to a very large degree. ⇒ The voice on the line was vaguely familiar, but Crook couldn't place it at first. ⇒ Arnold felt vaguely embarrassed. ⇒ Most farm workers were only vaguely aware that there was a storm on its way.2. → See also vague

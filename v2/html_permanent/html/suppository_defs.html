suppository

(səˈpɒzɪtərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -riesmedicine an encapsulated or solid medication for insertion into the vagina, rectum, or urethra, where it melts and releases the active substance

Word Origin

C14: from Medieval Latin suppositōrium, from Latin suppositus placed beneath, from suppōnere; see suppose

descended

(dɪˈsɛndɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(foll by from) connected by a blood relationship (to a dead or extinct individual, race, species, etc) ⇒  descended from some Scottish lord, ⇒ Caroline's family were descended from a French family., ⇒ He is descended from a family of Jewish immigrants., ⇒ Domestic chickens are descended from jungle fowl of Southeast Asia.

descended

Powered by COBUILD
(dɪsendɪd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveA person who is descended from someone who lived a long time ago is directly related to them. [+ from] ⇒ She told us she was descended from some Scottish Lord.2. adjectiveAn animal that is descended from another sort of animal has developed from the original sort. ⇒ Domestic chickens are descended from jungle fowl of Southeast Asia.

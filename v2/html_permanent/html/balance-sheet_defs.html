Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA balance sheet is a written statement of the amount of money and property that a company or person has, including amounts of money that are owed or are owing. Balance sheet is also used to refer to the general financial state of a company. [business] ⇒ Rolls-Royce needed a strong balance sheet.

innovation

(ˌɪnəˈveɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. something newly introduced, such as a new method or device2. the act of innovating

Definitions
1. countable nounAn innovation is a new thing or a new method of doing something. ⇒ The vegetarian burger was an innovation which was rapidly exported to Britain. ⇒ ...the transformation wrought by the technological innovations of the industrial age.2. uncount nounInnovation is the introduction of new ideas, methods, or things. ⇒ We must promote originality and encourage innovation.

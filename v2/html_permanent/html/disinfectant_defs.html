Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounDisinfectant is a substance that kills germs. It is used, for example, for cleaning kitchens and bathrooms. ⇒ Effluent from the sedimentation tank is dosed with disinfectant to kill any harmful organisms. ⇒ Salt is a natural disinfectant.

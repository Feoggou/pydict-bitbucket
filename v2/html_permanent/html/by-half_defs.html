by half

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrase [PHR after v]If you increase something by half, half of the original amount is added to it. If you decrease it by half, half of the original amount is taken away from it. ⇒ The number of 7 year olds who read poorly has increased by half over the past 5 years. ⇒ Cutting food intake by half is an incredibly difficult thing for anyone to do. →  half

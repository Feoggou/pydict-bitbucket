Swiss

(swɪs )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Switzerland, its inhabitants, or their dialects of German, French, and Italiannoun2. a native, inhabitant, or citizen of Switzerland

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveSwiss means belonging or relating to Switzerland, or to its people or culture. ⇒ ...Swiss cheese. ⇒ ...an avalanche in the Swiss alps.2. countable noun [usu pl]The Swiss are the people of Switzerland. ⇒ The report shows the Swiss are among the world's top wage earners.

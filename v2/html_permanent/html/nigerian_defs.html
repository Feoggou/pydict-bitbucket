Nigerian

(naɪˈdʒɪərɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to Nigeria or its inhabitantsnoun2. a native or inhabitant of Nigeria

Nigerian for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(naɪdʒɪəriən )
Word forms: Nigerians
Definitions
1. adjectiveNigerian means belonging or relating to Nigeria, its people, or its culture. ⇒ ...the Nigerian government. ⇒ ...a Nigerian accent.2. countable nounA Nigerian is a Nigerian citizen, or a person of Nigerian origin.

toughness

(ˈtʌfnɪs )
Definitions
noun1. the quality or an instance of being tough2. metallurgy the ability of a metal to withstand repeated twisting and bending, measured by the energy in kilojoules needed to break it. See brittleness (sense 2), softness (sense 2)

pinewood

(ˈpaɪnˌwʊd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a forest that consists mainly of pine trees ⇒ the hilly pinewoods of northeast Georgia2. a. the wood of the pine tree ⇒ furniture made from Italian pinewoodb. (as modifier) ⇒ a pinewood table

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

sixteen

(ˈsɪksˈtiːn )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the cardinal number that is the sum of ten and six. See also number (sense 1)2. a numeral, 16, XVI, etc, representing this number3. music the numeral 16 used as the lower figure of a time signature to indicate that the beat is measured in semiquavers4. something represented by, representing, or consisting of 16 unitsdeterminer5. a. amounting to sixteen ⇒ sixteen tonsb. (as pronoun) ⇒ sixteen are known to the police

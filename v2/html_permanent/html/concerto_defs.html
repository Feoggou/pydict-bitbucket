concerto

(kənˈtʃɛətəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -tos or -ti (-tɪ)1. a composition for an orchestra and one or more soloists. The classical concerto usually consisted of several movements, and often a cadenza. See also sonata (sense 1), symphony (sense 1)2. another word for ripieno
Word origin
C18: from Italian: concert

follicle

(ˈfɒlɪkəl )
Definitions
noun1. any small sac or cavity in the body having an excretory, secretory, or protective function ⇒ a hair follicle2. botany a dry fruit, formed from a single carpel, that splits along one side only to release its seeds: occurs in larkspur and columbine
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

follicular (fɒˈlɪkjʊlə ), folliculate (fɒˈlɪkjʊˌleɪt ) or folliculated (folˈlicuˌlated) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Latin folliculus small bag, from follis pair of bellows, leather money-bag

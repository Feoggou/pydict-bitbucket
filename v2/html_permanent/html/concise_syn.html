concise

adjective

  1. brief, short, to the point, compact, summary, compressed, condensed, terse, laconic, succinct, pithy, synoptic, epigrammatic, compendious • The text is concise and informative. OPPOSITES:  lengthy, rambling, long, diffuse, long-winded, wordy, garrulous, discursive, verbose, prolix

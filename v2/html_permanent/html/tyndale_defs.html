Tyndale

, Tindal or Tindale (ˈtɪndəl )
Definitions
noun William. ?1492–1536, English Protestant and humanist, who translated the New Testament (1525), the Pentateuch (1530), and the Book of Jonah (1531) into English. He was burnt at the stake as a heretic

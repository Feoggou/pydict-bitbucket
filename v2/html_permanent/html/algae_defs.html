algae

(ˈældʒiː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounWord forms: singular alga (ˈælɡə )unicellular or multicellular organisms formerly classified as plants, occurring in fresh or salt water or moist ground, that have chlorophyll and other pigments but lack true stems, roots, and leaves. Algae, which are now regarded as protoctists, include the seaweeds, diatoms, and spirogyra
Derived forms
algal (ˈælɡəl ) adjective
Word origin
C16: from Latin, plural of alga seaweed, of uncertain origin

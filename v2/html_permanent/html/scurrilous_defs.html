scurrilous

(ˈskʌrɪləs )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. grossly or obscenely abusive or defamatory2. characterized by gross or obscene humour

Derived Forms

scurrility (skəˈrɪlɪtɪ ) or scurrilousness (ˈscurrilousness) nounscurrilously (ˈscurrilously) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Latin scurrīlis derisive, from scurra buffoon

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Scurrilous accusations or stories are untrue and unfair, and are likely to damage the reputation of the person that they relate to. ⇒ Scurrilous and untrue stories were being invented. ⇒ ...scurrilous rumours.

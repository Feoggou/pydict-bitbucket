rotary

(ˈrəʊtərɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or operating by rotation2. turning or able to turn; revolvingnoun plural -ries3. a part of a machine that rotates about an axis4. US and Canadian a roundabout (for traffic)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Medieval Latin rotārius, from Latin rota a wheel

rotary

Powered by COBUILD
(roʊtəri ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Rotary means turning or able to turn round a fixed point. ⇒ ...turning linear into rotary motion. ⇒ ...heavy-duty rotary blades.2. adjective [ADJ n]Rotary is used in the names of some machines that have parts that turn round a fixed point. ⇒ ...a rotary engine.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf you say that time or money has been misspent, you disapprove of the way in which it has been spent. [disapproval] [be V-ed] ⇒ Much of the money was grossly misspent. [V-ed] ⇒ Ruby recalled getting stoned during her misspent youth.

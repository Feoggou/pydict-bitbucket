entablature

(ɛnˈtæblətʃə )
Definitions
noun architecture1. the part of a classical temple above the columns, having an architrave, a frieze, and a cornice2. any construction of similar form
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from French, from Italian intavolatura something put on a table, hence, something laid flat, from tavola table, from Latin tabula table

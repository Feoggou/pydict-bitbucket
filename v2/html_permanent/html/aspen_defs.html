aspen

(ˈæspən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of several trees of the salicaceous genus Populus, such as P. tremula of Europe, in which the leaves are attached to the stem by long flattened stalks so that they quiver in the wind. Archaic name: aspadjective2. archaic, mainly literary trembling
Word origin
Old English æspe; see asp2

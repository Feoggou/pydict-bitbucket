Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [v-link ADJ]If something is aglow, it is shining and bright with a soft, warm light. [literary] [+ with] ⇒ The night skies will be aglow with fireworks.2. adjective [v-link ADJ]If someone is aglow or if their face is aglow, they look excited. [literary] ⇒ 'It was incredible,' Kurt says, suddenly aglow. [Also + with]

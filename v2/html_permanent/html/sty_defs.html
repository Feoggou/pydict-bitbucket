sty

(staɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural sties1. a pen in which pigs are housed and fed2. any filthy or corrupt placeverbWord forms: sties, stying or stied3. to enclose or be enclosed in a sty

Word Origin

Old English stig; related to Old Norse stīa pen, fold, Old High German stīga, Middle Dutch stije

ticky-tacky

(ˈtɪkɪˌtækɪ)
Definitions
US informalnoun1. building material that is cheap and of low quality2. inferior or shoddy goodsadjective -tackier or -tackiest3. cheaply constructed ⇒ Local builders run up ticky-tacky boxes in the towns instead of restoring traditional architecture in the countryside.4. inferior or shoddy

sanctified

(ˈsæŋktɪˌfaɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. consecrated or made holy2. a less common word for sanctimonious

It seems to me now that her excessive devoutness is her way of trying to make amends for her marriage not being sanctified.

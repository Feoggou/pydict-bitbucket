malefactor

(ˈmælɪˌfæktə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna criminal; wrongdoer

Derived Forms

maleˌfaction (ˈmaleˌfaction) nounmaleˌfactress (ˈmaleˌfactress) feminine noun

Word Origin

C15: via Old French from Latin, from malefacere to do evil

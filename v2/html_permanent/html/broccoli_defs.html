broccoli

(ˈbrɒkəlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a cultivated variety of cabbage, Brassica oleracea italica, having branched greenish flower heads2. the flower head of this plant, eaten as a vegetable before the buds have opened3. a variety of this plant that does not form a head, whose stalks are eaten as a vegetable
Word origin
C17: from Italian, plural of broccolo a little sprout, from brocco sprout, spike; see brocade

silken

(ˈsɪlkən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. made of silk2. resembling silk in smoothness or gloss3. dressed in silk4. soft and delicate5. rare luxurious or elegant

silken

Powered by COBUILD
(sɪlkən )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Silken is used to describe things that are very pleasantly smooth and soft. [literary] ⇒ ...her long silken hair. ⇒ ...her long, silken legs.2. adjective [ADJ n]A silken garment, fabric, or rope is made of silk or a material that looks like silk. [literary] ⇒ ...a silken nightshirt. ⇒ ...silken cushions.

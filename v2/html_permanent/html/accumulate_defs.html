accumulate

(əˈkjuːmjʊˌleɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verbto gather or become gathered together in an increasing quantity; amass; collect
Derived forms
acˈcumulable adjectiveacˈcumulative adjectiveacˈcumulatively adverbacˈcumulativeness noun
Word origin
C16: from Latin accumulātus, past participle of accumulāre to heap up, from cumulus a heap

operating

(ˈɒpəreɪtɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. involved in the operations of a business ⇒ Operating income rose 8 per cent to £260.5 million, ⇒ The group made operating profits of £80m before interest.2. involved in surgical operations ⇒ the operating table

operating for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ɒpəreɪtɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]Operating profits and costs are the money that a company earns and spends in carrying out its ordinary trading activities, in contrast to such things as interest and investment. [business] ⇒ The group made operating profits of £80m before interest.

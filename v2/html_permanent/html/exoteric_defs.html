exoteric

(ˌɛksəʊˈtɛrɪk ) or exoterical (ˌɛksəʊˈtɛrɪkəl)
Definitions
adjective1. intelligible to or intended for more than a select or initiated minority ⇒ an exoteric account of a philosophical doctrine2. external; exterior
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

exoterically (ˌexoˈterically) adverbexotericism (ˌexoˈteriˌcism) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin exōtericus external, from Greek exōterikos, from exōterō further outside; see exo-

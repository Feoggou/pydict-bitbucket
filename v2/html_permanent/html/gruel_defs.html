gruel

(ˈɡruːəl )
Definitions
nouna drink or thin porridge, made by boiling meal, esp oatmeal, in water or milk
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French, of Germanic origin; see grout

confluent

(ˈkɒnflʊənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. flowing together or mergingnoun2. a stream that flows into another, usually of approximately equal size
Word origin
C17: from Latin confluēns, from confluere to flow together, from fluere to flow

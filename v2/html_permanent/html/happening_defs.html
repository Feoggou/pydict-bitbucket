happening

(ˈhæpənɪŋ , ˈhæpnɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. an occurrence; event2. an improvised or spontaneous display or performance consisting of bizarre and haphazard eventsadjective3. informal fashionable and up-to-the-minute
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

happening

Powered by COBUILD
(hæpənɪŋ ) for learners of English
Word forms: happenings
Definitions
1. countable noun [usu pl]Happenings are things that happen, often in a way that is unexpected or hard to explain. ⇒ The Budapest office plans to hire freelance reporters to cover the latest happenings.2. graded adjectiveIf you describe something or someone as happening, you mean that they are exciting or lively, and involved in the newest fashions or trends. [informal] ⇒ ...a definitive area-by-area guide to the hip and happening bands. ⇒ ...the most happening place at the moment, the Que Club.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

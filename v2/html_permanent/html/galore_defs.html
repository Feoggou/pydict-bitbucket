galore

(ɡəˈlɔː )
Definitions
determiner(immediately postpositive) in great numbers or quantity ⇒ there were daffodils galore in the park
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Irish Gaelic go leór to sufficiency

galore

Powered by COBUILD
(gəlɔːʳ ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [n ADJ]You use galore to emphasize that something you like exists in very large quantities. [informal, written, emphasis] ⇒ You'll be able to win prizes galore. ⇒ ...a popular resort with beaches galore.

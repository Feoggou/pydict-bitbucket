Romanian

(rəʊˈmeɪnɪən ), Rumanian or Roumanian
Definitions
noun1. the official language of Romania, belonging to the Romance group of the Indo-European family2. a native, citizen, or inhabitant of Romaniaadjective3. relating to, denoting, or characteristic of Romania, its people, or their language
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Romanian

Powered by COBUILD
(ruːmeɪniən ) also Rumanian for learners of English
Word forms: Romanians
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Romanian means belonging or relating to Romania, or to its people, language, or culture. ⇒ ...the Romanian capital, Bucharest. ⇒ ...the Romanian people.2. countable nounA Romanian is a person who comes from Romania.3. uncount nounRomanian is the language spoken in Romania.

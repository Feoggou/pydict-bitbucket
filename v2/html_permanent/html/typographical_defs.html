typographical

Powered by COBUILD
(taɪpəgræfɪkəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]Typographical relates to the way in which printed material is presented. ⇒ Owing to a typographical error, the town of Longridge was spelt as Longbridge.

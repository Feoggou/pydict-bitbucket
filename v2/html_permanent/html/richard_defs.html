Richard

(ˈrɪtʃəd )
Definitions
noun1.  Sir Cliff, real name Harry Rodger Webb. born 1940, British pop singer. Film musicals include The Young Ones (1961) and Summer Holiday (1962)2.  Maurice, known as Rocket. 1921–2000, Canadian ice-hockey player

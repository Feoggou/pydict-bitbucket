emolument

(ɪˈmɒljʊmənt )
Definitions
nounthe profit arising from an office or employment, usually in the form of fees or wages
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Latin ēmolumentum benefit; originally, fee paid to a miller, from ēmolere, from molere to grind

as the crow flies

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf you say that a place is a particular distance away as the crow flies, you mean that it is that distance away measured in a straight line. ⇒ I live at Mesa, Washington, about 10 miles as the crow flies from Hanford. →  crow
Example sentences containing 'as the crow flies'
It's not all that far as the crow flies , but the tracks are not easy. Stephanie Laurens SCANDAL'S BRIDE

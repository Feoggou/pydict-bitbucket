Definitions
phraseIf you do something or get something for free, you do it without being paid or get it without having to pay for it. [informal] ⇒ I wasn't expecting you to do it for free. ⇒ Why waste £250 when you could get it for free? →  free

Definitions
phrase [v n PHR]You say that you will express a critical opinion about something for free when you want to emphasize how strongly you feel about it. [emphasis] ⇒ I'll tell you one thing for free: I can't stand him. →  free

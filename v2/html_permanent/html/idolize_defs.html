idolize

or idolise (ˈaɪdəˌlaɪz )
Definitions
verb1. (transitive) to admire or revere greatly2. (transitive) to worship as an idol3. (intransitive) to worship idols
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

idolism (iˈdolism), idolization (ˌidoliˈzation) or idolisation (ˌidoliˈsation) nounidolist (ˈidolist), idolizer (ˈidolˌizer) or idoliser (ˈidolˌiser) noun

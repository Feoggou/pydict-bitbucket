incredulity

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnkrɪdjuːlɪti , US -duːl- ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIf someone reacts with incredulity to something, they are unable to believe it because it is very surprising or shocking. ⇒ The announcement has been met with incredulity. ⇒ The Vicar looked at him with open-mouthed incredulity.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

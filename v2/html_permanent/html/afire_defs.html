afire

(əˈfaɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb, adjective (postpositive)1. on fire; ablaze2. intensely interested or passionate ⇒ he was afire with enthusiasm for the new plan

Powered by COBUILD
(əfaɪəʳ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [v-link ADJ]If something is afire or is set afire, it is on fire or looks as if it is on fire. alight ⇒ The houses were set afire but there were only minor injuries to seven personnel. ⇒ The sun has already set, leaving the sky afire with orange and green light.2. adjective [v-link ADJ, oft ADJ with n]If someone is afire with emotion, they are extremely enthusiastic and excited about something. [literary] ⇒ All Dan's senses were afire. ⇒ Sydney is afire with Olympic enthusiasm.

Lorraine

(lɒˈreɪn , French lɔrɛn)
Definitions
noun1. a region and former province of E France; ceded to Germany in 1871 after the Franco-Prussian war and regained by France in 1919; rich iron-ore deposits. German name: Lothringen2. See Kingdom of Lorraine3. a former duchy in E France, once the S half of this kingdom

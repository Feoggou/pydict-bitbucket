amorphous

(əˈmɔːfəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. lacking a definite shape; formless2. of no recognizable character or type3. (of chemicals, rocks, etc) not having a crystalline structure
Derived forms
aˈmorphism nounaˈmorphously adverbaˈmorphousness noun
Word origin
C18: from New Latin, from Greek amorphos shapeless, from a-1 + morphē shape

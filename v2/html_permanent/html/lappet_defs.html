lappet

(ˈlæpɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a small hanging flap or piece of lace, etc, such as one dangling from a headdress2. zoology a lobelike hanging structure, such as the wattle on a bird's head
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lappeted (ˈlappeted) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from lap1 + -et

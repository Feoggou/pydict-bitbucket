Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA schoolboy is a boy who goes to school. ⇒ ...a group of ten-year-old schoolboys.2. adjective [ADJ n]If you think a man's sense of humour is silly or rude, you can describe it as schoolboy humour. [disapproval] ⇒ ...tiresome schoolboy jokes.

Definitions
nounmainly British a register of names showing the order in which people take their turn to perform certain duties

rota

Powered by COBUILD
(roʊtə ) for learners of English
Word forms: rotas
Definitions
countable nounA rota is a list which gives details of the order in which different people have to do a particular job. [mainly British] ⇒ I suggest that you work out a careful rota which will make it clear who tidies the room on which day. ⇒ ...the washing-up rota. ⇒ The tea is prepared on a rota basis by the lady members.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

docking1

(ˈdɒkɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of mooring (a vessel) at a dock or (of a vessel) being moored at a dock ⇒ the docking of the cruise liner2. (in molecular biology) a method which predicts the preferred orientation of one molecule to a second when bound to each other to form a stable complex ⇒ automated docking of ligands (small molecules like a candidate drug) to their macromolecular targets3. (of two spacecraft) the act of linking together in space

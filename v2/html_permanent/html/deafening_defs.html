Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA deafening noise is a very loud noise. ⇒ ...the deafening roar of fighter jets taking off.2. adjectiveIf you say there was a deafening silence, you are emphasizing that there was no reaction or response to something that was said or done. [emphasis] ⇒ When we ask people for suggestions we get a deafening silence.

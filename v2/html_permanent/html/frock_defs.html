frock

(frɒk )
Definitions
noun1. a girl's or woman's dress2. a loose garment of several types, such as a peasant's smock3. a coarse wide-sleeved outer garment worn by members of some religious ordersverb4. (transitive) to invest (a person) with the office or status of a cleric
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French froc; related to Old Saxon, Old High German hroc coat

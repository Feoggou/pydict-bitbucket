Definitions

nouna girl who acts or dresses in a boyish way, liking rough outdoor activities

Powered by COBUILD
(tɒmbɔɪ )
Word forms: tomboys
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf you say that a girl is a tomboy, you mean that she likes playing rough or noisy games, or doing things that were traditionally considered to be things that boys enjoy.

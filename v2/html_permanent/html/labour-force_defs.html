labour force

(ˈleɪbə fɔːs) British or labor force US
Definitions
nounbusiness the total number of people who are eligible to work (including employed and unemployed people) (of a country, the world, etc) ⇒ Unemployment in Britain rose to 8.1% of the labour force.

Definitions
countable noun [usu sing]The labour force consists of all the people who are able to work in a country or area, or all the people who work for a particular company. [business] ⇒ Unemployment in Britain rose to 8.1% of the labour force.

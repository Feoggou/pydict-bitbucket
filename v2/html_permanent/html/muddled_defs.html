Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is muddled, they are confused about something. ⇒ I'm afraid I'm a little muddled. I'm not exactly sure where to begin. ⇒ ...the muddled thinking of the Government's transport policy in recent years.
Example sentences containing 'muddled'
In the end they was so muddled they didn't know what they was saying. Barbara Erskine HIDING FROM THE LIGHTShe was a communist, an atheist -- it had been muddled thinking. Robert Wilson THE COMPANY OF STRANGERSYou are still the essential person inside, just a little bit muddled. Scarlett Thomas GOING OUT

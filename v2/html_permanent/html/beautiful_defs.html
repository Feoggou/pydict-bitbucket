Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. possessing beauty; aesthetically pleasing2. highly enjoyable; very pleasant ⇒ the party was beautiful
Derived forms

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA beautiful person is very attractive to look at. ⇒ She was a very beautiful woman. ⇒ To me he is the most beautiful child in the world.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as beautiful, you mean that it is very attractive or pleasing. delightful ⇒ New England is beautiful. ⇒ It was a beautiful morning. ⇒ He has beautiful manners.beautifully (bjuːtɪfli ) graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV after v] ⇒ The children behaved beautifully. ⇒ ...a beautifully clear, sunny day.3. graded adjective & adjectiveYou can describe something that someone does as beautiful when they do it very skilfully. ⇒ That's a beautiful shot!beautifully graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v, ADV -ed] ⇒ Arsenal played beautifully.

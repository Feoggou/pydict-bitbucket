cardiac

(ˈkɑːdɪˌæk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to the heart2. of or relating to the portion of the stomach connected to the oesophagusnoun3. a person with a heart disorder4. obsolete a drug that stimulates the heart muscle
Word origin
C17: from Latin cardiacus, from Greek, from kardia heart

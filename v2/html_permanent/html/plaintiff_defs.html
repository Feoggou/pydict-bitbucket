plaintiff

(ˈpleɪntɪf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(formerly) a person who brings a civil action in a court of law. Now replaced by: claimant Compare defendant (sense 1)

Word Origin

C14: from legal French plaintif, from Old French plaintif (adj) complaining, from plainte plaint

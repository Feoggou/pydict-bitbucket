acrid

(ˈækrɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. unpleasantly pungent or sharp to the smell or taste2. sharp or caustic, esp in speech or nature
Derived forms
acridity (əˈkrɪdɪtɪ ) or acridness (ˈacridness) nounacridly (ˈacridly) adverb
Word origin
C18: from Latin ācer sharp, sour; probably formed on the model of acid

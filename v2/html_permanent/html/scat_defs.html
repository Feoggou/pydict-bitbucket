scat1

(skæt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verbWord forms: scats, scatting or scatted(intr; usually imperative) informal to go away in haste

Word Origin

C19: perhaps from a hiss + the word cat, used to frighten away cats

scat2

(skæt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a type of jazz singing characterized by improvised vocal sounds instead of wordsverbWord forms: scats, scatting or scatted2. (intransitive) to sing jazz in this way

Word Origin

C20: perhaps imitative

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany marine and freshwater percoid fish of the Asian family Scatophagidae, esp Scatophagus argus, which has a beautiful coloration

Word Origin

C20: shortened from Scatophagus; see scato-

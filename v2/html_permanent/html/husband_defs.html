husband

(ˈhʌzbənd )
Definitions
noun1. one's (male) partner in marriage; a married man2. archaica. a manager of an estateb. a frugal personverb3. to manage or use (resources, finances, etc) thriftily4. archaica. (transitive) to find a husband forb. to marry (a man)5. (transitive) obsolete to till (the soil)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

husbander (ˈhusbander) nounhusbandless (ˈhusbandless) adjective

Word Origin

Old English hūsbonda, from Old Norse hūsbōndi, from hūs house + bōndi one who has a household, from bōa to dwell

Definitions

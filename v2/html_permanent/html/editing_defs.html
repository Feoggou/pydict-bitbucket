editing

(ˈɛdɪtɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the process of correcting and adapting a text, such as an article or book ⇒ Throughout the editing of this book, we have had much support and encouragement.2. the process of collecting together articles, etc, written by different writers, and preparing them for publishing ⇒ He was certainly not cut out to combine the jobs of editing and writing as a journalist.3. the process of preparing a film or a radio or television programme by selecting, rearranging, or rejecting previously filmed or taped material ⇒ He sat in on much of the filming and early editing.4. a. the modification of a computer file by, for example, deleting, inserting, moving, or copying textb. (as modifier) ⇒ editing tools, ⇒ an editing package, ⇒ editing software

impressionist

(ɪmˈprɛʃənɪst )
Definitions
noun1. (usually capital) any of the French painters of the late 19th century who were exponents of impressionism2. (sometimes capital) any artist, composer, or writer who uses impressionism3. an entertainer who impersonates famous peopleadjective4. (often capital) denoting, of, or relating to impressionism or the exponents of this style
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

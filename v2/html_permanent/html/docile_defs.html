docile

(ˈdəʊsaɪl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. easy to manage, control, or discipline; submissive2. rare ready to learn; easy to teach
Derived forms
docilely (ˈdocilely) adverbdocility (dəʊˈsɪlɪtɪ ) noun
Word origin
C15: from Latin docilis easily taught, from docēre to teach

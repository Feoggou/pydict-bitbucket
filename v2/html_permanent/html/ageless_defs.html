Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveIf you describe someone as ageless, you mean that they never seem to look any older. [literary] ⇒ She was rich, beautiful and seemingly ageless.2. adjectiveIf you describe something as ageless, you mean that it is impossible to tell how old it is, or that it seems to have existed for ever. [literary] ⇒ ...the ageless oceans.

devotion

(dɪˈvəʊʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (often foll by to) strong attachment (to) or affection (for a cause, person, etc) marked by dedicated loyalty2. religious zeal; piety3. (often plural) religious observance or prayers

(dɪvoʊʃən )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount noun [oft poss N]Devotion is great love, affection, or admiration for someone. ⇒ At first she was flattered by his devotion.2. uncount nounDevotion is commitment to a particular activity. [+ to] ⇒ ...devotion to the cause of the people and to socialism. ⇒ I don't mean to keep criticising his devotion to his job.3. uncount nounDevotion is religious worship or strong religious feeling. ⇒ He was kneeling by his bed in an attitude of devotion.

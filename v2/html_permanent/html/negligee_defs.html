negligee

, negligée or négligé (ˈnɛɡlɪˌʒeɪ )
Definitions
noun1. a woman's light dressing gown, esp one that is lace-trimmed2. a thin and revealing woman's nightdress3. any informal attire
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from French négligée, past participle (fem) of négliger to neglect

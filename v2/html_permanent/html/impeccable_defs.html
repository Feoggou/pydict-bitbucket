impeccable

(ɪmˈpɛkəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. without flaw or error; faultless ⇒ an impeccable record2. rare incapable of sinning
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

impeccability (imˌpeccaˈbility) nounimpeccably (imˈpeccably) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Late Latin impeccābilis sinless, from Latin im- (not) + peccāre to sin

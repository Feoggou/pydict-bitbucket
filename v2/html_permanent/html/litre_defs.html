litre

or US liter (ˈliːtə )
Definitions
noun1. one cubic decimetre2. (formerly) the volume occupied by 1 kilogram of pure water at 4°C and 760 millimetres of mercury. This is equivalent to 1.000028 cubic decimetres or about 1.76 pints
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from French, from Medieval Latin litra, from Greek: a unit of weight

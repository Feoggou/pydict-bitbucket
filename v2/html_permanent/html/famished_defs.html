famished

(ˈfæmɪʃt)
Definitions
adjective1. informal very hungry or weak ⇒ In my famished state it seemed like a feast., ⇒ You look famished., ⇒ I missed breakfast and I am famished.2. suffering from starvation ⇒ Many too famished simply died on the way., ⇒ There lay the famished skeleton of some child., ⇒ They wolfed down watery bean stew like famished dogs.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

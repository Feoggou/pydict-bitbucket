sallow1

(ˈsæləʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (esp of human skin) of an unhealthy pale or yellowish colourverb2. (transitive) to make sallow

Derived Forms

sallowish (ˈsallowish) adjectivesallowly (ˈsallowly) adverbsallowness (ˈsallowness) noun

Word Origin

Old English salu; related to Old Norse sol seaweed (Icelandic sōlr yellowish), Old High German salo, French sale dirty

sallow2

(ˈsæləʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of several small willow trees, esp the Eurasian Salix cinerea (common sallow), which has large catkins that appear before the leaves2. a twig or the wood of any of these trees

Derived Forms

sallowy (ˈsallowy) adjective

Word Origin

Old English sealh; related to Old Norse selja, Old High German salaha, Middle Low German salwīde, Latin salix

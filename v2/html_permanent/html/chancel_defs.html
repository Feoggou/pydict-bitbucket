chancel

(ˈtʃɑːnsəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe part of a church containing the altar, sanctuary, and choir, usually separated from the nave and transepts by a screen
Word origin
C14: from Old French, from Latin cancellī (plural) lattice

tuning

(ˈtjuːnɪŋ )
Definitions
noun music1. a set of pitches to which the open strings of a guitar, violin, etc, are tuned ⇒ the normal tuning on a violin is G, D, A, E2. the accurate pitching of notes and intervals by a choir, orchestra, etc; intonation

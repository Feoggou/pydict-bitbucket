(grædʒuəli ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]If something changes or is done gradually, it changes or is done in small stages over a long period of time, rather than suddenly. ⇒ Electricity lines to 30,000 homes were gradually being restored yesterday. ⇒ Gradually we learned to cope.

Soon we were below the timber line, small sticks at first, but the scrub becoming gradually taller and thicker. Innes, Hammond HIGH STANDHe wiped his hands on a rag, gradually recovering his breath.

sociable

(ˈsəʊʃəbəl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. friendly or companionable2. (of an occasion) providing the opportunity for friendliness and convivialitynoun3. mainly US another name for social (sense 9)4. a type of open carriage with two seats facing each other

Derived Forms

ˌsociability (ˌsociaˈbility) or sociableness (ˈsociableness) nounsociably (ˈsociably) adverb

Word Origin

C16: via French from Latin sociābilis, from sociāre to unite, from socius an associate

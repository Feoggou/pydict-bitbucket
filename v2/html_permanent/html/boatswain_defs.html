boatswain

, bosun or bo's'n (ˈbəʊsən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna petty officer on a merchant ship or a warrant officer on a warship who is responsible for the maintenance of the ship and its equipment
Word origin
Old English bātswegen; see boat, swain
Example sentences containing 'boatswain'

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as inopportune or if you say that it happens at an inopportune time, you mean that it happens at an unfortunate or unsuitable time, and causes trouble or embarrassment because of this. ⇒ The dismissals came at an inopportune time.

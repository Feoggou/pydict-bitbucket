studious

(ˈstjuːdɪəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. given to study2. of a serious, thoughtful, and hard-working character3. showing deliberation, care, or precision

Derived Forms

studiously (ˈstudiously) adverbstudiousness (ˈstudiousness) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Latin studiōsus devoted to, from studium assiduity

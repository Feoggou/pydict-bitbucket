at risk

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseTo be at risk means to be in a situation where something unpleasant might happen. ⇒ Up to 25,000 jobs are still at risk. ⇒ An estimated seven million people are at risk of starvation. ⇒ If they have the virus they are putting patients at risk. [Also + of] →  risk

Today, dozens of parrots found in continental areas are at risk of extinction.

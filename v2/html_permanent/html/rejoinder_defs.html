rejoinder

(rɪˈdʒɔɪndə )
Definitions
noun1. a reply or response to a question or remark, esp a quick witty one; retort2. law (in pleading) the answer made by a defendant to the claimant's reply
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French rejoindre to rejoin2

unwholesome

(ʌnˈhəʊlsəm )
Definitions
adjective1. detrimental to physical or mental health ⇒ an unwholesome climate2. morally harmful or depraved ⇒ unwholesome practices3. indicative of illness, esp in appearance4. (esp of food) of inferior quality
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unwholesomely (unˈwholesomely) adverbunwholesomeness (unˈwholesomeness) noun

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveUnwholesome food or drink is not healthy or good for you. ⇒ The fish were unwholesome and old.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone's feelings or behaviour as unwholesome, you are critical of them because they are unpleasant or unnatural.

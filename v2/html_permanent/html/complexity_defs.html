complexity

(kəmˈplɛksɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the state or quality of being intricate or complex2. something intricate or complex; complication

complexity

(kəmpleksɪti )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounComplexity is the state of having many different parts connected or related to each other in a complicated way. ⇒ ...a diplomatic tangle of great complexity. [+ of] ⇒ ...the increasing complexity of modern weapon systems.

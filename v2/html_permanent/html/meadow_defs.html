meadow

(ˈmɛdəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an area of grassland, often used for hay or for grazing of animals2. a low-lying piece of grassland, often boggy and near a river

Derived Forms

meadowy (ˈmeadowy) adjective

Word Origin

Old English mædwe, from mǣd mead2; related to māwan to mow1

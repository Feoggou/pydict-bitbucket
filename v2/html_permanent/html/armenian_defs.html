Armenian

(ɑːˈmiːnɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a native or inhabitant of Armenia or an Armenian-speaking person elsewhere2. the language of the Armenians: an Indo-European language probably belonging to the Thraco-Phrygian branch, but containing many non-Indo-European elements3. an adherent of the Armenian Church or its doctrinesadjective4. of or relating to Armenia, its inhabitants, their language, or the Armenian Church

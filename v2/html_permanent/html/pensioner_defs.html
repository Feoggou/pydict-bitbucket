pensioner

(ˈpɛnʃənə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who is receiving a pension, esp an old-age pension from the state2. a person dependent on the pay or bounty of another3. obsolete, British another name for gentleman-at-arms

(penʃənəʳ )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA pensioner is someone who receives a pension, especially a pension paid by the state to retired people.

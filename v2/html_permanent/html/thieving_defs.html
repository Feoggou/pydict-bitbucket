thieving

Powered by COBUILD
(θiːvɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounThieving is the act of stealing things from people. [old-fashioned] ⇒ ...an ex-con who says he's given up thieving.2. adjective [ADJ n]Thieving means involved in stealing things or intending to steal something. ⇒ He vowed to wreak vengeance on his unfaithful, thieving wife. ⇒ ...a string of convictions from a thieving career.

affronted

(əˈfrʌntɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveformal experiencing an insult ⇒ He pretended to be affronted, but inwardly he was pleased., ⇒ Reggie reacted with the same affronted horror Midge had felt.

flare-up

Definitions
noun1. a sudden burst of fire or light2. informal a sudden burst of emotion or violenceverb flare up (intr, adverb)3. to burst suddenly into fire or light4. informal to burst into anger

Word forms: flare-ups
Definitions
countable nounIf there is a flare-up of violence or of an illness, it suddenly starts or gets worse. ⇒ There's been a flare-up of violence in South Africa. ⇒ ...a flare-up in her arthritis.

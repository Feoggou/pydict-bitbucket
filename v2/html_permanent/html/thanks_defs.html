thanks

(θæŋks )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. an expression of appreciation or gratitude or an acknowledgment of services or favours given2. See thanks toexclamation3. informal an exclamation expressing acknowledgment, gratitude, or appreciation
Example sentences containing 'thanks'
Despite the noise, they hadn't got in, and Sam offered up silent thanks to the miller who had built so well. Garth Nix LIRAEL: DAUGHTER OF THE CLAYRShe could maybe even alter her hairstyle, thanks to the sweat of panic.

poison sumach

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan anacardiaceous swamp shrub, Rhus (or Toxicodendron) vernix of the southeastern US, that has greenish-white berries and causes an itching rash on contact with the skin. Also called: poison dogwood, poison elder See also sumach

prevailing

(prɪˈveɪlɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. generally accepted; widespread ⇒ the prevailing opinion2. most frequent or conspicuous; predominant ⇒ the prevailing wind is from the north

Derived Forms

prevailingly (preˈvailingly) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]The prevailing wind in an area is the type of wind that blows over that area most of the time. ⇒ The direction of the prevailing winds should be taken into account.

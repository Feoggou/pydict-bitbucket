brocade

(brəʊˈkeɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a rich fabric woven with a raised design, often using gold or silver threadsb. (as modifier) ⇒ brocade curtainsverb2. (transitive) to weave with such a design
Word origin
C17: from Spanish brocado, from Italian broccato embossed fabric, from brocco spike, from Latin brochus projecting; see broach1

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone's attitude as unequivocal, you mean that it is completely clear and very firm. [formal] ⇒ ...Richardson's unequivocal commitment to fair play. ⇒ Yesterday, the message to him was unequivocal: 'Get out.'unequivocally (ʌnɪkwɪvəkli ) graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj] ⇒ He stated unequivocally that the French forces were ready to go to war. ⇒ Temperature records have unequivocally confirmed the existence of global warming.

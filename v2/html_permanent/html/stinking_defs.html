stinking

(ˈstɪŋkɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having a foul smell2. informal unpleasant or disgusting3. (postpositive) slang very drunkadverb4. informal (intensifier, expressing contempt for the person referred to) ⇒ stinking rich

Derived Forms

stinkingly (ˈstinkingly) adverbstinkingness (ˈstinkingness) noun

solemnize

or solemnise (ˈsɒləmˌnaɪz )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to celebrate or observe with rites or formal ceremonies, as a religious occasion2. to celebrate or perform the ceremony of (marriage)3. to make solemn or serious4. to perform or hold (ceremonies, etc) in due manner

Derived Forms

ˌsolemnization (ˌsolemniˈzation) or ˌsolemnisation (ˌsolemniˈsation) nounsolemˌnizer (ˈsolemˌnizer) or solemˌniser (ˈsolemˌniser) noun

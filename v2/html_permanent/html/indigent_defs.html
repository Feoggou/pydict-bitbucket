indigent

(ˈɪndɪdʒənt )
Definitions
adjective1. so poor as to lack even necessities; very needy2. (usually foll by of) archaic lacking (in) or destitute (of)noun3. an impoverished person
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

indigence (ˈindigence) nounindigently (ˈindigently) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Latin indigēre to need, from egēre to lack

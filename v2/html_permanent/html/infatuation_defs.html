infatuation

(ɪnˌfætjʊˈeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of infatuating or state of being infatuated2. foolish or extravagant passion3. an object of foolish or extravagant passion

infatuation

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnfætʃueɪʃən ) for learners of English
Word forms: infatuations
Definitions
variable nounIf you have an infatuation for a person or thing, you have strong feelings of love or passion for them which make you unable to think clearly or sensibly about them. [+ with] ⇒ ...his infatuation with bullfighting. ⇒ ...Daisy's infatuation for the doctor. ⇒ Teenagers have their own infatuations.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

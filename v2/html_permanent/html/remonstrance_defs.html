remonstrance

(rɪˈmɒnstrəns )
Definitions
noun1. the act of remonstrating; protestation2. a protest or reproof, esp a petition presented in protest against something

Remonstrance

(rɪˈmɒnstrəns )
Definitions
noun history1. See Grand Remonstrance2. the statement of Arminian principles drawn up in 1610 in Gouda in the Netherlands

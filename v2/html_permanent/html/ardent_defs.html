ardent

(ˈɑːdənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. expressive of or characterized by intense desire or emotion; passionate ⇒ ardent love2. intensely enthusiastic; eager ⇒ an ardent longing3. glowing, flashing, or shining ⇒ ardent eyes4. rare burning ⇒ an ardent fever
Derived forms
ˈardency nounˈardently adverb
Word origin
C14: from Latin ārdēre to burn

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Ardent is used to describe someone who has extremely strong feelings about something or someone. ⇒ He's been one of the most ardent supporters of the administration's policy. ⇒ ...an ardent opponent of the Vietnam War.ardently graded adverb [usu ADV with v, also ADV adj] ⇒ Why had Hilton defended him so ardently? ⇒ His reports are often ardently pro-Russian.

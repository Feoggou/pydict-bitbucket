ostentation for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ɒstenteɪʃən )
Definitions
uncount nounIf you describe someone's behaviour as ostentation, you are criticizing them for doing or buying things in order to impress people. [formal, disapproval] ⇒ Consumers are abandoning the excess and ostentation of the 1980s. ⇒ On the whole she had lived modestly, with a notable lack of ostentation.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

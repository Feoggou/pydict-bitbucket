undeserved for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌndɪzɜːʳvd )
Definitions
adjectiveIf you describe something such as a reaction, treatment, or result as undeserved, you mean that the person who experiences it has not earned it and should not really have it. ⇒ Douglas has an undeserved reputation for being dull and dry. ⇒ Jim's treatment was harsh and undeserved.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

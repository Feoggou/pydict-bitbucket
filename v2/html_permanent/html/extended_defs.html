extended

(ɪkˈstɛndɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. stretched out in time, space, influence, application, etc2. (of a horse's pace) free-moving and with long steps ⇒ an extended trot3. printing another word for expanded (sense 1)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

extendedly (exˈtendedly) adverbextendedness (exˈtendedness) noun

(ɪkstendɪd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]If something happens for an extended period of time, it happens for a long period of time. ⇒ Obviously, any child who receives dedicated teaching over an extended period is likely to improve.2. → See also extend

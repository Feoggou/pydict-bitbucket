sensuality

(ˌsɛnsjʊˈælɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the quality or state of being sensual2. excessive indulgence in sensual pleasures

Derived Forms

sensualist (ˈsɛnsjʊəlɪst ) noun
Example sentences containing 'sensuality'
Her lips were too pale, too tight, yet nothing could hide the promise of sensuality in their full curves. Elizabeth Lowell PEARL COVE

Similar Words

arrears

(əˈrɪəz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: arrearage (əˈrɪərɪdʒ ) (sometimes singular) something outstanding or owed2. See in arrears
Word origin
C18: from obsolete arrear (adv) behindhand, from Old French arere, from Medieval Latin adretrō, from Latin ad to + retrō backwards

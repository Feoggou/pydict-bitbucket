Definitions

Derived Forms

hilariously (hiˈlariously) adverbhilariousness (hiˈlariousness) noun

Word Origin

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is hilarious, it is extremely funny and makes you laugh a lot. ⇒ We thought it was hilarious when we first heard about it. ⇒ He had a fund of hilarious tales on the subject.hilariously graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj] [ADV with v] ⇒ She found it hilariously funny. ⇒ Safire writes hilariously of being banished from presidential favor.

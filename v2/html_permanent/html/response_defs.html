response

(rɪˈspɒns )
Definitions
noun1. the act of responding; reply or reaction2. bridge a bid replying to a partner's bid or double3. (usually plural) Christianity a short sentence or phrase recited or sung by the choir or congregation in reply to the officiant at a church service4. electronics the ratio of the output to the input level, at a particular frequency, of a transmission line or electrical device5. any pattern of glandular, muscular, or electrical reactions that arises from stimulation of the nervous system
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

responseless (reˈsponseless) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Latin rēsponsum answer, from rēspondēre to respond

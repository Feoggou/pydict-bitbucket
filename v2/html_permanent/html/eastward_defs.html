eastward

(ˈiːstwəd )
Definitions
adjective1. situated or directed towards the eastadverb2. a variant of eastwardsnoun3. the eastward part, direction, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

eastwardly (ˈeastwardly) adverb, adjective

eastward

Powered by COBUILD
(iːstwəʳd ) also eastwards for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [usu ADV after v, oft n ADV]Eastward or eastwards means towards the east. ⇒ A powerful snow storm is moving eastward. ⇒ They were pressing on eastwards towards the city's small airfield.Eastward is also an adjective. ⇒ ...the eastward expansion of London.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

capstan

(ˈkæpstən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a machine with a drum that rotates round a vertical spindle and is turned by a motor or lever, used for hauling in heavy ropes, etc2. any similar device, such as the rotating shaft in a tape recorder that pulls the tape past the head
Word origin
C14: from Old Provençal cabestan, from Latin capistrum a halter, from capere to seize

colouring

or US coloring (ˈkʌlərɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the process or art of applying colour2. anything used to give colour, such as dye, paint, etc3. appearance with regard to shade and colour4. arrangements of colours and tones, as in the markings of birds and animals5. the colour of a person's features or complexion6. a false or misleading appearance

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
regional note:   in AM, use coloring1. uncount noun [usu with poss]The colouring of something is the colour or colours that it is. [+ of] ⇒ Other countries vary the coloring of their bank notes as well as their size. ⇒ ...the scenery was losing its bright colouring.2. uncount noun [usu with poss]Someone's colouring is the colour of their hair, skin, and eyes. ⇒ None of them had their father's dark colouring. ⇒ Choose shades which tone in with your natural colouring.3. uncount nounColouring is a substance that is used to give colour to food. ⇒ A few drops of green food coloring were added.4. → see also colour

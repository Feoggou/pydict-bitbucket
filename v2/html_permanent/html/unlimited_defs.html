unlimited

(ʌnˈlɪmɪtɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. without limits or bounds ⇒ unlimited knowledge2. not restricted, limited, or qualified ⇒ unlimited power3. finance, Britisha. (of liability) not restricted to any unpaid portion of nominal capital invested in a businessb. (of a business enterprise) having owners with such unlimited liability
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unlimitedly (unˈlimitedly) adverbunlimitedness (unˈlimitedness) noun

(ˈnʌrɪʃmənt )
Definitions
noun1. the act or state of nourishing2. a substance that nourishes; food; nutriment

nourishment for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(nʌrɪʃmənt , US nɜːr- )
Definitions
1. uncount nounIf something provides a person, animal, or plant with nourishment, it provides them with the food that is necessary for life, growth, and good health. ⇒ The mother provides the embryo with nourishment and a place to grow. ⇒ He was unable to take nourishment for several days.2. uncount nounThe action of nourishing someone or something, or the experience of being nourished, can be referred to as nourishment. ⇒ Sugar gives quick relief to hunger but provides no lasting nourishment.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

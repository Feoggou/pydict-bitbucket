Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf you describe someone as the standard bearer of a group, you mean that they act as the leader or public representative of a group of people who have the same aims or interests. ⇒ Farrakhan was a poor standard-bearer for the causes of African-Americans.

intricacy

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪntrɪkəsi ) for learners of English
Word forms: intricacies
Definitions
1. uncount nounIntricacy is the state of being made up of many small parts or details. [+ of] ⇒ The price depends on the intricacy of the work.2. plural nounThe intricacies of something are its complicated details. [+ of] ⇒ Rose explained the intricacies of the job.

baseball

(ˈbeɪsˌbɔːl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a team game with nine players on each side, played on a field with four bases connected to form a diamond. The object is to score runs by batting the ball and running round the bases2. the hard rawhide-covered ball used in this game

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounIn America, baseball is a game played by two teams of nine players. Each player from one team hits a ball with a bat and then tries to run around three bases and get to the home base before the other team can get the ball back.2. countable nounA baseball is a small hard ball which is used in the game of baseball.

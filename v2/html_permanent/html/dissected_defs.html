dissected

(dɪˈsɛktɪd , daɪ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. botany in the form of narrow lobes or segments ⇒ dissected leaves2. geology (of plains) cut by erosion into hills and valleys, esp following tectonic movements

cairn

(kɛən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a mound of stones erected as a memorial or marker2. Also called: cairn terrier a small rough-haired breed of terrier originally from Scotland
Word origin
C15: from Gaelic carn

cairn

Powered by COBUILD
(keəʳn )
Word forms: cairns
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA cairn is a pile of stones which marks a boundary, a route across rough ground, or the top of a mountain. A cairn is sometimes also built in memory of someone.

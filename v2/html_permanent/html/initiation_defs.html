initiation

(ɪˌnɪʃɪˈeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of initiating or the condition of being initiated2. the often secret ceremony initiating new members into an organization

initiation

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnɪʃieɪʃən ) for learners of English
Word forms: initiations
Definitions
1. uncount nounThe initiation of something is the starting of it. [+ of] ⇒ They announced the initiation of a rural development programme. ⇒ There was a year between initiation and completion.2. variable noun [N n]Someone's initiation into a particular group is the act or process by which they officially become a member, often involving special ceremonies. ⇒ After initiation, the youths started to interact with the older members. [+ into] ⇒ This was my initiation into the peace movement. ⇒ ...initiation ceremonies.

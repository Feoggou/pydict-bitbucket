rock bottom

Powered by COBUILD
also rock-bottom for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounIf something has reached rock bottom, it is at such a low level that it cannot go any lower. ⇒ Morale in the armed forces was at rock bottom. ⇒ Prices have hit rock bottom.2. uncount nounIf someone has reached rock bottom, they are in such a bad state or are so completely depressed that their situation could not get any worse. ⇒ She was at rock bottom. Her long-term love affair was breaking up and so was she.3. adjective [usu ADJ n]A rock-bottom price or level is a very low one, mainly in advertisements. [approval] ⇒ What they do offer is a good product at a rock-bottom price.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

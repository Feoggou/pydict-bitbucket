animadvert

(ˌænɪmædˈvɜːt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (intransitive)1. (usually foll by on or upon) to comment with strong criticism (upon); make censorious remarks (about)2. to make an observation or comment
Word origin
C16: from Latin animadvertere to notice, pay attention, from animus mind + advertere to turn to, from vertere to turn

retrieval

(rɪˈtriːvəl )
Definitions
noun1. the act or process of retrieving2. the possibility of recovery, restoration, or rectification (esp in the phrase beyond retrieval)3. a computer filing operation that recalls records or other data from a file

retrieval

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪtriːvəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounThe retrieval of information from a computer is the process of getting it back. ⇒ ...electronic storage and retrieval systems.2. uncount nounThe retrieval of something is the process of getting it back from a particular place, especially from a place where it should not be. [+ of] ⇒ Its real purpose is the launching and retrieval of small aeroplanes in flight.

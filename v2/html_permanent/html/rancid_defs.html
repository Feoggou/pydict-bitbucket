rancid

(ˈrænsɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. (of butter, bacon, etc) having an unpleasant stale taste or smell as the result of decomposition2. (of a taste or smell) rank or sour; stale
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

rancidity (rænˈsɪdɪtɪ ) or rancidness (ˈrancidness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin rancidus rank, from rancēre to stink

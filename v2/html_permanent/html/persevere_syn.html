Synonyms of persevere

persevere

verb

  1. keep going, continue, go on, carry on, endure, hold on (informal), hang on, persist, stand firm, plug away (informal), hold fast, remain firm, stay the course, keep your hand in, pursue your goal, be determined or resolved, keep on or at, stick at or to
    • the ability to persevere despite obstacles and setbacks
    OPPOSITES:  give up, quit, give in, hesitate, falter, waver, dither (mainly British), throw in the towel, vacillate, be irresolute, shillyshally (informal), swither (Scottish)

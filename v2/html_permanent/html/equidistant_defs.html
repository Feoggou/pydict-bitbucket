Definitions
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]A place that is equidistant from two other places is the same distance away from each of these places. [+ from/between] ⇒ Horsey is equidistant from Great Yarmouth and Mundesley.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

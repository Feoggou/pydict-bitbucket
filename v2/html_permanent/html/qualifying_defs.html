qualifying

(ˈkwɒlɪˌfaɪɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. sport (of a contest, competition, etc) played in order to decide which person or team will progress to the final stages; preliminary ⇒ a World Cup qualifying match2. (of a statement) containing extra detail or explanation (which makes a prior statement less strong or less general) ⇒ But there was a qualifying clause: ' … unless one of the parties makes an appeal'.. See also qualifying exam

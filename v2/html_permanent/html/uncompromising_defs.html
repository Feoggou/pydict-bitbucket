(ʌnkɒmprəmaɪzɪŋ )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as uncompromising, you mean that they are determined not to change their opinions or aims in any way. unyielding ⇒ Mrs Thatcher was a tough and uncompromising politician.uncompromisingly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj, oft ADV after v] ⇒ The company had once been uncompromisingly socialist. ⇒ He states uncompromisingly that he is opposed to any practices which oppress animals.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as uncompromising, you mean that it does not attempt to make something that is shocking or unpleasant any more acceptable to people. ⇒ ...a film of uncompromising brutality.uncompromisingly graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj] ⇒ ...the uncompromisingly modern decor.

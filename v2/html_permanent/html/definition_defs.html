definition

(ˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a formal and concise statement of the meaning of a word, phrase, etc2. the act of defining a word, phrase, etc3. specification of the essential properties of something, or of the criteria which uniquely identify it4. the act of making clear or definite5. the state or condition of being clearly defined or definite6. a measure of the clarity of an optical, photographic, or television image as characterized by its sharpness and contrast
Derived forms
ˌdefinitional (ˌdefiˈnitional) adjective

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA definition is a statement giving the meaning of a word or expression, especially in a dictionary. [+ of] ⇒ There is no general agreement on a standard definition of intelligence. ⇒ My definition of a good hospice is one where some of the patients start feeling too good to die.→  by definition2. uncount nounDefinition is the quality of being clear and distinct. ⇒ Give your brows extra definition with Outdoor Girl's Eyebrow Pencil in Brown. ⇒ The speakers criticised his new programme for lack of definition.

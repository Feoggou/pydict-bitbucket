unrighteous

(ʌnˈraɪtʃəs )
Definitions
adjective1. a. sinful; wickedb. (as collective noun; preceded by the) ⇒ the unrighteous2. not fair or right; unjust
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unrighteously (unˈrighteously) adverbunrighteousness (unˈrighteousness) noun

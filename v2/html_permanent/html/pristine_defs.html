pristine

(ˈprɪstaɪn , -tiːn)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or involving the earliest period, state, etc; original2. pure; uncorrupted3. fresh, clean, and unspoiled ⇒ his pristine new car▶ USAGE The use of pristine to mean fresh, clean, and unspoiled is considered by some people to be incorrect

Word Origin

C15: from Latin pristinus primitive; related to prīmus first, prime

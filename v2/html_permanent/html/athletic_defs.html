athletic

(æθˈlɛtɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. physically fit or strong; muscular or active2. of, relating to, or suitable for an athlete or for athletics3. of or relating to a person with a muscular and well-proportioned body. See also somatotype
Derived forms
athˈletically adverbathˈleticism noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Athletic means relating to athletes and athletics. ⇒ They have been given college scholarships purely on athletic ability. ⇒ Most athletic activities are about individual effort.athletically (æθletɪkli ) adverb [usu ADV adj] ⇒ ...she's academically able and athletically outstanding.2. graded adjective & adjectiveAn athletic person is fit, and able to perform energetic movements easily. ⇒ Xandra is an athletic 36-year-old with a 21-year-old's body. ⇒ He was tall, with an athletic build.athletically graded adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj] ⇒ He jumped athletically out of the car.

Word Frequency

keep off

Definitions
verb1. to stay or cause to stay at a distance (from)2. (preposition) not to eat or drink or prevent from eating or drinking3. (preposition) to avoid or cause to avoid (a topic)4. (intr, adverb) not to start ⇒ the rain kept off all day

toilet paper

or toilet tissue
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. thin absorbent paper, often wound in a roll round a cardboard cylinder (toilet roll), used for cleaning oneself after defecation or urinationverb toilet-paper2. to cover private property (esp a house) in long strands of unwound toilet paper as a prank. Abbreviation: TP

efficacious

(ˌɛfɪˈkeɪʃəs )
Definitions
adjectivecapable of or successful in producing an intended result; effective as a means, remedy, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

efficaciously (ˌeffiˈcaciously) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Latin efficāx powerful, efficient, from efficere to achieve; see effect

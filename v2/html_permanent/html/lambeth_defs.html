Lambeth

(ˈlæmbəθ )
Definitions
noun1. a borough of S Greater London, on the Thames: contains Lambeth Palace (the London residence of the Archbishop of Canterbury). Pop: 268 500 (2003 est). Area: 27 sq km (11 sq miles)2. the Archbishop of Canterbury in his official capacity

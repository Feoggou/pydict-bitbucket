sweater

(ˈswɛtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a garment made of knitted or crocheted material covering the upper part of the body, esp a heavy one worn for warmthb. (as modifier) ⇒ a sweater dress2. a person or thing that sweats3. an employer who overworks and underpays his or her employees

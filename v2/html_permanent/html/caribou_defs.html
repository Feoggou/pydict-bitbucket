caribou

(ˈkærɪˌbuː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -bou or -bousa large deer, Rangifer tarandus, of Arctic regions of North America, having large branched antlers in the male and female: also occurs in Europe and Asia, where it is called a reindeer. Also called (Canadian): tuktu
Word origin
C18: from Canadian French, of Algonquian origin; compare Micmac khalibu literally: scratcher

privity

(ˈprɪvɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. a legally recognized relationship existing between two parties, such as that between lessor and lessee and between the parties to a contract ⇒ privity of estate, ⇒ privity of contract2. secret knowledge that is shared

Word Origin

C13: from Old French priveté

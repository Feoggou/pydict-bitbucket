paramount

(ˈpærəˌmaʊnt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of the greatest importance or significance; pre-eminentnoun2. rare a supreme ruler

Derived Forms

paraˌmountcy (ˈparaˌmountcy) or paraˌmouncy (ˈparaˌmouncy) nounparaˌmountly (ˈparaˌmountly) adverb

Word Origin

C16: via Anglo-Norman from Old French paramont, from par by + -amont above, from Latin ad montem to the mountain

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveSomething that is paramount or of paramount importance is more important than anything else. ⇒ The child's welfare must be seen as paramount. ⇒ Nitrogen is of paramount importance to life on earth. ⇒ ...the paramount need for a peaceful solution to the crisis.

domesticated

(dəˈmɛstɪˌkeɪtɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of animals or plants) brought or kept under control or cultivation ⇒ sheep, cattle, and other domesticated animals2. accustomed to home life ⇒ He's very domesticated., ⇒ Mum wasn't very domesticated.

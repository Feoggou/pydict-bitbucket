waking

(ˈweɪkɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. used to describe time when one is awakenoun2. the dreamlike state between waking and sleeping3. the state of being awake as opposed to asleep ⇒ Whatever she says, waking or sleeping, you make a note.

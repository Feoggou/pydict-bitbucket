amenities

(əˈmiːnɪtɪz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. useful or pleasant facilities or services ⇒ Hotel amenities include health clubs and conference facilities., ⇒ The hotel has very good amenities., ⇒ Many of the region's people lack such basic amenities such as reliable drinking water, ⇒ public amenities such as libraries, ⇒ There are some local amenities, but larger ones can be found in Newmains, one to two miles away., ⇒ Large room, close to all amenities, share with one other.2. See amenity

Definitions
COBUILD Key Words for Hospitality
noun(Hospitality (hotel): Hotel facilities) Amenities are things such as stores or sports facilities that are provided for people's convenience, enjoyment, or comfort. ⇒ The hotel amenities include health clubs, conference facilities, and banqueting rooms. ⇒ The city has tried to update its tourist amenities by building new hotels and information centers. ⇒ Travelers stay at the hotel for its luxurious amenities such as the spa and hot tub.

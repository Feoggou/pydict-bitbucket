method

(ˈmɛθəd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a way of proceeding or doing something, esp a systematic or regular one2. orderliness of thought, action, etc3. (often plural) the techniques or arrangement of work for a particular field or subject4. bell-ringing any of several traditional sets of changes. See major (sense 19), minor (sense 8)

Word Origin

C16: via French from Latin methodus, from Greek methodos, literally: a going after, from meta- after + hodos way

Method

(ˈmɛθəd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(sometimes not capital)a. a technique of acting based on the theories of Stanislavsky, in which the actor bases his or her role on the inner motivation of the character being playedb. (as modifier) ⇒ a Method actor

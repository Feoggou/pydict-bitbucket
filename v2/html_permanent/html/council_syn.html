council

noun

  1. committee, governing body, board, panel, quango, jamaat • The city council has voted almost unanimously in favour of the proposal.
  2. governing body, house, parliament, congress, cabinet, ministry, diet, panel, assembly, chamber, convention, synod, conclave, convocation, conference, runanga (New Zealand) • The powers of the King had been handed over temporarily to a council of ministers.

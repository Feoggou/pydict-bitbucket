Definitions
COBUILD Key Words for Electrical Engineering
noun(Electrical engineering: Semiconductor and electronic circuitry) A detector is an instrument to detect the unbalance in a bridge circuit. ⇒ The further down the chain toward the output that a detector is placed, the more effect the non-linear distortion will have on the detection function. ⇒ The circuit acts as a phase detector by comparing the frequency of an oscillator with an incoming signal and then feeding back the output to keep the oscillator in phase with the incoming frequency. ⇒ A detector is an instrument to detect the unbalance in a bridge circuit.

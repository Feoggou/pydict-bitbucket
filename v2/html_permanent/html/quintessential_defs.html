Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Quintessential means representing a perfect or typical example of something. [formal] ⇒ This was quintessential Midwestern farming country. ⇒ Everybody thinks of him as the quintessential New Yorker.quintessentially graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj] ⇒ It is a familiar, and quintessentially British, ritual.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Quintessential means representing the central nature of something. [formal] ⇒ ...the quintessential charm of his songs.

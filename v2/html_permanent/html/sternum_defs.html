sternum

(ˈstɜːnəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -na (-nə) or -nums1. (in man) a long flat vertical bone, situated in front of the thorax, to which are attached the collarbone and the first seven pairs of ribs. Nontechnical name: breastbone2. the corresponding part in many other vertebrates3. a cuticular plate covering the ventral surface of a body segment of an arthropod. Compare tergum

Derived Forms

sternal (ˈsternal) adjective

Word Origin

C17: via New Latin from Greek sternon breastbone

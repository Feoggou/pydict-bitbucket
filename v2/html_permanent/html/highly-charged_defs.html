highly charged

(ˈhaɪlɪ tʃɑːdʒd) or highly-charged
Definitions
adjectivesomething which is highly charged, such as a situation, is extremely tense and emotional, and likely to make people have very strong reactions ⇒ Such was the highly-charged atmosphere, after 17 arrests in 48 hours., ⇒ The atmosphere was as highly charged as ever., ⇒ the highly-charged atmosphere of the Middle East, ⇒ the highly-charged debate on government plans to restrict the right to jury trial

elation

(ɪˈleɪʃən )
Definitions
nounjoyfulness or exaltation of spirit, as from success, pleasure, or relief; high spirits

elation

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪleɪʃən ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounElation is a feeling of great happiness and excitement about something that has happened. ⇒ His supporters have reacted to the news with elation.

lumpy

(ˈlʌmpɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: lumpier or lumpiest1. full of or having lumps2. (esp of the sea) rough3. (of a person) heavy or bulky
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lumpily (ˈlumpily) adverblumpiness (ˈlumpiness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is lumpy contains lumps or is covered with lumps. ⇒ When the rice isn't cooked properly it goes lumpy and gooey. ⇒ Many women, especially as they get older, develop lumpy veins in their legs.

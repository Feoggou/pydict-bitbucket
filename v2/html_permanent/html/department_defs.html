department

(dɪˈpɑːtmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a specialized division of a large concern, such as a business, store, or university ⇒ the geography department2. a major subdivision or branch of the administration of a government3. a branch or subdivision of learning ⇒ physics is a department of science4. a territorial and administrative division in several countries, such as France5. informal a specialized sphere of knowledge, skill, or activity ⇒ wine-making is my wife's department
Derived forms
departmental (ˌdiːpɑːtˈmɛntəl ) adjectiveˌdepartmentally (ˌdepartˈmentally) adverb
Word origin
C18: from French département, from départir to divide; see depart

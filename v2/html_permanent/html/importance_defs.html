importance

(ɪmˈpɔːtəns )
Definitions
noun1. the state of being important; significance2. social status; standing; esteem ⇒ a man of importance3. obsoletea. meaning or significationb. an important matterc. importunity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

importance

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmpɔːʳtəns ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounThe importance of something is its quality of being significant, valued, or necessary in a particular situation. [+ of] ⇒ We have always stressed the importance of economic reform. ⇒ Safety is of paramount importance.2. uncount nounImportance means having influence, power, or status.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

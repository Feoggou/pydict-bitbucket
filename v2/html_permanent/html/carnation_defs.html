carnation

(kɑːˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: clove pink a Eurasian caryophyllaceous plant, Dianthus caryophyllus, cultivated in many varieties for its white, pink, or red flowers, which have a fragrant scent of cloves2. the flower of this plant3. a. a pink or reddish-pink colourb. (as adjective) ⇒ a carnation dress4. (often plural) a flesh tint in painting
Word origin
C16: from French: flesh colour, from Late Latin carnātiō fleshiness, from Latin carō flesh

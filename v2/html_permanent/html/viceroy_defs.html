viceroy

(ˈvaɪsrɔɪ )
Definitions
nouna governor of a colony, country, or province who acts for and rules in the name of his or her sovereign or government ▶ Related adjective: viceregal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

viceroyship (ˈviceroyˌship) noun

Word Origin

C16: from French, from vice3 + roy king, from Latin rex

(ədmaɪərɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]An admiring expression shows that you like or respect someone or something. ⇒ He cast her an admiring glance. ⇒ Within moments, he'd attracted an admiring audience.admiringly graded adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ He glanced admiringly at the design.2. → see also admire

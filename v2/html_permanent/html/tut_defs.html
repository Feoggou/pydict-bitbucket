tut

Powered by COBUILD
(tʌt ) for learners of English
Word forms: tuts , tutting , tutted
Definitions
1. Tut is used in writing to represent the sound that you make with your tongue touching the top of your mouth when you want to indicate disapproval, annoyance, or sympathy.2. verbIf you tut, you make a sound with your tongue touching the top of your mouth when you want to indicate disapproval, annoyance, or sympathy. [V] ⇒ He tutted and shook his head.

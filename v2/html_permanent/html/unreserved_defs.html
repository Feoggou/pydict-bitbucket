unreserved

(ˌʌnrɪˈzɜːvd )
Definitions
adjective1. without reserve; having an open manner2. without reservation3. not booked or bookable
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unreservedly (ˌʌnrɪˈzɜːvɪdlɪ ) adverbunreservedness (ˌunreˈservedness) noun

Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]An unreserved opinion or statement is one that expresses a feeling or opinion completely and without any doubts. ⇒ Charles displays unreserved admiration for his grandfather. ⇒ Jones' lawyers are seeking an unreserved apology from the newspaper.unreservedly (ʌnrɪzɜːʳvɪdli ) adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ We apologise unreservedly for any imputation of incorrect behaviour by Mr Taylor.

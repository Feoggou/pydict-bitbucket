prevalent

(ˈprɛvələnt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. widespread or current2. superior in force or power; predominant

Derived Forms

prevalence (ˈprevalence) or prevalentness (ˈprevalentness) nounprevalently (ˈprevalently) adverb

Word Origin

C16 (in the sense: powerful): from Latin praevalens very strong, from praevalēre: see prevail

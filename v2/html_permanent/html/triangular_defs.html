triangular

(traɪˈæŋɡjʊlə )
Definitions
adjective1. Also: trigonal of, shaped like, or relating to a triangle; having three corners or sides2. of or involving three participants, pieces, or units3. mathematics having a base shaped like a triangle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

triangularity (traɪˌæŋɡjʊˈlærɪtɪ ) nountriangularly (triˈangularly) adverb

Definitions

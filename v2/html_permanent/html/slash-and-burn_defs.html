slash and burn

(slæʃ ənd bɜːn)slash-and-burn

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
modifier1. agriculture of a farming method in which trees and crops are cut and burned in order to plant new crops ⇒ Traditional slash and burn farming methods have exhausted the soil.2. drastic or ruthless ⇒ A slash-and-burn diplomatic mentality left Afghanistan to the tender mercies of the Taliban.

slash-and-burn

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjectivedenoting a short-term method of cultivation in which land is cleared by destroying and burning trees and other vegetation for temporary agricultural use

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [usu N n]Slash and burn is a method of farming that involves clearing land by destroying and burning all the trees and plants on it, farming there for a short time, and then moving on to clear a new piece of land. ⇒ Traditional slash and burn farming methods have exhausted the soil.

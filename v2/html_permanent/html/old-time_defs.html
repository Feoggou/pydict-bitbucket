old-time

Definitions
adjective(prenominal) of or relating to a former time; old-fashioned ⇒ old-time dancing

old-time for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]If you describe something as old-time, you mean that it was common or popular in the past but is not common or popular now. ⇒ ...an old-time dance hall which still has a tea dance on Monday afternoons.2. adjective [ADJ n]You can use old-time before the name of someone's job to show that they do their job in the way it was done in the past. ⇒ The effect of that singing, as the old-time reporters used to say, was electrical. ⇒ ...like an old-time sailor climbing the rigging.

forgiveness

(fəˈɡɪvnɪs )
Definitions
noun1. the act of forgiving or the state of being forgiven2. willingness to forgive
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

(fəʳgɪvnəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIf you ask for forgiveness, you ask to be forgiven for something wrong that you have done. ⇒ I offered up a short prayer for forgiveness. ⇒ ...a spirit of forgiveness and national reconciliation.

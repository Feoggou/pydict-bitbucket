Hindustani

, Hindoostani (ˌhɪndʊˈstɑːnɪ )Hindostani (ˌhɪndəʊˈstɑːnɪ )
Definitions
noun1. the dialect of Hindi spoken in Delhi: used as a lingua franca throughout India2. a group of languages or dialects consisting of all spoken forms of Hindi and Urdu considered togetheradjective3. of or relating to these languages or Hindustan

retrospect

(ˈrɛtrəʊˌspɛkt )
Definitions
noun1. the act of surveying things past (often in the phrase in retrospect)verb archaic2. to contemplate (anything past); look back on (something)3. (intransitive; often foll by to) to refer
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin retrōspicere to look back, from retro- + specere to look

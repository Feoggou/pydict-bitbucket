ABC

Powered by COBUILD
(eɪ biː siː )
Word forms: ABCs
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. singular nounThe ABC of a subject or activity is the parts of it that you have to learn first because they are the most important and basic. [+ of] ⇒ ...the ABC of Marxism.2. countable nounChildren who have learned their ABC or their ABCs have learned to recognize, write, or say the alphabet. [informal]

hitter

(ˈhɪtə )
Definitions
noun1. informal a boxer who has a hard punch rather than skill or finesse2. a person who hits something

hitter

Powered by COBUILD
(hɪtəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: hitters
Definitions
1. countable noun [adj N]In sports, you can use hitter to say how good someone is at hitting the ball. ⇒ The Georgian, aged 19, is not one of the game's big hitters.2. countable noun [adj N]If you refer to someone such as a politician or a businessman as a heavy hitter or a big hitter, you mean that they are powerful and influential. ⇒ ...friendships with heavy hitters like European industrialist Carlo De Benedetti.

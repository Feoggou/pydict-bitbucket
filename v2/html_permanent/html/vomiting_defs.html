vomiting

(ˈvɒmɪtɪŋ)
Definitions
nounmedicine the act of ejecting the contents of the stomach through the mouth as the result of involuntary muscular spasms of the stomach and oesophagus ⇒ Nausea, diarrhoea, and vomiting may accompany migraine.

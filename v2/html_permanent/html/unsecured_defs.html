unsecured

(ˌʌnsɪˈkjʊəd )
Definitions
adjective1. financea. (of a loan, etc) secured only against general assets and not against a specific assetb. (of a creditor) having no security against a specific asset and with a claim inferior to those of secure creditors2. not made secure; loose
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

unsecured for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnsɪkjʊəʳd )
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]Unsecured is used to describe loans or debts that are not guaranteed by a particular asset such as a person's home. OPPOSITE: secured ⇒ We can arrange unsecured loans for any amount from £500 to £7,500.

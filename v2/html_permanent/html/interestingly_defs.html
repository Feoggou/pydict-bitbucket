Definitions
graded adverb & adverbYou use interestingly to introduce a piece of information that you think is interesting or unexpected. ⇒ Interestingly enough, a few weeks later, Benjamin remarried. ⇒ Interestingly, it is not clear which solution the prime minister favors.

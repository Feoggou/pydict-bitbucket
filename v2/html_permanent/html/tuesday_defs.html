Tuesday

(ˈtjuːzdɪ , -deɪ)
Definitions
nounthe third day of the week; second day of the working week
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English tīwesdæg, literally: day of Tiw, representing Latin diēs Martis day of Mars; compare Old Norse tӯsdagr, Old High German zīostag; see Tiu, day

Definitions
variable nounTuesday is the day after Monday and before Wednesday. ⇒ He phoned on Tuesday, just before you came. ⇒ Talks are likely to start next Tuesday. ⇒ On Tuesdays and Saturdays the market comes to town. ⇒ They left Zeebrugge on Tuesday evening.

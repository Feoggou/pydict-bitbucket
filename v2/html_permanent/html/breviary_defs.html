breviary

(ˈbriːvjərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ries1. Roman Catholic Church a book of psalms, hymns, prayers, etc, to be recited daily by clerics in major orders and certain members of religious orders as part of the divine office2. a similar book in the Orthodox Church
Word origin
C16: from Latin breviārium an abridged version, from breviāre to shorten, from brevis short

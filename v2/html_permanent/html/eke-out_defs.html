eke out

Definitions
verb (tr, adverb)1. to make (a supply) last, esp by frugal use ⇒ they eked out what little food was left2. to support (existence) with difficulty and effort3. to add to (something insufficient), esp with effort ⇒ to eke out an income with evening work

pontifical

(pɒnˈtɪfɪkəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of a pontiff, the pope, or a bishop2. having an excessively authoritative manner; pompousnoun3. Roman Catholic Church, Church of England a book containing the prayers and ritual instructions for ceremonies restricted to a bishop

Derived Forms

pontifically (ponˈtifically) adverb

Similar Words

papal, ecclesiastical, apostolic, prelatic
pompous, pretentious, bloated, dogmatic, condescending, imperious, overbearing, portentous, magisterial, self-important

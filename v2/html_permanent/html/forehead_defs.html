forehead

(ˈfɒrɪd , ˈfɔːˌhɛd )
Definitions
nounthe part of the face between the natural hairline and the eyes, formed skeletally by the frontal bone of the skull; brow ▶ Related adjective: frontal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English forhēafod; related to Old Frisian forhāfd, Middle Low German vorhōved

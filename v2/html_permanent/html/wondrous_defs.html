wondrous

(ˈwʌndrəs ) archaic or literary
Definitions
adjective1. exciting wonder; marvellousadverb2. (intensifier) ⇒ it is wondrous cold
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

wondrously (ˈwondrously) adverbwondrousness (ˈwondrousness) noun

wondrous for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(wʌndrəs )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something as wondrous, you mean it is strange and beautiful or impressive. [literary] ⇒ We were driven across this wondrous vast land of lakes and forests.wondrously graded adverb [usu ADV adj/adv] ⇒ Ever since they had set eyes on each other they had been wondrously happy.

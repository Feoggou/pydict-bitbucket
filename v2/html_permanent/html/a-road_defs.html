A road

(eɪ rəʊd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(in Britain) a major road that is narrower than a motorway but wider and straighter than a B road ⇒ a busy A road, ⇒ Rural A roads are far more dangerous than motorways.

A-road

Powered by COBUILD
Word forms: A-roads
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIn some countries, an A-road is a major road. A-roads are narrower than motorways but are wider and straighter than B-roads.

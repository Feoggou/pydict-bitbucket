fairness

Powered by COBUILD
(feəʳnəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounFairness is the quality of being reasonable, right, and just. ⇒ He says the new document will guarantee fairness for blacks in Georgia. ⇒ ...concern about the fairness of the election campaign.2. →  in (all) fairness (to)

Egan said, `In fairness this might not be the Colonel's doing at all. Higgins, Jack A SEASON IN HELLI think in all fairness you should let him go, or you'll end up dragging him down with you. Clive Barker SACRAMENTIn fairness , you've got to understand her psychological state. Anthony Masters CASCADES - THE DAY OF THE DEAD

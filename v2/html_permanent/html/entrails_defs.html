entrails

(ˈɛntreɪlz )
Definitions
plural noun1. the internal organs of a person or animal; intestines; guts2. the innermost parts of anything
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old French entrailles, from Medieval Latin intrālia, changed from Latin interānea intestines, ultimately from inter between

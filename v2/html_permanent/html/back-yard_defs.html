back yard

or backyard (ˌbækˈjɑːd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an area, usually paved, at the rear of a building2. the immediate surroundings or locality of a person, organization, country, etc ⇒ an area that the United States regards as its own backyard3. See in one's own back yard

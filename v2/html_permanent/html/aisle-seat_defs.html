aisle seat

(aɪl siːt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna seat, esp on a plane, situated at the end of a row, adjacent to the aisle ⇒ Would you prefer an aisle seat or a window seat?
Example sentences containing 'aisle seat'
She took her bag from the overhead compartment, then sat down on the edge of the aisle seat waiting for Dennison to reappear. MacNeill, Alastair THE DEVIL'S DOORAs he passed Fergusson, sitting in an aisle seat , he fiddled with his glasses and dropped them. Forbes, Colin COVER STORY

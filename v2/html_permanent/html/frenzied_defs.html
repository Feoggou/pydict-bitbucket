Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Frenzied activities or actions are wild, excited, and uncontrolled. frantic ⇒ ...the frenzied activity of the general election. ⇒ The man was stabbed to death in a frenzied attack.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

For several seconds there was no response to her frenzied ringing, then she heard footsteps on the other side.

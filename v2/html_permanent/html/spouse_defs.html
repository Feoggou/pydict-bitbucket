spouse

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (spaʊs , spaʊz )1. a person's partner in marriage ▶ Related adjective: spousalverb (spaʊz , spaʊs )2. (transitive) obsolete to marry

Word Origin

C12: from Old French spus (masculine), spuse (feminine), from Latin sponsus, sponsa betrothed man or woman, from spondēre to promise solemnly

Definitions

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is adventurous is willing to take risks and to try new methods. Something that is adventurous involves new things or ideas. OPPOSITE: unadventurous ⇒ Warren was an adventurous businessman. ⇒ The menu could have been more adventurous.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is adventurous is eager to visit new places and have new experiences. OPPOSITE: unadventurous ⇒ He had always wanted an adventurous life in the tropics.

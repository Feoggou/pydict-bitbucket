uncleared

(ʌnˈklɪəd)
Definitions
adjective1. (of clutter or mess) not cleared up or away ⇒ the stench of uncleared rubbish2. not freed of clutter; not made clear of mess or obscurity ⇒ complaints about uncleared sidewalks3. not cleared of a criminal charge4. (of money) not cleared; that has not been passed through a clearing-house5. (of a bill or debt) that has not been settled or paid6. (of a plan) not cleared or approved by someone in authority7. (of people) not having been given security clearance ⇒ after checking that no uncleared persons were in the office8. (of people) not established as safe to be given knowledge of secure information ⇒ after checking that no uncleared persons were in the office9. (of information) that is considered too dangerous or of too great importance to security to be released ⇒ uncleared intelligence information

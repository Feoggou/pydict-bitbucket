moiety

(ˈmɔɪɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties archaic1. a half2. one of two parts or divisions of something

Word Origin

C15: from Old French moitié, from Latin mediētās middle, from medius
Example sentences containing 'moiety'
Marriage on O is a foursome, the sedoretu - a man and a woman from the Morning moiety and a man and a woman from the Evening moiety. Ursula K. Le Guin THE BIRTHDAY OF THE WORLD

Similar Words

half, part, share, piece, portion, fifty percent

debut

(ˈdeɪbjuː , ˈdɛbjuː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. the first public appearance of an actor, musician, etc, or the first public presentation of a showb. (as modifier) ⇒ debut album2. the presentation of a debutanteverb (intransitive)3. to make a debut
Word origin
C18: from French début, from Old French desbuter to play first (hence: make one's first appearance), from des- de- + but goal, target; see butt2

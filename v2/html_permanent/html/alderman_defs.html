alderman

(ˈɔːldəmən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -men1. (in England and Wales until 1974) one of the senior members of a local council, elected by other councillors2. (in the US, Canada, Australia, etc) a member of the governing body of a municipality3. history a variant spelling of ealdorman
Derived forms
aldermanic (ˌɔːldəˈmænɪk ) adjectiveˈaldermanry nounˈaldermanˌship noun
Word origin
Old English aldormann, from ealdor chief (comparative of eald old) + mann man

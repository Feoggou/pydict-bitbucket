Charles III

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  known as Charles the Fat. 839–888 ad, Holy Roman Emperor (881–887) and, as Charles II, king of France (884–887). He briefly reunited the empire of Charlemagne2.  1716–88, king of Spain (1759–88), who curbed the power of the Church and tried to modernize his country

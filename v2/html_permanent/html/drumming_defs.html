drumming

(ˈdrʌmɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. music the action and sound of playing the drums ⇒ The first thing to learn about drumming is to keep a steady beat., ⇒ His drumming is constantly inventive., ⇒ the sound of drumming2. the sound of continuous beating ⇒ The drumming of the fingers became heavier., ⇒ He pointed up to the roof, through which the steady drumming of rain could be heard.3. the feeling of continuous beating ⇒ His mouth was dry and he felt a drumming in his temples.

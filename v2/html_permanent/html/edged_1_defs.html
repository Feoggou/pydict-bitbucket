edged

(edʒd ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveIf something is edged with a particular thing, that thing forms a border around it. [+ with] ⇒ ...a large lawn edged with flowers and shrubs. ⇒ ...blank pages edged in black. [Also + in]Edged is also a combining form. ⇒ ...a lace-edged handkerchief.

bewilder

(bɪˈwɪldə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to confuse utterly; puzzle2. archaic to cause to become lost
Derived forms
bewilderˌment (beˈwilderˌment) noun
Word origin
C17: see be-, wilder

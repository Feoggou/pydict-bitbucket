vastly

Powered by COBUILD
(vɑːstli , væst-) for learners of English
Definitions
adverbVastly means to an extremely great degree or extent. ⇒ The jury has heard two vastly different accounts of what happened. ⇒ ...cars that are vastly more competitive.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

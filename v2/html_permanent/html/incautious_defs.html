Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnkɔːʃəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you say that someone is incautious, you are criticizing them because they do or say something without thinking or planning. [formal, disapproval] ⇒ In case you think I was incautious, take a look at the map.incautiously graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Incautiously, Crook had asked where she was.

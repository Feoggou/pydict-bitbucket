(ɪndʌldʒənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are indulgent, you treat a person with special kindness, often in a way that is not good for them. ⇒ His indulgent mother was willing to let him do anything he wanted.indulgently graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj] ⇒ Ned smiled at him indulgently.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

shining

(ˈʃaɪnɪŋ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. used to say that an achievement or quality is a very good one which should be greatly admired; outstanding ⇒ a shining example to someone, ⇒ The space-rocket project has been a shining success., ⇒ She is a shining example to us all.2. very bright and clear due to reflecting light; gleaming ⇒ surrounded by shining aluminum machines, ⇒ her shining blue eyes

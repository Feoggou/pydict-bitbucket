bird of prey

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna bird, such as a hawk, eagle, or owl, that hunts and kills other animals, esp vertebrates, for food. It has strong talons and a sharp hooked bill ▶ Related adjective: raptorial

bird of prey

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
a bird that preys on others for food▷ See prey

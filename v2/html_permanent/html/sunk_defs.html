sunk

(sʌŋk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. a past participle of sinkadjective2. informal with all hopes dashed; ruined ⇒ if the police come while we're opening the safe, we'll be sunk

sunk

Powered by COBUILD
(sʌŋk )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. Sunk is the past participle of sink.2. adjective [v-link ADJ]If you say that someone is sunk, you mean that they have no hope of avoiding trouble or failure. [informal] ⇒ Without him we'd be well and truly sunk.

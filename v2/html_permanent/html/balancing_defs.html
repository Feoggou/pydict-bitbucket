balancing

(ˈbælənsɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the process of achieving or maintaining equilibrium ⇒ For children, reading is a balancing of understanding meaning and recognizing letters and sounds., ⇒ a balancing of global power2. accounting, finance achieving equality of debit and credit totals in an account ⇒ balancing of accounts, ⇒ balancing of the books

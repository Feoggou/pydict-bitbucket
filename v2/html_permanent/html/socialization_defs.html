socialization

or socialisation (ˌsəʊʃəlaɪˈzeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. psychology the modification from infancy of an individual's behaviour to conform with the demands of social life2. the act of socializing or the state of being socialized

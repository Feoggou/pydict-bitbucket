gild1

(ɡɪld )
Definitions
verbWord forms: gilds, gilding, gilded or gilt (ɡɪlt ) (transitive)1. to cover with or as if with gold2. See gild the lily3. to give a falsely attractive or valuable appearance to4. archaic to smear with blood
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

gilder (ˈgilder) noun

Word Origin

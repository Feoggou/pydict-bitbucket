chit1

(tʃɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a voucher for a sum of money owed, esp for food or drink2. Also called: chitty (ˈtʃɪtɪ ) mainly Britisha. a note or memorandumb. a requisition or receipt
Word origin
C18: from earlier chitty, from Hindi cittha note, from Sanskrit citra brightly-coloured

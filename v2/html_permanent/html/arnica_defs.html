arnica

(ˈɑːnɪkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any N temperate or arctic plant of the genus Arnica, typically having yellow flowers: family Asteraceae (composites)2. the tincture of the dried flower heads of any of these plants, esp A. montana, used in treating bruises
Word origin
C18: from New Latin, of unknown origin

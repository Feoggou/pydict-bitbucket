measure out

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (tr, adverb)1. to pour or dole out ⇒ they measure out a pint of fluid2. to administer; mete out ⇒ they measured out harsh punishments

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrasal verbIf you measure out a certain amount of something, you measure that amount and take it or mark it because it is the amount that you want or need. [V P n] ⇒ I'd already measured out the ingredients. [V P n (not pron)] ⇒ He was on the front lawn, measuring out two circles. [Also V n P] →  measure

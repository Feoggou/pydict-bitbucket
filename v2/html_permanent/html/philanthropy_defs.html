philanthropy

(fɪˈlænθrəpɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -pies1. the practice of performing charitable or benevolent actions2. love of mankind in general

Derived Forms

philanthropist (phiˈlanthropist) or philanthrope (ˈfɪlənˌθrəʊp ) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin philanthrōpia, from Greek: love of mankind, from philos loving + anthrōpos man

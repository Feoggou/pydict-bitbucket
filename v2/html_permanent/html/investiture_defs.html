investiture

(ɪnˈvɛstɪtʃə )
Definitions
noun1. the act of presenting with a title or with the robes and insignia of an office or rank2. (in feudal society) the formal bestowal of the possessory right to a fief or other benefice3. a less common word for investment (sense 7)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

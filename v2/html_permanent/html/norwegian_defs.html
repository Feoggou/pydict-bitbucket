Norwegian

(nɔːˈwiːdʒən )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Norway, its language, or its peoplenoun2. any of the various North Germanic languages of Norway. See also Nynorsk, Bokmål. Compare Norse3. a native, citizen, or inhabitant of Norway

Norwegian for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(nɔːʳwiːdʒən )
Word forms: Norwegians
Definitions
1. adjectiveNorwegian means belonging or relating to Norway, or to its people, language, or culture. ⇒ The main road from Murmansk to the Norwegian border is still closed to foreigners. ⇒ I stood there breathing in the fresh Norwegian air.2. countable nounA Norwegian is a person who comes from Norway. ⇒ Many Norwegians feel that Norway is a culturally young country.3. uncount nounNorwegian is the language spoken in Norway. ⇒ It is interesting that Grainger spoke Norwegian.

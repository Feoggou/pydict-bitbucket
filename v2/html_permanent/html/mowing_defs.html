mowing

(ˈməʊɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of cutting down (grass, crops, etc) with a hand implement or machineadjective2. of or relating to cutting down (grass, crops, etc) with a hand implement or machine ⇒ mowing machine

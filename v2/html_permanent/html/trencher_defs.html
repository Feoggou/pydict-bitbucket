trencher1

(ˈtrɛntʃə )
Definitions
noun1. (esp formerly) a wooden board on which food was served or cut2. Also called: trencher cap another name for mortarboard (sense 1)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14 trenchour knife, plate for carving on, from Old French trencheoir, from trenchier to cut; see trench

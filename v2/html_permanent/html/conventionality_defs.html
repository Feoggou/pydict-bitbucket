conventionality

(kənˌvɛnʃəˈnælɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the quality or characteristic of being conventional, esp in behaviour, thinking, etc2. (often plural) something conventional, esp a normal or accepted rule of behaviour; propriety
Example sentences containing 'conventionality'
Those coddled straight-jackets, walled in by their own conventionality ? Nicola Barker BEHINDLINGSThe dual burdens of conventionality and rationality fell away when I realized I might die, I really might die. Patricia Gaffney THE SAVING GRACES

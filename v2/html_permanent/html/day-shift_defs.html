day shift

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a group of workers who work a shift during the daytime in an industry or occupation where a night shift or a back shift is also worked2. the period worked▷ See also back shift

The next day was Wednesday, but Darren was on the day shift. Skelton, Alison Scott AN OLDER WOMANJust before the day shift ended one of the nurses came back to Amy.

initially

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnɪʃəli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [ADV with v]Initially means soon after the beginning of a process or situation, rather than in the middle or at the end of it. ⇒ Forecasters say the gales may not be as bad as they initially predicted. ⇒ Initially, they were wary of Simon.

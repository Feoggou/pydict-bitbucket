Word forms: further off , furthest off
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a moment in time as far off, you mean that it is a long time from the present, either in the past or the future. ⇒ In those far off days it never entered anyone's mind that she could be Prime Minister. ⇒ Agreement is even further off.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as far off, you mean that it is a long distance from you or from a particular place. ⇒ ...stars in far-off galaxies.Far off is also an adverb. ⇒ The band was playing far off in their blue and yellow uniforms.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe God or a person in a position of authority as merciful, you mean that they show kindness and forgiveness to people. ⇒ ...a merciful God. ⇒ We can only hope the court is merciful.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe an event or situation as merciful, you mean that it is a good thing, especially because it stops someone's suffering or discomfort. ⇒ We were told when he was taken to hospital that his injuries were so severe death would be merciful. ⇒ Eventually the session came to a merciful end.

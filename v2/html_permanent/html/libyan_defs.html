Libyan

(ˈlɪbɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to Libya, its people, or its languagenoun2. a native or inhabitant of Libya3. the extinct Hamitic language of ancient Libya
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions

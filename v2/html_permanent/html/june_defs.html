June

(dʒuːn )
Definitions
nounthe sixth month of the year, consisting of 30 days
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English iunius, from Latin junius, probably from Junius name of Roman gens

Definitions
variable nounJune is the sixth month of the year in the Western calendar. ⇒ He spent two and a half weeks with us in June 1986. ⇒ I am moving out on 5 June. ⇒ Last June I decided to take a trip to Marbella.

détente - French

(deɪˈtɑːnt , detɑ̃t)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe relaxing or easing of tension, esp between nations
Word origin
French, literally: a loosening, from Old French destendre to release, from tendre to stretch

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft a N]Detente is a state of friendly relations between two countries when previously there had been problems between them. [formal] ⇒ ...their desire to pursue a policy of detente. ⇒ They have made the first move towards a detente.

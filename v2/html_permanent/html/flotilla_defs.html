flotilla

(fləˈtɪlə )
Definitions
nouna small fleet or a fleet of small vessels
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Spanish flota fleet, from French flotte, ultimately from Old Norse floti

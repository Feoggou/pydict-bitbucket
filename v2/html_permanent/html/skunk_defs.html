skunk

(skʌŋk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural skunks or skunk1. any of various American musteline mammals of the subfamily Mephitinae, esp Mephitis mephitis (striped skunk), typically having a black and white coat and bushy tail: they eject an unpleasant-smelling fluid from the anal gland when attacked2. informal a despicable person3. slang a strain of cannabis smoked for its exceptionally powerful psychoactive propertiesverb4. (transitive) US and Canadian slang to defeat overwhelmingly in a game
Word origin
C17: from Algonquian; compare Abnaki segākw skunk

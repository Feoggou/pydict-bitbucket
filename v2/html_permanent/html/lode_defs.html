lode

(ləʊd )
Definitions
noun1. a deposit of valuable ore occurring between definite limits in the surrounding rock; vein2. a deposit of metallic ore filling a fissure in the surrounding rock
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English lād course. Compare load

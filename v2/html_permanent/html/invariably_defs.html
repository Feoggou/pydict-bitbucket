invariably

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnveəriəbli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [ADV with v]If something invariably happens or is invariably true, it always happens or is always true. ⇒ They almost invariably get it wrong. ⇒ Their teamwork was invariably good. ⇒ Invariably, he keeps the refrigerator well stocked.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

rabi

(ˈrʌbɪ )
Definitions
noun(in Pakistan, India, etc) a crop that is harvested at the end of winter. Compare kharif
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Urdu: spring crop, from Arabic rabī' spring

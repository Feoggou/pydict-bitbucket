importune

(ɪmˈpɔːtjuːn )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to harass with persistent requests; demand of (someone) insistently2. to beg for persistently; request with insistence3. obsoletea. to anger or annoyb. to force; impel
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

importuner (imˈportuner) nounimportunity (ˌimporˈtunity) or importunacy (imˈportunacy) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin importūnus tiresome, from im- in-1 + -portūnus as in opportūnus opportune

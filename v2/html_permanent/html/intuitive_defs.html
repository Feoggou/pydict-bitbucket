intuitive

(ɪnˈtjuːɪtɪv )
Definitions
adjective1. resulting from intuition ⇒ an intuitive awareness2. of, characterized by, or involving intuition
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

intuitively (inˈtuitively) adverbintuitiveness (inˈtuitiveness) noun

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪntjuːətɪv , US -tuː- ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you have an intuitive idea or feeling about something, you feel that it is true although you have no evidence or proof of it. ⇒ A positive pregnancy test soon confirmed her intuitive feelings.intuitively graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj] ⇒ He seemed to know intuitively that I must be missing my mother. ⇒ Some of the ideas are very intriguing and sound intuitively plausible.

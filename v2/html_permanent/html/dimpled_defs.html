dimpled

(ˈdɪmpld)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having small natural dents or creases in the flesh, esp on the cheeks or chin ⇒ a man with a dimpled chin2. with slight depressions on the surface ⇒ a cup holder with a dimpled surface3. (of glass) with bubbles or dents ⇒ a long dimpled glass tube

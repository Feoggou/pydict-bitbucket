Sodom

(ˈsɒdəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Old Testament a city destroyed by God for its wickedness that, with Gomorrah, traditionally typifies depravity (Genesis 19:24)2. this city as representing homosexuality3. any place notorious for depravity

rectangular

(rɛkˈtæŋɡjʊlə )
Definitions
adjective1. shaped like a rectangle2. having or relating to right angles3. mutually perpendicular ⇒ rectangular coordinates4. having a base or section shaped like a rectangle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

rectangularity (recˌtanguˈlarity) nounrectangularly (recˈtangularly) adverb

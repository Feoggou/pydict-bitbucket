opener

(ˈəʊpənə )
Definitions
noun1. an instrument used to open sealed containers such as tins or bottles ⇒ a bottle opener2. a person who opens, esp the player who makes the first bid or play3. the first or opening section or episode in a series4. US the first song, act, etc, in a variety show5. (plural) a start; beginning (esp in the phrase for openers)

(laɪflaɪk ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is lifelike has the appearance of being alive. ⇒ ...a lifelike doll.2. graded adjectiveA lifelike work of art is so skilfully done that it appears very like the person or thing that it is supposed to represent. realistic ⇒ The painting depicted a hall so lifelike that a casual observer might believe himself to be looking through an open door.

untried

(ʌnˈtraɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. not tried, attempted, or proved; untested2. not tried by a judge or court

untried for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌntraɪd )
Definitions
adjectiveIf someone or something is untried, they have not yet experienced certain situations or have not yet been tried out, so you do not know what they will be like. ⇒ He was young and untried, with no reputation of his own. ⇒ ...a long legal battle through untried areas of law.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

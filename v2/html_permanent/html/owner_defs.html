owner for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(oʊnəʳ )
Word forms: owners
Definitions
1. countable nounThe owner of something is the person to whom it belongs. [+ of] ⇒ The owner of the store was sweeping his floor when I walked in. ⇒ Every pet owner knows their animal has its own personality. ⇒ New owners will have to wait until September before moving in.2. → See also home owner, landowner

Nordic

(ˈnɔːdɪk )
Definitions
adjectiveof, relating to, or belonging to a subdivision of the Caucasoid race typified by the tall blond blue-eyed long-headed inhabitants of N Britain, Scandinavia, N Germany, and the Netherlands
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from French nordique, from nord north

nordic

(ˈnɔːdɪk )
Definitions
adjective1. skiing of or relating to competitions in cross-country racing and ski-jumping. Compare alpine (sense 4)2. (of recreational walking) incorporating the use of poles that resemble ski poles to aid movement
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

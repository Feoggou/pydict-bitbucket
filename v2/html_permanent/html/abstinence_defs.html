abstinence

(ˈæbstɪnəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or practice of refraining from some action or from the use of something, esp alcohol2. mainly Roman Catholic Church the practice of refraining from specific kinds of food or drink, esp from meat, as an act of penance
Derived forms
ˈabstinent adjectiveˈabstinently adverb
Word origin
C13: via Old French from Latin abstinentia, from abstinēre to abstain

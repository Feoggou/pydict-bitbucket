Powered by COBUILD
(proʊ rɑːtə , US - reɪtə ) also pro-rata
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverb [ADV after v]If something is distributed pro rata, it is distributed in proportion to the amount or size of something. [formal] ⇒ All part-timers should be paid the same, pro rata, as full-timers doing the same job.Pro rata is also an adjective. ⇒ They are paid their salaries and are entitled to fringe benefits on a pro-rata basis.

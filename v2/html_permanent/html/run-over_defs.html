run over

Definitions
verb1. (tr, adverb) to knock down (a person) with a moving vehicle2. (intransitive) to overflow the capacity of (a container)3. (intr, preposition) to examine hastily or make a rapid survey of4. (intr, preposition) to exceed (a limit) ⇒ we've run over our time

Definitions
phrasal verbIf a vehicle or its driver runs a person or animal over, it knocks them down or drives over them. [V n P] ⇒ You can always run him over and make it look like an accident. [V P n] ⇒ He ran over a six-year-old child as he was driving back from a party. [V P n] ⇒ ...if I were ever run over by a bus. →  run

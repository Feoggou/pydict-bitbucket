cathedral

(kəˈθiːdrəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna. the principal church of a diocese, containing the bishop's official throneb. (as modifier) ⇒ a cathedral city, ⇒ cathedral clergy
Word origin
C13: from Late Latin (ecclesia) cathedrālis cathedral (church), from cathedra bishop's throne, from Greek kathedra seat

planter

(ˈplɑːntə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the owner or manager of a plantation2. a machine designed for rapid, uniform, and efficient planting of seeds in the ground3. a colonizer or settler4. a decorative pot or stand for house plants

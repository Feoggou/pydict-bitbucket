buffoon

(bəˈfuːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who amuses others by ridiculous or odd behaviour, jokes, etc2. a foolish person
Derived forms
buffoonery (bufˈfoonery) noun
Word origin
C16: from French bouffon, from Italian buffone, from Medieval Latin būfō, from Latin: toad

cadaverous

(kəˈdævərəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or like a corpse, esp in being deathly pale; ghastly2. thin and haggard; gaunt
Derived forms
cadaverously (caˈdaverously) adverbcadaverousness (caˈdaverousness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone as cadaverous, you mean they are extremely thin and pale. [written] ⇒ ...a tall man with a long, cadaverous face.

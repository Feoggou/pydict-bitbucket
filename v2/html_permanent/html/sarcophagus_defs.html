sarcophagus

(sɑːˈkɒfəɡəs )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -gi (-ˌɡaɪ) or -gusesa stone or marble coffin or tomb, esp one bearing sculpture or inscriptions

Word Origin

C17: via Latin from Greek sarkophagos flesh-devouring; from the type of stone used, which was believed to destroy the flesh of corpses

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nouna gripping tool consisting of two hinged arms with usually serrated jaws that close on the workpiece

Word Origin

C16: from ply1

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
plural noun [oft a pair of N]Pliers are a tool with two handles at one end and two hard, flat, metal parts at the other. Pliers are used for holding or pulling out things such as nails, or for bending or cutting wire.

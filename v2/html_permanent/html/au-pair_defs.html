au pair

(əʊ ˈpɛə , French o pɛr)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a young foreigner, usually a girl, who undertakes housework in exchange for board and lodging, esp in order to learn the languageb. (as modifier) ⇒ an au pair girl2. a young person who lives temporarily with a family abroad in exchange for a reciprocal arrangement with his or her own familyverb3. (intransitive) to work as an au pairadverb4. as an au pair ⇒ she worked au pair in Greece
Word origin
C20: from French: on an equal footing

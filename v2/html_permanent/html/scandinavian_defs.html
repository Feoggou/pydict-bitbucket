Scandinavian

(ˌskændɪˈneɪvɪən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Scandinavia, its inhabitants, or their languagesnoun2. a native or inhabitant of Scandinavia3. Also: Norse the northern group of Germanic languages, consisting of Swedish, Danish, Norwegian, Icelandic, and Faeroese

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveScandinavian means belonging or relating to a group of northern European countries that includes Denmark, Norway, and Sweden, or to the people, languages, or culture of those countries. ⇒ The Baltic republics have called on the Scandinavian countries for help. ⇒ ...a festival of Scandinavian culture in London.2. countable nounScandinavians are people from Scandinavian countries.

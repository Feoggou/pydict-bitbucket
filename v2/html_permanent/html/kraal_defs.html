kraal

(krɑːl ) South Africa
Definitions
noun1. a hut village in southern Africa, esp one surrounded by a stockade2. an enclosure for livestockadjective3. denoting or relating to the tribal aspects of the Black African way of lifeverb4. (transitive) to enclose (livestock) in a kraal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Afrikaans, from Portuguese curral pen; see corral

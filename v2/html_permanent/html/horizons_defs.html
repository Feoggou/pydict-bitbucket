horizons

(həˈraɪzənz)
Definitions
plural nounthe limits of what one wants to do or of what one is interested or involved in. ⇒ As your horizons expand, these new ideas can give a whole new meaning to life., ⇒ Experiencing other cultures helps to broaden our horizons., ⇒ You cannot close your eyes to new horizons., ⇒ His book must have lured innumerable Britons from their hearths in search of new horizons.

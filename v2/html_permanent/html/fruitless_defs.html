fruitless

(ˈfruːtlɪs )
Definitions
adjective1. yielding nothing or nothing of value; unproductive; ineffectual2. without fruit
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fruitlessly (ˈfruitlessly) adverbfruitlessness (ˈfruitlessness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveFruitless actions, events, or efforts do not achieve anything at all. ⇒ It was a fruitless search. ⇒ Talks have so far have been fruitless.fruitlessly adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Four years of negotiation ended fruitlessly last December.

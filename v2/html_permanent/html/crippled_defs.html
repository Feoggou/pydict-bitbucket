crippled

(ˈkrɪpəld)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. physically incapacitated ⇒ He heaved his crippled leg into an easier position., ⇒ crippled with arthritis2. unable to achieve something for emotional reasons ⇒ I'm not perfect but I'm also not emotionally crippled or lonely., ⇒ I was furious with myself for being so crippled by shyness., ⇒ a young man crippled by shyness3. having had its capacity to function damaged ⇒ Raising taxes on the wealthy will hurt an already crippled economy., ⇒ a crippled economy

Similar Words

disabled, handicapped, challenged, paralysed, lame, deformed, incapacitated, bedridden, housebound, enfeebled, laid up (informal)

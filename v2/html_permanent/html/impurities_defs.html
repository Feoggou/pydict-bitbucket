(ɪmpyʊərɪtiz) in the Oil and Gas Industry
Definitions
noun(Extractive engineering: General, Operations) Impurities are substances which make something less pure. ⇒ Sour gas is gas which is contaminated with chemical impurities, often CO₂ or H₂S. ⇒ Natural gas may contain undesirable impurities such as carbon dioxide, nitrogen, water vapor, or hydrogen sulfide. ⇒ Impurities are substances which make something less pure.

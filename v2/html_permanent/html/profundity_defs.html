profundity

Powered by COBUILD
(prəfʌndɪti )
Word forms: profundities
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounProfundity is great intellectual depth and understanding. [+ of] ⇒ The profundity of this book is achieved with breathtaking lightness.2. uncount nounIf you refer to the profundity of a feeling, experience, or change, you mean that it is deep, powerful, or serious. [+ of] ⇒ ...the profundity of the structural problems besetting the country.3. countable nounA profundity is a remark that shows great intellectual depth and understanding. ⇒ His work is full of profundities and asides concerning the human condition.

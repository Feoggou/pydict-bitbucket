unsettle

(ʌnˈsɛtəl )
Definitions
verb1. (usually tr) to change or become changed from a fixed or settled condition2. (transitive) to confuse or agitate (emotions, the mind, etc)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

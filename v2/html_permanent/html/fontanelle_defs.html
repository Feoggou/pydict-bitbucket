fontanelle

or mainly US fontanel (ˌfɒntəˈnɛl )
Definitions
nounanatomy any of several soft membranous gaps between the bones of the skull in a fetus or infant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16 (in the sense: hollow between muscles): from Old French fontanele, literally: a little spring, from fontaine fountain

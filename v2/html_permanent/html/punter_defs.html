Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA punter is a person who bets money, especially on horse races. [British, informal] ⇒ Punters are expected to gamble £70m on the Grand National.2. countable nounPeople sometimes refer to their customers or clients as punters. [mainly British, informal] ⇒ Should anyone attack the stripper, the punters know that it is likely to be the end of the show.3. countable nounIn American football, a punter is someone who kicks the ball by punting it.

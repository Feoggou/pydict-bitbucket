Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that something is inviting, you mean that it has good qualities that attract you or make you want to experience it. ⇒ The February air was soft, cool, and inviting. ⇒ There is an inviting restaurant with an outdoor terrace. ⇒ ...an inviting smile.invitingly graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj, ADV with v] ⇒ The waters of the tropics are invitingly clear.2. → See also invite

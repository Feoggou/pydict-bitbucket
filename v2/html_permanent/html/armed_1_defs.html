armed1

(ɑːmd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. equipped with or supported by arms, armour, etc2. prepared for conflict or any difficulty3. (of an explosive device) prepared for use; having a fuse or detonator installed4. (of plants) having the protection of thorns, spines, etc

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

bidding

(ˈbɪdɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an order; command (often in the phrases do or follow the bidding of, at someone's bidding)2. an invitation; summons3. the act of making bids, as at an auction or in bridge4. bridge a group of bids considered collectively, esp those made on a particular deal

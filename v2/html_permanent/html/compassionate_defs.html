Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone or something as compassionate, you mean that they feel or show pity, sympathy, and understanding for people who are suffering. [approval] ⇒ My father was a deeply compassionate man. ⇒ She has a wise, compassionate face. ⇒ Robert Tanitch describes the film as 'deeply compassionate'.compassionately graded adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ He smiled compassionately at her.
Example sentences containing 'compassionate'
Then, within a day or so, cooler, more compassionate heads apparently prevailed and the charges against her were dropped. S.M. Stirling T2: INFILTRATOR

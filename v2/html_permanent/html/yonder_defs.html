yonder

(ˈjɒndə )
Definitions
adverb1. at, in, or to that relatively distant place; over theredeterminer2. being at a distance, either within view or as if within view ⇒ yonder valleys
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old English geond yond; related to Old Saxon jendra, Old High German jenēr, Gothic jaind

(jɒndəʳ ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [ADV with v]Yonder is an old-fashioned or dialect word for 'over there'. ⇒ Now look yonder, just beyond the wooden post there.Yonder is also a determiner. ⇒ His wife, Claudia, lies under yonder tree.

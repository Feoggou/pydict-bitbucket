Levi1

(ˈliːvaɪ )
Definitions
noun1. Old Testamenta. the third son of Jacob and Leah and the ancestor of the tribe of Levi (Genesis 29:34)b. the priestly tribe descended from this patriarch (Numbers 18:21–24)2. New Testament another name for Matthew the apostle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Levi2 - Italian

( ˈlɛːvi)
Definitions
noun1.  Carlo. 1902–75, Italian physician, painter, and writer. Best known for his novel Christ Stopped at Eboli (1947), his other works include The Watch (1952) and Words are Stones (1958)2.  Primo (ˈpriːməʊ). 1919–87, Italian novelist. His book If This is a Man (1947) relates his experiences in Auschwitz. Other books include The Periodic Table (1956) and The Drowned and the Saved (1988), published after his suicide
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Levi3 - Hebrew

(ˈliːvaɪ , ˈlevi) or Levite (ˈliːvaɪt )
Definitions
nounJudaism a descendant of the tribe of Levi who has certain privileges in the synagogue service

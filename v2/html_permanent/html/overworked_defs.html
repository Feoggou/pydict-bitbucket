Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a word, expression, or idea as overworked, you mean it has been used so often that it no longer has much effect or meaning. overused ⇒ 'Ecological' has become one of the most overworked adjectives among manufacturers of garden supplies. ⇒ ...an overworked simile.

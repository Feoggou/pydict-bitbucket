Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to have doubts or suspicions about (someone or something)noun2. distrust

Word forms: mistrusts , mistrusting , mistrusted
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounMistrust is the feeling that you have towards someone who you do not trust. ⇒ There was mutual mistrust between the two men. [+ of] ⇒ ...a deep mistrust of state banks.2. verbIf you mistrust someone or something, you do not trust them. [V n] ⇒ It frequently appears that Bell mistrusts all journalists.

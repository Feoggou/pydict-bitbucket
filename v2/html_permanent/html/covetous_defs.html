covetous

(ˈkʌvɪtəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(usually postpositive; and foll by of) jealously eager for the possession of something (esp the property of another person)
Derived forms
covetously (ˈcovetously) adverbcovetousness (ˈcovetousness) or covetiveness (ˈkʌvɪtɪvnəs) noun

convoy

(ˈkɒnvɔɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a group of merchant ships with an escort of warships2. a group of land vehicles assembled to travel together3. the act of travelling or escorting by convoy (esp in the phrase in convoy)verb4. (transitive) to escort while in transit
Word origin
C14: from Old French convoier to convey

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [oft in N]A convoy is a group of vehicles or ships travelling together. ⇒ ...a U.N. convoy carrying food and medical supplies. ⇒ ...humanitarian relief convoys. ⇒ They travel in convoy with armed guards.2. verbTo convoy goods or people somewhere means to move them there in a convoy. [V n prep/adv] ⇒ He ordered the combined fleet to convoy troops to Naples. [Also V n]

soporific

(ˌsɒpəˈrɪfɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective also: archaic soporiferous1. inducing sleep2. drowsy; sleepynoun3. a drug or other agent that induces sleep

Derived Forms

ˌsoporifically (ˌsopoˈrifically) or ˌsoporiferously (ˌsopoˈriferously) adverb

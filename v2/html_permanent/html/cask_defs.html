cask

(kɑːsk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a strong wooden barrel used mainly to hold alcoholic drink ⇒ a wine cask2. any barrel3. the quantity contained in a cask4. Australian a lightweight cardboard container with plastic lining and a small tap, used to hold and serve wine5. engineering another name for flask (sense 6)
Word origin
C15: from Spanish casco helmet, perhaps from cascar to break

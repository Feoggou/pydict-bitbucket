Auriga

(ɔːˈraɪɡə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: Latin genitive Aurigae (ɔːˈraɪdʒiː )a conspicuous constellation in the N hemisphere between the Great Bear and Orion, at the edge of the Milky Way. It contains the first magnitude star Capella and the supergiant eclipsing binary star Epsilon Aurigae
Word origin
Latin: charioteer

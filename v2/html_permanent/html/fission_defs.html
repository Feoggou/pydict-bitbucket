fission

(ˈfɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act or process of splitting or breaking into parts2. biology a form of asexual reproduction in single-celled animals and plants involving a division into two or more equal parts that develop into new cells3. short for nuclear fission
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from Latin fissiō a cleaving

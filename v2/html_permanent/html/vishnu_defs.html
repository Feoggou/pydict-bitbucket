Vishnu

(ˈvɪʃnuː )
Definitions
nounHinduism the Pervader or Sustainer: originally a solar deity occupying a secondary place in the Hindu pantheon; later one of the three chief gods, the second member of the Trimurti; and, later still, the saviour appearing in many incarnations
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Vishnuism (ˈVishnuˌism) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Sanskrit Viṣṇu, literally: the one who works everywhere

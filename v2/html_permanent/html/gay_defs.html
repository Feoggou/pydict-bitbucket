gay

(ɡeɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. a. homosexualb. of or for homosexuals ⇒ a gay club2. a. carefree and merry ⇒ a gay temperamentb. brightly coloured; brilliant ⇒ a gay hatc. given to pleasure, esp in social entertainment ⇒ a gay lifenoun3. a homosexual▶ USAGE Gayness is the word used to refer to homosexuality. The noun which refers to being carefree and merry is gaiety
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gayness (ˈgayness) noun

Word Origin

C13: from Old French gai, from Old Provençal, of Germanic origin

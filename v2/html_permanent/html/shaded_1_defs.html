shaded

(ˈʃeɪdɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. protected from light, sunlight, etc ⇒ These plants will grow happily in a shaded spot.2. (of an area on a map, diagram, etc) coloured darker than the surrounding areas, so that it can be distinguished from them ⇒ The shaded area on the map represents his property.

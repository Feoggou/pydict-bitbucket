break in

  1. break and enter, burst in, enter, gain access • The thief had broken in through a first-floor window.
  2. interrupt, intervene, interfere, intrude, burst in, interject, butt in, barge in, interpose, put your oar in, put your two cents in (US, slang) • Suddenly, O'Leary broke in with a suggestion.

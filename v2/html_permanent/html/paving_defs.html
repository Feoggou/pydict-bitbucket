paving

(ˈpeɪvɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a paved surface; pavement2. material used for a pavement, such as paving stones, bricks, or asphaltadjective3. of or for a paved surface or pavement4. preparatory, facilitating, enabling ⇒ paving legislation

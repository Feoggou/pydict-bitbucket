cut across

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (preposition)1. (intransitive) to be contrary to ordinary procedure or limitations ⇒ opinion on European integration still cuts clean across party lines2. to cross or traverse, making a shorter route ⇒ she cut across the field quickly

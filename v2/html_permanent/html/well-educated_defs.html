well-educated

(ˌwɛlˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪd)
Definitions
adjective(well educated when postpositive) having had a good education ⇒ The accent could be that of any well-educated woman from the Home Counties., ⇒ Many of the immigrants are well educated.

countermine

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (ˈkaʊntəˌmaɪn )1. military a tunnel dug to defeat similar activities by an enemy2. a plot to frustrate another plotverb (ˌkaʊntəˈmaɪn )3. to frustrate by countermeasures4. military to take measures to defeat the underground operations of (an enemy)5. military to destroy enemy mines in (an area) with mines of one's own

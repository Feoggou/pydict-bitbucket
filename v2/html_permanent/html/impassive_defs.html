impassive

(ɪmˈpæsɪv )
Definitions
adjective1. not revealing or affected by emotion; reserved2. calm; serene; imperturbable3. rare unconscious or insensible
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

impassively (imˈpassively) adverbimpassiveness (imˈpassiveness) or impassivity (ˌɪmpæˈsɪvɪtɪ ) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is impassive or their face is impassive, they are not showing any emotion. [written] emotionless ⇒ He searched Hill's impassive face for some indication that he understood. ⇒ As the foreman of the jury announced the verdict, Miss Allan remained impassive.impassively adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ The lawyer looked impassively at him and said nothing.

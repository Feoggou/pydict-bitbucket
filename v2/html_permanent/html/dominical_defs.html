dominical

(dəˈmɪnɪkəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or emanating from Jesus Christ as Lord2. of or relating to Sunday as the Lord's Day
Word origin
C15: from Late Latin dominicālis, from Latin dominus lord

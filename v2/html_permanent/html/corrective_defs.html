Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Corrective measures or techniques are intended to put right something that is wrong. ⇒ Scientific institutions have been reluctant to take corrective action. ⇒ He has received extensive corrective surgery to his skull.2. countable nounIf something is a corrective to a particular view or account, it gives a more accurate or fairer picture than there would have been without it. [formal] [+ to] ⇒ ...a useful corrective to the mistaken view that all psychologists are behaviourists.

Arianism

(ˈɛərɪəˌnɪzəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe doctrine of Arius, pronounced heretical at the Council of Nicaea, which asserted that Christ was not of one substance with the Father, but a creature raised by the Father to the dignity of Son of God

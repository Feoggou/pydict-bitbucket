unforeseen for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnfəʳsiːn )
Definitions
adjectiveIf something that has happened was unforeseen, it was not expected to happen or known about beforehand. ⇒ Radiation may damage cells in a way that was previously unforeseen. ⇒ Unfortunately, due to unforeseen circumstances, this year's show has been cancelled. ⇒ Ring regularly to check that no unforeseen problems have arisen.

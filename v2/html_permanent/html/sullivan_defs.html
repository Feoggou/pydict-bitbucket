Sullivan

(ˈsʌlɪvən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Sir Arthur (Seymour). 1842–1900, English composer who wrote operettas, such as H.M.S. Pinafore (1878) and The Mikado (1885), with W. S. Gilbert as librettist2.  Louis (Henri). 1856–1924, US pioneer of modern architecture: he coined the slogan "form follows function"

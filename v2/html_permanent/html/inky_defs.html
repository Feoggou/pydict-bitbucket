inky

(ˈɪŋkɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: inkier or inkiest1. resembling ink, esp in colour; dark or black2. of, containing, or stained with ink ⇒ inky fingers
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

inkiness (ˈinkiness) noun

inky

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪŋki ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Inky means black or very dark blue. [literary] ⇒ The moon was rising in the inky sky. ⇒ ...the inky black of night.Inky is also a combining form. ⇒ ...looking out over an inky-blue ocean.2. adjectiveIf something is inky, it is covered in ink. ⇒ ...inky fingers.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

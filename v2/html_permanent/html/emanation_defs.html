emanation

(ˌɛməˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. an act or instance of emanating2. something that emanates or is produced; effusion3. a gaseous product of radioactive decay, such as radon
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

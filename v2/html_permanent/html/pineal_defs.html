pineal

(ˈpɪnɪəl , paɪˈniːəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. resembling a pine cone2. of or relating to the pineal gland

Word Origin

C17: via French from Latin pīnea pine cone
Example sentences containing 'pineal'
Danlo knew that the fount of intelligence lay inside the head, in the pineal gland which he called the third eye. Zindell, David THE BROKEN GOD

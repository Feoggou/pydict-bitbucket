letters

(ˈlɛtəz )
Definitions
noun (functioning as plural or singular)1. literary knowledge, ability, or learning ⇒ a man of letters2. literary culture in general3. an official title, degree, etc, indicated by an abbreviation ⇒ letters after one's name
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'letters'
At the top of every note she'd written in big bold letters URGENT. Stuart Harrison LOST SUMMERReceiving one of Nick's letters was always more of a forensic exercise than a matter of reading. Garth Nix LIRAEL: DAUGHTER OF THE CLAYRThe telexes and letters had been filed in chronological order. Robert Wilson BLOOD IS DIRT

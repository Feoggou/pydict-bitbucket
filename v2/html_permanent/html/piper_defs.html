piper

(ˈpaɪpə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who plays a pipe or bagpipes2. See pay the piper and call the tune

Piper

(ˈpaɪpə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun John. 1903–92, British artist. An official war artist in World War II, he is known esp for his watercolours of bombed churches and his stained glass in Coventry Cathedral

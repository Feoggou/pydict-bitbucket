Definitions
graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj/-ed]You use eminently in front of an adjective describing a positive quality in order to emphasize the quality expressed by that adjective. [emphasis] ⇒ His books on diplomatic history were eminently readable. ⇒ His family was eminently respectable.

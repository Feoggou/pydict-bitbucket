lade1

(leɪd )
Definitions
verbWord forms: lades, lading, laded, laden (ˈleɪdən ) or laded1. to put cargo or freight on board (a ship, etc) or (of a ship, etc) to take on cargo or freight2. (tr; usually passive; and foll by with) to burden or oppress3. (tr; usually passive; and foll by with) to fill or load4. to remove (liquid) with or as if with a ladle

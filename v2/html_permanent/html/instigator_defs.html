instigator

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnstɪgeɪtəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: instigators
Definitions
countable nounThe instigator of an event is the person who causes it to happen. [+ of] ⇒ He was accused of being the main instigator of the coup. ⇒ ...the key instigators of reform.

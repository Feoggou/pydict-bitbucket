phlegmatic

(flɛɡˈmætɪk ) or phlegmatical
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having a stolid or unemotional disposition2. not easily excited

Derived Forms

phlegmatically (phlegˈmatically) adverbphlegmaticalness (phlegˈmaticalness) or phlegmaticness (phlegˈmaticness) noun

smuggler

Powered by COBUILD
(smʌgələʳ )
Word forms: smugglers
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounSmugglers are people who take goods into or out of a country illegally. ⇒ ...drug smugglers.
Example sentences containing 'smuggler'
Yes, I was ready to accept I was the perfect smuggler 's lucky charm. Dexter Petley WHITE LIESShe lit a cigarette herself, blew out a cloud of pungent smoke and said calmly,'You're no smuggler , I can see that. Jack Higgins THE KEYS OF HELLAt first, the questions centred on the petroleum smuggler who'd been shot outside White Square. Mark Burnell CHAMELEON

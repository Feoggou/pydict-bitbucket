the provinces

Definitions
(plural) those parts of a country lying outside the capital and other large cities and regarded as outside the mainstream of sophisticated culture▷ See province

the provinces

Definitions
those parts of a country lying outside the capital and other large cities and regarded as outside the mainstream of sophisticated culture ⇒ He's a nobody, straight from the provinces., ⇒ The government plans to transfer some 30,000 government jobs from Paris to the provinces., ⇒ For someone who had spent his entire life in the capital, the idea of moving to the provinces was appalling.▷ See provinces

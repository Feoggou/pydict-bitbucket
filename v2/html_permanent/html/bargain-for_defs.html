bargain for

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(intr, preposition) to expect; anticipate (a style of behaviour, change in fortune, etc) ⇒ he got more than he bargained for

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrasal verbIf you have not bargained for or bargained on something that happens, you did not expect it to happen and so feel surprised or worried by it. reckon on [V P n] ⇒ The effects of this policy were more than the government had bargained for. [V P n] ⇒ He had not bargained on the mad jealousy which both women developed. →  bargain

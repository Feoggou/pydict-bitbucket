fixings

Powered by COBUILD
(fɪksɪŋz ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. plural nounFixings are extra items that are used to decorate or complete something, especially a meal. [US] ⇒ He bought a hot dog and had it covered with all the fixings.2. plural nounFixings are items such as nails and screws which are used to fix things such as furniture together. ⇒ Have you got all the screws and fixings you need?

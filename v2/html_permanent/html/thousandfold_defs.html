thousandfold

(ˈθaʊzəndˌfəʊld)
Definitions
adjective1. having a thousand times as many ⇒ the nearly thousandfold increase in the circulating concentrations of oestriol found during pregnancy2. consisting of a thousandadverb3. by a thousand times as much ⇒ Her pain shall return to them a thousandfold through many generations in the times and worlds to come.

loquacious

(lɒˈkweɪʃəs )
Definitions
adjectivecharacterized by or showing a tendency to talk a great deal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

loquaciously (loˈquaciously) adverbloquacity (lɒˈkwæsɪtɪ ) or loquaciousness (loˈquaciousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin loquāx from loquī to speak

reinstatement

Powered by COBUILD
(riːɪnsteɪtmənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount noun [usu with poss]Reinstatement is the act of giving someone back a job or position which has been taken away from them. ⇒ Parents campaigned in vain for her reinstatement.2. uncount noun [usu with poss]The reinstatement of a law, facility, or practice is the act of causing it to exist again. restoration [+ of] ⇒ He welcomed the reinstatement of the 10 per cent bank base rate.

authorize

or authorise (ˈɔːθəˌraɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to confer authority upon (someone to do something); empower2. to permit (someone to do or be something) with official sanction ⇒ a dealer authorized by a manufacturer to retail his products
Derived forms
ˌauthoriˈzation or ˌauthoriˈsation nounˈauthorˌizer or ˈauthorˌiser noun

ravish

(ˈrævɪʃ )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. (often passive) to give great delight to; enrapture2. to rape3. archaic to carry off by force
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ravisher (ˈravisher) nounravishment (ˈravishment) noun

Word Origin

C13: from Old French ravir, from Latin rapere to seize

ascending

(əˈsɛndɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. moving upwards; rising2. botany sloping or curving upwards ⇒ the ascending stem of a vine

ascending

Powered by COBUILD
(əsendɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]If a group of things is arranged in ascending order, each thing is bigger, greater, or more important than the thing before it. ⇒ Now draw or trace ten dinosaurs in ascending order of size. ⇒ ...an ascending spiral of antisocial behaviour.2. → see also ascend

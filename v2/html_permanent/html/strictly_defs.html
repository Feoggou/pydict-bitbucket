strictly

(strɪktli )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverbYou use strictly to emphasize that something is of one particular type, or intended for one particular thing or person, rather than any other. [emphasis] ⇒ He seemed fond of her in a strictly professional way. ⇒ This session was strictly for the boys.

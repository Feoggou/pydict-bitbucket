languishing

(ˈlæŋgwɪʃɪŋ ) in American English
Definitions
adjective that languishes; specif.,a.  becoming weak; droopingb.  lingeringc.  slow; not intensed.  pining; longinge.  tender; sentimental

Derived Forms

languishingly (ˈlanguishingly) adverb

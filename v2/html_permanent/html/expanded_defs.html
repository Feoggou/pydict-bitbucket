expanded

(ɪkˈspændɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. Also: extended (of printer's type) wider than usual for a particular height. Compare condensed2. (of a plastic) having been foamed during manufacture by the introduction of a gas in order to make a light packaging material or heat insulator ⇒ expanded polystyrene. See also expanded metal

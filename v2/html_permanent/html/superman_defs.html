superman

(ˈsuːpəˌmæn )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -men1. (in the philosophy of Nietzsche) an ideal man who through integrity and creativity would rise above good and evil and who represents the goal of human evolution2. any man of apparently superhuman powers

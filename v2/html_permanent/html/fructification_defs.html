fructification

(ˌfrʌktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən , ˌfrʊk-)
Definitions
noun1. the act or state of fructifying2. the fruit of a seed-bearing plant3. any spore-bearing structure in ferns, mosses, fungi, etc

glimmering

(ˈɡlɪmərɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. a faint sign ⇒ a glimmering of understanding, ⇒ the first glimmerings of civilization.adjective2. glowing or twinkling ⇒ the glimmering ocean
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

glimmeringly (ˈglimmeringly) adverb

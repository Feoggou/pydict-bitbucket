unhandy

(ʌnˈhændɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. not skilful with one's hands; clumsy; awkward2. inconvenient
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'unhandy'
Especially in any kind of wind, the barges had to be unhandy things to bring alongside. Clancy, Tom WITHOUT REMORSE

Powered by COBUILD
(θretənɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]You can describe someone's behaviour as threatening when you think that they are trying to harm you. ⇒ The police could have charged them with threatening behaviour. ⇒ She said Denny had received a threatening letter and asked me if I sent it.threateningly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ 'This ain't no affair of yours, boy!' McClosky said threateningly.2. → See also life-threatening, threaten

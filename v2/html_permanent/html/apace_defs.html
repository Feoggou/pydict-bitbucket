Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverb [ADV after v]If something develops or continues apace, it is developing or continuing quickly. [formal] ⇒ Land reclamation continues apace. ⇒ The plan is proceeding apace, with another 13 superstores opened in the first half of this year.

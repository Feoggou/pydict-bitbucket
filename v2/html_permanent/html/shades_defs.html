shades

(ʃeɪdz )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. gathering darkness at nightfall2. a slang word for sunglasses3. See the shades4. (foll by of) undertones or suggestions ⇒ shades of my father!
Example sentences containing 'shades'
At the top of the stair she emerged into a bedroom decorated entirely in shades of white. Amanda Hemingway THE GREENSTONE GRAIL: THE SANGREAL TRILOGY ONEI raise my hinged shades the better to examine what we have here. Trenhalle, John A MEANS TO EVILThere was a light on behind drawn shades in the second story of the building, probably her apartment. Terman, Douglas CORMORANT

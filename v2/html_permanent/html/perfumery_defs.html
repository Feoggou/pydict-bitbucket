perfumery

(pəˈfjuːmərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -eries1. a place where perfumes are sold2. a factory where perfumes are made3. the process of making perfumes4. perfumes in general

perfumery

Powered by COBUILD
(pəʳfjuːməri )
Word forms: perfumeries
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount noun [oft N n]Perfumery is the activity or business of producing perfume. ⇒ ...the perfumery trade. ⇒ Provence is the home of perfumery.2. countable nounA perfumery is a shop or a department in a store where perfume is the main product that is sold. ⇒ ...a perfumery called Bayley's of Bond Street. ⇒ ...the perfumery department.

unconverted

(ˌʌnkənˈvɜːtɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. not having been changed or adapted ⇒ an unconverted barn2. a. not having changed one's beliefs, opinions, etcb. (as collective noun; preceded by the) ⇒ he'll be preaching to the unconverted

hock1

(hɒk )
Definitions
noun1. the joint at the tarsus of a horse or similar animal, pointing backwards and corresponding to the human ankle2. the corresponding joint in domestic fowlverb3. another word for hamstring
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: short for hockshin, from Old English hōhsinu heel sinew

hock2

(hɒk )
Definitions
noun1. any of several white wines from the German Rhine2. (not in technical usage) any dry white wine
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: short for obsolete hockamore Hochheimer

hock3 - informal, mainly US and Canadian

(hɒk )
Definitions
verb1. (transitive) to pawn or pledgenoun2. the state of being in pawn (esp in the phrase in hock)3. See in hock

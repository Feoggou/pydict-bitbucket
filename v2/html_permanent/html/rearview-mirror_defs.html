rearview mirror

(ˈrɪrˌvju ) in American English
Definitions
a mirror in or on a vehicle that allows the driver to view the area behind the vehicle

rearview mirror

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪərvyu mɪrər) in Automotive Engineering
Word forms: (regular plural) rearview mirrors
Definitions
noun(Automotive engineering: Vehicle components, Bodywork, controls, and accessories) Inside a car, the rearview mirror is the mirror that allows you to see the traffic behind when you are driving. ⇒ She saw the reflection of a pair of headlamps in the rearview mirror. ⇒ He forgot to check his rearview mirror when he backed out of the parking space. ⇒ The headlamps of the car behind shone brightly in the rearview mirror.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

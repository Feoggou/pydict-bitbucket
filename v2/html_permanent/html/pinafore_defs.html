pinafore

(ˈpɪnəˌfɔː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. mainly British an apron, esp one with a bib2. mainly British short for pinafore dress3. mainly US an overdress buttoning at the back

Word Origin

C18: from pin + afore

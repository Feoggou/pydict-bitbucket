indolent

(ˈɪndələnt )
Definitions
adjective1. disliking work or effort; lazy; idle2. pathology causing little pain ⇒ an indolent tumour3. (esp of a painless ulcer) slow to heal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

indolence (ˈindolence) nounindolently (ˈindolently) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin indolēns not feeling pain, from in-1 + dolēns, from dolēre to grieve, cause distress

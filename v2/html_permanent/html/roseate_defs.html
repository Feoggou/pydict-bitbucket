roseate

(ˈrəʊzɪˌeɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. of the colour rose or pink2. excessively or idealistically optimistic
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

roseately (ˈroseˌately) adverb

millet

(ˈmɪlɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a cereal grass, Setaria italica, cultivated for grain and animal fodder2. a. an East Indian annual grass, Panicum miliaceum, cultivated for grain and forage, having pale round shiny seedsb. the seed of this plant3. any of various similar or related grasses, such as pearl millet and Indian milletmiliary

Word Origin

C14: via Old French from Latin milium; related to Greek melinē millet

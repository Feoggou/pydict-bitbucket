Round Table

Definitions
noun the Round Table1. (in Arthurian legend) the table of King Arthur, shaped so that his knights could sit around it without any having precedence2. Arthur and his knights collectively3. one of an organization of clubs of young business and professional men who meet in order to further social and business activities and charitable work4. (in New Zealand) an organization of businessmen supporting policies of the New Right
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

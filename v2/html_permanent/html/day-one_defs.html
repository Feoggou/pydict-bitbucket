day one

(deɪ wʌn)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (sometimes capitals) the first day; the very beginning2. See from day one3. See on day one

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
singular nounIf something happens from day one of a process, it happens right from the beginning. If it happens on day one, it happens right at the beginning. ⇒ This has been a bad inquiry from day one. ⇒ On day one of the election campaign the National and Liberal Party leaders were neck and neck.

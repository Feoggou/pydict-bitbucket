well-meaning

Definitions
adjective (well meaning when postpositive)having or indicating good or benevolent intentions, usually with unfortunate results

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that a person or their actions are well-meaning, you mean that they intend to be helpful or kind but they are unsuccessful or cause problems. ⇒ He is a well-meaning but ineffectual leader. ⇒ Even well-meaning attempts at conservation can bring problems.

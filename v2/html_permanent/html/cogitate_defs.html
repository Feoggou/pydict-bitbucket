Definitions

verbto think deeply about (a problem, possibility, etc); ponder
Derived forms
cogiˌtatingly (ˈcogiˌtatingly) adverbˌcogitation (ˌcogiˈtation) nouncogiˌtator (ˈcogiˌtator) noun

verbIf you are cogitating, you are thinking deeply about something. [formal] [V] ⇒ He sat silently cogitating. [V + on/about] ⇒ ...to cogitate on the meaning of life.cogitation (kɒdʒɪteɪʃən ) uncount noun ⇒ After much cogitation, we decided to move to the Isle of Wight.

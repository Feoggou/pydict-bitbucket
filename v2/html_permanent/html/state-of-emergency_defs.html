state of emergency

(steɪt əv ɪˈmɜːdʒənsɪ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna condition, declared by a government, in which martial law applies, usually because of civil unrest or natural disaster ⇒ Zambia's five opposition parties have condemned the imposition of a state of emergency, saying it is being abused by the government., ⇒ The President has declared a state of emergency.. See also emergency worker

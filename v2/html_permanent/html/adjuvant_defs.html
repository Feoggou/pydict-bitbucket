adjuvant

(ˈædʒəvənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. aiding or assistingnoun2. something that aids or assists; auxiliary3. medicine a drug or other substance that enhances the activity of another4. immunology a substance that enhances the immune response stimulated by an antigen when injected with the antigen
Derived forms
adjuvancy (ˈadjuvancy) noun
Word origin
C17: from Latin adjuvāns, present participle of adjuvāre, from juvāre to help

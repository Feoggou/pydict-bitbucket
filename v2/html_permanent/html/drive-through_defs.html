drive-through

or drive-thru (ˈdraɪvˌθruː)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a takeaway restaurant, shop, etc designed so that customers can use it without leaving their cars ⇒ the Kentucky Fried Chicken drive-thruadjective2. of or relating to a drive-through establishment ⇒ a drive-through burger bar, ⇒ a drive-through check-in area, ⇒ a 24-hour drive-through chapel in Las Vegas, ⇒ a hospital building with a drive-through pharmacy, ⇒ a fast-food drive-through window, ⇒ the need to keep the drive-through service quick and uncomplicated

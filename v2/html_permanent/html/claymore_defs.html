claymore

(ˈkleɪˌmɔː , Scottish ˌkleˈmor)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a large two-edged broadsword used formerly by Scottish Highlanders2. a US type of antipersonnel mine
Word origin
C18: from Gaelic claidheamh mōr great sword

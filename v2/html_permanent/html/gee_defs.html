gee1

(dʒiː )
Definitions
exclamation1. Also: gee up! an exclamation, as to a horse or draught animal, to encourage it to turn to the right, go on, or go fasterverbWord forms: gees, geeing or geed2. (usually foll by up) to move (an animal, esp a horse) ahead; urge on3. (foll by up) to encourage (someone) to greater effort or activity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: origin uncertain

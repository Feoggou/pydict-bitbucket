gratuitous

(ɡrəˈtjuːɪtəs )
Definitions
adjective1. given or received without payment or obligation2. without cause; unjustified3. law given or made without receiving any value in return ⇒ a gratuitous agreement
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gratuitously (graˈtuitously) adverbgratuitousness (graˈtuitousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin grātuītus, from grātia favour

contraction

(kənˈtrækʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an instance of contracting or the state of being contracted2. physiology any normal shortening or tensing of an organ or part, esp of a muscle, e.g. during childbirth3. pathology any abnormal tightening or shrinking of an organ or part4. a shortening of a word or group of words, often marked in written English by an apostrophe ⇒ I've come for I have come
Derived forms
contractive (conˈtractive) adjectivecontractively (conˈtractively) adverbcontractiveness (conˈtractiveness) noun

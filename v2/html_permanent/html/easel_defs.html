easel

(ˈiːzəl )
Definitions
nouna frame, usually in the form of an upright tripod, used for supporting or displaying an artist's canvas, blackboard, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Dutch ezel ass1; related to Gothic asilus, German Esel, Latin asinus ass

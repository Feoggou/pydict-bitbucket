Synonyms of grass

grass

noun

  1. turf, lawn
    • Small creatures stirred in the grass around the tent.
  2. cannabis, blow (slang), pot (slang), weed (slang), dope (slang), hemp, hash (slang), hashish, wacky baccy (slang), dagga (South Africa)
    • I started smoking grass when I was about sixteen.
  3. (British slang) betrayer, sneak, squealer (slang), Judas, accuser, stool pigeon, nark (British & Australian & & New Zealand, slang)
    • He preferred to take the rap rather than be a grass.

verb

  1. (often with on) inform on, shop (slang, mainly British), betray, denounce, snitch (slang), sing (slang, mainly US), give away, rat on (informal), sell (someone) down the river (informal), dob in (Australian, slang)
    • He was repeatedly attacked by other inmates, who accused him of grassing.
    • I'd never grass on a mate.

gramineous

Townshend

(ˈtaʊnzɛnd )
Definitions
noun1.  Charles, 2nd Viscount, nicknamed Turnip Townshend. 1674–1738, English politician and agriculturist2.  Pete born 1945, British rock guitarist, singer, and songwriter: member of the Who from 1964 and composer of much of their material

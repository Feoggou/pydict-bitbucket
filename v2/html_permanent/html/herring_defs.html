herring

(ˈhɛrɪŋ )
Definitions
noun plural -rings or -ringany marine soft-finned teleost fish of the family Clupeidae, esp Clupea harengus, an important food fish of northern seas, having an elongated body covered, except in the head region, with large fragile silvery scales
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English hǣring; related to Old High German hāring, Old Frisian hēring, Dutch haring

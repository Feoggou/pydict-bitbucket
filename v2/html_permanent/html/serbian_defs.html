Serbian

(ˈsɜːbɪən ) or Serb
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Serbia, its people, or their language (formerly regarded as a dialect of Serbo-Croat)noun2. the language spoken in Serbia3. a. a native or inhabitant of Serbiab. a speaker of the Serbian languageSee at Bosnian

cerium

(ˈsɪərɪəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna malleable ductile steel-grey element of the lanthanide series of metals, used in lighter flints and as a reducing agent in metallurgy. Symbol: Ce; atomic no: 58; atomic wt: 140.115; valency: 3 or 4; relative density: 6.770; melting pt: 798°C; boiling pt: 3443°C
Word origin
C19: New Latin, from Ceres (the asteroid) + -ium

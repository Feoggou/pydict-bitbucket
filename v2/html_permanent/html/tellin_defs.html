tellin

(ˈtɛlɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany of various slim marine bivalve molluscs of the genus Tellina (or Macoma) that live in intertidal sand, esp the smooth oval delicately tinted T. tenuis

Word Origin

from New Latin tellina, from Greek tellinē a shellfish

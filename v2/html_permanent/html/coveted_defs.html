graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]You use coveted to describe something that very many people would like to have. ⇒ Allan Little from Radio 4 took the coveted title of reporter of the year. ⇒ ...one of sport's most coveted trophies. ⇒ ...a supply of highly-coveted hard currency.

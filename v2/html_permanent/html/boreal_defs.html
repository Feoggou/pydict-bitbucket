Boreal

(ˈbɔːrɪəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or denoting the coniferous forests in the north of the N hemisphere2. designating a climatic zone having snowy winters and short summers3. designating a dry climatic period from about 7500 to 5500 bc, characterized by cold winters, warm summers, and a flora dominated by pines and hazels

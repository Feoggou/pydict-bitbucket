Synonyms of late

late

adjective

  1. overdue, delayed, last-minute, belated, tardy, behind time, unpunctual, behindhand, L8 (text messaging)
    • A few late arrivals were still straggling in.
    OPPOSITES:  timely, early, seasoned, prompt, beforehand, punctual
  2. dead, deceased, departed, passed on, old, former, previous, preceding, defunct, L8 (text messaging)
    • my late husband
    OPPOSITES:  existing, alive
  3. recent, new, advanced, fresh, L8 (text messaging)
    • some late news just in for the people of Merseyside
    OPPOSITE:  old

adverb

  1. behind time, belatedly, tardily, behindhand, dilatorily, unpunctually, L8 (text messaging)
    • The talks began some fifteen minutes late.
    OPPOSITES:  early, in advance, beforehand
  2. late at night, in the night, L8 (text messaging)
    • We went to bed late.
  3. after hours, overtime, till all hours, L8 (text messaging)
    • I was working late at the office.

PROVERBS:
Better late than never
It is never too late

rite

(raɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a formal act or procedure prescribed or customary in religious ceremonies ⇒ fertility rites, ⇒ the rite of baptism2. a particular body of such acts or procedures, esp of a particular Christian Church ⇒ the Latin rite3. a Christian Church ⇒ the Greek rite
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin rītus religious ceremony

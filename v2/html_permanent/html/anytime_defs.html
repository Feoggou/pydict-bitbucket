anytime

(ˌɛnɪˈtaɪm)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverbat a point in time which is not set or fixed ⇒ The college admits students anytime during the year., ⇒ I'm not going to be changing my job anytime soon., ⇒ With a mobile phone, you can be reached anytime, anywhere., ⇒ You can call me anytime., ⇒ He can leave anytime he wants., ⇒ Students can meet with an adviser anytime during the school term.

training ground

(ˈtreɪnɪŋ ɡraʊnd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. sport an area where people prepare for sporting competitions, with activities primarily concentrating on skills and fitness2. military an area where military personnel are trained for combat ⇒ Jowan had joined the Royal Field Artillery, whose training ground was at Lark Hill on Salisbury Plain.3. an opportunity to learn skills in a particular field or profession ⇒ Taking positions in societies and voluntary organizations is a good training ground for business.

While Mikel dreaded the thought of seeking Tarja out, he was looking forward to the opportunity to visit the training ground legitimately.

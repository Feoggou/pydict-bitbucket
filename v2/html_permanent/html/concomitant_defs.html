Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n, v-link ADJ with n]Concomitant is used to describe something that happens at the same time as another thing and is connected with it. [formal] ⇒ Cultures that were better at trading saw a concomitant increase in their wealth. [+ with] ⇒ This approach was concomitant with the move away from relying solely on official records.2. countable nounA concomitant of something is another thing that happens at the same time and is connected with it. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ The right to deliberately alter quotations is not a concomitant of a free press.

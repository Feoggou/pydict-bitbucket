well-supported

Definitions
adjective (well supported when postpositive)1. having good physical support ⇒ a sofa in which your back is well supported2. having a lot of support or encouragement ⇒ friendly matches were less well supported3. substantially upheld by evidence or facts ⇒ many well-supported theories

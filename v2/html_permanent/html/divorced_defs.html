divorced

Powered by COBUILD
(dɪvɔːʳst )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveSomeone who is divorced from their former husband or wife has separated from them and is no longer legally married to them. ⇒ Princess Margaret is divorced from Lord Snowdon. ⇒ He is divorced, with a young son.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that one thing is divorced from another, you mean that the two things are very different and separate from each other. [+ from] ⇒ ...speculative theories divorced from political reality.3. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that someone is divorced from a situation, you mean that they act as if they are not affected by it in any way. [+ from] ⇒ This just shows how divorced from reality she's become.

Similar Words

separated, deserted, living apart

cards

(kɑːdz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (usually functioning as singular)a. any game or games played with cards, esp playing cardsb. the playing of such a game2. an employee's national insurance and other documents held by the employer3. See get one's cards4. See on the cards5. See play one's cards6. See put one's cards on the table

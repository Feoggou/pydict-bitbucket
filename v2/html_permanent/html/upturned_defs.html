upturned

(ʌpˈtɜːnd)
Definitions
adjective1. turned up at the end ⇒ an upturned nose2. directed upwards ⇒ her upturned face, ⇒ his eyes closed and his palms upturned3. turned over or upside down ⇒ the upturned buckets, ⇒ He clung to the upturned boat, screaming for help.

Definitions

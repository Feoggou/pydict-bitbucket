Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveA deprecating attitude, gesture, or remark shows that you think that something is not very good, especially something associated with yourself. [written] ⇒ Erica made a little deprecating shrug.deprecatingly adverb [ADV after v] ⇒ He speaks deprecatingly of his father as a lonely man.

humidity

(hjuːˈmɪdɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun1. the state of being humid; dampness2. a measure of the amount of moisture in the air. See relative humidity, absolute humidity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions

Definitions
noun(Chemical Engineering: Drying and evaporation) Humidity is a measure of how much vapor there is in a mixture of gas and vapor. ⇒ Keeping humidity below 50 percent can prevent water condensation on building materials. ⇒ The concentration of water vapor in air is usually expressed as the relative humidity. ⇒ Humidity is a measure of how much vapor there is in a mixture of gas and vapor.

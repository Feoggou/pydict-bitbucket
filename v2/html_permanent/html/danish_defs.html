(ˈdeɪnɪʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Denmark, its people, or their languagenoun2. the official language of Denmark, belonging to the North Germanic branch of the Indo-European family

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Danish means relating to or belonging to Denmark, or to its people, language, or culture. ⇒ ...the Danish coast. ⇒ He was the son of a Danish father and a Venezuelan mother.2. uncount nounDanish is the language spoken in Denmark.

sima

(ˈsaɪmə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the silicon-rich and magnesium-rich rocks of the earth's oceanic crust, the most abundant individual rock being basalt2. the earth's continental lower crust, probably comprised of gabbro rather than basalt

Derived Forms

simatic (saɪˈmætɪk ) adjective

Word Origin

C20: from si(lica) + ma(gnesia)

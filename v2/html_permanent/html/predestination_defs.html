predestination

(priːˌdɛstɪˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. theologya. the act of God foreordaining every event from eternityb. the doctrine or belief, esp associated with Calvin, that the final salvation of some of mankind is foreordained from eternity by God2. the act of predestining or the state of being predestined

Definitions

uncount nounIf you believe in predestination, you believe that people have no control over events because everything has already been decided by a power such as God or fate.

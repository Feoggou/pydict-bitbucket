tadpole

(ˈtædˌpəʊl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe aquatic larva of frogs, toads, etc, which develops from a limbless tailed form with external gills into a form with internal gills, limbs, and a reduced tail

Word Origin

C15 taddepol, from tadde toad + pol head, poll

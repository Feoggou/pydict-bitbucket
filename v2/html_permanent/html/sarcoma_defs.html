sarcoma

(sɑːˈkəʊmə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -mata (-mətə) or -maspathology a usually malignant tumour arising from connective tissue

Derived Forms

sarcomaˌtoid (sarˈcomaˌtoid) or sarcomatous (sarˈcomatous) adjective

Word Origin

C17: via New Latin from Greek sarkōma fleshy growth; see sarco-, -oma

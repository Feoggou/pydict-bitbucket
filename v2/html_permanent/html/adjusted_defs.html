adjusted

(əˈdʒʌstɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. psychologically well or badly equipped to cope with reality and ordinary life and relationships ⇒ badly/well/normally adjusted, ⇒ Everyone took him for an ordinary, well-adjusted person., ⇒ a badly adjusted adult2. altered or fixed to suit the circumstances ⇒ badly adjusted headlights, ⇒ If your Bunsen burner flame is correctly adjusted you will have a dark blue flame with a light blue inner cone., ⇒ The choke may be incorrectly adjusted or worn., ⇒ I'm gradually becoming adjusted to living alone., ⇒ It took Richard some time to become adjusted to his new routine., ⇒ His eyes gradually became adjusted to the light.

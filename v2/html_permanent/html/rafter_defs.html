rafter

(ˈrɑːftə )
Definitions
nounany one of a set of sloping beams that form the framework of a roof
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English ræfter; related to Old Saxon rehter, Old Norse raptr, Old High German rāvo; see raft1

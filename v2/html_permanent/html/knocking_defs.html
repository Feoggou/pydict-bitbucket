knocking

(ˈnɒkɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. sharp rapping with the knuckles, a hard object, etc, esp to capture attention ⇒ They heard furious knocking coming from downstairs., ⇒ There was a loud knocking., ⇒ They were wakened by a loud knocking at the door.2. the sound that an engine or bearing makes as a result of wear or faulty combustion ⇒ There was a knocking coming from under the bonnet.

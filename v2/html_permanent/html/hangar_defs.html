hangar

(ˈhæŋə )
Definitions
nouna large workshop or building for storing and maintaining aircraft
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from French: shed, perhaps from Medieval Latin angārium shed used as a smithy, of obscure origin

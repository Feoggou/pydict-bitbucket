turnpike

(ˈtɜːnˌpaɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (between the mid-16th and late 19th centuries)a. gates or some other barrier set across a road to prevent passage until a toll had been paidb. a road on which a turnpike was operated2. an obsolete word for turnstile (sense 1)3. US a motorway for use of which a toll is charged

Word Origin

C15: from turn + pike2

subsoil

(ˈsʌbˌsɔɪl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. Also called: undersoil the layer of soil beneath the surface soil and overlying the bedrockb. (as modifier) ⇒ a subsoil ploughverb2. (transitive) to plough (land) to a depth below the normal ploughing level and so break up the subsoil

Derived Forms

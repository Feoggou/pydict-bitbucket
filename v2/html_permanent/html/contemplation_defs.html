contemplation

(ˌkɒntɛmˈpleɪʃən , -təm-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. thoughtful or long consideration or observation2. spiritual meditation, esp (in Christian religious practice) concentration of the mind and soul upon God. Compare meditation3. purpose or intention

What the Bismarck could do just did not bear contemplation. Maclean, Alistair THE LONELY SEAAfter a few minutes ' silent contemplation Anna says, `We need to talk about Christmas.

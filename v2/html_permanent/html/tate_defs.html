Tate

(teɪt )
Definitions
noun1.  (John Orley) Allen. 1899–1979, US poet and critic2.  Sir Henry. 1819–99, British sugar refiner and philanthropist; founder of the Tate Gallery3.  Nahum (ˈneɪʊm). 1652–1715, British poet, dramatist, and hymn-writer, born in Ireland: poet laureate (1692–1715). He is best known for writing a version of King Lear with a happy ending

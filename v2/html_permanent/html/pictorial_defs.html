pictorial

(pɪkˈtɔːrɪəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. relating to, consisting of, or expressed by pictures2. (of books, newspapers, etc) containing pictures3. of or relating to painting or drawing4. (of language, style, etc) suggesting a picture; vivid; graphicnoun5. a. a magazine, newspaper, etc, containing many picturesb. (capital when part of a name) ⇒ the Sunday Pictorial

Derived Forms

pictorially (picˈtorially) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin pictōrius, from Latin pictor painter, from pingere to paint

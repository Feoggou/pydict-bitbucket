Herculaneum

(ˌhɜːkjʊˈleɪnɪəm )
Definitions
nounan ancient city in SW Italy, of marked Greek character, on the S slope of Vesuvius: buried along with Pompeii by an eruption of the volcano (79 ad). Excavation has uncovered well-preserved streets, houses, etc

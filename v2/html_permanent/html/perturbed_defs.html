COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ, ADJ that]If someone is perturbed by something, they are worried by it. [formal] [+ by/at] ⇒ He apparently was not perturbed by the prospect of a policeman coming to call. ⇒ She was really quite perturbed at the prospect.

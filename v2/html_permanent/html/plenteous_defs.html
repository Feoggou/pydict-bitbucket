plenteous

(ˈplɛntɪəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. ample; abundant ⇒ a plenteous supply of food2. producing or yielding abundantly ⇒ a plenteous grape harvest

Derived Forms

plenteously (ˈplenteously) adverbplenteousness (ˈplenteousness) noun

Word Origin

C13 plenteus, from Old French plentivous, from plentif abundant, from plenté plenty

Similar Words

plentiful, liberal, generous, lavish, ample, infinite, abundant, overflowing, copious, inexhaustible, bountiful, profuse, thick on the ground, bounteous (literary)
productive, bumper, fertile, prolific, plentiful, fruitful, luxuriant

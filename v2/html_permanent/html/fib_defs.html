fib

(fɪb )
Definitions
noun1. a trivial and harmless lieverbWord forms: fibs, fibbing or fibbed2. (intransitive) to tell such a lie
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fibber (ˈfibber) noun

Word Origin

C17: perhaps from fibble-fable an unlikely story; see fable

Definitions
1. countable nounA fib is a small, unimportant lie. [informal] ⇒ She told innocent fibs like anyone else.2. verbIf someone is fibbing, they are telling lies. [informal] [V] ⇒ He laughs loudly when I accuse him of fibbing.

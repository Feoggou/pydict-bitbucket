clippers

(ˈklɪpəz ) or clips (ˈklɪps )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. a hand tool with two cutting blades for clipping fingernails, hedges, etc2. a hairdresser's tool, operated either by hand or electrically, with one fixed and one reciprocating set of teeth for cutting short hair

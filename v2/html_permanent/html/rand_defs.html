rand1

(rænd , rɒnt )
Definitions
nounthe standard monetary unit of the Republic of South Africa, divided into 100 cents
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: from Afrikaans, shortened from Witwatersrand, referring to the gold-mining there; related to rand2

rand2

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English; related to Old High German rant border, rim of a shield, Old Norse rönd shield, rim

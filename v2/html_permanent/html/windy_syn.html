Synonyms of windy

windy

adjective

  1. breezy, wild, stormy, boisterous, blustering, windswept, tempestuous, blustery, gusty, inclement, squally, blowy
    • It was a windy, overcast day.
    OPPOSITES:  still, calm, smooth, motionless, becalmed, windless
  2. pompous, empty, rambling, diffuse, meandering, long-winded, turgid, wordy, garrulous, bombastic, boastful, loquacious, verbose, prolix
    • I have a horror of turning into a windy old bore.
    OPPOSITES:  reserved, quiet, modest, shy, restrained, reticent, taciturn, unforthcoming
  3. (old-fashioned slang) frightened, afraid, scared, chicken (slang), nervous, fearful, cowardly, timid, nervy (informal), antsy (informal), chickenshit (US, slang)
    • `I'm a bit windy of being on my own,' he admitted.
    OPPOSITES:  brave, daring, bold, courageous, fearless, gallant, undaunted, unafraid

exhalation

(ˌɛkshəˈleɪʃən ; ˌ ɛksəˈleɪʃən ) in American English
Definitions
noun1.  an exhaling or being exhaled; expiration or evaporation2.  something exhaled, as air, steam, or an odor; emanation; effluvium

Word Origin

L exhalatio

astronomy

(əˈstrɒnəmɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe scientific study of the individual celestial bodies (excluding the earth) and of the universe as a whole. Its various branches include astrometry, astrodynamics, cosmology, and astrophysics
Word origin
C13: from Old French astronomie, from Latin astronomia, from Greek; see astro-, -nomy

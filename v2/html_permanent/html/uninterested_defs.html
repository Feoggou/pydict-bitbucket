Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are uninterested in something or someone, you do not want to know any more about them, because you think they have no special or exciting qualities. [+ in] ⇒ I was so uninterested in the result that I didn't even bother to look at it. ⇒ ...unhelpful and uninterested shop staff.

Definitions
COBUILD Key Words for the Oil and Gas Industry
noun(Extractive engineering: Refinery processes) Purification is the treatment of raw natural gas to remove contaminants. ⇒ The presence of carbon dioxide in natural gas reduces its combustion power efficiency, and therefore it must be removed using a process of purification. ⇒ Natural gas can be liquefied only after a purification process which consists of the removal of impurities such as water, carbon dioxide and hydrogen sulfide. ⇒ Purification is the treatment of raw natural gas to remove contaminants.

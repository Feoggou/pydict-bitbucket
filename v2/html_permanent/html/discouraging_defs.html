discouraging

(dɪsˈkʌrɪdʒɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivecausing a loss of confidence and determination ⇒ Today's report is rather more discouraging for the economy., ⇒ a discouraging set of results for the country's leading automobile manufacturer
Example sentences containing 'discouraging'
The mare snorted and stamped her front foot, discouraging a persistent fly. Elizabeth Lowell BEAUTIFUL DREAMER

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]Hallowed is used to describe something that is respected and admired, usually because it is old, important, or has a good reputation. ⇒ The hallowed turf of Twickenham is the venue for the Middlesex Rugby Sevens Finals. ⇒ They protested that there was no place for a school of commerce in their hallowed halls of learning.2. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]Hallowed is used to describe something that is considered to be holy. ⇒ ...hallowed ground.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

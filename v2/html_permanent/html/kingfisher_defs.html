kingfisher

(ˈkɪŋˌfɪʃə )
Definitions
nounany coraciiform bird of the family Alcedinidae, esp the Eurasian Alcedo atthis, which has a greenish-blue and orange plumage. Kingfishers have a large head, short tail, and long sharp bill and tend to live near open water and feed on fish
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: originally king's fisher

honours

or US honors (ˈɒnəz )
Definitions
plural noun1. observances of respect2. (often capital)a. (in a university degree or degree course) a rank of the highest academic standardb. (as modifier) ⇒ an honours degree. Abbreviation: Hons Compare general (sense 9), pass (sense 35)3. a high mark awarded for an examination; distinction4. See last honours5. See military honours

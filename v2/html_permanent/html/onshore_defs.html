onshore

(ˈɒnˈʃɔː )
Definitions
adjective, adverb1. towards the land ⇒ an onshore gale2. on land; not at sea

onshore for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ɒnʃɔːʳ )
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Onshore means happening on or near land, rather than at sea. ⇒ ...Western Europe's biggest onshore oilfield.Onshore is also an adverb. ⇒ They missed the ferry and remained onshore.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Onshore means happening or moving towards the land. ⇒ The onshore wind blew steadily past him.Onshore is also an adverb. ⇒ There was a bit of a wind and it was blowing onshore. ⇒ ...the gas comes onshore in great quantities.

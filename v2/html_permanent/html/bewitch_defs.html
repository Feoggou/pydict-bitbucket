bewitch

(bɪˈwɪtʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to attract and fascinate; enchant2. to cast a spell over
Derived forms
bewitching (beˈwitching) adjectivebewitchingly (beˈwitchingly) adverb
Word origin
C13 bewicchen; see be-, witch

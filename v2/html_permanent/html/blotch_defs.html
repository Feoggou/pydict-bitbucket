blotch

(blɒtʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an irregular spot or discoloration, esp a dark and relatively large one such as an ink stainverb2. to become or cause to become marked by such discoloration3. (intransitive) (of a pen or ink) to write or flow unevenly in blotches
Word origin
C17: probably from botch, influenced by blot1

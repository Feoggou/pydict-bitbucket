unknowingly for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnnoʊɪŋli )
Definitions
adverb [ADV with v]If someone does something unknowingly, they do it without being aware of it. ⇒ ...if people unknowingly move into more contaminated areas of the river. ⇒ ...the extent to which the workforce colludes knowingly or unknowingly with such criminal activity. ⇒ Art often imitates nature unknowingly.

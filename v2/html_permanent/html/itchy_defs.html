itchy

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪtʃi ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf a part of your body or something you are wearing is itchy, you have an unpleasant feeling on your skin that makes you want to scratch. [informal] ⇒ ...itchy, sore eyes. ⇒ Wigs are itchy and uncomfortable.2. →  itchy feet

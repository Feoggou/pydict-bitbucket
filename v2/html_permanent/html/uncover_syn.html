Synonyms of uncover

uncover

verb

  1. reveal, find, discover, expose, encounter, turn up, detect, disclose, unveil, come across, unearth, dig up, divulge, chance on, root out, unmask, lay bare, make known, blow the whistle on (informal), bring to light, smoke out, take the wraps off, blow wide open (slang), stumble on or across
    • Auditors said they had uncovered evidence of fraud.
    OPPOSITES:  hide, conceal, suppress, cover up, keep under wraps, cover
  2. open, unveil, unwrap, show, strip, expose, bare, lay bare, lift the lid, lay open
    • When the seedlings sprout, uncover the tray.

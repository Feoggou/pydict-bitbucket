(lʌkɪli ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adverb & adverbYou add luckily to a statement to indicate that it is good that a particular thing happened or is the case because otherwise the situation would have been difficult or unpleasant. ⇒ Luckily, we both love football. ⇒ Luckily for me, he spoke very good English.

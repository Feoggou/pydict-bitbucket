mademoiselle

(ˌmædmwəˈzɛl , French madmwazɛl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural mesdemoiselles (ˌmeɪdmwəˈzɛl , French medmwazɛl)1. a young unmarried French girl or woman: usually used as a title equivalent to Miss2. a French teacher or governess

Word Origin

C15: French, from ma my + demoiselle damsel

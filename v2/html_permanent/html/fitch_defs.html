fitch

(fɪtʃ ) or fitchet (ˈfɪtʃɪt )
Definitions
noun1. another name for polecat (sense 1)2. the fur of the polecat or ferret
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: probably from ficheux fitchew

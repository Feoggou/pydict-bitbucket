hours

(aʊəz )
Definitions
plural noun1. a period regularly or customarily appointed for work, business, etc2. one's times of rising and going to bed (esp in the phrases keep regular, irregular, or late hours)3. an indefinite period of time4. Also called (in the Roman Catholic Church): canonical hoursa. the seven times of the day laid down for the recitation of the prayers of the divine officeb. the prayers recited at these times5. See the small hours6. See till all hours

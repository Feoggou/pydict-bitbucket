puppy

(ˈpʌpɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -pies1. a young dog; pup2. informal, derogatory a brash or conceited young man; pup

Derived Forms

puppyˌhood (ˈpuppyˌhood) nounpuppyish (ˈpuppyish) adjective

Word Origin

C15 popi, from Old French popée doll; compare puppet

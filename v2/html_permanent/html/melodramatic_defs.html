Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveMelodramatic behaviour is behaviour in which someone treats a situation as much more serious than it really is. ⇒ 'Don't you think you're being rather melodramatic?' Jane asked. ⇒ She'd flung herself in a pose of melodramatic exhaustion.melodramatically (melədrəmætɪkli ) graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ 'For God's sake,' Michael said melodramatically, 'Whatever you do, don't look down.'

meridional

(məˈrɪdɪənəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. along, relating to, or resembling a meridian2. characteristic of or located in the south, esp of Europenoun3. an inhabitant of the south, esp of France

Derived Forms

meridionally (meˈridionally) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Late Latin merīdiōnālis southern; see meridian; for form, compare septentriōnālis septentrional

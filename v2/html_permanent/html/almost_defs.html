Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverb [ADV before v]You use almost to indicate that something is not completely the case but is nearly the case. ⇒ The couple had been dating for almost three years. ⇒ Storms have been hitting almost all of Britain recently. ⇒ The effect is almost impossible to describe. ⇒ He was almost as tall as Pete, but skinnier. ⇒ The arrested man will almost certainly be kept at this police station. ⇒ He contracted Spanish flu, which almost killed him.

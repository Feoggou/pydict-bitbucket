frankpledge

(ˈfræŋkˌplɛdʒ )
Definitions
noun (in medieval England)1. the corporate responsibility of members of a tithing for the good behaviour of each other2. a member of a tithing3. a tithing itself
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: via Anglo-French from Old French franc free (see frank) + plege pledge

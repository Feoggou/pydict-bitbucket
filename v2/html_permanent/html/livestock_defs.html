livestock

(ˈlaɪvˌstɒk )
Definitions
noun(functioning as singular or plural) cattle, horses, poultry, and similar animals kept for domestic use but not as pets, esp on a farm or ranch

livestock

Powered by COBUILD
(laɪvstɒk ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [with sing or pl verb]Animals such as cattle and sheep which are kept on a farm are referred to as livestock. ⇒ The heavy rains and flooding killed scores of livestock.

northeast

(ˌnɔːθˈiːst , nautical ˌnɔːrˈiːst )
Definitions
noun1. the point of the compass or direction midway between north and east, 45° clockwise from north2. See the northeastadjective also: northeastern3. (sometimes capital) of or denoting the northeastern part of a specified country, area, etc ⇒ northeast Lincolnshire4. situated in, proceeding towards, or facing the northeast5. (esp of the wind) from the northeastadverb6. in, to, towards, or (esp of the wind) from the northeast

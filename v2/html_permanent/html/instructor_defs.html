instructor

(ɪnˈstrʌktə )
Definitions
noun1. someone who instructs; teacher2. US and Canadian a university teacher ranking below assistant professor

(ɪnstrʌktəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: instructors
Definitions
countable noun [oft n N]An instructor is someone who teaches a skill such as driving or skiing. In American English, instructor can also be used to refer to a schoolteacher or to a university teacher of low rank. ⇒ ...tuition from an approved driving instructor.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

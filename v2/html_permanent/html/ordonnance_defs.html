ordonnance

(ˈɔːdənəns , French ɔrdɔnɑ̃s)
Definitions
noun1. the proper disposition of the elements of a building or an artistic or literary composition2. an ordinance, law, or decree, esp in French law
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Old French ordenance arrangement, influenced by ordonner to order

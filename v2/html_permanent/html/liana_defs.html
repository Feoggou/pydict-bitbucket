liana

(lɪˈɑːnə ) or liane (lɪˈɑːn )
Definitions
nounany of various woody climbing plants mainly of tropical forests
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lianoid (liˈanoid) adjective

Word Origin

C19: changed from earlier liane (through influence of French lier to bind), from French, of obscure origin

February

(ˈfɛbrʊərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ariesthe second month of the year, consisting of 28 or (in a leap year) 29 days
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Latin Februārius mēnsis month of expiation, from februa Roman festival of purification held on February 15, from plural of februum a purgation

tom1

(tɒm )
Definitions
nouna. the male of various animals, esp the catb. (as modifier) ⇒ a tom turkeyc. (in combination) ⇒ a tomcat
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: special use of the shortened form of Thomas, applied to any male, often implying a common or ordinary type of person, etc

Powered by COBUILD
(revərənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveIf you describe someone's behaviour as reverent, you mean that they are showing great respect for a person or thing. ⇒ ...the reverent hush of a rapt audience. ⇒ Ellen looks almost reverent.reverently adverb [usu ADV after v] ⇒ He got up and took the book out almost reverently.

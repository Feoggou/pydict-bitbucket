miscarry

(mɪsˈkærɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -ries, -rying or -ried (intransitive)1. to expel a fetus prematurely from the womb; abort2. to fail ⇒ all her plans miscarried3. British (of freight, mail, etc) to fail to reach a destination

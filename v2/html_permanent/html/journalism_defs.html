journalism

(ˈdʒɜːnəˌlɪzəm )
Definitions
noun1. the profession or practice of reporting about, photographing, or editing news stories for one of the mass media2. newspapers and magazines collectively; the press3. the material published in a newspaper, magazine, etc ⇒ this is badly written journalism4. news reports presented factually without analysis
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions

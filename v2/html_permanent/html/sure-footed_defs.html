sure-footed

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. unlikely to fall, slip, or stumble2. not likely to err or fail, as in judgment

Derived Forms

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA person or animal that is sure-footed can move easily over steep or uneven ground without falling. ⇒ My horse is small but wiry and sure-footed.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is sure-footed, they are confident in what they are doing. ⇒ The Labour Party was growing increasingly sure-footed.

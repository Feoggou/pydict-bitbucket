Word forms: disobeys , disobeying , disobeyed
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbWhen someone disobeys a person or an order, they deliberately do not do what they have been told to do. [V n] ⇒ ...a naughty boy who often disobeyed his mother and father. [V n] ⇒ He urged Russian soldiers to disobey orders if asked to fire on civilian targets. [V] ⇒ They were threatened with punishment if they disobeyed.

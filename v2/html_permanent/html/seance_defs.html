seance

or séance (ˈseɪɑ̃ns , -ɑːns)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a meeting at which spiritualists attempt to receive messages from the spirits of the dead2. a meeting of a society

Word Origin

C19: from French, literally: a sitting, from Old French seoir to sit, from Latin sedēre

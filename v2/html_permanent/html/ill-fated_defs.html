Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something as ill-fated, you mean that it ended or will end in an unsuccessful or unfortunate way. ⇒ England's footballers are back home after their ill-fated trip to Algeria.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Salisbury1

(ˈsɔːlzbərɪ , -brɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the former name (until 1982) of Harare2. a city in S Australia: an industrial suburb of N Adelaide. Pop: 118 422 (2006)3. a city in S England, in SE Wiltshire: nearby Old Sarum was the site of an Early Iron Age hill fort; its cathedral (1220–58) has the highest spire in England. Pop: 43 355 (2001). Ancient name: Sarum. Official name: New Sarum

Salisbury2

(ˈsɔːlzbərɪ , -brɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Robert Gascoyne Cecil (ˈɡæskɔɪn), 3rd Marquess of Salisbury. 1830–1903, British statesman; Conservative prime minister (1885–86; 1886–92; 1895–1902). His greatest interest was in foreign and imperial affairs

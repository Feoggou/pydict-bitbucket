gingham

(ˈɡɪŋəm )
Definitions
nountextilesa. a cotton fabric, usually woven of two coloured yarns in a checked or striped designb. (as modifier) ⇒ a gingham dress
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from French guingan, from Malay ginggang striped cloth

Definitions
uncount nounGingham is cotton cloth which has a woven pattern of small squares, usually in white and one other colour. ⇒ ...a gingham apron. ⇒ ...gingham check shorts.

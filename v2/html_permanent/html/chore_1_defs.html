chore

(tʃɔː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small routine task, esp a domestic one2. an unpleasant task
Word origin
C19: variant of Middle English chare; related to char3

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu sing]A chore is a task that you must do but that you find unpleasant or boring. ⇒ She sees exercise primarily as an unavoidable chore. ⇒ Making pasta by hand with a rolling pin can be a real chore.2. countable noun [usu pl]Chores are tasks such as cleaning, washing, and ironing that have to be done regularly at home. ⇒ My husband and I both go out to work so we share the household chores.

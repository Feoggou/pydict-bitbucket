Definitions
graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]If you have done something recently or if something happened recently, it happened only a short time ago. ⇒ The bank recently opened a branch in Germany. ⇒ It is only fairly recently that historians have begun to investigate the question. ⇒ He was until very recently the most powerful banker in the city.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveIf you describe something or someone as smashing, you mean that you like them very much. [British, informal, old-fashioned] ⇒ It was smashing. I really enjoyed it. ⇒ She's a smashing girl.2. adjective [ADJ n]A smashing success or victory is one that is impressive and definite. [emphasis] ⇒ We gave a party and everybody came and I think it was a smashing success. ⇒ Johnson's smashing victory in 1964 changed the political horizon substantially.

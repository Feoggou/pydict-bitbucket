heterodox

(ˈhɛtərəʊˌdɒks )
Definitions
adjective1. at variance with established, orthodox, or accepted doctrines or beliefs2. holding unorthodox opinions
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

heterodoxy (ˈheteroˌdoxy) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Greek heterodoxos holding another opinion, from hetero- + doxa opinion

Synonyms of primitive

primitive

adjective

  1. uncivilized, savage, barbarian, barbaric, undeveloped, uncultivated
    • studies of primitive societies
    OPPOSITES: developed, civilized
  2. early, first, earliest, original, primary, elementary, pristine, primordial, primeval
    • primitive birds from the dinosaur era
    OPPOSITES:  later, modern, advanced
  3. simple, naive, childlike, untrained, undeveloped, unsophisticated, untutored
    • primitive art
    OPPOSITES: trained, developed, adult, mature, sophisticated, tutored
  4. crude, simple, rough, rude, rudimentary, unrefined
    • primitive tools
    OPPOSITES:  elaborate, refined

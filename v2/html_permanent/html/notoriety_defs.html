Definitions
uncount nounTo achieve notoriety means to become well-known for something bad. ⇒ He achieved notoriety as chief counsel to President Nixon in the Watergate break-in. ⇒ ...Christian Lacroix, who gained notoriety as one of Paris's most flamboyant dress designers in the 1980s.

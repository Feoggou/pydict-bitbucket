soused

(saʊst)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. steeped or cooked (in a marinade or pickle) ⇒ The meat is generously soused in wine., ⇒ soused herrings2. intoxicated; drunk ⇒ I progressed from pleasantly soused to horribly smashed., ⇒ They were soused in rum.

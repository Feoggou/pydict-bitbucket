hod

(hɒd )
Definitions
noun1. an open metal or plastic box fitted with a handle, for carrying bricks, mortar, etc2. a tall narrow coal scuttle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: perhaps alteration of C13 dialect hot, from Old French hotte pannier, creel, probably from Germanic

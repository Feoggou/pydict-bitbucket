beaded

Powered by COBUILD
(biːdɪd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]A beaded dress, cushion, or other object is decorated with beads.2. adjective [v-link ADJ with n]If something is beaded with a liquid, it is covered in small drops of that liquid. ⇒ The man's bald head was beaded with sweat. ⇒ The cave walls were beaded with moisture.

Ugrian

(ˈuːɡrɪən , ˈjuː-)
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to a light-haired subdivision of the Turanian people, who include the Samoyeds, Voguls, Ostyaks, and Magyarsnoun2. a member of this group of peoples3. another word for Ugric
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from Old Russian Ugre Hungarians

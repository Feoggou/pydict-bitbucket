Brooke

(brʊk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Alan Francis. See Alanbrooke2.  Sir James. 1803–68, British soldier; first rajah of Sarawak (1841–63)3.  Rupert (Chawner). 1887–1915, British lyric poet, noted for his idealistic war poetry, which made him a national hero

Definitions

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrasal verbIf you back out, you decide not to do something that you previously agreed to do. [VP + of] ⇒ Madonna backed out of the project after much wrangling. [VP] ⇒ Wells was supposed to put up half the money, but later backed out. →  back

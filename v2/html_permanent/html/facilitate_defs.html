Definitions
verbTo facilitate an action or process, especially one that you would like to happen, means to make it easier or more likely to happen. [V n] ⇒ The new airport will facilitate the development of tourism. [be V-ed] ⇒ He argued that the economic recovery had been facilitated by his tough stance.

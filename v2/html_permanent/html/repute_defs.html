repute

(rɪˈpjuːt )
Definitions
verb1. (tr; usually passive) to consider (a person or thing) to be as specified ⇒ he is reputed to be intelligentnoun2. public estimation; reputation ⇒ a writer of little repute
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French reputer, from Latin reputāre to think over, from re- + putāre to think

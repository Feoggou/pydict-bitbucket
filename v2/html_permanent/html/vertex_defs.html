vertex

(ˈvɜːtɛks )
Definitions
noun plural -texes or -tices (-tɪˌsiːz)1. the highest point2. mathematicsa. the point opposite the base of a figureb. the point of intersection of two sides of a plane figure or anglec. the point of intersection of a pencil of lines or three or more planes of a solid figure3. astronomy a point in the sky towards which a star stream appears to move4. anatomy the crown of the head
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin: highest point, from vertere to turn

Similar Words

top, crown, summit, pinnacle, apex, height, crest, culmination, zenith, extremity, apogee, acme

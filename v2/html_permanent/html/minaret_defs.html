minaret

(ˌmɪnəˈrɛt , ˈmɪnəˌrɛt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a slender tower of a mosque having one or more balconies from which the muezzin calls the faithful to prayer2. any structure resembling this

Derived Forms

ˌminareted (ˌminaˈreted) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from French, from Turkish, from Arabic manārat lamp, from nār fire

lyrical

(ˈlɪrɪkəl )
Definitions
adjective1. another word for lyric (sense 1), lyric (sense 2), lyric (sense 3), lyric (sense 4)2. enthusiastic; effusive (esp in the phrase to wax lyrical)

lyrical

Powered by COBUILD
(lɪrɪkəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is lyrical is poetic and romantic. ⇒ His paintings became more lyrical. ⇒ ...its remarkable free-flowing and often lyrical style.lyrically graded adverb [ADV adj/-ed, ADV after v] ⇒ I'm trying to show children that it's lyrically beautiful out there, wherever you live.2. → to wax lyrical

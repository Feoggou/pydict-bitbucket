overwork

Definitions
verb (ˌəʊvəˈwɜːk ) (mainly tr)1. (also intr) to work or cause to work too hard or too long2. to use too much ⇒ to overwork an excuse3. to decorate the surface of4. to work upnoun (ˈəʊvəˌwɜːk )5. excessive or excessively tiring work
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
ergative verb & verbIf you overwork or if someone overworks you, you work too hard, and are likely to become very tired or ill. [V] ⇒ He's overworking and has got a lot on his mind. [V n] ⇒ He overworks and underpays the poor clerk whom he employs.Overwork is also a noun. ⇒ He died of a heart attack brought on by overwork.overworked graded adjective & adjective ⇒ ...an overworked doctor.

appanage

or apanage (ˈæpənɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. land or other provision granted by a king for the support of a member of the royal family, esp a younger son2. a natural or customary accompaniment or perquisite, as to a job or position
Word origin
C17: from Old French, from Medieval Latin appānāgium, from appānāre to provide for, from Latin pānis bread

vitreous

(ˈvɪtrɪəs )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or resembling glass2. made of, derived from, or containing glass3. of or relating to the vitreous humour or vitreous body
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vitreously (ˈvitreously) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin vitreus made of glass, from vitrum glass; probably related to vidēre to see

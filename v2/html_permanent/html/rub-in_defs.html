rub in

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. phrasal verbIf you rub a substance in, you press it into something by continuously moving it over its surface. [V P n] ⇒ When hair is dry, rub in a little oil to make it smooth and glossy. [Also V n P]2. phrasal verbIf someone keeps reminding you of something you would rather forget you can say that they are rubbing it in. [V n P] ⇒ Officials couldn't resist rubbing it in. [V P n] ⇒ The home side rubbed in their superiority with a further goal. →  rub

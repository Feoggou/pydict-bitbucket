grammar school

Definitions
noun1. British (esp formerly) a state-maintained secondary school providing an education with an academic bias for children who are selected by the eleven-plus examination, teachers' reports, or other means. Compare secondary modern school, comprehensive school2. US another term for elementary school3. Australian a private school, esp one controlled by a church4. New Zealand a secondary school forming part of the public education system

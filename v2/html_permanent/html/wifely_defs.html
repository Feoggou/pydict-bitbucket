wifely for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(waɪfli )
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]Wifely is used to describe things that are supposed to be typical of a good wife. ⇒ She strove to perform all her wifely functions perfectly. ⇒ ...the ideology of wifely duty.

beatific

(ˌbiːəˈtɪfɪk ) or beatifical (ˌbiːəˈtɪfɪkəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. displaying great happiness, calmness, etc ⇒ a beatific smile2. of, conferring, or relating to a state of celestial happiness
Derived forms
ˌbeatifically (ˌbeaˈtifically) adverb
Word origin
C17: from Late Latin beātificus, from Latin beātus, from beāre to bless + facere to make

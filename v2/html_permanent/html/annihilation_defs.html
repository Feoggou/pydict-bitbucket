annihilation

(əˌnaɪəˈleɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. total destruction2. the act of annihilating3. physics the destruction of a particle and its antiparticle when they collide. The annihilation of an electron with a positron generates two or, very rarely, three photons of annihilation radiation. The annihilation of a nucleon with its antiparticle generates several pions
Example sentences containing 'annihilation'
You have your blessed consciousness - never the fear of annihilation , never panic, never hatred, never pure retching despair. Zindell, David THE BROKEN GODCompared to the annihilation of an entire city, it seemed scarcely to matter.

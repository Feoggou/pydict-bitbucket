spinach

(ˈspɪnɪdʒ , -ɪtʃ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a chenopodiaceous annual plant, Spinacia oleracea, cultivated for its dark green edible leaves2. the leaves of this plant, eaten as a vegetable

Word Origin

C16: from Old French espinache, from Old Spanish espinaca, from Arabic isfānākh, from Persian

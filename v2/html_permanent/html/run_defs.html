Word Origin

Old English runnen, past participle of (ge)rinnan; related to Old Frisian, Old Norse rinna, Old Saxon, Gothic, Old High German rinnan

[V n] ⇒ Phyllis Smith ran a controlled race to qualify in 51.32 sec.3. ergative verb & verbWhen a horse runs in a race or when its owner runs it, it competes in a race.

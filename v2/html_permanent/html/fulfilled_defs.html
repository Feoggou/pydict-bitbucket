fulfilled

(fʊlˈfɪld)
Definitions
adjective1. completed or achieved2. carried out or executed3. possessing the feeling of having reached one's potential ⇒ She is nevertheless a happy and fulfilled woman., ⇒ I feel more fulfilled doing this than I've ever done., ⇒ She has courageously continued to lead a fulfilled life.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

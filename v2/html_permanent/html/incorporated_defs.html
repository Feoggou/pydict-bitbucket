incorporated

(ɪnˈkɔːpəˌreɪtɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. united or combined into a whole2. organized as a legal corporation, esp in commerce. Abbreviation: Inc, inc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

incorporatedness (inˈcorpoˌratedness) noun

craggy

(ˈkræɡɪ ) or cragged (ˈkræɡɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -gier or -giest1. having many crags2. (of the face) rugged; rocklike
Derived forms
craggily (ˈcraggily) adverbcragginess (ˈcragginess) or craggedness (ˈcraggedness) noun

craggy

(krægi )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A craggy cliff or mountain is steep and rocky. ⇒ ...tiny villages on craggy cliffs.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A craggy face has large features and deep lines. ⇒ He's a very small man with a lined, craggy face.

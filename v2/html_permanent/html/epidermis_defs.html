epidermis

(ˌɛpɪˈdɜːmɪs )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: cuticle the thin protective outer layer of the skin, composed of stratified epithelial tissue2. the outer layer of cells of an invertebrate3. the outer protective layer of cells of a plant, which may be thickened by a cuticle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

epidermal (ˌepiˈdermal), epidermic (ˌepiˈdermic) or epidermoid (ˌepiˈdermoid) adjective

Word Origin

C17: via Late Latin from Greek, from epi- + derma skin

book learning

(bʊk ˈlɜːnɪŋ) or book knowledge (bʊk ˈnɒlɪdʒ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. knowledge gained from books rather than from direct personal experience ⇒ Don't scorn book learning, but don't imagine either that it holds all the wisdom you need.2. old-fashioned formal education

returns

(rɪˈtɜːnz)
Definitions
plural noun1. profits accruing from an investment ⇒ Companies seek higher returns by investing in other corporations., ⇒ Higher returns and higher risk usually go hand in hand., ⇒ returns on investment2. a statement of the votes counted at an election or poll ⇒ Early returns show Bulgaria's opposition party may have won.3. See Many happy returns!

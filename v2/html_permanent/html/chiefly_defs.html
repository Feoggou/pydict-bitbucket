Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverb [ADV with v]You use chiefly to indicate that a particular reason, emotion, method, or feature is the main or most important one. ⇒ He joined the consular service, chiefly because this was one of the few job vacancies. ⇒ His response to attacks on his work was chiefly bewilderment.

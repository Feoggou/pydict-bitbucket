fifties

(ˈfɪftɪz)
Definitions
plural noun1. the years numbered between 50 and 59 in a particular century, esp the 20th century ⇒ He began performing in the fifties., ⇒ By the early fifties, anyone who felt they'd like to try acting, singing or writing could usually find a platform.2. the ages between 50 and 59 ⇒ I probably look as if I am in my fifties rather than my seventies.3. the range of temperature between 50 and 59 degrees

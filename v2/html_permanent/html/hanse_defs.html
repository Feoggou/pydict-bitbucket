Hanse

(hæns ) or Hansa (ˈhænsə , -zə)
Definitions
noun1. a medieval guild of merchants2. a fee paid by the new members of a medieval trading guild3. a. another name for the Hanseatic Leagueb. (as modifier) ⇒ a Hanse town
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C12: of Germanic origin; compare Old High German hansa, Old English hōs troop

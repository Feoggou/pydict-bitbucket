Definitions

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounIf someone shows disrespect, they speak or behave in a way that shows lack of respect for a person, law, or custom. [+ for] ⇒ ...young people with complete disrespect for authority.2. →  no disrespect (to sb/sth)

(aɪ drɒps) in the Pharmaceutical Industry
Definitions
noun(Pharmaceutical: Dosage forms) Eye drops are medicine that you put directly in your eyes one drop at a time. ⇒ Hold the dropper above your eye and put one of the eye drops in the lower eyelid. ⇒ Drop two eye drops into the infected eye every hour. ⇒ Eye drops are medicine that you put directly in your eyes one drop at a time. ▶  drops

tickling

(ˈtɪklɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the act of touching, stroking, or poking a person, part of the body, etc, so as to produce pleasure, laughter, or a twitching sensationadjective2. of or relating to a feeling of being tickled3. causing a sensation of itching, tingling, or irritation

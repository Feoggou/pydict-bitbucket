interests

(ˈɪntrəsts)
Definitions
plural noun1. business financial concerns; business ⇒ British interests in the Middle East, ⇒ the Hatch family, who controlled large dairy interests2. See to be in the interests of sb3. See to protect someone's interests4. See to act in someone's best interests

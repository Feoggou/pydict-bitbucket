(hoʊmsɪk ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are homesick, you feel unhappy because you are away from home and are missing your family, friends, and home very much. ⇒ She's feeling a little homesick.homesickness uncount noun ⇒ There were inevitable bouts of homesickness.

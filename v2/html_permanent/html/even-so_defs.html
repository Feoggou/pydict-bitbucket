Definitions
phraseYou use even so to introduce a surprising fact which relates to what you have just said. [spoken] ⇒ The bus was only half full. Even so, a young man asked Nina if the seat next to her was taken. ⇒ She has never given up her nationality. Even so, her opponents argue that she is not a true Burmese. →  even

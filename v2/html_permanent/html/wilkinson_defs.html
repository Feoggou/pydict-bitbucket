Wilkinson

(ˈwɪlkɪnsən )
Definitions
noun Jonny. born 1979, English Rugby Union player: scored 1,179 points (an England record) in 92 internationals (1998–2011); scored the last-minute drop goal that secured England victory in the final of the 2003 World Cup

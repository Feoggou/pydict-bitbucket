Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA battlefield is a place where a battle is fought. ⇒ ...the battlefields of the Somme.2. countable nounYou can refer to an issue or field of activity over which people disagree or compete as a battlefield. ⇒ ...the domestic battlefield of family life.

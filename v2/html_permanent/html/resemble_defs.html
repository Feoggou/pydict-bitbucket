resemble

(rɪˈzɛmbəl )
Definitions
verb(transitive) to possess some similarity to; be like
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

resembler (reˈsembler) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Old French resembler, from re- + sembler to look like, from Latin similis like

resemble

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪzembəl ) for learners of English
Word forms: resembles , resembling , resembled
Definitions
verb [no cont]If one thing or person resembles another, they are similar to each other. [V n] ⇒ Some of the commercially produced venison resembles beef in flavour. [V n] ⇒ She so resembles her mother.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

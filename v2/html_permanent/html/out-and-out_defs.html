Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]You use out-and-out to emphasize that someone or something has all the characteristics of a particular type of person or thing. [emphasis] ⇒ The Olympic theme tune 'Amigos para Siempre' (Friends for Life) proved an out-and-out success. ⇒ Much of what has been written about us is out-and-out lies.

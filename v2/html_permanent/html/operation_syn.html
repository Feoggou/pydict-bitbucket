Synonyms of operation

operation

noun

  1. undertaking, process, affair, organization, proceeding, procedure, coordination, oper8n (text messaging)
    • A major rescue operation is under way.
  2. manoeuvre, campaign, movement, exercise, assault, deployment, oper8n (text messaging)
    • a full-scale military operation
  3. business, concern, firm, organization, corporation, venture, enterprise, oper8n (text messaging)
    • The company has converted its mail-order operation into an e-business.
  4. surgery, surgical operation, surgical intervention, oper8n (text messaging)
    • an operation to reduce a bloodclot on the brain
  5. performance, working, running, action, movement, functioning, motion, manipulation, oper8n (text messaging)
    • Dials monitor every aspect of the operation of the aircraft.
  6. effect, force, activity, agency, influence, impact, effectiveness, instrumentality, oper8n (text messaging)
    • This change is due to the operation of several factors.
  • See in operation

festivity

(fɛsˈtɪvɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. merriment characteristic of a festival, party, etc2. any festival or other celebration3. (plural) festive proceedings; celebrations
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

festivity

Powered by COBUILD
(festɪvɪti ) for learners of English
Word forms: festivities
Definitions
1. uncount nounFestivity is the celebration of something in a happy way. ⇒ There was a general air of festivity and abandon.2. countable noun [usu pl]Festivities are events that are organized in order to celebrate something. celebrations ⇒ The festivities included a huge display of fireworks.

curvilinear

(ˌkɜːvɪˈlɪnɪə ) or curvilineal
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. consisting of, bounded by, or characterized by a curved line2. along a curved line ⇒ curvilinear motion3. mathematics (of a set of coordinates) determined by or determining a system of three orthogonal surfaces
Derived forms
ˌcurviˌlinearity (ˌcurviˌlineˈarity) nounˌcurvilinearly (ˌcurviˈlinearly) adverb

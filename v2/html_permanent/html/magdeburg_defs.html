Magdeburg

(ˈmæɡdəˌbɜːɡ , German ˈmakdəbʊrk)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan industrial city and port in central Germany, on the River Elbe, capital of Saxony-Anhalt: a leading member of the Hanseatic League, whose local laws, the Magdeburg Laws were adopted by many European cities. Pop: 227 535 (2003 est)

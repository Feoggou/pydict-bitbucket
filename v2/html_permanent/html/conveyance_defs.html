conveyance

(kənˈveɪəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of conveying2. a means of transport3. lawa. a transfer of the legal title to propertyb. the document effecting such a transfer
Derived forms
conveyancer (conˈveyancer) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA conveyance is a vehicle. [literary] ⇒ Mahoney had never seen such a conveyance before.2. uncount nounThe conveyance of something is the process of carrying or transporting it from one place to another. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ ...the conveyance of bicycles on Regional Railways trains.

inanimate

(ɪnˈænɪmɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. lacking the qualities or features of living beings; not animate ⇒ inanimate objects2. lacking any sign of life or consciousness; appearing dead3. lacking vitality; spiritless; dull
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inanimately (inˈanimately) adverbinanimateness (inˈanimateness) or inanimation (ɪnˌænɪˈmeɪʃən ) noun

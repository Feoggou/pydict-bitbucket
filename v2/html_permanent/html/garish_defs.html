(ˈɡɛərɪʃ )
Definitions
adjectivegay or colourful in a crude or vulgar manner; gaudy

Derived Forms

garishly (ˈgarishly) adverbgarishness (ˈgarishness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from earlier gaure to stare + -ish

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveYou describe something as garish when you dislike it because it is very bright in an unattractive, showy way. [disapproval] ⇒ They climbed the garish purple-carpeted stairs. ⇒ ...the restaurant's garish, illuminated signs.garishly graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj/-ed] ⇒ ...a garishly patterned three-piece suite.

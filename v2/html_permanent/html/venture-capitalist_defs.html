venture capitalist

Powered by COBUILD
(vɛntʃər kæpɪtəlɪst) or VC in Finance
Word forms: (regular plural) venture capitalists
Definitions
noun(Finance: Investment, Venture capitalism) A venture capitalist is someone who makes money by investing in very risky projects. ⇒ Many venture capitalists are making investments in software and networking businesses. ⇒ In the usual model, the venture capitalist is involved in management and mentoring of the startup. ⇒ A venture capitalist is someone who makes money by investing in very risky projects. ▶  venture capital

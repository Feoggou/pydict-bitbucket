Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that someone or something is dependable, you approve of them because you feel that you can be sure that they will always act consistently or sensibly, or do what you need them to do. [approval] ⇒ He was a good friend, a dependable companion.

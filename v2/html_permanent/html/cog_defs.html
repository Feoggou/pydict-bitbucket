cog1

(kɒɡ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of the teeth or projections on the rim of a gearwheel or sprocket2. a gearwheel, esp a small one3. a person or thing playing a small part in a large organization or processverbWord forms: cogs, cogging or cogged4. (transitive) metallurgy to roll (cast-steel ingots) to convert them into blooms
Word origin
C13: of Scandinavian origin; compare Danish kogge, Swedish kugge, Norwegian kug

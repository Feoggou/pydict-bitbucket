wakeful

(ˈweɪkfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. unable or unwilling to sleep2. sleepless3. alert

Derived Forms

wakefully (ˈwakefully) adverbwakefulness (ˈwakefulness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is wakeful finds it difficult to get to sleep and wakes up very often when they should be sleeping. ⇒ Wakeful babies will often continue to need little sleep as they grow older.wakefulness uncount noun ⇒ It is never a good idea to take sleeping tablets regularly for this kind of wakefulness.

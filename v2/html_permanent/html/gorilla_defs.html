gorilla

(ɡəˈrɪlə )
Definitions
noun1. the largest anthropoid ape, Gorilla gorilla, inhabiting the forests of central W Africa. It is stocky and massive, with a short muzzle and coarse dark hair2. informal a large, strong, and brutal-looking man
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gorilla-like (goˈrilla-ˌlike) adjectivegorillian (goˈrillian) or gorilline (ɡəˈrɪlaɪn ) adjectivegorilloid (goˈrilloid) adjective

Word Origin

C19: New Latin, from Greek Gorillai, an African tribe renowned for their hirsute appearance

organizing

or organising (ˈɔːɡənaɪzɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the activity or skill of coordinating people and events ⇒ she loves organizingadjective2. serving or existing to organize ⇒ Here, the main organizing principle in civil society is the market, which involves many individuals in simple trading or exchange relationships., ⇒ Queen Sofia heads the organizing committee for the Special Olympics.

blackcurrant

(ˌblækˈkʌrənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a N temperate shrub, Ribes nigrum, having red or white flowers and small edible black berries: family Grossulariaceae2. a. the fruit of this shrubb. (as modifier) ⇒ blackcurrant jelly

beard

(bɪəd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the hair growing on the lower parts of a man's face2. any similar growth in animals3. a tuft of long hairs in plants such as barley and wheat; awn4. the gills of an oyster5. a barb, as on an arrow or fish-hook6. slang a woman who accompanies a homosexual man to give the impression that he is heterosexual7. printing the part of a piece of type that connects the face with the shoulderverb (transitive)8. to oppose boldly or impertinently9. to pull or grasp the beard of
Derived forms
bearded (ˈbearded) adjective
Word origin
Old English beard; related to Old Norse barth, Old High German bart, Latin barba

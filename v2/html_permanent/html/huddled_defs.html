huddled

(ˈhʌdld)
Definitions
adjective1. crowded or nestled closely together ⇒ a huddled group of meerkats, ⇒ The chairs were huddled in a corner.2. (of a person) having their arms and legs close to their body ⇒ a huddled figure, ⇒ He was huddled over his books.

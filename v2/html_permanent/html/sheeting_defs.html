Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount noun [oft n N]Sheeting is metal, plastic, or other material that is made in the form of sheets. ⇒ They put plastic sheeting on the insides of our windows.2. uncount nounSheeting is cloth that is used for making sheets. ⇒ ...six yards of white sheeting.

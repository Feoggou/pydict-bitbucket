vegetation

(ˌvɛdʒɪˈteɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. plant life as a whole, esp the plant life of a particular region2. the process of vegetating3. pathology any abnormal growth, excrescence, etc4. a vegetative existence
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vegetational (ˌvegeˈtational) adjectivevegetatious (ˌvegeˈtatious) adjective

vegetation

Powered by COBUILD
(vedʒɪteɪʃən ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounPlants, trees, and flowers can be referred to as vegetation. [formal] ⇒ The inn has a garden of semi-tropical vegetation. ⇒ ...a smell of gently-rotting vegetation.

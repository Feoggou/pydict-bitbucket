Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveYou can describe something as abstruse if you find it difficult to understand, especially when you think it could be explained more simply. [formal, disapproval] ⇒ ...fruitless discussions about abstruse resolutions.

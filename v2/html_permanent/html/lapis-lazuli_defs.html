lapis lazuli

or lazuli (ˈlæpɪs )
Definitions
noun1. a brilliant blue variety of the mineral lazurite, used as a gemstone2. the deep blue colour of lapis lazuli
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin lapis stone + Medieval Latin lazulī, from lazulum, from Arabic lāzaward, from Persian lāzhuward, of obscure origin

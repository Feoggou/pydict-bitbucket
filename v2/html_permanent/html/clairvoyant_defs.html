clairvoyant

(klɛəˈvɔɪənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, possessing, or relating to clairvoyance2. having great insight or second sightnoun3. a person claiming to have the power to foretell future events
Derived forms
clairvoyantly (clairˈvoyantly) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveSomeone who is believed to be clairvoyant is believed to know about future events or to be able to communicate with dead people. ⇒ ...clairvoyant powers.clairvoyance uncount noun ⇒ ...his well-attested powers of telepathy and clairvoyance.2. countable nounA clairvoyant is someone who claims to be clairvoyant. ⇒ You did not have to be a clairvoyant to see that the war would go on.

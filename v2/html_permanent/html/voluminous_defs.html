voluminous

(vəˈluːmɪnəs )
Definitions
adjective1. of great size, quantity, volume, or extent2. (of writing) consisting of or sufficient to fill volumes3. prolific in writing or speech4. obsolete winding
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

voluminosity (vəˌluːmɪˈnɒsɪtɪ ) or voluminousness (voˈluminousness) nounvoluminously (voˈluminously) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin volūminōsus full of windings, from volūmen volume

(ɪnhɑːnsmənt , -hæns-) for learners of English

Definitions
variable nounThe enhancement of something is the improvement of it in relation to its value, quality, or attractiveness. [formal] ⇒ Music is merely an enhancement to the power of her words. ⇒ He was concerned with the enhancement of the human condition.

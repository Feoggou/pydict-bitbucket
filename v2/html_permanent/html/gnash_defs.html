gnash

(næʃ )
Definitions
verb1. to grind (the teeth) together, as in pain or anger2. (transitive) to bite or chew as by grinding the teethnoun3. the act of gnashing the teeth
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: probably of Scandinavian origin; compare Old Norse gnastan gnashing of teeth, gnesta to clatter

anthropology

(ˌænθrəˈpɒlədʒɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe study of humans, their origins, physical characteristics, institutions, religious beliefs, social relationships, etc. See also cultural anthropology, ethnology, physical anthropology, social anthropology
Derived forms

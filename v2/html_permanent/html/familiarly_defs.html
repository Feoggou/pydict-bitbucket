Definitions
familiarly known as/familiarly calledphraseIf you say that something or someone is familiarly known as a particular thing or familiarly called a particular thing, you are giving the name that people use informally to refer to it. ⇒ ...Ann Hamilton's father, familiarly known as "Hank."

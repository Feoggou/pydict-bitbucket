erring

(ˈɜːrɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. straying from the right moral course or accepted standards ⇒ Amy, this is your erring brother.2. unfaithful ⇒ to be married to a constantly erring husband, ⇒ an entire program of the festival dedicated to the theme of erring women

He strode in front of the erring father and daughter now, bristling with denunciations. Clive Barker EVERVILLEBut no, I was not Madame Borries asking questions about an erring husband.

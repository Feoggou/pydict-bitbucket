fractional

(ˈfrækʃənəl ) or fractionary (ˈfrækʃənərɪ)
Definitions
adjective1. relating to, containing, or constituting one or more fractions2. of or denoting a process in which components of a mixture are separated by exploiting differences in their physical properties, such as boiling points, solubility, etc ⇒ fractional distillation, ⇒ fractional crystallization3. very small or insignificant4. broken up; fragmented

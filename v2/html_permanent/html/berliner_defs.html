Berliner

(bɜːˈlɪnə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a native or inhabitant of Berlin2. a newspaper having a format between that of a broadsheet and a tabloid, approximately 18.5 inches by 12.4 inches (47 x 31.5 centimetres)
Word origin
C20: (for sense 2) this format was first adopted by Berlin newspapers

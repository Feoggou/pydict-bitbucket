quintal

(ˈkwɪntəl )
Definitions
noun1. a unit of weight equal to 100 pounds2. a unit of weight equal to 100 kilograms
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: via Old French from Arabic qintār, possibly from Latin centēnārius consisting of a hundred

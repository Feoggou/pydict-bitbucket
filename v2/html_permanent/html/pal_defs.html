pal

(pæl ) informal
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a close friend; comrade2. an accompliceverbWord forms: pals, palling or palled3. (intr; usually foll by with or about) to associate as friends

Word Origin

C17: from English Gypsy: brother, ultimately from Sanskrit bhrātar brother

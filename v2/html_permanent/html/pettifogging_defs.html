pettifogging

(petifɒgɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]You can describe an action or situation as pettifogging when you think that unnecessary attention is being paid to unimportant, boring details. [old-fashioned, disapproval] ⇒ ...pettifogging bureaucratic interference.

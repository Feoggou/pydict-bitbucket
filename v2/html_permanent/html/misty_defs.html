misty

(ˈmɪstɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: mistier or mistiest1. consisting of or resembling mist2. obscured by or as if by mist3. indistinct; blurred ⇒ the misty past

Derived Forms

mistily (ˈmistily) adverbmistiness (ˈmistiness) noun

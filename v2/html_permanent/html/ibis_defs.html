ibis

(ˈaɪbɪs )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural ibises or ibisany of various wading birds of the family Threskiornithidae, such as Threskiornis aethiopica (sacred ibis), that occur in warm regions and have a long thin down-curved bill: order Ciconiiformes (herons, storks, etc). Compare wood ibis
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: via Latin from Greek, from Egyptian hby

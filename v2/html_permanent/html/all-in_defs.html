all in

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (postpositive) informal completely exhausted; tired outadverb, adjective (all-in when prenominal)2. a. with all expenses or costs included in the price ⇒ the flat is one hundred pounds a week all inb. (prenominal) ⇒ the all-in price is thirty pounds

all-in

(ˌɔːlˈɪn)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. British with no extra costs; including everything2. wrestling another name for freestyle3. Australian informal denoting something, esp a fight, in which everyone or many people become involved ⇒ The incident sparked an all-in brawl and led to six players being sent off.

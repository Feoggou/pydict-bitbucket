unabashed for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnəbæʃt )
Definitions
adjectiveIf you describe someone as unabashed, you mean that they are not ashamed, embarrassed, or shy about something, especially when you think most people would be. [+ by] ⇒ He seems unabashed by his recent defeat. ⇒ He's an unabashed, old-fashioned romantic.

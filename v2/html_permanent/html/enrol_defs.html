enrol

or US enroll (ɪnˈrəʊl )
Definitions
verb -rols or US -rolls, -rolling or -rolled (mainly tr)1. to record or note in a roll or list2. (also intr) to become or cause to become a member; enlist; register3. to put on record; record4. rare to roll or wrap up

Definitions
regional note:   in AM, use enrollergative verb & verbIf you enrol or are enrolled at an institution or on a course, you officially join it and pay a fee for it. [be V-ed prep] ⇒ Cherny was enrolled at the University in 1945. [V prep] ⇒ She enrolled on a local Women Into Management course. [V prep] ⇒ At 33 he enrolled in a glass-making degree course at London's Royal College of Art. [V n prep] ⇒ I thought I'd enrol you with an art group at the school. [Also V]

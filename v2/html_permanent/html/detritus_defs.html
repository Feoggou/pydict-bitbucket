detritus

(dɪˈtraɪtəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a loose mass of stones, silt, etc, worn away from rocks2. an accumulation of disintegrated material or debris3. the organic debris formed from the decay of organisms
Derived forms
detrital (deˈtrital) adjective
Word origin
C18: from French détritus, from Latin dētrītus a rubbing away; see detriment

unprofitable

(ʌnˈprɒfɪtəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. not making a profit2. not fruitful or beneficial
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unprofitability (unˌprofitaˈbility) or unprofitableness (unˈprofitableness) noununprofitably (unˈprofitably) adverb

unprofitable for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnprɒfɪtəbəl )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveAn industry, company, or product that is unprofitable does not make any profit or does not make enough profit. [business] ⇒ ...unprofitable state-owned industries. ⇒ The newspaper is believed to have been unprofitable for at least the past decade.2. adjectiveUnprofitable activities or efforts do not produce any useful or helpful results. ⇒ ...an endless, unprofitable argument. ⇒ The day proved frustratingly unprofitable.

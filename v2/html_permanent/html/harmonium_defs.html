harmonium

(hɑːˈməʊnɪəm )
Definitions
nouna musical keyboard instrument of the reed organ family, in which air from pedal-operated bellows causes the reeds to vibrate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

bewilderment

Powered by COBUILD
(bɪwɪldəʳmənt )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft in N]Bewilderment is the feeling of being bewildered. ⇒ He shook his head in bewilderment.

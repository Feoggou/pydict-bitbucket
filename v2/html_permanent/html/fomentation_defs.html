fomentation

(ˌfoʊmɛnˈteɪʃən ; ˌfoʊmənˈteɪʃən ) in American English
Definitions
noun1.  treatment of bodily pain or injury by the application of warm, moist substances, as in a lotion or compress2.  a lotion, compress, etc. so applied3.  a stirring up of trouble; incitement

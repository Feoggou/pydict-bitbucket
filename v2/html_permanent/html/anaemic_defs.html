anaemic

or US anemic (əˈniːmɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. relating to or suffering from anaemia2. pale and sickly looking; lacking vitality
Derived forms
aˈnaemically adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
regional note:   in AM, use anemic1. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]Someone who is anaemic suffers from anaemia. ⇒ Losing a lot of blood makes you tired and anaemic.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as anaemic, you mean that it is not as strong or effective as you think it should be. ⇒ We will see some economic recovery, but it will be very anaemic. ⇒ They insist on tastier chocolate than the anaemic British stuff.

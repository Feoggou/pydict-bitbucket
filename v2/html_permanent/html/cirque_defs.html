cirque

(sɜːk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: corrie, cwm a semicircular or crescent-shaped basin with steep sides and a gently sloping floor formed in mountainous regions by the erosive action of a glacier2. archaeology an obsolete term for circle (sense 11)3. poetic a circle, circlet, or ring
Word origin
C17: from French, from Latin circus ring, circle, circus

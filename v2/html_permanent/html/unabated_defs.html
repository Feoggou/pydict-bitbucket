Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ after v, oft ADJ n, v-link ADJ]If something continues unabated, it continues without any reduction in intensity or amount. [+ for] ⇒ The fighting has continued unabated for over 24 hours. ⇒ ...his unabated enthusiasm for cinema.

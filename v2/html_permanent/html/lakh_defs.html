lakh

or lac (lɑːk )
Definitions
noun(in India and Pakistan) the number 100 000, esp when referring to this sum of rupees
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Hindi lākh, ultimately from Sanskrit lakshā a sign

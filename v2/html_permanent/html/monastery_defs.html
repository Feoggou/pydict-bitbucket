monastery

(ˈmɒnəstərɪ , -strɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -teriesthe residence of a religious community, esp of monks, living in seclusion from secular society and bound by religious vows

Derived Forms

monasterial (ˌmɒnəˈstɪərɪəl ) adjective

Word Origin

C15: from Church Latin monastērium, from Late Greek monastērion, from Greek monázein to live alone, from monos alone

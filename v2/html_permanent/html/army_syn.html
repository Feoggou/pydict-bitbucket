army

noun

  1. soldiers, military, troops, armed force, legions, infantry, military force, land forces, land force, soldiery • After returning from abroad, he joined the army.
  2. vast number, host, gang, mob, flock, array, legion, swarm, sea, pack, horde, multitude, throng, host (archaic) • data collected by an army of volunteers

QUOTATION:
 I don't know what effect these men will have upon the enemy, but, by God, they terrify me Duke of Wellington
PROVERB:
 An army marches on its stomach

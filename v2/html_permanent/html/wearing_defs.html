(weərɪŋ )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you say that a situation or activity is wearing, you mean that it requires a lot of energy and makes you feel mentally or physically tired. ⇒ She finds the continual confrontation very wearing. ⇒ Being in demand can be rather wearing.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

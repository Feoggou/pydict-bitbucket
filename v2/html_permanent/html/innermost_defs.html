innermost

(ˈɪnəˌməʊst )
Definitions
adjective1. being or located furthest within; central2. intimate; private ⇒ innermost beliefs

innermost

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnəʳmoʊst ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Your innermost thoughts and feelings are your most personal and secret ones. ⇒ ...expressing your innermost feelings. ⇒ ...revealing a company's innermost secrets.2. adjective [ADJ n]The innermost thing is the one that is nearest to the centre. ⇒ ...the innermost part of the eye. ⇒ She put the receipt into the innermost pocket of her bag.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

healthy

(ˈhɛlθɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: healthier or healthiest1. enjoying good health2. functioning well or being sound ⇒ the company's finances are not very healthy3. conducive to health; salutary4. indicating soundness of body or mind ⇒ a healthy appetite5. informal considerable in size or amount ⇒ a healthy sum

(helθi ) for learners of English
Word forms: healthier , healthiest
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is healthy is well and is not suffering from any illness. ⇒ Most of us need to lead more balanced lives to be healthy and happy. ⇒ She had a normal pregnancy and delivered a healthy child.healthily (helθɪli ) graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV after v] ⇒ What I really want is to live healthily for as long as possible.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If a feature or quality that you have is healthy, it makes you look well or shows that you are well. ⇒ ...the glow of healthy skin. ⇒ ...young adults with healthy appetites.3. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Something that is healthy is good for your health. ⇒ ...a great healthy outdoor pursuit. ⇒ ...a healthy diet.4. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A healthy organization or system is successful. ⇒ ...an economically healthy socialist state.5. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A healthy amount of something is a large amount that shows success. ⇒ He predicts a continuation of healthy profits in the current financial year. ⇒ ...a healthy bank account.6. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you have a healthy attitude about something, you show good sense. ⇒ She has a refreshingly healthy attitude to work. ⇒ It's very healthy to be afraid when there's something to be afraid of.healthily adverb [ADV adj] ⇒ I had never seen bombing on such a scale, and I was healthily apprehensive.

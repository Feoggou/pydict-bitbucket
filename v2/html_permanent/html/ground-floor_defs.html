Word forms: ground floors
Definitions
countable nounThe ground floor of a building is the floor that is level or almost level with the ground outside. [British] ⇒ She showed him around the ground floor of the empty house. ⇒ Jenny now lives in a terraced ground floor flat.regional note:   in AM, use first floor → floor

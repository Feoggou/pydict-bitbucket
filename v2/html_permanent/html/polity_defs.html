polity

(ˈpɒlɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. a form of government or organization of a state, church, society, etc; constitution2. a politically organized society, state, city, etc3. the management of public or civil affairs4. political organization

Word Origin

C16: from Latin polītīa, from Greek politeia citizenship, civil administration, from politēs citizen, from polis city

milestone

(ˈmaɪlˌstəʊn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a stone pillar that shows the distance in miles to or from a place2. a significant event in life, history, etc

Word forms: milestones
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA milestone is an important event in the history or development of something or someone. [+ in] ⇒ He said the launch of the party represented a milestone in Zambian history. ⇒ Starting school is a milestone for both children and parents.2. countable nounA milestone is a stone by the side of a road showing the distances to particular places.

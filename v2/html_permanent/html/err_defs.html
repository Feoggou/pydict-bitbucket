err

(ɜː )
Definitions
verb (intransitive)1. to make a mistake; be incorrect2. to stray from the right course or accepted standards; sin3. to act with bias, esp favourable bias ⇒ to err on the side of justice
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: erren to wander, stray, from Old French errer, from Latin errāre

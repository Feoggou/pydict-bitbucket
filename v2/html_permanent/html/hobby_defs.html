hobby1

(ˈhɒbɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -bies1. an activity pursued in spare time for pleasure or relaxation2. archaic or dialect a small horse or pony3. short for hobbyhorse (sense 1)4. an early form of bicycle, without pedals
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hobbyist (ˈhobbyist) noun

Word Origin

C14 hobyn, probably variant of proper name Robin; compare dobbin

hobby2

(ˈhɒbɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -biesany of several small Old World falcons, esp the European Falco subbuteo, formerly used in falconry
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French hobet, from hobe falcon; probably related to Middle Dutch hobbelen to roll, turn

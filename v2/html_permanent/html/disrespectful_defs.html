Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are disrespectful, you show no respect in the way that you speak or behave to someone. [+ to/of] ⇒ ...accusations that he had been disrespectful to the Queen. ⇒ They shouldn't treat their mother in this disrespectful way.disrespectfully graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ They get angry if they think they are being treated disrespectfully.

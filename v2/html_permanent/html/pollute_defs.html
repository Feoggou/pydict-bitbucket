pollute

(pəˈluːt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to contaminate, as with poisonous or harmful substances2. to make morally corrupt or impure; sully3. to desecrate or defile

Derived Forms

polluter (polˈluter) noun

Word Origin

C14 polute, from Latin polluere to defile

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbTo pollute water, air, or land means to make it dirty and dangerous to live in or to use, especially with poisonous chemicals or sewage. [V n] ⇒ Heavy industry pollutes our rivers with noxious chemicals. [V n] ⇒ A number of beaches in the region have been polluted by sewage pumped into the Irish Sea.polluted graded adjective & adjective ⇒ The police have warned the city's inhabitants not to bathe in the polluted river.

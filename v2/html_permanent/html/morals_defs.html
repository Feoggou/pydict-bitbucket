morals

(ˈmɒrəlz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounprinciples of behaviour in accordance with standards of right and wrong ⇒ Western ideas and morals, ⇒ They have no morals., ⇒ Films like this are a danger to public morals.

Her heart is kind, but her passions are great and her morals few. Weldon, Fay SPLITTINGI don't like it when the law overlaps with morals or ethics. Meek, M R D IN REMEMBRANCE OF ROSEEven if his morals got twisted up the way you suggest, he'd always insist on doing the risky part himself.

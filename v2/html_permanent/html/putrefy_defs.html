putrefy

(ˈpjuːtrɪˌfaɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -fies, -fying or -fied(of organic matter) to decompose or rot with an offensive smell

Derived Forms

putrefaction (ˌpjuːtrɪˈfækʃən ) nounˌputrefactive (ˌputreˈfactive) or putrefacient (ˌpjuːtrɪˈfeɪʃənt ) adjectiveputreˌfiable (ˈputreˌfiable) adjectiveputreˌfier (ˈputreˌfier) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Old French putrefier + Latin putrefacere, from puter rotten + facere to make

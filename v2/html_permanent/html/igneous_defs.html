igneous

(ˈɪɡnɪəs )
Definitions
adjective1. (of rocks) derived by solidification of magma or molten lava emplaced on or below the earth's surface. Compare sedimentary, metamorphic (sense 2)2. of or relating to fire
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin igneus fiery, from ignis fire

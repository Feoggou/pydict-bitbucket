sacrum

(ˈseɪkrəm , ˈsækrəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cra (-krə)1. (in man) the large wedge-shaped bone, consisting of five fused vertebrae, in the lower part of the back2. the corresponding part in some other vertebrates

Word Origin

C18: from Latin os sacrum holy bone, because it was used in sacrifices, from sacer holy

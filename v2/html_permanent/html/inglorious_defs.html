inglorious

(ɪnˈɡlɔːrɪəs )
Definitions
adjective1. without courage or glory; dishonourable, shameful, or disgraceful2. unknown or obscure
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ingloriously (inˈgloriously) adverbingloriousness (inˈgloriousness) noun

inglorious

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnglɔːriəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something as inglorious, you mean that it is something to be ashamed of. ⇒ He wouldn't have accepted such an inglorious outcome.ingloriously graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ If fighting worsens, the troops might be reinforced, or ingloriously withdrawn.

gold dust

Definitions
noun1. gold in the form of small particles or powder, as found in placer-mining2. a valuable or rare thing ⇒ tickets for this match are gold dust

Definitions
1. uncount nounGold dust is gold in the form of a fine powder.2. uncount nounIf you say that a type of thing is like gold dust or is gold dust, you mean that it is very difficult to obtain, usually because everyone wants it. [British] ⇒ Tickets were like gold dust.

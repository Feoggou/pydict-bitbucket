Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: steamier or steamiest1. of, resembling, full of, or covered with steam2. informal lustful or erotic ⇒ steamy nightlife

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Steamy means involving exciting sex. [informal] ⇒ ...a steamy thriller set in France. ⇒ He'd had a steamy affair with an office colleague.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A steamy place has hot, wet air. ⇒ ...a steamy cafe. ⇒ The air was hot and steamy from the heat of a hundred bodies.

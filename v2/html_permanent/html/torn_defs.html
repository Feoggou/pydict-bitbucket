torn

(tɔːn )
Definitions
verb1. the past participle of tear1 (sense 2)2. See that's torn itadjective3. split or cut4. divided or undecided, as in preference ⇒ he was torn between staying and leaving

torn

Powered by COBUILD
(tɔːʳn ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. Torn is the past participle of tear2.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are torn between two or more things, you cannot decide which to choose, and so you feel anxious or troubled. [+ between] ⇒ Robb is torn between becoming a doctor and a career in athletics. ⇒ I know the administration was very torn on this subject.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

cowardly

adjective

  1. faint-hearted, scared, spineless, gutless (informal), base, soft, yellow (informal), weak, chicken (slang), shrinking, fearful, craven, abject, dastardly, boneless, timorous, weak-kneed (informal), pusillanimous, chickenshit (US, slang), recreant (archaic), caitiff (archaic), chicken-hearted, lily-livered, white-livered, sookie (New Zealand) • I was too cowardly to complain. OPPOSITES:  brave, daring, bold, courageous, audacious, intrepid, valiant, plucky, doughty, dauntless

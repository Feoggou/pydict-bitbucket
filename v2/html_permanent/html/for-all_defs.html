for all

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
phraseYou use for all in phrases such as for all I know, and for all he cares, to emphasize that you do not know something or that someone does not care about something. [emphasis] ⇒ For all we know, he may even not be in this country. ⇒ You can go right now for all I care. →  all

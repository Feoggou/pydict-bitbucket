sumach

or US sumac (ˈsuːmæk , ˈʃuː-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any temperate or subtropical shrub or small tree of the anacardiaceous genus Rhus, having compound leaves, clusters of green flowers, and red hairy fruits. See also poison sumach2. a preparation of powdered leaves of certain species of Rhus, esp R. coriaria, used in dyeing and tanning3. the wood of any of these plants

Word Origin

C14: via Old French from Arabic summāq

detergent

(dɪˈtɜːdʒənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a cleansing agent, esp a surface-active chemical such as an alkyl sulphonate, widely used in industry, laundering, shampoos, etcadjective also: detersive (dɪˈtɜːsɪv )2. having cleansing power
Word origin
C17: from Latin dētergēns wiping off; see deterge

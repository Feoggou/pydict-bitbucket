robbery

(ˈrɒbərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -beries1. criminal law the stealing of property from a person by using or threatening to use force2. the act or an instance of robbing

Definitions
variable nounRobbery is the crime of stealing money or property from a bank, shop, or vehicle, often by using force or threats. ⇒ The gang members committed dozens of armed robberies. ⇒ The man was serving a sentence for robbery with violence.

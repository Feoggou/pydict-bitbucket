aqueduct

(ˈækwɪˌdʌkt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a conduit used to convey water over a long distance, either by a tunnel or more usually by a bridge2. a structure, usually a bridge, that carries such a conduit or a canal across a valley or river3. a channel in an organ or part of the body, esp one that conveys a natural body fluid
Word origin
C16: from Latin aquaeductus, from aqua water + dūcere to convey

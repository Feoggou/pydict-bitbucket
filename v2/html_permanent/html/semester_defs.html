semester

(sɪˈmɛstə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (in some universities) either of two divisions of the academic year, ranging from 15 to 18 weeks2. (in German universities) a session of six months

Derived Forms

semestral (seˈmestral) adjective

Word Origin

C19: via German from Latin sēmestris half-yearly, from sex six + mensis a month

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveAn object that is cracked has lines on its surface because it is damaged. ⇒ The ceiling was grey and cracked. ⇒ ...a cracked mirror.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA cracked voice or a cracked musical note sounds rough and unsteady. ⇒ When he spoke, his voice was hoarse and cracked.3. graded adjectiveIf you say that someone is cracked, you think that their behaviour or ideas are very strange. [informal, disapproval] crazy

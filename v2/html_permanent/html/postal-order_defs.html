Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA postal order is a piece of paper representing a sum of money which you can buy at a post office and send to someone as a way of sending them money by post. [British]regional note:   in AM, usually use money order
Example sentences containing 'postal order'

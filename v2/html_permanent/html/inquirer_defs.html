(ɪnkwaɪərəʳ ) also enquirer for learners of English
Word forms: inquirers
Definitions
1. countable nounAn inquirer is a person who asks for information about something or someone. [formal] ⇒ I send each inquirer a packet of information.2. countable nounInquirer is used in the names of some newspapers and magazines. ⇒ ...the National Enquirer.

Synonyms of tick 1

tick1

noun

  1. check mark, mark, line, stroke, dash
    • Place a tick in the appropriate box.
  2. click, tap, tapping, clicking, clack, ticktock
    • He sat listening to the tick of the grandfather clock.
  3. (British informal) moment, second, minute, shake (informal), flash, instant, sec (informal), twinkling, split second, jiffy (informal), trice, half a mo (British, informal), two shakes of a lamb's tail (informal), bat of an eye (informal)
    • I'll be back in a tick.

verb

  1. mark, indicate, mark off, check off, choose, select
    • Please tick here if you do not want to receive such mailings.
  2. click, tap, clack, ticktock
    • A clock ticked busily from the kitchen counter.

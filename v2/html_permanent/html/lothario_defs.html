Lothario

(ləʊˈθɑːrɪˌəʊ )
Definitions
noun plural -os(sometimes not capital) a rake, libertine, or seducer
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: after a seducer in Nicholas Rowe's tragedy The Fair Penitent (1703)

protégé

or feminine protégée (ˈprəʊtɪˌʒeɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna person who is protected and aided by the patronage of another person

Word Origin

C18: from French protéger to protect

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
language note:   The spelling protégée is often used when referring to a woman.countable nounThe protégé of an older and more experienced person is a young person who is helped and guided by them over a period of time. ⇒ He had been a protégé of Captain James. ⇒ ...Klimt's young protégé, Egon Schiele.

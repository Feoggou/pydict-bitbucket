inaccuracy

(ɪnˈækjʊrəsɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -cies1. lack of accuracy; imprecision2. an error, a mistake, or a slip
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnækjʊrəsi ) for learners of English
Word forms: inaccuracies
Definitions
variable nounThe inaccuracy of a statement or measurement is the fact that it is not accurate or correct. [+ of] ⇒ He was disturbed by the inaccuracy of the answers. ⇒ A reporter tries to guard against inaccuracies by checking with a variety of sources.

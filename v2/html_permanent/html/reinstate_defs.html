reinstate

Powered by COBUILD
(riːɪnsteɪt ) for learners of English
Word forms: reinstates , reinstating , reinstated
Definitions
1. verbIf you reinstate someone, you give them back a job or position which had been taken away from them. [V n] ⇒ The governor is said to have agreed to reinstate five senior workers who were dismissed. [V n] ⇒ Thailand's prime minister announced his resignation today, but he is widely expected to be reinstated within a few days.2. verbTo reinstate a law, facility, or practice means to start having it again. restore [V n] ⇒ ...the decision to reinstate the grant. [V n] ⇒ There have now been 139 executions since the death penalty was reinstated in 1976.

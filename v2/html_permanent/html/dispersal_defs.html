dispersal

(dɪˈspɜːsəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of dispersing or the condition of being dispersed2. the spread of animals, plants, or seeds to new areas

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounDispersal is the spreading of things over a wide area. ⇒ Plants have different mechanisms of dispersal for their spores.2. uncount nounThe dispersal of a crowd involves splitting it up and making the people leave in different directions. [+ of] ⇒ The police ordered the dispersal of the crowds gathered round the building.

familiarity

(fəˌmɪlɪˈærɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. reasonable knowledge or acquaintance, as with a subject or place2. close acquaintanceship or intimacy3. undue intimacy4. (sometimes plural) an instance of unwarranted intimacy

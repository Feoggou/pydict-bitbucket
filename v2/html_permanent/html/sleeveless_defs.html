sleeveless

Powered by COBUILD
(sliːvləs )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]A sleeveless dress, top, or other item of clothing has no sleeves. ⇒ She wore a sleeveless silk dress.

The torches brought out the glow of their terracotta-coloured, sleeveless tunics. Shah, Idries KARA KUSHHe wore baggy cotton pants and a loose sleeveless top and his hair was black and curly and his skin was pale.

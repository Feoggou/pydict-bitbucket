Definitions

lasting

Powered by COBUILD
(lɑːstɪŋ , læst-) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]You can use lasting to describe a situation, result, or agreement that continues to exist or have an effect for a very long time. ⇒ We are well on our way to a lasting peace. ⇒ She left a lasting impression on him. ⇒ ...but there was no lasting damage.2. → See also last
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

termination

(ˌtɜːmɪˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of terminating or the state of being terminated2. something that terminates3. a final result4. an induced abortion
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

terminational (ˌtermiˈnational) adjective

They caught her, and did the termination on a kitchen table - without an anaesthetic.

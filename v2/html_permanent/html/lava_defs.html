lava

(ˈlɑːvə )
Definitions
noun1. magma emanating from volcanoes and other vents2. any extrusive igneous rock formed by the cooling and solidification of molten lava
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Italian (Neapolitan dialect), from Latin lavāre to wash

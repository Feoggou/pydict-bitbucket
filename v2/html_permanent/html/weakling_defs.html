weakling

(ˈwiːklɪŋ )
Definitions
nouna person or animal that is lacking in strength or weak in constitution or character

weakling for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(wiːklɪŋ )
Word forms: weaklings
Definitions
1. countable nounIf you describe a person or an animal as a weakling, you mean that they are physically weak. [disapproval]2. countable nounIf you describe someone as a weakling, you mean that they have a weak character and are not at all brave. [disapproval] wimp ⇒ ...a moral weakling.

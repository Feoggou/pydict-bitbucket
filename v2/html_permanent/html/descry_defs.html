descry

(dɪˈskraɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -scries, -scrying or -scried (transitive)1. to discern or make out; catch sight of2. to discover by looking carefully; detect
Derived forms
descrier (deˈscrier) noun
Word origin
C14: from Old French descrier to proclaim, decry
Example sentences containing 'descry'
Danlo was perhaps the only one to descry Hanuman's true potential. Zindell, David THE BROKEN GODDimly through the mists they could descry the long arm of the mountains rising on their left. J.R.R. Tolkien THE LORD OF THE RINGS

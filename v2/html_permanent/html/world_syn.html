Synonyms of world

world

noun

  1. earth, planet, globe, earthly sphere
    • It's a beautiful part of the world.
  2. mankind, man, men, everyone, the public, everybody, humanity, human race, humankind, the race of man
    • The world was shocked by this heinous crime.
  3. sphere, system, area, field, environment, province, kingdom, realm, domain
    • The publishing world had never seen an event quite like this.
  4. life, nature, existence, creation, universe, cosmos
    • Be happy, in this world and the next!
  5. planet, star, orb, heavenly body
    • conditions which would support life on other worlds
  6. period, times, days, age, era, epoch
    • What was life like for the ordinary man in the medieval world?

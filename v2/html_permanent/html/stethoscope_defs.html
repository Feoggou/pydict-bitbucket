stethoscope

(ˈstɛθəˌskəʊp )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. medicine an instrument for listening to the sounds made within the body, typically consisting of a hollow disc that transmits the sound through hollow tubes to earpieces2. Also called: obstetric stethoscope a narrow cylinder expanded at both ends to receive and transmit fetal sounds

Derived Forms

stethoscopic (ˌstɛθəˈskɒpɪk ) adjectivestethoscopy (stɛˈθɒskəpɪ ) noun

Word Origin

C19: from French, from Greek stēthos breast + -scope

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA stethoscope is an instrument that a doctor uses to listen to your heart and breathing. It consists of a small disc that is placed on your body, connected to a hollow tube with two pieces that the doctor puts in his or her ears.

by-numbers

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveinformal done in an uninspired, simplistic, or formulaic way
Word origin
C20: from painting by numbers, a method of painting a picture in which the colours to be used are indicated by numbers

by numbers

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
military (of a drill procedure, etc) performed step by step, each move being made on the call of a number▷ See number

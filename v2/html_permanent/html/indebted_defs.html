indebted

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndetɪd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that you are indebted to someone for something, you mean that you are very grateful to them for something. [+ to] ⇒ I am deeply indebted to him for his help.indebtedness uncount noun [+ to] ⇒ Mortimer recounted his indebtedness to her in his autobiography.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Indebted countries, organizations, or people are ones that owe money to other countries, organizations, or people. ⇒ America's treasury secretary identified the most heavily indebted countries.indebtedness uncount noun ⇒ The company has reduced its indebtedness to just $15 million.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

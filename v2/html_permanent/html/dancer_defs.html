Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA dancer is a person who earns money by dancing, or a person who is dancing. ⇒ His previous girlfriend was a dancer with the Royal Ballet. ⇒ The dancers began to walk away from the floor.2. countable noun [adj N]If you say that someone is a good dancer or a bad dancer, you are saying how well or badly they can dance. ⇒ He was the best dancer in LA.

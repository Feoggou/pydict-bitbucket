Definitions

inaction

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnækʃən ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [oft with poss]If you refer to someone's inaction, you disapprove of the fact that they are doing nothing. [disapproval] [+ of] ⇒ He is bitter about the inaction of the other political parties.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

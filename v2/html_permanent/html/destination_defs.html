destination

(ˌdɛstɪˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the predetermined end of a journey or voyage2. the ultimate end or purpose for which something is created or a person is destined

(destɪneɪʃən )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounThe destination of someone or something is the place to which they are going or being sent. ⇒ Spain is still our most popular holiday destination. ⇒ Only half of the emergency supplies have reached their destination.

He left his hotel in a rented car the next morning in time to reach his destination at about half-past ten.

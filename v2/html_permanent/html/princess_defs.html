princess

(prɪnˈsɛs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (in Britain) a daughter of the sovereign or of one of the sovereign's sons2. a nonreigning female member of a sovereign family3. the wife and consort of a prince4. any very attractive or outstanding woman5. Also called: princess dress, princess line a style of dress with a fitted bodice and an A-line skirt that is shaped by seams from shoulder to hem without a seam at the waistline

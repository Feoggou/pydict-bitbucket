payment

(ˈpeɪmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of paying2. a sum of money paid3. something given in return; punishment or reward

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [oft n N]A payment is an amount of money that is paid to someone, or the act of paying this money. ⇒ Thousands of its customers are in arrears with loans and mortgage payments. [+ of] ⇒ The fund will make payments of just over £1 billion next year. [Also + to/on]2. uncount nounPayment is the act of paying money to someone or of being paid. [+ of] ⇒ He had sought to obtain payment of a sum which he had claimed was owed to him. [Also + for]3. → see also balance of payments, down payment

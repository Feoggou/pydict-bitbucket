fiddler

(ˈfɪdlə )
Definitions
noun1. a person who plays the fiddle, esp in folk music2. See fiddler crab3. a person who wastes time or acts aimlessly4. informal a cheat or petty rogue
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

fiddler

Powered by COBUILD
(fɪdləʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: fiddlers
Definitions
1. countable nounA fiddler is someone who plays the violin, especially one who plays folk music. ⇒ And the fiddler played another little tune.2. countable nounA fiddler is someone who lies or dishonestly alters financial documents in order to get money for themselves. [British, informal] ⇒ Other benefits such as dole money could be frozen and there will be a huge push to weed out fiddlers.

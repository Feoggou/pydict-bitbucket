one-time for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD

Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]One-time is used to describe something such as a job, position, or role which someone used to have, or something which happened in the past. [journalism] ⇒ She's 87 years old, and a one-time member of the Ziegfeld Follies. ⇒ The legislative body had voted to oust the country's onetime rulers.

unswervingly

(ʌnˈswɜːvɪŋlɪ)
Definitions
adverbin a constant and unswerving manner ⇒ He was unswervingly loyal to Churchill., ⇒ He held unswervingly to his course.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Whatever his counsel had been that night, Manfred would have followed it unswervingly. Mark Mills AMAGANSETTThe beautiful, lively Jennie was a particular favourite, although she was unswervingly faithful to her Gérard in Paris.

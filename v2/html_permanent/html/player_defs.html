player

(ˈpleɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who participates in or is skilled at some game or sport2. a person who plays a game or sport professionally3. a person who plays a musical instrument4. an actor5. informal a participant, esp a powerful one, in a particular field of activity ⇒ a leading city player6. See record player7. the playing mechanism in a Pianola

player

Powered by COBUILD
(pleɪəʳ )
Word forms: players
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA player in a sport or game is a person who takes part, either as a job or for fun. ⇒ ...his greatness as a player. ⇒ She was a good golfer and tennis player. ⇒ ...top chess-players.2. countable nounYou can use player to refer to a musician. For example, a piano player is someone who plays the piano. ⇒ ...a professional trumpet player.3. countable nounIf a person, country, or organization is a player in something, they are involved in it and important in it. [+ in] ⇒ Big business has become a major player in the art market. ⇒ America is not a party to the negotiations, yet it is a key player.4. countable nounA player is an actor. ⇒ ...a company of players. ⇒ Oscar nominations went to all five leading players.5. → see also cassette player, CD player, record player, team player

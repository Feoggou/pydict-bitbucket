disconnected

(ˌdɪskəˈnɛktɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. not rationally connected; confused or incoherent2. not connected or joined
Derived forms
ˌdisconnectedly (ˌdisconˈnectedly) adverbˌdisconnectedness (ˌdisconˈnectedness) noun

Definitions

graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Disconnected things are not linked in any way. ⇒ ...sequences of utterly disconnected events. ⇒ His ability to absorb bits of disconnected information was astonishing.

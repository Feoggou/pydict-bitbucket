hurricane

(ˈhʌrɪkən , -keɪn)
Definitions
noun1. a severe, often destructive storm, esp a tropical cyclone2. a. a wind of force 12 or above on the Beaufort scaleb. (as modifier) ⇒ a wind of hurricane force3. anything acting like such a wind
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Spanish huracán, from Taino hurakán, from hura wind

befall

(bɪˈfɔːl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -falls, -falling, -fell or -fallen archaic or literary1. (intransitive) to take place; come to pass2. (transitive) to happen to3. (intransitive; usually foll by to) to be due, as by right
Word origin
Old English befeallan; related to Old High German bifallan, Dutch bevallen; see be-, fall

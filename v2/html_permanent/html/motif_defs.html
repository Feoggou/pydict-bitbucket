motif

(məʊˈtiːf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a distinctive idea, esp a theme elaborated on in a piece of music, literature, etc2. Also: motive a recurring form or shape in a design or pattern3. a single added piece of decoration, such as a symbol or name on a jumper, sweatshirt, etc

Word Origin

C19: from French. See motive

motif

Powered by COBUILD
(moʊtiːf )
Word forms: motifs
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA motif is a design which is used as a decoration or as part of an artistic pattern. ⇒ ...a rose motif.2. countable nounA motif is a theme or idea that is frequently repeated throughout a piece of literature or music. theme ⇒ The motif of these volumes is that 'solitude is the richness of the soul, loneliness is its poverty.'

disarmament

(dɪsˈɑːməmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the reduction of offensive or defensive fighting capability, as by a nation2. the act of disarming or state of being disarmed

disarmament

Powered by COBUILD
(dɪsɑːʳməmənt )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounDisarmament is the act of reducing the number of weapons, especially nuclear weapons, that a country has. ⇒ ...the pace of nuclear disarmament. ⇒ ...unilateral disarmament.

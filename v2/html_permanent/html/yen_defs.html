yen

Powered by COBUILD
(jen ) for learners of English
Word forms: yen
Definitions
1. countable nounThe yen is the unit of currency that is used in Japan. ⇒ She's got a part-time job for which she earns 2,000 yen a month.The yen is also used to refer to the Japanese currency system. ⇒ ...sterling's devaluation against the dollar and the yen.2. singular noun [N to-inf]If you have a yen to do something, you have a strong desire to do it. [+ for] ⇒ Mike had a yen to try cycling. ⇒ He's a natural with any kind of engine but he has an unfortunate yen for speed.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

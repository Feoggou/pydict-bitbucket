nil for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(nɪl )
Definitions
1. numberNil means the same as zero. It is usually used to say what the score is in sports such as rugby or football. [British] ⇒ They beat Argentina one-nil in the final. → nought2. uncount nounIf you say that something is nil, you mean that it does not exist at all. ⇒ Their legal rights are virtually nil. ⇒ The heating in winter was almost nil.

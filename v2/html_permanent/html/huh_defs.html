huh

Powered by COBUILD
(hʌ , hɜː) for learners of English
Definitions
Huh is used in writing to represent a noise that people make at the end of a question if they want someone to agree with them or if they want someone to repeat what they have just said. Huh is also used to show that someone is surprised or not impressed. ⇒ Can we just get on with it, huh? ⇒ Clever, huh? ⇒ Huh? What's going on? You want to tell me what I did? ⇒ Huh. What are you so excited about?

lights1

(laɪts )
Definitions
plural nouna person's ideas, knowledge, or understanding ⇒ he did it according to his lights
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

lights2

(laɪts )
Definitions
plural nounthe lungs, esp of sheep, bullocks, and pigs, used for feeding pets and occasionally in human food
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: plural noun use of light2, referring to the light weight of the lungs

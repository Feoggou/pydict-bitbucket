fait accompli

Powered by COBUILD
(feɪt əkɒmpli , US - ækɔːmpliː ) for learners of English
Word forms: faits accomplis
Definitions
countable noun [usu sing]If something is a fait accompli, it has already been decided or done and cannot be changed. [formal] ⇒ They became increasingly annoyed that they were being presented with a fait accompli.

extenuating

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪkstenjueɪtɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you say that there are extenuating circumstances for a bad situation or wrong action, you mean that there are reasons or factors which partly excuse it. [formal] ⇒ The defendants decide to admit their guilt, but insist that there are extenuating circumstances.

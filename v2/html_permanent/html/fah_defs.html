fah

or fa (fɑː )
Definitions
noun music1. (in the fixed system of solmization) the note F2. (in tonic sol-fa) the fourth degree of any major scale; subdominant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: see gamut

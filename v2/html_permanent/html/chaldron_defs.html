chaldron

(ˈtʃɔːldrən ) or chalder (ˈtʃɔːldə)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna unit of capacity equal to 36 bushels. Formerly used in the US for the measurement of solids, being equivalent to 1.268 cubic metres. Used in Britain for both solids and liquids, it is equivalent to 1.309 cubic metres
Word origin
C17: from Old French chauderon cauldron

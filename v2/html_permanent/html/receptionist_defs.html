(rɪˈsɛpʃənɪst )
Definitions
nouna person employed in an office, hotel, doctor's surgery, etc, to receive clients, guests, or patients, answer the telephone, arrange appointments, etc

Definitions
1. countable nounIn a hotel, the receptionist is the person whose job is to book rooms for people and answer their questions. [mainly British]regional note:   in AM, use desk clerk2. countable nounIn an office or hospital, the receptionist is the person whose job is to answer the telephone, arrange appointments, and deal with people when they first arrive.

receptionist

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪsɛpʃənɪst) in Hospitality
Word forms: (regular plural) receptionists
Definitions
noun(Hospitality (hotel): Hotel personnel) In a hotel, the receptionist is the person whose job is to reserve rooms for people and answer their questions. ⇒ The receptionist greeted him before asking his name. ⇒ The receptionist will check you in and give you a key card. ⇒ Ask the receptionist to call a cab for you.

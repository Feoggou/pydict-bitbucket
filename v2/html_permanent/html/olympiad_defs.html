Olympiad

(əˈlɪmpɪˌæd )
Definitions
noun1. a staging of the modern Olympic Games2. the four-year period between consecutive celebrations of the Olympic Games; a unit of ancient Greek chronology dating back to 776 bc3. an international contest in chess, bridge, etc

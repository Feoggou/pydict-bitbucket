withal

(wɪˈðɔːl )
Definitions
adverb1. literary as well; likewise2. literary nevertheless3. archaic therewithpreposition4. (postpositive) an archaic word for with
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C12: from with + all

Who e'er rigged fair ship to lie in harbours, And not to seek new lands, or not to deal withal ?

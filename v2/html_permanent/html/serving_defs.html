Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA serving is an amount of food that is given to one person at a meal. [+ of] ⇒ Quantities will vary according to how many servings of soup you want to prepare. ⇒ Each serving contains 240 calories.2. adjective [ADJ n]A serving spoon or dish is used for giving out food at a meal. ⇒ Pile the potatoes into a warm serving dish.

indescribable

(ˌɪndɪˈskraɪbəbəl )
Definitions
adjectivebeyond description; too intense, extreme, etc, for words
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

indescribability (ˌindeˌscribaˈbility) or indescribableness (ˌindeˈscribableness) nounindescribably (ˌindeˈscribably) adverb

indescribable

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndɪskraɪbəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveYou use indescribable to emphasize that a quality or condition is very intense or extreme, and therefore cannot be properly described. [emphasis] ⇒ ...her indescribable joy when it was confirmed her son was alive. ⇒ The stench from the sewer is indescribable.indescribably (ɪndɪskraɪbəbli ) adverb [ADV adj] ⇒ ...indescribably filthy conditions.

drudge

(drʌdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person, such as a servant, who works hard at wearisome menial tasksverb2. (intransitive) to toil at such tasks
Derived forms
drudger (ˈdrudger) noundrudgingly (ˈdrudgingly) adverb
Word origin
C16: perhaps from druggen to toil

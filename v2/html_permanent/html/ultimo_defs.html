ultimo

(ˈʌltɪˌməʊ )
Definitions
adverb(esp abbreviated in formal correspondence) in or during the previous month ⇒ a letter of the 7th ultimo. Abbreviation: ult. Compare instant (sense 7), proximo
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin ultimō on the last

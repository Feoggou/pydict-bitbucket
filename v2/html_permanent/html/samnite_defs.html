Samnite

(in ancient Italy ˈsæmnaɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a member of an Oscan-speaking people of the S Apennines, who clashed repeatedly with Rome between 350 bc and 200 bcadjective2. of or relating to this people

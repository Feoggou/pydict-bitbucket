beneficiary

(ˌbɛnɪˈfɪʃərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ciaries1. a person who gains or benefits in some way from something2. law a person entitled to receive funds or other property under a trust, will, or insurance policy3. the holder of an ecclesiastical or other benefice4. New Zealand a person who receives government assistance ⇒ social security beneficiaryadjective5. of or relating to a benefice or the holder of a benefice

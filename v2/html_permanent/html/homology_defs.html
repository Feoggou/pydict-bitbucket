homology

(həʊˈmɒlədʒɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -gies1. the condition of being homologous2. chemistry the similarities in chemical behaviour shown by members of a homologous series3. zoology the measurable likenesses between animals, as used in grouping them according to the theory of cladistics
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Greek homologia agreement, from homologos agreeing; see homologate

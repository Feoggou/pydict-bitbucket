pouting

(ˈpaʊtɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having the lips sticking out, usually in order to show annoyance or to appear sexually attractive ⇒ gorgeous pouting models2. (esp of the lips) thick or full ⇒ the gorgeous, pouting lips of Hollywood starlets
Example sentences containing 'pouting'
`No," murmured Gaynor, shaking her pretty curls and pouting like a Victorian miss. Curzon, Clare THE QUEST FOR KCurly blonde hair in a single plait, thick fair eyebrows and a small, pouting mouth. Fraser, Anthea DEATH SPEAKS SOFTLYFrom the radiating warmth of her inner thigh, I looked up to find Ali propped on an elbow, in turns smiling and pouting. James Birrell THE MANANA MAN

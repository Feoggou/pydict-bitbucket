nutrition

(njuːˈtrɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a process in animals and plants involving the intake of nutrient materials and their subsequent assimilation into the tissues ▶ Related adjectives: alimentary, trophic2. the act or process of nourishing3. the study of nutrition, esp in humans
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

nutritional (nuˈtritional) or rare nutritionary (nuˈtritionary) adjectivenutritionally (nuˈtritionally) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Late Latin nūtrītiō, from nūtrīre to nourish

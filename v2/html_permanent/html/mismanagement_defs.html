mismanagement

Powered by COBUILD
(mɪsmænɪdʒmənt )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft poss N]Someone's mismanagement of a system or organization is the bad way they have dealt with it or organized it. ⇒ ...the Government's economic mismanagement. ⇒ His gross mismanagement left the company desperately in need of restructuring.
Example sentences containing 'mismanagement'
It's not only omissions through mismanagement , like your general. Penelope Fitzgerald HUMAN VOICES

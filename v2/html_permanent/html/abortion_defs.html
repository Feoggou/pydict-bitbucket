abortion

(əˈbɔːʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an operation or other procedure to terminate pregnancy before the fetus is viable2. the premature termination of pregnancy by spontaneous or induced expulsion of a nonviable fetus from the uterus3. the products of abortion; an aborted fetus4. the arrest of development of an organ5. a failure to develop to completion or maturity ⇒ the project proved an abortion6. a person or thing that is deformed
Derived forms
aˈbortional adjective

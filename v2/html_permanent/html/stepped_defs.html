stepped

(stɛpt)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjectivearranged in or supplied with a series of steps so as to avoid coincidence or symmetry ⇒ a gently stepped ramp, ⇒ wide, steeply stepped stairs, ⇒ Household water prices will be structured on a stepped scale.

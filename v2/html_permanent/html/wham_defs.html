wham

(wæm )
Definitions
noun1. a forceful blow or impact or the sound produced by such a blow or impactexclamation2. an exclamation imitative of this soundverbWord forms: whams, whamming or whammed3. to strike or cause to strike with great force
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: of imitative origin

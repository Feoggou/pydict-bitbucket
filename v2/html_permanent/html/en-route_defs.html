Definitions
phraseEn route to a place means on the way to that place. En route is sometimes spelled on route in non-standard English. on the way [+ to] ⇒ They have arrived in London en route to the United States. ⇒ One of the bags was lost en route. [Also + from/for] →  route

en route

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
phraseJournalists sometimes use en route when they are mentioning an event that happened as part of a longer process or before another event. [+ to] ⇒ The German set three tournament records and equalled two others en route to grabbing golf's richest prize. →  route

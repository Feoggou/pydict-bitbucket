olio

(ˈəʊlɪˌəʊ )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural olios1. a dish of many different ingredients2. a miscellany or potpourri
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Spanish olla stew, from Latin: jar

retired

(rɪˈtaɪəd )
Definitions
adjective1. a. having given up one's work, office, etc, esp on completion of the normal period of service ⇒ a retired headmistressb. (as collective noun; preceded by the) ⇒ the retired2. withdrawn; secluded ⇒ a retired life, ⇒ a retired cottage in the woods

retired

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪtaɪəʳd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]A retired person is an older person who has left his or her job and has usually stopped working completely. ⇒ ...a seventy-three-year-old retired teacher from Florida.2. → See also retire

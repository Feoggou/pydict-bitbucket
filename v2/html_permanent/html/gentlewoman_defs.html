gentlewoman

(ˈdʒɛntəlˌwʊmən )
Definitions
noun plural -women1. archaic a woman regarded as being of good family or breeding; lady2. rare a woman who is cultured, courteous, and well-educated3. history a woman in personal attendance on a high-ranking lady

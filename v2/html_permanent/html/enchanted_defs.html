enchanted

(ɪnˈtʃɑːntɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. under a spell; bewitched; magical2. utterly delighted or captivated; fascinated; charmed
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'enchanted'
There were enough diversions and wonders to keep them enchanted for decades to come. Clive Barker COLDHEART CANYONShe had driven him to many of Cornwall's secret and enchanted places, shown him ruins to which were attached wonderful stories. Gaskin, Catherine THE AMBASSADOR'S WOMENWhat enchanted me was that he always made a point of including me. Holt, Victoria THE BLACK OPAL

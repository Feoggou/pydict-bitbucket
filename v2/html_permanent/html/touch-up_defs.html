touch up

Definitions
verb (tr, adverb)1. to put extra or finishing touches to2. to enhance, renovate, or falsify by putting extra touches to ⇒ to touch up a photograph3. to stimulate or rouse as by a tap or light blow4. British slang to touch or caress (someone), esp to arouse sexual feelingsnoun touch-up, touchup5. (of a painting, etc) a renovation or retouching

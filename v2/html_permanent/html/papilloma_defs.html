papilloma

(ˌpæpɪˈləʊmə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -mata (-mətə) or -maspathology a benign tumour derived from epithelial tissue and forming a rounded or lobulated mass

Derived Forms

ˌpapillomatous (ˌpapilˈlomatous) adjectiveˌpapilˌlomatosis (ˌpapilˌlomaˈtosis) noun

Word Origin

C19: from papilla + -oma

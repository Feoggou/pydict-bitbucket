tiara

(tɪˈɑːrə )
Definitions
noun1. a woman's semicircular jewelled headdress for formal occasions2. a high headdress worn by Persian kings in ancient times3. Roman Catholic Churcha. a headdress worn by the pope, consisting of a beehive-shaped diadem surrounded by three coronetsb. the office or rank of pope
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tiaraed (tiˈaraed) adjective

Word Origin

C16: via Latin from Greek, of Oriental origin

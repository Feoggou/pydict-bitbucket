dung

(dʌŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. excrement, esp of animals; manureb. (as modifier) ⇒ dung cart2. something filthyverb3. (transitive) to cover (ground) with manure
Derived forms
dungy (ˈdungy) adjective
Word origin
Old English: prison; related to Old High German tunc cellar roofed with dung, Old Norse dyngja manure heap

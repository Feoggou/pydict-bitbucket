cotyledon

(ˌkɒtɪˈliːdən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a simple embryonic leaf in seed-bearing plants, which, in some species, forms the first green leaf after germination2. a tuft of villi on the mammalian placenta
Derived forms
ˌcotyledonal (ˌcotyˈledonal) adjectiveˌcotyledonary (ˌcotyˈledonary) adjectiveˌcotyledonous (ˌcotyˈledonous) or ˌcotyledoˌnoid (ˌcotyˈledoˌnoid) adjective
Word origin
C16: from Latin: a plant, navelwort, from Greek kotulēdōn, from kotulē cup, hollow

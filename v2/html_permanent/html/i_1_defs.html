i

or I (aɪ )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural i's, I's or Is1. the ninth letter and third vowel of the modern English alphabet2. any of several speech sounds represented by this letter, in English as in bite or hit3. a. something shaped like an Ib. (in combination) ⇒ an I-beam4. See dot the i's and cross the t's

industrial

(ɪnˈdʌstrɪəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, derived from, or characteristic of industry2. employed in industry ⇒ the industrial workforce3. relating to or concerned with workers in industry ⇒ industrial conditions4. used in industry ⇒ industrial chemicals
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

industrially (inˈdustrially) adverb

industrial

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndʌstriəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]You use industrial to describe things which relate to or are used in industry. ⇒ ...industrial machinery and equipment. ⇒ ...a link between industrial chemicals and cancer.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]An industrial city or country is one in which industry is important or highly developed. ⇒ ...ministers from leading western industrial countries.

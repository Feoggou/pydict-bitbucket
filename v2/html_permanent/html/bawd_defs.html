bawd

(bɔːd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun archaic1. a person who runs a brothel, esp a woman2. a prostitute
Word origin
C14: shortened from Old French baudetrot, from baude feminine of baud merry + trot one who runs errands; compare Old High German bald bold

Similar Words

madam, prostitute, whore, pimp, working girl (facetious) (slang), brothel-keeper, procuress

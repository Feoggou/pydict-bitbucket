Gibraltar2

(dʒɪˈbrɔltər ) in American English
Definitions
1.  small peninsula at the southern tip of Spain, extending into the Mediterranean: 2.5 sq mi (6.5 sq km); it consists mostly of a rocky hill (Rock of Gibraltar), 1,396 ft (426 m) high2.  British crown colony, including a port & naval base, on this peninsula: pop. 28,0003.  Strait ofstrait between Spain & Morocco, joining the Mediterranean & the Atlantic: c. 35 mi (56 km) long

(ˌtɔːˈbeɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a unitary authority in SW England, in Devon, consisting of Torquay and two neighbouring coastal resorts. Pop: 131 300 (2003 est). Area: 63 sq km (24 sq miles)2. Also: Tor Bay an inlet of the English Channel on the coast of SW England, near Torquay

nappy1

(ˈnæpɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -piesBritish a piece of soft material, esp towelling or a disposable material, wrapped around a baby in order to absorb its urine and excrement. Also called: napkin. US and Canadian name: diaper
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: changed from napkin

cress

(krɛs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany of various plants of the genera Lepidium, Cardamine, Arabis, etc, having pungent-tasting leaves often used in salads and as a garnish: family Brassicaceae (crucifers). See also watercress, garden cress
Word origin
Old English cressa; related to Old High German cresso cress, kresan to crawl

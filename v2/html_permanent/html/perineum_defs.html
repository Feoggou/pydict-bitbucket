perineum

(ˌpɛrɪˈniːəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -nea (-ˈniːə)1. the region of the body between the anus and the genital organs, including some of the underlying structures2. the nearly diamond-shaped surface of the human trunk between the thighs

Derived Forms

ˌperineal (ˌperiˈneal) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from New Latin, from Greek perinaion, from peri- + inein to empty out

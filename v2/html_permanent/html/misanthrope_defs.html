misanthrope

(ˈmɪzənˌθrəʊp ) or misanthropist (mɪˈzænθrəpɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna person who dislikes or distrusts other people or mankind in general

Derived Forms

misanthropic (ˌmɪzənˈθrɒpɪk ) or ˌmisanthropical (ˌmisanˈthropical) adjectiveˌmisanthropically (ˌmisanˈthropically) adverbmisanthropy (mɪˈzænθrəpɪ ) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Greek mīsanthrōpos, from misos hatred + anthrōpos man

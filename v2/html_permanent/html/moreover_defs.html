Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverbYou use moreover to introduce a piece of information that adds to or supports the previous statement. [formal] furthermore, what is more ⇒ She saw that there was indeed a man immediately behind her. Moreover, he was observing her strangely. ⇒ The young find everything so simple. The young, moreover, see it as their duty to be happy and do their best to be so.

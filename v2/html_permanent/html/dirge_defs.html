dirge

(dɜːdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a chant of lamentation for the dead2. the funeral service in its solemn or sung forms3. any mourning song or melody
Derived forms
dirgeful (ˈdirgeful) adjective
Word origin
C13: changed from Latin dīrigē direct (imperative), opening word of the Latin antiphon used in the office of the dead

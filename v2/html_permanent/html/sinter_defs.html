sinter

(ˈsɪntə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a whitish porous incrustation, usually consisting of silica, that is deposited from hot springs2. the product of a sintering process3. another name for cinder (sense 3)verb4. (transitive) to form large particles, lumps, or masses from (metal powders or powdery ores) by heating or pressure or both

Word Origin

C18: German: cinder

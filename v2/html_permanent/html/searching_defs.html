Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A searching question or look is intended to discover the truth about something. ⇒ They asked her some searching questions on moral philosophy and logic.2. → see also soul-searching
Example sentences containing 'searching'
Nathan had been searching for Woody again, still without success. Amanda Hemingway THE GREENSTONE GRAIL: THE SANGREAL TRILOGY ONEHe heard the uncertain steps coming closer, feet searching across each landing to the next flight. Robert Wilson THE COMPANY OF STRANGERSPeople will still be searching Elaine's face for the secret of goodness. Fay Weldon NOTHING TO WEAR AND NOWHERE TO HIDE: A COLLECTION OF SHORT STORIESThus far, they had found no need to avoid or in any way change course for searching U. S. warships or patrol aircraft.

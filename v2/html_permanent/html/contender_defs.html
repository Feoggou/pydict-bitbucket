(kəntendəʳ )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA contender is someone who takes part in a competition. [journalism] [+ for] ⇒ Her trainer said yesterday that she would be a strong contender for a place in Britain's Olympic squad. ⇒ Our British Affairs correspondent reports on how the three contenders reacted to the news of the resignation. [Also + in]

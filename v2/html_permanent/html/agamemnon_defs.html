Agamemnon

(ˌæɡəˈmɛmnɒn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounGreek mythology a king of Mycenae who led the Greeks at the siege of Troy. On his return home he was murdered by his wife Clytemnestra and her lover Aegisthus. See also Menelaus

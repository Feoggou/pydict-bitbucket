extremities

(ɪkˈstrɛmɪtɪz)
Definitions
plural noun1. See extremity2. formal hands and feet ⇒ The warmth spread outwards till it reached his extremities., ⇒ He found that his extremities grew cold., ⇒ Exercise is very important as it keeps the circulation moving and warms the extremities.

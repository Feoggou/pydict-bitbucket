odour

or US odor (ˈəʊdə )
Definitions
noun1. the property of a substance that gives it a characteristic scent or smell2. a pervasive quality about something ⇒ an odour of dishonesty3. repute or regard (in the phrases in good odour, in bad odour)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

odourless (ˈodourless) or US odorless (ˈodorless) adjective

Word Origin

C13: from Old French odur, from Latin odor; related to Latin olēre to smell, Greek ōzein

Heteroousian

(ˌhɛtərəʊˈuːsɪən , -ˈaʊsɪən)
Definitions
noun1. a Christian who maintains that God the Father and God the Son are different in substanceadjective2. of or relating to this belief
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Late Greek heteroousios, from Greek hetero- + ousia nature

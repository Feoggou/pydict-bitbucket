perversion

(pəˈvɜːʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any abnormal means of obtaining sexual satisfaction2. the act of perverting or the state of being perverted3. a perverted form or usage

perversion

(pəʳvɜːʳʃən , -ʒən)
Word forms: perversions
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounYou can refer to a sexual desire or action that you consider to be abnormal and unacceptable as a perversion. [disapproval]2. variable nounA perversion of something is a form of it that is bad or wrong, or the changing of it into this form. [disapproval] [+ of] ⇒ What monstrous perversion of the human spirit leads a sniper to open fire on a bus carrying children? ⇒ The past has been scarred by countless perversions of justice.

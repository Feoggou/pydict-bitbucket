wailing

(ˈweɪlɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. prolonged high-pitched cries, as of grief or misery ⇒ Mace still remembers the pitiful wailing of the trapped and the wounded.2. prolonged high-pitched sounds ⇒ Our artillery opened up and we heard a fearful wailing and screeching., ⇒ The silence was broken by the wailing of police sirens.

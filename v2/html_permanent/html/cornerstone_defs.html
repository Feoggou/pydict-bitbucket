cornerstone

(ˈkɔːnəˌstəʊn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a stone at the corner of a wall, uniting two intersecting walls; quoin2. a stone placed at the corner of a building during a ceremony to mark the start of construction3. a person or thing of prime importance; basis ⇒ the cornerstone of the whole argument

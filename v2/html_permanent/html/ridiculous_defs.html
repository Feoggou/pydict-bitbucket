ridiculous

(rɪˈdɪkjʊləs )
Definitions
adjectiveworthy of or exciting ridicule; absurd, preposterous, laughable, or contemptible
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ridiculously (riˈdiculously) adverbridiculousness (riˈdiculousness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin rīdiculōsus, from rīdēre to laugh

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that something or someone is ridiculous, you mean that they are very foolish. ⇒ It is ridiculous to suggest we are having a romance. ⇒ It was an absolutely ridiculous decision.

exhortation

(ˌɛɡzɔːˈteɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act or process of exhorting2. a speech or written passage intended to persuade, inspire, or encourage
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'exhortation'
Coincidentally, Wellington's exhortation is also a cogent description of Neville's business practice. Alex George LOVE YOU MADLYWithout further exhortation from John or anybody else, the battalion Lewis guns opened up.

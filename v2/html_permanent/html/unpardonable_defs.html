unpardonable for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnpɑːʳdənəbəl )
Definitions
adjectiveIf you say that someone's behaviour is unpardonable, you mean that it is very wrong or offensive, and completely unacceptable. ⇒ ...an unpardonable lack of discipline. ⇒ I must ask a question you may find unpardonable.

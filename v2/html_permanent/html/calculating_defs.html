Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as calculating, you disapprove of the fact that they deliberately plan to get what they want, often by hurting or harming other people. [disapproval] scheming ⇒ Northbridge is a cool, calculating and clever criminal who could strike again.
Example sentences containing 'calculating'
He glanced at his watch, calculating the hours left to kill. Mark Mills AMAGANSETTThe calculating Mr Livingstone, Arnold guessed, would be far more interested in profit than environment. Lewis, Roy A TROUT IN THE MILKHe slipped after a time into a kind of waking sleep, his mind lucid but frozen, contemplating nothing, calculating nothing. Robert Silverberg MAJIPOOR CHRONICLES

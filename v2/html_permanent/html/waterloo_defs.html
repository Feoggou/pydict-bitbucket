Waterloo

(ˌwɔːtəˈluː )
Definitions
noun1. a small town in central Belgium, in Walloon Brabant province south of Brussels: battle (1815) fought nearby in which British and Prussian forces under the Duke of Wellington and Blücher routed the French under Napoleon. Pop: 29 003 (2004 est)2. a total or crushing defeat (esp in meet one's Waterloo)

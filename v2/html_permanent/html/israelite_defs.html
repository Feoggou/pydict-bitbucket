Israelite

(ˈɪzrɪəˌlaɪt , -rə-)
Definitions
noun1. Bible a member of the ethnic group claiming descent from Jacob; a Hebrew2. Bible a citizen of the kingdom of Israel (922 to 721 bc) as opposed to Judah3. a member of any of various Christian sects who regard themselves as God's chosen people4. an archaic and sometimes offensive word for a Jew

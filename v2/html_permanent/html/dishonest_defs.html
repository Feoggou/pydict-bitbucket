Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that a person or their behaviour is dishonest, you mean that they are not truthful or honest and that you cannot trust them. ⇒ You have been dishonest with me. ⇒ It would be dishonest not to present the data as fairly as possible.dishonestly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ The key issue was whether the four defendants acted dishonestly.

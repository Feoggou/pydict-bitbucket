pomposity

(pɒmˈpɒsɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. vain or ostentatious display of dignity or importance2. the quality of being pompous3. ostentatiously lofty style, language, etc4. a pompous action, remark, etc

pomposity

Powered by COBUILD
(pɒmpɒsɪti )
Word forms: pomposities
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounPomposity means speaking or behaving in a very serious manner which shows that you think you are more important than you really are. [disapproval] ⇒ He hated pomposity and disliked being called a genius.

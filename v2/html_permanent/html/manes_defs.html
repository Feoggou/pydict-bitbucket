manes

(ˈmɑːneɪz , Latin ˈmɑːnɛs)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun (sometimes capital) (in Roman legend)1. the spirits of the dead, often revered as minor deities2. (functioning as singular) the shade of a dead person

Word Origin

C14: from Latin, probably: the good ones, from Old Latin mānus good

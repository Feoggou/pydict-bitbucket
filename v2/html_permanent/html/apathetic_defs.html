apathetic

(ˌæpəˈθɛtɪk ) or apathetical (ˌæpəˈθɛtɪkəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivehaving or showing little or no emotion; indifferent
Derived forms
ˌapaˈthetically adverb
Word origin
C18: from apathy + pathetic

(æpəθetɪk )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as apathetic, you are criticizing them because they do not seem to be interested in or enthusiastic about doing anything. [disapproval] ⇒ Even the most apathetic students are beginning to sit up and listen. ⇒ Others feel apathetic about the candidates in both parties.

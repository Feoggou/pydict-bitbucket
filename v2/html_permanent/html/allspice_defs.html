allspice

(ˈɔːlˌspaɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a tropical American myrtaceous tree, Pimenta officinalis, having small white flowers and aromatic berries2. the whole or powdered seeds of this berry used as a spice, having a flavour said to resemble a mixture of cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg

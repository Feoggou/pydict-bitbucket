onboard

Definitions
adjective (ˈɒnˌbɔːd)1. on a ship, train, aircraft, or spacecraft ⇒ onboard entertainment, ⇒ the onboard meal, ⇒ You can spend three days enjoying the onboard facilities., ⇒ an onboard bar, ⇒ An onboard computer can work out precisely where you are.adverb (ˌɒnˈbɔːd)2. into or onto a moveable craft, esp a ship

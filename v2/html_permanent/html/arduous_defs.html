arduous

(ˈɑːdjʊəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. requiring great physical or mental effort; difficult to accomplish; strenuous2. hard to endure; harsh ⇒ arduous conditions3. hard to overcome or surmount; steep or difficult ⇒ an arduous track
Derived forms
arduously (ˈarduously) adverbarduousness (ˈarduousness) noun
Word origin
C16: from Latin arduus steep, difficult

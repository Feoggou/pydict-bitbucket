northernmost for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(nɔːʳðəʳnmoʊst )
Definitions
superlative adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]The northernmost part of an area or the northernmost place is the one that is farthest towards the north. ⇒ ...the northernmost tip of the British Isles. ⇒ The Chablis vineyard is the northernmost in Burgundy.

gentleness

(ˈdʒɛntəlnɪs )
Definitions
noun1. the quality of being gentle2. physics a property of elementary particles, conserved in certain strong interactions. See also charm1 (sense 7)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'gentleness'
And his eyes... She looked away, something in her afraid, despite the gentleness. Appiganesi, Lisa DREAMS OF INNOCENCEHis eyes were a deep grey with an expression of singular gentleness in them. Ferrars, Elizabeth COME AND BE KILLEDWith great gentleness , he turned her head towards the vial. Clive Barker THE GREAT AND SECRET SHOW

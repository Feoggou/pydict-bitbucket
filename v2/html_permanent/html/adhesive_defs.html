adhesive

(ədˈhiːsɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. able or designed to adhere; sticky ⇒ adhesive tape2. tenacious or clingingnoun3. a substance used for sticking objects together, such as glue, cement, or paste

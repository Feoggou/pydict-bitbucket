baguette

or baguet (bæˈɡɛt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a narrow French stick loaf2. a small gem cut as a long rectangle3. the shape of such a gem4. architecture a small moulding having a semicircular cross section
Word origin
C18: from French, from Italian bacchetta a little stick, from bacchio rod, from Latin baculum walking stick

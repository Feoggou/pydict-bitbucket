insusceptible

(ˌɪnsəˈsɛptəbəl )
Definitions
adjective(when postpositive, usually foll by to) not capable of being affected (by); not susceptible (to)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

insusceptibility (ˌinsusˌceptiˈbility) nouninsusceptibly (ˌinsusˈceptibly) adverb

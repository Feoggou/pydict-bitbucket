laughingly

Powered by COBUILD
(lɑːfɪŋli , læf-) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [ADV with v]If you laughingly refer to something with a particular name or description, the description is not appropriate and you think that this is either amusing or annoying. ⇒ I spent much of what I laughingly call 'the holidays' working through 621 pages of typescript. ⇒ What I laughingly refer to as my garden is a corner lot by our postage-stamp size house.

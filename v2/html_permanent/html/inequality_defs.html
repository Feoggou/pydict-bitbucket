inequality

(ˌɪnɪˈkwɒlɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. the state or quality of being unequal; disparity2. an instance of disparity3. lack of smoothness or regularity4. social or economic disparity5. mathematicsa. a statement indicating that the value of one quantity or expression is not equal to another, as in x ≠ yb. a relationship between real numbers involving inequality: x may be greater than y, denoted by x>y, or less than y, denoted by x<y6. astronomy a departure from uniform orbital motion

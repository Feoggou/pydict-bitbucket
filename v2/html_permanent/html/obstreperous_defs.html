obstreperous

(əbˈstrɛpərəs )
Definitions
adjectivenoisy or rough, esp in resisting restraint or control. Also (dialect, jocular): obstropalous, obstropulous
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

obstreperously (obˈstreperously) adverbobstreperousness (obˈstreperousness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin, from obstrepere, from ob- against + strepere to roar

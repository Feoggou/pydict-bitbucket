(kənsɜːʳnɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. prepositionYou use concerning to indicate what a question or piece of information is about. [formal] ⇒ For more information concerning the club contact I. Coldwell. ⇒ ...various questions concerning pollution and the environment.2. adjectiveIf something is concerning, it causes you to feel concerned about it. ⇒ It is particularly concerning that he is working for non-British companies while advising on foreign policy.

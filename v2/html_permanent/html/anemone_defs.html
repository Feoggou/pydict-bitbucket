anemone

(əˈnɛmənɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany ranunculaceous woodland plant of the genus Anemone of N temperate regions, such as the white-flowered A. nemorosa (wood anemone or windflower). Some cultivated anemones have lilac, pale blue, pink, purple, or red flowers. See also pasqueflower. Compare sea anemone
Word origin
C16: via Latin from Greek: windflower, from anemos wind

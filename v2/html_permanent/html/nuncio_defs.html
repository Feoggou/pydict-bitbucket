nuncio

(ˈnʌnʃɪˌəʊ , -sɪ-)
Definitions
noun plural -ciosRoman Catholic Church a diplomatic representative of the Holy See, ranking above an internuncio and esp having ambassadorial status
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: via Italian from Latin nuntius messenger

laureate

(ˈlɔːrɪɪt )
Definitions
adjective (usually immediately postpositive)1. literary crowned with laurel leaves as a sign of honour2. archaic made of laurelnoun3. short for poet laureate4. a person honoured with an award for art or science ⇒ a Nobel laureate5. rare a person honoured with the laurel crown or wreath
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

laureateship (ˈlaureateˌship) nounlaureation (ˌlɔːrɪˈeɪʃən ) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Latin laureātus, from laurea laurel

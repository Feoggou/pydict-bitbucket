obsessed

(əbˈsɛst)
Definitions
adjectivehaving a complete preoccupation with someone or something, to an extent that seems extreme to others ⇒ letters from all these really obsessed people, ⇒ He's obsessed with trains., ⇒ He became absolutely obsessed with a girl reporter he saw on television., ⇒ He was obsessed with American gangster movies.

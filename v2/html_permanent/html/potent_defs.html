potent1

(ˈpəʊtənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. possessing great strength; powerful2. (of arguments, etc) persuasive or forceful3. influential or authoritative4. tending to produce violent physical or chemical effects ⇒ a potent poison5. (of a male) capable of having sexual intercourse

Derived Forms

potently (ˈpotently) adverbpotentness (ˈpotentness) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin potēns able, from posse to be able

satirical

(səˈtɪrɪkəl ) or satiric
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or containing satire2. given to the use of satire

Derived Forms

satirically (saˈtirically) adverbsatiricalness (saˈtiricalness) noun

elm

(ɛlm )
Definitions
noun1. any ulmaceous tree of the genus Ulmus, occurring in the N hemisphere, having serrated leaves and winged fruits (samaras): cultivated for shade, ornament, and timber2. the hard heavy wood of this tree▷ See also slippery elm, wahoo1, wych-elm
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English elm; related to Old Norse almr, Old High German elm, Latin ulmus

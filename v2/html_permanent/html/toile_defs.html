toile

(twɑːl )
Definitions
noun1. a transparent linen or cotton fabric2. a garment of exclusive design made up in cheap cloth so that alterations and experiments can be made
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from French, from Latin tēla a loom

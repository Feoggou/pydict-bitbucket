photocopy

(ˈfəʊtəʊˌkɒpɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -copies1. a photographic reproduction of written, printed, or graphic work. See also microcopyverb -copies, -copying or -copied2. to reproduce (written, printed, or graphic work) on photographic material

seventieth

(ˈsɛvəntɪɪθ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (usually prenominal)a. being the ordinal number of seventy in numbering or counting order, position, time, etc: often written 70thb. (as noun) ⇒ the seventieth in linenoun2. a. one of 70 approximately equal parts of somethingb. (as modifier) ⇒ a seventieth part3. the fraction equal to one divided by 70 (1⁄70)

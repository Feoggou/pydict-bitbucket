pome

(pəʊm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe fleshy fruit of the apple and related plants, consisting of an enlarged receptacle enclosing the ovary and seeds

Word Origin

C15: from Old French, from Late Latin pōma apple, pl (assumed to be sing) of Latin pōmum apple

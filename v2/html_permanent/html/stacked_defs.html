COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If a place or surface is stacked with objects, it is filled with piles of them. [+ with] ⇒ Shops in Ho Chi Minh City are stacked with goods. ⇒ ...his house has 20 rooms stacked with paintings.

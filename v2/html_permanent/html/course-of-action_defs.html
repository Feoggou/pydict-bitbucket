course of action

(kɔːs əv ˈækʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna way of proceeding ⇒ This would be the best course of action., ⇒ We have decided on this course of action for one reason alone, ⇒  … pondering the pros and cons before opting for a particular course of action, ⇒ My best course of action was to help Gill by being loyal, loving and endlessly sympathetic.
Example sentences containing 'course of action'
Somehow, Harry would have to persuade the headmaster that such a course of action would not be wise. Gaskin, Catherine THE AMBASSADOR'S WOMENTadeusz had sat through the third act, brooding over his course of action. Val McDermid THE LAST TEMPTATIONBy the time I reached the centre I'd decided there was only one sensible course of action. Adam, Paul A NASTY DOSE OF DEATH

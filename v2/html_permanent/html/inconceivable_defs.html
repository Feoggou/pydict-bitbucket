Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnkənsiːvəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you describe something as inconceivable, you think it is very unlikely to happen or be true. ⇒ It was inconceivable to me that Toby could have been my attacker. ⇒ Until now, a devaluation of the dollar seemed inconceivable.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

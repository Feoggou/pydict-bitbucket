strained

(streɪnd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of an action, performance, etc) not natural or spontaneous2. (of an atmosphere, relationship, etc) not relaxed; tense

strained

Powered by COBUILD
(streɪnd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone's appearance, voice, or behaviour is strained, they seem worried and nervous. ⇒ She looked a little pale and strained. ⇒ Gil sensed something wrong from her father's strained voice. ⇒ His laughter seemed a little strained.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf relations between people are strained, those people do not like or trust each other. ⇒ ...a period of strained relations between the prime minister and his deputy.

haddock

(ˈhædək )
Definitions
noun plural -docks or -docka North Atlantic gadoid food fish, Melanogrammus aeglefinus: similar to but smaller than the cod
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: of uncertain origin

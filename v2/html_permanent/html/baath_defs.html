Baath

(bɑθ ; bæθ )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
adjective designating or of a political party formed in Syria in the 1940s: separate factions of the party have ruled Syria since 1970 and ruled Iraq from 1968 to 2003: also written Ba'ath

Word forms: searchers
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu pl]Searchers are people who are looking for someone or something that is missing. ⇒ Searchers have found three mountain climbers missing since Saturday.2. countable nounA searcher is someone who is trying to find something such as the truth or the answer to a problem. [+ after/for] ⇒ He's not a real searcher after truth.
Example sentences containing 'searcher'
The best place to hide, she knew, is beneath the nose of the searcher. Weldon, Fay SPLITTINGThey started up one of the trails, each searcher looking carefully in every crevice and depression for the missing tablets. Keene, Carolyn THE SECRET OF THE FORGOTTEN CITYThat meant he wasn't investigating her possessions, which tended to confirm her theory that he had been the searcher. Val McDermid THE LAST TEMPTATION

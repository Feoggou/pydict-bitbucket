shifting

(ˈʃɪftɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveused to describe something which is made up of parts that are continuously moving and changing position in relation to other parts ⇒ shifting alliances/attitudes/patterns, ⇒ a shifting balance of power, ⇒ the shifting sand beneath their feet, ⇒ The Croatian town of Ilok is a classic case of shifting populations.

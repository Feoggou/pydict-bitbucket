antedate

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (ˈæntɪˌdeɪt , ˌæntɪˈdeɪt ) (transitive)1. to be or occur at an earlier date than2. to affix a date to (a document, etc) that is earlier than the actual date3. to assign a date to (an event, etc) that is earlier than its previously assigned date4. to cause to occur soonernoun (ˈæntɪˌdeɪt )5. an earlier date

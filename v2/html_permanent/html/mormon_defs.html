Mormon

(ˈmɔːmən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, founded in 1830 at La Fayette, New York, by Joseph Smith2. a prophet whose supposed revelations were recorded by Joseph Smith in the Book of Mormonadjective3. of or relating to the Mormons, their Church, or their beliefs

parotid

(pəˈrɒtɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. relating to or situated near the parotid glandnoun2. short for parotid gland

Word Origin

C17: via French, via Latin from Greek parōtis, from para-1 (near) + -ōtis, from ous ear
Example sentences containing 'parotid'
The first segment made me coy and put my parotid glands on red alert. Robert Wilson INSTRUMENTS OF DARKNESS

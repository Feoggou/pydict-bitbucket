actress

(ˈæktrɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a woman who acts in a play, film, broadcast, etc2. informal a woman who puts on a false manner in order to deceive others▶ USAGE Use of the word actress to refer to a female who acts is old-fashioned. The modern gender-neutral form is actor

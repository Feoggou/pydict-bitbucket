destruction

(dɪˈstrʌkʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of destroying or state of being destroyed; demolition2. a cause of ruin or means of destroying
Word origin
C14: from Latin dēstructiō a pulling down; see destroy

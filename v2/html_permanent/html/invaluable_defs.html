Definitions
adjectiveIf you describe something as invaluable, you mean that it is extremely useful. ⇒ I was able to gain invaluable experience over that year. [+ in] ⇒ The research should prove invaluable in the study of linguistics. ⇒ Their advice was invaluable to me at that stage of my work. [Also + to]

discoloured

(dɪsˈkʌləd) or US discolored
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivechanged from the original colour, in an undesirable way ⇒ Some of the prints were discoloured around the edges., ⇒ His left temple was bruised and discoloured., ⇒ He bared his clenched, discoloured teeth.
Example sentences containing 'discoloured'
Hardin didn't know him, but he did notice a dark, discoloured bruise on his right cheek. MacNeill, Alastair CODE BREAKERClara had never been vain, but now she complained that her pregnancy made her ugly, deformed her body and discoloured her skin. McCorquodale, Robin DANSVILLEYou could still see the discoloured patches on the window sill, where the bars had once been. Hugo Wilcken THE EXECUTION

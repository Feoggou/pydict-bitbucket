reverberations

(rɪˌvɜːbəˈreɪʃənz)
Definitions
plural nounremote or indirect consequences of an action; repercussions ⇒ The reverberations of this event could affect us all., ⇒ The reverberations from the assassination will be felt around the world., ⇒ Even insignificant events can cause widespread reverberations.

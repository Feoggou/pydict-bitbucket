headed

(ˈhɛdɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. a. having a head or headsb. (in combination) ⇒ two-headed, ⇒ bullet-headed2. having a heading ⇒ headed notepaper3. (in combination) having a mind or intellect as specified ⇒ thickheaded

troubles

(ˈtrʌbəlz)
Definitions
plural noun1. your troubles are the things that you are worried about ⇒ She told me all her troubles., ⇒ She kept her troubles to herself.2. politics political unrest or public disturbances ⇒ Troubles flared up into the night.3. See the Troubles

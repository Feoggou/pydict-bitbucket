Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Mervyn. 1911–68, English novelist, poet, and illustrator. In his trilogy Gormenghast (1946–59), he creates, with vivid imagination, a grotesque Gothic world2.  Tim(othy Nigel), born 1972, British astronaut; joined the crew of the International Space Station (2015); space walk (2016)

fitter

(ˈfɪtə )
Definitions
noun1. a person who fits a garment, esp when it is made for a particular person2. a person who is skilled in the assembly and adjustment of machinery, esp of a specified sort ⇒ an electrical fitter3. a person who supplies something for an expedition, activity, etc

activate

(ˈæktɪˌveɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to make active or capable of action2. physics to make radioactive3. chemistrya. to increase the rate of (a reaction)b. to treat (a substance, such as carbon or alumina) so as to increase powers of adsorption4. physiology to prepare by arousal (the body or one of its organs (e.g. the brain)) for action5. to purify (sewage) by aeration6. US military to create, mobilize, or organize (a unit)
Derived forms

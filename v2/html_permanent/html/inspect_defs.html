inspect

(ɪnˈspɛkt )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to examine closely, esp for faults or errors2. to scrutinize officially (a document, military personnel on ceremonial parade, etc)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inspectable (inˈspectable) adjectiveinspectingly (inˈspectingly) adverbinspection (inˈspection) nouninspectional (inˈspectional) adjectiveinspective (inˈspective) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Latin inspicere, from specere to look

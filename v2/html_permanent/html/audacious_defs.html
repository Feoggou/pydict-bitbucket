audacious

(ɔːˈdeɪʃəs ) or outdacious (aʊtˈdeɪʃəs)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. recklessly bold or daring; fearless2. impudent or presumptuous
Derived forms
auˈdaciously adverbauˈdaciousness or audacity (ɔːˈdæsɪtɪ ) noun
Word origin
C16: from Latin audāx bold, from audēre to dare

fuddled

(ˈfʌdəld)
Definitions
adjective1. in a muddled or confused state2. confused or intoxicated ⇒ Fuddled by brandy, her brain fumbled over the events of the night.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

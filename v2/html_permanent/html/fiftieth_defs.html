fiftieth

(ˈfɪftɪɪθ )
Definitions
adjective1. a. being the ordinal number of fifty in order, position, time, etc. Often written: 50thb. (as noun) ⇒ the fiftieth in the seriesnoun2. a. one of 50 equal or approximately equal parts of somethingb. (as modifier) ⇒ a fiftieth part3. the fraction equal to one divided by 50 (1⁄50)

truncated

(trʌŋˈkeɪtɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. mathematics (of a cone, pyramid, prism, etc) having an apex or end removed by a plane intersection that is usually nonparallel to the base2. (of a crystal) having edges or corners cut off3. shortened by or as if by cutting off; truncate

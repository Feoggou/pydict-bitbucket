lewd

(luːd )
Definitions
adjective1. characterized by or intended to excite crude sexual desire; obscene2. obsoletea. wickedb. ignorant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lewdly (ˈlewdly) adverblewdness (ˈlewdness) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Old English lǣwde lay, ignorant; see lay3

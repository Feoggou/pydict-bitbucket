(ɪlɛktrɪk kɜrənt) in Electrical Engineering
Definitions
noun(Electrical engineering: Circuits, Electrical power, Computing and control) An electric current is a flow of electricity through a wire or circuit. ⇒ The fuse wire is a thin wire that gets hot as the electric current passes through it. ⇒ Alternating current is electric current that repeatedly changes polarity from negative to positive and back again. ⇒ An electric current is a flow of electricity through a wire or circuit. ▶  current

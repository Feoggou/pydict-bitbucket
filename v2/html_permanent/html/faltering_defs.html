faltering

(ˈfɔːltərɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. unsteady; shaky ⇒ Leaning on Jon, Michael took faltering steps to the bathroom.2. not fluent; hesitant ⇒ 'Now I feel I can do it,' he said in faltering English.3. experiencing difficulties; struggling ⇒ the faltering peace process, ⇒ A faltering economy and a recent wave of labour unrest have affected the new party's popularity.

scrubby

(ˈskrʌbɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -bier or -biest1. covered with or consisting of scrub2. (of trees or vegetation) stunted in growth3. British informal messy

Derived Forms

scrubbiness (ˈscrubbiness) noun

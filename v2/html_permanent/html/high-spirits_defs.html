high spirits

(haɪ ˈspɪrɪts)
Definitions
plural nounif you talk about someone's high spirits, you mean their liveliness and cheerfulness ⇒ Joan's high spirits, obvious passion for her husband and jealous rages soon earn her a reputation., ⇒ We set off in high spirits., ⇒ Everyone will be naturally in high spirits and looking forward to the weekend.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

emendation

(ˌiːmɛnˈdeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a correction or improvement in a text2. the act or process of emending
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

emendator (ˈemenˌdator) nounemendatory (ɪˈmɛndətərɪ , -trɪ) adjective

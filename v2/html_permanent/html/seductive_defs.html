Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is seductive is very attractive or makes you want to do something that you would not otherwise do. ⇒ It's a seductive argument.seductively graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj, oft ADV with v] ⇒ ...his seductively simple assertion. ⇒ The film opens seductively.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA person who is seductive is very attractive sexually. ⇒ ...a seductive woman. ⇒ I love dressing up to look seductive.seductively graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj] ⇒ She was looking seductively over her shoulder. ⇒ Her mouth is seductively large and full.

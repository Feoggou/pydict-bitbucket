Salem

(ˈseɪləm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a city in S India, in Tamil Nadu: textile industries. Pop: 693 236 (2001)2. a city in NE Massachusetts, on the Atlantic: scene of the execution of 19 people after the witch hunts of 1692. Pop: 42 067 (2003 est)3. a city in the NW USA, the state capital of Oregon: food-processing. Pop: 142 914 (2003 est)4. an Old Testament name for Jerusalem (Genesis 14:18; Psalms 76:2). See Jerusalem

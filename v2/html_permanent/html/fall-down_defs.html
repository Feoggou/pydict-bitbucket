fall down

Definitions
verb (intr, adverb)1. to drop suddenly or collapse2. (often foll by on) informal to prove unsuccessful; fail
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
1. → See fall [sense 2]2. phrasal verbIf an argument, organization, or person falls down on a particular point, they are weak or unsatisfactory on that point. [V P + on] ⇒ Service was outstandingly friendly and efficient, falling down on only one detail. [V P] ⇒ That is where his argument falls down. →  fall

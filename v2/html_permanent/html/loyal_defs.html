loyal

(ˈlɔɪəl )
Definitions
adjective1. having or showing continuing allegiance2. faithful to one's country, government, etc3. of or expressing loyalty
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

loyally (ˈloyally) adverbloyalness (ˈloyalness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Old French loial, leial, from Latin lēgālis legal

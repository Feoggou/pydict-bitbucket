black coffee

(blæk ˈkɒfɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouncoffee with no milk, milk substitute, or cream added. Compare white coffee
Example sentences containing 'black coffee'
She was doing nothing more sinister than reading her newspaper and drinking black coffee. Val McDermid THE LAST TEMPTATIONShe brought a sausage roll and a cup of black coffee to the window table where I had always sat. Reeves, Robert DOUBTING THOMASYou might have needed some black coffee to stop your head going round like a top. Secombe, Fred GOODBYE CURATE

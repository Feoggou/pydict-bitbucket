(ˌænɛəˈrəʊbɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of an organism or process) requiring the absence of or not dependent on the presence of oxygen2. of or relating to anaerobes▷ Compare aerobic

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveAnaerobic creatures or processes do not need oxygen in order to function or survive. ⇒ Anaerobic respiration occurs only in particularly active tissues such as muscles during severe exercise.2. adjectiveAnaerobic exercise is exercise such as weight training that improves your strength but does not raise your heart rate. ⇒ ...an anaerobic workout.

moped

(ˈməʊpɛd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounBritish a light motorcycle, not over 50cc

Word Origin

C20: from motor + pedal1, originally equipped with auxiliary pedals

moped

(moʊped )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA moped is a small motorcycle which you can also pedal like a bicycle. [mainly British]

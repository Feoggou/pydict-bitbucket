aroma

(əˈrəʊmə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a distinctive usually pleasant smell, esp of spices, wines, and plants2. a subtle pervasive quality or atmosphere
Word origin
C18: via Latin from Greek: spice

bedding

(ˈbɛdɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. bedclothes, sometimes considered together with a mattress2. litter, such as straw, for animals3. something acting as a foundation, such as mortar under a brick4. the arrangement of a mass of rocks into distinct layers; stratification

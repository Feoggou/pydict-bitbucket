saline

(ˈseɪlaɪn )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, concerned with, consisting of, or containing common salt ⇒ a saline taste2. medicine of or relating to a saline3. of, concerned with, consisting of, or containing any chemical salt, esp a metallic salt resembling sodium chloridenoun4. medicine an isotonic solution of sodium chloride in distilled water

Derived Forms

salinity (səˈlɪnɪtɪ ) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Late Latin salīnus, from Latin sal salt

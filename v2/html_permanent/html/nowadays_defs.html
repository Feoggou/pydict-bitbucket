Word Origin

nowadays for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(naʊədeɪz )
Definitions
adverbNowadays means at the present time, in contrast with the past. these days ⇒ Nowadays it's acceptable for women to be ambitious. But it wasn't then. ⇒ I don't see much of Tony nowadays.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

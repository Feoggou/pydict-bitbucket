Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf people, places, or events come alive, they start to be lively again after a quiet period. If someone or something brings them alive, they cause them to come alive. ⇒ The doctor's voice had come alive and his small eyes shone. ⇒ ...the songs of birds that bring the garden alive. →  alive

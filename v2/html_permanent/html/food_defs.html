food

(fuːd )
Definitions
noun1. any substance containing nutrients, such as carbohydrates, proteins, and fats, that can be ingested by a living organism and metabolized into energy and body tissue ▶ Related adjective: alimentary2. nourishment in more or less solid form as opposed to liquid form ⇒ food and drink3. anything that provides mental nourishment or stimulus ⇒ food for thought
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

foodless (ˈfoodless) adjective

Word Origin

Old English fōda; related to Old Frisian fōdia to nourish, feed, Old Norse fœthi, Gothic fōdeins food; see feed, fodder

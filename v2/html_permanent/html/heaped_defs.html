heaped

(hiːpt)
Definitions
adjectivehaving a surface that rises above the level of the edge of a container, esp a spoon ⇒ a heaped plate of potatoes, ⇒ a heaped teaspoonful of sugar, ⇒ Add one heaped tablespoon of salt.

heaped

Powered by COBUILD
(hiːpt ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]A heaped spoonful has the contents of the spoon piled up above the edge. ⇒ Add one heaped tablespoon of salt.2. adjective [v-link ADJ with n]A container or a surface that is heaped with things has a lot of them in it or on it in a pile, often so many that it cannot hold any more. ⇒ The large desk was heaped with papers. ⇒ ...ashtrays heaped with cigarette butts.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

gruff

(ɡrʌf )
Definitions
adjective1. rough or surly in manner, speech, etc ⇒ a gruff reply2. (of a voice, bark, etc) low and throaty
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gruffish (ˈgruffish) adjectivegruffly (ˈgruffly) adverbgruffness (ˈgruffness) noun

Word Origin

C16: originally Scottish, from Dutch grof, of Germanic origin; compare Old High German girob; related to Old English hrēof, Lithuanian kraupùs

chunk

(tʃʌŋk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a thick solid piece, as of meat, wood, etc2. a considerable amount
Word origin
C17: variant of chuck2

(tʃʌŋk )
Word forms: chunks
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounChunks of something are thick solid pieces of it. ⇒ They had to be careful of floating chunks of ice. [+ of] ⇒ ...a chunk of meat. ⇒ Cut the melon into chunks.2. countable nounA chunk of something is a large amount or large part of it. [informal] [+ of] ⇒ The company owns a chunk of farmland near Gatwick Airport.

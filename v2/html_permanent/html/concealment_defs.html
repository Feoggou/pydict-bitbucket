concealment

Powered by COBUILD
(kənsiːlmənt )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounConcealment is the state of being hidden or the act of hiding something. ⇒ The criminals vainly sought concealment from the searchlight. [+ of] ⇒ ...the concealment of weapons.2. uncount nounThe concealment of information or a feeling involves keeping it secret. [+ of] ⇒ His concealment of his true motives was masterly. [+ of] ⇒ I think there was deliberate concealment of relevant documents.

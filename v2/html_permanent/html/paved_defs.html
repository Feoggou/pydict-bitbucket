paved

(peɪvd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of a road, path, etc) covered with a firm surface suitable for travel, as with paving stones or concrete ⇒ a small paved courtyard2. covered with a hard layer of something ⇒ shelves paved with marble

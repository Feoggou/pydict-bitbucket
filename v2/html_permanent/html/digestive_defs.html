digestive

(dɪˈdʒɛstɪv , daɪ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective also: digestant1. relating to, aiding, or subjecting to digestion ⇒ a digestive enzymenoun2. a less common word for digestant3. short for digestive biscuit
Derived forms
digestively (diˈgestively) adverb

(daɪdʒestɪv )
Word forms: digestives
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]You can describe things that are related to the digestion of food as digestive. ⇒ ...digestive juices that normally work on breaking down our food. ⇒ Peppermint oil is very good for regulating digestive disorders.2. countable nounA digestive or a digestive biscuit is a type of biscuit made from wholemeal flour. [British, trademark]

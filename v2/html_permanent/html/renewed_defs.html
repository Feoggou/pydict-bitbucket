renewed

(rɪˈnjuːd)
Definitions
adjectivehappening again after a pause, esp with more vigour, energy or enthusiasm than before ⇒ I hope it will stimulate renewed interest by British business in Romania., ⇒ Now you can face the future with renewed confidence., ⇒ Renewed optimism about the Japanese economy helped Tokyo's Nikkei 225 stock market average to a 20-month high., ⇒ Troops have again been called out in the southern Pakistani city of Karachi following renewed violence., ⇒ Spanish children are showing a renewed interest in traditional puzzles and board games., ⇒ The EU is pursuing economic reform with renewed vigour.

Antigone

(ænˈtɪɡənɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounGreek mythology daughter of Oedipus and Jocasta, who was condemned to death for cremating the body of her brother Polynices in defiance of an edict of her uncle, King Creon of Thebes
Example sentences containing 'Antigone'

attic

(ˈætɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a space or room within the roof of a house2. architecture a storey or low wall above the cornice of a classical façade
Word origin
C18: special use of Attic from the use of Attic-style pilasters to adorn the façade of the top storey

Attic

(ˈætɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Attica, its inhabitants, or the dialect of Greek spoken there, esp in classical times2. (often not capital) classically elegant, simple, or pure ⇒ an Attic stylenoun3. the dialect of Ancient Greek spoken and written in Athens: the chief literary dialect of classical Greek. See also Aeolic, Arcadic, Doric, Ionic

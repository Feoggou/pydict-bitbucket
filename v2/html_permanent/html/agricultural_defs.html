agricultural

Powered by COBUILD
(ægrɪkʌltʃərəl )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Agricultural means involving or relating to agriculture. ⇒ ...agricultural land. ⇒ ...corn and other agricultural products.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]An agricultural place or society is one in which agriculture is important or highly developed. ⇒ ...traditional agricultural societies.

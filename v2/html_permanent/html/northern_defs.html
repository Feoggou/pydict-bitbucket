northern

(ˈnɔːðən )
Definitions
adjective1. situated in or towards the north ⇒ northern towns2. directed or proceeding towards the north ⇒ a northern flow of traffic3. (esp of winds) proceeding from the north4. (sometimes capital) of, relating to, or characteristic of the north or North5. (sometimes capital) astronomy north of the celestial equator

Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]Northern means in or from the north of a region, state, or country. ⇒ Their two children were immigrants to Northern Ireland from Pennsylvania. ⇒ Prices at three-star hotels fell furthest in several northern cities.

carline1

(ˈkɑːlɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna Eurasian thistle-like plant, Carlina vulgaris, having spiny leaves and flower heads surrounded by raylike whitish bracts: family Asteraceae (composites). Also called: carline thistle
Word origin
C16: from French, probably from Latin cardō thistle

carline2

or carlin (ˈkɑːlɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. mainly Scottish an old woman, hag, or witch2. a variant of carling
Word origin
C14: from Old Norse kerling old woman, diminutive of karl man, churl

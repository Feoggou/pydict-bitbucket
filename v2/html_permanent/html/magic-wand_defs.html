magic wand

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a thin rod brandished by a conjuror in performing magic tricks2. any seemingly magical solution to a difficult problem ⇒ there is no magic wand for us to fix it

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA magic wand or a wand is a long thin rod that magicians and fairies wave when they are performing tricks and magic. 2. countable nounYou use magic wand, especially in the expression there is no magic wand, to indicate that someone is dealing with a difficult problem which cannot be solved quickly and easily. ⇒ There is no magic wand to secure a just peace. ⇒ People can't expect him to just wave a magic wand.

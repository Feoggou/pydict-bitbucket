pollution

(pəˈluːʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of polluting or the state of being polluted2. harmful or poisonous substances introduced into an environment

pollution

Powered by COBUILD
(pəluːʃən )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounPollution is the process of polluting water, air, or land, especially with poisonous chemicals. [+ of] ⇒ The fine was for the company's pollution of the air near its plants. ⇒ Recycling also helps control environmental pollution by reducing the need for waste dumps.2. uncount nounPollution is poisonous or dirty substances that are polluting the water, air, or land somewhere. ⇒ The level of pollution in the river was falling.

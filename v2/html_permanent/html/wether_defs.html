wether

(ˈwɛðə )
Definitions
nouna male sheep, esp a castrated one
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English hwæther; related to Old Frisian hweder, Old High German hwedar, Old Norse hvatharr

harpy

Powered by COBUILD
(hɑːʳpi ) for learners of English
Word forms: harpies
Definitions
1. countable noun [usu pl]In classical mythology, the harpies were creatures with the bodies of birds and the faces of women. They flew quickly and were cruel and greedy.2. countable nounIf you refer to a woman as a harpy, you mean that she is very cruel or violent. [literary, disapproval] ⇒ ...a snobby, scheming harpy who sells off the family silverware.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

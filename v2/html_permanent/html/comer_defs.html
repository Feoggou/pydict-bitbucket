Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (in combination) a person who comes ⇒ all-comers, ⇒ newcomers2. informal a potential success

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu pl]You can use comers to refer to people who arrive at a particular place. ⇒ I arrived at the church at two-thirty p.m. to find some early comers outside the main door. ⇒ The first comer was the Sultan himself.2. → see also all-comers, latecomer, newcomer

horseman

(ˈhɔːsmən )
Definitions
noun plural -men1. a person who is skilled in riding or horsemanship2. a person who rides a horse
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

horsewoman (ˈhorseˌwoman) feminine noun

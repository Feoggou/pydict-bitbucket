spiked

(spaɪkt)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having spikes ⇒ spiked railings, ⇒ spiked golf shoes2. (of hair) short and sticking up all over the head ⇒ kids with spiked hair3. containing drugs or alcohol ⇒ Campaigns warn of the danger from spiked drinks., ⇒ Then I would get up, drink my spiked coffee and go to work.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Something that is spiked has one or more spikes on it. ⇒ ...spiked railings. ⇒ ...spiked golf shoes.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]If someone has spiked hair, their hair is short and sticks up all over their head.3. → see also spike

achieve

(əˈtʃiːv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to bring to a successful conclusion; accomplish; attain2. to gain as by hard work or effort ⇒ to achieve success
Derived forms
aˈchievable adjectiveaˈchiever noun
Word origin
C14: from Old French achever to bring to an end, from the phrase a chef to a head, to a conclusion

Odyssey

(ˈɒdɪsɪ )
Definitions
noun1. a Greek epic poem, attributed to Homer, describing the ten-year homeward wanderings of Odysseus after the fall of Troy2. (often not capital) any long eventful journey
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

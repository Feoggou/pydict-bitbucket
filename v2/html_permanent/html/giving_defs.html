giving

(ˈɡɪvɪŋ)
Definitions
adjectiveaffectionate and generous where one's feelings are concerned ⇒ I'm not at all sure how to be a devoted, loving, endlessly giving mother - or even whether I want to be!, ⇒ Jake likes receiving cuddles and can be very giving and affectionate once he trusts adults.

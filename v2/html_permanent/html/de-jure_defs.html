de jure

Powered by COBUILD
(deɪ dʒʊəreɪ , US diː dʒʊri )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]De jure is used to indicate that something legally exists or is a particular thing. [law] ⇒ ...politicians and kings, de jure leaders of men.De jure is also an adverb. ⇒ The Synod's declarations prevailed de jure but not de facto in the Roman Catholic Church down to the Reformation era.

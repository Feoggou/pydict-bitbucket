deshabille

(ˌdeɪzæˈbiːl ) or dishabille
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the state of being partly or carelessly dressed2. archaic clothes worn in such a state
Word origin
C17: from French déshabillé undressed, from dés- dis-1 + habiller to dress; see habiliment

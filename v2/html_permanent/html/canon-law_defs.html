canon law

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe law governing the affairs of a Christian Church, esp the law created or recognized by papal authority in the Roman Catholic Church. See Corpus Juris Canonici, Codex Juris Canonici

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounCanon law is the law of the Christian church. It has authority only for that church and its members. ⇒ The Church's canon law forbids remarriage of divorced persons.

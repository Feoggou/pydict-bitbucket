vertebra

(ˈvɜːtɪbrə )
Definitions
noun plural -brae (-briː) or -brasone of the bony segments of the spinal column
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vertebral (ˈvertebral) adjectivevertebrally (ˈvertebrally) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin: joint of the spine, from vertere to turn

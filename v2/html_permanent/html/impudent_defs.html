impudent

(ˈɪmpjʊdənt )
Definitions
adjective1. mischievous, impertinent, or disrespectful2. an obsolete word for immodest
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

impudently (ˈimpudently) adverbimpudentness (ˈimpudentness) noun

impudent

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmpjʊdənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as impudent, you mean they are rude or disrespectful, or do something they have no right to do. [formal, disapproval] ⇒ Some of them were impudent and insulting.impudence uncount noun ⇒ One sister had the impudence to wear the other's clothes.

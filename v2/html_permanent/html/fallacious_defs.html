fallacious

(fəˈleɪʃəs )
Definitions
adjective1. containing or involving a fallacy; illogical; erroneous2. tending to mislead3. delusive or disappointing ⇒ a fallacious hope
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fallaciously (falˈlaciously) adverbfallaciousness (falˈlaciousness) noun

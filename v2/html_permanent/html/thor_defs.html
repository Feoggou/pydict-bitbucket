Thor

(θɔː )
Definitions
nounNorse mythology the god of thunder, depicted as wielding a hammer, emblematic of the thunderbolt
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English Thōr, from Old Norse thōrr thunder

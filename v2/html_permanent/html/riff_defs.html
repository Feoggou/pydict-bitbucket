riff

(rɪf ) music
Definitions
noun1. (in jazz or rock music) a short series of chordsverb2. (intransitive) to play or perform riffs in jazz or rock music3. informal to speak amusingly or make (amusing comments or remarks)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: probably altered and shortened from refrain2

Definitions
1. countable nounIn jazz and rock music, a riff is a short repeated tune.2. countable nounA riff is a short piece of speech or writing that develops a particular theme or idea. [+ on] ⇒ Rowe does a very clever riff on the nature of prejudice.

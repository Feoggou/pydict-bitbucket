characterization

or characterisation (ˌkærɪktəraɪˈzeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. description of character, traits, etc2. the act of characterizing

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
regional note:   in BRIT, also use characterisation1. variable nounCharacterization is the way an author or an actor describes or shows what a character is like. ⇒ As a writer I am interested in characterization. [+ of] ⇒ ...Chaucer's characterization of Criseyde.2. → see also characterize

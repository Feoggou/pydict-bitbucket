Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverbYou use albeit to introduce a fact or comment which reduces the force or significance of what you have just said. [formal] ⇒ Charles's letter was indeed published, albeit in a somewhat abbreviated form.
Example sentences containing 'albeit'
Her vulnerability, albeit a fleeting one, had disturbed him, cracked the edifice of personality he had constructed for her. Lewis, Roy A TROUT IN THE MILKIn practical terms, it represents the surfacing of a motivation that has been there from the beginning, albeit suppressed.

haft

(hɑːft )
Definitions
noun1. the handle of an axe, knife, etcverb2. (transitive) to provide with a haft
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hafter (ˈhafter) noun

Word Origin

Old English hæft; related to Old Norse hapt, Old High German haft fetter, hefti handle

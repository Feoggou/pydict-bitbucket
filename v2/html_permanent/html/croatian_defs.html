Croatian

(krəʊˈeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Croatia, its people, or their languagenoun2. the language that is spoken in Croatia, formerly regarded as a dialect of Serbo-Croat (Croato-Serb)3. a. a native or inhabitant of Croatiab. a speaker of CroatianSee at Bosnian

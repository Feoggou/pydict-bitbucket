Definitions
1. phrasal verbIf you go away, you leave a place or a person's company. [V P] ⇒ I think we need to go away and think about this.2. phrasal verbIf you go away, you leave a place and spend a period of time somewhere else, especially as a holiday. [V P] ⇒ When you go away on holiday, you need to take extra security precautions. [V P] ⇒ Why don't you and I go away this weekend? →  go

Definitions

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as approachable, you think that they are friendly and easy to talk to. [approval] ⇒ We found him very approachable and easy to talk with.2. graded adjectiveIf you describe an idea or piece of work as approachable, you think that it is presented in a way which people find enjoyable and easy to understand. [approval] accessible ⇒ The text is approachable, coping well with quite complicated subjects.

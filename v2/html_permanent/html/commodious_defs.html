commodious

(kəˈməʊdɪəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of buildings, rooms, etc) large and roomy; spacious2. archaic suitable; convenient
Derived forms
commodiously (comˈmodiously) adverbcommodiousness (comˈmodiousness) noun
Word origin
C15: from Medieval Latin commodiōsus, from Latin commodus convenient, from com- with + modus measure

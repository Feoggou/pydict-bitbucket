intermarry

(ˌɪntəˈmærɪ )
Definitions
verb -ries, -rying or -ried (intransitive)1. (of different groups, races, religions, creeds, etc) to become connected by marriage2. to marry within one's own family, clan, group, etc

intermarry

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪntəʳmæri ) for learners of English
Word forms: intermarries , intermarrying , intermarried
Definitions
verbWhen people from different social, racial, or religious groups intermarry, they marry each other. You can also say that one group intermarries with another group. [V] ⇒ They were allowed to intermarry. [V + with] ⇒ Some of the traders settled and intermarried with local women.

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as tiresome, you mean that you find them irritating or boring. ⇒ ...the tiresome old lady next door. ⇒ It would be too tiresome to wait in the queue.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

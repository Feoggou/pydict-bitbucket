hemisphere

(ˈhɛmɪˌsfɪə )
Definitions
noun1. one half of a sphere2. a. half of the terrestrial globe, divided into northern and southern hemispheres by the equator or into eastern and western hemispheres by some meridians, usually 0° and 180°b. a map or projection of one of the hemispheres3. either of the two halves of the celestial sphere that lie north or south of the celestial equator4. anatomy short for cerebral hemisphere
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hemispheric (ˌhɛmɪˈsfɛrɪk ) or hemispherical (ˌhemiˈspherical) adjective

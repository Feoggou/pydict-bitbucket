ex cathedra

(ɛks kəˈθiːdrə )
Definitions
adjective, adverb1. with authority2. Roman Catholic Church (of doctrines of faith or morals) defined by the pope as infallibly true, to be accepted by all Catholics
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Latin, literally: from the chair

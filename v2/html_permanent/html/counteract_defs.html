Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbTo counteract something means to reduce its effect by doing something that produces an opposite effect. [V n] ⇒ My husband has to take several pills to counteract high blood pressure. [V n] ⇒ This event will counteract such trends.

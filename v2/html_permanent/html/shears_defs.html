shears

(ʃɪəz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. a. large scissors, as for cutting cloth, jointing poultry, etcb. a large scissor-like and usually hand-held cutting tool with flat blades, as for cutting hedges2. any of various analogous cutting or clipping implements or machines3. short for sheerlegs4. See off the shears

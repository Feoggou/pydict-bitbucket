on trial for learners of English

Definitions
phraseIf someone is on trial, they are being tried in a court of law. ⇒ He is currently on trial accused of serious drugs charges. ⇒ He will go on trial later this month charged with murder. ⇒ Two skinheads were put on trial for those murders last week. →  trial

on trial for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
phraseIf you say that someone or something is on trial, you mean that they are in a situation where people are observing them to see whether they succeed or fail. ⇒ The President will be drawn into a damaging battle in which his credentials will be on trial. →  trial

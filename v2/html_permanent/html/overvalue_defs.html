overvalue

(ˌəʊvəˈvæljuː)
Definitions
verb (transitive)to place too high a value on (something) ⇒ a rate that does not overvalue the pound, ⇒ Many, perhaps all, Internet stocks are hugely overvalued., ⇒ It still can be argued that Japanese shares are overvalued in terms of the return they offer.

Word forms: overvalues , overvaluing , overvalued
Definitions
verbTo overvalue something, often a cost or rate of exchange, means to fix its value at too high a level compared with other similar things. [V n] ⇒ ...a rate which does not overvalue the pound. [be V-ed] ⇒ Many, perhaps all, Internet stocks are hugely overvalued.overvaluation (oʊvəʳvæljueɪʃən ) uncount noun [+ of] ⇒ These problems were aggravated by the overvaluation of the pound.overvalued graded adjective & adjective ⇒ It still can be argued that Japanese shares are overvalued in terms of the return they offer.

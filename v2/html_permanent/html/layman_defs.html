layman

(ˈleɪmən )
Definitions
noun plural -men1. a man who is not a member of the clergy2. a person who does not have specialized or professional knowledge of a subject ⇒ science for the layman

layman

Powered by COBUILD
(leɪmən ) for learners of English
Word forms: laymen
Definitions
1. countable nounA layman is a person who is not trained, qualified, or experienced in a particular subject or activity. ⇒ The mere mention of the words 'heart failure', can conjure up, to the layman, the prospect of imminent death. ⇒ There are basically two types called, in layman's terms, blue and white asbestos.2. countable nounA layman is a man who is involved with the Christian church but is not a member of the clergy or a monk. ⇒ In 1932, one Boston layman wrote to Archbishop William O'Connell in support of Father Coughlin.

Oslo

(ˈɒzləʊ , Norwegian ˈuslu)
Definitions
nounthe capital and chief port of Norway, in the southeast at the head of Oslo Fjord (an inlet of the Skagerrak): founded in about 1050; university (1811); a major commercial and industrial centre, producing about a quarter of Norway's total output. Pop: 521 886 (2004 est). Former name: (1624–1877) Christiania or (1877–1924) Kristiania

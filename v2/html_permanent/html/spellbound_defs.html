Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are spellbound by something or someone, you are so fascinated that you cannot think about anything else. [+ by] ⇒ His audience had listened like children, spellbound by his words. ⇒ He was in awe of her; she held him spellbound.

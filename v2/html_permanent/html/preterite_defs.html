preterite

or US preterit (ˈprɛtərɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
grammarnoun1. a tense of verbs used to relate past action, formed in English by inflection of the verb, as jumped, swam2. a verb in this tenseadjective3. denoting this tense

Word Origin

C14: from Late Latin praeteritum (tempus) past (time, tense), from Latin praeterīre to go by, from preter- + īre to go

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveA person or activity that is aimless has no clear purpose or plan. ⇒ Peters had been adrift and aimless. ⇒ After several hours of aimless searching they were getting low on fuel.aimlessly graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v] ⇒ I wandered around aimlessly.aimlessness uncount noun ⇒ His sense of aimlessness increased.

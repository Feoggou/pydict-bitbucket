(ˈəʊvəˌbɔːd )
Definitions

overboard for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(oʊvəʳbɔːʳd )
Definitions
1. adverb [ADV after v]If you fall overboard, you fall over the side of a boat into the water. ⇒ His sailing instructor fell overboard and drowned during a lesson. ⇒ He had jumped overboard in New York harbor and swum to shore.2. →  go overboard3. →  throw sth overboard

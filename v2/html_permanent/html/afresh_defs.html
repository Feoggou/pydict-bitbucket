Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverb [ADV after v]If you do something afresh, you do it again in a different way. ⇒ They believe that the only hope for the French left is to start afresh. ⇒ Only one expert source seemed prepared to analyse the problem afresh, from first principles.
Example sentences containing 'afresh'
Sitting forward on the bed, Jenny pressed her palm to my moist forehead, and frowned afresh. Pritchard, John NIGHT SISTERSI had almost succeeded in mastering my grief, and now she tore the wound afresh. Robin Hobb THE GOLDEN FOOL: BOOK TWO OF THE TAWNY MANNow the machinations of the room began to work on Tammy afresh. Clive Barker COLDHEART CANYONStartled, she caught herself before phrasing it, and the reality of his death struck her afresh. Spencer, LaVyrle FAMILY BLESSINGS

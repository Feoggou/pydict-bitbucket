Siva

(ˈsiːvə , ˈsɪvə ) or Shiva

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nounHinduism the destroyer, one of the three chief divinities of the later Hindu pantheon, the other two being Brahma and Vishnu. Siva is also the god presiding over personal destinies

Word Origin

from Sanskrit Śiva, literally: the auspicious (one)

liable

(ˈlaɪəbəl )
Definitions
adjective (postpositive)1. legally obliged or responsible; answerable2. susceptible or exposed; subject3. probable, likely, or capable ⇒ it's liable to happen soon▶ USAGE The use of liable to to mean likely to was formerly considered incorrect, but is now acceptable
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

liableness (ˈliableness) noun

Word Origin

C15: perhaps via Anglo-French, from Old French lier to bind, from Latin ligāre

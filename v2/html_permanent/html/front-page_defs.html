front page

(frʌnt peɪdʒ)
Definitions
noun1. the first page of a newspaper on which is the main story or headline ⇒ The Guardian's front page carries a photograph of the two foreign ministers2. See to make the front pageadjective3. important or newsworthy enough to be put on the front page of a newspaper ⇒ a front-page article in last week's paper.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions

purée

(ˈpjʊəreɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a smooth thick pulp of cooked and sieved fruit, vegetables, meat, or fishverb -rées, -réeing or -réed2. (transitive) to make (cooked foods) into a purée

Word Origin

C19: from French purer to purify

Word forms: purees , pureeing , pureed
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounPuree is food which has been crushed or beaten so that it forms a thick, smooth liquid. ⇒ ...a can of tomato puree. ⇒ Push the potatoes through a sieve to make a puree.2. verbIf you puree food, you make it into a puree. [V n] ⇒ Puree the apricots in a liquidiser until completely smooth.

unwritten

(ʌnˈrɪtən )
Definitions
adjective1. not printed or in writing2. effective only through custom; traditional3. without writing upon it
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

unwritten for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnrɪtən )
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Something such as a book that is unwritten has not been printed or written down. ⇒ Universal have agreed to pay £2.5 million for Grisham's next, as yet unwritten, novel.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]An unwritten rule, law, or agreement is one that is understood and accepted by everyone, although it may not have been formally or officially established. ⇒ They obey the one unwritten rule that binds them all–no talking.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is light-hearted is cheerful and happy. ⇒ They were light-hearted and prepared to enjoy life.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is light-hearted is intended to be entertaining or amusing, and not at all serious. ⇒ There have been many attempts, both light-hearted and serious, to locate the Loch Ness Monster.

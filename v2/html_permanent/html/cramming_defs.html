cramming

(ˈkræmɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounintensive study, esp in order to pass an exam ⇒ It would take two or three months of cramming to prepare for Vermont's bar exam.

Kate was hurt and angry and eventually, cramming the chaos back down, she started to type.

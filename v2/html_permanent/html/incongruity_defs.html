incongruity

(ˌɪnkɒŋˈɡruːɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. something incongruous2. the state or quality of being incongruous

(ɪnkɒŋgruːɪti ) for learners of English
Word forms: incongruities
Definitions
variable nounThe incongruity of something is its strangeness when considered together with other aspects of a situation. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ She smiled at the incongruity of the question. ⇒ ...the almost absurd incongruity between her wealth and her lifestyle. ⇒ ...the incongruities of life in war-time London.

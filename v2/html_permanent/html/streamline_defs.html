streamline

(ˈstriːmˌlaɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a contour on a body that offers the minimum resistance to a gas or liquid flowing around it2. an imaginary line in a fluid such that the tangent at any point indicates the direction of the velocity of a particle of the fluid at that pointverb (transitive)3. to make streamlined

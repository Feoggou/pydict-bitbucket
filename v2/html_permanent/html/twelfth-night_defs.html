Twelfth Night

Definitions
nouna. the evening of Jan 5, the eve of Twelfth Day, formerly observed with various festal celebrationsb. the evening of Twelfth Day itselfc. (as modifier) ⇒ Twelfth-Night customs

Twelfth-Night

(ˈtwɛlfθˌnaɪt)
Definitions
modifierecclesiastical of or relating to the evening of Jan 5, the eve of Twelfth Day, formerly observed with various festal celebrations ⇒ Twelfth-Night customs

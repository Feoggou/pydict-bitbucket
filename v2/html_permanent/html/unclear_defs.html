unclear for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnklɪəʳ )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If something is unclear, it is not known or not certain. ⇒ It is unclear how much popular support they have among the island's population. ⇒ Just what the soldier was doing in Bireij is unclear. ⇒ His itinerary is still unclear.2. graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ, ADJ as to wh/n, ADJ wh]If you are unclear about something, you do not understand it properly or are not sure about it. [+ about] ⇒ He is still unclear about his own future. ⇒ Experts remain unclear as to why the danger appears to have been steadily increasing.

Pythagorean

(paɪˌθæɡəˈriːən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Pythagoras2. denoting the diatonic scale of eight notes arrived at by Pythagoras and based on a succession of fifthsnoun3. a follower of Pythagoras

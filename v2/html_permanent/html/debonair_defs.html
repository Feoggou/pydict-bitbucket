debonair

, debonnaire or debonaire (ˌdɛbəˈnɛə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective (esp of a man or his manner)1. suave and refined2. carefree; light-hearted3. courteous and cheerful; affable
Derived forms
ˌdebonairly (ˌdeboˈnairly) adverbˌdebonairness (ˌdeboˈnairness) noun
Word origin
C13: from Old French debonaire, from de bon aire having a good disposition

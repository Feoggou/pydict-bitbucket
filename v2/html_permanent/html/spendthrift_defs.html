spendthrift

(ˈspɛndˌθrɪft )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who spends money in an extravagant manneradjective2. (usually prenominal) of or like a spendthrift ⇒ spendthrift economies

Word Origin

C17: from spend + thrift

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf you call someone a spendthrift, you mean that they spend too much money. [disapproval]Spendthrift is also an adjective. ⇒ ...his father's spendthrift ways.

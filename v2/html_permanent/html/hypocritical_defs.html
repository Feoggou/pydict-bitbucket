Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you accuse someone of being hypocritical, you mean that they pretend to have qualities, beliefs, or feelings that they do not really have. [disapproval] ⇒ It would be hypocritical to say I travel at 70mph simply because that is the law. ⇒ If someone is being hypocritical then it is fair to expose that.

insistence

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnsɪstəns ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [N that]Someone's insistence on something is the fact that they insist that it should be done or insist that it is the case. ⇒ ...Raeder's insistence that naval uniform be worn. ⇒ He admired your insistence on understanding things and making your point, even when he preferred his own. ⇒ She had attended an interview, at her boyfriend's insistence. [Also + on]

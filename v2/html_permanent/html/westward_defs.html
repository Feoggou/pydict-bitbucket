westward

(ˈwɛstwəd )
Definitions
adjective1. moving, facing, or situated in the westadverb2. Also: westwards towards the westnoun3. the westward part, direction, etc; the west
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

westwardly (ˈwestwardly) adjective, adverb

westward for learners of English

(westwəʳd ) also westwards
Definitions
adverb [usu ADV after v, oft n ADV]Westward or westwards means towards the west. [+ from] ⇒ He sailed westward from Palos de la Frontera. ⇒ Within hours, she was free to resume her journey westward.Westward is also an adjective. ⇒ ...the one-hour westward flight over the Andes to Lima.

particularize

or particularise (pəˈtɪkjʊləˌraɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to treat in detail; give details (about)2. (intransitive) to go into detail

Derived Forms

parˌticularization (parˌticulariˈzation) or parˌticularisation (parˌticulariˈsation) nounparticularˌizer (parˈticularˌizer) or particularˌiser (parˈticularˌiser) noun

regional note:   in BRIT, also use particulariseverbIf you particularize something that you have been talking about in a general way, you give details or specific examples of it.

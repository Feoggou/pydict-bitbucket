herb

(hɜːb , US English ɜːrb)
Definitions
noun1. a seed-bearing plant whose aerial parts do not persist above ground at the end of the growing season; herbaceous plant2. a. any of various usually aromatic plants, such as parsley, rue, and rosemary, that are used in cookery and medicineb. (as modifier) ⇒ a herb garden3. Caribbean a slang term for marijuana
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

herblike (ˈherbˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C13: from Old French herbe, from Latin herba grass, green plants

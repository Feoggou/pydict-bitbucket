labile

(ˈleɪbɪl )
Definitions
adjective1. chemistry (of a compound) prone to chemical change2. liable to change or move
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lability (ləˈbɪlɪtɪ ) noun

Word Origin

C15: via Late Latin lābilis, from Latin lābī to slide, slip
Example sentences containing 'labile'
Jackson's labile inner state was pressed still further by nagging thoughts of Ellen Thomas's secret. Salley Vickers MR GOLIGHTLY'S HOLIDAY

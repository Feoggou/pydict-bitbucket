gudgeon1

(ˈɡʌdʒən )
Definitions
noun1. a small slender European freshwater cyprinid fish, Gobio gobio, with a barbel on each side of the mouth: used as bait by anglers2. any of various other fishes, such as the goby3. bait or enticement4. slang a person who is easy to trick or cheatverb5. (transitive) slang to trick or cheat
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French gougon, probably from Latin gōbius; see goby

gudgeon2

(ˈɡʌdʒən )
Definitions
noun1. a. a pivot at the end of a beam or axleb. the female or socket portion of a pinned hinge2. nautical one of two or more looplike sockets, fixed to the transom of a boat, into which the pintles of a rudder are fitted
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French goujon, perhaps from Late Latin gulbia chisel

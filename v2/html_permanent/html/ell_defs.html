ell1

(ɛl )
Definitions
nounan obsolete unit of length equal to approximately 45 inches
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English eln the forearm (the measure originally being from the elbow to the fingertips); related to Old High German elina, Latin ulna, Greek ōlenē

ell2

(ɛl )
Definitions
noun1. an extension to a building, usually at right angles and located at one end2. a pipe fitting, pipe, or tube with a sharp right-angle bend
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: a spelling of L, indicating a right angle

Burney

(ˈbɜːnɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Charles. 1726–1814, English composer and music historian, whose books include A General History of Music (1776–89)2.  his daughter, Frances. known as Fanny; married name Madame D'Arblay. 1752–1840, English novelist and diarist: author of Evelina (1778). Her Diaries and Letters (1768–1840) are of historical interest

Synonyms of poised

poised

adjective

  1. ready, waiting, prepared, standing by, on the brink, in the wings, all set
    • US forces are poised for a massive air, land and sea assault.
  2. composed, calm, together (informal), collected, dignified, graceful, serene, suave, urbane, self-confident, unfazed (informal), debonair, unruffled, nonchalant, self-possessed
    • Rachel appeared poised and calm.
    OPPOSITES:  excited, disturbed, annoyed, irritated, worked up, agitated, ruffled, discomposed

poise

noun

  1. composure, cool (slang), presence, assurance, dignity, equilibrium, serenity, coolness, aplomb, calmness, equanimity, presence of mind, sang-froid, savoir-faire, self-possession
    • It took a moment for Mark to recover his poise.
  2. grace, balance, equilibrium, elegance
    • Ballet classes are important for poise.

verb

  1. position, hold, support, balance, hang, suspend, float, hover, hang suspended, hang in midair

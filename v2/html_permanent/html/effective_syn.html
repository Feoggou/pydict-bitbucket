Synonyms of effective

effective

adjective

  1. efficient, successful, useful, active, capable, valuable, helpful, adequate, productive, operative, competent, serviceable, efficacious, effectual
    • Antibiotics are effective against this organism.
    OPPOSITES:  inadequate, useless, vain, insufficient, incompetent, ineffective, inefficient, worthless, futile, unproductive, unimpressive
  2. powerful, strong, convincing, persuasive, telling, impressive, compelling, potent, forceful, striking, emphatic, weighty, forcible, cogent
    • You can't make an effective argument if all you do is stridently voice your opinion.
    OPPOSITES:  weak, tame, pathetic, powerless, feeble, ineffectual
  3. virtual, essential, practical, implied, implicit, tacit, unacknowledged
    • They have had effective control of the area.
  4. in operation, official, current, legal, real, active, actual, in effect, valid, operative, in force, in execution
    • The new rules will become effective in the next few days.
    OPPOSITES:  inactive, inoperative

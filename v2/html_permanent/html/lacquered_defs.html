lacquered

(ˈlækəd)
Definitions
adjective1. coated or varnished with a hard glossy coating made by dissolving cellulose derivatives or natural resins in a volatile solvent2. coated with a black resinous substance, obtained from certain trees ⇒ 17th-century lacquered cabinets3. (of hair) set or sprayed with a mixture of shellac and alcohol to hold it in place ⇒ perfectly lacquered hair and face powder

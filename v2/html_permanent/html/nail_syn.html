Synonyms of nail

nail

noun

  1. tack, spike, rivet, sprig (technical), hobnail, brad (technical), clout nail (technical)
    • A mirror hung on a nail above the washstand.
  2. fingernail, toenail, talon, thumbnail, claw, unguis (technical)
    • Keep your nails short and your hands clean.

verb

  1. fasten, fix, secure, attach, pin, hammer, tack, beat
    • Frank put the first plank down and nailed it in place.
  2. (informal) catch, arrest, capture, apprehend, lift (slang), trap, nab (informal), snare, ensnare, entrap, feel your collar (slang)
    • The police have been trying to nail him for years.
technical name:
unguis
related adjectives:
ungual
ungular

impossibility

(ɪmˌpɒsəˈbɪlɪtɪ , ˌɪmpɒs-)
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. the state or quality of being impossible2. something that is impossible
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

embroil

(ɪmˈbrɔɪl )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to involve (a person, oneself, etc) in trouble, conflict, or argument2. to throw (affairs) into a state of confusion or disorder; complicate; entangle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

embroiler (emˈbroiler) nounembroilment (emˈbroilment) noun

Word Origin

C17: from French embrouiller, from brouiller to mingle, confuse

cudgel

(ˈkʌdʒəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a short stout stick used as a weapon2. See take up the cudgelsverb -els, -elling, -elled or US -els, -eling or -eled3. (transitive) to strike with a cudgel or similar weapon4. See cudgel one's brains
Derived forms
cudgeller (ˈcudgeller) or US cudgeler (ˈcudgeler) noun
Word origin
Old English cycgel; related to Middle Dutch koghele stick with knob

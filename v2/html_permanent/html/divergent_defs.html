divergent

(daɪˈvɜːdʒənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. diverging or causing divergence2. (of opinions, interests, etc) different3. mathematics (of a series) having no limit; not convergent4. botany (of plant organs) farther apart at their tops than at their bases▶ USAGE The use of divergent to mean different as in they hold widely divergent views is considered by some people to be incorrect
Derived forms
divergently (diˈvergently) adverb

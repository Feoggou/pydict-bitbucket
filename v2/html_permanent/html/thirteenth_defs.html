thirteenth

(ˈθɜːˈtiːnθ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (usually prenominal)a. coming after the twelfth in numbering or counting order, position, time, etc; being the ordinal number of thirteen: often written 13thb. (as noun) ⇒ Friday the thirteenthnoun2. a. one of 13 equal or nearly equal parts of somethingb. (as modifier) ⇒ a thirteenth part3. the fraction equal to one divided by 13 (1⁄13)4. musica. an interval of one octave plus a sixth. See also interval (sense 5)b. short for thirteenth chord

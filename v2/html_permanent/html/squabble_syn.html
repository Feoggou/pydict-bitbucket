squabble

verb

  1. quarrel, fight, argue, row, clash, dispute, scrap (informal), fall out (informal), brawl, spar, wrangle, bicker, have words, fight like cat and dog, go at it hammer and tongs • Mother is devoted to Dad although they squabble all the time.

noun

  1. quarrel, fight, row, argument, dispute, set-to (informal), scrap (informal), disagreement, barney (informal), spat (US), difference of opinion, tiff, turf war (informal), bagarre (French) • There have been minor squabbles about phone bills.

(rɪdɪkjʊləsli ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj/adv]You use ridiculously to emphasize the fact that you think something is unreasonable or very surprising. [emphasis] ⇒ Dena bought rolls of silk that seemed ridiculously cheap. ⇒ She looked ridiculously young to be a mother.

whither

(ˈwɪðə ) archaic or poetic
Definitions
adverb1. to what place?2. to what end or purpose?conjunction3. to whatever place, purpose, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English hwider, hwæder; related to Gothic hvadrē; modern English form influenced by hither

whither for learners of English

(hwɪðəʳ )
Definitions
adverbWhither means to where. [literary, or old-fashioned] ⇒ Who are you and whither are you bound? ⇒ They knew not whither they went.

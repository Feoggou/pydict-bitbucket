thankless

(ˈθæŋklɪs )
Definitions
adjective1. receiving no thanks or appreciation ⇒ a thankless job2. ungrateful ⇒ a thankless pupil
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

thanklessly (ˈthanklessly) adverbthanklessness (ˈthanklessness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a job or task as thankless, you mean that it is hard work and brings very few rewards. ⇒ Soccer referees have a thankless task.

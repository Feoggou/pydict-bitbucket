pincers

(ˈpɪnsəz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. Also called: pair of pincers a gripping tool consisting of two hinged arms with handles at one end and, at the other, curved bevelled jaws that close on the workpiece: used esp for extracting nails2. the pair or pairs of jointed grasping appendages in lobsters and certain other arthropods

Word Origin

C14: from Old French pinceour, from Old French pincier to pinch

activity holiday

(ækˈtɪvɪtɪ ˈhɒlɪˌdeɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna type of holiday in which holiday-makers can take part in many different organized activities, esp sports ⇒ activity holidays where new skills and sports are taught

Translations for 'activity holiday'

  • British English: activity holiday /ækˈtɪvɪtɪ ˈhɒlɪˌdeɪ/ NOUN
    An activity holiday is a holiday on which you can take part in a lot of different organized activities, especially sports.

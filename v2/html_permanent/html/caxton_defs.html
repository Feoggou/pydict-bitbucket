Caxton1

(ˈkækstən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a book printed by William Caxton2. a style of type, imitating the Gothic, that Caxton used in his books

Caxton2

(ˈkækstən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun William. ?1422–91, English printer and translator: published, in Bruges, the first book printed in English (1475) and established the first printing press in England (1477)

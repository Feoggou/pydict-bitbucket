humanitarianism

(hjuːˌmænɪˈtɛərɪəˌnɪzəm )
Definitions
noun1. humanitarian principles2. ethicsa. the doctrine that man's duty is to strive to promote the welfare of mankindb. the doctrine that man can achieve perfection through his own resources3. theology the belief that Jesus Christ was only a mortal man

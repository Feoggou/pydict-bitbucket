remorse

(rɪˈmɔːs )
Definitions
noun1. a sense of deep regret and guilt for some misdeed2. compunction; pity; compassion
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

remorseful (reˈmorseful) adjectiveremorsefully (reˈmorsefully) adverbremorsefulness (reˈmorsefulness) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Medieval Latin remorsus a gnawing, from Latin remordēre to bite again, from re- + mordēre to bite

Definitions
uncount nounRemorse is a strong feeling of sadness and regret about something wrong that you have done. ⇒ He was full of remorse. ⇒ He has shown no remorse for his actions.

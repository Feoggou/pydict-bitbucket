Definitions
nounWord forms: plural loosBritish an informal word for lavatory (sense 1)

Word Origin

C20: perhaps from French lieux d'aisance water closet

loo2

(luː )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural loos1. a gambling card game2. a stake used in this game
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: shortened form of lanterloo, via Dutch from French lanterelu, originally a meaningless word from the refrain of a popular song

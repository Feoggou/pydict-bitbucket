benumbed

(bɪˈnʌmd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. made numb; very cold2. literary showing no feeling; stupefied ⇒ Motorists appear benumbed and line up meekly at the pumps.

Similar Words

paralysed, frozen, stunned, numb, dazed, insensitive, deadened, unresponsive, stupefied, immobilized, unfeeling, insensible, anaesthetized

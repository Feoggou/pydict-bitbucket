Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrasal verbIf you track down someone or something, you find them, or find information about them, after a difficult or long search. [V P n] ⇒ She had spent years trying to track down her parents. [V n P] ⇒ I don't know where that old story came from, I've never been able to track it down. →  track

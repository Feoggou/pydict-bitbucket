fritter1

(ˈfrɪtə )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. (usually foll by away) to waste or squander ⇒ to fritter away time2. to break or tear into small pieces; shrednoun3. a small piece; shred
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fritterer (ˈfritterer) noun

Word Origin

C18: probably from obsolete fitter to break into small pieces, ultimately from Old English fitt a piece

fritter2

(ˈfrɪtə )
Definitions
nouna piece of food, such as apple or clam, that is dipped in batter and fried in deep fat
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French friture, from Latin frictus fried, roasted, from frīgere to fry, parch

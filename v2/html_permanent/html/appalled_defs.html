appalled

(əˈpɔːld)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. shocked or dismayed ⇒ She had figured it out and was appalled , ⇒ They are appalled at these statements., ⇒ People were appalled by the news., ⇒ We are appalled that it's still on sale.2. filled with horror ⇒ We were absolutely appalled.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are appalled by something, you are shocked or disgusted because it is so bad or unpleasant. horrified ⇒ She said that the Americans are appalled at the statements made at the conference. ⇒ We are appalled that such items are still on sale.

redo

(riːˈduː )
Definitions
verb -does, -doing, -did or -done (transitive)1. to do over again2. informal to redecorate, esp thoroughly ⇒ we redid the house last summer
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

(riːduː ) for learners of English
Word forms: redoes , redoing , redid , redone
Definitions
verbIf you redo a piece of work, you do it again in order to improve it or change it. [V n] ⇒ They had redone their sums.

trimmings

(ˈtrɪmɪŋz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. parts that are cut off ⇒ Use any pastry trimmings to decorate the apples.2. dialect ornaments; decorations ⇒ Christmas trimmings3. See all the trimmings4. See with all the trimmings

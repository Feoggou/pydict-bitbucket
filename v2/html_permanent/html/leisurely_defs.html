leisurely

Powered by COBUILD
(leʒəʳli , US liːʒ- ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A leisurely action is done in a relaxed and unhurried way. ⇒ Lunch was a leisurely affair. ⇒ Tweed walked at a leisurely pace.Leisurely is also an adverb. ⇒ We walked leisurely into the hotel.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'leisurely'
They motored through the streets of Paris at a leisurely clip, no lights, no siren; just driving along. James W. Huston THE SHADOWS OF POWER

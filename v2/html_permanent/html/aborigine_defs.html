aborigine

(ˌæbəˈrɪdʒɪnɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan original inhabitant of a country or region who has been there from the earliest known times
Word origin
C16: back formation from aborigines, from Latin: inhabitants of Latium in pre-Roman times, probably representing some tribal name but associated in folk etymology with ab origine from the beginning

Aborigine

(ˌæbəˈrɪdʒɪnɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: Aboriginal a member of the indigenous people who were living in Australia when European settlers arrived2. any of the languages of this people. See also Australian (sense 3)

gibe1

or jibe (dʒaɪb )
Definitions
verb1. to make jeering or scoffing remarks (at); tauntnoun2. a derisive or provoking remark
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

giber (ˈgiber) or jiber (ˈjiber) noungibingly (ˈgibingly) or jibingly (ˈjibingly) adverb

Word Origin

C16: perhaps from Old French giber to treat roughly, of uncertain origin

impresario

(ˌɪmprəˈsɑːrɪˌəʊ )
Definitions
noun plural -sarios or -sari1. a producer or sponsor of public entertainments, esp musical or theatrical ones2. the director or manager of an opera, ballet, or other performing company
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Italian, literally: one who undertakes; see impresa

Maltese

(mɔːlˈtiːz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Malta, its inhabitants, or their languagenoun2. plural -tese a native or inhabitant of Malta3. the official language of Malta, a form of Arabic with borrowings from Italian, etc4. a breed of toy dog having a very long straight silky white coat5. a domestic fancy pigeon having long legs and a long neck

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Maltese means belonging or relating to Malta, or to its people, language, or culture. ⇒ ...the Maltese writer Francis Ebejer.2. countable nounA Maltese is a person who comes from Malta.3. uncount nounMaltese is a language spoken in Malta.

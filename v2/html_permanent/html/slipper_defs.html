slipper

(ˈslɪpə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a light shoe of some soft material, for wearing around the house2. a woman's evening or dancing shoe3. cricket informal a fielder in the slip positionverb4. (transitive) informal to hit or beat with a slipper

Derived Forms

slippered (ˈslippered) adjectiveslipper-ˌlike (ˈslipper-ˌlike) adjective

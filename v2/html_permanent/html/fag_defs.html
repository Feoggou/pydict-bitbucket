fag1

(fæɡ )
Definitions
noun1. informal a boring or wearisome task ⇒ it's a fag having to walk all that way2. British (esp formerly) a young public school boy who performs menial chores for an older boy or prefectverbWord forms: fags, fagging or fagged3. (when tr, often foll by out) informal to become or cause to become exhausted by hard toil or work4. (usually intr) British to do or cause to do menial chores in a public school ⇒ Brown fags for Lee
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: of obscure origin

drawer

(ˈdrɔːə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that draws, esp a draughtsman2. a person who draws a cheque. See draw (sense 15)3. a person who draws up a commercial paper4. archaic a person who draws beer, etc, in a bar5. (drɔː) a boxlike container in a chest, table, etc, made for sliding in and out

drawer

Powered by COBUILD
(drɔːəʳ )
Word forms: drawers
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA drawer is part of a desk, chest, or other piece of furniture that is shaped like a box and is designed for putting things in. You pull it towards you to open it. ⇒ He opened a drawer in his writing-table.2. plural noun [also a pair of N]Drawers are underpants, especially ones worn by women. [old-fashioned]3. → see also chest of drawers

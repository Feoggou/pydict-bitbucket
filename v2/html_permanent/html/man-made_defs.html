COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveMan-made things are created or caused by people, rather than occurring naturally. ⇒ Man-made and natural disasters have disrupted the Government's economic plans. ⇒ ...man-made lakes. ⇒ ...a variety of materials, both natural and man-made.

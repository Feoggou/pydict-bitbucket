mezzo

(ˈmɛtsəʊ ) music
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb1. moderately; quite ⇒ mezzo forte, ⇒ mezzo pianonoun plural -zos2. See mezzo-soprano (sense 1)

Word Origin

C19: from Italian, literally: half, from Latin medius middle

linguistic

(lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk ) or linguistical (lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪkəl)
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to language2. of or relating to linguistics
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

linguistically (linˈguistically) adverb

linguistic

Powered by COBUILD
(lɪŋgwɪstɪks ) for learners of English
Word forms: linguistics
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Linguistic abilities or ideas relate to language or linguistics. ⇒ ...linguistic skills. ⇒ ...linguistic theory.linguistically (lɪŋgwɪstɪkli ) adverb [usu ADV adj/-ed] ⇒ Somalia is an ethnically and linguistically homogeneous nation.2. uncount nounLinguistics is the study of the way in which language works. ⇒ Modern linguistics emerged as a distinct field in the nineteenth century. ⇒ ...applied linguistics.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

memorable

(ˈmɛmərəbəl , ˈmɛmrə-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveworth remembering or easily remembered; noteworthy

Derived Forms

ˌmemorability (ˌmemoraˈbility) or memorableness (ˈmemorableness) nounmemorably (ˈmemorably) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Latin memorābilis, from memorāre to recall, from memor mindful

Aubusson

(French obysɔ̃)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a town in central France, in the Creuse department: a centre for flat-woven carpets and for tapestries since the 16th century. Pop: 4662 (1999)adjective2. denoting or relating to these carpets or tapestries

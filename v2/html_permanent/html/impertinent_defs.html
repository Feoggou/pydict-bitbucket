impertinent

(ɪmˈpɜːtɪnənt )
Definitions
adjective1. rude; insolent; impudent2. irrelevant or inappropriate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

impertinently (imˈpertinently) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Latin impertinēns not belonging, from Latin im- (not) + pertinēre to be relevant; see pertain

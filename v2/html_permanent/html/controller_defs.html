controller

(kənˈtrəʊlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who directs, regulates, or restrains2. Also called: comptroller a business executive or government officer who is responsible for financial planning, control, etc3. the equipment concerned with controlling the operation of an electrical device
Derived forms

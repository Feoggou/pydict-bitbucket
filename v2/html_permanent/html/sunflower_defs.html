sunflower

(ˈsʌnˌflaʊə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of several American plants of the genus Helianthus, esp H. annuus, having very tall thick stems, large flower heads with yellow rays, and seeds used as food, esp for poultry: family Asteraceae (composites). See also Jerusalem artichoke2. See sunflower seed oil

at a loss

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
a. uncertain what to do; bewilderedb. rendered helpless (for lack of something) ⇒ at a loss for wordsc. at less than the cost of buying, producing, or maintaining (something) ⇒ the business ran at a loss for several years▷ See loss

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf a business produces something at a loss, they sell it at a price which is less than it cost them to produce it or buy it. [business] ⇒ New fashion designs have to be sold off at a loss if sales are poor. →  loss

intestine

(ɪnˈtɛstɪn )
Definitions
noun(usually plural) the part of the alimentary canal between the stomach and the anus. See large intestine, small intestine ▶ Related adjective: alvine
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

intestinal (ɪnˈtɛstɪnəl , ˌɪntɛsˈtaɪnəl ) adjectiveintestinally (inˈtestinally) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Latin intestīnum gut, from intestīnus internal, from intus within

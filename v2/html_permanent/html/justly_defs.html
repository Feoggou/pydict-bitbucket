(dʒʌstli )
Definitions
1. adverb [usu ADV adj, oft ADV with v]You use justly to show that you approve of someone's attitude towards something, because it seems to be based on truth or reality. [approval] ⇒ Australians are justly proud of their native wildlife.2. → See also just2

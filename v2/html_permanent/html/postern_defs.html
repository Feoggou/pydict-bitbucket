postern

(ˈpɒstən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a back door or gate, esp one that is for private useadjective2. situated at the rear or the side

Word Origin

C13: from Old French posterne, from Late Latin posterula (jānua) a back (entrance), from posterus coming behind; see posterior, posterity

You know Canterbury, possess a key to the postern gate and have access to a ready supply of poisons. Tolkien THE LORD OF THE RINGSThe boy repeated her name to himself as he ducked out of the postern gate and disappeared into the darkness of the Colchester Streets.

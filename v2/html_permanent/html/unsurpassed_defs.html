Definitions
adjectiveIf you describe something as unsurpassed, you are emphasizing that it is better or greater than anything else of its kind. [emphasis] ⇒ The quality of Smallbone furniture is unsurpassed. [+ for] ⇒ ...the Hamburg weekly, surely unsurpassed in the world for its intellectual range and quality.

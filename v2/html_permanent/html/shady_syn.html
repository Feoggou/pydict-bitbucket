Synonyms of shady

shady

adjective

  1. shaded, cool, shadowy, dim, leafy, bowery, bosky (literary), umbrageous
    • After flowering, place the pot in a shady spot.
    OPPOSITES:  bright, exposed, sunny, open, out in the open, sunlit, unshaded
  2. (informal) crooked, dodgy (British & Australian & & New Zealand, informal), unethical, suspect, suspicious, dubious, slippery, questionable, unscrupulous, fishy (informal), shifty, disreputable, untrustworthy, shonky (Australian & & New Zealand, informal)
    • Be wary of people who try to talk you into shady deals.
    OPPOSITES:  straight, ethical, respectable, upright, honourable, honest, reputable, trustworthy, above-board

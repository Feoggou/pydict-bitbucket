Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that someone is condescending, you are showing your disapproval of the fact that they talk or behave in a way which shows that they think they are superior to other people. [disapproval] ⇒ I'm fed up with your money and your whole condescending attitude. ⇒ They can be a bit condescending.condescendingly graded adverb [ADV after v] ⇒ 'The practical work you did for us in Brazil was of great value,' he went on condescendingly.

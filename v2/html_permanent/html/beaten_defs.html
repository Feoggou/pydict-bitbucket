beaten

(ˈbiːtən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. defeated or baffled2. shaped or made thin by hammering ⇒ a bowl of beaten gold3. much travelled; well trodden (esp in the phrase the beaten track)4. See off the beaten track5. (of food) mixed by beating; whipped6. tired out; exhausted7. hunting (of woods, undergrowth, etc) scoured so as to rouse game

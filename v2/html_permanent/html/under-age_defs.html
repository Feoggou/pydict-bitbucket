under age

(ˈʌndər eɪdʒ)
Definitions
adjective1. a person who is under age is legally too young to do something, for example to drink alcohol, have sex, or vote ⇒ Underage youths can obtain alcohol from their older friends., ⇒ Some girls have babies when they are under age.2. under age activities such as drinking or smoking are carried out by people who are legally too young to do them ⇒ his efforts to stop under age drinking and drug abuse

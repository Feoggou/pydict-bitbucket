kingly

(ˈkɪŋlɪ )
Definitions
adjective -lier or -liest1. appropriate to a king; majestic2. royaladverb3. poetic or archaic in a manner appropriate to a king
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

(kɪŋli )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Kingly means like a king, or related to the duties of a king. [literary] ⇒ ...a noble man, kingly in stature. ⇒ They thought that he should resume his kingly duties.

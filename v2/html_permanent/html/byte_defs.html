byte

(baɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun computing1. a group of bits, usually eight, processed as a single unit of data2. the storage space in a memory or other storage device that is allocated to such a group of bits3. a subdivision of a word
Word origin
C20: probably a blend of bit4 + bite

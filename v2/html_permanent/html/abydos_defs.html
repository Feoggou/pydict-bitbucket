Abydos

(əˈbaɪdəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an ancient town in central Egypt: site of many temples and tombs2. an ancient Greek colony on the Asiatic side of the Dardanelles (Hellespont): scene of the legend of Hero and Leander

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA brook is a small stream.2. verbIf someone in a position of authority will brook no interference or opposition, they will not accept any interference or opposition from others. [V n] ⇒ She'd had a plan of action, one that would brook no interference. [V n] ⇒ The army will brook no weakening of its power.

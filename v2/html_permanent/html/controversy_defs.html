controversy

(ˈkɒntrəˌvɜːsɪ , kənˈtrɒvəsɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -siesdispute, argument, or debate, esp one concerning a matter about which there is strong disagreement and esp one carried on in public or in the press
Derived forms
controversial (ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃəl ) adjectiveˌcontroversialˌism (ˌcontroˈversialˌism) nounˌcontroversialist (ˌcontroˈversialist) nounˌcontroversially (ˌcontroˈversially) adverb
Word origin
C14: from Latin contrōversia, from contrōversus turned in an opposite direction, from contra- + vertere to turn

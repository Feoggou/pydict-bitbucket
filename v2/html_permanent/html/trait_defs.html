trait

(treɪt , treɪ )
Definitions
noun1. a characteristic feature or quality distinguishing a particular person or thing2. rare a touch or stroke
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from French, from Old French: a pulling, from Latin tractus, from trahere to drag

Definitions
countable nounA trait is a particular characteristic, quality, or tendency that someone or something has. ⇒ The study found that some alcoholics had clear personality traits showing up early in childhood. ⇒ Creativity is a human trait.

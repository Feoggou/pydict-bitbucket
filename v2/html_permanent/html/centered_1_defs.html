centered

(ˈsɛntərd )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
adjective1.  being at the center2.  having (a specified thing) as the focus of interest or activityused in hyphenated compounds ⇒ consumer-centered3.  mentally and emotionally stable; balanced; collected

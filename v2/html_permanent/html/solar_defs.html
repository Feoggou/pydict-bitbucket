solar

(ˈsəʊlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to the sun ⇒ solar eclipse2. operating by or utilizing the energy of the sun ⇒ solar cell3. astronomy determined from the motion of the earth relative to the sun ⇒ solar year4. astrology subject to the influence of the sun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin sōlāris, from sōl the sun

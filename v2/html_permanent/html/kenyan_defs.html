Kenyan

(ˈkɛnjən , ˈkiːnjən )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to Kenya or its inhabitantsnoun2. a native or inhabitant of Kenya

(kenjən )

Definitions
1. adjectiveKenyan means belonging or relating to Kenya, or to its people or culture.2. countable nounA Kenyan is a Kenyan citizen, or a person of Kenyan origin.

naturalistic

(ˌnætʃrəˈlɪstɪk , -tʃərə-)
Definitions
adjective1. of, imitating, or reproducing nature in effect or characteristics2. of or characteristic of naturalism, esp in art or literature3. of or relating to naturalists4. (of an ethical theory) permitting the inference of ethical judgments from statements of nonethical fact. See Hume's law
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

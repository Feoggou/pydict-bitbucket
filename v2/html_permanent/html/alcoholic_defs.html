(ˌælkəˈhɒlɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person affected by alcoholismadjective2. of, relating to, containing, or resulting from alcohol

alcoholic

(ælkəhɒlɪk , US -hɔːl- )
Word forms: alcoholics
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounAn alcoholic is someone who cannot stop drinking large amounts of alcohol, even when this is making them ill. ⇒ He showed great courage by admitting that he is an alcoholic.2. graded adjective & adjectiveAlcoholic drinks are drinks that contain alcohol. ⇒ ...the serving of alcoholic drinks. ⇒ ...tea, coffee, and alcoholic beverages.

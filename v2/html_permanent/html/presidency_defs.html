presidency

(ˈprɛzɪdənsɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cies1. a. the office, dignity, or term of a presidentb. (often capital) the office of president of a republic, esp the office of the President of the US2. Mormonisma. a local administrative council consisting of a president and two executive membersb. (often capital) the supreme administrative body composed of the Prophet and two councillors

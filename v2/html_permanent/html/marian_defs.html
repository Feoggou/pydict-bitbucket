Marian

(ˈmɛərɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to the Virgin Mary, the mother of Jesus2. of or relating to some other Mary, such as Mary, Queen of Scots or Mary I of Englandnoun3. a person who has a special devotion to the Virgin Mary4. a supporter of some other Mary

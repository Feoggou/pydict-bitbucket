Assur

, Asur (ˈæsə )Asshur or Ashur (ˈæʃʊə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the supreme national god of the ancient Assyrians, chiefly a war god, whose symbol was an archer within a winged disc2. one of the chief cities of ancient Assyria, on the River Tigris about 100 km (60 miles) downstream from the present-day city of Mosul

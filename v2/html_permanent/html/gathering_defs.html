gathering

(ˈɡæðərɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. a group of people, things, etc, that are gathered together; assembly2. sewing a gather or series of gathers in material3. a. the formation of pus in a boilb. the pus so formed4. printing an informal name for section (sense 17)

gathering

Powered by COBUILD
(gæðərɪŋ ) for learners of English
Word forms: gatherings
Definitions
1. countable nounA gathering is a group of people meeting together for a particular purpose. ⇒ ...the twenty-second annual gathering of the South Pacific Forum.2. adjective [ADJ n]If there is gathering darkness, the light is gradually decreasing, usually because it is nearly night. ⇒ The lighthouse beam was quite distinct in the gathering dusk.3. → See also gather

Calabria

(kəˈlæbrɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a region of SW Italy: mostly mountainous and subject to earthquakes. Chief town: Reggio di Calabria. Pop: 2 007 392 (2003 est). Area: 15 080 sq km (5822 sq miles)2. an ancient region of extreme SE Italy (3rd century bc to about 668 ad); now part of Apulia

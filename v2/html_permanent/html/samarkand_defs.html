Samarkand

(ˈsæməˌkænd , Russian səmarˈkant)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna city in E Uzbekistan: under Tamerlane it became the chief economic and cultural centre of central Asia, on trade routes from China and India (the "silk road"). Pop: 289 000 (2005 est). Ancient name: Maracanda

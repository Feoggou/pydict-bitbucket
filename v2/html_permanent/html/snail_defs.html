snail

(sneɪl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of numerous terrestrial or freshwater gastropod molluscs with a spirally coiled shell, esp any of the family Helicidae, such as Helix aspersa (garden snail)2. any other gastropod with a spirally coiled shell, such as a whelk3. a slow-moving or lazy person or animal

Derived Forms

snail-ˌlike (ˈsnail-ˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

Old English snægl; related to Old Norse snigill, Old High German snecko

get round

or get around
Definitions
verb (intransitive)1. (preposition) to circumvent or overcome ⇒ he got round the problem by an ingenious trick2. (preposition) informal to have one's way with; cajole ⇒ that girl can get round anyone3. (preposition) to evade (a law or rules)4. (adverb; foll by to) to reach or come to at length ⇒ I'll get round to that job in an hour

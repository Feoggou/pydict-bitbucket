quantify

(ˈkwɒntɪˌfaɪ )
Definitions
verb -fies, -fying or -fied (transitive)1. to discover or express the quantity of2. logic to specify the quantity of (a term) by using a quantifier, such as all, some, or no
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

quantifiable (ˈquantiˌfiable) adjectivequantification (ˌquantifiˈcation) noun

Word Origin

C19: from Medieval Latin quantificāre, from Latin quantus how much + facere to make

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna cheque in any of various denominations sold for use abroad by a bank, etc, to the bearer, who signs it on purchase and can cash it by signing it again

Word forms: traveller's cheques
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
regional note:   in AM, use traveler's checkcountable noun [usu pl]Traveller's cheques are cheques that you buy at a bank and take with you when you travel, for example so that you can exchange them for the currency of the country that you are in.

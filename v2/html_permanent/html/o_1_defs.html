o

or O (əʊ )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural o's, O's or Os1. the 15th letter and fourth vowel of the modern English alphabet2. any of several speech sounds represented by this letter, in English as in code, pot, cow, move, or form3. another name for nought
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

O for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
also o (oʊ ) (O's, o's )
Definitions
1. variable nounO is the fifteenth letter of the English alphabet.2. numberO is used to mean zero, for example when you are telling someone a telephone number, or mentioning a year such as 1908. [spoken]3. exclamationO is used in exclamations, especially when you are expressing strong feelings. [literary, feelings] ⇒ O how mistaken you are! ⇒ O God, I want to go home.4. → See also oh5. O is used as an abbreviation for words beginning with o, such as 'old' or 'organization'.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

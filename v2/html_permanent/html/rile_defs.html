rile

(raɪl )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to annoy or anger; irritate2. US and Canadian to stir up or agitate (water, etc); roil or make turbid
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: variant of roil

nifty

(ˈnɪftɪ )
Definitions
adjective -tier or -tiest informal1. pleasing, apt, or stylish2. quick, agile ⇒ he's nifty on his feet
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

niftily (ˈniftily) adverbniftiness (ˈniftiness) noun

Word Origin

C19: of uncertain origin

nifty for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(nɪfti )
Word forms: niftier , niftiest
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something as nifty, you think it is neat and pleasing or cleverly done. [informal, approval] ⇒ Bridgeport was a pretty nifty place. ⇒ It was a nifty arrangement, a perfect partnership.

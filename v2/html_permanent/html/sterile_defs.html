sterile

(ˈstɛraɪl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. unable to produce offspring; infertile2. free from living, esp pathogenic, microorganisms; aseptic3. (of plants or their parts) not producing or bearing seeds, fruit, spores, stamens, or pistils4. lacking inspiration or vitality; fruitless5. economics, US (of gold) not being used to support credit creation or an increased money supply

Derived Forms

sterilely (ˈsterilely) adverbsterility (stɛˈrɪlɪtɪ ) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin sterilis

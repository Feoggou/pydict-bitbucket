spoke1

(spəʊk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. the past tense of speak2. archaic or dialect a past participle of speak

spoke2

(spəʊk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a radial member of a wheel, joining the hub to the rim2. a radial projection from the rim of a wheel, as in a ship's wheel3. a rung of a ladder4. See put a spoke in someone's wheelverb5. (transitive) to equip with or as if with spokes

Word Origin

Old English spāca

tropical

(ˈtrɒpɪkəl )
Definitions
adjective1. situated in, used in, characteristic of, or relating to the tropics2. (of weather) very hot, esp when humid3. rhetoric of or relating to a trope
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tropicality (ˌtropiˈcality) nountropically (ˈtropically) adverb

Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Tropical means belonging to or typical of the tropics. ⇒ ...tropical diseases. ⇒ ...a plan to preserve the world's tropical forests.2. graded adjective & adjectiveTropical weather is hot and damp weather that people believe to be typical of the tropics.

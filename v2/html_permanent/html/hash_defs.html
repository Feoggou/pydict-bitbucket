hash1

(hæʃ )
Definitions
noun1. a dish of diced cooked meat, vegetables, etc, reheated in a sauce2. something mixed up3. a reuse or rework of old material4. See make a hash of5. See settle someone's hashverb (transitive)6. to chop into small pieces7. to mix or mess up
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Old French hacher to chop up, from hache hatchet

hash3

(hæʃ ) or hashmark (ˈhæʃˌmɑːk)
Definitions
noun1. the character (#) used to precede a number2. this sign used in printing or writing to indicate that a space should be inserted

Definitions

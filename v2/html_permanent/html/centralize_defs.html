centralize

or centralise (ˈsɛntrəˌlaɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to draw or move (something) to or towards a centre2. to bring or come under central control, esp governmental control
Derived forms
ˌcentralization (ˌcentraliˈzation) or ˌcentralisation (ˌcentraliˈsation) nouncentralˌizer (ˈcentralˌizer) or centralˌiser (ˈcentralˌiser) noun

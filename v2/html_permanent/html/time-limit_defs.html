time limit

(taɪm ˈlɪmɪt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a time or date before which a particular task must be completed ⇒ We have extended the time limit to 30 June.2. the amount of time allowed before a particular task must be completed ⇒ The three month time limit will be up in mid-June.

marketable

(ˈmɑːkɪtəbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of commodities, assets, etc)a. being in good demand; saleableb. suitable for sale2. of or relating to buying or selling on a market ⇒ marketable value

Derived Forms

ˌmarketability (ˌmarketaˈbility) or marketableness (ˈmarketableness) nounmarketably (ˈmarketably) adverb

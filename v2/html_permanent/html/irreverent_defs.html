Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as irreverent, you mean that they do not show respect for people or things that are generally respected. [approval] ⇒ She's irreverent, fun and hugely popular. ⇒ Taylor combined great knowledge with an irreverent attitude to history.irreverence uncount noun [+ for] ⇒ His irreverence for authority marks him out as a troublemaker.irreverently graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj] ⇒ 'Jobs for the boys,' said Crosby irreverently.

Agnus Dei

(ˈæɡnʊs ˈdeɪɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Christianity1. the figure of a lamb bearing a cross or banner, emblematic of Christ2. a chant beginning with these words or a translation of them, forming part of the Roman Catholic Mass or sung as an anthem in the Anglican liturgy3. a wax medallion stamped with a lamb as emblem of Christ and blessed by the pope
Word origin
Latin: Lamb of God
Example sentences containing 'Agnus Dei'

unwarlike

(ʌnˈwɔːlaɪk)
Definitions
adjective1. not relating to war ⇒ the unwarlike nature of the trip2. not fit or ready for war ⇒ He was unwarlike and unaware.3. not hostile or belligerent ⇒ He summoned the old fighter to a private council of war, but found him distinctly unwarlike.

mentality

(mɛnˈtælɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the state or quality of mental or intellectual ability2. a way of thinking; mental inclination or character ⇒ his weird mentality

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu sing]Your mentality is your attitudes and your way of thinking. ⇒ ...a criminal mentality. ⇒ Running a business requires a very different mentality from being a salaried employee.

upper case

printing
Definitions
noun1. the top half of a compositor's type case in which capital letters, reference marks, and accents are keptadjective (upper-case when prenominal)2. of or relating to capital letters kept in this case and used in the setting or production of printed or typed matterverb upper-case3. (transitive) to print with upper-case letters; capitalize

upper-case

(ˈʌpəˌkeɪs)
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to print with upper-case letters; capitalize ⇒ The first letter of each word has been upper-cased.adjective2. (prenominal) a variant spelling of upper case

poisoning

(ˈpɔɪzənɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of killing someone using a poisonous substance ⇒ She was sentenced to twenty years' imprisonment for poisoning.2. accidental exposure to a toxic substance ⇒ The result of mercury poisoning is severe nervous system damage.

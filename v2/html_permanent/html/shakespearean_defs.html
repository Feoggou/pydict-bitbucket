Shakespearean

or Shakespearian (ʃeɪkˈspɪərɪən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Shakespeare or his worksnoun2. a student of or specialist in Shakespeare's works
Example sentences containing 'Shakespearean'
When, as in most cases, they get a blank look, they relapse into a sort of broken Shakespearean English. Anthony Masters CASCADES - THE DAY OF THE DEADIt was just that she was acting like a hammy Shakespearean actress about her mysterious problem. Faas Rice, Bebe CLASS TRIP

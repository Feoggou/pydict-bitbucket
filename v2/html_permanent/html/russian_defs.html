Russian

(ˈrʌʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the official language of Russia: an Indo-European language belonging to the East Slavonic branch2. the official language of the former Soviet Union3. a native or inhabitant of Russiaadjective4. of, relating to, or characteristic of Russia, its people, or their language
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Russian

Powered by COBUILD
(rʌʃən ) for learners of English
Word forms: Russians
Definitions
1. adjectiveRussian means belonging or relating to Russia, or to its people, language, or culture. ⇒ ...the Russian parliament.2. countable nounA Russian is a person who comes from Russia. ⇒ Three-quarters of Russians live in cities.3. uncount nounRussian is the language spoken in Russia, and other countries such as Belarus, Kazakhstan and Kyrgystan.

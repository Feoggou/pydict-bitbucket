limn

(lɪm )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to represent in drawing or painting2. archaic to describe in words3. an obsolete word for illuminate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

limner (ˈlɪmnə ) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Old French enluminer to illumine (a manuscript) from Latin inlūmināre to brighten, from lūmen light

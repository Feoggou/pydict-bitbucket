simile

(ˈsɪmɪlɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna figure of speech that expresses the resemblance of one thing to another of a different category, usually introduced by as or like. Compare metaphor

Word Origin

C14: from Latin simile something similar, from similis like

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA simile is an expression which describes a person or thing as being similar to someone or something else. For example, the sentences 'She runs like a deer' and 'He's as white as a sheet' contain similes.

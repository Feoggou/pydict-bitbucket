hatchet

(ˈhætʃɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a short axe used for chopping wood, etc2. a tomahawk3. (modifier) of narrow dimensions and sharp features ⇒ a hatchet face4. See bury the hatchet
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hatchet-like (ˈhatchet-ˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Old French hachette, from hache axe, of Germanic origin; compare Old High German happa knife

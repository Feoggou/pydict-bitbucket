invading

(ɪnˈveɪdɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. entering a country with the intent of conquest or occupation ⇒ the invading Romans, ⇒ The Romans and the Normans came to Britain as invading armies.2. penetrating with infective force; infesting ⇒ When injury or invading bacteria cause inflammation, histamine is secreted by the damaged cells.

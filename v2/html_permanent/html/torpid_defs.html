torpid

(ˈtɔːpɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. apathetic, sluggish, or lethargic2. (of a hibernating animal) dormant; having greatly reduced metabolic activity3. unable to move or feel
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

torpidity (torˈpidity) or rare torpidness (ˈtorpidness) nountorpidly (ˈtorpidly) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin torpidus, from torpēre to be numb, motionless

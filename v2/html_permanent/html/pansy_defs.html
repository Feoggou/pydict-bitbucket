pansy

(ˈpænzɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -sies1. any violaceous garden plant that is a variety of Viola tricolor, having flowers with rounded velvety petals, white, yellow, or purple in colour. See also wild pansy2. slang, offensive an effeminate or homosexual man or boy3. a. a strong violet colourb. (as adjective) ⇒ a pansy carpet

Word Origin

C15: from Old French pensée thought, from penser to think, from Latin pensāre

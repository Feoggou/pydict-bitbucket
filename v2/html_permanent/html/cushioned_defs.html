cushioned

(ˈkʊʃənd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. provided with cushions ⇒ a cushioned seat2. provided with a means of absorbing shock ⇒ cushioned running shoes, ⇒ cushioned soles
Example sentences containing 'cushioned'
She put a plate of fried eggs cushioned by buttered toast in front of him. Terman, Douglas CORMORANTOnly later did he realize how cushioned against the outside world his Service life had been. Meek, M R D A WORM OF DOUBTAnd once she was sitting, it seemed quite natural to lay her head on an upper step, cushioned by an outthrust arm. Garth Nix LIRAEL: DAUGHTER OF THE CLAYR

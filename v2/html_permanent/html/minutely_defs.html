Powered by COBUILD
(maɪnjuːtli , US -nuːt- )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]You use minutely to indicate that something is done in great detail. ⇒ The metal is then minutely examined to ensure there are no cracks. ⇒ They follow minutely the news from abroad on Cable News Network.2. graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj/-ed]You use minutely to indicate that the size or extent of something is very small. ⇒ The benefit of an x-ray far outweighs the minutely increased risk of cancer.

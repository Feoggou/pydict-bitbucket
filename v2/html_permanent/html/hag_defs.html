hag1

(hæɡ )
Definitions
noun1. an unpleasant or ugly old woman2. a witch3. short for hagfish4. obsolete a female demon
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

haggish (ˈhaggish) adjectivehaggishly (ˈhaggishly) adverbhaggishness (ˈhaggishness) nounhaglike (ˈhagˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

Old English hægtesse witch; related to Old High German hagazussa, Middle Dutch haghetisse

hag2

or hagg (hæɡ , hɑːɡ )
Definitions
noun Scottish and Northern England dialect1. a firm spot in a bog2. a soft place in a moor
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: of Scandinavian origin; compare Old Norse högg gap; see hew

harass

(ˈhærəs , həˈræs )
Definitions
verb(transitive) to trouble, torment, or confuse by continual persistent attacks, questions, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

harassed (ˈharassed) adjectiveharassedly (ˈharassedly) adverbharasser (haˈrasser) nounharassing (ˈharassing) adjective, nounharassingly (haˈrassingly) adverbharassment (ˈharassment) noun

Word Origin

C17: from French harasser, variant of Old French harer to set a dog on, of Germanic origin; compare Old High German harēn to cry out

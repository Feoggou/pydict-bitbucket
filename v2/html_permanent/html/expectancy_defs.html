expectancy

(ɪkˈspɛktənsɪ ) or expectance
Definitions
noun1. something expected, esp on the basis of a norm or average ⇒ his life expectancy was 30 years2. anticipation; expectation3. the prospect of a future interest or possession, esp in property ⇒ an estate in expectancy

lay away

Definitions
verb (tr, adverb)1. to store or reserve for future use2. to reserve (merchandise) for future delivery, while payments are being madenoun layaway3. Also called: layaway plan a method of payment by instalments in which the buyer takes possession of the merchandise only when the full price has been paid4. merchandise reserved in a layaway plan

Ethiopian

(ˌiːθɪˈəʊpɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Ethiopia, its people, or any of their languages2. of or denoting a zoogeographical region consisting of Africa south of the Sahara3. anthropology obsolete of or belonging to a postulated racial group characterized by dark skin, an oval elongated face, and thin lips, living chiefly in Africa south of the Saharanoun4. a native or inhabitant of Ethiopia5. any of the languages of Ethiopia, esp Amharicnoun, adjective6. an archaic word for Black

pilgrimage

(ˈpɪlɡrɪmɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a journey to a shrine or other sacred place2. a journey or long search made for exalted or sentimental reasonsverb3. (intransitive) to make a pilgrimage

pilgrimage

Powered by COBUILD
(pɪlgrɪmɪdʒ )
Word forms: pilgrimages
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounIf you make a pilgrimage to a holy place, you go there for a religious reason. [+ to] ⇒ ...the pilgrimage to Mecca.2. countable nounA pilgrimage is a journey that someone makes to a place that is very important to them. [+ to] ⇒ ...a private pilgrimage to family graves. ⇒ His father took him on a sentimental pilgrimage to Ireland.

three-quarter

Definitions
adjective1. being three quarters of something ⇒ a three-quarter turn2. being of three quarters the normal lengthnoun3. rugbya. any of the four players between the fullback and the halfbacksb. this positionc. (as modifier) ⇒ three-quarter play

three-quarter

Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]You can use three-quarter to describe something which is three fourths of the usual size or three fourths of a standard measurement. ⇒ Choose short or three-quarter sleeves for summer. ⇒ ...a session which lasted one and three-quarter hours. ⇒ ...three-quarter length coats. ⇒ There was a three-quarter moon tonight.

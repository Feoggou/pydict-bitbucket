Synonyms of go on

go on

  1. happen, occur, take place
    • I don't know what's going on.
  2. continue, last, stay, proceed, carry on, keep going
    • the necessity for the war to go on
  3. ramble on, carry on, chatter, waffle (informal, mainly British), witter (on) (informal), rabbit on (British, informal), prattle, blether, earbash (Australian & & New Zealand, slang)
    • They're always going on about choice and market forces.

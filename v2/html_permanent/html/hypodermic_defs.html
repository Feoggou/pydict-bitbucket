hypodermic

(ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːmɪk )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to the region of the skin beneath the epidermis2. injected beneath the skinnoun3. a hypodermic syringe or needle4. a hypodermic injection
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hypodermically (ˌhypoˈdermically) adverb

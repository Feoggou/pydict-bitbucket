old boy

Definitions
noun1. (sometimes capitals) British a male ex-pupil of a school2. informal, mainly Britisha. a familiar name used to refer to a manb. an old man

old boy for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
Word forms: old boys
Definitions
1. countable nounYou can refer to a man who used to be a pupil at a particular school or university as an old boy. [British] ⇒ ...Eton College, with all its traditions and long list of famous old boys. ⇒ ...Cardiff High School Old Boys rugby team.2. countable nounIf you refer to an old or middle-aged man as an old boy, you are referring to him in a disrespectful way. [British, informal, old-fashioned, disapproval] ⇒ Outside, two old boys lingered on the street corner discussing cattle.

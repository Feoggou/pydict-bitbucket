spectral

(ˈspɛktrəl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or like a spectre2. of or relating to a spectrum ⇒ spectral colours3. physics (of a physical quantity) relating to a single wavelength of radiation ⇒ spectral luminous efficiency

Derived Forms

spectrality (spɛkˈtrælɪtɪ ) or spectralness (ˈspectralness) nounspectrally (ˈspectrally) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as spectral, you mean that they look like a ghost. [literary] ⇒ She is compelling, spectral, fascinating, an unforgettably unique performer. ⇒ ...the spectral quality of the light.

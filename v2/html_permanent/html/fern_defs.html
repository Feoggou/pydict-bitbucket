fern

(fɜːn )
Definitions
noun1. any tracheophyte plant of the phylum Filicinophyta, having roots, stems, and fronds and reproducing by spores formed in structures (sori) on the fronds. See also tree fern2. any of certain similar but unrelated plants, such as the sweet fern
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fernlike (ˈfernˌlike) adjectiveferny (ˈferny) adjective

Word Origin

Old English fearn; related to Old High German farn, Sanskrit parná leaf

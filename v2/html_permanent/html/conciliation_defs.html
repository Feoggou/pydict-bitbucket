conciliation

(kənˌsɪlɪˈeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or process of conciliating2. a method of helping the parties in a dispute to reach agreement, esp divorcing or separating couples to part amicably

conciliation

Powered by COBUILD
(kənsɪlieɪʃən )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounConciliation is willingness to end a disagreement or the process of ending a disagreement. ⇒ Resolving the dispute will require a mood of conciliation on both sides. ⇒ The experience has left him sceptical about efforts at conciliation.

satellite dish

(ˈsætəˌlaɪt dɪʃ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nountelecommunications a piece of equipment which people need to have on their house in order to receive satellite television ⇒ These satellite dishes can transmit a television signal 24,000 miles up into the atmosphere and back down again within a quarter-of-a-second.

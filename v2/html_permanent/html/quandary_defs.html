quandary

(ˈkwɒndrɪ , -dərɪ)
Definitions
noun plural -riesa situation or circumstance that presents problems difficult to solve; predicament; dilemma
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: of uncertain origin; perhaps related to Latin quandō when

quandary

Powered by COBUILD
(kwɒndəri ) for learners of English
Word forms: quandaries
Definitions
countable noun [usu sing]If you are in a quandary, you have to make a decision but cannot decide what to do. [+ about] ⇒ The government appears to be in a quandary about what to do with so many people.

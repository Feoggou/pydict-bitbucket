Cologne

(kəˈləʊn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan industrial city and river port in W Germany, in North Rhine-Westphalia on the Rhine: important commercially since ancient times; university (1388). Pop: 965 954 (2003 est). German name: Köln

cologne

(kəˈləʊn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna perfumed liquid or solid made of fragrant essential oils and alcohol. Also called: Cologne water, eau de Cologne
Word origin
C18: Cologne water, from Cologne, where it was first manufactured (1709)

guided

(ˈɡaɪdɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. equipped with a guidance system ⇒ Guided missiles ensure accurate delivery of the weapons.2. accompanied by a guide or conducted with the help of a guidebook ⇒ There will be guided walks around the site.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

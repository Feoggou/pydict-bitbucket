conceptual

(kənˈsɛptjʊəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. relating to or concerned with concepts; abstract2. concerned with the definitions or relations of the concepts of some field of enquiry rather than with the facts
Derived forms
conceptually (conˈceptually) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]Conceptual means related to ideas and concepts formed in the mind. ⇒ ...replacing old laws with new within the same conceptual framework.conceptually adverb [usu ADV with v] [ADV adj] ⇒ The monograph is conceptually confused, unclear in its structure and weak in its methodology.

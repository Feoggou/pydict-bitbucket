redouble

(rɪˈdʌbəl )
Definitions
verb1. to make or become much greater in intensity, number, etc ⇒ to redouble one's efforts2. to send back (sounds) or (of sounds) to be sent back; echo or re-echo3. bridge to double (an opponent's double)noun4. the act of redoubling

(riːdʌbəl ) for learners of English
Word forms: redoubles , redoubling , redoubled
Definitions
ergative verb & verbIf you redouble your efforts, you try much harder to achieve something. If something redoubles, it increases in volume or intensity. [V n] ⇒ The president also called on nations to redouble their efforts to negotiate an international trade agreement. [V] ⇒ The applause redoubled.

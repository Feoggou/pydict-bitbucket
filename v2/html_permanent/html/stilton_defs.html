Stilton

(ˈstɪltən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nountrademark either of two rich cheeses made from whole milk, blue-veined (blue Stilton) or white (white Stilton), both very strong in flavour

Word Origin

C18: named after Stilton, Cambridgeshire, where it was originally sold

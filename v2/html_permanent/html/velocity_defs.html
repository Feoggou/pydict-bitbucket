velocity

(vɪˈlɒsɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. speed of motion, action, or operation; rapidity; swiftness2. physics a measure of the rate of motion of a body expressed as the rate of change of its position in a particular direction with time. It is measured in metres per second, miles per hour, etc. Symbol: u, v, w3. physics (not in technical usage) another word for speed (sense 3)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin vēlōcitās, from vēlōx swift; related to volāre to fly

groomed

(ɡruːmd, ɡrʊmd)
Definitions
adjectiveclean and tidy in appearance, clothes, etc ⇒ a very well groomed man, ⇒ Even in jeans she managed to look groomed and well-dressed.. See also well-groomed

groomed

Powered by COBUILD
(gruːmd ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu adv ADJ]You use groomed in expressions such as well groomed and badly groomed to say how neat, clean, and smart a person is. ⇒ ...a very well groomed man. ⇒ She always appeared perfectly groomed.

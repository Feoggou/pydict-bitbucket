foundry

(ˈfaʊndrɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ries1. a place in which metal castings are produced2. the science or practice of casting metal3. cast-metal articles collectively
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Old French fonderie, from fondre; see found3

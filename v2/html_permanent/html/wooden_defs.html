wooden

(ˈwʊdən )
Definitions
adjective1. made from or consisting of wood2. awkward or clumsy3. bereft of spirit or animation ⇒ a wooden expression4. obstinately unyielding ⇒ a wooden attitude5. mentally slow or dull6. not highly resonant ⇒ a wooden thudverb7. (transitive) Australian slang to fell or kill (a person or animal)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

woodenly (ˈwoodenly) adverbwoodenness (ˈwoodenness) noun

Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Wooden objects are made of wood. ⇒ ...the shop's bare brick walls and faded wooden floorboards.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe an actor as wooden, you are critical of them because their performance is not at all lively or natural. [disapproval]

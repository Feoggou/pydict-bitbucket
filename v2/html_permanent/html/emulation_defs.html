emulation

(ˌɛmjʊˈleɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of emulating or imitating2. the effort or desire to equal or surpass another or others3. archaic jealous rivalry
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'emulation'
A solid ten-inches long, trimmed and waxed in emulation of the beards of great, dead men: Kant, Nietzsche, Tolstoy. Clive Barker SACRAMENTMany of the young people today are doing this in emulation of Director Amés. Tony Daniel METAPLANETARY

Similar Words

imitation, following, copying, mirroring, reproduction, mimicry

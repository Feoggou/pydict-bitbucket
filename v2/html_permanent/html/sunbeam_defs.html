sunbeam

(ˈsʌnˌbiːm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a beam, ray, or stream of sunlight2. Australian slang a piece of crockery or cutlery laid for a meal but remaining unused

Derived Forms

sunˌbeamed (ˈsunˌbeamed) or sunˌbeamy (ˈsunˌbeamy) adjective

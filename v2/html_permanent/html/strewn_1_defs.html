(struːn )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [v-link ADJ with n]If a place is strewn with things, they are lying scattered there. ⇒ The front room was strewn with books and clothes. ⇒ The riverbed was strewn with big boulders.Strewn is also a combining form. ⇒ ...a litter-strewn street. ⇒ ...a rock-strewn hillside.2. Strewn is the past participle of strew.

tankard

(ˈtæŋkəd )
Definitions
nouna. a large one-handled drinking vessel, commonly made of silver, pewter, or glass, sometimes fitted with a hinged lidb. the quantity contained in a tankard
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: related to Middle Dutch tankaert, French tanquart

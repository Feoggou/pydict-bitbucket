alkali

(ˈælkəˌlaɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -lis or -lies1. chemistry a soluble base or a solution of a base2. a soluble mineral salt that occurs in arid soils and some natural waters
Word origin
C14: from Medieval Latin, from Arabic al-qili the ashes (of the plant saltwort)

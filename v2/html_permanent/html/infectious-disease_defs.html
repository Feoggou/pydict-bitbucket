infectious disease

(ɪnˈfɛkʃəs dɪˈziːz)
Definitions
nouna disease caused by microorganisms, such as bacteria, viruses, or protozoa ⇒ infectious diseases such as measles
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Secrecy is an infectious disease , a health hazard Julian Baker appreciated. Jon Cleary YESTERDAY'S SHADOWOf course there was promiscuity in other areas of Sydney, but it was kept under wraps as if it were some sort of infectious disease. Cleary, Jon MURDER SONG

hardening

(ˈhɑːdənɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. the act or process of becoming or making hard2. a substance added to another substance or material to make it harder
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'hardening'
It was as cold as meat, almost cold enough to begin hardening up like ice. Zindell, David THE BROKEN GOD"Remember that, later on," Owen said, his voice hardening. Clive Barker EVERVILLEHe was building up a seal of corrosion, hardening himself, packing himself under. Erdrich, Louise THE BINGO PALACE

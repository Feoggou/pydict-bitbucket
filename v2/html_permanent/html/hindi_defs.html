Hindi

(ˈhɪndɪ )
Definitions
noun1. a language or group of dialects of N central India. It belongs to the Indic branch of the Indo-European family and is closely related to Urdu. See also Hindustani2. a formal literary dialect of this language, the official language of India, usually written in Nagari script3. a person whose native language is Hindi
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Hindi hindī, from Hind India, from Old Persian Hindu the river Indus

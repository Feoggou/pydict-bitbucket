coccyx

(ˈkɒksɪks )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural coccyges (kɒkˈsaɪdʒiːz )a small triangular bone at the end of the spinal column in man and some apes, representing a vestigial tail
Derived forms
coccygeal (kɒkˈsɪdʒɪəl ) or coccygian (kɒkˈsɪdʒɪən) adjective
Word origin
C17: from New Latin, from Greek kokkux cuckoo, of imitative origin; from the likeness of the bone to a cuckoo's beak

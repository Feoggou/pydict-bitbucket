respire

(rɪˈspaɪə )
Definitions
verb1. to inhale and exhale (air); breathe2. (intransitive) to undergo the process of respiration3. literary to breathe again in a relaxed or easy manner, as after stress or exertion
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin rēspīrāre to exhale, from re- + spīrāre to breathe; see spirit1

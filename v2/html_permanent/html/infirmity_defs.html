infirmity

(ɪnˈfɜːmɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. the state or quality of being infirm2. physical weakness or debility; frailty3. a moral flaw or failing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

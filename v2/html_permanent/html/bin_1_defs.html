bin

(bɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a large container or enclosed space for storing something in bulk, such as coal, grain, or wool2. Also called: bread bin a small container for bread3. Also called: dustbin, rubbish bin a container for litter, rubbish, etc4. Britisha. a storage place for bottled wineb. one particular bottling of wineverbWord forms: bins, binning or binned5. (transitive) to store in a bin6. (transitive) to put in a wastepaper bin
Word origin
Old English binne basket, probably of Celtic origin; related to bindan to bind

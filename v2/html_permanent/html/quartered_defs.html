quartered

(ˈkwɔːtəd )
Definitions
adjective1. heraldry (of a shield) divided into four sections, each having contrasting arms or having two sets of arms, each repeated in diagonally opposite corners2. (of a log) sawn into four equal parts along two diameters at right angles to each other; quartersawn

venerate

(ˈvɛnəˌreɪt )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to hold in deep respect; revere2. to honour in recognition of qualities of holiness, excellence, wisdom, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

venerator (ˈvenerˌator) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin venerārī, from venus love

woodbine

(ˈwuːdˌbaɪn )
Definitions
noun1. a honeysuckle, Lonicera periclymenum, of Europe, SW Asia, and N Africa, having fragrant creamy flowers2. See American woodbine3. US another name for Virginia creeper (sense 1)4. Australian obsolete, slang an Englishman
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

sense 4 from the English brand of cigarettes so named

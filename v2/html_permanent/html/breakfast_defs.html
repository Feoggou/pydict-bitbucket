breakfast

(ˈbrɛkfəst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. the first meal of the dayb. (as modifier) ⇒ breakfast cereal, ⇒ a breakfast room2. the food at this meal3. (in the Caribbean) a midday mealverb4. to eat or supply with breakfast
Derived forms
breakfaster (ˈbreakfaster) noun
Word origin
C15: from break + fast2

Word forms: breakfasts , breakfasting , breakfasted
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounBreakfast is the first meal of the day. It is usually eaten in the early part of the morning. ⇒ What's for breakfast? ⇒ ...breakfast cereal.2. → see also bed and breakfast, continental breakfast, English breakfast3. countable noun [adj N]A cooked breakfast or a hot breakfast is a breakfast that consists of cooked food, such as bacon and eggs.4. verbWhen you breakfast, you have breakfast. [formal] [V adv/prep] ⇒ All the ladies breakfasted in their rooms. → meal

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]Someone who is aloof is not very friendly and does not like to spend time with other people. [disapproval] ⇒ He seemed aloof and detached.aloofness uncount noun ⇒ He had an air of aloofness about him.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone stays aloof from something, they do not become involved with it. [formal] [+ from] ⇒ The Government is keeping aloof from the controversy. ⇒ I will hold myself aloof from wrong and corruption.

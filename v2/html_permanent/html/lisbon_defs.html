Lisbon

(ˈlɪzbən )
Definitions
nounthe capital and chief port of Portugal, in the southwest on the Tagus estuary: became capital in 1256; subject to earthquakes and severely damaged in 1755; university (1911). Pop: 1 892 891 (2001). Portuguese name: Lisboa (liʒˈboə)

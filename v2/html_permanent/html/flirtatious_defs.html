flirtatious

(flɜːˈteɪʃəs )
Definitions
adjective1. given to flirtation2. expressive of playful sexual invitation ⇒ a flirtatious glance
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

flirtatiously (flirˈtatiously) adverbflirtatiousness (flirˈtatiousness) noun

loggerhead

(ˈlɒɡəˌhɛd )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: loggerhead turtle a large-headed turtle, Caretta caretta, occurring in most seas: family Chelonidae2. See loggerhead shrike3. a tool consisting of a large metal sphere attached to a long handle, used for warming liquids, melting tar, etc4. a strong round upright post in a whaleboat for belaying the line of a harpoon5. archaic or dialect a blockhead; dunce6. See at loggerheads
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

loggerheaded (ˈloggerˌheaded) adjective

Word Origin

C16: probably from dialect logger wooden block + head

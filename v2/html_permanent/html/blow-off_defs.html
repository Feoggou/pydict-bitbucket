blow off

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (adverb)1. to permit (a gas under pressure, esp steam) to be released2. (intransitive) British slang to emit wind noisily from the anus3. (transitive) informal to reject or jilt (someone)4. See blow off steamnoun blowoff5. a discharge of a surplus fluid, such as steam, under pressure6. a device through which such a discharge is made

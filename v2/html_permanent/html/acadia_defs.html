Acadia

(əˈkeɪdɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. the Atlantic Provinces of Canadab. the French-speaking areas of these provinces2. (formerly) a French colony in the present-day Atlantic Provinces: ceded to Britain in 1713

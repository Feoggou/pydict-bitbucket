terrier1

(ˈtɛrɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany of several usually small, active, and short-bodied breeds of dog, originally trained to hunt animals living underground

Word Origin

C15: from Old French chien terrier earth dog, from Medieval Latin terrārius belonging to the earth, from Latin terra earth

mutinous

(ˈmjuːtɪnəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. openly rebellious or disobedient ⇒ a mutinous child2. characteristic or indicative of mutiny

Derived Forms

mutinously (ˈmutinously) adverbmutinousness (ˈmutinousness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is mutinous, they are strongly dissatisfied with a person in authority and are likely to stop obeying them. ⇒ His own army, stung by defeats, is mutinous.

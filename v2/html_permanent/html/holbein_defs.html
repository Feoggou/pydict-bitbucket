Holbein

(German ˈhɔlbain)
Definitions
noun1.  Hans (hans), known as Holbein the Elder. 1465– 1524, German painter2.  his son, Hans, known as Holbein the Younger. 1497–1543, German painter and engraver; court painter to Henry VIII of England (1536–43). He is noted particularly for his portraits, such as those of Erasmus (1524; 1532) and Sir Thomas More (1526)

bloodthirsty

(ˈblʌdˌθɜːstɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -thirstier or -thirstiest1. murderous; cruel2. taking pleasure in bloodshed or violence3. describing or depicting killing and violence; gruesome ⇒ a bloodthirsty film
Derived forms
bloodˌthirstily (ˈbloodˌthirstily) adverbbloodˌthirstiness (ˈbloodˌthirstiness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveBloodthirsty people are eager to use violence or display a strong interest in violent things. You can also use bloodthirsty to refer to very violent situations. ⇒ They were savage and bloodthirsty. ⇒ ...some of the most tragic scenes witnessed even in this bloodthirsty war.

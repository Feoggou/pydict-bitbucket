tasteful

(ˈteɪstfʊl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. indicating good taste ⇒ a tasteful design2. a rare word for tasty

Derived Forms

Powered by COBUILD
(teɪstfʊl )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that something is tasteful, you consider it to be attractive, elegant, and in good taste. ⇒ The decor is tasteful and restrained. ⇒ ...tasteful jewelry.tastefully graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj] ⇒ ...a large and tastefully decorated home. ⇒ The rectory has been sensitively restored and tastefully furnished.

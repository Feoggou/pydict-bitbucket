university

(ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. an institution of higher education having authority to award bachelors' and higher degrees, usually having research facilities2. the buildings, members, staff, or campus of a university
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French universite, from Medieval Latin universitās group of scholars, from Late Latin: guild, society, body of men, from Latin: whole, totality, universe

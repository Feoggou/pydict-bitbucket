tonight

(təˈnaɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the night or evening of this present dayadverb2. in or during the night or evening of this day3. archaic last night

Word Origin

Old English tōniht, from to1 (at) + night

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverb [n ADV]Tonight is used to refer to the evening of today or the night that follows today. ⇒ I'm at home tonight. ⇒ Tonight, I think he proved to everybody what a great player he was. ⇒ There they will stay until 11 o'clock tonight.Tonight is also a noun. ⇒ Tonight is the opening night of the opera. ⇒ ...tonight's flight to London.

dead weight

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a heavy weight or load2. an oppressive burden; encumbrance3. the difference between the loaded and the unloaded weights of a ship4. another name for dead load5. (in shipping) freight chargeable by weight rather than by bulk

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA dead weight is a load which is surprisingly heavy and difficult to lift. ⇒ He hoisted the dead weight over his shoulder.2. countable noun [usu sing]You can refer to something that makes change or progress difficult as a dead weight. ⇒ ...the dead weight of traditional policies.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu with poss]The creator of something is the person who made it or invented it. [+ of] ⇒ ...Ian Fleming, the creator of James Bond. ⇒ I have always believed that a garden dies with its creator.2. proper nounGod is sometimes referred to as the Creator. ⇒ This was the first object placed in the heavens by the Creator.

congregate

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (ˈkɒŋɡrɪˌɡeɪt )1. to collect together in a body or crowd; assembleadjective (ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡɪt , -ˌɡeɪt)2. collected together; assembled3. relating to collecting; collective
Derived forms
congreˌgative (ˈcongreˌgative) adjectivecongreˌgativeness (ˈcongreˌgativeness) nouncongreˌgator (ˈcongreˌgator) noun
Word origin
C15: from Latin congregāre to collect into a flock, from grex flock

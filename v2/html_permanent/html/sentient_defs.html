sentient

(ˈsɛntɪənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having the power of sense perception or sensation; consciousnoun2. rare a sentient person or thing

Derived Forms

sentiently (ˈsentiently) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin sentiēns feeling, from sentīre to perceive

unacknowledged for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnæknɒlɪdʒd )
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something or someone as unacknowledged, you mean that people ignore their existence or presence, or are not aware of it. ⇒ Unresolved or unacknowledged fears can trigger sleepwalking.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something or someone as unacknowledged, you mean that their existence or importance is not recognized officially or publicly. ⇒ This tradition goes totally unacknowledged in official guidebooks. ⇒ Johnny and Guy are the greatest unacknowledged pop songwriters in Britain.

herpes

(ˈhɜːpiːz )
Definitions
nounany of several inflammatory diseases of the skin, esp herpes simplex, characterized by the formation of small watery blisters. See also genital herpes
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: via Latin from Greek: a creeping, from herpein to creep

hereabouts

Powered by COBUILD
(hɪərəbaʊts ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [ADV after v, n ADV]You use hereabouts to indicate that you are talking about something near you or in the same area as you. ⇒ It's a bit chilly and empty hereabouts. ⇒ The mountains hereabouts reach heights of over 2000 metres.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

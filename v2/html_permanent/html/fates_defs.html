Fates

(feɪts )
Definitions
plural noun1. Greek mythology the three goddesses who control the destinies of the lives of man, which are likened to skeins of thread that they spin, measure out, and at last cut. See Atropos, Clotho, Lachesis2. Norse mythology the Norns. See Norn1

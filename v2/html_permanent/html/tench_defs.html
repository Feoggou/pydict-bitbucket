tench

(tɛntʃ )
Definitions
nouna European freshwater cyprinid game fish, Tinca tinca, having a thickset dark greenish body with a barbel at each side of the mouth
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French tenche, from Late Latin tinca

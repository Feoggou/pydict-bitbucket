nonentity

(ˌnɒnˈɛntɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. an insignificant person or thing2. a nonexistent thing3. the state of not existing; nonexistence
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

(nɒnentɪti )

Definitions
countable nounIf you refer to someone as a nonentity, you mean that they are not special or important in any way. [disapproval] ⇒ Amidst the current bunch of nonentities, he is a towering figure. ⇒ She was written off then as a political nonentity.

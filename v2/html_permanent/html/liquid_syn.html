Synonyms of liquid

liquid

noun

  1. fluid, solution, juice, liquor, sap
    • Drink plenty of liquid.

adjective

  1. fluid, running, flowing, wet, melted, thawed, watery, molten, runny, liquefied, aqueous
    • Wash in warm water with liquid detergent.
  2. clear, bright, brilliant, shining, transparent, translucent, limpid
    • a mosaic of liquid cobalts and greens
  3. smooth, clear, soft, flowing, sweet, pure, melting, fluent, melodious, mellifluous, dulcet, mellifluent
    • He had a deep liquid voice.
  4. assets convertible, disposable, negotiable, realizable
    • The bank had sufficient liquid assets to continue operating.

indiscreet

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndɪskriːt ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as indiscreet, you mean that they do or say things in public which they should only do or say secretly or in private. [+ about] ⇒ He is notoriously indiscreet about his private life. ⇒ Had he been indiscreet in what he had said? ⇒ ...the duchess's indiscreet behaviour.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Psychiatric means relating to psychiatry. ⇒ We finally insisted that he seek psychiatric help.2. adjective [ADJ n]Psychiatric means involving mental illness. ⇒ About 4% of the prison population have chronic psychiatric illnesses.

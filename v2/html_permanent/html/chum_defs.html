chum1

(tʃʌm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. informal a close friendverbWord forms: chums, chumming or chummed2. (intransitive; usually foll by up with) to be or become an intimate friend (of)3. (transitive) Scottish to accompany ⇒ I'll chum you home
Word origin
C17 (meaning: a person sharing rooms with another): probably shortened from chamber fellow, originally student slang (Oxford); compare crony

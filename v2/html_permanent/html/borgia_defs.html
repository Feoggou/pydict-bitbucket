Borgia

(Italian ˈbordʒa)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Cesare (ˈtʃezare), son of Rodrigo Borgia (Pope Alexander VI). 1475–1507, Italian cardinal, politician, and military leader; model for Machiavelli's The Prince2.  his sister, Lucrezia (luˈkrɛttsja), daughter of Rodrigo Borgia. 1480–1519, Italian noblewoman. After her third marriage (1501), to the Duke of Ferrara, she became a patron of the arts and science3.  Rodrigo (rodˈriɡo). See Alexander VI

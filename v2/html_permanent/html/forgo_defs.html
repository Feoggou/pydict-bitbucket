forgo

or forego (fɔːˈɡəʊ )
Definitions
verb -goes, -going, -went or -gone (transitive)1. to give up or do without2. archaic to leave
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

forgoer (forˈgoer) or foregoer (foreˈgoer) noun

Word Origin

Old English forgān; see for-, go1

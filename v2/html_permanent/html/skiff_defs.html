skiff

(skɪf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany of various small boats propelled by oars, sail, or motor

Word Origin

C18: from French esquif, from Old Italian schifo a boat, of Germanic origin; related to Old High German schif ship

countable nounA skiff is a small light rowing boat or sailing boat, which usually has room for only one person.

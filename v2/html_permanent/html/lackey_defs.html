lackey

(ˈlækɪ )
Definitions
noun1. a servile follower; hanger-on2. a liveried male servant or valet3. a person who is treated like a servantverb4. (when intr, often foll by for) to act as a lackey (to)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: via French laquais, from Old French, perhaps from Catalan lacayo, alacayo; perhaps related to alcalde

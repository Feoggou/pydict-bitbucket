failed

(feɪld)
Definitions
adjective1. unsuccessful; abortive ⇒ a failed attempt to smuggle a shipment of weapons into the country, ⇒ She was accused of involvement with Venezuela's failed coup., ⇒ After a failed military offensive, all government troops and police were withdrawn from the island., ⇒ The truth is, I'm a failed comedy writer really., ⇒ a failed hotel business2. that has broken down ⇒ He had four failed marriages behind him, ⇒ the high rate of failed relationships among celebrities

Polynesia

(ˌpɒlɪˈniːʒə , -ʒɪə)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounone of the three divisions of islands in the Pacific, the others being Melanesia and Micronesia: includes Samoa, Society, Marquesas, Mangareva, Tuamotu, Cook, and Tubuai Islands, and Tonga

Word Origin

C18: via French from poly- + Greek nēsos island

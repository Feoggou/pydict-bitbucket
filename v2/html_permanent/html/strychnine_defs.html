strychnine

(ˈstrɪkniːn )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna white crystalline very poisonous alkaloid, obtained from the plant nux vomica: formerly used in small quantities as a stimulant of the central nervous system and the appetite. Formula: C21H22O2N2. Also called: strychnia

Word Origin

C19: via French from New Latin Strychnos, from Greek strukhnos nightshade

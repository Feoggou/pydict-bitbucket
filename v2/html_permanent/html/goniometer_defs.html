goniometer

(ˌɡəʊnɪˈɒmɪtə )
Definitions
noun1. an instrument for measuring the angles between the faces of a crystal2. an instrument consisting of a transformer circuit connected to two directional aerials, used to determine the bearing of a distant radio station
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

goniometric (ˌɡəʊnɪəˈmɛtrɪk ) or goniometrical (ˌgonioˈmetrical) adjectivegoniometrically (ˌgonioˈmetrically) adverbgoniometry (ˌgoniˈometry) noun

Word Origin

C18: via French from Greek gōnia angle

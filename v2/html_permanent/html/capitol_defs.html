Capitol

(ˈkæpɪtəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. another name for the Capitolineb. the temple on the Capitoline2. See the Capitol3. Also called: statehouse (sometimes not capital) (in the US) the building housing any state legislature
Word origin
C14: from Latin Capitōlium, from caput head

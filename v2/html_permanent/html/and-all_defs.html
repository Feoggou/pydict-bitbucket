Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseYou use and all when you want to emphasize that what you are talking about includes the thing mentioned, especially when this is surprising or unusual. [emphasis] ⇒ He dropped his sausage on the pavement and someone's dog ate it, mustard and all. →  all

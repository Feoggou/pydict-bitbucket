Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Circumstantial evidence is evidence that makes it seem likely that something happened, but does not prove it. [formal] ⇒ Fast work by the police in Birmingham had started producing circumstantial evidence.2. adjectiveSomething that is circumstantial is related to a particular circumstance. [formal] ⇒ The reasons for the project collapsing were circumstantial.

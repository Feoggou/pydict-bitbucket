congruous

(ˈkɒŋɡrʊəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. corresponding or agreeing2. suitable; appropriate
Derived forms
congruity (kənˈɡruːɪtɪ ) or congruousness (ˈcongruousness) nouncongruously (ˈcongruously) adverb
Word origin
C16: from Latin congruus suitable, harmonious; see congruent

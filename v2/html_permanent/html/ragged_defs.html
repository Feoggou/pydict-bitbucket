ragged

(ˈræɡɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. (of clothes) worn to rags; tattered2. (of a person) dressed in shabby tattered clothes3. having a neglected or unkempt appearance ⇒ ragged weeds4. having a loose, rough, or uneven surface or edge; jagged5. uneven or irregular ⇒ a ragged beat, ⇒ a ragged shout
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

raggedly (ˈraggedly) adverbraggedness (ˈraggedness) noun

Word Origin

C13: probably from ragge rag1

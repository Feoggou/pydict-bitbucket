cochineal

(ˌkɒtʃɪˈniːl , ˈkɒtʃɪˌniːl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: cochineal insect a Mexican homopterous insect, Dactylopius coccus, that feeds on cacti2. a crimson substance obtained from the crushed bodies of these insects, used for colouring food and for dyeing3. a. the colour of this dyeb. (as adjective) ⇒ cochineal shoes
Word origin
C16: from Old Spanish cochinilla, from Latin coccineus scarlet-coloured, from coccum cochineal kermes, from Greek kokkos kermes berry

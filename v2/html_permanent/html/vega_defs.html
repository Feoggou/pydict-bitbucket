Vega1

(ˈviːɡə )
Definitions
nounthe brightest star in the constellation Lyra and one of the most conspicuous in the N hemisphere. It is part of an optical double star having a faint companion. Distance: 25.3 light years; spectral type: A0V
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Medieval Latin, from Arabic (al nasr) al wāqi, literally: the falling (vulture), that is, the constellation Lyra

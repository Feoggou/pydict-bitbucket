larboard

(ˈlɑrbərd ; ˈlɑrˌbɔrd ) in American English
Definitions
noun1.  the left-hand side of a ship as one faces forward; port adjective2.  on or of this side ▶ see also port4
Webster’s New World College Dictionary, Fourth Edition, compiled by the editors of Webster’s New World Dictionaries. Copyright © 2010 by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. Published by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. All rights reserved.

Word Origin

ME laddeborde, orig. lading side < OE hladan, to lade + bord, side: sp. infl. by starboard

larboard

(ˈlɑːbəd )
Definitions
noun, adjectivenautical a former word for port2
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14 laddeborde (changed to larboard by association with starboard), from laden to load + borde board

fay1

(feɪ )
Definitions
noun1. a fairy or spriteadjective2. of or resembling a fay3. informal pretentious or precious
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French feie, ultimately from Latin fātum fate

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]An ailing organization or society is in difficulty and is becoming weaker. OPPOSITE: thriving ⇒ The rise in overseas sales is good news for the ailing American economy.2. adjectiveIf someone is ailing, they are ill and are not getting better. [old-fashioned] ⇒ He is said to be ailing at his home in Washington.

emergent

(ɪˈmɜːdʒənt )
Definitions
adjective1. coming into being or notice ⇒ an emergent political structure2. (of a nation) recently independentnoun3. an aquatic plant with stem and leaves above the water
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

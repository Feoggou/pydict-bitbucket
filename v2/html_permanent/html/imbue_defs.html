imbue

(ɪmˈbjuː )
Definitions
verb -bues, -buing or -bued (transitive; usually foll by with)1. to instil or inspire (with ideals, principles, etc) ⇒ his sermons were imbued with the spirit of the Reformation2. rare to soak, esp with moisture, dye, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

imbuement (imˈbuement) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin imbuere to stain, accustom

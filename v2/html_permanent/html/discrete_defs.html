discrete

(dɪsˈkriːt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. separate or distinct in form or concept2. consisting of distinct or separate parts3. statisticsa. (of a variable) having consecutive values that are not infinitesimally close, so that its analysis requires summation rather than integrationb. (of a distribution) relating to a discrete variable. Compare continuous (sense 4)Avoid confusion with discreet
Derived forms
discretely (disˈcretely) adverbdiscreteness (disˈcreteness) noun
Word origin
C14: from Latin discrētus separated, set apart; see discreet

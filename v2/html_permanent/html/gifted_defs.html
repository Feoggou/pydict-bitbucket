Definitions
adjectivehaving or showing natural talent or aptitude ⇒ a gifted musician, ⇒ a gifted performance

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is gifted has a natural ability to do something well. talented ⇒ ...one of the most gifted players in the world. ⇒ He was witty, amusing and gifted with a sharp business brain.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA gifted child is much more intelligent or talented than average. ⇒ ...a state program for gifted children.

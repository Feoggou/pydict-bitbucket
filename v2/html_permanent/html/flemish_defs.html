Flemish

(ˈflɛmɪʃ )
Definitions
noun1. one of the two official languages of Belgium, almost identical in form with Dutch2. See the Flemishadjective3. of, relating to, or characteristic of Flanders, the Flemings, or their language

Flemish

Powered by COBUILD
(flemɪʃ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveFlemish means belonging or relating to the region of Flanders in northern Europe, or to its people, language, or culture. ⇒ ...a splendid collection of Dutch, French and Flemish art. ⇒ ...this picturesque Flemish town.2. uncount nounFlemish is a language spoken in Belgium.

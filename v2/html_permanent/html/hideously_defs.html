hideously

Powered by COBUILD
(hɪdiəsli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adverb [usu ADV adj/-ed, oft ADV after v]You use hideously to emphasize that something is very ugly or unattractive. [emphasis] ⇒ Everything is hideously ugly. ⇒ He has been left hideously disfigured by plastic surgery.2. graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj/-ed]You can use hideously to emphasize that something is very unpleasant or unacceptable. [emphasis] horrendously ⇒ ...a hideously complex program. ⇒ ...a simple but undoubtedly hideously expensive black suit.

spray-on

(ˈspreɪɒn)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. applied by means of an aerosol spray ⇒ He'd had a spray-on tan and professional teeth-whitening.2. informal (of clothing) so tight on the body that it gives the appearance of having been sprayed on ⇒ a petite blonde woman in a spray-on red dress, ⇒ spray-on jeans
Example sentences containing 'spray-on'
It was pretty hard not to notice Angie, given the spray-on white dress she was wearing. Adam, Paul A NASTY DOSE OF DEATHHolly and Kenny followed her gaze to where a crowd of people had parted, revealing a petite blonde woman in a spray-on red dress. Cathy Kelly JUST BETWEEN US

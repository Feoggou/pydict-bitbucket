inveigh

(ɪnˈveɪ )
Definitions
verb(intransitive; foll by against) to speak with violent or invective language; rail
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inveigher (inˈveigher) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin invehī, literally: to be carried in, hence, assail physically or verbally, from in-2 + vehī to be carried, ride

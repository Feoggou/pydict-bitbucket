unsaturated

(ʌnˈsætʃəˌreɪtɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. not saturated2. (of a chemical compound, esp an organic compound) containing one or more double or triple bonds and thus capable of undergoing addition reactions3. (of a fat, esp a vegetable fat) containing a high proportion of fatty acids having double bonds4. (of a solution) containing less solute than a saturated solution

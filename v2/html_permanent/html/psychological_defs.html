psychological

(ˌsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to psychology2. of or relating to the mind or mental activity3. having no real or objective basis; arising in the mind ⇒ his backaches are all psychological4. affecting the mind

Derived Forms

ˌpsychologically (ˌpsychoˈlogically) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Psychological means concerned with a person's mind and thoughts. ⇒ John received constant physical and psychological abuse from his father. ⇒ Robyn's loss of memory is a psychological problem, rather than a physical one.psychologically (saɪkəlɒdʒɪkli ) adverb [ADV adj/adv] ⇒ It was very important psychologically for us to succeed. ⇒ ...a psychologically disturbed person.2. adjective [ADJ n]Psychological means relating to psychology. ⇒ ...psychological testing.

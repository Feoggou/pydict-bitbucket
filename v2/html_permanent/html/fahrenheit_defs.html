Fahrenheit

Powered by COBUILD
(færənhaɪt ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveFahrenheit is a scale for measuring temperature, in which water freezes at 32 degrees and boils at 212 degrees. It is represented by the symbol °F. ⇒ By mid-morning, the temperature was already above 100 degrees Fahrenheit.Fahrenheit is also a noun. ⇒ He was asked for the boiling point of water in Fahrenheit. → temperature

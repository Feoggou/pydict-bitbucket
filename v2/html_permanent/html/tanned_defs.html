tanned

(tænd)
Definitions
adjectiveBritish (of skin or person) brown or bronzed after exposure to the sun or UV rays ⇒ her long tanned legs, ⇒ He was tanned and muscular from working outdoors in the sun., ⇒ the lightly tanned skin of his face, ⇒ You're looking quite tanned.

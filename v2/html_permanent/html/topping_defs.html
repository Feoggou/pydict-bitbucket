topping

(ˈtɒpɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. something that tops something else, esp a sauce or garnish for food2. angling part of a brightly-coloured feather, usually from a golden pheasant crest, used to top some artificial fliesadjective3. high or superior in rank, degree, etc4. British slang excellent; splendid

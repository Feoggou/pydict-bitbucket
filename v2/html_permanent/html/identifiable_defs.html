identifiable

Powered by COBUILD
(aɪdentɪfaɪəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething or someone that is identifiable can be recognized. OPPOSITE: unidentifiable [+ as] ⇒ In the corridor were four dirty, ragged bundles, just identifiable as human beings. ⇒ Stan Dean, easily identifiable by his oddly-shaped hat, sat in a doorway. ⇒ ...if there is a clearly identifiable cause of stress in your life and there is nothing you can do to get rid of it. [Also + by/from]

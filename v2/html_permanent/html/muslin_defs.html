muslin

(ˈmʌzlɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna fine plain-weave cotton fabric

Word Origin

C17: from French mousseline, from Italian mussolina, from Arabic mawşilīy of Mosul, from Mawşil Mosul, Iraq, where it was first produced

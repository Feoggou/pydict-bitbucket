outspread

Definitions
verb (ˌaʊtˈsprɛd ) -spreads, -spreading or -spread1. to spread out or cause to spread outadjective (ˈaʊtˈsprɛd )2. spread or stretched out3. scattered or diffused widelynoun (ˈaʊtˌsprɛd )4. a spreading out

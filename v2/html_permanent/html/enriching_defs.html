enriching

(ɪnˈrɪtʃɪŋ)
Definitions
adjectivegiving increased enjoyment or appreciation ⇒ I have such interesting, enriching experiences in my work., ⇒ Learning and teaching are enriching experiences for both the student and the teacher.

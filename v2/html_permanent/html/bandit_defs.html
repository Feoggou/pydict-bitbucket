bandit

(ˈbændɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -dits or -ditti (-ˈdɪtɪ)a robber, esp a member of an armed gang; brigand
Derived forms
banditry (ˈbanditry) noun
Word origin
C16: from Italian bandito, literally: banished man, from bandire to proscribe, from bando edict, ban1

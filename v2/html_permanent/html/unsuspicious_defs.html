unsuspicious

(ˌʌnsəsˈpɪʃəs)
Definitions
adjective1. not feeling suspicion ⇒ People were unsuspicious of her comings and goings with Christopher.2. not arousing suspicion ⇒ 'And you're right, it's entirely unsuspicious,' she agreed., ⇒ an unsuspicious-looking man

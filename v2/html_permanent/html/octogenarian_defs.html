octogenarian

(ˌɒktəʊdʒɪˈnɛərɪən ) or less commonly octogenary (ɒkˈtɒdʒɪnərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -narians or -naries1. a person who is from 80 to 89 years oldadjective2. of or relating to an octogenarian
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from Latin octōgēnārius containing eighty, from octōgēnī eighty each

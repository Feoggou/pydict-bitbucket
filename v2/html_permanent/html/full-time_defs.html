full-time

Powered by COBUILD
also full time for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Full-time work or study involves working or studying for the whole of each normal working week rather than for part of it. ⇒ ...a full-time job. ⇒ ...full-time staff.Full-time is also an adverb. ⇒ Deirdre works full-time.2. →  a full-time job3. uncount nounIn games such as football, full-time is the end of a match. [British] ⇒ The score at full-time was Arsenal 1, Sampdoria 1.

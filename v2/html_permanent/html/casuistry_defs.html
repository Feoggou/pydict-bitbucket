casuistry

(ˈkæzjʊɪstrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ries1. philosophy the resolution of particular moral dilemmas, esp those arising from conflicting general moral rules, by careful distinction of the cases to which these rules apply2. reasoning that is specious, misleading, or oversubtle

overcrowded

(ˌəʊvəˈkraʊdɪd)
Definitions
adjective(of a room, vehicle, city, etc) filled with more people or things than is desirable ⇒ one of the most overcrowded prisons in the country, ⇒ a windswept, overcrowded, unattractive beach, ⇒ Morocco's capital, until independence, was badly overcrowded.

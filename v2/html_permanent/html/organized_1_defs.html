organized

or organised (ˈɔːɡəˌnaɪzd )
Definitions
adjective1. planned and controlled on a large scale and involving many people ⇒ organized crime2. orderly and efficient ⇒ a highly organized campaign3. (of the workers in a factory or office) belonging to a trade union ⇒ organized labour

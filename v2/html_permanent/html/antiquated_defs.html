Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as antiquated, you are criticizing it because it is very old or old-fashioned. [disapproval] outmoded ⇒ Many factories are so antiquated they are not worth saving. ⇒ Do we really want a return to an antiquated system of privilege and elitism?
Example sentences containing 'antiquated'
A plain cell that boasted only a desk, a chair, and an antiquated stereo. Clive Barker THE GREAT AND SECRET SHOWA short jolt, no more; but enough to set the antiquated chandelier that hung in the turret gently swaying and tinkling again.

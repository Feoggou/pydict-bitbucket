Syrian

(ˈsɪrɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Syria, its people, or their dialect of Arabic2. Eastern Churches of or relating to Christians who belong to churches with Syriac liturgiesnoun3. a native or inhabitant of Syria4. Eastern Churches a Syrian Christian

(sɪriən )
Word forms: Syrians
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveSyrian means belonging or relating to Syria, or to its people or culture. ⇒ ...the Syrian capital of Damascus. ⇒ ...a senior Syrian diplomat.2. countable nounA Syrian is a Syrian citizen, or a person of Syrian origin. ⇒ The crew-members were Syrians.

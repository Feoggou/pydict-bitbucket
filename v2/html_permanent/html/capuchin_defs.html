capuchin

(ˈkæpjʊtʃɪn , -ʃɪn)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any agile intelligent New World monkey of the genus Cebus, inhabiting forests in South America, typically having a cowl of thick hair on the top of the head2. a woman's hooded cloak3. (sometimes capital) a rare variety of domestic fancy pigeon
Word origin
C16: from French, from Italian cappuccino, from cappuccio hood; see capuche

Capuchin

(ˈkæpjʊtʃɪn , ˈkæpjʊʃɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna. a friar belonging to a strict and autonomous branch of the Franciscan order founded in 1525b. (as modifier) ⇒ a Capuchin friar
Word origin
C16: from French; see capuche

retailing

(ˈriːteɪlɪŋ)
Definitions
nouna. the act of selling goods to consumers ⇒ She spent fourteen years in retailing.b. (as modifier) ⇒ the car retailing industry

retailing

Powered by COBUILD
(riːteɪlɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [oft N n]Retailing is the activity of selling goods direct to the public, usually in small quantities. Compare wholesaling. [business] ⇒ She spent fourteen years in retailing. ⇒ ...the car retailing industry.

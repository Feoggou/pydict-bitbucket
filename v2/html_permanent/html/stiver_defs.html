stiver

(ˈstaɪvə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a former Dutch coin worth one twentieth of a guilder2. a small amount, esp of money

Word Origin

C16: from Dutch stuiver; related to Middle Low German stüver, Danish styver

maid

(meɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. archaic or literary a young unmarried girl; maiden2. a. a female servantb. (in combination) ⇒ a housemaid3. a spinster

Derived Forms

maidish (ˈmaidish) adjectivemaidishness (ˈmaidishness) noun

Word Origin

C12: shortened form of maiden

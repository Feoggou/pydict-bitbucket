congested

(kənˈdʒɛstɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. crowded to excess; overfull2. (of an organ or part) loaded or clogged with blood3. (of the nose) blocked with mucus

congested

(kəndʒestɪd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA congested road or area is extremely crowded and blocked with traffic or people. ⇒ He first promised two weeks ago to clear Britain's congested roads. [+ with] ⇒ Some areas are congested with both cars and people.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf a part of the body is congested, it is blocked. [formal] ⇒ The arteries in his neck had become fatally congested.

Definitions
noun(Accounting: Tax) A tax return is an official form that you fill in with details about your income so that the tax that you owe can be calculated. ⇒ Each shareholder reports his proportionate share of the loss on his tax return. ⇒ People complete an annual tax return which shows all receipts and all savings. ⇒ A tax return is an official form that you fill in with details about your income so that the tax that you owe can be calculated. ▶  tax

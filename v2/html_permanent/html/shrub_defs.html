shrub1

(ʃrʌb )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna woody perennial plant, smaller than a tree, with several major branches arising from near the base of the main stem

Derived Forms

shrubˌlike (ˈshrubˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

Old English scrybb; related to Middle Low German schrubben coarse, uneven, Old Swedish skrubba to scrub1

shrub2

(ʃrʌb )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a mixed drink of rum, fruit juice, sugar, and spice2. mixed fruit juice, sugar, and spice made commercially to be mixed with rum or other spirits

Word Origin

C18: from Arabic sharāb, variant of shurb drink; see sherbet

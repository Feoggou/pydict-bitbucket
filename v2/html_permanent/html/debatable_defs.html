or debateable (dɪˈbeɪtəbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. open to question; disputable2. law in dispute, as land or territory to which two parties lay claim

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you say that something is debatable, you mean that it is not certain. arguable ⇒ Whether we can stay in this situation is debatable. ⇒ It is debatable whether or not antibiotics would make any difference. ⇒ Whether older women miscarry more often or whether they report more miscarriages is a debatable point.
Example sentences containing 'debatable'
Just why Mr. Frodo was selling his beautiful hole was even more debatable than the price. J.R.R. Tolkien THE LORD OF THE RINGSFather Guillermo is less perceptive, and might deem your true repentance debatable. Colleen McCullough THE THORN BIRDS

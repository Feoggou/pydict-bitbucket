skive

verb

  1. (British informal) slack, idle, shirk, dodge, skulk, malinger, swing the lead, gold-brick (US, slang), bob off (British, slang), bludge (Australian & & New Zealand, informal), scrimshank (British, military, slang) • The company treated me as though I were skiving.

silvery

(ˈsɪlvərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or having the appearance of silver ⇒ the silvery moon2. containing or covered with silver3. having a clear ringing sound

Derived Forms

silveriness (ˈsilveriness) noun

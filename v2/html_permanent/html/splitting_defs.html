splitting

(ˈsplɪtɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of a headache) intolerably painful; acute2. (of the head) assailed by an overpowering unbearable painnoun3. psychoanalysis the Freudian defence mechanism in which an object or idea (or, alternatively, the ego) is separated into two or more parts in order to remove its threatening meaning

harmonics

(hɑːˈmɒnɪks )
Definitions
noun1. (functioning as singular) the science of musical sounds and their acoustic properties2. (functioning as plural) the overtones of a fundamental note, as produced by lightly touching the string of a stringed instrument at one of its node points while playing. See harmonic (sense 6)

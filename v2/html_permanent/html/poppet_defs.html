poppet

(ˈpɒpɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a term of affection for a small child or sweetheart2. Also called: poppet valve a mushroom-shaped valve that is lifted from its seating against a spring by applying an axial force to its stem: commonly used as an exhaust or inlet valve in an internal-combustion engine3. nautical a temporary supporting brace for a vessel hauled on land or in a dry dock

Word Origin

C14: early variant of puppet

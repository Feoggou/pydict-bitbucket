cockpit

(ˈkɒkˌpɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the compartment in a small aircraft in which the pilot, crew, and sometimes the passengers sit. Compare flight deck (sense 1)2. the driver's compartment in a racing car3. nauticala. an enclosed or recessed area towards the stern of a small vessel from which it is steeredb. (formerly) an apartment in a warship used as quarters for junior officers and as a first-aid station during combat4. the site of numerous battles or campaigns5. an enclosure used for cockfights

death sentence

(dɛθ ˈsɛntəns)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a sentence of capital punishment ⇒ His original death sentence was commuted to life in prison., ⇒ India's Supreme Court confirmed death sentences on four people.2. something that leads inevitably to death ⇒ Multiple myeloma is not an inevitable death sentence., ⇒ Breast cancer used to be regarded as a death sentence.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA death sentence is a punishment of death given by a judge to someone who has been found guilty of a serious crime such as murder. ⇒ His original death sentence was commuted to life in prison.

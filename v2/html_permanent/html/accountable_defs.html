Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are accountable to someone for something that you do, you are responsible for it and must be prepared to justify your actions to that person. answerable [+ for] ⇒ Public officials can finally be held accountable for their actions. ⇒ The major service industries should be accountable to their customers. [Also + to]accountability (əkaʊntəbɪlɪti ) uncount noun ⇒ ...an impetus towards democracy and greater accountability.

Saunders

(ˈsɔːndəz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Dame Cicely. 1918–2005, British philanthropist: founded St Christopher's Hospice in 1967 for the care of the terminally ill, upon which the modern hospice movement is modelled. Her books include Living with Dying (1983)

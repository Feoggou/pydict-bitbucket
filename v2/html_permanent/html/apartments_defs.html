apartments

(əˈpɑːtmənts)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nouna suite of rooms in a grand residence such as a palace distinguished from any public rooms and designated for the use of a particular person or group ⇒ the private apartments of the Prince of Wales in St James's Palace

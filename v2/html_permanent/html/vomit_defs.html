vomit

(ˈvɒmɪt )
Definitions
verb -its, -iting or -ited1. to eject (the contents of the stomach) through the mouth as the result of involuntary muscular spasms of the stomach and oesophagus2. to eject or be ejected forcefully; spew forthnoun3. the matter ejected in vomiting4. the act of vomiting5. a drug or agent that induces vomiting; emetic
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vomiter (ˈvomiter) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Latin vomitāre to vomit repeatedly, from vomere to vomit

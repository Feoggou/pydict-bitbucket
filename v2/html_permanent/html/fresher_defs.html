Powered by COBUILD
(freʃəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: freshers
Definitions
1. Fresher is the comparative form of fresh.2. countable noun [usu pl]Freshers are students who have just started their first year at university or college. [British, informal]regional note:   in AM, use freshmen

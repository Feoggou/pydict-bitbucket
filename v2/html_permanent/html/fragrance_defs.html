fragrance

(ˈfreɪɡrəns ) or fragrancy
Definitions
noun plural -grances or -grancies1. a pleasant or sweet odour; scent; perfume2. the state of being fragrant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Powered by COBUILD
(freɪgrəns ) for learners of English
Word forms: fragrances
Definitions
1. variable nounA fragrance is a pleasant or sweet smell. ⇒ ...a shrubby plant with a strong characteristic fragrance. [+ of] ⇒ ...the fragrance of his cologne.2. variable nounFragrance is a pleasant-smelling liquid which people put on their bodies to make themselves smell nice. ⇒ The advertisement is for a male fragrance.

chalet

(ˈʃæleɪ , French ʃalɛ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a type of wooden house of Swiss origin, typically low, with wide projecting eaves2. a similar house used esp as a ski lodge, garden house, etc
Word origin
C19: from French (Swiss dialect)

uncanny

(ʌnˈkænɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. characterized by apparently supernatural wonder, horror, etc2. beyond what is normal or expected ⇒ an uncanny accuracy
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

uncannily (unˈcannily) adverbuncanniness (unˈcanniness) noun

uncanny for learners of English

(ʌnkæni )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as uncanny, you mean that it is strange and difficult to explain. ⇒ The hero, Danny, bears an uncanny resemblance to Kirk Douglas. ⇒ I had this uncanny feeling that Alice was warning me.uncannily (ʌnkænɪli ) graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj/adv] ⇒ They have uncannily similar voices. ⇒ It fits Orwell's guidelines almost uncannily.

shaggy

(ˈʃæɡɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective -gier or -giest1. having or covered with rough unkempt fur, hair, wool, etc ⇒ a shaggy dog2. rough or unkempt3. (in textiles) having a nap of long rough strands

Derived Forms

shaggily (ˈshaggily) adverbshagginess (ˈshagginess) noun

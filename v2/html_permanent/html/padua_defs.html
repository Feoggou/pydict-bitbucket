(ˈpædʒʊə , ˈpædjʊə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna city in NE Italy, in Veneto: important in Roman and Renaissance times; university (1222); botanical garden (1545). Pop: 204 870 (2001). Latin name: Patavium (pəˈteɪvɪəm ). Italian name: Padova

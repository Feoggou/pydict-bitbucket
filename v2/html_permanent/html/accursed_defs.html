accursed

(əˈkɜːsɪd , əˈkɜːst ) or accurst (əˈkɜːst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. under or subject to a curse; doomed2. (prenominal) hateful; detestable; execrable
Derived forms
accursedly (əˈkɜːsɪdlɪ ) adverbacˈcursedness noun
Word origin
Old English ācursod, past participle of ācursian to put under a curse

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

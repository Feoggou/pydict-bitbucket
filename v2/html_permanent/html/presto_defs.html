presto

(ˈprɛstəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective, adverb1. music to be played very fastadverb2. immediately, suddenly, or at once (esp in the phrase hey presto)noun plural -tos3. music a movement or passage directed to be played very quickly

Word Origin

C16: from Italian: fast, from Late Latin praestus (adj) ready to hand, Latin praestō (adv) present

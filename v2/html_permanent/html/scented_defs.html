scented

Powered by COBUILD
(sentɪd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveScented things have a pleasant smell, either naturally or because perfume has been added to them. ⇒ The white flowers are pleasantly scented. ⇒ ...scented body lotion.
Example sentences containing 'scented'
There was nothing here to remind her of the languid, scented warmth of summertime. Thomas, Rosie THE WHITE DOVEThe texture of her clothes was warm and soft, faintly scented. Malcolm, John WHISTLER IN THE DARKHe leaned back in his chair, a rose scented kerchief draped over his eyes. Robin Hobb THE GOLDEN FOOL: BOOK TWO OF THE TAWNY MAN

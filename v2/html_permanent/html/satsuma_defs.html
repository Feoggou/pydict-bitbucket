satsuma

(sætˈsuːmə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small citrus tree, Citrus nobilis var. unshiu, cultivated, esp in Japan, for its edible fruit2. the fruit of this tree, which has a loose rind and easily separable segments
Word origin
C19: originally from the province of Satsuma, Japan

boiled

(bɔɪld)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. that has been brought to boiling point ⇒ drink bottled or boiled water2. cooked by boiling in water ⇒ roast or boiled meat with greens, ⇒ broccoli and curly kale, lightly steamed or boiled

Definitions
COBUILD Key Words for Hospitality
adjective(Hospitality (hotel): Food and drink, cooking) Boiled food is food cooked in boiling water. ⇒ The lamb is served with boiled potatoes and vegetables. ⇒ Boiled lobsters are prepared by dropping them in boiling water when they are alive. ⇒ You can have boiled vegetables, cooked in water, or steamed vegetables, cooked over water.

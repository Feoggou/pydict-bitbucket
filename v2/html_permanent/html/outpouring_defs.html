outpouring

(ˈaʊtˌpɔːrɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. a passionate or exaggerated outburst; effusion2. another word for outpour (sense 1), outpour (sense 2)

outpouring for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(aʊtpɔːrɪŋ )
Word forms: outpourings
Definitions
countable noun [usu sing]An outpouring of something such as an emotion or a reaction is the expression of it in an uncontrolled way. [+ of] ⇒ The news of his death produced an instant outpouring of grief. ⇒ ...an outpouring of anti-Western feeling.

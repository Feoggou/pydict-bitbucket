Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ, ADJ to-inf, ADJ that/how]If you are surprised at something, you have a feeling of surprise, because it is unexpected or unusual. [+ at/by] ⇒ This lady was genuinely surprised at what happened to her pet. ⇒ Chang seemed surprised to find the big living-room empty. ⇒ Reading the ideas of the so-called experts, I am not surprised the country is in such a state.2. → see also surprise

He had been surprised to learn that most of Damin's Raiders spoke several languages.

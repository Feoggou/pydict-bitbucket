unwashed for learners of English

(ʌnwɒʃt )
Definitions
1. adjectiveUnwashed people or objects are dirty and need to be washed. ⇒ They looked pale and unhealthy, with unwashed hair and sunken cheeks. ⇒ Leftover food and unwashed dishes cover the dirty counters.2. →  the (great) unwashed

riser

(ˈraɪzə )
Definitions
noun1. a person who rises, esp from bed ⇒ an early riser2. the vertical part of a stair or step3. a vertical pipe, esp one within a building

Definitions

riser

(raɪzər) in the Oil and Gas Industry
Word forms: (regular plural) risers
Definitions
noun(Extractive engineering: Field development, Drilling) A riser is a large steel pipe which is connected to the seabed and used as a conduit for the drill string. ⇒ The riser provides a continuous pathway for the drill string and fluids emanating from the wellbore. ⇒ A riser is used on floating offshore drilling rigs to establish a seal between the top of the wellbore, which is on the ocean floor, and the drilling equipment, located above the surface of the water. ⇒ A riser is a large steel pipe which is connected to the seabed and used as a conduit for the drill string.

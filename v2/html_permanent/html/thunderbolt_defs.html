thunderbolt

(ˈθʌndəˌbəʊlt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a flash of lightning accompanying thunder2. the imagined agency of destruction produced by a flash of lightning3. (in mythology) the destructive weapon wielded by several gods, esp the Greek god Zeus. See also Thor4. something very startling

unprotected for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnprətektɪd )
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n, v-link ADJ, ADJ after v]An unprotected person or place is not looked after or defended, and so they may be harmed or attacked. ⇒ What better target than an unprotected girl, going along that river walkway in the dark? ⇒ The landing beaches would be unprotected.2. adjective [ADJ n, v-link ADJ, ADJ after v]If something is unprotected, it is not covered or treated with anything, and so it may easily be damaged. ⇒ Exposure of unprotected skin to the sun carries the risk of developing skin cancer. ⇒ This leaves fertile soil unprotected and prone to erosion.3. adjective [ADJ n]If two people have unprotected sex, they do not use a condom when they have sex.

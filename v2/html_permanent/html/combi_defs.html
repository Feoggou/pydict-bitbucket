combi

(ˈkɒmbɪ) informal
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. denoting a machine that has two or more uses or functions ⇒ a combi oven, ⇒ a combi fridge/freezer, ⇒ a combi boilernoun2. a vehicle or aircraft that has two or more possible uses, for example to carry passengers or to transport goods
Example sentences containing 'combi'
He knew her emotions like a thief knows his business with safes; he had studied and learned the combi nation to every one of them. McCorquodale, Robin DANSVILLE

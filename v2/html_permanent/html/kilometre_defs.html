kilometre

or US kilometer (kɪˈlɒmɪtə , ˈkɪləˌmiːtə )
Definitions
nounone thousand metres, equal to 0.621371 miles. Symbol: km
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

kilometric (ˌkɪləʊˈmɛtrɪk ) or kilometrical (ˌkiloˈmetrical) adjective

Definitions
regional note:   in AM, use kilometercountable noun [num N]A kilometre is a metric unit of distance or length. One kilometre is a thousand metres and is equal to 0.62 miles. [+ from] ⇒ ...about twenty kilometres from the border. [+ of] ⇒ The fire destroyed some 40,000 square kilometres of forest.

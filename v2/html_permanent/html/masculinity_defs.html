(mæskjʊlɪnɪti )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounA man's masculinity is the fact that he is a man. ⇒ ...a project on the link between masculinity and violence.2. uncount nounMasculinity means the qualities, especially sexual qualities, which are considered to be typical of men. ⇒ The old ideas of masculinity do not work for most men. ⇒ ...being unable to prove his masculinity to society by getting his partner pregnant.

Synonyms of mercy

mercy

noun

  1. compassion, charity, pity, forgiveness, quarter, favour, grace, kindness, clemency, leniency, benevolence, forbearance
    • Neither side showed its prisoners any mercy.
    OPPOSITES:  cruelty, brutality, severity, harshness, inhumanity, pitilessness
  2. blessing, relief, boon, godsend, piece of luck, benison (archaic)
    • It was a mercy he'd gone so quickly in the end.

QUOTATION:
Yet I shall temper soJustice with mercyJohn Milton – Paradise Lost

genre

(ˈʒɑːnrə )
Definitions
noun1. a. kind, category, or sort, esp of literary or artistic workb. (as modifier) ⇒ genre fiction2. a. a category of painting in which domestic scenes or incidents from everyday life are depictedb. as modifier ⇒ genre painting
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from French, from Old French gendre; see gender

Definitions
countable nounA genre is a particular type of literature, painting, music, film, or other art form which people consider as a class because it has special characteristics. [formal] ⇒ ...his love of films and novels in the horror genre.

kiosk

(ˈkiːɒsk )
Definitions
noun1. a small sometimes movable booth from which cigarettes, newspapers, light refreshments, etc, are sold2. mainly British a telephone box3. mainly US a thick post on which advertisements are posted4. (in Turkey, Iran, etc, esp formerly) a light open-sided pavilion
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from French kiosque bandstand, from Turkish kösk, from Persian kūshk pavilion

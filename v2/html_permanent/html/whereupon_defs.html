Definitions

whereupon for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(hweərəpɒn )
Definitions
coordinating conjunction & conjunctionYou use whereupon to say that one thing happens immediately after another thing, and usually as a result of it. [formal] at which ⇒ Mr Muite refused to talk to them except in the company of his legal colleagues, whereupon the police officers departed. ⇒ 'Well, get on with it then,' said Dobson, whereupon Davies started to explain.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

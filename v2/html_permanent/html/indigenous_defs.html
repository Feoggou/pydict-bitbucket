indigenous

(ɪnˈdɪdʒɪnəs )
Definitions
adjective (when postpositive, foll by to)1. originating or occurring naturally (in a country, region, etc); native2. innate (to); inherent (in)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

indigenously (inˈdigenously) adverbindigenousness (inˈdigenousness) or indigenity (ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛnɪtɪ ) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin indigenus, from indigena indigene, from indi- in + gignere to beget

indigenous

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndɪdʒɪnəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveIndigenous people or things belong to the country in which they are found, rather than coming there or being brought there from another country. [formal] ⇒ ...the country's indigenous population. ⇒ ...animals that are indigenous to Asia.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

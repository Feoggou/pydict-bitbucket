technology

(tɛkˈnɒlədʒɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -gies1. the application of practical sciences to industry or commerce2. the methods, theory, and practices governing such application ⇒ a highly developed technology3. the total knowledge and skills available to any human society for industry, art, science, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

technological (ˌtɛknəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ) adjectivetechnologically (ˌtechnoˈlogically) adverbtechnologist (techˈnologist) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Greek tekhnologia systematic treatment, from tekhnē art, skill

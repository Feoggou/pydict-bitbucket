cavalier

(ˌkævəˈlɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. showing haughty disregard; offhandnoun2. a gallant or courtly gentleman, esp one acting as a lady's escort3. archaic a horseman, esp one who is armed
Derived forms
ˌcavalierly (ˌcavaˈlierly) adverb
Word origin
C16: from Italian cavaliere, from Old Provençal cavalier, from Late Latin caballārius rider, from caballus horse, of obscure origin

Italian

(ɪˈtæljən )
Definitions
noun1. the official language of Italy and one of the official languages of Switzerland: the native language of approximately 60 million people. It belongs to the Romance group of the Indo-European family, and there is a considerable diversity of dialects2. a native, citizen, or inhabitant of Italy, or a descendant of one3. See Italian vermouthadjective4. relating to, denoting, or characteristic of Italy, its inhabitants, or their language

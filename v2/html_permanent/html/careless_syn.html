careless

adjective

  1. slapdash, irresponsible, sloppy (informal), cavalier, offhand, neglectful, slipshod, lackadaisical, inattentive, regardless • He pleaded guilty to careless driving. OPPOSITES:  alert, attentive, correct, careful, cautious, wary, on the ball (informal), watchful
  2. negligent, hasty, unconcerned, cursory, perfunctory, thoughtless, indiscreet, unthinking, forgetful, absent-minded, inconsiderate, heedless, remiss, incautious, unmindful, regardless • She's careless about her personal hygiene. OPPOSITES:  concerned, careful, anxious, painstaking, accurate
  3. nonchalant, casual, offhand, artless, unstudied, regardless • With a careless flip of his wrists, he sent the ball on its way. OPPOSITES:  careful, painstaking, accurate

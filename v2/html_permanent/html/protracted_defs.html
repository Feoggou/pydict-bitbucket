graded adjective & adjectiveSomething, usually something unpleasant, that is protracted lasts a long time, especially longer than usual or longer than you hoped. [formal] ⇒ After protracted negotiations, Ogden got the deal he wanted. ⇒ ...a protracted civil war. ⇒ The struggle would be bitter and protracted.

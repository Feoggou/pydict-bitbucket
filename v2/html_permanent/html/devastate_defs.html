devastate

(ˈdɛvəˌsteɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to lay waste or make desolate; ravage; destroy2. to confound or overwhelm, as with grief or shock
Derived forms
ˌdevastation (ˌdevasˈtation) noundevasˌtative (ˈdevasˌtative) adjectivedevasˌtator (ˈdevasˌtator) noun
Word origin
C17: from Latin dēvastāre, from de- + vastāre to ravage; related to vastus waste, empty

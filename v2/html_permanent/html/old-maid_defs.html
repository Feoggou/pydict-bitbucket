old maid

Definitions
noun1. a woman regarded as unlikely ever to marry; spinster2. informal a prim, fastidious, or excessively cautious person3. a card game using a pack from which one card has been removed, in which players try to avoid holding the unpaired card at the end of the game

Word forms: old maids
Definitions
countable nounPeople sometimes refer to an old or middle-aged woman as an old maid when she has never married and they think that it is unlikely that she ever will marry. This use could cause offence. [disapproval] ⇒ Alex is too young to be already thinking of herself as an old maid.

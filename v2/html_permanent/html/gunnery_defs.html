gunnery

(ˈɡʌnərɪ )
Definitions
noun1. the art and science of the efficient design and use of ordnance, esp artillery2. guns collectively3. the use and firing of guns4. (modifier) of, relating to, or concerned with heavy guns, as in warfare ⇒ a gunnery officer

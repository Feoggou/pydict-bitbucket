Word Frequency

well-turned

Definitions
adjective (well turned when postpositive)1. (of a phrase, speech, etc) apt and pleasingly sonorous2. having a pleasing shape ⇒ a well-turned leg
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

voiceless

(ˈvɔɪslɪs )
Definitions
adjective1. without a voice; mute2. not articulated ⇒ voiceless misery3. lacking a musical voice4. silent5. without the power or right to express an opinion6. phonetics articulated without accompanying vibration of the vocal cords ⇒ in English (p) is a voiceless consonant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
1. adjectiveA voiceless speech sound is one that is produced without vibration of the vocal cords. [technical] ⇒ ...the voiceless 'th'.2. adjectiveSomeone who is voiceless is unable to speak. [written] ⇒ His voiceless lips formed the words 'Thank you'.

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪkskluːdɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
prepositionYou use excluding before mentioning a person or thing to show that you are not including them in your statement. ⇒ The families questioned, excluding those on income support, have a net income of £200.20 a week. ⇒ Excluding water, half of the body's weight is protein.

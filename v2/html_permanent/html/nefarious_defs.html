nefarious

(nɪˈfɛərɪəs )
Definitions
adjectiveevil; wicked; sinful
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

nefariously (neˈfariously) adverbnefariousness (neˈfariousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin nefārius, from nefās unlawful deed, from nē not + fās divine law

nefarious for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(nɪfeəriəs )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe an activity as nefarious, you mean that it is wicked and immoral. [literary] ⇒ Why make a whole village prisoner if it was not to some nefarious purpose?

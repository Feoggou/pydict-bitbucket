Lloyd

(lɔɪd )
Definitions
noun1.  Clive (Hubert). born 1944, West Indian (Guyanese) cricketer; played in 110 tests (1966–84), scoring 7,515 runs; captained the West Indies in 74 tests and to two World Cup wins (1975, 1979)2.  Harold (Clayton). 1893–1971, US comic film actor3.  Marie, real name Matilda Alice Victoria Wood. 1870–1922, English music-hall entertainer

unswerving for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnswɜːʳvɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone's attitude, feeling, or way of behaving as unswerving, you mean that it is strong and firm and does not weaken or change. ⇒ In his diary of 1944 he proclaims unswerving loyalty to the monarchy. ⇒ ...her unswerving belief in her father's innocence.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

pickled

(ˈpɪkəld )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. preserved in a pickling liquid2. informal intoxicated; drunk

pickled

Powered by COBUILD
(pɪkəld )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Pickled food, such as vegetables, fruit, and fish, has been kept in vinegar or salt water to preserve it. ⇒ ...a jar of pickled fruit. ⇒ ...little tins of pickled herring.2. graded adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you say that someone is pickled, you mean that they are drunk. [informal] plastered

Caribbean

(ˌkærɪˈbiːən , US English kəˈrɪbɪən)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to the Caribbean Sea and its islands2. of or relating to the Carib or any of their languagesnoun3. See the Caribbean4. short for the Caribbean Sea5. a member of any of the peoples inhabiting the islands of the Caribbean Sea, such as a West Indian or a Carib

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. proper nounThe Caribbean is the sea which is between the West Indies, Central America and the north coast of South America.2. adjectiveCaribbean means belonging or relating to the Caribbean Sea and its islands, or to its people. ⇒ ...the Caribbean island of St Thomas.3. countable nounA Caribbean is a person from a Caribbean island. ⇒ Caribbeans settled in Sheffield in the early '50s.4. → see also Afro-Caribbean

making

(ˈmeɪkɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. the act of a person or thing that makes or the process of being madeb. (in combination) ⇒ watchmaking2. See be the making of3. See in the making4. something made or the quantity of something made at one time5. make-up; composition

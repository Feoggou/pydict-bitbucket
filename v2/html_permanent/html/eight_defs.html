eight

(eɪt )
Definitions
noun1. the cardinal number that is the sum of one and seven and the product of two and four. See also number (sense 1)2. a numeral, 8, VIII, etc, representing this number3. music the numeral 8 used as the lower figure in a time signature to indicate that the beat is measured in quavers4. the amount or quantity that is one greater than seven5. something representing, represented by, or consisting of eight units, such as a playing card with eight symbols on it6. rowinga. a racing shell propelled by eight oarsmenb. the crew of such a shell7. Also called: eight o'clock eight hours after noon or midnight8. See have one over the eight9. See figure of eightdeterminer10. a. amounting to eightb. (as pronoun) ⇒ I could only find eightocta-octo-
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English eahta; related to Old High German ahto, Old Norse ātta, Old Irish ocht, Latin octō, Greek okto, Sanskrit astau

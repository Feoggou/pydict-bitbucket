tedious

(ˈtiːdɪəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. causing fatigue or tedium; monotonous2. obsolete progressing very slowly

Derived Forms

tediously (ˈtediously) adverbtediousness (ˈtediousness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something such as a job, task, or situation as tedious, you mean it is boring and rather frustrating. ⇒ Such lists are long and tedious to read. ⇒ ...the tedious business of line-by-line programming.tediously graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj] ⇒ ...the most tediously boring aspects of international relations.

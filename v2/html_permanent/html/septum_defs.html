septum

(ˈsɛptəm )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ta (-tə)1. biology, anatomy a dividing partition between two tissues or cavities2. a dividing partition or membrane between two cavities in a mechanical device

Word Origin

C18: from Latin saeptum wall, from saepīre to enclose; related to Latin saepēs a fence

gooseberry

(ˈɡʊzbərɪ , -brɪ)
Definitions
noun plural -ries1. a Eurasian shrub, Ribes uva-crispa (or R. grossularia), having greenish, purple-tinged flowers and ovoid yellow-green or red-purple berries: family Grossulariaceae. See also currant (sense 2)2. a. the berry of this plantb. (as modifier) ⇒ gooseberry jam3. British informal an unwanted single person in a group of couples, esp a third person with a couple (often in the phrase play gooseberry)4. See Cape gooseberry

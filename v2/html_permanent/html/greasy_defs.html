greasy

(ˈɡriːzɪ , -sɪ)
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: greasier or greasiest1. coated or soiled with or as if with grease2. composed of or full of grease3. resembling grease4. unctuous or oily in mannernounWord forms: plural greasies Australian slang5. a shearer6. an outback cook, esp cooking for a number of men

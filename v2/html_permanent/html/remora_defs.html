remora

(ˈrɛmərə )
Definitions
nounany of the marine spiny-finned fishes constituting the family Echeneidae. They have a flattened elongated body and attach themselves to larger fish, rocks, etc, by a sucking disc on the top of the head
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin, from re- + mora delay; an allusion to its alleged habit of delaying ships

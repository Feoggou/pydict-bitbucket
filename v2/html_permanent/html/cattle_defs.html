cattle

(ˈkætəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (functioning as plural)1. bovid mammals of the tribe Bovini (bovines), esp those of the genus Bos2. Also called: domestic cattle any domesticated bovine mammals, esp those of the species Bos taurus (domestic ox)bovine
Word origin
C13: from Old Northern French catel, Old French chatel chattel

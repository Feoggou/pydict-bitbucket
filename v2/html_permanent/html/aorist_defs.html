aorist

(ˈeɪərɪst , ˈɛərɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noungrammar a tense of the verb in classical Greek and in certain other inflected languages, indicating past action without reference to whether the action involved was momentary or continuous. Compare perfect (sense 8), imperfect (sense 4)
Derived forms
ˌaoˈristic adjectiveˌaoˈristically adverb
Word origin
C16: from Greek aoristos not limited, from a-1 + horistos restricted, from horizein to define

States General

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. the bicameral legislature of the Netherlands2. historya. an assembly of the estates of an entire country in contrast to those of a single provinceb. Also called: Estates General the assembly of the estates of all France, last meeting in 1789c. the sovereign body of the Dutch republic from the 16th to 18th century

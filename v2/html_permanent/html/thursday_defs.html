Thursday

(ˈθɜːzdɪ , -deɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe fifth day of the week; fourth day of the working week

Word Origin

Old English Thursdæg, literally: Thor's day; related to Old High German Donares tag; see Thor, thunder, day

probable

adjective

  1. likely, possible, apparent, reasonable to think, most likely, presumed, credible, plausible, feasible, odds-on, on the cards, presumable
    • It is probable that food prices will increase.
    OPPOSITES:  unlikely, doubtful, not likely, improbable

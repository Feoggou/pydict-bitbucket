bran

(bræn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. husks of cereal grain separated from the flour by sifting2. food prepared from these husksfurfuraceous
Word origin
C13: from Old French, probably of Gaulish origin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounBran is the outer skin of grain that is left when the grain has been used to make flour. ⇒ ...oat bran. ⇒ Fresh fruits and vegetables are important and so is bran.
Example sentences containing 'bran'
Mr. Naughton had a single bowl of oat bran , some fresh strawberries, and a glass of orange juice.

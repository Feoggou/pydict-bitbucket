resurrection

(ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a supposed act or instance of a dead person coming back to life2. belief in the possibility of this as part of a religious or mystical system3. the condition of those who have risen from the dead ⇒ we shall all live in the resurrection4. the revival of something ⇒ a resurrection of an old story
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

resurrectional (ˌresurˈrectional) or resurrectionary (ˌresurˈrectionary) adjective

Word Origin

C13: via Old French from Late Latin resurrectiō, from Latin resurgere to rise again; see resurge

Resurrection

(ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən )
Definitions
noun Christian theology1. the rising again of Christ from the tomb three days after his death2. the rising again from the dead of all mankind at the Last Judgment
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

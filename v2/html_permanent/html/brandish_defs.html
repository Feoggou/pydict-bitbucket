brandish

(ˈbrændɪʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to wave or flourish (a weapon) in a triumphant, threatening, or ostentatious waynoun2. a threatening or defiant flourish
Derived forms
brandisher (ˈbrandisher) noun
Word origin
C14: from Old French brandir, from brand sword, of Germanic origin; compare Old High German brant weapon

Philemon1

(faɪˈliːmɒn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun New Testament1. a Christian of Colossae whose escaped slave came to meet Paul2. the book (in full The Epistle of Paul the Apostle to Philemon), asking Philemon to forgive the slave for escaping

Definitions

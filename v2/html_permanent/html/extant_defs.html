extant

(ɛkˈstænt , ˈɛkstənt )
Definitions
adjective1. still in existence; surviving2. archaic standing out; protruding▶ USAGE Extant is sometimes wrongly used simply to say that something exists, without any connotation of survival: plutonium is perhaps the deadliest element in existence (not the deadliest element extant)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin exstāns standing out, from exstāre, from stāre to stand

Definitions
adjectiveSomething that is imperceptible is so small that it is not noticed or cannot be seen. ⇒ Brian's hesitation was almost imperceptible.imperceptibly (ɪmpərseptɪbli ) adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj] ⇒ The disease develops gradually and imperceptibly.

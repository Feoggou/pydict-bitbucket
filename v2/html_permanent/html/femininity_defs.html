Powered by COBUILD
(femɪnɪnɪti ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounA woman's femininity is the fact that she is a woman. ⇒ ...the drudgery behind the ideology of motherhood and femininity.2. uncount nounFemininity means the qualities that are considered to be typical of women. ⇒ ...this courageous German tennis star's unique blend of strength and femininity.

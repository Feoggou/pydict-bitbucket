compute

(kəmˈpjuːt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to calculate (an answer, result, etc), often with the aid of a computernoun2. calculation; computation (esp in the phrase beyond compute)
Derived forms
computable (comˈputable) adjectivecomˌputability (comˌputaˈbility) noun
Word origin
C17: from Latin computāre, from putāre to think

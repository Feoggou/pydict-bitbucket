pupillage

or US pupilage (ˈpjuːpɪlɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the condition of being a pupil or duration for which one is a pupil2. (in England) the period spent by a newly called barrister in the chambers of a member of the bar
Example sentences containing 'pupillage'

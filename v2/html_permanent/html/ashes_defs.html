ashes

(ˈæʃɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. ruins or remains, as after destruction or burning ⇒ the city was left in ashes2. the remains of a human body after cremation

Ashes

(ˈæʃɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounSee the Ashes
Word origin
from the mock obituary of English cricket in The Times in 1882 after a great Australian victory at the Oval, in which it was said that the body would be cremated and the ashes taken to Australia

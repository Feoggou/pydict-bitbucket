ligament

(ˈlɪɡəmənt )
Definitions
noun1. anatomy any one of the bands or sheets of tough fibrous connective tissue that restrict movement in joints, connect various bones or cartilages, support muscles, etc2. any physical or abstract connection or bond
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Medieval Latin ligāmentum, from Latin (in the sense: bandage), from ligāre to bind

(poʊzəʳ )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA poser is the same as a poseur. [disapproval] ⇒ He's such a poser.2. countable nounA poser is a difficult problem or puzzle. [informal, old-fashioned] ⇒ Here is a little poser for you.

affidavit

(ˌæfɪˈdeɪvɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounlaw a declaration in writing made upon oath before a person authorized to administer oaths, esp for use as evidence in court
Word origin
C17: from Medieval Latin, literally: he declares on oath, from affīdare to trust (oneself) to; see affiance

wraith

(reɪθ )
Definitions
noun1. the apparition of a person living or thought to be alive, supposed to appear around the time of his death2. a ghost or any apparition3. an insubstantial copy of something4. something pale, thin, and lacking in substance, such as a column of smoke
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

wraithlike (ˈwraithˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C16: Scottish, of unknown origin

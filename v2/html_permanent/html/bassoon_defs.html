bassoon

(bəˈsuːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a woodwind instrument, the tenor of the oboe family. Range: about three and a half octaves upwards from the B flat below the bass staff2. an orchestral musician who plays the bassoon
Derived forms
bassoonist (basˈsoonist) noun
Word origin
C18: from French basson, from Italian bassone, from basso deep; see base2

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]If you say that someone, especially a man, does something manfully, you mean that they do it in a very determined or brave way. ⇒ They stuck to their task manfully. ⇒ ...simple people who manfully bear the deprivations that are their lot.
Example sentences containing 'manfully'
Hard by him two aged gentlemen were squatting on the floor, manfully sucking at a bubbling hookah. Maclean, Alistair THE LONELY SEAThe watchman had not expected such a question, but he saw his friend's shoulders square and heard his voice deepen manfully. Heller, Keith MAN'S LOVING FAMILY

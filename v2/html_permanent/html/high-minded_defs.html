high-minded

Definitions
adjective1. having or characterized by high moral principles2. archaic arrogant; haughty

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that someone is high-minded, you think they have strong moral principles. ⇒ The President's hopes for the country were high-minded, but too vague. ⇒ She belonged to a high-minded group of ladies who met and discussed Works of Literature.

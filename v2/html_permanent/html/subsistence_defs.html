subsistence

(səbˈsɪstəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the means by which one maintains life2. the act or condition of subsisting3. a thing that has real existence4. the state of being inherent5. philosophy an inferior mode of being ascribed to the references of general terms which do not in fact exist. See also nonbeing

subsistence

Powered by COBUILD
(səbsɪstəns )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount noun [oft N n]Subsistence is the condition of just having enough food or money to stay alive. ⇒ ...below the subsistence level. ⇒ The standard of living today is on the edge of subsistence.2. adjective [ADJ n]In subsistence farming or subsistence agriculture, farmers produce food to eat themselves rather than to sell. ⇒ Many black Namibians are subsistence farmers who live in the arid borderlands.

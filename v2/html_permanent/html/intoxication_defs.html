intoxication

(ɪnˌtɒksɪˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. drunkenness; inebriation2. great elation3. the act of intoxicating4. poisoning

intoxication

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪntɒksɪkeɪʃən ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounIntoxication is the state of being drunk. [formal] ⇒ Intoxication interferes with memory and thinking, speech and coordination.2. uncount nounYou use intoxication to refer to a quality that something has that makes you feel very excited. [literary] [+ of] ⇒ ...the intoxication of greed and success.

colloquial

(kəˈləʊkwɪəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to conversation2. denoting or characterized by informal or conversational idiom or vocabulary. Compare informal
Derived forms
colloquially (colˈloquially) adverbcolloquialness (colˈloquialness) noun

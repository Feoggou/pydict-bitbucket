circulating capital

(ˈsɜːkjʊˌleɪtɪŋ ˈkæpɪtəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouneconomics the non-permanent raw materials and operating expenses that are used up to produce other goods or services ⇒ According to Smith, the capital stock of the factory owner consists of fixed capital (machines, tools, plant) and circulating capital, a fund used for buying raw materials and for paying labor.

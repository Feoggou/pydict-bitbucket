educated

(ˈɛdjʊˌkeɪtɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. having an education, esp a good one2. displaying culture, taste, and knowledge; cultivated3. (prenominal) based on experience or information (esp in the phrase an educated guess)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

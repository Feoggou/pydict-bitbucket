pearly

(ˈpɜːlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: pearlier or pearliest1. resembling a pearl, esp in lustre2. of the colour pearl; pale bluish-grey3. decorated with pearls or mother-of-pearlnounWord forms: plural pearlies (in Britain)4. a London costermonger who wears on ceremonial occasions a traditional dress of dark clothes covered with pearl buttons5. (plural) the clothes or the buttons themselves

Derived Forms

adjective [usu ADJ n]Something that is pearly is pale and shines softly, like a pearl. ⇒ ...the pearly light of early morning.Pearly is also a combining form. ⇒ ...pearly-pink lipstick. ⇒ Her skin was pearly white.

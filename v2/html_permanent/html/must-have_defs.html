must-have

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an essential possession ⇒ the mobile phone is now a must-have for childrenadjective2. essential ⇒ a must-have fashion accessory

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA must-have is something modern that many people want to have. ⇒ The mobile phone is now a must-have for children.Must-have is also an adjective. ⇒ ...a must-have fashion accessory.

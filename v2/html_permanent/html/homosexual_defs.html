homosexual

(ˌhəʊməʊˈsɛksjʊəl , ˌhɒm-)
Definitions
noun1. a person who is sexually attracted to members of the same sexadjective2. of or relating to homosexuals or homosexuality3. of or relating to the same sex▷ Compare heterosexual

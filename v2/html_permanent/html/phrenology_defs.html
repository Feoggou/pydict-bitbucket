phrenology

(frɪˈnɒlədʒɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(formerly) the branch of science concerned with localization of function in the human brain, esp determination of the strength of the faculties by the shape and size of the skull overlying the parts of the brain thought to be responsible for them

Derived Forms

phrenological (ˌfrɛnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ) or phrenologic (ˌfrɛnəˈlɒdʒɪk) adjectivephrenologist (phreˈnologist) noun

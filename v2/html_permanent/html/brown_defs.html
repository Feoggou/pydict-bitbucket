brown

(braʊn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of various colours, such as those of wood or earth, produced by low intensity light in the wavelength range 620–585 nanometres2. a dye or pigment producing these colours3. brown cloth or clothing ⇒ dressed in brown4. any of numerous mostly reddish-brown butterflies of the genera Maniola, Lasiommata, etc, such as M. jurtina (meadow brown): family Satyridaeadjective5. of the colour brown6. (of bread) made from a flour that has not been bleached or bolted, such as wheatmeal or wholemeal flour7. deeply tanned or sunburntverb8. to make (esp food as a result of cooking) brown or (esp of food) to become brown
Derived forms
brownish (ˈbrownish) or browny (ˈbrowny) adjectivebrownness (ˈbrownness) noun
Word origin
Old English brūn; related to Old Norse brūnn, Old High German brūn, Greek phrunos toad, Sanskrit babhru reddish-brown

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. colourSomething that is brown is the colour of earth or of wood. ⇒ ...her deep brown eyes. ⇒ The stairs are decorated in golds and earthy browns.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]You can describe a white-skinned person as brown when they have been sitting in the sun until their skin has become darker than usual. ⇒ I don't want to be really really brown, just have a nice light golden colour.3. verbIf someone browns in the sun they become brown in colour. [V] ⇒ Her skin was of the fortunate kind that could brown in the sun without burning. [V n] ⇒ There were many gorgeous females busy browning themselves.4. adjective [usu ADJ n]A brown person is someone who belongs to a race of people who have brown-coloured skins. ⇒ ...a slim brown man with a speckled turban.5. adjective [usu ADJ n]Brown is used to describe grains that have not had their outer layers removed, and foods made from these grains. ⇒ ...brown bread. ⇒ ...spicy tomato sauce served over a bed of brown rice.6. ergative verb & verbWhen food browns or when you brown food, you cook it, usually for a short time on a high flame. [V] ⇒ Cook for ten minutes until the sugar browns. [V n] ⇒ He browned the chicken in a frying pan.

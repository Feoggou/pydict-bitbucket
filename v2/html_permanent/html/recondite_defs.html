recondite

(rɪˈkɒndaɪt , ˈrɛkənˌdaɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. requiring special knowledge to be understood; abstruse2. dealing with abstruse or profound subjects
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

reconditely (reˈconditely) adverbreconditeness (reˈconditeness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin reconditus hidden away, from re- + condere to conceal

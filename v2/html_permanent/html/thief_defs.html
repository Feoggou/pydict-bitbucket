thief

(θiːf )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural thieves (θiːvz )1. a person who steals something from another2. criminal law a person who commits theft
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

thievish (ˈthievish) adjectivethievishly (ˈthievishly) adverbthievishness (ˈthievishness) noun

Word Origin

Old English thēof; related to Old Frisian thiāf, Old Saxon thiof, Old High German diob, Old Norse thjōfr, Gothic thiufs

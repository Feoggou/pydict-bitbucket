stow

(stəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. (often foll by away) to pack or store2. to fill by packing3. nautical to pack or put away (cargo, sails and other gear, etc)4. to have enough room for5. (usually imperative) British slang to cease from ⇒ stow your noise!, ⇒ stow it!

Word Origin

Old English stōwian to keep, hold back, from stōw a place; related to Old High German stouwen to accuse, Gothic stōjan to judge, Old Slavonic staviti to place

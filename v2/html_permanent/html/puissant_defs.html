puissant

(ˈpjuːɪsənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivearchaic or poetic powerful

Derived Forms

puissantly (ˈpuissantly) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Old French, ultimately from Latin potēns mighty, from posse to have power

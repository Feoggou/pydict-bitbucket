beer

(bɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an alcoholic drink brewed from malt, sugar, hops, and water and fermented with yeast. Compare ale2. a slightly fermented drink made from the roots or leaves of certain plants ⇒ ginger beer, ⇒ nettle beer3. (modifier) relating to or used in the drinking of beer ⇒ beer glass, ⇒ beer mat4. (modifier) in which beer is drunk, esp (of licensed premises) having a licence to sell beer ⇒ beer house, ⇒ beer cellar, ⇒ beer garden
Word origin
Old English beor; related to Old Norse bjōrr, Old Frisian biār, Old High German bior

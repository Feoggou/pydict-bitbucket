Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. Drawn is the past participle of draw.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone or their face looks drawn, their face is thin and they look very tired, ill, worried, or unhappy. ⇒ She looked drawn and tired when she turned towards me.

precautionary

(prɪkɔːʃənri , US -neri )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Precautionary actions are taken in order to prevent something dangerous or unpleasant from happening. [formal] ⇒ The local administration says the curfew is a precautionary measure.
Example sentences containing 'precautionary'
They would take appropriate precautionary measures, intensify the security ring around her, and wait and see how the cookie crumbled. Fay Weldon THE PRESIDENT'S CHILDThen they asked me to wait outside while they finished taping her up and sent her for precautionary X-rays of her ribs. Anita Anderson SOMEBODYWith a precautionary glance through the living-room window, she let herself quickly in. Stewart, Michael COMPULSION

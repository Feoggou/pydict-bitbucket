casing

(ˈkeɪsɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a protective case or cover2. material for a case or cover3. Also called: case a frame containing a door, window, or staircase4. the intestines of cattle, pigs, etc, or a synthetic substitute, used as a container for sausage meat5. the outer cover of a pneumatic tyre6. a pipe or tube used to line a hole or shaft7. the outer shell of a steam or gas turbine

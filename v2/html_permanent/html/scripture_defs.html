scripture

(ˈskrɪptʃə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna sacred, solemn, or authoritative book or piece of writing

Word Origin

C13: from Latin scriptūra written material, from scrībere to write

Scripture

(ˈskrɪptʃə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: Holy Scripture, Holy Writ, the Scriptures Christianity the Old and New Testaments2. any book or body of writings, esp when regarded as sacred by a particular religious group

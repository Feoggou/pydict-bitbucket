hard-pressed

Definitions
adjective1. in difficulties; struggling ⇒ the swimmer was hard-pressed, ⇒ he was hard-pressed to get the work done on time2. subject to severe competition3. subject to severe attack4. closely pursued
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

hard-pressed

Powered by COBUILD
also hard pressed for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is hard-pressed, they are under a great deal of strain and worry, usually because they have not got enough money. [journalism] ⇒ The region's hard-pressed consumers are spending less on luxuries.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you will be hard-pressed to do something, you will have great difficulty doing it. ⇒ This year the airline will be hard-pressed to make a profit.

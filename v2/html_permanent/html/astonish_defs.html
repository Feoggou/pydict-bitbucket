astonish

(əˈstɒnɪʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(transitive) to fill with amazement; surprise greatly
Word origin
C15: from earlier astonyen (see astonied), from Old French estoner, from Vulgar Latin extonāre (unattested) to strike with thunder, from Latin tonāre to thunder

Definitions

verbIf something or someone astonishes you, they surprise you very much. [V n] ⇒ My news will astonish you. [V n] ⇒ Her dedication constantly astonishes me.

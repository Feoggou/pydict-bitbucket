Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft under N]Supervision is the supervising of people, activities, or places. ⇒ A toddler requires close supervision and firm control at all times. [+ of] ⇒ The plan calls for a cease-fire and U.N. supervision of the country. ⇒ First-time licence holders have to work under supervision.

No trips to the Eastern sector for at least six months, and then only under strictest supervision.

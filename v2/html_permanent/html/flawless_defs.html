Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that something or someone is flawless, you mean that they are extremely good and that there are no faults or problems with them. ⇒ She attributed her flawless complexion to the moisturiser she used. ⇒ Discovery's takeoff this morning from Cape Canaveral was flawless.flawlessly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj] ⇒ Each stage of the battle was carried off flawlessly.

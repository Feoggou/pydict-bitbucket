excesses

(ɪkˈsɛsɪz)
Definitions
plural noun1. overindulgence of sex, alcohol, drugs etc ⇒ The emperor's excesses were notorious., ⇒ This hugely talented but troubled artist surrounded himself with aides who encouraged his excesses rather than protecting him from himself.2. extreme, cruel or violent actions ⇒ the bloody excesses of warfare and empire-building

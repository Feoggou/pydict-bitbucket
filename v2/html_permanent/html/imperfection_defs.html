imperfection

(ˌɪmpəˈfɛkʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the condition or quality of being imperfect2. a fault or defect

imperfection

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmpəʳfekʃən ) for learners of English
Word forms: imperfections
Definitions
1. variable nounAn imperfection in someone or something is a fault, weakness, or undesirable feature that they have. [+ in] ⇒ He concedes that there are imperfections in the socialist system. ⇒ I was obsessed by my physical imperfections.2. countable nounAn imperfection in something is a small mark or damaged area which may spoil its appearance. [+ in] ⇒ ...imperfections in the cloth.

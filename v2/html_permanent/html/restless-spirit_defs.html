restless spirit

(ˈrɛstləs ˈspɪrɪt)
Definitions
noun1. a wandering spirit ⇒ the restless spirit of an unknown murderer2. formal an unhappy, dissatisfied person ⇒ Always a restless spirit, she was 13 when her mother died and she seems never to have recovered from the shock of the loss.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

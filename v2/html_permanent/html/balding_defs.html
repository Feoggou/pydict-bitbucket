Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveSomeone who is balding is beginning to lose the hair on the top of their head. ⇒ He wore a straw hat to keep his balding head from getting sunburned.

He was a little, round man of about forty with a balding head. Martin, Joy THE IMAGE OF LAURAFrom a distance he appeared to be in middle age, stocky, balding.

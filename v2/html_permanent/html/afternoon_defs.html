afternoon

(ˌɑːftəˈnuːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. the period of the day between noon and eveningb. (as modifier) ⇒ afternoon tea2. a middle or later part ⇒ the afternoon of life

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounThe afternoon is the part of each day which begins at lunchtime and ends at about six o'clock. ⇒ He's arriving in the afternoon. ⇒ He had stayed in his room all afternoon. ⇒ ...an afternoon news conference.

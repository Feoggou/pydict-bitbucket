swaddling clothes

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. long strips of linen or other cloth formerly wrapped round a newly born baby2. restrictions or supervision imposed on the immature
Example sentences containing 'swaddling clothes'
As they walked through the loggia, babies in swaddling clothes , circled as if by a deep blue sky, looked down on them. Haines, Pamela THE GOLDEN LIONI expect I was terrified in my swaddling clothes , because who wouldn't be at that age? Sillitoe, Alan THE OPEN DOOR

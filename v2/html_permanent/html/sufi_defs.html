Sufi

(ˈsuːfɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -fisan adherent of any of various Muslim mystical orders or teachings, which emphasize the direct personal experience of God

Derived Forms

Sufic (ˈSufic) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Arabic sūfīy, literally: (man) of wool, from sūf wool; probably from the ascetic's woollen garments

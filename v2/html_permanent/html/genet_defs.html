genet1

(ˈdʒɛnɪt ) or genette (dʒɪˈnɛt )
Definitions
noun1. any agile catlike viverrine mammal of the genus Genetta, inhabiting wooded regions of Africa and S Europe, having an elongated head, thick spotted or blotched fur, and a very long tail2. the fur of such an animal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French genette, from Arabic jarnayt

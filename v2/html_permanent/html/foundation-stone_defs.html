Word forms: foundation stones
Definitions
1. countable noun [oft with poss]A foundation stone is a large block of stone built into a large public building near the bottom. It is often involved in a ceremony for the opening of the building, and has writing on it recording this. [mainly British] ⇒ The Princess of Wales laid the foundation stone for the extension to the Cathedral.regional note:   in AM, use cornerstone2. countable nounThe foundation stone of something is the basic, important thing which its existence or success depends on. [+ of] ⇒ ...these foundation stones of the future: education, training, research, development.

energies

(ˈɛnədʒɪz)
Definitions
plural noun1. See energy2. Your energies are your efforts and attention, which you direct towards a particular aim. ⇒ She devoted her energies to teaching., ⇒ the creative energies of young people, ⇒ We must concentrate our energies on treating addiction first., ⇒ He put all his energies into building the business, and neglected his family.

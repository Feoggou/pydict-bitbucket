Barbados

(bɑːˈbeɪdəʊs , -dəʊz, -dɒs)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan island in the Caribbean, in the E Lesser Antilles: a British colony from 1628 to 1966, now an independent state within the Commonwealth. Language: English. Currency: Barbados dollar. Capital: Bridgetown. Pop: 288 725 (2013 est). Area: 430 sq km (166 sq miles)

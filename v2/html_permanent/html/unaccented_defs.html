unaccented

(ˌʌnækˈsɛntɪd) or unaccentuated (ˌʌnækˈsɛntjʊeɪtɪd)
Definitions
adjectivephonetics not accented or stressed ⇒ He spoke in a low, unaccented voice., ⇒ They spoke unaccented English., ⇒ Don't let the unaccented syllables be lost.

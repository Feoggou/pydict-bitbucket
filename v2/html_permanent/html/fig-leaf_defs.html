fig leaf

Definitions
noun1. a leaf from a fig tree2. a representation of a leaf, usually a vine leaf rather than an actual fig leaf, used in painting or sculpture to cover the genitals of nude figures3. a device intended to conceal something regarded as shameful or indecent

Word forms: fig leaves
Definitions
1. countable nounA fig leaf is a large leaf which comes from the fig tree. A fig leaf is sometimes used in painting and sculpture to cover the genitals of a naked body.2. countable nounPeople sometimes refer disapprovingly to something which is intended to hide or prevent an embarrassing situation as a fig leaf. [journalism, disapproval] ⇒ This deal is little more than a fig leaf for the continued destruction of the landscape.

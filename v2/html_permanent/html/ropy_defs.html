ropy

(ˈroʊpi ) in American English
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: ˈropier or ˈropiest1.  forming sticky, stringy threads, as some liquids do; glutinous2.  like a rope or ropes3.  British, Slanga.  bad, poor, inferior, etc.b.  somewhat unwell also ˈropey

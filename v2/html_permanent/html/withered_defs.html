Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a person or a part of their body as withered, you mean that they are thin and their skin looks old. wizened ⇒ ...her withered hands. ⇒ ...the bartender's withered face.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Withered is used to describe someone's leg or arm when it is thin and weak because of disease or injury. ⇒ She has one slightly withered leg, noticeably thinner than the other.

separatist

(ˈsɛpərətɪst , ˈsɛprə-) or separationist

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna. a person who advocates or practises secession from an organization or groupb. (as modifier) ⇒ a separatist movement

Derived Forms

(ˈsɛpərətɪst , ˈsɛprə-)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun(sometimes not capital) a person who advocates the secession of a province, esp Quebec, from Canada

separatist

Powered by COBUILD
(sepərətɪst )
Word forms: separatists
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Separatist organizations and activities within a country involve members of a group of people who want to establish their own separate government or are trying to do so. ⇒ ...the Basque separatist movement. ⇒ Nearly 2,500 have died in separatist violence this year.2. countable nounSeparatists are people who want their own separate government or are involved in separatist activities. ⇒ The army has come under attack by separatists.

jackdaw

(ˈdʒækˌdɔː )
Definitions
nouna large common Eurasian passerine bird, Corvus monedula, in which the plumage is black and dark grey: noted for its thieving habits: family Corvidae (crows)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from jack1 + daw

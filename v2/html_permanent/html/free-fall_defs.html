free fall

Definitions
noun1. free descent of a body in which the gravitational force is the only force acting on it2. the part of a parachute descent before the parachute opens

Word forms: free falls
Definitions
1. variable noun [oft into/in N]If the value or price of something goes into free fall, it starts to fall uncontrollably. [journalism] ⇒ Sterling went into free fall. ⇒ The price did a free fall.2. uncount nounIn parachuting, free fall is the part of the jump before the parachute opens.

inconstant

(ɪnˈkɒnstənt )
Definitions
adjective1. not constant; variable2. fickle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inconstancy (inˈconstancy) nouninconstantly (inˈconstantly) adverb
Example sentences containing 'inconstant'
It was this inconstant wind, he remembered, that had frozen Tamara's face and nearly killed her. Zindell, David THE BROKEN GODLife is too absurd and inconstant and contradictory to stop her from having it all, having it any way she wants. Alex Shakar THE SAVAGE GIRL

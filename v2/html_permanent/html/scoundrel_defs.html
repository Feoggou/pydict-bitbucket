Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf you refer to a man as a scoundrel, you mean that he behaves very badly towards other people, especially by cheating them or deceiving them. [old-fashioned, disapproval] ⇒ He is a lying scoundrel!

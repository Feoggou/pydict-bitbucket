Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adverb & adverbDecidedly means to a great extent and in a way that is very obvious. ⇒ Sometimes he is decidedly uncomfortable at what he sees on the screen. ⇒ Representatives of the other branches adopted a decidedly different view. ⇒ It soon became clear that authors were decidedly in the majority.

maddening

(ˈmædənɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. serving to send mad2. extremely annoying; exasperating

Derived Forms

maddeningly (ˈmaddeningly) adverbmaddeningness (ˈmaddeningness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as maddening, you mean that it makes you feel angry, irritated, or frustrated. ⇒ Shopping in the January sales can be maddening. ⇒ It is, in its way, a maddening biography.maddeningly graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj] [ADV after v] ⇒ The service is maddeningly slow. ⇒ 'Aha!' Dave nodded maddeningly. ⇒ Slowly, maddeningly, a smile surfaced on his face.

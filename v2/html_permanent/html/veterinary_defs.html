veterinary

(ˈvɛtərɪnərɪ , ˈvɛtrɪnrɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveof or relating to veterinary medicine
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Latin veterīnārius concerning draught animals, from veterīnae draught animals; related to vetus mature (hence able to bear a burden)

veterinary

Powered by COBUILD
(vetərənəri , US -neri ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]Veterinary is used to describe the work of a person whose job is to treat sick or injured animals, or to describe the medical treatment of animals. ⇒ It was decided that our veterinary screening of horses at events should be continued.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

extravagance

(ɪkˈstrævəɡəns ) or extravagancy (ɪkˈstrævəɡənsɪ)
Definitions
noun1. excessive outlay of money; wasteful spending2. immoderate or absurd speech or behaviour

extravagance

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪkstrævəgəns ) for learners of English
Word forms: extravagances
Definitions
1. uncount nounExtravagance is the spending of more money than is reasonable or than you can afford. overspending ⇒ ...gross mismanagement and financial extravagance. ⇒ When the company went under, tales of his extravagance surged through the industry.2. countable nounAn extravagance is something that you spend money on but cannot really afford. ⇒ Her only extravagance was horses. ⇒ Why waste money on such extravagances?
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

stucco

(ˈstʌkəʊ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -coes or -cos1. a weather-resistant mixture of dehydrated lime, powdered marble, and glue, used in decorative mouldings on buildings2. any of various types of cement or plaster used for coating outside walls3. Also called: stuccowork decorative work moulded in stuccoverb -coes, -cos, -coing or -coed4. (transitive) to apply stucco to

Derived Forms

stuccoer (ˈstuccoer) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Italian, of Germanic origin; compare Old High German stukki a fragment, crust, Old English stycce

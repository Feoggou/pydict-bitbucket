gilt1

(ɡɪlt )
Definitions
verb1. a past tense and past participle of gild1noun2. gold or a substance simulating it, applied in gilding3. another word for gilding (sense 1), gilding (sense 2)4. superficial or false appearance of excellence; glamour5. a gilt-edged security6. See take the gilt off the gingerbreadadjective7. covered with or as if with gold or gilt; gilded
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

gilt2

Definitions
nouna young female pig, esp one that has not had a litter
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old Norse gyltr; related to Old English gelte, Old High German gelza, Middle Low German gelte

Definitions

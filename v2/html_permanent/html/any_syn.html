any

determiner

  1. a scrap of, a bit of, a grain of, a fragment of, a small piece of, a speck of, the slightest bit of, a crumb of, an atom of, an iota of, a jot of, NE (text messaging) • We're doing all this without any support.
  2. whichever, every, NE (text messaging) • Any actor will tell you that it's easier to act than to be themselves.
  3. a single one, any one, a single member, NE (text messaging) • There was nothing any of us could do.

adverb

  1. at all, somewhat, in the least, to an extent, NE (text messaging) • Things aren't getting any easier for graduates.

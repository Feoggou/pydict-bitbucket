injure

(ˈɪndʒə )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to cause physical or mental harm or suffering to; hurt or wound2. to offend, esp by an injustice
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

injurable (ˈinjurable) adjectiveinjured (ˈinjured) adjectiveinjurer (ˈinjurer) noun

Word Origin

C16: back formation from injury

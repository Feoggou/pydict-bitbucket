Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrase [usu v-link PHR]If you describe a regular activity or task as a full-time job, you mean that it takes up so much of your time it is like doing a paid job. ⇒ The way she did it, mothering was a full-time job. →  full-time

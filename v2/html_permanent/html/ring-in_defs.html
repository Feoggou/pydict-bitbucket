ring in

Definitions
verb (adverb)1. (intransitive) mainly British to report to someone by telephone2. (transitive) to accompany the arrival of with bells (esp in the phrase ring in the new year)3. (transitive) Australian to substitute (a horse) fraudulently for another horse in a race4. (transitive) Australian and New Zealand informal to recruit or include (a person)noun ring-in5. Australian informal a horse that serves as a substitute6. Australian and New Zealand informal a person or thing that is not normally a member of a particular group; outsider

bop1

(bɒp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a form of jazz originating in the 1940s, characterized by rhythmic and harmonic complexity and instrumental virtuosity. Originally called: bebop2. informal a session of dancing to pop musicverbWord forms: bops, bopping or bopped3. (intransitive) informal to dance to pop music
Derived forms
bopper (ˈbopper) noun
Word origin
C20: shortened from bebop

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

troubled

Powered by COBUILD
(trʌbəld ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is troubled is worried because they have problems. ⇒ Rose sounded deeply troubled. ⇒ She was conscious of a troubled expression on Ann's face.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A troubled place, situation, organization, or time has many problems or conflicts. ⇒ There is much we can do to help this troubled country. ⇒ Anyone who has been in a troubled relationship will sympathise with Sue. ⇒ But these are troubled times and many people fear for the future.3. → to pour oil on troubled waters

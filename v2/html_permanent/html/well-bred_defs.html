well-bred

Definitions
adjective (well bred when postpositive)1. Also: well-born of respected or noble lineage2. indicating good breeding ⇒ well-bred manners3. of good thoroughbred stock ⇒ a well-bred spaniel
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

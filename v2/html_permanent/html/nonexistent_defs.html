nonexistent

(ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɪstənt )
Definitions
adjective1. not having being or existence2. not present under specified conditions or in a specified place
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

nonexistence (ˌnonexˈistence) noun

give over

Definitions
verb (adverb)1. (transitive) to transfer, esp to the care or custody of another2. (transitive) to assign or resign to a specific purpose or function ⇒ the day was given over to pleasure3. informal to cease (an activity) ⇒ give over fighting, will you!

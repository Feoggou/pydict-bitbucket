Powered by COBUILD

Definitions
adverbHenceforth means from this time onwards. [formal] from now on, in future ⇒ Henceforth, parties which fail to get 5% of the vote will not be represented in parliament. ⇒ We were finally released with a formal warning that we were henceforth barred from the base.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

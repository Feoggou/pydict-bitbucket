memoir

(ˈmɛmwɑː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a biography or historical account, esp one based on personal knowledge2. an essay or monograph, as on a specialized topic3. obsolete a memorandum

Derived Forms

memoirist (ˈmemoirist) noun

Word Origin

C16: from French, from Latin memoria memory

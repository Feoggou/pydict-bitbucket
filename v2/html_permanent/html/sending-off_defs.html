sending-off

(ˌsɛndɪŋˈɒf)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun(in football) an instance of a player being told to leave the field by the referee, as a punishment for a serious offence ⇒ It was his third sending-off of the season., ⇒ He is about to begin a three-match ban after his third sending-off of the season.

sending-off

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [oft poss N]If there is a sending-off during a game of football, a player is told to leave the field by the referee, as a punishment for seriously breaking the rules. ⇒ He is about to begin a three-match ban after his third sending-off of the season.

schoolmaster

(ˈskuːlˌmɑːstə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a man who teaches in or runs a school2. a person or thing that acts as an instructor3. a food fish, Lutjanus apodus, of the warm waters of the Caribbean and Atlantic: family Lutjanidae (snappers)verb (intransitive)4. to be a schoolmaster

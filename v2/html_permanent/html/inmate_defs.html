inmate

(ˈɪnˌmeɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a person who is confined to an institution such as a prison or hospital. See also resident (sense 2)2. obsolete a person who lives with others in a house
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

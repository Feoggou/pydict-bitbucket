Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA press conference is a meeting held by a famous or important person in which they answer journalists' questions. ⇒ She gave her reaction to his release at a press conference. ⇒ The opposition parties called a press conference to denounce the actions of the police.

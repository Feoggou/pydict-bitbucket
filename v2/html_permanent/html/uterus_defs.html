uterus

(ˈjuːtərəs )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural uteri (ˈjuːtəˌraɪ )1. anatomy a hollow muscular organ lying within the pelvic cavity of female mammals. It houses the developing fetus and by contractions aids in its expulsion at parturition. Nontechnical name: womb2. the corresponding organ in other animals
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin; compare Greek hustera womb, hoderos belly, Sanskrit udara belly

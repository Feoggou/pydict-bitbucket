eremite

(ˈɛrɪˌmaɪt )
Definitions
nouna Christian hermit or recluse. Compare coenobite
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

eremitic (ˌɛrɪˈmɪtɪk ) or eremitical (ˌereˈmitical) adjectiveeremitism (ˈɛrɪmaɪˌtɪzəm ) noun

Word Origin

C13: see hermit
Example sentences containing 'eremite'
After all, she wasn't an anchorite - or was it an eremite - with her thoughts as her raison d'être. Aird, Catherine A DEAD LIBERTY

Similar Words

hermit, solitary, recluse, anchorite

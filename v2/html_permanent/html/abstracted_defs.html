Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is abstracted is thinking so deeply that they are not fully aware of what is happening around them. [written] ⇒ Meg was so abstracted she scarcely noticed that the train had stopped. ⇒ The same abstracted look was still on his face.abstractedly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ She nodded abstractedly.

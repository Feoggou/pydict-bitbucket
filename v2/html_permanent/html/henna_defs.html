henna

(ˈhɛnə )
Definitions
noun1. a lythraceous shrub or tree, Lawsonia inermis, of Asia and N Africa, with white or reddish fragrant flowers2. a reddish dye obtained from the powdered leaves of this plant, used as a cosmetic and industrial dye3. a reddish-brown or brown colourverb4. (transitive) to dye with henna
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Arabic hinnā'; see alkanet

lay in

Definitions
verb(tr, adverb) to accumulate and store ⇒ we must lay in food for the party

lay in

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
phrasal verbIf you lay in an amount of something, you buy it and store it to be used later. [V P n] ⇒ They began to lay in extensive stores of food supplies. →  lay

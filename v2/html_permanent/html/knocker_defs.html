knocker

(ˈnɒkə )
Definitions
noun1. an object, usually ornamental and made of metal, attached to a door by a hinge and used for knocking2. informal a person who finds fault or disparages3. (usually plural) slang a female breast4. a person or thing that knocks5. See on the knocker

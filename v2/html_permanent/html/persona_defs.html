persona

(pɜːˈsəʊnə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -nae (-niː)1. (often plural) a character in a play, novel, etc2. an assumed identity or character3. (in Jungian psychology) the mechanism that conceals a person's true thoughts and feelings, esp in his adaptation to the outside world

Word Origin

Latin: mask

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounSomeone's persona is the aspect of their character or nature that they present to other people, perhaps in contrast to their real character or nature. [formal] ⇒ ...the contradictions between her private life and the public persona. ⇒ From time to time he will take on a new persona.2. → see also persona non grata

Minotaur

(ˈmaɪnətɔː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounGreek mythology a monster with the head of a bull and the body of a man. It was kept in the Labyrinth in Crete, feeding on human flesh, until destroyed by Theseus

Word Origin

C14: via Latin from Greek Minōtauros, from Minos + tauros bull

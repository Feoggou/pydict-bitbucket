benignity

(bɪˈnɪɡnɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the quality of being benign; favourable attitude2. a kind or gracious act
Example sentences containing 'benignity'
He was tall, with wispy brown hair, a face of great benignity set on a gangling body. Brandon, Ruth LEFT, RIGHT AND CENTRE`Please sit down, Mr Secretary," he said, with formality, with courtesy, with a slight warmth of benignity , and returned to his papers. Harvey, John COUP D'ETAT

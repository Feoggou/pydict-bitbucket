contortion

(kənˈtɔːʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or process of contorting or the state of being contorted2. a twisted shape or position3. something twisted or out of the ordinary in character, meaning, etc ⇒ mental contortions
Derived forms
contortional (conˈtortional) adjectivecontortioned (conˈtortioned) adjective

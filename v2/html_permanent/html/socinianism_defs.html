Socinianism

(soʊˈsɪniənˌɪzəm )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun the teachings of Faustus Socinus (1539-1604), It. rationalistic religious reformer, denying the Trinity, the divinity of Christ, etc., and holding that salvation is attained solely by practicing the virtues exemplified by Christ

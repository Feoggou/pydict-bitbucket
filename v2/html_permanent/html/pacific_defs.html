pacific

(pəˈsɪfɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. tending or conducive to peace; conciliatory2. not aggressive; opposed to the use of force3. free from conflict; peaceful

Derived Forms

pacifically (paˈcifically) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Old French pacifique, from Latin pācificus, from pāx peace + facere to make

Pacific

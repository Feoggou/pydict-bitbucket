efficacy

Powered by COBUILD
(efɪkəsi ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [usu with poss]If you talk about the efficacy of something, you are talking about its effectiveness and its ability to do what it is supposed to. [formal] ⇒ Recent medical studies confirm the efficacy of a healthier lifestyle.

murderous

(ˈmɜːdərəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. intending, capable of, or guilty of murder2. informal very dangerous, difficult, or unpleasant ⇒ a murderous road

Derived Forms

murderously (ˈmurderously) adverbmurderousness (ˈmurderousness) noun

murderous

Powered by COBUILD
(mɜːʳdərəs )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Someone who is murderous is likely to murder someone and may already have murdered someone. ⇒ This murderous lunatic could kill them both without a second thought.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A murderous attack or other action is very violent and intended to result in someone's death. ⇒ He made a murderous attack on his wife that evening.

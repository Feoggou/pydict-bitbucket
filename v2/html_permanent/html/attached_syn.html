attached

adjective

  1. spoken for, married, partnered, engaged, accompanied • I wondered if he was attached.
  • See attached to

attach

verb

  1. affix, stick, secure, bind, unite, add, join, couple, link, tie, fix, connect, lash, glue, adhere, fasten, annex, truss, yoke, append, make fast, cohere, subjoin, bootstrap to • Attach labels to things before you file them away. OPPOSITES:  remove, separate, retire, withdraw, detach, loosen, disconnect, dissociate, untie
  2. ascribe, connect, attribute, assign, place, associate, lay on, accredit, invest with, impute, reattribute • They have attached much significance to your visit.
  • See attach yourself to

unpalatable

(ʌnˈpælətəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. unpleasant to taste2. difficult to accept ⇒ the unpalatable truth

unpalatable for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnpælɪtəbəl )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe an idea as unpalatable, you mean that you find it unpleasant and difficult to accept. ⇒ It is an unpalatable fact that rape makes a good news story. ⇒ It was only then that I began to learn the unpalatable truth about John.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe food as unpalatable, you mean that it is so unpleasant that you can hardly eat it. ⇒ ...a lump of dry, unpalatable cheese.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

placable

(ˈplækəbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveeasily placated or appeased

Derived Forms

ˌplacability (ˌplacaˈbility) or placableness (ˈplacableness) nounplacably (ˈplacably) adverb

Word Origin

C15: via Old French from Latin plācābilis, from plācāre to appease; related to placēre to please

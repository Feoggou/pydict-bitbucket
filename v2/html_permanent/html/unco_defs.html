unco1

(ˈʌŋkəʊ ) Scottish
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: uncoer or uncoest1. unfamiliar, strange, or odd2. remarkable or strikingadverb3. very; extremely4. See the unco guidnounWord forms: plural uncos or uncoes5. a novel or remarkable person or thing6. obsolete a stranger7. (plural) news

Definitions
phraseIf you say that something is of necessity the case, you mean that it is the case because nothing else is possible or practical in the circumstances. [formal] ⇒ The assembly line of necessity kept moving. ⇒ Negotiations between the enemies are of necessity indirect. →  necessity

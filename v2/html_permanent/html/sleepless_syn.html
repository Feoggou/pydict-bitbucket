Synonyms of sleepless

sleepless

adjective

  1. wakeful, disturbed, restless, insomniac, unsleeping
    • I have sleepless nights worrying about her.
  2. alert, vigilant, watchful, wide awake, unsleeping
    • his sleepless vigilance

butane

(ˈbjuːteɪn , bjuːˈteɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna colourless flammable gaseous alkane that exists in two isomeric forms, both of which occur in natural gas. The stable isomer, n-butane, is used mainly in the manufacture of rubber and fuels (such as Calor Gas). Formula: C4H10
Word origin
C19: from but(yl) + -ane

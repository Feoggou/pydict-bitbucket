Definitions

Word forms: Austrians
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveAustrian means belonging or relating to Austria, or to its people or culture. ⇒ ...the Austrian government.2. countable nounAn Austrian is a person who comes from Austria.

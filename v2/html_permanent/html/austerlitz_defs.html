Austerlitz

(ˈɔːstəlɪts )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna town in the Czech Republic, in Moravia: site of Napoleon's victory over the Russian and Austrian armies in 1805. Pop: 1795 (2007 est). Czech name: Slavkov

laying

(ˈleɪɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. fitting ⇒ the laying of carpet2. preparation before a meal ⇒ the laying of the table3. setting down ⇒ the laying of wreathsadjective4. producing eggs ⇒ a laying hen

airtight

(ˈɛəˌtaɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. not permitting the passage of air either in or out2. having no weak points; rigid or unassailable ⇒ this categorization is hardly airtight

airtight

(eəʳtaɪt ) also air-tight
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveIf a container is airtight, its lid fits so tightly that no air can get in or out. ⇒ Store the cookies in an airtight tin.2. adjectiveAn airtight alibi, case, argument, or agreement is one that has been so carefully put together that nobody will be able to find a fault in it. [US] ⇒ Mick had an airtight alibi.regional note:   in BRIT, use watertight

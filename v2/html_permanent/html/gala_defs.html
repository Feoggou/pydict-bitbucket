gala

(ˈɡɑːlə , ˈɡeɪlə )
Definitions
noun1. a. a celebration; festive occasionb. (as modifier) ⇒ a gala occasion2. mainly British a sporting occasion involving competitions in several events ⇒ a swimming gala
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from French or Italian, from Old French gale pleasure, from Old French galer to make merry, probably of Germanic origin; compare gallant

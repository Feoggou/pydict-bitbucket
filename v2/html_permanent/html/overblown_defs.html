overblown

(ˌəʊvəˈbləʊn )
Definitions
adjective1. overdone or excessive2. bombastic; turgid ⇒ overblown prose3. (of flowers, such as the rose) past the stage of full bloom
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

overblown for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is overblown makes something seem larger, more important, or more significant than it really is. ⇒ Warnings of disaster may be overblown. ⇒ The reporting of the hostage story was fair, if sometimes overblown. ⇒ ...overblown dreams.

futurity

(fjuːˈtjʊərɪtɪ ) or futurition (ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪʃən)
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. a less common word for future2. the quality of being in the future3. a future event
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'futurity'
There would be no offspring to carry this face into futurity. Clive Barker SACRAMENTIt occurred to her that this was the pleasure of a man who scorned to assume more than the briefest fragment of futurity. Ruell, Patrick THE ONLY GAME

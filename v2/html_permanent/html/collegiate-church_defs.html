collegiate church

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Roman Catholic Church, Church of England a church that has an endowed chapter of canons and prebendaries attached to it but that is not a cathedral2. US Protestantism one of a group of churches presided over by a body of pastors3. Scottish Protestantism a church served by two or more ministers4. a large church endowed in the Middle Ages to become a school5. a chapel either endowed by or connected with a college

en suite

Powered by COBUILD
(ɒn swiːt ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]An en suite bathroom is next to a bedroom and can only be reached by a door in the bedroom. An en suite bedroom has an en suite bathroom. [British] ⇒ The master bedroom has its own en suite bathroom. ⇒ ...a small hotel with 14 en suite bedrooms.regional note:   in AM, usually use private bathroom

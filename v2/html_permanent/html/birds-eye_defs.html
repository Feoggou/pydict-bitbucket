bird's-eye

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. a. seen or photographed from high aboveb. summarizing the main points of a topic; summary (esp in the phrase bird's-eye view)2. having markings resembling birds' eyesnoun3. See bird's-eye primrose4. See bird's-eye speedwell5. any of several other plants having flowers of two contrasting colours6. a pattern in linen and cotton fabrics, made up of small diamond shapes with a dot in the centre of each7. a linen or cotton fabric with such a pattern

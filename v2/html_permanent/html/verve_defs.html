verve

(vɜːv )
Definitions
noun1. great vitality, enthusiasm, and liveliness; sparkle2. a rare word for talent
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Old French: garrulity, from Latin verba words, chatter

(vɜːʳv ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounVerve is lively and forceful enthusiasm. [written] ⇒ He looked for the dramatic, like the sunset in this painting, and painted it with great verve. ⇒ She revelled in big MGM musicals with their colour and verve.

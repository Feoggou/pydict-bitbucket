embossed

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmbɒst , US -bɔːst ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If a surface such as paper or wood is embossed with a design, the design stands up slightly from the surface. [+ with] ⇒ The paper on the walls was pale gold, embossed with swirling leaf designs.

Japanese

(ˌdʒæpəˈniːz )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Japan, its people, or their languagenoun2. plural -nese a native or inhabitant of Japan or a descendant of one3. the official language of Japan: the native language of approximately 100 million people: considered by some scholars to be part of the Altaic family of languages

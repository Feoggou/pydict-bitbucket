Tempe

(tɛmˈpi ; ˈtɛmpi ; for 2 ˈtɛmpi ) in American English
Definitions
1.  city in SC Ariz., on the Salt River: suburb of Phoenix: pop. 159,0002.  Vale ofvalley of the Piniós River in NE Thessaly, Greece, between Mounts Olympus & Ossa: anciently regarded as sacred to Apollo

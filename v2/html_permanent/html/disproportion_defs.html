disproportion

(ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. lack of proportion or equality2. an instance of disparity or inequalityverb3. (transitive) to cause to become exaggerated or unequal
Derived forms
ˌdisproportionable (ˌdisproˈportionable) adjectiveˌdisproportionableness (ˌdisproˈportionableness) nounˌdisproportionably (ˌdisproˈportionably) adverbˌdisproportional (ˌdisproˈportional) adjective

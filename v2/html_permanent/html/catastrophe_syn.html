catastrophe

noun

  1. disaster, tragedy, calamity, meltdown (informal), cataclysm, trouble, trial, blow, failure, reverse, misfortune, devastation, adversity, mishap, affliction, whammy (informal, mainly US), bummer (slang), mischance, fiasco • The world is heading towards an environmental catastrophe.

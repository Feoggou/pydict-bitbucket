ketchup

(ˈkɛtʃəp ), catchup or catsup
Definitions
nounany of various piquant sauces containing vinegar ⇒ tomato ketchup
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Chinese (Amoy) kōetsiap brine of pickled fish, from kōe seafood + tsiap sauce

moist

(mɔɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. slightly damp or wet2. saturated with or suggestive of moisture

Derived Forms

moistly (ˈmoistly) adverbmoistness (ˈmoistness) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Old French, ultimately related to Latin mūcidus musty, from mūcus mucus

whiskers

(ˈwɪskəz)
Definitions
plural noun1. the stiff sensory hairs growing on the face of a cat, rat, or other mammal2. a beard or that part of it growing on the sides of the face ⇒ The hair on his head was dark, but the whiskers on his chin were white., ⇒ men with long whiskers3. a moustache ⇒ wild, savage-looking fellows, with large whiskers, unshaven beards, and dirty faces

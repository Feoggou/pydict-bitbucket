Scot

(skɒt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a native or inhabitant of Scotland2. a member of a tribe of Celtic raiders from the north of Ireland who carried out periodic attacks against the British mainland coast from the 3rd century ad, eventually settling in N Britain during the 5th and 6th centuries

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA Scot is a person of Scottish origin.2. uncount nounScots is a dialect of the English language that is spoken in Scotland. ⇒ There are things you can express in Scots that you can't say in English.3. adjective [usu ADJ n]Scots means the same as Scottish. ⇒ ...his guttural Scots accent. ⇒ ...the Scots Tories.

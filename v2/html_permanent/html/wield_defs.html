wield

(wiːld )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to handle or use (a weapon, tool, etc)2. to exert or maintain (power or authority)3. obsolete to rule
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

wieldable (ˈwieldable) adjectivewielder (ˈwielder) noun

Word Origin

Old English wieldan, wealdan; related to Old Norse valda, Old Saxon waldan, German walten, Latin valēre to be strong

Definitions

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectivePurplish means slightly purple in colour. ⇒ The large, purplish blue flowers appear in early June.

The paint on the woodwork was everywhere a dark, purplish chocolate. Gee, Maggie LOST CHILDRENStill no hint of that strange, tell-tale purplish taste in her mouth.

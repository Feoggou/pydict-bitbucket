unconcerned

(ˌʌnkənˈsɜːnd )
Definitions
adjective1. lacking in concern or involvement2. not worried; untroubled
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnkənsɜːʳnd )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If a person is unconcerned about something, usually something that most people would care about, they are not interested in it or worried about it. [+ about] ⇒ Paul was unconcerned about what he had done. [+ by] ⇒ He seems totally unconcerned by real dangers.

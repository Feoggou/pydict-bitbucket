Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna mountain in NW Wales, in Gwynedd: the highest peak in Wales. Height: 1085 m (3560 ft). Welsh name: Yr Wyddfa

Snowdon2

(ˈsnəʊdən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun 1st Earl of, title of Antony Armstrong-Jones. born 1930, British photographer, whose work includes television documentaries, photographic books, and the design of the Snowdon Aviary, London Zoo (1965). His marriage (1960–78) to Princess Margaret ended in divorce

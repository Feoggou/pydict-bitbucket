Olympian

(əˈlɪmpɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to Mount Olympus or to the classical Greek gods2. majestic or godlike in manner or bearing3. superior to mundane considerations, esp when impractical4. of or relating to ancient Olympia or its inhabitantsnoun5. a god of Olympus6. an inhabitant or native of ancient Olympia7. a competitor in the Olympic Games

Olympian for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(əlɪmpiən )
Word forms: Olympians
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Olympian means very powerful, large, or impressive. [formal] ⇒ Getting his book into print has been an Olympian task in itself.2. countable nounAn Olympian is a competitor in the Olympic Games. ⇒ The importance of being an Olympian will vary from athlete to athlete.

bullock

(ˈbʊlək )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a gelded bull; steer2. archaic a bull calfverb3. (intransitive) Australian and New Zealand informal to work hard and long
Word origin
Old English bulluc; see bull1, -ock

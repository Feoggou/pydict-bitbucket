belfry

(ˈbɛlfrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -fries1. the part of a tower or steeple in which bells are hung2. a tower or steeple. Compare campanile3. the timber framework inside a tower or steeple on which bells are hung4. (formerly) a movable tower for attacking fortifications
Word origin
C13: from Old French berfrei, of Germanic origin; compare Middle High German bercfrit fortified tower, Medieval Latin berfredus tower

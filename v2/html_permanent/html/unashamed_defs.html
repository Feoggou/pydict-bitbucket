unashamed

(ˌʌnəˈʃeɪmd )
Definitions
adjective1. lacking moral restraints2. not embarrassed, contrite, or apologetic
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unashamedly (ˌʌnəˈʃeɪmɪdlɪ ) adverbunashamedness (ˌunaˈshamedness) noun

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnəʃeɪmd )
Definitions
adjectiveIf you describe someone's behaviour or attitude as unashamed, you mean that they are open and honest about things that other people might find embarrassing or shocking. ⇒ I grinned at him in unashamed delight. [+ of] ⇒ ...a man rightly unashamed of his own talent.unashamedly (ʌnəʃeɪmɪdli ) adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Drugs are sold unashamedly in broad daylight. ⇒ ...an unashamedly traditional view of geology.

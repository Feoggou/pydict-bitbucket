lessen

Powered by COBUILD
(lesən ) for learners of English
Word forms: lessens , lessening , lessened
Definitions
ergative verb & verbIf something lessens or you lessen it, it becomes smaller in size, amount, degree, or importance. [V] ⇒ He is used to a lot of attention from his wife, which will inevitably lessen when the baby is born. [V n] ⇒ Make sure that your immunisations are up to date to lessen the risk of serious illness.lessening uncount noun [+ of/in] ⇒ ...increased trade and a lessening of tension on the border.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If someone is seasick when they are travelling in a boat, they vomit or feel sick because of the way the boat is moving. ⇒ It was quite rough at times, and she was seasick.seasickness uncount noun ⇒ He was very prone to seasickness and already felt queasy.

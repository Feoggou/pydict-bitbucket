encrusted

or incrusted (ɪnˈkrʌstɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. covered or overlaid with a crust or hard coating ⇒ snow-encrusted mountain paths, ⇒ blankets drenched and encrusted with mud2. lavishly and thickly decorated ⇒ a blue uniform coat that was thickly encrusted with gold loops, ⇒ a jewel-encrusted ring, ⇒ a beautiful dress encrusted with jewels

encrusted

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnkrʌstɪd ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf an object is encrusted with something, its surface is covered with a layer of that thing. [+ with] ⇒ ...a blue uniform coat that was thickly encrusted with gold loops.Encrusted is also a combining form. ⇒ ...a jewel-encrusted ring. ⇒ ...snow-encrusted mountain paths.

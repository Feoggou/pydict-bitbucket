marvellous

or US marvelous (ˈmɑːvələs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. causing great wonder, surprise, etc; extraordinary2. improbable or incredible3. excellent; splendid

Derived Forms

marvellously (ˈmarvellously) or US marvelously (ˈmarvelously) adverbmarvellousness (ˈmarvellousness) or US marvelousness (ˈmarvelousness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
regional note:   in AM, use marvelousgraded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as marvellous, you are emphasizing that they are very good. ⇒ He certainly is a marvellous actor. ⇒ She made marvellous fish pie. ⇒ He looked marvellous.marvellously graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj/adv] ⇒ He always painted marvellously. ⇒ Isabel gave me a marvellously funny birthday card.

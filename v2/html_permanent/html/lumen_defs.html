lumen

(ˈluːmɪn )
Definitions
noun plural -mens or -mina (-mɪnə)1. the derived SI unit of luminous flux; the flux emitted in a solid angle of 1 steradian by a point source having a uniform intensity of 1 candela. Symbol: lm2. anatomy a passage, duct, or cavity in a tubular organ3. a cavity within a plant cell enclosed by the cell walls
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lumenal (ˈlumenal) or luminal (ˈluminal) adjective

Word Origin

C19: New Latin, from Latin: light, aperture

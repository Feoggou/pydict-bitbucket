entertainment

(ˌɛntəˈteɪnmənt )
Definitions
noun1. the act or art of entertaining or state of being entertained2. an act, production, etc, that entertains; diversion; amusement

variable nounEntertainment consists of performances of plays and films, and activities such as reading and watching television, that give people pleasure. ⇒ ...the world of entertainment and international stardom. ⇒ There were feasts and banquets, theatrical entertainments and sporting competitions between local groups.

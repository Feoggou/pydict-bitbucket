Synonyms of tease

tease

verb

  1. mock, bait, wind up (British, slang), worry, bother, provoke, annoy, needle (informal), plague (informal), rag, rib (informal), torment, ridicule, taunt, aggravate (informal), badger, pester, vex, goad, bedevil, take the mickey out of (informal), twit, take the piss out of (taboo & slang), chaff, guy (informal), gibe, pull someone's leg (informal), make fun of
    • He teased me mercilessly about going there.
  2. tantalize, lead on, flirt with, titillate
    • When did you last flirt with him or tease him?

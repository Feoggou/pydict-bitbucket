afield

(əˈfiːld )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb, adjective (postpositive)1. away from one's usual surroundings or home (esp in the phrase far afield)2. off the subject; away from the point (esp in the phrase far afield)3. in or to the field, esp the battlefield

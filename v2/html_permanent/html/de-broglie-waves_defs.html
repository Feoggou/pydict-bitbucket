de Broglie waves

(də ˈbrəʊɡlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounphysics the set of waves that represent the behaviour of an elementary particle, or some atoms and molecules, under certain conditions. The de Broglie wavelength, λ, is given by λ = h/mv, where h is the Planck constant, m the mass, and v the velocity of the particle. Also called: matter waves
Word origin
C20: named after Prince Louis Victor de Broglie (1892–1987), French physicist

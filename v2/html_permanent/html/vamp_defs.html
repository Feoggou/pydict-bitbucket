vamp1

(væmp ) informal
Definitions
noun1. a seductive woman who exploits men by use of her sexual charmsverb2. to exploit (a man) in the fashion of a vamp
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vamper (ˈvamper) nounvampish (ˈvampish) adjectivevampishly (ˈvampishly) adverb

Word Origin

C20: short for vampire

vamp2

(væmp )
Definitions
noun1. something patched up to make it look new2. the reworking of a theme, story, etc3. an improvised accompaniment, consisting largely of chords4. the front part of the upper of a shoeverb5. (transitive; often foll by up) to give a vamp to; make a renovation of6. to improvise (an accompaniment) to (a tune)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vamper (ˈvamper) noun

Word Origin

C13: from Old French avantpié the front part of a shoe (hence, something patched), from avant- fore- + pié foot, from Latin pēs

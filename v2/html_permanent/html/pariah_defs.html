pariah

(pəˈraɪə , ˈpærɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a social outcast2. (formerly) a member of a low caste in S India

Word Origin

C17: from Tamil paraiyan drummer, from parai drum; so called because members of the caste were the drummers at festivals

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf you describe someone as a pariah, you mean that other people dislike them so much that they refuse to associate with them. [disapproval] ⇒ His landlady had treated him like a dangerous criminal, a pariah.

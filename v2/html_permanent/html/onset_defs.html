onset

(ˈɒnˌsɛt )
Definitions
noun1. an attack; assault2. a start; beginning

onset for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ɒnset )
Definitions
singular nounThe onset of something is the beginning of it, used especially to refer to something unpleasant. [+ of] ⇒ Most of the passes have been closed with the onset of winter. ⇒ With the onset of war, oil prices climbed past $30 a barrel.

disheartening

(dɪsˈhɑːtənɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivemaking one feel disappointed and less confident or less hopeful ⇒ The disheartening statistics say that a woman's chances of conceiving through IVF after age 35 nosedive., ⇒ The right suffered a disheartening defeat at the elections., ⇒ This is a very disheartening experience for a young child.
Derived forms
dishearteningly (disˈhearteningly) adverb

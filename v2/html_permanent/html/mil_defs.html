mil1

(mɪl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a unit of length equal to one thousandth of an inch2. an obsolete pharmaceutical unit of volume equal to one millilitre3. a unit of angular measure, used in gunnery, equal to one sixty-four-hundredth of a circumference

Word Origin

C18: short for Latin millēsimus thousandth

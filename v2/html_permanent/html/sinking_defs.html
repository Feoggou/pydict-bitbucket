sinking

(ˈsɪŋkɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna. a feeling in the stomach caused by hunger or uneasinessb. (as modifier) ⇒ a sinking feeling

sinking

Powered by COBUILD
(sɪŋkɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]If you have a sinking feeling, you suddenly become depressed or lose hope. ⇒ I began to have a sinking feeling that I was not going to get rid of her.2. → see also sink

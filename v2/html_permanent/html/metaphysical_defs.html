metaphysical

(ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪkəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. relating to or concerned with metaphysics2. (of a statement or theory) having the form of an empirical hypothesis, but in fact immune from empirical testing and therefore (in the view of the logical positivists) literally meaningless3. (popularly) abstract, abstruse, or unduly theoretical4. incorporeal; supernatural

Derived Forms

ˌmetaphysically (ˌmetaˈphysically) adverb

Metaphysical

(ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪkəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. denoting or relating to certain 17th-century poets who combined intense feeling with ingenious thought and often used elaborate imagery and conceits. Notable among them were Donne, Herbert, and Marvellnoun2. a poet of this group

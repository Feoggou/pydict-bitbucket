emergence

(ɪˈmɜːdʒəns )
Definitions
noun1. the act or process of emerging2. an outgrowth, such as a prickle, that contains no vascular tissue and does not develop into stem, leaf, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

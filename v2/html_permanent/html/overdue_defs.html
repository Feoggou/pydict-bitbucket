overdue for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(oʊvəʳdjuː , -duː)
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you say that a change or an event is overdue, you mean that you think it should have happened before now. ⇒ This debate is long overdue. ⇒ I'll go home and pay an overdue visit to my mother.2. graded adjective & adjectiveOverdue sums of money have not been paid, even though it is later than the date on which they should have been paid. ⇒ Teachers have joined a strike aimed at forcing the government to pay overdue salaries and allowances.3. graded adjective & adjectiveAn overdue library book has not been returned to the library, even though the date on which it should have been returned has passed.

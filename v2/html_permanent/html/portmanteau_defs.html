portmanteau

(pɔːtˈmæntəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -teaus or -teaux (-təʊz)1. (formerly) a large travelling case made of stiff leather, esp one hinged at the back so as to open out into two compartments2. (modifier) embodying several uses or qualities ⇒ the heroine is a portmanteau figure of all the virtues. Also called: portmantle

Word Origin

C16: from French: cloak carrier, from porter to carry + manteau cloak, mantle

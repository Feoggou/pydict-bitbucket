seventh heaven

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the final state of eternal bliss, esp according to Talmudic and Muslim eschatology2. a state of supreme happiness

Word Origin

C19: so named from the belief that there are seven levels of heaven, the seventh and most exalted being the abode of God and the angels

seventh heaven

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [in N]If you say that you are in seventh heaven, you mean that you are in a state of complete happiness. [informal] ⇒ After I was given my first camera I was in seventh heaven.

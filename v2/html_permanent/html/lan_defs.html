Definitions
countable nounA LAN is a group of personal computers and associated equipment that are linked by cable, for example in an office building, and that share a communications line. LAN is an abbreviation for 'local area network'. [computing] ⇒ You can take part in multiplayer games either on a LAN network or via the internet.

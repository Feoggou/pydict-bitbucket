evident

Powered by COBUILD
(evɪdənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is evident, you notice it easily and clearly. ⇒ His footprints were clearly evident in the heavy dust. ⇒ The threat of inflation is already evident in bond prices. ⇒ ...the best-publicised cases of evident injustice.2. graded adjective & adjectiveYou use evident to show that you are certain about a situation or fact and your interpretation of it. [emphasis] ⇒ It was evident that she had once been a beauty. ⇒ The cities are bombarded day after day in an evident effort to force their surrender.3. → See also self-evident

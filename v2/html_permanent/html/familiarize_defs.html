familiarize

or familiarise (fəˈmɪljəˌraɪz )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to make (oneself or someone else) familiar, as with a particular subject2. to make (something) generally known or accepted
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

familiarization (faˌmiliariˈzation) or familiarisation (faˌmiliariˈsation) nounfamiliarizer (faˈmiliarˌizer) or familiariser (faˈmiliarˌiser) noun

Definitions
regional note:   in BRIT, also use familiariseverbIf you familiarize yourself with something, or if someone familiarizes you with it, you learn about it and start to understand it. [V pron-refl + with] ⇒ I was expected to familiarise myself with the keyboard. [V n + with] ⇒ The goal of the experiment was to familiarize the people with the new laws.

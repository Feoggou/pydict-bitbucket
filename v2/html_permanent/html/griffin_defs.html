griffin1

(ˈɡrɪfɪn ), griffon or gryphon
Definitions
nouna winged monster with an eagle-like head and the body of a lion
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French grifon, from Latin grӯphus, from Greek grups, from grupos hooked

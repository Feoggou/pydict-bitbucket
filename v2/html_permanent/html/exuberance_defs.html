exuberance

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪgzjuːbərəns , US -zuːb- ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounExuberance is behaviour which is energetic, excited, and cheerful. ebullience, effervescence ⇒ Her burst of exuberance and her brightness overwhelmed me.2. uncount noun [oft N of n]If you talk about the exuberance of something, you like it because it is lively, exciting, and full of energy and life. vitality ⇒ The sheer exuberance of the sculpture was exhilarating.

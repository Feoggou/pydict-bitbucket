Wilkes

(wɪlks )
Definitions
noun1.  Charles. 1798–1877, US explorer of Antarctica2.  John. 1727–97, English politician, who was expelled from the House of Commons and outlawed for writing scurrilous articles about the government. He became a champion of parliamentary reform

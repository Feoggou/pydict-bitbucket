melon

(ˈmɛlən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of several varieties of two cucurbitaceous vines, cultivated for their edible fruit. See muskmelon, watermelon2. the fruit of any of these plants, which has a hard rind and juicy flesh3. See cut a melon

Word Origin

C14: via Old French from Late Latin mēlo, shortened form of mēlopepō, from Greek mēlopepōn, from mēlon apple + pepōn gourd

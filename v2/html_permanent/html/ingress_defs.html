ingress

(ˈɪŋɡrɛs )
Definitions
noun1. the act of going or coming in; an entering2. a way in; entrance3. the right or permission to enter4. astronomy another name for immersion (sense 2)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ingression (ɪnˈɡrɛʃən ) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin ingressus, from ingredī to go in, from gradī to step, go

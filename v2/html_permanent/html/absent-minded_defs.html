Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is absent-minded forgets things or does not pay attention to what they are doing, often because they are thinking about something else. ⇒ In his later life he became even more absent-minded.absent-mindedly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Elizabeth absent-mindedly picked a thread from his lapel.absent-mindedness uncount noun [oft with poss] ⇒ You will have to put up with Grace's absent-mindedness.

risible

(ˈrɪzɪbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. having a tendency to laugh2. causing laughter; ridiculous
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

risibly (ˈrisibly) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Late Latin rīsibilis, from Latin rīdēre to laugh

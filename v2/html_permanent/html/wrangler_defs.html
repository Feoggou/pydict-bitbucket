wrangler

(ˈræŋɡlə )
Definitions
noun1. one who wrangles2. Western US and Canadian a herder; cowboy3. a person who handles or controls animals involved in the making of a film or television programme ⇒ a snake wrangler4. British (at Cambridge University) a candidate who has obtained first-class honours in Part II of the mathematics tripos. The wrangler with the highest marks is called the senior wrangler

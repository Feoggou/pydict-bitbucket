raked

(reɪkt)
Definitions
adjective1. constructed with a backward slope ⇒ The action takes place on a steeply raked stage.2. nautical (of a ship's mast or funnel) inclined from the vertical by a perceptible degree, esp towards the stern ⇒ the dear old Waverley, with her raked funnels and churning paddles

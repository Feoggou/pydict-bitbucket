January

(ˈdʒænjʊərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ariesthe first month of the year, consisting of 31 days
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin Jānuārius, from adj: (month) of Janus1

January for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(dʒænjəri , US -jueri )

Definitions
variable nounJanuary is the first month of the year in the Western calendar. ⇒ We always have snow in January. ⇒ She was born on January 6, 1946. ⇒ I haven't seen my own daughter since last January.

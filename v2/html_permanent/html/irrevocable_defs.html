irrevocable

(ɪˈrɛvəkəbəl )
Definitions
adjectivenot able to be revoked, changed, or undone; unalterable
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

irrevocable

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪrevəkəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveIf a decision, action, or change is irrevocable, it cannot be changed or reversed. [formal] ⇒ It may well be worth waiting for better times before making any irrevocable commitment. ⇒ He said the decision was irrevocable.irrevocably (ɪrevəkəbli ) adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj] ⇒ My relationships with friends have been irrevocably altered by my illness.

snack

(snæk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a light quick meal eaten between or in place of main meals2. a sip or bite3. rare a share4. Australian informal a very easy taskverb5. (intransitive) to eat a snack
Word origin
C15: probably from Middle Dutch snacken, variant of snappen to snap

(snæk )
Word forms: snacks , snacking , snacked
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA snack is a simple meal that is quick to cook and to eat. ⇒ Lunch was a snack in the fields.2. countable nounA snack is something such as a chocolate bar that you eat between meals. ⇒ Do you eat sweets, cakes or sugary snacks?3. verbIf you snack, you eat snacks between meals. [V + on] ⇒ Instead of snacking on crisps and chocolate, nibble on celery or carrot. [V] ⇒ She would improve her diet if she ate less fried food and snacked less.

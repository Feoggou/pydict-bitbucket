Palestinian

(ˌpælɪˈstɪnɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Palestinenoun2. a native or inhabitant of the former British mandate, or their descendants, esp such Arabs now living in the Palestinian Administered Territories, Jordan, Lebanon, or Israel, or as refugees from Israeli-occupied territory

(pælɪstɪniən )
Word forms: Palestinians
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectivePalestinian means belonging or relating to the region between the River Jordan and the Mediterranean Sea which used to be called Palestine, or to the Arabs who come from this region.2. countable noun [usu pl]A Palestinian is an Arab who comes from the region that used to be called Palestine.

ignominy

(ˈɪɡnəˌmɪnɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -minies1. disgrace or public shame; dishonour2. a cause of disgrace; a shameful act
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ignominious (ˌignoˈminious) adjectiveignominiously (ˌignoˈminiously) adverbignominiousness (ˌignoˈminiousness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin ignōminia disgrace, from ig- (see in-2) + nōmen name, reputation

rainy

(ˈreɪnɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: rainier or rainiest1. characterized by a large rainfall ⇒ a rainy climate2. wet or showery; bearing rain

(reɪni ) for learners of English

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]During a rainy day, season, or period it rains a lot. ⇒ They walked along the promenade on a rainy night. ⇒ The rainy season in the Andes normally starts in December.2. →  for a rainy day

Highlands

(ˈhaɪləndz )
Definitions
noun the Highlands1. a. the part of Scotland that lies to the northwest of the great fault that runs from Dumbarton to Stonehavenb. a smaller area consisting of the mountainous north of Scotland: distinguished by Gaelic culture2. (often not capital) the highland region of any country

fast-growing

(ˌfɑːstˈɡrəʊɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. growing very quickly ⇒ They are very fast growing, reaching maturity at two years.2. expanding very quickly ⇒ the world's fastest growing economy, ⇒ It is one of the fastest-growing areas of surgical innovation.

West Indian

Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to the West Indies, its inhabitants, or their language or culture2. native to or derived from the West Indiesnoun3. a native or inhabitant of the West Indies4. a person of West Indian descent▶ USAGE Using West Indian to refer to people of this ancestry living in Britain may cause offence. Possible alternatives are Black and Afro-Caribbean

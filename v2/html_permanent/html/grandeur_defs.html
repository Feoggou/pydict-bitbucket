grandeur

(ˈɡrændʒə )
Definitions
noun1. personal greatness, esp when based on dignity, character, or accomplishments2. magnificence; splendour3. pretentious or bombastic behaviour

grandeur

Powered by COBUILD
(grændʒəʳ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounIf something such as a building or a piece of scenery has grandeur, it is impressive because of its size, its beauty, or its power. ⇒ ...the grandeur and natural beauty of South America. ⇒ Only inside do you appreciate the church's true grandeur.2. uncount noun [oft poss N]Someone's grandeur is the great importance and social status that they have, or think they have. ⇒ He is wholly concerned with his own grandeur.3. → delusions of grandeur
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

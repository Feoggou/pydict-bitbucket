hereby

Powered by COBUILD
(hɪəʳbaɪ ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [ADV before v]You use hereby when officially or formally saying what you are doing. [formal] ⇒ I hereby sentence you for life after all the charges against you have been proven true. ⇒ You are hereby appointed Sub-Lieutenant RNVR of HMS Tartar. ⇒ I am offended that your entertainment expert should make such a mistake and hereby request a formal apology.

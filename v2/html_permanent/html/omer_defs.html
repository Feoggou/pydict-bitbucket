(ˈəʊmə )
Definitions
nounan ancient Hebrew unit of dry measure equal to one tenth of an ephah

Word Origin

C17: from Hebrew `ōmer a measure

Omer

(ˈəʊmə )
Definitions
nounJudaism a period of seven weeks extending from the second day of Passover to the first day of Shavuoth, and observed as a period of semimourning
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

named because sacrifices of an omer of grain were made

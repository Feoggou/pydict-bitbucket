razor

(ˈreɪzə )
Definitions
noun1. a sharp implement used esp by men for shaving the face2. See on a razor's edgeverb3. (transitive) to cut or shave with a razor
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old French raseor, from raser to shave; see raze

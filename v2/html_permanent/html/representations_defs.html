representations

(ˌrɛprɪzɛnˈteɪʃənz)
Definitions
plural nounstatements of facts, true or alleged, esp set forth by way of remonstrance or expostulation ⇒ The enquiry arose from representations made by local residents., ⇒ We will make representations at whatever level is necessary., ⇒ The commission ought to make representations to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission., ⇒ The POA will be making representations to the Home Secretary on the issue.

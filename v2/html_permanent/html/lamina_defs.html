lamina

(ˈlæmɪnə )
Definitions
noun plural -nae (-ˌniː) or -nas1. a thin plate or layer, esp of bone or mineral2. botany the flat blade of a leaf, petal, or thallus
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

laminar (ˈlaminar), laminary (ˈlaminary), laminose (ˈlæmɪˌnəʊs , -ˌnəʊz) or laminous (ˈlæmɪnəs) adjective

Word Origin

C17: New Latin, from Latin: thin plate

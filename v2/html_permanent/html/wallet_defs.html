wallet

(ˈwɒlɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a small folding case, usually of leather, for holding paper money, documents, etc2. a bag used to carry tools3. archaic, mainly British a rucksack or knapsack
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: of Germanic origin; compare Old English weallian, Old High German wallōn to roam, German wallen to go on a pilgrimage

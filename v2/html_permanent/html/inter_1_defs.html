inter

(ɪnˈtɜː )
Definitions
verb -ters, -terring or -terred(transitive) to place (a body) in the earth; bury, esp with funeral rites
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French enterrer, from Latin in-2 + terra earth

bathed

(beɪðd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. covered, esp with a liquid ⇒ Chantal was writhing in pain and bathed in perspiration., ⇒ bathed in sweat2. suffused (with some quality) ⇒ a relationship bathed in trust, ⇒ a physical sensation of being bathed in love, ⇒ bathed in sunshine

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveIf someone is bathed in sweat, they are sweating a great deal. [+ in] ⇒ Chantal was writhing in pain and bathed in perspiration.2. adjectiveIf someone is bathed in a particular emotion such as love, they feel it constantly in a pleasant way. [literary] [+ in] ⇒ ...a physical sensation of being bathed in love. ⇒ ...a relationship bathed in trust.

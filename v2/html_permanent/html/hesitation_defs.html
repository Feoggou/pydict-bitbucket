hesitation

Powered by COBUILD
(hezɪteɪʃən ) for learners of English
Word forms: hesitations
Definitions
1. variable nounHesitation is an unwillingness to do something, or a delay in doing it, because you are uncertain, worried, or embarrassed about it. [+ in] ⇒ He promised there would be no more hesitations in pursuing reforms. ⇒ ...the prime minister's hesitation to accept a ceasefire.2. → See also hesitate3. →  have no hesitation4. →  without hesitation

refrigerate

(rɪˈfrɪdʒəˌreɪt )
Definitions
verbto make or become frozen or cold, esp for preservative purposes; chill or freeze
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

refrigeration (reˌfrigerˈation) nounrefrigerative (reˈfrigerative) adjectiverefrigeratory (reˈfrigeratory) adjective, noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin refrīgerāre to make cold, from re- + frīgus cold

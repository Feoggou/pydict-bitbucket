(dɪsˈɡreɪst)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivehaving lost the approval and respect of the public or of people in authority ⇒ the disgraced leader of the coup, ⇒ Gary Glitter, the disgraced former rock star

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]You use disgraced to describe someone whose bad behaviour has caused them to lose the approval and respect of the public or of people in authority. ⇒ ...the disgraced leader of the coup.

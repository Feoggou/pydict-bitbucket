mislay

(mɪsˈleɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -lays, -laying or -laid (transitive)1. to lose (something) temporarily, esp by forgetting where it is2. to lay (something) badly

Derived Forms

mislayer (misˈlayer) noun

(mɪsleɪ )
Word forms: mislays , mislaying , mislaid
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf you mislay something, you put it somewhere and then forget where you have put it. [V n] ⇒ I appear to have mislaid my jumper.

Similar Words

lose, misplace, miss, be unable to find, lose track of, be unable to put or lay your hand on, forget the whereabouts of

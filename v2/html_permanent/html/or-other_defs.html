or other

Definitions
(preceded by a phrase or word with some) used to add vagueness to the preceding pronoun, noun, noun phrase, or adverb ⇒ some dog or other bit him, ⇒ he's somewhere or other▷ See other

or other for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
or somehow or other
Definitions
phraseYou use or other in expressions like somehow or other and someone or other to indicate that you cannot or do not want to be more precise about the information that you are giving. [vagueness] ⇒ I was going to have him called away from the house on some pretext or other. ⇒ The Foundation is holding a dinner in honour of something or other. ⇒ Somehow or other he's involved. →  other

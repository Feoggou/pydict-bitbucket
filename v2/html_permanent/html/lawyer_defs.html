lawyer

(ˈlɔːjə , ˈlɔɪə )
Definitions
noun1. a member of the legal profession, esp a solicitor. See also advocate, barrister, solicitor2. a popular name for burbot
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from law1

encampment

(ɪnˈkæmpmənt )
Definitions
noun1. the act of setting up a camp2. the place where a camp, esp a military camp, is set up
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

encampment

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnkæmpmənt ) for learners of English
Word forms: encampments
Definitions
countable nounAn encampment is a group of tents or other shelters in a particular place, especially when they are used by soldiers, refugees, or gypsies. ⇒ ...a large military encampment.

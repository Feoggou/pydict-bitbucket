Synonyms of mop

mop

noun

  1. squeegee, sponge, swab
    • She was standing outside the door with a mop and bucket.
  2. mane, shock, mass, tangle, mat, thatch
    • He was dark-eyed with a mop of tight curls.

verb

  1. clean, wash, wipe, sponge, swab, squeegee
    • There was a woman mopping the stairs.
  • See mop something up

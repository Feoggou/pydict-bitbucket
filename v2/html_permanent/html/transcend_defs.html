transcend

(trænˈsɛnd )
Definitions
verb1. to go above or beyond (a limit, expectation, etc), as in degree or excellence2. (transitive) to be superior to3. philosophy, theology (esp of the Deity) to exist beyond (the material world)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

transcendingly (transˈcendingly) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Latin trānscendere to climb over, from trans- + scandere to climb

Semitic

(sɪˈmɪtɪk ) or less commonly Shemitic
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a branch or subfamily of the Afro-Asiatic family of languages that includes Arabic, Hebrew, Aramaic, Amharic, and such ancient languages as Akkadian and Phoenicianadjective2. denoting, relating to, or belonging to this group of languages3. denoting, belonging to, or characteristic of any of the peoples speaking a Semitic language, esp the Jews or the Arabs4. another word for Jewish

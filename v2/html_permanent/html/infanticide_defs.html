infanticide

(ɪnˈfæntɪˌsaɪd )
Definitions
noun1. the killing of an infant2. the practice of killing newborn infants, still prevalent in some primitive tribes3. a person who kills an infant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a person or an organization as acquisitive, you do not approve of them because you think they are too concerned with getting new possessions. [disapproval] ⇒ We live in an acquisitive society. ⇒ The most acquisitive firms tend to be engineering groups.acquisitiveness uncount noun ⇒ His villa is filled with evidence of his acquisitiveness.

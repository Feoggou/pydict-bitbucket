Diana

(daɪˈænə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the virginal Roman goddess of the hunt and the moon. Greek counterpart: Artemis2.  title Diana, Princess of Wales, original name Lady Diana Frances Spencer. 1961–97, she married Charles, Prince of Wales, in 1981; they were divorced in 1996: died in a car crash

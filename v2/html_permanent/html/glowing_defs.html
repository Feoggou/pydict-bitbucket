glowing

(ˈɡləʊɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. emitting a steady bright light without flames ⇒ glowing embers2. warm and rich in colour ⇒ the room was decorated in glowing shades of gold and orange3. flushed and rosy, as from exercise or excitement ⇒ glowing cheeks4. displaying or indicative of extreme satisfaction, pride, or emotion ⇒ he gave a glowing account of his son's achievements
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

glowingly (ˈglowingly) adverb

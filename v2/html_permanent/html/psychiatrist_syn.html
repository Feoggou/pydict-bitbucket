Synonyms of psychiatrist

psychiatrist

noun

  1. psychotherapist, analyst, therapist, psychologist, shrink (slang), psychoanalyst, psychoanalyser, headshrinker (slang)
    • He has been seeing a psychiatrist for years.

QUOTATIONS:
We believe that civilization has been created under the pressure of the exigencies of life at the cost of satisfaction of the instinctsSigmund Freud – Introductory Lectures on Psychoanalysis
Anyone who goes to see a psychiatrist needs his head examinedSamuel Goldwyn
To us he is no more a person now but a whole climate of opinionW.H. Auden (of Sigmund Freud) – In Memory of Sigmund Freud

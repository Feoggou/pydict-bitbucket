fife

(faɪf )
Definitions
noun1. a small high-pitched flute similar to the piccolo and usually having no keys, used esp in military bandsverb2. to play (music) on a fife
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fifer (ˈfifer) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Old High German pfīfa; see pipe1

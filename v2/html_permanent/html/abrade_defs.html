abrade

(əˈbreɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(transitive) to scrape away or wear down by friction; erode
Derived forms
aˈbradant nounaˈbrader noun
Word origin
C17: from Latin abrādere to scrape away, from ab-1 + rādere to scrape

verbTo abrade something means to scrape or wear down its surface by rubbing it. [formal] [be V-ed] ⇒ My skin was abraded and very tender.

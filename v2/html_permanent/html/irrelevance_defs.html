irrelevance

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪrelɪvəns ) for learners of English
Word forms: irrelevances
Definitions
1. uncount nounIf you talk about the irrelevance of something, you mean that it is irrelevant. [+ of] ⇒ ...the utter irrelevance of the debate.2. countable nounIf you describe something as an irrelevance, you have a low opinion of it because it is not important in a situation. irrelevancy ⇒ The Patriotic Front has been a political irrelevance.

glutted

(ˈɡlʌtɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. fed or supplied beyond capacity2. supplied with a commodity in excess of the demand for it ⇒ The market became glutted with computer games., ⇒ The market may be glutted, leading to a fall in prices.3. crammed full or choked up ⇒ The region is glutted with hospitals.

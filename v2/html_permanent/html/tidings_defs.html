Word Origin

tidings

Powered by COBUILD
(taɪdɪŋz ) for learners of English
Definitions
plural noun [usu adj N]You can use tidings to refer to news that someone tells you. [formal, old-fashioned] ⇒ He hated always to be the bearer of bad tidings. ⇒ I dare to hope that your heart will be touched by the tidings of my survival. [Also + of]

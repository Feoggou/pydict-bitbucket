cypress1

(ˈsaɪprəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any coniferous tree of the N temperate genus Cupressus, having dark green scalelike leaves and rounded cones: family Cupressaceae. See also Leyland cypress2. any of several similar and related trees, such as the widely cultivated Chamaecyparis lawsoniana (Lawson's cypress), of the western US3. any of various other coniferous trees, esp the swamp cypress4. the wood of any of these trees
Word origin
Old English cypresse, from Latin cyparissus, from Greek kuparissos; related to Latin cupressus

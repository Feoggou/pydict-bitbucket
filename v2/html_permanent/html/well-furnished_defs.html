well-furnished

Definitions
adjective (well furnished when postpositive)1. (of a room, house, etc) fitted out or decorated with attractive or good quality furniture, carpets, etc ⇒ well furnished with tapestries and porcelain2. amply stocked, equipped, or supplied ⇒ he was well furnished with notebooks

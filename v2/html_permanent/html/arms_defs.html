arms

(ɑːmz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. weapons collectively. See also small arms2. military exploits ⇒ prowess in arms3. the official heraldic symbols of a family, state, etc, including a shield with distinctive devices, and often supports, a crest, or other insignia4. See bear arms5. See in arms6. See lay down one's arms7. See present arms8. See take arms9. See to arms!10. See up in arms
Word origin
C13: from Old French armes, from Latin arma; see arm2

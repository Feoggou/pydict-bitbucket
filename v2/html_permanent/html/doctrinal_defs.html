doctrinal

Powered by COBUILD
(dɒktraɪnəl , US dɑːktrɪnəl )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Doctrinal means relating to doctrines. [formal] ⇒ Doctrinal differences were vigorously debated among religious leaders.
Example sentences containing 'doctrinal'
They kept their children away from school because of squabbles over uniform - or doctrinal teaching. Hilton, John Buxton THE INNOCENTS AT HOME (A SUPERINTENDENT KENWORTHY NOVEL)

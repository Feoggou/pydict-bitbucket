weekly

(ˈwiːklɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. happening or taking place once a week or every week2. determined or calculated by the weekadverb3. once a week or every weeknoun plural -lies4. a newspaper or magazine issued every week
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

weekly for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(wiːkli )
Word forms: weeklies
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]A weekly event or publication happens or appears once a week or every week. ⇒ Each course comprises 10-12 informal weekly meetings. ⇒ We go and do the weekly shopping every Thursday. ⇒ ...a weekly newspaper.Weekly is also an adverb. ⇒ The group meets weekly. ⇒ ...a magazine published weekly since 2 January 1909.2. adjective [ADJ n]Weekly quantities or rates relate to a period of one week. ⇒ In addition to my weekly wage, I got a lot of tips.3. countable nounA weekly is a newspaper or magazine that is published once a week. ⇒ Two of the four national daily papers are to become weeklies.

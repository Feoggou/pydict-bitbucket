sylph

(sɪlf )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a slender graceful girl or young woman2. any of a class of imaginary beings assumed to inhabit the air. Also (rare): sylphid

Derived Forms

sylphˌlike (ˈsylphˌlike) or rare sylphic (ˈsylphic), sylphish (ˈsylphish), sylphy (ˈsylphy), sylphidine (ˈsylphidine) or sylphine (ˈsylphine) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from New Latin sylphus, probably coined from Latin silva wood + Greek numphē nymph

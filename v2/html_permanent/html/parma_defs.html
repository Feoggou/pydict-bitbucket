Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (Italian ˈparma) a city in N Italy, in Emilia-Romagna: capital of the duchy of Parma and Piacenza from 1545 until it became part of Italy in 1860; important food industry (esp Parmesan cheese). Pop: 163 457 (2001)2. (ˈpɑːmə ) a city in NE Ohio, south of Cleveland. Pop: 83 861 (2003 est)

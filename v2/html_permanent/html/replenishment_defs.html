replenishment

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪplenɪʃmənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounReplenishment is the process by which something is made full or complete again. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ There is a concern about replenishment of the population. ⇒ ...cell replenishment.

replenishment

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪplɛnɪʃmənt) in Retail
Definitions
noun(Retail: Merchandising) Stock replenishment is the process of adding more stock to replace what has been sold. ⇒ The purchasers buy a certain quantity of the product, and when it is sold out, replenishment from the supplier takes place. ⇒ The replenishment rate depends on sales volume as well as consistency of supply. ⇒ Stock replenishment is the process of adding more stock to replace what has been sold. ▶  replenish

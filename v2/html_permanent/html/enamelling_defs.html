enamelling

(ɪˈnæməlɪŋ) or US enameling
Definitions
noun1. the art or process of decorating something such as jewellery in enamel ⇒ the traditional English craft of enamelling2. the decoration produced in this way ⇒ The enamelling was exceptionally fine.

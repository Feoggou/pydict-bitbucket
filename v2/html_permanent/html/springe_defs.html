springe

(sprɪndʒ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a snare set to catch small wild animals or birds and consisting of a loop attached to a bent twig or branch under tensionverb2. (intransitive) to set such a snare3. (transitive) to catch (small wild animals or birds) with such a snare

Word Origin

C13: related to Old English springan to spring

spoonful

(ˈspuːnˌfʊl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -fuls1. the amount that a spoon is able to hold2. a small quantity

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounYou can refer to an amount of food resting on a spoon as a spoonful of food. [+ of] ⇒ He took a spoonful of the stew and ate it. ⇒ ...three spoonfuls of sugar.

disputed

(dɪˈspjuːtɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(of a territory) claimed by more than one sovereign state ⇒ the disputed Soviet region of Nagorno-Karabakh, ⇒ the disputed border in the state of Kashmir

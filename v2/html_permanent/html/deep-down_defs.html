Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf you know something deep down or deep down inside, you know that it is true, but you are not always conscious of it or willing to admit it to yourself. ⇒ We knew deep down that we could do it. ⇒ Deep down, we had always detested each other. →  deep

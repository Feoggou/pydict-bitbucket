originate

(əˈrɪdʒɪˌneɪt )
Definitions
verb1. to come or bring into being2. (intransitive) US and Canadian (of a bus, train, etc) to begin its journey at a specified point
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

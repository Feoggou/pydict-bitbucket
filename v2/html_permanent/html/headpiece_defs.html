headpiece

(ˈhɛdˌpiːs )
Definitions
noun1. printing a decorative band at the top of a page, chapter, etc2. any covering for the head, esp a helmet3. archaic the intellect4. a less common word for crownpiece (sense 2)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

yap

(jæp )
Definitions
verbWord forms: yaps, yapping or yapped (intransitive)1. (of a dog) to bark in quick sharp bursts; yelp2. informal to talk at length in an annoying or stupid way; jabbernoun3. a high-pitched or sharp bark; yelp4. slang annoying or stupid speech; jabber5. slang a derogatory word for mouthexclamation6. (usually reiterated) an imitation or representation of the sound of a dog yapping or people jabbering
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

yapper (ˈyapper) nounyappy (ˈyappy) adjective

Word Origin

C17: of imitative origin

Yap

(jɑːp , jæp )
Definitions
nouna group of four main islands in the W Pacific, in the W Caroline Islands: administratively a district of the US Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands from 1947; became self-governing in 1979 as part of the Federated States of Micronesia; important Japanese naval base in World War II. Pop: 11 647 (2006 est). Area: 101 sq km (39 sq miles)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

sturgeon

(ˈstɜːdʒən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nounany primitive bony fish of the family Acipenseridae, of temperate waters of the N hemisphere, having an elongated snout and rows of spines along the body: valued as a source of caviar and isinglass

Word Origin

C13: from Old French estourgeon, of Germanic origin; related to Old English styria, Old High German sturio

loch

(lɒx , lɒk )
Definitions
noun1. a Scot word for lake12. Also called: sea loch a long narrow bay or arm of the sea in Scotland
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Gaelic

Definitions
countable noun [oft in names]A loch is a large area of water in Scotland that is completely or almost completely surrounded by land. ⇒ ...twenty miles north of Loch Ness.

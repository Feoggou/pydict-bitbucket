thoroughfare

(ˈθʌrəˌfɛə )
Definitions
noun1. a road from one place to another, esp a main road2. way through or access ⇒ no thoroughfare

countable nounA thoroughfare is a main road in a town or city which usually has shops along it and a lot of traffic. [formal] ⇒ ...a busy thoroughfare.

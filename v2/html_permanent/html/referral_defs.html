Definitions
variable nounReferral is the act of officially sending someone to a person or authority that is qualified to deal with them. A referral is an instance of this. [+ to] ⇒ Legal Aid can often provide referral to other types of agencies. ⇒ Ask your doctor for a referral to a clinical psychologist.

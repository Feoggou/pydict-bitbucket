FSA

Powered by COBUILD
(ɛf ɛs eɪ) or Flexible Spending Account in Insurance
Definitions
abbreviation(Insurance: Medical insurance) In the U.S., an FSA is an account that allows employees to set aside pretax income for routine medical expenses. ⇒ When you offer your employees a medical FSA, the plan reimburses participants for eligible health care expenses with pre-tax dollars. ⇒ An FSA allows your employees to deduct pre-tax dollars to pay for qualified medical and dependent care expenses. ⇒ In the U.S., an FSA is an account that allows employees to set aside pretax income for routine medical expenses.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as tiring, you mean that it makes you tired so that you want to rest or sleep. ⇒ It had been a long and tiring day. ⇒ Travelling is tiring.
Example sentences containing 'tiring'

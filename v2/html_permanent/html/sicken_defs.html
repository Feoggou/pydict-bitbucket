sicken

(ˈsɪkən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to make or become sick, nauseated, or disgusted2. (intransitive; often foll by for) to show symptoms (of an illness)

Word forms: sickens , sickening , sickened
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. verbIf something sickens you, it makes you feel disgusted. [V n] ⇒ The notion that art should be controlled by intellectuals sickened him. [V n] ⇒ What he saw there sickened him, despite all his years of police work.2. verbIf you sicken, you become ill. [old-fashioned] [V] ⇒ Many of them sickened and died.

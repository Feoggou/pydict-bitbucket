upshot

(ˈʌpˌʃɒt )
Definitions
noun1. the final result; conclusion; outcome2. archery the final shot in a match
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from up + shot1

upshot for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌpʃɒt )
Definitions
singular nounThe upshot of a series of events or discussions is the final result of them, usually a surprising result. ⇒ The upshot is that we have lots of good but not very happy employees. ⇒ So the upshot is we're going for lunch on Friday.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

staring

(ˈstɛərɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. gazing curiously ⇒ far from the staring crowds2. having a fixed expression, because of madness, death, etc ⇒ I was thinking about the staring eyes Jessica had had when she'd been pretending to be dead., ⇒ the staring eyes of a visionary, ⇒ an excited little man with the staring eyes of a visionary
Example sentences containing 'staring'
I just sat there smoking my cigarette staring into the night sky. Hugo Wilcken THE EXECUTIONMarianne was sitting up in her bed, staring into space, wearing a hospital gown that opened at the front.

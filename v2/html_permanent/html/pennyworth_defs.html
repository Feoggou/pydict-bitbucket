pennyworth

(ˈpɛnɪˌwɜːθ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the amount that can be bought for a penny2. a small amount ⇒ he hasn't got a pennyworth of sense
Example sentences containing 'pennyworth'
One-half pennyworth of blood to this intolerable deal of bandages, eh? Elizabeth Peters LION IN THE VALLEY

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA swimmer is a person who swims, especially for sport or pleasure, or a person who is swimming. ⇒ You don't have to worry about me. I'm a good swimmer. ⇒ She wants to become an Olympic swimmer. ⇒ Apart from a few swimmers, the rest of us stretched out on the bank to sunbathe.

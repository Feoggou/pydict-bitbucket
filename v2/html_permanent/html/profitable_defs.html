Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA profitable organization or practice makes a profit. ⇒ Drug manufacturing is the most profitable business in America. [+ for] ⇒ It was profitable for them to produce large amounts of food.profitably (prɒfɪtəbli ) graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ The 28 French stores are trading profitably.profitability (prɒfɪtəbɪlɪti ) uncount noun ⇒ Changes were made in operating methods in an effort to increase profitability.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Something that is profitable results in some benefit for you. ⇒ ...collaboration which leads to a profitable exchange of personnel and ideas.profitably graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ In fact he could scarcely have spent his time more profitably.

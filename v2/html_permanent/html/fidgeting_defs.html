fidgeting

(ˈfɪdʒɪtɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. (of a person) moving about restlessly ⇒ a fidgeting official2. (of a person) fiddling with something ⇒ The fidgeting boy rubbed the front of his jersey as he spoke.

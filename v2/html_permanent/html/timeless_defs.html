timeless

(ˈtaɪmlɪs )
Definitions
adjective1. unaffected or unchanged by time; ageless2. eternal3. an archaic word for untimely

Powered by COBUILD
(taɪmləs ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as timeless, you mean that it is so good or beautiful that it cannot be affected by changes in society or fashion. ⇒ There is a timeless quality to his best work. ⇒ ...the timeless appeal of her designs.timelessness uncount noun ⇒ Maybe it was the trees that gave this place its atmosphere of mystery and timelessness.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Synonyms of inside

inside

noun

  1. interior, contents, core, nucleus, inner part, inner side
    • Cut off the top and scoop out the inside with a teaspoon.

plural noun

  1. (informal) stomach, gut, guts, belly, bowels, internal organs, innards (informal), entrails, viscera, vitals
    • My insides ached from eating too much.

adjective

  1. inner, internal, interior, inward, innermost
    • four-berth inside cabins with en suite bathrooms
    OPPOSITES:  outside, external, outer, outward, exterior, outermost
  2. confidential, private, secret, internal, exclusive, restricted, privileged, classified
    • The editor denies he had any inside knowledge.

adverb

  1. indoors, in, within, under cover
    • They chatted briefly on the doorstep before going inside.
  2. within, privately, deep down, secretly, at heart, to yourself, inwardly, in your inmost heart
    • Do you get a feeling inside when you write something you like?
  3. in prison, in jail, imprisoned, behind bars (informal), incarcerated, banged up (British, informal)
    • He's been inside three times now.

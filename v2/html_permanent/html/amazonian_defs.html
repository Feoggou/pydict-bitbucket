Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Amazonian means related to the area around the river Amazon. ⇒ ...the Amazonian rainforest.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]People sometimes describe a tall, strong woman as Amazonian. ⇒ ...an Amazonian blonde.3. adjective [usu ADJ n]Amazonian means belonging to or connected with the Amazons in Greek mythology. ⇒ ...Amazonian queens. ⇒ ...Amazonian mythology.

prothonotary

(ˌprəʊθəˈnəʊtərɪ , -trɪ, prəʊˈθɒnə-) or protonotary
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -taries(formerly) a chief clerk in certain law courts

Derived Forms

prothonotarial (prəʊˌθɒnəˈtɛərɪəl ) or proˌtonotarial (proˌtonoˈtarial) adjective

Word Origin

C15: from Medieval Latin prōthonotārius, from prōtho- proto- + Late Latin notārius notary

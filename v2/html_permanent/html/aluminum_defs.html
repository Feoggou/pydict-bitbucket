aluminum

(əˈlumənəm )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  a silvery, lightweight, easily worked, metallic chemical element that resists corrosion and is found abundantly, but only in combination: symbol, Al; at. no., 13 adjective2.  of, containing, or made of aluminum

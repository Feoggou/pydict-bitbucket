(ˈmɛʒəmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or process of measuring2. an amount, extent, or size determined by measuring3. a system of measures based on a particular standard

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA measurement is a result, usually expressed in numbers, that you obtain by measuring something. ⇒ We took lots of measurements. ⇒ The measurements are extraordinarily accurate.2. variable nounMeasurement of something is the process of measuring it in order to obtain a result expressed in numbers. [+ of] ⇒ Measurement of blood pressure can be undertaken by practice nurses.3. variable nounThe measurement of the quality, value, or effect of something is the activity of deciding how great it is. [+ of] ⇒ ...the measurement of output in the non-market sector.4. plural noun [with poss]Your measurements are the size of your waist, chest, hips, and other parts of your body, which you need to know when you are buying clothes.

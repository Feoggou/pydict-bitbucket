petticoat

(ˈpɛtɪˌkəʊt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a woman's light undergarment in the form of an underskirt or including a bodice supported by shoulder straps2. informala. a humorous or mildly disparaging name for a womanb. (as modifier) ⇒ petticoat politics

Word Origin

C15: see petty, coat

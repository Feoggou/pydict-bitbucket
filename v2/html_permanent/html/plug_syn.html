Synonyms of plug

plug

noun

  1. stopper, cork, bung, spigot, stopple
    • A plug had been inserted in the drill hole.
  2. (informal) mention, advertisement, advert (British, informal), push, promotion, publicity, puff, hype, good word, namecheck
    • The show was little more than a plug for her new film.
  3. wad, twist, cake, chew, quid, pigtail
    • a plug of tobacco

verb

  1. seal, close, stop, fill, cover, block, stuff, pack, cork, choke, stopper, bung, stop up, stopple
    • Crews are working to plug a major oil leak.
  2. (informal) mention, push, promote, publicize, advertise, build up, puff, hype, write up, namecheck
    • If I hear another actor plugging his latest book I will scream.
  3. (slang) shoot, pop, pot, blow away (slang, mainly US), gun down, pick off, put a bullet in
    • I'd pull out my .38 and plug him in the gut.
  • See plug away

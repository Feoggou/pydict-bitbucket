clover

(ˈkləʊvə ) or clovergrass (ˈkləʊvəˌɡrɑːs)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any plant of the leguminous genus Trifolium, having trifoliate leaves and dense flower heads. Many species, such as red clover, white clover, and alsike, are grown as forage plants2. any of various similar or related plants3. See sweet clover4. See pin clover5. See in clover
Word origin
Old English clāfre; related to Old High German klēo, Middle Low German klēver, Dutch klāver

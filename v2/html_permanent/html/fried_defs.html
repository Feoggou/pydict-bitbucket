fried

Powered by COBUILD
(fraɪd) in Hospitality
Definitions
adjective(Hospitality (hotel): Food and drink, cooking) Fried food is food cooked in a pan that contains hot fat or oil. ⇒ Our fried foods are cooked in olive oil. ⇒ To make the fried prawns, cook them in a shallow pan of oil with garlic and chilli. ⇒ The fried vegetables are too oily.Related wordsCompare fried with deep-fried, which means cooked in a deep pan that contains a large amount of fat or oil.

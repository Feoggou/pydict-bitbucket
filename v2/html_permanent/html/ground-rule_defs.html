Word forms: ground rules
Definitions
countable noun [usu pl]The ground rules for something are the basic principles on which future action will be based. [+ for/of] ⇒ The panel says the ground rules for the current talks should be maintained.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

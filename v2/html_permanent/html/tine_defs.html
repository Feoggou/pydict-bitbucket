tine

(taɪn )
Definitions
noun1. a slender prong, esp of a fork2. any of the sharp terminal branches of a deer's antler
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tined adjective

Word Origin

Old English tind; related to Old Norse tindr, Old High German zint

fluorescent

Powered by COBUILD
(flʊəresənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]A fluorescent surface, substance, or colour has a very bright appearance when light is directed onto it, as if it is actually shining itself. ⇒ ...a piece of fluorescent tape.fluorescence uncount noun ⇒ ...the green fluorescence it gives off under ultraviolet radiation.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]A fluorescent light shines with a very hard, bright light and is usually in the form of a long strip. ⇒ ...fluorescent light tubes.

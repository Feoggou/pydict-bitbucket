forte1

(fɔːt , ˈfɔːteɪ )
Definitions
noun1. something at which a person excels; strong point ⇒ cooking is my forte2. fencing the stronger section of a sword blade, between the hilt and the middle. Compare foible
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from French fort, from fort (adj) strong, from Latin fortis

forte2 - music

(ˈfɔːtɪ )
Definitions
adjective, adverb1. loud or loudly. Symbol: fnoun2. a loud passage in music
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Italian, from Latin fortis strong

clove1

(kləʊv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a tropical evergreen myrtaceous tree, Syzygium aromaticum, native to the East Indies but cultivated elsewhere, esp Zanzibar2. the dried unopened flower buds of this tree, used as a pungent fragrant spice
Word origin
C14: from Old French clou de girofle, literally: nail of clove, clou from Latin clāvus nail + girofle clove tree

clove2

(kləʊv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany of the segments of a compound bulb that arise from the axils of the scales of a large bulb
Word origin
Old English clufu bulb; related to Old High German klovolouh garlic; see cleave1

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounCloves are small dried flower buds used as a spice. ⇒ ...chicken soup with cloves.2. countable nounA clove of garlic is one of the sections of a garlic bulb.3. Clove is a past tense of cleave.

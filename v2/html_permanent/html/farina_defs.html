farina

(fəˈriːnə )
Definitions
noun1. flour or meal made from any kind of cereal grain2. mainly British starch, esp prepared from potato flour
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Latin fār spelt, coarse meal

paralytic

(ˌpærəˈlɪtɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or of the nature of paralysis2. afflicted with or subject to paralysis3. British informal very drunknoun4. a person afflicted with paralysis

Derived Forms

ˌparalytically (ˌparaˈlytically) adverb

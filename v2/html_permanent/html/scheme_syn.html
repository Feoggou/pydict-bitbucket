Synonyms of scheme

scheme

noun

  1. plan, programme, strategy, system, design, project, theory, proposal, device, tactics, course of action, contrivance
    • a private pension scheme
  2. plot, dodge, ploy, ruse, game (informal), shift, intrigue, conspiracy, manoeuvre, machinations, subterfuge, stratagem
    • a quick money-making scheme
  3. diagram, system, pattern, schedule, arrangement, chart, draft, outline, blueprint, layout, disposition, schema, codification

verb

  1. plot, plan, intrigue, manoeuvre, conspire, contrive, collude, wheel and deal, machinate
    • Everyone's always scheming and plotting.
  2. plan, design, project, imagine, frame, work out, devise, contrive, lay plans

QUOTATION:
The best laid schemes o' mice an' menGang aft a-gleyRobert Burns – To a Mouse

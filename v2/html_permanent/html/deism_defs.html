deism

(ˈdiːɪzəm , ˈdeɪ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounbelief in the existence of God based solely on natural reason, without reference to revelation. Compare theism
Derived forms
deist (ˈdeist) noun, adjectivedeistic (deˈistic) or deistical (deˈistical) adjectivedeistically (deˈistically) adverb
Word origin
C17: from French déisme, from Latin deus god

unmitigated

(ʌnˈmɪtɪˌɡeɪtɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. not diminished in intensity, severity, etc2. (prenominal) (intensifier) ⇒ an unmitigated disaster
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unmitigatedly (unˈmitiˌgatedly) adverb

unmitigated for learners of English

Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]You use unmitigated to emphasize that a bad situation or quality is totally bad. [emphasis] ⇒ Last year's cotton crop was an unmitigated disaster. ⇒ She leads a life of unmitigated misery.

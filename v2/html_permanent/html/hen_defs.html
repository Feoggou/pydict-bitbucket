hen

(hɛn )
Definitions
noun1. the female of any bird, esp the adult female of the domestic fowl2. the female of certain other animals, such as the lobster3. informal a woman regarded as gossipy or foolish4. Scottish dialect a term of address (often affectionate), used to women and girls5. See scarce as hen's teeth
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English henn; related to Old High German henna, Old Frisian henne

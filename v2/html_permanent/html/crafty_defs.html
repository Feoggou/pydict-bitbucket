Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as crafty, you mean that they achieve what they want in a clever way, often by deceiving people. ⇒ ...a crafty, lying character who enjoys plotting against others. ⇒ A crafty look came to his eyes. ⇒ That was my crafty little plan.craftily graded adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ The government has craftily put up all the hidden taxes.

blouse

(blaʊz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a woman's shirtlike garment made of cotton, nylon, etc2. a loose-fitting smocklike garment, often knee length and belted, worn esp by E European peasants3. a loose-fitting waist-length belted jacket worn by soldiersverb4. to hang or make so as to hang in full loose folds
Word origin
C19: from French, of unknown origin

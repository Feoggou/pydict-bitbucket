bole1

(bəʊl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe trunk of a tree
Word origin
C14: from Old Norse bolr; related to Middle High German bole plank

bole2

(bəʊl ) or bolus
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a reddish soft variety of clay used as a pigment2. a moderate reddish-brown colour
Word origin
C13: from Late Latin bōlus lump, from Greek bōlos

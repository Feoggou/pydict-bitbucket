groundwork

(ˈɡraʊndˌwɜːk )
Definitions
noun1. preliminary work as a foundation or basis2. the ground or background of a painting, etc

groundwork

Powered by COBUILD
(graʊndwɜːʳk ) for learners of English
Definitions
singular nounThe groundwork for something is the early work on it which forms the basis for further work. ⇒ Yesterday's meeting was to lay the groundwork for the task ahead. ⇒ These courses provide the groundwork of statistical theory.

oner

(ˈwʌnə )
Definitions
noun British informal1. a single continuous action (esp in the phrase down it in a oner)2. an outstanding person or thing3. a heavy blow
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: from one

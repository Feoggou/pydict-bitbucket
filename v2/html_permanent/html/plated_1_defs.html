plated

(ˈpleɪtɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. a. coated with a layer of metalb. (in combination) ⇒ gold-plated2. (of a fabric) knitted in two different yarns so that one appears on the face and the other on the back

(pleɪtɪd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [v-link ADJ with n]If something made of metal is plated with a thin layer of another type of metal, it is covered with it. [+ with] ⇒ ...a range of jewellery, plated with 22-carat nickel-free gold.

aspiration

noun

  1. aim, longing, end, plan, hope, goal, design, dream, wish, desire, object, intention, objective, ambition, craving, endeavour, yearning, eagerness, Holy Grail (informal), hankering • the needs and aspirations of our pupils

QUOTATIONS:
 We are all in the gutter, but some of us are looking at the starsOscar Wilde – Lady Windermere's Fan An aspiration is a joy for ever, a possession as solid as a landed estate, a fortune which we can never exhaust and which gives us year by a year a revenue of pleasurable activity Robert Louis Stevenson – El Dorado

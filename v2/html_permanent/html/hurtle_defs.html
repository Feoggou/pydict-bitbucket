hurtle

(ˈhɜːtəl )
Definitions
verb1. to project or be projected very quickly, noisily, or violently2. (intransitive) rare to collide or crash
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13 hurtlen, from hurten to strike; see hurt1

check up

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. phrasal verbIf you check up on something, you find out information about it. [V P + on] ⇒ It is certainly worth checking up on your benefit entitlements. [V P] ⇒ The Government employs tax inspectors to check up and make sure people pay all their tax.2. → see also check-up3. phrasal verbIf you check up on someone, you obtain information about them, usually secretly. [V P + on] ⇒ I'm sure he knew I was checking up on him. →  check

check-up

Powered by COBUILD

Word forms: check-ups
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA check-up is a medical examination by your doctor or dentist to make sure that there is nothing wrong with your health. ⇒ The disease was detected during a routine check-up. ⇒ ...a regular check-up at the dentist.

by yourselves

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
a. if you are by yourselves, or all by yourselves, you are alone ⇒ Do you mean that you were left at home all day by yourselves?b. if you do something by yourselves or all by yourselves, you do it without any help from anybody else ⇒ You must answer all the questions by yourselves.▷ See yourselves

tutelage

(ˈtjuːtɪlɪdʒ )
Definitions
noun1. the act or office of a guardian or tutor2. instruction or guidance, esp by a tutor3. the condition of being under the supervision of a guardian or tutor
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin tūtēla a caring for, from tuērī to watch over; compare tuition

Definitions
uncount noun [usu under N]If one person, group, or country does something under the tutelage of another, they do it while they are being taught or guided by them. [formal]

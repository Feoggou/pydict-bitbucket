lamination

(ˌlæmɪˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of laminating or the state of being laminated2. a layered structure3. a layer; lamina4. one of a set of iron plates forming the core of an electrical transformer5. geology laminar stratification, typically shown by shales

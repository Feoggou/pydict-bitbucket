bamboozle

(bæmˈbuːzəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive) informal1. to cheat; mislead2. to confuse
Derived forms
bamboozler (bamˈboozler) nounbamboozlement (bamˈboozlement) noun
Word origin
C18: of unknown origin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbTo bamboozle someone means to confuse them greatly and often trick them. [V n + into] ⇒ He bamboozled Mercer into defeat. [be V-ed] ⇒ He was bamboozled by con men.

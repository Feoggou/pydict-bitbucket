artifice

(ˈɑːtɪfɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a clever expedient; ingenious stratagem2. crafty or subtle deception3. skill; cleverness4. a skilfully contrived device5. obsolete craftsmanship
Word origin
C16: from Old French, from Latin artificium skill, from artifex one possessed of a specific skill, from ars skill + -fex, from facere to make

cottage

(ˈkɒtɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small simple house, esp in a rural area2. US and Canadian a small house in the country or at a resort, used for holiday purposes3. US one of several housing units, as at a hospital, for accommodating people in groups4. slang a public lavatory
Word origin
C14: from cot2

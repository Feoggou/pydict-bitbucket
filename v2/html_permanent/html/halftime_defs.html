halftime

Powered by COBUILD
(hæftaɪm ) for learners of American English
Definitions
uncount nounHalftime is the short period of time between the two parts of a sports event such as a football, rugby, or basketball game, when the players take a short rest. ⇒ The game started in brilliant sunshine but during halftime fog closed in.

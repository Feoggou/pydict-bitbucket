Definitions

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounAn appointee is someone who has been chosen for a particular job or position of responsibility. [formal] ⇒ ...Becket, a recent appointee to the Supreme Court. ⇒ ...Diane Ravitch, a political appointee in charge of federal education research.

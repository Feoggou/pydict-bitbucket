Synonyms of heaped

heaped

adjective

  1. piled high, covered, loaded, overspread
    • The large desk was heaped with papers.

heap

noun

  1. pile, lot, collection, store, mountain, mass, stack, rick, mound, accumulation, stockpile, hoard, aggregation
    • a heap of bricks
  2. (informal)(often plural) a lot, lots, plenty, masses, load(s) (informal), ocean(s), great deal, quantities, tons (informal), stack(s), lashings (British, informal), abundance, oodles (informal)
    • You have heaps of time.

verb

  1. (sometimes with up) pile, store, collect, gather, stack, accumulate, mound, amass, stockpile, hoard, bank
    • They were heaping up wood for a bonfire.
  • See heap something on someone

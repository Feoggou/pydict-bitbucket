killer

(ˈkɪlə )
Definitions
noun1. a. a person or animal that kills, esp habituallyb. (as modifier) ⇒ a killer shark2. something, esp a task or activity, that is particularly taxing or exhausting3. Australian and New Zealand an animal selected to be slaughtered for food
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

killer for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(kɪləʳ )
Word forms: killers
Definitions
1. countable nounA killer is a person who has killed someone, or who intends to kill someone. ⇒ The police are searching for his killers. ⇒ He's a psychopath, a killer.2. countable nounYou can refer to something that causes death or is likely to cause death as a killer. [+ of] ⇒ Heart disease is the biggest killer of men in most developed countries.

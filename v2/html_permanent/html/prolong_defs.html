prolong

(prəˈlɒŋ ) or prolongate (prəʊˈlɒŋˌɡeɪt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(transitive) to lengthen in duration or space; extend

Derived Forms

prolongation (ˌprəʊlɒŋˈɡeɪʃən ) nounprolonger (proˈlonger) nounprolongment (proˈlongment) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Late Latin prōlongāre to extend, from Latin pro-1 + longus long

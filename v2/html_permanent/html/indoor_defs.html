indoor

(ˈɪnˌdɔː )
Definitions
adjective (prenominal)of, situated in, or appropriate to the inside of a house or other building ⇒ an indoor tennis court, ⇒ indoor amusements
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

indoor

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndɔːʳ ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]Indoor activities or things are ones that happen or are used inside a building and not outside. ⇒ No smoking in any indoor facilities. ⇒ ...an indoor market. ⇒ ...indoor plants.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

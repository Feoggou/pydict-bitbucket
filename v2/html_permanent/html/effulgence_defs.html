Word Frequency

effulgence

(ɛˈfʌldʒəns ; ɪˈfʌldʒɛns ) in American English
Definitions
noun great brightness; radiance; brilliance
Webster’s New World College Dictionary, Fourth Edition, compiled by the editors of Webster’s New World Dictionaries. Copyright © 2010 by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. Published by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. All rights reserved.

Derived Forms

effulgent (efˈfulgent) adjective

Word Origin

< L effulgere, prp. of effulgere < ex-, forth + fulgere, to shine: see flagrant
Example sentences containing 'effulgence'
Cazaril could still see her faint effulgence with his eyes closed. Lois McMaster Bujold THE CURSE OF CHALION

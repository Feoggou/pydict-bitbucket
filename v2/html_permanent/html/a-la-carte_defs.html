à la carte

(ɑː lɑː ˈkɑːt , æ lə , French a la kart)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective, adverb1. (of a menu or a section of a menu) having dishes listed separately and individually priced. Compare table d'hôte2. (of a dish) offered on such a menu; not part of a set meal
Word origin
C19: from French, literally: according to the card

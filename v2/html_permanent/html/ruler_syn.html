Synonyms of ruler

ruler

noun

  1. governor, leader, lord, commander, controller, monarch, sovereign, head of state, potentate, crowned head, emperor or empress, king or queen, prince or princess
    • He was an indecisive ruler.
  2. measure, rule, yardstick, straight edge
    • taking measurements with a ruler

(ɪksiːdɪŋli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [usu ADV adj, oft ADV after v]Exceedingly means very or very much. [old-fashioned] ⇒ We had an exceedingly good lunch. ⇒ This was an exceedingly difficult decision to take. ⇒ I have a case that troubles me exceedingly.

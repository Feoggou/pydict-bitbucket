citron

(ˈsɪtrən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small Asian rutaceous tree, Citrus medica, having lemon-like fruit with a thick aromatic rind. See also citron wood2. the fruit of this tree3. Also called: citron melon a variety of watermelon, Citrullus vulgaris citroides, that has an inedible fruit with a hard rind4. the rind of either of these fruits, candied and used for decoration and flavouring of foods5. a greenish-yellow colour
Word origin
C16: from Old French, from Old Provençal, from Latin citrus citrus tree

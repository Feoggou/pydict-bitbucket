unfriendly

(ʌnˈfrɛndlɪ )
Definitions
adjective -lier or -liest1. not friendly; hostile2. unfavourable or disagreeableadverb3. rare in an unfriendly manner

unfriendly for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnfrendli )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a person, organization, or their behaviour as unfriendly, you mean that they behave towards you in an unkind or rather hostile way. [+ to] ⇒ It is not fair for him to be permanently unfriendly to someone who has hurt him. ⇒ People always complain that the big banks and big companies are unfriendly and unhelpful. ⇒ Judy spoke in a loud, rather unfriendly voice.

(ædhɪərəns )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounAdherence is the fact of adhering to a particular rule, agreement, or belief. [+ to] ⇒ ...strict adherence to the constitution.

Perversely, I feel liberated by the thought of adherence to a strict set of rules.

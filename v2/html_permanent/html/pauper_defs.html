pauper

(ˈpɔːpə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who is extremely poor2. (formerly) a destitute person supported by public charity

Derived Forms

pauperˌism (ˈpauperˌism) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin: poor

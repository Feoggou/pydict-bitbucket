Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveA fact or event that is noteworthy is interesting, remarkable, or significant in some way. [formal] ⇒ It is noteworthy that the programme has been shifted from its original August slot to July. ⇒ I found nothing particularly noteworthy to report. ⇒ The most noteworthy feature of the list is that there are no women on it.

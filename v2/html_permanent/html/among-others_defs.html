among others

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
used to indicate that there are several more people like the one or ones mentioned, but that you do not intend to mention them all ⇒ He is expected to be supported at the meeting by Dennis Skinner and Tony Benn among others., ⇒ The gallery has an excellent collection of Impressionist works by, among others, Manet and Renoir.▷ See others

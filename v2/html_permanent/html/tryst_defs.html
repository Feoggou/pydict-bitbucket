tryst

(trɪst , traɪst ) archaic or literary
Definitions
noun1. an appointment to meet, esp secretly2. the place of such a meeting or the meeting itselfverb3. (intransitive) to meet at or arrange a tryst
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tryster (ˈtryster) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Old French triste lookout post, apparently of Scandinavian origin; compare Old Norse traust trust

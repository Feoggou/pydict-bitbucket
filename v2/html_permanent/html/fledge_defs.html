fledge

(flɛdʒ )
Definitions
verb1. (transitive) to feed and care for (a young bird) until it is able to fly2. Also called: fletch (transitive) to fit (something, esp an arrow) with a feather or feathers3. (intransitive) (of a young bird) to grow feathers4. (transitive) to cover or adorn with or as if with feathers
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English -flycge, as in unflycge unfledged; related to Old High German flucki able to fly; see fly1

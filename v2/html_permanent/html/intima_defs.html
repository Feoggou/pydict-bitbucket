intima

(ˈɪntɪmə )
Definitions
noun plural -mae (-ˌmiː)anatomy, zoology the innermost layer of an organ or part, esp of a blood vessel
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

intimal (ˈintimal) adjective

Word Origin

C19: from Latin, feminine of intimus innermost; see intimate1

agency

(ˈeɪdʒənsɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cies1. a business or other organization providing a specific service ⇒ an employment agency2. the place where an agent conducts business3. the business, duties, or functions of an agent4. action, power, or operation ⇒ the agency of fate5. intercession or mediation6. one of the administrative organizations of a government
Word origin
C17: from Medieval Latin agentia, from Latin agere to do

veal

(viːl )
Definitions
noun1. the flesh of the calf used as food2. Also called: veal calf a calf, esp one bred for eatingvituline
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French veel, from Latin vitellus a little calf, from vitulus calf

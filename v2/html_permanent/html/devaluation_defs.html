devaluation

(diːˌvæljuːˈeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a decrease in the exchange value of a currency against gold or other currencies, brought about by a government. Compare depreciation (sense 4)2. a reduction in value, status, importance, etc

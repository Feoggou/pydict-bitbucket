supernal

(suːˈpɜːnəl , sjuː-)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective literary1. of or from the world of the divine; celestial2. of or emanating from above or from the sky

Derived Forms

supernally (suˈpernally) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Medieval Latin supernālis, from Latin supernus that is on high, from super above

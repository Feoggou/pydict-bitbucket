virtu

or vertu (vɜːˈtuː )
Definitions
noun1. a taste or love for curios or works of fine art; connoisseurship2. such objects collectively3. the quality of being rare, beautiful, or otherwise appealing to a connoisseur (esp in the phrases articles of virtu; objects of virtu)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Italian virtù; see virtue

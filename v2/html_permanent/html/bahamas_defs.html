Bahamas

(bəˈhɑməz ; bəˈheɪməz )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
country on a group of islands (Bahama Islands) in the West Indies, southeast of Fla. & north of Cuba: formerly a British possession, it became independent (1973) & a member of the Commonwealth: 5,353 sq mi (13,864 sq km); pop. 264,000; cap. Nassau: official name Commonwealth of the Bahamas

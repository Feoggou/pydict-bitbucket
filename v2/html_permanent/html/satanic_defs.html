satanic

(səˈtænɪk ) or now rarely satanical
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Satan2. supremely evil or wicked; diabolic

Derived Forms

satanically (saˈtanically) adverbsatanicalness (saˈtanicalness) noun

(sətænɪk )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Something that is satanic is considered to be caused by or influenced by Satan. ⇒ ...satanic cults. ⇒ ...satanic ritual.2. graded adjective [usu ADJ n]A person or thing that is satanic is extremely wicked and evil. ⇒ ...satanic mass murderers.

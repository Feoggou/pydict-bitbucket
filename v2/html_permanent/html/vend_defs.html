vend

(vɛnd )
Definitions
verb1. to sell or be sold2. to sell (goods) for a living3. (transitive) rare to utter or publish (an opinion, etc)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vendition (vɛnˈdɪʃən ) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin vendere, contraction of vēnum dare to offer for sale

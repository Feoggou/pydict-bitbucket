evangelical

(ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪkəl ) Christianity
Definitions
adjective1. of, based upon, or following from the Gospels2. denoting or relating to any of certain Protestant sects or parties, which emphasize the importance of personal conversion and faith in atonement through the death of Christ as a means of salvation3. another word for evangelisticnoun4. an upholder of evangelical doctrines or a member of an evangelical sect or party, esp the Low-Church party of the Church of England

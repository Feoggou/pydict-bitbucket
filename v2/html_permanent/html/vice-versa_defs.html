Definitions
phraseVice versa is used to indicate that the reverse of what you have said is true. For example 'women may bring their husbands with them, and vice versa' means that men may also bring their wives with them. ⇒ Teachers qualified to teach in England are not accepted in Scotland and vice versa. →  vice versa

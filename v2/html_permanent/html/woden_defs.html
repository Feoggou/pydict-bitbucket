Woden

or Wodan (ˈwəʊdən )
Definitions
nounthe foremost Anglo-Saxon god. Norse counterpart: Odin
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English Wōden; related to Old Norse Ōthinn, Old High German Wuotan, German Wotan; see Wednesday

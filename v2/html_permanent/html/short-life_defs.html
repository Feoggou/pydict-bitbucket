short-life

(ˌʃɔːtˈlaɪf)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. not designed to last ⇒ short-life products whose fate is to be renewed rather than mended2. available for occupation for a limited time (until demolition or sale) ⇒ However, short-life housing is becoming increasingly scarce.

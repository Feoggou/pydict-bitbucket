nostril

(ˈnɒstrɪl )
Definitions
nouneither of the two external openings of the nose ▶ Related adjectives: narial, narine
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English nosthyrl, from nosu nose + thyrel hole

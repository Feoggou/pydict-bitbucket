waterfall

(ˈwɔːtəˌfɔːl )
Definitions
nouna cascade of falling water where there is a vertical or almost vertical step in a river

waterfall for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(wɔːtəʳfɔːl )
Word forms: waterfalls
Definitions
countable nounA waterfall is a place where water flows over the edge of a steep, high cliff in hills or mountains, and falls into a pool below. ⇒ ...Angel Falls, the world's highest waterfall.

intrepid

(ɪnˈtrɛpɪd )
Definitions
adjectivefearless; daring; bold
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

intrepidity (ˌintreˈpidity) or intrepidness (inˈtrepidness) nounintrepidly (inˈtrepidly) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin intrepidus, from in-1 + trepidus fearful, timid

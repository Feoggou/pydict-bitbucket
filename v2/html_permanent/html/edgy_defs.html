edgy

(ˈɛdʒɪ )
Definitions
adjective -ier or -iest1. (usually postpositive) nervous, irritable, tense, or anxious2. (of paintings, drawings, etc) excessively defined3. innovative, or at the cutting edge, with the concomitant qualities of intensity and excitement
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

edgily (ˈedgily) adverbedginess (ˈedginess) noun

beginnings

(bɪˈɡɪnɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. the early stages; the first signs ⇒ I had the beginnings of a headache., ⇒ The discussions were the beginnings of a dialogue with Moscow., ⇒ I had the beginnings of a migraine.2. a person's family background ⇒ His views come from his own humble beginnings.

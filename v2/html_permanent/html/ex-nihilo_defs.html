ex nihilo

(ɛks ˈnɪhɪləʊ)
Definitions
adverbformal out of nothing ⇒ We are the only animals we know of that can create ex nihilo: we can write poetry, build bridges, invent lifesaving machines.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'ex nihilo'
To act from Whim or whim is to act ex nihilo , to create from nothing. Luke Rhinehart THE BOOK OF THE DIE

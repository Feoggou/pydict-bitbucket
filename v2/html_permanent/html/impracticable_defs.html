impracticable

(ɪmˈpræktɪkəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. incapable of being put into practice or accomplished; not feasible2. unsuitable for a desired use; unfit3. an archaic word for intractable
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

impracticability (imˌpracticaˈbility) or impracticableness (imˈpracticableness) nounimpracticably (imˈpracticably) adverb

Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If something such as a course of action is impracticable, it is impossible to do. ⇒ Such measures would be highly impracticable and almost impossible to apply.

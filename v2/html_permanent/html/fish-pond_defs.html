fish pond

(fɪʃ pɒnd) or fishpond
Definitions
noun1. an ornamental pond in a garden, which has fish in it ⇒ The main feature of the garden is a fish pond.2. an area of water used in fish farming ⇒ Many of the fish ponds have been flooded, the fish stocks gone, and some of the crops are underwater.

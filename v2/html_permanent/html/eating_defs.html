eating

(ˈiːtɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. food, esp in relation to its quality or taste ⇒ this fruit makes excellent eatingadjective2. relating to or suitable for eating, esp uncooked ⇒ eating pears3. relating to or for eating ⇒ an eating house

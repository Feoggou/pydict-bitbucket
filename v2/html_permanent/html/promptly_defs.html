Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]If you do something promptly, you do it immediately. ⇒ Sister Francesca entered the chapel, took her seat, and promptly fell asleep.2. adverb [ADV with v]If you do something promptly at a particular time, you do it at exactly that time. [+ at/on] ⇒ Promptly at a quarter past seven, we left the hotel.

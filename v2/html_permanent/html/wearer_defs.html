Word forms: wearers
Definitions
countable noun [n N]You can use wearer to indicate that someone is wearing a certain thing on a particular occasion or that they often wear a certain thing. ⇒ These suits are designed to protect the wearer from cold shock as they enter the water. ⇒ The mascara is suitable for contact lens wearers. [Also + of]

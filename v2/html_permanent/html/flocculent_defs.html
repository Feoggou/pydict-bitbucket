flocculent

(ˈflɒkjʊlənt )
Definitions
adjective1. like wool; fleecy2. chemistry aggregated in woolly cloudlike masses ⇒ a flocculent precipitate3. biology covered with tufts or flakes of a waxy or wool-like substance

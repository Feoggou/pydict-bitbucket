shampoo

(ʃæmˈpuː )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a liquid or cream preparation of soap or detergent to wash the hair2. a similar preparation for washing carpets, etc3. the process of shampooingverb -poos, -pooing or -pooed4. (transitive) to wash (the hair, etc) with such a preparation

Derived Forms

shampooer (shamˈpooer) noun

Word Origin

C18: from Hindi chāmpo, from chāmpnā to knead

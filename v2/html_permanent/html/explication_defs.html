explication

(ˌɛksplɪˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act or process of explicating2. analysis or interpretation, esp of a literary passage or work or philosophical doctrine3. a comprehensive exposition or description
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Similar Words

explanation, resolution, demonstration, description, definition, interpretation, illustration, clarification, exposition, simplification, elucidation

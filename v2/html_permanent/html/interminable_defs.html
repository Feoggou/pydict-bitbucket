interminable

(ɪnˈtɜːmɪnəbəl )
Definitions
adjectiveendless or seemingly endless because of monotony or tiresome length
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

interminability (inˌterminaˈbility) or interminableness (inˈterminableness) nouninterminably (inˈterminably) adverb

interminable

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪntɜːʳmɪnəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveIf you describe something as interminable, you are emphasizing that it continues for a very long time and indicating that you wish it was shorter or would stop. [emphasis] ⇒ ...an interminable meeting.interminably adverb [usu ADV after v] ⇒ He talked to me interminably about his first wife.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

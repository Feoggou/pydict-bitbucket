reactionary

(rɪˈækʃənərɪ , -ʃənrɪ) or reactionist
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or characterized by reaction, esp against radical political or social changenoun plural -aries or -ists2. Also: reactionarist a person opposed to radical change

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveA reactionary person or group tries to prevent changes in the political or social system of their country. [disapproval] ⇒ It grew ever more clear to everyone that the Minister was too reactionary, too blinkered. ⇒ As long as I have strength, I shall be trying to remove the reactionary forces from the party. ⇒ ...narrow and reactionary ideas about family life.A reactionary is someone with reactionary views. ⇒ Critics viewed him as a reactionary.

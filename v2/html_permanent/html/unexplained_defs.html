unexplained for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnɪkspleɪnd )
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something as unexplained, you mean that the reason for it or cause of it is unclear or is not known. ⇒ The demonstrations were provoked by the unexplained death of an opposition leader. ⇒ Soon after leaving Margate, for some unexplained reason, the train was brought to a standstill. ⇒ How such extensive damage could have been sustained so quickly remains unexplained.

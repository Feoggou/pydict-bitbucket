spelling

(ˈspɛlɪŋ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or process of writing words by using the letters conventionally accepted for their formation; orthography2. the art or study of orthography3. the actual way in which a word is spelt4. the ability of a person to spell ⇒ John's spelling is good

spelling

Powered by COBUILD
(spelɪŋ )
Word forms: spellings
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA spelling is the correct order of the letters in a word. ⇒ In most languages adjectives have slightly different spellings for masculine and feminine. ⇒ If we got a spelling wrong we were forced to get a dictionary out.2. uncount nounSpelling is the ability to spell words in the correct way. It is also an attempt to spell a word in the correct way. ⇒ His spelling is very bad. ⇒ ...basic skills in reading, writing, grammar and spelling. ⇒ Spelling mistakes are often just the result of haste.3. → see also spell

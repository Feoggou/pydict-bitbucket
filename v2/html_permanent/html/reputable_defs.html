reputable

(ˈrɛpjʊtəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. having a good reputation; honoured, trustworthy, or respectable2. (of words) acceptable as good usage; standard
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

reputability (ˌreputaˈbility) nounreputably (ˈreputably) adverb

lop1

(lɒp )
Definitions
verbWord forms: lops, lopping or lopped (transitive; usually foll by off)1. to sever (parts) from a tree, body, etc, esp with swift strokes2. to cut out or eliminate from as excessivenoun3. a part or parts lopped off, as from a tree
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lopper (ˈlopper) noun

Word Origin

C15 loppe branches cut off; compare lob1

lop2

(lɒp )
Definitions
verbWord forms: lops, lopping or lopped1. to hang or allow to hang loosely2. (intransitive) to slouch about or move awkwardly3. (intransitive) a less common word for lope
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: perhaps related to lop1; compare lob1

lop3

Definitions

Word Origin

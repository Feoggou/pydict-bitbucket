beeswax

(ˈbiːzˌwæks )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a yellowish or dark brown wax secreted by honeybees for constructing honeycombsb. this wax after refining, purifying, etc, used in polishes, ointments, and for modellingverb2. (transitive) to polish with such wax

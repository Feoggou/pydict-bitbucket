apprise

or apprize (əˈpraɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(transitive; often foll by of) to make aware; informSee note at appraise
Word origin
C17: from French appris, from apprendre to teach; learn; see apprehend

Word forms: apprises , apprising , apprised
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbWhen you are apprised of something, someone tells you about it. [formal] [be V-ed + of] ⇒ Have customers been fully apprised of the advantages? [V n + of] ⇒ We must apprise them of the dangers that may be involved.

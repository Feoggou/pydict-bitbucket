Synonyms of misleading

misleading

adjective

  1. confusing, false, ambiguous, deceptive, spurious, evasive, disingenuous, tricky (informal), deceitful, specious, delusive, delusory, sophistical, casuistical, unstraightforward
    • The article contains several misleading statements.
    OPPOSITES:  open, clear, true, direct, simple, frank, obvious, correct, plain, genuine, straightforward, explicit, sincere, honest, candid, truthful, dinkum (Australian & & New Zealand, informal)

mislead

verb

  1. deceive, fool, delude, take someone in (informal), bluff, beguile, misdirect, misinform, hoodwink, lead astray, pull the wool over someone's eyes (informal), take someone for a ride (informal), misguide, give someone a bum steer (informal, mainly US)
    • Ministers knowingly misled the public.

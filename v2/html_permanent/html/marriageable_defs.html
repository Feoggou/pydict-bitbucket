Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone as marriageable, you mean that they are suitable for marriage, especially that they are the right age to marry. [old-fashioned] ⇒ ...girls of marriageable age. ⇒ ...a marriageable daughter.

Powered by COBUILD
(θɜːʳdli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverbYou use thirdly when you want to make a third point or give a third reason for something. ⇒ First of all, there are not many of them, and secondly, they have little money and, thirdly, they have few big businesses.

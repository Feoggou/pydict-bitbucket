golf

(ɡɒlf )
Definitions
noun1. a. a game played on a large open course, the object of which is to hit a ball using clubs, with as few strokes as possible, into each of usually 18 holesb. (as modifier) ⇒ a golf bagverb2. (intransitive) to play golf
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: perhaps from Middle Dutch colf club

womb

(wuːm )
Definitions
noun1. the nontechnical name for uterus ▶ Related adjective: uterine2. a hollow space enclosing something, esp when dark, warm, or sheltering3. a place where something is conceived ⇒ the Near East is the womb of western civilization4. obsolete the belly
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

wombed adjectivewomblike (ˈwomblike) adjective

Word Origin

Old English wamb; related to Old Norse vomb, Gothic wamba, Middle Low German wamme, Swedish våmm

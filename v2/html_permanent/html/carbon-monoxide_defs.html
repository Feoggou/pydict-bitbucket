Definitions
COBUILD Key Words for Automotive Engineering
noun(Automotive engineering: Fuels, oils, emissions, and other fluids) Carbon monoxide is a poisonous gas that is produced especially by the engines of vehicles. ⇒ The limit for carbon monoxide is 4.5 percent of the exhaust gas. ⇒ Catalytic converters reduce the amount of carbon monoxide in exhaust fumes. ⇒ The train releases only 60 percent of the carbon monoxide emitted by cars needed to transport the same number of passengers.

troth

(trəʊθ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun archaic1. a pledge or oath of fidelity, esp a betrothal2. truth (esp in the phrase in troth)3. loyalty; fidelity

Word Origin

Old English trēowth; related to Old High German gitriuwida loyalty; see truth

illuminated

(ɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. lit up ⇒ Much of the ancient city is illuminated at night., ⇒ an illuminated display in a shop window, ⇒ an illuminated sign2. decorated with lights ⇒ an illuminated Christmas tree3. (of a letter, page, etc) decorated by the application of colours, gold, or silver ⇒ illuminated manuscripts from the 12th to 16th century
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
1. adjectiveSomething that is illuminated is lit up, usually by electric lighting. ⇒ ...an illuminated sign. ⇒ Much of the ancient city is illuminated at night.2. adjective [ADJ n]Illuminated manuscripts, books, and official documents have brightly coloured drawings and designs round the writing. illustrated ⇒ ...illuminated manuscripts from the 12th to 16th century.

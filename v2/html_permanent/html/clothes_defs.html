clothes

(kləʊðz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. a. articles of dressb. (as modifier) ⇒ clothes brush ▶ Related adjective: vestiary2. mainly British short for bedclothes
Word origin
Old English clāthas, plural of clāth cloth

clothes

Powered by COBUILD
(kloʊðz )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. plural nounClothes are the things that people wear, such as shirts, coats, trousers, and dresses. ⇒ Moira walked upstairs to change her clothes. ⇒ He dressed quickly in casual clothes.2. → see also plain-clothes

His freckled face was almost totally obscured by blood, his clothes torn, his eyes burning with hatred.

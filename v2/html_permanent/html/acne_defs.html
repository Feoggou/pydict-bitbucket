acne

(ˈæknɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounAlso called: acne vulgaris a chronic skin disease common in adolescence, involving inflammation of the sebaceous glands and characterized by pustules on the face, neck, and upper trunk. See also rosacea
Word origin
C19: New Latin, from a misreading of Greek akmē eruption on the face. See acme

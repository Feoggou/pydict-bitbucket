Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as misleading, you mean that it gives you a wrong idea or impression. ⇒ It would be misleading to say that we were friends. ⇒ The article contains several misleading statements.misleadingly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj] ⇒ The data had been presented misleadingly.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf you describe someone as a manipulator, you mean that they skilfully control events, situations, or people, often in a way that other people disapprove of. ⇒ Jean Brodie is a manipulator. She cons everybody. ⇒ ...some of the best PR manipulators in the business.

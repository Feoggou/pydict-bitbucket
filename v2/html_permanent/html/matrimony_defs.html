matrimony

(ˈmætrɪmənɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -nies1. the state or condition of being married2. the ceremony or sacrament of marriage3. a. a card game in which the king and queen together are a winning combinationb. such a combination

Word Origin

C14: via Norman French from Latin mātrimōnium wedlock, from māter mother

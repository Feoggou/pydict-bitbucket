ultra

(ˈʌltrə )
Definitions
adjective1. extreme or immoderate, esp in beliefs or opinionsnoun2. an extremist
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from Latin: beyond, from ulter distant

vat

(væt )
Definitions
noun1. a large container for holding or storing liquids2. chemistry a preparation of reduced vat dyeverbWord forms: vats, vatting or vatted3. (transitive) to place, store, or treat in a vat
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English fæt; related to Old Frisian fet, Old Saxon, Old Norse fat, Old High German faz

VAT

(sometimes væt )
Definitions
abbreviation for (in Britain)value-added tax: a tax levied on the difference between the cost of materials and the selling price of a commodity or service

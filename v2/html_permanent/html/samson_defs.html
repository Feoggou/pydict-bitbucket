Samson

(ˈsæmsən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a judge of Israel, who performed herculean feats of strength against the Philistine oppressors until he was betrayed to them by his mistress Delilah (Judges 13–16)2. any man of outstanding physical strength

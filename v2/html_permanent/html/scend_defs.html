scend

or send (sɛnd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nauticalverbWord forms: scends, scending, scended, sends, sending or sent1. (of a vessel) to surge upwards in a heavy seanoun2. the upward heaving of a vessel pitching3. the forward lift given a vessel by the sea

Word Origin

C17: perhaps from descend or ascend

obtrude

(əbˈtruːd )
Definitions
verb1. to push (oneself, one's opinions, etc) on others in an unwelcome way2. (transitive) to push out or forward
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

obtruder (obˈtruder) nounobtrusion (əbˈtruːʒən ) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin obtrūdere, from ob- against + trūdere to push forward

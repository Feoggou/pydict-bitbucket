elapse

(ɪˈlæps )
Definitions
verb(intransitive) (of time) to pass by
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin ēlābī to slip away, from lābī to slip, glide

verbWhen time elapses, it passes. [formal] [V] ⇒ Forty-eight hours have elapsed since his arrest.

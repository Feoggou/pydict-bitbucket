Brabant

(brəˈbænt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a former duchy of W Europe: divided when Belgium became independent (1830), the south forming the Belgian provinces of Antwerp and Brabant and the north forming the province of North Brabant in the Netherlands2. a former province of central Belgium; replaced in 1995 by the provinces of Flemish Brabant and Walloon Brabant

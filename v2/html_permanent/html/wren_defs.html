wren

(rɛn )
Definitions
noun1. any small brown passerine songbird of the chiefly American family Troglodytidae, esp Troglodytes troglodytes (wren in Britain, winter wren in the US and Canada). They have a slender bill and feed on insects2. any of various similar birds of the families Muscicapidae (Australian warblers), Xenicidae (New Zealand wrens), etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English wrenna, werna; related to Old High German wrendo, rentilo, Old Norse rindill

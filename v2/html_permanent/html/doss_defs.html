doss

(dɒs ) British slang
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (intransitive; often foll by down) to sleep, esp in a dosshouse2. (intransitive; often foll by around) to pass time aimlesslynoun3. a bed, esp in a dosshouse4. a slang word for sleep5. short for dosshouse6. a task or pastime requiring little effort ⇒ making a film is a bit of a doss
Word origin
C18: of uncertain origin

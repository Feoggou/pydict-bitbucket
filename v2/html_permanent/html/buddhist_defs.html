Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA Buddhist is a person whose religion is Buddhism.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Buddhist means relating or referring to Buddhism. ⇒ ...Buddhist monks. ⇒ ...Buddhist philosophy.

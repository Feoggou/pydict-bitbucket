Synonyms of executive

executive

noun

  1. administrator, official, director, manager, chairman, managing director, controller, chief executive officer, senior manager, chairwoman, chairperson
    • Her husband is a senior bank executive.
  2. administration, government, directors, management, leadership, hierarchy, directorate
    • the executive of the National Union of Students

adjective

  1. administrative, controlling, directing, governing, regulating, decision-making, managerial
    • He sits on the executive committee of the company.

forbearing

(fɔːˈbɛərɪŋ)
Definitions
adjectiveformal showing self-control and patience ⇒ You've been remarkably forbearing, Ken., ⇒ Susan was forbearing and sympathetic, but I was hard to live with., ⇒ 'Ah,' she said, with another forbearing smile.. Also (rare): forbearant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is forbearing behaves in a calm and sensible way at a time when they would have a right to be very upset or angry. [formal, approval]

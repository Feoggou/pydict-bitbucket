Mesopotamia

(ˌmɛsəpəˈteɪmɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna region of SW Asia between the lower and middle reaches of the Tigris and Euphrates rivers: site of several ancient civilizations

Word Origin

Latin from Greek mesopotamia (khōra) (the land) between rivers

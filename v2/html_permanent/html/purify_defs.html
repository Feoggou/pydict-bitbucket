purify

(ˈpjʊərɪˌfaɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -fies, -fying or -fied1. to free (something) of extraneous, contaminating, or debasing matter2. (transitive) to free (a person, etc) from sin or guilt3. (transitive) to make clean, as in a ritual, esp the churching of women after childbirth

Derived Forms

ˌpurification (ˌpurifiˈcation) nounpurificatory (ˈpjʊərɪfɪˌkeɪtərɪ ) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Old French purifier, from Late Latin pūrificāre to cleanse, from pūrus pure + facere to make

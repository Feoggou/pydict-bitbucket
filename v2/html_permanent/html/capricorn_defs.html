Capricorn

(ˈkæprɪˌkɔːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. astrologya. Also called: the Goat, Capricornus the tenth sign of the zodiac, symbol ♑, having a cardinal earth classification and ruled by the planet Saturn. The sun is in this sign between about Dec 22 and Jan 19b. a person born during the period when the sun is in this sign2. astronomy another name for Capricornus3. See tropic of Capricornadjective4. astrology born under or characteristic of Capricorn
Word origin
C14: from Latin Capricornus (translating Greek aigokerōs goat-horned), from caper goat + cornū horn

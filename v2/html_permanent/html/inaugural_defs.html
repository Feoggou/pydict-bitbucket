inaugural

(ɪnˈɔːɡjʊrəl )
Definitions
adjective1. characterizing or relating to an inaugurationnoun2. a speech made at an inauguration, esp by a president of the US
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

inaugural

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnɔːgjʊrəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]An inaugural meeting or speech is the first meeting of a new organization or the first speech by the new leader of an organization or a country. ⇒ In his inaugural address, the President appealed for unity.

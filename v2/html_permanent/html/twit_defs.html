twit1

(twɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verbWord forms: twits, twitting or twitted1. (transitive) to tease, taunt, or reproach, often in jestnoun2. US and Canadian informal a nervous or excitable state3. rare a reproach; taunt

Word Origin

Old English ætwītan, from æt against + wītan to accuse; related to Old High German wīzan to punish

twit2

(twɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouninformal, mainly British a foolish or stupid person; idiot

Word Origin

C19: from twit1 (originally in the sense: a person given to twitting)

tup

(tʌp )
Definitions
noun1. mainly British an uncastrated male sheep; ram2. the head of a pile-driver or steam hammerverbWord forms: tups, tupping or tupped (transitive)3. to cause (a ram) to mate with a ewe, or (of a ram) to mate with (a ewe)4. Lancashire dialect to butt (someone), as in a fight

protrude

(prəˈtruːd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to thrust or cause to thrust forwards or outwards2. to project or cause to project from or as if from a surface

Derived Forms

protrudable (proˈtrudable) adjectiveprotrudent (proˈtrudent) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Latin, from pro-2 + trudere to thrust

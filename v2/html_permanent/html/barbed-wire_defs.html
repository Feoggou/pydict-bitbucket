Definitions

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft N n]Barbed wire is strong wire with sharp points sticking out of it, and is used to make fences. ⇒ The factory was surrounded by barbed wire. ⇒ ...a barbed-wire fence.

mercantile

(ˈmɜːkənˌtaɪl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of trade or traders; commercial2. of or relating to mercantilism

Word Origin

C17: from French, from Italian, from mercante merchant

adjective [ADJ n]Mercantile means relating to or involved in trade. [formal] ⇒ ...the emergence of a new mercantile class.

sold out

(səʊld aʊt)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. if a performance, sports event, or other entertainment is sold out, all the tickets for it have been sold ⇒ The premiere on Monday is sold out.2. if a shop is sold out of something, it has sold all of it that it had ⇒ People have been calling for a month and we've been sold out for a week or so., ⇒ The stores are sometimes sold out of certain groceries.

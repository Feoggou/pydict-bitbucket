goatskin

(ˈɡəʊtˌskɪn )
Definitions
noun1. the hide of a goat2. a. something made from the hide of a goat, such as leather or a container for wineb. (as modifier) ⇒ a goatskin rug
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'goatskin'
It looks awfully like goatskin to me," Vea said, examining the discolored, time-tattered rag in the glass case. Ursula K. Le Guin THE DISPOSSESSED

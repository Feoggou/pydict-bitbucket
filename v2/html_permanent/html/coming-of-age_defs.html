coming of age

(ˈkʌmɪŋ əv eɪdʒ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the moment when a person or thing reaches an important stage of development ⇒ postwar Germany's final coming-of-age as an independent sovereign state, ⇒ I think he feels it is part of his coming of age as a leader.2. a. the time when a person reaches legal adulthoodb. (as modifier) ⇒ a coming-of-age ceremony

coming of age

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. singular nounWhen something reaches an important stage of development and is accepted by a large number of people, you can refer to this as its coming of age. ⇒ ...postwar Germany's final coming-of-age as an independent sovereign state.2. singular noun [with poss]Someone's coming of age is the time when they become legally an adult. ⇒ ...traditional coming-of-age ceremonies.

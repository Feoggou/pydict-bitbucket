growing

(ˈɡrəʊɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. increasing ⇒ We are getting a growing number of complaints., ⇒ She expressed concern at the growing refugee numbers., ⇒ There is growing concern about the spread of HIV., ⇒ In parliament there is growing pressure for an investigation., ⇒ Cocaine is a growing problem in Europe., ⇒ measures to tackle the growing problem of homelessness, ⇒ a rapidly growing demand for new houses, ⇒ his growing interest in the spiritual world, ⇒ growing evidence that the economy is slowing down. See also fast-growing2. getting bigger because of natural growth ⇒ advice on providing a healthy diet for growing children, ⇒ He has two growing boys to take care of., ⇒ In spring, feed growing plants with a high-quality fertiliser., ⇒ fields of growing crops

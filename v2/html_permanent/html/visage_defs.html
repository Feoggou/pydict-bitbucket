visage

(ˈvɪzɪdʒ )
Definitions
noun mainly literary1. face or countenance2. appearance; aspect
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old French: aspect, from vis face, from Latin vīsus appearance, from vidēre to see

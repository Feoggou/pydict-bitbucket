starvation

(stɑːˈveɪʃən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna. the act or an instance of starving or state of being starvedb. (as modifier) ⇒ a starvation diet, ⇒ starvation wages

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [usu of/from N]Starvation is extreme suffering or death, caused by lack of food. ⇒ Over three hundred people have died of starvation since the beginning of the year.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If places, roads, or other things outside are dusty, they are covered with tiny bits of earth or sand, usually because it has not rained for a long time. ⇒ They started strolling down the dusty road in the moonlight. ⇒ ...a dusty old car.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf a room, house, or object is dusty, it is covered with very small pieces of dirt. ⇒ ...a dusty attic. ⇒ The books looked faded, dusty and unused.

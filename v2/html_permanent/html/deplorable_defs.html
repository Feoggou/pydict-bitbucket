deplorable

(dɪˈplɔːrəbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. lamentable ⇒ a deplorable lack of taste2. worthy of censure or reproach; very bad ⇒ deplorable behaviour
Derived forms
deplorableness (deˈplorableness) or deˌplorability (deˌploraˈbility) noundeplorably (deˈplorably) adverb

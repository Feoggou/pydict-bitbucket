well-balanced

Definitions
adjective (well balanced when postpositive)1. having good balance or proportions2. of balanced mind; sane or sensible
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

well-balanced for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
also well balanced
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as well-balanced, you mean that they are sensible and do not have many emotional problems. ⇒ ...a fun-loving, well-balanced individual.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something that is made up of several parts as well-balanced, you mean that the way that the different parts are put together is good, because there is not too much or too little of any one part. ⇒ ...a well-balanced diet.

napkin

(ˈnæpkɪn )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: table napkin a usually square piece of cloth or paper used while eating to protect the clothes, wipe the mouth, etc; serviette2. rare a similar piece of cloth used for example as a handkerchief or headscarf3. a more formal name for nappy14. a less common term for sanitary towel
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French, from nape tablecloth, from Latin mappa small cloth, towel; see map

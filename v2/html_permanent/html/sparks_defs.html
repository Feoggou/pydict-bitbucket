sparks

(spɑːks )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (functioning as singular) informal1. an electrician2. a radio officer, esp on a ship
Example sentences containing 'sparks'
She threw it down the path, the tip splintering into sparks. Robert Wilson INSTRUMENTS OF DARKNESSHis eyes had red sparks in them, like the eyes of an animal at the edge of firelight. Tepper, Sheri S. A PLAGUE OF ANGELSThe hosts of Morgul intent on their prey, taken at unawares in wild career, broke, scattering like sparks in a gale. J.R.R. Tolkien THE LORD OF THE RINGS

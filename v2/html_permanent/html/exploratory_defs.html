exploratory

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪksplɒrətri , US -plɔːrətɔːri ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveExploratory actions are done in order to discover something or to learn the truth about something. ⇒ Exploratory surgery revealed her liver cancer. ⇒ The Prime Minister's talks with the leaders of the Democratic Party were largely exploratory.

fingered

(ˈfɪŋɡəd )
Definitions
adjective1. marked or dirtied by handling2. a. having a finger or fingersb. (in combination) ⇒ nine-fingered, ⇒ red-fingered3. (of a musical part) having numerals indicating the necessary fingering

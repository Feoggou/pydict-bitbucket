(kənˈsɪdərɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. thoughtful towards other people; kind2. rare carefully thought out; considered
Derived forms

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is considerate pays attention to the needs, wishes, or feelings of other people. [approval] ⇒ I think he's the most charming, most considerate man I've ever known. [+ of] ⇒ I've always understood one should try and be considerate of other people.considerately graded adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ He treats everyone equally and considerately.

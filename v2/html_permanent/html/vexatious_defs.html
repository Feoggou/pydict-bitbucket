vexatious

(vɛkˈseɪʃəs )
Definitions
adjective1. vexing or tending to vex2. vexed3. law (of a legal action or proceeding) instituted without sufficient grounds, esp so as to cause annoyance or embarrassment to the defendant ⇒ vexatious litigation
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vexatiously (vexˈatiously) adverbvexatiousness (vexˈatiousness) noun

syllabus

(ˈsɪləbəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -buses or -bi (-ˌbaɪ)1. an outline of a course of studies, text, etc2. Britisha. the subjects studied for a particular courseb. a document which lists these subjects and states how the course will be assessed

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin, erroneously from Latin sittybus parchment strip giving title and author, from Greek sittuba

affluence

(ˈæflʊəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an abundant supply of money, goods, or property; wealth2. rare abundance or profusion

affluence

Powered by COBUILD
(æfluəns )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounAffluence is the state of having a lot of money or a high standard of living. [formal] OPPOSITE: poverty, penury ⇒ The postwar era was one of new affluence for the working class.

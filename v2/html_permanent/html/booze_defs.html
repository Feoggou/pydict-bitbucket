booze

(buːz ) informal
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. alcoholic drink2. a drinking bout or partyverb3. (usually intr) to drink (alcohol), esp in excess
Derived forms
boozed adjectiveboozing (ˈboozing) noun
Word origin
C13: from Middle Dutch būsen

Word forms: boozes , boozing , boozed
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount noun [oft the N]Booze is alcoholic drink. [informal] ⇒ ...booze and cigarettes. ⇒ ...empty bottles of booze.2. verbIf people booze, they drink alcohol. [informal] [V] ⇒ ...a load of drunken businessmen who had been boozing all afternoon.boozing uncount noun ⇒ She had to contend with the boozing and girl-chasing of her husband.

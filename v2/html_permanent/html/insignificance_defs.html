insignificance

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnsɪgnɪfɪkəns ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounInsignificance is the quality of being insignificant. ⇒ The cost pales into insignificance when compared with the damage done to his reputation. ⇒ The event was regarded as of such insignificance that not one major newspaper carried a report.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'insignificance'
But all these petty aggravations fade into insignificance compared with the things Bev and I have seen this week. Isabel Wolff RESCUING ROSEHe might have thought he was in trouble, but he needn't have worried; to the Los Angeles police, his insignificance was immeasurable. Wright, L R SLEEP WHILE I SING

untrue

(ʌnˈtruː )
Definitions
adjective1. incorrect or false2. disloyal3. diverging from a rule, standard, or measure; inaccurate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

untrueness (unˈtrueness) noununtruly (unˈtruly) adverb

untrue for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌntruː )
Definitions
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If a statement or idea is untrue, it is false and not based on facts. ⇒ The allegations were completely untrue. ⇒ It was untrue to say that all political prisoners have been released. ⇒ Such remarks are both offensive and untrue.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

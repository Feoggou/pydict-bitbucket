maritime

(ˈmærɪˌtaɪm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to navigation, shipping, etc; seafaring2. of, relating to, near, or living near the sea3. (of a climate) having small temperature differences between summer and winter; equable

Word Origin

C16: from Latin maritimus from mare sea

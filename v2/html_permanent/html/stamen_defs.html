stamen

(ˈsteɪmɛn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural stamens or stamina (ˈstæmɪnə )the male reproductive organ of a flower, consisting of a stalk (filament) bearing an anther in which pollen is produced

Derived Forms

staminal (ˈstæmɪnəl ) adjectivestaminiferous (ˌstæmɪˈnɪfərəs ) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Latin: the warp in an upright loom, from stāre to stand

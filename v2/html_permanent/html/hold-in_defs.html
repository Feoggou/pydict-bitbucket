hold in

Definitions
verb (tr, adverb)1. to curb, control, or keep in check2. to conceal or restrain (feelings)

Definitions
phrasal verbIf you hold in an emotion or feeling, you do not allow yourself to express it, often making it more difficult to deal with. [V P n] ⇒ Depression can be traced to holding in anger. [V n P] ⇒ Go ahead and cry. Don't hold it in. →  hold

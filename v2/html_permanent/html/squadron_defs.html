squadron

(ˈskwɒdrən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a subdivision of a naval fleet detached for a particular taskb. a number of naval units usually of similar type and consisting of two or more divisions2. a cavalry unit comprising two or more troops, headquarters, and supporting arms3. the basic tactical and administrative air force unit comprising two or more flights

Word Origin

C16: from Italian squadrone soldiers drawn up in square formation, from squadro square

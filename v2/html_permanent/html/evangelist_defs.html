evangelist

(ɪˈvændʒɪlɪst )
Definitions
noun1. an occasional preacher, sometimes itinerant and often preaching at meetings in the open air2. a preacher of the Christian gospel3. any zealous advocate of a cause4. another word for revivalist (sense 1)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Evangelist

(ɪˈvændʒɪlɪst )
Definitions
noun1. any of the writers of the New Testament Gospels: Matthew, Mark, Luke, or John2. a senior official or dignitary of the Mormon Church
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Synonyms of propel

propel

verb

  1. drive, launch, start, force, send, shoot, push, thrust, shove, set in motion
    • The rocket is designed to propel the spacecraft.
    OPPOSITES:  stop, pull, check, slow, hold back
  2. impel, drive, push, prompt, spur, motivate
    • He is propelled by the need to avenge his father.
    OPPOSITES:  check, delay, hold back

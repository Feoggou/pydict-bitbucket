scattering

noun

  1. sprinkling, few, handful, scatter, smattering, smatter • the scattering of houses on the east of the village

scatter

verb

  1. throw about, spread, sprinkle, strew, broadcast, shower, fling, litter, sow, diffuse, disseminate • He began by scattering seed and putting in plants. OPPOSITES:  collect, gather
  2. disperse, separate, break up, dispel, disband, dissipate, go their separate ways, disunite, go in different directions, put to flight • After dinner, everyone scattered. OPPOSITES:  assemble, converge, congregate, unite, rally, gather
  3. dot, spot, sprinkle, pepper, litter, fleck, stipple • bays picturesquely scattered with rocky islets

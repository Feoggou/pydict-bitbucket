abundant

(əˈbʌndənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. existing in plentiful supply2. (postpositive; foll by in) having a plentiful supply (of)3. (of a chemical element or mineral) occurring to an extent specified in relation to other elements or minerals in the earth's crust or some other specified environment4. (of an isotope) occurring to an extent specified in relation to other isotopes in a mixture of isotopes
Word origin
C14: from Latin abundant-, present participle of abundāre to abound

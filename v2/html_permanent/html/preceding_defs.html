preceding

(prɪˈsiːdɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(prenominal) going or coming before; former
Example sentences containing 'preceding'
It was a sensation I had known a few times before, usually preceding a ghastly piece of news. Ian St James Awards AT THE STROKE OF TWELVEHe had worked ceaselessly in the preceding week to prepare the room for them. Clive Barker THE HELLBOUND HEARTHe grinned when he saw her, innocently, as if he had not missed two appointments in the preceding two weeks. Skelton, Alison Scott AN OLDER WOMANThe last few hours, they meandered on the ice through the small islands preceding Stockholm. Paullina Simons THE BRONZE HORSEMAN

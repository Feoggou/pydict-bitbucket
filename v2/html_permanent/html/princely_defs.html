princely

(ˈprɪnslɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -lier or -liest1. generous or lavish2. of, belonging to, or characteristic of a princeadverb3. in a princely manner

Derived Forms

princeliness (ˈprinceliness) noun

Powered by COBUILD
(prɪnsli )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A princely sum of money is a large sum of money. ⇒ It'll cost them the princely sum of seventy-five pounds.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Princely means belonging to a prince or suitable for a prince. ⇒ It was the embodiment of princely magnificence.

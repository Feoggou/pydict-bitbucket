noun

(naʊn )
Definitions
nouna. a word or group of words that refers to a person, place, or thing or any syntactically similar wordb. (as modifier) ⇒ a noun phrase Abbreviation: N, n ▶ Related adjective: nominal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

nounal (ˈnounal) adjectivenounally (ˈnounally) adverbnounless (ˈnounless) adjective

Word Origin

C14: via Anglo-French from Latin nōmen name

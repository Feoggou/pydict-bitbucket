Definitions
adjectivenot able to be retrieved, recovered, or repaired

irretrievable

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪrɪtriːvəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveIf you talk about irretrievable damage or an irretrievable situation, you mean that the damage or situation is so bad that there is no possibility of putting it right. [formal] ⇒ What a tragedy if the sport suffers irretrievable damage because this issue is ducked. ⇒ ...a country in irretrievable decline.irretrievably (ɪrɪtriːvəbli ) adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ Eventually her marriage broke down irretrievably.

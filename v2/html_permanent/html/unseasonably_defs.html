(ʌnsiːzənəbli )
Definitions
adverb [ADV adj]Unseasonably warm, cold, or mild weather is warmer, colder, or milder than it usually is at the time of year. ⇒ ...a spell of unseasonably warm weather. ⇒ It was unseasonably mild for late January.

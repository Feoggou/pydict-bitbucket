spirituous

(ˈspɪrɪtjʊəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. characterized by or containing alcohol2. (of a drink) being a spirit
Derived forms
spirituosity (ˌspɪrɪtjʊˈɒsɪtɪ ) or spirituousness (ˈspirituousness) noun

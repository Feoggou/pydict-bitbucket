eleventh

(ɪˈlɛvənθ )
Definitions
adjective1. (usually prenominal)a. coming after the tenth in numbering or counting order, position, time, etc; being the ordinal number of eleven: often written 11thb. (as pronoun) ⇒ the eleventh in successionnoun2. a. one of 11 equal or nearly equal parts of an object, quantity, measurement, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ an eleventh part3. the fraction equal to one divided by 11 (1⁄11)4. musica. an interval of one octave plus one fourthb. See eleventh chord

Neapolis

(niːˈæpəlɪs)
Definitions
nouna port in E Greece, in Macedonia East and Thrace region on the Bay of Kaválla an important Macedonian fortress of the Byzantine empire; ceded to Greece by Turkey after the Balkan War (1912–13). Pop: 58 576 (1991). another name for Kaválla

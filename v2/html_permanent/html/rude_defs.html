rude

(ruːd )
Definitions
adjective1. insulting or uncivil; discourteous; impolite ⇒ he was rude about her hairstyle2. lacking refinement; coarse or uncouth3. vulgar or obscene ⇒ a rude joke4. unexpected and unpleasant ⇒ a rude awakening to the facts of economic life5. roughly or crudely made ⇒ we made a rude shelter on the island6. rough or harsh in sound, appearance, or behaviour7. humble or lowly8. (prenominal) robust or sturdy ⇒ in rude health9. (prenominal) approximate or imprecise ⇒ a rude estimate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

rudely (ˈrudely) adverbrudeness (ˈrudeness) or informal rudery (ˈrudery) noun

Word Origin

C14: via Old French from Latin rudis coarse, unformed

rude

Powered by COBUILD
(ruːd ) for learners of English
Word forms: ruder , rudest
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveWhen people are rude, they act in an impolite way towards other people or say impolite things about them. [+ to/about] ⇒ He's rude to her friends and obsessively jealous. ⇒ People were quite often rude about him, often the people he had helped. ⇒ Unfair bosses and rude customers make us unhappy on the job.rudely graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ I could not understand why she felt compelled to behave so rudely to a friend.rudeness uncount noun [oft with poss] ⇒ She was angry at Steve's rudeness, but I could forgive it.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Rude is used to describe words and behaviour that are likely to embarrass or offend people, because they relate to sex or to body functions. [mainly British] ⇒ Fred keeps cracking rude jokes with the guests. ⇒ Luke made a rude gesture with his finger.regional note:   in AM, usually use dirty3. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]If someone receives a rude shock, something unpleasant happens unexpectedly. ⇒ It will come as a rude shock when their salary or income-tax refund cannot be cashed.rudely graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ People were awakened rudely by a siren just outside their window.4. adjective [ADJ n]Objects can be described as rude when they are very simply and roughly made. [literary] ⇒ Roden had already constructed a rude cabin for himself and his family in case of necessity.5. →  rude health6. → rude awakening

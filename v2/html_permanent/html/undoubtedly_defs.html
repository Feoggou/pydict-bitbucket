undoubtedly

(ʌnˈdaʊtɪdlɪ )
Definitions
adverb1. certainly or definitely; unquestionably ⇒ he is undoubtedly talented2. (sentence modifier) without doubt; certainly or indisputably ⇒ undoubtedly there will be changes
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'undoubtedly'
Kagg loved the machine, undoubtedly bought with royalties that were rightfully Stone's. Terman, Douglas CORMORANTBut his arms were strong and his chest broad and he was undoubtedly attractive. Martin, Joy THE IMAGE OF LAURAOn the open page was a picture of the same woman: with blonde hair but undoubtedly the same. Loraine, Philip LAST SHOT

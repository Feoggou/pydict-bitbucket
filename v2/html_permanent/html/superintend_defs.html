superintend

(ˌsuːpərɪnˈtɛnd , ˌsuːprɪn-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verbto undertake the direction or supervision (of); manage

Derived Forms

ˌsuperintendence (ˌsuperinˈtendence) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Church Latin superintendere, from Latin super- + intendere to give attention to

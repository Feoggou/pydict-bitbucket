commissioner

(kəˈmɪʃənə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person authorized to perform certain tasks or endowed with certain powers2. governmenta. any of several types of civil servantb. an ombudsman. See also Health Service Commissioner, Parliamentary Commissioner3. a member of a commission
Derived forms

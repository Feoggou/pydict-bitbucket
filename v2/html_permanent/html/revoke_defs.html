revoke

(rɪˈvəʊk )
Definitions
verb1. (transitive) to take back or withdraw; cancel; rescind ⇒ to revoke a law2. (intransitive) cards to break a rule of play by failing to follow suit when able to do so; renegenoun3. cards the act of revoking; a renege
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

revoker (reˈvoker) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Latin revocāre to call back, withdraw, from re- + vocāre to call

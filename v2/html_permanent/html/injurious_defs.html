injurious

(ɪnˈdʒʊərɪəs )
Definitions
adjective1. causing damage or harm; deleterious; hurtful2. abusive, slanderous, or libellous
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

injuriously (inˈjuriously) adverbinjuriousness (inˈjuriousness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is injurious to someone or to their health or reputation is harmful or damaging to them. [formal] [+ to] ⇒ ...substances that are injurious to health. ⇒ Stress in itself is not necessarily injurious.

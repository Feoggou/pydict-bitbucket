petulant

(ˈpɛtjʊlənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveirritable, impatient, or sullen in a peevish or capricious way

Derived Forms

petulance (ˈpetulance) or petulancy (ˈpetulancy) nounpetulantly (ˈpetulantly) adverb

Word Origin

C16: via Old French from Latin petulāns bold, from petulāre (unattested) to attack playfully, from petere to assail

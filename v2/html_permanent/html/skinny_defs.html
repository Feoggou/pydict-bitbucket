skinny

(ˈskɪnɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -nier or -niest1. lacking in flesh; thin2. consisting of or resembling skin

Derived Forms

skinniness (ˈskinniness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveA skinny person is extremely thin, often in a way that you find unattractive. [informal] ⇒ He was quite a skinny little boy. ⇒ She had stringy hair and skinny legs.

confidant

(ˌkɒnfɪˈdænt , ˈkɒnfɪˌdænt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna person, esp a man, to whom private matters are confided
Word origin
C17: from French confident, from Italian confidente, n use of adj: trustworthy, from Latin confīdens confident

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu with poss]Someone's confidant is a man who they are able to discuss their private problems with. [+ of] ⇒ ...a close confidant of the president.

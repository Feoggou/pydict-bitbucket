poon1

(puːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of several trees of the SE Asian genus Calophyllum having lightweight hard wood and shiny leathery leaves: family Clusiaceae2. the wood of any of these trees, used to make masts and spars

Word Origin

C17: from Sinhalese pūna

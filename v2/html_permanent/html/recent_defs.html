recent

(ˈriːsənt )
Definitions
adjectivehaving appeared, happened, or been made not long ago; modern, fresh, or new

Derived Forms

recently (ˈrecently) adverbrecentness (ˈrecentness) or recency (ˈrecency) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin recens fresh; related to Greek kainos new

roost

(ruːst )
Definitions
noun1. a place, perch, branch, etc, where birds, esp domestic fowl, rest or sleep2. a temporary place to rest or stay3. See rule the roostverb (intransitive)4. to rest or sleep on a roost5. to settle down or stay6. See come home to roost
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English hrōst; related to Old Saxon hrost loft, German Rost grid

(ruːst ) for learners of English
Word forms: roosts , roosting , roosted
Definitions
1. countable nounA roost is a place where birds or bats rest or sleep.2. verbWhen birds or bats roost somewhere, they rest or sleep there. [V prep/adv] ⇒ The peacocks roost in nearby shrubs.3. →  come home to roost4. →  rule the roost

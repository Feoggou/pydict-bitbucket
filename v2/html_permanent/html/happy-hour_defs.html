Powered by COBUILD
(hæpi aʊər) in Hospitality
Word forms: (regular plural) happy hours
Definitions
noun(Hospitality (hotel): Hotel bar) In a bar, happy hour is a period when drinks are sold more cheaply than usual to encourage people to come to the bar. ⇒ Don't forget happy hour 7-9pm, when all drinks are half price. ⇒ During happy hour a pint of beer is cheaper than usual. ⇒ The bar has a happy hour where you can save money on drinks.

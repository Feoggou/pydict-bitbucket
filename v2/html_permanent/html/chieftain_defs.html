chieftain

(ˈtʃiːftən , -tɪn)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the head or leader of a tribe or clan2. the chief of a group of people
Derived forms
chieftaincy (ˈchieftaincy) or chieftainˌship (ˈchieftainˌship) noun
Word origin
C14: from Old French chevetaine, from Late Latin capitāneus commander; see captain

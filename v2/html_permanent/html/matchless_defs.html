Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]You can use matchless to emphasize that you think something is extremely good. [emphasis] ⇒ A timeless comic actor–his simplicity and his apparent ease are matchless. ⇒ The Savoy provides a matchless hotel experience.

The girl was younger than she expected, and in her present condition she was far from the matchless beauty Damin had described.

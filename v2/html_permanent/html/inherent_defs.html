Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]The inherent qualities of something are the necessary and natural parts of it. ⇒ Stress is an inherent part of dieting. [+ in] ⇒ ...the dangers inherent in an outbreak of war.inherently adverb [usu ADV adj] ⇒ Aeroplanes are not inherently dangerous. ⇒ War is inherently a dirty business.

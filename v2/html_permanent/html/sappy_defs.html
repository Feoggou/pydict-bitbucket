sappy

(ˈsæpɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective -pier or -piest1. (of plants) full of sap2. full of energy or vitality3. slang silly or fatuous

Derived Forms

sappily (ˈsappily) adverbsappiness (ˈsappiness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSappy stems or leaves contain a lot of liquid. ⇒ Do not overfeed them, as this will encourage soft sappy growth.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as sappy, you think they are foolish. [US, informal, disapproval] ⇒ I wrote this sappy love song.

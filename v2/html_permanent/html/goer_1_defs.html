goer

(ˈɡəʊə )
Definitions
noun1. a. a person who attends something regularlyb. (in combination) ⇒ filmgoer2. an energetic person3. informal an acceptable or feasible idea, proposal, etc4. Australian and New Zealand informal a person trying to succeed

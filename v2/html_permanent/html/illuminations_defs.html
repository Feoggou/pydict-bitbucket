illuminations

(ɪˌluːmɪˈneɪʃənz)
Definitions
plural nounBritish lights, esp coloured lights, used as decoration in streets, parks, etc ⇒ the illuminations in Regent Street at Christmas, ⇒ the famous Blackpool illuminations

gold mine

Definitions
noun1. a place where gold ore is mined2. a source of great wealth, profit, etc

singular nounIf you describe something such as a business or idea as a gold mine, you mean that it produces large profits. ⇒ The book is a goldmine. ⇒ The programme was a gold mine for small production companies.

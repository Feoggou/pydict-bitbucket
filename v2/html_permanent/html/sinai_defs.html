(ˈsaɪnaɪ , ˈsaɪnɪˌaɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a mountainous peninsula of NE Egypt at the N end of the Red Sea, between the Gulf of Suez and the Gulf of Aqaba: occupied by Israel in 1967; fully restored by 19822. See Mount Sinai

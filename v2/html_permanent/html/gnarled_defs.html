gnarled

(nɑːld )
Definitions
adjective1. having gnarls2. (esp of hands) rough, twisted, and weather-beaten in appearance3. perverse or ill-tempered

gnarled

Powered by COBUILD
(nɑːʳld ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA gnarled tree is twisted and strangely shaped because it is old. ⇒ ...a large and beautiful garden full of ancient gnarled trees.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA person who is gnarled looks very old because their skin has lines on it or their body is bent. If someone has gnarled hands, their hands are twisted as a result of old age or illness. ⇒ ...gnarled old men. ⇒ His hands were gnarled with arthritis.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

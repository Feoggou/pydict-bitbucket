eleven

(ɪˈlɛvən )
Definitions
noun1. the cardinal number that is the sum of ten and one2. a numeral 11, XI, etc, representing this number3. something representing, represented by, or consisting of 11 units4. (functioning as singular or plural) a team of 11 players in football, cricket, hockey, etc5. Also called: eleven o'clock eleven hours after noon or midnightdeterminer6. a. amounting to eleven ⇒ eleven chancesb. (as pronoun) ⇒ have another eleven today
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English endleofan; related to Old Norse ellefo, Gothic ainlif, Old Frisian andlova, Old High German einlif

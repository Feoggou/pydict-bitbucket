statesman

(ˈsteɪtsmən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -men1. a political leader whose wisdom, integrity, etc, win great respect2. a person active and influential in the formulation of high government policy, such as a cabinet member3. a politician

Derived Forms

statesman-ˌlike (ˈstatesman-ˌlike) or statesmanly (ˈstatesmanly) adjectivestatesmanship (ˈstatesmanship) nounstatesˌwoman (ˈstatesˌwoman) feminine noun

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

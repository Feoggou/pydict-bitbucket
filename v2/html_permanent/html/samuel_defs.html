Samuel

(ˈsæmjʊəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Old Testament1. a Hebrew prophet, seer, and judge, who anointed the first two kings of the Israelites (I Samuel 1–3; 8–15)2. either of the two books named after him, I and II Samuel

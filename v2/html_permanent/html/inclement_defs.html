inclement

(ɪnˈklɛmənt )
Definitions
adjective1. (of weather) stormy, severe, or tempestuous2. harsh, severe, or merciless
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inclemency (inˈclemency) or inclementness (inˈclementness) nouninclemently (inˈclemently) adverb

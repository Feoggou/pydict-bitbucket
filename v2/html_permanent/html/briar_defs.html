briar1

or brier (ˈbraɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: tree heath an ericaceous shrub, Erica arborea, of S Europe, having a hard woody root (briarroot)2. a tobacco pipe made from the root of this plant
Derived forms
briary (ˈbriary) or briery (ˈbriery) adjective
Word origin
C19: from French bruyère heath, from Late Latin brūcus, of Gaulish origin

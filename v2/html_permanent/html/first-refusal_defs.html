Definitions
phraseIf someone has first refusal on something that is being sold or offered, they have the right to decide whether or not to buy it or take it before it is offered to anyone else. ⇒ If you have a good rapport with a dealer, they will always let you have first refusal on anything interesting. ⇒ A tenant may have a right of first refusal if a property is offered for sale. →  refusal

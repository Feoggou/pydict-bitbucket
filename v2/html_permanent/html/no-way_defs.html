no way

(nəʊ weɪ)
Definitions
exclamation1. informal used to make an emphatic refusal, denial etc ⇒ Would I ever do such a thing again? No way!, ⇒ I am not going to risk my money on such a crazy venture, no way!, ⇒ 'Can I borrow your car, Mommy?' - 'No way!'adverb2. especially US in no manner; not at all; nowise ⇒ I would no way condone such behaviour., ⇒ They no way deserved to lose that game., ⇒ There is no way that John was just pretending., ⇒ There was no way Carl would ever do this. It's just ridiculous!, ⇒ No way was that my fault!

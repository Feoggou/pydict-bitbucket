ensure

(ɛnˈʃʊə , -ˈʃɔː) or especially US insure
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. (may take a clause as object) to make certain or sure; guarantee ⇒ this victory will ensure his happiness2. to make safe or secure; protect
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnʃʊəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: ensures , ensuring , ensured
Definitions
verbTo ensure something, or to ensure that something happens, means to make certain that it happens. [formal] [V that] ⇒ Britain's negotiators had ensured that the treaty which resulted was a significant change in direction. [V that] ⇒ Ensure that it is written into your contract. [V n] ⇒ ...the President's Council, which ensures the supremacy of the National Party.

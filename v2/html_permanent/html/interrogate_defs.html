interrogate

(ɪnˈtɛrəˌɡeɪt )
Definitions
verbto ask questions (of), esp to question (a witness in court, spy, etc) closely
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

interrogatingly (inˈterroˌgatingly) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Latin interrogāre to question, examine, from rogāre to ask

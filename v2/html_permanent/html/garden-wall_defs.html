garden wall

(ˈɡɑːdən wɔːl)
Definitions
nouna wall surrounding a garden or separating two gardens ⇒ I went back out through the verandah and hopped over the garden wall., ⇒ He lives just over the garden wall from us.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

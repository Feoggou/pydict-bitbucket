well-known

Definitions
adjective (well known when postpositive)1. widely known; famous; celebrated2. known fully or clearly

well-known for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
also well known
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA well-known person or thing is known about by a lot of people and is therefore famous or familiar. If someone is well-known for a particular activity, a lot of people know about them because of their involvement with that activity. ⇒ He surrounds himself with attractive, intelligent, or well-known people. ⇒ He is well-known to the local police. [+ for] ⇒ Hubbard was well-known for his work in the field of drug rehabilitation. → famous2. graded adjective & adjectiveA well-known fact is a fact that is known by people in general. ⇒ It may be a well-known fact, but I didn't know it. ⇒ It is well-known that bamboo shoots are a panda's staple diet.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

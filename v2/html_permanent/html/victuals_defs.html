victuals

(ˈvɪtəlz )
Definitions
plural noun(sometimes singular) food or provisions
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

I gathered a supply of candles, a pillow from his bed and a blanket, a bottle of wine and some victuals. Robin Hobb THE GOLDEN FOOL: BOOK TWO OF THE TAWNY MANAs indeed it had: no change for the worse had yet come upon the beer or the victuals at The Pony, at any rate. J.R.R.

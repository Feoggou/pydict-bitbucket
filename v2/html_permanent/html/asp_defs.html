asp1

(æsp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the venomous snake, probably Naja haje (Egyptian cobra), that caused the death of Cleopatra and was formerly used by the Pharaohs as a symbol of their power over life and death. See also uraeus2. Also called: asp viper a viper, Vipera aspis, that occurs in S Europe and is very similar to but smaller than the adder3. See horned asp
Word origin
C15: from Latin aspis, from Greek

disavowal

Powered by COBUILD
(dɪsəvaʊəl )
Word forms: disavowals
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA disavowal of something is a statement that you are not connected with it or responsible for it, or that you no longer agree with or believe in it. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ ...a public disavowal of his beliefs.

Similar Words

denial, rejection, contradiction, disclaimer, renunciation, retraction, repudiation, gainsaying (archaic) (literary), recantation, abjuration

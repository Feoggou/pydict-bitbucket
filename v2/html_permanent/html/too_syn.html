Synonyms of too

too

adverb

  1. also, as well, further, in addition, moreover, besides, likewise, to boot, into the bargain
    • Depression may be expressed physically too.
  2. excessively, very, extremely, overly, unduly, unreasonably, inordinately, exorbitantly, immoderately, over-
    • I'm afraid you're too late; she's gone.
see very

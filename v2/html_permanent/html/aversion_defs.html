aversion

(əˈvɜːʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (usually foll by to or for) extreme dislike or disinclination; repugnance2. a person or thing that arouses this ⇒ he is my pet aversion

(əvɜːʳʃən , US -ʒən )
Word forms: aversions
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounIf you have an aversion to someone or something, you dislike them very much. [+ to/for] ⇒ Many people have a natural and emotional aversion to insects.

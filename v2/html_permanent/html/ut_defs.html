ut

(ʌt , uːt )
Definitions
noun music1. the syllable used in the fixed system of solmization for the note C2. the first note of a hexachord in medieval music
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin ut; see gamut

Althorp House

(ˈɔːlθɔːp , -θrʌp)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna mansion in Northamptonshire: seat of the Earls Spencer since 1508; originally a medieval house; altered (1787) to its present neoclassical style by Henry Holland. Diana, Princess of Wales is buried on Round Oval Island in the centre of the ornamental lake in Althorp Park

tweezers

(ˈtwiːzəz )
Definitions
plural nouna small pincer-like instrument for handling small objects, plucking out hairs, etc. Also called: pair of tweezers or (esp US) tweezer
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: plural of tweezer (on the model of scissors, etc), from tweeze case of instruments, from French étuis cases (of instruments), from Old French estuier to preserve, from Vulgar Latin studiāre (unattested) to keep, from Latin studēre to care about

(væksɪneɪt ) for learners of English
Word forms: vaccinates , vaccinating , vaccinated
Definitions
verb [usu passive]If a person or animal is vaccinated, they are given a vaccine, usually by injection, to prevent them from getting a disease. inoculate [be V-ed + against] ⇒ Dogs must be vaccinated against distemper. [have n V-ed against n] ⇒ Have you had your child vaccinated against whooping cough? [be/get V-ed] ⇒ Measles, mumps and whooping cough are spreading again because children are not being vaccinated.vaccination (væksɪneɪʃən )Word forms: vaccinations variable noun ⇒ Parents were too frightened to bring their children for vaccination. ⇒ Anyone who wants to avoid the flu should consider getting a vaccination.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf something that someone says or writes is meaningless, it has no meaning, or appears to have no meaning. ⇒ The sentence 'kicked the ball the man' is meaningless. ⇒ She is fascinated by algebra while he considers it meaningless nonsense.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is meaningless in a particular situation is not important or relevant. ⇒ Widespread political and economic disarray threatens to make the constitution meaningless. ⇒ Fines are meaningless to guys earning millions.3. graded adjective & adjectiveIf something that you do is meaningless, it has no purpose and is not at all worthwhile. ⇒ They seek strong sensations to dull their sense of a meaningless existence.

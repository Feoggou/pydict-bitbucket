or caviare (ˈkævɪˌɑː , ˌkævɪˈɑː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe salted roe of sturgeon, esp the beluga, usually served as an hors d'oeuvre
Word origin
C16: from earlier cavery, from Old Italian caviari, plural of caviaro caviar, from Turkish havyār

some day

or someday (sʌm deɪ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adverbat a date in the future that is unknown or that has not yet been decided ⇒ He said he wants to be a supervisor some day., ⇒ He took her left hand, hoping that it would someday bear a gold ring on the third finger., ⇒ I hope someday we'll have enough money to retire., ⇒ Some day I'll be a pilot.

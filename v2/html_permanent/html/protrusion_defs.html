protrusion

(prəˈtruːʒən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. something that protrudes2. the state or condition of being protruded3. the act or process of protruding

Definitions

countable nounA protrusion is something that sticks out from something. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ He grabbed at a protrusion of rock with his right hand.

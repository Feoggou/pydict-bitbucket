Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]If you say that someone has a can-do attitude, you approve of them because they are confident and willing to deal with problems or new tasks, rather than complaining or giving up. [informal, approval] ⇒ He is known for his optimistic can-do attitude. ⇒ America is once again being seen as a strong, can-do nation.

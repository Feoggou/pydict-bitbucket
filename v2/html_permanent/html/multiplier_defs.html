multiplier

(ˈmʌltɪˌplaɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that multiplies2. the number by which another number, the multiplicand, is multiplied. See also multiplicand3. physics any device or instrument, such as a photomultiplier, for increasing an effect4. economicsa. the ratio of the total change in income (resulting from successive rounds of spending) to an initial autonomous change in expenditureb. (as modifier) ⇒ multiplier effects

bora1

(ˈbɔːrə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(sometimes capital) a violent cold north wind blowing from the mountains to the E coast of the Adriatic, usually in winter
Word origin
C19: from Italian (Venetian dialect), from Latin borēas the north wind

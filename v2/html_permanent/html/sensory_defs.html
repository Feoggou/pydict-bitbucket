sensory

(ˈsɛnsərɪ ) or less commonly sensorial (sɛnˈsɔːrɪəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to the senses or the power of sensation2. of or relating to those processes and structures within an organism that receive stimuli from the environment and convey them to the brain

Word Origin

C18: from Latin sensōrius, from sentīre to feel

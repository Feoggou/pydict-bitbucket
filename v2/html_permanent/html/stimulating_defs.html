stimulating

(ˈstɪmjʊˌleɪtɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. inspiring new ideas or enthusiasm2. (of a physical activity) making one feel refreshed and energetic
Derived forms
stimuˌlatingly (ˈstimuˌlatingly) adverb
Example sentences containing 'stimulating'
In vexation, he tried to blanket the pain and succeeded only in stimulating it further.

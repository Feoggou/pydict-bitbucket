free market

Definitions
nouna. an economic system that allows supply and demand to regulate prices, wages, etc, rather than government policyb. (as modifier) ⇒ a free-market economy

Definitions
countable noun [usu sing]A free market is an economic system in which business organizations decide things such as prices and wages, and are not controlled by the government. [business] ⇒ ...the creation of a free market. ⇒ ...free market economies.

dissertation

(ˌdɪsəˈteɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a written thesis, often based on original research, usually required for a higher degree2. a formal discourse
Derived forms

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA dissertation is a long formal piece of writing on a particular subject, especially for a university degree. [+ on] ⇒ He is currently writing a dissertation on the Somali civil war.

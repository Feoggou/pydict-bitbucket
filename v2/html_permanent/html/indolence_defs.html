indolence

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndələns ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIndolence means laziness. [formal] ⇒ He was noted for his indolence.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'indolence'
Nourished by my indolence , it grew into a mammoth, impregnable oak of jealousy, bearing the most poisonous fruit. Alex George LOVE YOU MADLYIt would be so easy to drift into the indolence that surrounded Turtle Ridge. Lawson, Jonell ROSES ARE FOR THE RICHStrength lurked somewhere but indolence was pleasant, and he preferred to let time do its work. Sillitoe, Alan THE OPEN DOOR

read out

(riːd )
Definitions
verb (adverb)1. (transitive) to read (something) aloud2. to retrieve (information) from a computer memory or storage device3. (transitive) US and Canadian to expel (someone) from a political party or other societynoun read-out4. a. the act of retrieving information from a computer memory or storage deviceb. the information retrieved

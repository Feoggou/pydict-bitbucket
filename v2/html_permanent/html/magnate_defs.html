magnate

(ˈmæɡneɪt , -nɪt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person of power and rank in any sphere, esp in industry2. history a great nobleman3. (formerly) a member of the upper chamber in certain European parliaments, as in Hungary

Derived Forms

magnateˌship (ˈmagnateˌship) noun

Word Origin

C15: back formation from earlier magnates from Late Latin: great men, plural of magnās, from Latin magnus great

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are resentful, you feel resentment. [+ about] ⇒ At first I felt very resentful and angry about losing my job. ⇒ He turned away in sullen, resentful silence.resentfully graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ For a moment she continued to look at him resentfully.

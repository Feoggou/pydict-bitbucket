incoming

(ˈɪnˌkʌmɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. coming in; entering2. about to come into office; succeeding3. (of interest, dividends, etc) being received; accruingnoun4. the act of coming in; entrance5. (usually plural) income or revenue
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

incoming

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnkʌmɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]An incoming message or phone call is one that you receive. ⇒ We keep a tape of incoming calls.2. adjective [ADJ n]An incoming plane or passenger is one that is arriving at a place. ⇒ The airport was closed for incoming flights.3. adjective [ADJ n]An incoming official or government is one that has just been appointed or elected. ⇒ ...the problems confronting the incoming government.4. adjective [ADJ n]An incoming tide or wave is coming towards the shore.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

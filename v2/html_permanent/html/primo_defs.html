primo

(ˈpriːməʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -mos or -mi (-mɪ)1. music the upper or right-hand part in a piano duet. Compare secondo2. Also: primo tempo at the same speed as at the beginning of the piece

Word Origin

Italian: first, from Latin prīmus

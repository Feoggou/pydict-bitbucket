Word Frequency

goggles

(ˈɡɒɡəl)
Definitions
plural nounlarge glasses, often of coloured glass or covered with gauze, that fit closely to your face around your eyes to protect them from such things as water, wind, or dust ⇒ a swimmer's goggles, ⇒ a motorcyclist's goggles

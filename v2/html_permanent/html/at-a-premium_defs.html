at a premium

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf you buy or sell something at a premium, you buy or sell it at a higher price than usual, for example because it is in short supply. ⇒ He eventually sold the shares back to the bank at a premium. →  premium

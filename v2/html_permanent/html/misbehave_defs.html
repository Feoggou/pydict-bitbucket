Powered by COBUILD
(mɪsbɪheɪv )
Word forms: misbehaves , misbehaving , misbehaved
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf someone, especially a child, misbehaves, they behave in a way that is not acceptable to other people. [V] ⇒ When the children misbehaved she was unable to cope.

panda

(ˈpændə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: giant panda a large black-and-white herbivorous bearlike mammal, Ailuropoda melanoleuca, related to the raccoons and inhabiting the high mountain bamboo forests of China: family Procyonidae2. See lesser panda

Word Origin

C19: via French from a native Nepalese word

countable nounA panda or a giant panda is a large animal rather like a bear, which has black and white fur and lives in the bamboo forests of China.

longe

(lʌndʒ ) in American English
Definitions
noun1.  a long rope fastened to a horse's head and held by the trainer, who causes the horse to move around in a circle2.  the use of the longe in training horses verb transitiveWord forms: longed, ˈlongeing or ˈlonging3.  to put (a horse) through his paces, using a longe
Webster’s New World College Dictionary, Fourth Edition, compiled by the editors of Webster’s New World Dictionaries. Copyright © 2010 by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. Published by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. All rights reserved.

Word Origin

Fr, back-form. < allonge, extension < allonger < LL elongare: see elongate

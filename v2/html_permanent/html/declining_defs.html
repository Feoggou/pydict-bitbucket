declining

(dɪˈklaɪnɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. deteriorating gradually, as in quality, health, or character ⇒  … a declining birth rate., ⇒ he's in declining health, ⇒ a declining industry2. of the time of a person's life during which he or she grows old and increasingly frail ⇒ in his declining years, ⇒ she spent her declining years in Devon
Example sentences containing 'declining'
Aware of my irritation declining I found myself recalling with reluctance the extraordinary deductive skill Darrow had shown earlier. Howatch, Susan ULTIMATE PRIZESHelena stared resolutely in front of her, declining to acknowledge him, though she was all too palpably aware of his presence. Appiganesi, Lisa DREAMS OF INNOCENCEThey were discussing declining standards of manners in Britain. Salley Vickers INSTANCES OF THE NUMBER 3

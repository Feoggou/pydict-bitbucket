antiseptic

adjective

  1. hygienic, clean, pure, sterile, sanitary, uncontaminated, unpolluted, germ-free, aseptic • These herbs have strong antiseptic qualities. OPPOSITES:  dirty, infected, contaminated, polluted, impure, septic, unhygienic, insanitary

noun

  1. disinfectant, purifier, bactericide, germicide • She bathed the cut with antiseptic.

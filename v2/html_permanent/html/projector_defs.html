projector

(prəˈdʒɛktə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an optical instrument that projects an enlarged image of individual slides onto a screen or wall. Full name: slide projector2. an optical instrument in which a strip of film is wound past a lens at a fixed speed so that the frames can be viewed as a continuously moving sequence on a screen or wall. Full name: film projector, cine projector3. a device for projecting a light beam4. a person who devises projects

unmanageable for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnmænɪdʒəbəl )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as unmanageable, you mean that it is difficult to use, deal with, or control. ⇒ People were visiting the house every day, sometimes in unmanageable numbers. ⇒ ...her freckles and unmanageable hair.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone, especially a young person, as unmanageable, you mean that they behave in an unacceptable way and are difficult to control. ⇒ The signs are that indulged children tend to become unmanageable when they reach their teens.

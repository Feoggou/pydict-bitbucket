khan1

(kɑːn )
Definitions
noun1. a. (formerly) a title borne by medieval Chinese emperors and Mongol and Turkic rulers: usually added to a name ⇒ Kublai Khanb. such a ruler2. a title of respect borne by important personages in Afghanistan and central Asia
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French caan, from Medieval Latin caanus, from Turkish khān, contraction of khāqān ruler

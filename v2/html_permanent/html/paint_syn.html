Synonyms of paint

paint

noun

  1. colouring, colour, stain, dye, tint, pigment, emulsion
    • a pot of red paint
  2. make-up (informal), face (informal), cosmetics, greasepaint, war paint (informal), maquillage (French)
    • She dabbed a little paint on her face and got ready to go out.

verb

  1. colour, cover, coat, decorate, stain, whitewash, daub, distemper, apply paint to
    • They painted the walls yellow.
  2. depict, draw, portray, figure, picture, represent, sketch, delineate, catch a likeness
    • He was painting a portrait of his wife.
  3. describe, capture, portray, depict, evoke, recount, bring to life, make you see, conjure up a vision, put graphically, tell vividly
    • The report paints a grim picture of life in the city.

QUOTATIONS:
And those who paint 'em truest praise 'em mostJoseph Addison – The Campaign
Every time I paint a portrait I lose a friendJohn Singer Sargent

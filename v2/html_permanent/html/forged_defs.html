forged

(fɔːdʒd)
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to metals that have been shaped by heating and hammering2. of or relating to that which has been fraudulently imitated ⇒ forged banknotes
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

solus

(ˈsəʊləs )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. alone; separate2. of or denoting the position of an advertising poster or press advertisement that is separated from competing advertisements ⇒ a solus position3. of or denoting a retail outlet, such as a petrol station, that sells the products of one company exclusively ⇒ a solus site4. Word forms: feminine sola alone; by oneself (formerly used in stage directions)

Word Origin

C17: from Latin sōlus alone

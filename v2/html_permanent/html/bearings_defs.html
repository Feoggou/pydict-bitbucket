bearings

(ˈbɛərɪŋz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. a sense of one's relative position or situation; orientation (esp in the phrases lose, get, or take one's bearings)2. nautical the position or direction, as of a ship, fixed with reference to two or more known points3. See to get your bearings
Example sentences containing 'bearings'
Stopping to take my bearings I saw the range of hills on the horizon. Brierley, David SNOWLINEWhitlock paid the driver then entered the diner, pausing momentarily in the doorway to get his bearings. MacNeill, Alistair ALASTAIR MCLEAN'S 'NIGHT WATCH'It took me a moment to get my bearings , then I chose my door. Val McDermid DEAD BEAT

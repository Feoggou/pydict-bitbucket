subdivide

(ˌsʌbdɪˈvaɪd , ˈsʌbdɪˌvaɪd )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to divide (something) resulting from an earlier division2. (transitive) US and Canadian to divide (land) into lots for sale

Derived Forms

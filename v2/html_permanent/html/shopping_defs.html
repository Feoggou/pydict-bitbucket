shopping

(ˈʃɒpɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a number or collection of articles purchased2. the act or an instance of making purchases

shopping

Powered by COBUILD
(ʃɒpɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounWhen you do the shopping, you go to shops and buy things. ⇒ I'll do the shopping this afternoon.2. → see also window shopping3. uncount nounYour shopping is the things that you have bought from shops, especially food. ⇒ We put the shopping away.

Similar Words

purchases, buys, acquisitions

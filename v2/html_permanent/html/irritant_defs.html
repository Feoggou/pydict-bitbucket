(ɪrɪtənt ) for learners of English

Definitions
1. countable nounIf you describe something as an irritant, you mean that it keeps annoying you. [formal] ⇒ He said the issue was not a major irritant.2. countable nounAn irritant is a substance which causes a part of your body to itch or become sore. [formal] ⇒ Many pesticides are irritants.

wanting

(ˈwɒntɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective (postpositive)1. lacking or absent; missing2. not meeting requirements or expectations ⇒ you have been found wantingpreposition3. without4. archaic minus
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

wanting for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(wɒntɪŋ )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If you find something or someone wanting, they are not of as high a standard as you think they should be. ⇒ He analysed his game and found it wanting. ⇒ Eleanor was scrutinized, too, and often found wanting. ⇒ He is wanting in moral constraints. [Also + in]

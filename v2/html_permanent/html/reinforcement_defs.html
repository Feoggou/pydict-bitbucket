Word forms: reinforcements
Definitions
1. plural nounReinforcements are soldiers or policemen who are sent to join an army or group of police in order to make it stronger. ⇒ ...the despatch of police and troop reinforcements.2. variable nounThe reinforcement of something is the process of making it stronger. [+ of] ⇒ I am sure that this meeting will contribute to the reinforcement of peace and security all over the world. ⇒ What the teacher now has to do is remove the reinforcement for this bad behaviour. ⇒ There are reinforcement bars on all doors.

Antichrist

(ˈæntɪˌkraɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. New Testament the antagonist of Christ, expected by early Christians to appear and reign over the world until overthrown at Christ's Second Coming2. (sometimes not capital) an enemy of Christ or Christianity
Derived forms
ˌAntiˈchristian adjective

spinning

(ˈspɪnɪŋ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. the act or process of spinningb. (as modifier) ⇒ spinning yarn2. the act or technique of casting and drawing a revolving lure through the water so as to imitate the movement of a live fish, etc

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA sea wall is a wall built along the edge of the sea to stop the sea flowing over the land or destroying it. ⇒ Cherbourg had a splendid harbour enclosed by a long sea wall.

Frothy waves are bouncing off the sea wall and the sun glints on the surface of the sea in dancing sparks.

hob1

(hɒb )
Definitions
noun1. British the flat top part of a cooking stove, or a separate flat surface, containing hotplates or burners2. a shelf beside an open fire, for keeping kettles, etc, hot3. a steel pattern used in forming a mould or die in cold metal4. a hard steel rotating cutting tool used in machines for cutting gearsverbWord forms: hobs, hobbing or hobbed5. (transitive) to cut or form with a hob
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: variant of obsolete hubbe, of unknown origin; perhaps related to hub

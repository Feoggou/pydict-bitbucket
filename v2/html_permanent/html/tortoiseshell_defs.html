tortoiseshell

(ˈtɔːtəsˌʃɛl )
Definitions
noun1. a horny translucent yellow-and-brown mottled substance obtained from the outer layer of the shell of the hawksbill turtle: used for making ornaments, jewellery, etc2. a similar synthetic substance, esp plastic or celluloid, now more widely used than the natural product3. Also called: tortoiseshell cat a breed of domestic cat, usually female, having black, cream, and brownish markings4. any of several nymphalid butterflies of the genus Nymphalis, and related genera, having orange-brown wings with black markings5. See tortoiseshell turtle6. a. a yellowish-brown mottled colourb. (as adjective) ⇒ a tortoiseshell décor7. (modifier) made of tortoiseshell ⇒ a tortoiseshell comb

tortoiseshell

Powered by COBUILD
(tɔːʳtəsʃel ) for learners of English
Word forms: tortoiseshells
Definitions
1. uncount nounTortoiseshell is the hard shell of a kind of sea turtle. It is brown and yellow in colour and is often polished and used to make jewellery and ornaments.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Tortoiseshell means made of tortoiseshell or made of a material which resembles tortoiseshell. ⇒ He wears huge spectacles with thick tortoiseshell frames.3. countable nounA tortoiseshell is a butterfly with brown and orange wings.

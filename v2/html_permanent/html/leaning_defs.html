(liːnɪŋ ) for learners of English
Word forms: leanings
Definitions
countable noun [usu pl]Your particular leanings are the beliefs, ideas, or aims you hold or a tendency you have towards them. ⇒ Many companies are wary of their socialist leanings. [+ towards] ⇒ I always had a leaning towards sport.

conclusive

(kənˈkluːsɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. putting an end to doubt; decisive; final2. approaching or involving an end or conclusion
Derived forms
conclusively (conˈclusively) adverbconclusiveness (conˈclusiveness) noun

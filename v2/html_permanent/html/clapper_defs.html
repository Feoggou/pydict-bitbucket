clapper

(ˈklæpə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that claps2. a contrivance for producing a sound of clapping, as for scaring birds3. Also called: tongue a small piece of metal suspended within a bell that causes it to sound when made to strike against its side4. a slang word for tongue (sense 1)5. See go like the clappers

humid

(ˈhjuːmɪd )
Definitions
adjectivemoist; damp ⇒ a humid day
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

humidly (ˈhumidly) adverbhumidness (ˈhumidness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin ūmidus, from ūmēre to be wet; see humectant, humour

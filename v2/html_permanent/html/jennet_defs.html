jennet

, genet or gennet (ˈdʒɛnɪt )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: jenny a female donkey or ass2. a small Spanish riding horse
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French genet, from Catalan ginet, horse of the type used by the Zenete, from Arabic Zanātah the Zenete, a Moorish people renowned for their horsemanship

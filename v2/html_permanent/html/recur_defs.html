recur

(rɪˈkɜː )
Definitions
verb -curs, -curring or -curred (intransitive)1. to happen again, esp at regular intervals2. (of a thought, idea, etc) to come back to the mind3. (of a problem, etc) to come up again4. mathematics (of a digit or group of digits) to be repeated an infinite number of times at the end of a decimal fraction
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

recurring (reˈcurring) adjectiverecurringly (reˈcurringly) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Latin recurrere, from re- + currere to run

handicraft

(ˈhændɪˌkrɑːft )
Definitions
noun1. skill or dexterity in working with the hands2. a particular skill or art performed with the hands, such as weaving, pottery, etc3. Also called: handcraft the work produced by such a skill or art ⇒ local handicraft is on sale
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

handicraftsman (ˈhandiˌcraftsman) or handicrafter (ˈhandiˌcrafter) noun

Word Origin

C15: changed from handcraft through the influence of handiwork, which was analysed as if handy + work

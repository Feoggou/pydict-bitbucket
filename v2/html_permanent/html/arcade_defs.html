arcade

(ɑːˈkeɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a set of arches and their supporting columns2. a covered and sometimes arched passageway, usually with shops on one or both sides3. a building, or part of a building, with an arched roof
Word origin
C18: from French, from Italian arcata, from arco, from Latin arcus bow, arch

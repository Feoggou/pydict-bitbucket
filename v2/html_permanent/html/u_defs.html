u

or U (juː )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural u's, U's or Us1. the 21st letter and fifth vowel of the modern English alphabet2. any of several speech sounds represented by this letter, in English as in mute, cut, hurt, sure, pull, or minus3. a. something shaped like a Ub. (in combination) ⇒ a U-bolt, ⇒ a U-turn

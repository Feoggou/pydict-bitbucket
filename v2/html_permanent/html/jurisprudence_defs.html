jurisprudence

(ˌdʒʊərɪsˈpruːdəns )
Definitions
noun1. the science or philosophy of law2. a system or body of law3. a branch of law ⇒ medical jurisprudence
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

jurisprudential (ˌdʒʊərɪspruːˈdɛnʃəl ) adjectivejurisprudentially (ˌjurispruˈdentially) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin jūris prūdentia; see jus, prudence

chin

(tʃɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the protruding part of the lower jaw2. the front part of the face below the lips ▶ Related adjective: genial3. See keep one's chin up4. See take it on the chinverbWord forms: chins, chinning or chinned5. gymnastics to raise one's chin to (a horizontal bar, etc) when hanging by the arms6. (transitive) informal to punch or hit (someone) on the chin
Word origin
Old English cinn; related to Old Norse kinn, Old High German kinni, Latin gena cheek, Old Irish gin mouth, Sanskrit hanu

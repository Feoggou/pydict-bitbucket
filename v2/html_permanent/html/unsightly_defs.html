(ʌnsaɪtli )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as unsightly, you mean that it is unattractive to look at. ⇒ My mother has had unsightly varicose veins for years. ⇒ The Polish market in Berlin was considered unsightly and shut down.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

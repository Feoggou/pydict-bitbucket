unsettling

(ʌnˈsɛtəlɪŋ)
Definitions
adjectivemaking one feel rather worried or uncertain ⇒ We fully appreciate that coming into hospital can be an unsettling experience., ⇒ His sense of humour was really unsettling., ⇒ It is a disturbing and unsettling film which makes for powerful viewing., ⇒ The prospect of change of this kind has an unsettling effect on any organisation.

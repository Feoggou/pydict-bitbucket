attorney

(əˈtɜːnɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person legally appointed or empowered to act for another2. US a lawyer qualified to represent clients in legal proceedings3. South Africa a solicitor
Derived forms
atˈtorneyˌship noun
Word origin
C14: from Old French atourné, from atourner to direct to; see attorn

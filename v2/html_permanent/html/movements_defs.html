movements

(ˈmuːvmənts)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. a person's location and activities during a specific time ⇒ I want a full account of your movements the night Mr Gower was killed., ⇒ He was questioned about his movements on the weekend that the girl vanished., ⇒ his movements on 1 July last year2. See to monitor sb's movements
Example sentences containing 'movements'
Adam had traced every hour of Liz's movements for the week prior to her getting on that train. Stuart Harrison LOST SUMMERHe was almost certain that he could hear stealthy movements at the tree's foot far below. J.R.R. Tolkien THE LORD OF THE RINGSBeing jealous and afraid of Gandalf he sets spies to watch all his movements ; and notes his interest in the Shire. J.R.R. Tolkien THE LORD OF THE RINGS

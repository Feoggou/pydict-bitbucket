cohabit

(kəʊˈhæbɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(intransitive) to live together in a conjugal relationship, esp without being married
Derived forms
ˌcohabitee (ˌcohabiˈtee), cohabitant (coˈhabitant), cohabiter (coˈhabiter) or cohabitor (coˈhabitor) noun
Word origin
C16: via Late Latin, from Latin co- together + habitāre to live

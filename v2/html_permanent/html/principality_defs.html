principality

(ˌprɪnsɪˈpælɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. a. a territory ruled by a princeb. a territory from which a prince draws his title2. the dignity or authority of a prince

Word Origin

C14 (in the sense: pre-eminence): via Old French from Latin principālis principal

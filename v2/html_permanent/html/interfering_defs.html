(ɪntəʳfɪərɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]If you describe someone as interfering, you are criticizing them because they try to get involved in other people's affairs or to give them advice, especially when the advice is not wanted. [disapproval] ⇒ When she was a child she had regarded her mother as an interfering busybody. ⇒ ...interfering neighbours.

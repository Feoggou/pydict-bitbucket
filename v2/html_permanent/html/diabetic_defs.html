(ˌdaɪəˈbɛtɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective also: diabetical1. of, relating to, or having diabetes2. for the use of diabetics ⇒ diabetic chocolatenoun3. a person who has diabetes▶ USAGE Rather than talking about a diabetic or diabetics, it is better to talk about a person with diabetes, people with diabetes

Word forms: diabetics
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA diabetic is a person who suffers from diabetes. ⇒ ...an insulin-dependent diabetic.Diabetic is also an adjective. ⇒ ...diabetic patients.2. adjective [ADJ n]Diabetic means relating to diabetes. ⇒ He found her in a diabetic coma.3. adjective [ADJ n]Diabetic foods are suitable for diabetics. ⇒ ...diabetic jams and marmalades.

eightieth

(ˈeɪtɪɪθ )
Definitions
adjective1. (usually prenominal)a. being the ordinal number of eighty in numbering or counting order, position, time, etc: often written 80thb. (as noun) ⇒ the eightieth in successionnoun2. a. one of 80 approximately equal parts of somethingb. (as modifier) ⇒ an eightieth part3. the fraction equal to one divided by 80 (1⁄80)

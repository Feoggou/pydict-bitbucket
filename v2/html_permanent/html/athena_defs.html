Athena

(əˈθiːnə ) or Athene (əˈθiːnɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounGreek mythology a virgin goddess of wisdom, practical skills, and prudent warfare. She was born, fully armed, from the head of Zeus. Also called: Pallas Athena, Pallas. Roman counterpart: Minerva

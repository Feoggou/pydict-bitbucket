Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
passive verbA word or code number that is prefixed by one or more letters or numbers has them as its prefix. [be V-ed + by] ⇒ Sulphur-containing compounds are often prefixed by the term 'thio'. [be V-ed + with] ⇒ Calls to Dublin should now be prefixed with 010 3531.

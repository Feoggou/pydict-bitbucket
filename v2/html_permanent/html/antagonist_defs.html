antagonist

(ænˈtæɡənɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an opponent or adversary, as in a contest, drama, sporting event, etc2. any muscle that opposes the action of another. Compare agonist (sense 1)3. a drug that counteracts the effects of another drug. Compare synergist (sense 1)

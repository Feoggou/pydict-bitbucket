eventual

(ɪˈvɛntʃʊəl )
Definitions
adjective(prenominal) happening in due course of time; ultimate ⇒ the eventual outcome was his defeat

eventual

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪventʃuəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]You use eventual to indicate that something happens or is the case at the end of a process or period of time. ⇒ There are many who believe that civil war will be the eventual outcome of the racial tension in the country. ⇒ The eventual aim is reunification.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Arts and Crafts (movement)

Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
a social and artistic movement of the second half of the 19th cent. emphasizing a return to handwork, skilled craftsmanship, and attention to design in the decorative arts, from the mechanization and mass production of the Industrial Revolution: also written Arts and Crafts Movement

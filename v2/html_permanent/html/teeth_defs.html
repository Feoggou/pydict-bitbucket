teeth

(tiːθ )
Definitions
noun1. the plural of tooth2. the most violent part ⇒ the teeth of the gale3. the power to produce a desired effect ⇒ that law has no teeth4. See by the skin of one's teeth5. See get one's teeth into6. See in the teeth of7. See show one's teeth8. See to the teeth

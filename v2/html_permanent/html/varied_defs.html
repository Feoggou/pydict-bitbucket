varied

(ˈvɛərɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. displaying or characterized by variety; diverse2. modified or altered ⇒ the amount may be varied without notice3. varicoloured; variegated
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

variedly (ˈvariedly) adverbvariedness (ˈvariedness) noun

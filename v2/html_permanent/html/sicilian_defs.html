Definitions

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveSicilian means belonging or relating to Sicily, or to its people or culture. ⇒ ...the Sicilian village of Corleone.2. countable nounA Sicilian is a person who comes from Sicily.

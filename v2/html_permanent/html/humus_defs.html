humus

(ˈhjuːməs )
Definitions
nouna dark brown or black colloidal mass of partially decomposed organic matter in the soil. It improves the fertility and water retention of the soil and is therefore important for plant growthAvoid confusion with hummus
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Latin: soil, earth

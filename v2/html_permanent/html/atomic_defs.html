atomic

(əˈtɒmɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, using, or characterized by atomic bombs or atomic energy ⇒ atomic warfare2. of, related to, or comprising atoms ⇒ atomic hydrogen3. extremely small; minute4. logic (of a sentence, formula, etc) having no internal structure at the appropriate level of analysis. In predicate calculus, Fa is an atomic sentence and Fx an atomic predicate
Derived forms
aˈtomically adverb

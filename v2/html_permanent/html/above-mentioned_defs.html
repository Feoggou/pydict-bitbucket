above-mentioned

(əˌbʌvˈmɛnʃənd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveused in a text to refer back to a person or thing that has already been mentioned ⇒ In the above-mentioned book there is a chapter about the village's history., ⇒ the activities which resulted in his arrest in the above-mentioned case

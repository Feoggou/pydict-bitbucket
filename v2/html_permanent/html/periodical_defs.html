Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a publication issued at regular intervals, usually monthly or weeklyadjective2. of or relating to such publications3. published at regular intervals4. periodic or occasional

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounPeriodicals are magazines, especially serious or academic ones, that are published at regular intervals. ⇒ The walls would be lined with books and periodicals. ⇒ ...a monthly periodical.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Periodical events or situations happen occasionally, at fairly regular intervals. ⇒ She made periodical visits to her dentist. ⇒ ...periodical screening for cancer.periodically (pɪəriɒdɪkli ) adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Meetings are held periodically to monitor progress on the case. ⇒ Police were periodically patrolling the area.

servitude

(ˈsɜːvɪˌtjuːd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the state or condition of a slave; bondage2. the state or condition of being subjected to or dominated by a person or thing ⇒ servitude to drink3. law a burden attaching to an estate for the benefit of an adjoining estate or of some definite person. See also easement4. short for penal servitude

Word Origin

C15: via Old French from Latin servitūdō, from servus a slave

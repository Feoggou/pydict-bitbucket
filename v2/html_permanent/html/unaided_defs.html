unaided for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌneɪdɪd )
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ after v, oft ADJ n]If you do something unaided, you do it without help from anyone or anything else. ⇒ There have been at least thirteen previous attempts to reach the North Pole unaided. ⇒ She brought us up completely unaided. ⇒ ...the smallest speck of matter visible to the unaided eye.

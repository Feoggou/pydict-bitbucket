heighten

(ˈhaɪtən )
Definitions
verb1. to make or become high or higher2. to make or become more extreme or intense

(haɪtən ) for learners of English
Word forms: heightens , heightening , heightened
Definitions
ergative verb & verbIf something heightens a feeling or if the feeling heightens, the feeling increases in degree or intensity. [V n] ⇒ The move has heightened tension in the state. [V n] ⇒ These latest murders have heightened fears of further attacks. [V] ⇒ Cross's interest heightened. [V-ed] ⇒ ...a heightened awareness of the dangers that they now face.

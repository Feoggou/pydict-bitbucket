flat screen

(flæt skriːn)
Definitions
noun1. electronics, television a slimline screen for a television set or computer monitor without a cathode-ray tubemodifier2. (of a television set or computer monitor) having a slim screen without a cathode-ray tube ⇒ flat screen technology, ⇒ a flatscreen television

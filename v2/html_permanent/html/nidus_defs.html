nidus

(ˈnaɪdəs )
Definitions
noun plural -di (-daɪ)1. the nest in which insects or spiders deposit their eggs2. pathology a focus of infection3. a cavity in which plant spores develop
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

nidal (ˈnidal) adjective

Word Origin

C18: from Latin nest

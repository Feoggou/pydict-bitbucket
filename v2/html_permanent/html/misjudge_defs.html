Word forms: misjudges , misjudging , misjudged
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf you say that someone has misjudged a person or situation, you mean that they have formed an incorrect idea or opinion about them, and often that they have made a wrong decision as a result of this. [V n] ⇒ Perhaps I had misjudged him, and he was not so predictable after all. [V n] ⇒ As I swung down from out of my bunk, I got dizzy and misjudged the distance.

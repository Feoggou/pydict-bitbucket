ghostly

(ˈɡəʊstlɪ )
Definitions
adjective -lier or -liest1. of or resembling a ghost; spectral ⇒ a ghostly face appeared at the window2. suggesting the presence of ghosts; eerie3. archaic of or relating to the soul or spirit
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

ghostliness (ˈghostliness) noun

ghostly

Powered by COBUILD
(goʊstli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Something that is ghostly seems unreal or unnatural and may be frightening because of this. ⇒ The moon shone, shedding a ghostly light on the fields. ⇒ ...Sonia's ghostly laughter.2. adjective [ADJ n]A ghostly presence is the ghost or spirit of a dead person. ⇒ ...the ghostly presences which haunt these islands. ⇒ Charles is haunted by ghostly children.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

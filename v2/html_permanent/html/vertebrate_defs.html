vertebrate

(ˈvɜːtɪˌbreɪt , -brɪt)
Definitions
noun1. any chordate animal of the subphylum Vertebrata, characterized by a bony or cartilaginous skeleton and a well-developed brain: the group contains fishes, amphibians, reptiles, birds, and mammalsadjective2. of, relating to, or belonging to the subphylum Vertebrata

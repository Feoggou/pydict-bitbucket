ground plan

Definitions
noun1. a drawing of the ground floor of a building, esp one to scale. See also plan (sense 3). Compare elevation (sense 5)2. a preliminary or basic outline

Word forms: ground plans
Definitions
1. countable nounIn British English, a ground plan is a plan of the ground floor of a building. In American English, a ground plan is a plan of any floor of a building.2. countable nounA ground plan is a basic plan for future action.

ken

(kɛn )
Definitions
noun1. range of knowledge or perception (esp in the phrases beyond or in one's ken)verbWord forms: kens, kenning, kenned or kent (kɛnt )2. Scottish and Northern England dialect to know3. Scottish and Northern England dialect to understand; perceive4. (transitive) archaic to see
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English cennan; related to Old Norse kenna to perceive, Old High German kennen to make known; see can1

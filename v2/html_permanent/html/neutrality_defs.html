neutrality

(njuːˈtrælɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun1. the state or character of being neutral, esp in a dispute, contest, etc2. the condition of being chemically or electrically neutral
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Kenworthy did his best to preserve an expression of neutrality , did not allow himself to show a trait of surprise.

personage

(ˈpɜːsənɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an important or distinguished person2. another word for person (sense 1) ⇒ a strange personage3. rare a figure in literature, history, etc

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA personage is a famous or important person. [formal] ⇒ ...MPs, film stars and other important personages.2. countable nounA personage is a character in a play or book, or in history. [formal] ⇒ There is no evidence for such a historical personage. ⇒ ...Shakespeare's famous personages.

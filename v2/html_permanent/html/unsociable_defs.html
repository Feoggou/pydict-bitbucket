unsociable

(ʌnˈsəʊʃəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. (of a person) disinclined to associate or fraternize with others2. unconducive to social intercourse ⇒ an unsociable neighbourhood
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unsociability (unˌsociaˈbility) or unsociableness (unˈsociableness) noununsociably (unˈsociably) adverb

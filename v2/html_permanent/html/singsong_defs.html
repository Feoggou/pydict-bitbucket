singsong

(ˈsɪŋˌsɒŋ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an accent, metre, or intonation that is characterized by an alternately rising and falling rhythm, as in a person's voice, piece of verse, etc2. British an informal session of singing, esp of popular or traditional songsadjective3. having a regular or monotonous rising and falling rhythm ⇒ a singsong accent

modification

(ˌmɒdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of modifying or the condition of being modified2. something modified; the result of a modification3. a small change or adjustment4. grammar the relation between a modifier and the word or phrase that it modifies

Derived Forms

modifiˌcatory (ˈmodifiˌcatory) or modifiˌcative (ˈmodifiˌcative) adjective

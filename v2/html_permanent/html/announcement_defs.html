announcement

(əˈnaʊnsmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a public statement2. a brief item or advertisement, as in a newspaper3. a formal printed or written invitation4. the act of announcing

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [N that]An announcement is a statement made to the public or to the media which gives information about something that has happened or that will happen. ⇒ Sir Robert made his announcement after talks with the President. ⇒ There has been no formal announcement by either government.2. singular nounThe announcement of something that has happened is the act of telling people about it. [+ of] ⇒ ...the announcement of their engagement. ⇒ There has been no official announcement of the arrests.3. countable nounAn announcement in a public place, such as a newspaper or the window of a shop, is a short piece of writing telling people about something or asking for something. ⇒ He will place an announcement in the personal column of The Daily Telegraph.

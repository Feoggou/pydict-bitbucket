Synonyms of kiss

kiss

verb

  1. peck (informal), osculate, snog (British, slang), neck (informal), smooch (informal), canoodle (slang), x (text messaging), buss (archaic)
    • She kissed me hard on the mouth.
  2. brush, touch, shave, scrape, graze, caress, glance off, stroke
    • The wheels of the aircraft kissed the runway.

noun

  1. peck (informal), snog (British, slang), smacker (slang), smooch (informal), French kiss, osculation, x (text messaging), buss (archaic)
    • I put my arms around her and gave her a kiss.
related adjective:
oscular

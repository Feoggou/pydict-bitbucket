blackthorn

(ˈblækˌθɔːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: sloe a thorny Eurasian rosaceous shrub, Prunus spinosa, with black twigs, white flowers, and small sour plumlike fruits2. a walking stick made from its wood

pitchfork

(ˈpɪtʃˌfɔːk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a long-handled fork with two or three long curved tines for lifting, turning, or tossing hayverb (transitive)2. to use a pitchfork on (something)3. to thrust (someone) unwillingly into a position

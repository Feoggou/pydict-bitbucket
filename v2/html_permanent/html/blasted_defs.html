(blɑːstɪd , blæstɪd)
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Some people use blasted to express anger or annoyance at something or someone. [informal, old-fashioned, feelings] ⇒ I couldn't get that blasted door open.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]A blasted landscape has very few plants or trees, and makes you feel sad or depressed when you look at it. [literary] ⇒ ...the blasted landscape where the battle was fought.

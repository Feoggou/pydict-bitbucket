(wʌrid , US wɜːrid )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [ADJ that]When you are worried, you are unhappy because you keep thinking about problems that you have or about unpleasant things that might happen in the future. ⇒ He seemed very worried. [+ about] ⇒ If you're at all worried about his progress, do discuss it with one of his teachers. ⇒ The unions are worried that at least 100,000 jobs will disappear as a result of privatization.worriedly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ 'You don't have to go, you know,' she said worriedly.

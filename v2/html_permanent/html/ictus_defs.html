ictus

(ˈɪktəs )
Definitions
noun plural -tuses or -tus1. prosody metrical or rhythmic stress in verse feet, as contrasted with the stress accent on words2. medicine a sudden attack or stroke
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ictal (ˈictal) adjective

Word Origin

C18: from Latin icere to strike

HIV

Powered by COBUILD
(eɪtʃ aɪ viː ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount noun [oft N n]HIV is a virus which reduces people's resistance to illness and can cause AIDS. HIV is an abbreviation for 'human immunodeficiency virus'.2. →  HIV positive

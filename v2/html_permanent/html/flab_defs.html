flab

(flæb )
Definitions
noununsightly or unwanted fat on the body; flabbiness
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: back formation from flabby

flab

Powered by COBUILD
(flæb ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIf you say that someone has flab, you mean they have loose flesh on their body because they are rather fat, especially when you are being critical of them. [disapproval] ⇒ Don had a hefty roll of flab overhanging his waistband.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

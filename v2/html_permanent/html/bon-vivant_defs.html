bon vivant

French (bɔ̃ vivɑ̃)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural bons vivants (bɔ̃ vivɑ̃)a person who enjoys luxuries, esp good food and drink. Also called (but not in French): bon viveur (ˌbɒn viːˈvɜː )
Word origin
literally: good living (man)
Example sentences containing 'bon vivant'
The bon vivant in thin superposition over the hard expression of the classic bloodsucker. Gagman, Maurice DOUBTFUL MOTIVESThat didn't mean I began to live like an anchorite; far from it: I suppose I will always be true to my nature as a bon vivant. Adair, Tom (Intro) THREE KINDS OF KISSING - SCOTTISH SHORT STORIESMay I present my father, Michael Tolliver, the famous raconteur and bon vivant , and my mother, the gynecologist! Maupin, Armistead TALES OF THE CITY

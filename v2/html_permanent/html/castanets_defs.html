castanets

(ˌkæstəˈnɛts )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nouncurved pieces of hollow wood, usually held between the fingers and thumb and made to click together: used esp by Spanish dancers
Word origin
C17 castanet, from Spanish castañeta, diminutive of castaña chestnut

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
plural noun [oft a pair of N]Castanets are a Spanish musical instrument consisting of two small round pieces of wood or plastic held together by a cord. You hold the castanets in your hand and knock the pieces together with your fingers.

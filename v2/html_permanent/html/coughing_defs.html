coughing

(ˈkɒfɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe action of expelling air or solid matter from the lungs abruptly and explosively through the partially closed vocal cords ⇒ the pressure created by coughing and sneezing, ⇒ He was then overcome by a fit of coughing.

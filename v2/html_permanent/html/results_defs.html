results

(rɪˈzʌlts)
Definitions
plural noun1. good results; success ⇒ Men realise it is results that matter, not the hours put in., ⇒ We have managed to get results without reaching our full potential, ⇒ The efforts of the government are very solid and are showing results., ⇒ The European Community initiative in Yugoslavia, he said, was already showing results., ⇒ I'm a workaholic who gets results., ⇒ A methodical approach will produce results., ⇒ A real pizza oven gives better results than an ordinary home oven.2. numbers or information obtained from carrying out a test, trial, or examination, esp a medical examination ⇒ The results of his research did not support his theory., ⇒ The results were published in August 1941, when it was reported that the effects of the drug were promising., ⇒ the results of my blood test3. especially British marks or grades in an examination ⇒ Kate's exam results were excellent., ⇒ my exam results

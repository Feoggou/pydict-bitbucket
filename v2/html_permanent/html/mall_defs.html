mall

(mæl , mɔːl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a shaded avenue, esp one that is open to the public2. US, Canadian, Australian and New Zealand short for shopping mall

Word Origin

C17: after The Mall, in St James's Park, London. See pall-mall

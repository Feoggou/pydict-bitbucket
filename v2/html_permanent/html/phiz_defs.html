phiz

(fɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounslang, mainly British the face or a facial expression ⇒ an ugly phiz. Also called: phizog (ˈfɪzɒɡ , fɪˈzɒɡ )

Word Origin

C17: colloquial shortening of physiognomy

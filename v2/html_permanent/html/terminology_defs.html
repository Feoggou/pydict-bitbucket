terminology

(ˌtɜːmɪˈnɒlədʒɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -gies1. the body of specialized words relating to a particular subject2. the study of terms
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

terminological (ˌtɜːmɪnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ) adjectiveterminologically (ˌterminoˈlogically) adverbterminologist (ˌtermiˈnologist) noun

Word Origin

C19: from Medieval Latin terminus term, from Latin: end

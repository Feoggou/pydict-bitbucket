intimate1

(ˈɪntɪmɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. characterized by a close or warm personal relationship ⇒ an intimate friend2. deeply personal, private, or secret3. (often postpositive; foll by with) euphemistic having sexual relations (with)4. a. (postpositive; foll by with) having a deep or unusual knowledge (of)b. (of knowledge) deep; extensive5. having a friendly, warm, or informal atmosphere ⇒ an intimate nightclub6. of or relating to the essential part or nature of something; intrinsic7. denoting the informal second person of verbs and pronouns in French and other languagesnoun8. a close friend
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

intimately (ˈintimately) adverbintimateness (ˈintimateness) or archaic intimity (ɪnˈtɪmɪtɪ) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin intimus very close friend, from (adj): innermost, deepest, from intus within

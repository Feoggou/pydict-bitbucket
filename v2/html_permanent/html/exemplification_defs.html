exemplification

(ɛgˌzɛmpləfɪˈkeɪʃən ; ɪgˌzɛmpləfɪˈkeɪʃən ) in American English
Definitions
noun1.  a showing by example2.  something that exemplifies; example3.  Law a legally attested or certified copy or transcript

Word Origin

ME < ML exemplificatio: see exemplify

example, illustration, representation, prototype, manifestation, paradigm, embodiment, epitome, exemplar, personification

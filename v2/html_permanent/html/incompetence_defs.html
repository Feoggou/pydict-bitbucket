incompetence

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnkɒmpɪtəns ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIf you refer to someone's incompetence, you are criticizing them because they are unable to do their job or a task properly. [disapproval] [+ of] ⇒ The incompetence of government officials is appalling. ⇒ ...his incompetence in failing to conduct full inquiries.

oversee

(ˌəʊvəˈsiː )
Definitions
verb -sees, -seeing, -saw or -seen (transitive)1. to watch over and direct; supervise2. to watch secretly or accidentally

Definitions
verbIf someone in authority oversees a job or an activity, they make sure that it is done properly. [V n] ⇒ Use a surveyor or architect to oversee and inspect the different stages of the work. [V n] ⇒ ...the agreement to set up a commission to oversee the peace process.

ovate

(ˈəʊveɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. shaped like an egg2. (esp of a leaf) shaped like the longitudinal section of an egg, with the broader end at the base. Compare obovate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ovately (ˈovately) adverb

Word Origin

C18: from Latin ōvātus egg-shaped; see ovum

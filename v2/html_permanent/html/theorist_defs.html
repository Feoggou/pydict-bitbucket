Powered by COBUILD
(θiːərɪst ) for learners of English
Word forms: theorists
Definitions
countable nounA theorist is someone who develops an abstract idea or set of ideas about a particular subject in order to explain it. ⇒ ...theorists unaligned with any particular doctrine.

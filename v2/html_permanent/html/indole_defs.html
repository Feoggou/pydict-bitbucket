indole

(ˈɪndəʊl ) or indol (ˈɪndəʊl , -dɒl)
Definitions
nouna white or yellowish crystalline heterocyclic compound extracted from coal tar and used in perfumery, medicine, and as a flavouring agent; 1-benzopyrrole. Formula: C8H7N
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from ind(igo) + -ole1

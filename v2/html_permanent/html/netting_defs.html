netting for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(netɪŋ )
Definitions
uncount nounNetting is a kind of material made of pieces of thread or metal wires. These are woven together so that there are equal spaces between them. ⇒ ...mosquito netting. ⇒ ...wire netting.

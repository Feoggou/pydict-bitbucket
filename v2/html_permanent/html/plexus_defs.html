plexus

(ˈplɛksəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -uses or -us1. any complex network of nerves, blood vessels, or lymphatic vessels2. an intricate network or arrangement

Word Origin

C17: New Latin, from Latin plectere to braid, plait

collegium

(kəˈliːdʒɪəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -giums or -gia (-dʒɪə)1. (in the former Soviet Union) a board in charge of a department2. another term for College of Cardinals, Sacred College
Word origin
Latin: college
Example sentences containing 'collegium'
`You, I and the fair Thomasina have been invited to sup tonight with our collegium of doctors near Queningate. Grace, C.L A SHRINE OF MURDERS

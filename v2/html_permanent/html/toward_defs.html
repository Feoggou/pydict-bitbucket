toward

Definitions
adjective (ˈtəʊəd )1. rare in progress; afoot2. obsolete about to happen; imminent3. obsolete promising or favourablepreposition (təˈwɔːd , tɔːd )4. a variant of towards
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

towardness (ˈtowardness) noun

Word Origin

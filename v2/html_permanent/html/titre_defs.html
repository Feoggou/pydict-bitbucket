titre

or US titer (ˈtaɪtə , ˈtiː-)
Definitions
noun1. a. the concentration of a solution as determined by titrationb. the minimum quantity of a solution required to complete a reaction in a titration2. the quantity of antibody present in an organism
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from French titre proportion of gold or silver in an alloy, from Old French title title

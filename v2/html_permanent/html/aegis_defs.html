aegis

or sometimes US egis (ˈiːdʒɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. sponsorship or protection; auspices (esp in the phrase under the aegis of)2. Greek mythology the shield of Zeus, often represented in art as a goatskin
Word origin
C18: from Latin, from Greek aigis shield of Zeus, perhaps related to aig-, stem of aix goat

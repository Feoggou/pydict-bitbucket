detached house

(dɪˈtætʃt haʊs)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounBritish a house that is not joined to any other house ⇒ a large detached house surrounded by a garden
Example sentences containing 'detached house'
The property in the file was a detached house on an exclusive development in Whitefield.

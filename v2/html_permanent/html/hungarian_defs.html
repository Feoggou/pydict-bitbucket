Hungarian

(hʌŋˈɡɛərɪən )
Definitions
noun1. the official language of Hungary, also spoken in Romania and elsewhere, belonging to the Finno-Ugric family and most closely related to the Ostyak and Vogul languages of NW Siberia2. a native, inhabitant, or citizen of Hungary3. a Hungarian-speaking person who is not a citizen of Hungaryadjective4. of or relating to Hungary, its people, or their language▷ Compare Magyar

divisive

(dɪˈvaɪsɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. causing or tending to cause disagreement or dissension2. archaic having the quality of distinguishing
Derived forms
divisively (diˈvisively) adverbdivisiveness (diˈvisiveness) noun

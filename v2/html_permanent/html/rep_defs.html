rep

Powered by COBUILD
(rep ) for learners of English
Word forms: reps
Definitions
1. countable nounA rep is a person whose job is to sell a company's products or services, especially by travelling round and visiting other companies. Rep is short for representative. [+ for] ⇒ I'd been working as a sales rep for a photographic company.2. → See also holiday rep3. countable nounA rep is a person who acts as a representative for a group of people, usually a group of people who work together. ⇒ Contact the health and safety rep at your union.4. uncount nounIn the theatre, rep is the same as repertory. ⇒ A play is tested in rep before ever hitting a West End stage.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

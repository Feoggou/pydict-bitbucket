rags

(ræɡz )
Definitions
plural noun1. torn, old, or shabby clothing2. cotton or linen cloth waste used in the manufacture of rag paper3. See from rags to riches4. See glad rags
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

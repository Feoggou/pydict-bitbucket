all-round

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. efficient in all respects, esp in sport; versatile ⇒ an all-round player2. comprehensive; many-sided; not narrow ⇒ an all-round education

all-round

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
regional note:   in AM, also use all-around1. adjective [ADJ n]An all-round person is good at a lot of different skills, academic subjects, or sports. ⇒ He is a great all-round player.2. adjective [ADJ n]All-round means doing or relating to all aspects of a job or activity. ⇒ He demonstrated the all-round skills of a quarterback. ⇒ ...if you want the best all-round quality of life. ⇒ ...an excellent all-round guide on how to shop for the very best foods.

all round

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseYou say all round to emphasize that something affects all parts of a situation or all members of a group. [mainly British, emphasis] ⇒ It ought to make life much easier all round. ⇒ Nerves are frayed all round. →  round

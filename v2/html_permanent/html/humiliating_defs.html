Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is humiliating, it embarrasses you and makes you feel ashamed and stupid. ⇒ The Conservatives have suffered a humiliating defeat. ⇒ It was so humiliating, a terrible blow to my self-esteem.humiliatingly adverb [usu ADV after v] [ADV adj/-ed] ⇒ Thousands of men struggled humiliatingly for jobs. ⇒ He was caught cheating during the Seoul Olympics and humiliatingly stripped of his title.

cancerous

Powered by COBUILD
(kænsərəs )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveCancerous cells or growths are cells or growths that are the result of cancer. ⇒ ...production of cancerous cells. ⇒ Nine out of ten lumps are not cancerous.
Example sentences containing 'cancerous'
He - my cousin, my brother- was already known to them, as were various members of his cancerous cell. Appiganesi, Lisa DREAMS OF INNOCENCEThey'd been just plain hallucinations, plain cancerous hallucinations, brought about by a tumour on the brain. Stewart, Michael GRACE

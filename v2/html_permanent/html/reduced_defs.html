reduced

(rɪˈdjuːst)
Definitions
adjective1. (of a commodity) brought down in price ⇒ I only bought it because it was reduced., ⇒ Bankers involved in the talks said they still expected a sale to go through early next week, albeit at a reduced price.2. See reduced circumstances

fermentation

(ˌfɜːmɛnˈteɪʃən )
Definitions
nouna chemical reaction in which a ferment causes an organic molecule to split into simpler substances, esp the anaerobic conversion of sugar to ethyl alcohol by yeast. Also called: ferment ▶ Related adjective: zymotic

cargo

(ˈkɑːɡəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -goes or -gos1. a. goods carried by a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle; freightb. (as modifier) ⇒ a cargo vessel2. any load ⇒ the train pulled in with its cargo of new arrivals
Word origin
C17: from Spanish: from cargar to load, from Late Latin carricāre to load a vehicle, from carrus car

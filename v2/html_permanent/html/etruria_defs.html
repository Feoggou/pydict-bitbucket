Etruria

(ɪˈtrʊərɪə )
Definitions
noun1. an ancient country of central Italy, between the Rivers Arno and Tiber, roughly corresponding to present-day Tuscany and part of Umbria2. a factory established in Staffordshire by Josiah Wedgwood in 1769

miasma

(mɪˈæzmə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -mata (-mətə) or -mas1. an unwholesome or oppressive atmosphere2. pollution in the atmosphere, esp noxious vapours from decomposing organic matter

Derived Forms

miasmal (miˈasmal), miasmatic (ˌmiːəzˈmætɪk ), ˌmiasmatical (ˌmiasˈmatical) or miasmic (miˈasmic) adjective

Word Origin

C17: New Latin, from Greek: defilement, from miainein to defile

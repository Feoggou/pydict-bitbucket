boiler

(ˈbɔɪlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a closed vessel or arrangement of enclosed tubes in which water is heated to supply steam to drive an engine or turbine or provide heat2. a domestic device burning solid fuel, gas, or oil, to provide hot water, esp for central heating3. a large tub for boiling laundry4. a tough old chicken for cooking by boiling

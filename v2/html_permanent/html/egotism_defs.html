egotism

(ˈiːɡəˌtɪzəm , ˈɛɡə-)
Definitions
noun1. an inflated sense of self-importance or superiority; self-centredness2. excessive reference to oneself
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Latin ego I + -ism

Agrippina

(ˌæɡrɪˈpiːnə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  called the Elder. c. 14 bc–33 ad, Roman matron: granddaughter of Augustus, wife of Germanicus, mother of Caligula and Agrippina the Younger2.  called the Younger. 15–59 ad, mother of Nero, who put her to death after he became emperor

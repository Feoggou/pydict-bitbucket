handbrake

Powered by COBUILD
(hændbreɪk) in Automotive Engineering
Word forms: (regular plural) handbrakes
Definitions
noun(Automotive engineering: Vehicle components, Bodywork, controls, and accessories) The handbrake in a vehicle is a brake which the driver operates with his or her hand, for example, when parking.
SIMILAR WORDS: 
parking brake, emergency brake
COLLOCATIONS: 
release the ~apply the ~ ⇒ She released the handbrake, turned the ignition key, and reversed into the road. ⇒ The handbrake secures the car when it is parked or stationary for more than a short pause. ⇒ He forgot to put the handbrake on, and the car rolled down the hill.

Henry II

Definitions
noun1.  known as Henry the Saint. 973–1024, king of Germany and Holy Roman Emperor (1014–24): canonized in 11452.  1133–89, first Plantagenet king of England (1154–89): extended his Anglo-French domains and instituted judicial and financial reforms. His attempts to control the church were opposed by Becket3.  1519–59, king of France (1547–59); husband of Catherine de' Medici. He recovered Calais from the English (1558) and suppressed the Huguenots

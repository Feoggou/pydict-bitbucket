farrier

(ˈfærɪə )
Definitions
noun mainly British1. a person who shoes horses2. archaic another name for veterinary surgeon3. military a noncommissioned officer who looks after horses
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Old French ferrier, from Latin ferrārius smith, from ferrum iron

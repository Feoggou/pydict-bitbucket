dudgeon2

(ˈdʌdʒən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. obsolete a wood used in making the handles of knives, daggers, etc2. archaic a dagger, knife, etc, with a dudgeon hilt
Word origin
C15: from Anglo-Norman digeon, of obscure origin

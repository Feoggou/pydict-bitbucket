marrow bone

or marrowbone (ˈmærəʊ bəʊn)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a bone containing edible marrow ⇒ Ask the butcher for soup bones - marrow bones are best.modifier2. of or relating to bones containing edible marrow ⇒ marrowbone jelly

Word forms: marrow bones
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounMarrow bones are the bones of certain animals, especially cows, that contain a lot of bone marrow. They are used in cooking and in dog food. ⇒ Ask the butcher for soup bones (marrow bones are best). ⇒ ...marrowbone jelly.

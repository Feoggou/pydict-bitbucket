submerged

(səbˈmɜːdʒd ) or submersed (səbˈmɜːst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of plants or plant parts) growing beneath the surface of the water2. hidden; obscured3. overwhelmed or overburdened

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveIf something is submerged, it is below the surface of some water. ⇒ My right toe struck against a submerged rock. ⇒ Most of the mouth of the cave was submerged in the lake.

Christchurch

(ˈkraɪstˌtʃɜːtʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a city in New Zealand, on E South Island: manufacturing centre of a rich agricultural region; suffered major damage and loss of life in a series of earthquakes in 2010–11. Pop: 366 000 (2013 est)2. a town and resort in S England, in SE Dorset. Pop: 47 900 (2011)

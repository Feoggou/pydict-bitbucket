coloratura

(ˌkɒlərəˈtʊərə ) or colorature (ˈkɒlərəˌtjʊə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun music1. a. (in 18th- and 19th-century arias) a florid virtuoso passageb. (as modifier) ⇒ a coloratura aria2. Also called: coloratura soprano a lyric soprano who specializes in such music
Word origin
C19: from obsolete Italian, literally: colouring, from Latin colōrāre to colour

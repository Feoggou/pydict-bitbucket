thorax

(ˈθɔːræks )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural thoraxes or thoraces (ˈθɔːrəˌsiːz , θɔːˈreɪsiːz )1. the part of the human body enclosed by the ribs2. the corresponding part in other vertebrates3. the part of an insect's body between the head and abdomen, which bears the wings and legs
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: via Latin from Greek thōrax breastplate, chest

discompose

(ˌdɪskəmˈpəʊz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to disturb the composure of; disconcert2. rare to disarrange
Derived forms
ˌdiscomposedly (ˌdiscomˈposedly) adverbˌdiscomposingly (ˌdiscomˈposingly) adverbˌdiscomposure (ˌdiscomˈposure) noun

Anne

(æn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Princess, the Princess Royal. born 1950, daughter of Elizabeth II of Great Britain and Northern Ireland; a noted horsewoman and president of the Save the Children Fund2.  Queen. 1665–1714, queen of Great Britain and Ireland (1702–14), daughter of James II, and the last of the Stuart monarchs3.  Saint. (in Christian tradition) the mother of the Virgin Mary. Feast day: July 26 or 25

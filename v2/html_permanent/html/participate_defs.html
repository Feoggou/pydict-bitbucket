participate

(pɑːˈtɪsɪˌpeɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(intransitive; often foll by in) to take part, be or become actively involved, or share (in)

Derived Forms

participant (parˈticipant) adjective, nounparˌticipation (parˌticiˈpation) nounparticiˌpator (parˈticiˌpator) nounparticipatory (parˈticipatory) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Latin participāre, from pars part + capere to take

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf you participate in an activity, you take part in it. [V + in] ⇒ They expected him to participate in the ceremony. [V in n] ⇒ Over half the population of this country participate in sport. [V-ing] ⇒ ...special contracts at lower rates for participating corporations.participation (pɑːʳtɪsɪpeɪʃən ) uncount noun [+ in] ⇒ ...participation in religious activities.

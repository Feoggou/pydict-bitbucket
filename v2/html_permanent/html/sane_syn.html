Synonyms of sane

sane

adjective

  1. rational, normal, all there (informal), lucid, of sound mind, compos mentis (Latin), in your right mind, mentally sound, in possession of all your faculties
    • He seemed perfectly sane.
    OPPOSITES:  mad, crazy, nuts (slang), daft (informal), insane, mentally ill, loony (slang), bonkers (slang, mainly British), loopy (informal), crackpot (informal), out to lunch (informal), doolally (slang), off your head (slang), off your trolley (slang), non compos mentis (Latin), round the bend or twist (slang), a sausage short of a fry-up (slang), off the air (Australian, slang)
  2. sensible, sound, reasonable, balanced, moderate, sober, judicious, level-headed, grounded
    • a sane and safe energy policy
    OPPOSITES:  stupid, foolish, unreasonable, unsound, dumb-ass (slang), wacko or whacko (informal)

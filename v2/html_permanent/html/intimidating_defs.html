intimidating

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪntɪmɪdeɪtɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone or something as intimidating, you mean that they are frightening and make people lose confidence. ⇒ He was a huge, intimidating figure.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

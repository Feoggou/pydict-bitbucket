coordination

or co-ordination (kəʊˌɔːdɪˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounbalanced and effective interaction of movement, actions, etc
Word origin
C17: from Late Latin coordinātiō, from Latin ordinātiō an arranging; see ordinate

menses

(ˈmɛnsiːz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural menses1. another name for menstruation2. the period of time, usually from three to five days, during which menstruation occurs3. the matter discharged during menstruation

Word Origin

C16: from Latin, pl of mensis month

unilateral

(ˌjuːnɪˈlætərəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, having, affecting, or occurring on only one side2. involving or performed by only one party of several ⇒ unilateral disarmament3. law (of contracts, obligations, etc) made by, affecting, or binding one party only and not involving the other party in reciprocal obligations4. botany having or designating parts situated or turned to one side of an axis5. sociology relating to or tracing the line of descent through ancestors of one sex only. Compare bilateral (sense 5)6. phonetics denoting an (l) sound produced on one side of the tongue only
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unilateralism (ˌuniˈlateralism) or unilaterality (ˌuniˌlaterˈality) noununilaterally (ˌuniˈlaterally) adverb

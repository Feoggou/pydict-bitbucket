Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [v-link ADJ, ADJ after v, ADJ n]Someone who is barefoot or barefooted is not wearing anything on their feet. ⇒ I wore a white dress and was barefoot. ⇒ Alan came running barefoot through the house. ⇒ ...barefoot little children.

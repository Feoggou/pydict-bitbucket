Toulouse

(tuːˈluːz )
Definitions
nouna city in S France, on the Garonne River: scene of severe religious strife in the early 13th and mid-16th centuries; university (1229). Pop: 444 392 (2006). Ancient name: Tolosa (təˈləʊsə )

poco

(ˈpəʊkəʊ , Italian ˈpɔːko) or un poco
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective, adverbmusic (in combination) a little; to a small degree ⇒ poco rit, ⇒ un poco meno mosso

Word Origin

from Italian: little, from Latin paucus few, scanty

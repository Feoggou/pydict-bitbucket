wherein for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(hweərɪn )
Definitions
1. relative pronoun & pronounWherein means in which place or thing. [formal, literary, or old-fashioned] ⇒ ...a riding school wherein we could learn the art of horsemanship. ⇒ Adequate housing is possible in developed, mixed economies wherein the interests of the poor have prevailed.2. adverbWherein means in which part or respect. [formal] ⇒ Wherein lies the truth?Wherein is also a conjunction. ⇒ It is difficult to know wherein Mr Ritchie hoped to find salvation for his country.

goings-on

Definitions
plural noun informal1. actions or conduct, esp when regarded with disapproval2. happenings or events, esp when mysterious or suspicious ⇒ there were strange goings-on up at the Hall

Definitions
plural nounIf you describe events or activities as goings-on, you mean that they are strange, interesting, amusing, or dishonest. ⇒ The girl had found out about the goings-on in the factory. ⇒ ...strange goings-on between Cantrell and his men.

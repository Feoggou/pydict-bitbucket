gnome1

(nəʊm )
Definitions
noun1. one of a species of legendary creatures, usually resembling small misshapen old men, said to live in the depths of the earth and guard buried treasure2. the statue of a gnome, esp in a garden3. a very small or ugly person4. facetious or derogatory an international banker or financier (esp in the phrase gnomes of Zürich)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gnomish (ˈgnomish) or gnome-like (ˈgnome-like) adjective

Word Origin

C18: from French, from New Latin gnomus, coined by Paracelsus, of obscure origin

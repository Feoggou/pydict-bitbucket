timely

(ˈtaɪmlɪ )
Definitions
-lier or -liestadjective, adverb1. at the right or an opportune or appropriate time2. an archaic word for early
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveA timely event happens at a moment when it is useful, effective, or relevant. [approval] ⇒ The recent outbreak is a timely reminder that this disease is a serious health hazard. ⇒ The exhibition is timely, since 'self-taught' art is catching on in a big way.

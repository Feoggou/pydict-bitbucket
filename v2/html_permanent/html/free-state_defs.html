Free State

Definitions
noun1. a province of central South Africa; replaced the former province of Orange Free State in 1994: gold and uranium mining. Capital: Bloemfontein. Pop: 2 745 590 (2011 est). Area: 129 480 sq km (49 992 sq miles)2. US history (before the Civil War) any state prohibiting slavery3. short for Irish Free State

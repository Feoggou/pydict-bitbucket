Southern Cross

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small conspicuous constellation in the S hemisphere lying in the Milky Way near Centaurus. The four brightest stars form a cross, the longer arm of which points to the south celestial pole. Formal names: Crux, Crux Australis2. Australian the flag flown at the Eureka Stockade

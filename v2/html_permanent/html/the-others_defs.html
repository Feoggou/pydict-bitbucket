the others

Definitions
used to refer to the rest of the people or things in a group, when you are talking about particular people or things ⇒ I shall wait until the others come back., ⇒ Dan is on his way here, with the others., ⇒ This is the only book of his worth reading. The others are rubbish.▷ See others
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

angular

(ˈæŋɡjʊlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. lean or bony2. awkward or stiff in manner or movement3. having an angle or angles4. placed at an angle5. measured by an angle or by the rate at which an angle changes
Derived forms
ˈangularly adverbˈangularness noun
Word origin
C15: from Latin angulāris, from angulus angle1

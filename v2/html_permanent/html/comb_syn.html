comb

verb

  1. untangle, arrange, groom, dress • Her reddish hair was cut short and neatly combed.
  2. search, hunt through, sweep, rake, sift, scour, rummage, ransack, forage, fossick (Australian & & New Zealand), go through with a fine-tooth comb • Officers combed the woods for the murder weapon.
  3. [flax, wool, etc] card, tease, heckle, hackle, teazle, teasel, hatchel

unhesitatingly for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnhezɪteɪtɪŋli )
Definitions
adverb [usu ADV with v]If you say that someone does something unhesitatingly, you mean that they do it immediately and confidently, without any doubt or anxiety. ⇒ I would unhesitatingly choose the latter option. ⇒ So is there any taboo she wouldn't touch? Unhesitatingly she replies, 'Politics.'

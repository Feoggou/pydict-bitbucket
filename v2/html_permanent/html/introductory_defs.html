Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]An introductory remark, talk, or part of a book gives a small amount of general information about a particular subject, often before a more detailed explanation. ⇒ ...an introductory course in religion and theology.2. adjective [ADJ n]An introductory offer or price on a new product is something such as a free gift or a low price that is meant to attract new customers. [business] ⇒ ...just out on the shelves at an introductory price of £2.99.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

high-tech

, hi-tech, hi tec (ˌhaɪˈtɛk) or high-technology (ˌhaɪtɛkˈnɒlədʒɪ)
Definitions
adjective(of software, electronics, etc) extremely advanced and highly sophisticated ⇒ high-tech industries, ⇒ high-tech computers, ⇒ a high-tech company, ⇒ a high-tech sector, ⇒ high-tech medicine, ⇒ It can be very distressing to see your baby surrounded by high-tech equipment., ⇒ And now we've got Third World countries clamouring for high-tech weapons., ⇒ the latest high-tech medical gadgetry

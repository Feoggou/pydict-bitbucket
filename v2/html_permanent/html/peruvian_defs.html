Peruvian

(pəˈruːvɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Peru or its inhabitantsnoun2. a native or inhabitant of Peru

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectivePeruvian means belonging or related to Peru, or to its people or culture. ⇒ ...the high, fertile valleys of the Peruvian Andes.A Peruvian is someone who is Peruvian. ⇒ The disease has killed thousands of Peruvians this year.

arena

(əˈriːnə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. an enclosure or platform, usually surrounded by seats on all sides, in which sports events, contests, entertainments, etc, take place ⇒ a boxing arenab. (as modifier) ⇒ arena stage2. the central area of an ancient Roman amphitheatre, in which gladiatorial contests and other spectacles were held3. a sphere or scene of conflict or intense activity ⇒ the political arena
Word origin
C17: from Latin harēna sand, place where sand was strewn for the combats

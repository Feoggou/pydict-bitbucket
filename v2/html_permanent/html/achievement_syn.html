achievement

noun

  1. accomplishment, effort, feat, deed, stroke, triumph, coup, exploit, act, attainment, feather in your cap • a conference celebrating women's achievements
  2. fulfilment, effecting, performance, production, execution, implementation, completion, accomplishment, realization, attainment, acquirement, carrying out or through • It is the achievement of these goals that will bring lasting peace.

QUOTATIONS:
 When I look at the works of the masters, I see what they have done. When I consider my own trifles, I see what I ought to have done Johann Wolfgang von Goethe If there were an instrument by which to measure desire, one could foretell achievementWilla Cather – The Professor's House

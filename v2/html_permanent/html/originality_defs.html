originality

(əˌrɪdʒɪˈnælɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. the quality or condition of being original2. the ability to create or innovate3. something original
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'originality'

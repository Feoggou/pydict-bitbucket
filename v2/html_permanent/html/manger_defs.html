manger

(ˈmeɪndʒə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a trough or box in a stable, barn, etc, from which horses or cattle feed2. nautical a basin-like construction in the bows of a vessel for catching water draining from an anchor rode or coming in through the hawseholes

Word Origin

C14: from Old French maingeure food trough, from mangier to eat, ultimately from Latin mandūcāre to chew

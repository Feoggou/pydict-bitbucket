kir

(kɜː , kir )
Definitions
nouna drink made from dry white wine and cassis
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

named after Canon F. Kir (1876–1968), mayor of Dijon, who is reputed to have invented it

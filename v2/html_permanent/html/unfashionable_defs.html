unfashionable

(ʌnˈfæʃənəbəl )
Definitions
adjectivenot fashionable ⇒ dull unfashionable clothes, ⇒ an unfashionable view

unfashionable for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnfæʃənəbəl )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is unfashionable, it is not approved of or done by most people. ⇒ Wearing fur has become deeply unfashionable. ⇒ The couple hold the unfashionable view that marriage is a sacred union.unfashionably graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj] ⇒ He wears his blonde hair unfashionably long.

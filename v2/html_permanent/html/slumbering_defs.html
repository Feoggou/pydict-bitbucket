slumbering

(ˈslʌmbərɪŋ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. sleeping peacefully ⇒ a slumbering genie2. quiescent or dormant ⇒ a slumbering volcano
Example sentences containing 'slumbering'
It was a peculiar feeling, standing with all that dark latent power just slumbering around her.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj]You use bitterly when you are describing an attitude which involves strong, unpleasant emotions such as anger or dislike. ⇒ We are bitterly upset at what has happened. ⇒ ...Lucy Gannon's fine, bitterly funny play.

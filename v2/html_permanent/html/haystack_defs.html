haystack

Powered by COBUILD
(heɪstæk ) for learners of English
Word forms: haystacks
Definitions
1. countable nounA haystack is a large, solid pile of hay, often covered with a straw roof to protect it, which is left in the field until it is needed.2. →  like looking for a needle in a haystack

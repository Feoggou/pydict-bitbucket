graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that something is disconcerting, you mean that it makes you feel anxious, confused, or embarrassed. unsettling ⇒ The reception desk is not at street level, which is a little disconcerting.disconcertingly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ She looks disconcertingly like a familiar aunt or grandmother. ⇒ At times she could be almost disconcertingly absent-minded.

chevalier

(ˌʃɛˈvælɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a member of certain orders of merit, such as the French Legion of Honour2. French historya. a mounted soldier or knight, esp a military cadetb. the lowest title of rank in the old French nobility3. an archaic word for knight4. a chivalrous man; gallant
Word origin
C14: from Old French, from Medieval Latin caballārius horseman, cavalier

Chevalier

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (ˌʃɛvəˈlɪə) Albert. 1861–1923, British music hall entertainer, remembered for his cockney songs2. (ˌʃɛˈvælɪə, French ʃəvalje) Maurice (mɔris). 1888–1972, French singer and film actor

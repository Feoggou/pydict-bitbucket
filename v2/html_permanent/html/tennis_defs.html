tennis

(ˈtɛnɪs )
Definitions
nouna. a racket game played between two players or pairs of players who hit a ball to and fro over a net on a rectangular court of grass, asphalt, clay, etc. See also lawn tennis, real tennis, court tennis, table tennisb. (as modifier) ⇒ tennis court, ⇒ tennis racket
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: probably from Anglo-French tenetz hold (imperative), from Old French tenir to hold, from Latin tenēre

jackass

(ˈdʒækˌæs )
Definitions
noun1. a male donkey2. a stupid person; fool3. See laughing jackass
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from jack1 (male) + ass1

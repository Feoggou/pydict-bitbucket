calico

(ˈkælɪˌkəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -coes or -cos1. a white or unbleached cotton fabric with no printed design2. mainly US a coarse printed cotton fabric3. (modifier) made of calico
Word origin
C16: based on Calicut, town in India

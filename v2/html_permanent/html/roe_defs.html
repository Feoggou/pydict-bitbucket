roe1

(rəʊ )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: hard roe the ovary of a female fish filled with mature eggs2. Also called: soft roe the testis of a male fish filled with mature sperm3. the ripe ovary of certain crustaceans, such as the lobster
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Middle Dutch roge, from Old High German roga; related to Old Norse hrogn

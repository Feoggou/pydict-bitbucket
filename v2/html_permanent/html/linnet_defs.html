linnet

(ˈlɪnɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a brownish Old World finch, Acanthis cannabina: the male has a red breast and forehead2. Also called: house finch a similar and related North American bird, Carpodacus mexicanus
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Old French linotte, ultimately from Latin līnum flax (because the bird feeds on flaxseeds)

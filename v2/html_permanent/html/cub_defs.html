cub

(kʌb )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the young of certain animals, such as the lion, bear, etc2. a young or inexperienced personverbWord forms: cubs, cubbing or cubbed3. to give birth to (cubs)
Derived forms
cubbish (ˈcubbish) adjectivecubbishly (ˈcubbishly) adverb
Word origin
C16: perhaps from Old Norse kubbi young seal; see cob1

sculptured

(ˈskʌlptʃəd)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. carved, cast, or fashioned three-dimensionally ⇒ a beautifully sculptured bronze horse2. resembling a sculpture ⇒ wonderful clothes which clung to their sculptured thighs and buttocks, ⇒ a thin, handsome man with two-toned sculptured hair

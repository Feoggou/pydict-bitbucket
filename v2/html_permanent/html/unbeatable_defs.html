unbeatable for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnbiːtəbəl )
Definitions
1. adjectiveIf you describe something as unbeatable, you mean that it is the best thing of its kind. [emphasis] ⇒ These resorts, like Magaluf and Arenal, remain unbeatable in terms of price. ⇒ ...unbeatable Italian cars.2. adjectiveIn a game or competition, if you describe a person or team as unbeatable, you mean that they win so often, or perform so well that they are unlikely to be beaten by anyone. ⇒ The opposition was unbeatable. ⇒ With two more days of competition to go China is in an unbeatable position.

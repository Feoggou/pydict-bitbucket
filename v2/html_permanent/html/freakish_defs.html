freakish

(ˈfriːkɪʃ )
Definitions
adjective1. of, related to, or characteristic of a freak; abnormal or unusual2. unpredictable or changeable ⇒ freakish weather
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Definitions

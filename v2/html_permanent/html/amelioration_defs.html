amelioration

(əˌmiːljəˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or an instance of ameliorating or the state of being ameliorated2. something that ameliorates; an improvement3. Also called: elevation linguistics (of the meaning of a word) a change from pejorative to neutral or positively pleasant. The word nice has achieved its modern meaning by amelioration from the earlier sense foolish, silly

Similar Words

improvement, advance, recovery, amendment, correction, enhancement, change for the better, upswing, betterment, melioration

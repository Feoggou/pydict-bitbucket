diabetes

(ˌdaɪəˈbiːtɪs , -tiːz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany of various disorders, esp diabetes mellitus, characterized by excretion of an abnormally large amount of urine
Word origin
C16: from Latin: siphon, from Greek, literally: a passing through (referring to the excessive urination), from diabainein to pass through, cross over; see diabase

manus

(ˈmeɪnəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -nus1. anatomy the wrist and hand2. the corresponding part in other vertebrates3. Roman law the authority of a husband over his wife4. English law (formerly) an oath or the person taking an oath

Word Origin

C19: Latin: hand

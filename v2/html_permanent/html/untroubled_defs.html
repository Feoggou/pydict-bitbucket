untroubled for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌntrʌbəld )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are untroubled by something, you are not affected or worried by it. [+ by] ⇒ She is untroubled by the fact that she didn't win. ⇒ ...an untroubled night's sleep.

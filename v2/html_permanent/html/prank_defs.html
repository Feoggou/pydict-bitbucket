Definitions

prank2

(præŋk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (transitive) to dress or decorate showily or gaudily2. (intransitive) to make an ostentatious display

Word Origin

C16: from Middle Dutch pronken; related to German Prunk splendour, prangen to be in full splendour

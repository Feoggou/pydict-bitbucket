assorted

(əˈsɔːtɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. consisting of various kinds mixed together; miscellaneous ⇒ assorted sweets2. arranged in sorts; classified ⇒ assorted categories3. matched; suited (esp in the combinations well-assorted, ill-assorted)

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]A group of assorted things is a group of similar things that are of different sizes or colours or have different qualities. ⇒ It should be a great week, with overnight stops in assorted hotels in the West Highlands. ⇒ ...swimsuits, sizes 12-18, in assorted colours.

refreshing

(rɪˈfrɛʃɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. able to or tending to refresh; invigorating2. pleasantly different or novel
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveYou say that something is refreshing when it is pleasantly different from what you are used to. ⇒ It's refreshing to hear somebody speaking common sense. ⇒ It made a refreshing change to see a good old-fashioned movie.refreshingly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ He was refreshingly honest.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA refreshing bath or drink makes you feel energetic or cool again after you have been tired or hot. ⇒ Herbs have been used for centuries to make refreshing drinks.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

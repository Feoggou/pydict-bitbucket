furnace

(ˈfɜːnɪs )
Definitions
noun1. an enclosed chamber in which heat is produced to generate steam, destroy refuse, smelt or refine ores, etc2. a very hot or stifling place
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

furnace-like (ˈfurnace-ˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C13: from Old French fornais, from Latin fornax oven, furnace; related to Latin formus warm

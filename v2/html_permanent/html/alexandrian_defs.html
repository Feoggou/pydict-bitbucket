Alexandrian

(ˌælɪɡˈzændrɪən , -ˈzɑːn-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Alexander the Great2. of or relating to Alexandria in Egypt3. relating to the Hellenistic philosophical, literary, and scientific ideas that flourished in Alexandria in the last three centuries bc4. (of writers, literary works, etc) erudite and imitative rather than original or creativenoun5. a native or inhabitant of Alexandria

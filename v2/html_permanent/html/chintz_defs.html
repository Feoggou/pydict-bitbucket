chintz

(tʃɪnts )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a printed patterned cotton fabric, with glazed finish2. a painted or stained Indian calico
Word origin
C17: from Hindi chīnt, from Sanskrit citra gaily-coloured

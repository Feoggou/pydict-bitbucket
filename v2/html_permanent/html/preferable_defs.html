Definitions

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you say that one thing is preferable to another, you mean that it is more desirable or suitable. [+ to] ⇒ A big earthquake a long way off is preferable to a smaller one nearby. [+ to] ⇒ The hazards of the theatre seemed preferable to joining the family paint business. ⇒ Was an evening with Peter in their company preferable to being left at home alone?preferably (prefrəbli ) graded adverb & adverb ⇒ Do something creative or take exercise, preferably in the fresh air.

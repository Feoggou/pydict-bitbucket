gazelle

(ɡəˈzɛl )
Definitions
noun plural -zelles or -zelleany small graceful usually fawn-coloured antelope of the genera Gazella and Procapra, of Africa and Asia, such as G. thomsoni (Thomson's gazelle)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gazelle-like (gaˈzelle-ˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Old French, from Arabic ghazāl

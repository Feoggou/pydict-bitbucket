pigsty

(ˈpɪɡˌstaɪ ) or US and Canadian pigpen
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -sties1. a pen for pigs; sty2. British a dirty or untidy place

(pɪgstaɪ ) also pig sty
Word forms: pigsties
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA pigsty is an enclosed place where pigs are kept on a farm. [mainly British]regional note:   in AM, usually use pigpen2. countable noun [usu sing]If you describe a room or a house as a pigsty, you are criticizing the fact that it is very dirty and untidy. [mainly British, informal, disapproval] ⇒ The office is a pigsty.

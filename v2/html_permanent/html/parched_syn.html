Synonyms of parched

parched

adjective

  1. dried out or up, dry, withered, scorched, arid, torrid, shrivelled, dehydrated, waterless
    • Showers poured down upon the parched earth.
  2. thirsty, dry, dehydrated, drouthy (Scottish)
    • After all that exercise, I was parched.

parch

verb

  1. dry up, burn, sear, blister, wither, evaporate, scorch, shrivel, dehydrate, desiccate, make thirsty

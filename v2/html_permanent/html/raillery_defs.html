raillery

(ˈreɪlərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -leries1. light-hearted satire or ridicule; banter2. an example of this, esp a bantering remark
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from French, from railler to tease, banter; see rail2
Example sentences containing 'raillery'
"His tone had lost its raillery and the words were spat out. Meek, M R D IN REMEMBRANCE OF ROSEWas she reading too much into what appeared to be humorous raillery ? Ellis, Julie LASTING TREASURES

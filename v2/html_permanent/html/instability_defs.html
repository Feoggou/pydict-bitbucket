instability

(ˌɪnstəˈbɪlɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. lack of stability or steadiness2. tendency to variable or unpredictable behaviour3. physics a fast growing disturbance or wave in a plasma
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

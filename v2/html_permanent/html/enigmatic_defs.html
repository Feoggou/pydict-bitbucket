Powered by COBUILD
(enɪgmætɪk ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone or something that is enigmatic is mysterious and difficult to understand. ⇒ Haley studied her, an enigmatic smile on his face. ⇒ She starred in one of Welles's most enigmatic films.enigmatically graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v, ADV -ed/adj] ⇒ 'Corbiere didn't deserve this,' she said enigmatically.

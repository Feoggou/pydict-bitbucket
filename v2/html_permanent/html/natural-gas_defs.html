natural gas in Mechanical Engineering

Powered by COBUILD
(nætʃərəl gæs)
Definitions
noun(Mechanical engineering: Energy, thermodynamics and heat transfer) Natural gas is a fuel consisting mostly of methane gas, extracted from under the ocean or ground. ⇒ Natural gas contains methane, ethane, propane, and butane and can often be used directly without any processing. ⇒ Methane is the principal component of natural gas. ⇒ Natural gas is a fuel consisting mostly of methane gas, extracted from under the ocean or ground.

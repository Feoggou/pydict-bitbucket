stooge

(stuːdʒ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an actor who feeds lines to a comedian or acts as his foil or butt2. slang someone who is taken advantage of by anotherverb (intransitive)3. slang to act as a stooge4. (foll by about or around) slang (esp in the RAF) to fly or move about aimlessly

Word Origin

C20: of unknown origin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf you refer to someone as a stooge, you are criticizing them because they are used by someone else to do unpleasant or dishonest tasks. [disapproval] ⇒ He has vehemently rejected claims that he is a government stooge. ⇒ The latter had for decades acted largely as a stooge for the party leaders.

deeds

(diːdz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounlaw formal legal documents signed, witnessed, and delivered to effect a conveyance or transfer of property or to create a legal obligation or contract ⇒ To complete the sale, the deeds are signed in front of the notary., ⇒ We sent the deeds over to his bank in London., ⇒ the deeds to his father's property

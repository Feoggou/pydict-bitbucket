monition

(məʊˈnɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a warning or caution; admonition2. Christianity a formal notice from a bishop or ecclesiastical court requiring a person to refrain from committing a specific offence

Word Origin

C14: via Old French from Latin monitiō, from monēre to warn

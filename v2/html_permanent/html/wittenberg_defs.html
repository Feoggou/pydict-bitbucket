Wittenberg

(German ˈvɪtənbɛrk, English ˈwɪtənˌbɜːɡ)
Definitions
nouna city in E Germany, on the River Elbe, in Brandenburg: Martin Luther, as a philosophy teacher at Wittenberg university, began the Reformation here in 1517 by nailing his 95 theses to the doors of a church. Pop: 46 295 (2003 est)

experienced

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪkspɪəriənst ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as experienced, you mean that they have been doing a particular job or activity for a long time, and therefore know a lot about it or are very skilful at it. [+ in] ⇒ ...lawyers who are experienced in these matters. ⇒ It's a team packed with experienced and mature professionals. ⇒ Perhaps I'm a bit more experienced about life than my contemporaries.

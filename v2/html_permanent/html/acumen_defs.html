acumen

(ˈækjʊˌmɛn , əˈkjuːmən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe ability to judge well; keen discernment; insight
Derived forms
aˈcuminous adjective
Word origin
C16: from Latin: sharpness, from acuere to sharpen, from acus needle

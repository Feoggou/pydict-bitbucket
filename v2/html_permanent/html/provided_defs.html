Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
subordinating conjunction & conjunctionIf you say that something will happen provided or provided that something else happens, you mean that the first thing will happen only if the second thing also happens. ⇒ The other banks are going to be very eager to help, provided that they see that he has a specific plan. ⇒ Provided they are fit I see no reason why they shouldn't go on playing for another four or five years.

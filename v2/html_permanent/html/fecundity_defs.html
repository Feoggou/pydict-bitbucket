fecundity

(fɪˈkʌndɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun1. fertility; fruitfulness2. intellectual fruitfulness; creativity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'fecundity'
It was as if he was aroused by the evidence of his own fecundity ; muttering words of adoration against her body. Clive Barker GALILEENot their beauty, not their particularity, just their smothering, deafening fecundity. Clive Barker SACRAMENTSeventy miles south of Neverness there is an island famous for the profusion and fecundity of its bird life. Zindell, David THE BROKEN GOD

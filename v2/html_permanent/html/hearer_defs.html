(hɪərəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: hearers
Definitions
countable nounYour hearers are the people who are listening to you speak. [formal] ⇒ He knew that his hearers wanted to hear this story. ⇒ Communication of whatever sort involves not just a speaker but a hearer too.

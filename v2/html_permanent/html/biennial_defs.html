biennial

(baɪˈɛnɪəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. occurring every two years2. lasting two years. Compare biannualnoun3. a plant, such as the carrot, that completes its life cycle within two years, developing vegetative storage parts during the first year and flowering and fruiting in its second year. Compare annual (sense 3), perennial (sense 3)4. an event that takes place every two years
Derived forms
biennially (biˈennially) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

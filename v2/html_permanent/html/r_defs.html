R

Powered by COBUILD
also r (ɑːʳ ) (R's, r's ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. variable nounR is the eighteenth letter of the English alphabet.2. → See also three Rs3. title noun [n N]R is a written abbreviation meaning king or queen. R is short for the Latin words 'rex' and 'regina'. ⇒ ...Elizabeth R.4. R is used as an abbreviation for words beginning with r. For example, it is used on maps as a written abbreviation for 'river'.5. In the United States, some cinema films are marked R to show that children under 17 years old are only allowed to see them if an adult is with them.

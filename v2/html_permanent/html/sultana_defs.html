sultana

(sʌlˈtɑːnə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. the dried fruit of a small white seedless grape, originally produced in SW Asia: used in cakes, curries, etc; seedless raisinb. the grape itself2. Also called: sultaness a wife, concubine, or female relative of a sultan3. a mistress; concubine
Word origin
C16: from Italian, feminine of sultano sultan

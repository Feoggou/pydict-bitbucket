authorize

verb

  1. empower, commission, enable, entitle, mandate, accredit, give authority to • They authorized him to use force if necessary.
  2. permit, allow, suffer, grant, confirm, agree to, approve, sanction, endure, license, endorse, warrant, tolerate, ratify, consent to, countenance, accredit, vouch for, give leave, give the green light for, give a blank cheque to, give authority for • We are willing to authorize a police raid. OPPOSITES:  ban, exclude, rule out, veto, forbid, prohibit, outlaw, preclude, disallow, proscribe, debar, criminalize

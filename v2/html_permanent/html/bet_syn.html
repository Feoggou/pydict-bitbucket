bet

verb

  1. gamble, chance, stake, venture, hazard, speculate, punt (mainly British), wager, put money, risk money, pledge money, put your shirt on • I bet on a horse called Premonition.
  2. imagine, expect, suspect, assume, guess (informal, mainly US & & Canadian), fancy, deem, infer, deduce, conjecture • I bet you were good at games when you were at school.

noun

  1. gamble, risk, stake, venture, pledge, speculation, hazard, flutter (informal), ante, punt, wager, long shot • He made a 30 mile trip to the casino to place a bet.
  2. option, plan, choice, alternative, course of action • Your best bet is to choose a guest house.
  3. guess, feeling, view, opinion, prediction • My bet is that next year will be different.

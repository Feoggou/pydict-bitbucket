try out

Definitions
verb (adverb)1. (transitive) to test or put to experimental use ⇒ I'm going to try the new car out2. (when intr, usually foll by for) US and Canadian (of an athlete, actor, etc) to undergo a test or to submit (an athlete, actor, etc) to a test to determine suitability for a place in a team, an acting role, etcnoun tryout3. mainly US and Canadian a trial or test, as of an athlete or actor

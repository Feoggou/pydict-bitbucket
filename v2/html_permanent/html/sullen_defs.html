sullen

(ˈsʌlən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. unwilling to talk or be sociable; sulky; morose2. sombre; gloomy ⇒ a sullen day3. literary sluggish; slow ⇒ a sullen stream4. obsolete threateningnoun5. (plural) archaic a sullen mood

Derived Forms

sullenly (ˈsullenly) adverbsullenness (ˈsullenness) noun

Word Origin

C16: perhaps from Anglo-French solain (unattested), ultimately related to Latin sōlus alone

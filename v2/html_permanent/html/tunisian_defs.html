Tunisian

(tjuːˈnɪzɪən , -ˈnɪsɪən)
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to Tunisia or its inhabitantsnoun2. a native or inhabitant of Tunisia

Tunisian

Powered by COBUILD
(tjuːnɪziən , US tuːn- ) for learners of English
Word forms: Tunisians
Definitions
1. adjectiveTunisian means belonging to or relating to Tunisia, or to its people or culture. ⇒ ...the Tunisian coast. ⇒ ...the Tunisian Foreign Minister.2. countable nounA Tunisian is a person who comes from Tunisia.

philosopher

(fɪˈlɒsəfə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a student, teacher, or devotee of philosophy2. a person of philosophical temperament, esp one who is patient, wise, and stoical3. (formerly) an alchemist or devotee of occult science4. a person who establishes the ideology of a cult or movement ⇒ the philosopher of the revolution

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA philosopher is a person who studies or writes about philosophy. ⇒ ...the Greek philosopher Plato.2. countable nounIf you refer to someone as a philosopher, you mean that they think deeply and seriously about life and other basic matters.

slave

noun

  1. servant, serf, vassal, bondsman, slavey (British, informal), varlet (archaic), villein, bondservant • still living as slaves in the desert
  2. drudge, skivvy (mainly British), scullion (archaic), bitch (slang) • Mum says to Dad, `I'm not your slave, you know!'

verb

  1. toil, labour, grind (informal), drudge, sweat, graft, slog, sweat blood, skivvy (British), work your fingers to the bone, work your guts out, keep your nose to the grindstone • slaving over a hot stove

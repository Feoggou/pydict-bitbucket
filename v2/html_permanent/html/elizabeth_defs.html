Elizabeth1

(ɪˈlɪzəbəθ )
Definitions
noun1. a city in NE New Jersey, on Newark Bay. Pop: 123 215 (2003 est)2. a town in SE South Australia, part of Adelaide. Pop: 26 428 (2006)

Elizabeth2

(ɪˈlɪzəbəθ )
Definitions
noun1. See Saint Elizabeth2.  pen name Carmen Sylva. 1843–1916, queen of Romania (1881–1914) and author3.  Russian name Yelizaveta Petrovna. 1709–62, empress of Russia (1741–62); daughter of Peter the Great4.  title the Queen Mother; original name Lady Elizabeth Bowes-Lyon. 1900–2002, queen of Great Britain and Northern Ireland (1936–52) as the wife of George VI; mother of Elizabeth II

(kɪnzfoʊk )
Definitions
language note:   The spellings kinfolk, and sometimes in American English kinfolks are also used.plural noun [oft poss N]Your kinsfolk or kinfolk are the people who are related to you. [literary] ⇒ Poor Emily. Her kinsfolk should come to her. ⇒ I sent my other son to the country to stay with kinfolk.

teat

(tiːt )
Definitions
noun1. a. the nipple of a mammary glandb. (in cows, etc) any of the projections from the udder through which milk is discharged. See nipple2. something resembling a teat in shape or function, such as the rubber mouthpiece of a feeding bottle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old French tete, of Germanic origin; compare Old English titt, Middle High German zitze

exhaustion

(ɪɡˈzɔːstʃən )
Definitions
noun1. extreme tiredness; fatigue2. the condition of being used up; consumption ⇒ exhaustion of the earth's resources3. the act of exhausting or the state of being exhausted
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

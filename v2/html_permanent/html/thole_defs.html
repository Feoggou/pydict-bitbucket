thole1

(θəʊl ) or tholepin (ˈθəʊlˌpɪn )
Definitions
nouna wooden pin or one of a pair, set upright in the gunwales of a rowing boat to serve as a fulcrum in rowing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English tholl, related to Middle Low German dolle, Norwegian toll, Icelandic thollr

thole2

(θəʊl )
Definitions
verb1. (transitive) Scottish and Northern England dialect to put up with; bear2. an archaic word for suffer
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English tholian; related to Old Saxon, Old High German tholōn, Old Norse thola to endure: compare Latin tollere to bear up

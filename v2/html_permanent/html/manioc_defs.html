manioc

(ˈmænɪˌɒk ) or manioca (ˌmænɪˈəʊkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounanother name for cassava (sense 1)

Word Origin

C16: from Tupi mandioca; earlier form manihot from French, from Guarani mandio

chilly

(ˈtʃɪlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -lier or -liest1. causing or feeling cool or moderately cold2. without warmth; unfriendly3. (of people) sensitive to cold
Derived forms
chilliness (ˈchilliness) noun

chilly

Powered by COBUILD
(tʃɪli )
Word forms: chillier , chilliest
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is chilly is unpleasantly cold. ⇒ It was a chilly afternoon. ⇒ The rooms had grown chilly. → cold2. graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If you feel chilly, you feel rather cold. ⇒ I'm a bit chilly.3. graded adjective & adjectiveYou say that relations between people are chilly or that a person's response is chilly when they are not friendly, welcoming, or enthusiastic. ⇒ I was slightly afraid of their chilly distant politeness.

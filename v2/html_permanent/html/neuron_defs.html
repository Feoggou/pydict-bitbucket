(njʊərɒn , US nʊr- ) also neurone
Word forms: neurons
Definitions
1. countable nounA neuron is a cell which is part of the nervous system. Neurons send messages to and from the brain. [technical] ⇒ Information is transferred along each neuron by means of an electrical impulse.2. → See also motor neurone disease

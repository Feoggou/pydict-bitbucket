well-fortified

Definitions
adjective (well fortified when postpositive)1. (of a position, garrison, city, etc) having been made defensible2. (of a person) having strengthened oneself or been strengthened physically, mentally, or morally ⇒ the police were well fortified with steaming mugs of tea

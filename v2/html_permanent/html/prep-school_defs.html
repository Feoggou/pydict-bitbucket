Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable noun [oft prep N]In Britain, a prep school is a private school where children are educated until the age of 11 or 13.2. variable nounIn the United States, a prep school is a private school for students who intend to go to college after they leave. ⇒ ...an exclusive prep school in Washington.

helpmate

Powered by COBUILD
(helpmeɪt ) for learners of English
Word forms: helpmates
Definitions
countable nounIf you say that one person is another person's helpmate, you mean that they help the other person in their life or work, especially by doing boring but necessary jobs for them such as cooking and cleaning. [old-fashioned]
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

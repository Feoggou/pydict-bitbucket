unavailable for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnəveɪləbəl )
Definitions
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]When things or people are unavailable, you cannot obtain them, meet them, or talk to them. ⇒ Mr Hicks is out of the country and so unavailable for comment. ⇒ Basic food products are frequently unavailable in the state shops.

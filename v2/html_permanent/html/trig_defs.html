trig1

(trɪɡ ) archaic or dialect
Definitions
adjective1. neat or spruceverbWord forms: trigs, trigging or trigged2. to make or become trim or spruce
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

trigly (ˈtrigly) adverbtrigness (ˈtrigness) noun

Word Origin

C12 (originally: trusty): of Scandinavian origin; related to Old Norse tryggr true

trig2 - mainly dialect

(trɪɡ )
Definitions

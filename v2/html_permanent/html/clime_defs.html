clime

(klaɪm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounpoetic a region or its climate
Word origin
C16: from Late Latin clima; see climate

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu pl, usu adj N]You use clime in expressions such as warmer climes and foreign climes to refer to a place that has a particular kind of climate. [literary] [+ of] ⇒ He left Britain for the sunnier climes of Southern France. ⇒ We always take our holidays in foreign climes.

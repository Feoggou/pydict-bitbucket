frontage

(ˈfrʌntɪdʒ )
Definitions
noun1. the façade of a building or the front of a plot of ground2. the extent of the front of a shop, plot of land, etc, esp along a street, river, etc3. the direction in which a building faces ⇒ a frontage on the river

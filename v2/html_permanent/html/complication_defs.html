complication

(ˌkɒmplɪˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a condition, event, etc, that is complex or confused2. the act or process of complicating3. a situation, event, or condition that complicates or frustrates ⇒ her coming was a serious complication4. a disease or disorder arising as a consequence of another disease

Word forms: complications
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA complication is a problem or difficulty that makes a situation harder to deal with. [+ to] ⇒ The age difference was a complication to the relationship. ⇒ An added complication is the growing concern for the environment.2. countable nounA complication is a medical problem that occurs as a result of another illness or disease. [+ of] ⇒ Blindness is a common complication of diabetes. [+ from] ⇒ He died of complications from a heart attack.

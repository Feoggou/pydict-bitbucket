tot1

(tɒt )
Definitions
noun1. a young child; toddler2. mainly British a small amount of anything3. a small measure of spirits
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: perhaps short for totterer; see totter

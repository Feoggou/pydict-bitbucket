lorry

(ˈlɒrɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ries1. a large motor vehicle designed to carry heavy loads, esp one with a flat platform. US and Canadian name: truck See also articulated vehicle2. See off the back of a lorry3. any of various vehicles with a flat load-carrying surface, esp one designed to run on rails
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: perhaps related to northern English dialect lurry to pull, tug

impedimenta

(ɪmˌpɛdɪˈmɛntə )
Definitions
plural noun1. the baggage and equipment carried by an army2. any objects or circumstances that impede progress3. a plural of impediment (sense 3)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin, plural of impedīmentum hindrance; see impede
Example sentences containing 'impedimenta'
And time too bursts back into my consciousness with all its impedimenta of mind and matter, rule and law. Anthony Masters CASCADES - THE DAY OF THE DEAD

Similar Words

baggage, things, effects, stuff, equipment, traps, gear, possessions, junk (informal), luggage, belongings, trappings, paraphernalia, odds and ends, accoutrements, movables

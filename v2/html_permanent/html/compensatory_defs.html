(kɒmpənseɪtəri , US kəmpensətɔːri )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Compensatory payments involve money paid as compensation. [formal] ⇒ The jury awarded $11.2 million in compensatory damages.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Compensatory measures are designed to help people who have special problems or disabilities. [formal] ⇒ Money should be spent on compensatory programmes for deprived pre-school and infant-school children.

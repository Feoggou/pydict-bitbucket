skein

(skeɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a length of yarn, etc, wound in a long coil2. something resembling this, such as a lock of hair3. a flock of geese flying. Compare gaggle (sense 2)

Word Origin

C15: from Old French escaigne, of unknown origin

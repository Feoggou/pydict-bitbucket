Definitions
phraseIf something happens in reverse or goes into reverse, things happen in the opposite way to what usually happens or to what has been happening. ⇒ Amis tells the story in reverse, from the moment the man dies. ⇒ The downward trend went into reverse and the scores started to creep up again. →  reverse

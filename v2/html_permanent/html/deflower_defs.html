deflower

(diːˈflaʊə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to deprive of virginity, esp by rupturing the hymen through sexual intercourse2. to despoil of beauty, innocence, etc; mar; violate3. to rob or despoil of flowers
Derived forms
deflowerer (deˈflowerer) noun

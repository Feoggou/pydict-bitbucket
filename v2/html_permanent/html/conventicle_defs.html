conventicle

(kənˈvɛntɪkəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a secret or unauthorized assembly for worship2. a small meeting house or chapel for a religious assembly, esp of Nonconformists or Dissenters
Derived forms
conventicler (conˈventicler) noun
Word origin
C14: from Latin conventiculum a meeting, from conventus; see convent

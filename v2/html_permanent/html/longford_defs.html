Longford

(ˈlɒŋfəd )
Definitions
noun1. a county of N Republic of Ireland, in Leinster province. County town: Longford. Pop: 31 068 (2002). Area: 1043 sq km (403 sq miles)2. a town in N Republic of Ireland, county town of Co Longford. Pop: 7557 (2002)

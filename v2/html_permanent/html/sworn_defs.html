sworn

(swɔːʳn )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. Sworn is the past participle of swear.2. adjective [ADJ n]If you make a sworn statement or declaration, you swear that everything that you have said in it is true. ⇒ The allegations against them were made in sworn evidence to the inquiry.3. adjective [ADJ n]If two people or two groups of people are sworn enemies, they dislike each other very much. ⇒ It somehow seems hardly surprising that Ms Player is now his sworn enemy.

ball bearing

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a bearing consisting of a number of hard steel balls rolling between a metal sleeve fitted over the rotating shaft and an outer sleeve held in the bearing housing, so reducing friction between moving parts while providing support for the shaft2. a metal ball, esp one used in such a bearing

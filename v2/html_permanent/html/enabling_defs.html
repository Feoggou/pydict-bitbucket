enabling

(ɪnˈeɪbəlɪŋ)
Definitions
adjectiveproviding the power, means, opportunity, or authority to allow someone to do something ⇒ the role of the enabling state assisting everything from farming and science, ⇒ an enabling and facilitative organization, ⇒ a process of assigning decision-making authority to executives and employees and creating an enabling and facilitative organization

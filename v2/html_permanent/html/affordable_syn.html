affordable

adjective

  1. inexpensive, fair, cheap, reasonable, moderate, modest, low-price, low-cost, economical • beautiful clothes at affordable prices OPPOSITES:  dear, expensive, costly, exorbitant, prohibitively expensive, unaffordable, uneconomical, beyond your means
  2. manageable, sustainable, bearable

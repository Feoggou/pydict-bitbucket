yacht

(jɒt )
Definitions
noun1. a vessel propelled by sail or power, used esp for pleasure cruising, racing, etc2. short for sand yacht, ice yachtverb3. (intransitive) to sail or cruise in a yacht
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from obsolete Dutch jaghte, short for jahtschip, from jagen to chase + schip ship

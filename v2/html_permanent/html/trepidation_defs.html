trepidation

(ˌtrɛpɪˈdeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a state of fear or anxiety2. a condition of quaking or palpitation, esp one caused by anxiety
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin trepidātiō, from trepidāre to be in a state of alarm; compare intrepid

trepidation

(trepɪdeɪʃən ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [oft with N]Trepidation is fear or anxiety about something that you are going to do or experience. [formal] ⇒ It was with some trepidation that I viewed the prospect of cycling across Uganda.

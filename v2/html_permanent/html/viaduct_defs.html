viaduct

(ˈvaɪəˌdʌkt )
Definitions
nouna bridge, esp for carrying a road or railway across a valley, etc, consisting of a set of arches supported by a row of piers or towers
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from Latin via way + dūcere to bring, on the model of aqueduct

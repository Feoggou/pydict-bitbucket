oblation

(ɒˈbleɪʃən )
Definitions
noun Christianity1. the offering of the bread and wine of the Eucharist to God2. any offering made for religious or charitable purposes
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

oblatory (ˈɒblətərɪ , -trɪ) or oblational (obˈlational) adjective

Word Origin

C15: from Church Latin oblātiō; see oblate2

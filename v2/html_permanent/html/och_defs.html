och

(ɒx ) Scottish and Irish
Definitions
exclamation1. an expression of surprise, contempt, annoyance, impatience, or disagreementsentence connector2. an expression used to preface a remark, gain time, etc ⇒ och, I suppose so
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

och for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ɒx )
Definitions
conventionOch is used to express surprise at something, or to emphasize agreement or disagreement with what has just been said. [Irish, Scottish] ⇒ 'Och be quiet then,' Shawn said. ⇒ Och aye. I always liked him.

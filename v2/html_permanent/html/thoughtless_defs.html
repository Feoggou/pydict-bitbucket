thoughtless

(ˈθɔːtlɪs )
Definitions
adjective1. inconsiderate ⇒ a thoughtless remark2. having or showing lack of thought ⇒ a thoughtless essay3. unable to think; not having the power of thought
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

thoughtlessly (ˈthoughtlessly) adverbthoughtlessness (ˈthoughtlessness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as thoughtless, you are critical of them because they forget or ignore other people's wants, needs, or feelings. [disapproval] ⇒ ...a small minority of thoughtless and inconsiderate people. [+ of] ⇒ It was thoughtless of her to mention it. ⇒ It was just a thoughtless remark.thoughtlessly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ They thoughtlessly planned a picnic without him. ⇒ Nobody is perfect, and everyone acts thoughtlessly on occasion.thoughtlessness uncount noun ⇒ What women mistake as thoughtlessness is often just diffidence.

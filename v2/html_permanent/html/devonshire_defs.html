Devonshire

(ˈdɛvənʃɪə , -ʃə)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun 8th Duke of, title of Spencer Compton Cavendish. 1833–1908, British politician, also known (1858–91) as Lord Hartington. He led the Liberal Party (1874–80) and left it to found the Liberal Unionist Party (1886)

Pharisee

(ˈfærɪˌsiː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Judaism a member of an ancient Jewish sect that was opposed to the Sadducees, teaching strict observance of Jewish tradition as interpreted rabbinically and believing in life after death and in the coming of the Messiah2. (often not capital) a self-righteous or hypocritical person

Word Origin

Old English Farīsēus, ultimately from Aramaic perīshāiyā, pl of perīsh separated

anathema

(əˈnæθəmə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -mas1. a detested person or thing ⇒ he is anathema to me2. a formal ecclesiastical curse of excommunication or a formal denunciation of a doctrine3. the person or thing so cursed4. a strong curse; imprecation
Word origin
C16: via Church Latin from Greek: something accursed, dedicated (to evil), from anatithenai to dedicate, from ana- + tithenai to set

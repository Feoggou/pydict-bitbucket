biography

(baɪˈɒɡrəfɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -phies1. an account of a person's life by another2. such accounts collectively
Derived forms
biographer (biˈographer) nounbiographical (ˌbaɪəˈɡræfɪkəl ) or archaic ˌbiographic (ˌbioˈgraphic) adjectiveˌbiographically (ˌbioˈgraphically) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [oft with poss]A biography of someone is an account of their life, written by someone else.2. uncount nounBiography is the branch of literature which deals with accounts of people's lives. ⇒ ...a volume of biography and criticism.

altruism

(ˈæltruːˌɪzəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the principle or practice of unselfish concern for the welfare of others2. the philosophical doctrine that right action is that which produces the greatest benefit to others▷ Compare egoism▷ See also utilitarianism
Derived forms
ˈaltruist nounˌaltruˈistic adjectiveˌaltruˈistically adverb
Word origin
C19: from French altruisme, from Italian altrui others, from Latin alterī, plural of alter other

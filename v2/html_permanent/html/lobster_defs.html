lobster

(ˈlɒbstə )
Definitions
noun plural -sters or -ster1. any of several large marine decapod crustaceans of the genus Homarus, esp H. vulgaris, occurring on rocky shores and having the first pair of limbs modified as large pincers2. any of several similar crustaceans, esp the spiny lobster3. the flesh of any of these crustaceans, eaten as a delicacy
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English loppestre, from loppe spider

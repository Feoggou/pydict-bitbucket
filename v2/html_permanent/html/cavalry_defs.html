cavalry

(ˈkævəlrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ries1. (esp formerly) the part of an army composed of mounted troops2. the armoured element of a modern army3. (as modifier) ⇒ a cavalry unit, ⇒ a cavalry charge
Derived forms
cavalryman (ˈcavalryman) noun
Word origin
C16: from French cavallerie, from Italian cavalleria, from cavaliere horseman; see cavalier

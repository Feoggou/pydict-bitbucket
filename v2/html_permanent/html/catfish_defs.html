catfish

(ˈkætˌfɪʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -fish or -fishes1. any of numerous mainly freshwater teleost fishes having whisker-like barbels around the mouth, esp the silurids of Europe and Asia and the horned pouts of North America2. another name for wolffish

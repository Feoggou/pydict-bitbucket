physical science

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany of the sciences concerned with nonliving matter, energy, and the physical properties of the universe, such as physics, chemistry, astronomy, and geology. Compare life science

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu pl]The physical sciences are branches of science such as physics, chemistry, and geology that are concerned with natural forces and with things that do not have life. ⇒ ...the rapid growth of interest in both the natural and physical sciences.

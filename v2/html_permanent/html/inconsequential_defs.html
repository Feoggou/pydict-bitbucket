inconsequential

(ˌɪnkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəl , ɪnˌkɒn-) or inconsequent (ɪnˈkɒnsɪkwənt )
Definitions
adjective1. not following logically as a consequence2. trivial or insignificant3. not in a logical sequence; haphazard
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inconsequentiality (ˌinconseˌquentiˈality), inconsequentialness (ˌinconseˈquentialness), inconsequence (inˈconsequence) or inconsequentness (inˈconsequentness) nouninconsequentially (ˌinconseˈquentially) or inconsequently (inˈconsequently) adverb

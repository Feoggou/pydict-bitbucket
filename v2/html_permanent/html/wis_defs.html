wis

(wɪs )
Definitions
verbarchaic to know or suppose (something)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: a form derived from iwis, mistakenly interpreted as I wis I know, as if from Old English witan to know

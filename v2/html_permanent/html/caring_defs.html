caring

(ˈkɛərɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. feeling or showing care and compassion ⇒ a caring attitude2. of or relating to professional social or medical care ⇒ nursing is a caring jobnoun3. the practice or profession of providing social or medical care

caring

Powered by COBUILD
(keərɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is caring, they are affectionate, helpful, and sympathetic. ⇒ He is a lovely boy, very gentle and caring. ⇒ ...a loving, caring husband.2. adjective [ADJ n]The caring professions are those such as nursing and social work that are involved with looking after people who are ill or who need help in coping with their lives. [British] ⇒ The course is also suitable for those in the caring professions. ⇒ ...the caring services.3. → see also care

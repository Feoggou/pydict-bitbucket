Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n, Also v-link ADJ with/in n]Something that is marbled has a pattern or colouring like that of marble. ⇒ ...green marbled soap. ⇒ If the meat is marbled with fat it should be tender.
Example sentences containing 'marbled'
There were piles of thick, warm towels and new cakes of green marbled soap. Thomas, Rosie THE WHITE DOVEAnd, in a small book with marbled paper, its tan leather cover cracked and smelling of camphor, I found in the index, `Siegwald". Harris, Elizabeth TIME OF THE WOLFHe talked to Matt and together, before dark, they had the steer: a fine two-year-old that would have marbled steaks and large, firm roasts. McCorquodale, Robin DANSVILLE"Half an hour later she was standing under the shower in the spacious marbled bathroom of their hotel suite. Susan Elizabeth Phillips LADY BE GOOD

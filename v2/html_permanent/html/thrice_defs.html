thrice

(θraɪs )
Definitions
adverb1. three times2. in threefold degree3. archaic greatly
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English thrīwa, thrīga; see three

thrice

Powered by COBUILD
(θraɪs ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adverb [ADV with v, ADV adv, ADV n]Something that happens thrice happens three times. [old-fashioned] ⇒ They should think not twice, but thrice, before ignoring such advice. ⇒ She plays tennis thrice weekly.2. adverb [ADV n]You can use thrice to indicate that something is three times the size, value, or intensity of something else. [old-fashioned] ⇒ The metal had been valued at twice or thrice its current price. ⇒ ...moving at thrice the speed of sound.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

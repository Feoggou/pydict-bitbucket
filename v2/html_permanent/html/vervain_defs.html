vervain

(ˈvɜːveɪn )
Definitions
nounany of several verbenaceous plants of the genus Verbena, having square stems and long slender spikes of purple, blue, or white flowers
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French verveine, from Latin verbēna sacred bough; see verbena
Example sentences containing 'vervain'
She had a small herb garden she was extremely proud of: dill, vervain , mint, rosemary, thyme, and a wild expanse of parsley. Neil Gaiman SMOKE AND MIRRORS

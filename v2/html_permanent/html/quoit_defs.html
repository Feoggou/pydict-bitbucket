quoit

(kɔɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a ring of iron, plastic, rope, etc, used in the game of quoits2. Australian slang a variant spelling of coit
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: of unknown origin

Definitions

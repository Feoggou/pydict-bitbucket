epileptic

(ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk )
Definitions
adjective also: epileptical1. of, relating to, or having epilepsynoun2. a person who has epilepsy▶ USAGE Rather than talking about an epileptic or epileptics, it is better to talk about a person with epilepsy and people with epilepsy
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is epileptic suffers from epilepsy. ⇒ He was epileptic and refused to take medication for his condition.An epileptic is someone who is epileptic. ⇒ His wife is an epileptic.2. adjective [ADJ n]An epileptic fit is caused by epilepsy. ⇒ He suffered an epileptic fit. ⇒ Paula had never seen an epileptic seizure.

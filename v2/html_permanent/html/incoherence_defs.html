Word Frequency

incoherence

(ˌɪnkoʊˈhɪrəns ; also, ˌɪnkoʊˈhɛrəns ) in American English
Word forms: plural ˌincoˈherencies
Definitions
noun1.  lack of coherence; the quality or state of being incoherent2.  incoherent speech, thought, etc.

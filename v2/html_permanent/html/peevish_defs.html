peevish

(ˈpiːvɪʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. fretful or irritable ⇒ a peevish child2. obsolete perverse

Derived Forms

peevishly (ˈpeevishly) adverbpeevishness (ˈpeevishness) noun

Word Origin

C14: of unknown origin

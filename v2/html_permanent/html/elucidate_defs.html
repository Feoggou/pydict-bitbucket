elucidate

(ɪˈluːsɪˌdeɪt )
Definitions
verbto make clear (something obscure or difficult); clarify

Derived Forms

elucidation (eˌluciˈdation) nounelucidative (eˈluciˌdative) or elucidatory (eˈluciˌdatory) adjectiveelucidator (eˈluciˌdator) noun

Word Origin

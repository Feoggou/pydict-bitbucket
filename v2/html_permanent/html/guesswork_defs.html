guesswork

(ˈɡɛsˌwɜːk )
Definitions
noun1. a set of conclusions, estimates, etc, arrived at by guessing2. the process of making guesses

guesswork

Powered by COBUILD
(geswɜːʳk ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounGuesswork is the process of trying to guess or estimate something without knowing all the facts or information. ⇒ The question of who planted the bomb remains a matter of guesswork.

celery

(ˈsɛlərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an umbelliferous Eurasian plant, Apium graveolens dulce, whose blanched leafstalks are used in salads or cooked as a vegetable. See also celeriac2. See wild celery
Word origin
C17: from French céleri, from Italian (Lombardy) dialect selleri (plural), from Greek selinon parsley

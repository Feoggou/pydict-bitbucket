heights

(ˈhaɪts)
Definitions
noun1. an area of high ground ⇒ There has been fierce fighting on the heights above the bay., ⇒ the Golan Heights2. the state of being far above the ground ⇒ I'm not afraid of heights., ⇒ Heights don't bother me.3. a high level ⇒ the mid-1980s, when house prices rose to absurd heights, ⇒ One wondered what heights of ecstasy the winner reached.4. See to reach new heights5. a position of influence, fame, or power ⇒ the giddy heights of government office, ⇒ an inevitable descent from the heights of political power▶ USAGE The abbreviation for height is h. or H. or hgt or ht

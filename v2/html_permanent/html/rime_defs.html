rime1

(raɪm )
Definitions
noun1. frost formed by the freezing of supercooled water droplets in fog onto solid objectsverb2. (transitive) to cover with rime or something resembling rime
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English hrīm; related to Dutch rijm, Middle High German rīmeln to coat with frost

sword

(sɔːd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a thrusting, striking, or cutting weapon with a long blade having one or two cutting edges, a hilt, and usually a crosspiece or guard2. such a weapon worn on ceremonial occasions as a symbol of authority3. something resembling a sword, such as the snout of a swordfish4. See cross swords5. See the sword

Derived Forms

swordless (ˈswordless) adjectiveswordˌlike (ˈswordˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

Old English sweord; related to Old Saxon swerd, Old Norse sverth, Old High German swert

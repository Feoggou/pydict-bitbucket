infallible

(ɪnˈfæləbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. not fallible; not liable to error2. not liable to failure; certain; sure ⇒ an infallible cure3. completely dependable or trustworthynoun4. a person or thing that is incapable of error or failure
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

infallibility (inˌfalliˈbility) or infallibleness (inˈfallibleness) nouninfallibly (inˈfallibly) adverb

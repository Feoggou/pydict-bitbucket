scarcity

(ˈskɛəsɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. inadequate supply; dearth; paucity2. rarity or infrequent occurrence

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounIf there is a scarcity of something, there is not enough of it for the people who need it or want it. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ ...an ever-increasing scarcity of water.

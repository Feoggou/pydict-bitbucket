emotional

(ɪˈməʊʃənəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, characteristic of, or expressive of emotion2. readily or excessively affected by emotion3. appealing to or arousing emotion ⇒ an emotional piece of music4. caused, determined, or actuated by emotion rather than reason ⇒ an emotional argument
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

emotionality (eˌmotionˈality) nounemotionally (eˈmotionally) adverb

emotional

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmoʊʃənəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Emotional means concerned with emotions and feelings. ⇒ I needed this man's love, and the emotional support he was giving me. ⇒ Victims are left with emotional problems that can last for life.emotionally adverb [ADV adj/-ed] ⇒ Are you saying that you're becoming emotionally involved with me?2. graded adjective & adjectiveAn emotional situation or issue is one that causes people to have strong feelings. ⇒ It's a very emotional issue. How can you advocate selling the ivory from elephants?emotionally graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj/-ed] ⇒ In an emotionally charged speech, he said he was resigning.3. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is or becomes emotional, they show their feelings very openly, especially because they are upset. ⇒ He is a very emotional man. ⇒ I don't get as emotional as I once did.

officious

(əˈfɪʃəs )
Definitions
adjective1. unnecessarily or obtrusively ready to offer advice or services2. marked by such readiness3. diplomacy informal or unofficial4. obsolete attentive or obliging
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

officiously (ofˈficiously) adverbofficiousness (ofˈficiousness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin officiōsus kindly, from officium service; see office

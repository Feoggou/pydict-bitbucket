clew

(kluː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a ball of thread, yarn, or twine2. nautical either of the lower corners of a square sail or the after lower corner of a fore-and-aft sail3. (usually plural) the rigging of a hammock4. a rare variant of clueverb5. (transitive) to coil or roll into a ball
Word origin
Old English cliewen (vb); related to Old High German kliu ball

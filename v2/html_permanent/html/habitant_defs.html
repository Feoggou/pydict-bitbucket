habitant

(ˈhæbɪtənt )
Definitions
noun1. a less common word for inhabitant2. (ˈhæbɪtənt, French abitɑ̃)a. an early French settler in Canada or Louisiana, esp a small farmerb. a descendant of these settlers, esp a farmer

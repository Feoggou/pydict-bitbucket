thereof

Powered by COBUILD
(ðeərɒv ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [n ADV]Thereof is used after a noun to relate that noun to a situation or thing that you have just mentioned. [formal] ⇒ ...his belief in God–or the lack thereof. ⇒ ...a charge of £5 per hour or part thereof.

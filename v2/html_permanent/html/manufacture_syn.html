Synonyms of manufacture

manufacture

verb

  1. make, build, produce, construct, form, create, process, shape, turn out, assemble, compose, forge, mould, put together, fabricate, mass-produce
    • The first three models are being manufactured at our factory in Manchester.
  2. concoct, make up, invent, devise, hatch, fabricate, think up, cook up (informal), trump up
    • He said the allegations were manufactured on the flimsiest evidence.

noun

  1. making, production, construction, assembly, creation, produce, fabrication, mass-production
    • the manufacture of nuclear weapons

mildly

Powered by COBUILD
(maɪldli )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced American English Dictionary
1. → see mild2. put it mildlyphraseYou use to put it mildly to indicate that you are describing something in language that is much less strong, direct, or critical than what you really think. ⇒ But not all the money, to put it mildly, has been used wisely.

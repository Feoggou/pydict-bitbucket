maternity leave

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna period of paid absence from work, in Britain currently six months, to which a woman is legally entitled during the months immediately before and after childbirth
Example sentences containing 'maternity leave'
Apart from the maternity leave , she's never missed a day for mumps and measles and the rest. Dobbs, Michael THE TOUCH OF INNOCENTS`I'll take maternity leave like every other woman executive who's had a baby. Pacter, Trudi YELLOW BIRD

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounYour CV is a brief written account of your personal details, your education, and the jobs you have had. You can send a CV when you are applying for a job. CV is an abbreviation for 'curriculum vitae'. [mainly British] ⇒ Send them a copy of your CV.regional note:   in AM, use résumé

abscess

(ˈæbsɛs , -sɪs)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a localized collection of pus formed as the product of inflammation and usually caused by bacteriaverb2. (intransitive) to form such a collection of pus
Derived forms
ˈabscessed adjective
Word origin
C16: from Latin abscessus a going away, a throwing off of bad humours, hence an abscess, from abscēdere to go away

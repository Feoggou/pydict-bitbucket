coda

(ˈkəʊdə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. music the final, sometimes inessential, part of a musical structure2. a concluding part of a literary work, esp a summary at the end of a novel of further developments in the lives of the characters
Word origin
C18: from Italian: tail, from Latin cauda

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA coda is a separate passage at the end of something such as a book or a speech that finishes it off.2. countable nounIn music, a coda is the final part of a fairly long piece of music which is added in order to finish it off in a pleasing way.

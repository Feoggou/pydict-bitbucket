evangelism

(ɪˈvændʒɪˌlɪzəm )
Definitions
noun1. (in Protestant churches) the practice of spreading the Christian gospel. RC Church term: evangelization, evangelisation2. ardent or missionary zeal for a cause3. the work, methods, or characteristic outlook of a revivalist or evangelist preacher4. a less common word for evangelicalism. See evangelicalism

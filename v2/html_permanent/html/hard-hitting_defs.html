Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a report or speech as hard-hitting, you like the way it talks about difficult or serious matters in a bold and direct way. [journalism, approval] ⇒ In a hard-hitting speech to the IMF, he urged third world countries to undertake sweeping reforms. ⇒ ...a hard-hitting account of violence in the home.

(ɒptɪmɪstɪk )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [ADJ that]Someone who is optimistic is hopeful about the future or the success of something in particular. ⇒ The President says she is optimistic that an agreement can be worked out soon. ⇒ Michael was in a jovial and optimistic mood. [Also + about]optimistically graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] OPPOSITE: pessimistically ⇒ Both sides have spoken optimistically about the talks.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. Done is the past participle of do1.2. adjective [v-link ADJ]A task or activity that is done has been completed successfully. ⇒ When her deal is done, the client emerges with her purchase.3. adjective [v-link ADJ]When something that you are cooking is done, it has been cooked long enough and is ready. ⇒ As soon as the cake is done, remove it from the oven.4. conventionYou say 'Done' when you are accepting a deal, arrangement, or bet that someone has offered to make with you. [spoken, formulae] ⇒ 'You lead and we'll look for it.'—'Done.'5. →  over and done with

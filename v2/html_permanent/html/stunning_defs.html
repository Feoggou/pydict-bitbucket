Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A stunning person or thing is extremely beautiful or impressive. ⇒ She was 55 and still a stunning woman. ⇒ A stunning display of fireworks lit up the sky.stunningly graded adverb [ADV adj] ⇒ The video is filmed in stunningly beautiful countryside.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A stunning event is extremely unusual or unexpected. ⇒ The minister resigned last night after a stunning defeat in Sunday's vote. ⇒ The secret that the priest had confided to him was a stunning piece of news.stunningly graded adverb ⇒ Sometimes people were quite stunningly rude to him.

leguminous

(lɪˈɡjuːmɪnəs )
Definitions
adjectiveof, relating to, or belonging to the Fabaceae (formerly Leguminosae), a family of flowering plants having pods (or legumes) as fruits and root nodules enabling storage of nitrogen-rich material: includes peas, beans, clover, gorse, acacia, and carob
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin legūmen; see legume

Synonyms of fog

fog

noun

  1. mist, gloom, haze, smog, murk, miasma, murkiness, peasouper (informal)
    • The crash happened in thick fog.
  2. stupor, confusion, trance, daze, haze, disorientation
    • He was in a fog when he got up.

verb

  1. mist over or up, cloud over, steam up, become misty
    • The windows fogged immediately.
  2. daze, cloud, dim, muddle, blind, confuse, obscure, bewilder, darken, perplex, stupefy, befuddle, muddy the waters, obfuscate, blear, becloud, bedim
    • His mind was fogged with fatigue.
related phobia:
homichlophobia

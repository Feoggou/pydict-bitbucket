larceny

(ˈlɑːsɪnɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -nieslaw (formerly) a technical word for theft
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

larcenist (ˈlarcenist) or larcener (ˈlarcener) nounlarcenous (ˈlarcenous) adjectivelarcenously (ˈlarcenously) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Old French larcin, from Latin lātrocinium robbery, from latrō robber

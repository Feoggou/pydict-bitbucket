toot1

(tuːt )
Definitions
verb1. to give or cause to give (a short blast, hoot, or whistle) ⇒ to toot a horn, ⇒ to toot a blast, ⇒ the train tootednoun2. the sound made by or as if by a horn, whistle, etc3. slang any drug for snorting, esp cocaine4. US and Canadian slang a drinking spree5. (tʊt) Australian slang a lavatory

Word Origin

toot

Powered by COBUILD
(tuːt ) for learners of English
Word forms: toots , tooting , tooted
Definitions
ergative verb & verbIf someone toots their car horn or if a car horn toots, it produces a short sound or series of sounds. [V n] ⇒ People set off fireworks and tooted their car horns. [V] ⇒ Car horns toot as cyclists dart precariously through the traffic. [V] ⇒ A man behind her tooted angrily.Toot is also a noun. ⇒ The driver gave me a wave and a toot.

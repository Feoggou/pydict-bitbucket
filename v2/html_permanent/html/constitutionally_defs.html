constitutionally

(ˌkɑnstəˈtuʃənəli ; ˈkɑnstəˈtjuʃənəli )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
adverb1.  in composition or physique ⇒ constitutionally frail2.  by nature or temperament ⇒ constitutionally incapable of lying3.  in accordance with the (or a) constitution

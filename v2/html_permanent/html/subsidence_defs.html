subsidence

(səbˈsaɪdəns , ˈsʌbsɪdəns ) or subsidency (ˈsʌbsɪdənsɪ, səbˈsaɪdənsɪ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or process of subsiding or the condition of having subsided2. geology the gradual sinking of landforms to a lower level as a result of earth movements, mining operations, etc

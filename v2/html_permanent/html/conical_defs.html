conical

(ˈkɒnɪkəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having the shape of a cone ⇒ stone huts with conical roofs, ⇒ a small conical tent, ⇒ Old women in large conical hats and Vietnamese clothing sit on the steps.2. of or relating to a cone ⇒ a conical shape
Derived forms
conically (ˈconically) adverb

disfigure

(dɪsˈfɪɡə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to spoil the appearance or shape of; deface2. to mar the effect or quality of
Derived forms

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. verb [usu passive]If someone is disfigured, their appearance is spoiled. [be V-ed] ⇒ Many of the wounded had been badly disfigured.disfigured graded adjective & adjective ⇒ She tried not to look at the scarred, disfigured face. ⇒ Two dogs attacked him, leaving him horribly disfigured.2. verbTo disfigure an object or a place means to spoil its appearance. [V n] ⇒ Wind turbines are large and noisy and they disfigure the landscape.

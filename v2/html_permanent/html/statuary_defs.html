statuary

(ˈstætjʊərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. statues collectively2. the art of making statuesadjective3. of, relating to, or suitable for statues

Word Origin

C16: from Latin statuārius

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounIf you talk about the statuary in a place, you are referring to all the statues and sculpture there. [formal]

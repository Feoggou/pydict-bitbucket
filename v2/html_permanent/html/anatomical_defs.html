Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Anatomical means relating to the structure of the bodies of people and animals. ⇒ ...minute anatomical differences between insects.anatomically (ænətɒmɪkli ) adverb ⇒ I need my pictures to be anatomically correct.

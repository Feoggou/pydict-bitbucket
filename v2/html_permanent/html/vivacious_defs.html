vivacious

(vɪˈveɪʃəs )
Definitions
adjective1. full of high spirits and animation; lively or vital2. obsolete having or displaying tenacity of life
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vivaciously (viˈvaciously) adverbvivaciousness (viˈvaciousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin vīvax lively; see vivace

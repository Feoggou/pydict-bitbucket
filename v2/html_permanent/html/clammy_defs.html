clammy

(ˈklæmɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -mier or -miest1. unpleasantly sticky; moist ⇒ clammy hands2. (of the weather, atmosphere, etc) close; humid
Derived forms
clammily (ˈclammily) adverbclamminess (ˈclamminess) noun
Word origin
C14: from Old English clǣman to smear; related to Old Norse kleima, Old High German kleimen

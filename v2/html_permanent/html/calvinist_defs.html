Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Calvinist means belonging or relating to a strict Protestant church started by John Calvin. ⇒ ...Calvinist doctrine. ⇒ ...the Calvinist work ethic.2. countable nounA Calvinist is a member of the Calvinist church.

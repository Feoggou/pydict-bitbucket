melee

or mêlée (ˈmɛleɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna noisy riotous fight or brawl

Word Origin

C17: from French mêlée. See medley

Word forms: melees
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu sing]A melee is a noisy confusing fight between the people in a crowd. [written] ⇒ A policeman was killed and scores of people were injured in the melee.2. singular nounA melee of things is a large, confusing, disorganized group of them. [written] [+ of] ⇒ ...the melee of streets around the waterfront.

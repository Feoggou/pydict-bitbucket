chaos

(ˈkeɪɒs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. complete disorder; utter confusion2. (usually capital) the disordered formless matter supposed to have existed before the ordered universe3. an obsolete word for abyss
Derived forms
chaotic (keɪˈɒtɪk ) adjectivechaotically (chaˈotically) adverb
Word origin
C15: from Latin, from Greek khaos; compare chasm, yawn

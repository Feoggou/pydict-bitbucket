faucet

(ˈfɔːsɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a tap fitted to a barrel2. US and Canadian a valve by which a fluid flow from a pipe can be controlled by opening and closing an orifice. Also called (in Britain and certain other countries): tap
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French fausset, from Provençal falset, from falsar to bore

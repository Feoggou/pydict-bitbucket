admiration

(ˌædməˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. pleasurable contemplation or surprise2. a person or thing that is admired ⇒ she was the admiration of the court3. archaic wonder

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [in N]Admiration is a feeling of great liking and respect for a person or thing. [+ for] ⇒ I have always had the greatest admiration for him. ⇒ Meg's eyes widened in admiration. [Also + of]
Example sentences containing 'admiration'
He's rather a younger man - with a great admiration for what went on in those days. He asked me to thank you.

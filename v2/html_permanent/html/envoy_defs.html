envoy1

(ˈɛnvɔɪ )
Definitions
noun1. Formal name: envoy extraordinary and minister plenipotentiary a diplomat of the second class, ranking between an ambassador and a minister resident2. an accredited messenger, agent, or representative
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

envoyship (ˈenvoyship) noun

Word Origin

C17: from French envoyé, literally: sent, from envoyer to send, from Vulgar Latin inviāre (unattested) to send on a journey, from in-2 + via road

envoy2

or envoi (ˈɛnvɔɪ )
Definitions
noun1. a brief dedicatory or explanatory stanza concluding certain forms of poetry, notably ballades2. a postscript in other forms of verse or prose
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French envoye, from envoyer to send; see envoy1

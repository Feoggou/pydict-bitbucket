dissection

(dɪˈsɛkʃən ; also daɪˈsɛkʃən ; ˈdaɪˌsɛkʃən )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  a dissecting or being dissected2.  anything dissected, as a plant or animal for study3.  analysis part by part; detailed examination

exotic

(ɪɡˈzɒtɪk )
Definitions
adjective1. originating in a foreign country, esp one in the tropics; not native ⇒ an exotic plant2. having a strange or bizarre allure, beauty, or quality3. New Zealand (of trees, esp pine trees) native to the northern hemisphere but cultivated in New Zealand ⇒ an exotic forest4. of or relating to stripteasenoun5. an exotic person or thing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

exotically (exˈotically) adverbexoticism (exˈotiˌcism) nounexoticness (exˈoticness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin exōticus, from Greek exōtikos foreign, from exō outside

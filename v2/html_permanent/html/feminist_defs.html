feminist

(ˈfɛmɪnɪst )
Definitions
noun1. a person who advocates equal rights for womenadjective2. of, relating to, or advocating feminism

feminist

Powered by COBUILD
(femɪnɪst ) for learners of English
Word forms: feminists
Definitions
1. countable nounA feminist is a person who believes in and supports feminism. ⇒ Only 16 per cent of young women in a 1990 survey considered themselves feminists. ⇒ ...a daunting feminist academic.2. adjective [ADJ n]Feminist groups, ideas, and activities are involved in feminism. ⇒ ...the concerns addressed by the feminist movement. ⇒ ...the reconstruction of history from a feminist perspective.

wares

(wɛəz )
Definitions
plural noun1. articles of manufacture considered as being for sale2. any talent or asset regarded as a commercial or saleable commodity3. (Caribbean) earthenware
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

wares for learners of English

(weəʳz )
Definitions
plural nounSomeone's wares are the things that they sell, usually in the street or in a market. [old-fashioned] ⇒ Vendors displayed their wares in baskets or on the ground.

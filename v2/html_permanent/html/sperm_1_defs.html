sperm

Powered by COBUILD
(spɜːʳm )
Word forms: sperms , sperm
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA sperm is a cell which is produced in the sex organs of a male animal and can enter a female animal's egg and fertilize it. ⇒ Conception occurs when a single sperm fuses with an egg. ⇒ Any disease which undermines a man's general health will interfere with his sperm production. ⇒ Doctor believed that his low sperm count was the problem.2. uncount nounSperm is used to refer to the liquid that contains sperm when it is produced. ⇒ ...a sperm donor.

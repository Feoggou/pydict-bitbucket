Brahma1

(ˈbrɑːmə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a Hindu god: in later Hindu tradition, the Creator who, with Vishnu, the Preserver, and Shiva, the Destroyer, constitutes the triad known as the Trimurti2. another name for Brahman (sense 2)
Word origin
from Sanskrit, from brahman praise

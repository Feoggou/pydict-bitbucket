sidelong

(ˈsaɪdˌlɒŋ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective (prenominal)1. directed or inclining to one side2. indirect or obliqueadverb3. from the side; obliquely

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]If you give someone a sidelong look, you look at them out of the corner of your eyes. ⇒ She gave him a quick sidelong glance.

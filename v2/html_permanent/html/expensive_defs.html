graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is expensive, it costs a lot of money. ⇒ Wine's so expensive in this country. ⇒ I get very nervous because I'm using a lot of expensive equipment.expensively graded adverb & adverb [ADV -ed, ADV after v] ⇒ She was expensively dressed, with fine furs and jewels. ⇒ They do a third-rate job very expensively.

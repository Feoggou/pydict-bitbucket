handmade

Powered by COBUILD
(hændmeɪd ) also hand-made for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveHandmade objects have been made by someone using their hands or using tools rather than by machines. ⇒ As they're handmade, each one varies slightly. ⇒ ...handmade chocolates.2. passive verbIf something is handmade, it is made by someone using their hands or using tools rather than by machines. [be V-ed] ⇒ The beads they use are handmade in the Jura mountains in central France. [be V-ed] ⇒ These exquisitely ornate boxes and cabinets have been handmade and hand-painted by Indian craftspeople.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

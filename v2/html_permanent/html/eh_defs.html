eh

Powered by COBUILD
(eɪ ) for learners of English
Definitions
conventionEh is used in writing to represent a noise that people make as a response in conversation, for example to express agreement or to ask for something to be explained or repeated. ⇒ Let's talk all about it outside, eh? ⇒ 'He's um ill in bed.'—'Eh?'—'He's ill in bed.'

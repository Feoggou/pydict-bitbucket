nomination for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(nɒmɪneɪʃən )
Word forms: nominations
Definitions
1. countable nounA nomination is an official suggestion of someone as a candidate in an election or for a job. ⇒ ...his candidacy for the Republican presidential nomination. [+ for] ⇒ ...a list of nominations for senior lectureships.2. countable nounA nomination for an award is an official suggestion that someone or something should be given that award. [+ for] ⇒ They say he's certain to get a nomination for best supporting actor. ⇒ Alan Parker's film 'The Commitments' has six nominations.3. variable nounThe nomination of someone to a particular job or position is their appointment to that job or position. [+ of] ⇒ They opposed the nomination of a junior officer to the position of Inspector General of Police. ⇒ On the death of Leo there were two main candidates for nomination as his replacement.

Manila

(məˈnɪlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the chief port of the Philippines, on S Luzon on Manila Bay: capital of the republic until 1948 and from 1976; seat of the Far Eastern University and the University of Santo Tomas (1611). Pop: 10 677 000 (2005 est)2. a type of cigar made in this city3. (often not capital) short for Manila hemp, Manila paper

quadrant

(ˈkwɒdrənt )
Definitions
noun1. geometrya. a quarter of the circumference of a circleb. the area enclosed by two perpendicular radii of a circle and its circumferencec. any of the four sections into which a plane is divided by two coordinate axes2. a piece of a mechanism in the form of a quarter circle, esp one used as a cam or a gear sector3. an instrument formerly used in astronomy and navigation for measuring the altitudes of stars, consisting of a graduated arc of 90° and a sighting mechanism attached to a movable arm
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

quadrantal (kwɒˈdræntəl ) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Latin quadrāns a quarter

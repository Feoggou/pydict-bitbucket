hang together

Definitions
verb (intr, adverb)1. to be cohesive or united2. to be consistent ⇒ your statements don't quite hang together

hang together

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. phrasal verbIf two people or groups hang together, they stay with each other and support each other even though they may disagree on some things. stick together [V P] ⇒ He urged his supporters to hang together.2. phrasal verbIf things such as ideas or the parts of something hang together, they are properly organized and fit together reasonably. [V P] ⇒ Her ideas don't always hang together very well as a plot. →  hang

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu sing, usu with poss, oft on N]If someone is on their deathbed, they are in a bed and about to die. ⇒ He promised his mother on her deathbed that he would never marry. ⇒ ...after the man who murdered him nearly 40 years ago made a deathbed confession.

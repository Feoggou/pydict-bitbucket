elector

(ɪˈlɛktə )
Definitions
noun1. someone who is eligible to vote in the election of a government2. (often capital) a member of the US electoral college3. (often capital) (in the Holy Roman Empire) any of the German princes entitled to take part in the election of a new emperor
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

pucker

(ˈpʌkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to gather or contract (a soft surface such as the skin of the face) into wrinkles or folds, or (of such a surface) to be so gathered or contractednoun2. a wrinkle, crease, or irregular fold

Word Origin

C16: perhaps related to poke2, from the creasing into baglike wrinkles

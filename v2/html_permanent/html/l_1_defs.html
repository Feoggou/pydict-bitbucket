also l (el ) (L's, l's ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. variable nounL is the twelfth letter of the English alphabet.2. variable nounL is the symbol for 'learner driver' in Britain. A large red 'L' on a white background is attached to cars in which people are learning to drive.3. L or l is used as an abbreviation for words beginning with l, such as 'litre' and 'lire'.

cretin

(ˈkrɛtɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. old-fashioned a person afflicted with cretinism2. offensive a person considered to be extremely stupid
Derived forms
cretinˌoid (ˈcretinˌoid) adjectivecretinous (ˈcretinous) adjective
Word origin
C18: from French crétin, from Swiss French crestin, from Latin Chrīstiānus Christian, alluding to the humanity of such people, despite their handicaps

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]A fact or situation that is self-evident is so obvious that there is no need for proof or explanation. ⇒ It is self-evident that we will never have enough resources to meet the demand. ⇒ The implications for this country are self-evident.self-evidently graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj] ⇒ The task was self-evidently impossible. ⇒ Self-evidently a handful of companies will benefit.

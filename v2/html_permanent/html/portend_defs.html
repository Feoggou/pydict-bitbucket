portend

(pɔːˈtɛnd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to give warning of; predict or foreshadow2. obsolete to indicate or signify; mean

Word Origin

C15: from Latin portendere to indicate, foretell; related to prōtendere to stretch out

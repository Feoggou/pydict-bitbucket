acutely

Powered by COBUILD
(əkjuːtli )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj, ADV with v]If you feel or notice something acutely, you feel or notice it very strongly. ⇒ He was acutely aware of the odour of cooking oil. ⇒ Those tensions have been felt most acutely by women.2. graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj, ADV with v]If a feeling or quality is acutely unpleasant, it is extremely unpleasant. ⇒ It was an acutely uncomfortable journey back to London.

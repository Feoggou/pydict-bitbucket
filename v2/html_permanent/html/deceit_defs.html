deceit

(dɪˈsiːt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or practice of deceiving2. a statement, act, or device intended to mislead; fraud; trick3. a tendency to deceive
Word origin
C13: from Old French deceite, from deceivre to deceive

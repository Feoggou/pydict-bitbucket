annoyance

(əˈnɔɪəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the feeling of being annoyed2. the act of annoying3. a person or thing that annoys

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount noun [oft with poss]Annoyance is the feeling that you get when someone makes you feel fairly angry or impatient. ⇒ To her annoyance the stranger did not go away. ⇒ He denied there was any annoyance with the British among other EC members.2. countable noun [usu sing]An annoyance is something that makes you feel angry or impatient. ⇒ Inconsiderate neighbours can be more than an annoyance.

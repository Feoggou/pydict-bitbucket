moving

(ˈmuːvɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. arousing or touching the emotions2. changing or capable of changing position3. causing motion

Derived Forms

movingly (ˈmovingly) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is moving, it makes you feel strongly an emotion such as sadness, pity, or sympathy. ⇒ It is very moving to see how much strangers can care for each other. ⇒ It was a moving moment for Marianne.movingly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ You write very movingly of your sister Amy's suicide.2. adjective [ADJ n]A moving model or part of a machine moves or is able to move.3. →  moving spirit/force

interested

(ˈɪntrɪstɪd , -tərɪs-)
Definitions
adjective1. showing or having interest2. (usually prenominal) personally involved or implicated ⇒ the interested parties met to discuss the business

interested

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪntrestɪd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ, ADJ to-inf]If you are interested in something, you think it is important and want to learn more about it or spend time doing it. [+ in] ⇒ I thought she might be interested in Paula's proposal. ⇒ I think the young man is getting interested in gardening. ⇒ I'd be interested to meet her.2. adjective [ADJ n]An interested party or group of people is affected by or involved in a particular event or situation. ⇒ All the interested parties eventually agreed to the idea.3. → See also self-interested

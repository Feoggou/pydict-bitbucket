sprinkler

(ˈsprɪŋklə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a device perforated with small holes that is attached to a garden hose or watering can and used to spray plants, lawns, etc, with water2. a person or thing that sprinkles3. See sprinkler system

flashlight

(ˈflæʃˌlaɪt )
Definitions
noun1. mainly US and Canadian a small portable electric lamp powered by one or more dry batteries. Also called (in Britain and certain other countries): torch2. photography the brief bright light emitted by an electronic flash unit. Sometimes shortened to: flash3. mainly US and Canadian a light that flashes, used for signalling, in a lighthouse, etc

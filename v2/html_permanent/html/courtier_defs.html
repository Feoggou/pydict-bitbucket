courtier

(ˈkɔːtɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an attendant at a court2. a person who seeks favour in an ingratiating manner
Word origin
C13: from Anglo-French courteour (unattested), from Old French corteier to attend at court

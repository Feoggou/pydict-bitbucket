consumptive

(kənˈsʌmptɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. causing consumption; wasteful; destructive2. pathology relating to or affected with consumption, esp tuberculosis of the lungsnoun3. pathology a person who suffers from consumption
Derived forms
consumptively (conˈsumptively) adverbconsumptiveness (conˈsumptiveness) or consumptivity (ˌkɒnsʌmpˈtɪvɪtɪ) noun

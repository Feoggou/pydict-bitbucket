pusillanimous

(ˌpjuːsɪˈlænɪməs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivecharacterized by a lack of courage or determination

Derived Forms

pusillanimity (ˌpjuːsɪləˈnɪmɪtɪ ) nounˌpusillanimously (ˌpusilˈlanimously) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Late Latin pusillanimis from Latin pusillus weak +animus courage

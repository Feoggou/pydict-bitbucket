Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [v-link ADJ]If a place is bursting with people or things, it is full of them. teeming [+ with] ⇒ The place appears to be bursting with women directors. [+ with] ⇒ ...a terraced vegetable garden, bursting with produce.2. adjective [v-link ADJ with n]If you say that someone is bursting with a feeling or quality, you mean that they have a great deal of it. [+ with] ⇒ I was bursting with curiosity. [+ with] ⇒ ...a character bursting with energy and vivacity.3. adjectiveIf you are bursting to do something, you are very eager to do it. [informal] ⇒ She was bursting to tell everyone. ⇒ We'll go there bursting to give it our all.4. adjective [v-link ADJ]If someone says they are bursting, they are emphasizing that they need to urinate very soon. [informal, emphasis]5. →  bursting at the seams/full to bursting6. → see also burst

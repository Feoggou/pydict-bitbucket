disquisition

(ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna formal written or oral examination of a subject
Derived forms
ˌdisquisitional (ˌdisquiˈsitional) adjective
Word origin
C17: from Latin disquīsītiō, from disquīrere to make an investigation, from dis-1 + quaerere to seek

income

(ˈɪnkʌm , ˈɪnkəm )
Definitions
noun1. the amount of monetary or other returns, either earned or unearned, accruing over a given period of time2. receipts; revenue3. rare an inflow or influx
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13 (in the sense: arrival, entrance): from Old English incumen a coming in

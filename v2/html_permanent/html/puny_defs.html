puny

(ˈpjuːnɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -nier or -niest1. having a small physique or weakly constitution2. paltry; insignificant

Derived Forms

punily (ˈpunily) adverbpuniness (ˈpuniness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Old French puisne puisne

graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone or something that is puny is very small or weak. ⇒ ...a puny, bespectacled youth. ⇒ The resources at the central banks' disposal are simply too puny.

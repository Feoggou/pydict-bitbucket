lithograph

(ˈlɪθəˌɡrɑːf , -ˌɡræf)
Definitions
noun1. a print made by lithographyverb2. (transitive) to reproduce (pictures, text, etc) by lithography
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lithographic (ˌlɪθəˈɡræfɪk ) or lithographical (ˌlithoˈgraphical) adjectivelithographically (ˌlithoˈgraphically) adverb

handling

(ˈhændlɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. the act or an instance of picking up, turning over, or touching something2. treatment, as of a theme in literature3. a. the process by which a commodity is packaged, transported, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ handling charges4. law the act of receiving property that one knows or believes to be stolen

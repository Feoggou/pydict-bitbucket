buying

(ˈbaɪɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna. the action or an instance of purchasing something ⇒ There is huge speculative buying right now., ⇒ The bulk buying of foodstuffs has been similarly successful.b. (as modifier) ⇒ buying patterns

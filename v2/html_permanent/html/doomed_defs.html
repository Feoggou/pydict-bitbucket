Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [v-link ADJ, ADJ to-inf]If something is doomed to happen, or if you are doomed to a particular state, something unpleasant is certain to happen, and you can do nothing to prevent it. [+ to] ⇒ Their plans seemed doomed to failure. ⇒ He knew that if he lived, he would be doomed to spend the war as a prisoner.2. adjectiveSomeone or something that is doomed is certain to fail or be destroyed. ⇒ Fireman battled through the smoke in a doomed attempt to rescue the children. ⇒ I used to pour time and energy into projects that were doomed from the start.

Lithuanian

(ˌlɪθjʊˈeɪnɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Lithuania, its people, or their languagenoun2. the official language of Lithuania: belonging to the Baltic branch of the Indo-European family3. a native or inhabitant of Lithuania
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Lithuanian

Powered by COBUILD
(lɪθjueɪniən ) for learners of English
Word forms: Lithuanians
Definitions
1. adjectiveLithuanian means belonging or relating to Lithuania, or to its people, language, or culture. ⇒ ...the Lithuanian parliament.2. countable nounA Lithuanian is a person who comes from Lithuania.3. uncount nounLithuanian is the language spoken in Lithuania.

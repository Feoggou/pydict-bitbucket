minder

(ˈmaɪndə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. someone who looks after someone or something2. short for childminder3. slang an aide to someone in public life, esp a politician or political candidate, who keeps control of press and public relations4. slang someone acting as a bodyguard, guard, or assistant, esp in the criminal underworld

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA minder is a person whose job is to protect someone, especially someone famous. [mainly British, informal] 2. countable nounA minder is the same as a childminder. [British]

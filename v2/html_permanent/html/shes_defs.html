she's

Powered by COBUILD
(ʃiːz , ʃɪz )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. She's is the usual spoken form of 'she is'. ⇒ She's an exceptionally good cook. ⇒ She's having a baby in October.2. She's is a spoken form of 'she has', especially when 'has' is an auxiliary verb. ⇒ She's been married for seven years and has two daughters.

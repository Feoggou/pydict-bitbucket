endless

(ˈɛndlɪs )
Definitions
adjective1. having or seeming to have no end; eternal or infinite2. continuing too long or continually recurring3. formed with the ends joined ⇒ an endless belt
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that something is endless, you mean that it is very large or lasts for a very long time, and it seems as if it will never stop. ⇒ They turned into an endless street. ⇒ The war was endless.endlessly graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v, ADV adj] ⇒ They talk about it endlessly. ⇒ ...endlessly long arcades of shops.

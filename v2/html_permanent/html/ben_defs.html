ben1

(bɛn ) Scottish
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an inner room in a house or cottagepreposition, adverb2. in; within; inside; into the inner part (of a house)adjective3. inner▷ Compare but2
Word origin
Old English binnan, from be- + innan inside

ben2

(bɛn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of several Asiatic trees of the genus Moringa, esp M. oleifera of Arabia and India, whose seeds yield oil of ben, used in manufacturing perfumes and cosmetics, lubricating delicate machinery, etc: family Moringaceae2. the seed of such a tree
Word origin
C15: from Arabic bān

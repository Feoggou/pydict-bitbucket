corporeal

(kɔːˈpɔːrɪəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of the nature of the physical body; not spiritual2. of a material nature; physical
Derived forms
corˌporeality (corˌporeˈality) or corporealness (corˈporealness) nouncorporeally (corˈporeally) adverb
Word origin
C17: from Latin corporeus, from corpus body

Definitions
phraseWhen something takes place, it happens, especially in a controlled or organized way. ⇒ The discussion took place in a famous villa on the lake's shore. ⇒ She wanted Hugh's wedding to take place quickly. ⇒ Elections will now take place on November the twenty-fifth. →  place

summit

noun

  1. meeting, talks, conference, discussion, negotiation, dialogue • a NATO summit held in Rome
  2. peak, top, tip, pinnacle, apex, head, crown, crest • the first man to reach the summit of Mount Everest OPPOSITES:  base, foot, bottom
  3. height, pinnacle, culmination, peak, high point, zenith, acme, crowning point • This is just a molehill on the way to the summit of her ambitions. OPPOSITES: depths, nadir, lowest point

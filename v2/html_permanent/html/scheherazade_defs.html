Scheherazade

(ʃəˌhɛrəˈzɑd ; ʃəˌhɛrəˈzɑdə )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun in The Arabian Nights, the Sultan's bride, who saves her life by maintaining the Sultan's interest in the suspenseful tales she tells

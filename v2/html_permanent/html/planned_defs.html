planned

(ˈplænd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. politics, business organized ⇒ The planned merger has brought pressure from the City to cut costs., ⇒ A planned demonstration has been called off by its organisers., ⇒ Canberra is a planned, purpose-built capital for a young and modern country., ⇒ Parenthood is not always the result of a planned pregnancy.2. premeditated ⇒ You actively took part in a planned robbery when violence took place., ⇒ He alone conceived a planned attack on Los Angeles International Airport.3. proposed or intended ⇒ The workforce voted to strike against planned redundancies.

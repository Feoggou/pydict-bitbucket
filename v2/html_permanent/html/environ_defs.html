environ

(ɪnˈvaɪrən )
Definitions
verb(transitive) to encircle or surround
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French environner to surround, from environ around, from en-1 + viron a circle, from virer to turn, veer1

Similar Words

surround, ring, invest (rare), enclose, encompass, besiege, hem, beset, envelop, encircle, gird, engird

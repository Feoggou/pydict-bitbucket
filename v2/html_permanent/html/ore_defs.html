ore

(ɔː )
Definitions
nounany naturally occurring mineral or aggregate of minerals from which economically important constituents, esp metals, can be extracted
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English ār, ōra; related to Gothic aiz, Latin aes, Dutch oer

(ˈørə)

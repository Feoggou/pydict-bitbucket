expanding

(ɪkˈspændɪŋ)
Definitions
adjectivebecoming, or capable of becoming, greater in extent, volume, size, or scope ⇒ a rapidly expanding industry, ⇒ a watch with an expanding bracelet, ⇒ ring binders, clipboards, and expanding files

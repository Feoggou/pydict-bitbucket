patriotism

(ˈpætrɪəˌtɪzəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noundevotion to one's own country and concern for its defence. Compare nationalism

patriotism

Powered by COBUILD
(pætriətɪzəm , peɪt- )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounPatriotism is love for your country and loyalty towards it. ⇒ He was a country boy who had joined the army out of a sense of patriotism and adventure. ⇒ We live in an age when patriotism is often sneered at.

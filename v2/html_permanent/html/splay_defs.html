splay

(spleɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. spread out; broad and flat2. turned outwards in an awkward mannerverb3. to spread out; turn out or expand4. (transitive) veterinary science to dislocate (a joint)noun5. a surface of a wall that forms an oblique angle to the main flat surfaces, esp at a doorway or window opening6. enlargement
Word origin
C14: short for display

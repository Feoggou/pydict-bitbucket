Linlithgow

(lɪnˈlɪθɡəʊ )
Definitions
noun1. a town in SE Scotland, in West Lothian: ruined palace, residence of Scottish kings and birthplace of Mary, Queen of Scots. Pop: 13 370 (2001)2. the former name of West Lothian

tola

(ˈtəʊlə )
Definitions
nouna unit of weight, used in India, the eightieth part of a ser and equal to 180 grains
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Hindi tolā, from Sanskrit tulā scale, from tul to weigh

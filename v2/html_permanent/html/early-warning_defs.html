early warning

Definitions
nouna. advance notice of some impending event or developmentb. (as modifier) ⇒ an effective early-warning system, ⇒ Early-warning radars were not attacked., ⇒ The money supply can be a useful early-warning signal.

Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]An early warning system warns people that something bad is likely to happen, for example that a machine is about to stop working, or that a country is being attacked.

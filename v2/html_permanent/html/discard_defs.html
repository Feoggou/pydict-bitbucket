discard

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (dɪsˈkɑːd )1. (transitive) to get rid of as useless or undesirable2. cards to throw out (a card or cards) from one's hand3. cards to play (a card not of the suit led nor a trump) when unable to follow suitnoun (ˈdɪskɑːd )4. a person or thing that has been cast aside5. cards a discarded card6. the act of discarding
Derived forms
discarder (disˈcarder) noun

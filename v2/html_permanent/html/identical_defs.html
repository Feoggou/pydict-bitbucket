identical

(aɪˈdɛntɪkəl )
Definitions
adjective1. Also called: numerically identical being one and the same individual ⇒ Cicero and Tully are identical2. Also called: quantitatively identical exactly alike, equal, or agreeing3. designating either or both of a pair of twins of the same sex who developed from a single fertilized ovum that split into two. Compare fraternal (sense 3)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

identically (iˈdentically) adverbidenticalness (iˈdenticalness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Medieval Latin identicus, from Latin idem the same

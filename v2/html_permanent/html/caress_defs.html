caress

(kəˈrɛs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a gentle touch or embrace, esp one given to show affectionverb2. (transitive) to touch or stroke gently with affection or as with affection ⇒ the wind caressed her face
Derived forms
caresser (caˈresser) nouncaressingly (caˈressingly) adverb
Word origin
C17: from French caresse, from Italian carezza, from Latin cārus dear

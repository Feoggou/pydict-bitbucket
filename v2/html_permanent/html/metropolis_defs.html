metropolis

(mɪˈtrɒpəlɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -lises1. the main city, esp of a country or region; capital2. a centre of activity3. the chief see in an ecclesiastical province

Word Origin

C16: from Late Latin from Greek: mother city or state, from mētēr mother + polis city

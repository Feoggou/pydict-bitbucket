spermaceti

(ˌspɜːməˈsɛtɪ , -ˈsiːtɪ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna white waxy substance obtained from oil from the head of the sperm whale: used in cosmetics, candles, ointments, etc

Word Origin

C15: from Medieval Latin sperma cētī whale's sperm, from sperma sperm1 + Latin cētus whale, from Greek kētos

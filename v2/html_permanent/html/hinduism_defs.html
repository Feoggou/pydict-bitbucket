Hinduism

or Hindooism (ˈhɪndʊˌɪzəm )
Definitions
nounthe complex of beliefs, values, and customs comprising the dominant religion of India, characterized by the worship of many gods, including Brahma as supreme being, a caste system, belief in reincarnation, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

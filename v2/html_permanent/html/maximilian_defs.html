Maximilian

(ˌmæksɪˈmɪlɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun full name Ferdinand Maximilian Joseph. 1832–67, archduke of Austria and emperor of Mexico (1864–67). After the French had partially conquered Mexico, he was offered the throne but was defeated and shot by the Mexicans under Juárez

reck

(rɛk )
Definitions
verb archaic (used mainly with a negative)1. to mind or care about (something) ⇒ to reck nought2. (usually impersonal) to concern or interest (someone)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English reccan; related to Old High German ruohhen to take care, Old Norse rækja, Gothic rakjan

leggings

(ˈlɛɡɪŋz )
Definitions
plural noun1. an extra outer covering for the lower legs2. close-fitting trousers worn by women and children

leggings

Powered by COBUILD
(legɪŋz ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. plural noun [oft a pair of N]Leggings are close-fitting trousers, usually made out of a stretchy fabric, that are worn by women and girls. ⇒ She is wearing tight, black leggings and a baggy green jersey.2. plural noun [oft a pair of N]Leggings are an outer covering of leather or other strong material, often in the form of trousers, that you wear over your normal trousers in order to protect them. ⇒ ...a pair of leggings to slip on over your other clothes.

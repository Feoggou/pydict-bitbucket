Wyndham

(ˈwɪndəm )
Definitions
noun John, pseudonym of John Wyndham Parkes Lucas Beynon Harris. 1903–69, British writer of science fiction novels and stories. His works include The Day of the Triffids (1951), The Kraken Wakes (1953), and The Midwich Cuckoos (1957)

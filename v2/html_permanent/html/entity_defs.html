entity

(ˈɛntɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. something having real or distinct existence; a thing, esp when considered as independent of other things2. existence or being3. the essence or real nature
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

entitative (ˈɛntɪtətɪv ) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Medieval Latin entitās, from ēns being; see ens

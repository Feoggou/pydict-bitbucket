requite

(rɪˈkwaɪt )
Definitions
verb(transitive) to make return to (a person for a kindness or injury); repay with a similar action
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

requitable (reˈquitable) adjectiverequitement (reˈquitement) nounrequiter (reˈquiter) noun

Word Origin

C16: re- + obsolete quite to discharge, repay; see quit

Similar Words

return, pay, respond, reward, satisfy, compensate, repay, make good, redress, retaliate, get even, reimburse, make amends, reciprocate, recompense, make restitution, give in return, remunerate, give tit for tat, pay (someone) back in his or her own coin, return like for like

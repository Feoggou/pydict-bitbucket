furtherance

(ˈfɜːðərəns )
Definitions
noun1. the act of furthering; advancement2. something that furthers or advances

uncount nounThe furtherance of something is the activity of helping it to be successful or be achieved. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ The thing that matters is the furtherance of research in this country.

drubbing

(ˈdrʌbɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a beating, as with a stick, cudgel, etc2. a comprehensive or heavy defeat, esp in a sporting competition

Definitions

countable noun [usu sing]If someone gets a drubbing, they are defeated easily. [informal] ⇒ Enough people voted for the Labour Party to give the Conservatives a drubbing.

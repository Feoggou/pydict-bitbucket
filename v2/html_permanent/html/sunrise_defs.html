sunrise

(ˈsʌnˌraɪz )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the daily appearance of the sun above the horizon2. the atmospheric phenomena accompanying this appearance3. Also called (esp US): sunup the time at which the sun rises at a particular locality4. (modifier) of or relating to sunrise industry ⇒ sunrise technology, ⇒ sunrise sector

(sʌnraɪz )
Word forms: sunrises
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounSunrise is the time in the morning when the sun first appears in the sky. ⇒ The rain began before sunrise.2. countable nounA sunrise is the colours and light that you see in the eastern part of the sky when the sun first appears. ⇒ There was a spectacular sunrise yesterday.

at once

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf you do something at once, you do it immediately. ⇒ I have to go, I really must, at once. ⇒ Remove from the heat, add the parsley, toss and serve at once. ⇒ The audience at once greeted him warmly. →  once

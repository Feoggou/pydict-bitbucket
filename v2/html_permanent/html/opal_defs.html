opal

(ˈəʊpəl )
Definitions
nounan amorphous, usually iridescent, mineral that can be of almost any colour, found in igneous rocks and around hot springs. It is used as a gemstone. Composition: hydrated silica. Formula: SiO2.nH2O
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

opal-like (ˈopal-ˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Latin opalus, from Greek opallios, from Sanskrit upala precious stone

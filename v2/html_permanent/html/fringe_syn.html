Synonyms of fringe

fringe

noun

  1. border, edging, edge, binding, trimming, hem, frill, tassel, flounce
    • The jacket had leather fringes.
  2. edge, limits, border, margin, march, marches, outskirts, perimeter, periphery, borderline
    • They lived together on the fringe of the campus.

modifier

  1. unofficial, alternative, radical, innovative, avant-garde, unconventional, unorthodox
    • numerous fringe meetings held during the conference

verb

  1. border, edge, surround, bound, skirt, trim, enclose, flank
    • Swampy islands of vegetation fringe the coastline.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone or something that is annoying makes you feel fairly angry and impatient. ⇒ You must have found my attitude annoying. ⇒ The annoying thing about the scheme is that it's confusing.annoyingly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj] ⇒ Alex looked annoyingly cheerful.2. → see also annoy

instantaneous

(ˌɪnstənˈteɪnɪəs )
Definitions
adjective1. occurring with almost no delay; immediate2. happening or completed within a moment ⇒ instantaneous death3. mathematicsa. occurring at or associated with a particular instantb. equal to the limit of the average value of a given variable as the time interval over which the variable is considered approaches zero ⇒ instantaneous velocity

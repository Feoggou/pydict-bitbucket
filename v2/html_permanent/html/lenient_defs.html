lenient

(ˈliːnɪənt )
Definitions
adjective1. showing or characterized by mercy or tolerance2. archaic caressing or soothing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

leniency (ˈleniency) or lenience (ˈlenience) nounleniently (ˈleniently) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin lēnīre to soothe, from lēnis soft

backsliding

(ˈbækslaɪdɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna return to former bad habits or vices from a state of virtue ⇒ the government's backsliding on free market reforms, ⇒ This may help to maintain the gains you've made and to prevent backsliding.

(bækslaɪdɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounIf you accuse someone of backsliding, you disapprove of them because they have failed to do something they promised or agreed to do, or have started again doing something undesirable that they had previously stopped doing. [disapproval] ⇒ ...the government's backsliding on free market reforms. ⇒ This may help to maintain the gains you've made and to prevent backsliding.

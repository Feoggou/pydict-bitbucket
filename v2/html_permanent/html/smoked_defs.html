smoked

(sməʊkt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. cookery (of meat, fish, cheese, etc) cured by treating with smoke ⇒ Would you like smoked or unsmoked bacon?2. darkened or tinted by exposure to smoke ⇒ a white van with smoked glass windows

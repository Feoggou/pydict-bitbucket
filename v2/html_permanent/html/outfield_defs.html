outfield

(ˈaʊtˌfiːld )
Definitions
noun1. cricket the area of the field relatively far from the pitch; the deep. Compare infield (sense 1)2. baseballa. the area of the playing field beyond the lines connecting first, second, and third basesb. the positions of the left fielder, centre fielder, and right fielder taken collectively. Compare infield (sense 2)3. agriculture farmland most distant from the farmstead

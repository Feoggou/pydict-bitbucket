canonical

(kəˈnɒnɪkəl ) or canonic
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. belonging to or included in a canon of sacred or other officially recognized writings2. belonging to or in conformity with canon law3. according to recognized law; accepted4. music in the form of a canon5. of or relating to a cathedral chapter
Derived forms
canonically (caˈnonically) adverb

dynamics

(daɪˈnæmɪks )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (functioning as singular) the branch of mechanics concerned with the forces that change or produce the motions of bodies. Compare statics, kinematics2. (functioning as singular) the branch of mechanics that includes statics and kinetics. See statics, kinetics3. (functioning as singular) the branch of any science concerned with forces4. those forces that produce change in any field or system5. musica. the various degrees of loudness called for in performanceb. Also called: dynamic marks, dynamic markings directions and symbols used to indicate degrees of loudness

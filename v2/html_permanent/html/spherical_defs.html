spherical

(ˈsfɛrɪkəl ) or spheric

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. shaped like a sphere2. of or relating to a sphere ⇒ spherical geometry3. geometry formed on the surface of or inside a sphere ⇒ a spherical triangle4. a. of or relating to heavenly bodiesb. of or relating to the spheres of the Ptolemaic or the Copernican system

Derived Forms

spherically (ˈspherically) adverbsphericalness (ˈsphericalness) noun

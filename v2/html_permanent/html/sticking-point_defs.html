Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu sing]A sticking point in a discussion or series of negotiations is a point on which the people involved cannot agree and which may delay or stop the talks. A sticking point is also one aspect of a problem which you have trouble dealing with. ⇒ The main sticking point was the question of taxes.

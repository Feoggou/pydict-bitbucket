grief

(ɡriːf )
Definitions
noun1. deep or intense sorrow or distress, esp at the death of someone2. something that causes keen distress or suffering3. informal trouble or annoyance ⇒ people were giving me grief for leaving ten minutes early4. See come to grief5. See tune someone grief
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

griefless (ˈgriefless) adjective

Word Origin

C13: from Anglo-French gref, from grever to grieve1

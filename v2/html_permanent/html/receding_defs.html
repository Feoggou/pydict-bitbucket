receding

(rɪˈsiːdɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. (of a man's hair) ceasing to grow at the temples and above the forehead ⇒ He had receding temples., ⇒ His receding hair was slicked back from his forehead.2. sloping backwards ⇒ a receding chinline

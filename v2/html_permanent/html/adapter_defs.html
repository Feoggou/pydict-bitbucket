adapter

(əˈdæptər ) or aˈdaptor (əˈdæptər )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  a person or thing that adapts2.  a contrivance for adapting apparatus to new uses3.  a connecting device for parts that would not otherwise fit together

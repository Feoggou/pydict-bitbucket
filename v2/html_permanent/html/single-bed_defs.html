single bed

(ˈsɪŋɡəl bɛd)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna bed that is narrower than a double bed and is intended for one person ⇒ There was a single bed with a blue carpet, a dressing-table and a mirror.
Example sentences containing 'single bed'
Naked beside Caroline in the single bed with a duvet tucked around them like a swollen toga, he charted the weeks and months ahead. Forbes, Bryan THE ENDLESS GAMEI think I see someone turning the corner, down the corridor to the bedroom where my father Henry now sleeps in a single bed. Hilary Mantel LEARNING TO TALK: SHORT STORIESThey showed me a bare cube with a single bed , a cupboard, and one uncurtained window with a view of the barren fells. Gash, Jonathan THE TARTAN RINGERS

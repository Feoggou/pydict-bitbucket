cellular

(ˈsɛljʊlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, resembling, or composed of a cell or cells2. having cells or small cavities; porous3. divided into a network of cells4. textiles woven with an open texture ⇒ a cellular blanket5. designed for or involving cellular radio
Derived forms
cellularity (ˌsɛljʊˈlærɪtɪ ) noun

gawky

(ˈɡɔːkɪ ) or gawkish
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: gawkier or gawkiest1. clumsy or ungainly; awkward2. West Yorkshire dialect left-handed
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gawkily (ˈgawkily) or gawkishly (ˈgawkishly) adverbgawkiness (ˈgawkiness) or gawkishness (ˈgawkishness) noun

gawky

Powered by COBUILD
(gɔːki ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone, especially a young person, as gawky, you mean they are awkward and clumsy. ⇒ ...a gawky lad with spots.

acknowledgment

or acknowledgement (əkˈnɒlɪdʒmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of acknowledging or state of being acknowledged2. something done or given as an expression of thanks, as a reply to a message, etc3. (plural) an author's statement acknowledging his use of the works of other authors, usually printed at the front of a book
Example sentences containing 'acknowledgment'
Alison had not followed her, nor had she given any acknowledgment of Kate's comment. Erskine, Barbara MIDNIGHT IS A LONELY PLACEI reached for him, and in a moment felt his acknowledgment. Robin Hobb THE GOLDEN FOOL: BOOK TWO OF THE TAWNY MANHe made no further acknowledgment of their presence, until he got to the hedgerow adjacent to the Courthouse. Clive Barker SACRAMENTHe maintained only the slightest touch on her shoulder, simply an acknowledgment that he required her to guide him. Anderson, Kevin J. THE X FILES 3: GROUND ZERO

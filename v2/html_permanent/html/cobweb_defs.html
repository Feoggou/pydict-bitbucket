cobweb

(ˈkɒbˌwɛb )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a web spun by certain spiders, esp those of the family Theridiidae, often found in the corners of disused rooms2. a single thread of such a web3. something like a cobweb, as in its flimsiness or ability to trap
Derived forms
cobˌwebbed (ˈcobˌwebbed) adjectivecobˌwebby (ˈcobˌwebby) adjective
Word origin
C14 cob, from coppe, from Old English (ātor)coppe spider; related to Middle Dutch koppe spider, Swedish (dialect) etterkoppa

infinitive

(ɪnˈfɪnɪtɪv )
Definitions
noun grammara form of the verb not inflected for grammatical categories such as tense and person and used without an overt subject. In English, the infinitive usually consists of the word to followed by the verb
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

infinitival (ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtaɪvəl ) adjectiveinfinitively (inˈfinitively) or infinitivally (ˌinfiniˈtivally) adverb

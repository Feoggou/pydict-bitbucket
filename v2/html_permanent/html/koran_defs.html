Koran

(kɔːˈrɑːn )
Definitions
nounthe sacred book of Islam, believed by Muslims to be the infallible word of God dictated to Mohammed through the medium of the angel Gabriel. Also: Qur'an
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Koranic (Koˈranic) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Arabic qur'ān reading, book; related to qara'a to read, recite

thirtieth

(ˈθɜːtɪɪθ )
Definitions
adjective1. (usually prenominal)a. being the ordinal number of thirty in counting order, position, time, etc: often written 30thb. (as noun) ⇒ the thirtieth of the monthnoun2. a. one of 30 approximately equal parts of somethingb. (as modifier) ⇒ a thirtieth part3. the fraction equal to one divided by 30 (1⁄30)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

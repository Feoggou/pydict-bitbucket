loud

(laʊd )
Definitions
adjective1. (of sound) relatively great in volume ⇒ a loud shout2. making or able to make sounds of relatively great volume ⇒ a loud voice3. clamorous, insistent, and emphatic ⇒ loud protests4. (of colours, designs, etc) offensive or obtrusive to look at5. characterized by noisy, vulgar, and offensive behaviouradverb6. in a loud manner7. See out loud
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

loudly (ˈloudly) adverbloudness (ˈloudness) noun

Word Origin

Old English hlud; related to Old Swedish hlūd, German laut

loud

Powered by COBUILD
(laʊd ) for learners of English
Word forms: louder , loudest
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf a noise is loud, the level of sound is very high and it can be easily heard. Someone or something that is loud produces a lot of noise. ⇒ Suddenly there was a loud bang. ⇒ His voice became harsh and loud. ⇒ The band was starting to play a fast, loud number. ⇒ ...amazingly loud discos.Loud is also an adverb. ⇒ She wonders whether Paul's hearing is OK because he turns the television up very loud.loudly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ His footsteps echoed loudly in the tiled hall.loudness uncount noun ⇒ The students began to enter the classroom and Anna was startled at their loudness.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is loud in their support for or criticism of something, they express their opinion very often and in a very strong way. vociferous [+ in] ⇒ Mr Adams' speech yesterday was very loud in condemnation of the media. ⇒ Mr Jones received loud support from his local community.loudly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Mac talked loudly in favour of the good works done by the Church.3. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something, especially a piece of clothing, as loud, you dislike it because it has very bright colours or very large, bold patterns which look unpleasant. [disapproval] ⇒ He liked to shock with his gold chains and loud clothes. ⇒ I once paid £120 for an extremely loud shirt which I've yet to wear.4. →  loud and clear5. →  out loud6. → for crying out loud

millionaire

or millionnaire (ˌmɪljəˈnɛə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna person whose assets are worth at least a million of the standard monetary units of his or her country

Derived Forms

ˌmillionairess (ˌmillionˈairess) or ˌmillionnairess (ˌmillionˈnairess) feminine noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA millionaire is a very rich person who has money or property worth at least a million pounds or dollars. ⇒ By the time he died, he was a millionaire.

equestrian

(ɪˈkwɛstrɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to horses and riding2. on horseback; mounted3. depicting or representing a person on horseback ⇒ an equestrian statue4. of, relating to, or composed of Roman equites5. of, relating to, or composed of knights, esp the imperial free knights of the Holy Roman Empirenoun6. a person skilled in riding and horsemanship
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

equestrianism (eˈquestrianˌism) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin equestris, from eques horseman, knight, from equus horse

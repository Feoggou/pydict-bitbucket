trunks

(trʌŋks )
Definitions
plural noun1. Also called: swimming trunks a man's garment worn for swimming, either fairly loose and extending from the waist to the thigh or briefer and close-fitting2. shorts worn for some sports3. mainly British men's underpants with legs that reach midthigh

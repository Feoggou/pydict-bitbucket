Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]You use all-out to describe actions that are carried out in a very energetic and determined way, using all the resources available. ⇒ He launched an all-out attack on his critics. ⇒ ...an all-out effort to bring the fire under control.All out is also an adverb. ⇒ We will be going all out to ensure it doesn't happen again.

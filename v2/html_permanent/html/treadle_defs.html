treadle

(ˈtrɛdəl )
Definitions
noun1. a. a rocking lever operated by the foot to drive a machineb. (as modifier) ⇒ a treadle sewing machineverb2. to work (a machine) with a treadle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

treadler (ˈtreadler) noun

Word Origin

Old English tredel, from trǣde something firm, from tredan to tread

severity

(səˈvɛrəti )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  the quality or condition of being severe; specif.,a.  strictness; harshnessb.  gravity, as of expressionc.  rigid accuracyd.  extreme plainness or restraint, as in stylee.  keenness, as of pain; intensityf.  rigorous or trying character2. Word forms: plural seˈverities something severe, as a punishment

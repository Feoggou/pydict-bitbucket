scruples

(ˈskruːpəlz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noundoubts or hesitation as to what is morally right in a certain situation ⇒ a man with no moral scruples, ⇒ He had no scruples about appropriating other people's property., ⇒ It took time to overcome my scruples.
Example sentences containing 'scruples'
We know what Hugh de Lusignan is; the man has the scruples of a snake. Penman, Sharon HERE BE DRAGONSWell-bred, beautifully educated, but underneath the sleek patina he had the scruples of a sewer rat. Adam, Paul A NASTY DOSE OF DEATH

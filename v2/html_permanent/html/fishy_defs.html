fishy

(ˈfɪʃɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: fishier or fishiest1. of, involving, or suggestive of fish2. abounding in fish3. informal suspicious, doubtful, or questionable ⇒ their leaving at the same time looked fishy4. dull and lifeless ⇒ a fishy look
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fishily (ˈfishily) adverbfishiness (ˈfishiness) noun

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA fishy taste or smell reminds you of fish.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a situation as fishy, you feel that someone is not telling the truth or behaving completely honestly. [informal] ⇒ There seems to be something fishy going on.

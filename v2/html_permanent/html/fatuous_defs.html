Definitions

Word Origin

Powered by COBUILD
(fætʃuəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a person, action, or remark as fatuous, you think that they are extremely silly, showing a lack of intelligence or thought. [formal, disapproval] ⇒ The Chief was left speechless by this fatuous remark.

gold leaf

Definitions
nounvery thin gold sheet with a thickness usually between 0.076 and 0.127 micrometre, produced by rolling or hammering gold and used for gilding woodwork, etc

gold leaf

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounGold leaf is gold that has been beaten flat into very thin sheets and is used for decoration, for example to form the letters on the cover of a book.

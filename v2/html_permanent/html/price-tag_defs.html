price tag

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a ticket or label on an article for sale, showing its price2. the cost, esp of something not usually priced ⇒ the price tag on a top footballer

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounIf something has a price tag of a particular amount, that is the amount that you must pay in order to buy it. [written] ⇒ I can't say it justifies the price tag of £150. ⇒ The price tag on the 34-room white Regency mansion is £17.5 million.2. countable nounIn a shop, the price tag on an article for sale is a small piece of card or paper which is attached to the article and which has the price written on it.

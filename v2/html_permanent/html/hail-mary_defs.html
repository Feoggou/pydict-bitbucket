Hail Mary

Definitions
noun1. Also called: Ave Maria Roman Catholic Church a prayer to the Virgin Mary, based on the salutations of the angel Gabriel (Luke 1:28) and Elizabeth (Luke 1:42) to her2. American football slang a very long high pass into the end zone, made in the final seconds of a half or of a game

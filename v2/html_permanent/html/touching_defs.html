touching

(ˈtʌtʃɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. evoking or eliciting tender feelings ⇒ your sympathy is touchingpreposition2. on the subject of; relating to

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is touching, it causes feelings of sadness or sympathy. ⇒ Her story is the touching tale of a wife who stood by the husband she loved. ⇒ It was a very, very touching moment.touchingly graded adverb [usu ADV adj] ⇒ He was touchingly naive about sex.2. → See also touch

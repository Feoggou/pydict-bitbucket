misnamed

(ˈmɪsˈneɪmd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivehaving been given an inappropriate name ⇒ the grotesquely misnamed Golden Delicious, which taste like old blankets to me

misnamed

Powered by COBUILD
(mɪsneɪmd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
passive verbIf you say that something or someone is misnamed, you mean that they have a name that describes them incorrectly. [be V-ed n] ⇒ ...a high school teacher who was misnamed Mr. Witty. [V-ed] ⇒ ...the misnamed Grand Hotel. [be V-ed] ⇒ The truth is that junk bonds were misnamed, and therefore misunderstood.

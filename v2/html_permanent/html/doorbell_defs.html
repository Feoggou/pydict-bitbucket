doorbell

(ˈdɔːbɛl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna bell that is rung by visitors to a house to alert someone inside to their arrival ⇒ A few days later, the doorbell rang at the home of designer Halston., ⇒ There was a long ring on the doorbell., ⇒ I rang the doorbell but no-one answered.

Grecian

(ˈɡriːʃən )
Definitions
adjective1. (esp of beauty or architecture) conforming to Greek ideals, esp in being classically simplenoun2. a scholar of or expert in the Greek language or literatureadjective, noun3. another word for Greek

Grecian

Powered by COBUILD
(griːʃən ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]Grecian is used to describe something which is in the style of things from ancient Greece. ⇒ ...elegant Grecian columns. ⇒ ...a vaguely Grecian gown of flowing blue.

(dʒəʊˈsaɪə )
Definitions
noundied ?609 bc, king of Judah (?640–?609). After the discovery of a book of law (probably Deuteronomy) in the Temple he began a programme of religious reform. Douay spelling: Josias (dʒəʊˈsaɪəs )

intended

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪntendɪd ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]You use intended to describe the thing you are trying to achieve or person you are trying to affect. ⇒ The intended target had been a military building. ⇒ Keith hoped the obvious sarcasm would have its intended effect. ⇒ It's never occurred to me that the intended victim of the fire was Margaret.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

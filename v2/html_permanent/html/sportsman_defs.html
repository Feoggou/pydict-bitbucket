sportsman

(ˈspɔːtsmən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -men1. a man who takes part in sports, esp of the outdoor type2. a person who exhibits qualities highly regarded in sport, such as fairness, generosity, observance of the rules, and good humour when losing

Derived Forms

sportsman-ˌlike (ˈsportsman-ˌlike) or sportsmanly (ˈsportsmanly) adjectivesportsmanˌship (ˈsportsmanˌship) noun

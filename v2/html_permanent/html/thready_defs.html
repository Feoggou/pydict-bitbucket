thready

(ˈθrɛdɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: threadier or threadiest1. of, relating to, or resembling a thread or threads2. medicine (of the pulse) barely perceptible; weak; fine3. sounding thin, weak, or reedy ⇒ a thready tenor
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

threadiness (ˈthreadiness) noun

dubbing1

(ˈdʌbɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun cinema, television1. the replacement of a soundtrack in one language by one in another language2. the combination of several soundtracks into a single track3. the addition of a soundtrack to a film or broadcast

dubbing2

(ˈdʌbɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. angling hair or fur spun on waxed silk and added to the body of an artificial fly to give it shape2. a variant of dubbin

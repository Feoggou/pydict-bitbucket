transportation

(ˌtrænspɔːˈteɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a means or system of transporting2. the act of transporting or the state of being transported3. (esp formerly) deportation to a penal colony4. mainly US a ticket or fare

transportation

Powered by COBUILD
(trænspɔːʳteɪʃən ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounTransportation refers to any type of vehicle that you can travel in or carry goods in. [mainly US] ⇒ The company will provide transportation.regional note:   in BRIT, usually use transport2. uncount nounTransportation is a system for taking people or goods from one place to another, for example using buses or trains. [mainly US] ⇒ Campuses are usually accessible by public transportation. ⇒ ...our national transportation policy.regional note:   in BRIT, usually use transport3. uncount nounTransportation is the activity of taking goods or people from one place to another in a vehicle. [mainly US] ⇒ The baggage was being rapidly stowed away for transportation. [+ of] ⇒ Oxfam may also help with the transportation of refugees.regional note:   in BRIT, usually use transport

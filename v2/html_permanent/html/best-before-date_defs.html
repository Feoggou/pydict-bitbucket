best-before date

or best before date (ˌbɛstbɪˈfɔː deɪt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna date on packaged food indicating how long it is safe to keep it

Translations for 'best-before date'

  • British English: best-before date /ˌbɛstbɪˈfɔː deɪt/ NOUN
    The best-before date on a food container is the date by which the food should be used before it starts to decay.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveTo be disdainful means to dislike something or someone because you think they are unimportant or not worth your attention. [+ of] ⇒ He is highly disdainful of anything to do with the literary establishment. ⇒ Edgar cast a disdainful look at his twin.disdainfully graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ 'We know all about you,' she said disdainfully.

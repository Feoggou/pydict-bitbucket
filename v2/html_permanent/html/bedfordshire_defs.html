Bedfordshire

(ˈbɛdfədˌʃɪə , -ʃə)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna county of S central England, administered since 2009 by the unitary authorities of Bedford and Central Bedfordshire: mainly low-lying, with the Chiltern Hills in the south: the geographical county includes Luton, which became a separate unitary authority in 1997. Area (excluding Luton): 1192 sq km (460 sq miles). Abbreviation: Beds

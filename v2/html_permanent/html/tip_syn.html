Synonyms of tip 1

tip1

noun

  1. end, point, head, extremity, sharp end, nib, prong
    • She poked and shifted things with the tip of her walking stick.
  2. peak, top, summit, pinnacle, crown, cap, zenith, apex, spire, acme, vertex
    • After dusk, the tip of the cone will light up.
  3. cap, cover, ferrule
    • the protective plastic tip of a shoelace

verb

  1. cap, top, crown, surmount, finish
    • a missile tipped with three warheads

tip2

verb

  1. pour, drop, empty, dump, drain, spill, discharge, unload, jettison, offload, slop (informal), slosh (informal), decant
    • She took the plate and tipped the contents into the bin.
  2. (British) dump, empty, ditch (slang), unload, pour out
    • the costs of tipping rubbish in landfills
  3. tilt, lean, angle, bend, cock, incline
    • She tipped her head back to breathe.

noun

  1. (British) dump, midden, rubbish heap, refuse heap
    • I took a load of rubbish and grass cuttings to the tip.
  • See tip over
  • See tip off
  • See tip something over

tip3

noun

  1. gratuity, gift, reward, present, sweetener (informal), perquisite, baksheesh, pourboire (French)
    • I gave the barber a tip.
  2. hint, suggestion, piece of information, piece of advice, gen (British, informal), pointer, piece of inside information, heads up (US & & Canadian)
    • A good tip is to buy the most expensive lens you can afford.

verb

  1. reward, remunerate, give a tip to, sweeten (informal)
    • Do you think it's customary to tip the waiters?
  2. predict, back, recommend, think of
    • He was widely tipped for success.
  • See tip someone off

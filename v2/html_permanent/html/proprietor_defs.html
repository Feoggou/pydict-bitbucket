proprietor

(prəˈpraɪətə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an owner of an unincorporated business enterprise2. a person enjoying exclusive right of ownership to some property3. US history a governor or body of governors of a proprietary colony

Derived Forms

proprietorship (proˈprietorship) nounproprietorial (prəˌpraɪəˈtɔːrɪəl ) adjectiveproprietress (proˈprietress) or proprietrix (proˈprietrix) feminine noun

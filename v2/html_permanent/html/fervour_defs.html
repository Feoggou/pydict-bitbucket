fervour

or US fervor (ˈfɜːvə )
Definitions
noun1. great intensity of feeling or belief; ardour; zeal2. rare intense heat
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin fervor heat, from fervēre to glow, boil

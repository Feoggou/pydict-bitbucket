Caledonian

(ˌkælɪˈdəʊnɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Scotland2. of or denoting a period of mountain building in NW Europe in the Palaeozoic eranoun3. literary a native or inhabitant of Scotland

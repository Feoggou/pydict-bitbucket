strapping

(ˈstræpɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(prenominal) tall and sturdy

Word Origin

C17: from strap (in the archaic sense: to work vigorously)

strapping

(stræpɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone as strapping, you mean that they are tall and strong, and look healthy. [approval] ⇒ He was a bricklayer–a big, strapping fellow.

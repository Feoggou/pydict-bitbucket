rifleman

(ˈraɪfəlmən )
Definitions
noun plural -men1. a person skilled in the use of a rifle, esp a soldier2. Also called: titipounamu a wren, Acanthisitta chloris, of New Zealand: family Xenicidae. See also bush wren

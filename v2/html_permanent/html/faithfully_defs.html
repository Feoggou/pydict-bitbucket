faithfully

Powered by COBUILD
(feɪθfʊli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. conventionWhen you start a formal or business letter with 'Dear Sir' or 'Dear Madam', you write Yours faithfully before your signature at the end. [British]regional note:   in AM, use Sincerely yours2. → See also faithful

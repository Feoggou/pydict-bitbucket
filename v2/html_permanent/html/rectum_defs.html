rectum

(ˈrɛktəm )
Definitions
noun plural -tums or -ta (-tə)the lower part of the alimentary canal, between the sigmoid flexure of the colon and the anus
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: shortened from New Latin rectum intestinum the straight intestine

Synonyms of parody

parody

noun

  1. takeoff (informal), imitation, satire, caricature, send-up (British, informal), spoof (informal), lampoon, skit, burlesque, piss-take (informal)
    • a parody of a well-know soap opera
  2. travesty, farce, caricature, mockery, apology for
    • His trial was a parody of justice.

verb

  1. take off (informal), mimic, caricature, send up (British, informal), spoof (informal), travesty, lampoon, poke fun at, burlesque, take the piss out of (taboo & slang), satirize, do a takeoff of (informal)
    • It was easy to parody his rather pompous manner of speaking.

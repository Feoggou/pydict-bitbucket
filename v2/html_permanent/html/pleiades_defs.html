Pleiades1

(ˈplaɪəˌdiːz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounGreek mythology the seven daughters of Atlas, placed as stars in the sky either to save them from the pursuit of Orion or, in another account, after they had killed themselves for grief over the death of their half-sisters the Hyades

Pleiades2

(ˈplaɪəˌdiːz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nouna young conspicuous open star cluster approximately 370 light years away in the constellation Taurus, containing several thousand stars only six or seven of which are visible to the naked eye. Compare Hyades1

relations

(rɪˈleɪʃənz )
Definitions
plural noun1. social, political, or personal connections or dealings between or among individuals, groups, nations, etc ⇒ to enjoy good relations2. family or relatives3. euphemistic sexual intercourse

For a brief interlude he had been back in contact with something like normal human relations. Murray, Stephen DEATH AND TRANSFIGURATIONHis job, it seemed, was to develop relations with the locals, a task he had clearly taken to heart. Mark Mills AMAGANSETT

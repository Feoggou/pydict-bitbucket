patterning

(ˈpætənɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. markings or decorative patterns ⇒ geometric patterning, ⇒ a jazzy patterning of lights, ⇒ the patterning of the bird's winter/summer plumage2. psychology conditioning; learning through practice or repetition ⇒ social patterning, ⇒ the patterning of behaviour

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounPatterning is the forming of fixed ways of behaving or of doing things by constantly repeating something or copying other people. [formal] ⇒ ...social patterning. [+ of] ⇒ ...the patterning of behaviour.2. uncount nounYou can refer to lines, spots, or other patterns as patterning. ⇒ ...geometric patterning. [+ of] ⇒ ...a jazzy patterning of lights.

ley

(leɪ , liː )
Definitions
noun1. arable land put down to grass; grassland or pastureland2. Also called: ley line a line joining two prominent points in the landscape, thought to be the line of a prehistoric track
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: variant of lea1

nearby for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(nɪəʳbaɪ ) also near by also near-by
Definitions
adverb [ADV after v, n ADV, from ADV]If something is nearby, it is only a short distance away. ⇒ He might easily have been seen by someone who lived nearby. ⇒ He spoke softly to a couple standing nearby. ⇒ There is less expensive accommodation nearby. ⇒ There were one or two suspicious looks from nearby.Nearby is also an adjective. ⇒ At a nearby table a man was complaining in a loud voice. ⇒ ...the nearby village of Crowthorne.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

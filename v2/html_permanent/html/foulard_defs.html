foulard

(fuːˈlɑːd , ˈfuːlɑː )
Definitions
noun1. a soft light fabric of plain-weave or twill-weave silk or rayon, usually with a printed design2. something made of this fabric, esp a scarf or handkerchief
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from French, of unknown origin

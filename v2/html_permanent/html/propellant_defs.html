propellant

or propellent (prəˈpɛlənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. something that provides or causes propulsion, such as the explosive charge in a gun or the fuel in a rocket2. the gas used to carry the liquid droplets in an aerosol sprayadjective3. able or tending to propel

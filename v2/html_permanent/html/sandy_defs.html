sandy

(ˈsændɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: sandier or sandiest1. consisting of, containing, or covered with sand2. (esp of hair) reddish-yellow3. resembling sand in texture

Derived Forms

sandiness (ˈsandiness) noun

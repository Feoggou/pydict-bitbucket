teetotum

(tiːˈtəʊtəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun archaic1. a spinning top bearing letters of the alphabet on its four sides2. such a top used as a die in gambling games

Word Origin

C18: from T totum, from T initial inscribed on one of the faces + totum the name of the toy, from Latin tōtum the whole

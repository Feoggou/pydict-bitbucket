hoax

(həʊks )
Definitions
noun1. a deception, esp a practical jokeverb2. (transitive) to deceive or play a joke on (someone)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hoaxer (ˈhoaxer) noun

Word Origin

C18: probably from hocus

hoax

Powered by COBUILD
(hoʊks ) for learners of English
Word forms: hoaxes
Definitions
countable noun [oft N n]A hoax is a trick in which someone tells people a lie, for example that there is a bomb somewhere when there is not, or that a picture is genuine when it is not. ⇒ A series of bomb hoaxes has disrupted Christmas shopping in the city centre. ⇒ He denied making the hoax call but was convicted after a short trial.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

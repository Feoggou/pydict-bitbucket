Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe an event or a period of time as eventful, you mean that a lot of interesting, exciting, or important things have happened during it. ⇒ Her eventful life included holding senior positions in the Colonial Service. ⇒ It was an eventful and controversial race. ⇒ Our next journey was longer and much more eventful.

tea table

(tiː ˈteɪbəl)
Definitions
nounBritish a table that is being used for a meal eaten in the late afternoon or early evening ⇒ They sat at the tea table., ⇒ The subject was raised at the tea table., ⇒ cakes and sandwiches on the tea table, ⇒ to set the tea table, ⇒ She set the tea table before her guests arrived.

vermin

(ˈvɜːmɪn )
Definitions
noun1. (functioning as plural) small animals collectively, esp insects and rodents, that are troublesome to man, domestic animals, etc2. plural -min an unpleasant, obnoxious, or dangerous person
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old French vermine, from Latin vermis a worm

digit

(ˈdɪdʒɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a finger or toe2. Also called: figure any of the ten Arabic numerals from 0 to 93. another name for finger (sense 4)4. astronomy one twelfth of the diameter of the sun or moon, used to express the magnitude of an eclipse
Word origin
C15: from Latin digitus toe, finger

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA digit is a written symbol for any of the ten numbers from 0 to 9. ⇒ Her telephone number differs from mine by one digit.2. countable nounA digit is a finger, thumb, or toe. [formal] ⇒ Many animals have five digits.

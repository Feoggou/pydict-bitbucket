unworldly

(ʌnˈwɜːldlɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. not concerned with material values or pursuits2. lacking sophistication; naive3. not of this earth or world

unworldliness (unˈworldliness) noun

unworldly for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnwɜːʳldli )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as unworldly, you mean that they have not experienced many things in their life and do not know what sort of things usually happen to other people during their lives. ⇒ She was so young, so unworldly. [+ about] ⇒ He is a little unworldly about such matters.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as unworldly, you mean that they are not interested in having a lot of money or possessions. ⇒ Kitty's family was unworldly, unimpressed by power, or money.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

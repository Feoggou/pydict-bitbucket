cresting

(ˈkrɛstɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an ornamental ridge along the top of a roof, wall, etc2. carpentry a shaped decorative toprail or horizontal carved ornament surmounting a chair, mirror, etc
Example sentences containing 'cresting'
The ride was rough as a cob, most of the time down in the weeds, weaving through valleys and cresting ridges by the slimmest of margins. Terman, Douglas CORMORANTMagisterial/ magnanimous Seigl rose like a cresting dolphin out of the choppy sea to invite" my friends" to have drinks, sandwiches on him. Joyce Carol Oates THE TATTOOED GIRL

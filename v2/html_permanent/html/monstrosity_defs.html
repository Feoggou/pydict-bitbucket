monstrosity

(mɒnˈstrɒsɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. an outrageous or ugly person or thing; monster2. the state or quality of being monstrous

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf you describe something, especially something large, as a monstrosity, you mean that you think it is extremely ugly. [disapproval] ⇒ Most of the older buildings have been torn down and replaced by modern monstrosities.

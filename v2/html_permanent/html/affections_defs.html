affections

(əˈfɛkʃəns)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounfeelings of fondness, esteem ⇒ He has a special place in the affections of the public., ⇒ her fear of being replaced in his affections., ⇒ Jack Russell has a special place in the affections of the cricketing public.

victor

(ˈvɪktə )
Definitions
noun1. a. a person, nation, etc, that has defeated an adversary in war, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ the victor army2. the winner of any contest, conflict, or struggle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin, from vincere to conquer

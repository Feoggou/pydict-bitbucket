ointment

(ˈɔɪntmənt )
Definitions
noun1. a fatty or oily medicated formulation applied to the skin to heal or protect2. a similar substance used as a cosmetic
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French oignement, from Latin unguentum unguent

embodiment

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmbɒdimənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
singular nounIf you say that someone or something is the embodiment of a quality or idea, you mean that that is their most noticeable characteristic or the basis of all they do. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ A baby is the embodiment of vulnerability.

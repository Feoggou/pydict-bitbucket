teacher

(ˈtiːtʃə )
Definitions
noun1. a person whose occupation is teaching others, esp children2. a personified concept that teaches ⇒ nature is a good teacher
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

teacherless (ˈteacherless) adjective

Definitions
1. countable nounA teacher is a person who teaches, usually as a job at a school or similar institution. ⇒ I'm a teacher with 21 years' experience. ⇒ ...her chemistry teacher.2. → See also supply teacher

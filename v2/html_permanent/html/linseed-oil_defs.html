linseed oil

Definitions
nouna yellow oil extracted from seeds of the flax plant. It has great drying qualities and is used in making oil paints, printer's ink, linoleum, etc

linseed oil

Powered by COBUILD
(lɪnsiːd ɔɪl ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounLinseed oil is an oil made from seeds of the flax plant. It is used to make paints and inks, or to rub into wooden surfaces to protect them.

dereliction

(ˌdɛrɪˈlɪkʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. deliberate, conscious, or wilful neglect (esp in the phrase dereliction of duty)2. the act of abandoning or deserting or the state of being abandoned or deserted3. lawa. accretion of dry land gained by the gradual receding of the sea or by a river changing its courseb. the land thus left

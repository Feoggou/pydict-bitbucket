elusive

(ɪˈluːsɪv )
Definitions
adjective1. difficult to catch ⇒ an elusive thief2. preferring or living in solitude and anonymity3. difficult to remember ⇒ an elusive thoughtSee note at illusory
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

elusively (eˈlusively) adverbelusiveness (eˈlusiveness) noun

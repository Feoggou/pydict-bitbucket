ware1

(wɛə )
Definitions
noun (often in combination)1. (functioning as singular) articles of the same kind or material ⇒ glassware, ⇒ silverware2. porcelain or pottery of a specified type ⇒ agateware, ⇒ jasper ware
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English waru; related to Old Frisian were, Old Norse vara, Middle Dutch Ware

ware2 - archaic

(wɛə )
Definitions
verb1. another word for bewareadjective2. another word for wary, wise1
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English wær; related to Old Saxon, Old High German giwar, Old Norse varr, Gothic war, Latin vereor. See aware, beware

ware3

(wɛə )
Definitions
verb(transitive) Northern England and British dialect to spend or squander
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: of Scandinavian origin; related to Icelandic verja

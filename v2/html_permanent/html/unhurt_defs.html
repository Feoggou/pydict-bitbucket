unhurt for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnhɜːʳt )
Definitions
adjective [ADJ after v, v-link ADJ]If someone who has been attacked, or involved in an accident, is unhurt, they are not injured. unharmed, uninjured ⇒ The lorry driver escaped unhurt, but a pedestrian was injured. ⇒ The two girls suddenly emerged from among the trees. Both seemed to be calm and unhurt.

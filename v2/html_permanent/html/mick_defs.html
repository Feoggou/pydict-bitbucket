Mick

(mɪk ) or Mickey (ˈmɪkɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (sometimes not capital) derogatory a slang name for an Irishman or a Roman Catholic2. Australian the tails side of a coin

Word Origin

C19: from the nickname for Michael

ruth

(ruːθ )
Definitions
noun archaic1. pity; compassion2. repentance; remorse3. grief or distress
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C12: from rewen to rue1

Ruth

(ruːθ )
Definitions
noun1. Old Testamenta. a Moabite woman, who left her own people to remain with her mother-in-law Naomi, and became the wife of Boaz; an ancestress of Davidb. the book in which these events are recounted2.  George Herman, nicknamed Babe. 1895–1948, US professional baseball player from 1914 to 1935

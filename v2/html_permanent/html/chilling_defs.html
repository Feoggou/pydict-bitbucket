Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something as chilling, you mean it is frightening. ⇒ He described in chilling detail how he attacked her during one of their frequent rows.chillingly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj] ⇒ ...since the murder of a London teenager in chillingly similar circumstances in February.

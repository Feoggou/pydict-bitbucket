arched

(ɑːtʃt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. provided with or spanned by an arch or arches2. shaped like an arch; curved

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]An arched roof, window, or doorway is curved at the top.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]An arched bridge has arches as part of its structure. ⇒ ...a fortified arched bridge spanning the River Severn.3. adjective [usu ADJ n]You use arched to describe something that is curved like an arch. ⇒ A frown formed between her arched brows.

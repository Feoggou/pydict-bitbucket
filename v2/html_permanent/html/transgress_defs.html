transgress

(trænzˈɡrɛs )
Definitions
verb1. to break (a law, rule, etc)2. to go beyond or overstep (a limit)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

transgressor (transˈgressor) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin transgredī, from trans- + gradī to step

patriarchy

(ˈpeɪtrɪˌɑːkɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -chies1. a form of social organization in which a male is the head of the family and descent, kinship, and title are traced through the male line2. any society governed by such a system

patriarchy

(peɪtriɑːʳki )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounPatriarchy is a system in which men have all or most of the power and importance in a society or group. ⇒ The main cause of women's and children's oppression is patriarchy.2. countable nounA patriarchy is a patriarchal society.

prelacy

(ˈprɛləsɪ ) or prelaty (ˈprɛlətɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cies1. Also called: prelature (ˈprɛlɪtʃə )a. the office or status of a prelateb. prelates collectively2. Also called: prelatism (ˈprɛləˌtɪzəm ) often derogatory government of the Church by prelates

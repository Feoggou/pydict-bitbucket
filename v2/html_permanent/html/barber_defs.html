barber

(ˈbɑːbə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person whose business is cutting men's hair and shaving or trimming beardsverb (transitive)2. to cut the hair of3. to shave or trim the beard of
Word origin
C13: from Old French barbeor, from barbe beard, from Latin barba

tacking

(ˈtækɪŋ)
Definitions
nounsewing long loose temporary stitches used in dressmaking, etc ⇒ a special adhesive fabric stick which can be used instead of tacking to hold fabrics together ready for stitching, ⇒ Take out the tacking from the seams after you have sewn them.

dazzling

(ˈdæzlɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. so bright as to blind someone temporarily2. extremely clever, attractive, or impressive; brilliant; amazing
Derived forms
dazzlingly (ˈdazzlingly) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is dazzling is very impressive or beautiful. ⇒ He gave Alberg a dazzling smile.dazzlingly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ The view was dazzlingly beautiful.2. adjectiveA dazzling light is very bright and makes you unable to see properly for a short time. ⇒ He shielded his eyes against the dazzling declining sun.dazzlingly graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj] ⇒ The loading bay seemed dazzlingly bright.

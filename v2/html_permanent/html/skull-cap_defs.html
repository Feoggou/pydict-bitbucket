skull cap

or skullcap (skʌl kæp)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small close-fitting cap without a peak, for example the one worm by Jewish men or Roman Catholic priests ⇒ Then she sees a Jewish guy with skullcap and two corgis resting against a wall.2. showjumping a protective helmet worn by people riding a horse ⇒ The bay somersaulted twice before crashing to a halt, the unfortunate jockey tangled in a scrum of flaying hooves, one of which split his skullcap, while another injured his right wrist.3. anatomy the top part of the skull ⇒ Dr Milcent immediately noticed that the skullcap had been sawn, above the eye sockets, as Dr Pelletan had described at the autopsy.4. a plant of the mint family Scutellaria Labiatae that has tubular flowers with a helmet-shaped cup ⇒ Herbs such as skullcap and valerian may be used to alleviate depression and anxiety.

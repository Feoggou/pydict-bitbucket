saucer

(ˈsɔːsə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small round dish on which a cup is set2. any similar dish

Derived Forms

saucerful (ˈsaucerful) nounsaucerless (ˈsaucerless) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Old French saussier container for sauce

Definitions

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounIf there is a disparity between two or more things, there is a noticeable difference between them. [formal] [+ between/in] ⇒ ...the economic disparities between East and West Berlin. ⇒ ...the great disparity of wealth between rich and poor countries.
Example sentences containing 'disparity'
Sometimes marriages are quite successful when there is a disparity in ages. Holt, Victoria THE ROAD TO PARADISE ISLANDHe wanted to be helpful to his young friend, but the disparity between their ages was too great. Bringle, Mary DEATH OF AN UNKNOWN MANThe INS had rejected the fix because of the disparity between where it thought it was and where Fogelman decided it was. Brown, Dale CHAINS OF COMMAND

stopping

(ˈstɒpɪŋ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. British informal a dental filling2. a solid barrier in a mine tunnel to seal off harmful gases, fire, fresh air from used air, etcadjective3. mainly British making many stops in a journey ⇒ a stopping train

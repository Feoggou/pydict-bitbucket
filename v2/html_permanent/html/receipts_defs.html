receipts

(rɪˈsiːts)
Definitions
plural nounbusiness the amount of money received during a particular period, for example by a shop or theatre; takings ⇒ On Saturdays Amos takes the week's receipts to the bank., ⇒ He was tallying the day's receipts., ⇒ About £80,000 has come from gate receipts and collections at the previous two home games., ⇒ the Pre-Budget Report showed that tax receipts were £7 billion less last year, ⇒ The film opened to healthy box office receipts.

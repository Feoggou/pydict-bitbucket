Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small amount2. physics the process in which particles, atoms, etc, are deflected as a result of collision

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA scattering of things or people is a small number of them spread over an area. [+ of] ⇒ ...the scattering of houses east of the village. ⇒ Mr. James had had a scattering of very wealthy friends.

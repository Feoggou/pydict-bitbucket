Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A spacious room or other place is large in size or area, so that you can move around freely in it. ⇒ The house has a spacious kitchen and dining area.spaciousness uncount noun ⇒ A high ceiling creates a feeling of spaciousness.

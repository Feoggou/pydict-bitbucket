Indus1

(ˈɪndəs )
Definitions
nouna faint constellation in the S hemisphere lying between Telescopium and Tucano

Indus2

(ˈɪndəs )
Definitions
nouna river in S Asia, rising in SW Tibet in the Kailas Range of the Himalayas and flowing northwest through Kashmir, then southwest across Pakistan to the Arabian Sea: important throughout history, esp for the Indus Civilization (about 3000 to 1500 bc), and for irrigation. Length: about 2900 km (1800 miles)

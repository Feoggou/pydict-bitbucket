Jaffa

(ˈdʒæfə , ˈdʒɑː-)
Definitions
noun1. a port in W Israel, on the Mediterranean: incorporated into Tel Aviv in 1950; an old Canaanite city. Biblical name: Joppa. Hebrew name: Yafo2. a large variety of orange, having a thick skin

gift

(ɡɪft )
Definitions
noun1. something given; a present2. a special aptitude, ability, or power; talent3. the power or right to give or bestow (esp in the phrases in the gift of, in (someone's) gift)4. the act or process of giving5. See look a gift-horse in the mouthverb (transitive)6. to present (something) as a gift to (a person)7. (often foll by with) to present (someone) with a gift8. rare to endow with; bestow
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

giftless (ˈgiftless) adjective

Word Origin

Old English gift payment for a wife, dowry; related to Old Norse gipt, Old High German gift, Gothic fragifts endowment, engagement; see give

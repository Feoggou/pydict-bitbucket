yester

(ˈjɛstə )
Definitions
adjectivearchaic of or relating to yesterday ⇒ yester sun. Also: yestern (ˈjɛstən )
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English geostror; related to Old High German gestaron, Gothic gistra, Old Norse ī gǣr

Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Fleecy clothes, blankets, or other objects are made of a soft light material. ⇒ ...fleecy walking jackets.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is fleecy is light and soft in appearance. ⇒ It was a lovely afternoon with a blue sky and a few fleecy white clouds.

second fiddle

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun informal1. a. the second violin in a string quartet or one of the second violins in an orchestrab. the musical part assigned to such an instrument2. a secondary status3. a person who has a secondary status
Example sentences containing 'second fiddle'
She would be tied down in some middle-class suburb, cooking dinner parties and playing second fiddle to a newspaper executive. Pacter, Trudi YELLOW BIRDGreat, but Sean was second fiddle to Carlos: she missed the blind man's tenderness and romanticism, his words of love. José Latour HAVANA BEST FRIENDS

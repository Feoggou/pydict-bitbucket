exhibition

(ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a public display of art, products, skills, activities, etc ⇒ a judo exhibition2. the act of exhibiting or the state of being exhibited3. See make an exhibition of oneself4. British an allowance or scholarship awarded to a student at a university or school

exhibition

Powered by COBUILD
(eksɪbɪʃən ) for learners of English
Word forms: exhibitions
Definitions
1. countable nounAn exhibition is a public event at which pictures, sculptures, or other objects of interest are displayed, for example at a museum or art gallery. [+ of] ⇒ ...an exhibition of expressionist art. ⇒ ...an exhibition on the natural history of the area.2. singular nounAn exhibition of a particular skilful activity is a display or example of it that people notice or admire. [+ of] ⇒ He responded in champion's style by treating the fans to an exhibition of power and speed.3. → See also exhibit

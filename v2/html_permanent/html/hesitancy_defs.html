hesitancy

(ˈhɛzɪtənsi ) in American English
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural ˈhesitancies hesitation or indecision; doubt: also ˈhesitance (ˈhɛzətəns )

Word Origin

L haesitantia, a stammering < haesitans, prp. of haesitare

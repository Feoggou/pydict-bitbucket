ideology

(ˌaɪdɪˈɒlədʒɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -gies1. a body of ideas that reflects the beliefs and interests of a nation, political system, etc and underlies political action2. philosophy, sociology the set of beliefs by which a group or society orders reality so as to render it intelligible3. speculation that is imaginary or visionary4. the study of the nature and origin of ideas
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

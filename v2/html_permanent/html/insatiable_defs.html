insatiable

(ɪnˈseɪʃəbəl , -ʃɪə-) or insatiate (ɪnˈseɪʃɪɪt )
Definitions
adjectivenot able to be satisfied or satiated; greedy or unappeasable

insatiable adj. (ɪnˈseɪʃəbəl, -ʃɪə-). 1.

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmbærəst ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]A person who is embarrassed feels shy, ashamed, or guilty about something. ⇒ He looked a bit embarrassed. ⇒ ...an embarrassed silence.

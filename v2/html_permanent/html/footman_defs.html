footman

(ˈfʊtmən )
Definitions
noun plural -men1. a male servant, esp one in livery2. a low four-legged metal stand used in a fireplace for utensils, etc3. (formerly) a foot soldier4. any of several arctiid moths related to the tiger moths, esp the common footman (Eilema lurideola), with yellowish hind wings and brown forewings with a yellow front stripe; they produce woolly bear larvae

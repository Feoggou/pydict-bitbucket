upward for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌpwəʳd )
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]An upward movement or look is directed towards a higher place or a higher level. ⇒ She started once again on the steep upward climb. ⇒ She gave him a quick, upward look, then lowered her eyes.2. adjective [ADJ n]If you refer to an upward trend or an upward spiral, you mean that something is increasing in quantity or price. ⇒ ...the Army's concern that the upward trend in the numbers avoiding military service may continue. ⇒ Oil prices continued an upward swing in New York this morning. ⇒ ...if prices continue their inexorably upward spiral.

indubitable

(ɪnˈdjuːbɪtəbəl )
Definitions
adjectiveincapable of being doubted; unquestionable
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

indubitability (inˌdubitaˈbility) or indubitableness (inˈdubitableness) noun

Word Origin

C18: from Latin indubitābilis, from in-1 + dubitāre to doubt

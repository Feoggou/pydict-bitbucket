judicature

(ˈdʒuːdɪkətʃə )
Definitions
noun1. the administration of justice2. the office, function, or power of a judge3. the extent of authority of a court or judge4. a body of judges or persons exercising judicial authority; judiciary5. a court of justice or such courts collectively

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA pharmacist is a person who is qualified to prepare and sell medicines. 2. countable nounA pharmacist or a pharmacist's is a shop in which drugs and medicines are sold by a pharmacist. [mainly British] chemist, chemist'sregional note:   in AM, usually use pharmacy

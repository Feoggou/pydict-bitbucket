packaging

(ˈpækɪdʒɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. the box or wrapping in which a product is offered for saleb. the design of such a box or wrapping, esp with reference to its ability to attract customers2. the presentation of a person, product, television programme, etc, to the public in a way designed to build up a favourable image3. the work of a packager

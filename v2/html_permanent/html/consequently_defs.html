Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverbConsequently means as a result. [formal] as a result ⇒ Grandfather Dingsdale had sustained a broken back while working in the mines. Consequently, he spent the rest of his life in a wheelchair. ⇒ Relations between the two companies had, consequently, never been close.

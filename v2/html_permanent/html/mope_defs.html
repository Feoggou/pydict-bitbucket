mope

(məʊp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (intransitive)1. to be gloomy or apathetic ⇒ there's no time to mope2. to move or act in an aimless way ⇒ he moped around the flatnoun3. a gloomy person

Derived Forms

moper (ˈmoper) nounmopy (ˈmopy) adjective

Word Origin

C16: perhaps from obsolete mope fool and related to mop2

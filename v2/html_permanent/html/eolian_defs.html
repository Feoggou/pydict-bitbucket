Eolian

(iˈoʊliən ; iˈoʊljən ) in American English
Definitions
adjective1.  alt. sp. of Aeolian2.  [usually e-] carried, formed, eroded, or deposited by the wind, as sand dunes, sediment, etc. noun3.  alt. sp. of Aeolian

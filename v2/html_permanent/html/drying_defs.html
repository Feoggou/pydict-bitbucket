drying

(ˈdraɪɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the action or process of making or becoming dry2. Also called (not now in technical usage): seasoning the processing of timber until it has a moisture content suitable for the purposes for which it is to be usedadjective3. causing dryness ⇒ a drying wind
Example sentences containing 'drying'
There had been several cruel downpours, without the chance of drying clothes in sun the following day. Townsend, Eileen IN LOVE AND WARShe collected her boots from the small drying room by the back door. Mark Burnell CHAMELEONHis hands became heavily scratched, attracting hordes of flies to the lines of drying blood. Garth Nix LIRAEL: DAUGHTER OF THE CLAYR

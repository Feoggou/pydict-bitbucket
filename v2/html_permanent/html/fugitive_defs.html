fugitive

(ˈfjuːdʒɪtɪv )
Definitions
noun1. a person who flees2. a thing that is elusive or fleetingadjective3. fleeing, esp from arrest or pursuit4. not permanent; fleeting; transient5. moving or roving about
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fugitively (ˈfugitively) adverbfugitiveness (ˈfugitiveness) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Latin fugitīvus fleeing away, from fugere to take flight, run away

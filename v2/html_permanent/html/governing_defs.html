governing

(ˈɡʌvənɪŋ)
Definitions
adjectivehaving the power to direct and control the actions, affairs, policies, functions, etc, of a political unit, organization, nation, etc; rule ⇒ Discontent has been fed by the antics of the governing elite., ⇒ The opposition says there's civil war within the governing party.

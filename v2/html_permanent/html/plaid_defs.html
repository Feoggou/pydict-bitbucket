plaid

(plæd , pleɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a long piece of cloth of a tartan pattern, worn over the shoulder as part of Highland costume2. a. a crisscross weave or clothb. (as modifier) ⇒ a plaid scarf

Word Origin

C16: from Scottish Gaelic plaide, of obscure origin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable noun [oft N n]Plaid is material with a check design on it. Plaid is also the design itself. ⇒ Eddie wore blue jeans and a plaid shirt.2. countable nounA plaid is a long piece of tartan material that is worn over the shoulder as part of the Scottish Highland traditional dress.

airborne

(ˈɛəˌbɔːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. conveyed by or through the air2. (of aircraft) flying; in the air

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [v-link ADJ]If an aircraft is airborne, it is in the air and flying. ⇒ The pilot did manage to get airborne.2. adjective [ADJ n]Airborne troops use parachutes to get into enemy territory. ⇒ The allies landed thousands of airborne troops.3. adjective [usu ADJ n]Airborne means in the air or carried in the air. ⇒ Many people are allergic to airborne pollutants such as pollen.

indiscriminate

(ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. lacking discrimination or careful choice; random or promiscuous2. jumbled; confused
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

(ɪndɪskrɪmɪnət ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe an action as indiscriminate, you are critical of it because it does not involve any careful thought or choice. [disapproval] ⇒ The indiscriminate use of fertilisers is damaging to the environment. ⇒ ...the indiscriminate killing of refugees.indiscriminately graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj] ⇒ The men opened fire indiscriminately. ⇒ I'm afraid this disease strikes indiscriminately.

Camelot

(ˈkæmɪˌlɒt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (in Arthurian legend) the English town where King Arthur's palace and court were situated2. (in the US) the supposedly golden age of the presidency of John F. Kennedy, 1961–63

(ˌɪnhjʊˈmeɪn)
Definitions
adjectivetreating people in a cruel way ⇒ He was kept under inhumane conditions., ⇒ He got his first insight into how inhumane employers can be.

inhumane

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnhjʊmeɪn ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as inhumane, you mean that it is extremely cruel. ⇒ He was kept under inhumane conditions. ⇒ He got his first insight into how inhumane employers can be.

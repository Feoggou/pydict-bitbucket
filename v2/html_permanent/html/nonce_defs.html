nonce1

(nɒns )
Definitions
nounthe present time or occasion (now only in the phrase for the nonce)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C12: from the phrase for the nonce, a mistaken division of for then anes, literally: for the once, from then dative singular of the + anes once

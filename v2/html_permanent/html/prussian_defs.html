Prussian

(ˈprʌʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Prussia or its people, esp of the Junkers and their formal military traditionnoun2. a German native or inhabitant of Prussia3. a member of a Baltic people formerly inhabiting the coastal area of the SE Baltic4. See Old Prussian

(riːəlɪstɪkəli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adverbYou use realistically when you want to emphasize that what you are saying is true, even though you would prefer it not to be true. [emphasis] ⇒ Realistically, there is never one right answer.2. → See also realistic

Definitions
COBUILD Key Words for Electrical Engineering
adjective(Electrical engineering: Computing and control) If equipment or devices are servo, they relate to, form part of, or are operated by a servomechanism. ⇒ A servo motor is a motor, very often sold as a complete module, which is used within a position-control or speed-control feedback control system. ⇒ A servo motor is an electromechanical device in which an electrical input determines the position of the armature of a motor. ⇒ If equipment or devices are servo, they relate to, form part of, or are operated by a servomechanism.

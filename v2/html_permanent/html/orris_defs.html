orris1

or orrice (ˈɒrɪs )
Definitions
noun1. any of various irises, esp Iris florentina, that have fragrant rhizomes2. Also called: orrisroot the rhizome of such a plant, prepared and used as perfume
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: variant of iris

orris2

(ˈɒrɪs )
Definitions
nouna kind of lace made of gold or silver, used esp in the 18th century
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

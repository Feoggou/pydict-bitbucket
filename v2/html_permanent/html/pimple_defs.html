pimple

(ˈpɪmpəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small round usually inflamed swelling of the skin2. any of the bumps on the surface of a table tennis bat

Derived Forms

pimpled (ˈpimpled) adjectivepimply (ˈpimply) adjectivepimpliness (ˈpimpliness) noun

Word Origin

C14: related to Old English pipilian to break out in spots; compare Latin papula pimple

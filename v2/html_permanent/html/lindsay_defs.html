Lindsay

(ˈlɪndzɪ )
Definitions
noun1. See (Sir David) Lyndsay2.  Norman Alfred William. 1879–1969, Australian artist and writer3.  (Nicholas) Vachel (ˈveɪtʃəl). 1879–1931, US poet; best known for General William Booth (1913) and The Congo (1914)

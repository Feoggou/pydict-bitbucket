dusk

noun

  1. twilight, evening, evo (Australian, slang), nightfall, sunset, dark, sundown, eventide, gloaming (Scottish, poetic) • We arrived home at dusk. OPPOSITES:  morning, dawn, daybreak, dawning, sunlight, daylight, aurora (poetic), sunup, cockcrow
  2. (poetic) shade, darkness, gloom, obscurity, murk, shadowiness • She turned and disappeared into the dusk.

sentimentality

(ˌsɛntɪmɛnˈtælɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the state, quality, or an instance of being sentimental2. an act, statement, etc, that is sentimental
Example sentences containing 'sentimentality'
It's not out of sentimentality or squeamishness,' he said. Val McDermid THE LAST TEMPTATIONDeterminedly though Burnell tried to banish this ludicrous piece of sentimentality from his head, he could not forget it. Aldiss, Brian SOMEWHERE EAST OF LIFE`Yeah,' said Johnny, whose pitiless education had freed him of sentimentality. Salley Vickers MR GOLIGHTLY'S HOLIDAYShe suppressed the pang she'd felt at the thought of someone else killing Tricker; she couldn't afford sentimentality. S.M. Stirling T2©: RISING STORM

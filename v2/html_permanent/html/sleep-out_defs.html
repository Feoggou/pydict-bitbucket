sleep out

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
verb (intr, adverb)1. (esp of a tramp) to sleep in the open air2. to sleep away from the place of worknoun sleepout3. Australian and New Zealand an area of a veranda that has been glassed in or partitioned off so that it may be used as a bedroom

musty

(ˈmʌstɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -tier or -tiest1. smelling or tasting old, stale, or mouldy2. old-fashioned, dull, or hackneyed ⇒ musty ideas

Derived Forms

mustily (ˈmustily) adverbmustiness (ˈmustiness) noun

Word Origin

C16: perhaps a variant of obsolete moisty, influenced by must3

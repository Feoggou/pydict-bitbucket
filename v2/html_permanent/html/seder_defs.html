Seder

(ˈseɪdə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nounJudaism a ceremonial meal with prescribed ritual reading of the Haggadah observed in Jewish homes on the first night or first two nights of Passover

Word Origin

from Hebrew sēdher order

Synonyms of pool 1

pool1

noun

  1. swimming pool, lido, swimming bath(s) (British), bathing pool (archaic)
    • a heated indoor pool
  2. pond, lake, mere, tarn
    • Beautiful gardens filled with pools and fountains.
  3. puddle, drop, patch, splash
    • There were pools of water on the gravel drive.

pool2

noun

  1. supply, reserve, fall-back
    • the available pool of manpower
  2. kitty, bank, fund, stock, store, pot, jackpot, stockpile, hoard, cache
    • a reserve pool of cash

verb

  1. combine, share, merge, put together, amalgamate, lump together, join forces on
    • We pooled our savings to start up a new business.

noun

  1. syndicate, group, team, trust, combine, collective, consortium

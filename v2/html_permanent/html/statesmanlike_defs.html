Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone, especially a political leader, as statesmanlike, you approve of them because they give the impression of being very able and experienced. [approval] ⇒ He was widely respected as a wise and statesmanlike governor. ⇒ He is trying to project a more dignified, statesmanlike image in this election year.

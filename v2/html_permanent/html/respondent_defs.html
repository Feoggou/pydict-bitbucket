respondent

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪspɒndənt ) for learners of English
Word forms: respondents
Definitions
1. countable noun [usu pl]A respondent is a person who replies to something such as a survey or set of questions. ⇒ 60 percent of the respondents said they disapproved of the president's performance.2. countable nounA respondent is someone who has to defend a case in a law court. [law] OPPOSITE: complainant, appellant ⇒ In addition, the respondent disclosed professional confidences to one of the patients.

brick wall

(brɪk wɔːl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a wall made out of brick ⇒ a tiny garden surrounded by high brick walls2. See banging one's head against a brick wall3. See hit a brick wall

This time, he came up against the brick wall of a demand for a password.

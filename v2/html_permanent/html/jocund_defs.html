jocund

(ˈdʒɒkənd )
Definitions
adjectiveof a humorous temperament; merry
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

jocundity (dʒəʊˈkʌndɪtɪ ) or jocundness (ˈjocundness) nounjocundly (ˈjocundly) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Late Latin jocundus, from Latin jūcundus pleasant, from juvāre to please

Tartarus

(ˈtɑːtərəs )
Definitions
noun Greek mythology1. an abyss under Hades where the Titans were imprisoned2. a part of Hades reserved for evildoers3. the underworld; Hades4. a primordial god who became the father of the monster Typhon
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin, from Greek Tartaros, of obscure origin

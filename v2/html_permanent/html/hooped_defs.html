(huːpt ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]If something is hooped, it is decorated with hoops or horizontal stripes, or it contains hoops as part of its structure. [mainly British] ⇒ ...a hooped arbour of iron rods. ⇒ ...red hooped sleeves.

drippings

(ˈdrɪpɪŋz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounliquid that falls in drops ⇒ There was no trace of tyre tracks or oil drippings., ⇒ the motorcycle drippings in the driveway, ⇒ I'll spread a newspaper underneath to catch the drippings.
Example sentences containing 'drippings'

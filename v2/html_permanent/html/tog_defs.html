tog1

(tɒɡ ) informal
Definitions
verbWord forms: togs, togging or togged(often foll by up or out) to dress oneself, esp in smart clothes
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: probably short for obsolete cant togemans coat, from Latin toga toga + -mans, of uncertain origin

tog2

(tɒɡ )
Definitions
nouna. a unit of thermal resistance used to measure the power of insulation of a fabric, garment, quilt, etc. The tog-value of an article is equal to ten times the temperature difference between its two faces, in degrees Celsius, when the flow of heat across it is equal to one watt per m2b. (as modifier) ⇒ tog-rating
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: arbitrary coinage from tog1 (noun)

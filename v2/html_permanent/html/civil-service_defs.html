civil service

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the service responsible for the public administration of the government of a country. It excludes the legislative, judicial, and military branches. Members of the civil service have no official political allegiance and are not generally affected by changes of governments2. the members of the civil service collectively

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
singular nounThe Civil Service of a country consists of its government departments and all the people who work in them. In many countries, the departments concerned with military and legal affairs are not part of the Civil Service. ⇒ ...a job in the Civil Service.

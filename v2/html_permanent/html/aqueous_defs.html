aqueous

(ˈeɪkwɪəs , ˈækwɪ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, like, or containing water2. dissolved in water ⇒ aqueous ammonia3. (of rocks, deposits, etc) formed from material laid down in water
Word origin
C17: from Medieval Latin aqueus, from Latin aqua water

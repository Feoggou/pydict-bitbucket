have on

Definitions
verb (transitive)1. (usually adverb) to wear2. (usually adverb) to have (a meeting or engagement) arranged as a commitment ⇒ what does your boss have on this afternoon?3. (adverb) informal to trick or tease (a person)4. (preposition) to have available (information or evidence, esp when incriminating) about (a person) ⇒ the police had nothing on him, so they let him go

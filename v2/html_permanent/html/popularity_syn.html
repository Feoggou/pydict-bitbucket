Synonyms of popularity

popularity

noun

  1. favour, fame, esteem, acclaim, regard, reputation, approval, recognition, celebrity, vogue, adoration, renown, repute, idolization, lionization
    • His authority and popularity have declined.
  2. currency, acceptance, circulation, vogue, prevalence
    • This theory has enjoyed tremendous popularity among sociologists.

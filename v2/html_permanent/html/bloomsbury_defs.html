Bloomsbury

(ˈbluːmzbərɪ , -brɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a district of central London in the borough of Camden: contains the British Museum, part of the University of London, and many publishers' officesadjective2. relating to or characteristic of the Bloomsbury Group

benevolence

(bɪˈnɛvələns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also: benevolentness inclination or tendency to help or do good to others; charity2. an act of kindness3. (in the Middle Ages) a forced loan or contribution exacted by English kings from their nobility and subjects

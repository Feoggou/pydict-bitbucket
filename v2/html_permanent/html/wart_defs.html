wart

(wɔːt )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: verruca pathology any firm abnormal elevation of the skin caused by a virus2. botany a small rounded outgrowth3. See warts and all
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

warted (ˈwarted) adjectivewartlike (ˈwartˌlike) adjectivewarty (ˈwarty) adjective

Word Origin

Old English weart(e); related to Old High German warza, Old Norse varta

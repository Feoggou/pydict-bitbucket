terminus

(ˈtɜːmɪnəs )
Definitions
noun plural -ni (-naɪ) or -nuses1. the last or final part or point2. either end of a railway, bus route, etc, or a station or town at such a point3. a goal aimed for4. a boundary or boundary marker5. architecture another name for term (sense 10)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin: end; related to Greek termōn boundary

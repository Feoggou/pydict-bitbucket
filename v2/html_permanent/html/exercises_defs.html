exercises

(ˈɛksəˌsaɪzɪz)
Definitions
plural noun1. military another word for exercise (sense 13)2. exercises are a series of movements or actions which you do in order to get fit, remain healthy, or practise for a particular physical activity ⇒ These stomach exercises will tighten abdominal muscles., ⇒ I do special neck and shoulder exercises., ⇒ That's when I try to meditate or do some deep-breathing exercises., ⇒ We did a few exercises to warm up.3. US and Canadian ceremonies or formal routines, esp at a school or college ⇒ opening exercises, ⇒ Vicky and Gary attended the graduation exercises at Columbia.

lettuce

(ˈlɛtɪs )
Definitions
noun1. any of various plants of the genus Lactuca, esp L. sativa, which is cultivated in many varieties for its large edible leaves: family Asteraceae (composites)2. the leaves of any of these varieties, which are eaten in salads3. any of various plants that resemble true lettuce, such as lamb's lettuce and sea lettuce
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: probably from Old French laitues, pl of laitue, from Latin lactūca, from lac- milk, because of its milky juice

earnings

Powered by COBUILD
(ɜrnɪŋz) in Accounting
Definitions
noun(Accounting: Basic) Earnings are the amount of profit that a person or company receives after taxes have been paid. ⇒ Average weekly earnings rose by 1.5 percent in July. ⇒ The company warned of lower-than-expected earnings for the fiscal year that ended June 30. ⇒ Earnings are the amount of profit that a person or company receives after taxes have been paid. ▶  net earnings, retained earnings, statement of earnings and comprehensive income

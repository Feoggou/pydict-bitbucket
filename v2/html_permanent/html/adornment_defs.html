Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounAn adornment is something that is used to make a person or thing more beautiful. ⇒ It was a building without any adornment or decoration.2. uncount nounAdornment is the process of making something more beautiful by adding something to it. ⇒ Cosmetics are used for adornment.

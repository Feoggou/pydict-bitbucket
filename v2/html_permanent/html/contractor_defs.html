contractor

(ˈkɒntræktə , kənˈtræk-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or firm that contracts to supply materials or labour, esp for building2. something that contracts, esp a muscle3. law a person who is a party to a contract4. the declarer in bridge

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [oft n N]A contractor is a person or company that does work for other people or organizations. [business] ⇒ We told the building contractor that we wanted a garage big enough for two cars. ⇒ ...a major U.S. defense contractor.

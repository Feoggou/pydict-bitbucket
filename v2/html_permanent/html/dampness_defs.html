(dæmpnəs )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounDampness is moisture in the air, or on the surface of something. ⇒ I could see big circles of dampness under each arm. ⇒ The tins had to be kept away from dampness.2. uncount nounDampness is the quality of being damp. ⇒ The dampness of the forest did not agree with him physically.

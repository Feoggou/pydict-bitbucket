dependence

Powered by COBUILD
(dɪpendəns )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounYour dependence on something or someone is your need for them in order to succeed or be able to survive. [+ on] ⇒ ...the city's traditional dependence on tourism. ⇒ Do you look forward to old age, or do you dread frailty, loss of memory and dependence on others?2. uncount noun [usu n N]If you talk about drug dependence or alcohol dependence, you are referring to a situation where someone is addicted to drugs or is an alcoholic. ⇒ French doctors tend to regard drug dependence as a form of deep-rooted psychological disorder.3. uncount nounYou talk about the dependence of one thing on another when the first thing will be affected or determined by the second. ⇒ ...the dependence of circulation on production.

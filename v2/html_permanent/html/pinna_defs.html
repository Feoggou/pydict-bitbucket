pinna

(ˈpɪnə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -nae (-niː) or -nas1. any leaflet of a pinnate compound leaf2. zoology a feather, wing, fin, or similarly shaped part3. another name for auricle (sense 2)

Word Origin

C18: via New Latin from Latin: wing, feather, fin

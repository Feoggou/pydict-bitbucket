wan

(wɒn )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: wanner or wannest1. unnaturally pale, esp from sickness, grief, etc2. characteristic or suggestive of ill health, unhappiness, etc3. (of light, stars, etc) faint or dimverbWord forms: wans, wanning or wanned4. to make or become wan
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

wanly (ˈwanly) adverbwanness (ˈwanness) noun

Word Origin

Old English wann dark; related to wanian to wane

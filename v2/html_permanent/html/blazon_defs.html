blazon

(ˈbleɪzən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. (often foll by abroad) to proclaim loudly and publicly2. heraldry to describe (heraldic arms) in proper terms3. to draw and colour (heraldic arms) conventionallynoun4. heraldry a conventional description or depiction of heraldic arms5. any description or recording, esp of good qualities
Derived forms
blazoner (ˈblazoner) noun
Word origin
C13: from Old French blason coat of arms

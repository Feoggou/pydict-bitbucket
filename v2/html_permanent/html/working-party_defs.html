working party

Definitions
noun1. a committee established to investigate a problem, question, etc2. a group of soldiers or prisoners assigned to perform some manual task or duty

Definitions
countable noun [with sing or pl verb]A working party is a committee which is formed to investigate a particular situation or problem and to produce a report containing its opinions and suggestions. [mainly British] ⇒ They set up a working party to look into the issue. ⇒ ...a finance working party.regional note:   in AM, usually use working group

booty

Powered by COBUILD
(buːti )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounBooty is a collection of valuable things stolen from a place, especially by soldiers after a battle. ⇒ Troops destroyed the capital and confiscated many works of art as war booty.2. countable noun [oft poss N]Someone's booty is their bottom. [informal, rude]

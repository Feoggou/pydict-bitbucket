committed

(kəˈmɪtɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having a strong commitment to an ideology, religion, etc ⇒ a committed socialist2. supporting; in favour of ⇒ He said the government remained committed to peace.

The old man feared that having committed himself to the quest, Dutiful must actually go.

steps

(stɛps)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. a course followed by a person in walking or as walking ⇒ She followed the steps of the girl beside her., ⇒ Her mother was a writer and Con had always determined to follow in her steps.2. a flight of stairs, esp out of doors ⇒ He clumped up the steps.3. British another name for stepladder4. See take steps to do something

Anthony Masters CASCADES - THE DAY OF THE DEADFinally, they arrived on level ground and took a half dozen steps forward. Garth Nix LIRAEL: DAUGHTER OF THE CLAYR

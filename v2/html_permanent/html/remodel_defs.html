remodel

Definitions
verb (riːˈmɒdəl ) -els, -elling, -elled or US -els, -eling or -eled (transitive)1. to change or alter the structure, style, or form of (something) ⇒ expand and remodel the kitchen2. to model again in clay, wax, etc; remouldnoun (ˈriːˌmɒdəl )3. something that has been remodelled

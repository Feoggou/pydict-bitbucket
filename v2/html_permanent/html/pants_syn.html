Synonyms of pants

pants

plural noun

  1. (British) underpants, briefs, drawers, knickers, panties, boxer shorts, Y-fronts®, broekies (South Africa), underdaks (Australian, slang)
    • a matching set of bra and pants
  2. (US) trousers, slacks
    • He was wearing brown corduroy pants and a white shirt.

pant

verb

  1. puff, blow, breathe, gasp, throb, wheeze, huff, heave, palpitate
    • He was panting with the effort of the climb.

noun

  1. gasp, puff, wheeze, huff
    • His breath was coming in short pants.
  • See pant for something

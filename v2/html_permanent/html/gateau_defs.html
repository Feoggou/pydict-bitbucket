gateau

or gâteau (ˈɡætəʊ )
Definitions
noun plural -teaux (-təʊz)any of various elaborate cakes, usually layered with cream and richly decorated
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

French: cake

Definitions
variable nounA gateau is a very rich, elaborate cake, especially one with cream in it. [mainly British] ⇒ ...a slice of gateau. ⇒ ...a selection of gateaux, cakes and pastries.

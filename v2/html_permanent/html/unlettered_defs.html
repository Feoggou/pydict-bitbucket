unlettered

(ʌnˈlɛtəd )
Definitions
adjective1. uneducated; illiterate2. not marked with letters ⇒ an unlettered tombstone
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'unlettered'
My desk still remained; my unlettered neighbour would have little use for a writing desk. Robin Hobb THE GOLDEN FOOL: BOOK TWO OF THE TAWNY MANWhen she straightened up again, I saw she was holding a book: a black-bound tome with a worn, unlettered cover. Pritchard, John NIGHT SISTERSCertain villages have decided to squabble, when even the unlettered tribes band together to defend us. C.J. Cherryh HAMMERFALL

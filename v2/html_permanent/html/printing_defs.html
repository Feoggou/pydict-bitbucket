printing

(ˈprɪntɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. the process, business, or art of producing printed matterb. (as modifier) ⇒ printing ink2. printed text3. Also called: impression all the copies of a book or other publication printed at one time4. a form of writing in which letters resemble printed letters

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

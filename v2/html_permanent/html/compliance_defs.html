compliance

(kəmˈplaɪəns ) or compliancy
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of complying; acquiescence2. a disposition to yield to or comply with others3. a measure of the ability of a mechanical system to respond to an applied vibrating force, expressed as the reciprocal of the system's stiffness. Symbol: C

compliance

Powered by COBUILD
(kəmplaɪəns )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounCompliance with something, for example a law, treaty, or agreement means doing what you are required or expected to do. [formal] ⇒ Inspectors were sent to visit nuclear sites and verify compliance with the treaty. [+ with] ⇒ The company says it is in full compliance with U.S. labor laws. ⇒ The Security Council aim to ensure compliance by all sides, once an agreement is signed.

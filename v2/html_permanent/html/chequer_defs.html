chequer

or US checker (ˈtʃɛkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of the marbles, pegs, or other pieces used in the game of Chinese chequers2. a. a pattern consisting of squares of different colours, textures, or materialsb. one of the squares in such a patternverb (transitive)3. to make irregular in colour or character; variegate4. to mark off with alternating squares of colour
Word origin
C13: chessboard, from Anglo-French escheker, from eschec check

appease

(əˈpiːz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to calm, pacify, or soothe, esp by acceding to the demands of2. to satisfy or quell (an appetite or thirst, etc)
Derived forms
apˈpeasable adjectiveapˈpeaser noun
Word origin
C16: from Old French apaisier, from pais peace, from Latin pax

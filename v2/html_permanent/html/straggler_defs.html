Word forms: stragglers
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu pl]The stragglers are the people in a group who are moving more slowly or making less progress than the others. ⇒ There were two stragglers twenty yards back. ⇒ Any straggler that fell behind or got lost in the darkness was easy prey for the enemy.2. countable noun [usu pl]The stragglers at an event such as a party are the people who are still there after most of the other people have left. ⇒ ...round about two o'clock in the morning when there were only a few stragglers left.

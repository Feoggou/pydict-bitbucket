Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(tr, adverb) to introduce in a gradual or cautious manner ⇒ the legislation will be phased in over two years

phase in

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrasal verbIf a new way of doing something is phased in, it is introduced gradually. [be V-ed P] ⇒ The Health Secretary told Parliament that the reforms would be phased in over three years. [V P n] ⇒ The change is part of the government's policy of phasing in Arabic as the official academic language. [Also V n P] →  phase

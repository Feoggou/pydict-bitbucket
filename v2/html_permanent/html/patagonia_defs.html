Patagonia

(ˌpætəˈɡəʊnɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the southernmost region of South America, in Argentina and Chile extending from the Andes to the Atlantic. Area: about 777 000 sq km (300 000 sq miles)2. an arid tableland in the southernmost part of Argentina, rising towards the Andes in the west

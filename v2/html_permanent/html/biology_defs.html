biology

(baɪˈɒlədʒɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the study of living organisms, including their structure, functioning, evolution, distribution, and interrelationships2. the structure, functioning, etc, of a particular organism or group of organisms3. the animal and plant life of a particular region
Derived forms
biologist (biˈologist) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounBiology is the science which is concerned with the study of living things.biologist (baɪɒlədʒɪst )Word forms: biologists countable noun ⇒ ...biologists studying the fruit fly.2. uncount nounThe biology of a living thing is the way in which its body or cells behave. ⇒ The biology of these diseases is terribly complicated. ⇒ ...human biology.3. → see also molecular biology

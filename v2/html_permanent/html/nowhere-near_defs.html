Definitions
phraseIf you use nowhere near in front of a word or expression, you are emphasizing that the real situation is very different from, or has not yet reached, the state which that word or expression suggests. [emphasis] ⇒ He's nowhere near recovered yet from his experiences. ⇒ The chair he sat in was nowhere near as comfortable as the custom-designed one behind his desk. →  nowhere

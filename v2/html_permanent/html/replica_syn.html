Synonyms of replica

replica

noun

  1. reproduction, model, copy, imitation, facsimile, carbon copy
    • It was a replica, for display only.
    OPPOSITES:  original, master, prototype
  2. duplicate, copy, clone, spitting image, dead ringer, carbon copy
    • The child was a replica of her mother.

friable

(ˈfraɪəbəl )
Definitions
adjectiveeasily broken up; crumbly
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

friability (ˌfriaˈbility) or friableness (ˈfriableness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin friābilis, from friāre to crumble; related to Latin fricāre to rub down

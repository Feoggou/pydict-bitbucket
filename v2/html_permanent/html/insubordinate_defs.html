insubordinate

(ˌɪnsəˈbɔːdɪnɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. not submissive to authority; disobedient or rebellious2. not in a subordinate position or ranknoun3. an insubordinate person
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

insubordinately (ˌinsubˈordinately) adverbinsubordination (ˌinsubˌordiˈnation) noun

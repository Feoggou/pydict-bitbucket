Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. pp is written before a person's name at the bottom of a formal or business letter in order to indicate that they have signed the letter on behalf of the person whose name appears before theirs. [business] ⇒ ...J.R. Adams, pp D. Philips.2. pp. is the plural of 'p.' and means 'pages'. [written] ⇒ See chapter 6, pp. 137-41.3. In a piece of music, pp is a written abbreviation for pianissimo.

borough

(ˈbʌrə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a town, esp (in Britain) one that forms the constituency of an MP or that was originally incorporated by royal charter. See also burgh2. any of the 32 constituent divisions that together with the City of London make up Greater London3. any of the five constituent divisions of New York City4. (in the US) a self-governing incorporated municipality5. (in medieval England) a fortified town or village or a fort6. (in New Zealand) a small municipality with a governing body
Word origin
Old English burg; related to beorgan to shelter, Old Norse borg wall, Gothic baurgs city, Old High German burg fortified castle

accounts

(əˈkaʊnts)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. a. a chronological list of debits and credits relating to a specified asset, liability, expense, or income of a business and forming part of the ledger ⇒ Once again, the company has failed to file its accounts on time., ⇒ He had to submit his accounts for inspection., ⇒ He has to keep detailed accounts., ⇒ You should keep detailed accounts of all your transactions., ⇒ I do the accounts and look after the shop., ⇒ She spends half a day a week doing the accounts.b. (as modifier) ⇒ an accounts booknoun2. another name for accounts department

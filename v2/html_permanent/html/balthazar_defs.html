Balthazar1

(ˈbælθəˌzɑː , bælˈθæzə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna wine bottle holding the equivalent of sixteen normal bottles (approximately 12 litres)
Word origin
C20: named after Balthazar (Belshazzar) from his drinking wine at a great feast (Daniel 5:1)

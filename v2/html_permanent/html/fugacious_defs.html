fugacious

(fjuːˈɡeɪʃəs )
Definitions
adjective1. passing quickly away; transitory; fleeting2. botany lasting for only a short time ⇒ fugacious petals
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fugaciously (fuˈgaciously) adverbfugaciousness (fuˈgaciousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin fugax inclined to flee, swift, from fugere to flee; see fugitive

inflorescence

(ˌɪnflɔːˈrɛsəns )
Definitions
noun1. the part of a plant that consists of the flower-bearing stalks2. the arrangement of the flowers on the stalks3. the process of flowering; blossoming
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inflorescent (ˌinfloˈrescent) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from New Latin inflōrēscentia, from Late Latin inflōrescere to blossom, from flōrescere to bloom

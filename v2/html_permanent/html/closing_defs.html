closing

(ˈkləʊzɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivecomprising the final part of an activity or period of time ⇒ the closing stages of the election campaign, ⇒ He entered RAF service in the closing stages of the war., ⇒ They almost won the match in the closing minutes., ⇒ The north cannot achieve sustained economic development without the south, he added in his closing remarks.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]The closing part of an activity or period of time is the final part of it. ⇒ He entered RAF service in the closing stages of the war. ⇒ They almost won the match in the closing minutes. ⇒ The north cannot achieve sustained economic development without the south, he added in his closing remarks.2. → see also close1

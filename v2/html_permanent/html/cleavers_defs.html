cleavers

(ˈkliːvəz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(functioning as singular) a Eurasian rubiaceous plant, Galium aparine, having small white flowers and prickly stems and fruits. Also called: goosegrass, hairif, sticky willie
Word origin
Old English clīfe; related to clīfan to cleave2

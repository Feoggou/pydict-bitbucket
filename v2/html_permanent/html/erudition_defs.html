erudition

Powered by COBUILD
(erʊdɪʃən , US erjə- ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounErudition is great academic knowledge. [formal] ⇒ His erudition was apparently endless.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'erudition'
It was the kind of thing he did from time to time and almost always followed it by undercutting his own erudition. Delman, David DEATH OF A NYMPH`By Jove, good fellow, I am impressed by your erudition , but more so by your perspicacity. Baxter, Stephen ANTI-ICEHe has brought along some of his favorite thrillers-a surprisingly low taste for a person of his erudition. Elizabeth Peters LION IN THE VALLEY

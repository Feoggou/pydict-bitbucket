rapine

(ˈræpaɪn )
Definitions
nounthe seizure of property by force; pillage
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Latin rapīna plundering, from rapere to snatch
Example sentences containing 'rapine'
And then embarked on a career of rapine in the women's quarters while Cazaril, helpless to stop him, watched. Lois McMaster Bujold THE CURSE OF CHALION

Similar Words

pillage, rape, sack, theft, robbery, looting, seizure, plundering, ransacking, marauding, depredation, despoliation, spoliation, despoilment

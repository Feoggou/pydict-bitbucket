querulous

(ˈkwɛrʊləs , ˈkwɛrjʊ-)
Definitions
adjective1. inclined to make whining or peevish complaints2. characterized by or proceeding from a complaining fretful attitude or disposition ⇒ a querulous tone
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

querulously (ˈquerulously) adverbquerulousness (ˈquerulousness) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin querulus from querī to complain

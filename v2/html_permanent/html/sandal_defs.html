sandal

(ˈsændəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a light shoe consisting of a sole held on the foot by thongs, straps, etc2. a strap passing over the instep or around the ankle to keep a low shoe on the foot

Derived Forms

sandalled (ˈsandalled) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Latin sandalium, from Greek sandalion a small sandal, from sandalon sandal

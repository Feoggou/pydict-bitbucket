cramped

(kræmpt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. closed in; restricted2. (esp of handwriting) small and irregular; difficult to read

graded adjective & adjectiveA cramped room or building is not big enough for the people or things in it. ⇒ There are hundreds of families living in cramped conditions on the floor of the airport lounge. ⇒ In later years he lived in a rather cramped little flat in Bristol.

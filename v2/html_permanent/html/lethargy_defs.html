lethargy

(ˈlɛθədʒɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -gies1. sluggishness, slowness, or dullness2. an abnormal lack of energy, esp as the result of a disease
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lethargic (lɪˈθɑːdʒɪk ) or lethargical (leˈthargical) adjectivelethargically (leˈthargically) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Late Latin lēthargīa, from Greek lēthargos drowsy, from lēthē forgetfulness

degeneracy

(dɪˈdʒɛnərəsɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cies1. the act or state of being degenerate2. the process of becoming degenerate3. physics the number of degenerate quantum states of a particular orbital, degree of freedom, energy level, etc

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary

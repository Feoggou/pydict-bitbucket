unanimity for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(juːnənɪmɪti )
Definitions
uncount nounWhen there is unanimity among a group of people, they all agree about something or all vote for the same thing. ⇒ All decisions would require unanimity.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'unanimity'
The rumble ascended the scale, lost its unanimity , and broke into individual shouts of hatred or support. Aldiss, Brian SOMEWHERE EAST OF LIFE

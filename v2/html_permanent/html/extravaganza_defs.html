extravaganza

(ɪkˌstrævəˈɡænzə )
Definitions
noun1. an elaborately staged and costumed light entertainment2. any lavish or fanciful display, literary or other composition, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Italian: extravagance

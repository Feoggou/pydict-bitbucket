unreflecting

(ˌʌnrɪˈflɛktɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. not reflecting ⇒ The surface of the lake was calm but unreflecting, like the matte finish on a photograph.2. impulsive or unconsidered ⇒ She was preoccupied and unreflecting., ⇒ an unreflecting mental attitude

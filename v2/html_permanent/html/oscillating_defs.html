oscillating

(ˈɒsɪleɪtɪŋ)
Definitions
adjectivemoving repeatedly from one position to another and back again; vibrating ⇒ an oscillating motion, ⇒ the oscillating motion of the waves, ⇒ an oscillating signal of microwave frequency

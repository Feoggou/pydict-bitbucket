(sɪˈkluːdɪd )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. kept apart from the company of others ⇒ a secluded life2. sheltered; private

Derived Forms

secludedly (seˈcludedly) adverbsecludedness (seˈcludedness) noun

graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A secluded place is quiet and private. ⇒ We were tucked away in a secluded corner of the room. ⇒ We found a secluded beach a few miles further on.

unattached

(ˌʌnəˈtætʃt )
Definitions
adjective1. not connected with any specific thing, body, group, etc; independent2. not engaged or married3. (of property) not seized or held as security or in satisfaction of a judgment
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

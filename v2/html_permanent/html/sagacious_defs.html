sagacious

(səˈɡeɪʃəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having or showing sagacity; wise2. obsolete (of hounds) having an acute sense of smell

Derived Forms

sagaciously (saˈgaciously) adverbsagaciousness (saˈgaciousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin sagāx, from sāgīre to be astute

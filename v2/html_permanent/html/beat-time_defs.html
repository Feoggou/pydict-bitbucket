COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf you beat time to a piece of music, you move your hand or foot up and down in time with the music. A conductor beats time to show the choir or orchestra how fast they should sing or play the music. ⇒ He beats time with hands and feet. →  beat

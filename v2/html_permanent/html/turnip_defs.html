turnip

(ˈtɜːnɪp )
Definitions
noun1. a widely cultivated plant, Brassica rapa, of the Mediterranean region, with a large yellow or white edible root: family Brassicaceae (crucifers)2. the root of this plant, which is eaten as a vegetable3. any of several similar or related plants4. another name for kohlrabi
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from earlier turnepe, perhaps from turn (indicating its rounded shape) + nepe, from Latin nāpus turnip; see neep

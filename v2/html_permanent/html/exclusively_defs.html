(ɪkskluːsɪvli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [ADV with v]Exclusively is used to refer to situations or activities that involve only the thing or things mentioned, and nothing else. ⇒ ...an exclusively male domain. ⇒ Instruction in these subjects in undergraduate classes is almost exclusively by lecture.

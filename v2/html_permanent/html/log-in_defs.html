log in

Powered by COBUILD
or log on for learners of English
Definitions
phrasal verbWhen someone logs in or logs on, or logs into a computer system, they start using the system, usually by typing their name or identity code and a password. [V P] ⇒ Customers pay to log on and gossip with other users. [V P n] ⇒ They would log into their account and take a look at prices and decide what they'd like to do. →  log

optic

(ˈɒptɪk )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to the eye or vision2. a less common word for opticalnoun3. an informal word for eye1
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Medieval Latin opticus, from Greek optikos, from optos visible, seen; related to ōps eye

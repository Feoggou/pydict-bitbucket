trend

(trɛnd )
Definitions
noun1. general tendency or direction2. fashion; modeverb (intransitive)3. to take a certain trend4. to become widely discussed on a social media site
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English trendan to turn; related to Middle Low German trenden

trend

Powered by COBUILD
(trend ) for learners of English
Word forms: trends
Definitions
1. countable nounA trend is a change or development towards something new or different. ⇒ This is a growing trend. [+ towards] ⇒ ...a trend towards part-time employment. ⇒ ...the downward trend in gasoline prices.2. countable noun [usu sing]To set a trend means to do something that becomes accepted or fashionable, and that a lot of other people copy. ⇒ The record has already proved a success and may well start a trend.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If someone is composed, they are calm and able to control their feelings. ⇒ Laura was very calm and composed. ⇒ It wasn't the peaceful, composed experience I had expected.

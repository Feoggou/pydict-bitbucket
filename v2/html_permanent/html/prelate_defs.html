prelate

(ˈprɛlɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna Church dignitary of high rank, such as a cardinal, bishop, or abbot

Derived Forms

prelatic (prɪˈlætɪk ) or prelatical (preˈlatical) adjective

Word Origin

C13: from Old French prélat, from Church Latin praelātus, from Latin praeferre to hold in special esteem, prefer

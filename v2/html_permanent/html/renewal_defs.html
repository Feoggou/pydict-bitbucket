renewal

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪnjuːəl , -nuː-) for learners of English
Word forms: renewals
Definitions
1. singular nounIf there is a renewal of an activity or a situation, it starts again. [+ of] ⇒ They will discuss the possible renewal of diplomatic relations. ⇒ Is he really considering a renewal of hostilities at this stage?2. variable nounThe renewal of a document such as a licence or a contract is an official increase in the period of time for which it remains valid. ⇒ His contract came up for renewal. ⇒ The premium should be received in this office within fourteen days of the renewal date.3. uncount nounRenewal of something lost, dead, or destroyed is the process of it growing again or being replaced. ⇒ ...urban renewal and regeneration. ⇒ Now it is spring, a time of renewal.

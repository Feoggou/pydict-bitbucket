supper

(ˈsʌpə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an evening meal, esp a light one2. an evening social event featuring a supper3. See sing for one's supperverb4. (transitive) rare to give supper to5. (intransitive) rare to eat supper

Derived Forms

supperless (ˈsupperless) adjective

Word Origin

C13: from Old French soper; see sup1

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounSome people refer to the main meal eaten in the early part of the evening as supper. ⇒ Some guests like to dress for supper. → meal2. variable nounSupper is a simple meal eaten just before you go to bed at night. ⇒ She gives the children their supper, then puts them to bed.3. →  sing for your supper

littered

(ˈlɪtəd)
Definitions
adjective1. containing litter or debris; messy ⇒ The yard was littered with debris., ⇒ The garden is littered with rubbish., ⇒ The entrance hall is littered with toys and wellington boots., ⇒ His desk was littered with papers.2. abundant ⇒ Resorts are littered across the mountainsides., ⇒ The article is littered with mistakes., ⇒ His speeches are littered with references to law and order.

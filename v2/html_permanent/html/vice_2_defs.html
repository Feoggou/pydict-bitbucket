vice-

Powered by COBUILD
(vaɪs- ) for learners of English
Definitions
prefixVice- is used before a rank or title to indicate that someone is next in importance to the person who holds the rank or title mentioned. ⇒ ...America's vice-president. ⇒ Tim Munton becomes the new vice-captain.

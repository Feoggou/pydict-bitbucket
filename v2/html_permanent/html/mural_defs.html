mural

(ˈmjʊərəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a large painting or picture on a walladjective2. of or relating to a wall

Derived Forms

muralist (ˈmuralist) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin mūrālis, from mūrus wall

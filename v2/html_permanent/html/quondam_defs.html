quondam

(ˈkwɒndæm )
Definitions
adjective(prenominal) of an earlier time; former ⇒ her quondam lover
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin adv: formerly
Example sentences containing 'quondam'
Contrary to Patrick's quondam daydreams of the last year, the tone of outraged innocence in his voice fails to instantly convince.

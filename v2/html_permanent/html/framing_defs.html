framing

(ˈfreɪmɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. a frame, framework, or system of frames2. the way in which something is framed3. adjustment of the longitudinal position of the film in a projector gate to secure proper vertical positioning of the picture on the screen
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

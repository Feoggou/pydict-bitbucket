rickets

(ˈrɪkɪts )
Definitions
noun(functioning as singular or plural) pathology a disease mainly of children, characterized by softening of developing bone, and hence bow legs, malnutrition, and enlargement of the liver and spleen, caused by a deficiency of vitamin D
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: of unknown origin

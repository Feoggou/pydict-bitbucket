gregarious

(ɡrɪˈɡɛərɪəs )
Definitions
adjective1. enjoying the company of others2. (of animals) living together in herds or flocks. Compare solitary (sense 6)3. (of plants) growing close together but not in dense clusters4. of, relating to, or characteristic of crowds or communities
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gregariously (greˈgariously) adverbgregariousness (greˈgariousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin gregārius belonging to a flock, from grex flock

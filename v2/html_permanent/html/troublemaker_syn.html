Synonyms of troublemaker

troublemaker

noun

  1. mischief-maker, firebrand, instigator, agitator, bad apple (US, informal), rabble-rouser, agent provocateur (French), stirrer (informal), incendiary, rotten apple (British, informal), meddler, stormy petrel
    • powers to expel suspected troublemakers
    OPPOSITES: appeaser, arbitrator, peace-maker, conciliator, pacifier

QUOTATION:
Better to have him inside the tent pissing out, than outside pissing inLyndon B Johnson (of J Edgar Hoover)

richness

(ˈrɪtʃnɪs )
Definitions
noun1. the state or quality of being rich2. ecology the number of individuals of a species in a given area
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

It still seemed a cold room, for all the richness of the leather-bound books and the flowers.

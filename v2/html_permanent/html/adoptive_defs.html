adoptive

(əˈdɒptɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. acquired or related by adoption ⇒ an adoptive father2. of or relating to adoption. Compare adopted

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Someone's adoptive family is the family that adopted them. ⇒ He was brought up by adoptive parents in London. ⇒ ...her adoptive mother.2. adjective [ADJ n]Someone's adoptive country or city is the one that they choose for their home, although they were not born there. ⇒ They threatened to expel him from his adoptive country. ⇒ Galway in Ireland is my adoptive home.

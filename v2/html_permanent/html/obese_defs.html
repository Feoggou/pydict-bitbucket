obese

(əʊˈbiːs )
Definitions
adjectiveexcessively fat or fleshy; corpulent
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

obesity (oˈbesity) or obeseness (oˈbeseness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin obēsus, from ob- (intensive) + edere to eat

nothingness

(ˈnʌθɪŋnɪs )
Definitions
noun1. the state or condition of being nothing; nonexistence2. absence of consciousness or life3. complete insignificance or worthlessness4. something that is worthless or insignificant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions

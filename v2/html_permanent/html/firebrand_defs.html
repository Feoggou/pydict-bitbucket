firebrand

(ˈfaɪəˌbrænd )
Definitions
noun1. a piece of burning or glowing wood or other material2. a person who causes unrest or is very energetic
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

firebrand

(faɪəʳbrænd ) for learners of English
Word forms: firebrands
Definitions
countable nounIf you describe someone as a firebrand, especially someone who is very active in politics, you mean that they are always trying to make people take strong action. ⇒ ...his reputation as a young firebrand.

shin1

(ʃɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the front part of the lower leg2. the front edge of the tibia3. mainly British a cut of beef, the lower forelegverbWord forms: shins, shinning or shinned4. (when intr, often foll by up) to climb (a pole, tree, etc) by gripping with the hands or arms and the legs and hauling oneself up. Also (US): shinny5. (transitive) to kick (a person) in the shins

Word Origin

Old English scinu; related to Old High German scina needle, Norwegian dialect skina small disc

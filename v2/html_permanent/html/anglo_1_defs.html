Anglo

(ˈæŋɡləʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -glos1. US a White inhabitant of the United States who is not of Latin extraction2. Australian an Australian of Anglo-Celtic descent3. Canadian an English-speaking Canadian, esp one of Anglo-Celtic origin; an Anglo-Canadian
Example sentences containing 'Anglo'
`How, much later, the native Britons got invaded by the Vikings and then the Anglo Saxons. Harris, Elizabeth TIME OF THE WOLFBut here, on Ioligam, on I'itoi's sacred mountain, the Anglo God seemed far away and deaf besides. J. A. Jance HOUR OF THE HUNTER

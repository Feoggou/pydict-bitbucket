Synonyms of novice

novice

noun

  1. beginner, pupil, amateur, newcomer, trainee, apprentice, learner, neophyte, tyro, probationer, newbie (slang), proselyte
    • I'm a novice at these things. You're the professional.
    OPPOSITES:  teacher, professional, expert, master, ace, guru, boffin (British, informal), old hand, grandmaster, wonk (informal), maven, fundi (South Africa), doyen or (fem.) doyenne
  2. novitiate, convert
    • She had entered the monastery as a novice many months previously.

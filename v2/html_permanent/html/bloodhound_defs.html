bloodhound

(ˈblʌdˌhaʊnd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a large breed of hound having a smooth glossy coat of red, tan, or black and loose wrinkled skin on its head: formerly much used in tracking and police work2. informal a detective

Definitions

countable nounA bloodhound is a large dog with a very good sense of smell. Bloodhounds are often used to find people or other animals by following their scent.

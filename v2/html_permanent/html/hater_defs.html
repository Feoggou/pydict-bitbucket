hater

(ˈheɪtə )
Definitions
noun1. a. someone who hates a specified person or thing ⇒ a passionate hater of tyrannyb. (in combination) ⇒ a woman-hater, ⇒ a cop-hater2. informal, mainly US and Canadian a grudging or spiteful person, esp one who disparages others ⇒ Don't let the haters get you down

hater

Powered by COBUILD
(heɪtəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: haters
Definitions
countable nounIf you call someone a hater of something, you mean that they strongly dislike that thing. [+ of] ⇒ Braccio was a hater of idleness.Hater is also a combining form. ⇒ He was reputed to be a woman-hater. ⇒ A mustache-hater, he is said once to have made an employee shave before he got a raise.

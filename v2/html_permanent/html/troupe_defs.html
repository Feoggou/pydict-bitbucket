troupe

(truːp )
Definitions
noun1. a company of actors or other performers, esp one that travelsverb2. (intransitive) (esp of actors) to move or travel in a group
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from French; see troop

stamina1

(ˈstæmɪnə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounenduring energy, strength, and resilience

Derived Forms

staminal (ˈstaminal) adjective

Word Origin

C19: identical with stamina2 from Latin stāmen thread, hence the threads of life spun out by the Fates, hence energy, etc

(stæmɪnə )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounStamina is the physical or mental energy needed to do a tiring activity for a long time. ⇒ You have to have a lot of stamina to be a top-class dancer.
Example sentences containing 'stamina'
Desmond just doesn't have the emotional stamina to sustain a double-life with a blackmailer.

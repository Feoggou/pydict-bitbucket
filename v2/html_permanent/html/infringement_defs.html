Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnfrɪndʒmənt ) for learners of English
Word forms: infringements
Definitions
1. variable nounAn infringement is an action or situation that interferes with your rights and the freedom you are entitled to. [+ of] ⇒ ...infringement of privacy. [+ on] ⇒ They see it as an infringement on their own freedom of action.2. variable nounAn infringement of a law or rule is the act of breaking it or disobeying it. [+ of] ⇒ There might have been an infringement of the rules. ⇒ Infringement of the regulation is punishable by a fine.

talon

(ˈtælən )
Definitions
noun1. a sharply hooked claw, esp of a bird of prey2. anything resembling a bird's claw3. the part of a lock that the key presses on when it is turned4. cards the pile of cards left after the deal5. architecture another name for ogee6. stock exchange a printed slip attached to some bearer bonds to enable the holder to apply for a new sheet of coupons
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

taloned (ˈtaloned) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Old French: heel, from Latin tālus heel

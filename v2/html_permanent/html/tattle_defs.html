tattle

(ˈtætəl )
Definitions
verb1. (intransitive) to gossip about another's personal matters or secrets2. (transitive) to reveal by gossiping ⇒ to tattle a person's secrets3. (intransitive) to talk idly; chatnoun4. the act or an instance of tattling5. a scandalmonger or gossip
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15 (in the sense: to stammer, hesitate): from Middle Dutch tatelen to prate, of imitative origin

padre

(ˈpɑːdrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun informal (sometimes capital)1. father: used to address or refer to a clergyman, esp a priest2. a chaplain to the armed forces

Word Origin

via Spanish or Italian from Latin pater father

hairdresser

(ˈhɛəˌdrɛsə )
Definitions
noun1. a person whose business is cutting, curling, colouring, and arranging hair, esp that of women2. a hairdresser's establishment ▶ Related adjective: tonsorial
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

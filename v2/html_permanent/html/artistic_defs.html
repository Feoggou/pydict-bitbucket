artistic

(ɑːˈtɪstɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or characteristic of art or artists2. performed, made, or arranged decoratively and tastefully; aesthetically pleasing3. appreciative of and sensitive to beauty in art4. naturally gifted with creative skill
Derived forms
arˈtistically adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is artistic is good at drawing or painting, or arranging things in a beautiful way. ⇒ They encourage boys to be sensitive and artistic. ⇒ Mary's got it all so nice–you remember how artistic she always was with colors.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Artistic means relating to art or artists. ⇒ ...the campaign for artistic freedom. ⇒ ...their 1,300 year old artistic traditions.artistically (ɑːʳtɪstɪkli ) adverb [usu ADV adj/-ed] ⇒ ...artistically gifted children. ⇒ Artistically, the photographs are stunning.3. graded adjective & adjectiveAn artistic design or arrangement is beautiful. ⇒ ...an artistic arrangement of stone paving.artistically graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v, ADV -ed] ⇒ ...artistically carved garden ornaments. ⇒ ...vegetarian dishes which can be presented artistically.

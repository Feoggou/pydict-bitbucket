pellucid

(pɛˈluːsɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. transparent or translucent2. extremely clear in style and meaning; limpid

Derived Forms

pellucidly (pelˈlucidly) adverbˌpellucidity (ˌpelluˈcidity) or pellucidness (pelˈlucidness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin pellūcidus, variant of perlūcidus, from perlūcēre to shine through, from per through + lūcēre to shine

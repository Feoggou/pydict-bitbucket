jumbo

(ˈdʒʌmbəʊ )
Definitions
noun plural -bos1. informala. a very large person or thingb. (as modifier) ⇒ a jumbo box of detergent2. See jumbo jet
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: after the name of a famous elephant exhibited by P. T. Barnum, from Swahili jumbe chief

jumbo for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(dʒʌmboʊ )
Word forms: jumbos
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Jumbo means very large; used mainly in advertising and in the names of products. ⇒ ...a jumbo box of tissues. ⇒ ...grilled jumbo prawns.2. countable nounA jumbo or a jumbo jet is a very large jet aircraft that can carry several hundred passengers. Boeing 747 ⇒ ...a British Airways jumbo.

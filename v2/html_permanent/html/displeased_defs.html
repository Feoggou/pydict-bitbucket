Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ, ADJ to-inf]If you are displeased with something, you are annoyed or rather angry about it. [+ with/at] ⇒ Businessmen are displeased with erratic economic policy-making. ⇒ He was not displeased at the way he had handled the meeting.

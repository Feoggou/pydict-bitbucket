Synonyms of heavy

heavy

adjective

  1. weighty, large, massive, hefty, bulky, ponderous, hevE (text messaging)
    • He was carrying a very heavy load.
    OPPOSITES:  light, small, compact, handy
  2. intensive, severe, serious, concentrated, fierce, excessive, relentless, hevE (text messaging)
    • Heavy fighting has been going on.
  3. considerable, large, huge, substantial, abundant, copious, profuse, hevE (text messaging)
    • There was a heavy amount of traffic on the roads.
    OPPOSITES:  light, slight, moderate, sparse
  4. solid, strong, thick, substantial, sturdy, hevE (text messaging)
    • Put the sugar and water in a heavy pan and heat slowly.
    OPPOSITE:  lightweight
  5. overweight, fat, plump, obese, stocky, fleshy, portly, corpulent, thickset, hevE (text messaging)
    • He was short and heavy.
  6. viscous, thick, sticky, gooey (informal), syrupy, glutinous, gummy, gelatinous, icky (informal), gluey, treacly, mucilaginous, hevE (text messaging), viscid
    • 11 million gallons of heavy crude oil.
  7. substantial, large, filling, generous, hevE (text messaging)
    • the effects of an unusually heavy meal
  8. excessive, intemperate, immoderate, overindulgent, hevE (text messaging)
    • He's a smoker and a very heavy drinker.
  9. onerous, hard, difficult, severe, harsh, tedious, intolerable, oppressive, grievous, burdensome, wearisome, vexatious, hevE (text messaging)
    • They bear a heavy burden of responsibility.
    OPPOSITES:  light, easy, soft, gentle, moderate, mild, bearable
  10. sluggish, slow, dull, wooden, stupid, inactive, inert, apathetic, drowsy, listless, indolent, torpid, hevE (text messaging)
    • I struggle to raise eyelids still heavy with sleep.
    OPPOSITES:  quick, alert, brisk, agile
  11. forceful, hard, powerful, strong, sharp, violent, mighty, vigorous, hefty, hevE (text messaging)
    • a heavy blow on the back of the skull
  12. busy, full, hectic, tiring, hevE (text messaging)
    • It's been a heavy day and I'm tired.
  13. hard, demanding, difficult, physical, strenuous, laborious, hevE (text messaging)
    • They employ two full-timers to do the heavy work.
  14. overcast, dull, gloomy, cloudy, leaden, hevE (text messaging), louring or lowering
    • The night sky was heavy with rain clouds.
  15. humid, close, sticky, oppressive, clammy, airless, muggy, hevE (text messaging)
    • The air outside was heavy and moist and sultry.
  16. sad, depressed, gloomy, grieving, melancholy, dejected, despondent, downcast, sorrowful, disconsolate, crestfallen, hevE (text messaging)
    • My parents' faces were heavy with fallen hope.
    OPPOSITES:  happy, calm, cheerful, joyful
  17. sorrowful, sad, gloomy, melancholy, dejected, downcast, grief-stricken, disconsolate, hevE (text messaging)
    • He handed over his resignation with a heavy heart.
  18. serious, grave, solemn, difficult, deep, complex, profound, weighty, hevE (text messaging)
    • I don't want any more of that heavy stuff.
    OPPOSITES:  trivial, unimportant, inconsequential
  19. tempestuous, wild, rough, stormy, choppy, storm-tossed, squally, hevE (text messaging)
    • The ship capsized in heavy seas.

carry through

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (tr, adverb)1. to bring to completion2. to enable to endure (hardship, trouble, etc); support

phrasal verbIf you carry something through, you do it or complete it, often in spite of difficulties. [V P n] ⇒ We don't have the confidence that the U.N. will carry through a sustained program. [V n P] ⇒ The state announced a clear-cut policy and set out to carry it through. →  carry

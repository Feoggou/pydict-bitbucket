hoary

(ˈhɔːrɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: hoarier or hoariest1. having grey or white hair2. white or whitish-grey in colour3. ancient or venerable
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hoarily (ˈhoarily) adverbhoariness (ˈhoariness) noun

(hɔːri ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a problem or subject as hoary, you mean that it is old and familiar. ⇒ ...the hoary old myth that women are unpredictable.

rennet

(ˈrɛnɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a. the membrane lining the fourth stomach (abomasum) of a young calfb. the stomach of certain other young animals2. a substance, containing the enzyme rennin, prepared esp from the stomachs of calves and used for curdling milk in making cheese and junket
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: related to Old English gerinnan to curdle, run

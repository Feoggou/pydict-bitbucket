evil eye

Definitions
noun the evil eye1. a look or glance superstitiously supposed to have the power of inflicting harm or injury2. the power to inflict harm, etc, by such a look

Definitions
1. singular nounSome people believe that the evil eye is a magical power to cast a spell on someone or something by looking at them, so that bad things happen to them.2. singular noun [usu the]If someone gives you the evil eye, they look at you in an unpleasant way, usually because they dislike you or are jealous of you. ⇒ When we go out, girls are always giving me the evil eye.

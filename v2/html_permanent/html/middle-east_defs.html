Middle East

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (loosely) the area around the E Mediterranean, esp Israel and the Arab countries from Turkey to North Africa and eastwards to Iran2. (formerly) the area extending from the Tigris and Euphrates to Myanmar

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
proper nounThe Middle East is the area around the eastern Mediterranean that includes Iran and all the countries in Asia to the west and south-west of Iran. ⇒ The two great rivers of the Middle East rise in the mountains of Turkey.

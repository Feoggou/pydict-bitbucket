Powered by COBUILD
(rɪzentmənt ) for learners of English
Word forms: resentments
Definitions
uncount nounResentment is bitterness and anger that someone feels about something. [+ at] ⇒ She expressed resentment at being interviewed by a social worker. ⇒ But the problems of inflation and unemployment still cause a lot of resentment.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

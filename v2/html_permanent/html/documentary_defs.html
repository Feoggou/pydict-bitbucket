documentary

(ˌdɒkjʊˈmɛntərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. Also: documental consisting of, derived from, or relating to documents2. presenting factual material with little or no fictional additions ⇒ the book gives a documentary account of the warnoun plural -ries3. a factual film or television programme about an event, person, etc, presenting the facts with little or no fiction
Derived forms
ˌdocumentarily (ˌdocuˈmentarily) adverb

(dɒkjəmentri )
Word forms: documentaries
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA documentary is a television or radio programme, or a film, which shows real events or provides information about a particular subject. ⇒ ...a TV documentary on homelessness.2. adjective [ADJ n]Documentary evidence consists of things that are written down. ⇒ We have documentary evidence that they were planning military action.
Example sentences containing 'documentary'

Maryland

(ˈmɛərɪˌlænd , ˈmɛrɪlənd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna state of the eastern US, on the Atlantic: divided into two unequal parts by Chesapeake Bay: mostly low-lying, with the Alleghenies in the northwest Capital: Annapolis. Pop: 5 508 909 (2003 est). Area: 31 864 sq km (12 303 sq miles). Abbreviation: Md or (with zip code) MD

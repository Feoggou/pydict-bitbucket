conjunct

(kənˈdʒʌŋkt , ˈkɒndʒʌŋkt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. joined; united2. music relating to or denoting two adjacent degrees of a scalenoun3. logic one of the propositions or formulas in a conjunction
Derived forms
conjunctly (conˈjunctly) adverb
Word origin
C15: from Latin conjunctus, from conjugere to unite; see conjoin

detonation

(ˌdɛtəˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an explosion or the act of exploding2. the spontaneous combustion in an internal-combustion engine of part of the mixture before it has been reached by the flame front, causing the engine to knock3. physics rapid combustion, esp that occurring within a shock wave
Derived forms
detoˌnative (ˈdetoˌnative) adjective

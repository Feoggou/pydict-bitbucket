frankincense

(ˈfræŋkɪnˌsɛns )
Definitions
nounan aromatic gum resin obtained from trees of the burseraceous genus Boswellia, which occur in Asia and Africa. Also called: olibanum
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French franc free, pure + encens incense1; see frank

demy

(dɪˈmaɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -mies1. a. a size of printing paper, 171⁄2 by 221⁄2 inches (444.5 × 571.5 mm)b. a size of writing paper, 151⁄2 by 20 inches (Brit) (393.7 × 508 mm) or 16 by 21 inches (US) (406.4 × 533.4 mm)2. either one of two book sizes, 81⁄2 by 51⁄2 inches (demy octavo) or (chiefly Brit) 113⁄4 by 85⁄8 inches (demy quarto)
Word origin
C16: see demi-

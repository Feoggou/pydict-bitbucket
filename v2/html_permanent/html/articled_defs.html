Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]In Britain, someone who is articled to a firm of lawyers or accountants is employed by the firm and is training to become qualified. [+ to] ⇒ He was initially articled to a solicitor. ⇒ ...an articled clerk.

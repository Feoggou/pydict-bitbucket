impressed

(ɪmˈprɛst)
Definitions
adjectivehaving had an impression made on oneself; experiencing a strong, lasting, or favourable effect ⇒ I'm very impressed!, ⇒ I was very impressed by one young man at my lectures., ⇒ I'm very impressed with the new airport.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

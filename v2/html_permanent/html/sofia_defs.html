Sofia

(ˈsəʊfɪə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nounthe capital of Bulgaria, in the west: colonized by the Romans in 29 ad; became capital of Bulgaria in 1879; university (1880). Pop: 1 045 000 (2005 est). Ancient name: Serdica. Bulgarian name: Sofiya (ˈsɔfiˌja )

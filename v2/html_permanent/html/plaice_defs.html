plaice

(pleɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural plaice or plaices1. a European flatfish, Pleuronectes platessa, having an oval brown body marked with red or orange spots and valued as a food fish: family Pleuronectidae2. US and Canadian any of various other fishes of the family Pleuronectidae, esp Hippoglossoides platessoides

Word Origin

C13: from Old French plaïz, from Late Latin platessa flatfish, from Greek platus flat

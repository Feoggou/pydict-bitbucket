Fuji

(ˈfudʒi ) in American English
Definitions
Mountdormant volcano on Honshu island, Japan, southwest of Tokyo: highest peak in Japan: 12,388 ft (3,776 m): also ˌFujiˈyama (ˌfudʒiˈjɑmə ), ˌFujiˈsan (ˌFudʒiˈsɑn)

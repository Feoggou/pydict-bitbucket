come back

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. phrasal verbIf something that you had forgotten comes back to you, you remember it. [V P + to] ⇒ He was also an MP–I'll think of his name in a moment when it comes back to me. [V P] ⇒ When I thought about it, it all came back.2. phrasal verbWhen something comes back, it becomes fashionable again. [V P] ⇒ I'm glad hats are coming back.3. → see also comeback →  come
Example sentences containing 'come back'
From all accounts, Linton had given up a very promising career in Atlanta to come back to Grant. Karin Slaughter BLINDSIGHTED

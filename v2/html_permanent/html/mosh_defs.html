mosh

(mɒʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a type of dance, performed to loud rock music, in which people throw themselves about in a frantic and violent mannerverb2. (intransitive) to dance in this manner

Word Origin

C20: of uncertain origin

(mɒʃ )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf people at a rock concert mosh, they jump up and down together in front of the stage, often pushing each other. [V] ⇒ Moshing down the front, crushed against the stage in a sweat-drenched T-shirt is all part of the gig experience.

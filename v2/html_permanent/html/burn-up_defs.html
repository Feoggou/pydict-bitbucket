Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. ergative phrasal verb & phrasal verbIf something burns up or if fire burns it up, it is completely destroyed by fire or strong heat. [V P] ⇒ The satellite re-entered the atmosphere and burned up. [V P n] ⇒ Fires have burned up 180,000 acres of timber. [Also V n P]2. phrasal verbIf something burns up fuel or energy, it uses it. [V P n] ⇒ Brisk walking burns up more calories than slow jogging.

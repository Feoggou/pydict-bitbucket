(əlɑːʳmɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is alarming makes you feel afraid or anxious that something unpleasant or dangerous might happen. ⇒ The disease has spread at an alarming rate. ⇒ ...the alarming increase in crime.alarmingly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ ...the alarmingly high rate of heart disease.

She set about easing her position without alarming the frightened girl at the other side of the bed.

affectionate

adjective

  1. fond, loving, kind, caring, warm, friendly, attached, devoted, tender, doting, warm-hearted • They seemed devoted to each other and were openly affectionate. OPPOSITES:  cold, cool, indifferent, stony, glacial, uncaring, unresponsive, unfeeling, undemonstrative

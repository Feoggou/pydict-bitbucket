Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is satisfying makes you feel happy, especially because you feel you have achieved something. ⇒ I found wood carving satisfying.satisfyingly graded adverb [usu ADV adj] ⇒ ...a series of satisfyingly detailed and painstakingly constructed documentaries.

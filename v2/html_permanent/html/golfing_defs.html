golfing

(ˈɡɒlfɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the activity of playing golf ⇒ You can play tennis or go golfing.adjective2. relating to, involving, or used while playing golf ⇒ He was wearing a cream silk shirt and a tartan golfing cap., ⇒ a golfing holiday in Spain

(gɒlfɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Golfing is used to describe things that involve the playing of golf or that are used while playing golf. ⇒ He was wearing a cream silk shirt and a tartan golfing cap. ⇒ ...a golfing holiday in Spain.2. uncount nounGolfing is the activity of playing golf. ⇒ You can play tennis or go golfing.

measured

(ˈmɛʒəd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. determined by measurement2. slow, stately, or leisurely3. carefully considered; deliberate

Derived Forms

measuredly (ˈmeasuredly) adverbmeasuredness (ˈmeasuredness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]You use measured to describe something that is careful and deliberate. ⇒ The men spoke in soft, measured tones. ⇒ Her more measured response will appeal to voters. ⇒ They have to proceed at a measured pace.

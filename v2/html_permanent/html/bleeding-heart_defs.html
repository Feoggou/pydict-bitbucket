bleeding heart

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of several plants of the genus Dicentra, esp the widely cultivated Japanese species D. spectabilis, which has finely divided leaves and heart-shaped nodding pink flowers: family Fumariaceae2. informala. a person who is excessively softheartedb. (as modifier) ⇒ a bleeding-heart liberal

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [oft N n]If you describe someone as a bleeding heart, you are criticizing them for being sympathetic towards people who are poor and suffering, without doing anything practical to help. [disapproval] ⇒ I'm not a bleeding heart liberal.

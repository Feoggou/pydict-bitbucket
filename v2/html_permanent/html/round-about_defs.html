round about

(raʊnd əˈbaʊt)
Definitions
adverb1. on all sides ⇒ The sheltering trees round about rustled as the wind picked up., ⇒ What do the people round about think?2. British informal approximately ⇒ The major heart surgery commenced at round about a quarter to eleven.preposition3. on all sides of ⇒ The flaming fire was round about him.4. British informal approximately ⇒ Round about nine o'clock they ran out of money.

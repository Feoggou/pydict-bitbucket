manners

(ˈmænəz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. social conduct ⇒ he has the manners of a pig2. a socially acceptable way of behaving
Example sentences containing 'manners'
The watchman suddenly ran over to where Abraham Sinclair's head steward was standing, the fellow with the manners of an Irishman. Heller, Keith MAN'S LOVING FAMILYHe bowed slightly, the familiarity of instilled manners providing him with a space to gather himself. Val McDermid THE LAST TEMPTATION

  • British English: manners /ˈmænəz/ NOUN
    If someone has good manners, they are polite and observe social customs. If someone has bad manners, they are impolite and do not observe these customs.He dressed well and had impeccable manners.

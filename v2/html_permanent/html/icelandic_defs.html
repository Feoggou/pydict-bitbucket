Icelandic

(aɪsˈlændɪk )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Iceland, its people, or their languagenoun2. the official language of Iceland, belonging to the North Germanic branch of the Indo-European family. See also Old Icelandic
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

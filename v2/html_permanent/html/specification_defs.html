specification

(ˌspɛsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or an instance of specifying2. (in patent law) a written statement accompanying an application for a patent that describes the nature of an invention3. a detailed description of the criteria for the constituents, construction, appearance, performance, etc, of a material, apparatus, etc, or of the standard of workmanship required in its manufacture4. an item, detail, etc, specified

golf ball

Definitions
noun1. a small resilient, usually white, ball of either two-piece or three-piece construction, the former consisting of a solid inner core with a thick covering of toughened material, the latter consisting of a liquid centre, rubber-wound core, and a thin layer of balata2. (in some electric typewriters) a small detachable metal sphere, around the surface of which type characters are arranged

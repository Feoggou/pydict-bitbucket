merger

(ˈmɜːdʒə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. business the combination of two or more companies, either by the creation of a new organization or by absorption by one of the others. Often called (Brit): amalgamation2. law the extinguishment of an estate, interest, contract, right, offence, etc, by its absorption into a greater one3. the act of merging or the state of being merged

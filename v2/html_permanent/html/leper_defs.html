leper

(ˈlɛpə )
Definitions
noun1. a person who has leprosy2. derogatory a person who is ignored or despised▶ USAGE Rather than talking about a leper or lepers, it is better to talk about a person with leprosy and people with leprosy
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: via Late Latin from Greek lepra, noun use of lepros scaly, from lepein to peel

as ... as

Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
a correlative construction used to indicate the equality or sameness of two things ⇒ as large as, as heavy as, as many as, etc.. see also good, well2, much, far

    You use the structure as ... as when you are comparing things, or emphasizing how large or small something is.I never went through a final exam that was as difficult as that one.

garner

(ˈɡɑːnə )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to gather or store in or as if in a granarynoun2. an archaic word for granary3. archaic a place for storage or safekeeping
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C12: from Old French gernier granary, from Latin grānārium, from grānum grain

Garner

(ˈɡɑːnə )
Definitions
noun1.  Erroll. 1921–77, US jazz pianist and composer, noted for the jazz standard 'Misty' (1954)2.  Helen. born 1942. Australian novelist and journalist. Her books include the novels Monkey Grip (1977), The Idea of Perfection (2002), The Children's Bach (1984), and The Spare Room (2008), and the nonfiction The First Stone (1995)

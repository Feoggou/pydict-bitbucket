hight

(haɪt )
Definitions
verb(tr; used only as a past tense in the passive or as a past participle) archaic, poetic to name; call ⇒ a maid hight Mary
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English heht, from hatan to call; related to Old Norse heita, Old Frisian hēta, Old High German heizzan

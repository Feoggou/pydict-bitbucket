Minorca

(mɪˈnɔːkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an island in the W Mediterranean, northeast of Majorca: the second largest of the Balearic Islands. Chief town: Mahón. Pop: 78 796 (2002 est). Area: 702 sq km (271 sq miles). Spanish name: Menorca2. a breed of light domestic fowl with glossy white, black, or blue plumage

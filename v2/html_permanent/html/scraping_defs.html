scraping

(ˈskreɪpɪŋ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of scraping2. a sound produced by scraping3. (often plural) something scraped off, together, or up; a small amount
Example sentences containing 'scraping'
She pressed a little harder, scraping the skin with her nail. Melinda Metz BETRAYED

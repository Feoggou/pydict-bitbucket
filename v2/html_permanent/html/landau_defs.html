landau

(ˈlændɔː )
Definitions
nouna four-wheeled carriage, usually horse-drawn, with two folding hoods that meet over the middle of the passenger compartment
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: named after Landau (a town in Bavaria), where it was first made

Landau - Russian

( lanˈdau)
Definitions
noun Lev Davidovich (ljɛf daˈvidəvitʃ). 1908–68, Soviet physicist, noted for his researches on quantum theory and his work on the theories of solids and liquids: Nobel prize for physics 1962

rematch

Powered by COBUILD
(riːmætʃ ) for learners of English
Word forms: rematches
Definitions
1. countable nounA rematch is a second game that is played between two people or teams, for example because their first match was a draw or because there was a dispute about some aspect of it. [mainly British] ⇒ Duff said he would be demanding a rematch.2. countable nounA rematch is a second game or contest between two people or teams who have already faced each other. [mainly US] ⇒ Stanford will face UCLA in a rematch. ⇒ The debate this week was a rematch of last year's.regional note:   in BRIT, usually use return match

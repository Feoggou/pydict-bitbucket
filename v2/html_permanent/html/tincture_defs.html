tincture

(ˈtɪŋktʃə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. pharmacology a medicinal extract in a solution of alcohol2. a tint, colour, or tinge3. a slight flavour, aroma, or trace4. any one of the colours or either of the metals used on heraldic arms5. obsolete a dye or pigmentverb6. (transitive) to give a tint or colour to

Word Origin

C14: from Latin tinctūra a dyeing, from tingere to dye

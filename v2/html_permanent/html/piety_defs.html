piety

(ˈpaɪɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. dutiful devotion to God and observance of religious principles2. the quality or characteristic of being pious3. a pious action, saying, etc4. rare devotion and obedience to parents or superiors

Word Origin

C13 piete, from Old French, from Latin pietās piety, dutifulness, from pius pious

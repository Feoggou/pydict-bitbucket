wherefore

or wherefor (ˈwɛəˌfɔː )
Definitions
noun1. (usually plural) an explanation or reason (esp in the phrase the whys and wherefores) ⇒ He prefers not to discuss the whys and wherefores of what exactly happened.adverb2. archaic for what reason? why?sentence connector3. archaic or formal for which reason: used as an introductory word in legal preambles

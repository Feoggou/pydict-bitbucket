brooding

Powered by COBUILD
(bruːdɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Brooding is used to describe an atmosphere or feeling that makes you feel anxious or slightly afraid. [literary] ⇒ The same heavy, brooding silence descended on them.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If someone's expression or appearance is brooding, they look as if they are thinking deeply and seriously about something, especially something that is making them unhappy. [literary] ⇒ She kissed him and gazed into his dark, brooding eyes.

detrimental

(ˌdɛtrɪˈmɛntəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(when postpositive, foll by to) harmful; injurious; prejudicial ⇒ smoking can be detrimental to health
Derived forms
ˌdetrimentally (ˌdetriˈmentally) adverb

detrimental

Powered by COBUILD
(detrɪmentəl )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is detrimental to something else has a harmful or damaging effect on it. [+ to] ⇒ ...foods suspected of being detrimental to health. ⇒ The government's policy of high interest rates is having a detrimental effect on industry.

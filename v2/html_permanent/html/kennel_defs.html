kennel1

(ˈkɛnəl )
Definitions
noun1. a hutlike shelter for a dog. US name: doghouse2. (usually plural) an establishment where dogs are bred, trained, boarded, etc3. the lair of a fox or other animal4. a ramshackle house; hovel5. a pack of houndsverb -nels, -nelling, -nelled or US -nels, -neling or -neled6. to put or go into a kennel; keep or stay in a kennel
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French chenil, from Vulgar Latin canīle (unattested), from Latin canis dog

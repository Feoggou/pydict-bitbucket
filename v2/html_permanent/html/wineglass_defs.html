wineglass

(ˈwaɪnˌɡlɑːs )
Definitions
noun1. a glass drinking vessel, typically having a small bowl on a stem, with a flared foot2. Also called: wineglassful the amount that such a glass will hold
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'wineglass'
`It's a wonderful show," she said, burying her face in a wineglass. Maupin, Armistead TALES OF THE CITY

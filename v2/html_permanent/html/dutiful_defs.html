dutiful

(ˈdjuːtɪfʊl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. exhibiting or having a sense of duty2. characterized by or resulting from a sense of duty ⇒ a dutiful answer
Derived forms
dutifully (ˈdutifully) adverbdutifulness (ˈdutifulness) noun

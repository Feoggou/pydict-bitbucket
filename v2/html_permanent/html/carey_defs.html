(ˈkɛərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  George (Leonard). born 1935, Archbishop of Canterbury (1991–2002)2.  Peter. born 1943, Australian novelist and writer; his novels include the Booker Prize-winning Oscar and Lucinda (1988) and True History of the Kelly Gang (2001)3.  William. 1761–1834, British orientalist and pioneer Baptist missionary in India

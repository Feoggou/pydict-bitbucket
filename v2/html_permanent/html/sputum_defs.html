sputum

(ˈspjuːtəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ta (-tə)1. a mass of salivary matter ejected from the mouth2. saliva ejected from the mouth mixed with mucus or pus exuded from the respiratory passages, as in bronchitis or bronchiectasis

Word Origin

C17: from Latin: spittle, from spuere to spit out

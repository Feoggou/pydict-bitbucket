Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Competing ideas, requirements, or interests cannot all be right or satisfied at the same time. ⇒ They talked about the competing theories of the origin of life. ⇒ ...the competing interests of beach development and sea turtle protection. ⇒ ...the competing demands of work and family.2. → see also compete

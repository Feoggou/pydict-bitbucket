discontented

(dɪskəntentɪd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are discontented, you are not satisfied with your situation. [+ with] ⇒ The government tried to appease discontented workers. ⇒ ...farmers discontented with low prices for their produce.

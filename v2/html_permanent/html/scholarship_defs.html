scholarship

(ˈskɒləʃɪp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. academic achievement; erudition; learning2. a. financial aid provided for a scholar because of academic meritb. the position of a student who gains this financial aidc. (as modifier) ⇒ a scholarship student3. the qualities of a scholar

Powered by COBUILD
(skɒləʳʃɪp )
Word forms: scholarships
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounIf you get a scholarship to a school or university, your studies are paid for by the school or university or by some other organization. [+ to] ⇒ He got a scholarship to the Pratt Institute of Art. ⇒ ...scholarships for women over 30.2. uncount nounScholarship is serious academic study and the knowledge that is obtained from it. ⇒ I want to take advantage of your lifetime of scholarship.

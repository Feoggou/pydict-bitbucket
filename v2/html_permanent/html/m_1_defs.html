Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounM is the thirteenth letter of the English alphabet.2. m is a written abbreviation for metres or metre. ⇒ The island is only 200m wide at its narrowest point.3. m is a written abbreviation for the number million. ⇒ Last year exports reached $150m in value. ⇒ ...500m tonnes of coal.4. M or m is a written abbreviation for words beginning with m, such as 'minutes', 'married', 'male', and 'masculine'.

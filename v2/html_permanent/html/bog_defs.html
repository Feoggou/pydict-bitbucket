bog

(bɒɡ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. wet spongy ground consisting of decomposing vegetation, which ultimately forms peat2. an area of such ground3. a place or thing that prevents or slows progress or improvement4. a slang word for lavatory (sense 1)5. Australian slang the act or an instance of defecating▷ See also bog down, bog in, bog off
Derived forms
boggy (ˈboggy) adjectivebogginess (ˈbogginess) noun
Word origin
C13: from Gaelic bogach swamp, from bog soft

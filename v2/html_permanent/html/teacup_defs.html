teacup

(ˈtiːˌkʌp )
Definitions
noun1. a cup out of which tea may be drunk, larger than a coffee cup2. Also called: teacupful the amount a teacup will hold, about four fluid ounces
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

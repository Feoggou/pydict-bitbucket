taps

(tæps )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (functioning as singular)1. mainly USa. (in army camps, etc) a signal given on a bugle, drum, etc, indicating that lights are to be put outb. any similar signal, as at a military funeral2. (in the Guide movement) a closing song sung at an evening camp fire or at the end of a meeting

Word Origin

C19: from tap1

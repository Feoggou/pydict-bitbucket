newel

or newell (ˈnjuːəl )
Definitions
noun1. the central pillar of a winding staircase, esp one that is made of stone2. short for newel post
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French nouel knob, from Medieval Latin nōdellus, diminutive of nōdus node

go wrong

Definitions
a. to turn out other than intendedb. to make a mistakec. (of a machine, etc) to cease to function properlyd. to go astray morally▷ See wrong

Definitions
phraseIf a situation goes wrong, it stops progressing in the way that you expected or intended, and becomes much worse. ⇒ It all went horribly wrong. ⇒ Nearly everything that could go wrong has gone wrong. →  wrong

Definitions
pronounno person; nobody▶ USAGE No-one and nobody are interchangeable, as are everyone and everybody, and someone and somebody.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

no one for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
also no-one
Definitions
negative indefinite pronoun & pronounNo one means not a single person, or not a single member of a particular group or set. ⇒ Everyone wants to be a hero, but no one wants to die. ⇒ No one can open mail except the person to whom it has been addressed.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

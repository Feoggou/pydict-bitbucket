due date

(djuː deɪt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the date when something is due to be paid ⇒ To avoid paying interest, pay in full by the due date every month.2. the date on which, according to medical calculations, a pregnant woman is most likely to give birth ⇒ I'd had a textbook pregnancy until three weeks before my due date, when I became very sick., ⇒ The due date for the baby is Aug. 14.

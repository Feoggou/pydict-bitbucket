vapid

(ˈvæpɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. bereft of strength, sharpness, flavour, etc; flat2. boring or dull; lifeless ⇒ vapid talk
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vapidity (vaˈpidity) nounvapidly (ˈvapidly) adverbvapidness (ˈvapidness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin vapidus; related to vappa tasteless or flat wine, and perhaps to vapor warmth

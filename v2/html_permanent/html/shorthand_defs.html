Definitions

nouna. a system of rapid handwriting employing simple strokes and other symbols to represent words or phrasesb. (as modifier) ⇒ a shorthand typist

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounShorthand is a quick way of writing and uses signs to represent words or syllables. Shorthand is used by secretaries and journalists to write down what someone is saying. OPPOSITE: longhand ⇒ Ben took notes in shorthand.2. uncount noun [oft a N]You can use shorthand to mean a quick or simple way of referring to something. ⇒ 'Third World' is an abstraction, a form of shorthand. ⇒ The fiction that 'he' is a neutral shorthand for 'he or she' is no longer acceptable to many.

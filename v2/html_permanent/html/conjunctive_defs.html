conjunctive

(kənˈdʒʌŋktɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. joining; connective2. joined3. of or relating to conjunctions or their use4. logic relating to, characterized by, or containing a conjunctionnoun5. a less common word for conjunction (sense 3)
Derived forms
conjunctively (conˈjunctively) adverb
Word origin
C15: from Late Latin conjunctīvus, from Latin conjungere to conjoin

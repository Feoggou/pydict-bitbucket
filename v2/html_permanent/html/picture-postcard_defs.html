picture postcard

(ˈpɪktʃə ˈpəʊstˌkɑːd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna card with a picture on the front, typically sent from a holiday ⇒ I promised to send them a picture postcard.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
language note:   The spelling picture-postcard is also used for meaning [sense 2].1. countable nounA picture postcard is a postcard with a photograph of a place on it. People often buy picture postcards of places they visit when on holiday.2. adjective [ADJ n]You can use picture postcard to describe a place that is very attractive. ⇒ ...picture-postcard Normandy villages.

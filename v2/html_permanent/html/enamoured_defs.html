enamoured

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnæməʳd ) for learners of English
Definitions
regional note:   in AM, use enamored1. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are enamoured of something, you like or admire it a lot. If you are not enamoured of something, you dislike or disapprove of it. [literary] [+ of/with] ⇒ I became totally enamored of the wildflowers there. [+ of/with] ⇒ The religious conservatives are not enamoured of the West and its values.2. graded adjective [usu v-link ADJ, usu ADJ of/with n]If you are enamoured of a person, you are in love with them. [literary] ⇒ I remember being very young and being totally enamored of him.

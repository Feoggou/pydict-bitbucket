Word Frequency

stereotyped

(ˈstɛrɪəˌtaɪpt , ˈstɪər-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. lacking originality or individuality; conventional; trite2. reproduced from or on a stereotype printing plate
Example sentences containing 'stereotyped'
I myself don't like to make that kind of stereotyped statement. Lowry, Lois ANASTASIA KRUPNIK (3-IN-1)His face was stereotyped - handsome, classic, the hair formally curled. Erskine, Barbara MIDNIGHT IS A LONELY PLACEThe stereotyped old winos - maybe like your Altoona man - they stuck to themselves, too.

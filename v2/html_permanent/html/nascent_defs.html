nascent

(ˈnæsənt , ˈneɪ-)
Definitions
adjective1. starting to grow or develop; being born2. chemistry (of an element or simple compound, esp hydrogen) created within the reaction medium in the atomic form and having a high activity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

nascence (ˈnascence) or nascency (ˈnascency) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin nascēns present participle of nāscī to be born

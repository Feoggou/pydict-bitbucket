hexameter

(hɛkˈsæmɪtə )
Definitions
noun prosody1. a verse line consisting of six metrical feet2. (in Greek and Latin epic poetry) a verse line of six metrical feet, of which the first four are usually dactyls or spondees, the fifth almost always a dactyl, and the sixth a spondee or trochee
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hexametral (hexaˈmetral), hexametric (ˌhɛksəˈmɛtrɪk ) or hexametrical (ˌhexaˈmetrical) adjective

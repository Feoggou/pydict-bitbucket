inattention

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnətenʃən ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounA person's inattention is their lack of attention. ⇒ Vital evidence had been lost through a moment's inattention.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'inattention'
Claire switched off, depending on intermittent nods of agreement to disguise her inattention. Fraser, Anthea DEATH SPEAKS SOFTLYBut she was speaking again and they dared not risk any inattention. Brent-Dyer, Elinor CHALLENGE FOR THE CHALET SCHOOLHe had known too many occasions when vital evidence had been destroyed as a result of a moment's negligence or inattention.

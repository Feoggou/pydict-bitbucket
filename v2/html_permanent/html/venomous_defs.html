Powered by COBUILD

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a person or their behaviour as venomous, you mean that they show great bitterness and anger towards someone. ⇒ ...his terrifying and venomous Aunt Bridget. ⇒ He heaped abuse on Waite and made venomous personal attacks. ⇒ He was surprised by the venomous tone of the anonymous calls.venomously graded adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ 'You betrayed me first!' she answered venomously.2. adjectiveA venomous snake, spider, or other creature uses poison to attack other creatures. ⇒ The adder is Britain's only venomous snake.

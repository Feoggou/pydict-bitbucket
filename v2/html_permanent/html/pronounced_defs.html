pronounced

(prəˈnaʊnst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. strongly marked or indicated2. (of a sound) articulated with vibration of the vocal cords; voiced

Derived Forms

pronouncedly (prəˈnaʊnsɪdlɪ ) adverb

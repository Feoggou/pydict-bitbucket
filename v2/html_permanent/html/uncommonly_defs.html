uncommonly

(ʌnˈkɒmənlɪ )
Definitions
adverb1. in an uncommon or unusual manner or degree; rarely2. (intensifier) ⇒ you're uncommonly friendly
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

husk1

(hʌsk )
Definitions
noun1. the external green or membranous covering of certain fruits and seeds2. any worthless outer coveringverb3. (transitive) to remove the husk from
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

husker (ˈhusker) nounhusklike (ˈhuskˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C14: probably based on Middle Dutch huusken little house, from hūs house; related to Old English hosu husk, hūs house

closer

(ˈkloʊzər )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  someone or something that closes2.  a person adept at completing a business deal, an assignment, etc. successfully3.  Baseball a relief pitcher who specializes in pitching the final innings of a close game

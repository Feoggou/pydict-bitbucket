hung up

(hʌŋ ʌp)
Definitions
adjective1. See hung up on sb/sth2. informal impeded by some difficulty or delay ⇒ I got hung up at the station.3. informal in a state of confusion; emotionally disturbed ⇒ He was hung up.

Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If you say that someone is hung up about a particular person or thing, you are criticizing them for thinking or worrying too much about that person or thing. [informal, disapproval] [+ about/on] ⇒ It was a time when people weren't so hung-up about health. ⇒ Are you really that hung up on her?

quarters

(ˈkwɔːtəz )
Definitions
plural noun1. housing or accommodation, esp as provided for military personnel and their families2. the stations assigned to military personnel, esp to each crew member of a warship ⇒ general quarters3. (in India) housing provided by an employer or by the government4. (functioning as singular) military slang short for quartermaster

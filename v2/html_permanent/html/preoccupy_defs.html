preoccupy

(priːˈɒkjʊˌpaɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -pies, -pying or -pied (transitive)1. to engross the thoughts or mind of2. to occupy before or in advance of another

Word Origin

C16: from Latin praeoccupāre to capture in advance, from prae before + occupāre to seize, take possession of

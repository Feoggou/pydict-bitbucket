Synonyms of goods

goods

plural noun

  1. merchandise, stock, products, stuff, commodities, wares

  2. property, things, effects, gear, furniture, movables, possessions, furnishings, belongings, trappings, paraphernalia, chattels, appurtenances
    • You can give all your unwanted goods to charity.

    OPPOSITES:  terrible, inferior, lousy, bad quality
  3. proficient, able, skilled, capable, expert, talented, efficient, clever, accomplished, reliable, first-class, satisfactory, competent, thorough, tasty (British, informal), adept, first-rate, adroit, dexterous

  19. sunny, clear, fair, bright, calm, mild, clement, halcyon, balmy, cloudless, sunshiny

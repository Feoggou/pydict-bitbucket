Definitions

adjective1. of or relating to Angola or its inhabitantsnoun2. a native or inhabitant of Angola

Word forms: Angolans
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Angolan means belonging or relating to Angola or its people. ⇒ ...the Angolan government.2. countable nounAn Angolan is someone who comes from Angola. ⇒ Angolans are worrying about a return to war.

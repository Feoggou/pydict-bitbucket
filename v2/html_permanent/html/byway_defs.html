byway

(ˈbaɪˌweɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a secondary or side road, esp in the country2. an area, field of study, etc, that is very obscure or of secondary importance

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu pl]A byway is a small road which is not used by many cars or people. [+ of] ⇒ ...the highways and byways of America. ⇒ ...the narrow city byways.2. countable noun [usu pl]The byways of a subject are the less important or less well known areas of it. [+ of] ⇒ My research focuses on the byways of children's literature.

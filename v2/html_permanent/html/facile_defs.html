facile

(ˈfæsaɪl )
Definitions
adjective1. easy to perform or achieve2. working or moving easily or smoothly3. without depth; superficial ⇒ a facile solution4. archaic relaxed in manner; easygoing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

facilely (ˈfacilely) adverbfacileness (ˈfacileness) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin facilis easy, from facere to do

belter

(ˈbɛltə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun informal1. an event, person, quality, etc, that is admirable, outstanding, or thrilling ⇒ a real belter of a match2. a. a rousing or spirited popular song that is sung loudly and enthusiasticallyb. a person who sings popular songs in a loud and spirited manner

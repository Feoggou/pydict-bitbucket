inn

(ɪn )
Definitions
noun1. a pub or small hotel providing food and accommodation2. (formerly, in England) a college or hall of residence for students, esp of law, now only in the names of such institutions as the Inns of Court
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English; compare Old Norse inni inn, house, place of refuge

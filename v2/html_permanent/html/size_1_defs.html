size1

(saɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the dimensions, proportions, amount, or extent of something2. large or great dimensions, etc3. one of a series of graduated measurements, as of clothing ⇒ she takes size 4 shoes4. informal state of affairs as summarized ⇒ he's bankrupt, that's the size of itverb5. to sort according to size6. (transitive) to make or cut to a particular size or sizes▶ USAGE The use of -size and -sized after large or small is redundant, except when describing something which is made in specific sizes: a large (not large-size) organization. Similarly, in size is redundant in the expressions large in size and small in size

Derived Forms

sizer (ˈsizer) noun

Word Origin

C13: from Old French sise, shortened from assise assize

size2

(saɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: sizing a thin gelatinous mixture, made from glue, clay, or wax, that is used as a sealer or filler on paper, cloth, or plaster surfacesverb2. (transitive) to treat or coat (a surface) with size

Derived Forms

sizy (ˈsizy) adjective

Word Origin

C15: perhaps from Old French sise; see size1

size

Powered by COBUILD
(saɪz )
Word forms: sizes , sizing , sized
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounThe size of something is how big or small it is. Something's size is determined by comparing it to other things, counting it, or measuring it. [+ of] ⇒ Scientists have found the bones of a hoofed grazing animal about the size of a small horse. [+ of] ⇒ In 1970 the average size of a French farm was 19 hectares. ⇒ Iraq itself has oil reserves second in size only to Saudi Arabia's. ⇒ ...shelves containing books of various sizes.2. uncount nounThe size of something is the fact that it is very large. [+ of] ⇒ He knows the size of the task. ⇒ Jack walked around the hotel and was mesmerized by its sheer size.3. countable nounA size is one of a series of graded measurements, especially for things such as clothes or shoes. ⇒ My sister is the same height but only a size 12. ⇒ I tried them on and they were the right size.4. →  to cut someone down to size5. →  be cut to size6. →  for size7. → comes in all shapes and sizes► →  size up

lullaby

(ˈlʌləˌbaɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -bies1. a quiet song to lull a child to sleep2. the music for such a songverb -bies, -bying or -bied3. (transitive) to quiet or soothe with or as if with a lullaby
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: perhaps a blend of lull + goodbye

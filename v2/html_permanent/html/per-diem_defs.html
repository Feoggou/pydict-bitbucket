per diem

(ˈpɜː ˈdaɪɛm , ˈdiːɛm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb1. every day or by the daynoun2. a. an allowance for daily expenses, usually those incurred while workingb. (as modifier) ⇒ a per-diem allowance

Word Origin

from Latin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
singular noun [oft N n]A per diem is an amount of money that someone is given to cover their daily expenses while they are working. [mainly US] ⇒ He received a per diem allowance to cover his travel expenses.

recommended

(ˌrɛkəˈmɛndɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. officially suggested or advised ⇒ Three tablets a day was the recommended dose., ⇒ A study has shown that four out of ten mums-to-be have less than half the recommended intake of iodine., ⇒ The recommended daily dose is 12 to 24 grams., ⇒ the recommended price, ⇒ We're knocking £ 10 off the recommended price by offering it to readers for a bargain £ 8.992. commended by someone as good or useful ⇒ Though ten years old, this book is highly recommended.

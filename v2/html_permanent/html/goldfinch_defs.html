goldfinch

(ˈɡəʊldˌfɪntʃ )
Definitions
noun1. a common European finch, Carduelis carduelis, the adult of which has a red-and-white face and yellow-and-black wings2. any of several North American finches of the genus Spinus, esp the yellow-and-black species S. tristis

heavenly

(ˈhɛvənlɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. informal alluring, wonderful, or sublime2. of or occurring in space ⇒ a heavenly body3. divine; holy
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

heavenliness (ˈheavenliness) noun

heavenly

Powered by COBUILD
(hevənli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Heavenly things are things that are connected with the religious idea of heaven. ⇒ ...heavenly beings whose function it is to serve God.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is heavenly is very pleasant and enjoyable. [informal] ⇒ The idea of spending two weeks with him may seem heavenly.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

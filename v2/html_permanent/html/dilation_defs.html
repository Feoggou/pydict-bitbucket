dilation

(daɪˈleɪʃən ; dɪˈleɪʃən )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  a dilating or being dilated, as of the pupil of an eye, a blood vessel, or the cervix during childbirth. see also dilatation2.  a dilated part

arable

(ˈærəbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of land) being or capable of being tilled for the production of crops2. of, relating to, or using such land ⇒ arable farmingnoun3. arable land or farming
Word origin
C15: from Latin arābilis that can be ploughed, from arāre to plough

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Arable farming involves growing crops such as wheat and barley rather than keeping animals or growing fruit and vegetables. Arable land is land that is used for arable farming. ⇒ ...arable farmers. ⇒ ...arable crops.

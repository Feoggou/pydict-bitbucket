hilly

Powered by COBUILD
(hɪli ) for learners of English
Word forms: hillier , hilliest
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveA hilly area has many hills. ⇒ The areas where the fighting is taking place are hilly and densely wooded.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

May held it against her chest, which was too hilly to tell her anything useful. Richard Francis PROSPECT HILL`The graveyards... well, usually you find them in hilly areas, with water nearby. Trenhalle, John A MEANS TO EVILFrom the pillowed brow of a snow hilly , I glimpsed the summerland. Robin Hobb THE GOLDEN FOOL: BOOK TWO OF THE TAWNY MANThe world of Masters and Folk seemed very far away in these hilly parts. Robert Silverberg THE LONGEST WAY HOME

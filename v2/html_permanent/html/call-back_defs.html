call back

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrasal verbIf you call someone back, you telephone them again or in return for a telephone call that they have made to you. [V n P] ⇒ If we're not around she'll take a message and we'll call you back. [V P] ⇒ If you want further advice, you can call back and speak to the same adviser. [Also V P n (not pron)] →  call
Example sentences containing 'call back'
Thus his original message, requesting I call back without identifying me by name. Lawrence Block HOPE TO DIE

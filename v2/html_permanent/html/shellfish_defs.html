shellfish

(ˈʃɛlˌfɪʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -fish or -fishesany aquatic invertebrate having a shell or shell-like carapace, esp such an animal used as human food. Examples are crustaceans such as crabs and lobsters and molluscs such as oysters

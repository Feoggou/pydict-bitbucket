patriot

(ˈpeɪtrɪət , ˈpæt-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna person who vigorously supports his country and its way of life

Derived Forms

patriotic (ˌpætrɪˈɒtɪk ) adjectiveˌpatriotically (ˌpatriˈotically) adverb

Word Origin

C16: via French from Late Latin patriōta, from Greek patriotēs, from patris native land; related to Greek patēr father; compare Latin pater father, patria fatherland

forethought

(ˈfɔːˌθɔːt )
Definitions
noun1. advance consideration or deliberation2. thoughtful anticipation of future events
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

forethoughtful (foreˈthoughtful) adjectiveforethoughtfully (foreˈthoughtfully) adverbforethoughtfulness (foreˈthoughtfulness) noun

Definitions
uncount nounIf you act with forethought, you think carefully before you act about what will be needed, or about what the consequences will be. ⇒ With a little forethought many accidents could be avoided.

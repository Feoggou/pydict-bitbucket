chronometer

(krəˈnɒmɪtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna timepiece designed to be accurate in all conditions of temperature, pressure, etc, used esp at sea
Derived forms
chronometric (ˌkrɒnəˈmɛtrɪk ) or ˌchronometrical (ˌchronoˈmetrical) adjectiveˌchronometrically (ˌchronoˈmetrically) adverb

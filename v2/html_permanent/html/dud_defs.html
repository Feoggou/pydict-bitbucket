dud

(dʌd ) informal
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that proves ineffectual or a failure2. a shell, etc, that fails to explode3. (plural) old-fashioned clothes or other personal belongingsadjective4. failing in its purpose or function ⇒ a dud cheque
Word origin
C15 (in the sense: an article of clothing, a thing, used disparagingly): of unknown origin

Definitions

adjective [ADJ n]Dud means not working properly or not successful. [informal] ⇒ He replaced a dud valve.Dud is also a noun. ⇒ The mine was a dud.

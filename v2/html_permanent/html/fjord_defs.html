fjord

or fiord (fjɔːd )
Definitions
noun(esp on the coast of Norway) a long narrow inlet of the sea between high steep cliffs formed by glacial action
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Norwegian, from Old Norse fjörthr; see firth, ford

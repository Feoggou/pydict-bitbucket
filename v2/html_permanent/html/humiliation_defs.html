humiliation

Powered by COBUILD
(hjuːmɪlieɪʃən ) for learners of English
Word forms: humiliations
Definitions
1. uncount nounHumiliation is the embarrassment and shame you feel when someone makes you appear stupid, or when you make a mistake in public. ⇒ She faced the humiliation of discussing her husband's affair.2. countable nounA humiliation is an occasion or a situation in which you feel embarrassed and ashamed. ⇒ The result is a humiliation for the prime minister.

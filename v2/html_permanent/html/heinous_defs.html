Definitions

Word Origin

heinous

Powered by COBUILD
(heɪnəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something such as a crime as heinous, you mean that it is extremely evil or horrible. [formal] ⇒ Her life has been permanently blighted by his heinous crime. ⇒ They are capable of the most heinous acts.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

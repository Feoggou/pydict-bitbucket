fresh air

(frɛʃ ɛə)
Definitions
nounthe air outside, especially when considered good for you because it does not contain dirt or dangerous substances ⇒ You should go outside and get some fresh air., ⇒ 'Let's take the baby outside,' I suggested. 'We all need some fresh air.', ⇒ I need some fresh air., ⇒ Take exercise, preferably in the fresh air.

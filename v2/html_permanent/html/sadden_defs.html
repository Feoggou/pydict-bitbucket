verb [no cont]If something saddens you, it makes you feel sad. [V n] ⇒ The cruelty in the world saddens me incredibly.saddened graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ] ⇒ He was disappointed and saddened that legal argument had stopped the trial.saddening graded adjective & adjective ⇒ I find public squalor very saddening. ⇒ ...a saddening experience.

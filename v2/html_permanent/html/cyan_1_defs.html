cyan

(ˈsaɪæn , ˈsaɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a highly saturated green-blue that is the complementary colour of red and forms, with magenta and yellow, a set of primary coloursadjective2. of this colour ⇒ a cyan filter
Word origin
C19: from Greek kuanos dark blue

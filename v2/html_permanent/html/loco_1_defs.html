loco1

(ˈləʊkəʊ )
Definitions
nouninformal short for locomotive

loco2

(ˈləʊkəʊ )
Definitions
adjective1. slang, mainly US insane2. (of an animal) affected with loco diseasenoun plural -cos3. short for locoweedverb (transitive)4. to poison with locoweed5. US slang to make insane
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: via Mexican Spanish from Spanish: crazy

loco3

(ˈləʊkəʊ )
Definitions
adjectivedenoting a price for goods, esp goods to be exported, that are in a place specified or known, the buyer being responsible for all transport charges from that place ⇒ loco Bristol, ⇒ a loco price
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: from Latin locō from a place

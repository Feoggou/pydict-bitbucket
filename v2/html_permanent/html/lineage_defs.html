lineage1

(ˈlɪnɪɪdʒ )
Definitions
noun1. direct descent from an ancestor, esp a line of descendants from one ancestor2. a less common word for derivation
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French lignage, from Latin līnea line1

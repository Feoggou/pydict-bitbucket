outskirts

(ˈaʊtˌskɜːts )
Definitions
plural noun(sometimes singular) outlying or bordering areas, districts, etc, as of a city

outskirts for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(aʊtskɜːʳts )
Definitions
plural nounThe outskirts of a city or town are the parts of it that are farthest away from its centre. [+ of] ⇒ Hours later we reached the outskirts of New York.

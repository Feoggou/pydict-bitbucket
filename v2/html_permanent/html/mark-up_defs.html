mark-up

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a percentage or amount added to the cost of a commodity to provide the seller with a profit and to cover overheads, costs, etc2. a. an increase in the price of a commodityb. the amount of this increaseverb mark up (tr, adverb)3. to add a percentage for profit, overheads, etc, to the cost of (a commodity)4. to increase the price of

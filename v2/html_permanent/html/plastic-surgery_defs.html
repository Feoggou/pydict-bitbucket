plastic surgery

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe branch of surgery concerned with therapeutic or cosmetic repair or re-formation of missing, injured, or malformed tissues or parts. Also called: anaplasty

Derived Forms

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounPlastic surgery is the practice of performing operations to repair or replace skin which has been damaged, or to improve people's appearance. ⇒ She even had plastic surgery to change the shape of her nose.

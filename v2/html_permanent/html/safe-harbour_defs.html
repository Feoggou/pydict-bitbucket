safe harbour

(seɪf ˈhɑːbə) British or safe harbor US

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a place that offers protection from the weather, attack, etc2. a refuge or break from suffering, etc ⇒ her search for a safe harbour from her punishing life3. something that protects and allows something to flourish ⇒ those who protect and give safe harbour to terrorism4. law a legal statute that eliminates blame or liability of someone, as long as their actions were performed honestly5. another name for safe haven (sense 5)6. US another name for safe harbour

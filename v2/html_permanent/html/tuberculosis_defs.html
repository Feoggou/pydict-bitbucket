tuberculosis

(tjʊˌbɜːkjʊˈləʊsɪs )
Definitions
nouna communicable disease caused by infection with the tubercle bacillus, most frequently affecting the lungs (pulmonary tuberculosis). Also called: consumption, phthisis. Abbreviation: TB
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from New Latin; see tubercle, -osis

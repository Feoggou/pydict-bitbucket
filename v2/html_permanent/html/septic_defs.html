septic

(ˈsɛptɪk )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or caused by sepsis. Also (archaic): septical2. of, relating to, or caused by putrefactionnoun3. Australian and New Zealand informal short for septic tank

Derived Forms

septically (ˈseptically) adverbsepticity (sɛpˈtɪsɪtɪ ) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin sēpticus, from Greek sēptikos, from sēptos decayed, from sēpein to make rotten

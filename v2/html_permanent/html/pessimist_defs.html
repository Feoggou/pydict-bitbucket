pessimist

Powered by COBUILD
(pesɪmɪst )
Word forms: pessimists
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA pessimist is someone who thinks that bad things are going to happen. ⇒ I'm a natural pessimist; I usually expect the worst. ⇒ It looks as if the pessimists are being proved right.
Example sentences containing 'pessimist'
Maybe you were misrepresented, but I must say you seemed very much the professional pessimist. Clive Barker SACRAMENT

Similar Words

defeatist, cynic, melancholic, worrier, killjoy, prophet of doom, misanthrope, wet blanket (informal), gloom merchant (informal), doomster

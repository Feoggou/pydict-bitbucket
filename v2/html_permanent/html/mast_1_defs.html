mast1

(mɑːst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. nautical any vertical spar for supporting sails, rigging, flags, etc, above the deck of a vessel or any components of such a composite spar2. any sturdy upright pole used as a support3. Also called: captain's mast nautical a hearing conducted by the captain of a vessel into minor offences of the crew4. See before the mastverb5. (transitive) nautical to equip with a mast or masts

Derived Forms

mastless (ˈmastless) adjectivemastˌlike (ˈmastˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

Old English mæst; related to Middle Dutch mast and Latin mālus pole

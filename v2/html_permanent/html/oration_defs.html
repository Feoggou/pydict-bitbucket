oration

(ɔːˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a formal public declaration or speech2. any rhetorical, lengthy, or pompous speech3. an academic exercise or contest in public speaking
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin ōrātiō speech, harangue, from ōrāre to plead, pray

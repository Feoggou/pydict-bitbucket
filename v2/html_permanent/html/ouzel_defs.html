ouzel

or ousel (ˈuːzəl )
Definitions
noun1. the ring ouzel or water ouzel. See ring ouzel, dipper2. an archaic name for the (European) blackbird
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English ōsle, related to Old High German amsala (German Amsel), Latin merula merle1

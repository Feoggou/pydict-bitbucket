abbreviate

(əˈbriːvɪˌeɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to shorten (a word or phrase) by contraction or omission of some letters or words2. to shorten (a speech or piece of writing) by omitting sections, paraphrasing, etc3. to cut short
Derived forms
abˈbreviˌator noun
Word origin
C15: from the past participle of Late Latin abbreviāre, from Latin brevis brief

edging

(ˈɛdʒɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. anything placed along an edge to finish it, esp as an ornament, fringe, or border on clothing or along a path in a garden2. the act of making an edgeadjective3. relating to or used for making an edge ⇒ edging shears

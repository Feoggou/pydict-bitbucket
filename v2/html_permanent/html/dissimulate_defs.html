Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbWhen people dissimulate, they hide their true feelings, intentions, or nature. [formal] [V] ⇒ This man was too injured to dissimulate well. [V n] ⇒ They were decked out in tracksuits, seemingly to dissimulate their true function.

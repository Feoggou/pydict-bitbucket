last-born

(ˈlɑːstˌbɔːn)
Definitions
Australian and USadjective1. (of a child) last to be born in a family ⇒ The last-born children of a large family have the advantage of intellectual stimulation from older siblings.noun2. the last child to be born ⇒ His wife had given him six sons and she died giving birth to their last-born.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

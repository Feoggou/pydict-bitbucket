Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivefurnished or decorated with panels or flat sections ⇒ The panelled walls were covered with portraits., ⇒ The doors are panelled., ⇒ The kitchen has pine units with panelled doors., ⇒ a large, comfortable, panelled room, ⇒ The cheerful room was panelled in pine., ⇒ He glanced round the panelled room.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
regional note:   in AM, use paneled1. adjective [usu ADJ n]A panelled room has decorative wooden panels covering its walls. ⇒ ...a large, comfortable, panelled room. ⇒ The cheerful room was panelled in pine.-panelled combines with nouns to form adjectives that describe the way a room or wall is decorated or the way a door or window is made. ⇒ ...a wood-panelled dining room. ⇒ The walls are oak-panelled.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]A panelled wall, door, or window does not have a flat surface but has square or rectangular areas set into its surface. ⇒ The panelled walls were covered with portraits. ⇒ The doors are panelled.

coinage

(ˈkɔɪnɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. coins collectively2. the act of striking coins3. the currency of a country4. the act of inventing something, esp a word or phrase5. a newly invented word, phrase, usage, etc

coinage

Powered by COBUILD
(kɔɪnɪdʒ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounCoinage is the coins which are used in a country. ⇒ The city produced its own coinage from 1325 to 1864. ⇒ ...the world's finest collection of medieval European coinage.2. uncount nounCoinage is the system of money used in a country. ⇒ It took four years for Britain just to decimalise its own coinage.

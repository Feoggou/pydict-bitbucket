crafty

adjective

  1. cunning, scheming, sly, devious, knowing, designing, sharp, calculating, subtle, tricky, shrewd, astute, fraudulent, canny, wily, insidious, artful, foxy, deceitful, duplicitous, tricksy, guileful • That crafty old devil had taken us all for a ride. OPPOSITES:  open, simple, frank, innocent, ethical, naive, honest, candid, ingenuous, wet behind the ears, as green as grass

'crafty' in Other Languages

  • British English: crafty ADJECTIVE
    If you describe someone as crafty, you mean that they achieve what they want in a clever way, often by deceiving people....a crafty, lying character who enjoys plotting against others.

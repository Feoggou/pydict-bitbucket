preferred

(prɪˈfɜːd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveliked better or valued more highly; having preference ⇒ Her own preferred methods of exercise are hiking and long cycle rides., ⇒ I don't want to leave and my preferred option is to stay for another couple of years., ⇒ Mr Howard's rating as preferred prime minister fell by 5 percent., ⇒ Ipswich is the preferred site for an extension of the Queensland University campus.▶ USAGE The abbreviation for preferred is pf. or pref.

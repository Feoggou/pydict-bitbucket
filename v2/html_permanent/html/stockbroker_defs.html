stockbroker

(ˈstɒkˌbrəʊkə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna person who buys and sells securities on a commission basis for customers. Often shortened to: broker

Derived Forms

stockbrokerage (ˈstɒkˌbrəʊkərɪdʒ ) or stockˌbroking (ˈstockˌbroking) noun

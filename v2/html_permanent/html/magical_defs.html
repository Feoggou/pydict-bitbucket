Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveSomething that is magical seems to use magic or to be able to produce magic. ⇒ ...the story of Sin-Sin, a little boy who has magical powers.magically (mædʒɪkli ) adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ ...the story of a young boy's adventures after he is magically transported through the cinema screen.2. graded adjective & adjectiveYou can say that a place or object is magical when it has a special mysterious quality that makes it seem wonderful and exciting. ⇒ The beautiful island of Cyprus is a magical place to get married.

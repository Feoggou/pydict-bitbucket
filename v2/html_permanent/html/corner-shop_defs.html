(ˈkɔːnə ʃɒp) or corner store (ˈkɔːnə stɔː)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
USnouna small, general shop serving a small neighbourhood and typically situated on a street corner ⇒ You used to be able to pop out to the corner shop for a packet of cigarettes.

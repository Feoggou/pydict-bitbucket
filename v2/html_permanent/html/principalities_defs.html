principalities

(ˌprɪnsɪˈpælɪtɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun(often capital) the seventh of the nine orders into which the angels are divided in medieval angelology. Also called: princedoms
Example sentences containing 'principalities'
Previously, Nepal had been divided into small principalities on the lines of English and Scottish counties. Leasor, James TANK OF SERPENTS

undisguised for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌndɪsgaɪzd )
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone's feelings as undisguised, you mean that they show them openly and do not make any attempt to hide them. ⇒ ...undisguised glee. ⇒ Hean looked down at Bauer in undisguised disgust. ⇒ By mid-season the hostility between the two was undisguised.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

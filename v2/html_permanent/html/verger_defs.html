verger

(ˈvɜːdʒə )
Definitions
noun mainly Church of England1. a church official who acts as caretaker and attendant, looking after the interior of a church and often the vestments and church furnishings2. an official who carries the verge or rod of office before a bishop, dean, or other dignitary in ceremonies and processions
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French, from verge, from Latin virga rod, twig

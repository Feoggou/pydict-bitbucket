fire extinguisher

(faɪəʳ ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃə)
Definitions
nouna portable device for extinguishing fires, usually consisting of a canister with a directional nozzle used to direct a spray of water, chemically generated foam, inert gas, or fine powder onto the fire

remaining

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪmeɪnɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]The remaining things or people out of a group are the things or people that still exist, are still present, or have not yet been dealt with. ⇒ The three parties will meet next month to work out remaining differences. ⇒ The United States has withdrawn the remaining staff from its embassy. ⇒ Stir in the remaining ingredients.2. → See also remain

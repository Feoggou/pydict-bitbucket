tripartite

(traɪˈpɑːtaɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. divided into or composed of three parts2. involving three participants3. (esp of leaves) consisting of three parts formed by divisions extending almost to the base
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tripartitely (triˈpartitely) adverb

adjective [usu ADJ n]You can use tripartite to describe something that has three parts or that involves three groups of people. [formal] ⇒ ...tripartite meetings between Government ministers, trades union leaders and industrialists.

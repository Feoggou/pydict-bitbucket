toon1

(tuːn )
Definitions
noun1. a large meliaceous tree, Cedrela toona, of the East Indies and Australia, having clusters of flowers from which a dye is obtained2. the close-grained red wood of this tree, used for furniture, carvings, etc

Word Origin

from Hindi tūn, from Sanskrit tunna

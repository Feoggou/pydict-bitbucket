yesterday

(ˈjɛstədɪ , -ˌdeɪ)
Definitions
noun1. the day immediately preceding today2. (often plural) the recent pastadverb3. on or during the day before today4. in the recent past
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

yesterday

Powered by COBUILD
(jestəʳdeɪ , -di ) for learners of English
Word forms: yesterdays
Definitions
1. adverbYou use yesterday to refer to the day before today. ⇒ She left yesterday. ⇒ Yesterday she announced that she is quitting her job.Yesterday is also a noun. ⇒ In yesterday's games, Switzerland beat the United States two-one.2. uncount nounYou can refer to the past, especially the recent past, as yesterday. ⇒ The worker of today is different from the worker of yesterday. ⇒ ...a world without yesterdays or tomorrows.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

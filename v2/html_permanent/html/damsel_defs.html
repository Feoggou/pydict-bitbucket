damsel

(ˈdæmzəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounarchaic or poetic a young unmarried woman; maiden
Word origin
C13: from Old French damoisele, from Vulgar Latin domnicella (unattested) young lady, from Latin domina mistress; see dame

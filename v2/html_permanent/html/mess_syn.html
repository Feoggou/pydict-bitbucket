Synonyms of mess

mess

noun

  1. untidiness, disorder, confusion, chaos, turmoil, litter, clutter, disarray, jumble, disorganization, grot (slang), dirtiness
    • Linda can't stand mess.
  2. shambles, botch, hash, cock-up (British, slang), state, balls-up (taboo & slang), fuck-up (offensive & taboo & slang), bodge (informal), pig's breakfast (informal)
    • I've made such a mess of my life.
  3. difficulty, dilemma, plight, spot (informal), hole (informal), fix (informal), jam (informal), hot water (informal), stew (informal), mix-up, muddle, pickle (informal), uphill (South Africa), predicament, deep water, perplexity, tight spot, imbroglio, fine kettle of fish (informal)
    • I've got myself into a bit of a mess.

  • See mess with something

depository

(dɪˈpɒzɪtərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ries1. a store, such as a warehouse, for furniture, valuables, etc; repository2. a variant spelling of depositary (sense 1)
Word origin
C17 (in the sense: place of a deposit): from Medieval Latin dēpositōrium; C18 (in the sense: depositary): see deposit, -ory1

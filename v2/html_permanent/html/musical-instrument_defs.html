nounanything that is used to produce music ⇒ The drum is one of the oldest musical instruments.

Powered by COBUILD
Word forms: musical instruments
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA musical instrument is an object such as a piano, guitar, or violin which you play in order to produce music. ⇒ The drum is one of the oldest musical instruments.

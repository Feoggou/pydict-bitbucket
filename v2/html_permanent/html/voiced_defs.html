voiced

(vɔɪst )
Definitions
adjective1. declared or expressed by the voice2. (in combination) having a voice as specified ⇒ loud-voiced3. phonetics articulated with accompanying vibration of the vocal cords ⇒ in English (b) is a voiced consonant. Compare voiceless

flout

(flaʊt )
Definitions
verb(when intr, usually foll by at) to show contempt (for); scoff or jeer (at)See note at flaunt
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

flouter (ˈflouter) nounfloutingly (ˈfloutingly) adverb

Word Origin

C16: perhaps from Middle English flouten to play the flute, from Old French flauter compare Dutch fluiten; see flute

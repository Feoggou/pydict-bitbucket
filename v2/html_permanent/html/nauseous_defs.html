nauseous

(ˈnɔːzɪəs , -sɪ-)
Definitions
adjective1. feeling sick2. causing nausea3. distasteful to the mind or senses; repulsive
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

nauseously (ˈnauseously) adverbnauseousness (ˈnauseousness) noun

rectitude

(ˈrɛktɪˌtjuːd )
Definitions
noun1. moral or religious correctness2. correctness of judgment
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Late Latin rectitūdō, from Latin rectus right, straight, from regere to rule

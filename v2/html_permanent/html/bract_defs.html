bract

(brækt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna specialized leaf, usually smaller than the foliage leaves, with a single flower or inflorescence growing in its axil
Derived forms
bracteal (ˈbracteal) adjectivebractless (ˈbractless) adjective
Word origin
C18: from New Latin bractea, Latin: thin metal plate, gold leaf, variant of brattea, of obscure origin

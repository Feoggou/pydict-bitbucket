(dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. noble or dignified in appearance or behaviour2. eminent; famous; celebrated

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a person or their work as distinguished, you mean that they have been very successful in their career and have a good reputation. ⇒ ...a distinguished academic family. ⇒ ...a distinguished career.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as distinguished, you mean that they look very noble and respectable. ⇒ He looked very distinguished. ⇒ ...a distinguished gentleman.

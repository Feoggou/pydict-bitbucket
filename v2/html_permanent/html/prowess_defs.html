prowess

(ˈpraʊɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. outstanding or superior skill or ability2. bravery or fearlessness, esp in battle

Word Origin

C13: from Old French proesce, from prou good; see proud

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounSomeone's prowess is their great skill at doing something. [formal] ⇒ He's always bragging about his prowess as a cricketer. ⇒ The best and the brightest pupils competed to demonstrate their intellectual prowess.

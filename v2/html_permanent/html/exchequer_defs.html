exchequer

(ɪksˈtʃɛkə )
Definitions
noun1. (often capital) government (in Britain and certain other countries) the accounting department of the Treasury, responsible for receiving and issuing funds2. informal personal funds; finances
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13 (in the sense: chessboard, counting table): from Old French eschequier, from eschec check

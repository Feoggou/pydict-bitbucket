irritable

(ˈɪrɪtəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. quickly irritated; easily annoyed; peevish2. (of all living organisms) capable of responding to such stimuli as heat, light, and touch3. pathology abnormally sensitive
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

irritability (ˌirritaˈbility) nounirritableness (ˈirritableness) nounirritably (ˈirritably) adverb

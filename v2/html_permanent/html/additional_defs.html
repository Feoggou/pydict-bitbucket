Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Additional things are extra things apart from the ones already present. ⇒ The U.S. is sending additional troops to the region. ⇒ The insurer will also have to pay the additional costs of the trial.
Example sentences containing 'additional'
That the stab wounds were in the back is additional indication of this. Goldsborough, Robert MURDER IN E MINORThe result was an additional five dead, including a child eighteen months old. Forbes, Bryan THE ENDLESS GAMEAnywhere else on the journey and he would not now be faced by this additional problem. Forrest, Roberta WHEN THE APRICOTS BLOOMThere had to be additional programming they hadn't told me about. Michael Crichton PREY

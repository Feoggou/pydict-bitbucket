smith

(smɪθ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a person who works in metal, esp one who shapes metal by hammeringb. (in combination) ⇒ a silversmith2. See blacksmith

Word Origin

Old English; related to Old Norse smithr, Old High German smid, Middle Low German smīde jewellery, Greek smilē carving knife

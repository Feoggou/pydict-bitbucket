recreant

(ˈrɛkrɪənt ) archaic
Definitions
adjective1. cowardly; faint-hearted2. disloyalnoun3. a disloyal or cowardly person
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

recreance (ˈrecreance) or recreancy (ˈrecreancy) nounrecreantly (ˈrecreantly) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Old French, from recroire to surrender, from re- + Latin crēdere to believe; compare miscreant

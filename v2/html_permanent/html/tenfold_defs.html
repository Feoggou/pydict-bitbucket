tenfold

(ˈtɛnˌfəʊld )
Definitions
adjective1. equal to or having 10 times as many or as much ⇒ a tenfold increase in population2. composed of 10 partsadverb3. by or up to 10 times as many or as much ⇒ the population increased tenfold

thesaurus

(θɪˈsɔːrəs )
Definitions
noun plural -ruses or -ri (-raɪ)1. a book containing systematized lists of synonyms and related words2. a dictionary of selected words or topics3. rare a treasury
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Latin, Greek: treasure

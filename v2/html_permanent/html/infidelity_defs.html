infidelity

(ˌɪnfɪˈdɛlɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. lack of faith or constancy, esp sexual faithfulness2. lack of religious faith; disbelief3. an act or instance of disloyalty
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

variable nounInfidelity occurs when a person who is married or in a steady relationship has sex with another person. ⇒ George ignored his partner's infidelities. ⇒ I divorced him for infidelity.

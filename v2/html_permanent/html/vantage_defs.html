vantage

(ˈvɑːntɪdʒ )
Definitions
noun1. a state, position, or opportunity affording superiority or advantage2. superiority or benefit accruing from such a position, state, etc3. tennis short for advantage
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vantageless (ˈvantageless) adjective

Word Origin

C13: from Old French avantage advantage

allowable

(əˈlaʊəbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. permissible; admissible2. (of financial costs) that can be allowed or justified
Derived forms
alˈlowably adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveIf people decide that something is allowable, they let it happen without trying to stop it. ⇒ Capital punishment is allowable only under exceptional circumstances.2. adjectiveAllowable costs or expenses are amounts of money that you do not have to pay tax on. [business] ⇒ Her expenses were allowable deductions.

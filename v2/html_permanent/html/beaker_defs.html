beaker

(ˈbiːkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a cup usually having a wide mouth ⇒ a plastic beaker2. a cylindrical flat-bottomed container used in laboratories, usually made of glass and having a pouring lip3. the amount a beaker holds
Word origin
C14: from Old Norse bikarr; related to Old High German behhāri, Middle Dutch bēker beaker, Greek bikos earthenware jug

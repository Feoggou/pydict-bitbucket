subversion

(səbˈvɜːʃən ) or subversal (səbˈvɜːsəl)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or an instance of subverting or overthrowing a legally constituted government, institution, etc2. the state of being subverted; destruction or ruin3. something that brings about an overthrow

Word Origin

C14: from Late Latin subversiō destruction, from Latin subvertere to subvert

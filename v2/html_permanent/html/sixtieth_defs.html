sixtieth

(ˈsɪkstɪəθ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (usually prenominal)a. being the ordinal number of sixty in numbering or counting order, position, time, etc: often written 60thb. (as noun) ⇒ the sixtieth in a rownoun2. a. one of 60 approximately equal parts of somethingb. (as modifier) ⇒ a sixtieth part3. the fraction equal to one divided by 60 (1⁄60)

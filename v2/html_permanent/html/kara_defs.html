Kara

(ˈkʌrə)
Definitions
nounthe steel bangle traditionally worn by Sikhs as a symbol of their religious and cultural loyalty, symbolizing unity with God: originally worn as a wristguard by swordsmen. See also five Ks
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Punjabi karā

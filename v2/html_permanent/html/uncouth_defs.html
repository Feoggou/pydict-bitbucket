uncouth

(ʌnˈkuːθ )
Definitions
adjectivelacking in good manners, refinement, or grace
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

uncouthly (unˈcouthly) adverbuncouthness (unˈcouthness) noun

Word Origin

Old English uncūth, from un-1 + cūth familiar; related to Old High German kund known, Old Norse kunnr

dement

(dɪˈmɛnt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (intransitive) to deteriorate mentally, esp because of old age2. (transitive) rare to drive mad; make insane
Word origin
C16: from Late Latin dēmentāre to drive mad, from Latin de- + mēns mind

(ˈæbəˌlɑːd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Peter. French name Pierre Abélard. 1079–1142, French scholastic philosopher and theologian whose works include Historia Calamitatum and Sic et Non (1121). His love for Héloïse is recorded in their correspondence

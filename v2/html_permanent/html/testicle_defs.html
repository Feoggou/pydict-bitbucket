testicle

(ˈtɛstɪkəl )
Definitions
nouneither of the two male reproductive glands, in most mammals enclosed within the scrotum, that produce spermatozoa and the hormone testosterone. Also called: testis
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

testicular (tɛˈstɪkjʊlə ) adjective

Word Origin

C15: from Latin testiculus, diminutive of testis testicle

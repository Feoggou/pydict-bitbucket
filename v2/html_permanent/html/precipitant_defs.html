precipitant

(prɪˈsɪpɪtənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. hasty or impulsive; rash2. rushing or falling rapidly or without heed3. abrupt or suddennoun4. chemistry a substance or agent that causes a precipitate to form

Derived Forms

precipitance (preˈcipitance) or precipitancy (preˈcipitancy) nounprecipitantly (preˈcipitantly) adverb

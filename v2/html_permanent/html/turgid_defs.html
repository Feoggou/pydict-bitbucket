turgid

(ˈtɜːdʒɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. swollen and distended; congested2. (of style or language) pompous and high-flown; bombastic

Derived Forms

turgidity (turˈgidity) or turgidness (ˈturgidness) nounturgidly (ˈturgidly) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin turgidus, from turgēre to swell

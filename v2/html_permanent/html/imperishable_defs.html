imperishable

(ɪmˈpɛrɪʃəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. not subject to decay or deterioration ⇒ imperishable goods2. not likely to be forgotten ⇒ imperishable truths
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

imperishability (imˌperishaˈbility) or imperishableness (imˈperishableness) nounimperishably (imˈperishably) adverb

convulsive

Powered by COBUILD
(kənvʌlsɪv )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]A convulsive movement or action is sudden and cannot be controlled. [formal] ⇒ She thought she could never stop until convulsive sobs racked her even more.convulsively adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ His arms and legs jerked convulsively.
Example sentences containing 'convulsive'
She did not make a sound, but began to shake, head still in her hands; short, convulsive bursts of shuddering. Ian St James Awards AT THE STROKE OF TWELVEHeaving up again, wrenching away from the pain, she bent her writhing limbs beneath her and sprang backwards in a convulsive leap. J.R.R. Tolkien THE LORD OF THE RINGSOr that the thought of never seeing him again felt like the onset of a convulsive illness?

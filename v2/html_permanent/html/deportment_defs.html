deportment

(dɪˈpɔːtmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe manner in which a person behaves, esp in physical bearing ⇒ military deportment
Word origin
C17: from French déportement, from Old French deporter to conduct (oneself); see deport

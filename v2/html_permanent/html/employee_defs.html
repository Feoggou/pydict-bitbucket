employee

(ɛmˈplɔɪiː , ˌɛmplɔɪˈiː ) or sometimes US employe
Definitions
nouna person who is hired to work for another or for a business, firm, etc, in return for payment. Also called (esp formerly): employé
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
countable nounAn employee is a person who is paid to work for an organization or for another person. [+ of] ⇒ He is an employee of Fuji Bank. ⇒ Many of its employees are women. ⇒ ...a government employee.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverb [ADV with v]You use primarily to say what is mainly true in a particular situation. ⇒ ...a book aimed primarily at high-energy physicists. ⇒ Public order is primarily an urban problem. ⇒ Investment remains tiny primarily because of the exorbitant cost of land.

Synonyms of vacuum

vacuum

noun

  1. gap, lack, absence, space, deficiency, void
    • The collapse of the army left a vacuum in the area.
  2. emptiness, space, void, gap, empty space, nothingness, vacuity
    • The spinning turbine creates a vacuum.
  3. vacuum cleaner, Hoover®, vac (British, informal)
    • Get the breakfast and take the vacuum round the house before the boys wake.

verb

  1. vacuum-clean, hoover
    • I vacuumed the carpets today.

QUOTATION:
Nature abhors a vacuumFrançois Rabelais – Gargantua

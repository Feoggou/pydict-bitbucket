confluence

(ˈkɒnflʊəns ) or conflux (ˈkɒnflʌks )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a merging or flowing together, esp of rivers2. a gathering together, esp of people

confluence

Powered by COBUILD
(kɒnfluəns )
Word forms: confluences
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. singular nounThe confluence of two rivers is the place where they join and become one larger river. [+ of] ⇒ The 160-metre falls mark the dramatic confluence of the rivers Nera and Velino.2. countable noun [oft N of n]If there is a confluence of two things, they join, combine, or come together. [formal] ⇒ Like most cases of extreme weather, its severity was due to an unusual confluence of events. ⇒ ...the confluence of African and Portuguese cultures in Brazil.

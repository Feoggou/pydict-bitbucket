practitioner

(prækˈtɪʃənə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who practises a profession or art2. Christian Science a person authorized to practise spiritual healing

Word Origin

C16: from practician, from Old French praticien, from pratiquer to practise

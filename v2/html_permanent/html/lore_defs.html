lore1

(lɔː )
Definitions
noun1. collective knowledge or wisdom on a particular subject, esp of a traditional nature2. knowledge or learning3. archaic teaching, or something that is taught
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English lār; related to leornian to learn

lore2

(lɔː )
Definitions
noun1. the surface of the head of a bird between the eyes and the base of the bill2. the corresponding area in a snake or fish
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from New Latin lōrum, from Latin: strap

colt

(kəʊlt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a male horse or pony under the age of four2. an awkward or inexperienced young person3. sporta. a young and inexperienced playerb. a member of a junior team
Word origin
Old English colt young ass, of obscure origin; compare Swedish dialect kult young animal, boy

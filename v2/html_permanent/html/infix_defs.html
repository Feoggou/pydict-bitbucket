Word Frequency

infix

Definitions
verb (ɪnˈfɪks , ˈɪnˌfɪks )1. (transitive) to fix firmly in2. (transitive) to instil or inculcate3. grammar to insert (an affix) or (of an affix) to be inserted into the middle of a wordnoun (ˈɪnˌfɪks )4. grammar an affix inserted into the middle of a word
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

infixation (ˌinfixˈation) or infixion (ɪnˈfɪkʃən ) noun

Similar Words

implant, place, set, introduce, insert, fasten, inset, engraft
instil, impress, entrench, inculcate, drum into, ingrain

licentious

(laɪˈsɛnʃəs )
Definitions
adjective1. sexually unrestrained or promiscuous2. rare showing disregard for convention
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

licentiously (liˈcentiously) adverblicentiousness (liˈcentiousness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin licentiōsus capricious, from licentia licence

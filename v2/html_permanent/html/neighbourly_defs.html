(neɪbəʳli )
Definitions
regional note:   in AM, use neighborlygraded adjective & adjectiveIf the people who live near you are neighbourly, they are friendly and helpful. If you live in a neighbourly place, it has a friendly atmosphere. ⇒ The noise would have provoked alarm and neighbourly concern. ⇒ The older people had stopped being neighbourly to each other. ⇒ ...a small, neighbourly seaside resort.neighbourliness uncount noun ⇒ The head of state said his country had always attached great importance to good neighbourliness.

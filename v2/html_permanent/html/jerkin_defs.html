jerkin

(ˈdʒɜːkɪn )
Definitions
noun1. a sleeveless and collarless short jacket worn by men or women2. a man's sleeveless and collarless fitted jacket, often made of leather, worn in the 16th and 17th centuries
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: of unknown origin

Palestine

(ˈpælɪˌstaɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: the Holy Land, Canaan the area between the Jordan River and the Mediterranean Sea in which most of the biblical narrative is located2. the province of the Roman Empire in this region3. the former British mandatory territory created by the League of Nations in 1922 (but effective from 1920), and including all of the present territories of Israel and Jordan between whom it was partitioned by the UN in 1948

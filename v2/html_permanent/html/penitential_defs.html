penitential

(ˌpɛnɪˈtɛnʃəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, showing, or constituting penancenoun2. mainly Roman Catholic Church a book or compilation of instructions for confessors3. a less common word for penitent (sense 2), penitent (sense 3)

Derived Forms

ˌpenitentially (ˌpeniˈtentially) adverb

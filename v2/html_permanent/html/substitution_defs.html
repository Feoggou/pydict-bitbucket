substitution

(ˌsʌbstɪˈtjuːʃən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of substituting or state of being substituted2. something or someone substituted3. mathematics the replacement of a term of an equation by another that is known to have the same value in order to simplify the equation4. mathematics, logica. the uniform replacement of one expression by anotherb. See substitution instance

polarized

or polarised (ˈpəʊləˌraɪzd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. consisting of two separate groups with opposite opinions or positions ⇒ our ideologically polarized society, ⇒ a racially polarized election2. physics (of light waves, etc) restricted to certain directions of vibration, usually specified in terms of the electric field vector ⇒ polarized light

There was light from the flame globes, brilliant streams of light reflected from polished basalt in lovely polarized waves.

(dɪsgrʌntəld )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are disgruntled, you are cross and dissatisfied because things have not happened the way that you wanted them to happen. ⇒ Disgruntled employees recently called for his resignation. ⇒ Party members are disgruntled at the way the campaign is being handled. [Also + by/at/over]

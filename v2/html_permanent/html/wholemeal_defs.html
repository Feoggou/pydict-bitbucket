wholemeal

(ˈhəʊlˌmiːl )
Definitions
adjectiveBritish (of flour, bread, etc) made from the entire wheat kernel. US and Canadian term: whole-wheat

wholemeal for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(hoʊlmiːl )
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Wholemeal flour is made from the complete grain of the wheat plant, including the outer part. Wholemeal bread or pasta is made from wholemeal flour. [British] ⇒ ...a slice of wholemeal toast.regional note:   in AM, use wholewheat2. uncount nounWholemeal means wholemeal bread or wholemeal flour. [British] ⇒ ...one slice of white and one of wholemeal.regional note:   in AM, use wholewheat

extraneous

(ɪkˈstreɪnɪəs )
Definitions
adjective1. not essential2. not pertinent or applicable; irrelevant3. coming from without; of external origin4. not belonging; unrelated to that to which it is added or in which it is contained
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

extraneously (exˈtraneously) adverbextraneousness (exˈtraneousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin extrāneus external, from extrā outside

extraneous

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪkstreɪniəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]Extraneous things are not relevant or essential to the situation you are involved in or the subject you are talking about. [formal] ⇒ We ought not to bring in extraneous matters in trying to find a basis for a settlement. ⇒ I can choose to ignore these extraneous thoughts or certainly choose not to act on them.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

infill

(ˈɪnfɪl ) or infilling (ˈɪnfɪlɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. the act of filling or closing gaps, etc, in something, such as a row of buildings2. material used to fill a cavity, gap, hole, etc3. an acrylic gel application that fills in the gap between a false nail and the root of the real nail, which is created as the real nail grows

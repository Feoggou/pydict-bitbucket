founding

(ˈfaʊndɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the activity or process of beginning an institution; foundation ⇒ I have been a member of The Sunday Times Wine Club since its founding in 1973.adjective2. first or original; present at the foundation ⇒ The committee held its founding congress in the capital, Riga., ⇒ a founding director

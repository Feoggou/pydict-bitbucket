thunderous

(ˈθʌndərəs )
Definitions
adjective1. resembling thunder, esp in loudness ⇒ thunderous clapping2. threatening and extremely angry ⇒ she gave him a thunderous look
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

thunderously (ˈthunderously) adverb

thunderous

(θʌndərəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a noise as thunderous, you mean that it is very loud and deep. ⇒ The audience responded with thunderous applause. ⇒ ...the thunderous roar of an explosion.

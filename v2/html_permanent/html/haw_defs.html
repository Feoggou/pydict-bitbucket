haw1

(hɔː )
Definitions
noun1. the round or oval fruit (a pome) of the hawthorn, usually red or yellow, containing one to five seeds2. another name for hawthorn
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English haga, identical with haga hedge; related to Old Norse hagi pasture

haw2

(hɔː )
Definitions
noun, exclamation1. an inarticulate utterance, as of hesitation, embarrassment, etc; hemverb2. (intransitive) to make this sound3. See hem and haw
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: of imitative origin

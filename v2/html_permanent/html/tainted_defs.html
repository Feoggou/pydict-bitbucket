tainted

(ˈteɪntɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. contaminated or infected ⇒ a dirty world of sudden illness and doubtful water and scanty, tainted food, ⇒ It's hot, and people will drink tainted water if they aren't aware that it carries disease.2. tarnished ⇒ By her own rules, she was a tainted woman., ⇒ It was tainted money, surely, and not hers to spend., ⇒ He came out only slightly tainted by telling millions of viewers he and his wife had had marital problems.

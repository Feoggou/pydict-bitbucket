maker

(ˈmeɪkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who makes (something); fabricator; constructor2. a person who executes a legal document, esp one who signs a promissory note3. archaic, Scottish a poet. Also called (esp Scot): makar

Maker

(ˈmeɪkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a title given to God2. See go to meet one's Maker

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounThe maker of a product is the firm that manufactures it. ⇒ ...Japan's two largest car makers. ⇒ ...the makers of chocolates, sweets and biscuits.2. countable nounYou can refer to the person who makes something as its maker. ⇒ ...the makers of news and current affairs programmes.3. → see also peacemaker

tumour

or US tumor (ˈtjuːmə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. pathologya. any abnormal swellingb. a mass of tissue formed by a new growth of cells, normally independent of the surrounding structures2. obsolete pompous style or language

Derived Forms

tumorous (ˈtumorous) or tumoral (ˈtumoral) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Latin, from tumēre to swell

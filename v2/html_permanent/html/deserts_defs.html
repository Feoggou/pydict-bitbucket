deserts

(dɪˈzɜːts)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. something that is deserved or merited; just reward or punishment ⇒ He has now received his deserts., ⇒ I have been blessed with an astonishing number of wonderful friends, far beyond my deserts.2. See to get your just deserts

subway

(ˈsʌbˌweɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. British an underground passage or tunnel enabling pedestrians to cross a road, railway, etc2. an underground passage or tunnel for traffic, electric power supplies, etc3. mainly US and Canadian an underground railway

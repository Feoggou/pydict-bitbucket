polling

(ˈpəʊlɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. the casting or registering of votes at an electionb. (as modifier) ⇒ polling day2. the conducting of a public opinion poll3. computing the automatic interrogation of terminals by a central controlling machine to determine if they are ready to receive or transmit messages

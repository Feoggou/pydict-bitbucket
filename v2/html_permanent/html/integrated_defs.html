integrated

(ˈɪntɪˌɡreɪtɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. characterized by integration2. denoting a works which combines various processes normally carried out at different locations ⇒ an integrated steelworks3. biology denoting a virus the DNA of which is incorporated into the chromosomes of the host cell
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

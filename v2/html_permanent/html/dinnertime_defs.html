dinnertime

(ˈdɪnətaɪm)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the time of evening dinner ⇒ The telephone call came shortly before dinnertime.2. the time of midday dinner ⇒ By dinnertime, it was all over the school., ⇒ Sunday dinnertime

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft prep N]Dinnertime is the period of the day when most people have their dinner. ⇒ The telephone call came shortly before dinnertime. ⇒ ...Sunday dinnertime.

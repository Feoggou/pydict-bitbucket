valuation

(ˌvæljʊˈeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of valuing, esp a formal assessment of the worth of property, jewellery, etc2. the price arrived at by the process of valuing ⇒ the valuation of this property is considerable, ⇒ I set a high valuation on technical ability
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

valuational (ˌvaluˈational) adjectivevaluationally (ˌvaluˈationally) adverb

unambitious

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnæmbɪʃəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveAn unambitious person is not particularly interested in improving their position in life or in being successful, rich, or powerful. ⇒ He was a reliable, unambitious officer who did as he was told.2. graded adjective & adjectiveAn unambitious idea or plan is not very complicated, risky, or new, and is easy to carry out successfully. ⇒ This was not by any means a daring expedition into the outback, it was a very unambitious exploration.

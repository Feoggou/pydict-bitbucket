refrigerator

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪfrɪdʒəreɪtər) in Chemical Engineering
Word forms: (regular plural) refrigerators
Definitions
noun(Chemical Engineering: Heat transfer) A refrigerator is a device which uses the boiling and condensation of a refrigerant to cool something. ⇒ A regular refrigerator uses a compressor to increase the pressure on the gas, and then condenses the higher pressure gas back to a liquid by heat exchange with a coolant. ⇒ Refrigerators are designed so that the refrigerant leaves the evaporator either saturated or slightly superheated. ⇒ A refrigerator is a device which uses the boiling and condensation of a refrigerant to cool something.

spam

(spæm ) computing slang
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verbWord forms: spams, spamming or spammed1. to send unsolicited electronic mail or text messages simultaneously to a number of e-mail addresses or mobile phonesnoun2. unsolicited electronic mail or text messages sent in this way

Derived Forms

spammer (ˈspammer) noun

Word Origin

C20: from the repeated use of the word Spam in a popular sketch from the British television show Monty Python's Flying Circus, first broadcast in 1969

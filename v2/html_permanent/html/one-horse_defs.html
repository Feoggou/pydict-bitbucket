one-horse

Definitions
adjective1. drawn by or using one horse2. (prenominal) informal small or obscure ⇒ a one-horse town
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

one-horse for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]If someone describes a town as a one-horse town, they mean it is very small, dull, and old-fashioned. [disapproval] ⇒ Would you want to live in a small, one-horse town for your whole life?2. adjective [ADJ n]If a contest is described as a one-horse race, it is thought that one person or thing will obviously win it. ⇒ He described the referendum as a one-horse race. ⇒ Last season looked to be a one-horse race for much of the time, with the reigning champions coasting to an 11-point lead.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

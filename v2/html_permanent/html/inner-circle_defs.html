inner circle

(ˈɪnə ˈsɜːkəl)
Definitions
nouna clique or a group of people or who share a common interest, aim or purpose ⇒ the inner circle of scientists who produced the atomic bomb, ⇒ the president's inner circle of advisers

Word forms: inner circles
Definitions
countable noun [usu sing]An inner circle is a small group of people within a larger group who have a lot of power, influence, or special information. [+ of] ⇒ ...Mr Blair's inner circle of advisers.

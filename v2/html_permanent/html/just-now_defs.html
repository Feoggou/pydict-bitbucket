just now for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
phraseYou use just now when you want to say that a particular situation exists at the time when you are speaking, although it may change in the future. [spoken] at the moment ⇒ I'm pretty busy just now. ⇒ Mr Goldsworth is not available just now. →  now

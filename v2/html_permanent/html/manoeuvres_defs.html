manoeuvres

British or maneuvers US (məˈnuːvəz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounmilitary tactical exercises, usually on a large scale ⇒ Allied troops begin maneuvers tomorrow to show how quickly forces could be mobilized in case of a new invasion., ⇒ The camp was used for military manoeuvres., ⇒ Russian warships were on manoeuvres between Malta and Sicily.

spotting

(ˈspɒtɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. British the hobby of watching for and noting particular examples of something, such as birds, numbers or types of trains, buses, etc ⇒ binoculars for bird spotting, ⇒ train/plane spottingpresent participle of verb2. See spot

relatively

Powered by COBUILD
(relətɪvli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [ADV adj/adv]Relatively means to a certain degree, especially when compared with other things of the same kind. ⇒ The sums needed are relatively small. ⇒ I like to think I'm relatively easy to get along with.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

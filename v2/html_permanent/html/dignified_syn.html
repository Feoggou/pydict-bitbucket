dignified

adjective

  1. distinguished, august, reserved, imposing, formal, grave, noble, upright, stately, solemn, lofty, exalted, decorous • He conducted the interview in a dignified and professional manner. OPPOSITES:  vulgar, crass, unseemly, undignified, unbecoming, inelegant

dignify

verb

  1. distinguish, honour, grace, raise, advance, promote, elevate, glorify, exalt, ennoble, aggrandize • I see no point in dignifying this kind of speculation with a response

'dignified' in Other Languages

  • British English: dignified ADJECTIVE
    If you say that someone or something is dignified, you mean they are calm, impressive, and deserve respect.He seemed a very dignified and charming man.

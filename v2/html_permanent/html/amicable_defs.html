amicable

(ˈæmɪkəbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivecharacterized by friendliness ⇒ an amicable agreement
Derived forms
ˌamicaˈbility or ˈamicableness nounˈamicably adverb
Word origin
C15: from Late Latin amīcābilis, from Latin amīcus friend; related to amāre to love

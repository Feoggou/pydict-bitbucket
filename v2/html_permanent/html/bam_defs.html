Comment by dcornwall84 on 2 Sep 2014
In Scotland the word "bam" is used as a term of offense. It is a contraction of the word "bampot" which means the same thing. For example in an episode of River City one character says "That bam et ma piece" meaning "he ate my sandwich". The phrase "ya mad bam" is also used widely, but not necessarily in a negative sense - it could be meant affectionately.

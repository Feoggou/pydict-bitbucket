Definitions

Derived Forms

Word Origin

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone's behaviour or actions as inane, you think they are very silly or stupid. [disapproval] ⇒ He always had this inane grin. ⇒ She started asking me inane questions.inanely graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v] ⇒ He lurched through the bar, grinning inanely.inanity (ɪnænɪti ) uncount noun [+ of] ⇒ ...the inanity of the conversation.

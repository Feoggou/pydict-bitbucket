hemiplegia

(ˌhɛmɪˈpliːdʒɪə )
Definitions
nounparalysis of one side of the body, usually as the result of injury to the brain. Compare paraplegia, quadriplegia

hemiplegic (ˌhemiˈplegic) adjective

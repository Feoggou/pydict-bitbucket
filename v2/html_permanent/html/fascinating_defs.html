fascinating

(ˈfæsɪˌneɪtɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. arousing great interest2. enchanting or alluring ⇒ a fascinating woman
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fascinatingly (ˈfasciˌnatingly) adverb

(fæsɪneɪtɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as fascinating, you find it very interesting and attractive, and your thoughts tend to concentrate on it. ⇒ Madagascar is the most fascinating place I have ever been to. ⇒ Her perceptions and intuitions about human nature were fascinating.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

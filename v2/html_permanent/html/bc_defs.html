BC

Powered by COBUILD
(biː siː )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
You use BC in dates to indicate a number of years or centuries before the year in which Jesus Christ is believed to have been born. Compare AD. ⇒ The brooch dates back to the fourth century BC.

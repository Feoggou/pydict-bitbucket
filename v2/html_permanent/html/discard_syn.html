discard

verb

  1. get rid of, drop, remove, throw away or out, reject, abandon, dump (informal), shed, scrap, axe (informal), ditch (slang), junk (informal), chuck (informal), dispose of, relinquish, dispense with, jettison, repudiate, cast aside • Read the instructions before discarding the box. OPPOSITES:  keep, save, reserve, retain, hold back, hang or hold on to

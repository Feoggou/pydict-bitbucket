cubical

(ˈkjuːbɪkəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or related to volume ⇒ cubical expansion2. shaped like a cube3. of or involving the third power
Derived forms
cubically (ˈcubically) or cubicly (ˈcubicly) adverbcubicalness (ˈcubicalness) noun
Example sentences containing 'cubical'
She was thick through from front to back and side to side as Folk often were, practically cubical in shape. Robert Silverberg THE LONGEST WAY HOME

crayfish

(ˈkreɪˌfɪʃ ) or especially US crawfish
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -fish or -fishes1. any freshwater decapod crustacean of the genera Astacus and Cambarus, resembling a small lobster2. any of various similar crustaceans, esp the spiny lobster
Word origin
C14: cray, by folk etymology, from Old French crevice crab, from Old High German krebiz + fish

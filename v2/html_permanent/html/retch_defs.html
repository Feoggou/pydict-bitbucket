retch

(rɛtʃ , riːtʃ )
Definitions
verb1. (intransitive) to undergo an involuntary spasm of ineffectual vomiting; heave2. to vomitnoun3. an involuntary spasm of ineffectual vomiting
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English hrǣcan; related to Old Norse hrǣkja to spit

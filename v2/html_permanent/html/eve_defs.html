eve

(iːv )
Definitions
noun1. a. the evening or day before some special event or festivalb. (capital when part of a name) ⇒ New Year's Eve2. the period immediately before an event ⇒ on the eve of civil war3. an archaic word for evening
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: variant of even2

Eve

(iːv )
Definitions
nounOld Testament the first woman; mother of the human race, fashioned by God from the rib of Adam (Genesis 2:18-25)

Definitions
1. countable noun [usu sing]The eve of a particular event or occasion is the day before it, or the period of time just before it. [journalism] [+of] ⇒ ...on the eve of his 27th birthday.2. → See also Christmas Eve, New Year's Eve

lite

(laɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. (of food and drink) containing few calories or little alcohol or fat2. denoting a more restrained or less extreme version of a person or thing ⇒ reggae lite
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: variant spelling of light2

lite

Powered by COBUILD
(laɪt ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveLite is used to describe foods or drinks that contain few calories or low amounts of sugar, fat, or alcohol. ⇒ ...lite beer. ⇒ ...lite yogurt.2. adjective [oft n ADJ]If you describe something as lite, you dislike it because it is a weaker and less demanding version of something else. [disapproval] ⇒ ... an extended, self congratulatory session of easy-listening jazz-lite.

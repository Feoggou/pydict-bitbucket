(ˌdɛvəˈtiː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person ardently enthusiastic about or devoted to something, such as a sport or pastime2. a zealous follower of a religion

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounSomeone who is a devotee of a subject or activity is very enthusiastic about it. [+ of] ⇒ Mr Carpenter is obviously a devotee of Britten's music.2. countable nounA devotee is a member of a religious group. ⇒ Monks shave their heads, as do devotees of the Hare Krishna movement.

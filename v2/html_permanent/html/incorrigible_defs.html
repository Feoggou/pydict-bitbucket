incorrigible

(ɪnˈkɒrɪdʒəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. beyond correction, reform, or alteration2. firmly rooted; ineradicable3. philosophy (of a belief) having the property that whoever honestly believes it cannot be mistaken. Compare defeasiblenoun4. a person or animal that is incorrigible
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

incorrigibility (inˌcorrigiˈbility) or incorrigibleness (inˈcorrigibleness) nounincorrigibly (inˈcorrigibly) adverb

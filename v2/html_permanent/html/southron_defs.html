Southron

(ˈsʌðrən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. mainly Scottish a Southerner, esp an Englishman2. Scottish the English language as spoken in England3. dialect, mainly Southern US an inhabitant of the South, esp at the time of the Civil Waradjective4. mainly Scottish of or relating to the South or to England

Word Origin

C15: Scottish variant of Southern

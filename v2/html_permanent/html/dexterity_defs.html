dexterity

(dɛkˈstɛrɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. physical, esp manual, skill or nimbleness2. mental skill or adroitness; cleverness3. rare the characteristic of being right-handed
Word origin
C16: from Latin dexteritās aptness, readiness, prosperity; see dexter1

(lɛd fri) in Automotive Engineering
Definitions
adjective(Automotive engineering: Fuels, oils, emissions, and other fluids) Lead-free gasoline is made without lead, or has no lead added to it.
SIMILAR WORDS: 
unleaded ⇒ Many classic cars cannot run on lead-free gasoline. ⇒ Levels of lead in the environment have plummeted in the country since the advent of lead-free gasoline. ⇒ There should be more environmental requirements for the auto industry, apart from simply catalytic converters and lead-free gasoline.

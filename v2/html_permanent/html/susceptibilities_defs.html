susceptibilities

(səˌsɛptəˈbɪlɪtɪz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. pl of susceptibility2. emotional sensibilities; feelings ⇒ This was no more than a display of insensitivity towards local culture and a profound disregard for Spanish susceptibilities., ⇒ I am well aware that in saying this I shall outrage a few susceptibilities.
Example sentences containing 'susceptibilities'
The close proximity of her body was no more attractive to him than it would be to anyone else of normal human susceptibilities. Hilton, John Buxton THE INNOCENTS AT HOME (A SUPERINTENDENT KENWORTHY NOVEL)Discussion was rigorous, tending to broaden and enliven susceptibilities to stories each of us liked.

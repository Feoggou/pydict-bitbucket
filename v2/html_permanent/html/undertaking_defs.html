undertaking

(ˈʌndəˌteɪkɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. something undertaken; task, venture, or enterprise2. an agreement to do something3. the business of an undertaker4. informal the practice of overtaking on an inner lane a vehicle which is travelling in an outer lane
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

undertaking for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌndəʳteɪkɪŋ )
Word forms: undertakings
Definitions
1. countable nounAn undertaking is a task or job, especially a large or difficult one. ⇒ Organizing the show has been a massive undertaking.2. countable noun [oft N to-inf]If you give an undertaking to do something, you formally promise to do it. ⇒ The MOD gave an undertaking to Saville that it had provided him with all relevant material.

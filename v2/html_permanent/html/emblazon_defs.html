emblazon

(ɪmˈbleɪzən )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to describe, portray, or colour (arms) according to the conventions of heraldry2. to portray heraldic arms on (a shield, one's notepaper, etc)3. to make bright or splendid, as with colours, flowers, etc4. to glorify, praise, or extol, often so as to attract great publicity ⇒ his feat was emblazoned on the front page
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

emblazonment (emˈblazonment) noun

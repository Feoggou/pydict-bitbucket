white hat

Definitions
nouninformala. a computer hacker who is hired by an organization to undertake nonmalicious hacking work in order to discover computer-security flawsb. (as modifier) ⇒ a white-hat hacker. Compare black hat

white-hat

(waɪt hæt)
Definitions
modifierinformal of or relating to a computer hacker who is hired by an organization to undertake nonmalicious hacking work in order to discover computer-security flaws ⇒ a white-hat hacker

Synonyms of lazy

lazy

adjective

  1. idle, inactive, indolent, slack, negligent, inert, remiss, workshy, slothful, shiftless
    • I was too lazy to learn how to read music.
    OPPOSITES:  active, stimulated, energetic, diligent, industrious, assiduous
  2. lethargic, languorous, slow-moving, languid, sleepy, sluggish, drowsy, somnolent, torpid
    • We would have a lazy lunch and then lie on the beach in the sun.
    OPPOSITE:  quick

socialist

(ˈsəʊʃəlɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a supporter or advocate of socialism or any party promoting socialism (socialist party)adjective2. of, characteristic of, implementing, or relating to socialism3. (sometimes capital) of, characteristic of, or relating to socialists or a socialist party

Word forms: socialists
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Socialist means based on socialism or relating to socialism. ⇒ ...members of the ruling Socialist party. ⇒ ...low-inflation policies practised by the socialist government. ⇒ Ethiopia was declared a socialist state.2. countable nounA socialist is a person who believes in socialism or who is a member of a socialist party. ⇒ The French electorate voted out the socialists.

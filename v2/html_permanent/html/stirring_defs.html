stirring

(ˈstɜːrɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. exciting the emotions; stimulating2. active, lively, or busy

Derived Forms

stirringly (ˈstirringly) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A stirring event, performance, or account of something makes people very excited or enthusiastic. ⇒ The Prime Minister made a stirring speech. ⇒ Stowe gives a stirring performance as a strong spirited female. ⇒ ...a stirring account of the final months of the old regime.2. countable nounA stirring of a feeling or thought is the beginning of one. [+ of] ⇒ I feel a stirring of curiosity. ⇒ ...the first stirrings of a sense of guilt.

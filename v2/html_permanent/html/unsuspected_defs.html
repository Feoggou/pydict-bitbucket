unsuspected

(ˌʌnsəˈspɛktɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. not under suspicion2. not known to exist

(ʌnsəspektɪd )
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something as unsuspected, you mean that people do not realize it or are not aware of it. ⇒ A surprising number of ailments are caused by unsuspected environmental factors. ⇒ He died in 1984 of an unsuspected brain tumour.

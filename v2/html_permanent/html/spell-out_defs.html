spell out

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (tr, adverb)1. to make clear, distinct, or explicit; clarify in detail ⇒ let me spell out the implications2. to read laboriously or with difficulty, working out each word letter by letter3. to discern by study; puzzle out

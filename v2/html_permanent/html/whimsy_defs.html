whimsy

or whimsey (ˈwɪmzɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -sies or -seys1. a capricious idea or notion2. light or fanciful humour3. something quaint or unusualadjective -sier or -siest4. quaint, comical, or unusual, often in a tasteless way
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from whim; compare flimsy

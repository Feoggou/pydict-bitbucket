improvident

(ɪmˈprɒvɪdənt )
Definitions
adjective1. not provident; thriftless, imprudent, or prodigal2. heedless or incautious; rash
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

improvidence (imˈprovidence) nounimprovidently (imˈprovidently) adverb
Example sentences containing 'improvident'
She'd placed him in the strictest military boarding schools she could find to fend off his improvident nature. Spellman, Cathy Cash PAINT THE WIND

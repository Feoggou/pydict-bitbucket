titled

Powered by COBUILD
(taɪtəld ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveSomeone who is titled has a title such as 'Lord', 'Lady', 'Sir', or 'Princess' before their name, showing that they have a high rank in society. ⇒ Her mother was a titled lady.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

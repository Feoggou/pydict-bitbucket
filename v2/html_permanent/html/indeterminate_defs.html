indeterminate

(ˌɪndɪˈtɜːmɪnɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. uncertain in extent, amount, or nature2. not definite; inconclusive ⇒ an indeterminate reply3. unable to be predicted, calculated, or deduced4. physics (of an effect) not obeying the law of causality; noncausal5. mathematicsa. having no numerical meaning, as 0.00 or 0/0b. (of an equation) having more than one variable and an unlimited number of solutions6. botany another word for indefinite (sense 4)7. (of a structure, framework, etc) comprising forces that cannot be fully analysed, esp by vector analysis
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

indeterminacy (ˌindeˈterminacy), indetermination (ˌindeˌtermiˈnation) or indeterminateness (ˌindeˈterminateness) nounindeterminately (ˌindeˈterminately) adverb

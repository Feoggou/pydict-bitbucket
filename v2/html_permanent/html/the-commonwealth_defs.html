the Commonwealth

Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
1.  the government in England under the Cromwells and Parliament from 1649 to 1660. see also protectorate2.  association of independent nations (54 in Feb., 2010), all former components of the British Empire, united for purposes of consultation and mutual assistance: all members acknowledge the British sovereign as symbolic head of the association: in full the Commonwealth of Nations

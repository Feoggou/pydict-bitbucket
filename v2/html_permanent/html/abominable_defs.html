abominable

(əˈbɒmɪnəbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. offensive; loathsome; detestable2. informal very bad, unpleasant, or inferior ⇒ abominable weather, ⇒ abominable workmanship
Derived forms
aˈbominably adverb
Word origin
C14: from Latin abōminābilis, from abōminārī to abominate

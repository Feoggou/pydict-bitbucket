Ming

(mɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the imperial dynasty of China from 1368 to 1644adjective2. of or relating to Chinese porcelain produced during the Ming dynasty, characterized by the use of brilliant colours and a fine-quality body

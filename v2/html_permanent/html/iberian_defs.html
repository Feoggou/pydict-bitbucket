Iberian

(aɪˈbɪərɪən )
Definitions
noun1. a member of a group of ancient Caucasoid peoples who inhabited the Iberian Peninsula in preclassical and classical times. See also Celtiberian2. a native or inhabitant of the Iberian Peninsula; a Spaniard or Portuguese3. a native or inhabitant of ancient Iberia in the Caucasusadjective4. denoting or relating to the pre-Roman peoples of the Iberian Peninsula or of Caucasian Iberia5. of or relating to the Iberian Peninsula, its inhabitants, or any of their languages

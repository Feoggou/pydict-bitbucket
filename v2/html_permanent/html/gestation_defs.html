gestation

(dʒɛˈsteɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a. the development of the embryo of a viviparous mammal, between conception and birth: about 266 days in humans, 624 days in elephants, and 63 days in catsb. (as modifier) ⇒ gestation period2. the development of an idea or plan in the mind3. the period of such a development
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gestational (gesˈtational) or gestative (ˈdʒɛstətɪv , dʒɛˈsteɪ-) adjectivegestatory (ˈgestatory) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Latin gestātiō a bearing, from gestāre to bear, frequentative of gerere to carry

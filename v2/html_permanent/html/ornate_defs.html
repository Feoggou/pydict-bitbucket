ornate

(ɔːˈneɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. heavily or elaborately decorated2. (of style in writing) overembellished; flowery
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ornately (orˈnately) adverbornateness (orˈnateness) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin ornāre to decorate

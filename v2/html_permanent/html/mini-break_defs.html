mini-break

(ˈmɪnɪbreɪk)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna short holiday, usually consisting of a weekend or a few days ⇒ Five-day mini-breaks start at £95., ⇒ My three-day winter mini-break began on Friday with an evening flight from Heathrow.

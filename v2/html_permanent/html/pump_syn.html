Synonyms of pump

pump

verb

  1. drive out, empty, drain, force out, bail out, siphon, draw off
    • drill rigs that are busy pumping natural gas
  2. supply, send, pour, inject
    • The government must pump more money into community care.
  3. interrogate, probe, quiz, cross-examine, grill (informal), worm out of, give someone the third degree, question closely
    • He ran in every five minutes to pump me for details.
  4. spurt, run, course, flow, flood, pour, jet, stream, spill, gush, spout, squirt
    • blood pumping from a head wound
  5. fire, shoot, discharge, let off
    • A gunman burst in and pumped five bullets into her head.
  • See pump something up

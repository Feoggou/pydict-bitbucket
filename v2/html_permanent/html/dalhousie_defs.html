Dalhousie

(dælˈhaʊzɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  9th Earl of, title of George Ramsay. 1770–1838, British general; governor of the British colonies in Canada (1819–28)2.  his son, 1st Marquis and 10th Earl of, title of James Andrew Broun Ramsay. 1812–60, British statesman: governor general of India (1848–56)

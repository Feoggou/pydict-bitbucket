occurrence

(əˈkʌrəns )
Definitions
noun1. something that occurs; a happening; event2. the act or an instance of occurring ⇒ a crime of frequent occurrence
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

occurrence for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(əkʌrəns , US -kɜːr- )
Word forms: occurrences
Definitions
1. countable nounAn occurrence is something that happens. [formal] ⇒ Complaints seemed to be an everyday occurrence. ⇒ The food queues have become a daily occurrence across the country.2. countable nounThe occurrence of something is the fact that it happens or is present. [+ of] ⇒ The greatest occurrence of coronary heart disease is in those over 65.

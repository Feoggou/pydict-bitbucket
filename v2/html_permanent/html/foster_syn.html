Synonyms of foster

foster

verb

  1. bring up, mother, raise, nurse, look after, rear, care for, take care of, nurture
    • She has fostered more than 100 children.
  2. develop, support, further, encourage, feed, promote, stimulate, uphold, nurture, cultivate, foment
    • They are keen to foster trading links with the West.
    OPPOSITES:  oppose, resist, combat, curb, suppress, restrain, inhibit, subdue, withstand, curtail, hold out against
  3. cherish, sustain, entertain, harbour, accommodate, nourish
    • She fostered a fierce ambition.

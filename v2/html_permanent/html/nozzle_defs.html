nozzle

(ˈnɒzəl )
Definitions
noun1. a projecting pipe or spout from which fluid is discharged2. Also called: propelling nozzle a pipe or duct, esp in a jet engine or rocket, that directs the effluent and accelerates or diffuses the flow to generate thrust3. a socket, such as the part of a candlestick that holds the candle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17 nosle, nosel, diminutive of nose

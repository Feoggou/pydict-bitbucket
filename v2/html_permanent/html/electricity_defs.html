electricity

(ɪlɛkˈtrɪsɪtɪ , ˌiːlɛk-)
Definitions
noun1. any phenomenon associated with stationary or moving electrons, ions, or other charged particles2. the science concerned with electricity3. an electric current or charge ⇒ a motor powered by electricity4. emotional tension or excitement, esp between or among people

Definitions

plutocracy

(pluːˈtɒkrəsɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cies1. the rule or control of society by the wealthy2. a state or government characterized by the rule of the wealthy3. a class that exercises power by virtue of its wealth

Derived Forms

plutocratic (ˌpluːtəˈkrætɪk ) or ˌplutocratical (ˌplutoˈcratical) adjectiveˌplutocratically (ˌplutoˈcratically) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Greek ploutokratia government by the rich, from ploutos wealth + -kratia rule, power

subtitles

(ˈsʌbˌtaɪtəlz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. a written translation superimposed on a film or television programme that has foreign dialogue ⇒ The dialogue is in Spanish, with English subtitles.2. a written transcription superimposed on a film or television programme, for people who are deaf or hard of hearing ⇒ European disability legislation could mean that cinemas have to provide subtitles for deaf people.3. explanatory text on a silent film

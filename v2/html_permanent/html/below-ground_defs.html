Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf something is below ground or below the ground, it is in the ground. ⇒ They have designed a system which pumps up water from 70m below ground. ⇒ Coils buried in tunnels below the ground might soon be used to store large amounts of electricity. →  below

cast iron

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. iron containing so much carbon (1.7 to 4.5 per cent) that it cannot be wrought and must be cast into shapeadjective cast-iron2. made of cast iron3. rigid, strong, or unyielding ⇒ a cast-iron decision4. certain to be effective; absolutely reliable ⇒ I'd like a cast-iron guarantee that any tax increases will be used for health and education.

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. possessing or demonstrating accomplishment, skill, or special training2. (prenominal) involving skill or special training ⇒ a skilled job

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is skilled has the knowledge and ability to do something well. [+ in/at] ⇒ Not all doctors are skilled in helping their patients make choices. ⇒ ...a network of amateur but highly skilled observers of wildlife.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Skilled work can only be done by people who have had some training. ⇒ New industries demanded skilled labour not available locally. ⇒ ...skilled workers, such as plumbers and electricians.

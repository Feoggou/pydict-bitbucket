Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounSomeone who has an apprenticeship works for a fixed period of time for a person who has a particular skill in order to learn the skill. Apprenticeship is the system of learning a skill like this. ⇒ After serving his apprenticeship as a toolmaker, he became a manager. ⇒ ...a period of apprenticeship.

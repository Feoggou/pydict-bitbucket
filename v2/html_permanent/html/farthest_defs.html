farthest

(ˈfɑːðɪst )
Definitions
adverb1. to or at the greatest distance in space or timeadjective2. most distant in space or time3. most extended
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14 ferthest, from ferther further

Northampton

(nɔːˈθæmptən , nɔːθˈhæmp-)
Definitions
noun1. a town in central England, administrative centre of Northamptonshire, on the River Nene: footwear and engineering industries. Pop: 189 474 (2001)2. short for Northamptonshire

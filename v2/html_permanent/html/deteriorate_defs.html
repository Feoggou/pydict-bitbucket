deteriorate

(dɪˈtɪərɪəˌreɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to make or become worse or lower in quality, value, character, etc; depreciate2. (intransitive) to wear away or disintegrate
Derived forms
deˌterioration (deˌterioˈration) noundeteriorative (deˈteriorative) adjective
Word origin
C16: from Late Latin dēteriōrāre, from Latin dēterior worse

coquet

(kəʊˈkɛt , kɒ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -quets, -quetting or -quetted (intransitive)1. to behave flirtatiously2. to dally or trifle
Word origin
C17: from French: a gallant, literally: a little cock, from coq cock

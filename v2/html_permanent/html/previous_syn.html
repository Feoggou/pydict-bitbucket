Synonyms of previous

previous

adjective

  1. earlier, former, past, prior, one-time, preceding, sometime, erstwhile, antecedent, anterior, quondam, ex-
    • He had a daughter from a previous marriage.
    OPPOSITES:  later, following, succeeding, subsequent, consequent
  2. preceding, past, prior, foregoing
    • He recalled what Bob had told him the previous night.
  3. (with to) before, ahead of, preceding, leading up to, earlier than, in advance of, anterior to (rare)
    • a six-month period previous to this date
  4. (informal) premature, too early, too soon, precipitate, untimely, ahead of yourself

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounSomething or someone that is an asset is considered useful or helps a person or organization to be successful. [+ of] ⇒ Her leadership qualities were the greatest asset of the Conservative Party. ⇒ His Republican credentials made him an asset.2. plural nounThe assets of a company or a person are all the things that they own. [business] [+ of] ⇒ By the end of 1989 the group had assets of 3.5 billion francs.

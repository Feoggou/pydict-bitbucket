cower

(ˈkaʊə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(intransitive) to crouch or cringe, as in fear
Word origin
C13: from Middle Low German kūren to lie in wait; related to Swedish kura to lie in wait, Danish kure to squat

verbIf you cower, you bend forward and downwards because you are very frightened. [V] ⇒ The hostages cowered in their seats.

Definitions
COBUILD Key Words for Accounting
noun(Accounting: Investing) Compound interest is interest that is paid both on an original sum of money and on interest that has already been paid on that sum. ⇒ When money is invested at compound interest, each interest payment is reinvested to earn more interest in subsequent periods. ⇒ We compute compound interest on principal and on any interest earned that has not been paid or withdrawn. ⇒ Compound interest is interest that is paid both on an original sum of money and on interest that has already been paid on that sum. ▶  interest

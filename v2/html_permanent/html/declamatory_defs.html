declamatory

(dɪˈklæmətərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. relating to or having the characteristics of a declamation2. merely rhetorical; empty and bombastic
Derived forms
declamatorily (deˈclamatorily) adverb

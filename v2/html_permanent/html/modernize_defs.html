modernize

or modernise (ˈmɒdəˌnaɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (transitive) to make modern in appearance or style ⇒ to modernize a room2. (intransitive) to adopt modern ways, ideas, etc

Derived Forms

ˌmodernization (ˌmoderniˈzation) or modernisation (moderniˈsation) nounmodernˌizer (ˈmodernˌizer) or modernˌiser (ˈmodernˌiser) noun

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
regional note:   in BRIT, also use moderniseverbTo modernize something such as a system or a factory means to change it by replacing old equipment or methods with new ones. [V n] ⇒ ...plans to modernize the refinery.

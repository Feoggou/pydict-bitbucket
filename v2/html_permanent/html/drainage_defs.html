drainage

(ˈdreɪnɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the process or a method of draining2. a system of watercourses or drains3. liquid, sewage, etc, that is drained away

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounDrainage is the system or process by which water or other liquids are drained from a place. ⇒ Line the pots with pebbles to ensure good drainage. ⇒ The drainage system has collapsed because of too much rain.

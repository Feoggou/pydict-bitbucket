enamour

or US enamor (ɪnˈæmə )
Definitions
verb(tr; usually passive; and foll by of) to inspire with love; captivate; charm
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French enamourer, from amour love, from Latin amor

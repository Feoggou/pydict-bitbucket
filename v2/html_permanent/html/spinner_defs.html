spinner

(ˈspɪnə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that spins2. informal a spin doctor3. cricketa. a ball that is bowled with a spinning motionb. a bowler who specializes in bowling such balls4. a streamlined fairing that fits over and revolves with the hub of an aircraft propeller5. a fishing lure with a fin or wing that revolves when drawn through the water6. an angler's name for the mature adult form (imago) of various flies, esp the mayflies. Compare dun2 (sense 3)

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA spinner is a cricketer who makes the ball spin when he or she bowls it so that it changes direction when it hits the ground or the bat.2. countable nounA spinner is a person who makes thread by spinning.

synopsis

(sɪˈnɒpsɪs )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ses (-siːz)a condensation or brief review of a subject; summary

Word Origin

C17: via Late Latin from Greek sunopsis, from syn- + opsis view

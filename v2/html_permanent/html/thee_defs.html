thee

(ðiː )
Definitions
pronoun1. the objective form of thou12. (subjective) rare refers to the person addressed: used mainly by members of the Society of Friends
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English thē; see thou1

thee

Powered by COBUILD
(ðiː ) for learners of English
Definitions
singular pronoun & pronounThee is an old-fashioned, poetic, or religious word for 'you' when you are talking to only one person. It is used as the object of a verb or preposition. ⇒ I miss thee, beloved father.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

perpetuate

(pəˈpɛtjʊˌeɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(transitive) to cause to continue or prevail ⇒ to perpetuate misconceptionsSee note at perpetrate

Derived Forms

perˌpetuation (perˌpetuˈation) or perpetuance (perˈpetuance) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin perpetuāre to continue without interruption, from perpetuus perpetual

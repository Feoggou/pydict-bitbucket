bemoan

(bɪˈməʊn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verbto grieve over (a loss, etc); mourn; lament (esp in the phrase bemoan one's fate)
Word origin
Old English bemǣnan; see be-, moan

Word forms: bemoans , bemoaning , bemoaned
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf you bemoan something, you express sorrow or dissatisfaction about it. [formal] [V n] ⇒ Universities and other research establishments bemoan their lack of funds.

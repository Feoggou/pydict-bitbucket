Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounIn Britain, an MP is a person who has been elected to represent the people from a particular area in the House of Commons. MP is an abbreviation for 'Member of Parliament'. ⇒ ...Colin Pickthall, MP for West Lancashire. ⇒ Several Conservative MPs have voted against the government. → government2. MP is written after someone's name to indicate that they have been elected as an MP. ⇒ ...Margaret Beckett MP.

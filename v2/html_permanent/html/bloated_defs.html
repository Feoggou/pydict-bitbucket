bloated

Powered by COBUILD
(bloʊtɪd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone's body or a part of their body is bloated, it is much larger than normal, usually because it has a lot of liquid or gas inside it. ⇒ ...the bloated body of a dead bullock. ⇒ His face was bloated.2. graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If you feel bloated after eating a large meal, you feel very full and uncomfortable. ⇒ Diners do not want to leave the table feeling bloated.3. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe an organization as bloated, you mean that it is larger and less efficient than it should be. ⇒ ...its massive state apparatus and bloated bureaucracy.

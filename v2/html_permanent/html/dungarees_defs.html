dungarees

(ˌdʌŋɡəˈriːz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. a suit of workman's overalls made of this material consisting of trousers with a bib attached ⇒ a big burly fellow in dungarees, carrying a spanner2. a casual garment resembling this, usually worn by women or children ⇒ a young woman in dungarees3. US trousers

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
plural noun [oft a pair of N]Dungarees are a one-piece garment consisting of trousers, a piece of cloth which covers your chest, and straps which go over your shoulders. In American English, dungarees can also refer to jeans.

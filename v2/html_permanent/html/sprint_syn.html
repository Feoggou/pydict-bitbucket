sprint

verb

  1. run, race, shoot, tear, dash, barrel (along) (informal, mainly US & & Canadian), dart, hare (British, informal), whizz (informal), scamper, hotfoot, go like a bomb (British & & New Zealand, informal), put on a burst of speed, go at top speed
    • He sprinted to the car.

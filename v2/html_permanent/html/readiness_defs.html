readiness

(ˈrɛdɪnɪs )
Definitions
noun1. the state of being ready or prepared, as for use or action2. See in readiness3. willingness or eagerness to do something4. ease or promptness
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

readiness

Powered by COBUILD
(redɪnəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounIf someone is very willing to do something, you can talk about their readiness to do it. ⇒ ...their readiness to co-operate with the new U.S. envoy.2. uncount noun [usu in N]If you do something in readiness for a future event, you do it so that you are prepared for that event. [+ for] ⇒ Security tightened in the capital in readiness for the president's arrival.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

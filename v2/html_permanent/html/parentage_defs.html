(ˈpɛərəntɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. ancestry2. derivation from a particular origin3. rare parenthood

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft of adj N]Your parentage is the identity and origins of your parents. For example, if you are of Greek parentage, your parents are Greek. ⇒ She's a Londoner of mixed parentage (English and Jamaican). ⇒ We are all the result of our parentage and up-bringing.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are smitten, you find someone so attractive that you are or seem to be in love with them. [+ with/by] ⇒ They were totally smitten with each other.2. graded adjective [usu v-link ADJ, oft ADJ with/by n]If you are smitten by something, you are very impressed by it and enthusiastic about it. ⇒ Simon Fraser was smitten by the landscapes he found in the wild southwest of the United States.3. Smitten is the past participle of smite.

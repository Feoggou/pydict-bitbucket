ret

(rɛt )
Definitions
verbWord forms: rets, retting or retted(transitive) to moisten or soak (flax, hemp, jute, etc) to promote bacterial action in order to facilitate separation of the fibres from the woody tissue by beating
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: of Germanic origin; related to Middle Dutch reeten, Swedish röta, German rösten; see rot1

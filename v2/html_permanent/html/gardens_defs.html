gardens

(ˈɡɑːdənz)
Definitions
plural noun1. an area of land used for the cultivation of ornamental plants, etc, that is open to the public, sometimes part of a park ⇒ Kensington Gardens, ⇒ The Gardens are open from 10.30am until 5pm.2. an area of land surrounding a large house, usually planted with grass, trees, flowerbeds, etc ⇒ He took me on a tour of the gardens.

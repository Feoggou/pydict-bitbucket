Shylock

(ˈʃaɪˌlɒk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna heartless or demanding creditor

Word Origin

C19: after Shylock, the name of the heartless usurer in Shakespeare's The Merchant of Venice (1596)
Example sentences containing 'Shylock'
I shrugged like Shylock , not really having a suitable answer to that. James Birrell THE MANANA MANThe Irish, always ready to be allied with genius, swore that the character of Shylock was based on Youghal's Jewish mayor of the time. Salley Vickers INSTANCES OF THE NUMBER 3

Deucalion

(djuːˈkeɪlɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe son of Prometheus and, with his wife Pyrrha, the only survivor on earth of a flood sent by Zeus (Deucalion's flood). Together, they were allowed to repopulate the world by throwing stones over their shoulders, which became men and women

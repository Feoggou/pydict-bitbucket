boar

(bɔː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an uncastrated male pig2. See wild boar
Word origin
Old English bār; related to Old High German bēr

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
language note:   The plural boar can also be used for meaning [sense 1].1. countable nounA boar or a wild boar is a wild pig. ⇒ Wild boar are numerous in the valleys.2. countable nounA boar is a male pig.

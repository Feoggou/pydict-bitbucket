protuberant

(prəˈtjuːbərənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveswelling out from the surrounding surface; bulging

Derived Forms

protuberance (proˈtuberance) or protuberancy (proˈtuberancy) nounprotuberantly (proˈtuberantly) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin prōtūberāre to swell, from pro-1 + tūber swelling

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Protuberant eyes, lips, noses, or teeth stick out more than usual from the face. [formal] ⇒ ...a high-beaked nose and large protuberant eyes.

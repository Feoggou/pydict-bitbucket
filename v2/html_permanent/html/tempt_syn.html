Synonyms of tempt

tempt

verb

  1. attract, draw, appeal to, allure, whet the appetite of, make your mouth water
    • Can I tempt you with a little puff pastry?
  2. entice, lure, lead on, invite, woo, seduce, coax, decoy, inveigle
    • Don't let credit tempt you to buy something you can't afford.
    OPPOSITES:  discourage, deter, put off, inhibit, hinder, dissuade
  3. provoke, try, test, risk, dare, bait, fly in the face of
    • As soon as you talk about never losing, it's tempting fate.

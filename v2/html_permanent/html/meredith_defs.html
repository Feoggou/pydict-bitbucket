Meredith

(ˈmɛrɪdɪθ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun George. 1828–1909, English novelist and poet. His works, notable for their social satire and analysis of character, include the novels Beauchamp's Career (1876) and The Egoist (1879) and the long tragic poem Modern Love (1862)

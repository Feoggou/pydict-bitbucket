Word Frequency

straight-line

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (modifier)1. (of a machine) having components that are arranged in a row or that move in a straight line when in operation2. of or relating to a method of depreciation whereby equal charges are made against gross profit for each year of an asset's expected life

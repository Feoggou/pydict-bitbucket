raiding

(ˈreɪdɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the activity of carrying out raids ⇒ The men had distinguished themselves in battle and raiding.adjective2. taking part in a raid ⇒ for communal defence against raiding Saracens, ⇒ bands of raiding Vikings

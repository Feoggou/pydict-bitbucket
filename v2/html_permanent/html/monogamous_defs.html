Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveSomeone who is monogamous or who has a monogamous relationship has a sexual relationship with only one partner. ⇒ Do you believe that men are not naturally monogamous? ⇒ She wouldn't want to be in a monogamous relationship.2. adjectiveMonogamous animals have only one sexual partner during their lives or during each mating season.

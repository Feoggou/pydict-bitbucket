hoodwink

(ˈhʊdˌwɪŋk )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to dupe; trick2. obsolete to cover or hide
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hoodwinker (ˈhoodˌwinker) noun

Word Origin

C16: originally, to cover the eyes with a hood, blindfold

Synonyms of hasty

hasty

adjective

  1. speedy, fast, quick, prompt, rapid, fleet, hurried, urgent, swift, brisk, expeditious
    • They need to make a hasty escape.
    OPPOSITES:  slow, leisurely
  2. brief, short, quick, passing, rushed, fleeting, superficial, cursory, perfunctory, transitory
    • After the hasty meal, they took up their positions.
    OPPOSITES:  long, protracted
  3. rash, premature, reckless, precipitate, impulsive, headlong, foolhardy, thoughtless, impetuous, indiscreet, imprudent, heedless, incautious, unduly quick
    • Let's not be hasty.
    OPPOSITES:  detailed, careful, cautious, thorough, thoughtful
  4. impatient, excited, passionate, fiery, irritable, snappy, brusque, irascible, hot-headed, hot-tempered, quick-tempered OPPOSITE:  dispassionate

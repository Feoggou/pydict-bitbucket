steppe

(stɛp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(often plural) an extensive grassy plain usually without trees. Compare prairie, pampas

Word Origin

C17: from Old Russian step lowland

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounSteppes are large areas of flat grassy land where there are no trees, especially the area that stretches from Eastern Europe across the south of the former Soviet Union to Siberia.

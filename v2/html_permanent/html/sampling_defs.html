sampling

(ˈsɑːmplɪŋ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the process of selecting a random sample2. a variant of sample (sense 2)3. the process of taking a short extract from (a record) and mixing it into a different backing track4. a process in which a continuous electrical signal is approximately represented by a series of discrete values, usually regularly spaced

For the first hour or so, we stood around, sampling the fare, exchanging pleasantries, joining in. Edward Docx THE CALLIGRAPHER/ Superintendent Sutcliffe was sampling election meetings.

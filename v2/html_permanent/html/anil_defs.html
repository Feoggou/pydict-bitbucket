anil

(ˈænɪl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna leguminous West Indian shrub, Indigofera suffruticosa: a source of indigo. Also called: indigo
Word origin
C16: from Portuguese, from Arabic an-nīl, the indigo, from Sanskrit nīla dark blue

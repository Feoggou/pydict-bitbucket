envelop

(ɪnˈvɛləp )
Definitions
verb -lops, -loping or -loped (transitive)1. to wrap or enclose in or as if in a covering2. to conceal or obscure, as from sight or understanding ⇒ a plan enveloped in mystery3. to surround or partially surround (an enemy force)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

envelopment (enˈvelopment) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Old French envoluper, from en-1 + voluper, voloper, of obscure origin

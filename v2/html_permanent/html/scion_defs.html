scion

(ˈsaɪən ) or sient (ˈsaɪənt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a descendant, heir, or young member of a family2. a shoot or twig of a plant used to form a graft

Word Origin

C14: from Old French cion, of Germanic origin; compare Old High German chīnan to sprout

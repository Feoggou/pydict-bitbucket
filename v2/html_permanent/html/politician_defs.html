politician

(ˌpɒlɪˈtɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person actively engaged in politics, esp a full-time professional member of a deliberative assembly2. a person who is experienced or skilled in the art or science of politics, government, or administration; statesman3. derogatory, mainly US a person who engages in politics out of a wish for personal gain, as realized by holding a public office

in mourning

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
phraseIf you are in mourning, you are dressed or behaving in a particular way because someone you love or respect has died. ⇒ Yesterday the whole of Greece was in mourning. ⇒ The boys wore black tail coats in mourning for George III. →  mourning

sardine1

(sɑːˈdiːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -dines or -dine1. any of various small marine food fishes of the herring family, esp a young pilchard. See also sild2. See like sardines
Word origin
C15: via Old French from Latin sardīna, diminutive of sarda a fish suitable for pickling

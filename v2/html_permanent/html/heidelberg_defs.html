Heidelberg

(ˈhaɪdəlˌbɜːɡ , German ˈhaidəlbɛrk)
Definitions
nouna city in SW Germany, in NW Baden-Württemberg on the River Neckar: capital of the Palatinate from the 13th century until 1719; famous castle (begun in the 12th century) and university (1386), the oldest in Germany. Pop: 142 959 (2003 est)

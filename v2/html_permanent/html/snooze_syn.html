snooze (informal)

noun

  1. doze, nap, kip (British, slang), siesta, catnap, forty winks (informal) • The bird is enjoying a snooze.

verb

  1. doze, drop off (informal), nap, kip (British, slang), nod off (informal), catnap, drowse, take forty winks (informal) • He snoozed in front of the television.

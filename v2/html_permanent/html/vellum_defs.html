vellum

(ˈvɛləm )
Definitions
noun1. a fine parchment prepared from the skin of a calf, kid, or lamb2. a work printed or written on vellum3. a creamy coloured heavy paper resembling vellumadjective4. made of or resembling vellum5. (of a book) bound in vellum
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French velin, from velin of a calf, from veel veal

ferity

(ˈfɛrɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties rare1. the state of being wild or uncultivated2. savagery; ferocity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin feritās, from ferus savage, untamed

Definitions

go along

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. phrasal verbIf you go along to a meeting, event, or place, you attend or visit it. [V P + to] ⇒ I went along to the meeting. [V P] ⇒ You should go along and have a look.2. phrasal verb [usu cont]If you describe how something is going along, you describe how it is progressing. [V P adv] ⇒ Things were going along fairly well. →  go

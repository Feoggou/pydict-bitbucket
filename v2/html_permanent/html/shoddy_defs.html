shoddy

(ˈʃɒdɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -dier or -diest1. imitating something of better quality2. of poor quality; trashy3. made of shoddy materialnoun plural -dies4. a yarn or fabric made from wool waste or clippings5. anything of inferior quality that is designed to simulate superior quality

Derived Forms

shoddily (ˈshoddily) adverbshoddiness (ˈshoddiness) noun

Word Origin

C19: of unknown origin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Shoddy work or a shoddy product has been done or made carelessly or badly. ⇒ I'm normally quick to complain about shoddy service. ⇒ American customers no longer tolerate shoddy goods.shoddily graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ These products are shoddily produced. ⇒ ...shoddily-built cars.

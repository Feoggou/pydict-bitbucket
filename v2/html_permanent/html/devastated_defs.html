COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If you are devastated by something, you are very shocked and upset by it. ⇒ Bishop Daly said he was devastated by news of the Cardinal's death. ⇒ Teresa was devastated, her dreams shattered.

watering

(ˈwɔːtərɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. horticulture the provision of water to plants ⇒ Frequent watering is needed.2. spraying or moistening ⇒ The watering of pitches was officially sanctioned except in the months of November to February inclusive.3. See watering of the eyes

enthral

or US enthrall (ɪnˈθrɔːl )
Definitions
verb -thrals or US -thralls, -thralling or -thralled (transitive)1. to hold spellbound; enchant; captivate2. obsolete to hold as thrall; enslave
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

enthraller (enˈthraller) nounenthralment (enˈthralment) or US enthrallment (enˈthrallment) noun

Word Origin

C16: from en-1 + thrall

golf club

Definitions
noun1. any of various long-shafted clubs with wood or metal heads used to strike a golf ball2. a. an association of golf players, usually having its own course and facilitiesb. the premises of such an association

Word forms: golf clubs
Definitions
1. countable nounA golf club is a long, thin, metal stick with a piece of wood or metal at one end that you use to hit the ball in golf.2. countable nounA golf club is a social organization which provides a golf course and a building to meet in for its members.

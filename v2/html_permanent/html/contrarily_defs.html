Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb1. (kənˈtrɛərɪlɪ) in a perverse or obstinate manner2. (ˈkɒntrərɪlɪ) on the other hand; from the opposite point of view3. (ˈkɒntrərɪlɪ) in an opposite, adverse, or unexpected way

wings

(ˈwɪŋz)
Definitions
plural noun1. the space offstage to the right or left of the acting area in a theatre ⇒ Most nights I watched the start of the play from the wings.2. an insignia in the form of stylized wings worn by a qualified aircraft pilot ⇒ In 1945, Heath earned his wings to fly fighter planes., ⇒ He had no sooner got his wings than the Korean conflict broke out.3. See clip someone's wings4. See in the wings5. See on wings6. See spread your wings

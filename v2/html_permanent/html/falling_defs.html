falling

(ˈfɔːlɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. declining; deteriorating ⇒ falling living standards and emerging mass unemployment, ⇒ falling prices/ profits/inflation2. coming down from a higher level ⇒ 'Beware falling rocks', ⇒ the sound of falling water, ⇒ falling leaves, ⇒ Twenty people were injured by falling masonry.

sterling

(ˈstɜːlɪŋ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. British money ⇒ pound sterlingb. (as modifier) ⇒ sterling reserves2. the official standard of fineness of British coins: for gold 0.91666 and for silver 0.9253. a. short for sterling silverb. (as modifier) ⇒ a sterling bracelet4. an article or articles manufactured from sterling silver5. a former British silver pennyadjective6. (prenominal) genuine and reliable; first-class ⇒ sterling quality

Word Origin

C13: probably from Old English steorra star + -ling1; referring to a small star on early Norman pennies; related to Old French esterlin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounSterling is the money system of Great Britain. ⇒ The stamps had to be paid for in sterling.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Sterling means very good in quality; used to describe someone's work or character. [formal, approval] ⇒ Those are sterling qualities to be admired in anyone. ⇒ ...his years of sterling service.

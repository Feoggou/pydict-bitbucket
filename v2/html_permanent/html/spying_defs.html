spying

(ˈspaɪɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe activity of trying to obtain secret information from rival countries, organizations, companies, etc ⇒ a ten-year sentence for spying
Example sentences containing 'spying'
Repelled by the thought of him spying on my neighbours dressed in a floral nightie, I decided to give him a wide berth. Isabel Wolff RESCUING ROSEAt least my spying had made me forget my own concerns for a short time. Robin Hobb THE GOLDEN FOOL: BOOK TWO OF THE TAWNY MANOne will have to expect that the servants will be spying on us. Gaskin, Catherine THE AMBASSADOR'S WOMEN

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
language note:   In British English, the spelling anymore is sometimes considered incorrect, and any more is used instead.adverb [ADV after v]If something does not happen or is not true anymore, it has stopped happening or is no longer true. any longer ⇒ I don't ride my motorbike much anymore. ⇒ I couldn't trust him anymore. ⇒ People are not interested in movies anymore.

penetration

(ˌpɛnɪˈtreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or an instance of penetrating2. the ability or power to penetrate3. keen insight or perception4. military an offensive manoeuvre that breaks through an enemy's defensive position5. Also called: market penetration the proportion of the total number of potential purchasers of a product or service who either are aware of its existence or actually buy it6. another name for depth of field
Example sentences containing 'penetration'
If the Americans, for instance, have infiltrated penetration agents into the Kabul records departments your cover could be exposed.

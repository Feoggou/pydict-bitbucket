evaluate

(ɪˈvæljʊˌeɪt )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to ascertain or set the amount or value of2. to judge or assess the worth of; appraise3. mathematics, logic to determine the unique member of the range of a function corresponding to a given member of its domain
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

evaluation (eˌvaluˈation) nounevaluator (eˈvaluˌator) noun

Word Origin

C19: back formation from evaluation, from French, from evaluer to evaluate; see value

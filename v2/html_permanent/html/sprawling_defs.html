sprawling

(ˈsprɔːlɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. sitting or lying in an ungainly manner with one's limbs spread out2. spreading out in a straggling fashion ⇒ a sprawling and semi-abandoned chateau, ⇒ The sprawling city contained some 4m people., ⇒ He keeps forgetting the words and the song is a sprawling mess.

fix up

Definitions
verb (tr, adverb)1. to arrange ⇒ let's fix up a date2. (often foll by with) to provide ⇒ I'm sure we can fix you up with a room3. informal to repair or rearrange ⇒ to fix up one's house

fix up

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. phrasal verbIf you fix something up, you arrange it. [V P n] ⇒ I fixed up an appointment to see her. [V P n (not pron)] ⇒ Accommodation is never fixed up in advance. [Also V n P]2. phrasal verbIf you fix something up, you do work that is necessary in order to make it more suitable or attractive. [V P n] ⇒ I've fixed up Matthew's old room. [V P n (not pron)] ⇒ The whole block is being fixed up. [Also V n P]3. phrasal verbIf you fix someone up with something they need, you provide it for them. [V n P with n] ⇒ We'll fix him up with a tie. [be V-ed P + with] ⇒ He was fixed up with a job. [Also V n P] →  fix

large white

Definitions
noun1. Also called: cabbage white a large white butterfly, Pieris brassicae, with scanty black markings, the larvae of which feed on brassica leaves2. (often capitals) a white large-bodied breed of pig commonly kept for pork, bacon, and for fattening

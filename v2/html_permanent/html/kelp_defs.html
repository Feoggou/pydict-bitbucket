kelp

(kɛlp )
Definitions
noun1. any large brown seaweed, esp any in the order Laminariales2. the ash of such seaweed, used as a source of iodine and potash
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: of unknown origin

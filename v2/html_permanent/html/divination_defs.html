divination

(ˌdɪvɪˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the art, practice, or gift of discerning or discovering future events or unknown things, as though by supernatural powers2. a prophecy3. a presentiment or guess
Derived forms
divinatory (dɪˈvɪnətərɪ , -trɪ) adjective

epicurean

(ˌɛpɪkjʊˈriːən )
Definitions
adjective1. devoted to sensual pleasures, esp food and drink; hedonistic2. suitable for an epicure ⇒ an epicurean feastnoun3. an epicure; gourmet
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Epicurean

(ˌɛpɪkjʊˈriːən )
Definitions

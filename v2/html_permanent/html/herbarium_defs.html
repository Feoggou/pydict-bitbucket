herbarium

(hɜːˈbɛərɪəm )
Definitions
noun plural -iums or -ia (-ɪə)1. a collection of dried plants that are mounted and classified systematically2. a building, room, etc, in which such a collection is kept
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

herbarial (herˈbarial) adjective

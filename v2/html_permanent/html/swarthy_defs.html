swarthy

(ˈswɔːðɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: swarthier or swarthiestdark-hued or dark-complexioned. Also (rare or obsolete): swarty

Derived Forms

swarthily (ˈswarthily) adverbswarthiness (ˈswarthiness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from obsolete swarty, from swart + -y1

respecter

(rɪˈspɛktə )
Definitions
noun1. a person who respects someone or something2. See no respecter of persons

respecter

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪspektəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: respecters
Definitions
1. countable nounIf you say that someone is a respecter of something such as a belief or idea, you mean that they behave in a way which shows that they have a high opinion of it. [+ of] ⇒ Ford was a respecter of proprieties and liked to see things done properly.2. →  no respecter of

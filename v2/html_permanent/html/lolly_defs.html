lolly

(ˈlɒlɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -lies1. an informal word for lollipop2. British short for ice lolly3. British, Australian and New Zealand a slang word for money4. Australian and New Zealand informal a sweet, esp a boiled one5. See do the lolly
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

shortened from lollipop

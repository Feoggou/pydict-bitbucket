ortolan

(ˈɔːtələn )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: ortolan bunting a brownish Old World bunting, Emberiza hortulana, regarded as a delicacy2. any of various other small birds eaten as delicacies, esp the bobolink
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: via French from Latin hortulānus, from hortulus, diminutive of hortus garden

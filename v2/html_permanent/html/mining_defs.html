mining

(ˈmaɪnɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act, process, or industry of extracting coal, ores, etc, from the earth2. military the process of laying mines

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounMining is the industry and activities connected with getting valuable or useful minerals from the ground, for example coal, diamonds, or gold. ⇒ ...traditional industries such as coal mining and steel making. ⇒ He was born at Aberdare in South Wales, the son of a mining engineer.

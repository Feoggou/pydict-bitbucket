crayon

(ˈkreɪən , -ɒn)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small stick or pencil of charcoal, wax, clay, or chalk mixed with coloured pigment2. a drawing made with crayonsverb3. to draw or colour with crayons
Derived forms
crayoner (ˈcrayoner) or crayonist (ˈcrayonist) noun
Word origin
C17: from French, from craie, from Latin crēta chalk

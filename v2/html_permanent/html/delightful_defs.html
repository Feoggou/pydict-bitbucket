Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something or someone as delightful, you mean they are very pleasant. ⇒ It was the most delightful garden I had ever seen. ⇒ She remembered Lucy as beautiful, charming and absolutely delightful.delightfully graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj/-ed] ⇒ ...a delightfully refreshing cologne. ⇒ ...fine cheese and delightfully packaged foie gras.

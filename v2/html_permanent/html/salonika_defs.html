Salonika

(səˈlɑnɪkə ; ˌ sæləˈnikə )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
1.  seaport in Macedonia, N Greece, at the head of the Gulf of Salonika: pop. 384,000. see also Thessaloniki2.  Gulf ofN arm of the Aegean Sea: c. 70 mi (113 km) long

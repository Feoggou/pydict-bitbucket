Trinitarian

(ˌtrɪnɪˈtɛərɪən )
Definitions
noun1. a person who believes in the doctrine of the Trinity2. a member of the Holy Trinity. See Trinity (sense 3)adjective3. of or relating to the doctrine of the Trinity or those who uphold it4. of or relating to the Holy Trinity

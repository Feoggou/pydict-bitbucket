flu

(fluː )
Definitions
noun informal1. See the flu2. any of various viral infections, esp a respiratory or intestinal infection

flu

Powered by COBUILD
(fluː ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [oft the N]Flu is an illness which is similar to a bad cold but more serious. It often makes you feel very weak and makes your muscles hurt. ⇒ I got flu. ⇒ He had come down with the flu.

grist

(ɡrɪst )
Definitions
noun1. a. grain intended to be or that has been groundb. the quantity of such grain processed in one grinding2. brewing malt grains that have been cleaned and cracked3. See grist to the mill
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English grīst; related to Old Saxon grist-grimmo gnashing of teeth, Old High German grist-grimmōn

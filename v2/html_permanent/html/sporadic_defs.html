sporadic

(spəˈrædɪk ) or archaic sporadical (spəˈrædɪkəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. occurring at irregular points in time; intermittent ⇒ sporadic firing2. scattered; isolated ⇒ a sporadic disease

Derived Forms

sporadically (spoˈradically) adverbsporadicalness (spoˈradicalness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Medieval Latin sporadicus, from Greek sporadikos, from sporas scattered; related to Greek speirein to sow; see spore

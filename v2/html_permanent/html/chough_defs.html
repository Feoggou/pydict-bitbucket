chough

(tʃʌf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a large black passerine bird, Pyrrhocorax pyrrhocorax, of parts of Europe, Asia, and Africa, with a long downward-curving red bill: family Corvidae (crows)2. See alpine chough
Word origin
C14: of uncertain origin; probably related to Old French cauwe, Old English cēo

ff

Definitions
symbol for1. folios2. following (pages, lines, etc)3. music fortissimo: an instruction to play very loudly

ff.

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. In a book or magazine, when ff. is written it refers to the page or line mentioned and two or more pages or lines after it. ⇒ ...p. 173 ff.2. In a piece of music, ff is a written abbreviation for fortissimo.

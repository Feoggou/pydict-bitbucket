rediscover

(ˌriːdɪˈskʌvə )
Definitions
verb (transitive)to discover (something) again ⇒ rediscover the joys of life

Word forms: rediscovers , rediscovering , rediscovered
Definitions
verbIf you rediscover something good or valuable that you had forgotten or lost, you become aware of it again or find it again. [V n] ⇒ ...a one-time rebel who had rediscovered his faith. [V n] ⇒ Some of Naomi's earlier writings have been rediscovered in the 1980s and 1990s. [V-ed] ⇒ Despite the newly rediscovered friendship, there's no doubt that more difficulties lie ahead.

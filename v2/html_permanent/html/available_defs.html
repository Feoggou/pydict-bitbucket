Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. obtainable or accessible; capable of being made use of; at hand2. US politics derogatory suitable for public office, usually as a result of having an inoffensive character ⇒ Smith was a particularly available candidate

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveIf something you want or need is available, you can find it or obtain it. ⇒ Since 1978, the amount of money available to buy books has fallen by 17%. [+ for] ⇒ There are three small boats available for hire. ⇒ According to the best available information, the facts are these.availability (əveɪləbɪlɪti ) uncount noun [+ of] ⇒ ...the easy availability of guns.2. adjective [v-link ADJ]Someone who is available is not busy and is therefore free to talk to you or to do a particular task. [+ for] ⇒ Mr Leach is on holiday and was not available for comment.

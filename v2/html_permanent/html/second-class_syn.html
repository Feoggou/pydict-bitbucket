second-class

adjective

  1. inferior, lesser, second-best, unimportant, second-rate, low-class • Too many airlines treat our children as second-class citizens.
  2. mediocre, second-rate, mean, middling, ordinary, inferior, indifferent, commonplace, insignificant, so-so (informal), outclassed, uninspiring, undistinguished, uninspired, bog-standard (British & & Irish, slang), no great shakes (informal), déclassé, half-pie (New Zealand, informal), fair to middling (informal) • a second-class education

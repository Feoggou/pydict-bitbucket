imbecility

(ˌɪmbəˈsɪləti ) in American English
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural ˌimbeˈcilities1.  the state of being an imbecile2.  behavior like that of an imbecile; great foolishness or stupidity3.  an imbecile act or remark

Word Origin

Fr imbécillité < L imbecillitas

occupied

(ˈɒkjʊpaɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. busy doing or thinking about something ⇒ the constantly occupied shop assistant, ⇒ I was thinking of ways to keep him occupied for three or four hours., ⇒ keep the brain occupied!, ⇒ I had forgotten all about it because I had been so occupied with other things., ⇒ The Prime Minister was occupied with important matters of state., ⇒ she was fully occupied with driving2. in use and so not available for anyone else ⇒ At the pub, his usual seat was occupied., ⇒ Every table in the dining room was occupied., ⇒ That seat is occupied.3. politics under the control of an occupying military force ⇒ in the occupied territory

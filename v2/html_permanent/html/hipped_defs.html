hipped1

(hɪpt )
Definitions
adjective1. a. having a hip or hipsb. (in combination) ⇒ broad-hipped, ⇒ low-hipped2. (esp of cows, sheep, reindeer, elk, etc) having an injury to the hip, such as a dislocation of the bones3. architecture having a hip or hips

hipped2

adjective(often postpositive; foll by on) US and Canadian old-fashioned, slang very enthusiastic (about)

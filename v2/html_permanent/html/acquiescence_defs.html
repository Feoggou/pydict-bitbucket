acquiescence

Powered by COBUILD
(ækwiesəns )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounAcquiescence is agreement to do what someone wants, or acceptance of what they do even though you do not agree with it. [formal] ⇒ Deirdre smiled her acquiescence. [+ in/to] ⇒ Fear of rapid social change made temporary acquiescence in slavery tolerable in the South.

Ellel was confused, almost disappointed by this ready acquiescence. Tepper, Sheri S. A PLAGUE OF ANGELSWhich meant he had two sons, both of whom had been taken from him with his passive acquiescence.

exaggerated

(ɪɡˈzædʒəˌreɪtɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. unduly or excessively magnified; enlarged beyond truth or reasonableness2. pathology abnormally enlarged ⇒ an exaggerated spleen
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

exaggeratedly (exˈaggerˌatedly) adverb

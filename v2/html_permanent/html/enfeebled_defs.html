enfeebled

(ɪnˈfiːbəld)
Definitions
adjective1. (of a person) having been physically weakened ⇒ enfeebled by our sedentary lifestyles2. (of a person, organization, system, etc) having been weakened in some way ⇒ He finds himself politically enfeebled., ⇒ the already enfeebled newspaper, ⇒ This would mean that an extra burden would be put on our already enfeebled economy.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

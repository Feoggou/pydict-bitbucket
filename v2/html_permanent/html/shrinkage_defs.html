shrinkage

(ˈʃrɪŋkɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or fact of shrinking2. the amount by which anything decreases in size, value, weight, etc3. the loss in body weight during shipment and preparation of livestock for marketing as meat4. the loss of merchandise in a retail store through theft or damage

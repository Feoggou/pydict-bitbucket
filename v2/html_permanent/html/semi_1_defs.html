Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
pronunciation note:   The pronunciation (semaɪ) can also be used for meaning [sense 3].1. countable nounA semi is a semi-detached house. [British, informal]2. countable noun [usu pl]In a sporting competition, the semis are the semi-finals. [British, informal] ⇒ He reached the semis after beating Lendl in the quarterfinal.3. countable nounA semi is a semi-trailer. [US]

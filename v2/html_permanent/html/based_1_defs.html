Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [v-link ADJ]If you are based in a particular place, that is the place where you live or do most of your work. See also base. ⇒ Both firms are based in Kent. ⇒ Based on the edge of Lake Matt, Sunbeam Yachts started boatbuilding in 1870.

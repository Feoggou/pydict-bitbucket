turbot

(ˈtɜːbət )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -bot or -bots1. a European flatfish, Scophthalmus maximus, having a pale brown speckled scaleless body covered with tubercles: family Bothidae. It is highly valued as a food fish2. any of various similar or related fishes

Word Origin

C13: from Old French tourbot, from Medieval Latin turbō, from Latin: spinning top, from a fancied similarity in shape; see turbit, turbine

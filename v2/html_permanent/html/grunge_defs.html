grunge

(ɡrʌndʒ )
Definitions
noun1. US slang dirt or rubbish2. a style of rock music originating in the US in the late 1980s, featuring a distorted guitar sound3. a deliberately untidy and uncoordinated fashion style
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: possibly a coinage imitating grime + sludge

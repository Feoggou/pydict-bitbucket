resemblance

(rɪˈzɛmbləns )
Definitions
noun1. the state or quality of resembling; likeness or similarity in nature, appearance, etc2. the degree or extent to which or the respect in which a likeness exists3. something resembling something else; semblance; likeness
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

resemblant (reˈsemblant) adjective

(rɪzembləns ) for learners of English

Definitions
variable noun [oft adj N]If there is a resemblance between two people or things, they are similar to each other. [+ between] ⇒ There was a remarkable resemblance between him and Pete. ⇒ Our tour prices bore little resemblance to those in the holiday brochures. [Also + to]

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable noun [oft n N]Addiction is the condition of taking harmful drugs and being unable to stop taking them. ⇒ She helped him fight his drug addiction. [+ to] ⇒ ...long-term addiction to nicotine.2. variable nounAn addiction to something is a very strong desire or need for it. [+ to] ⇒ He needed money to feed his addiction to gambling. ⇒ I discovered an addiction to housework which I had never felt before.

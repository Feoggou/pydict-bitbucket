Cinderella

(ˌsɪndəˈrɛlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a girl who achieves fame after being obscure2. a. a poor, neglected, or unsuccessful person or thingb. (as modifier) ⇒ a Cinderella service within the NHS3. (modifier) relating to dramatic success ⇒ a Cinderella story
Word origin
C19: after Cinderella, the heroine of a fairy tale who is aided by a fairy godmother

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu sing, oft N n]If you describe a person or organization as a Cinderella, you mean that they receive very little attention and that they deserve to receive more. [+ of] ⇒ It is a Cinderella of charities, and needs more help.

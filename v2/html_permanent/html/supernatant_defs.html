supernatant

(ˌsuːpəˈneɪtənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. floating on the surface or over something2. chemistry (of a liquid) lying above a sediment or settled precipitate

Derived Forms

ˌsupernatation (ˌsupernaˈtation) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin supernatāre to float, from super- + natāre to swim

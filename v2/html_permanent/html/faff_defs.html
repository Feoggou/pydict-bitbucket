faff

(fæf )
Definitions
verb1. (intransitive; often foll by about) British informal to dither or fussnoun2. a thing that is awkward or time-consuming to do ⇒ Changing the bulb was a bit of a faff.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: of obscure origin

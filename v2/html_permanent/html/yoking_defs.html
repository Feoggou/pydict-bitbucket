yoking

(ˈjəʊkɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. mining a piece of wood tied together with others in a mine to reinforce the earth2. a land parcel measured in units of about 16 yards3. a shift of work at the plough or with a cart4. the act of taking part in a contest5. the act of joining animals (esp oxen) together under a wooden beam

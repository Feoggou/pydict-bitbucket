envious

(ˈɛnvɪəs )
Definitions
adjectivefeeling, showing, or resulting from envy

Derived Forms

enviously (ˈenviously) adverbenviousness (ˈenviousness) noun

Word Origin

C13: from Anglo-Norman, ultimately from Latin invidiōsus full of envy, invidious; see envy

equipment

(ɪˈkwɪpmənt )
Definitions
noun1. an act or instance of equipping2. the items so provided3. a set of tools, devices, kit, etc, assembled for a specific purpose, such as a soldier's kit and weapons
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
noun(Electrical engineering: General) Electrical equipment is material, fittings, devices, and appliances that are used as a part of, or in connection with, an electrical installation. ⇒ When using portable equipment, the risk of electric shock can be reduced by using a low voltage supply. ⇒ As with all electrical equipment, it is important to keep fuses and breakers from getting wet. ⇒ Electrical equipment is material, fittings, devices, and appliances that are used as a part of, or in connection with, an electrical installation.

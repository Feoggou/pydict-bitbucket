Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnjuːmərəbəl , US -nuː- ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]Innumerable means very many, or too many to be counted. [formal] ⇒ He has invented innumerable excuses, told endless lies.

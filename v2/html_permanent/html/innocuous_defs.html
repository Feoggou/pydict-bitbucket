innocuous

(ɪˈnɒkjʊəs )
Definitions
adjectivehaving little or no adverse or harmful effect; harmless
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

innocuously (inˈnocuously) adverbinnocuousness (inˈnocuousness) or innocuity (ˌɪnəˈkjuːɪtɪ ) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin innocuus harmless, from in-1 + nocēre to harm

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is innocuous is not at all harmful or offensive. [formal] ⇒ Both mushrooms look innocuous but are in fact deadly. ⇒ Even seemingly innocuous words are offensive in certain contexts.

compass

noun

  1. range, field, area, reach, scope, sphere, limit, stretch, bound, extent, zone, boundary, realm • Within the compass of a book of this size, such a comprehensive survey is not practicable.
  2. range, round, circle, circuit, enclosure, circumference

verb

  1. surround, invest (rare), enclose, blockade, encompass, besiege, beset, encircle, circumscribe, hem in, environ • The Christians fled when they saw the city being compassed with armies.

Scorpio

(ˈskɔːpɪˌəʊ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: the Scorpion astrologya. the eighth sign of the zodiac, symbol ♏, having a fixed water classification and ruled by the planet Mars and the dwarf planet Pluto. The sun is in this sign between about Oct 23 and Nov 21b. a person born during a period when the sun is in this sign2. astronomy another name for Scorpiusadjective3. astrology born under or characteristic of Scorpio

Word Origin

Latin: scorpion

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounScorpio is one of the twelve signs of the zodiac. Its symbol is a scorpion. People who are born approximately between the 23rd of October and the 21st of November come under this sign.2. countable nounA Scorpio is a person whose sign of the zodiac is Scorpio.

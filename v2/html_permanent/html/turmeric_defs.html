turmeric

(ˈtɜːmərɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a tropical Asian zingiberaceous plant, Curcuma longa, having yellow flowers and an aromatic underground stem2. the powdered stem of this plant, used as a condiment and as a yellow dye3. any of several other plants with similar roots

Word Origin

C16: from Old French terre merite, from Medieval Latin terra merita, literally: meritorious earth, name applied for obscure reasons to curcuma

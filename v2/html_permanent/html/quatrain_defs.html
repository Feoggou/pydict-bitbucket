quatrain

(ˈkwɒtreɪn )
Definitions
nouna stanza or poem of four lines, esp one having alternate rhymes
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from French, from quatre four, from Latin quattuor
Example sentences containing 'quatrain'
Nevertheless," murmured Palli dryly," one feels that your quatrain yesterday morning to Lady Betriz's nose was a tactical mistake. Lois McMaster Bujold THE CURSE OF CHALION

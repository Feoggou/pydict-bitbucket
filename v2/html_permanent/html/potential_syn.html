Synonyms of potential

potential

adjective

  1. possible, future, likely, promising, budding, embryonic, undeveloped, unrealized, probable
    • potential customers
  2. hidden, possible, inherent, dormant, latent
    • We are aware of the potential dangers.

noun

  1. ability, possibilities, capacity, capability, the makings, what it takes (informal), aptitude, wherewithal, potentiality
    • The boy has potential.

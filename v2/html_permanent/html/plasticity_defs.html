plasticity

(plæˈstɪsɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the quality of being plastic or able to be moulded2. (in pictorial art) the quality of depicting space and form so that they appear three-dimensional
Example sentences containing 'plasticity'
The wretched features in front of her became sorrowful, something in their plasticity almost making a caricature of that emotion. Clive Barker THE GREAT AND SECRET SHOW

Similar Words

pliability, flexibility, suppleness, malleability, pliableness

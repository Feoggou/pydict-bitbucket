Synonyms of emergency

emergency

noun

  1. crisis, danger, difficulty, accident, disaster, necessity, pinch, plight, scrape (informal), strait, catastrophe, predicament, calamity, extremity, quandary, exigency, critical situation, urgent situation, MergNC (text messaging)
    • He has the ability to deal with emergencies quickly.

adjective

  1. urgent, crisis, immediate, MergNC (text messaging)
    • She made an emergency appointment.
  2. alternative, extra, additional, substitute, replacement, temporary, makeshift, stopgap, MergNC (text messaging)
    • The plane is carrying emergency supplies.

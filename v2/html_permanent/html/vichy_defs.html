Vichy

(French viʃi, English ˈviːʃiː)
Definitions
nouna town and spa in central France, on the River Allier: seat of the collaborationist government under Marshal Pétain (1940–44); mineral waters bottled for export. Pop: 26 555 (2006). Latin name: Vicus Calidus (ˈviːkəs ˈkælɪdəs )

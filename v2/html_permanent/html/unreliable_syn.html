Synonyms of unreliable

unreliable

adjective

  1. inaccurate, unconvincing, implausible, mistaken, false, uncertain, fake, deceptive, erroneous, unsound, fallible, specious, delusive
    • The figures were unreliable because the sample sizes were too small.
    OPPOSITES:  accurate, infallible
  2. undependable, irresponsible, untrustworthy, unstable, treacherous, disreputable, not conscientious
    • She had proved to be an unreliable witness and had lied to police.
    OPPOSITES:  reliable, dependable, trustworthy, responsible, stable, conscientious

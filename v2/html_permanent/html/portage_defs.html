portage

(ˈpɔːtɪdʒ , French pɔrtaʒ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of carrying; transport2. the cost of carrying or transporting3. the act or process of transporting boats, supplies, etc, overland between navigable waterways4. the route overland used for such transportverb5. to transport (boats, supplies, etc) overland between navigable waterways

Word Origin

C15: from French, from Old French porter to carry

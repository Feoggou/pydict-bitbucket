creel

(kriːl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a wickerwork basket, esp one used to hold fish2. a wickerwork trap for catching lobsters, etc3. the framework on a spinning machine that holds the bobbins4. West Yorkshire dialect a wooden frame suspended from a ceiling, used for drying clothes
Word origin
C15: from Scottish, of obscure origin

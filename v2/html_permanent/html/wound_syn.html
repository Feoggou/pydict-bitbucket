Synonyms of wound

wound

noun

  1. injury, cut, damage, hurt, harm, slash, trauma (pathology), gash, lesion, laceration
    • Six soldiers are reported to have died of their wounds.
  2. (often plural) trauma, injury, shock, pain, offence, slight, torture, distress, insult, grief, torment, anguish, heartbreak, pang, sense of loss
    • Her experiences have left deep psychological wounds.

verb

  1. injure, cut, hit, damage, wing, hurt, harm, slash, pierce, irritate, gash, lacerate
    • The driver of the bus was wounded by shrapnel.
  2. offend, shock, pain, hurt, distress, annoy, sting, grieve, mortify, cut to the quick, hurt the feelings of, traumatize
    • He was deeply wounded by the treachery of his closest friends.

QUOTATION:
what wound did ever heal but by degrees?William Shakespeare – Othello

wind2

verb

  1. meander, turn, bend, twist, curve, snake, ramble, twist and turn, deviate, zigzag
    • The Moselle winds through some 160 miles of tranquil countryside.
  2. wrap, twist, reel, curl, loop, coil, twine, furl, wreathe
    • She wound the sash round her waist.
  3. coil, curl, spiral, encircle, twine
    • The snake wound around my leg.

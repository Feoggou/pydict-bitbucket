congratulation

(kənˌgrætʃəˈleɪʃən ; kənˌgrædʒəˈleɪʃən )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  the act of congratulating2.  [pl.] expressions of pleasure and best wishes on the occasion of another's good fortune or success

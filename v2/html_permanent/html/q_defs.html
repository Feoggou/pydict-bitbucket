q

or Q (kjuː )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural q's, Q's or Qs1. the 17th letter and 13th consonant of the modern English alphabet2. a speech sound represented by this letter, in English usually a voiceless velar stop, as in unique and quick

Definitions
1. variable nounQ is the seventeenth letter of the English alphabet.2. Q or q is used as an abbreviation for words beginning with q, such as 'question' or 'queen'. ⇒ Q: Should I dress up or dress down on the first date? A: It depends.

cupola

(ˈkjuːpələ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a roof or ceiling in the form of a dome2. a small structure, usually domed, on the top of a roof or dome3. a protective dome for a gun on a warship4. a vertical air-blown coke-fired cylindrical furnace in which iron is remelted for casting
Derived forms
cupolated (ˈkjuːpəˌleɪtɪd ) adjective
Word origin
C16: from Italian, from Late Latin cūpula a small cask, from Latin cūpa tub

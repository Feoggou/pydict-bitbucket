abound

(əˈbaʊnd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (intransitive)1. to exist or occur in abundance; be plentiful ⇒ a swamp in which snakes abound2. (foll by with or in) to be plentifully supplied (with); teem (with) ⇒ the gardens abound with flowers, ⇒ the fields abound in corn
Word origin
C14: via Old French from Latin abundāre to overflow, from undāre to flow, from unda wave

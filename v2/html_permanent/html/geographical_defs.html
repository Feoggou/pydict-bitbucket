Definitions
language note:   The form geographic (dʒiːəgræfɪk ) is also used.adjective [usu ADJ n]Geographical or geographic means concerned with or relating to geography. ⇒ Its geographical location stimulated overseas mercantile enterprise. ⇒ ...a vast geographical area.geographically (dʒiːəgræfɪkli ) adverb ⇒ It is geographically more diverse than any other continent.

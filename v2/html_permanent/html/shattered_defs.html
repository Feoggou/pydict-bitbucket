shattered

(ˈʃætɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. broken into many small pieces2. impaired or destroyed3. dumbfounded or thoroughly upset4. informal tired out or exhausted

shattered

Powered by COBUILD
(ʃætəʳd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are shattered by something, you are extremely shocked and upset about it. ⇒ It is desperately sad news and I am absolutely shattered to hear it.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you say you are shattered, you mean you are extremely tired and have no energy left. [British, informal] ⇒ He was shattered and too tired to concentrate on schoolwork.

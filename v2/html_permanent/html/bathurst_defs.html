Bathurst

(ˈbæθəst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a town in SE Australia, in E New South Wales: scene of a gold rush in 1851. Pop: 27 036 (2001)2. a port in E Canada, in NE New Brunswick: rich mineral resources. Pop: 16 427 (2001)3. the former name (until 1973) of Banjul

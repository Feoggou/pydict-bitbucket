bleary

(ˈblɪərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: blearier or bleariest1. Also : bleared (of eyes or vision) dimmed or blurred, as by tears or tiredness2. indistinct or unclear3. exhausted; tired
Derived forms
blearily (ˈblearily) adverbbleariness (ˈbleariness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveIf your eyes are bleary, they look dull or tired, as if you have not had enough sleep or have drunk too much alcohol. ⇒ I arrived bleary-eyed and rumpled. ⇒ He stared at Leo with great bleary eyes.

Even to his bleary eyes it must have been perfectly apparent that it was no natural wind that was pushing to come in.

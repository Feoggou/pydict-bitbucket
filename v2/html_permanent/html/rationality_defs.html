rationality

(ˌræʃəˈnælɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. the state or quality of being rational or logical2. the possession or utilization of reason or logic3. a reasonable or logical opinion4. economics the assumption that an individual will compare all possible combinations of goods and their prices when making purchases

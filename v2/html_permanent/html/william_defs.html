William

(ˈwɪljəm )
Definitions
noun1.  known as William the Lion. ?1143–1214, king of Scotland (1165–1214)2.  Prince. born 1982, Duke of Cambridge, first son of Prince Charles and Diana, Princess of Wales. In 2011 he married Kate Middleton (born 1982); their son, Prince George, was born in 2013; their daughter, Princess Charlotte, was born in 2015

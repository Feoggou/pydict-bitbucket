lost soul

(lɒst səʊl)
Definitions
noun1. a lonely, forlorn person ⇒ They just clung to each other like two lost souls., ⇒ He drifted away like the lost soul he is.2. theology a person who has lost their soul; a sinner ⇒ I am trying to help sinners and bring lost souls back to the fold.

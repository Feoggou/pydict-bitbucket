juices

(dʒuːs)
Definitions
plural noun1. the liquid that comes out of a piece of meat when it is cooked ⇒ When cooked, drain off the juices and put the meat in a processor or mincer., ⇒ spooning the juices over the top of a leg of lamb2. the liquid that occurs naturally in or is secreted by plant or animal tissue ⇒ gastric juices

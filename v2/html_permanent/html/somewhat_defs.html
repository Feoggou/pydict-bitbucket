Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverbYou use somewhat to indicate that something is the case to a limited extent or degree. [formal] ⇒ He concluded that Oswald was somewhat abnormal. ⇒ He explained somewhat unconvincingly that the company was paying for everything. ⇒ Although his relationship with his mother had improved somewhat, he was still depressed. ⇒ 'I believe you know him'—'Somewhat.'

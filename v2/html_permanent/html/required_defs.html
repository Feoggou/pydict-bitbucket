required

(rɪˈkwaɪəd)
Definitions
adjective1. essential, needed, or necessary ⇒ failure to maintain the required level of security2. set out by rule; compulsory ⇒ Check that the machines meet required standards., ⇒ Parliamentary approval is required for any scheme., ⇒ What qualifications are required?, ⇒ Rear seatbelts are now required by law.

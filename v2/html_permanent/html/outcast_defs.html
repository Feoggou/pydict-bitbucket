outcast

(ˈaʊtˌkɑːst )
Definitions
noun1. a person who is rejected or excluded from a social group2. a vagabond or wanderer3. anything thrown out or rejectedadjective4. rejected, abandoned, or discarded; cast out
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

custard

(ˈkʌstəd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a baked sweetened mixture of eggs and milk2. a sauce made of milk and sugar and thickened with cornflour
Word origin
C15: alteration of Middle English crustade kind of pie, probably from Old Provençal croustado, from crosta crust

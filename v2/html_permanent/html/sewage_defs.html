sewage

(ˈsuːɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounwaste matter from domestic or industrial establishments that is carried away in sewers or drains for dumping or conversion into a form that is not toxic

Word Origin

C19: back formation from sewer1

unemployed

(ˌʌnɪmˈplɔɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. a. without remunerative employment; out of workb. (as collective noun; preceded by the) ⇒ the unemployed2. not being used; idle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

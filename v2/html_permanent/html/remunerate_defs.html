remunerate

(rɪˈmjuːnəˌreɪt )
Definitions
verb(transitive) to reward or pay for work, service, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

remunerability (reˌmuneraˈbility) nounremunerable (reˈmunerable) adjectiveremunerator (reˈmunerˌator) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin remūnerārī to reward, from re- + mūnerāre to give, from mūnus a gift; see munificent

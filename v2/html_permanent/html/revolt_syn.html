Synonyms of revolt

revolt

noun

  1. uprising, rising, revolution, riot, rebellion, uprising, mutiny, defection, insurrection, subversion, insurgency, putsch, sedition, insurgence
    • a revolt by ordinary people against the leaders

verb

  1. rebel, rise up, resist, defect, mutiny, take to the streets, take up arms (against)
    • The townspeople revolted.
  2. disgust, offend, turn off (informal), sicken, repel, repulse, nauseate, gross out (US, slang), shock, turn your stomach, make your flesh creep, give you the creeps (informal)
    • He entirely revolts me.

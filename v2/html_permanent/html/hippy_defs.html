hippy1

or hippie (ˈhɪpɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -piesa. (esp during the 1960s) a person whose behaviour, dress, use of drugs, etc, implied a rejection of conventional valuesb. (as modifier) ⇒ hippy language
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: see hip4

vegetable

(ˈvɛdʒtəbəl )
Definitions
noun1. any of various herbaceous plants having parts that are used as food, such as peas, beans, cabbage, potatoes, cauliflower, and onions2. offensive a person who has lost control of his or her mental faculties, limbs, etc, as from an injury, disease, etc3. a. a dull inactive personb. (as modifier) ⇒ a vegetable life4. (modifier) consisting of or made from edible vegetables ⇒ a vegetable diet5. (modifier) of, relating to, characteristic of, derived from, or consisting of plants or plant material ⇒ vegetable oils6. rare any member of the plant kingdom
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

c14 (adj): from Late Latin vegetābilis animating, from vegetāre to enliven, from Latin vegēre to excite

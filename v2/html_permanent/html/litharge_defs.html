litharge

(ˈlɪθˌɑrdʒ ; lɪˈθɑrdʒ ) in American English
Definitions
noun an oxide of lead, PbO, used in storage batteries, ceramic cements, paints, etc.
Webster’s New World College Dictionary, Fourth Edition, compiled by the editors of Webster’s New World Dictionaries. Copyright © 2010 by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. Published by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. All rights reserved.

Word Origin

OFr litarge < L lithargyrus < Gr lithargyros, spume or foam of silver < lithos, a stone + argyros, silver

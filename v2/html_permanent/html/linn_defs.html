linn

(lɪn )
Definitions
noun mainly Scottish1. a waterfall or a pool at the foot of it2. a ravine or precipice
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: probably from a confusion of two words, Scottish Gaelic linne pool and Old English hlynn torrent

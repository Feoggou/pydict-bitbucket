greyhound

(ˈɡreɪˌhaʊnd )
Definitions
nouna tall slender fast-moving dog of an ancient breed originally used for coursing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English grīghund, related to Old Norse greyhundr, from grey bitch, hundr dog

greyhound

Powered by COBUILD
(greɪhaʊnd ) for learners of English
Word forms: greyhounds
Definitions
countable nounA greyhound is a dog with a thin body and long thin legs, which can run very fast. Greyhounds sometimes run in races and people bet on them.

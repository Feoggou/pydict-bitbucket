Definitions
regional note:   in BRIT, also use unauthorisedadjectiveIf something is unauthorized, it has been produced or is happening without official permission. ⇒ ...a new unauthorized biography of the Russian President. ⇒ ...the unauthorized use of a military vehicle. ⇒ It has also been made quite clear that the trip was unauthorised.

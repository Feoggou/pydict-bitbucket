Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as gullible, you mean they are easily tricked because they are too trusting. ⇒ What point is there in admitting that the stories fed to the gullible public were false? ⇒ I'm so gullible I would have believed him.gullibility (gʌləbɪlɪti ) uncount noun [oft with poss] ⇒ Was she taking part of the blame for her own gullibility?

businessman

(ˈbɪznɪsˌmæn , -mən)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -mena person, esp a man, engaged in commercial or industrial business, esp as an owner or executive▶ USAGE The gender-neutral form of businessman and businesswoman is businessperson, but it does not appear to be very widely used. The plural businesspeople is quite widely used, and if you wish to avoid being gender-specific, you could try rewriting your sentence to use the plural

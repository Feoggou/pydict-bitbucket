pergola

(ˈpɜːɡələ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna horizontal trellis or framework, supported on posts, that carries climbing plants and may form a covered walk

Word Origin

C17: via Italian from Latin pergula projection from a roof, from pergere to go forward

countable nounIn a garden, a pergola is an arch or a structure with a roof over which climbing plants can be grown.

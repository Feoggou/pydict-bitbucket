regulus

(ˈrɛɡjʊləs )
Definitions
noun plural -luses or -li (-ˌlaɪ)impure metal forming beneath the slag during the smelting of ores
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

reguline (ˈreguline) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Latin: a petty king, from rēx king; formerly used for antimony, because it combines readily with gold, thought of as the king of metals

Regulus1

(ˈrɛɡjʊləs )
Definitions
noun Marcus Atilius (ˈmɑːkəs əˈtɪlɪəs). died ?250 bc, Roman general; consul (267; 256). Captured by the Carthaginians in the First Punic War, he was sent to Rome on parole to deliver the enemy's peace terms, advised the Senate to refuse them, and was tortured to death on his return to Carthage
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

(ˈrɛɡjʊləs )
Definitions
nounthe brightest star in the constellation Leo. Visual magnitude: 1.3; spectral type: B8; distance: 69 light years

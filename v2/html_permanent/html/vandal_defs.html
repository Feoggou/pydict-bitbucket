vandal

(ˈvændəl )
Definitions
nouna. a person who deliberately causes damage or destruction to personal or public propertyb. (as modifier) ⇒ vandal instincts
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Vandal, from Latin Vandallus, of Germanic origin

Vandal

(ˈvændəl )
Definitions
nouna member of a Germanic people that raided Roman provinces in the 3rd and 4th centuries ad before devastating Gaul (406–409), conquering Spain and N Africa, and sacking Rome (455): crushed by Belisarius at Carthage (533)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

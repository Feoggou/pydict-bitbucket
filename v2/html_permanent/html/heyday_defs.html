heyday

Powered by COBUILD
(heɪdeɪ ) for learners of English
Definitions
singular noun [with poss]Someone's heyday is the time when they are most powerful, successful, or popular. ⇒ In its heyday, the studio's boast was that it had more stars than there are in heaven.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

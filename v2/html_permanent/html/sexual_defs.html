sexual

(ˈsɛksjʊəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characterized by sex or sexuality2. (of reproduction) characterized by the union of male and female gametes. Compare asexual (sense 2)

Derived Forms

sexually (ˈsexually) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin sexuālis; see sex

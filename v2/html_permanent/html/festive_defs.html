festive

Powered by COBUILD
(festɪv ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Something that is festive is special, colourful, or exciting, especially because of a holiday or celebration. ⇒ The town has a festive holiday atmosphere. ⇒ The Captain's Party on Saturday evening is the cruise's most festive event.2. adjective [ADJ n]Festive means relating to a holiday or celebration, especially Christmas. ⇒ The factory was due to shut for the festive period. ⇒ The government's armed forces have halved their strength for the festive break.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

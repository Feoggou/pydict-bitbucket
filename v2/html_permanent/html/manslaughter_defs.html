manslaughter

(ˈmænˌslɔːtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. law the unlawful killing of one human being by another without malice aforethought. Compare murder. See also homicide, malice aforethought2. (loosely) the killing of a human being

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounManslaughter is the illegal killing of a person by someone who did not intend to kill them. [law] ⇒ A judge accepted her plea that she was guilty of manslaughter, not murder.

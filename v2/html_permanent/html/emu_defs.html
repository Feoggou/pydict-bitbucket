emu

(ˈiːmjuː )
Definitions
nouna large Australian flightless bird, Dromaius novaehollandiae, similar to the ostrich but with three-toed feet and grey or brown plumage: order Casuariiformes. See also ratite
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: changed from Portuguese ema ostrich, from Arabic Na-`amah ostrich

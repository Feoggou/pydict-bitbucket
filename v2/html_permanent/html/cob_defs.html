cob1

(kɒb )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a male swan2. a thickset short-legged type of riding and draught horse3. short for corncob, corncob pipe, cobnut4. British another name for hazel (sense 1)5. a small rounded lump or heap of coal, ore, etc6. British and New Zealand a building material consisting of a mixture of clay and chopped straw7. Also called: cob loaf British a round loaf of breadverbWord forms: cobs, cobbing or cobbed8. (transitive) British informal to beat, esp on the buttocks
Word origin
C15: of uncertain origin; probably related to Icelandic kobbi seal; see cub

cob2

or cobb (kɒb )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan archaic or dialect name for the greater black-backed gull (Larus marinus). See also gull1
Word origin
C16: of Germanic origin; related to Dutch kob, kobbe

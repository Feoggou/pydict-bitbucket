trumpeter

(ˈtrʌmpɪtə )
Definitions
noun1. a person who plays the trumpet, esp one whose duty it is to play fanfares, signals, etc2. any of three birds of the genus Psophia of the forests of South America, having a rounded body, long legs, and a glossy blackish plumage: family Psophiidae, order Gruiformes (cranes, rails, etc)3. (sometimes capital) a breed of domestic fancy pigeon with a long ruff

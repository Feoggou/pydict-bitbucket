mummery

(ˈmʌmərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -meries1. a performance by mummers2. hypocritical or ostentatious ceremony
Example sentences containing 'mummery'
A bit,' I replied, trying to look past him to discover for whom this mummery was played out. Robin Hobb THE GOLDEN FOOL: BOOK TWO OF THE TAWNY MANHe knew better than anyone what empty mummery such a spectacle as this really was. Robert Silverberg LORD PRESTIMION

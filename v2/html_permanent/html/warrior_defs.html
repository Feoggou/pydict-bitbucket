warrior

(ˈwɒrɪə )
Definitions
nouna. a person engaged in, experienced in, or devoted to warb. (as modifier) ⇒ a warrior nation
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old Northern French werreieor, from werre war

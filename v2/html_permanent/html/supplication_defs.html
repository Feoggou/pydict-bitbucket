(ˌsʌplɪˈkeɪʃən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of supplicating2. a humble entreaty or petition; prayer

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounA supplication is a prayer to God or a respectful request to someone in authority for help. [formal] ⇒ He raised his arms in a gesture of supplication. ⇒ The Labour government has to date resisted all supplications.

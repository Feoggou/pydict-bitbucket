fortified

(ˈfɔːtɪˌfaɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. (of a place) defensible, due to walls, trenches, etc ⇒ He remains barricaded inside his heavily-fortified mansion., ⇒ fortified castles and villages2. strengthened physically, mentally, or morally3. (of a garment, structure, etc) strengthened, supported, or reinforced4. (of wine) having spirits or alcohol added in order to produce sherry, port, etc5. (of food) having increased nutritious value due to added vitamins and minerals ⇒ fortified milk, ⇒ fortified cereal products6. supported or confirmed

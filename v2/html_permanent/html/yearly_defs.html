yearly

(ˈjɪəlɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. occurring, done, appearing, etc, once a year or every year; annual2. lasting or valid for a year; annual ⇒ a yearly subscriptionadverb3. once a year; annuallynoun plural -lies4. a publication, event, etc, that occurs once a year
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

yearly

Powered by COBUILD
(jɪəʳli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]A yearly event happens once a year or every year. ⇒ The seven major industrial countries will have their yearly meeting in London.Yearly is also an adverb. ⇒ Clients normally pay fees in advance, monthly, quarterly, or yearly.2. adjective [ADJ n]You use yearly to describe something such as an amount that relates to a period of one year. ⇒ In Holland, the government sets a yearly budget for health care.Yearly is also an adverb. ⇒ Novello says college students will spend $4.2 billion yearly on alcoholic beverages.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

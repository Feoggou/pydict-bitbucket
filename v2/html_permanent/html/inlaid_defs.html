inlaid

(ˈɪnˌleɪd , ɪnˈleɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. set in the surface, as a design in wood2. having such a design or inlay ⇒ an inlaid table

inlaid

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnleɪd ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveAn object that is inlaid has a design on it which is made by putting materials such as wood, gold, or silver into the surface of the object. [+ with] ⇒ ...a box delicately inlaid with little triangles.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

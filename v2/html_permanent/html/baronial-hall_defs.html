baronial hall

(bəˈrəʊnɪəl hɔːl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. heraldry a large building or room owned by a baron ⇒ Life for the settled knight revolved around his lord's baronial hall., ⇒ He converted the building from baronial hall to Tudor palace2. a large, impressive, and old-fashioned room that has the appearance of belonging to someone from the upper classes ⇒ We go and get a cup of coffee in the baronial hall.

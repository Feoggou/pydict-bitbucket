billiard

(ˈbɪljəd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(modifier) of or relating to billiards ⇒ a billiard table, ⇒ a billiard cue, ⇒ a billiard ball
Example sentences containing 'billiard'
Priss turned to the billiard table, looking first at it, then back at Bloom. Asimov, Isaac THE COMPLETE STORIES VOLUME 2Which was why, half an hour after she'd seen Gerrard and Vane head for the billiard room, she slipped into the adjacent conservatory. Stephanie Laurens A RAKE'S VOW

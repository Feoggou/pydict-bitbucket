Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]The trickle-down theory is the theory that benefits given to people at the top of a system will eventually be passed on to people lower down the system. For example, if the rich receive tax cuts, they will pass these benefits on to the poor by creating jobs. ⇒ The government is not simply relying on trickle-down economics to tackle poverty.

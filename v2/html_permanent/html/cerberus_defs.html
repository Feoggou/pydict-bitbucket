Cerberus

(ˈsɜːbərəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Greek mythology a dog, usually represented as having three heads, that guarded the entrance to Hades2. See a sop to Cerberus
Derived forms
Cerberean (səˈbɪərɪən ) adjective

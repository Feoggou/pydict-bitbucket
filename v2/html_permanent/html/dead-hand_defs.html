dead hand

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an oppressive or discouraging influence or factor ⇒ the dead hand of centralized control2. law a less common word for mortmain

dead hand

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
singular nounYou can refer to the dead hand of a particular thing when that thing has a bad or depressing influence on a particular situation. [mainly British] ⇒ ...the dead hand of bureaucracy.

re-route

Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to route or direct (traffic, a road, a river, etc) in a different direction2. to change the direction of (a project, funds, etc)

Word forms: re-routes , re-routing , re-routed
Definitions
verbIf vehicles or planes are re-routed, they are directed along a different route because the usual route cannot be used. [be V-ed] ⇒ The heavy traffic was re-routed past my front door. [V n] ⇒ They rerouted the planes at La Guardia airport.

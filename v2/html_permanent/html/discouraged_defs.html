discouraged

(dɪsˈkʌrɪdʒd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivehaving had one's confidence and determination reduced ⇒ I felt discouraged and wondered whether the task I had set myself was worth the effort., ⇒ Don't be discouraged.

Haines, Pamela THE GOLDEN LIONHe came with horrible ease, but she refused to be discouraged.

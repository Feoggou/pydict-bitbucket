Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that someone is down-to-earth, you approve of the fact that they concern themselves with practical things and actions, rather than with abstract theories. [approval] ⇒ Gloria is probably the most down to earth person I've ever met. ⇒ ...her sincerity and her down-to-earth common sense. ⇒ Their ideas seem to be far more down to earth and sensible.

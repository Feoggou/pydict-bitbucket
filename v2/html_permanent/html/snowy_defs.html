snowy

(ˈsnəʊɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: snowier or snowiest1. covered with or abounding in snow ⇒ snowy hills2. characterized by snow ⇒ snowy weather3. resembling snow in whiteness, purity, etc

Derived Forms

snowily (ˈsnowily) adverbsnowiness (ˈsnowiness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A snowy place is covered in snow. A snowy day is a day when a lot of snow has fallen. ⇒ ...the snowy peaks of the Bighorn Mountains. ⇒ ...a cold, snowy day in mid-February.

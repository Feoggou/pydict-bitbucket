queen

(kwiːn )
Definitions
noun1. a female sovereign who is the official ruler or head of state2. the wife or widow of a king3. a woman or a thing personified as a woman considered the best or most important of her kind ⇒ a beauty queen, ⇒ the queen of ocean liners4. slang an effeminate male homosexual5. a. the only fertile female in a colony of social insects, such as bees, ants, and termites, from the eggs of which the entire colony developsb. (as modifier) ⇒ a queen bee6. an adult female cat7. one of four playing cards in a pack, one for each suit, bearing the picture of a queen8. a chess piece, theoretically the most powerful piece, able to move in a straight line in any direction or diagonally, over any number of squaresverb9. chess to promote (a pawn) to a queen when it reaches the eighth rank10. (transitive) to crown as queen11. (intransitive) informal (of a gay man) to flaunt one's homosexuality12. (intransitive) to reign as queen13. See queen it
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English cwēn; related to Old Saxon quān wife, Old Norse kvæn, Gothic qēns wife

queen

Powered by COBUILD
(kwiːn ) for learners of English
Word forms: queens
Definitions
1. title noun & countable nounA queen is a woman who rules a country as its monarch. ⇒ ...Queen Victoria. ⇒ She met the Queen last week.2. title noun & countable nounA queen is a woman who is married to a king. ⇒ The king and queen had fled.3. countable noun [n N]If you refer to a woman as the queen of a particular activity, you mean that she is well-known for being very good at it. [+ of] ⇒ ...the queen of crime writing.4. → See also beauty queen5. countable nounA queen is a male homosexual who dresses and speaks rather like a woman. [informal]6. countable nounIn chess, the queen is the most powerful piece. It can be moved in any direction.7. countable nounA queen is a playing card with a picture of a queen on it. [+ of] ⇒ ...the queen of spades.8. countable nounA queen or a queen bee is a large female bee which can lay eggs.

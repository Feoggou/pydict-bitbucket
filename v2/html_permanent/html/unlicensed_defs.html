unlicensed

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnlaɪsənst) in the Pharmaceutical Industry
Definitions
adjective(Pharmaceutical: Administration) If something, such as an activity or product, is unlicensed, it is done or produced without the official permission of an authority. ⇒ An unlicensed drug is not on the list of drugs approved by the FDA. ⇒ Doctors should not prescribe unlicensed drugs if an appropriate licensed drug is available for prescription. ⇒ If something, such as an activity or product, is unlicensed, it is done or produced without the official permission of an authority.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveIf something is caked with mud, blood, or dirt, it is covered with a thick dry layer of it. [+ with/in] ⇒ Her shoes were caked with mud.Caked is also a combining form. ⇒ ...blood-caked bandages. ⇒ ...herds of mud-caked cattle and sheep.

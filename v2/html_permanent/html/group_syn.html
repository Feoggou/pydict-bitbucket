Synonyms of group

group

noun

  1. crowd, company, party, band, troop, pack, gathering, gang, bunch, congregation, posse (slang), bevy, assemblage
    • The trouble involved a small group of football supporters.
  2. organization, body, association, league, circle
    • Members of an environmental group are staging a protest inside a chemical plant.
  3. faction, set, camp, clique, coterie, schism, cabal
    • a radical group within the Communist Party
  4. category, class, section, grouping, order, sort, type, division, rank, grade, classification
    • The recipes are divided into groups according to their main ingredients.
  5. band, ensemble, combo
    • ELP were the progressive rock group par excellence.
  6. cluster, collection, formation, clump, aggregation
    • a small group of islands off northern Japan

verb

  1. arrange, order, sort, class, range, gather, organize, assemble, put together, classify, dispose, marshal, bracket, assort
    • The fact sheets are grouped into seven sections.
  2. unite, associate, gather, cluster, get together, congregate, band together
    • We want to encourage them to group together as one big purchaser.

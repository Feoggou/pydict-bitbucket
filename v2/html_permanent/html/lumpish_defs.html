lumpish

(ˈlʌmpɪʃ )
Definitions
adjective1. resembling a lump2. stupid, clumsy, or heavy
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lumpishly (ˈlumpishly) adverblumpishness (ˈlumpishness) noun
Example sentences containing 'lumpish'
It was roughly man-shaped, but its head was a lumpish affair with no features that he could pick out, elven sight or no. Kerr, Katharine A TIME OF WARCristobel was a lumpish thirty-odd, and Bernard a bony about-the-same. Barnard, Robert DEATH IN PURPLE PROSEThe savage girl is remote again, a solitary figure seated next to a lumpish garbage bag billowing in the misty breeze. Alex Shakar THE SAVAGE GIRL

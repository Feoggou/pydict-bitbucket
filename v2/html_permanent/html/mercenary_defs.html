mercenary

(ˈmɜːsɪnərɪ , -sɪnrɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. influenced by greed or desire for gain2. of or relating to a mercenary or mercenariesnoun plural -naries3. a man hired to fight for a foreign army, etc4. rare any person who works solely for pay

Derived Forms

mercenarily (ˈmercenarily) adverbmercenariness (ˈmercenariness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin mercēnārius, from mercēs wages

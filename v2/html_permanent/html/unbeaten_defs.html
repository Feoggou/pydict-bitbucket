unbeaten

(ʌnˈbiːtən )
Definitions
adjective1. having suffered no defeat2. not worn down; untrodden3. not mixed or stirred by beating ⇒ unbeaten eggs4. not beaten or struck
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
adjectiveIn sport, if a person or their performance is unbeaten, nobody else has performed well enough to beat them. undefeated ⇒ He's unbeaten in 20 fights. ⇒ Sampdoria lost their unbeaten record with a 2-1 home defeat against Genoa.

purgative

(ˈpɜːɡətɪv ) medicine
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a drug or agent for purging the bowelsadjective2. causing evacuation of the bowels; cathartic

Derived Forms

purgatively (ˈpurgatively) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA purgative is a medicine that causes you to get rid of unwanted waste from your body. [formal] ⇒ The doctors attempted to reduce his high fever by inducing diarrhea with a purgative.2. adjective [ADJ n]A purgative substance acts as a purgative. [formal] ⇒ ...purgative oils. ⇒ ...a purgative tea.

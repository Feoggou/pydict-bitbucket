parquet

(ˈpɑːkeɪ , -kɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a floor covering of pieces of hardwood fitted in a decorative pattern; parquetry2. Also called: parquet floor a floor so covered3. US the stalls of a theatre4. the main part of the Paris Bourse, where officially listed securities are traded. Compare coulisse (sense 3)5. (in France) the department of government responsible for the prosecution of crimesverb (transitive)6. to cover (a floor) with parquet

Word Origin

C19: from Old French: small enclosure, from parc enclosure; see park

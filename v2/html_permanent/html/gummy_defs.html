gummy1

(ˈɡʌmɪ )
Definitions
adjective -mier or -miest1. sticky or tacky2. consisting of, coated with, or clogged by gum or a similar substance3. producing gum
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gumminess (ˈgumminess) noun

Word Origin

C14: from gum1 + -y1

gummy2

(ˈɡʌmɪ )
Definitions
adjective -mier or -miest1. toothless; not showing one's teethnoun plural -mies2. Australian a small crustacean-eating shark, Mustelus antarcticus, with bony ridges resembling gums in its mouth3. New Zealand an old ewe that has lost its incisor teeth
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gummily (ˈgummily) adverb

Word Origin

C20: from gum2 + -y1

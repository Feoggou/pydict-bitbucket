Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA calendar month is one of the twelve months of the year. ⇒ Winners will be selected at the end of each calendar month.2. countable nounA calendar month is the period from a particular date in one month to the same date in the next month, for example from April 4th to May 4th.

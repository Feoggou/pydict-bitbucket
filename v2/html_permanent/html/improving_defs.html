improving

(ɪmˈpruːvɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. becoming better ⇒ The patient is in an improving condition., ⇒ an improving economy2. tending to educate or edify ⇒ an improving book on Norman architecture.3. making things better ⇒ an improving landlord

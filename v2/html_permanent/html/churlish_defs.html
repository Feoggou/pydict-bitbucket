Definitions

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is churlish is unfriendly, bad-tempered, or impolite. [disapproval] ⇒ She would think him churlish if he refused. ⇒ The room was so lovely it seemed churlish to argue.

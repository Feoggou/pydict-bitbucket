tor

(tɔː )
Definitions
noun1. a high hill, esp a bare rocky one2. mainly Southwest England a prominent rock or heap of rocks, esp on a hill
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English torr, probably of Celtic origin; compare Scottish Gaelic torr pile, Welsh twr

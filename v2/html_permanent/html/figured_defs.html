figured

(ˈfɪɡəd )
Definitions
adjective1. depicted as a figure in graphic art, painting, or sculpture2. decorated or patterned with a design3. having a form4. musica. ornamentalb. (of a bass part) provided with numerals indicating accompanying harmonies

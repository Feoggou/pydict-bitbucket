lost property

(lɒst ˈprɒpətɪ)
Definitions
noun British1. personal belongings that have been lost or accidentally left in public places such as trains or schools ⇒ Lost property should be handed to the driver.2. a place where lost property is kept ⇒ I was enquiring in Lost Property at Derby.

lost property

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounLost property consists of things that people have lost or accidentally left in a public place, for example on a train or in a school. ⇒ Lost property should be handed to the driver.2. uncount nounLost property is a place where lost property is kept. [British] ⇒ I was enquiring in Lost Property at Derby.regional note:   in AM, use lost and found

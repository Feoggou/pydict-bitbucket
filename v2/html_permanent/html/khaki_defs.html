khaki

(ˈkɑːkɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -kis1. a. a dull yellowish-brown colourb. (as adjective) ⇒ a khaki background2. a. a hard-wearing fabric of this colour, used esp for military uniformsb. (as modifier) ⇒ a khaki jacket
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from Urdu, from Persian: dusty, from khāk dust

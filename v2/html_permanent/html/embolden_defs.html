(ɪmboʊldən ) for learners of English

Definitions
verbIf you are emboldened by something, it makes you feel confident enough to behave in a particular way. [be V-ed] ⇒ The Prime Minister was steadily emboldened by the discovery that he faced no opposition. [V n] ⇒ Four days of non-stop demonstrations have emboldened the anti-government protesters.

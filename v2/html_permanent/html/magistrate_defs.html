magistrate

(ˈmædʒɪˌstreɪt , -strɪt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a public officer concerned with the administration of law ▶ Related adjective: magisterial2. another name for justice of the peace3. New Zealand the former name for district court judge

Derived Forms

magisˌtrateship (ˈmagisˌtrateship) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin magistrātus, from magister master

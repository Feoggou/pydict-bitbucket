Synonyms of regarding

regarding

preposition

  1. concerning, about, as to, on the subject of, re, respecting, in respect of, as regards, with reference to, in re, in the matter of, apropos, in or with regard to
    • He refused to divulge any information regarding the man's whereabouts.

regard

verb

  1. consider, see, hold, rate, view, value, account, judge, treat, think of, esteem, deem, look upon, adjudge
    • I regard creativity as both a gift and a skill.
  2. look at, view, eye, watch, observe, check, notice, clock (British, slang), remark, check out (informal), gaze at, behold, eyeball (US, slang), scrutinize, get a load of (informal), take a dekko at (British, slang)
    • She regarded him curiously for a moment.
  3. heed, mind, note, respect, attend, listen to, pay attention to, take into consideration, take notice of

noun

  1. respect, esteem, deference, store, thought, love, concern, care, account, note, reputation, honour, consideration, sympathy, affection, attachment, repute
    • I have a very high regard for him and what he has achieved.
  2. look, gaze, scrutiny, stare, glance
    • This gave a look of calculated menace to his regard.
  3. look, concern, bearing, relation, connection, reference, relevance
  4. heed, interest, mind, attention, notice
  5. (plural) good wishes, respects, greetings, compliments, best wishes, salutations, devoirs, RGDS (text messaging)
    • Give my regards to your family.
  • See as regards
  • See in this regard
  • See with regard to

usage note:   The word regard in the expression with regard to is singular, and has no s at the end. People often make the mistake of saying with regards to, perhaps being influenced by the phrase as regards.

glancing

Powered by COBUILD
(glɑːnsɪŋ , glæns-) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]A glancing blow is one that hits something at an angle rather than from directly in front. ⇒ The car struck him a glancing blow on the forehead.

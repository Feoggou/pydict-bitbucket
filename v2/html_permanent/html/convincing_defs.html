(kənˈvɪnsɪŋ )
Definitions

adjective1. credible or plausible2. mainly law persuading by evidence or argument

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as convincing, you mean that they make you believe that a particular thing is true, correct, or genuine. ⇒ Scientists say there is no convincing evidence that power lines have anything to do with cancer. ⇒ He sounded very convincing.convincingly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj] ⇒ He argued forcefully and convincingly that they were likely to bankrupt the budget. ⇒ Benito Garozzo won convincingly.

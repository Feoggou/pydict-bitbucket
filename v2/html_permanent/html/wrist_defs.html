wrist

(rɪst )
Definitions
noun1. anatomy the joint between the forearm and the hand. Technical name: carpus2. the part of a sleeve or glove that covers the wrist3. machinerya. See wrist pinb. a joint in which a wrist pin forms the pivot
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English; related to Old High German, Old Norse rist. See wriggle, wry

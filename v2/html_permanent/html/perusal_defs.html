Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft a N]Perusal of something such as a letter, article, or document is the action of reading it. [formal] ⇒ Peter Cooke undertook to send each of us a sample contract for perusal. ⇒ A perusal of the letters which we have published has satisfied him of the reality of our claim. ⇒ ...a casual perusal of this list.

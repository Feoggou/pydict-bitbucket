Definitions
adjectiveelated or jubilant, esp because of triumph or success

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are exultant, you feel very happy and proud about something you have done. [formal] ⇒ An exultant party leader said: 'He will be an excellent MP.'exultantly adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ 'We cannot lose the war!' he shouted exultantly.

sobriquet

or soubriquet (ˈsəʊbrɪˌkeɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna humorous epithet, assumed name, or nickname

Word Origin

C17: from French soubriquet, of uncertain origin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu sing]A sobriquet is a humorous name that people give someone or something. [written] ⇒ From his staff he earned the sobriquet 'Mumbles'.

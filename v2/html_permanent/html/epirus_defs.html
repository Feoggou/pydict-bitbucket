Epirus

(ɪˈpaɪərəs )
Definitions
noun1. a region of NW Greece, part of ancient Epirus ceded to Greece after independence in 18302. (in ancient Greece) a region between the Pindus mountains and the Ionian Sea, straddling the modern border with Albania

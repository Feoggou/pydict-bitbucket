Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounYour common sense is your natural ability to make good judgments and to behave in a practical and sensible way. ⇒ Use your common sense. ⇒ She always had a lot of common sense. ⇒ ...a common-sense approach.

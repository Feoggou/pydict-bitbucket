Mongolian

(mɒŋˈɡəʊlɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Mongolia, its people, or their languagenoun2. a native of Mongolia3. the language of Mongolia. See Khalkha

Mongolian

Powered by COBUILD
(mɒŋgoʊliən )
Word forms: Mongolians
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveMongolian means belonging or relating to Mongolia, or to its people, language, or culture. ⇒ ...the Mongolian capital, Ulan Bator.2. countable nounA Mongolian is a person who comes from Mongolia.3. uncount nounMongolian is the language that is spoken in Mongolia. ⇒ Until August of this year there was no complete translation of the New Testament in Mongolian.

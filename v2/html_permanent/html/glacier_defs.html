glacier

(ˈɡlæsɪə , ˈɡleɪs-)
Definitions
nouna slowly moving mass of ice originating from an accumulation of snow. It can either spread out from a central mass (continental glacier) or descend from a high valley (alpine glacier)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from French (Savoy dialect), from Old French glace ice, from Late Latin glacia, from Latin glaciēs ice

saving grace

(ˈseɪvɪŋ ɡreɪs)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounA saving grace is a good quality or feature in a person or thing that prevents them from being completely bad or worthless. ⇒ Ageing's one saving grace is you worry less about what people think., ⇒ His sense of humour is his saving grace., ⇒ The saving grace of the play was the high quality of the acting.

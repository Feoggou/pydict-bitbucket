Synonyms of message

message

noun

  1. communication, note, bulletin, word, letter, notice, memo, dispatch, memorandum, communiqué, missive, intimation, tidings, email or e-mail, text or text message, SMS, IMS, MSG (text messaging), tweet on the Twitter website, mention or @mention on the Twitter website
    • Would you like to leave a message?
  2. point, meaning, idea, moral, theme, import, purport, MSG (text messaging)
    • The report's message was unequivocal.
  3. (Scottish) errand, job, task, commission, mission, MSG (text messaging)
    • I was employed to run messages for him in 1957.

verb

  1. (text messaging computing) text, send, communicate, email or e-mail, SMS, IM or instant message, DM or direct message, poke on the Facebook website, tweet on the Twitter website, chat (computing)
  • See get the message

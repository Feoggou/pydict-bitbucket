Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as crooked, especially something that is usually straight, you mean that it is bent or twisted. ⇒ ...the crooked line of his broken nose. ⇒ ...a crooked little tree.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA crooked smile is uneven and bigger on one side than the other. ⇒ Polly gave her a crooked grin.crookedly adverb ⇒ Nick was smiling crookedly at her.3. adjectiveIf you describe a person or an activity as crooked, you mean that they are dishonest or criminal. [informal] ⇒ ...a crooked cop. ⇒ She might expose his crooked business deals to her tax inspector brother.

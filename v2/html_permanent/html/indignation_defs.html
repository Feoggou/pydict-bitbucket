indignation

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndɪgneɪʃən ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIndignation is the feeling of shock and anger which you have when you think that something is unjust or unfair. [+ at] ⇒ She was filled with indignation at the conditions under which miners were forced to work. ⇒ No wonder he could hardly contain his indignation.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

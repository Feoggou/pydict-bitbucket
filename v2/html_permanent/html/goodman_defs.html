goodman

(ˈɡʊdmən )
Definitions
noun plural -men archaic1. a husband2. a man not of gentle birth: used as a title3. a master of a household

Goodman

(ˈɡʊdmən )
Definitions
noun Benny, full name Benjamin David Goodman. 1909–86, US jazz clarinetist and bandleader, whose treatment of popular songs created the jazz idiom known as swing

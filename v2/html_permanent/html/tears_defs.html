tears

(tɪəz )
Definitions
plural noun1. the clear salty solution secreted by the lacrimal glands that lubricates and cleanses the surface of the eyeball and inner surface of the eyelids ▶ Related adjective: lachrymal2. a state of intense frustration (esp in the phrase bored to tears)3. See in tears4. See without tears

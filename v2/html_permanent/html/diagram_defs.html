diagram

(ˈdaɪəˌɡræm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a sketch, outline, or plan demonstrating the form or workings of something2. mathematics a pictorial representation of a quantity or of a relationship ⇒ a Venn diagramverb -grams, -gramming, -grammed or US -grams, -graming or -gramed3. to show in or as if in a diagram
Derived forms
diagrammatic (ˌdaɪəɡrəˈmætɪk ) or diagrammatical (ˌdaɪəɡrəˈmætɪkəl) adjectiveˌdiagrammatically (ˌdiagramˈmatically) adverb
Word origin
C17: from Latin diagramma, from Greek, from diagraphein, from graphein to write

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
regional note:   in AM, use diagraming, diagramed1. countable nounA diagram is a simple drawing which consists mainly of lines and is used, for example, to explain how a machine works. ⇒ ...a circuit diagram. ⇒ You can reduce long explanations to simple charts or diagrams.2. verbTo diagram something means to draw a diagram of it or to explain it using a diagram. [mainly US, formal] [be V-ed] ⇒ The sound waves of the voice could be diagramed as in B. [V n] ⇒ ...diagramming the movement of a system's variables.

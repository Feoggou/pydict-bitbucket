vaporous

(ˈveɪpərəs )
Definitions
adjective1. resembling or full of vapour2. another word for vaporific3. lacking permanence or substance; ephemeral or fanciful4. given to foolish imaginings5. dulled or obscured by an atmosphere of vapour
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vaporously (ˈvaporously) adverbvaporousness (ˈvaporousness) or vaporosity (ˌveɪpəˈrɒsɪtɪ ) noun

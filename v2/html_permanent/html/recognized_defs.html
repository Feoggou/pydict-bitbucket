recognized

or recognised (ˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪzd)
Definitions
adjective1. formally acknowledged or accepted as valid ⇒ a recognized qualification, ⇒ a directory of recognized teachers, ⇒ anyone who is not a member of a recognized organisation2. widely accepted as being true ⇒ a recognized fact3. known or identifiable ⇒ He is a recognized authority on European politics., ⇒ a nationally recognized expert on psychology4. formally acknowledged ⇒ recognized and non-recognized states

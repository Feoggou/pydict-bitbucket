commodore

(ˈkɒməˌdɔː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. British a naval rank junior to rear admiral and senior to captain2. the senior captain of a shipping line3. the officer in command of a convoy of merchant ships4. the senior flag office of a yacht or boat club
Word origin
C17: probably from Dutch commandeur, from French, from Old French commander to command

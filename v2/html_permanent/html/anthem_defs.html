anthem

(ˈænθəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a song of loyalty or devotion, as to a nation or college ⇒ a national anthem2. a musical composition for a choir, usually set to words from the Bible, sung as part of a church service3. a religious chant sung antiphonally4. a popular rock or pop song
Derived forms
anthemic (ænˈθɛmɪk ) adjective
Word origin
Old English antemne, from Late Latin antiphōna antiphon

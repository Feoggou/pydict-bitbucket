clarify

(ˈklærɪˌfaɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -fies, -fying or -fied1. to make or become clear or easy to understand2. to make or become free of impurities3. to make (fat, butter, etc) clear by heating, etc, or (of fat, etc) to become clear as a result of such a process
Derived forms
ˌclarification (ˌclarifiˈcation) nounclariˌfier (ˈclariˌfier) noun
Word origin
C14: from Old French clarifier, from Late Latin clārificāre, from Latin clārus clear + facere to make

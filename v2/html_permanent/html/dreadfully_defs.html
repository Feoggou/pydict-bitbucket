dreadfully

(ˈdrɛdfʊlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb1. in a shocking, or disagreeable manner2. (intensifier) ⇒ you're dreadfully kind

He said, by the by, he'd lost her mobile phone: he was dreadfully sorry.

(ʌnbeərəbəl )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as unbearable, you mean that it is so unpleasant, painful, or upsetting that you feel unable to accept it or deal with it. [+ for] ⇒ War has made life almost unbearable for the civilians remaining in the capital. ⇒ I was in terrible, unbearable pain.unbearably (ʌnbeərəbli ) graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj/-ed] ⇒ By the evening it had become unbearably hot.

womanish

(ˈwʊmənɪʃ )
Definitions
adjective1. having qualities or characteristics regarded as unsuitable to a strong character of either sex, esp a man2. characteristic of or suitable for a woman
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

womanishly (ˈwomanishly) adverbwomanishness (ˈwomanishness) noun

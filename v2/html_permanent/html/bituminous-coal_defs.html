bituminous coal

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna soft black coal, rich in volatile hydrocarbons, that burns with a smoky yellow flame. Fixed carbon content: 46–86 per cent; calorific value: 1.93 × 107 – 3.63 × 107 J/kg. Also called: soft coal

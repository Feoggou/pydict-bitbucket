orgy

(ˈɔːdʒɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -gies1. a wild gathering marked by promiscuous sexual activity, excessive drinking, etc2. an act of immoderate or frenzied indulgence3. (often plural) secret religious rites of Dionysus, Bacchus, etc, marked by drinking, dancing, and songs
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

orgiastic (ˌorgiˈastic) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from French orgies, from Latin orgia, from Greek: nocturnal festival

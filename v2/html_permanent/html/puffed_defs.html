puffed

(pʌft)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of a part of the body) swollen because of an injury or illness ⇒ His face was a little puffed.2. informal breathing with difficulty after exertion ⇒ She looked somewhat puffed., ⇒ Do you get puffed out running up and down the stairs?

puffed

Powered by COBUILD
(pʌft )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If a part of your body is puffed or puffed up, it is swollen because of an injury or because you are unwell. ⇒ His face was a little puffed. ⇒ His mouth was all puffed up where he had taken a rifle butt.2. graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If you are puffed or puffed out, you are breathing with difficulty because you have been using a lot of energy. [informal] ⇒ Do you get puffed out running up and down the stairs?

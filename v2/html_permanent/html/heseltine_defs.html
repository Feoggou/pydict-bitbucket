Heseltine

(ˈhɛzəlˌtaɪn )
Definitions
noun1.  Michael (Ray Dibden) Baron. born 1933, British Conservative politician; secretary of state for defence (1983–86); secretary of state for the environment (1990–92); secretary of state for trade and industry (1992–95); deputy prime minister (1995–97)2.  Philip Arnold. the real name of composer Peter Warlock.

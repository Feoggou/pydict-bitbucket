Synonyms of pepper

pepper

noun

  1. seasoning, flavour, spice
    • Season the mixture with salt and pepper.

verb

  1. pelt, hit, shower, scatter, blitz, riddle, rake, bombard, assail, strafe, rain down on
    • He was peppered with shrapnel.
  2. sprinkle, spot, scatter, dot, stud, fleck, intersperse, speck, spatter, freckle, stipple, bespatter
    • The road was peppered with glass.

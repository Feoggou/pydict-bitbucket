affirmation

(ˌæfəˈmeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of affirming or the state of being affirmed2. a statement of the existence or truth of something; assertion3. law a solemn declaration permitted on grounds of conscientious objection to taking an oath
Example sentences containing 'affirmation'
Miller had briefed her on both Hardin and Caldere, and he nodded in affirmation. MacNeill, Alastair CODE BREAKEROnly the asarya is able to say `yes" to all of creation, for one must see everything as it truly is before making the final affirmation. Zindell, David THE BROKEN GODNot everybody had found evidence of that affirmation in his work. Clive Barker SACRAMENTI couldn't hear the words of affirmation they spoke to themselves.

torchlight

(ˈtɔːtʃlaɪt)
Definitions
nouna. the light produced by a torch or torches ⇒ Surgeons are performing operations in tents by torchlight.b. (as modifier) ⇒ a torchlight procession

torchlight

Powered by COBUILD
(tɔːʳtʃlaɪt ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [oft by N, N n]If you do something by torchlight, you do it using the light that is produced by a torch or torches. ⇒ Surgeons are performing operations in tents by torchlight. ⇒ The marchers held a torchlight procession.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

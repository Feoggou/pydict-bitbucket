feathery

Powered by COBUILD
(feðəri ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is feathery, it has an edge divided into a lot of thin parts so that it looks soft. ⇒ The foliage was soft and feathery.2. graded adjective & adjectiveFeathery is used to describe things that are soft and light. ⇒ ...flurries of small, feathery flakes of snow.

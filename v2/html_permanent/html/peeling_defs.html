peeling

(ˈpiːlɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(usually plural) a strip of skin, rind, bark, etc, that has been peeled off ⇒ a potato peeling

I could see the onset of decay in the walls and ceiling now: the crumbled plaster and peeling paint. Pritchard, John NIGHT SISTERSShe fell back also on frozen peas, then exerted herself to the extent of peeling some potatoes.

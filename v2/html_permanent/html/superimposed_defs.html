superimposed

(ˌsuːpərɪmˈpəʊzd)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. placed on top of something else ⇒ digitally superimposed images2. transferred to another situation ⇒ Today the ancient festivals are inextricably mixed with superimposed Christianity.
Example sentences containing 'superimposed'
Slowly, the darkness ebbed, and Lirael saw a room, strangely superimposed upon the river of Death she saw through her other eye.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]Something that is askew is not straight or not level with what it should be level with. ⇒ She stood there, hat askew. ⇒ There were no shutters at the windows, and some of the doors hung askew.

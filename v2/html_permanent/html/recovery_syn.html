Synonyms of recovery

recovery

noun

  1. improvement, return to health, rally, healing, revival, mending, recuperation, convalescence, turn for the better
    • He made a remarkable recovery from a shin injury.
  2. revival, improvement, rally, restoration, rehabilitation, upturn, betterment, amelioration
    • In many sectors of the economy the recovery has started.
  3. retrieval, repossession, reclamation, restoration, repair, redemption, recapture
    • the recovery of a painting by Turner
  • See in recovery

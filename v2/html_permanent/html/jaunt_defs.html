jaunt

(dʒɔːnt )
Definitions
noun1. a short pleasurable excursion; outingverb2. (intransitive) to go on such an excursion
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

jauntingly (ˈjauntingly) adverb

Word Origin

C16: of unknown origin

anemia

(əˈnimiə )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  Medicine a condition in which there is a reduction of the number, or volume, of red blood corpuscles or of the total amount of hemoglobin in the bloodstream, resulting in paleness, generalized weakness, etc.2.  lack of vigor or vitality; lifelessness

Word origin

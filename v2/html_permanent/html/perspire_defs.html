perspire

(pəˈspaɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verbto secrete or exude (perspiration) through the pores of the skin. Also (rare): perspirate

Derived Forms

perspiringly (perˈspiringly) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin perspīrāre to blow, from per- (through) + spīrāre to breathe; compare inspire

verbWhen you perspire, a liquid comes out on the surface of your skin, because you are hot or frightened. [formal] [V] ⇒ He began to perspire heavily. [V-ing] ⇒ ...mopping their perspiring brows.

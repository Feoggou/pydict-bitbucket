tomahawk

(ˈtɒməˌhɔːk )
Definitions
noun1. a fighting axe, with a stone or later an iron head, used by the North American Indians2. mainly Australian the usual word for hatchet
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Virginia Algonquian tamahaac

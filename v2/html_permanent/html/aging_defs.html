Definitions
COBUILD Advanced American English Dictionary
1. adjectiveSomeone or something that is aging is becoming older and less healthy or efficient. ⇒ John lives with his aging mother.2. uncount nounAging is the process of becoming old or becoming worn out. ⇒ The only signs of aging are the flecks of gray that speckle his dark hair.

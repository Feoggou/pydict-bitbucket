laundry

(ˈlɔːndrɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -dries1. a place where clothes and linen are washed and ironed2. the clothes or linen washed and ironed3. the act of laundering
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: changed from C14 lavendry; see launder

laundry

Powered by COBUILD
(lɔːndri ) for learners of English
Word forms: laundries
Definitions
1. uncount nounLaundry is used to refer to clothes, sheets, and towels that are about to be washed, are being washed, or have just been washed. ⇒ I'll do your laundry. ⇒ ...the room where I hang the laundry. [+ in] ⇒ He'd put his dirty laundry in the clothes basket.2. countable nounA laundry is a firm that washes and irons clothes, sheets, and towels for people. ⇒ We had to have the washing done at the laundry.3. countable noun [usu sing]A laundry or a laundry room is a room in a house, hotel, or institution where clothes, sheets, and towels are washed. [+ at] ⇒ He worked in the laundry at Oxford prison.4. → to wash your dirty laundry in public
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

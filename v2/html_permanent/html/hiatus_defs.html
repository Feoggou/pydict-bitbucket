hiatus

(haɪˈeɪtəs )
Definitions
noun plural -tuses or -tus1. (esp in manuscripts) a break or gap where something is missing2. a break or interruption in continuity3. a break between adjacent vowels in the pronunciation of a word4. anatomy a natural opening or aperture; foramen5. anatomy a less common word for vulva
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hiatal (hiˈatal) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Latin: gap, cleft, aperture, from hiāre to gape, yawn

prawn

(prɔːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of various small edible marine decapod crustaceans of the genera Palaemon, Penaeus, etc, having a slender flattened body with a long tail and two pairs of pincers2. See come the raw prawn

Derived Forms

prawner (ˈprawner) noun

Word Origin

C15: of obscure origin

glebe

(ɡliːb )
Definitions
noun1. British land granted to a clergyman as part of his benefice2. poetic land, esp when regarded as the source of growing things
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin glaeba

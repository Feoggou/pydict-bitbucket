unluckily for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnlʌkɪli )
Definitions
adverb [ADV with v]You use unluckily as a comment on something bad or unpleasant that happens to someone, in order to suggest sympathy for them or that it was not their fault. [+ for] ⇒ Unluckily for him, the fraud officers were watching this flight too. ⇒ Some people unluckily achieve suicide when they only meant to attempt it.

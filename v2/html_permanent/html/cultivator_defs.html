cultivator

(ˈkʌltɪˌveɪtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a farm implement equipped with shovels, blades, etc, used to break up soil and remove weeds2. a person or thing that cultivates3. a person who grows, tends, or improves plants or crops

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA cultivator is a tool or machine which is used to break up the earth or to remove weeds, for example in a garden or field.2. countable nounA cultivator is someone who prepares the ground and grows crops in it. [formal]

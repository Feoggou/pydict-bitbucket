luxuriate

(lʌɡˈzjʊərɪˌeɪt )
Definitions
verb (intransitive)1. (foll by in) to take voluptuous pleasure; revel2. to flourish extensively or profusely3. to live in a sumptuous way
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

luxuriation (luxˌuriˈation) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin luxuriāre

Definitions
verbIf you luxuriate in something, you relax in it and enjoy it very much, especially because you find it comfortable and luxurious. [V + in] ⇒ Lie back and luxuriate in the scented oil. [V + in] ⇒ Ralph was luxuriating in the first real holiday he'd had in years.

greeting

(ˈɡriːtɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. the act or an instance of welcoming or saluting on meeting2. (often plural)a. an expression of friendly salutationb. (as modifier) ⇒ a greetings card
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

greeting

Powered by COBUILD
(griːtɪŋ ) for learners of English
Word forms: greetings
Definitions
1. variable nounA greeting is something friendly that you say or do when you meet someone. ⇒ His greeting was familiar and friendly. ⇒ They exchanged greetings. ⇒ He raised a hand in greeting.2. convention'Greetings' is an old-fashioned greeting.

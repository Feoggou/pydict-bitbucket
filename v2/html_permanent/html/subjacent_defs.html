subjacent

(sʌbˈdʒeɪsənt )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. forming a foundation; underlying2. lower than though not directly below ⇒ tall peaks and their subjacent valley

Derived Forms

subjacency (subˈjacency) nounsubjacently (subˈjacently) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Latin subjacēre to lie close, adjoin, be under, from sub- + jacēre to lie

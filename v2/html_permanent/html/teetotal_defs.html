teetotal

(tiːˈtəʊtəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or practising abstinence from alcoholic drink2. dialect complete
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

teetotaller (teeˈtotaller) or teetotalist (teeˈtotalist) nounteetotally (teeˈtotally) adverbteetotalism (teeˈtotalism) noun

Word Origin

C19: allegedly coined in 1833 by Richard Turner, English advocate of total abstinence from alcoholic liquors; probably from total, with emphatic reduplication

corrosion

(kəˈrəʊʒən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a process in which a solid, esp a metal, is eaten away and changed by a chemical action, as in the oxidation of iron in the presence of water by an electrolytic process2. slow deterioration by being eaten or worn away3. the condition produced by or the product of corrosion

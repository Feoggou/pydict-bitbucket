stiffening

(ˈstɪfənɪŋ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or process of becoming stiff ⇒ The stiffening of muscles in Susan's right arm became worse.2. a material or substance used to stiffen something ⇒ At the lower edge of the stiffening, pinch the pleats together and stitch in place., ⇒ For the stiffening, use either buckram or a modern self-adhesive stiffener., ⇒ Using the pattern, cut out one piece of pelmet stiffening.

nobody

(ˈnəʊbədɪ )
Definitions
pronoun1. no person; no-onenoun plural -bodies2. an insignificant personSee note at no-one

nobody for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(noʊbɒdi )
Word forms: nobodies
Definitions
1. negative indefinite pronoun & pronounNobody means not a single person, or not a single member of a particular group or set. ⇒ They were shut away in a little room where nobody could overhear. ⇒ Nobody realizes how bad things are. ⇒ Nobody ever spoke to me at press conferences. ⇒ Nobody else in the neighbourhood can help.2. countable nounIf someone says that a person is a nobody, they are saying in an unkind way that the person is not at all important. [disapproval] ⇒ A man in my position has nothing to fear from a nobody like you.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

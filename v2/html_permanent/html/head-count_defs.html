head count

or headcount (ˈhɛdˌkaʊnt)
Definitions
noun1. a check of the number of people present ⇒ He left his cell only for meals and inmate head counts.2. business the number of people employed by a company or organization ⇒ He said he had reduced the head count by about 35 per cent.

Word forms: head counts
Definitions
countable nounIf you do a head count, you count the number of people present. You can also use head count to talk about the number of people that are present at an event, or that an organization employs.

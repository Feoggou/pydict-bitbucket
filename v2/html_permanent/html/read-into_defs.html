read into

(riːd )
Definitions
verb(tr, preposition) to discern in or infer from a statement (meanings not intended by the speaker or writer)

read into

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
phrasal verbIf you read a meaning into something, you think it is there although it may not actually be there. [V n P n] ⇒ The addict often reads disapproval into people's reactions to him even where it does not exist. [V n P n] ⇒ It would be wrong to try to read too much into such a light-hearted production. [Also V P n n] →  read

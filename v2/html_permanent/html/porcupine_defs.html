porcupine

(ˈpɔːkjʊˌpaɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany of various large hystricomorph rodents of the families Hystricidae, of Africa, Indonesia, S Europe, and S Asia, and Erethizontidae, of the New World. All species have a body covering of protective spines or quills

Derived Forms

porcuˌpinish (ˈporcuˌpinish) adjectiveporcuˌpiny (ˈporcuˌpiny) adjective

Word Origin

C14 porc despyne pig with spines, from Old French porc espin; see pork, spine

Synonyms of wheel

wheel

noun

  1. disc, ring, hoop
    • a bicycle wheel
  2. circle, turn, roll, revolution, spin, rotation, whirl, pivot, twirl, gyration

verb

  1. push, trundle, roll
    • He wheeled his bike into the alley beside the house.
  2. turn, swing, spin, revolve, rotate, whirl, swivel
    • He wheeled around to face her.
  3. circle, orbit, go round, twirl, gyrate
    • A flock of crows wheeled overhead.

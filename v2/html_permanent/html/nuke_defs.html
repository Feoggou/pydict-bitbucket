nuke

(njuːk ) slang
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to attack or destroy with nuclear weaponsnoun2. a nuclear bomb3. a military strike with nuclear weapons4. nuclear power5. mainly US a nuclear power plant

(njuːk , US nuːk )
Word forms: nukes , nuking , nuked
Definitions
1. countable nounA nuke is a nuclear weapon. [informal] ⇒ They have nukes, and if they're sufficiently pushed, they'll use them.2. verbIf one country nukes another, it attacks it using nuclear weapons. [informal] [V n] ⇒ He wanted to nuke the area.

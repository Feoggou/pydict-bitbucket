sleep

noun

  1. slumber(s), rest, nap, doze, kip (British, slang), snooze (informal), repose, hibernation, siesta, dormancy, beauty sleep (informal), forty winks (informal), shuteye (slang), zizz (British, informal) • Try and get some sleep.

verb

  1. slumber, drop off (informal), doze, kip (British, slang), snooze (informal), snore, hibernate, nod off (informal), take a nap, catnap, drowse, go out like a light, take forty winks (informal), zizz (British, informal), be in the land of Nod, rest in the arms of Morpheus • I've not been able to sleep for the last few nights.
  2. accommodate, take, house, hold, lodge, cater for, have space for, have beds for • The villa sleeps 10.
related mania:
hypnomania
related phobia:
hypnophobia

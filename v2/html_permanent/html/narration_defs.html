narration

(nəˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act or process of narrating2. a narrated account or story; narrative3. (in traditional rhetoric) the third step in making a speech, the putting forward of the question
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'narration'
She was appalled at his blasé narration , not its content, simply the tone. Thomas, Craig THE LAST RAVENSometimes he would switch his narration from English to pidgin, or to a rhythmic, humorous combination of the two that was unique to him. Elizabeth Lowell EDEN BURNING

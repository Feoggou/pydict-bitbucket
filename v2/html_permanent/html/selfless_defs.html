Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that someone is selfless, you approve of them because they care about other people more than themselves. [approval] ⇒ She was a wonderful companion and her generosity to me was entirely selfless. ⇒ ...the selfless love of a mother for her child.selflessly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ I've never known anyone who cared so selflessly about children.selflessness uncount noun ⇒ I have enormous regard for his selflessness on behalf of his fellow man.

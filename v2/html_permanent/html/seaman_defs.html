seaman

(ˈsiːmən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -men1. a rating trained in seamanship as opposed to electrical engineering, etc2. a man who serves as a sailor3. a person skilled in seamanship

Derived Forms

seaman-ˌlike (ˈseaman-ˌlike) adjectiveseamanly (ˈseamanly) adjective, adverb

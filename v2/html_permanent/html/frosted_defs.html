frosted

(ˈfrɒstɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. covered or injured by frost2. covered with icing, as a cake3. (of glass, etc) having a surface roughened, as if covered with frost, to prevent clear vision through it

frosted

Powered by COBUILD
(frɒstɪd , US frɔːst- ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveFrosted glass is glass that you cannot see through clearly. ⇒ The top half of the door to his office was of frosted glass.2. adjectiveFrosted means covered with frost. ⇒ ...the frosted trees.3. adjectiveFrosted means covered with something that looks like frost. ⇒ ...frosted blue eye shadow.4. adjectiveFrosted means covered with icing. [US] ⇒ ...a plate of frosted cupcakes.regional note:   in BRIT, usually use iced

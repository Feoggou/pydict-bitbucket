neighing

(ˈneɪɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the action or sound of the high-pitched cry of horses ⇒ the neighing of horsesadjective2. characteristic of the high-pitched cry of a horse ⇒ Jack put his head down and made a neighing sound before convulsing with laughter., ⇒ He laughed, that same mirthless, neighing laugh.

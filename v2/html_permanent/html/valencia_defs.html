Valencia

(Spanish baˈlenθja)
Definitions
noun1. a port in E Spain, capital of Valencia province, on the Mediterranean: the third largest city in Spain; capital of the Moorish kingdom of Valencia (1021–1238); university (1501). Pop: 780 653 (2003 est). Latin name: Valentia (vəˈlɛntɪə )2. a region and former kingdom of E Spain, on the Mediterranean3. a city in N Venezuela: one of the two main industrial centres in Venezuela. Pop: 2 330 000 (2005 est)

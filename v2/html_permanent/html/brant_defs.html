brant

(brænt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural brants or brantUS and Canadian a small goose, Branta bernicla, that has a dark grey plumage and short neck and occurs in most northern coastal regions. Also called (in Britain and certain other countries): brent goose

Synonyms of magazine

magazine

noun

  1. journal, paper, publication, supplement, rag (informal), issue, glossy (informal), pamphlet, periodical, fanzine (informal), ezine or e-zine
    • Her face is on the cover of a dozen or more magazines.
  2. storehouse, store, arsenal, warehouse, depot, ammunition dump, powder room (obsolete)

bequest

(bɪˈkwɛst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. the act of bequeathingb. something that is bequeathed2. law a gift of property by will, esp personal property. Compare devise (sense 4), devise (sense 5)
Word origin
C14: be- + Old English -cwiss degree; see bequeath

Definitions

obdurate

(ˈɒbdjʊrɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. not easily moved by feelings or supplication; hardhearted2. impervious to persuasion, esp to moral persuasion
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

obduracy (ˈobduracy) or obdurateness (ˈobdurateness) nounobdurately (ˈobdurately) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Latin obdūrāre to make hard, from ob- (intensive) + dūrus hard; compare endure

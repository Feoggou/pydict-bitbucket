indomitable

(ɪnˈdɒmɪtəbəl )
Definitions
adjective(of courage, pride, etc) difficult or impossible to defeat or subdue
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

indomitability (inˌdomitaˈbility) or indomitableness (inˈdomitableness) nounindomitably (inˈdomitably) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin indomitābilis, from Latin indomitus untamable, from in-1 + domitus subdued, from domāre to tame

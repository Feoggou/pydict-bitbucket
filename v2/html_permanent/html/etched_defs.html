etched

(ɛtʃt)
Definitions
adjective1. imprinted vividly on someone's mind or memory ⇒ The ugly scene in the study was still etched in her mind., ⇒ It is etched on my memory.2. (of a feeling) clearly visible on someone's face ⇒ His grief was etched into every line of his face.

instinctive

(ɪnˈstɪŋktɪv ) or instinctual
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or resulting from instinct2. conditioned so as to appear innate ⇒ an instinctive movement in driving
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

instinctively (inˈstinctively) or instinctually (inˈstinctually) adverb

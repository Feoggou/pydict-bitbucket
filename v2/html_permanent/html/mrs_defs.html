Mrs

(ˈmɪsɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural Mrs or Mesdamesa title used before the name or names of a married woman

Word Origin

C17: originally an abbreviation of mistress

regional note:   in AM, use Mrs.title nounMrs is used before the name of a married woman when you are speaking or referring to her. ⇒ Hello, Mrs Miles. ⇒ ...Mrs Anne Pritchard. ⇒ ...Mr and Mrs D H Alderson.

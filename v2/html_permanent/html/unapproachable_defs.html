unapproachable

(ˌʌnəˈprəʊtʃəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. discouraging intimacy, friendliness, etc; aloof2. inaccessible3. not to be rivalled
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unapproachableness (ˌunapˈproachableness) noununapproachably (ˌunapˈproachably) adverb

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as unapproachable, you mean that they seem to be difficult to talk to and not very friendly. ⇒ She was somewhat unapproachable but I'm sure she wanted to be friendly.

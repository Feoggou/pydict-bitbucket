overdone

(ˌəʊvəˈdʌn)
Definitions
adjective1. cooked or baked for too long ⇒ The meat was overdone and the vegetables disappointing.2. exaggerated or excessive ⇒ The panic was overdone., ⇒ The panic is overdone. The drought has been confined to the south and east.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

overdone for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(oʊvəʳdʌn )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf food is overdone, it has been spoiled by being cooked for too long. OPPOSITE: underdone ⇒ The meat was overdone and the vegetables disappointing.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you say that something is overdone, you mean that you think it is excessive or exaggerated. ⇒ In fact, the panic is overdone. As the map shows, the drought has been confined to the south and east of Britain.

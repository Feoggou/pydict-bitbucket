pep

(pɛp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. high spirits, energy, or vitalityverbWord forms: peps, pepping or pepped2. (transitive; usually foll by up) to liven by imbuing with new vigour

Word Origin

C20: short for pepper

PEP

(pɛp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun acronym for1. personal equity plan: a method of saving in the U.K. with certain tax advantages, in which investments up to a fixed annual value can be purchased: replaced by the ISA in 1999 but arrangements for existing PEPs remain unchangedabbreviation for2. political and economic planning

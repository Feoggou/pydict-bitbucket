Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]A dulcet voice is one that is gentle and pleasant to listen to. [literary] ⇒ Quickly, in her dulcet voice, Tamara told him what had happened.2. →  dulcet tones

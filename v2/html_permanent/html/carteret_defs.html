Carteret

(ˈkɑːtərɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun John, 1st Earl Granville. 1690–1763, British statesman, diplomat, and orator who led the opposition to Walpole (1730–42), after whose fall he became a leading minister as secretary of state (1742–44)

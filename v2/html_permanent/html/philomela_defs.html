Philomela

(ˌfɪləʊˈmiːlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounGreek mythology an Athenian princess, who was raped and had her tongue cut out by her brother-in-law Tereus, and subsequently was transformed into a nightingale. See Procne

night-time

Definitions
nouna. the time from sunset to sunrise; night as distinct from dayb. (as modifier) ⇒ a night-time prowler

Definitions
uncount noun [oft N n]Night-time is the period of time between when it gets dark and when the sun rises. ⇒ They wanted someone responsible to look after the place at night-time. ⇒ A twelve hour night time curfew is in force.

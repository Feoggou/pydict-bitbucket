mildewed

(ˈmɪlˌdjuːd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of a plant) having mildew ⇒ Acanthus live virtually for ever, although they may become tired and mildewed.2. (of a surface) affected by mould ⇒ houses with overflowing septic tanks, mildewed walls and leaky ceilings

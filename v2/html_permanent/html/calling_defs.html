calling

(ˈkɔːlɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a strong inner urge to follow an occupation, etc; vocation2. an occupation, profession, or trade

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu sing]A calling is a profession or career which someone is strongly attracted to, especially one which involves helping other people. ⇒ He was a consultant physician, a serious man dedicated to his calling.

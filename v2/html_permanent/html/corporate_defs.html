corporate

(ˈkɔːpərɪt , -prɪt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. forming a corporation; incorporated2. of or belonging to a corporation or corporations ⇒ corporate finance3. of or belonging to a united group; joint
Derived forms
corporately (ˈcorporately) adverb
Word origin
C15: from Latin corporātus made into a body, from corporāre, from corpus body

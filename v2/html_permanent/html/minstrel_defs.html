minstrel

(ˈmɪnstrəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a medieval wandering musician who performed songs or recited poetry with instrumental accompaniment2. a performer in a minstrel show3. archaic or poetic any poet, musician, or singer

Word Origin

C13: from Old French menestral, from Late Latin ministeriālis an official, from Latin minister

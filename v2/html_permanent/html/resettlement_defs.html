(riːˈsɛtəlmənt )
Definitions
nouna. the act or instance of settling or being settled in another placeb. (as modifier) ⇒ resettlement procedures

resettlement

Powered by COBUILD
(riːsetəlmənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounResettlement is the process of moving people to a different place to live, because they are no longer allowed to stay in the area where they used to live. ⇒ Only refugees are eligible for resettlement abroad. ⇒ ...the forcible resettlement of villagers from the countryside into towns. ⇒ ...resettlement agencies. [Also + of]

warehouse

Definitions
noun (ˈwɛəˌhaʊs )1. a place where goods are stored prior to their use, distribution, or sale2. See bonded warehouse3. mainly British a large commercial, esp wholesale, establishmentverb (ˈwɛəˌhaʊz , -ˌhaʊs)4. (transitive) to store or place in a warehouse, esp a bonded warehouse

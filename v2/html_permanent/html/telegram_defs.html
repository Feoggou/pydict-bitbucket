telegram

(ˈtɛlɪˌɡræm )
Definitions
nouna communication transmitted by telegraph. See also cable (sense 5), Telemessage
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

telegrammatic (ˌtɛlɪɡrəˈmætɪk ) or telegrammic (ˌteleˈgrammic) adjective

Powered by COBUILD
(telɪgræm ) for learners of English
Word forms: telegrams
Definitions
countable noun [oft by N]A telegram is a message that is sent by telegraph and then printed and delivered to someone's home or office. ⇒ Scores of congratulatory telegrams and letters greeted Franklin on his return. ⇒ The President received a briefing by telegram.

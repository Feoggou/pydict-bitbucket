supernatural

(ˌsuːpəˈnætʃrəl , -ˈnætʃərəl)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to things that cannot be explained according to natural laws2. characteristic of or caused by or as if by a god; miraculous3. of, involving, or ascribed to occult beings4. exceeding the ordinary; abnormalnoun5. See the supernatural

Derived Forms

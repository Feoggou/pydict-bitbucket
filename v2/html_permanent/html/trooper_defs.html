trooper

(ˈtruːpə )
Definitions
noun1. a soldier in a cavalry regiment2. US and Australian a mounted policeman3. US a state policeman4. a cavalry horse5. informal, mainly British a troopship

trooper

Powered by COBUILD
(truːpəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: troopers
Definitions
1. countable noun & title nounA trooper is a soldier of low rank in the cavalry or in an armoured regiment in the army. ⇒ ...a trooper from the 7th Cavalry. ⇒ 'Where to, Corporal?' asked Trooper Fane respectfully.2. countable nounIn the United States, a trooper is a police officer in a state police force. ⇒ Once long ago he had considered becoming a state trooper.3. → See also storm trooper

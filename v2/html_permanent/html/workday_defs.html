workday for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(wɜːʳkdeɪ ) also work day
Word forms: workdays
Definitions
1. countable noun [usu sing]A workday is the amount of time during a day which you spend doing your job. [mainly US] working day ⇒ His workday starts at 3.30 a.m. and lasts 12 hours.regional note:   in BRIT, usually use working day2. countable nounA workday is a day on which people go to work. ⇒ What's he doing home on a workday?

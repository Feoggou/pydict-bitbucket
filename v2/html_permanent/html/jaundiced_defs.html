(dʒɔːndɪst )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If someone has a jaundiced view of something, they can see only the bad aspects of it. ⇒ The financial markets are taking a jaundiced view of the Government's motives. ⇒ The general watched the proceedings with a jaundiced eye.

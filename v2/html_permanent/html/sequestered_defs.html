sequestered

(sɪˈkwɛstəd)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. secluded2. kept isolated or apart ⇒ This jury is expected to be sequestered for at least two months.3. law requisitioned or confiscated, esp until the claims of creditors are satisfied or a court order is complied with

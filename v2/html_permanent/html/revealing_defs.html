revealing

(rɪˈviːlɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. of significance or import ⇒ a very revealing experience2. showing or designed to show more of the body than is usual or conventional ⇒ a revealing costume
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

revealingly (reˈvealingly) adverbrevealingness (reˈvealingness) noun

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA revealing statement, account, or action tells you something that you did not know, especially about the person doing it or making it. ⇒ ...a revealing interview. ⇒ ...Sophie Tucker's revealing autobiography.revealingly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ Even more revealingly, he says: 'There's no such thing as failure.'2. graded adjective & adjectiveRevealing clothes allow more of a person's body to be seen than is usual. ⇒ She was wearing a tight and revealing gold dress.

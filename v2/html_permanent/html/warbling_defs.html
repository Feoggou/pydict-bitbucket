warbling

(ˈwɔːblɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the act or an instance of singing with trills, runs, and other embellishments2. (of an inanimate object) the act of making a high, trilling sound, or the sound made
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

The warbling of the telephone inside the boat increased her happiness with a bound, because it could only be Masters. Kippax, Frank THE SCARLying in bed, Kate closes her eyes and listens to the sudden sharp warbling of seabirds down on the foreshore as the wind gets up.

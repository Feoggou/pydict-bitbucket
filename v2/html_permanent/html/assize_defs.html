assize

(əˈsaɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (in the US)a. a sitting of a legislative assembly or administrative bodyb. an enactment or order of such an assembly2. English history a trial or judicial inquest, the writ instituting such inquest, or the verdict3. Scots lawa. trial by juryb. another name for jury1
Word origin
C13: from Old French assise session, from asseoir to seat, from Latin assidēre to sit beside; see assess

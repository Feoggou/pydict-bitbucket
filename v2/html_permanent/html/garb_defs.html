garb

(ɡɑːb )
Definitions
noun1. clothes, esp the distinctive attire of an occupation or profession ⇒ clerical garb2. style of dress; fashion3. external appearance, covering, or attireverb4. (transitive) to clothe or cover; attire
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

garbless (ˈgarbless) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Old French garbe graceful contour, from Old Italian garbo grace, probably of Germanic origin

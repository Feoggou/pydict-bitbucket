neglectful

(nɪˈɡlɛktfʊl )
Definitions
adjective(when postpositive, foll by of) not giving due care and attention (to); careless; heedless
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

neglectfully (neˈglectfully) adverbneglectfulness (neˈglectfulness) noun

neglectful for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(nɪglektfʊl )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as neglectful, you think they fail to do everything they should do to look after someone or something properly. ⇒ ...neglectful parents.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is neglectful of something, they do not give it the attention or consideration that it should be given. [+ of] ⇒ Have I been neglectful of my friend, taking him for granted?
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

happily

Powered by COBUILD
(hæpɪli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adverb & adverbYou can add happily to a statement to indicate that you are glad that something happened or is true. ⇒ Happily, his neck injuries were not serious. ⇒ Happily, Lisa Martineau takes an opposite approach.2. → See also happy

senna

(ˈsɛnə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of various tropical plants of the leguminous genus Cassia, esp C. angustifolia (Arabian senna) and C. acutifolia (Alexandrian senna), having typically yellow flowers and long pods2. See senna leaf3. See senna pods▷ See also bladder senna

Word Origin

C16: via New Latin from Arabic sanā

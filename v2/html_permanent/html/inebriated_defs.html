inebriated

(ɪnˈiːbrɪeɪtɪd)
Definitions
adjectiveformal drunk, esp habitually ⇒ Scott was obviously inebriated by the time the dessert was served., ⇒ In the bar, he'd entertained a captive and inebriated audience with tales of growing up in Russia.

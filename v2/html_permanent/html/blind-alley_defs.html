blind alley

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an alley open at one end only; cul-de-sac2. informal a situation in which no further progress can be made

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf you describe a situation as a blind alley, you mean that progress is not possible or that the situation can have no useful results. ⇒ The Internet has proved a blind alley for many firms.

Comment by richard_bradshaw_watson_ii on 10 Sep 2015
==Scientific explanation== The Planet Venus is so bright that at certain times it casts a shadow on Earth. The ancient mystics saw this as a very special event and tried to capture the light of the goddess in their temples. The one-God Hebrews broke away from the practice of treating the astronomical objects as gods practiced by their neighbors the Sumerians/Babylonians, Egyptians, Persians, Greeks, etc., therefore, the Hebrew mystics kept the source of the Shekinah very secret.

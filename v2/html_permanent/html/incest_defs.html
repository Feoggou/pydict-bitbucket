incest

(ˈɪnsɛst )
Definitions
nounsexual intercourse between two persons commonly regarded as too closely related to marry
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Latin incestus incest (from adj: impure, defiled), from in-1 + castus chaste

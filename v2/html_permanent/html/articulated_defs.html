Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]An articulated vehicle, especially a lorry, is made in two or more sections which are joined together by metal bars, so that the vehicle can turn more easily. [British]regional note:   in AM, usually use rig, trailer truck

beep

(biːp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a short high-pitched sound, esp one made by the horn of a car, bicycle, etc, or by electronic apparatusverb2. to make or cause to make such a noise
Derived forms
beeper (ˈbeeper) noun
Word origin

(biːp )
Word forms: beeps , beeping , beeped
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA beep is a short, loud sound like that made by a car horn or a telephone answering machine.2. ergative verb & verbIf something such as a horn beeps, or you beep it, it makes a short, harsh sound. [V] ⇒ A cellular telephone beeped. [V n] ⇒ He beeped the horn.

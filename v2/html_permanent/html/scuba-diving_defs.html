(ˈskuːbə ˈdaɪvɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe practice of diving with scuba equipment ⇒ There's a variety of activities from scuba diving to windsurfing., ⇒ The couple often sailed and went scuba diving together.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounScuba diving is the activity of swimming underwater using special breathing equipment. The equipment consists of cylinders of air which you carry on your back and which are connected to your mouth by rubber tubes.scuba dive verb [V] ⇒ I signed up to learn how to scuba dive.

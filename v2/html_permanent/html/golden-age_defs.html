golden age

Definitions
noun1. classical mythology the first and best age of mankind, when existence was happy, prosperous, and innocent2. the most flourishing and outstanding period, esp in the history of an art or nation ⇒ the golden age of poetry3. the great classical period of Latin literature, occupying approximately the 1st century bc and represented by such writers as Cicero and Virgil

Definitions
countable nounA golden age is a period of time during which a very high level of achievement is reached in a particular field of activity, especially in art or literature. [+ of] ⇒ You grew up in the golden age of American children's books.

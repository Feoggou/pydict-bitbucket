potion

(ˈpəʊʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a drink, esp of medicine, poison, or some supposedly magic beverage2. a rare word for beverage

Word Origin

C13: via Old French from Latin pōtiō a drink, especially a poisonous one, from pōtāre to drink

(prəfɪʃənsi )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounIf you show proficiency in something, you show ability or skill at it. [+ in] ⇒ Evidence of basic proficiency in English is part of the admission requirement.

Similar Words

skill, ability, know-how (informal), talent, facility, craft, expertise, competence, accomplishment, mastery, knack, aptitude, dexterity, expertness, skilfulness

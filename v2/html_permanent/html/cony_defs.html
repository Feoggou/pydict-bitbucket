cony

or coney (ˈkəʊnɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -nies or -neys1. a rabbit or fur made from the skin of a rabbit2. (in the Bible) another name for the hyrax, esp the Syrian rock hyrax3. another name for pika4. archaic a fool or dupe
Word origin
C13: back formation from conies, from Old French conis, plural of conil, from Latin cunīculus rabbit

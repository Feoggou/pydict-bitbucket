warlock

(ˈwɔːˌlɒk )
Definitions
noun1. a man who practises black magic; sorcerer2. a fortune-teller, conjuror, or magician
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English wǣrloga oath breaker, from wǣr oath + -loga liar, from lēogan to lie1

Warlock

(ˈwɔːˌlɒk )
Definitions
noun Peter, real name Philip Arnold Heseltine. 1894–1930, British composer and scholar of early English music. His works include song cycles, such as The Curlew (1920–22), and the Capriol Suite (1926) for strings
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

cannibal

(ˈkænɪbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a person who eats the flesh of other human beingsb. (as modifier) ⇒ cannibal tribes2. an animal that feeds on the flesh of others of its kind
Word origin
C16: from Spanish Canibales, name used by Columbus to designate the Caribs of Cuba and Haiti, from Arawak caniba, variant of Carib

Ithaca

(ˈɪθəkə )
Definitions
nouna Greek island in the Ionian Sea, the smallest of the Ionian Islands: regarded as the home of Homer's Odysseus. Area: 93 sq km (36 sq miles). Modern Greek name: Itháki (iˈθaki)

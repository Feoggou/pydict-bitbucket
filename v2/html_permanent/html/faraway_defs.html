faraway

(ˈfɑːrəˌweɪ )
Definitions
adjective (far away when postpositive)1. very distant; remote2. dreamy or absent-minded

faraway

Powered by COBUILD
(fɑːrəweɪ ) also far-away for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]A faraway place is a long distance from you or from a particular place. ⇒ They have just returned from faraway places with wonderful stories to tell. ⇒ ...photographs of a far away country.2. graded adjective [ADJ n]If you describe someone or their thoughts as faraway, you mean that they are thinking about something that is very different from the situation around them. dreamy ⇒ She smiled with a faraway look in her eyes.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

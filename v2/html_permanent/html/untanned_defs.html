untanned

(ʌnˈtænd)
Definitions
adjective1. not converted into leather by treating it with a tanning agent, such as vegetable tannins, chromium salts, fish oils, or formaldehyde ⇒ a camp of untanned cattle-hide tents2. not brown after exposure to ultraviolet rays ⇒ pale, untanned skin

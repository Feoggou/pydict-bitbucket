brigadier

(ˌbrɪɡəˈdɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an officer of the British Army or Royal Marines who holds a rank junior to a major general but senior to a colonel, usually commanding a brigade2. an equivalent rank in other armed forces3. US army short for brigadier general4. history a noncommissioned rank in the armies of Napoleon I
Word origin
C17: from French, from brigade

prostate

(ˈprɒsteɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: prostate gland a gland in male mammals that surrounds the neck of the bladder and urethra and secretes a liquid constituent of the semenadjective2. Also: prostatic (prɒˈstætɪk ) of or relating to the prostate gland. See also PSA

Word Origin

C17: via Medieval Latin from Greek prostatēs something standing in front (of the bladder), from pro- in front + histanai to cause to stand

or else for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
phraseYou say 'or else' after a command to warn someone that if they do not obey, you will be angry and may harm or punish them. [spoken] ⇒ Behave, or else! ⇒ He told us to put it right, or else. →  else

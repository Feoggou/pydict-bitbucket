misfortune

(mɪsˈfɔːtʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. evil fortune; bad luck2. an unfortunate or disastrous event; calamity

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounA misfortune is something unpleasant or unlucky that happens to someone. [+ of] ⇒ She seemed to enjoy the misfortunes of others. ⇒ He had his full share of misfortune.

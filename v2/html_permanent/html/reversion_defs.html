reversion

(rɪˈvɜːʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a return to or towards an earlier condition, practice, or belief; act of reverting2. the act of reversing or the state of being reversed; reversal3. biologya. the return of individuals, organs, etc, to a more primitive condition or typeb. the reappearance of primitive characteristics in an individual or group4. property lawa. an interest in an estate that reverts to the grantor or his heirs at the end of a period, esp at the end of the life of a granteeb. an estate so revertingc. the right to succeed to such an estate5. the benefit payable on the death of a life-insurance policyholder
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

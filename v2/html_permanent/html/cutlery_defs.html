cutlery

(ˈkʌtlərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. implements used for eating, such as knives, forks, and spoons2. instruments used for cutting3. the art or business of a cutler

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounCutlery consists of the knives, forks, and spoons that you eat your food with. [British] ⇒ She arranged plates and cutlery on a small table.regional note:   in AM, use silverware, flatware2. uncount nounYou can refer to knives and tools used for cutting as cutlery. [US]

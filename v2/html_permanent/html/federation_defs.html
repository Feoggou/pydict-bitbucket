federation

(ˌfɛdəˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of federating2. the union of several provinces, states, etc, to form a federal union3. a political unit formed in such a way4. any league, alliance, or confederacy5. a union of several parties, groups, etc6. any association or union for common action

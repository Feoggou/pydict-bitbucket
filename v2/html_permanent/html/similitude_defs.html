similitude

(sɪˈmɪlɪˌtjuːd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. likeness; similarity2. a thing or sometimes a person that is like or the counterpart of another3. archaic a simile, allegory, or parable

Word Origin

C14: from Latin similitūdō, from similis like
Example sentences containing 'similitude'
The philosophers are misled, I have come to believe, when they teach us that sleep is death's similitude. Clive Barker GALILEE

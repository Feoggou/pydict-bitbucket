attar

(ˈætə ), otto (ˈɒtəʊ ) or ottar (ˈɒtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan essential oil from flowers, esp the damask rose, used pure or as a base for perfume ⇒ attar of roses
Word origin

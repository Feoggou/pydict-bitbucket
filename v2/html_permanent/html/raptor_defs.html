raptor

(ˈræptə )
Definitions
noun1. another name for bird of prey2. informal a carnivorous bipedal dinosaur of the late Cretaceous period
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin: plunderer, from rapere to take by force

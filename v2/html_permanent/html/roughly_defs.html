roughly

(ˈrʌflɪ )
Definitions
adverb1. without being exact or fully authenticated; approximately ⇒ roughly half the candidates were successful2. in a clumsy, coarse, or violent manner ⇒ his captors did not treat him roughly3. in a crude or primitive manner ⇒ a slab of roughly hewn stone
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Her parents, who were killed in a car accident, lived there - roughly , I guess, in the late seventies.

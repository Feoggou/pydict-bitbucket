naevus

or US nevus (ˈniːvəs )
Definitions
noun plural -vi (-vaɪ)any congenital growth or pigmented blemish on the skin; birthmark or mole. Also called: naeve
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

naevoid (ˈnaevoid) or US nevoid (ˈnevoid) adjective

Word Origin

C19: from Latin; related to (g)natus born, produced by nature

Similar Words

birthmark, mole

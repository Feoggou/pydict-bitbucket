wilderness

(ˈwɪldənɪs )
Definitions
noun1. a wild, uninhabited, and uncultivated region2. any desolate tract or area3. a confused mass or collection4. See a voice in the wilderness5. See in the wilderness
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English wildēornes, from wildēor wild beast (from wild + dēor beast, deer) + -ness; related to Middle Dutch wildernisse, German Wildernis

Definitions
1. countable noun [usu sing]A wilderness is a desert or other area of natural land which is not used by people. ⇒ ...the icy Canadian wilderness. ⇒ He is proud of the garden he made from a wilderness. ⇒ ...one of the largest wilderness areas in North America.2. →  in the wilderness

Enoch

(ˈiːnɒk )
Definitions
noun Old Testament1. the eldest son of Cain after whom the first city was named (Genesis 4:17)2. the father of Methuselah: said to have walked with God and to have been taken by God at the end of his earthly life (Genesis 5:24)

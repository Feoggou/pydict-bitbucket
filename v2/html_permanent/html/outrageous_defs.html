outrageous

(aʊtˈreɪdʒəs )
Definitions
adjective1. being or having the nature of an outrage2. grossly offensive to decency, authority, etc3. violent or unrestrained in behaviour or temperament4. extravagant or immoderate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

outrageously (outˈrageously) adverboutrageousness (outˈrageousness) noun

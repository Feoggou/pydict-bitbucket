condolence

(kənˈdəʊləns ) or condolement (kənˈdəʊlmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(often plural) an expression of sympathy with someone in grief, etc

condolence

Powered by COBUILD
(kəndoʊləns )
Word forms: condolences
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounA message of condolence is a message in which you express your sympathy for someone because one of their friends or relatives has died recently. ⇒ Neil sent him a letter of condolence.2. plural nounWhen you offer or express your condolences to someone, you express your sympathy for them because one of their friends or relatives has died recently. ⇒ He expressed his condolences to the families of the people who died in the incident.

Synonyms of faithful

faithful

adjective

  1. loyal, true, committed, constant, attached, devoted, dedicated, reliable, staunch, truthful, dependable, trusty, steadfast, unwavering, true-blue, immovable, unswerving
    • Older Americans are among this country's most faithful voters.
    • She had remained faithful to her husband.
    OPPOSITES: doubting, false, untrue, unreliable, treacherous, fickle, unfaithful, disloyal, faithless, untrustworthy, perfidious, untruthful, traitorous, unbelieving, inconstant, false-hearted, recreant (archaic)
  2. accurate, just, close, true, strict, exact, precise
    • His screenplay is faithful to the novel.
  • See the faithful

Highness

Powered by COBUILD
(haɪnɪs ) for learners of English
Word forms: Highnesses
Definitions
countable nounExpressions such as 'Your Highness' or 'His Highness' are used to address or refer to a member of the royal family other than a king or queen. [politeness] ⇒ That would be best, Your Highness. ⇒ Her Royal Highness the Duchess of Kent opened an art exhibition recently.

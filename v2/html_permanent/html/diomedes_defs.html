Diomedes

(ˌdaɪəˈmiːdiːz ), Diomede or Diomed (ˈdaɪəˌmɛd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Greek mythology1. a king of Argos, and suitor of Helen, who fought with the Greeks at Troy2. a king of the Bistones in Thrace whose savage horses ate strangers
Example sentences containing 'Diomedes'
Then he'd have to get a smaller plane to take them to the Diomedes. Maupin, Armistead FURTHER TALES OF THE CITY

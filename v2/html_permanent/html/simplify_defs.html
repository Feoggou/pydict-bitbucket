simplify

(ˈsɪmplɪˌfaɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -fies, -fying or -fied (transitive)1. to make less complicated, clearer, or easier2. mathematics to reduce (an equation, fraction, etc) to a simpler form by cancellation of common factors, regrouping of terms in the same variable, etc

Derived Forms

ˌsimplification (ˌsimplifiˈcation) nounsimplificative (ˈsimplificative) adjectivesimpliˌfier (ˈsimpliˌfier) noun

Word Origin

C17: via French from Medieval Latin simplificāre, from Latin simplus simple + facere to make

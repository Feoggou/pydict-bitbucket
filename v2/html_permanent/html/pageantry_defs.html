(ˈpædʒəntrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ries1. spectacular display or ceremony2. archaic pageants collectively

pageantry

(pædʒəntri )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounPeople use pageantry to refer to the colourful and formal things that are done for special official or royal occasions, for example the wearing of special clothes and the playing of special music. ⇒ ...all the pageantry of an official state visit.

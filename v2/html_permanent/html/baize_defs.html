baize

(beɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a woollen fabric resembling felt, usually green, used mainly for the tops of billiard tablesverb2. (transitive) to line or cover with such fabric
Word origin
C16: from Old French baies, plural of baie baize, from bai reddish brown, bay5, perhaps the original colour of the fabric

immanent

(ˈɪmənənt )
Definitions
adjective1. existing, operating, or remaining within; inherent2. of or relating to the pantheistic conception of God, as being present throughout the universe. Compare transcendent (sense 3)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

immanence (ˈimmanence) or immanency (ˈimmanency) nounimmanently (ˈimmanently) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Latin immanēre to remain in, from im- (in) + manēre to stay

first-generation

(ˌfɜːstdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən)
Definitions
adjective1. belonging to the first generation of people who go to live in another country ⇒ A first-generation American, he came from a poor Italian migrant family.2. of the first type of a particular machine or piece of equipment to be produced ⇒ the nuclear industry's first-generation Magnox reactors

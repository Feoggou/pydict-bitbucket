globule

(ˈɡlɒbjuːl )
Definitions
noun1. a small globe, esp a drop of liquid2. astronomy a small dark nebula thought to be a site of star formation
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin globulus, diminutive of globus globe

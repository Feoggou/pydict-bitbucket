grits

(ɡrɪts )
Definitions
plural noun1. hulled and coarsely ground grain2. US See hominy grits
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English grytt; related to Old High German gruzzi; see great, grit

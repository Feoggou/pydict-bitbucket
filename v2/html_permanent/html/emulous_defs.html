emulous

(ˈɛmjʊləs )
Definitions
adjective1. desiring or aiming to equal or surpass another; competitive2. characterized by or arising from emulation or imitation3. archaic envious or jealous
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

emulously (ˈemulously) adverbemulousness (ˈemulousness) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Latin aemulus rivalling; see emulate

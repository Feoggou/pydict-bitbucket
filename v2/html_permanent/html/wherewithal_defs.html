wherewithal for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(hweəʳwɪðɔːl )
Definitions
singular noun [oft N to-inf]If you have the wherewithal for something, you have the means, especially the money, that you need for it. ⇒ She didn't have the financial wherewithal to do it. ⇒ Some of the companies illegally sent the wherewithal for making chemical weapons. [Also + for]
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

griping

(ˈɡraɪpɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. informal the act of complaining ⇒ Still, the griping went on., ⇒ That led to griping from some members.adjective2. sudden and intense ⇒ griping pains in her stomach, ⇒ I was seized with terrible griping pains.

homicide

(ˈhɒmɪˌsaɪd )
Definitions
noun1. the killing of a human being by another person2. a person who kills another
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French, from Latin homo man + caedere to slay

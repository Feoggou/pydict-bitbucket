gean

(ɡiːn )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: wild cherry a white-flowered rosaceous tree, Prunus avium, of Europe, W Asia, and N Africa, the ancestor of the cultivated sweet cherries2. See sweet cherry (sense 2)

Word Origin

runt

(rʌnt )
Definitions
noun1. the smallest and weakest young animal in a litter, esp the smallest piglet in a litter2. derogatory an undersized or inferior person3. a large pigeon, originally bred for eating
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

runtish (ˈruntish) adjectiverunty (ˈrunty) adjectiveruntiness (ˈruntiness) noun

Word Origin

C16: origin unknown

runt

Powered by COBUILD
(rʌnt ) for learners of English
Word forms: runts
Definitions
1. countable nounThe runt of a group of animals born to the same mother at the same time is the smallest and weakest of them. [+ of] ⇒ Animals reject the runt of the litter.2. countable nounYou call a small person a runt when you are annoyed with them and are expressing your dislike for them. [British, informal, offensive, disapproval] ⇒ You little runt! ⇒ My research owes nothing to anybody, least of all to a little runt like you.

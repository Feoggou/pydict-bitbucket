Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as comical, you mean that it makes you want to laugh because it seems funny or silly. ⇒ Her expression is almost comical. ⇒ Events took a comical turn.comically (kɒmɪkli ) graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj] ⇒ She raised her eyebrows comically. ⇒ The display of prehistoric monsters is comically naive.

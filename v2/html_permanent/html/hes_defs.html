he's

Powered by COBUILD
(hɪz , hiːz ) for learners of English
Definitions
He's is the usual spoken form of 'he is' or 'he has', especially when 'has' is an auxiliary verb. ⇒ He's working maybe twenty-five hours a week. ⇒ From day one he's been a great asset to the company.

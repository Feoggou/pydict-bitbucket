awash

(əˈwɒʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb, adjective (postpositive) nautical1. at a level even with the surface of the sea2. washed over by the waves

(əwɒʃ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [v-link ADJ]If the ground or a floor is awash, it is covered in water, often because of heavy rain or as the result of an accident. flooded ⇒ The bathroom floor was awash.2. adjective [v-link ADJ]If a place is awash with something, it contains a large amount of it. [+ with] ⇒ This, after all, is a company which is awash with cash.

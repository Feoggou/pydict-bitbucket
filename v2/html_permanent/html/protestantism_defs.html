Protestantism

(ˈprɒtɪstənˌtɪzəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the religion or religious system of any of the Churches of Western Christendom that are separated from the Roman Catholic Church and adhere substantially to principles established by Luther, Calvin, etc, in the Reformation2. the Protestant Churches collectively3. adherence to the principles of the Reformation

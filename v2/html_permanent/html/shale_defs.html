shale

(ʃeɪl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna dark fine-grained laminated sedimentary rock formed by compression of successive layers of clay-rich sediment

Derived Forms

shaly (ˈshaly) adjective

Word Origin

Old English scealu shell; compare German Schalstein laminated limestone; see scale1, scale2

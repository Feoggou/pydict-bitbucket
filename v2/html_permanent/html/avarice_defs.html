Definitions

nounextreme greed for riches; cupidity
Derived forms
ˌavaˈricious adjectiveˌavaˈriciously adverbˌavaˈriciousness noun
Word origin
C13: from Old French, from Latin avaritia, from avārus covetous, from avēre to crave

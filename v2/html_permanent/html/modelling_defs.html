modelling

or US modeling (ˈmɒdəlɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or an instance of making a model2. the practice or occupation of a person who models clothes3. a technique in psychotherapy in which the therapist encourages the patient to model his behaviour on his own
Example sentences containing 'modelling'
During one of these modelling sessions she was interrupted by her mother. Melville, Anne THE HARDIE INHERITANCEBut -- "She took the lid off the old dustbin in which she kept her modelling clay.

dethrone

(dɪˈθrəʊn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(transitive) to remove from a throne or deprive of any high position or title; depose ⇒ the champion was dethroned by a young boxer
Derived forms
dethronement (deˈthronement) noundethroner (deˈthroner) noun

verb [usu passive]If a king, queen, or other powerful person is dethroned, they are removed from their position of power. [be V-ed] ⇒ He was dethroned and went into exile.

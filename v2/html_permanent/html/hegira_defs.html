Hegira

or Hejira (ˈhɛdʒɪrə )
Definitions
noun1. the departure of Mohammed from Mecca to Medina in 622 ad; the starting point of the Muslim era2. the Muslim era itself. See also AH3. (often not capital) an emigration escape or flight
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Medieval Latin, from Arabic hijrah emigration or flight

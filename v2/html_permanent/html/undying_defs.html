Powered by COBUILD
(ʌndaɪɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you refer to someone's undying feelings, you mean that the feelings are very strong and are unlikely to change. [literary] ⇒ Dianne declared her undying love for Sam. ⇒ He had won her undying gratitude. ⇒ You need an undying belief in your own ability.

culmination

(ˌkʌlmɪˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the final, highest, or decisive point2. the act of culminating3. astronomy the highest or lowest altitude attained by a heavenly body as it crosses the meridian

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
singular nounSomething, especially something important, that is the culmination of an activity, process, or series of events happens at the end of it. [+ of] ⇒ Their arrest was the culmination of an operation in which 120 other people were detained.

annual

(ˈænjʊəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. occurring, done, etc, once a year or every year; yearly ⇒ an annual income2. lasting for a year ⇒ an annual subscriptionnoun3. a plant that completes its life cycle in less than one year. Compare perennial (sense 3), biennial (sense 3)4. a book, magazine, etc, published once every year
Derived forms
ˈannually adverb
Word origin
C14: from Late Latin annuālis, from Latin annuus yearly, from annus year

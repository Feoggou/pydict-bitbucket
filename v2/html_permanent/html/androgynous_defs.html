androgynous

(ænˈdrɒdʒɪnəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. botany having male and female flowers in the same inflorescence, as cuckoo pint2. having male and female characteristics; hermaphrodite
Derived forms
anˈdrogyny noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]In biology, an androgynous person, animal, or plant has both male and female sexual characteristics. [technical] 2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone as androgynous, you mean that they are not distinctly masculine or feminine in appearance or in behaviour. ⇒ Belinda was always attracted to men with an androgynous quality to them. ⇒ The style was quite androgynous.

herbaceous

(hɜːˈbeɪʃəs )
Definitions
adjective1. designating or relating to plants or plant parts that are fleshy as opposed to woody ⇒ a herbaceous plant2. (of petals and sepals) green and leaflike3. of or relating to herbs
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

herbaceously (herˈbaceously) adverb

side-on

(ˌsaɪdˈɒn)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. involving the side of a vehicle ⇒ steel beams built into the doors for protection against a side-on crash2. directed from or towards a side ⇒ Fergie had seen only a side-on view of the incident.adverb3. from the side ⇒ I careered side-on into a wall, smashed off one of the handles, and landed in an embarrassed heap., ⇒ Their car was hit side-on by a speeding truck.

unmistakable

or unmistakeable (ˌʌnmɪsˈteɪkəbəl )
Definitions
adjectivenot mistakable; clear, obvious, or unambiguous

unmistakable for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnmɪsteɪkəbəl ) also unmistakeable
Definitions
adjectiveIf you describe something as unmistakable, you mean that it is so obvious that it cannot be mistaken for anything else. ⇒ He didn't give his name, but the voice was unmistakable. ⇒ ...the unmistakable smell of marijuana drifted down.unmistakably (ʌnmɪsteɪkəbli ) adverb [usu ADV group, oft ADV with v] ⇒ It's still unmistakably a Minnelli movie. ⇒ She's unmistakably Scandinavian. ⇒ He had unmistakably been waving his flag to attract the referee's attention.

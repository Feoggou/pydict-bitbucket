phone call

(fəʊn kɔːl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounif you make a phone call, you dial someone's phone number and speak to them by phone ⇒ I had three phone calls last night., ⇒ There's a phone call for you., ⇒ Wait there a minute. I just have to make a phone call., ⇒ Can I make a phone call?

localized

or localised (ˈləʊkəˌlaɪzd)
Definitions
adjectiveoccurring or concentrating in a particular place; restricted or limited ⇒ She had localized breast cancer and both of her doctors had advised surgery., ⇒ Community radio is a concept which is much more localised.

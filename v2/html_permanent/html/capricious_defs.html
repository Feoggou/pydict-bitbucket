[literary] unpredictable ⇒ Both sides were troubled throughout by a capricious wind. ⇒ ...a theatre with notoriously capricious acoustics.capriciousness uncount noun [usu N of n] unpredictability ⇒ Pat stuck it out, despite the capriciousness and inhospitality of the English weather.

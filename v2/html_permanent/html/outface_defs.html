outface

(ˌaʊtˈfeɪs )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to face or stare down2. to confront boldly or defiantly
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'outface'
`We know that we are weak, that we dare not outface this evil man. Townsend, Eileen IN LOVE AND WAR

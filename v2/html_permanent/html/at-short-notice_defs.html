at short notice

Powered by COBUILD
or at/(on) short notice/a moment's notice/minutes, hours, days notice
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseNotice is used in expressions such as 'at short notice', 'at a moment's notice' or 'at twenty-four hours' notice', to indicate that something can or must be done within a short period of time. ⇒ There's no one available at such short notice to take her class. ⇒ All our things stayed in our suitcase, as if we had to leave at a moment's notice. →  notice

simultaneous

(ˌsɪməlˈteɪnɪəs , US English ˌsaɪməlˈteɪnɪəs)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. occurring, existing, or operating at the same time; concurrentnoun2. chess a display in which one player plays a number of opponents at once, walking from board to board. Sometimes shortened to: simul

Derived Forms

ˌsimultaneously (ˌsimulˈtaneously) adverbˌsimultaneousness (ˌsimulˈtaneousness) or simultaneity (ˌsɪməltəˈniːɪtɪ, US English ˌsaɪməltəˈniːɪtɪ) noun

Word Origin

C17: formed on the model of instantaneous from Latin simul at the same time, together

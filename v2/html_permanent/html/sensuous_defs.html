sensuous

(ˈsɛnsjʊəs )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. aesthetically pleasing to the senses2. appreciative of or moved by qualities perceived by the senses3. of, relating to, or derived from the senses

Derived Forms

sensuously (ˈsensuously) adverbsensuousness (ˈsensuousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: apparently coined by Milton to avoid the unwanted overtones of sensual; not in common use until C19: from Latin sēnsus sense + -ous

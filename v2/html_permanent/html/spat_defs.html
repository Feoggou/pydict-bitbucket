spat1

(spæt )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. rare a slap or smack2. a slight quarrelverbWord forms: spats, spatting or spatted3. rare to slap (someone)4. (intransitive) US, Canadian and New Zealand to have a slight quarrel

Word Origin

C19: probably imitative of the sound of quarrelling

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. Spat is the past tense and past participle of spit.2. countable nounA spat between people, countries, or organizations is a disagreement between them. ⇒ ...a spat between America and Germany over interest rates and currencies.3. plural nounSpats are specially shaped pieces of cloth or leather which button down one side and which were worn in former times by men over their ankles and part of their shoes.

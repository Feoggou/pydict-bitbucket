fortunate

(ˈfɔːtʃənɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. having good luck; lucky2. occurring by or bringing good fortune or luck; auspicious
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fortunate

Powered by COBUILD
(fɔːʳtʃʊnɪt ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [oft ADJ to-inf]If you say that someone or something is fortunate, you mean that they are lucky. ⇒ He was extremely fortunate to survive. ⇒ Central London is fortunate in having so many large parks and open spaces. ⇒ It was fortunate that the water was shallow. ⇒ She is in the fortunate position of having plenty of choice.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

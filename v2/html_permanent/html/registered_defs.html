registered

(ˈrɛdʒɪstəd)
Definitions
adjective1. officially entered on a register or list; formally recorded2. British (of mail) sent using a Post Office service by which compensation is paid for loss or damage to mail for which a registration fee has been paid ⇒ an urgent registered letter, ⇒ He asked his mother to send it by registered mail.▶ USAGE The abbreviation for registered is R. or r. or regd

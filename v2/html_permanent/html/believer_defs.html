(bɪliːvəʳ )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu adj N]If you are a great believer in something, you think that it is good, right, or useful. [+ in] ⇒ Mum was a great believer in herbal medicines.2. countable nounA believer is someone who is sure that God exists or that their religion is true. ⇒ I made no secret of the fact that I was not a believer.

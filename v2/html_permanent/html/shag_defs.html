shag1

(ʃæɡ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a matted tangle, esp of hair, wool, etc2. a napped fabric, usually a rough wool3. shredded coarse tobaccoverbWord forms: shags, shagging or shagged4. (transitive) to make shaggy

Word Origin

Old English sceacga; related to sceaga shaw1, Old Norse skegg beard, skagi tip, skōgr forest

Word Origin

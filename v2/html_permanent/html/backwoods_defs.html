backwoods

(ˈbækwʊdz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. mainly US and Canadian partially cleared, sparsely populated forests2. any remote sparsely populated place3. (modifier) of, from, or like the backwoods4. (modifier) uncouth; rustic

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
plural nounIf you refer to an area as the backwoods, you mean that it is a long way from large towns and is isolated from modern life. ⇒ ...the backwoods of Louisiana. ⇒ ...a backwoods section of Kentucky.

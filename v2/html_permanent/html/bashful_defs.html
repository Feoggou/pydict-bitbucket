bashful

(ˈbæʃfʊl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. disposed to attempt to avoid notice through shyness or modesty; diffident; timid2. indicating or characterized by shyness or modesty
Derived forms
bashfully (ˈbashfully) adverbbashfulness (ˈbashfulness) noun
Word origin
C16: from bash, short for abash + -ful

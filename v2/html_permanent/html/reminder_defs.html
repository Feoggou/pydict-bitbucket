reminder

(rɪˈmaɪndə )
Definitions
noun1. something that recalls the past2. a note to remind a person of something not done

reminder

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪmaɪndəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: reminders
Definitions
1. countable noun [usu sing, N that]Something that serves as a reminder of another thing makes you think about the other thing. [written] [+ of] ⇒ The British are about to be given a sharp reminder of what fighting abroad really means. ⇒ Violence has broken out in the capital, a stark reminder that the religious tensions are refusing to go away.2. countable nounA reminder is a letter or note that is sent to tell you that you have not done something such as pay a bill or return library books. [mainly British] ⇒ ...the final reminder for the gas bill.

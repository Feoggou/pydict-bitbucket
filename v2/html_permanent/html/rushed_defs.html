rushed

(rʌʃt)
Definitions
adjective1. undertaken quickly or without much care; hurried ⇒ a rushed job2. (of a person) forced to do something quickly or without much care ⇒ At no time did I feel rushed or under pressure.3. See be rushed off your feet

feria

(ˈfɪərɪə )
Definitions
noun plural -rias or -riae (-rɪˌiː)Roman Catholic Church a weekday, other than Saturday, on which no feast occurs
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from Late Latin: day of the week (as in prīma fēria Sunday), singular of Latin fēriae festivals

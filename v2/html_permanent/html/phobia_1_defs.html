phobia

(ˈfəʊbɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounpsychiatry an abnormal intense and irrational fear of a given situation, organism, or object. Also called: phobism (ˈfəʊbɪzəm)

Word Origin

C19: from Greek phobos fear

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA phobia is a very strong irrational fear or hatred of something. [+ about/of] ⇒ The man had a phobia about flying.

Synonyms of glimpse

glimpse

noun

  1. look, sighting, sight, glance, peep, peek, squint, butcher's (British, slang), quick look, gander (informal), brief view, shufti (British, slang)
    • The fans waited outside the hotel to get a glimpse of their heroine.

verb

  1. catch sight of, spot, sight, view, clock (British, informal), spy, espy, descry
    • She glimpsed a group of people standing on the bank of a river.

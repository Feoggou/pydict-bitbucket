unlawful

(ʌnˈlɔːfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. illegal2. illicit; immoral ⇒ unlawful love3. an archaic word for illegitimate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unlawfully (unˈlawfully) adverbunlawfulness (unˈlawfulness) noun

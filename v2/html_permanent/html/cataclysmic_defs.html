Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveA cataclysmic event is one that changes a situation or society very greatly, especially in an unpleasant way. [formal] ⇒ ...the cataclysmic events that were destroying his faith in humanity. ⇒ Few had expected that change to be as cataclysmic as it turned out to be.

Synonyms of turn out

turn out

  1. prove to be, transpire, become apparent, happen, emerge, become known, develop, roll up, come to light, crop up (informal)
    • It turned out that I knew the person who got shot.
  2. end up, happen, result, work out, evolve, come to be, come about, transpire, pan out (informal), eventuate
    • Things don't always turn out the way we expect.
  3. come, be present, turn up, show up (informal), go, appear, attend, gather, assemble, put in an appearance
    • Thousands of people turned out for the funeral.
See turn

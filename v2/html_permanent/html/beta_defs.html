beta

(ˈbiːtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the second letter in the Greek alphabet (Β, β), a consonant, transliterated as b2. the second highest grade or mark, as in an examination3. (modifier)a. involving or relating to electrons ⇒ beta emitterb. relating to one of two or more allotropes or crystal structures of a solid ⇒ beta ironc. relating to one of two or more isomeric forms of a chemical compound
Word origin
from Greek bēta, from Hebrew; see beth

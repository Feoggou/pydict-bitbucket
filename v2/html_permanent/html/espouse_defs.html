espouse

(ɪˈspaʊz )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to adopt or give support to (a cause, ideal, etc) ⇒ to espouse socialism2. archaic (esp of a man) to take as spouse; marry
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

espouser (esˈpouser) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Old French espouser, from Latin spōnsāre to affiance, espouse

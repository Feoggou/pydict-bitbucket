raze

or rase (reɪz )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to demolish (a town, buildings, etc) completely; level (esp in the phrase raze to the ground)2. to delete; erase3. archaic to graze
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

razer (ˈrazer) or raser (ˈraser) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Old French raser from Latin rādere to scrape

feu

(fjuː )
Definitions
noun1. Scottish legal historya. a feudal tenure of land for which rent was paid in money or grain instead of by the performance of military serviceb. the land so held2. Scots law a right to the use of land in return for a fixed annual payment (feu duty)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French; see fee

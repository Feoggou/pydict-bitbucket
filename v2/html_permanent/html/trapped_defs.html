trapped

(træpt)
Definitions
adjective1. (of an animal) caught in a trap2. unable to move or escape as a result of obstruction ⇒ Many people are still trapped under the rubble.3. unable to escape, as from a situation ⇒ people who think of themselves as trapped in mundane jobs, ⇒ Many women are trapped in loveless marriages., ⇒ He follows me everywhere and it makes me feel so trapped.4. unable to be moved as a result of obstruction ⇒ The train was trapped underground by a fire.5. jammed; entangled or caught ⇒ Her hair got trapped in the zip of her jacket.6. (of gas, water, or energy) prevented from escaping ⇒ The volume of gas trapped on these surfaces can be considerable.

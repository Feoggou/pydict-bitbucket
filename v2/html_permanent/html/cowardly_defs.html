Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as cowardly, you disapprove of them because they are easily frightened and avoid doing dangerous and difficult things. [disapproval] ⇒ I was too cowardly to complain. ⇒ ...a cowardly act of violence.

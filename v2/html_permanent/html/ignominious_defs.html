ignominious

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪgnəmɪniəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe an experience or action as ignominious, you mean it is embarrassing because it shows a great lack of success. [formal] ⇒ ...their ignominious defeat. ⇒ Many thought that he was doomed to ignominious failure.ignominiously graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Their soldiers had to retreat ignominiously after losing hundreds of lives.

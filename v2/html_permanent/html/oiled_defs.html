oiled for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ɔɪld )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Something that is oiled has had oil put into or onto it, for example to make it work smoothly or to protect it. ⇒ Oiled wood is water-resistant and won't flake.2. → See also well-oiled

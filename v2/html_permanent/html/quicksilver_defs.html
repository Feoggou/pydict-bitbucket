quicksilver

(ˈkwɪkˌsɪlvə )
Definitions
noun1. another name for mercury (sense 1)adjective2. rapid or unpredictable in movement or change ⇒ a quicksilver temper
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English, from cwicu alive + seolfer silver

Definitions

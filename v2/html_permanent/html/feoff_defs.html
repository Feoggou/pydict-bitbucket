feoff

(fiːf ) medieval history
Definitions
noun1. a variant spelling of fiefverb2. (transitive) to invest with a benefice or fief
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

feoffor (ˈfeoffor) or feoffer (ˈfeoffer) noun

Word Origin

C13: from Anglo-French feoffer, from feoff a fief

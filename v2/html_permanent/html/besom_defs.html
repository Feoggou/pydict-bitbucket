besom1

(ˈbiːzəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a broom, esp one made of a bundle of twigs tied to a handle2. curling a broom or brush used to sweep the ice in front of the stone to make it slide fartherverb (transitive)3. to sweep with a besom
Word origin
Old English besma; related to Old High German besmo broom

(ˈbɪzəm , ˈbizəm )

nounScottish and Northern England dialect a derogatory term for a woman

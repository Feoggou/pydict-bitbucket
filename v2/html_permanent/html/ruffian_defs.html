ruffian

(ˈrʌfɪən )
Definitions
nouna violent or lawless person; hoodlum or villain
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ruffianism (ˈruffianism) nounruffianly (ˈruffianly) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Old French rufien, from Italian ruffiano, perhaps related to Langobardic hruf scurf, scabbiness

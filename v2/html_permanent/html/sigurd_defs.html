Sigurd

(ˈsɪɡʊəd , German ˈziːɡʊrt)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nounNorse mythology a hero who killed the dragon Fafnir to gain the treasure of Andvari, won Brynhild for Gunnar by deception, and then was killed by her when she discovered the fraud. His wife was Gudrun. German counterpart: Siegfried

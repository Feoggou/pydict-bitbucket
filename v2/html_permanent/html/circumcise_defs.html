circumcise

(ˈsɜːkəmˌsaɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to remove the foreskin of (a male)2. to incise surgically the skin over the clitoris of (a female)3. to remove the clitoris of (a female)4. to perform the religious rite of circumcision on (someone)
Derived forms
circumˌciser (ˈcircumˌciser) noun
Word origin
C13: from Latin circumcīdere, from circum- + caedere to cut

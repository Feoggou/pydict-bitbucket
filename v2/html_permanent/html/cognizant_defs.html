cognizant

or cognisant (ˈkɒɡnɪzənt , ˈkɒnɪ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(usually foll by of) aware; having knowledge

Definitions

regional note:   in BRIT, also use cognisantgraded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If someone is cognizant of something, they are aware of it or understand it. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ We are cognizant of the problem.

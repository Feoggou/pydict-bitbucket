mallow

(ˈmæləʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any plant of the malvaceous genus Malva, esp M. sylvestris of Europe, having purple, pink, or white flowers. See also dwarf mallow, musk mallow2. any of various related plants, such as the marsh mallow, rose mallow, Indian mallow, and tree mallow

Word Origin

Old English mealuwe, from Latin malva; probably related to Greek malakhē mallow
Example sentences containing 'mallow'
Hollyhocks and mallow crowded the beds with roses and honeysuckle vying for position on the front wall. Barbara Erskine HIDING FROM THE LIGHT

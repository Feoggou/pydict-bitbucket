(əˌpɒkəˈlɪptɪk )
Definitions

adjective1. outstanding in revelation, prophecy, or significance2. of or like an apocalypse
Derived forms

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Apocalyptic means relating to the total destruction of something, especially of the world. ⇒ ...the reformer's apocalyptic warnings that the nation was running out of natural resources.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Apocalyptic means relating to or involving predictions about future disasters and the destruction of the world. ⇒ ...a gloomy and apocalyptic vision of a world hastening towards ruin.

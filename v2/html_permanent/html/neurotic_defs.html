neurotic

(njʊˈrɒtɪk )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or afflicted by neurosisnoun2. a person who is afflicted with a neurosis or who tends to be emotionally unstable or unusually anxious
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

neurotically (neuˈrotically) adverb

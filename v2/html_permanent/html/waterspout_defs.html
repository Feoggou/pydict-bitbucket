waterspout

(ˈwɔːtəˌspaʊt )
Definitions
noun1. meteorologya. a tornado occurring over water that forms a column of water and mist extending between the surface and the clouds aboveb. a sudden downpour of heavy rain2. a pipe or channel through which water is discharged, esp one used for drainage from the gutters of a roof

headgear

(ˈhɛdˌɡɪə )
Definitions
noun1. any head covering, esp a hat2. any part of a horse's harness that is worn on the head3. the hoisting mechanism at the pithead of a mine
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

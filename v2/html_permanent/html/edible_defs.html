edible

(ˈɛdɪbəl )
Definitions
adjectivefit to be eaten; eatable
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

edibility (ˌediˈbility) or edibleness (ˈedibleness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin edibilis, from Latin edere to eat

inflame

(ɪnˈfleɪm )
Definitions
verb1. to arouse or become aroused to violent emotion2. (transitive) to increase or intensify; aggravate3. to produce inflammation in (a tissue, organ, or part) or (of a tissue, etc) to become inflamed4. to set or be set on fire; kindle5. (transitive) to cause to redden
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inflamer (inˈflamer) nouninflamingly (inˈflamingly) adverb

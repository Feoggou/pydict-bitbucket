jointed

(ˈdʒɔɪntɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. a. having a joint or jointsb. (in combination) ⇒ large-jointed2. (of a plant stem or similar part) marked with constrictions, at which the stem breaks into separate portions
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

jointedly (ˈjointedly) adverbjointedness (ˈjointedness) noun

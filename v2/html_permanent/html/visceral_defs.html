visceral

(ˈvɪsərəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or affecting the viscera2. characterized by intuition or instinct rather than intellect
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

viscerally (ˈviscerally) adverb

(vɪsərəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Visceral feelings are feelings that you feel very deeply and find it difficult to control or ignore, and that are not the result of thought. [literary] ⇒ I never overcame a visceral antipathy for the monarchy. ⇒ ...the sheer visceral joy of being alive.

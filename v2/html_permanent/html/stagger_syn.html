stagger

verb

  1. totter, reel, sway, falter, lurch, wobble, waver, teeter • He was staggering and had to lean on the bar.
  2. lurch, reel, stumble, sway, totter • a government that staggered from crisis to crisis
  3. astound, amaze, stun, surprise, shock, shake, overwhelm, astonish, confound, take (someone) aback, bowl over (informal), stupefy, strike (someone) dumb, throw off balance, give (someone) a shock, dumbfound, nonplus, flabbergast (informal), take (someone's) breath away • The whole thing staggers me.

politicize

or politicise (pəˈlɪtɪˌsaɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (transitive) to render political in tone, interest, or awareness2. (intransitive) to participate in political discussion or activity

Derived Forms

poˌliticization (poˌliticiˈzation) or poˌliticisation (poˌliticiˈsation) noun

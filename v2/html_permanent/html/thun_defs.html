Thun

(German tuːn)
Definitions
noun1. a town in central Switzerland, in Bern canton on Lake Thun. Pop: 40 377 (2000)2. a lake in central Switzerland, formed by a widening of the Aar River. Length: about 17 km (11 miles). Width: 3 km (2 miles). German name: Thuner See

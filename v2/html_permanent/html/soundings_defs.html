soundings

(ˈsaʊndɪŋz)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. meteorology measurements of atmospheric conditions, as made using a radiosonde or rocketsonde2. nautical a place where a sounding line will reach the bottom, esp less than 100 fathoms in depth3. See on soundings4. See off soundings5. See take soundings

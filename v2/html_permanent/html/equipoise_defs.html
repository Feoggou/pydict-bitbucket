equipoise

(ˈɛkwɪˌpɔɪz )
Definitions
noun1. even balance of weight or other forces; equilibrium2. a counterbalance; counterpoiseverb3. (transitive) to offset or balance in weight or force; balance
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'equipoise'
And lo, in a flash, with perfect equipoise , Flick spun wildly about the point of the needle. David Zindell THE LIGHTSTONE: BOOK ONE, PART ONE OF THE EA CYCLE

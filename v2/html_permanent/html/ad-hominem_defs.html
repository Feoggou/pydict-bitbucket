ad hominem

Latin (æd ˈhɒmɪˌnɛm)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective, adverb1. directed against a person rather than against his arguments2. based on or appealing to emotion rather than reason▷ Compare ad rem▷ See also argumentum ad hominem
Word origin
literally: to the man
Example sentences containing 'ad hominem'
Even Cecilia realized ad hominem attacks should be suspended after a man had been murdered. Laura Lippman IN A STRANGE CITY

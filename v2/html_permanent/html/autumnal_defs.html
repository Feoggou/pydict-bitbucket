Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveAutumnal means having features that are characteristic of autumn. [literary] ⇒ ...the autumnal colours of the trees. ⇒ We used shades of gold and green to give the room a fresh, autumnal look.2. adjectiveAutumnal means happening in autumn. ⇒ ...the autumnal equinox. ⇒ ...autumnal gales.

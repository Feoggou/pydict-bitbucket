Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Blooming is used by some people to emphasize what they are saying, especially when they are annoyed. [British, informal, rude, emphasis] bloody ⇒ It's a blooming nuisance because it frightens my dog to death.Blooming is also an adverb. ⇒ Even though we split up she was blooming marvellous.2. adjectiveSomeone who is blooming looks attractively healthy and full of energy. ⇒ If they were blooming with confidence they wouldn't need me. ⇒ She's in blooming health.

insides

(ˈɪnˌsaɪdz)
Definitions
plural noun1. See sb's insides2. the internal organs of an animal's body, esp when being skinned and gutted to be eaten ⇒ He skinned the rabbit and removed the insides.3. the inner part of a fruit or vegetable ⇒ Kiwi fruit can be eaten by cutting off the tops and scooping out the insides with a teaspoon.

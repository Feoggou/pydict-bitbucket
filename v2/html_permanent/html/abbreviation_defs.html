abbreviation

(əˌbriːvɪˈeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a shortened or contracted form of a word or phrase used in place of the whole2. the process or result of abbreviating

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounAn abbreviation is a short form of a word or phrase, made by leaving out some of the letters or by using only the first letter of each word. ⇒ The postal abbreviation for Kansas is KS.

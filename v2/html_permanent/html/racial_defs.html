racial

(ˈreɪʃəl )
Definitions
adjective1. denoting or relating to the division of the human species into races on grounds of physical characteristics2. characteristic of any such group3. relating to or arising from differences between the races ⇒ racial harmony4. of or relating to a subspecies
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

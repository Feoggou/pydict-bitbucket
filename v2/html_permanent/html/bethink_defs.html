bethink

(bɪˈθɪŋk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -thinks, -thinking or -thought archaic or dialect1. to cause (oneself) to consider or meditate2. (transitive; often foll by of) to remind (oneself)
Example sentences containing 'bethink'
The evil King and his daughter were using telepathy to communicate, which made Tory wonder how they had the power to bethink each other. Traci Harding THE ANCIENT FUTURE: THE DARK AGE

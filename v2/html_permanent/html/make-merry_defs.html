Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrase [V inflects]If people make merry, they enjoy themselves and have fun, for example by singing, dancing, and drinking together. [old-fashioned] ⇒ Neighbours approached their boundaries from opposite sides and made merry together. →  merry

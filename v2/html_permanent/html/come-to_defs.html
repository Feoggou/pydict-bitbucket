come to

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (intransitive)1. (adverb; or prep. and reflexive) to regain consciousness or return to one's normal state2. (adverb) nautical to slow a vessel or bring her to a stop3. (preposition) to amount to (a sum of money) ⇒ your bill comes to four pounds4. (preposition) to arrive at (a certain state) ⇒ what is the world coming to?

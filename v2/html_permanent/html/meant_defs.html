Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. Meant is the past tense and past participle of mean1.2. adjectiveYou use meant to to say that something or someone was intended to be or do a particular thing, especially when they have failed to be or do it. be supposed to ⇒ I can't say any more, it's meant to be a big secret. ⇒ The decor was meant to keep the mind concentrated on the making of money. ⇒ I'm meant to be on holiday.3. adjectiveIf something is meant for particular people or for a particular situation, it is intended for those people or for that situation. intended [+ for] ⇒ Fairy tales weren't just meant for children. [+ for] ⇒ The seeds were not meant for human consumption. ⇒ The letter might not have been meant for me at all.4. →  be meant to5. →  be meant to

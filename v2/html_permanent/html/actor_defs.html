actor

(ˈæktə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who acts in a play, film, broadcast, etc2. informal a person who puts on a false manner in order to deceive others (often in the phrase bad actor)▶ USAGE The term actor is almost universally used nowadays to refer to people of either gender who act

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounAn actor is someone whose job is acting in plays or films. 'Actor' in the singular usually refers to a man, but some women who act prefer to be called 'actors' rather than 'actresses'. ⇒ His father was an actor in the Cantonese Opera Company. ⇒ You have to be a very good actor to play that part.

Synonyms of oversight

oversight

noun

  1. mistake, error, slip, fault, misunderstanding, blunder, lapse, omission, boob (British, slang), gaffe, slip-up (informal), delinquency, inaccuracy, carelessness, howler (informal), goof (informal), bloomer (British, informal), clanger (informal), miscalculation, error of judgment, faux pas, inattention, laxity, boo-boo (informal), erratum, barry or Barry Crocker (Australian, slang)
    • By an unfortunate oversight, full instructions do not come with the product.
  2. supervision, keeping, control, charge, care, management, handling, administration, direction, custody, stewardship, superintendence
    • I had the oversight of their collection of manuscripts.

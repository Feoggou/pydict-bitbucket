hanger

(ˈhæŋə )
Definitions
noun1. a. any support, such as a hook, strap, peg, or loop, on or by which something may be hungb. See coat hanger2. a. a person who hangs somethingb. (in combination) ⇒ paperhanger3. a bracket designed to attach one part of a mechanical structure to another, such as the one that attaches the spring shackle of a motor car to the chassis4. a wood on a steep hillside, characteristically beech growing on chalk in southern England5. a. a loop or strap on a sword belt from which a short sword or dagger was hungb. the weapon itself

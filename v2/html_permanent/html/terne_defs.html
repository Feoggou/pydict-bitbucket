terne

(tɜːn )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: terne metal an alloy of lead containing tin (10–20 per cent) and antimony (1.5–2 per cent)2. Also called: terneplate steel plate coated with this alloy
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: perhaps from French terne dull, from Old French ternir to tarnish

intolerance

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪntɒlərəns ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIntolerance is unwillingness to let other people act in a different way or hold different opinions from you. [disapproval] [+ of] ⇒ ...his intolerance of any opinion other than his own. ⇒ ...religious intolerance.

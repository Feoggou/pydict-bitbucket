Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseYou use at times to say that something happens or is true on some occasions or at some moments. ⇒ The debate was highly emotional at times. ⇒ At times she had an overwhelming desire to see him. ⇒ He went on listening to her, at times impatient and at times fascinated. →  time

shop

noun

  1. store, market, supermarket, mart, boutique, emporium, hypermarket, dairy (New Zealand) • It's not available in the shops.

verb

  1. go shopping, buy things, do the shopping, go to the shops • customers who shop once a week
  2. (British informal) betray, grass on (British, slang), inform on or against, sell out (informal), double-cross (informal), stab in the back, sell down the river (informal), grass up (slang), put the finger on (informal), dob in (Australian, slang) • Fraudsters are often shopped by honest friends and neighbours.

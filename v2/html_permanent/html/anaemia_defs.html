anaemia

or US anemia (əˈniːmɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a deficiency in the number of red blood cells or in their haemoglobin content, resulting in pallor, shortness of breath, and lack of energy2. lack of vitality or vigour3. pallid complexion
Word origin
C19: from New Latin, from Greek anaimia lack of blood, from an- + haima blood

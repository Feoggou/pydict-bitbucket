Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount noun [oft with poss]If you win someone's approval for something that you ask for or suggest, they agree to it. ⇒ ...efforts to win congressional approval for an aid package for Moscow. ⇒ The chairman has also given his approval for an investigation into the case. ⇒ The proposed modifications met with widespread approval.2. variable nounApproval is a formal or official statement that something is acceptable. ⇒ The testing and approval of new drugs will be speeded up.3. uncount noun [usu with poss]If someone or something has your approval, you like and admire them. ⇒ His son had an obsessive drive to gain his father's approval. ⇒ The president's approval rating had risen.4. →  seal of approvalpragmatics note:   approvalIn this dictionary, the label approval indicates that you use the word or expression to show that you like or admire the person or thing you are talking about. An example of a word with this label is broad-minded.

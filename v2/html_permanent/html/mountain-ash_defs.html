mountain ash

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of various trees of the rosaceous genus Sorbus, such as S aucuparia (European mountain ash or rowan), having clusters of small white flowers and bright red berries2. any of several Australian eucalyptus trees, such as Eucalyptus regnans

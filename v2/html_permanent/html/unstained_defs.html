unstained

(ʌnˈsteɪnd)
Definitions
adjective1. not dyed ⇒ The mahogany is unstained and has an extremely rich colour., ⇒ an unstained plywood door2. not marked or discoloured with patches of something that dirties ⇒ She reached her cashmere sweater, careful to use the hand with the unstained fingers.3. not disgraced or shamed ⇒ Though my weapons are bloodied, my soul is unstained.4. biology not coloured for microscopic study by treatment with a dye or similar reagent ⇒ It provided a convenient way of producing phase contrast in the electron microscope in the case of unstained specimens.

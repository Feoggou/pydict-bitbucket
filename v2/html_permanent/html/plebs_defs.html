plebs

(plɛbz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (functioning as plural) the common people; the masses2. (functioning as singular or plural) common people of ancient Rome. Compare patrician

Word Origin

C17: from Latin: the common people of ancient Rome

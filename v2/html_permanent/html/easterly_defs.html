easterly

(ˈiːstəlɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or situated in the eastadverb, adjective2. towards or in the direction of the east3. from the east ⇒ an easterly windnoun plural -lies4. a wind from the east
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

easterly

Powered by COBUILD
(iːstəʳli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]An easterly point, area, or direction is to the east or towards the east. ⇒ He progressed slowly along the coast in an easterly direction.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]An easterly wind is a wind that blows from the east. ⇒ ...the cold easterly winds from Scandinavia.

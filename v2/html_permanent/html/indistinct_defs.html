indistinct

(ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋkt )
Definitions
adjectiveincapable of being clearly distinguished, as by the eyes, ears, or mind; not distinct

graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is indistinct is unclear and difficult to see, hear, or recognize. ⇒ The lettering is fuzzy and indistinct. ⇒ ...the indistinct murmur of voices.indistinctly graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v] ⇒ He speaks so indistinctly that many listeners haven't a clue what he is saying.

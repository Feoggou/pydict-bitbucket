nomenclature

(nəʊˈmɛnklətʃə , US English ˈnəʊmənˌkleɪtʃər)
Definitions
nounthe terminology used in a particular science, art, activity, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin nōmenclātūra list of names; see nomenclator

Definitions
uncount nounThe nomenclature of a particular set of things is the system of naming those things. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ ...mistakes arising from ignorance of the nomenclature of woody plants. ⇒ ...the internationally agreed rules of chemical nomenclature.

stock car

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a car, usually a production saloon, strengthened and modified for a form of racing in which the cars often collideb. Also: stock-car (as modifier) ⇒ stock-car racing2. US and Canadian a railway wagon designed for carrying livestock. Also called (in Britain and certain other countries): cattle truck

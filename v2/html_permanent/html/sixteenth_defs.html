sixteenth

(ˈsɪksˈtiːnθ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (usually prenominal)a. coming after the fifteenth in numbering or counting order, position, time, etc; being the ordinal number of sixteen: often written 16thb. (as noun) ⇒ the sixteenth of the monthnoun2. a. one of 16 equal or nearly equal parts of somethingb. (as modifier) ⇒ a sixteenth part3. the fraction that is equal to one divided by 16 (1⁄16)

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. ordinal numberThe sixteenth item in a series is the one that you count as number sixteen. ⇒ ...the sixteenth century AD.2. fractionA sixteenth is one of sixteen equal parts of something. ⇒ ...a sixteenth of a second. ⇒ ...fifteen sixteenths of an inch.

odorous

(ˈəʊdərəs ) or odourful (ˈəʊdəfʊl)
Definitions
adjectivehaving or emitting a characteristic smell or odour. Also (esp in compounds): odoured
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

odorously (ˈodorously) adverbodorousness (ˈodorousness) noun

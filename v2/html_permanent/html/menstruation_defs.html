menstruation

(ˌmɛnstrʊˈeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe approximately monthly discharge of blood and cellular debris from the uterus by nonpregnant women from puberty to the menopause. Also called: menses. Nontechnical name: period

Derived Forms

menstruous (ˈmɛnstrʊəs ) adjective

Similar Words

period, menstrual cycle, menses, courses (physiology), flow (informal), monthly (informal), the curse (informal), catamenia (physiology)

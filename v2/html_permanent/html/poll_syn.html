Synonyms of poll

poll

noun

  1. survey, figures, count, sampling, returns, ballot, tally, census, canvass, Gallup Poll, (public) opinion poll
    • Polls show that the party is losing support.
  2. election, vote, voting, referendum, ballot, plebiscite
    • In 1945, Churchill was defeated at the polls.

verb

  1. question, interview, survey, sample, ballot, canvass
    • More than 18,000 people were polled.
  2. gain, return, record, register, tally
    • He had polled enough votes to force a second ballot.

regeneration

(rɪˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act or process of regenerating or the state of being regenerated; rebirth or renewal2. the regrowth by an animal or plant of an organ, tissue, or part that has been lost or destroyed3. electronics the use of positive feedback to increase the amplification of a radio frequency stage

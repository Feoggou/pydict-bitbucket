Wednesday

(ˈwɛnzdɪ , -deɪ)
Definitions
nounthe fourth day of the week; third day of the working week
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English Wōdnes dæg Woden's day, translation of Latin mercurii dies Mercury's day; related to Old Frisian wōnsdei, Middle Dutch wōdensdach (Dutch woensdag)

Wednesday for learners of English

Word forms: Wednesdays
Definitions
variable nounWednesday is the day after Tuesday and before Thursday. ⇒ Come and have supper with us on Wednesday, if you're free. ⇒ Did you happen to see her leave last Wednesday? ⇒ David always collects Alistair from school on Wednesdays. ⇒ On a Wednesday afternoon, the shop was shut.

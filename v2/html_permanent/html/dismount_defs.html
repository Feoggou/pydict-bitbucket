dismount

(dɪsˈmaʊnt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to get off a horse, bicycle, etc2. (transitive) to disassemble or remove from a mountingnoun3. the act of dismounting
Derived forms
dismountable (disˈmountable) adjective

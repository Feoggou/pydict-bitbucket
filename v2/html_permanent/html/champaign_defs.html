champaign

(ʃæmˈpeɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: campagna an expanse of open level or gently undulating country2. an obsolete word for battlefield
Word origin
C14: from Old French champaigne, from Late Latin campānia; see campaign

elegance

(ˈɛlɪɡəns ) or elegancy
Definitions
noun plural -gances or -gancies1. dignified grace in appearance, movement, or behaviour2. good taste in design, style, arrangement, etc3. something elegant; a refinement
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

He wouldn't be tall, but he was built neatly and he moved with fluid elegance. Thomas, Rosie THE WHITE DOVEThe dining room was elegance itself, upholding the old Kentucky traditions. Lawson, Jonell ROSES ARE FOR THE RICHIt certainly did not have the sort of elegance which might be expected of someone Alex McClintock would marry.

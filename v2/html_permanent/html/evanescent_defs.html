evanescent

(ˌɛvəˈnɛsənt )
Definitions
adjective1. passing out of sight; fading away; vanishing2. ephemeral or transitory
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

evanescence (ˌevaˈnescence) nounevanescently (ˌevaˈnescently) adverb

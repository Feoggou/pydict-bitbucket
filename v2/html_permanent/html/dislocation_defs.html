dislocation

(ˌdɪsləˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of displacing or the state of being displaced; disruption2. (esp of the bones in a joint) the state or condition of being dislocated3. a line, plane, or region in which there is a discontinuity in the regularity of a crystal lattice4. geology a less common word for fault (sense 6)

book

noun

  1. work, title, guide, volume, publication, companion, manual, paperback, textbook, tract, hardback, tome, treatise, e-book or ebook, blook • a book about witches
  2. notebook, album, journal, diary, pad, record book, Filofax®, notepad, exercise book, jotter, memorandum book, e-book or ebook, blook • I had several names in my little black book that I called regularly.

plural noun

  1. accounts, records, balance sheet, ledger, financial statement • He usually left the books to his managers and accountants.

verb

  1. reserve, schedule, engage, line up, organize, charter, arrange for, procure, make reservations, e-book or ebook • She booked herself a flight home last night.
  2. charge • They took him to the police station and booked him for assault.
related mania:
bibliomania
related enthusiast:
bibliophile
related phobia:
bibiliophobia

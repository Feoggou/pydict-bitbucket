Promethean

(prəˈmiːθɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Prometheus2. creative, original, or life-enhancingnoun3. a person who resembles Prometheus
Example sentences containing 'Promethean'
I'm not gifted with Promethean foresight, and emotional involvement makes one a poor judge. Colleen McCullough THE THORN BIRDS

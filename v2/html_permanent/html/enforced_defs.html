enforced

(ɪnˈfɔːst)
Definitions
adjectiveimposed by force, despite efforts to prevent it ⇒ September 16 1992 saw Britain's enforced departure from the ERM, ⇒ The plane journey was short, but the enforced idleness gave him ample time to worry, ⇒ the enforced silence of many terrified victims of abuse, ⇒ David is now living in Beirut again after an enforced absence.

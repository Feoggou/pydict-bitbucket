forsaken

Powered by COBUILD
(fəʳseɪkən ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]A forsaken place is not lived in, used, or looked after. [literary] ⇒ The delta region of the Rio Grande river was a forsaken land of thickets and swamps. ⇒ ...a forsaken church and a derelict hotel.2. → See also godforsaken

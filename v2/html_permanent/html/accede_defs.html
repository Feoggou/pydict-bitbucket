accede

(ækˈsiːd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (intransitive; usually foll by to)1. to assent or give one's consent; agree2. to enter upon or attain (to an office, right, etc) ⇒ the prince acceded to the throne3. international law to become a party (to an agreement between nations, etc), as by signing a treaty
Derived forms
acˈcedence nounacˈceder noun
Word origin
C15: from Latin accēdere to approach, agree, from ad- to + cēdere to go, yield

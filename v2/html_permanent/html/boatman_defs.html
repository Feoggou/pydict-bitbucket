boatman

(ˈbəʊtmən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -men1. a man who works on, hires out, repairs, or operates a boat or boats2. short for water boatman

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA boatman is a man who is paid by people to take them across an area of water in a small boat, or a man who hires boats out to them for a short time.

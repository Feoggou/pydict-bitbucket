Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseYou use as much in expressions such as 'I thought as much' and 'I guessed as much' after you have just been told something and you want to say that you already believed or expected it to be true. ⇒ You're waiting for a woman–I thought as much. →  much

assizes

(əˈsaɪzɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun(formerly in England and Wales) the sessions, usually held four times a year, of the principal court in each county, exercising civil and criminal jurisdiction, attended by itinerant judges: replaced in 1971 by crown courts

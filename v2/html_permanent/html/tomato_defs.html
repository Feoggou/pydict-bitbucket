tomato

(təˈmɑːtəʊ )
Definitions
noun plural -toes1. a solanaceous plant, Lycopersicon (or Lycopersicum) esculentum, of South America, widely cultivated for its red fleshy many-seeded edible fruits2. the fruit of this plant, which has slightly acid-tasting flesh and is eaten in salads, as a vegetable, etc3. US and Canadian slang a girl or woman
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17 tomate, from Spanish, from Nahuatl tomatl

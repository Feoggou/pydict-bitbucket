Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA diplomatic bag is a bag or container in which mail is sent to and from foreign embassies. Diplomatic bags are protected by law, so that they are not opened by anyone except the official or embassy they are addressed to. [British]regional note:   in AM, use diplomatic pouch

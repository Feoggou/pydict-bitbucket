Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf something, often something unpleasant, is ceaseless, it continues for a long time without stopping or changing. [formal] ⇒ There is a ceaseless struggle from noon to night.ceaselessly adverb [usu ADV with v] endlessly ⇒ The characters complain ceaselessly about food queues, prices and corruption.

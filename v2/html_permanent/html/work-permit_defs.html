work permit

(wɜːk ˈpɜːmɪt)
Definitions
nounlaw a document giving permission to work in a particular country ⇒ I would like to work but I haven't been able to get a work permit yet.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'work permit'
She had already been checked out, and her papers and work permit were in order. Forbes, Bryan A SONG AT TWILIGHTThe cook's Mexican, but we don't mention that because she hasn't got a work permit.

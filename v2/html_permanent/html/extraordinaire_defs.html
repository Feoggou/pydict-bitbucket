extraordinaire

Powered by COBUILD
(ekstrɔːʳdɪneəʳ ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [n ADJ]If you describe someone as being, for example, a musician extraordinaire, you are saying in a slightly humorous way that you think they are an extremely good musician. ⇒ ...George Kuchar, film-maker extraordinaire.

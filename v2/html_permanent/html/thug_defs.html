thug

(θʌɡ )
Definitions
noun1. a tough and violent man, esp a criminal2. (sometimes capital) (formerly) a member of an organization of robbers and assassins in India who typically strangled their victims
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

thuggery (ˈthuggery) nounthuggish (ˈthuggish) adjective

Word Origin

C19: from Hindi thag thief, from Sanskrit sthaga scoundrel, from sthagati to conceal

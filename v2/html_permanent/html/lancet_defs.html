lancet

(ˈlɑːnsɪt )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: lance a pointed surgical knife with two sharp edges2. short for lancet arch, lancet window
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15 lancette, from Old French: small lance

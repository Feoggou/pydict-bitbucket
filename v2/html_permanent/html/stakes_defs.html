stakes

(steɪks)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. the money that a player has available for gambling ⇒ poker games with stakes of many hundreds of dollars, ⇒ The game was usually played for high stakes between two large groups.2. a prize in a race, etc, esp one made up of contributions from contestants or owners3. a race in which all owners of competing horses contribute to the prize money4. risks ⇒ For Mr Blair the political stakes could hardly have been higher.

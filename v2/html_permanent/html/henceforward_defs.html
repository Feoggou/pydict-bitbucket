henceforward

Powered by COBUILD
(hensfɔːʳwəʳd ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverbHenceforward means from this time on. [formal] ⇒ Henceforward France and Britain had a common interest. ⇒ He announced that it would henceforward be the custom to give him five rands each time a cow was slaughtered.

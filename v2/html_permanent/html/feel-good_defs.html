Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]A feel-good film is a film which presents people and life in a way which makes the people who watch it feel happy and optimistic. ⇒ This could be the feel-good movie of the autumn. ⇒ ...a bright and enjoyable feelgood romance.2. →  feel-good factor

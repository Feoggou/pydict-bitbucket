landed

(ˈlændɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. owning land ⇒ landed gentry2. consisting of or including land ⇒ a landed estate

landed

Powered by COBUILD
(lændɪd ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]Landed means owning or including a large amount of land, especially land that has belonged to the same family for several generations. ⇒ Most of them were the nobility and the landed gentry. ⇒ ...the erosion of the aristocracy's landed wealth.

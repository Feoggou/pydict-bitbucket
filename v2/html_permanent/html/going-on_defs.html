going on

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
phraseYou can use going on before a number to say that something has almost reached that number. For example, you can say that someone is going on 40 to indicate that they are nearly 40. ⇒ We've been married for going on two years. ⇒ This is 1980 going on 1981. →  going

compendium

(kəmˈpɛndɪəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -diums or -dia (-dɪə)1. British a book containing a collection of useful hints2. British a selection, esp of different games or other objects in one container3. a concise but comprehensive summary of a larger work
Word origin
C16: from Latin: a saving, literally: something weighed, from pendere to weigh

nutty

(ˈnʌtɪ )
Definitions
adjective -tier or -tiest1. containing or abounding in nuts2. resembling nuts, esp in taste3. Also: nutsy informal insane; crazy4. (foll by over or about) informal extremely fond (of) or enthusiastic (about)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

nuttily (ˈnuttily) adverbnuttiness (ˈnuttiness) noun

nutty for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(nʌti )
Word forms: nuttier , nuttiest
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe food as nutty, you mean that it tastes of nuts, has the texture of nuts, or is made with nuts. ⇒ ...nutty butter cookies. ⇒ Chick peas have a distinctive, delicious and nutty flavour.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as nutty, you mean that their behaviour is very strange or foolish. [informal, disapproval] ⇒ He looked like a nutty professor. ⇒ That's a nutty idea.

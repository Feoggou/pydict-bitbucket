Synonyms of tutor

tutor

noun

  1. teacher, coach, instructor, educator, guide, governor, guardian, lecturer, guru, mentor, preceptor, master or mistress, schoolmaster or schoolmistress
    • He surprised his tutors by failing the exam.

verb

  1. teach, educate, school, train, coach, guide, discipline, lecture, drill, instruct, edify, direct
    • She was at home, being tutored with her brothers.

pend

(pɛnd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (intransitive)1. to await judgment or settlement2. dialect to hang; dependnoun3. Scottish an archway or vaulted passage

Word Origin

C15: from Latin pendēre to hang; related to Latin pendere to suspend

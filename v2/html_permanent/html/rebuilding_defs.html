rebuilding

(ˌriːˈbɪldɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. a. the process of building something (such as a city, building, etc) again after it has been damaged or destroyed ⇒ plans for the rebuilding of the stadium, ⇒ the rebuilding of the house's Georgian kitchenb. (as modifier) ⇒ rebuilding work, ⇒ a rebuilding programme, ⇒ They will spread the cost of this huge rebuilding programme over 30 years.2. the restoration of a system or situation to a previous condition ⇒ the rebuilding of Afghanistan, ⇒ the rebuilding of the economy

cohesion

(kəʊˈhiːʒən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or state of cohering; tendency to unite2. physics the force that holds together the atoms or molecules in a solid or liquid, as distinguished from adhesion3. botany the fusion in some plants of flower parts, such as petals, that are usually separate
Word origin
C17: from Latin cohaesus stuck together, past participle of cohaerēre to cohere

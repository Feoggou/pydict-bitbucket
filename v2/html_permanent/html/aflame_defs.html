aflame

(əˈfleɪm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb, adjective (postpositive)1. in flames; ablaze2. deeply aroused, as with passion ⇒ he was aflame with desire3. (of the face) red or inflamed

(əfleɪm )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [v-link ADJ]If something is on fire, you can say it is aflame. [literary] alight, ablaze ⇒ Hundreds of tightly rolled newspapers were set aflame among the 50,000 crowd.2. adjective [v-link ADJ, oft ADJ with n]If you say that something is aflame, you mean it is filled with light or colour. [literary] aglow ⇒ The shop windows were aflame with Christmas lights.

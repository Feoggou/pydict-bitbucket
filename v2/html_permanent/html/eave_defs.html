eave

(iːv)
Definitions
adjective1. relating to the eaves of a building ⇒ an eave edgeverb (transitive)2. to give cover or protection under the eaves of a building
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'eave'
I walked to the edge of the street, and reached up to take a handful of somewhat clean snow from an eave edge. Robin Hobb THE GOLDEN FOOL: BOOK TWO OF THE TAWNY MANInstead, from an eave window, Leo saw buckets of water poured on the leaf-clad man. Appiganesi, Lisa DREAMS OF INNOCENCE

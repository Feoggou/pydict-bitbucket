latterly

Powered by COBUILD
(lætəʳli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverbYou can use latterly to indicate that a situation or event is the most recent one. [written] ⇒ He was to remain active in the association, latterly as vice president, for the rest of his life. ⇒ City centres were abandoned first by residents, then by shops, and latterly by businesses. ⇒ Latterly, he has written extensively about alternative medicine.

theological

(ˌθɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or based on theology2. based on God's revelation to man of his nature, his designs, and his will3. informal difficult to understand; esoteric
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

theologically (ˌtheoˈlogically) adverb

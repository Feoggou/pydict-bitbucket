conformable

(kənˈfɔːməbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. corresponding in character; similar2. obedient; submissive3. (foll by to) in agreement or harmony (with); consistent (with)4. (of rock strata) lying in a parallel arrangement so that their original relative positions have remained undisturbed5. mathematics (of two matrices) related so that the number of columns in one is equal to the number of rows in the other
Derived forms
conˌformability (conˌformaˈbility) or conformableness (conˈformableness) nounconformably (conˈformably) adverb

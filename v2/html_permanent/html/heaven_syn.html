Synonyms of heaven

heaven

noun

  1. paradise, next world, hereafter, nirvana (Buddhism & Hinduism), bliss, Zion (Christianity), Valhalla (Norse mythology), Happy Valley, happy hunting ground (mythology), life to come, life everlasting, abode of God, Elysium or Elysian fields (Greek mythology)
    • I believed that when I died I would go to heaven.
  2. (informal) happiness, paradise, ecstasy, bliss, felicity, utopia, contentment, rapture, enchantment, dreamland, seventh heaven, transport, sheer bliss
    • My idea of heaven is drinking champagne with friends on a sunny day.
related phobia:
ouranophobia

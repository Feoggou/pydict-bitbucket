beading

(ˈbiːdɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. another name for bead (sense 6)2. Also called: beadwork (ˈbiːdˌwɜːk ) a narrow strip of some material used for edging or ornamentation

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounBeading is a narrow strip of wood that is used for decorating or edging furniture and doors.2. uncount nounBeading is an arrangement of beads used for decorating clothes. ⇒ ...a black velvet bodice with jet black beading.

em

(ɛm )
Definitions
noun printing1. Also called: mutton, mut the square of a body of any size of type, used as a unit of measurement2. Also called: pica em, pica a unit of measurement used in printing, equal to one sixth of an inch
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from the name of the letter M

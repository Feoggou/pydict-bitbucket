Synonyms of fraud

fraud

noun

  1. deception, deceit, treachery, swindling, guile, trickery, duplicity, double-dealing, chicanery, sharp practice, imposture, fraudulence, spuriousness
    • He was jailed for two years for fraud.
    OPPOSITES:  virtue, integrity, honesty, fairness, good faith, probity, rectitude, trustworthiness
  2. scam, craft, cheat, sting (informal), deception (slang), artifice, humbug, canard, stratagems, chicane
    • a fraud involving pension and social security claims
  3. hoax, trick, cheat, con (informal), deception, sham, spoof (informal), prank, swindle, ruse, practical joke, joke, fast one (informal), imposture, fastie (Australian, slang)
    • He never wrote the letter; it was a fraud.
  4. (informal) impostor, cheat, fake, bluffer, sham, hoax, hoaxer, forgery, counterfeit, pretender, charlatan, quack, fraudster, swindler, mountebank, grifter (slang, mainly US & & Canadian), double-dealer, phoney or phony (informal)
    • He believes many psychics are frauds.

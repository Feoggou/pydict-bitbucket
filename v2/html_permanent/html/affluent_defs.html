affluent

(ˈæflʊənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. rich; wealthy2. abundant; copious3. flowing freelynoun4. archaic a tributary stream
Word origin
C15: from Latin affluent-, present participle of affluere to flow towards, from fluere to flow

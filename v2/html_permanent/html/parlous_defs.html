parlous

(ˈpɑːləs ) archaic or humorous
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. dangerous or difficult2. cunningadverb3. extremely

Derived Forms

parlously (ˈparlously) adverbparlousness (ˈparlousness) noun

Word Origin

C14 perlous, variant of perilous

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If something is in a parlous state, it is in a bad or dangerous condition. [formal] ⇒ ...the parlous state of our economy.

perspiration

(ˌpɜːspəˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or process of insensibly eliminating fluid through the pores of the skin, which evaporates immediately2. the sensible elimination of fluid through the pores of the skin, which is visible as droplets on the skin3. the salty fluid secreted through the pores of the skin; sweat

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounPerspiration is the liquid which comes out on the surface of your skin when you are hot or frightened. [formal] ⇒ His hands were wet with perspiration. ⇒ ...night sweats and excessive perspiration.

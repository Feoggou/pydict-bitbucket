LP1

Definitions
noun1. a. a long-playing gramophone record: usually one 12 inches (30 cm) or 10 inches (25 cm) in diameter, designed to rotate at 331⁄3 revolutions per minute. Compare EPb. (as modifier) ⇒ an LP sleeve2. long play: a slow-recording facility on a VCR which allows twice the length of material to be recorded on a tape from that of standard play

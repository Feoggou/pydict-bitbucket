Lippi

(Italian ˈlippi)
Definitions
noun1.  Filippino (filipˈpiːno). ?1457–1504, Italian painter of the Florentine school2.  his father, Fra Filippo (fra fiˈlippo). ?1406–69, Italian painter of the Florentine school, noted particularly for his frescoes at Prato Cathedral (1452–64)

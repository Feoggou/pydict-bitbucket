(helpɪŋ ) for learners of English
Word forms: helpings
Definitions
1. countable noun [adj N]A helping of food is the amount of it that you get in a single serving. [+ of] ⇒ She gave them extra helpings of ice-cream.2. countable nounYou can refer to an amount of something, especially a quality, as a helping of that thing. [informal] [+ of] ⇒ It took a generous helping of entrepreneurial confidence to persevere during this incident.

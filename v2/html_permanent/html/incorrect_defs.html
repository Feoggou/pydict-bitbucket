incorrect

(ˌɪnkəˈrɛkt )
Definitions
adjective1. false; wrong ⇒ an incorrect calculation2. not fitting or proper ⇒ incorrect behaviour
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

incorrect

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnkərekt ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveSomething that is incorrect is wrong and untrue. ⇒ He denied that his evidence about the telephone call was incorrect. ⇒ People often have incorrect information about food.incorrectly adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ The magazine suggested incorrectly that he was planning to retire.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Something that is incorrect is not the thing that is required or is most suitable in a particular situation. ⇒ ...injuries caused by incorrect posture. ⇒ ...incorrect diet.incorrectly adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ He was told that the doors had been fitted incorrectly.

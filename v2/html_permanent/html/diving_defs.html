diving

(ˈdaɪvɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe sport or activity of diving into water or spending time under water ⇒ equipment and accessories for diving, ⇒ Weight in diving affects the size of splash.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounDiving is the activity of working or looking around underwater, using special breathing equipment. ⇒ ...equipment and accessories for diving.2. uncount nounDiving is the sport or activity in which you jump into water head first with your arms held straight above your head, usually from a diving board. ⇒ Weight is crucial in diving because the aim is to cause the smallest splash possible.

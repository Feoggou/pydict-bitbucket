dilettante

(ˌdɪlɪˈtɑːntɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -tantes or -tanti (-ˈtɑːntɪ)1. a person whose interest in a subject is superficial rather than professional2. a person who loves the artsadjective3. of or characteristic of a dilettante
Derived forms
ˌdilettantish (ˌdiletˈtantish) or ˌdilettanteish (ˌdiletˈtanteish) adjectiveˌdilettantism (ˌdiletˈtantism) or ˌdilettanteism (ˌdiletˈtanteism) noun
Word origin
C18: from Italian, from dilettare to delight, from Latin dēlectāre

Definitions

countable nounYou can use dilettante to talk about someone who seems interested in a subject, especially in art, but who does not really know very much about it. [formal, disapproval]

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that something such as behaviour or a situation is disgraceful, you disapprove of it strongly, and feel that the person or people responsible should be ashamed of it. [disapproval] ⇒ It's disgraceful that they have detained him for so long. ⇒ I complained about his disgraceful behaviour. ⇒ I think what's happening is disgraceful.disgracefully graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v, ADV adj/-ed] ⇒ He felt that his brother had behaved disgracefully.

Synonyms of temporary

temporary

adjective

  1. impermanent, passing, transitory, brief, fleeting, interim, short-lived, fugitive, transient, momentary, ephemeral, evanescent, pro tem, here today and gone tomorrow, pro tempore (Latin), fugacious
    • a temporary loss of memory
    OPPOSITES: long-term, permanent, enduring, eternal, durable, long-lasting, everlasting
  2. short-term, acting, interim, supply, stand-in, fill-in, caretaker, provisional, stopgap, pop-up
    • She was working as a temporary teacher at a Belfast school.

ratty

(ˈrætɪ )
Definitions
adjective -tier or -tiest1. British and New Zealand informal irritable; annoyed2. informal (of the hair) unkempt or greasy3. US and Canadian slang shabby; dilapidated4. Australian slanga. angryb. mad5. of, like, or full of rats

Powered by COBUILD
(ræti ) for learners of English
Word forms: rattier , rattiest
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is ratty, they get angry and irritated easily. [British, informal] ⇒ I had spent too many hours there and was beginning to get a bit ratty and fed up.2. graded adjective & adjectiveRatty clothes and objects are torn or in bad condition, especially because they are old. [US] ⇒ ...my ratty old flannel pyjamas.

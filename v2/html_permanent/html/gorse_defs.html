gorse

(ɡɔːs )
Definitions
nounany evergreen shrub of the leguminous genus Ulex, esp the European species U. europeaus, which has yellow flowers and thick green spines instead of leaves. Also called: furze, whin
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gorsy (ˈgorsy) adjective

Word Origin

Old English gors; related to Old Irish garb rough, Latin horrēre to bristle, Old High German gersta barley, Greek khēr hedgehog

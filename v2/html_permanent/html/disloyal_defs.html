Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is disloyal to their friends, family, or country does not support them or does things that could harm them. [+ to] ⇒ She was so disloyal to her deputy she made his position untenable. ⇒ Brian sided with his sister, which led his mother to accuse him of being disloyal.

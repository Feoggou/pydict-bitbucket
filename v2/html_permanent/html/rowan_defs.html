rowan

(ˈrəʊən , ˈraʊ-)
Definitions
nounanother name for the (European) mountain ash
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Scandinavian; compare Norwegian rogn, raun, Old Norse reynir

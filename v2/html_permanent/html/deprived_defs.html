Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Deprived people or people from deprived areas do not have the things that people consider to be essential in life, for example acceptable living conditions or education. underprivileged ⇒ ...probably the most severely deprived children in the country. ⇒ ...the problems associated with life in a deprived inner city area.

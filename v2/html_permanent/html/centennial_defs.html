centennial

(sɛnˈtɛnɪəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. relating to, lasting for, or completing a period of 100 years2. occurring every 100 yearsnoun3. mainly US and Canadian another name for centenary
Derived forms
centennially (cenˈtennially) adverb
Word origin
C18: from Latin centum hundred, on the model of biennial

Powered by COBUILD
(eksələnt ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveSomething that is excellent is very good indeed. ⇒ The recording quality is excellent. ⇒ Luckily, Sue is very efficient and does an excellent job as Fred's personal assistant.excellently graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v, ADV adj/-ed] ⇒ They're both playing excellently. ⇒ The tournament was excellently organised.2. exclamationSome people say 'Excellent!' to show that they approve of something. [feelings]

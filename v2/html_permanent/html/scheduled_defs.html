scheduled

(ˈʃɛdjuːld )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. arranged or planned according to a programme, timetable, etc ⇒ a scheduled meeting, ⇒ a change to the scheduled programmes on TV tonight2. (of an aircraft or a flight) part of a regular service, not specially chartered3. British (of a building, place of historic interest, etc) entered on a list of places to be preserved. See also listed building

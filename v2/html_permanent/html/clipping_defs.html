clipping

(ˈklɪpɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. something cut out or trimmed off, esp an article from a newspaper; cutting2. the distortion of an audio or visual signal in which the tops of peaks with a high amplitude are cut off, caused by, for example, overloading of amplifier circuitsadjective3. (prenominal) informal fast ⇒ a clipping pace

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [oft n N]A clipping is an article, picture, or advertisement that has been cut from a newspaper or magazine. ⇒ ...bulletin boards crowded with newspaper clippings.2. countable noun [usu pl, oft n N]Clippings are small pieces of something that have been cut from something larger. ⇒ Having mown the lawn, there are all those grass clippings to get rid of. ⇒ ...nail clippings.

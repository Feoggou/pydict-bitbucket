(dɒmɪneɪtɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A dominating person has a very strong personality and influences the people around them. ⇒ She certainly was a dominating figure in politics. ⇒ The boy was brought up by his mother, who was a dominating influence in his life.

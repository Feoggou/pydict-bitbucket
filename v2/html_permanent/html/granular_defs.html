granular

(ˈɡrænjʊlə )
Definitions
adjective1. of, like, containing, or resembling a granule or granules2. having a grainy or granulated surface
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

granularly (ˈgranularly) adverb

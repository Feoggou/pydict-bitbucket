phosphate

(ˈfɒsfeɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any salt or ester of any phosphoric acid, esp a salt of orthophosphoric acid2. (often plural) any of several chemical fertilizers containing phosphorous compounds

Derived Forms

phosphatic (fɒsˈfætɪk ) adjective

Word Origin

C18: from French phosphat; see phosphorus, -ate1

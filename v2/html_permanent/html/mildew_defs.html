mildew

(ˈmɪlˌdjuː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of various diseases of plants that affect mainly the leaves and are caused by parasitic fungi. See also downy mildew, powdery mildew2. any fungus causing this kind of disease3. another name for mould2verb4. to affect or become affected with mildew

Derived Forms

milˌdewy (ˈmilˌdewy) adjective

Word Origin

Old English mildēaw, from mil- honey (compare Latin mel, Greek mēli) + dēaw dew

intervention

(ˌɪntəˈvɛnʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of intervening2. any interference in the affairs of others, esp by one state in the affairs of another3. economics the action of a central bank in supporting the international value of a currency by buying large quantities of the currency to keep the price up4. business the action of the EU in buying up surplus produce when the market price drops to a certain value

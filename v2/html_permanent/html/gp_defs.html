Word forms: GPs
Definitions
countable noun [oft poss N]A GP is a doctor who does not specialize in any particular area of medicine, but who has a medical practice in which he or she treats all types of illness. GP is an abbreviation for 'general practitioner'. ⇒ Her husband called their local GP.

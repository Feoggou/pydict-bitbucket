annexe

or especially US annex (ˈænɛks )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. an extension to a main buildingb. a building used as an addition to a main building nearby2. something added or annexed, esp a supplement to a document

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounAn annexe is a building which is joined to or is next to a larger main building. ⇒ ...setting up a museum in an annexe to the theatre.2. countable nounAn annexe to a document is a section added to it at the end. ⇒ The Annex lists and discusses eight titles.

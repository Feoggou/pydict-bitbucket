Viborg

Definitions
noun1. (ˈviːbɔrj) the Swedish name for Vyborg2. (Danish ˈvibɔr) a town in N central Denmark, in Jutland: formerly a royal town and capital of Jutland. Pop: 33 192 (2004 est)

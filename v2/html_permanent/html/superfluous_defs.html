superfluous

(suːˈpɜːflʊəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. exceeding what is sufficient or required2. not necessary or relevant; uncalled-for3. obsolete extravagant in expenditure or oversupplied with possessions

Derived Forms

superfluously (suˈperfluously) adverbsuperfluousness (suˈperfluousness) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin superfluus overflowing, from super- + fluere to flow

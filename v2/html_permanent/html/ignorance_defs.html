ignorance

(ˈɪɡnərəns ) or rare ignorantness (ˈɪɡnərəntnɪs)
Definitions
nounlack of knowledge, information, or education; the state of being ignorant

ignorance

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪgnərəns ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIgnorance of something is lack of knowledge about it. [+ of/about] ⇒ I am embarrassed by my complete ignorance of history. ⇒ There is so much ignorance about mental illness. ⇒ In my ignorance I had never heard country & western music.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

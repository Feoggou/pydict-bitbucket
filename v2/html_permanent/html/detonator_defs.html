detonator

(ˈdɛtəˌneɪtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small amount of explosive, as in a percussion cap, used to initiate a larger explosion2. a device, such as an electrical generator, used to set off an explosion from a distance3. a substance or object that explodes or is capable of exploding

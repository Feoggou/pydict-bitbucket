(bɪˈwɪldəd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivepuzzled; confused ⇒ 'What is she talking about?' I said bewildered., ⇒ He looked bewildered., ⇒ Some shoppers looked bewildered by the sheer variety.

typhoon

(taɪˈfuːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a violent tropical storm or cyclone, esp in the China seas and W Pacific2. a violent storm of India

Derived Forms

typhonic (taɪˈfɒnɪk ) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Chinese tai fung great wind, from tai great + fung wind; influenced by Greek tuphōn whirlwind

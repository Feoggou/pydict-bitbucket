Word Frequency

quarter-hour

Definitions
noun1. a period of 15 minutes2. any of the points on the face of a timepiece that mark 15 minutes before or after the hour, and sometimes 30 minutes after
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

quarter-hourly (ˌquarter-ˈhourly) adverb, adjective

quadrate

Definitions
noun (ˈkwɒdrɪt , -reɪt)1. a cube, square, or a square or cubelike object2. one of a pair of bones of the upper jaw of fishes, amphibians, reptiles, and birds that articulates with the lower jaw. In mammals it forms the incusadjective (ˈkwɒdrɪt , -reɪt)3. of or relating to this bone4. square or rectangularverb (kwɒˈdreɪt )5. (transitive) to make square or rectangular6. (often foll by with) to conform or cause to conform
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin quadrāre to make square

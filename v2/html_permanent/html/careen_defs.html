careen

(kəˈriːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to sway or cause to sway dangerously over to one side2. (transitive) nautical to cause (a vessel) to keel over to one side, esp in order to clean or repair its bottom3. (intransitive) nautical (of a vessel) to keel over to one side
Derived forms
careenage (caˈreenage) nouncareener (caˈreener) noun
Word origin
C17: from French carène keel, from Italian carena, from Latin carīna keel

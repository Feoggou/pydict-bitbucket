feathered

Powered by COBUILD
(feðəʳd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveIf you describe something as feathered, you mean that it has feathers on it. ⇒ ...the ceremonial feathered hat worn by Hong Kong's governor.2. →  feathered friends

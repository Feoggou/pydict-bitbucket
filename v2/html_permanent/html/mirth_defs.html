Powered by COBUILD
(mɜːʳθ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounMirth is amusement which you express by laughing. [literary] ⇒ That caused considerable mirth amongst pupils and sports masters alike. ⇒ It was all he could do to stop tears of mirth falling down his cheeks.

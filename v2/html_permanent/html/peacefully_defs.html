peacefully

(piːsfʊli )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adverb [ADV after v]If you say that someone died peacefully, you mean that they suffered no pain or violence when they died. ⇒ He died peacefully on 10th December after a short illness.2. → see also peaceful
Example sentences containing 'peacefully'
Only then he hadn't been sitting peacefully on a porch, enjoying the tranquil serenity around him. MacNeill, Alastair THE DEVIL'S DOORShe pounded the wooden table, sending her empty glass flying and waking Benjy, who had been dozing peacefully in her lap. Dobbs, Michael THE TOUCH OF INNOCENTSShe, Dad and Trevor were reading peacefully at the kitchen table. Hodgman, Ann MY BABYSITTER HAS FANGS

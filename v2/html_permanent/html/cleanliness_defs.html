cleanliness

Powered by COBUILD
(klenlɪnəs )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounCleanliness is the degree to which people keep themselves and their surroundings clean. ⇒ Many of Britain's beaches fail to meet minimum standards of cleanliness. ⇒ ...the importance of personal cleanliness.
Example sentences containing 'cleanliness'
He radiated health, open air, cleanliness , gleaming white shirt front. Haines, Pamela THE GOLDEN LIONGoss's girl, the girl he was to marry, the girl who worked in the Northern Bank, she was a stickler for personal cleanliness. Seymour, Gerald FIELD OF BLOODOf course Mother Lupescu's definition of moral cleanliness had been purely her own. Clive Barker COLDHEART CANYON

Definitions
singular nounIf there is an exodus of people from a place, a lot of people leave that place at the same time. ⇒ Lieutenant Malcolm said she saw no sign that the exodus from Haiti was abating. [+ of] ⇒ The medical system is facing collapse because of an exodus of doctors.

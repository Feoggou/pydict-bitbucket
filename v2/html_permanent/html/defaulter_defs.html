defaulter

(dɪˈfɔːltə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who defaults2. mainly British a person, esp a soldier, who has broken the disciplinary code of his or her service

Definitions

countable nounA defaulter is someone who does not do something that they are legally supposed to do, such as make a payment at a particular time, or appear in a court of law.

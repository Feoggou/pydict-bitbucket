boxer

(ˈbɒksə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who boxes, either professionally or as a hobby; pugilist2. a medium-sized smooth-haired breed of dog with a short nose and a docked tail

Boxer

(ˈbɒksə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna. a member of a nationalistic Chinese secret society that led an unsuccessful rebellion in 1900 against foreign interests in Chinab. (as modifier) ⇒ the Boxer Rebellion
Word origin
C18: rough translation of Chinese I Ho Ch'üan, literally: virtuous harmonious fist, altered from I Ho T'uan virtuous harmonious society

gale1

(ɡeɪl )
Definitions
noun1. a strong wind, specifically one of force seven to ten on the Beaufort scale or from 45 to 90 kilometres per hour2. (often plural) a loud outburst, esp of laughter3. archaic, poetic a gentle breeze
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: of unknown origin

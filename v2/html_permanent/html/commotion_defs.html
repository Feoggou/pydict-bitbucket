commotion

(kəˈməʊʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. violent disturbance; upheaval2. political insurrection; disorder3. a confused noise; din
Derived forms
commotional (comˈmotional) adjective
Word origin
C15: from Latin commōtiō, from commovēre to throw into disorder, from com- (intensive) + movēre to move

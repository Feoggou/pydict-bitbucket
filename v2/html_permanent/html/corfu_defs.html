(kɔːˈfuː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an island in the Ionian Sea, in the Ionian Islands: forms, with neighbouring islands, a department of Greece. Pop: 107 879 (2001). Area: 641 sq km (247 sq miles)2. a port on E Corfu island. Pop (municipality): 41 532 (2001)

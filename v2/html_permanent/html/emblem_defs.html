emblem

(ˈɛmbləm )
Definitions
noun1. a visible object or representation that symbolizes a quality, type, group, etc, esp the concrete symbol of an abstract idea ⇒ the dove is an emblem of peace2. an allegorical picture containing a moral lesson, often with an explanatory motto or verses, esp one printed in an emblem book
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

emblematic (ˌemblemˈatic) or emblematical (ˌemblemˈatical) adjectiveemblematically (ˌemblemˈatically) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Latin emblēma raised decoration, mosaic, from Greek, literally: something inserted, from emballein to insert, from ballein to throw

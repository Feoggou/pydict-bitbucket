corny

(ˈkɔːnɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: cornier or corniest slang1. trite or banal2. sentimental or mawkish3. abounding in corn
Word origin
C16 (C20 in the sense rustic, banal): from corn1 + -y1

(kɔːʳni )
Word forms: cornier , corniest
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as corny, you mean that it is obvious or sentimental and not at all original. [disapproval] ⇒ I know it sounds corny, but I'm really not motivated by money. ⇒ ...corny jokes.

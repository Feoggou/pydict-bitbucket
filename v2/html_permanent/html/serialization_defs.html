Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
regional note:   in BRIT, also use serialisation1. uncount nounSerialization is the act of serializing a book.2. countable nounA serialization is a story, originally written as a book, which is being published or broadcast in a number of parts. [+ of] ⇒ ...in the serialisation of Jane Austen's Pride and Prejudice.

kinematics

(ˌkɪnɪˈmætɪks , ˌkaɪ-)
Definitions
noun(functioning as singular) the study of the motion of bodies without reference to mass or force. Compare dynamics (sense 1)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

kinematic (ˌkineˈmatic) adjectivekinematically (ˌkineˈmatically) adverb

Word Origin

C19: from Greek kinēma movement; see cinema, -ics

longing

(ˈlɒŋɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. a prolonged unfulfilled desire or needadjective2. having or showing desire or need ⇒ a longing look
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

longingly (ˈlongingly) adverb

Word forms: longings
Definitions
variable noun [N to-inf]If you feel longing or a longing for something, you have a rather sad feeling because you want it very much. [+ for] ⇒ He felt a longing for the familiar. [+ to-inf] ⇒ Imelda spoke of her longing to return home. ⇒ I was overwhelmed with longing for those innocent days of early childhood.

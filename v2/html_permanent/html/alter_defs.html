alter

(ˈɔːltə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to make or become different in some respect; change2. (transitive) informal, mainly US a euphemistic word for castrate, spay
Derived forms
ˈalterable adjectiveˈalterably adverbˌalteraˈbility noun
Word origin
C14: from Old French alterer, from Medieval Latin alterāre to change, from Latin alter other

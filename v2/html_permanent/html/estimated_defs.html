estimated

(ˈɛstɪmeɪtɪd)
Definitions
adjectiveapproximate; roughly calculated ⇒ The estimated cost for this folly was £150,000., ⇒ The estimated jackpot this week is £4,100,000., ⇒ There are an estimated 90,000 gangsters in the country.

Chippendale

(ˈtʃɪpənˌdeɪl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Thomas. ?1718–79, English cabinet-maker and furniture designeradjective2. (of furniture) designed by, made by, or in the style of Thomas Chippendale, characterized by the use of Chinese and Gothic motifs, cabriole legs, and massive carving

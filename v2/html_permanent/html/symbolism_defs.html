symbolism

(ˈsɪmbəˌlɪzəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the representation of something in symbolic form or the attribution of symbolic meaning or character to something2. a system of symbols or symbolic representation3. a symbolic significance or quality4. (often capital) a late 19th-century movement in art that sought to express mystical or abstract ideas through the symbolic use of images. See also synthetism5. theology any symbolist interpretation of the Eucharist

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounSymbolism is the use of symbols in order to represent something. ⇒ The scene is so rich in symbolism that any explanation risks spoiling the effect. ⇒ ...a film much praised at the time for its visual symbolism.2. uncount nounYou can refer to the symbolism of an event or action when it seems to show something important about a situation. [+ of] ⇒ The symbolism of every gesture will be of vital importance during the short state visit.

handcuffs

(ˈhændkʌf)
Definitions
plural nouna pair of locking metal rings joined by a short bar or chain for securing prisoners, etc ⇒ The guards produced a pair of handcuffs and Kolchinsky's wrists were manacled behind his back., ⇒ He was led away to jail in handcuffs.

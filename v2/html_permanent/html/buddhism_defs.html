Buddhism

(ˈbʊdɪzəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna religious teaching propagated by the Buddha and his followers, which declares that by destroying greed, hatred, and delusion, which are the causes of all suffering, man can attain perfect enlightenment. See nirvana
Derived forms
Buddhist (ˈBuddhist) noun, adjective

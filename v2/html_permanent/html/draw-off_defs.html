Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrasal verbIf a quantity of liquid is drawn off from a larger quantity, it is taken from it, usually by means of a needle or pipe. [be V-ed P] ⇒ The fluid can be drawn off with a syringe. [V P n] ⇒ Doctors drew off a pint of his blood. [Also V n P] →  draw

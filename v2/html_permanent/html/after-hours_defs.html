after-hours

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]You use after-hours to describe activities which happen after the end of the usual time for them. ⇒ The school offers after-hours childcare. ⇒ In after-hours trading, shares surged by another estimated 30 points or more.

after hours

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf you do something after hours, you do it outside normal business hours or the time when you are usually at work. ⇒ ...a local restaurant where steel workers unwind after hours. ⇒ Daly kept this school open after hours so it doubled as a community center. →  hour

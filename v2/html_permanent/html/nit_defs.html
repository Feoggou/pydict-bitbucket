nit1

(nɪt )
Definitions
noun1. the egg of a louse, especially when adhering to human hair2. the larva of a louse or similar insect
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English hnitu; related to Dutch neet, Old High German hniz

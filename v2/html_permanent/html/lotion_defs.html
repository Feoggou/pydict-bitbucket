lotion

(ˈləʊʃən )
Definitions
nouna liquid preparation having a soothing, cleansing, or antiseptic action, applied to the skin, eyes, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: via Old French from Latin lōtiō a washing, from lōtus past participle of lavāre to wash

(loʊʃən) in the Pharmaceutical Industry

Definitions
noun(Pharmaceutical: Dosage forms) A lotion is a medicated liquid that you apply directly to a part of the body. ⇒ The lotion is a transparent liquid for use on the skin. ⇒ A lotion is easier to apply to sore skin than a cream, as you don't need pressure to rub in the liquid. ⇒ A lotion is a medicated liquid that you apply directly to a part of the body. ▶  scalp lotion

sapphire

(ˈsæfaɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. any precious corundum gemstone that is not red, esp the highly valued transparent blue variety. A synthetic form is used in electronics and precision apparatus. Formula: Al2O3b. (as modifier) ⇒ a sapphire ring2. a. the blue colour of sapphireb. (as adjective) ⇒ sapphire eyes

Word Origin

C13 safir, from Old French, from Latin sapphīrus, from Greek sappheiros, perhaps from Hebrew sappīr, ultimately perhaps from Sanskrit śanipriya, literally: beloved of the planet Saturn, from śani Saturn + priya beloved

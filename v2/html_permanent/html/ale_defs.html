ale

(eɪl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a beer fermented in an open vessel using yeasts that rise to the top of the brew. Compare beer, lager12. (formerly) an alcoholic drink made by fermenting a cereal, esp barley, but differing from beer by being unflavoured by hops3. mainly British another word for beer
Word origin
Old English alu, ealu; related to Old Norse öl, Old Saxon alofat

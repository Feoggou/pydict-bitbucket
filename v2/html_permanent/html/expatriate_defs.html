expatriate

Definitions
adjective (ɛksˈpætrɪɪt , -ˌeɪt)1. resident in a foreign country2. exiled or banished from one's native country ⇒ an expatriate Americannoun (ɛksˈpætrɪɪt , -ˌeɪt)3. a person who lives in a foreign country4. an exile; expatriate personverb (ɛksˈpætrɪˌeɪt ) (transitive)5. to exile (oneself) from one's native country or cause (another) to go into exile6. to deprive (oneself or another) of citizenship
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

expatriation (exˌpatriˈation) noun

Word Origin

C18: from Medieval Latin expatriāre, from Latin ex-1 + patria native land

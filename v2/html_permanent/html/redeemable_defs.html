redeemable

(rɪˈdiːməbəl ) or redemptible (rɪˈdɛmptəbəl )
Definitions
adjective (of bonds, shares, etc)1. subject to cancellation by repayment at a specified date or under specified conditions2. payable in or convertible into cash
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

redeemability (reˌdeemaˈbility) or rare redeemableness (reˈdeemableness) nounredeemably (reˈdeemably) adverb

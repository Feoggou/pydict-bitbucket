puffed up

(pʌft ʌp)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. if you describe someone as puffed up, you disapprove of them because they are very proud of themselves and think that they are very important ⇒ He was too puffed up with his own importance, too blinded by vanity to accept their verdict on him.2. another name for puffed (sense 1)

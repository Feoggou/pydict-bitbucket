Synonyms of perversion

perversion

noun

  1. deviation, vice, abnormality, aberration, kink (British, informal), wickedness, depravity, immorality, debauchery, unnaturalness, kinkiness (slang), vitiation
    • The most frequent sexual perversion is fetishism.
  2. distortion, twisting, corruption, misuse, misrepresentation, misinterpretation, falsification
    • a monstrous perversion of justice

miner

(ˈmaɪnə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who works in a mine2. Also called: continuous miner a large machine for the automatic extraction of minerals, esp coal, from a mine3. any of various insects or insect larvae that bore into and feed on plant tissues. See also leaf miner4. Australian any of several honey-eaters of the genus Manorina, esp M. melanocephala (noisy miner), of scrub regions. See noisy miner

cromlech

(ˈkrɒmlɛk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a circle of prehistoric standing stones2. (no longer in technical usage) a megalithic chamber tomb or dolmen
Word origin
C17: from Welsh, from crom, feminine of crwm bent, arched + llech flat stone

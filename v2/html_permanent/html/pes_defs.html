pes

(peɪz , piːz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural pedes (ˈpɛdiːz )1. the technical name for the human foot2. the corresponding part in higher vertebrates3. any footlike part

Word Origin

C19: New Latin: foot

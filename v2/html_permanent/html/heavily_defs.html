heavily

Powered by COBUILD
(hevɪli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adverb [ADV after v]If someone says something heavily, they say it in a slow way which shows a feeling such as sadness, tiredness, or annoyance. ⇒ 'I didn't even think about her,' he said heavily.2. → See also heavy

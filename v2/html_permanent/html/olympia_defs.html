Olympia

(əˈlɪmpɪə )
Definitions
noun1. a plain in Greece, in the NW Peloponnese: in ancient times a major sanctuary of Zeus and site of the original Olympic Games2. a port in W Washington, the state capital, on Puget Sound. Pop: 43 963 (2003 est)

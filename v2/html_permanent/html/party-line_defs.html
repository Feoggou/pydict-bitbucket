party line

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a telephone line serving two or more subscribers2. the policies or dogma of a political party, to which all members are expected to subscribe3. mainly US the boundary between adjoining property

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
singular nounThe party line on a particular issue is the official view taken by a political party, which its members are expected to support. ⇒ They ignored the official party line.

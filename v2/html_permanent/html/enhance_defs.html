enhance

(ɪnˈhɑːns )
Definitions
verb(transitive) to intensify or increase in quality, value, power, etc; improve; augment
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

enhancement (enˈhancement) nounenhancer (enˈhancer) nounenhancive (enˈhancive) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Old French enhaucier, from en-1 + haucier to raise, from Vulgar Latin altiāre (unattested), from Latin altus high

acidity

(əˈsɪdɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the quality or state of being acid2. the amount of acid present in a solution, often expressed in terms of pH3. another name for hyperacidity
Example sentences containing 'acidity'
`No, but it's the best place for testing the acidity of alkalinity of this hillside. Ross, Melville LOHENGRIN`Who had eyes only for Jenny," said Esme, with perhaps rather more acidity than the occasion demanded. Brandon, Ruth LEFT, RIGHT AND CENTREThe cold-hot mint tea brought out the fruit's acidity and quite succeeded in waking up the taste buds along his tongue.

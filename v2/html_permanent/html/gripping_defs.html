gripping

(ˈɡrɪpɪŋ)
Definitions
adjectiveable to hold the interest or attention of someone ⇒ Craig Thomas's gripping tale of espionage, ⇒ Her reckless, desperate story makes a gripping thriller., ⇒ The writing is gripping, consistently superb.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

grippingly (ˈgrippingly) adverb

chubby

(ˈtʃʌbɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -bier or -biest(esp of the human form) plump and round
Derived forms
chubbily (ˈchubbily) adverbchubbiness (ˈchubbiness) noun
Word origin
C17: perhaps from chub, with reference to the plump shape of the fish

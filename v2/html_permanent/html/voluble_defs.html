voluble

(ˈvɒljʊbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. talking easily, readily, and at length; fluent2. archaic easily turning or rotating, as on an axis3. rare (of a plant) twining or twisting
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

volubility (ˌvoluˈbility) or volubleness (ˈvolubleness) nounvolubly (ˈvolubly) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Latin volūbilis turning readily, fluent, from volvere to turn

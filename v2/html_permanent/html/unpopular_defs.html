Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf something or someone is unpopular, most people do not like them. ⇒ It was a painful and unpopular decision. ⇒ In high school, I was very unpopular, and I did encounter a little prejudice. [+ with] ⇒ The Chancellor is deeply unpopular with voters.unpopularity (ʌnpɒpjʊlærɪti ) uncount noun [usu with poss] [+ among] ⇒ ...his unpopularity among his colleagues. ⇒ ...the unpopularity of the new tax.

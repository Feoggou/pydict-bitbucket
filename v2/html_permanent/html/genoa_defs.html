Genoa

(ˈdʒɛnəʊə )
Definitions
nouna port in NW Italy, capital of Liguria, on the Gulf of Genoa: Italy's main port; an independent commercial city with many colonies in the Middle Ages; university (1243); heavy industries. Pop: 610 307 (2001). Italian name: Genova

genoa

(ˈdʒɛnəʊə )
Definitions
nounyachting a large triangular jib sail, often with a foot that extends as far aft as the clew of the mainsail. Also called: genoa jib. Sometimes shortened to: genny, jenny

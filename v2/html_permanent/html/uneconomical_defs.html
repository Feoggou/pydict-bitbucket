uneconomical for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌniːkənɒmɪkəl , -ek- )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that an action, a method, or a product is uneconomical, you mean that it does not make a profit. [business] ⇒ It would be uneconomical to send a brand new tape. ⇒ The methods employed are old-fashioned and uneconomical. ⇒ Even the successful flying boats proved, in the end, uneconomical. ⇒ ...the uneconomical duplication of jobs.

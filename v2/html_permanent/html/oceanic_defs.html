oceanic

(ˌəʊʃɪˈænɪk )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to the ocean2. living in the depths of the ocean beyond the continental shelf at a depth exceeding 200 metres ⇒ oceanic fauna3. huge or overwhelming4. (of geological formations) of volcanic origin, arising from the ocean ⇒ oceanic islands
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Oceanic

(ˌəʊʃɪˈænɪk )
Definitions
noun1. a branch, group, or subfamily of the Malayo-Polynesian family of languages, comprising Polynesian and Melanesianadjective2. of, relating to, or belonging to this group of languages3. of or relating to Oceania

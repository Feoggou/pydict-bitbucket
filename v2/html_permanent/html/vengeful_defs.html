vengeful

(ˈvɛndʒfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. desiring revenge; vindictive2. characterized by or indicating a desire for revenge ⇒ a vengeful glance3. inflicting or taking revenge ⇒ with vengeful blows
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vengefully (ˈvengefully) adverbvengefulness (ˈvengefulness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as vengeful, you are critical of them because they feel a great desire for revenge. [disapproval] ⇒ He was stabbed to death by his vengeful wife. ⇒ He did not think he was any more cruel, any more vengeful than other men.

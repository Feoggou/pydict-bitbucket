loiter

(ˈlɔɪtə )
Definitions
verb(intransitive) to stand or act aimlessly or idly
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

loiterer (ˈloiterer) nounloitering (ˈloitering) noun, adjective

Word Origin

C14: perhaps from Middle Dutch löteren to wobble: perhaps related to Old English lūtian to lurk

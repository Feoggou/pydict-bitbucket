transept

(ˈtrænsɛpt )
Definitions
nouneither of the two wings of a cruciform church at right angles to the nave
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

transeptal (tranˈseptal) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Anglo-Latin transeptum, from Latin trans- + saeptum enclosure

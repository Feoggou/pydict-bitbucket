black flag

Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
1.  a flag that is all or mostly black, esp. such a flag flown by a pirate ship. see also Jolly Roger2.  in auto racing, a signal to a driver during a race, made by waving a solid black flag, indicating that the driver must stop and consult an official

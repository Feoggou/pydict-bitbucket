Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverb [ADV with v]You use sometimes to say that something happens on some occasions rather than all the time. ⇒ During the summer, my skin sometimes gets greasy. ⇒ Sometimes I think he dislikes me. ⇒ You must have noticed how tired he sometimes looks. ⇒ Other people's jobs were exactly the same–sometimes good, sometimes bad.

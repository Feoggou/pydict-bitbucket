Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveOn sunless days, the sun does not shine. ⇒ The day dawned sunless and with a low cloud base.2. adjective [ADJ n]Sunless places are not lit by the sun. ⇒ Carmen stayed behind in the dark, sunless room.

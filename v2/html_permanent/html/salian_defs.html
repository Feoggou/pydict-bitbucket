Salian

(ˈseɪlɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. denoting or relating to a group of Franks (the Salii) who settled in the Netherlands in the 4th century ad and later conquered large areas of Gaul, esp in the northnoun2. a member of this group

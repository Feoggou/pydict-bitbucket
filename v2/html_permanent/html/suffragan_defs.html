suffragan

(ˈsʌfrəɡən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. a. (of any bishop of a diocese) subordinate to and assisting his superior archbishop or metropolitanb. (of any assistant bishop) having the duty of assisting the bishop of the diocese to which he is appointed but having no ordinary jurisdiction in that diocesenoun2. a suffragan bishop
Derived forms
suffraganship (ˈsuffraganship) noun
Word origin
C14: from Medieval Latin suffragāneus, from suffrāgium assistance, from Latin: suffrage

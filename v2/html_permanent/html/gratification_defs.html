gratification

(ˌɡrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of gratifying or the state of being gratified2. something that gratifies3. an obsolete word for gratuity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

croup1

(kruːp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna throat condition, occurring usually in children, characterized by a hoarse cough and laboured breathing, resulting from inflammation and partial obstruction of the larynx
Derived forms
croupy (ˈcroupy) or croupous (ˈcroupous) adjectivecroupily (ˈcroupily) adverbcroupiness (ˈcroupiness) noun
Word origin
C16 croup to cry hoarsely, probably of imitative origin

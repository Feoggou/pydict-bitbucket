grizzled

(ˈɡrɪzəld )
Definitions
adjective1. streaked or mixed with grey; grizzly; griseous2. having grey or partly grey hair

grizzled

Powered by COBUILD
(grɪzəld ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]A grizzled person or a person with grizzled hair has hair that is grey or partly grey. ⇒ ...a grizzled old age pensioner. ⇒ He was an old man with grey, grizzled hair.

foreknowledge

Powered by COBUILD
(fɔːʳnɒlɪdʒ ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIf you have foreknowledge of an event or situation, you have some knowledge of it before it actually happens. [+ of] ⇒ She has maintained that the General had foreknowledge of the plot.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

victorious

(vɪkˈtɔːrɪəs )
Definitions
adjective1. having defeated an adversary ⇒ the victorious nations2. of, relating to, indicative of, or characterized by victory ⇒ a victorious conclusion
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

victoriously (vicˈtoriously) adverbvictoriousness (vicˈtoriousness) noun

Definitions

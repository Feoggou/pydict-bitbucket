Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. people or things regarded as unimportant2. young children3. young or small fishes

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounSmall fry is used to refer to someone or something that is considered to be unimportant. ⇒ What they owe to the Inland Revenue is small fry compared to the overall £1.2 million debt. ⇒ It's the small fry who are usually the last to get paid.

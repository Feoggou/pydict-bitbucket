Dumbarton

(dʌmˈbɑːtən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna town in W Scotland, in West Dunbartonshire near the confluence of the Rivers Leven and Clyde: centred around the Rock of Dumbarton, an important stronghold since ancient times; engineering and distilling. Pop: 20 527 (2001)

panther

(ˈpænθə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -thers or -ther1. another name for the leopard, esp the black variety, which is known as the black panther2. US and Canadian any of various related animals, esp the puma

Word Origin

C14: from Old French pantère, from Latin panthēra, from Greek panthēr; perhaps related to Sanskrit pundarīka tiger

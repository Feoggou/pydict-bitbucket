abnormal

(æbˈnɔːməl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. not normal; deviating from the usual or typical; extraordinary2. informal odd in behaviour or appearance; strange
Derived forms
abnormally (abˈnormally) adverb
Word origin
C19: ab-1 + normal, replacing earlier anormal from Medieval Latin anormalus, a blend of Late Latin anōmalus anomalous + Latin abnormis departing from a rule

foist

(fɔɪst )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. (often foll by off or on) to sell or pass off (something, esp an inferior article) as genuine, valuable, etc2. (usually foll by in or into) to insert surreptitiously or wrongfully
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: probably from obsolete Dutch vuisten to enclose in one's hand, from Middle Dutch vuist fist

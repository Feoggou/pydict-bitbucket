inexcusable

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnɪkskjuːzəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that something is inexcusable, you are emphasizing that it cannot be justified or tolerated because it is extremely bad. [emphasis] ⇒ He said the killing of innocent people was inexcusable. ⇒ The Home Office has been accused of inexcusable incompetence.inexcusably (ɪnɪkskjuːzəbli ) graded adverb & adverb ⇒ She had been inexcusably careless.

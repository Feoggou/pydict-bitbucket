Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]In chemistry, a catalytic substance or a substance with catalytic properties is a substance which increases the speed of a chemical reaction. ⇒ ...carbon molecules with unusual chemical and catalytic properties.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a person or thing as having a catalytic effect, you mean that they cause things to happen or they increase the speed at which things happen. [formal] ⇒ Governments do, however, have a vital catalytic role in orchestrating rescue operations.

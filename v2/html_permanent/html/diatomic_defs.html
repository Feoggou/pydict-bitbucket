diatomic

(ˌdaɪəˈtɒmɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(of a compound or molecule)a. containing two atomsb. containing two characteristic groups or atoms ⇒ ethylene glycol is a diatomic alcohol
Derived forms

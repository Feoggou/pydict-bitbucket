Definitions

every inch

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
phraseIf you say that someone looks every inch a certain type of person, you are emphasizing that they look exactly like that kind of person. [emphasis] ⇒ He looks every inch the City businessman. ⇒ There stood Gertrude, looking every inch a star. →  inch

mantel

or less commonly mantle (ˈmæntəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a wooden or stone frame around the opening of a fireplace, together with its decorative facing2. Also called: mantelshelf a shelf above this frame

Word Origin

C15: from French, variant of mantle

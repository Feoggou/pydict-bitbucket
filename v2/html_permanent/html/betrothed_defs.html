betrothed

(bɪˈtrəʊðd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. engaged to be married ⇒ he was betrothed to hernoun2. the person to whom one is engaged; fiancé or fiancée

betrothed

Powered by COBUILD
(bɪtroʊðd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are betrothed to someone, you have agreed to marry them. [old-fashioned] ⇒ She was betrothed to his brother.Your betrothed is the person you are betrothed to. ⇒ She is here without her betrothed.

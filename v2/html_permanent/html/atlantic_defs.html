Atlantic

(ətˈlæntɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. See the Atlanticadjective2. of or relating to or bordering the Atlantic Ocean3. of or relating to Atlas or the Atlas Mountains
Word origin
C15: from Latin Atlanticus, from Greek (pelagos) Atlantikos (the sea) of Atlas (so called because it lay beyond the Atlas Mountains)

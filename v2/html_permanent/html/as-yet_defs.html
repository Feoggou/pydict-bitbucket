Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseYou use as yet with negative statements to describe a situation that has existed up until the present time. [formal] ⇒ As yet it is not known whether the crash was the result of an accident. ⇒ We have not as yet received a response. →  yet

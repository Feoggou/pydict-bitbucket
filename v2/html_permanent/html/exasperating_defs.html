(ɪgzɑːspəreɪtɪŋ , -zæs-) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you describe someone or something as exasperating, you mean that you feel angry or frustrated by them or by what they do. [+to] ⇒ Hardie could be exasperating to his colleagues. ⇒ She really is the most exasperating woman.

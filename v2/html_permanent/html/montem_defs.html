Montem

(ˈmɒntəm)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouneducation, British a former money-raising practice for the benefit of the senior college at Eton school, whereby pupils dressed up in fancy dress and walked to a hill near Slough and asked for donations from anyone they saw on the way there

brill1

(brɪl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural brill or brillsa European food fish, Scophthalmus rhombus, a flatfish similar to the turbot but lacking tubercles on the body: family Bothidae
Word origin
C15: probably from Cornish brӯthel mackerel, from Old Cornish brӯth speckled; related to Welsh brith spotted

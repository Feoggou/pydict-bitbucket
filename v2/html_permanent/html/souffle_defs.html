nounmedicine a blowing sound or murmur heard in auscultation

Word Origin

soufflé

(ˈsuːfleɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a very light fluffy dish made with egg yolks and stiffly beaten egg whites combined with cheese, fish, etc2. a similar sweet or savoury cold dish, set with gelatineadjective also: souffléed3. made light and puffy, as by beating and cooking

Word Origin

C19: from French, from souffler to blow, from Latin sufflāre

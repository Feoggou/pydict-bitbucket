quartet

or quartette (kwɔːˈtɛt )
Definitions
noun1. a group of four singers or instrumentalists or a piece of music composed for such a group. See string quartet2. any group of four ⇒ a quartet of fast bowlers
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Italian quartetto, diminutive of quarto fourth

quartet

Powered by COBUILD
(kwɔːʳtet ) for learners of English
Word forms: quartets
Definitions
1. countable noun [with sing or pl verb]A quartet is a group of four people who play musical instruments or sing together. ⇒ ...a string quartet. [+ of] ⇒ ...a quartet of singers.2. countable nounA quartet is a piece of music for four instruments or four singers.3. countable noun [usu N of n]A quartet of people or things is a group or set of four people or things. [written] ⇒ ...a quartet of books. ⇒ ...a quartet of local women in their mid-forties.

devastation

Powered by COBUILD
(devəsteɪʃən )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounDevastation is severe and widespread destruction or damage. ⇒ A huge bomb blast brought chaos and devastation to the centre of Belfast yesterday.

And now there was the brute fact of the devastation around him. Appiganesi, Lisa DREAMS OF INNOCENCEBerlin had not been his childhood home as it had been Mutti's but the devastation was still shocking.

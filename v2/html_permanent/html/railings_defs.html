railings

(ˈreɪlɪŋz)
Definitions
plural nouna fence, balustrade, or barrier that consists of rails supported by posts ⇒ He saw that the paint on his railings was powdery., ⇒ She leaned against the railings., ⇒ the iron railings of the convent grounds

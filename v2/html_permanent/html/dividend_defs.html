dividend

(ˈdɪvɪˌdɛnd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. financea. a distribution from the net profits of a company to its shareholdersb. a pro-rata portion of this distribution received by a shareholder2. the share of a cooperative society's surplus allocated at the end of a period to members3. insurance a sum of money distributed from a company's net profits to the holders of certain policies4. something extra; bonus5. a number or quantity to be divided by another number or quantity. Compare divisor6. law the proportion of an insolvent estate payable to the creditors
Word origin
C15: from Latin dīvidendum what is to be divided; see divide

Word Frequency

fixed point

Definitions
noun1. physics a reproducible invariant temperature; the boiling point, freezing point, or triple point of a substance, such as water, that is used to calibrate a thermometer or define a temperature scale2. mathematics a point that is not moved by a given transformation

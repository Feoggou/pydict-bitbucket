Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]A demonstrable fact or quality can be shown to be true or to exist. [formal] ⇒ The road safety programme is having a demonstrable effect on road users. ⇒ Despite its demonstrable speed and safety, the boat failed to become popular.demonstrably (dɪmɒnstrəbli ) adverb ⇒ ...demonstrably false statements.

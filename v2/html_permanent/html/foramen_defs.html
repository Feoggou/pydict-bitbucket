foramen

(fɒˈreɪmɛn )
Definitions
noun plural -ramina (-ˈræmɪnə) or -ramensa natural hole, esp one in a bone through which nerves and blood vessels pass
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

foraminal (fɒˈræmɪnəl ) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Latin, from forāre to bore, pierce

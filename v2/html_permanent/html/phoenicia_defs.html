Phoenicia

(fəˈnɪʃɪə , -ˈniː-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan ancient maritime country extending from the Mediterranean Sea to the Lebanon Mountains, now occupied by the coastal regions of Lebanon and parts of Syria and Israel: consisted of a group of city-states, at their height between about 1200 and 1000 bc, that were leading traders of the ancient world

consequences

(ˈkɒnsɪkwənsɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun(functioning as singular) British a game in which each player writes down a part of a story, folds over the paper, and passes it on to another player who continues the story. After several stages, the resulting (nonsensical) stories are read out

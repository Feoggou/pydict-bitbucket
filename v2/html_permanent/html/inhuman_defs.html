inhuman

(ɪnˈhjuːmən )
Definitions
adjective1. Also: inhumane (ˌɪnhjuːˈmeɪn ) lacking humane feelings, such as sympathy, understanding, etc; cruel; brutal2. not human
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

inhuman

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnhjuːmən ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe treatment or an action as inhuman, you mean that it is extremely cruel. ⇒ The detainees are often held in cruel and inhuman conditions. ⇒ The barbaric slaughter of whales is unnecessary and inhuman.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as inhuman, you mean that they are strange or bad because they do not seem human in some way. ⇒ ...inhuman shrieks that chilled my heart.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [oft poss N]A collaborator is someone that you work with to produce a piece of work, especially a book or some research. ⇒ The Irvine group and their collaborators are testing whether lasers do the job better.2. countable nounA collaborator is someone who helps an enemy who is occupying their country during a war. [disapproval] ⇒ Two alleged collaborators were shot dead by masked activists.

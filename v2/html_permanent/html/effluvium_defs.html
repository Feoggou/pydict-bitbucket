effluvium

(ɛˈfluːvɪəm )
Definitions
noun plural -via (-vɪə) or -viumsan unpleasant smell or exhalation, as of gaseous waste or decaying matter
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

effluvial (efˈfluvial) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Latin: a flowing out; see effluent

Similar Words

smell, exhaust, fumes, stink, odour, stench, reek, pong (British) (informal), miasma, exhalation, niff (British) (slang), malodour, mephitis

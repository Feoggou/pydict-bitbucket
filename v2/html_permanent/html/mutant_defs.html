mutant

(ˈmjuːtənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: mutation an animal, organism, or gene that has undergone mutationadjective2. of, relating to, undergoing, or resulting from change or mutation

Word Origin

C20: from Latin mutāre to change

countable nounA mutant is an animal or plant that is physically different from others of the same species because of a change in its genes.

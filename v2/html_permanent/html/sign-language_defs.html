Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounSign language is movements of your hands and arms used to communicate. There are several official systems of sign language, used for example by deaf people. Movements are also sometimes invented by people when they want to communicate with someone who does not speak the same language. ⇒ Her son used sign language to tell her what happened.

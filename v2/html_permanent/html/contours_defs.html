contours

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounthe shape or surface, esp of a curving form ⇒ pads of fat that give a face its rounded contours, ⇒  … the contours of the body, ⇒ I cradled my video camera nervously on my lap, but its cold contours did nothing to comfort me.

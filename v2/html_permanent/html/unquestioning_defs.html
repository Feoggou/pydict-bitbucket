unquestioning for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnkwestʃənɪŋ )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe a person or their beliefs as unquestioning, you are emphasizing that they accept something without any doubt or disagreement. [emphasis] ⇒ Isabella had been taught unquestioning obedience. ⇒ For the last 20 years, I have been an unquestioning supporter of comprehensive schools.unquestioningly adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ She supported him unquestioningly.

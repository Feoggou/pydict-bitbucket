ladylike

(ˈleɪdɪˌlaɪk )
Definitions
adjective1. like or befitting a lady in manners and bearing; refined and fastidious2. derogatory (of a man) effeminate

Powered by COBUILD
(leɪdilaɪk ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that a woman or girl is ladylike, you mean that she behaves in a polite, dignified, and graceful way. ⇒ I hate to be blunt, Frankie, but she just didn't strike me as being very ladylike. ⇒ She crossed the room with quick, ladylike steps.

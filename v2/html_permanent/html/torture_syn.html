Synonyms of torture

torture

verb

  1. torment, abuse, persecute, afflict, martyr, scourge, molest, crucify, mistreat, ill-treat, maltreat, put on the rack
    • Police are convinced she was tortured and killed.
    OPPOSITES:  comfort, soothe, console, alleviate, solace, mollify, salve
  2. distress, torment, worry, trouble, pain, rack, afflict, harrow, agonize, give someone grief (British & & South Africa), inflict anguish on
    • He would not torture her further by arguing.

noun

  1. ill-treatment, abuse, torment, persecution, martyrdom, maltreatment, harsh treatment
    • alleged cases of torture and murder by security forces
  2. agony, suffering, misery, anguish, hell, distress, torment, heartbreak
    • Waiting for the result was torture.
    OPPOSITES:  delight, pleasure, joy, well-being, happiness, enjoyment, amusement, bliss

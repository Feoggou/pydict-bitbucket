Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounPeople sometimes refer to a young man as a stripling when they want to indicate in a slightly humorous way that although he is no longer a boy, he is not yet really a man. [old-fashioned] ⇒ ...a stripling of 20.

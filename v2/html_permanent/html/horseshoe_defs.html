horseshoe

(ˈhɔːsˌʃuː )
Definitions
noun1. a piece of iron shaped like a U with the ends curving inwards that is nailed to the underside of the hoof of a horse to protect the soft part of the foot from hard surfaces: commonly thought to be a token of good luck2. an object of similar shapeverb -shoes, -shoeing or -shoed3. (transitive) to fit with a horseshoe; shoe

Definitions
1. countable nounA horseshoe is a piece of metal shaped like a U, which is fixed with nails to the bottom of a horse's foot in order to protect it.2. countable nounA horseshoe is an object in the shape of a horseshoe which is used as a symbol of good luck, especially at a wedding.

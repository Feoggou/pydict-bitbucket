broken-down

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. worn out, as by age or long use; dilapidated ⇒ a broken-down fence2. not in working order ⇒ a broken-down tractor3. physically or mentally ill

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]A broken-down vehicle or machine no longer works because it has something wrong with it. ⇒ ...a broken-down car.

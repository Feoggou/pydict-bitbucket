uncomfortable

(ʌnˈkʌmftəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. not comfortable2. feeling or causing discomfort or unease; disquieting

uncomfortable

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnkʌmftəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ, ADJ -ing]If you are uncomfortable, you are slightly worried or embarrassed, and not relaxed and confident. ⇒ The request for money made them feel uncomfortable. [+ with] ⇒ If you are uncomfortable with your counsellor or therapist, you must discuss it. ⇒ I feel uncomfortable lying. [Also + about]uncomfortably (ʌnkʌmftəbli ) graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj/-ed, oft ADV after v] ⇒ Sandy leaned across the table, his face uncomfortably close to Brad's. ⇒ I became uncomfortably aware that the people at the next table were watching me. ⇒ He smiled uncomfortably.2. graded adjective & adjective [oft ADJ to-inf]Something that is uncomfortable makes you feel slight pain or physical discomfort when you experience it or use it. ⇒ Wigs are hot and uncomfortable to wear constantly. ⇒ The Metro journey back to the centre of the town was hot and uncomfortable. ⇒ ...an uncomfortable chair.uncomfortably graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj] ⇒ The water was uncomfortably cold.3. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are uncomfortable, you are not physically content and relaxed, and feel slight pain or discomfort. ⇒ I sometimes feel uncomfortable after eating in the evening. ⇒ People living or working with smokers can find it uncomfortable to wear contact lenses because the smoke causes irritation.uncomfortably graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj, ADV after v] ⇒ He felt uncomfortably hot. ⇒ He awoke to find himself lying uncomfortably on a pile of firewood.4. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ to-inf]You can describe a situation or fact as uncomfortable when it is difficult to deal with and causes problems and worries. ⇒ It is uncomfortable to think of our own death, but we need to. ⇒ Such questions are uncomfortable to answer. ⇒ The decree put the president in an uncomfortable position.

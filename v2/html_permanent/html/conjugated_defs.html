conjugated

(ˈkɒndʒʊˌɡeɪtɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. chemistrya. (of a molecule, compound, or substance) containing two or more double bonds alternating with single bondsb. (of a double bond) separated from another double bond by one single bond2. chemistry formed by the union of two compounds ⇒ a conjugated protein

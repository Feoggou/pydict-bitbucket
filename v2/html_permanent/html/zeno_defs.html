Zeno

(ˈzinoʊ ) in American English
Definitions
1.  fl. 5th cent. b.c.; Gr. Eleatic philosopher: also Zeno of Elea (ˈiliə ) (town in Italy)2.  334?-261? b.c.; Gr. philosopher: founder of Stoicism: also Zeno of Citium (ˈsɪʃiəm ) (city in Cyprus)

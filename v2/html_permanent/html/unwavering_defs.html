unwavering for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnweɪvərɪŋ )
Definitions
adjectiveIf you describe a feeling or attitude as unwavering, you mean that it is strong and firm and does not weaken. ⇒ She has been encouraged by the unwavering support of her family. ⇒ ...his unwavering commitment to public education. ⇒ His attitude was unwavering.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

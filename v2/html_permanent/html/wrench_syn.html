Synonyms of wrench

wrench

verb

  1. twist, force, pull, tear, rip, tug, jerk, yank, wring, wrest
    • They wrenched open the passenger door and got into the car.
  2. sprain, strain, rick, distort
    • He had wrenched his ankle badly in the fall.

noun

  1. twist, pull, rip, tug, jerk, yank
    • The rope stopped his fall with a wrench that broke his neck.
  2. sprain, strain, twist
    • We are hoping the injury is just a wrench.
  3. blow, shock, pain, ache, upheaval, uprooting, pang
    • I knew it would be a wrench to leave home.
  4. spanner, adjustable spanner, shifting spanner
    • He took a wrench from his toolbox.

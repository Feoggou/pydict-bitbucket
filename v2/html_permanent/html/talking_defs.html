talking

(ˈtɔːkɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the act of expressing one's thoughts, feelings, or desires by means of words; speaking ⇒ That's enough talking!, ⇒ No talking!, ⇒ When we went back it was Alice who did the talking., ⇒ He did all the talking.adjective2. having the ability to talk ⇒ a talking doll, ⇒ a talking parrot

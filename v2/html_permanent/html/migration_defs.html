migration

(maɪˈɡreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or an instance of migrating2. a group of people, birds, etc, migrating in a body3. chemistry a movement of atoms, ions, or molecules, such as the motion of ions in solution under the influence of electric fields

Derived Forms

migrational (miˈgrational) adjective

Some of us believe our kindreds "outward migration was intended to be only the first wave of a continuing process. Tepper, Sheri S.

natron

(ˈneɪtrən )
Definitions
nouna whitish or yellow mineral that consists of hydrated sodium carbonate and occurs in saline deposits and salt lakes. Formula: Na2CO3.10H2O
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: via French and Spanish from Arabic natrūn, from Greek nitron

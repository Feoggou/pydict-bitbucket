Baptist

(ˈbæptɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a member of any of various Christian sects that affirm the necessity of baptism (usually of adults and by immersion) following a personal profession of the Christian faith2. See the Baptistadjective3. denoting, relating to, or characteristic of any Christian sect that affirms the necessity of baptism following a personal profession of the Christian faith

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA Baptist is a Christian who believes that people should not be baptized until they are old enough to understand the meaning of baptism.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Baptist means belonging or relating to Baptists. ⇒ ...a Baptist church.

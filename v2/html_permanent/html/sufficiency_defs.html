sufficiency

(səˈfɪʃənsɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cies1. the quality or condition of being sufficient2. an adequate amount or quantity, as of income3. archaic efficiency

sufficiency

Powered by COBUILD
(səfɪʃənsi )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount noun [oft a N]Sufficiency of something is enough of that thing to achieve a purpose or to fulfil a need. [formal] ⇒ When foods from different plant sources are eaten together, deficiency in one is compensated for by sufficiency in another. ⇒ There's a sufficiency of drama in these lives to sustain your interest.2. → see also self-sufficiency

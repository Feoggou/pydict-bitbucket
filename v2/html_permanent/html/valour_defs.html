valour

or US valor (ˈvælə )
Definitions
nouncourage or bravery, esp in battle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

valorous (ˈvalorous) adjectivevalorously (ˈvalorously) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Late Latin valor, from valēre to be strong

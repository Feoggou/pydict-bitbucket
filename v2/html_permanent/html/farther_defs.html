farther

(ˈfɑːðə )
Definitions
adverb1. to or at a greater distance in space or time2. in additionadjective3. more distant or remote in space or time4. additional▶ USAGE Farther, farthest, further, and furthest can all be used to refer to literal distance, but further and furthest are regarded as more correct for figurative senses denoting greater or additional amount, time, etc: further to my letter. Further and furthest are also preferred for figurative distance
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: see far, further

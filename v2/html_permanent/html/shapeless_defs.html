shapeless

(ˈʃeɪplɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having no definite shape or form ⇒ a shapeless mass, ⇒ a shapeless argument2. lacking a symmetrical or aesthetically pleasing shape ⇒ a shapeless figure

Derived Forms

shapelessly (ˈshapelessly) adverbshapelessness (ˈshapelessness) noun

(ʃeɪpləs )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Something that is shapeless does not have a distinct or attractive shape. ⇒ Aunt Mary wore shapeless black dresses.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adverb [ADV after v]If you buy or sell something privately, you buy it from or sell it to another person directly, rather than in a shop or through a business. ⇒ The whole process makes buying a car privately as painless as buying from a garage. ⇒ A great deal of food is distributed and sold privately without ever reaching the shops.2. → see also private
Example sentences containing 'privately'
That isn't to say his wanting to talk to me privately hasn't got me worried. Mosco, Maisie OUT OF THE ASHES`I'm not a politician," Ryle said, privately adding, Thank God. Harcourt, Palma DOUBLE DECEITSo we suggested going to his room to discuss the matter more privately. Fraser, Anthea THE GOSPEL MAKERS

sentiments

(ˈsɛntɪmənts)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounthoughts, opinions, or attitudes based on people's thoughts or feelings ⇒ Nationalist sentiments threaten to split the country., ⇒ I must agree with the sentiments expressed by John Prescott., ⇒ These sentiments were echoed by other speakers.

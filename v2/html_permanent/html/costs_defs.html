costs

(kɒsts)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. business the costs involved in producing goods or services ⇒ Moulton's have had to raise their prices to cover increased costs., ⇒ Costs have been cut by 30 to 50 per cent., ⇒ The company admits its costs are still too high.2. law the expenses of judicial proceedings ⇒ She lost the case and had to pay both parties' legal costs., ⇒ He was jailed for 18 months and ordered to pay £ 550 costs.

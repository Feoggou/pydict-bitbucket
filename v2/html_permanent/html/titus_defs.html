Titus

(ˈtaɪtəs )
Definitions
noun1. New Testamenta.  Saint. a Greek disciple and helper of Saint Paul. Feast day: Jan 26 or Aug 25b. the book written to him (in full The Epistle of Paul the Apostle to Titus), containing advice on pastoral matters2.  full name Titus Flavius Sabinus Vespasianus. ?40–81 ad, Roman emperor (78–81 ad)

audio

(ˈɔːdɪˌəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (modifier)1. of or relating to sound or hearing ⇒ audio frequency2. relating to or employed in the transmission, reception, or reproduction of sound3. of, concerned with, or operating at audio frequencies▷ Compare video
Word origin
C20: independent use of audio-

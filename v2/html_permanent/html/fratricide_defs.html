fratricide

(ˈfrætrɪˌsaɪd , ˈfreɪ-)
Definitions
noun1. the act of killing one's brother2. a person who kills his brother3. military the destruction of or interference with a nuclear missile before it can strike its target caused by the earlier explosion of a warhead at a nearby target
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fratricidal (ˌfratriˈcidal) adjective

Word Origin

C15: from Latin frātricīda; see frater1, -cide

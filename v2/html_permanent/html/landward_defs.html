landward

(ˈlændwəd )
Definitions
adjective1. lying, facing, or moving towards land2. in the direction of the landadverb3. a variant of landwards

landward

Powered by COBUILD
(lændwəʳd ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]The landward side of something is the side nearest to the land or facing the land, rather than the sea. ⇒ Rebels surrounded the city's landward sides.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

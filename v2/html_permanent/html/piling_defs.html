piling

(ˈpaɪlɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of driving piles2. a number of piles3. a structure formed of piles

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu pl]Pilings are wooden, concrete, or metal posts which are pushed into the ground and on which buildings or bridges are built. Pilings are often used in very wet areas so that the buildings do not flood. ⇒ ...bridges set on stone pilings.

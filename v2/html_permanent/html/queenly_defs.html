queenly

(ˈkwiːnlɪ )
Definitions
adjective -lier or -liest1. resembling or appropriate to a queen2. having the rank of queenadverb3. in a manner appropriate to a queen
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

queenliness (ˈqueenliness) noun

queenly

Powered by COBUILD
(kwiːnli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]You use queenly to describe a woman's appearance or behaviour if she looks very dignified or behaves as if she is very important. ⇒ She was a queenly, organizing type.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

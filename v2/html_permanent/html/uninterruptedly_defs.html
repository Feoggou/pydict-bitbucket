uninterruptedly

(ˌʌnˌɪntəˈrʌptɪdlɪ)
Definitions
adverbin a manner that is not broken, discontinued, or hindered ⇒ The convoy stretched uninterruptedly for three or four miles., ⇒ It rained uninterruptedly for almost the whole of February.

active voice

(ˈæktɪv vɔɪs)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noungrammar a voice of verbs used to indicate that the subject of a sentence is performing the action or causing the event or process described by the verb, as kicked in the boy kicked the football ⇒ Use the active voice and avoid adverbs whenever possible.

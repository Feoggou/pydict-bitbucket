concede

verb

  1. admit, allow, accept, acknowledge, own, grant, confess • She finally conceded that he was right. OPPOSITES:  deny, protest, reject, dispute, contest, refute, disclaim
  2. give up, yield, hand over, surrender, relinquish, cede • The central government has never conceded that territory to the Kurds. OPPOSITES:  beat, defeat, conquer, make a stand, fight to the bitter end
  • See concede defeat

'concede' in Other Languages

    If you concede something, you admit, often unwillingly, that it is true or correct.He finally conceded that she was right.

Arcadian

(ɑːˈkeɪdɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Arcadia or its inhabitants, esp the idealized Arcadia of pastoral poetry2. rustic or bucolic ⇒ a life of Arcadian simplicitynoun3. an inhabitant of Arcadia4. a person who leads or prefers a quiet simple rural life
Derived forms
Arˈcadianism noun

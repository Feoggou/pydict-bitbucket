fabled

Powered by COBUILD
(feɪbəld ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]If you describe a person or thing as fabled, especially someone or something remarkable, you mean that they are well known because they are often talked about or a lot of stories are told about them. ⇒ You cannot go home without visiting the fabled art collections of the Prado. ⇒ ...the fabled city of Troy.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

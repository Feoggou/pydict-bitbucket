percentage

(pəˈsɛntɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. proportion or rate per hundred parts2. business the interest, tax, commission, or allowance on a hundred items3. any proportion in relation to the whole4. informal profit or advantage

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA percentage is a fraction of an amount expressed as a particular number of hundredths of that amount. [+ of] ⇒ Only a few vegetable-origin foods have such a high percentage of protein.

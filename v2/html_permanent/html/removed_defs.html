removed

(rɪˈmuːvd )
Definitions
adjective1. separated by distance or abstract distinction2. (postpositive) separated by a degree of descent or kinship ⇒ the child of a person's first cousin is his first cousin once removed
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

removed

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪmuːvd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveIf you say that an idea or situation is far removed from something, you mean that it is very different from it. [+ from] ⇒ He found it hard to concentrate on conversation so far removed from his present preoccupations. ⇒ The country had witnessed scenes of tumult not far removed from civil war. ⇒ His style could scarcely be further removed from that of his predecessor.2. adjective [n ADJ]If someone is your cousin once removed, they are your cousin's child or your parent's cousin.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

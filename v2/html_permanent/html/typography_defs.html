typography

(taɪˈpɒɡrəfɪ )
Definitions
noun1. the art, craft, or process of composing type and printing from it2. the selection and planning of type for printed publications
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

typographical (ˌtaɪpəˈɡræfɪkəl ) or typographic (ˌtypoˈgraphic) adjectivetypographically (ˌtypoˈgraphically) adverb

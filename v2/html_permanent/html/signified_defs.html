signified

(ˈsɪɡnɪfaɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounlinguistics the meaning expressed by a sign rather than the physical form it is expressed in
Example sentences containing 'signified'
She tried not to think too hard about what it was, or what its appearance signified. Clive Barker THE GREAT AND SECRET SHOWOnly sixteen guests were expected, and among them only two who signified. Daisy Waugh TEN STEPS TO HAPPINESSShe seemed to have difficulty in remembering quite how it had got there and what it signified.

suckling

(ˈsʌklɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an infant or young animal that is still taking milk from the mother2. a very young child

Word Origin

C15: see suck, -ling1; related to Middle Dutch sūgeling, Middle High German sōgelinc

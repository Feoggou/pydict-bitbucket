(ˈsɔːltɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: saltier or saltiest1. of, tasting of, or containing salt2. (esp of humour) sharp; piquant3. relating to life at sea
Derived forms
saltily (ˈsaltily) adverbsaltiness (ˈsaltiness) noun

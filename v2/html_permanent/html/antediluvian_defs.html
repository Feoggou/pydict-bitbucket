antediluvian

(ˌæntɪdɪˈluːvɪən , -daɪ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. belonging to the ages before the biblical Flood (Genesis 7, 8)2. old-fashioned or antiquatednoun3. an antediluvian person or thing
Word origin
C17: from ante- + Latin dīluvium flood

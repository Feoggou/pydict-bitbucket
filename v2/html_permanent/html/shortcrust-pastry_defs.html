shortcrust pastry

(ˈʃɔːtˌkrʌst )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna basic type of pastry that is made with half the quantity of fat to flour, and has a crisp but crumbly texture. Also called: short pastry
Example sentences containing 'shortcrust pastry'
`Go on then," said John, affably chewing on his shortcrust pastry , `go on, don't leave us in suspense. Ian St James Awards AT THE STROKE OF TWELVE

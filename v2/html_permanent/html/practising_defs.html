practising

or US practicing (ˈpræktɪsɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. active with regard to a particular religion or belief system ⇒ a practising Catholic, ⇒ He's a Catholic, isn't he? – Yes, but not practising., ⇒ All employees must be practising Christians., ⇒ He has been a practising Pagan for many years.2. active in a particular profession ⇒ An art historian and collector, he was also a practising architect., ⇒ a practising dentist, ⇒ a practising doctor3. active with regard to a particular way of life or condition ⇒ He is a practising vegetarian, but just sometimes can be persuaded into a juicy steak., ⇒ a practising homosexual, ⇒ She is a practising bisexual., ⇒ He is a practising alcoholic.

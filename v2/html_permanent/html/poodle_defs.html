poodle

(ˈpuːdəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a breed of dog, with varieties of different sizes, having curly hair, which is often clipped from ribs to tail for showing: originally bred to hunt waterfowl2. a person who is servile; lackey

Word Origin

C19: from German Pudel, short for Pudelhund, from pudeln to splash + Hund dog; the dogs were formerly trained as water dogs; see puddle, hound1

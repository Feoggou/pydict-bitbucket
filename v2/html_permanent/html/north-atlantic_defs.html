North Atlantic

(nɔːθ ətˈlæntɪk)
Definitions
noun1. See the North Atlanticmodifier2. relating to the North Atlantic and, often, the countries bordering it ⇒ passenger traffic on North Atlantic routes was down 17.6 per cent., ⇒ This gradual recovery extended across BAA's markets, with North Atlantic traffic down 10.1 per cent in January, ⇒ NATO should be ready to act outside the North Atlantic area.

Albanian

(ælˈbeɪnɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the official language of Albania: of uncertain relationship within the Indo-European family, but thought to be related to ancient Illyrian2. a. a native, citizen, or inhabitant of Albaniab. a native speaker of Albanianadjective3. of or relating to Albania, its people, or their language

(ælbeɪniən )
Word forms: Albanians
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveAlbanian means belonging or relating to Albania, its people, language, or culture. ⇒ Her parents were Albanian. ⇒ ...the Albanian coast.2. countable nounAn Albanian is a person who comes from Albania.3. uncount nounAlbanian is the language spoken by people who live in Albania.

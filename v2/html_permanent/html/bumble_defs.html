bumble1

(ˈbʌmbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to speak or do in a clumsy, muddled, or inefficient way ⇒ he bumbled his way through his speech2. (intransitive) to proceed unsteadily; stumblenoun3. a blunder or botch
Derived forms
bumbler (ˈbumbler) nounbumbling (ˈbumbling) noun, adjectivebumblingly (ˈbumblingly) adverb
Word origin
C16: perhaps a blend of bungle + stumble

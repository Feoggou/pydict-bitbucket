signet

(ˈsɪɡnɪt )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small seal, esp one as part of a finger ring2. a seal used to stamp or authenticate documents3. the impression made by such a sealverb4. (transitive) to stamp or authenticate with a signet

Word Origin

C14: from Medieval Latin signētum a little seal, from Latin signum a sign

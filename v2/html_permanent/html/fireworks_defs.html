fireworks

(ˈfaɪəˌwɜːks )
Definitions
plural noun1. a show in which large numbers of fireworks are let off simultaneously2. informal an exciting or spectacular exhibition, as of musical virtuosity or wit3. informal a burst of temper
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

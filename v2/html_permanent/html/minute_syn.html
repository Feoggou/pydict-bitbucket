Synonyms of minute 1

minute1

noun

  1. sixty seconds, sixtieth of an hour
    • A minute later she came to the front door.
  2. moment, second, bit, shake (informal), flash, instant, tick (British, informal), sec (informal), short time, little while, jiffy (informal), trice
    • I'll be with you in a minute.
  3. point in time, time, point, moment, stage, instant, juncture
    • What was going on at that very minute?
  • See any minute
  • See at the minute
  • See this minute
  • See up to the minute
  • See wait a minute

minute2

adjective

  1. small, little, tiny, miniature, slender, fine, microscopic, diminutive, minuscule, infinitesimal, teeny-weeny, Lilliputian, teensy-weensy
    • Only a minute amount is needed.
    OPPOSITES:  huge, great, grand, enormous, generous, immense, gigantic, monstrous, ginormous (informal)
  2. negligible, slight, petty, trivial, trifling, unimportant, paltry, puny, piddling (informal), inconsiderable, picayune (US)
    • gambling large sums on the minute chance of a big win
    OPPOSITES:  major, important, significant, vital
  3. precise, close, detailed, critical, exact, meticulous, exhaustive, painstaking, punctilious
    • We will have to pore over this report in minute detail.
    OPPOSITES:  rough, careless, quick, loose, superficial, haphazard, imprecise, inexact

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. singular nounSomeone's advocacy of a particular action or plan is their act of recommending it publicly. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ I support your advocacy of free trade.2. uncount nounAdvocacy is the way in which lawyers deal with cases in court. [formal] ⇒ Sir Peter would also like to see the current adversarial system of advocacy examined by the royal commission.3. uncount noun [usu N n]An advocacy group or organization is one that tries to influence the decisions of a government or other authority. [US] ⇒ Consumer advocacy groups are not so enthusiastic about removing restrictions on the telephone companies.

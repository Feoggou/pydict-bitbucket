groschen

(ˈɡrəʊʃən , German ˈɡrɔʃən)
Definitions
noun plural -schen1. a former Austrian monetary unit worth one hundredth of a schilling2. a former German coin worth ten pfennigs3. a former German silver coin
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from German: Bohemian dialect alteration of Middle High German grosse, from Medieval Latin (denarius) grossus thick (penny); see gross, groat

totalitarian

(təʊˌtælɪˈtɛərɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. of, denoting, relating to, or characteristic of a dictatorial one-party state that regulates every realm of life. Also called: totalisticnoun2. a person who advocates or practises totalitarian policies
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

totalitarianism (toˌtaliˈtarianism) noun

Word Origin

from totality + -arian

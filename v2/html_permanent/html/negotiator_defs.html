(nɪgoʊʃieɪtəʳ )
Word forms: negotiators
Definitions
countable nounNegotiators are people who take part in political or financial negotiations. ⇒ ...the rebels' chief negotiator at the peace talks. ⇒ The two American negotiators are calling for substantial cuts in external subsidies.

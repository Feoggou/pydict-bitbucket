greens

(ɡriːnz)
Definitions
plural noun1. cookery the edible leaves and stems of certain plants, eaten as a vegetable ⇒ You must eat your greens.2. freshly cut branches of ornamental trees, shrubs, etc, used as a decoration3. slang sexual intercourse

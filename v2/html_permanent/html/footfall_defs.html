footfall

(ˈfʊtˌfɔːl )
Definitions
noun1. the sound of a footstep2. the number of visitors to a shop or tourist attraction

footfall

Powered by COBUILD
(fʊtfɔːl ) for learners of English
Word forms: footfalls
Definitions
countable nounA footfall is the sound that is made by someone walking each time they take a step. [literary] ⇒ She heard the priest's familiar, flat footfall on the staircase.

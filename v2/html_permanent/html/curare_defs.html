curare

or curari (kjʊˈrɑːrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. black resin obtained from certain tropical South American trees, esp Chondrodendron tomentosum, acting on the motor nerves to cause muscular paralysis: used medicinally as a muscle relaxant and by South American Indians as an arrow poison2. any of various trees of the genera Chondrodendron (family Menispermaceae) and Strychnos (family Loganiaceae) from which this resin is obtained
Word origin
C18: from Portuguese and Spanish, from Carib kurari

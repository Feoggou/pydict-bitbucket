caramel

(ˈkærəməl , -ˌmɛl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. burnt sugar, used for colouring and flavouring food2. a chewy sweet made from sugar, butter, milk, etc▷ See also crème caramel
Word origin
C18: from French, from Spanish caramelo, of uncertain origin

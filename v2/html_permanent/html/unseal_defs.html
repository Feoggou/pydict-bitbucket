unseal

(ʌnˈsiːl )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to remove or break the seal of2. to reveal or free (something concealed or closed as if sealed) ⇒ to unseal one's lips
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unsealable (unˈsealable) adjective

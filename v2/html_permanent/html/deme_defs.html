deme

(diːm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. (in preclassical Greece) the territory inhabited by a tribeb. (in ancient Attica) a geographical unit of local government2. biology a group of individuals within a species that possess particular characteristics of cytology, genetics, etc
Word origin
C19: from Greek dēmos district in local government, the populace

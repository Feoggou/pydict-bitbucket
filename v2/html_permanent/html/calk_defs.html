calk2

(kɔːk ) or calkin (ˈkɔːkɪn , ˈkæl-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a metal projection on a horse's shoe to prevent slipping2. mainly US and Canadian a set of spikes or a spiked plate attached to the sole of a boot, esp by loggers, to prevent slippingverb (transitive)3. to provide with calks4. to wound with a calk
Word origin
C17: from Latin calx heel

calk3

(kɔːk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(transitive) to transfer (a design) by tracing it with a blunt point from one sheet backed with loosely fixed colouring matter onto another placed underneath
Word origin
C17: from French calquer to trace; see calque

havoc

(ˈhævək )
Definitions
noun1. destruction; devastation; ruin2. informal confusion; chaos3. See cry havoc4. See play havocverb -ocs, -ocking or -ocked5. (transitive) archaic to lay waste
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French havot pillage, probably of Germanic origin

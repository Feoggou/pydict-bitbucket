addendum

(əˈdɛndəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -da (-də)1. something added; an addition2. a supplement or appendix to a book, magazine, etc3. the radial distance between the major and pitch cylinders of an external screw thread4. the radial distance between the pitch circle and tip of a gear tooth
Word origin
C18: from Latin, literally: a thing to be added, neuter gerundive of addere to add

Bruce1

(bruːs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  James. 1730–94, British explorer, who discovered the source of the Blue Nile (1770)2.  Lenny. 1925–66, US comedian, whose satirical sketches, esp of the sexual attitudes of his contemporaries, brought him prosecutions for obscenity, but are now regarded as full of insight as well as wit3.  Robert the Bruce. See Robert I4.  Stanley Melbourne, 1st Viscount Bruce of Melbourne. 1883–1967, Australian statesman; prime minister, in coalition with Sir Earle Page's Country Party, of Australia (1923–29)

fated

Powered by COBUILD
(feɪtɪd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [oft ADJ to-inf]If you say that a person is fated to do something, or that something is fated, you mean that it seems to have been decided by fate before it happens, and nothing can be done to avoid or change it. ⇒ He was fated not to score. ⇒ ...stories of desperation, fated love, treachery and murder.2. → See also ill-fated
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

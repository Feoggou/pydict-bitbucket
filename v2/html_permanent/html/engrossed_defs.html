engrossed

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪngroʊst ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are engrossed in something, it holds your attention completely. [+ in] ⇒ Tony didn't notice because he was too engrossed in his work.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

On several occasions she and Klaus came across Johannes and Bettina engrossed in conversation. Appiganesi, Lisa DREAMS OF INNOCENCEChristina sat staring unseeingly at the television screen in which her husband seemed so engrossed.

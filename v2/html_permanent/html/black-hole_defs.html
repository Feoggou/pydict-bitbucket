Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an object in space so dense that its escape velocity exceeds the speed of light2. any place regarded as resembling a black hole in that items or information entering it cannot be retrieved

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounBlack holes are areas in space, where gravity is so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape from them. Black holes are thought to be formed by collapsed stars.2. countable noun [usu sing]If you say that something, especially money, has gone into a black hole, you mean that it has disappeared and cannot be recovered.

rhetorician

(ˌrɛtəˈrɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a teacher of the art of rhetoric2. a stylish or eloquent writer or speaker3. a person whose speech is pompous or extravagant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

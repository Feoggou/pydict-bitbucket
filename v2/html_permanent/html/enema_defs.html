enema

(ˈɛnɪmə )
Definitions
noun plural -mas or -mata (-mətə) medicine1. the introduction of liquid into the rectum to evacuate the bowels, medicate, or nourish2. the liquid so introduced
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from New Latin, from Greek: injection, from enienai to send in, from hienai to send

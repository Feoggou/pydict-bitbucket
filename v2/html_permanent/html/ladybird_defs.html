ladybird

(ˈleɪdɪˌbɜːd )
Definitions
nounany of various small brightly coloured beetles of the family Coccinellidae, such as Adalia bipunctata (two-spotted ladybird), which has red elytra marked with black spots. Usual US and Canadian name: ladybug
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: named after Our Lady, the Virgin Mary

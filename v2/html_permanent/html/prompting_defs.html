prompting

(ˈprɒmptɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. encouragement or urging ⇒ New York needed little prompting from their coach Bill Parcells., ⇒ the promptings of your subconscious2. the act of refreshing the memory of3. the act of giving rise to by suggestion

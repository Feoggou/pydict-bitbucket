commerce

(ˈkɒmɜːs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the activity embracing all forms of the purchase and sale of goods and services2. social relations and exchange, esp of opinions, attitudes, etc
Word origin
C16: from Latin commercium trade, from commercārī, from mercārī to trade, from merx merchandise

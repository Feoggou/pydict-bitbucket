adjutant

(ˈædʒətənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an officer who acts as administrative assistant to a superior officer. Abbreviation: adjt, adj2. short for adjutant bird
Derived forms
adjutancy (ˈadjutancy) noun
Word origin
C17: from Latin adjūtāre to aid

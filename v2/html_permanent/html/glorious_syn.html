Synonyms of glorious

glorious

adjective

  1. splendid, beautiful, bright, brilliant, shining, superb, divine, gorgeous, dazzling, radiant, resplendent, effulgent, splendiferous (facetious)
    • a glorious Edwardian opera house
    OPPOSITES:  awful, dull, horrible, unpleasant, gloomy, dreary, unimpressive
  2. delightful, fine, wonderful, excellent, heavenly (informal), marvellous, splendid, gorgeous, pleasurable, splendiferous (facetious)
    • We opened the window and let in the glorious evening air.
  3. illustrious, famous, celebrated, distinguished, noted, grand, excellent, honoured, magnificent, noble, renowned, elevated, eminent, triumphant, majestic, famed, sublime
    • He had a glorious career spanning more than six decades.
    OPPOSITES:  ordinary, minor, unknown, trivial, unimportant

ungodly

(ʌnˈɡɒdlɪ )
Definitions
adjective -lier or -liest1. a. wicked; sinfulb. (as collective noun; preceded by the) ⇒ the ungodly2. informal unseemly; outrageous (esp in the phrase an ungodly hour)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ungodliness (unˈgodliness) nounungodlily (unˈgodlily) adverb

ungodly for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌngɒdli )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as ungodly, you mean that they are morally bad or are opposed to religion. ⇒ Such a view implies that our bodies and sexual nature are inherently ungodly.2. adjective [ADJ n]If you refer to a time as an ungodly hour, you are emphasizing that it is very early in the morning. [emphasis] ⇒ ...at the ungodly hour of 4.00am.3. adjective [ADJ n]If you refer to the amount or volume of something as ungodly, you mean that it is excessive or unreasonable. ⇒ ...a power struggle of ungodly proportions.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

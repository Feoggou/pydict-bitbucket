notional

(ˈnəʊʃənəl )
Definitions
adjective1. relating to, expressing, or consisting of notions or ideas2. not evident in reality; hypothetical or imaginary ⇒ a notional tax credit3. characteristic of a notion or concept, esp in being speculative or imaginary; abstract4. grammara. (of a word) having lexical meaningb. another word for semantic

attentions

(əˈtenʃənz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounacts of consideration, courtesy, or gallantry indicating affection or love ⇒ The meeting was held away from the attentions of the media., ⇒ Some men are flattered by the attentions of a young woman.
Example sentences containing 'attentions'
It was all becoming a little too matter-of-fact, her attentions drifting away from the center of his body towards the extremities. Mark Mills AMAGANSETTThe Dog was either less quick on the uptake or was less practiced in evading the attentions of the sendings of Abhorsen's House. Garth Nix LIRAEL: DAUGHTER OF THE CLAYRShe had been spared the attentions of her husband last night which must have been a great relief. Holt, Victoria THE ROAD TO PARADISE ISLAND

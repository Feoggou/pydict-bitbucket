nought

(nɔːt )
Definitions
noun also: naught, ought, aught1. the digit 0; zero: used esp in counting or numberingnoun, adjective, adverb2. a variant spelling of naught
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English nōwiht, from ne not, no + ōwiht something; see whit

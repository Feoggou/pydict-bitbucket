speak to

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
verb (intr, preposition)1. to address (a person)2. to reprimand ⇒ your father will speak to you later3. formal to give evidence of or comments on (a subject) ⇒ who will speak to this item?
Example sentences containing 'speak to'
Tarja debated stopping by the largest tent to speak to Mistress Miffany. Jennifer Fallon TREASON KEEPHe gathered himself for a few moments; the conflicting emotions needed to be reined in before he went to speak to his mother. Robert Wilson THE COMPANY OF STRANGERSThen he got up and said he'd speak to Franconelli, ask permission. Robert Wilson A DARKENING STAINI said that I'd been unable to wait any longer to speak to her.

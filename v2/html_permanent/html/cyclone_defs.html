cyclone

(ˈsaɪkləʊn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. another name for depression (sense 6)2. a violent tropical storm; hurricane
Derived forms
cyclonic (saɪˈklɒnɪk ), cyclonical (cyˈclonical) or cyclonal (ˈcyclonal) adjectivecyclonically (cyˈclonically) adverb
Word origin
C19: from Greek kuklōn a turning around, from kukloun to revolve, from kuklos wheel

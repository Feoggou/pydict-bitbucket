labouring

or US laboring (ˈleɪbərɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to working (esp in a job of a physical nature) ⇒ a labouring man, ⇒ the interests of the labouring man, ⇒ the labouring class(es)2. hardworking; drudging ⇒ labouring slaves

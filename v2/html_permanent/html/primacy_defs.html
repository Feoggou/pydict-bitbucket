primacy

(ˈpraɪməsɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cies1. the state of being first in rank, grade, etc2. Christianity the office, rank, or jurisdiction of a primate or senior bishop or (in the Roman Catholic Church) the pope

primacy

Powered by COBUILD
(praɪməsi )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounThe primacy of something is the fact that it is the most important or most powerful thing in a particular situation. [formal] ⇒ The political idea at the heart of this is the primacy of the individual. ⇒ ...the primacy of experience over analysis.

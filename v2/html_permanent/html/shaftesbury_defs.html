Shaftesbury

(ˈʃɑːftsbərɪ , -brɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  1st Earl of, title of Anthony Ashley Cooper. 1621–83, English statesman, a major figure in the Whig opposition to Charles II2.  7th Earl of, title of Anthony Ashley Cooper. 1801–85, English evangelical churchman and social reformer. He promoted measures to improve conditions in mines (1842), factories (1833; 1847; 1850), and schools

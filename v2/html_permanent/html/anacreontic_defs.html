Anacreontic

(əˌnækrɪˈɒntɪk ) sometimes not capital
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. in the manner of the Greek lyric poet Anacreon (?572–?488 bc), noted for his short songs celebrating love and wine2. (of verse) in praise of love or wine; amatory or convivialnoun3. an Anacreontic poem
Derived forms
Aˌnacreˈontically adverb

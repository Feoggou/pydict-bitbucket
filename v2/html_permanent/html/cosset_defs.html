cosset

(ˈkɒsɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -sets, -seting or -seted (transitive)1. to pamper; coddle; petnoun2. any pet animal, esp a lamb
Word origin
C16: of unknown origin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verb [usu passive]If someone is cosseted, everything possible is done for them and they are protected from anything unpleasant. [be V-ed] ⇒ Our kind of travel is definitely not suitable for people who expect to be cosseted.cosseted graded adjective ⇒ I don't want to be treated like a cosseted movie queen.

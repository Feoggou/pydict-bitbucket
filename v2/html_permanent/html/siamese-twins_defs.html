Siamese twins

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
plural nounnon-technical name for conjoined twins

Word Origin

C19: named after a famous pair of conjoined twins, Chang and Eng (1811–74), who were born in Siam

Siamese twins

Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
any pair of twins born with bodies joined together in some way

black market

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. any system in which goods or currencies are sold and bought illegally, esp in violation of controls or rationingb. (as modifier) ⇒ black market lamb2. the place where such a system operatesverb black-market3. to sell (goods) on the black market

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf something is bought or sold on the black market, it is bought or sold illegally. ⇒ There is a plentiful supply of arms on the black market. ⇒ ...black market food prices.

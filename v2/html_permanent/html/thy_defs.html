thy

(ðaɪ )
Definitions
determiner(usually preceding a consonant) archaic belonging to or associated in some way with you (thou) ⇒ thy goodness and mercy. Compare thine
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C12: variant of thine

thy

Powered by COBUILD
(ðaɪ ) for learners of English
Definitions
possessive determiner & determinerThy is an old-fashioned, poetic, or religious word for 'your' when you are talking to one person. ⇒ Honour thy father and thy mother.

offertory

(ˈɒfətərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -tories1. the oblation of the bread and wine at the Eucharist2. the offerings of the worshippers at this service3. the prayers said or sung while the worshippers' offerings are being received
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Church Latin offertōrium place appointed for offerings, from Latin offerre to offer

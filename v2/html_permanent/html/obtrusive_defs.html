obtrusive

(əbˈtruːsɪv )
Definitions
adjective1. obtruding or tending to obtrude2. sticking out; protruding; noticeable
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

obtrusively (obˈtrusively) adverbobtrusiveness (obˈtrusiveness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that someone or something is obtrusive, you think they are noticeable in an unpleasant way. ⇒ 'You are rude and obtrusive, Mr Galbraith,' said Tommy. ⇒ These heaters are less obtrusive and are easy to store away in the summer.obtrusively graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Hawke got up and walked obtrusively out of the building.

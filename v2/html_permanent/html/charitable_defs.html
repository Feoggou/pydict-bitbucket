charitable

(ˈtʃærɪtəbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. generous in giving to the needy2. kind or lenient in one's attitude towards others3. concerned with or involving charity

(tʃærɪtəbəl )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]A charitable organization or activity helps and supports people who are ill, very poor, or who have a disability. ⇒ ...charitable work for cancer awareness.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]Someone who is charitable to people is kind or understanding towards them. [+ towards] ⇒ They were rather less than charitable towards the referee.charitably (tʃærɪtəbli ) graded adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Still, he reflected charitably, it was hardly her fault.

tripe

(traɪp )
Definitions
noun1. the stomach lining of an ox, cow, or other ruminant, prepared for cooking2. informal something silly; rubbish3. (plural) archaic, informal intestines; belly
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old French, of unknown origin

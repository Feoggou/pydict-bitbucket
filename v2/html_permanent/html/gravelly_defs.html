gravelly

(ˈɡrævəlɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. consisting of or abounding in gravel2. of or like gravel3. (esp of a voice) harsh and grating
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

gravelly

Powered by COBUILD
(grævəli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A gravelly voice is low and rather rough and harsh. ⇒ There's a triumphant note in his gravelly voice.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA gravelly area of land is covered in or full of small stones. ⇒ Water runs through the gravelly soil very quickly.

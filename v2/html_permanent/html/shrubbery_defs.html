shrubbery

(ˈʃrʌbərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -beries1. a place where a number of shrubs are planted2. shrubs collectively

(ʃrʌbəri )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA shrubbery is a part of a garden where a lot of shrubs are growing. [British]2. uncount nounYou can refer to a lot of shrubs or to shrubs in general as shrubbery.

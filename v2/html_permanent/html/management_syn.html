Synonyms of management

management

noun

  1. administration, control, rule, government, running, charge, care, operation, handling, direction, conduct, command, guidance, supervision, manipulation, governance, superintendence
    • the responsibility for its day-to-day management
  2. directors, board, executive(s), bosses (informal), administration, employers, directorate
    • The management is doing its best to control the situation.

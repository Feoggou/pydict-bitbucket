Waldenses

(wɒlˈdɛnsiːz )
Definitions
plural nounthe members of a small sect founded as a reform movement within the Roman Catholic Church by Peter Waldo, a merchant of Lyons in the late 12th century, which in the 16th century joined the Reformation movement. Also called: Vaudois

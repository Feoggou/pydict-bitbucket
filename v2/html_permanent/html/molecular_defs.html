molecular

(məʊˈlɛkjʊlə , mə-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to molecules ⇒ molecular hydrogen2. logic (of a sentence, formula, etc) capable of analysis into atomic formulae of the appropriate kind

Derived Forms

molecularity (məʊˌlɛkjʊˈlærɪtɪ ) nounmolecularly (moˈlecularly) adverb

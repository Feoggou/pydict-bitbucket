cannonball

(ˈkænənˌbɔːl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a projectile fired from a cannon: usually a solid round metal shot2. tennisa. a very fast low serveb. (as modifier) ⇒ a cannonball serve3. a jump into water by a person who has his or her arms tucked into the body to form a ballverb (intransitive)4. (often foll by along, etc) to rush along, like a cannonball5. to execute a cannonball jumpadjective6. very fast or powerful

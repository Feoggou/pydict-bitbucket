Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveCanadian means belonging or relating to Canada, or to its people or culture. ⇒ ...the Canadian government.2. countable nounA Canadian is a Canadian citizen, or a person of Canadian origin.

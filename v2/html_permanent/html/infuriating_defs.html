(ɪnfjʊərieɪtɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is infuriating annoys you very much. ⇒ I was in the middle of typing when Robert rang. It was infuriating! ⇒ Steve accelerated with infuriating slowness.infuriatingly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj, oft ADV with v] ⇒ This book is infuriatingly repetitious. ⇒ 'It seems obvious to me,' she said infuriatingly.

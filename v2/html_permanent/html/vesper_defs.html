vesper

(ˈvɛspə )
Definitions
noun1. an evening prayer, service, or hymn2. an archaic word for evening3. (modifier) of or relating to vespers
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin: evening, the evening star; compare Greek hesperos evening; see west

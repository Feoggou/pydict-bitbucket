amplifier

(ˈæmplɪˌfaɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an electronic device used to increase the strength of the signal fed into it2. such a device used for the amplification of audio frequency signals in a radio, etc3. photography an additional lens for altering the focal length of a camera lens4. a person or thing that amplifies

astrology

(əˈstrɒlədʒɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the study of the motions and relative positions of the planets, sun, and moon, interpreted in terms of human characteristics and activities2. the primitive study of celestial bodies, which formed the basis of astronomy
Derived forms
astrologer (asˈtrologer) or astrologist (asˈtrologist) nounastrological (ˌæstrəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ) adjectiveˌastrologically (ˌastroˈlogically) adverb
Word origin
C14: from Old French astrologie, from Latin astrologia, from Greek, from astrologos (originally: astronomer); see astro-, -logy

accounting

(əˈkaʊntɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna. the skill or practice of maintaining and auditing accounts and preparing reports on the assets, liabilities, etc, of a businessb. (as modifier) ⇒ an accounting period, ⇒ accounting entity

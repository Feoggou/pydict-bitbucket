bushel1

(ˈbʊʃəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (formerly) a Brit unit of dry or liquid measure equal to 8 Imperial gallons. 1 Imperial bushel is equivalent to 0.036 37 cubic metres2. a US unit of dry measure equal to 64 US pints. 1 US bushel is equivalent to 0.035 24 cubic metres3. a container with a capacity equal to either of these quantities4. US informal a large amount; great deal5. See hide one's light under a bushel
Word origin
C14: from Old French boissel, from boisse one sixth of a bushel, of Gaulish origin

New England

Definitions
noun1. the NE part of the US, consisting of the states of Maine, New Hampshire, Vermont, Massachusetts, Rhode Island, and Connecticut: settled originally chiefly by Puritans in the mid-17th century2. a region in SE Australia, in the northern tablelands of New South Wales

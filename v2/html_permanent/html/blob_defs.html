blob

(blɒb )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a soft mass or drop, as of some viscous liquid2. a spot, dab, or blotch of colour, ink, etc3. an indistinct or shapeless form or object4. a slang word for condomverbWord forms: blobs, blobbing or blobbed5. (transitive) to put blobs, as of ink or paint, on
Derived forms
blobby (ˈblobby) adjective
Word origin
C15: perhaps of imitative origin; compare bubble

(blɒb )
Word forms: blobs
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA blob of thick or sticky liquid is a small, often round, amount of it. [informal] ⇒ ...a blob of chocolate mousse. ⇒ It's only necessary to use just a very small blob.2. countable nounYou can use blob to refer to something that you cannot see very clearly, for example because it is in the distance. [informal] ⇒ You could just see vague blobs of faces. ⇒ ...a blob in the distance.

lave1

(leɪv ) in American English
Definitions
verb transitiveWord forms: laved or ˈlaving OLD-FASHIONED, Poetic1.  to wash or bathe2.  to flow along or against3.  to dip or pour with or as with a ladle verb intransitive4.  to wash or bathe

Word Origin

ME laven < OE & OFr: OE lafian (akin to MDu laven, OHG labon) < L lavare; OFr laver < L lavare < IE base *lou-, to wash > lather, lye

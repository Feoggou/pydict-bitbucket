academic year

(ˌækəˈdɛmɪk jɪə)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe period of the year during which students attend school or university ⇒ The academic year starts in September.
Example sentences containing 'academic year'
During the next academic year Louis sensed a change in her, a change he did not like. Robert Wilson THE COMPANY OF STRANGERS

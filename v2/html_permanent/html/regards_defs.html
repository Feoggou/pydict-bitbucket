regards

(rɪˈɡɑːdz)
Definitions
plural noungood wishes or greetings ⇒ My best regards to Mary., ⇒ Give my regards to Alice., ⇒ Give my regards to your family., ⇒ Louis sends his regards., ⇒ I send this to you with kindest regards.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Groningen

(ˈɡrəʊnɪŋən , Dutch ˈxroːnɪŋə)
Definitions
noun1. a province in the NE Netherlands: mainly agricultural. Capital: Groningen. Pop: 573 000 (2003 est). Area: 2336 sq km (902 sq miles)2. a city in the NE Netherlands, capital of Groningen province. Pop: 177 000 (2003 est)

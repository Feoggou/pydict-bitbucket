gunshot

(ˈɡʌnˌʃɒt )
Definitions
noun1. a. shot fired from a gunb. (as modifier) ⇒ gunshot wounds2. the range of a gun3. the shooting of a gun

gunshot

Powered by COBUILD
(gʌnʃɒt ) for learners of English
Word forms: gunshots
Definitions
1. uncount noun [usu N n]Gunshot is used to refer to bullets that are fired from a gun. ⇒ They had died of gunshot wounds. ⇒ ...avoiding the volleys of gunshot.2. countable nounA gunshot is the firing of a gun or the sound of a gun being fired. ⇒ They heard thousands of gunshots. ⇒ A balloon popped, sounding like a gunshot.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

undoing

(ʌnˈduːɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. ruin; downfall2. the cause of downfall ⇒ drink was his undoing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

undoing for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnduːɪŋ )
Definitions
singular noun [with poss]If something is someone's undoing, it is the cause of their failure. ⇒ His lack of experience may prove to be his undoing. ⇒ Issues of national defense have been the undoing of Democratic candidates.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

matted

(ˈmætɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. tangled into a thick mass ⇒ matted hair2. covered with or formed of matting

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone's hair as matted, you mean that it has become a thick untidy mass, often because it is wet or dirty. ⇒ She had matted hair and torn dusty clothes.

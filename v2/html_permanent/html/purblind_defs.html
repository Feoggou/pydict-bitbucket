purblind

(ˈpɜːˌblaɪnd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. partly or nearly blind2. lacking in insight or understanding; obtuse

Word Origin

C13: see pure, blind; compare parboil
Example sentences containing 'purblind'
There's something about parenthood that makes you purblind. Gee, Maggie LOST CHILDREN

huff

(hʌf )
Definitions
noun1. a passing mood of anger or pique (esp in the phrase in a huff)verb2. to make or become angry or resentful3. (intransitive) to blow or puff heavily4. Also: blow draughts to remove (an opponent's draught) from the board for failure to make a capture5. (transitive) obsolete to bully6. See huffing and puffing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

huffish (ˈhuffish) or huffy (ˈhuffy) adjectivehuffily (ˈhuffily) or huffishly (ˈhuffishly) adverbhuffiness (ˈhuffiness) or huffishness (ˈhuffishness) noun

Word Origin

C16: of imitative origin; compare puff

large-scale

Definitions
adjective1. wide-ranging or extensive2. (of maps and models) constructed or drawn to a big scale

large-scale

Powered by COBUILD

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]A large-scale action or event happens over a very wide area or involves a lot of people or things. ⇒ ...a large scale military operation.2. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]A large-scale map or diagram represents a small area of land or a building or machine on a scale that is large enough for small details to be shown. ⇒ ...a large-scale map of the county.

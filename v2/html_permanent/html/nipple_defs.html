nipple

(ˈnɪpəl )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: mamilla, papilla, teat the small conical projection in the centre of the areola of each breast, which in women contains the outlet of the milk ducts ▶ Related adjective: mamillary2. something resembling a nipple in shape or function3. Also called: grease nipple a small drilled bush, usually screwed into a bearing, through which grease is introduced4. US and Canadian an informal word for dummy (sense 11)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from earlier neble, nible, perhaps from neb, nib

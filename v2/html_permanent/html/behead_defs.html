behead

(bɪˈhɛd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(transitive) to remove the head from; decapitate
Word origin
Old English behēafdian, from be- + heafod head; related to Middle High German behoubeten

behead

(bɪhed )
Word forms: beheads , beheading , beheaded
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verb [usu passive]If someone is beheaded, their head is cut off, usually because they have been found guilty of a crime. [be V-ed] ⇒ Charles I was beheaded by the Cromwellians.

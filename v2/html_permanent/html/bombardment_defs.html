Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounA bombardment is a strong and continuous attack of gunfire or bombing. ⇒ The city has been flattened by heavy artillery bombardments. ⇒ The capital is still under constant bombardment by the rebel forces.2. variable nounA bombardment of ideas, demands, questions, or criticisms is an aggressive and exhausting stream of them. [+ of] ⇒ ...the constant bombardment of images urging that work was important.

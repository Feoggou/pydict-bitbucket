helium

(ˈhiːlɪəm )
Definitions
nouna very light nonflammable colourless odourless element that is an inert gas, occurring in certain natural gases: used in balloons and in cryogenic research. Symbol: He; atomic no: 2; atomic wt: 4.002602; density: 0.1785 kg/m3; at normal pressures it is liquid down to absolute zero; melting pt: below –272.2°C; boiling pt: –268.90°C. See also alpha particle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: New Latin, from helio- + -ium; named from its having first been detected in the solar spectrum

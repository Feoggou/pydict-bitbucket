Memnon

(ˈmɛmnɒn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Greek mythology a king of Ethiopia, son of Eos: slain by Achilles in the Trojan War2. a colossal statue of Amenhotep III at Thebes in ancient Egypt, which emitted a sound thought by the Greeks to be the voice of Memnon

Derived Forms

Memnonian (mɛmˈnəʊnɪən ) adjective

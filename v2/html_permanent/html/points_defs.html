points

(pɔɪnt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. automobiles the two electrical contacts that make or break the current flow in the distributor of an internal-combustion engine ⇒ We'll need to clean the points.2. zoology the extremities, such as the tail, ears, or feet, of a domestic animal3. ballet the tips of the toes ⇒ She danced on points.4. railways, British (on a railway track) the levers and rails at a place where two tracks join or separate, that enable a train to move from one track to another ⇒ He could hear the rattle of the wheels across the points.

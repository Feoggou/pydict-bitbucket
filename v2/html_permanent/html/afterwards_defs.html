Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
language note:   The form afterward is also used, mainly in American English.adverbIf you do something or if something happens afterwards, you do it or it happens after a particular event or time that has already been mentioned. ⇒ Shortly afterwards, police arrested four suspects. ⇒ James was taken to hospital but died soon afterwards. ⇒ Not long afterward she received five calls in one day. → after

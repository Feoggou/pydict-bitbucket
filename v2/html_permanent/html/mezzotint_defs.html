mezzotint

(ˈmɛtsəʊˌtɪnt ) or obsolete mezzotinto (ˈmɛtsəʊˈtɪntəʊ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a method of engraving a copper plate by scraping and burnishing the roughened surface2. a print made from a plate so treatedverb3. (transitive) to engrave (a copper plate) in this fashion

Derived Forms

mezzoˌtinter (ˈmezzoˌtinter) noun

Word Origin

C18: from Italian mezzotinto half tint

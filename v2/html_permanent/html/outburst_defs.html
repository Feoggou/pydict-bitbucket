outburst

(ˈaʊtˌbɜːst )
Definitions
noun1. a sudden and violent expression of emotion2. an explosion or eruption

outburst for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(aʊtbɜːʳst )
Word forms: outbursts
Definitions
1. countable nounAn outburst of an emotion, especially anger, is a sudden strong expression of that emotion. ⇒ ...an outburst of anger. [+ of] ⇒ ...a spontaneous outburst of cheers and applause. [+ against] ⇒ There has been another angry outburst against the new local tax introduced today.2. countable nounAn outburst of violent activity is a sudden period of this activity. [+ of] ⇒ Five people were reported killed today in a fresh outburst of violence. [+ of] ⇒ ...this first great outburst of nationalist student protest.

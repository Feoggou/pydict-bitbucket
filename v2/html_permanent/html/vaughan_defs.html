Vaughan

(vɔːn )
Definitions
noun1.  Henry. 1622–95, Welsh mystic poet, best known for his Silex Scintillans (1650; 1655)2.  Dame Janet (Maria). 1899–1993, British physician and university official: helped set up Britain's first National Blood Transfusion Service (1939): after World War II, became Britain's expert on the effects of radiation on humans; Principal of Somerville College, Oxford (1945–67)3.  Sarah (Lois). 1924–90, US jazz vocalist and pianist, noted esp for her skill in vocal improvisation

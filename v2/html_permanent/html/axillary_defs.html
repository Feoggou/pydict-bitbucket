axillary

(ækˈsɪlərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or near the armpit2. botany growing in or related to the axil ⇒ an axillary budnoun plural -laries3. Also called: axillar (ækˈsɪlə , ˈæksɪlə ) (usually plural) one of the feathers growing from the axilla of a bird's wing
Example sentences containing 'axillary'
The mantid had not cut deep enough to reach the axillary artery. Gregg Andrew Hurwitz MINUTES TO BURN

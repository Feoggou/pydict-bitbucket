continuo

(kənˈtɪnjʊˌəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -os1. musica. a shortened form of basso continuo. See thorough bassb. (as modifier) ⇒ a continuo accompaniment2. the thorough-bass part as played on a keyboard instrument, often supported by a cello, bassoon, etc
Word origin
Italian, literally: continuous

artist

(ˈɑːtɪst )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who practises or is skilled in an art, esp painting, drawing, or sculpture2. a person who displays in his work qualities required in art, such as sensibility and imagination3. a person whose profession requires artistic expertise, esp a designer ⇒ a commercial artist4. a person skilled in some task or occupation ⇒ an artist at bricklaying5. obsolete an artisan6. slang a person devoted to or proficient in something ⇒ a booze artist, ⇒ a con artist

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounAn artist is someone who draws or paints pictures or creates sculptures as a job or a hobby. ⇒ ...the studio of a great artist. ⇒ Each poster is signed by the artist. ⇒ I'm not a good artist.2. countable nounAn artist is a person who creates novels, poems, films, or other things which can be considered as works of art. ⇒ His books are enormously easy to read, yet he is a serious artist. ⇒ Engel is quoted as saying that balanced people do not become artists.3. countable nounAn artist is a performer such as a musician, actor, or dancer. ⇒ ...a popular artist who has sold millions of records.4. countable nounIf you say that someone is an artist at a particular activity, you mean they are very skilled at it. ⇒ He is an exceptional footballer–an artist.

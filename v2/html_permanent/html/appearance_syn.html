appearance

noun

  1. look, face, form, air, figure, image, looks, bearing, aspect, manner, expression, demeanour, mien (literary) • He had the appearance of a college student.
  2. arrival, appearing, presence, turning up, introduction, showing up (informal), emergence, advent, coming • The sudden appearance of a few bags of rice could start a riot.
  3. occurrence, development, emergence, manifestation • the appearance of a number of cases of cholera
  4. impression, air, front, image, illusion, guise, façade, pretence, veneer, semblance, outward show • They gave the appearance of being on both sides.

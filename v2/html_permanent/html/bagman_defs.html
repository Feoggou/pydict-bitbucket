bagman

(ˈbæɡmən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -men1. British informal a travelling salesman2. slang, mainly US a person who collects or distributes money for racketeers3. informal, mainly Canadian a person who solicits money or subscriptions for a political party4. Australian history a tramp or swagman, esp one on horseback5. Also called: bagswinger Australian slang someone who takes money for a bookmaker

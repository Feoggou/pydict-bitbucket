loculus

(ˈlɑkjuləs ; ˈlɑkjələs ) in American English
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural ˈlocuˌli (ˈlɑkjuˌlaɪ ; ˈlɑkjəˌlaɪ ) any small cavity or chamber in plant or animal tissue: also ˈlocˌule (ˈlɑkˌjul )

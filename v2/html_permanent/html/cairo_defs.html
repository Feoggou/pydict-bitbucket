Cairo

(ˈkaɪrəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe capital of Egypt, on the Nile: the largest city in Africa and in the Middle East; industrial centre; site of the university and mosque of Al Azhar (founded in 972). Pop: 11 146 000 (2005 est). Arabic name: El Qahira (ɛl ˈkahiːrɔ)
Derived forms
Cairene (ˈCairene) noun, adjective

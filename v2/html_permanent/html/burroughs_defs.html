Burroughs

(ˈbʌrəʊz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Edgar Rice. 1875–1950, US novelist, author of the Tarzan stories2.  William S(eward). 1914–97, US novelist, noted for his experimental works exploring themes of drug addiction, violence, and homosexuality. His novels include Junkie (1953), The Naked Lunch (1959), and Interzone (1989)

agar

(ˈeɪɡə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna complex gelatinous carbohydrate obtained from seaweeds, esp those of the genus Gelidium, used as a culture medium for bacteria, a laxative, in food such as ice cream as a thickening agent (E406), etc. Also called: agar-agar
Word origin
C19: Malay

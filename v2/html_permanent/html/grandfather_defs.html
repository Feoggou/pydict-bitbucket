grandfather

(ˈɡrænˌfɑːðə , ˈɡrænd-)
Definitions
noun1. the father of one's father or mother2. (often plural) a male ancestor3. (often capital) a familiar term of address for an old man4. dialect a caterpillar or woodlouse
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

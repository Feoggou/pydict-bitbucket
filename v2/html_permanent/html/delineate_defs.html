delineate

(dɪˈlɪnɪˌeɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to trace the shape or outline of; sketch2. to represent pictorially, as by making a chart or diagram; depict3. to portray in words, esp with detail and precision; describe
Derived forms
delineable (deˈlineable) adjectivedeˌlineation (deˌlineˈation) noundelineative (deˈlineative) adjective
Word origin
C16: from Latin dēlīneāre to sketch out, from līnea line1

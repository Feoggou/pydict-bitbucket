toque

(təʊk )
Definitions
noun1. a woman's small round brimless hat, popular esp in Edwardian times2. a hat with a small brim and a pouched crown, popular in the 16th century3. Canadian same as tuque (sense 2)4. a chef's tall white hat
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from French, from Old Spanish toca headdress, probably from Basque tauka hat

residue

(ˈrɛzɪˌdjuː )
Definitions
noun1. matter remaining after something has been removed2. law what is left of an estate after the discharge of debts and distribution of specific gifts
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French residu, from Latin residuus remaining over, from residēre to stay behind, reside

residue

Powered by COBUILD
(rezɪdjuː , US -duː ) for learners of English
Word forms: residues
Definitions
countable nounA residue of something is a small amount that remains after most of it has gone. ⇒ Always using the same shampoo means that a residue can build up on the hair.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'residue'
Anger and longing follow, a gritty residue of frustration and sorrow.

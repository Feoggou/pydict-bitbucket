glandular

(ˈɡlændjʊlə ) or glandulous (ˈɡlændjʊləs )
Definitions
adjectiveof, relating to, containing, functioning as, or affecting a gland ⇒ glandular tissue
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

glandularly (ˈglandularly) or glandulously (ˈglandulously) adverb

Word Origin

C18: from Latin glandula, literally: a little acorn; see glanders

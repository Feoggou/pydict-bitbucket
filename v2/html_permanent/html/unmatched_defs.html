unmatched

(ʌnˈmætʃt )
Definitions
adjective1. not equalled ⇒ a landscape of unmatched beauty2. (of socks, clothes, etc) not matching ⇒ unmatched dresses and stockings
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

unmatched for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnmætʃt )
Definitions
adjectiveIf you describe something as unmatched, you are emphasizing that it is better or greater than all other things of the same kind. [emphasis] ⇒ ...a landscape of unmatched beauty. [+ for] ⇒ Brian's old-fashioned cuisine was unmatched for flavour.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

ingenious

(ɪnˈdʒiːnjəs , -nɪəs)
Definitions
adjective1. possessing or done with ingenuity; skilful or clever2. obsolete having great intelligence; displaying genius
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ingeniously (inˈgeniously) adverbingeniousness (inˈgeniousness) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin ingeniōsus, from ingenium natural ability; see engine

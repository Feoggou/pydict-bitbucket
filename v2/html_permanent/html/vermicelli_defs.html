vermicelli

(ˌvɜːmɪˈsɛlɪ , Italian vermiˈtʃɛlli)
Definitions
noun1. very fine strands of pasta, used in soups2. tiny chocolate strands used to coat cakes, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Italian: little worms, from verme a worm, from Latin vermis

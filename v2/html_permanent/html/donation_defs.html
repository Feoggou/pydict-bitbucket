donation

(dəʊˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of giving, esp to a charity2. a contribution
Word origin
C15: from Latin dōnātiō a presenting, from dōnāre to give, from dōnum gift

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA donation is something which someone gives to a charity or other organization. [+ to/of/from] ⇒ Employees make regular donations to charity. ⇒ Charities appealed for donations of food and clothing for victims of the hurricane.2. → see also donate

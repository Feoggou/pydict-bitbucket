petrified

(ˈpɛtrɪfaɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. stunned or dazed with horror, fear, etc ⇒ I was absolutely petrified., ⇒ I've always been petrified of the dark., ⇒ Most people seem to be petrified of snakes.2. geology (of organic material) converted into a fossilized form by impregnation with dissolved minerals so that the original appearance is preserved ⇒ Look at the grain in this petrified wood.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ that]If you are petrified, you are extremely frightened, perhaps so frightened that you cannot think or move. [+ of] ⇒ I've always been petrified of being alone. [+ of] ⇒ Most people seem to be petrified of snakes.2. adjective [ADJ n]A petrified plant or animal has died and has gradually turned into stone. ⇒ ...a block of petrified wood.

capitation

(ˌkæpɪˈteɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a tax levied on the basis of a fixed amount per head2. See capitation grant3. the process of assessing or numbering by counting heads
Derived forms
capitative (ˈcapitative) adjective
Word origin
C17: from Late Latin capitātiō, from Latin caput head

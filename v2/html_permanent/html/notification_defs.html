notification

(ˌnəʊtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of notifying2. a formal announcement3. something that notifies; a notice4. (usually pl)a. an icon or sound on a smartphone, tablet, or other electronic device indicating the user has one or more messages, activity alerts, updates to apps, etcb. the message, activity alert, update, etc, indicated by the icon or sound

arbour

(ˈɑːbə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a leafy glade or bower shaded by trees, vines, shrubs, etc, esp when trained about a trellis2. obsolete an orchard, garden, or lawn
Word origin
C14 erber, from Old French herbier, from Latin herba grass

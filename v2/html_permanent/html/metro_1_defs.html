metro

(ˈmɛtrəʊ ) or métro (French metro)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -rosan underground, or largely underground, railway system in certain cities, esp in Europe, such as that in Paris

Word Origin

C20: from French, short for chemin de fer métropolitain metropolitan railway

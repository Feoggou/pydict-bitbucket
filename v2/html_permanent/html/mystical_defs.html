mystical

(ˈmɪstɪkəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. relating to or characteristic of mysticism2. Christianity having a divine or sacred significance that surpasses natural human apprehension3. having occult or metaphysical significance, nature, or force4. a less common word for mysterious

Derived Forms

mystically (ˈmystically) adverbmysticalness (ˈmysticalness) noun

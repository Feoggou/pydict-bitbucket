appendage

(əˈpɛndɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an ancillary or secondary part attached to a main part; adjunct2. zoology any organ that projects from the trunk of animals such as arthropods3. botany any subsidiary part of a plant, such as a branch or leaf

countable nounAn appendage is something that is joined to or connected with something larger or more important.

petit

(ˈpɛtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(prenominal) mainly law of little or lesser importance; small ⇒ petit jury

Word Origin

C14: from Old French: little, of obscure origin

Petit - French

( pəti)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Roland (rɔlɑ̃). 1924–2011, French ballet dancer and choreographer. His innovative ballets include Carmen (1949), Kraanerg (1969), and The Blue Angel (1985); he also choreographed films, such as Anything Goes (1956) and Black Tights (1960)

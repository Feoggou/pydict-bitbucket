woebegone

(ˈwəʊbɪˌɡɒn )
Definitions
adjective1. sorrowful or sad in appearance2. archaic afflicted with woe
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from a phrase such as me is wo begon woe has beset me

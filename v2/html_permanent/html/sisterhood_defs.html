sisterhood

(ˈsɪstəˌhʊd )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the state of being related as a sister or sisters2. a religious body or society of sisters, esp a community, order, or congregation of nuns3. the bond between women who support the Women's Movement

clod

(klɒd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a lump of earth or clay2. earth, esp when heavy or in hard lumps3. Also called: clodpole, clod poll, clodpate a dull or stupid person4. a cut of beef taken from the shoulder
Derived forms
cloddy (ˈcloddy) adjectivecloddish (ˈcloddish) adjectivecloddishly (ˈcloddishly) adverbcloddishness (ˈcloddishness) noun
Word origin
Old English clod- (occurring in compound words) lump; related to cloud

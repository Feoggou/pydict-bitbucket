unfilled

(ʌnˈfɪld )
Definitions
adjective1. (of a container, receptacle, etc) not having become or been made full ⇒ unfilled stomachs2. (of a job, role, etc) not occupied3. (of a cake, doughnut, etc) with no filling ⇒ unfilled choux pastry will freeze

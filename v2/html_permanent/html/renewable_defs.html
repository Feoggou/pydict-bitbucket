(rɪnjuːəbəl , US -nuː- ) for learners of English
Word forms: renewables
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Renewable resources are natural ones such as wind, water, and sunlight which are always available. ⇒ ...renewable energy sources.2. countable noun [usu pl]Renewables are natural energy sources such as wind, water, and sunlight which are always available.3. adjectiveIf a contract or agreement is renewable, it can be extended when it reaches the end of a fixed period of time. ⇒ A formal contract is signed which is renewable annually.

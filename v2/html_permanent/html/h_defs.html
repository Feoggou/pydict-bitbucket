h

or H (eɪtʃ )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural h's, H's or Hs1. the eighth letter and sixth consonant of the modern English alphabet2. a speech sound represented by this letter, in English usually a voiceless glottal fricative, as in hat3. a. something shaped like an Hb. (in combination) ⇒ an H-beam

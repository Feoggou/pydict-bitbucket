expenditure

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪkspendɪtʃəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: expenditures
Definitions
1. variable nounExpenditure is the spending of money on something, or the money that is spent on something. [formal] ⇒ Policies of tax reduction must lead to reduced public expenditure. ⇒ They should cut their expenditure on defence. ⇒ An expenditure for clothing will qualify as a trade or business expense.2. uncount nounExpenditure of something such as time or energy is the using of that thing for a particular purpose. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ The financial rewards justified the expenditure of effort.

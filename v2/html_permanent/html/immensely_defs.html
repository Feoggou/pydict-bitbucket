(ɪmensli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [usu ADV adj, oft ADV after v]You use immensely to emphasize the degree or extent of a quality, feeling, or process. [emphasis] ⇒ Wind surfing can be strenuous but immensely exciting. ⇒ I enjoyed this movie immensely.

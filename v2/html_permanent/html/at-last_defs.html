Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf you say that something has happened at last or at long last you mean it has happened after you have been hoping for it for a long time. ⇒ I'm so glad that we've found you at last! ⇒ Here, at long last, was the moment he had waited for. ⇒ At last the train arrived in the station. ⇒ 'All right', he said at last. 'You may go.' →  last

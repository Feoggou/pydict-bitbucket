Rothschild

(ˈrɒθtʃaɪld , ˈrɒθs-)
Definitions
noun1.  Lionel Nathan, Baron de Rothschild. 1809–79, British banker and first Jewish member of Parliament2.  his grandfather Meyer Amschel (ˈmaiər ˈamʃəl). 1743–1812, German financier and founder of the Rothschild banking firm3.  his son, Nathan Meyer, Baron de Rothschild. 1777–1836, British banker, born in Germany

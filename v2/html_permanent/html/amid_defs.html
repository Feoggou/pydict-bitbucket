Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
language note:   The form amidst is also used, but is more literary.1. prepositionIf something happens amid noises or events of some kind, it happens while the other things are happening. amidst ⇒ A senior leader cancelled a trip to Britain yesterday amid growing signs of a possible political crisis. ⇒ Children were changing classrooms amid laughter and shouts.2. prepositionIf something is amid other things, it is surrounded by them. [literary] ⇒ ...a tiny bungalow amid clusters of trees.

overtaking

(ˌəʊvəˈteɪkɪŋ)
Definitions
nounan act or the process of moving past another vehicle or person travelling in the same direction ⇒ Overtaking on a bend is dangerous., ⇒ One in ten failed to recognise signs for no overtaking or the national speed limit.

Plantagenet

(plænˈtædʒɪnɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna line of English kings, ruling from the ascent of Henry II (1154) to the death of Richard III (1485)

Word Origin

C12: from Old French, literally: sprig of broom, with reference to the crest of the Angevin kings, from Latin planta sprig + genista broom

tightening

(ˈtaɪtənɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. (of a part of the body) tensing or stiffening ⇒ Headaches caused by tension can result in tightening of the muscles in the neck., ⇒ I felt a slight tightening of the throat.2. the act of making a rule, policy, or system stricter or more efficient ⇒ There was a tightening of state control over press and broadcasting.

deploy

(dɪˈplɔɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb mainly military1. to adopt or cause to adopt a battle formation, esp from a narrow front formation2. (transitive) to redistribute (forces) to or within a given area
Derived forms
deployment (deˈployment) noun
Word origin
C18: from French déployer, from Latin displicāre to unfold; see display

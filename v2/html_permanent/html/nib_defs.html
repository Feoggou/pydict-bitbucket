nib

(nɪb )
Definitions
noun1. the writing point of a pen, esp an insertable tapered metal part with a split tip2. a point, tip, or beak3. (plural) crushed cocoa beansverbWord forms: nibs, nibbing or nibbed (transitive)4. to provide with a nib5. to prepare or sharpen the nib of
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

niblike (ˈnibˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C16 (in the sense: beak): origin obscure; compare Northern German nibbe tip. See neb, nibble

Charybdis

(kəˈrɪbdɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna ship-devouring monster in classical mythology, identified with a whirlpool off the north coast of Sicily, lying opposite Scylla on the Italian coast. Compare Scylla
Derived forms
Charybdian (Chaˈrybdian) adjective

midshipman

(ˈmɪdˌʃɪpmən ) or midshipmate (ˈmɪdˌʃɪpmeɪt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -men or -mates1. a probationary rank held by young naval officers under training, or an officer holding such a rank2. any of several American toadfishes of the genus Porichthys, having small light-producing organs on the undersurface of their bodies
Example sentences containing 'midshipman'
When a young midshipman asked for her papers, Tatiana showed him her Red Cross travel document from the Soviet Union. Paullina Simons THE BRONZE HORSEMAN

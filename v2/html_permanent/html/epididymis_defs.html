epididymis

(ˌɛpɪˈdɪdɪmɪs )
Definitions
noun plural -didymides (-dɪˈdɪmɪˌdiːz)anatomy a convoluted tube situated along the posterior margin of each testis, in which spermatozoa are stored and conveyed to the vas deferens
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

epididymal (ˌepiˈdidymal) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Greek epididumis, from epi- + didumos twin, testicle; see didymous

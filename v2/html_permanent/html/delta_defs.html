delta

(ˈdɛltə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the fourth letter in the Greek alphabet (Δ, δ), a consonant transliterated as d2. an object resembling a capital delta in shape3. (capital when part of name) the flat alluvial area at the mouth of some rivers where the mainstream splits up into several distributaries ⇒ the Mississippi Delta4. mathematics a finite increment in a variable
Derived forms
deltaic (dɛlˈteɪɪk ) or deltic (ˈdeltic) adjective
Word origin
C16: via Latin from Greek, of Semitic origin; compare Hebrew dāleth

ice-skating

(ˈaɪsˌskeɪtɪŋ)
Definitions
nounthe sport or pastime of gliding swiftly over ice on ice skates ⇒ I used to go ice-skating every Tuesday., ⇒ I love watching ice-skating on television.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
verb [only cont]If you go ice-skating, you move about on ice wearing ice-skates. This activity is also a sport. [V] ⇒ They took me ice-skating on a frozen lake.Ice-skating is also a noun. ⇒ I love watching ice-skating on television. ⇒ ...British ice-skating champion Joanne Conway.

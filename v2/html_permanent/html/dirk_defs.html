dirk

(dɜːk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a dagger esp as formerly worn by Scottish Highlandersverb (transitive)2. to stab with a dirk
Word origin
C16: from Scottish durk, perhaps from German Dolch dagger

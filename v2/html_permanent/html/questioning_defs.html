questioning

(ˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. proceeding from or characterized by a feeling of doubt or uncertainty2. enthusiastic or eager for philosophical or other investigations; intellectually stimulated ⇒ an alert and questioning mind
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

questioningly (ˈquestioningly) adverb

questioning

(kwestʃənɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]If someone has a questioning expression on their face, they look as if they want to know the answer to a question. [written] ⇒ He raised a questioning eyebrow.questioningly adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Brenda looked questioningly at Daniel.2. → See also question

luge

(luːʒ )
Definitions
noun1. a racing toboggan on which riders lie on their backs, descending feet firstverb2. (intransitive) to ride on a luge
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: from French

luge

Powered by COBUILD
(luːʒ ) for learners of English
Word forms: luges
Definitions
countable nounA luge is an object that is designed to be used for racing downhill over snow or ice. Riders lie on their backs and travel with their feet pointing towards the front of the luge.

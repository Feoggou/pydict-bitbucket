goblet

(ˈɡɒblɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a vessel for drinking, usually of glass or metal, with a base and stem but without handles2. archaic a large drinking cup shaped like a bowl
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French gobelet a little cup, from gobel ultimately of Celtic origin

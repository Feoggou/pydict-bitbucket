expectant

(ɪkˈspɛktənt )
Definitions
adjective1. expecting, anticipating, or hopeful ⇒ an expectant glance2. having expectations, esp of possession of something or prosperity3. pregnant ⇒ an expectant mothernoun4. a person who expects something5. obsolete a candidate for office, esp for ecclesiastical preferment
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

expectantly (exˈpectantly) adverb

(ɪkspektənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is expectant, they are excited because they think something interesting is about to happen. ⇒ An expectant crowd gathered. ⇒ She turned to me with an expectant look on her face.expectantly adverb [ADV after v] ⇒ The others waited, looking at him expectantly.2. adjective [ADJ n]An expectant mother or father is someone whose baby is going to be born soon.

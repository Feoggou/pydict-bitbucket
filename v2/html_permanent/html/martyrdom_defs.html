martyrdom

(ˈmɑːtədəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the sufferings or death of a martyr2. great suffering or torment

martyrdom

Powered by COBUILD
(mɑːʳtəʳdəm )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounIf someone suffers martyrdom, they are killed or made to suffer greatly because of their religious or political beliefs. [+ of] ⇒ ...the martyrdom of Bishop Feliciano. ⇒ He suffered martyrdom by stoning.2. uncount nounIf you describe someone's behaviour as martyrdom, you are critical of them because they are exaggerating their suffering in order to gain sympathy or praise. [disapproval] ⇒ She sat picking at her small plate of rice salad with an air of martyrdom.

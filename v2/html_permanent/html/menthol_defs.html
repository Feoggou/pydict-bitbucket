menthol

(ˈmɛnθɒl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan optically active organic compound found in peppermint oil and used as an antiseptic, in inhalants, and as an analgesic. Formula: C10H20O

Word Origin

C19: from German, from Latin mentha mint1

(menθɒl , US -θɔːl )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounMenthol is a substance that smells a bit like peppermint and is used to flavour things such as cigarettes and toothpaste. It is also used in some medicines, especially for curing colds.

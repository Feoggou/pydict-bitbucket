Whitby

(ˈwɪtbɪ )
Definitions
nouna fishing port and resort in NE England, in E North Yorkshire at the mouth of the River Esk: an important ecclesiastical centre in Anglo-Saxon times; site of an abbey founded in 656. Pop: 13 594 (2001). See also Synod of Whitby

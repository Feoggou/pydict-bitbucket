notify

(ˈnəʊtɪˌfaɪ )
Definitions
verb -fies, -fying or -fied (transitive)1. to inform; tell2. mainly British to draw attention to; make known; announce
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

notifier (ˈnotiˌfier) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Old French notifier, from Latin notificāre to make known, from nōtus known + facere to make

notify for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(noʊtɪfaɪ )
Word forms: notifies , notifying , notified
Definitions
verbIf you notify someone of something, you officially inform them about it. [formal] [V n + of/about] ⇒ The skipper notified the coastguard of the tragedy. [be V-ed that] ⇒ Earlier this year they were notified that their homes were to be cleared away. [V n] ⇒ She confirmed that she would notify the police and the hospital. [Also V n that]

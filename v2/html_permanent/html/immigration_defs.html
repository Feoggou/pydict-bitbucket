immigration

(ˌɪmɪˈɡreɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the movement of non-native people into a country in order to settle there2. the part of a port, airport, etc where government employees examine the passports, visas, etc of foreign nationals entering the country
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions

hark

(hɑːk )
Definitions
verb(intr; usually imperative) to listen; pay attention
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English heorcnian to hearken; related to Old Frisian herkia, Old High German hōrechen; see hear

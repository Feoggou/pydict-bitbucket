spatial

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to space2. existing or happening in space

Derived Forms

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Spatial is used to describe things relating to areas. ⇒ ...the spatial distribution of black employment and population in South Africa. ⇒ ...spatial constraints. ⇒ ...the claustrophobic spatial dividers found in business offices.spatially adverb ⇒ The growth of home ownership has been both socially and spatially uneven. ⇒ ...jobs that are more spatially dispersed throughout the country.2. adjective [ADJ n]Your spatial ability is your ability to see and understand the relationships between shapes, spaces, and areas. ⇒ His manual dexterity and fine spatial skills were wasted on routine tasks. ⇒ ...spatial awareness.

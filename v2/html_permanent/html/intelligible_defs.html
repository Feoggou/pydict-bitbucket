intelligible

(ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. able to be understood; comprehensible2. philosophya. capable of being apprehended by the mind or intellect aloneb. (in metaphysical systems such as those of Plato or Kant) denoting that metaphysical realm which is accessible to the intellect as opposed to the world of mere phenomena accessible to the senses
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

intelligibility (inˌtelligiˈbility) or intelligibleness (inˈtelligibleness) nounintelligibly (inˈtelligibly) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Latin intellegibilis; see intellect

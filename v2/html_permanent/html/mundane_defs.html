mundane

(ˈmʌndeɪn , mʌnˈdeɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. everyday, ordinary, or banal2. relating to the world or worldly matters

Derived Forms

mundanely (ˈmundanely) adverbmundanity (munˈdanity) or mundaneness (ˈmundaneness) noun

Word Origin

C15: from French mondain, via Late Latin, from Latin mundus world

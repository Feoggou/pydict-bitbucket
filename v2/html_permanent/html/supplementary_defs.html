supplementary

(ˌsʌplɪˈmɛntərɪ , -trɪ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. forming or acting as a supplement. Also (chiefly US): supplemental (ˌsʌplɪˈmɛntəl )noun plural -ries2. a person or thing that is a supplement

Derived Forms

ˌsupplementarily (ˌsuppleˈmentarily) or rare ˌsupplementally (ˌsuppleˈmentally) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Supplementary things are added to something in order to improve it. ⇒ ...the question of whether or not we need to take supplementary vitamins. ⇒ Provide them with additional background or with supplementary information.

corresponding

(ˌkɒrɪˈspɒndɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveparallel; equivalent ⇒ March and April sales this year were up 8 per cent on the corresponding period in 1992., ⇒ the corresponding figures for last year, ⇒ the inexorable rise in Britain's fortunes and the corresponding decline of France as an international power
Derived forms
ˌcorrespondingly (ˌcorreˈspondingly) adverb

cutlet

(ˈkʌtlɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a piece of meat taken esp from the best end of neck of lamb, pork, etc2. a flat croquette of minced chicken, lobster, etc
Word origin
C18: from Old French costelette, literally: a little rib, from coste rib, from Latin costa

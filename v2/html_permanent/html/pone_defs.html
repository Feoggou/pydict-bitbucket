pone1

(pəʊn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Southern US1. Also called: pone bread, cornpone bread made of maize2. a loaf or cake of this

Word Origin

C17: from Algonquian; compare Delaware apán baked

pone2

(pəʊn , ˈpəʊnɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouncards the player to the right of the dealer, or the nondealer in two-handed games

Word Origin

C19: from Latin: put!, that is, play, from ponere to put

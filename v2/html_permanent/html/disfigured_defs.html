disfigured

(dɪsˈfɪɡəd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivespoiled in appearance; scarred ⇒ She tried not to look at the scarred disfigured face., ⇒ Two dogs attacked him, leaving him horribly disfigured., ⇒ He was afraid the accident might leave him disfigured for life.

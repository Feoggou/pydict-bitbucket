Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounA calculation is something that you think about and work out mathematically. Calculation is the process of working something out mathematically. ⇒ Leonard made a rapid calculation: he'd never make it in time. [+ of] ⇒ ...the calculation of their assets.2. variable nounA calculation is something that you think carefully about and arrive at a conclusion on after having considered all the relevant factors. ⇒ For the President, the calculations are equally difficult. If the peacekeeping operation goes wrong, he risks appearing weak.3. uncount nounIf you describe someone's behaviour as calculation, you are criticizing them because they seem to think only of themselves and not of other people. [disapproval] ⇒ ...cold, unspeakably cruel calculation.

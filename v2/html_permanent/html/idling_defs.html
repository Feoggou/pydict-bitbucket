idling

(ˈaɪdlɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective(of an engine) running at low speed with the transmission disengaged ⇒ An idling engine costs you 3p a minute., ⇒ explosive acceleration from idling speed, ⇒ He drove over the marked spot twice at idling speed.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

bareheaded

(beəʳhedɪd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu v-link ADJ, ADJ after v]Someone who is bareheaded is not wearing a hat or any other covering on their head. ⇒ He was bareheaded in the rain. ⇒ I rode bareheaded. ⇒ One of the police officers was bareheaded, though in uniform.

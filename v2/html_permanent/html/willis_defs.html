Willis

(ˈwɪlɪs )
Definitions
noun1.  Norman (David). 1933–2014, British trade union leader; general secretary of the Trades Union Congress (1984–93)2.  Ted. Baron Willis of Chislehurst. 1918–92, British author. His works include the play Hot Summer Night (1959) and the novel Death May Surprise Us (1974)

waged

(weɪdʒd)
Definitions
adjective1. (of workers) receiving money regularly for doing a job ⇒ Many women have joined the waged workforce.2. (of work) that one is paid to do ⇒ They want secure, waged employment.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
1. Waged is the past tense and past participle of wage.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Waged workers receive money regularly for doing a job. Waged work is work that you are paid to do. ⇒ ...the influx of women into the waged workforce. ⇒ They want secure, waged employment.

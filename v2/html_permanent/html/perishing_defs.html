perishing

(ˈpɛrɪʃɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. informal (of weather, etc) extremely cold2. slang (intensifier qualifying something undesirable) ⇒ it's a perishing nuisance!

Derived Forms

perishingly (ˈperishingly) adverb

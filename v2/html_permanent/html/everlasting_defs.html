everlasting

(ˌɛvəˈlɑːstɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. never coming to an end; eternal2. lasting for an indefinitely long period3. lasting so long or occurring so often as to become tedious; incessant ⇒ I cannot bear her everlasting complaintsnoun4. endless duration; eternity5. Also called: everlasting flower another name for immortelle. See also cat's-foot
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Powered by COBUILD
(evəʳlɑːstɪŋ , -læst-) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveSomething that is everlasting never comes to an end. ⇒ ...a message of peace and everlasting life.2. adjectiveIf you describe something as everlasting, you mean that it seems never to change or end. [literary] endless ⇒ I have loved you with an everlasting love! ⇒ Sometimes the work can feel unrewarding and everlasting.

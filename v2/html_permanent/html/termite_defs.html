termite

(ˈtɜːmaɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany whitish ant-like social insect of the order Isoptera, of warm and tropical regions. Some species feed on wood, causing damage to furniture, buildings, trees, etc. Also called: white ant

Derived Forms

termitic (tɜːˈmɪtɪk ) adjective

Word Origin

C18: from New Latin termitēs white ants, pl of termes, from Latin: a woodworm; related to Greek tetrainein to bore through

inbred

(ˈɪnˈbrɛd )
Definitions
adjective1. produced as a result of inbreeding2. deeply ingrained; innate ⇒ inbred good manners

inbred

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnbred ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveInbred means the same as inborn. ⇒ She had that inbred politeness, it was a part of her. ⇒ ...behaviour patterns that are inbred.2. adjective [usu v-link ADJ]People who are inbred have ancestors who are all closely related to each other. ⇒ The whole population is so inbred that no genetic differences remain. ⇒ He came from an old inbred family.

Pickering

(ˈpɪkərɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Edward Charles. 1846–1919, US astronomer, who invented the meridian photometer2.  his brother, William Henry. 1858–1938, US astronomer, who discovered Phoebe, the ninth satellite of Saturn, and predicted (1919) the existence and position of Pluto

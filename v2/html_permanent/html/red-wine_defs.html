red wine

(rɛd waɪn)
Definitions
nounwine having a predominantly red colour derived from the skin pigment in the red or purple grapes used in making it ⇒ a glass of red wine, ⇒ a delicious South African red wine
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

red wine

Powered by COBUILD
(rɛd waɪn) in Hospitality
Word forms: (regular plural) red wines
Definitions
noun (count) (noncount)(Hospitality (hotel): Food and drink, alcoholic drinks) Red wine is wine that is dark red in color. ⇒ Red wine goes well with meat and white wine goes well with fish. ⇒ Some customers prefer red wine to white wine. ⇒ White wine should be served cool, but red wine should not be chilled.

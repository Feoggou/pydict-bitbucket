pram1

(præm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounBritish a cot-like four-wheeled carriage for a baby. US and Canadian term: baby carriage

Word Origin

C19: shortened and altered from perambulator

pram2

(prɑːm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounnautical a light tender with a flat bottom and a bow formed from the ends of the side and bottom planks meeting in a small raised transom

Word Origin

C16: from Middle Dutch prame; related to Old Frisian prām

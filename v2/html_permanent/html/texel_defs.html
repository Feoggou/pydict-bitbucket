Texel

(ˈtɛksəl )
Definitions
nouna breed of sheep originating from the Netherlands having a heavy white fleece: kept for the production of lean lambs
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: named after Texel, one of the West Frisian Islands off the Netherlands

bailiwick

(ˈbeɪlɪwɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. law the area over which a bailiff has jurisdiction2. a person's special field of interest, authority, or skill
Word origin
C15: from baili(e) + wick2
Example sentences containing 'bailiwick'
I had the impression that artificial intelligence was sort of your bailiwick. S.M. Stirling T2©: RISING STORM

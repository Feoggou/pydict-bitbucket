obtain

(əbˈteɪn )
Definitions
verb1. (transitive) to gain possession of; acquire; get2. (intransitive) to be customary, valid, or accepted ⇒ a new law obtains in this case3. (transitive) archaic to arrive at4. (intransitive) archaic to win a victory; succeed
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

obtainable (obˈtainable) adjectiveobtainability (obˌtainaˈbility) nounobtainer (obˈtainer) nounobtainment (obˈtainment) noun

Word Origin

C15: via Old French from Latin obtinēre to take hold of, from ob- (intensive) + tenēre to hold

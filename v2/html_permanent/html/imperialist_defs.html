Word forms: imperialists
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]Imperialist means relating to or based on imperialism. ⇒ The developed nations have all benefited from their imperialist exploitation.An imperialist is someone who has imperialist views. ⇒ He claims that imperialists are trying to re-establish colonial rule in the country.

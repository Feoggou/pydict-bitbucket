faulty

(ˈfɔːltɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: faultier or faultiest1. defective or imperfect2. archaic culpable

faulty

Powered by COBUILD
(fɔːlti ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA faulty piece of equipment has something wrong with it and is not working properly. ⇒ The money will be used to repair faulty equipment.2. adjectiveIf you describe someone's argument or reasoning as faulty, you mean that it is wrong or contains mistakes, usually because they have not been thinking in a logical way. ⇒ Their interpretation was faulty–they had misinterpreted things.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

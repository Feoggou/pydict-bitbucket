felicitous

(fɪˈlɪsɪtəs )
Definitions
adjective1. well-chosen; apt2. possessing an agreeable style3. producing or marked by happiness
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

felicitously (feˈlicitously) adverbfelicitousness (feˈlicitousness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a remark or idea as felicitous, you approve of it because it seems particularly suitable in the circumstances. [formal, approval] ⇒ Her prose style is not always felicitous; she tends to repetition.

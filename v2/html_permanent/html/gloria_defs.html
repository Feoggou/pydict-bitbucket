gloria

(ˈɡlɔːrɪə )
Definitions
noun1. a silk, wool, cotton, or nylon fabric used esp for umbrellas2. a halo or nimbus, esp as represented in art
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin: glory

Gloria

(ˈɡlɔːrɪə , -ˌɑː)
Definitions
noun1. any of several doxologies beginning with the word Gloria, esp the Greater and the Lesser Doxologies2. a musical setting of one of these
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

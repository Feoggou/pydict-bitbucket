runway

(ˈrʌnˌweɪ )
Definitions
noun1. a hard level roadway or other surface from which aircraft take off and on which they land2. an enclosure for domestic animals; run3. forestry, US and Canadian a chute for sliding logs down4. a narrow ramp extending from the stage into the audience in a theatre, nightclub, etc, esp as used by models in a fashion show

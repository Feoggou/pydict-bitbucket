Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmpətəns ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounImpotence is a lack of power to influence people or events. ⇒ ...a sense of impotence in the face of deplorable events.2. uncount nounImpotence is a man's sexual problem in which his penis fails to get hard or stay hard. ⇒ Impotence affects 10 million men in the U.S. alone.

chivalrous

(ˈʃɪvəlrəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. gallant; courteous2. involving chivalry
Derived forms
chivalrously (ˈchivalrously) adverbchivalrousness (ˈchivalrousness) noun
Word origin

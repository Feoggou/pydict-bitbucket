flummery

(ˈflʌmərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -meries1. informal meaningless flattery; nonsense2. mainly British a cold pudding of oatmeal, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Welsh llymru

values

(ˈvæljuːz)
Definitions
plural nounthe moral principles and beliefs or accepted standards of a person or social group ⇒ young Muslims who feel little sympathy for the values of their adopted country, ⇒ The countries of South Asia also share many common values., ⇒ the need for a system of moral values, ⇒ The Health Secretary called for a return to traditional family values.

shirt

(ʃɜːt )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a garment worn on the upper part of the body, esp by men, usually of light material and typically having a collar and sleeves and buttoning up the front2. short for nightshirt, undershirt3. See keep your shirt on4. See put one's shirt on5. See lose one's shirt on

Word Origin

Old English scyrte; related to Old English sceort short, Old Norse skyrta skirt, Middle High German schurz apron

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA shirt is a piece of clothing that you wear on the upper part of your body. Shirts have a collar, sleeves, and buttons down the front.2. → see also dress shirt, stuffed shirt, sweatshirt, T-shirt

fortuitous

(fɔːˈtjuːɪtəs )
Definitions
adjectivehappening by chance, esp by a lucky chance; unplanned; accidental
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fortuitously (forˈtuitously) adverbfortuitousness (forˈtuitousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin fortuitus happening by chance, from forte by chance, from fors chance, luck

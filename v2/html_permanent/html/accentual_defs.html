accentual

(ækˈsɛntʃʊəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or having accents; rhythmic2. prosody of or relating to verse based on the number of stresses in a line rather than on the number of syllables. Compare quantitative (sense 3)
Derived forms
acˈcentually adverb

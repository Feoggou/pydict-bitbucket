farcical

(ˈfɑːsɪkəl )
Definitions
adjective1. ludicrous; absurd2. of or relating to farce
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

farcicality (ˌfarciˈcality) or farcicalness (ˈfarcicalness) nounfarcically (ˈfarcically) adverb

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a situation or event as farcical, you mean that it is so silly or extreme that you are unable to take it seriously. [disapproval] ⇒ ...a farcical nine months' jail sentence imposed yesterday on a killer.

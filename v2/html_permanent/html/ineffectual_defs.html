ineffectual

(ˌɪnɪˈfɛktʃʊəl )
Definitions
adjective1. having no effect or an inadequate effect2. lacking in power or forcefulness; impotent ⇒ an ineffectual ruler
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ineffectuality (ˌinefˌfectuˈality) or ineffectualness (ˌinefˈfectualness) nounineffectually (ˌinefˈfectually) adverb

chert

(tʃɜːt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna microcrystalline form of silica usually occurring as bands or layers of pebbles in sedimentary rock. Formula: SiO2. Varieties include flint, lyddite (Lydian stone). Also called: hornstone
Derived forms
cherty (ˈcherty) adjective
Word origin
C17: of obscure origin

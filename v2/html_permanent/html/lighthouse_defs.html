lighthouse

(ˈlaɪtˌhaʊs )
Definitions
nouna fixed structure in the form of a tower equipped with a light visible to mariners for warning them of obstructions, for marking harbour entrances, etc

(laɪthaʊs ) for learners of English
Word forms: lighthouses
Definitions
countable nounA lighthouse is a tower containing a powerful flashing lamp that is built on the coast or on a small island. Lighthouses are used to guide ships or to warn them of danger.

sonic

(ˈsɒnɪk )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, involving, or producing sound2. having a speed about equal to that of sound in air: 331 metres per second (741 miles per hour) at 0°C

Word Origin

C20: from Latin sonus sound

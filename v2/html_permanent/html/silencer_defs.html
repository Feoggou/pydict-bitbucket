silencer

(ˈsaɪlənsə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any device designed to reduce noise, esp the tubular device containing baffle plates in the exhaust system of a motor vehicle. US and Canadian name: muffler2. a tubular device fitted to the muzzle of a firearm to deaden the report3. a person or thing that silences

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA silencer is a device that is fitted onto a gun to make it very quiet when it is fired. ⇒ ...a pistol that was equipped with a silencer.2. countable nounA silencer is a device on a car exhaust that makes it quieter. [British]regional note:   in AM, use muffler

powdered

(ˈpaʊdəd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. in the form of a powder ⇒ powdered gelatine, ⇒ powdered egg, ⇒ powdered coffee, ⇒ I found some powdered battery acid in the engine-room., ⇒ Spirulina is available in capsules or in powdered form.2. covered with powder ⇒ the old woman's powdered face

powdered

Powered by COBUILD
(paʊdəʳd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]A powdered substance is one which is in the form of a powder although it can come in a different form. ⇒ There are only two tins of powdered milk left. ⇒ ...powdered gelatine.

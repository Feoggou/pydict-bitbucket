placitum

(ˈplæsɪtəm)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. law a plea made in court on behalf of a person or group2. law, Australian and US a legal decision made by a judge or court3. legal history obsolete a court or a legal proceeding in a court or assembly during the Middle Ages

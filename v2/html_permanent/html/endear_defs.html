Word forms: endears , endearing , endeared
Definitions
verbIf something endears you to someone or if you endear yourself to them, you become popular with them and well liked by them. [V n + to] ⇒ Their taste for gambling has endeared them to Las Vegas casino owners. [V pron-refl + to] ⇒ He has endeared himself to the American public.

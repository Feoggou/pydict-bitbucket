boil over

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (adverb)1. to overflow or cause to overflow while boiling2. (intransitive) to burst out in anger or excitement ⇒ she boiled over at the mention of his name

boil over

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. phrasal verbWhen a liquid that is being heated boils over, it rises and flows over the edge of the container. [V P] ⇒ Heat the liquid in a large, wide container rather than a high narrow one, or it can boil over.2. phrasal verbWhen someone's feelings boil over, they lose their temper or become violent. [V P] ⇒ Sometimes frustration and anger can boil over into direct and violent action. →  boil

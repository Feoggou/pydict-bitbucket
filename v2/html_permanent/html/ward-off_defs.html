Definitions
phrasal verbTo ward off a danger or illness means to prevent it from affecting you or harming you. [V P n] ⇒ She may have put up a fight to try to ward off her assailant. [V P n] ⇒ Mass burials are now under way in an effort to ward off an outbreak of cholera. [Also V n P] →  ward

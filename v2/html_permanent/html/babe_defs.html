babe

(beɪb )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a baby2. informal a naive, gullible, or unsuspecting person (often in the phrase a babe in arms)3. informal a young woman or man perceived as being sexually attractive

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounSome people use babe as an affectionate way of addressing someone they love. [US, informal, feelings] ⇒ I'm sorry, babe. I didn't mean it.2. countable nounSome men refer to an attractive young woman as a babe. This use could cause offence. [informal]3. countable nounA babe is the same as a baby. [old-fashioned] ⇒ ...newborn babes.

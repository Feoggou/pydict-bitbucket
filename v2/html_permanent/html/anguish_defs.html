anguish

(ˈæŋɡwɪʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. extreme pain or misery; mental or physical torture; agonyverb2. to afflict or be afflicted with anguish
Word origin
C13: from Old French angoisse a strangling, from Latin angustia narrowness, from angustus narrow

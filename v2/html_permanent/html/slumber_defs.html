slumber

(ˈslʌmbə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (intransitive) to sleep, esp peacefully2. (intransitive) to be quiescent or dormant3. (transitive; foll by away) to spend (time) sleepingnoun4. (sometimes plural) sleep5. a dormant or quiescent state

Derived Forms

slumberer (ˈslumberer) nounslumberless (ˈslumberless) adjective

Word Origin

Old English slūma sleep (n); related to Middle High German slummeren, Dutch sluimeren

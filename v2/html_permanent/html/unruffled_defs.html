unruffled

(ʌnˈrʌfəld )
Definitions
adjective1. unmoved; calm2. still ⇒ the unruffled seas

unruffled for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnrʌfəld )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as unruffled, you mean that they are calm and do not seem to be affected by surprising or frightening events. ⇒ Anne had remained unruffled, very cool and controlled.

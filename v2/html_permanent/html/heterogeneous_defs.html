heterogeneous

(ˌhɛtərəʊˈdʒiːnɪəs )
Definitions
adjective1. composed of unrelated or differing parts or elements2. not of the same kind or type3. chemistry of, composed of, or concerned with two or more different phases. Compare homogeneous
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

heterogeneity (ˌhɛtərəʊdʒɪˈniːɪtɪ ) or heterogeneousness (ˌheteroˈgeneousness) nounheterogeneously (ˌheteroˈgeneously) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Medieval Latin heterogeneus, from Greek heterogenēs, from hetero- + genos sort

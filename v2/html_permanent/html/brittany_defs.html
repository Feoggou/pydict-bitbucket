Brittany1

(ˈbrɪtənɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna region of NW France, the peninsula between the English Channel and the Bay of Biscay: settled by Celtic refugees from Wales and Cornwall during the Anglo-Saxon invasions; disputed between England and France until 1364. Breton name: Breiz (braɪz ). French name: Bretagne ▶ Related adjective: Breton

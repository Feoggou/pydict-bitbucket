imprinted

(ɪmˈprɪntɪd)
Definitions
adjective(of a mark, impression, etc) produced on a surface by pressure, printing, or stamping ⇒ the imprinted pattern, ⇒ Your face is imprinted on the memory of every cop in France., ⇒ the images imprinted on his brain

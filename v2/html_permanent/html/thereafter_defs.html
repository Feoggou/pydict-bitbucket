thereafter

Powered by COBUILD
(ðeərɑːftəʳ , -æftəʳ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverbThereafter means after the event or date mentioned. [formal] ⇒ Inflation will fall and thereafter so will interest rates. ⇒ It was the only time she had ever discouraged him from dangerous activities and she regretted it thereafter.

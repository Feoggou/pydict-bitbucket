(niːdləs )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is needless is completely unnecessary. ⇒ But his death was so needless. ⇒ 'I have never knowingly exposed any patient to needless risks,' he said.needlessly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v, oft ADV adj] ⇒ Half a million women die needlessly each year during childbirth. ⇒ He said something to me so mean, so needlessly cruel.2. →  needless to say

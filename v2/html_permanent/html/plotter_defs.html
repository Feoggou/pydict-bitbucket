plotter

(ˈplɒtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an instrument for plotting lines or angles on a chart2. a person who plots; conspirator

(plɒtəʳ )
Word forms: plotters
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu pl]A plotter is a person who secretly plans with others to do something that is illegal or wrong, usually against a person or government. ⇒ Coup plotters tried to seize power in Moscow.2. countable nounA plotter is a person or instrument that marks the position of something such as a ship on a map or chart.

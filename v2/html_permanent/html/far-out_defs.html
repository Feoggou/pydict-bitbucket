far out

or far-out when postpositive (fɑːr aʊt)
Definitions
adjective slang1. bizarre or avant-garde ⇒ Fantasies cannot harm you, no matter how bizarre or far out they are., ⇒ We need to do something really far out, like swimming across the Hudson River.2. excellent; wonderful ⇒ Everything Muscio has ever said is completely neat, cool, and far out.exclamation3. slang an expression of amazement of delight ⇒ 'Far out!' Moonchild said., ⇒ Far out, dudes!, ⇒ Far out, man, pass the joint.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

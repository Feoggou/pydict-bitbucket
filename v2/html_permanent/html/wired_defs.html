wired

(waɪəd )
Definitions
adjective informal1. edgy from stimulant intake2. excited, nervous, or tense3. using computers to send and receive information, esp via the internet
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

wired for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(waɪəʳd )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If someone is wired, they are tense, nervous, and unable to relax. [mainly US, informal] ⇒ Tonight he is manic, wired and uptight.2. adjectiveA computer, organization, or person that is wired has the equipment that is necessary to use the Internet. [informal] ⇒ Once more people are wired, the potential to change the mainstream media will be huge.3. adjective [usu ADJ n]Wired is used to describe material or clothing that has wires sewn into it in order to keep it stiff. ⇒ ...a length of wired ribbon.

incubation

(ˌɪnkjəˈbeɪʃən ; ˌ ɪŋkjəˈbeɪʃən ) in American English
Definitions
noun1.  an incubating or being incubated2.  the phase in the development of a disease between the infection and the first appearance of symptoms

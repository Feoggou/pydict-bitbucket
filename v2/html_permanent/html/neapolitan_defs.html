Neapolitan

(ˌnɪəˈpɒlɪtən )
Definitions
noun1. a native or inhabitant of Naplesadjective2. of or relating to Naples
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Latin Neāpolītānus, ultimately from Greek Neapolis new town

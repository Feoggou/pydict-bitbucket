statue

(ˈstætjuː )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna wooden, stone, metal, plaster, or other kind of sculpture of a human or animal figure, usually life-size or larger

Word Origin

C14: via Old French from Latin statua, from statuere to set up; compare statute

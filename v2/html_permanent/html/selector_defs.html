selector

(sɪˈlɛktə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that selects2. a device used in automatic telephone switching that connects one circuit with any one of a number of other circuits3. British a person who chooses the members of a sports team4. Australian the holder of a tract of land acquired by free-selection

fall back

Definitions
verb (intr, adverb)1. to recede or retreat2. (foll by on or upon) to have recourse (to)noun fallback3. a retreat4. a reserve, esp money, that can be called upon in need5. a. anything to which one can have recourse as a second choiceb. (as modifier) ⇒ a fallback position

fall back

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. phrasal verbIf you fall back, you move backwards a short distance away from someone or something. [V P] ⇒ He fell back in embarrassment when he saw that Ross had no hair at all. [V P + from] ⇒ The congregation fell back from them slightly as they entered.2. phrasal verbIf an army falls back during a battle or war, it withdraws. [V P] ⇒ The Prussian garrison at Charleroi was falling back. →  fall

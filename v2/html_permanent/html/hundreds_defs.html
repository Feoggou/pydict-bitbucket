hundreds

(ˈhʌndrədz)
Definitions
plural noun1. a large but unspecified number, amount, or quantity ⇒ Pilgrims gather there by the hundreds., ⇒ There will be hundreds of people there., ⇒ Hundreds of tree species face extinction., ⇒ Today you can buy hundreds of flavours of ice-cream., ⇒ He handed me hundreds of forms.2. See the hundreds3. See the hundreds4. See the hundreds5. the 100 years of a specified century ⇒ In the sixteen hundredspronoun6. a large but unspecified number of people or things ⇒ Hundreds died in the fighting.

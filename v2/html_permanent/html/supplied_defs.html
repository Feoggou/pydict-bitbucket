Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ with n]If you say that a person or place is well supplied with particular things, you mean that they have a large number of them. ⇒ France is abundantly supplied with excellent family-run hotels.2. → see also supply

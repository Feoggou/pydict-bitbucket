(naɪsli )
Definitions
1. graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]If something is happening or working nicely, it is happening or working in a satisfactory way or in the way that you want it to. ⇒ She has a bit of private money, so they manage quite nicely. ⇒ The crowds had been soaked and were now nicely drying out.2. → See also nice3. →  do nicely4. →  do nicely

arbitration

(ˌɑːbɪˈtreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. law the hearing and determination of a dispute, esp an industrial dispute, by an impartial referee selected or agreed upon by the parties concerned2. international law the procedure laid down for the settlement of international disputes

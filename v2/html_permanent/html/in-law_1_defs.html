Word Frequency

in-law

Definitions
noun1. a relative by marriageadjective2. (postpositive; in combination) related by marriage ⇒ a father-in-law
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: back formation from father-in-law, etc

The responsibility for any delay must, applying the usual spousal/ in-law guilt allocation rules, be lain firmly at my door.

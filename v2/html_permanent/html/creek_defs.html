creek

(kriːk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. mainly British a narrow inlet or bay, esp of the sea2. US, Canadian, Australian and New Zealand a small stream or tributary3. See up the creek
Word origin
C13: from Old Norse kriki nook; related to Middle Dutch krēke creek, inlet

Creek

(kriːk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Word forms: plural Creek or Creeks a member of a confederacy of Native American peoples formerly living in Georgia and Alabama, now chiefly in Oklahoma2. any of the languages of these peoples, belonging to the Muskhogean family

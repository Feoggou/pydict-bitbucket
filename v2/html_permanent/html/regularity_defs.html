regularity

Powered by COBUILD
(regjʊlærɪti ) for learners of English
Word forms: regularities
Definitions
1. countable nounA regularity is the fact that the same thing always happens in the same circumstances. [formal] ⇒ Children seek out regularities and rules in acquiring language.2. → See also regular

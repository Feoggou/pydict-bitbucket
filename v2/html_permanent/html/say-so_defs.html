Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun informal1. an arbitrary assertion2. an authoritative decision3. the authority to make a final decision

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
singular noun [oft with poss]If you do something on someone's say-so, they tell you to do it or they give you permission to do it. [informal] ⇒ Directors call the shots and nothing happens on set without their say-so.

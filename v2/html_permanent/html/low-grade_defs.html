low-grade

(ˈləʊɡreɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. inferior ⇒ low-grade coal, ⇒ low-grade paper, ⇒ low-grade hostels2. lesser in rank, intensity, or degree ⇒ a low-grade fever/tumour/lesions/inflammation/depression
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

With men it's some low-grade business rival or a dullard bank manager who once had the nerve to stop them making the millions they deserved.

(kɒnvɜːʳsli , kənvɜːʳsli )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverbYou say conversely to indicate that the situation you are about to describe is the opposite or reverse of the one you have just described. [formal] ⇒ In real life, nobody was all bad, nor, conversely, all good.

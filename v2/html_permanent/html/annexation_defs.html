annexation

(ˌænɪkˈseɪʃən , -ɛk-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of annexing, esp territory, or the condition of being annexed2. something annexed
Derived forms
ˌannexˈational adjectiveˌannexˈationism nounˌannexˈationist noun

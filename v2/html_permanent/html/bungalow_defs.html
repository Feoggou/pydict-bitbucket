bungalow

(ˈbʌŋɡəˌləʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a one-storey house, sometimes with an attic2. (in India) a one-storey house, usually surrounded by a veranda
Word origin
C17: from Hindi banglā (house) of the Bengal type

bramble

(ˈbræmbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of various prickly herbaceous plants or shrubs of the rosaceous genus Rubus, esp the blackberry. See also stone bramble2. Scottisha. a blackberryb. (as modifier) ⇒ bramble jelly3. any of several similar and related shrubsverb (intransitive)4. to gather blackberries
Derived forms
brambly (ˈbrambly) adjective
Word origin
Old English brǣmbel; related to Old Saxon brāmal, Old High German brāmo

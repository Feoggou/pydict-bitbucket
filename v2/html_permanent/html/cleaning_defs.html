cleaning

(ˈkliːnɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of removing dirt or something undesirable ⇒ Give it a good cleaning before you put it away.2. the job or activity of keeping a place clean ⇒ Her mother was always busy with the cleaning and dusting.
Example sentences containing 'cleaning'
She could hear Peggy, who was cleaning the silver, singing softly to herself in the kitchen. Fraser, Anthea THE GOSPEL MAKERSIt's bad enough trying to work out what to say to my cleaning lady. St. James, Ian FINAL RESORT

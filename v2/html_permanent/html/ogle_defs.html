ogle

(ˈəʊɡəl )
Definitions
verb1. to look at (someone) amorously or lustfully2. (transitive) to stare or gape atnoun3. a flirtatious or lewd look
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ogler (ˈogler) noun

Word Origin

C17: probably from Low German oegeln, from oegen to look at

Definitions
verbIf you say that one person is ogling another, you disapprove of them continually staring at that person in a way that indicates a strong sexual interest. [disapproval] [V n] ⇒ All she did was hang around ogling the men in the factory. [V + at] ⇒ Paula is not used to everyone ogling at her while she undresses backstage. [Also V]

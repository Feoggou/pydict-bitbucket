Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone's eyes as glazed, you mean that their expression is dull or dreamy, usually because they are tired or are having difficulty concentrating on something. glassy ⇒ Doctors with glazed eyes sat chain-smoking in front of a television set. ⇒ There was a glazed look in her eyes.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Glazed pottery is covered with a thin layer of a hard shiny substance.3. adjectiveA glazed window or door has glass in it.

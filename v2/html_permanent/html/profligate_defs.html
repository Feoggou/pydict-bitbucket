profligate

(ˈprɒflɪɡɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. shamelessly immoral or debauched2. wildly extravagant or wastefulnoun3. a profligate person

Derived Forms

profligacy (ˈprɒflɪɡəsɪ ) nounprofligately (ˈprofligately) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Latin prōflīgātus corrupt, from prōflīgāre to overthrow, from pro-1 + flīgere to beat

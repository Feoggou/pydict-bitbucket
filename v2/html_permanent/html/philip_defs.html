Philip

(ˈfɪlɪp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. New Testamenta.  one of the twelve apostles of Jesusb. Also: Philip the Evangelist one of the seven deacons appointed by the early Churchc. Also: Philip the Tetrarch one of the sons of Herod the Great, who was ruler of part of former Judaea (4 bc–34 ad) (Luke 3:1)2.  King, American Indian name Metacomet. died 1676, American Indian chief, the son of Massasoit. He waged King Philip's War against the colonists of New England (1675–76) and was killed in battle3. Prince. another name for the (Duke of) Edinburgh2

ordinarily for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ɔːʳdɪnərəli , US -nerɪli )
Definitions
adverb [oft ADV adj] [ADV before v]If you say what is ordinarily the case, you are saying what is normally the case. ⇒ The streets would ordinarily have been full of people. There was no one. ⇒ Similar arrangements apply to students who are ordinarily resident in Scotland. ⇒ ...places where the patient does not ordinarily go.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are resigned to an unpleasant situation or fact, you accept it without complaining because you realize that you cannot change it. reconciled [+ to] ⇒ He is resigned to the noise and mess. ⇒ Pauline was already resigned to losing her home.resignedly (rɪzaɪnɪdli ) adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ 'I know you don't believe me,' I said resignedly.

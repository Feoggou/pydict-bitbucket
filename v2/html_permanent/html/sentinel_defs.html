sentinel

(ˈsɛntɪnəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person, such as a sentry, assigned to keep guard2. computing a character used to indicate the beginning or end of a particular block of informationverb -nels, -nelling or -nelled (transitive)3. to guard as a sentinel4. to post as a sentinel5. to provide with a sentinel

Word Origin

C16: from Old French sentinelle, from Old Italian sentinella, from sentina watchfulness, from sentire to notice, from Latin

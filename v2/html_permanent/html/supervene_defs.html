supervene

(ˌsuːpəˈviːn )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
verb (intransitive)1. to follow closely; ensue2. to occur as an unexpected or extraneous development

Derived Forms

ˌsupervenience (ˌsuperˈvenience) or supervention (ˌsuːpəˈvɛnʃən ) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin supervenīre to come upon, from super- + venīre to come

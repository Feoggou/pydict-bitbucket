rumpled

(ˈrʌmpəld)
Definitions
adjectivewrinkled, crumpled, ruffled, or dishevelled ⇒ He had not shaved and his clothes were rumpled., ⇒ a sprawl of white, rumpled sheets, ⇒ He arrived, somewhat rumpled and unshaven., ⇒ He came downstairs, his hair still rumpled from sleep.

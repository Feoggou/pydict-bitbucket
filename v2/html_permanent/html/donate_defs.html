Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. verbIf you donate something to a charity or other organization, you give it to them. [V n + to] ⇒ He frequently donates large sums to charity. [V n] ⇒ Others donated secondhand clothes. [V n] ⇒ The silver trophy was donated by a Leicester businessman.donation (doʊneɪʃən ) uncount noun [+ of] ⇒ ...the donation of his collection to the art gallery.2. verbIf you donate your blood or a part of your body, you allow doctors to use it to help someone who is ill. [V n] ⇒ ...people who are willing to donate their organs for use after death. [V-ed] ⇒ All donated blood is screened for HIV.donation uncount noun ⇒ ...measures aimed at encouraging organ donation.

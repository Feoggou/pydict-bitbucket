attributable

Powered by COBUILD
(ətrɪbjʊtəbəl )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveIf something is attributable to an event, situation, or person, it is likely that it was caused by that event, situation or person. [+ to] ⇒ 10,000 deaths a year from chronic lung disease are attributable to smoking. ⇒ The mess was directly attributable to a corrupt and incompetent official.
Example sentences containing 'attributable'
`-- Especially where the injuries are attributable to the actions of the enemy. Townsend, Eileen IN LOVE AND WAR

summer house

(ˈsʌmə haʊs)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small building in a garden where you can sit in warm weather ⇒ There was a summer house at the end of the garden.2. a house that someone uses for summer holidays ⇒ He visited relatives at their summer house on the river.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA summer house is a small building in a garden. It contains seats, and people can sit there in the summer.2. countable nounSomeone's summer house is a house in the country or by the sea where they spend the summer. ⇒ He visited relatives at their summer house on the river.

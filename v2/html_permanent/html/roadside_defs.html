roadside

(ˈrəʊdˌsaɪd)
Definitions
nouna. the area at the edge of a road ⇒ I sat down by the roadside and cried., ⇒ Bob left the car at the roadside and ran for help.b. (as modifier) ⇒ roadside diners, ⇒ a roadside check, ⇒ Roadside cafes are now a big part of the catering industry.

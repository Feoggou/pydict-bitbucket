left-hand drive

(ˈlɛftˌhænd draɪv)
Definitions
noun automobiles1. a car with the steering wheel on the left ⇒ She has bought a left-hand drive.2. the left-hand position for the steering wheel ⇒ The car has left-hand drive.adjective3. automobiles having the steering wheel on the left ⇒ She has a left-hand drive mini that she got in France.▶ USAGE The abbreviation for left-hand drive is lhd

Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]A left-hand drive vehicle has the steering wheel on the left side, and is designed to be used in countries where people drive on the right-hand side of the road.

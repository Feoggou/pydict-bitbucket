sheikh

or sheik (ʃeɪk )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun (in Muslim countries)a. the head of an Arab tribe, village, etcb. a venerable old manc. a high priest or religious leader, esp a Sufi master

Word Origin

C16: from Arabic shaykh old man

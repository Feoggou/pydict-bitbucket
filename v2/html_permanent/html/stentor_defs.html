English definition: Stentor, stentor

Stentor

(ˈstɛntɔː )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nounGreek mythology a Greek herald with a powerful voice who died after he lost a shouting contest with Hermes, herald of the gods

stentor

(ˈstɛntɔː )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person with an unusually loud voice2. any trumpet-shaped protozoan of the genus Stentor, having a ciliated spiral feeding funnel at the wider end: phylum Ciliophora (ciliates)

Word Origin

C19: after Stentor

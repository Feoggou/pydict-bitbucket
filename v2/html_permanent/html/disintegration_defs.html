disintegration

(dɪsˌɪntəˈgreɪʃən )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  the act or process of disintegrating2.  Nuclear Physics any change in a nucleus of an atom, whether spontaneous or induced, in which one or more particles, photons, etc. are emitted

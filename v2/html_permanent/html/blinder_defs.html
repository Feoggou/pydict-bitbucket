Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu sing]If you say that someone such as a sports player or musician has played a blinder, you are emphasizing that they have played something very well. [British, informal, emphasis]2. plural nounBlinders are the same as blinkers. [US]

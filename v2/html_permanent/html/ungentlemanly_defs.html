ungentlemanly

(ʌnˈdʒɛntlmənlɪ)
Definitions
adjectivenot appropriate to or befitting a gentleman ⇒ Sniping or sharpshooting may have been ungentlemanly, but it had become a fact of war., ⇒ He was found guilty of ungentlemanly conduct after swearing at officials during Liverpool's draw at Crystal Palace in March.

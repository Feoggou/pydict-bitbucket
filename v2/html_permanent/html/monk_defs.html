monk

(mʌŋk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a male member of a religious community bound by vows of poverty, chastity, and obedience ▶ Related adjective: monastic2. (sometimes capital) a fancy pigeon having a bald pate and often large feathered feet

Word Origin

Old English munuc, from Late Latin monachus, from Late Greek: solitary (man), from Greek monos alone

hearsay

Powered by COBUILD
(hɪəʳseɪ ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounHearsay is information which you have been told but do not know to be true. ⇒ Much of what was reported to them was hearsay. ⇒ Rumour, myth and hearsay obscure the truth after months of bloodshed.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

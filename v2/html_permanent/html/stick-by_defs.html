stick by

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. phrasal verbIf you stick by someone, you continue to give them help or support. [V P n] ⇒ ...friends who stuck by me during the difficult times as Council Leader. [V P n] ⇒ She'd stuck by Bob through thick and thin.2. phrasal verbIf you stick by a promise, agreement, decision, or principle, you do what you said you would do, or do not change your mind. [V P n] ⇒ But I made my decision then and stuck by it. →  stick

evensong

(ˈiːvənˌsɒŋ )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: Evening Prayer, vespers Church of England the daily evening service of Bible readings and prayers prescribed in the Book of Common Prayer2. archaic another name for vespers3. an archaic or poetic word for evening

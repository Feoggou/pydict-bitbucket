torso

(ˈtɔːsəʊ )
Definitions
noun plural -sos or -si (-sɪ)1. the trunk of the human body2. a statue of a nude human trunk, esp without the head or limbs3. something regarded as incomplete or truncated
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Italian: stalk, stump, from Latin: thyrsus

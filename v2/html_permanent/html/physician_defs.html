physician

(fɪˈzɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person legally qualified to practise medicine, esp one specializing in areas of treatment other than surgery; doctor of medicine2. archaic any person who treats diseases; healer

Word Origin

C13: from Old French fisicien, from fisique physic

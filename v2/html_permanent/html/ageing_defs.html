ageing

or aging (ˈeɪdʒɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the process of growing old or developing the appearance and characteristics of old age2. the change of properties that occurs in some metals after heat treatment or cold workingadjective3. becoming or appearing older or elderly ⇒ an ageing car4. giving or creating the appearance of age or elderliness ⇒ that dress is really ageing on her

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Someone or something that is ageing is becoming older and less healthy or efficient. ⇒ John lives with his ageing mother. ⇒ Ageing aircraft need more frequent safety inspections.2. uncount nounAgeing is the process of becoming old or becoming worn out.

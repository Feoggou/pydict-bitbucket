insomuch

(ˌɪnsəʊˈmʌtʃ )
Definitions
adverb1. (foll by as or that) to such an extent or degree2. (foll by as) because of the fact (that); inasmuch (as)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'insomuch'
His reassurance worked insomuch as her evident vulnerability was instantly replaced by an air of nepotal admiration and trust. Anthony Masters CASCADES - THE DAY OF THE DEAD

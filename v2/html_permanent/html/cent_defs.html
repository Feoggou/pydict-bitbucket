Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA cent is a small unit of money worth one hundredth of some currencies, for example the dollar and the euro. ⇒ A cup of rice which cost thirty cents a few weeks ago is now being sold for up to one dollar. ⇒ We haven't got a cent.2. → see also per cent

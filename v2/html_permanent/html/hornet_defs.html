hornet

(ˈhɔːnɪt )
Definitions
noun1. any of various large social wasps of the family Vespidae, esp Vespa crabro of Europe, that can inflict a severe sting2. See hornet's nest
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English hyrnetu; related to Old Saxon hornut, Old High German hornuz

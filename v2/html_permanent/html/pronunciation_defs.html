pronunciation

(prəˌnʌnsɪˈeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act, instance, or manner of pronouncing sounds2. the supposedly correct manner of pronouncing sounds in a given language3. a phonetic transcription of a word

grove

(ɡrəʊv )
Definitions
noun1. a small wooded area or plantation2. a. a road lined with houses and often trees, esp in a suburban areab. (capital as part of a street name) ⇒ Ladbroke Grove
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English grāf; related to grǣfa thicket, greave, Norwegian greivla to intertwine

Definitions

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseYou say sure enough, especially when telling a story, to confirm that something was really true or was actually happening. ⇒ We found the English treacle pudding too good to resist. Sure enough, it was delicious. ⇒ I was in a shop when I saw a lady looking carefully at me. She'd recognised me, and sure enough, she came across. →  sure

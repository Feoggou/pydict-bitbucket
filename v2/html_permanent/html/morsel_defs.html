morsel

(ˈmɔːsəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small slice or mouthful of food2. a small piece; bit3. Irish informal a term of endearment for a child

Word Origin

C13: from Old French, from mors a bite, from Latin morsus, from mordēre to bite

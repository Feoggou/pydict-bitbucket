wardrobe

(ˈwɔːdrəʊb )
Definitions
noun1. a tall closet or cupboard, with a rail or hooks on which to hang clothes2. the total collection of articles of clothing belonging to one person3. the collection of costumes belonging to a theatre or theatrical company
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old Northern French warderobe, from warder to guard + robe robe

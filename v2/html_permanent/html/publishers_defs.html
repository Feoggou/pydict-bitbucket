publishers

(ˈpʌblɪʃəz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. a publishing company ⇒ The publishers planned to produce the journal on a weekly basis.2. the company which publishes a writer's books ⇒ I have a responsibility towards my publishers.

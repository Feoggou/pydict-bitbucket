pass by

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (intransitive) to go or move past2. (tr, adverb) to overlook or disregard ⇒ to pass by difficult problems

pass by

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrasal verbIf you pass by something, you go past it or near it on your way to another place. [V P n] ⇒ I see them pass by my house every day. [V P] ⇒ They were injured when a parked car exploded as their convoy passed by. →  pass

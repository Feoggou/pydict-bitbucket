grotto

(ˈɡrɒtəʊ )
Definitions
noun plural -toes or -tos1. a small cave, esp one with attractive features2. a construction in the form of a cave, esp as in landscaped gardens during the 18th century
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Old Italian grotta, from Late Latin crypta vault; see crypt

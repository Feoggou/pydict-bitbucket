tempered

(ˈtɛmpəd )
Definitions
adjective1. musica. (of a scale) having the frequency differences between notes adjusted in accordance with the system of equal temperament. See temperamentb. (of an interval) expanded or contracted from the state of being pure2. (in combination) having a temper or temperament as specified ⇒ ill-tempered

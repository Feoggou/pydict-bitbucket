Somali

(səʊˈmɑːlɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. plural -lis or -li a member of a tall dark-skinned people inhabiting Somalia2. the language of this people, belonging to the Cushitic subfamily of the Afro-Asiatic family of languagesadjective3. of, relating to, or characteristic of Somalia, the Somalis, or their language

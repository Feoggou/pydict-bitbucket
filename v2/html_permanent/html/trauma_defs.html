trauma

(ˈtrɔːmə )
Definitions
noun plural -mata (-mətə) or -mas1. psychology a powerful shock that may have long-lasting effects2. pathology any bodily injury or wound
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

traumatic (trɔːˈmætɪk ) adjectivetraumatically (trauˈmatically) adverb

Word Origin

C18: from Greek: a wound

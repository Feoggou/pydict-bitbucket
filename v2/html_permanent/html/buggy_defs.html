buggy1

(ˈbʌɡɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -gies1. a light horse-drawn carriage having either four wheels (esp in the US and Canada) or two wheels (esp in Britain and India)2. short for beach buggy3. short for Baby Buggy. See baby carriage4. a small motorized vehicle designed for a particular purpose ⇒ a golf buggy, ⇒ a moon buggy
Word origin
C18: of unknown origin

buggy2

Definitions

adjective -gier or -giest1. infested with bugs2. US slang insane3. informal (of a system or machine, esp a computer program) containing errors or faults
Derived forms

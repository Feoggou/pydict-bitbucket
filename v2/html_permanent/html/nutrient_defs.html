nutrient

(ˈnjuːtrɪənt )
Definitions
noun1. any of the mineral substances that are absorbed by the roots of plants for nourishment2. any substance that nourishes an organismadjective3. providing or contributing to nourishment ⇒ a nutrient solution
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin nūtrīre to nourish

Sumbawa

(suːmˈbɑːwə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna mountainous island in Indonesia, in the Lesser Sunda Islands, between Lombok and Flores islands. Pop: 1 540 000 (2000). Area: 14 750 sq km (5695 sq miles). Former spelling: Soembawa

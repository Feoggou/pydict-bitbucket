hyperbole

(haɪˈpɜːbəlɪ )
Definitions
nouna deliberate exaggeration used for effect ⇒ he embraced her a thousand times
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hyperbolism (hyˈperbolism) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Greek: from hyper- + bolē a throw, from ballein to throw

hyperbole

Powered by COBUILD
(haɪpɜːʳbəli ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIf someone uses hyperbole, they say or write things that make something sound much more impressive than it really is. [technical, formal] ⇒ ...the hyperbole that portrays him as one of the greatest visionaries in the world.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

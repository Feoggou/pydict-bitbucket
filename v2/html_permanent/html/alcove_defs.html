alcove

(ˈælkəʊv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a recess or niche in the wall of a room, as for a bed, books, etc2. any recessed usually vaulted area, as in a garden wall3. any covered or secluded spot, such as a summerhouse
Word origin
C17: from French alcôve, from Spanish alcoba, from Arabic al-qubbah the vault, arch

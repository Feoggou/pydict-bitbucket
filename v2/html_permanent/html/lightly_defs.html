lightly

(ˈlaɪtli ) in American English
Definitions
adverb1.  with little weight, pressure, or motion; gently2.  to a small degree or amount ⇒ to spend lightly3.  nimbly; deftly4.  cheerfully; merrily5. a.  with indifference or neglectb.  so as to slight6.  with little or no reason7.  with little or no punishment ⇒ to let someone off lightly8.  Rare with ease; readily

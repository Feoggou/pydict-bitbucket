unintelligent

(ˌʌnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənt )
Definitions
adjective1. lacking intelligence; stupid; foolish2. not endowed with a mind or intelligence
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unintelligence (ˌuninˈtelligence) noununintelligently (ˌuninˈtelligently) adverb

unintelligent for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnɪntelɪdʒənt )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a person as unintelligent, you mean that they are stupid, or do not show any sensible ideas or thoughts. ⇒ He believes him to be a weak and unintelligent man. ⇒ He certainly was not unintelligent.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

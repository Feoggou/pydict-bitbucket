breastplate

(ˈbrɛstˌpleɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a piece of armour covering the chest2. the strap of a harness covering a horse's breast3. Judaism an ornamental silver plate hung on the scrolls of the Torah4. Old Testament a square vestment ornamented with 12 precious stones, representing the 12 tribes of Israel, worn by the high priest when praying before the holy of holies5. zoology a nontechnical name for plastron

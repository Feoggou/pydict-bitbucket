beamy

(ˈbimi )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: ˈbeamier or ˈbeamiest1.  sending out beams of light; radiant; bright2.  beamlike; broad; massive3.  Nautical having a broad beam (sense 1) beam (sense 1h)

Babel1

(ˈbeɪbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Old Testamenta. Also called: Tower of Babel a tower presumptuously intended to reach from earth to heaven, the building of which was frustrated when Jehovah confused the language of the builders (Genesis 11:1–9)b. the city, probably Babylon, in which this tower was supposedly built2. (often not capital)a. a confusion of noises or voicesb. a scene of noise and confusion
Word origin
from Hebrew Bābhél, from Akkadian Bāb-ilu, literally: gate of God

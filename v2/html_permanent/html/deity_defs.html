deity

(ˈdeɪtɪ , ˈdiːɪ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. a god or goddess2. the state of being divine; godhead3. the rank, status, or position of a god4. the nature or character of God
Word origin
C14: from Old French, from Late Latin deitās, from Latin deus god

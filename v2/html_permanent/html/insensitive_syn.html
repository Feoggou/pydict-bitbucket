adjective

  1. unfeeling, indifferent, unconcerned, uncaring, tough, hardened, callous, crass, unresponsive, thick-skinned, obtuse, tactless, imperceptive, unsusceptible
    • My husband is very insensitive about my problem.
    OPPOSITES:  concerned, caring, sensitive, tender, sympathetic, susceptible, responsive, perceptive, tactful

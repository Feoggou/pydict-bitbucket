reversible

(rɪˈvɜːsəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. capable of being reversed ⇒ a reversible decision2. capable of returning to an original condition3. chemistry, physics capable of assuming or producing either of two possible states and changing from one to the other ⇒ a reversible reaction4. thermodynamics (of a change, process, etc) occurring through a number of intermediate states that are all in thermodynamic equilibrium5. (of a fabric or garment) woven, printed, or finished so that either side may be used as the outer sidenoun6. a reversible garment, esp a coat

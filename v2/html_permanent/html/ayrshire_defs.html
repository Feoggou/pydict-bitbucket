Ayrshire

(ˈɛəʃɪə , -ʃə)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a historical county of SW Scotland, formerly part of Strathclyde region (1975–96), now divided into the council areas of North Ayrshire, South Ayrshire, and East Ayrshire2. any one of a hardy breed of brown-and-white dairy cattle

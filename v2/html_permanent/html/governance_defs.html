governance

(ˈɡʌvənəns )
Definitions
noun1. government, control, or authority2. the action, manner, or system of governing

governance

Powered by COBUILD
(gʌvəʳnəns ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounThe governance of a country is the way in which it is governed. [formal] ⇒ They believe that a fundamental change in the governance of Britain is the key to all other necessary changes.2. uncount nounThe governance of a company or organization is the way in which it is managed. [formal] ⇒ ...a dramatic move away from the traditional view of governance in American education.

Synonyms of main

main

adjective

  1. chief, leading, major, prime, head, special, central, particular, necessary, essential, premier, primary, vital, critical, crucial, supreme, outstanding, principal, cardinal, paramount, foremost, predominant, pre-eminent, must-have
    • My main concern now is to protect the children.
    OPPOSITES:  least, minor, secondary, dependent, lesser, subordinate, trivial, insignificant, unimportant, auxiliary
  2. sheer, direct, entire, pure, mere, absolute, utter, utmost, downright, brute, undisguised
    • She had to be held back by main force.

noun

  1. force, might, power, effort, strength, potency, puissance

plural noun

  1. pipeline, channel, pipe, conduit, duct
    • the water supply from the mains
  2. cable, line, electricity supply, mains supply
    • amplifiers which plug into the mains
  • See in the main

tapping1

(ˈtæpɪŋ)
Definitions
nounthe act or noise of touching something gently, esp so as to make a soft knocking sounds ⇒ He heard a gentle tapping on his door., ⇒ The only movement was the rhythmical tapping of his finger on the arm of the chair., ⇒ I could hear some quick tapping of keys, then, after just a second or two, he came back to me.

Hawke

(hɔːk )
Definitions
noun1.  Edward, 1st Baron. 1705–81, British admiral. He destroyed the French fleet in Quiberon Bay (1759), preventing a French invasion of England2.  Robert (James Lee), known as Bob. Born 1929, Australian statesman; prime minister of Australia (1983–91)

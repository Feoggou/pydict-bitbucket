playing

(ˈpleɪɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of taking part in a game or sport ⇒ There was some good playing in the second half.2. the act of performing a piece of music ⇒ The orchestra's playing of the symphony was uninspired., ⇒ There was some fine playing in the violin concerto.

Seven hundred miles south of the Clayr's Glacier, twenty-two boys were playing cricket.

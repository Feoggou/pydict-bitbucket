inoffensive

(ˌɪnəˈfɛnsɪv )
Definitions
adjective1. not giving offence; unobjectionable2. not unpleasant, poisonous, or harmful
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inoffensively (ˌinofˈfensively) adverbinoffensiveness (ˌinofˈfensiveness) noun

inoffensive

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnəfensɪv ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as inoffensive, you mean that they are not unpleasant or unacceptable in any way, but are perhaps rather dull. ⇒ He's a mild inoffensive man. ⇒ It's a very nice song. Catchy, and inoffensive.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

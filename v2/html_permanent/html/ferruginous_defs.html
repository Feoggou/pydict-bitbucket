ferruginous

(fɛˈruːdʒɪnəs )
Definitions
adjective1. (of minerals, rocks, etc) containing iron ⇒ a ferruginous clay2. rust-coloured
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin ferrūgineus of a rusty colour, from ferrūgō iron rust, from ferrum iron

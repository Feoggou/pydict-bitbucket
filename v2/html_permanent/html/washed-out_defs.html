washed out

Definitions
adjective (washed-out when prenominal)1. faded or colourless2. exhausted, esp when being pale in appearance

washed-out

or washed out (ˌwɒʃtˈaʊt)
Definitions
adjective1. very pale ⇒ He stared at me out of those washed-out blue eyes., ⇒ suits in washed-out pastels2. very tired and lacking in energy ⇒ She looked washed out and listless.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

washed-out for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
also washed out
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Washed-out colours are very pale. ⇒ He stared at me out of those washed-out blue eyes. ⇒ ...suits in washed-out pastels.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If someone looks washed-out, they look very tired and lacking in energy. ⇒ She looked washed out and listless.

Powered by COBUILD
(næroʊli )
Definitions
1. adverb [ADV after v]If you look at someone narrowly, you look at them in a concentrated way, often because you think they are not giving you full information about something. ⇒ He grimaced and looked narrowly at his colleague.2. → See also narrow

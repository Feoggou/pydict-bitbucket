Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [oft adv ADJ]Accepted ideas are agreed by most people to be correct or reasonable. ⇒ There is no generally accepted definition of life. ⇒ It is accepted wisdom that science has been partly responsible for the decline of religion.2. → see also accept

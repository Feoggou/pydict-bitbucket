Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as banal, you do not like it because you think that it is so ordinary that it is not at all effective or interesting. [disapproval] hackneyed ⇒ The text is banal. ⇒ Bland, banal music tinkled discreetly from hidden loudspeakers.You can refer to banal things as the banal. ⇒ The allegations ranged from the banal to the bizarre.banality (bənælɪti )Word forms: banalities variable noun ⇒ ...the banality of life. ⇒ Neil's ability to utter banalities never ceased to amaze me.

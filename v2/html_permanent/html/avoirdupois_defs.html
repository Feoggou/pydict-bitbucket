avoirdupois

or avoirdupois weight (ˌævədəˈpɔɪz , ˌævwɑːdjuːˈpwɑː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna system of weights used in many English-speaking countries. It is based on the pound, which contains 16 ounces or 7000 grains. 100 pounds (US) or 112 pounds (Brit) is equal to 1 hundredweight and 20 hundredweights equals 1 ton. Abbreviation: avdp, avoir
Word origin
C14: from Old French aver de peis goods of weight

(ɪntjuːɪt , US -tuː- ) for learners of English
Word forms: intuits , intuiting , intuited
Definitions
verbIf you intuit something, you guess what it is on the basis of your intuition or feelings, rather than on the basis of knowledge. [formal] [V n] ⇒ They would confidently intuit your very thoughts. [V that] ⇒ He was probably right to intuit that it was universal.

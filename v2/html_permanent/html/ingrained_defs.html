ingrained

or engrained (ɪnˈɡreɪnd )
Definitions
adjective1. deeply impressed or instilled ⇒ his fears are deeply ingrained2. (prenominal) complete or inveterate; utter ⇒ an ingrained fool3. (esp of dirt) worked into or through the fibre, grain, pores, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ingrainedly or engrainedly (ɪnˈɡreɪnɪdlɪ ) adverbingrainedness (inˈgrainedness) or engrainedness (enˈgrainedness) noun

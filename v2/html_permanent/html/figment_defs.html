figment

(ˈfɪɡmənt )
Definitions
nouna fantastic notion, invention, or fabrication ⇒ a figment of the imagination
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Late Latin figmentum a fiction, from Latin fingere to shape

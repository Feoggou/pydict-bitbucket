Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [oft adv ADJ]Digestible food is food that is easy to digest. ⇒ Bananas are easily digestible.digestibility (daɪdʒestɪbɪlɪti ) uncount noun ⇒ Processing and refining can alter digestibility.2. graded adjective [usu v-link ADJ]Food that is digestible is pleasant to eat. ⇒ Fish is inexpensive, easy to cook and very digestible.3. graded adjective & adjectiveIf a theory or idea is digestible, it is easy to understand. ⇒ The book's aim was to make economic theory more digestible.

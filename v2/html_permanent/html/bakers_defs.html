baker's

(ˈbeɪkəz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna bakery or shop run by a baker selling bread and usually cakes, buns etc ⇒ The nearest baker's is in the High Street., ⇒ They're freshly baked. I fetched them from the baker's this morning.

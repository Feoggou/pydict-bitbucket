Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu pl]If you refer to people as doubters, you mean that they have doubts about something, especially their religious or political system. ⇒ Some doubters fear this news may not be as good as it appears.
Example sentences containing 'doubter'
Yes, I know I shouldn't need convincing but I was always a very good doubter. Anthony Masters CASCADES - THE DAY OF THE DEAD`In our hands lies the future of the trans-Atlantic alliance, "he had told one doubter. Dobbs, Michael THE TOUCH OF INNOCENTS

disoblige

(ˌdɪsəˈblaɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to disregard the desires of2. to slight; insult3. informal to cause trouble or inconvenience to
Derived forms
ˌdisobliging (ˌdisoˈbliging) adjectiveˌdisobligingly (ˌdisoˈbligingly) adverbˌdisobligingness (ˌdisoˈbligingness) noun

gimlet

(ˈɡɪmlɪt )
Definitions
noun1. a small hand tool consisting of a pointed spiral tip attached at right angles to a handle, used for boring small holes in wood2. US a cocktail consisting of half gin or vodka and half lime juice3. a eucalyptus of W Australia having a twisted boleverb4. (transitive) to make holes in (wood) using a gimletadjective5. penetrating; piercing (esp in the phrase gimlet-eyed)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French guimbelet, of Germanic origin, see wimble

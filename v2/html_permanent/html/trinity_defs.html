trinity

(ˈtrɪnɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. a group of three2. the state of being threefold

Word Origin

C13: from Old French trinite, from Late Latin trīnitās, from Latin trīnus triple

Trinity

(ˈtrɪnɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: Holy Trinity, Blessed Trinity Christian theology the union of three persons, the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, in one Godhead2. See Trinity Sunday3. See Holy Trinity

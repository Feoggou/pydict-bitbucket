duet

(djuːˈɛt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called (esp for instrumental compositions): duo a musical composition for two performers or voices2. an action or activity performed by a pair of closely connected individualsverbWord forms: duets, duetting or duetted3. (intransitive) to perform a duet
Derived forms
duettist (duˈettist) noun
Word origin
C18: from Italian duetto a little duet, from duo duet, from Latin: two

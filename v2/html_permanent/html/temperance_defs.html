temperance

(ˈtɛmpərəns )
Definitions
noun1. restraint or moderation, esp in yielding to one's appetites or desires2. abstinence from alcoholic drink
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin temperantia, from temperāre to regulate

temperance

Powered by COBUILD
(tempərəns ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounIf you believe in temperance, you disapprove of drinking alcohol. ⇒ ...a reformed alcoholic extolling the joys of temperance.2. uncount nounA person who shows temperance has a strong mind and does not eat too much, drink too much, or do too much of anything. [formal] ⇒ The age of hedonism is being ushered out by a new era of temperance.

Powered by COBUILD
(heɪtrɪd ) for learners of English
Word forms: hatreds
Definitions
uncount nounHatred is an extremely strong feeling of dislike for someone or something. [+ of/for] ⇒ Her hatred of them would never lead her to murder. ⇒ My hatred for her is so intense it seems to be destroying me. ⇒ He has been accused of inciting racial hatred.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

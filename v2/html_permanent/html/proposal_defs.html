proposal

(prəˈpəʊzəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of proposing2. something proposed, as a plan3. an offer, esp of marriage

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [N to-inf]A proposal is a plan or an idea, often a formal or written one, which is suggested for people to think about and decide upon. [+ for] ⇒ The President is to put forward new proposals for resolving the country's constitutional crisis. ⇒ ...the government's proposals to abolish free health care. ⇒ The Security Council has rejected the latest peace proposal.2. countable nounA proposal is the act of asking someone to marry you. [+ of] ⇒ After a three-weekend courtship, Pamela accepted Randolph's proposal of marriage.

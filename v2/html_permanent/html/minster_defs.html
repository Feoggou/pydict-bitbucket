minster

(ˈmɪnstə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounBritish any of certain cathedrals and large churches, usually originally connected to a monastery

Word Origin

Old English mynster, probably from Vulgar Latin monisterium (unattested), variant of Church Latin monastērium monastery

(skelɪtəl )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Skeletal means relating to the bones in your body. ⇒ ...the skeletal remains of seven adults. ⇒ ...the skeletal system.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA skeletal person is so thin that you can see their bones through their skin. ⇒ ...a hospital filled with skeletal children.3. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is skeletal has been reduced to its basic structure. ⇒ Passenger services can best be described as skeletal.

He crouched by his fence in a corner, skeletal in his blue overalls.

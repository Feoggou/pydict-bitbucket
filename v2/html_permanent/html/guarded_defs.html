guarded

(ˈɡɑːdɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. protected or kept under surveillance2. prudent, restrained, or noncommittal ⇒ a guarded reply
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

guardedly (ˈguardedly) adverbguardedness (ˈguardedness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as guarded, you mean that they are careful not to show their feelings or give away information. cagey ⇒ The boy gave him a guarded look. ⇒ In the office, Dr. Lahey seemed less guarded, more relaxed.guardedly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] [ADV adj] ⇒ 'I am happy, so far,' he says guardedly. ⇒ They are guardedly optimistic that the market is on the road to recovery.

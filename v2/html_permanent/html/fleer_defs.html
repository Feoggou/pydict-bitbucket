fleer

(flɪə ) archaic
Definitions
verb1. to grin or laugh at; scoff; sneernoun2. a derisory glance or grin
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fleeringly (ˈfleeringly) adverb

Word Origin

C14: of Scandinavian origin; compare Norwegian flire to snigger

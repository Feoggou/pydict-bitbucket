pestiferous

(pɛˈstɪfərəs ) or pestful (ˈpɛstfʊl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. informal troublesome; irritating2. breeding, carrying, or spreading infectious disease3. corrupting; pernicious

Derived Forms

pestiferously (pesˈtiferously) adverbpestiferousness (pesˈtiferousness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin pestifer, from pestis contagious disease, pest + ferre to bring

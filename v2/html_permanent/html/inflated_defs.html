inflated

(ɪnˈfleɪtɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. inflated amounts or prices are higher than they should be ⇒ His offence had been to quote inflated prices.2. making something seem better or more important than it really is; exaggerated ⇒ inflated notions of romance3. filled with air ⇒ an inflated mattress

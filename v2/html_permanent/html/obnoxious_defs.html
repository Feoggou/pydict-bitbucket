obnoxious

(əbˈnɒkʃəs )
Definitions
adjective1. extremely unpleasant2. obsolete exposed to harm, injury, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

obnoxiously (obˈnoxiously) adverbobnoxiousness (obˈnoxiousness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin obnoxius, from ob- to + noxa injury, from nocēre to harm

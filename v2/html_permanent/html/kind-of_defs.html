Definitions
phraseYou use kind of when you want to say that something or someone can be roughly described in a particular way. [spoken, vagueness] sort of ⇒ It was kind of sad, really. ⇒ She wasn't beautiful. But she was kind of cute. ⇒ It kind of gives us an idea of what's happening. →  kind

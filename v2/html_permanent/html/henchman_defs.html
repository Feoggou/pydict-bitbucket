henchman

(ˈhɛntʃmən )
Definitions
noun plural -men1. a faithful attendant or supporter2. archaic a squire; page
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: hengestman, from Old English hengest stallion + man; related to Old Norse hestr horse, Old High German hengist gelding

henchman

Powered by COBUILD
(hentʃmən ) for learners of English
Word forms: henchmen
Definitions
countable noun [usu poss N]If you refer to someone as another person's henchman, you mean that they work for or support the other person, especially by doing unpleasant, violent, or dishonest things on their behalf. [disapproval]

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrasal verbIf someone or something bears a person out or bears out what that person is saying, they support what that person is saying. [V P n] ⇒ Recent studies have borne out claims that certain perfumes can bring about profound psychological changes. [Also V n P] →  bear

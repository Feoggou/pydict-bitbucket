roll-top desk

Definitions
nouna desk having a slatted wooden panel that can be pulled down over the writing surface when not in use. Also called: roll-top

Definitions
countable nounA roll-top desk is a desk which has a wooden cover which can be pulled down over the writing surface when the desk is not being used.

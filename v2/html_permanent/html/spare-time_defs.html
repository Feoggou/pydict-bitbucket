spare time

(spɛə taɪm)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe time during which you do not have to work and you can do whatever you like ⇒ I do a lot of drawing in my spare time., ⇒ What do you do in your spare time?, ⇒ In her spare time she read books on cooking., ⇒ I spend a lot of my spare time playing video games.

spare-time

Definitions

adjectiveof or relating to the time during which one does not have to work ⇒ spare-time activities

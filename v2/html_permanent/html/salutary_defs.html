salutary

(ˈsæljʊtərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. promoting or intended to promote an improvement or beneficial effect ⇒ a salutary warning2. promoting or intended to promote health
Derived forms
salutarily (ˈsalutarily) adverbsalutariness (ˈsalutariness) noun
Word origin
C15: from Latin salūtāris wholesome, from salūs safety

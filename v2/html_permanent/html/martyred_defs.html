martyred

(ˈmɑːtəd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having suffered death rather than renounce religious beliefs ⇒ the martyred Orthodox Christians of the Soviet lands2. having suffered greatly or having died for a cause, belief, etc ⇒ another lifelong idolizer of the martyred Charles I, ⇒ whether its martyred leader is released or not3. pejorative feigning suffering to gain sympathy, help, etc ⇒ With a faintly martyred air, Eric poured more of his precious cognac., ⇒ You put on your martyred expression.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]If you describe a person or their behaviour as martyred, you mean that they often exaggerate their suffering in order to gain sympathy or praise. [literary, disapproval] ⇒ 'As usual,' muttered his martyred wife. ⇒ You put on your martyred expression, sigh and say, 'If you really want to...'. ⇒ ...with a lot of sighs, moans and a martyred air.

wayside

(ˈweɪˌsaɪd )
Definitions
noun1. a. the side or edge of a roadb. (modifier) situated by the wayside ⇒ a wayside inn2. See fall by the wayside3. See go by the wayside
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

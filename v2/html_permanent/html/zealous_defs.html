Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is zealous spends a lot of time or energy in supporting something that they believe in very strongly, especially a political or religious ideal. ⇒ She was a zealous worker for charitable bodies. ⇒ He was a recent Catholic convert, and very zealous.zealously graded adverb [usu ADV with v] ⇒ Details of its past activities were zealously guarded.

fatality

(fəˈtælɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. an accident or disaster resulting in death2. a person killed in an accident or disaster3. the power of causing death or disaster; deadliness4. the quality or condition of being fated5. something caused or dictated by fate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

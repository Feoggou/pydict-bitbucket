bowls

(bəʊlz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (functioning as singular)1. a. a game played on a bowling green in which a small bowl (the jack) is pitched from a mark and two opponents or opposing teams take turns to roll biased wooden bowls towards it, the object being to finish as near the jack as possibleb. (as modifier) ⇒ a bowls tournament2. skittles or tenpin bowling

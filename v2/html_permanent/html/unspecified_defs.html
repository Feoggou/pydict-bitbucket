unspecified for learners of English

(ʌnspesɪfaɪd )
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]You say that something is unspecified when you are not told exactly what it is. ⇒ The government said an unspecified number of bandits were killed. ⇒ He was arrested on unspecified charges.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

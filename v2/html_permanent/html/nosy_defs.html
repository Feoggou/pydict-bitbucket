nosy for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(noʊzi ) also nosey
Word forms: nosier , nosiest
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as nosy, you mean that they are interested in things which do not concern them. [informal, disapproval] ⇒ He was having to whisper in order to avoid being overheard by their nosy neighbours. ⇒ I agree that the press is often too nosy about a candidate's personal history.

prodding

(ˈprɒdɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or an instance of poking or jabbing with or as if with a pointed object2. the act or an instance of rousing or urging to action ⇒ The children always needed prodding into tidying up their rooms., ⇒ No amount of prodding could ever persuade him to help., ⇒ She did her chores without prodding.
Example sentences containing 'prodding'
An arm groped its way round her back, prodding her to one side, moving her towards a door.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is apposite is suitable for or appropriate to what is happening or being discussed. [formal] ⇒ Recent events have made his central theme even more apposite. ⇒ She thought in both languages, and selected the most apposite phrase from either.

But the phrase Webb had used - a walking time-bomb - struck Tom as strangely apposite , though he didn't know why, either.

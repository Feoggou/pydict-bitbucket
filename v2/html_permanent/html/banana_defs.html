banana

(bəˈnɑːnə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of several tropical and subtropical herbaceous treelike plants of the musaceous genus Musa, esp M. sapientum, a widely cultivated species propagated from suckers and having hanging clusters of edible fruit2. the crescent-shaped fruit of any of these plants. Compare plantain2
Word origin
C16: from Spanish or Portuguese, of African origin

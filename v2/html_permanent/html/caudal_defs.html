caudal

(ˈkɔːdəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. anatomy of or towards the posterior part of the body2. zoology relating to, resembling, or in the position of the tail
Derived forms
caudally (ˈcaudally) adverb
Word origin
C17: from New Latin caudālis, from cauda

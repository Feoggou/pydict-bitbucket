Definitions

inimitable

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnɪmɪtəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]You use inimitable to describe someone, especially a performer, when you like or admire them because of their special qualities. [formal, approval] ⇒ The major box office attraction was, of course, the inimitable Peter Hunningale. ⇒ He makes his own point in his own inimitable way.

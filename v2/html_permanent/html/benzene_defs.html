benzene

(ˈbɛnziːn , bɛnˈziːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna colourless flammable toxic aromatic liquid used in the manufacture of styrene, phenol, etc, as a solvent for fats, resins, etc, and as an insecticide. Formula: C6H6. See also benzene ring

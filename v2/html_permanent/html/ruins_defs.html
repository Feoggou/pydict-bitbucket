ruins

(ˈruːɪnz)
Definitions
plural noun1. the parts of something that remain after it has been severely damaged or weakened ⇒ He built a vast commercial empire on the ruins of the Soviet economy., ⇒ He stood very still, staring in at the ruins of his work., ⇒ the ruins of a shattered life, ⇒ the ruins of a political career, ⇒ the ruins of a great empire2. the parts of a building that remain after the rest has fallen down or been destroyed ⇒ One dead child was found in the ruins hours after the explosion., ⇒ Some of the houses were no more than ruins., ⇒ the ruins of the castle3. See in ruins

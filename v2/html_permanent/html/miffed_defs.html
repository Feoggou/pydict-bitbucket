miffed

(mɪft)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveinformal annoyed or upset ⇒ But he is right to take action, however miffed the party's fat-cat donors are., ⇒ I was a bit miffed about that., ⇒ Philip was pretty miffed at being cut out of his father's will.

Powered by COBUILD
(mɪft )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are miffed, you are slightly annoyed and hurt because of something which someone has said or done to you. [informal] [+ about] ⇒ I was a bit miffed about that. ⇒ Philip was pretty miffed at being cut out of his father's will.

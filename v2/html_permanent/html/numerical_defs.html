numerical

(njuːˈmɛrɪkəl ) or numeric
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or denoting a number or numbers2. measured or expressed in numbers ⇒ numerical value3. mathematicsa. containing or using constants, coefficients, terms, or elements represented by numbers ⇒ 3x2 + 4y = 2 is a numerical equation. Compare literal (sense 6)b. another word for absolute (sense 11a)

no longer

or not any longer
Definitions
something that is no longer the case used to be the case but is not the case now. You can also say that something is not the case any longer ⇒ She could no longer afford to keep him at school., ⇒ I noticed that he wasn't sitting by the door any longer., ⇒ They're no longer going out together., ⇒ I can't stand it any longer., ⇒ He no longer comes.▷ See longer

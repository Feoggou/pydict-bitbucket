famous

(ˈfeɪməs )
Definitions
adjective1. known to or recognized by many people; renowned2. informal excellent; splendid3. archaic of ill repute
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

famousness (ˈfamousness) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Latin fāmōsus; see fame

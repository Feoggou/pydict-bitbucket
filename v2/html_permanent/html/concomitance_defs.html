concomitance

(kənˈkɒmɪtəns ) or concomitancy (kənˈkɒmɪtənsɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. existence or occurrence together or in connection with another2. a thing that exists in connection with another3. Christian theology the doctrine that the body and blood of Christ are present in the Eucharist

cuddle

(ˈkʌdəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to hold (another person or thing) close or (of two people, etc) to hold each other close, as for affection, comfort, or warmth; embrace; hug2. (intransitive; foll by up) to curl or snuggle up into a comfortable or warm positionnoun3. a close embrace, esp when prolonged
Derived forms
cuddlesome (ˈcuddlesome) adjectivecuddly (ˈcuddly) adjective
Word origin
C18: of uncertain origin

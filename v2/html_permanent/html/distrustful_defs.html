Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are distrustful of someone or something, you think that they are not honest, reliable, or safe. [+ of] ⇒ Voters are deeply distrustful of all politicians. ⇒ The older you get the more distrustful you become.

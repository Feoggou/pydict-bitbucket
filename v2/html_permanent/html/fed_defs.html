Word forms: feds
Definitions
1. Fed is the past tense and past participle of feed. See also fed up.2. countable noun [usu pl]The feds are federal agents, for example of the American security agency, the FBI, or of the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, and Firearms. [US, informal]

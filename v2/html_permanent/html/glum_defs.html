glum

Powered by COBUILD
(glʌm ) for learners of English
Word forms: glummer , glummest
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]Someone who is glum is sad and quiet because they are disappointed or unhappy about something. ⇒ She was very glum and was obviously missing her children.glumly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ When Eleanor returned, I was still sitting glumly on the settee.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

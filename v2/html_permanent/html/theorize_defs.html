Word forms: theorizes , theorizing , theorized
Definitions
regional note:   in BRIT, also use theoriseverbIf you theorize that something is true or theorize about it, you develop an abstract idea or set of ideas about something in order to explain it. [V that] ⇒ Police are theorizing that the killers may be posing as hitchhikers. [V + about] ⇒ By studying the way people behave, we can theorize about what is going on in their mind. [Also V]theorizing uncount noun ⇒ This was no time for theorizing.

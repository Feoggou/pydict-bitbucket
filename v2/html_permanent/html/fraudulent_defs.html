fraudulent

(ˈfrɔːdjʊlənt )
Definitions
adjective1. acting with or having the intent to deceive2. relating to or proceeding from fraud or dishonest action
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fraudulence (ˈfraudulence) or fraudulency (ˈfraudulency) nounfraudulently (ˈfraudulently) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Latin fraudulentus deceitful

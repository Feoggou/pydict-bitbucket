rustling

Powered by COBUILD
(rʌsəlɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount noun [usu n N]Rustling is the activity of stealing farm animals, especially cattle. [mainly US] ⇒ ...cattle rustling and horse stealing.2. → See also rustle

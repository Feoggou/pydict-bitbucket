Jain

(dʒaɪn ) or Jaina (ˈdʒaɪnə )
Definitions
noun1. an adherent of Jainism2. one of the saints believed to be the founders of Jainismadjective3. of or relating to Jainism or the Jains
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from Hindi jaina saint, literally: overcomer, from Sanskrit

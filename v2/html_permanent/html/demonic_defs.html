demonic

(dɪˈmɒnɪk ) or demonical (dɪˈmɒnɪkəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of a demon; fiendish2. inspired or possessed by a demon, or seemingly so ⇒ demonic laughter
Derived forms
demonically (deˈmonically) adverb

demonic

Powered by COBUILD
(dɪmɒnɪk ) also daemonic
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Demonic means coming from or belonging to a demon or being like a demon. ⇒ ...demonic forces. ⇒ ...a demonic grin.2. adjective [ADJ n]If someone has demonic energy, drive, or abilities, they are more energetic, determined, or clever than most people. ⇒ ...a demonic drive to succeed.

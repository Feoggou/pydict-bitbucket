Salvador

(ˈsælvəˌdɔː , Portuguese salvaˈdor)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna port in E Brazil, capital of Bahia state: founded in 1549 as capital of the Portuguese colony, which it remained until 1763; a major centre of the African slave trade in colonial times. Pop: 3 331 000 (2005 est). Former name: Bahia. Official name: São Salvador da Bahia de Todos os Santos (sɑ̃u salvaˈdor ˈdəː baˈia ˈdəː ˈtoːduʃ uʃ ˈsəntuʃ)

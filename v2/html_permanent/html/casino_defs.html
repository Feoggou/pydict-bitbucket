casino

(kəˈsiːnəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -nos1. a public building or room in which gaming takes place, esp roulette and card games such as baccarat and chemin de fer2. a variant spelling of cassino
Word origin
C18: from Italian, diminutive of casa house, from Latin

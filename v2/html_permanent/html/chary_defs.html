chary

(ˈtʃɛərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: charier or chariest1. wary; careful2. choosy; finicky3. shy4. sparing; mean
Word origin
Old English cearig; related to caru care, Old High German charag sorrowful

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If you are chary of doing something, you are fairly cautious about doing it. [+ of] ⇒ I am rather chary of making too many idiotic mistakes. [Also + about]

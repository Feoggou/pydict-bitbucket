invader

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnveɪdəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: invaders
Definitions
1. countable noun [usu pl]Invaders are soldiers who are invading a country. ⇒ The invaders were only finally crushed when troops overcame them at Glenshiel in June 1719.2. countable noun [usu sing]You can refer to a country or army that has invaded or is about to invade another country as an invader. ⇒ ...action against a foreign invader.

restrained

(rɪˈstreɪnd )
Definitions
adjective1. (of a person or person's manner) calm and unemotional2. (of clothes, décor, etc) subtle and tasteful
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

restrainedly (rɪˈstreɪnɪdlɪ ) adverb

restrained

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪstreɪnd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is restrained is very calm and unemotional. ⇒ In the circumstances he felt he'd been very restrained. ⇒ Livy thought Caroline's greeting seemed a little restrained.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone's clothes or the decorations in a house as restrained, you mean that you like them because they are simple and not too brightly-coloured. [approval] ⇒ Her black suit was restrained and expensive.

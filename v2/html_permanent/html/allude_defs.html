allude

(əˈluːd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (intransitive; foll by to)1. to refer indirectly, briefly, or implicitly2. (loosely) to mentionAvoid confusion with elude
Word origin
C16: from Latin allūdere, from lūdere to sport, from lūdus a game

(əluːd )
Word forms: alludes , alluding , alluded
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf you allude to something, you mention it in an indirect way. [formal] [V + to] ⇒ She also alluded to her rival's past marital troubles.

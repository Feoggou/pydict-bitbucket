pinched

(pɪntʃt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of someone's face) thin and pale, usually because of illness or old age ⇒ Her face was pinched and drawn., ⇒ She was a small woman with pinched features.2. deprived ⇒ They lived in pinched circumstances., ⇒ Many candidates were pinched for campaign funds., ⇒ The town's a little pinched for money., ⇒ The car was pinched for rear seat passenger space.

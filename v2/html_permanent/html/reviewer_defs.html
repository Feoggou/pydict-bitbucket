Powered by COBUILD
(rɪvjuːəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: reviewers
Definitions
countable nounA reviewer is a person who reviews new books, films, television programmes, CDs, plays, or concerts. ⇒ ...the reviewer for the Times Literary Supplement.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

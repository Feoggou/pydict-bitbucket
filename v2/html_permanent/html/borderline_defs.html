borderline

(ˈbɔːdəˌlaɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a border; dividing line; line of demarcation2. an indeterminate position between two conditions or qualities ⇒ the borderline between friendship and loveadjective3. on the edge of one category and verging on another ⇒ a borderline failure in the exam

Word forms: borderlines
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounThe borderline between two different or opposite things is the division between them. borders [+ between] ⇒ ...a task which involves exploring the borderline between painting and photography. [Also + of]2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is borderline is only just acceptable as a member of a class or group. ⇒ Some were obviously unsuitable and could be ruled out at once. Others were borderline cases.

riding1

(ˈraɪdɪŋ )
Definitions
nouna. the art or practice of horsemanshipb. (as modifier) ⇒ a riding school, ⇒ riding techniques
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

riding2

(ˈraɪdɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. (capital when part of a name) any of the three former administrative divisions of Yorkshire: North Riding, East Riding, and West Riding2. (in Canada) a parliamentary constituency3. (in New Zealand) a rural electorate for local government
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

from Old English thriding, from Old Norse thrithjungr a third. The th- was lost by assimilation to the -t or -th that preceded it, as in west thriding, etc

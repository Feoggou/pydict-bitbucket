untasted

(ʌnˈteɪstɪd)
Definitions
adjective1. not tasted ⇒ the untasted fish, ⇒ The food lay untasted on the plate., ⇒ He left the meal untested.2. not experienced ⇒ the untasted honours of the English peerage

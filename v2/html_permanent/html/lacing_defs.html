lacing

(ˈleɪsɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. mainly British a course of bricks, stone, etc, for strengthening a rubble or flint wall2. another word for lace (sense 2), lace (sense 3)3. informal a severe beating (esp in the phrase give someone a lacing)

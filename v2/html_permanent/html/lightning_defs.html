lightning

(ˈlaɪtnɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. a flash of light in the sky, occurring during a thunderstorm and caused by a discharge of electricity, either between clouds or between a cloud and the earth ▶ Related adjectives: fulgurous, fulminous2. (modifier) fast and sudden ⇒ a lightning raid
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: variant of lightening

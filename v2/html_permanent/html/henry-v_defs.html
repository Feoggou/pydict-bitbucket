Henry V

Definitions
noun1.  1081–1125, king of Germany (1089–1125) and Holy Roman Emperor (1111–25)2.  1387–1422, king of England (1413–22); son of Henry IV. He defeated the French at the Battle of Agincourt (1415), conquered Normandy (1419), and was recognized as heir to the French throne (1420)

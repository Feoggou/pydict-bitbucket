lout

Powered by COBUILD
(laʊt ) for learners of English
Word forms: louts
Definitions
countable nounIf you describe a man or boy as a lout, you are critical of them because they behave in an impolite or aggressive way. [disapproval] ⇒ ...a drunken lout.

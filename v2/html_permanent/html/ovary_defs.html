ovary

(ˈəʊvərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ries1. either of the two female reproductive organs, which produce ova and secrete oestrogen hormones2. the corresponding organ in vertebrate and invertebrate animals3. botany the hollow basal region of a carpel containing one or more ovules. In some plants the carpels are united to form a single compound ovary
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ovarian (əʊˈvɛərɪən ) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from New Latin ōvārium, from Latin ōvum egg

Definitions
adjectiveSomething that is non-stop continues without any pauses or interruptions. ⇒ Many U.S. cities now have non-stop flights to Aspen. ⇒ ...80 minutes of non-stop music. ⇒ The training was non-stop and continued for three days.Non-stop is also an adverb. ⇒ Amy and her group had driven non-stop through Spain. [+ for] ⇒ The snow fell non-stop for 24 hours.

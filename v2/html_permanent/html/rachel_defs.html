Rachel

Definitions
noun1. (ˈreɪtʃəl) Old Testament the second and best-loved wife of Jacob; mother of Joseph and Benjamin (Genesis 29–35)2. (French raʃɛl) original name Elisa Félix. 1820–58, French tragic actress, famous for her roles in the plays of Racine and Corneille

according to

(əˈkɔːdɪŋ tə)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
preposition1. if someone says that something is true according to a particular person, book, or other source of information, they are indicating where they got their information ⇒ According to Mr Hemming, Philip stayed at the hotel., ⇒ According to him, everyone had gone., ⇒ the gospel according to Luke, ⇒ The road was forty kilometres long according to my map., ⇒ the gospel according to St Matthew2. if something is done according to a particular set of principles, these principles are used as a basis for the way it is done ⇒ They must take their own decision according to their own legal advice., ⇒ She played the game according to the rules.3. if something varies according to a changing factor, it varies in a way that is determined by this factor ⇒ The route that the boatmen choose varies according to the water level., ⇒ Prices vary according to the quantity ordered., ⇒ classified according to size4. See according to plan

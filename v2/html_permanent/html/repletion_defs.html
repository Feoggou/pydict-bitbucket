repletion

(rɪˈpliːʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the state or condition of being replete; fullness, esp excessive fullness due to overeating2. the satisfaction of a need or desire
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'repletion'
On a sigh of pleasant repletion , she laid down her knife and fork. Stephanie Laurens ON A WILD NIGHT

Similar Words

fullness, satiation, satiety, overfullness

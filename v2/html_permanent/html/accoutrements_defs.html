accoutrements

(əˈkuːtrəmənts) or US accouterments
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounformal or humorous all the things you have with you when you travel or take part in a particular activity, such as clothing and equipment ⇒ We'd pack the car with children, toys, nappies and all the other accoutrements and head off for Europe every summer., ⇒ the correct accoutrements for any form of sport, ⇒ I loved stationery and all the accoutrements of writing.

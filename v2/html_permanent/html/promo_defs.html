promo

(ˈprəʊməʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -mos1. a. informal something that is used to promote a product,b. (as modifier) ⇒ a promo videoverb2. informal to promote a product,

Word Origin

C20: shortened from promotion

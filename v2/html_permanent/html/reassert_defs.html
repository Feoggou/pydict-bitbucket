reassert

Powered by COBUILD
(riːəsɜːʳt ) for learners of English
Word forms: reasserts , reasserting , reasserted
Definitions
1. verbIf you reassert your control or authority, you make it clear that you are still in a position of power, or you strengthen the power that you had. [V n] ⇒ ...the government's continuing effort to reassert its control in the region. [V n] ⇒ The adults had reasserted their old authority.2. verbIf something such as an idea or habit reasserts itself, it becomes noticeable again. [V pron-refl] ⇒ His sense of humour was beginning to reassert itself.

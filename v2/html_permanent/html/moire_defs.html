moire

(mwɑː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna fabric, usually silk, having a watered effect

Word Origin

C17: from French, earlier mouaire, from mohair

moiré

(ˈmwɑːreɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having a watered or wavelike patternnoun2. such a pattern, impressed on fabrics by means of engraved rollers3. any fabric having such a pattern; moire4. Also: moiré pattern a pattern seen when two geometrical patterns, such as grids, are visually superimposed

Word Origin

C17: from French, from moire mohair
Example sentences containing 'moire'

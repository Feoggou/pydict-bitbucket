(əvaʊd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]If you are an avowed supporter or opponent of something, you have declared that you support it or oppose it. [formal] ⇒ She is an avowed vegetarian.avowedly (əvaʊɪdli ) adverb [usu ADV adj/-ed] ⇒ He remained for some years avowedly radical in his political outlook.2. adjective [ADJ n]An avowed belief or aim is one that you have declared formally or publicly. [formal] ⇒ ...the council's avowed intention to stamp on racism.

curled

(kɜːld)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivein a curved or spiral shape or position ⇒ his luxuriant curled hair, ⇒ the curled leaf bud of a fern, ⇒ The kitten was curled on a cushion on the sofa.

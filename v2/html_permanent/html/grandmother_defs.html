grandmother

(ˈɡrænˌmʌðə , ˈɡrænd-)
Definitions
noun1. the mother of one's father or mother2. (often plural) a female ancestor3. (often capital) a familiar term of address for an old woman4. See teach one's grandmother to suck eggs
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

awfully

(ˈɔːfəlɪ , ˈɔːflɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb1. in an unpleasant, bad, or reprehensible manner2. informal (intensifier) ⇒ I'm awfully keen to come3. archaic so as to express or inspire awe
Example sentences containing 'awfully'
They seemed a long way underground, and the darkness was awfully black. Arthur, Robert THREE IN ONEYou know, I think we've come to live among awfully nice people. Ferrars, Elizabeth MURDER MOVES INHot and awfully humid, with mosquitoes and sandflies into the bargain.

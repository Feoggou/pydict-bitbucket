enrapture

(ɪnˈræptʃə )
Definitions
verb(transitive) to fill with delight; enchant

enrapture

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnræptʃəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: enraptures , enrapturing , enraptured
Definitions
verbIf something or someone enraptures you, you think they are wonderful or fascinating. [literary] [V n] ⇒ The place at once enraptured me. [V-ed] ⇒ The 20,000-strong audience listened, enraptured. [V-ed] ⇒ ...an enraptured audience.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

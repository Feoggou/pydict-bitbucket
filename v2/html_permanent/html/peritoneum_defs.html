peritoneum

(ˌpɛrɪtəˈniːəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -nea (-ˈniːə) or -neumsa thin translucent serous sac that lines the walls of the abdominal cavity and covers most of the viscera. Also called: peritonaeum

Derived Forms

ˌperitoneal (ˌperitoˈneal) adjectiveˌperitoneally (ˌperitoˈneally) adverb

Word Origin

C16: via Late Latin from Greek peritonaion, from peritonos stretched around, from peri- + tenein to stretch

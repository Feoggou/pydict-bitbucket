Hyderabad

(ˈhaɪdərəˌbɑːd , -ˌbæd, ˈhaɪdrə-)
Definitions
noun1. a city in S central India, capital of Andhra Pradesh state and capital of former Hyderabad state; university (1918). Pop: 3 449 878 (2001)2. a former state of S India: divided in 1956 between the states of Andhra Pradesh, Mysore, and Maharashtra3. a city in SW Pakistan, on the River Indus: seat of the University of Sind (1947). Pop: 1 392 000 (2005 est)

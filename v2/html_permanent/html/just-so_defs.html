just so for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
conventionJust so is used to agree with or confirm a statement that has been made. [British, old-fashioned, spoken] quite, exactly ⇒ 'She has a large flat in Mayfair.'—'Just so.' →  just

just so for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
phrase [usu v-link PHR]If things are just so, they are done or arranged exactly as they should be or exactly as someone wants them. ⇒ I do her hair, and it has to be just so. →  just

mohair

(ˈməʊˌhɛə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: angora the long soft silky hair that makes up the outer coat of the Angora goat2. a. a fabric made from the yarn of this hair and cotton or woolb. (as modifier) ⇒ a mohair suit

Word Origin

C16: variant (influenced by hair) of earlier mocayare, ultimately from Arabic mukhayyar, literally: choice, from khayyara to choose

homeland

(ˈhəʊmˌlænd )
Definitions
noun1. the country in which one lives or was born2. the official name for a Bantustan
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

homeland

Powered by COBUILD
(hoʊmlænd ) for learners of English
Word forms: homelands
Definitions
1. countable noun [usu poss N]Your homeland is your native country. [mainly written] ⇒ Many are planning to return to their homeland.2. countable nounThe homelands were regions within South Africa in which black South Africans had a limited form of self-government.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Lamentations

(ˌlæmɛnˈteɪʃənz )
Definitions
noun (functioning as singular)1. a book of the Old Testament, traditionally ascribed to the prophet Jeremiah, lamenting the destruction of Jerusalem2. a musical setting of these poems

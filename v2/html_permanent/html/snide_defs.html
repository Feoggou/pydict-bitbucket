(snaɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. Also: snidey (ˈsnaɪdɪ ) (of a remark, etc) maliciously derogatory; supercilious2. counterfeit; shamnoun3. slang sham jewellery

Derived Forms

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A snide comment or remark is one which criticizes someone in an unkind and often indirect way. ⇒ He made a snide comment about her weight. ⇒ They kept making snide remarks about each other. ⇒ She couldn't tell if he was being snide, so she took the question straight.snidely adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ 'What are you doing here?' he asked snidely.

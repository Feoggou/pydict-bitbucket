scurvy

(ˈskɜːvɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a disease caused by a lack of vitamin C, characterized by anaemia, spongy gums, bleeding beneath the skin, and (in infants) malformation of bones and teeth ▶ Related adjective: scorbuticadjective -vier or -viest2. mean or despicable ⇒ a scurvy knave

Derived Forms

scurvily (ˈscurvily) adverbscurviness (ˈscurviness) noun

Word Origin

C16: see scurf

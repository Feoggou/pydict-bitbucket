Definitions
phrasal verbIf you think something out, you consider all the aspects and details of it before doing anything or making a decision. [V n P] ⇒ I need time alone to think things out. [V-ed P] ⇒ The book is detailed and well thought out. [V P n] ⇒ He chewed at the end of his pencil, thinking out the next problem. →  think

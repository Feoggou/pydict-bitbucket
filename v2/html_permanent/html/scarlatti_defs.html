Scarlatti

(skɑːˈlætɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Alessandro (alesˈsandro). ?1659–1725, Italian composer; regarded as the founder of modern opera2.  his son, (Giuseppe) Domenico (doˈmeːniko). 1685–1757, Italian composer and harpsichordist, in Portugal and Spain from 1720. He wrote over 550 single-movement sonatas for harpsichord, many of them exercises in virtuoso technique

taker

Powered by COBUILD
(teɪkəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: takers
Definitions
countable noun [usu pl]If there are no takers for something such as an investment or a challenge, nobody is willing to accept it. ⇒ Over 100 buyers or investors were approached, but there were no takers. ⇒ He hasn't found any takers for that idea. [Also + for]

Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Your fave thing or person of a particular type is the one you like the most. [journalism, British, informal] ⇒ Vote for your fave song by dialing 0906 474 8000.2. countable nounA fave is a thing or person of a particular type that you like the most.

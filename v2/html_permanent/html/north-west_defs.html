north-west for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
also northwest
Definitions
1. uncount noun [oft the N]The north-west is the direction which is halfway between north and west. ⇒ ...Ushant, five miles out to the north-west.2. singular nounThe north-west of a place, country, or region is the part which is towards the north-west. ⇒ Labour took its pre-election campaign to the North-West. [+ of] ⇒ ...the extreme north-west of South America.3. adverb [ADV after v]If you go north-west, you travel towards the north-west. ⇒ Take the narrow lane going north-west parallel with the railway line.4. adverbSomething that is north-west of a place is positioned to the north-west of it. [+ of] ⇒ This was situated to the north-west of the town, a short walk from the railway station.5. graded adjective & adjective [ADJ n]The north-west part of a place, country, or region is the part which is towards the north-west. ⇒ ...the North-West Regional Health Authority. ⇒ ...the north-west coast of the United States. ⇒ ...Sydney's north-west suburbs.6. adjective [ADJ n]A north-west wind is a wind that blows from the north-west. ⇒ A brisk north-west wind swept across the region.

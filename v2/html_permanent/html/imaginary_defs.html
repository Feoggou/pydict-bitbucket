imaginary

(ɪˈmædʒɪnərɪ , -dʒɪnrɪ)
Definitions
adjective1. existing in the imagination; unreal; illusory2. mathematics involving or containing imaginary numbers. The imaginary part of a complex number, z, is usually written Imz
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

imaginarily (imˈaginarily) adverbimaginariness (imˈaginariness) noun

Definitions

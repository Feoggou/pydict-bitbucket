colonel

(ˈkɜːnəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan officer of land or air forces junior to a brigadier but senior to a lieutenant colonel
Derived forms
colonelcy (ˈcolonelcy) or colonelˌship (ˈcolonelˌship) noun
Word origin
C16: via Old French, from Old Italian colonnello column of soldiers, from colonna column

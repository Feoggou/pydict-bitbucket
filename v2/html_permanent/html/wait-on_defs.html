wait on

Definitions
verb (intr, preposition)1. to serve at the table of2. to act as an attendant or servant to3. archaic to visitexclamation4. Australian and New Zealand stop! hold on!

Definitions
phrasal verbIf you are waiting on something, you are waiting for it to happen, for example before you do or decide anything. [US] [V P n] ⇒ Since then I've been waiting on events. [V P n] ⇒ We cannot wait on the government to make changes at its own pace. →  wait

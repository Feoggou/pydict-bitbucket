aromatic

(ˌærəˈmætɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having a distinctive, usually fragrant smell2. (of an organic compound) having an unsaturated ring containing alternating double and single bonds, esp containing a benzene ring; exhibiting aromaticity. Compare aliphaticnoun3. something, such as a plant or drug, giving off a fragrant smell
Derived forms
ˌaromatically (ˌaroˈmatically) adverb

fixed costs

Powered by COBUILD
(fɪkst kɔsts) in Accounting
Definitions
noun(Accounting: Management) Fixed costs are costs that do not vary depending on how much of a product is made. ⇒ Most companies have high fixed costs, and only once those costs are covered, profits can surge. ⇒ Building an infrastructure for new brands or markets means adding to fixed costs no matter what the volume of sales achieved. ⇒ Fixed costs are costs that do not vary depending on how much of a product is made. ▶  cost

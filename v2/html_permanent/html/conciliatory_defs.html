graded adjective & adjectiveWhen you are conciliatory in your actions or behaviour, you show that you are willing to end a disagreement with someone. ⇒ The next time he spoke he used a more conciliatory tone. ⇒ The President's speech was hailed as a conciliatory gesture toward business.

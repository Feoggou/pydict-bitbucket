Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is courteous is polite and respectful to other people. OPPOSITE: discourteous, rude ⇒ He was a kind and courteous man. ⇒ My friend's reply was courteous but firm.courteously graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj] ⇒ Then he nodded courteously to me and walked off to perform his unpleasant duty.

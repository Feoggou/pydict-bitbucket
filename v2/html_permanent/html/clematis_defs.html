clematis

(ˈklɛmətɪs , kləˈmeɪtɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany N temperate ranunculaceous climbing plant or erect shrub of the genus Clematis, having plumelike fruits. Many species are cultivated for their large colourful flowers. See also traveller's joy
Word origin
C16: from Latin, from Greek klēmatis climbing plant, brushwood, from klēma twig

Definitions

variable nounA clematis is a type of flowering shrub which can be grown to climb up walls or fences. There are many different varieties of clematis.

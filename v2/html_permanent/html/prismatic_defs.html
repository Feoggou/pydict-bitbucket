prismatic

(prɪzˈmætɪk ) or prismatical (prɪzˈmætɪkəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. concerned with, containing, or produced by a prism2. exhibiting bright spectral colours ⇒ prismatic light3. crystallography another word for orthorhombic

Derived Forms

prismatically (prisˈmatically) adverb
Example sentences containing 'prismatic'
An arc showed in the fog, beyond it a second arc of faint prismatic colors. Annie Proulx THE SHIPPING NEWS

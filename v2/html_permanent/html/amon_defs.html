Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun Egyptian Mythology; Originally a local god of fertility and life in Egyptian Thebes: later associated with Re as the chief deity of Egypt ( Amon-Re): identified by the Greeks (and Romans) with Zeus (and Jupiter): also sp. ˈAmun

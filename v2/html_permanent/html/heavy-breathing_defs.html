heavy breathing

(ˈhɛvɪ ˈbriːðɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. medicine stertorous breathing or breathing done with difficulty ⇒ What is heavy breathing a symptom of?2. an imitation of such sounds, as being suggestive of sexual excitement ⇒ The familiar sound of heavy breathing came down the phone.

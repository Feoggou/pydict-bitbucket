promiscuous

(prəˈmɪskjʊəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. indulging in casual and indiscriminate sexual relationships2. consisting of a number of dissimilar parts or elements mingled in a confused or indiscriminate manner3. indiscriminate in selection4. casual or heedless

Derived Forms

promiscuously (proˈmiscuously) adverbpromiscuousness (proˈmiscuousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin prōmiscuus indiscriminate, from pro-1 + miscēre to mix

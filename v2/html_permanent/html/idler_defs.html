idler

(ˈaɪdlə )
Definitions
noun1. a person who idles2. another name for idle pulley, idle wheel3. nautical a ship's crew member, such as a carpenter, sailmaker, etc, whose duties do not include standing regular watches
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Lydian

(ˈlɪdɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to ancient Lydia, its inhabitants, or their language2. music of or relating to an authentic mode represented by the ascending natural diatonic scale from F to F. See also Hypo-. Compare Hypolydiannoun3. an inhabitant of Lydia4. the extinct language of the Lydians, thought to belong to the Anatolian group or family

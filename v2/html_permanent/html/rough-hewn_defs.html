rough-hewn

(ˌrʌfˈhjuːn)
Definitions
adjective1. cut or hewn roughly, with an unfinished surface ⇒ the rough-hewn beams supporting his house, ⇒ It is a rough-hewn carving of a cat's head.2. shaped roughly or crudely ⇒ rough-hewn, tangerine jumpsuits
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Rough-hewn wood or stone has been cut into a shape but has not yet been smoothed or finished off. ⇒ It is a rough-hewn carving of a cat's head.

lustral

(ˈlʌstrəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to a ceremony of purification2. taking place at intervals of five years; quinquennial
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin lūstrālis adj from lustrum

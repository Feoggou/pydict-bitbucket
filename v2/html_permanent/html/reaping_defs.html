reaping

(ˈriːpɪŋ)
Definitions
nouna. the cutting or harvesting of cropsb. (as modifier) ⇒ a reaping scythe, ⇒ a reaping fork/scythe/sickle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'reaping'
Only later, when they realized she was unable to bear children, did her endless planting and tending and reaping make any sense. Mark Mills AMAGANSETTNot so that you can sit here reaping profits, eating the sweat off our backs. Appiganesi, Lisa DREAMS OF INNOCENCEHe had a point -- well, a sort of point -- but I was too busy reaping the whirlwind of the vodka. David Cavanagh MUSIC FOR BOYS

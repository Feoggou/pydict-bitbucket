overweening

(ˌəʊvəˈwiːnɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. (of a person) excessively arrogant or presumptuous2. (of opinions, appetites, etc) excessive; immoderate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

overweeningly (ˌoverˈweeningly) adverboverweeningness (ˌoverˈweeningness) noun

Word Origin

C14: over- + weening, from Old English wēnan: see ween

Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you want to emphasize your disapproval of someone's great ambition or pride, you can refer to their overweening ambition or pride. [formal, disapproval] ⇒ 'Your modesty is a cover for your overweening conceit,' she said.

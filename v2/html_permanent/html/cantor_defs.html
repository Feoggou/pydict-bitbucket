cantor

(ˈkæntɔː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: chazan Judaism a man employed to lead synagogue services, esp to traditional modes and melodies2. Christianity the leader of the singing in a church choir
Word origin
C16: from Latin: singer, from canere to sing

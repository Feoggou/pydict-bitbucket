Wentworth

(ˈwɛntwəθ )
Definitions
noun1. Thomas. See (Earl of) Strafford2.  William Charles. 1790–1872, Australian explorer and statesman who was a member of the exploring party that first crossed the Blue Mountains in 1813 and was later a leader in the movement for self-government in New South Wales

Cambrian

(ˈkæmbrɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, denoting, or formed in the first 65 million years of the Palaeozoic era, during which marine invertebrates, esp trilobites, flourished2. of or relating to Walesnoun3. See the Cambrian4. a Welsh person

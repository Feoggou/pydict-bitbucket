(ˈsɛkəndz)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. goods of inferior quality ⇒ Some of the articles you see are seconds., ⇒ It's a shop selling discounted lines and seconds.2. a second helping of food ⇒ There's seconds if you want them.3. the second course of a meal; dessert

Definitions
COBUILD Key Words for Retail
noun(Retail: Products) Seconds are goods that are not top quality, either because they are flawed or last season's. ⇒ Stores known as off-price retailers buy manufacturers' seconds, overruns, returns, and off-season merchandise for resale to consumers at deep discounts. ⇒ Seconds are items with definite defects which prevent the manufacturer from selling them to their first-line customers. ⇒ Seconds are goods that are not top quality, either because they are flawed or last season's.Related wordsCompare seconds with firsts which are saleable goods of the highest quality.

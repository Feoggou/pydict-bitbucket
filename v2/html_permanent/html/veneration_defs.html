veneration

(ˌvɛnəˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a feeling or expression of awe or reverence2. the act of venerating or the state of being venerated
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

venerational (ˌvenerˈational) adjective
Example sentences containing 'veneration'
It is a service that I perform for her out of loyalty and love and veneration. Nicola Barker BEHINDLINGSHis brain had been beautifully prepared for such veneration and false remembrance. Zindell, David THE BROKEN GODThroughout recorded history, whether in this hemisphere or the next, pearls were objects of veneration. Elizabeth Lowell PEARL COVE

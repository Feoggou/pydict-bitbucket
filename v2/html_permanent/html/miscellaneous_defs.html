miscellaneous

(ˌmɪsəˈleɪnɪəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. composed of or containing a variety of things; mixed; varied2. having varied capabilities, sides, etc

Derived Forms

ˌmiscellaneously (ˌmiscelˈlaneously) adverbˌmiscellaneousness (ˌmiscelˈlaneousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin miscellāneus, from miscellus mixed, from miscēre to mix

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v]If something is done safely, it is done in a way that makes it unlikely that anyone will be harmed. ⇒ The waste is safely locked away until it is no longer radioactive. ⇒ 'Drive safely,' he said and waved goodbye. ⇒ More people will be doing the work with proper supervision and thus, more safely.2. adverb [usu ADV with v]You also use safely to say that there is no risk of a situation being changed. ⇒ Once events are safely in the past, this idea seems to become less alarming. ⇒ The number two seed is safely through to the second round of the tournament.3. → see also safe

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A patriarchal society, family, or system is one in which the men have all or most of the power and importance. ⇒ To feminists she is a classic victim of the patriarchal society. ⇒ I think marriage is used to uphold patriarchal control.

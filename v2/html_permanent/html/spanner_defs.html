spanner

(ˈspænə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a steel hand tool with a handle carrying jaws or a hole of particular shape designed to grip a nut or bolt head2. British informal a source of impediment or annoyance (esp in the phrase throw a spanner in the works)

Word Origin

C17: from German, from spannen to stretch, span1

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA spanner is a metal tool whose end fits round a nut so that you can turn it to loosen or tighten it. [mainly British]regional note:   in AM, usually use wrench, monkey wrench2. →  to throw a spanner in the works

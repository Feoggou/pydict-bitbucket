forswear

(fɔːˈswɛə )
Definitions
verb -swears, -swearing, -swore or -sworn1. (transitive) to reject or renounce with determination or as upon oath2. (transitive) to deny or disavow absolutely or upon oath ⇒ he forswore any knowledge of the crime3. to perjure (oneself)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

forswearer (forˈswearer) noun

Word Origin

Old English forswearian

motley

(ˈmɒtlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. made up of elements of varying type, quality, etc2. multicolourednoun3. a motley collection or mixture4. the particoloured attire of a jester5. obsolete a jester

Word Origin

C14: perhaps from mot speck, mote1

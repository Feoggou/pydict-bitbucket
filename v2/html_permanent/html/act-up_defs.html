act up

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(intr, adverb) informal to behave in a troublesome way ⇒ the engine began to act up when we were miles from anywhere

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. phrasal verb [usu cont]If something is acting up, it is not working properly. [informal] [V P] ⇒ She was messing with the coffee pot, which was acting up again.2. phrasal verb [usu cont]If a child is acting up, they are behaving badly. [informal] [V P] ⇒ I could hear Jonathan acting up downstairs. →  act

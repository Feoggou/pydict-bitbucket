appropriation

(əˌprəʊprɪˈeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of setting apart or taking for one's own use2. a sum of money set apart for a specific purpose, esp by a legislature

appropriation

(əproʊprieɪʃən )
Word forms: appropriations
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounAn appropriation is an amount of money that a government or organization reserves for a particular purpose. [formal] ⇒ The government raised defence appropriations by 12 per cent.2. uncount nounAppropriation of something that belongs to someone else is the act of taking it, usually without having the right to do so. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ Other charges include fraud and illegal appropriation of land.

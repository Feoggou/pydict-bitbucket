isthmus

(ˈɪsθməs, ˈɪstməs, ˈɪsməs )
Definitions
noun plural -muses or -mi (-maɪ)1. a narrow strip of land connecting two relatively large land areas2. anatomya. a narrow band of tissue connecting two larger parts of a structureb. a narrow passage connecting two cavities
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

isthmoid (ˈisthmoid) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Latin, from Greek isthmos

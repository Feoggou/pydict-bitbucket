Cambridgeshire

(ˈkeɪmbrɪdʒˌʃɪə , -ʃə)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna county of E England, in East Anglia: includes the former counties of the Isle of Ely and Huntingdon and lies largely in the Fens: Peterborough became an independent unitary authority in 1998. Administrative centre: Cambridge. Pop (excluding Peterborough): 571 000 (2003 est). Area (excluding Peterborough): 3068 sq km (184 sq miles)

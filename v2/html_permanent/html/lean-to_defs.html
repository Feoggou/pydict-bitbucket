lean-to

Definitions
noun plural -tos1. a roof that has a single slope with its upper edge adjoining a wall or building2. a shed or outbuilding with such a roof

Definitions
countable nounA lean-to is a building such as a shed or garage which is attached to one wall of a larger building, and which usually has a sloping roof.

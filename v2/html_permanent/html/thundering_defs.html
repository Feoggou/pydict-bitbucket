thundering

(ˈθʌndərɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective(prenominal) slang very great or excessive ⇒ a thundering idiot
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

thunderingly (ˈthunderingly) adverb
Example sentences containing 'thundering'
She pushed herself further back against the old brick wall and held her breath, aware of her heartbeat thundering in her ears. Barbara Erskine HIDING FROM THE LIGHTOne of the knights sent to ride point was thundering toward them. Jennifer Fallon TREASON KEEPThe waves threw off the snow, thundering up the beach in clouds of spray. Erskine, Barbara MIDNIGHT IS A LONELY PLACEWell past midnight a restless peasant wondered at the steady drum of hooves thundering by his cottage. Kathleen E. Woodiwiss THE WOLF AND THE DOVE

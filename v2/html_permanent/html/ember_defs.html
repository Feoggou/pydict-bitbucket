ember

(ˈɛmbə )
Definitions
noun1. a glowing or smouldering piece of coal or wood, as in a dying fire2. the fading remains of a past emotion ⇒ the embers of his love
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English ǣmyrge; related to Old Norse eimyrja ember, eimr smoke, Old High German eimuria ember

emphatically

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmfætɪkli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]If you say something emphatically, you say it in a forceful way which shows that you feel very strongly about what you are saying. ⇒ 'No fast food', she said emphatically. ⇒ Mr Davies has emphatically denied the charges.2. graded adverb & adverbYou use emphatically to emphasize the statement you are making. [emphasis] ⇒ Making people feel foolish is emphatically not my strategy. ⇒ Politics is most emphatically back on the agenda.

opprobrium

(əˈprəʊbrɪəm )
Definitions
noun1. the state of being abused or scornfully criticized2. reproach or censure3. a cause of disgrace or ignominy
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin ob- against + probrum a shameful act

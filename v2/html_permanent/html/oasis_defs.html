oasis

(əʊˈeɪsɪs )
Definitions
noun plural -ses (-siːz)1. a fertile patch in a desert occurring where the water table approaches or reaches the ground surface2. a place of peace, safety, or happiness in the midst of trouble or difficulty
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: via Latin from Greek, probably of Egyptian origin

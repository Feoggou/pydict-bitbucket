shoestring

(ˈʃuːˌstrɪŋ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. another word for shoelace2. informala. a very small or petty amount of money (esp in the phrase on a shoestring)b. (as modifier) ⇒ a shoestring budget

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu pl]Shoestrings are long, narrow pieces of material like pieces of string that you use to fasten your shoes. [US]regional note:   in BRIT, use shoelaces2. adjective [ADJ n]A shoestring budget is one where you have very little money to spend. ⇒ The British-produced film was made on a shoestring budget.3. →  on a shoestring

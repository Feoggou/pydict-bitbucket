oratorio

(ˌɒrəˈtɔːrɪəʊ )
Definitions
noun plural -riosa dramatic but unstaged musical composition for soloists, chorus, and orchestra, based on a religious theme
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from Italian, literally: oratory2, referring to the Church of the Oratory at Rome where musical services were held

associative

(əˈsəʊʃɪətɪv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or causing association or union2. mathematics, logica. being independent of the grouping of numbers, symbols, or terms within a given set, as in conjunction or in an expression such as (2 × 3) × 4 = 2 × (3 × 4)b. referring to this property ⇒ the associative laws of arithmetic

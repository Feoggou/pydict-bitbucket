(rɪˈzʌmpʃən )
Definitions

Derived Forms

resumptive (reˈsumptive) adjectiveresumptively (reˈsumptively) adverb

Word Origin

C15: via Old French from Late Latin resumptiō, from Latin resūmere to resume

crux

(krʌks )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural cruxes or cruces (ˈkruːsiːz )1. a vital or decisive stage, point, etc (often in the phrase the crux of the matter)2. a baffling problem or difficulty3. mountaineering the most difficult and often decisive part of a climb or pitch4. a rare word for cross
Word origin
C18: from Latin: cross

alder

(ˈɔːldə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any N temperate betulaceous shrub or tree of the genus Alnus, having toothed leaves and conelike fruits. The bark is used in dyeing and tanning and the wood for bridges, etc because it resists underwater rot2. any of several similar trees or shrubs
Word origin
Old English alor; related to Old High German elira, Latin alnus

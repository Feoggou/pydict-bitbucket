radix

(ˈreɪdɪks )
Definitions
noun plural -dices (-dɪˌsiːz) or -dixes1. mathematics any number that is the base of a number system or of a system of logarithms ⇒ 10 is the radix of the decimal system2. biology the root or point of origin of a part or organ3. linguistics a less common word for root1 (sense 9)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin rādīx root; compare Greek rhadix small branch, rhiza root

cosy

or US cozy (ˈkəʊzɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -sier, -siest or US -zier or -ziest1. warm and snug2. intimate; friendly3. convenient, esp for devious purposes ⇒ a cosy dealnoun plural -sies or US -zies4. Also called: cosey a cover for keeping things warm ⇒ egg cosy
Derived forms
cosily (ˈcosily) or US cozily (ˈcozily) adverbcosiness (ˈcosiness) or US coziness (ˈcoziness) noun
Word origin
C18: from Scots, of unknown origin

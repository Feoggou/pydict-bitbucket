urbane

(ɜːˈbeɪn )
Definitions
adjectivecharacterized by elegance or sophistication
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

urbanely (urˈbanely) adverburbaneness (urˈbaneness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin urbānus belonging to the town; see urban

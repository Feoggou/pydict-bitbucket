treatise

(ˈtriːtɪz )
Definitions
noun1. a formal work on a subject, esp one that deals systematically with its principles and conclusions2. an obsolete word for narrative
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Anglo-French tretiz, from Old French tretier to treat

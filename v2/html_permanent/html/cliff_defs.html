cliff

(klɪf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna steep high rock face, esp one that runs along the seashore and has the strata exposed
Derived forms
cliffy (ˈcliffy) adjective
Word origin
Old English clif; related to Old Norse kleif, Middle Low German klēf, Dutch klif; see cleave2

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA cliff is a high area of land with a very steep side, especially one next to the sea. ⇒ The car rolled over the edge of a cliff.

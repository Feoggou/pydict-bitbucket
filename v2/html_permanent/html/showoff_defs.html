showoff

(ˈʃoʊˌɔf )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  the act of showing off; vain or showy display2.  a person who shows off

  • British English: show-off /ˈʃəʊɒf/ NOUN
    If you say that someone is a show-off, you are criticizing them for trying to impress people by showing in a very obvious way what they can do or what they own.I was an awful show-off as a child.

Stratford-upon-Avon

(ˈstrætfərd əˈpɑn eɪvɑn )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
town in S Warwickshire, England, on the Avon: birthplace & burial place of Shakespeare: pop. of county district (called Stratford-on-Avon) 106,000

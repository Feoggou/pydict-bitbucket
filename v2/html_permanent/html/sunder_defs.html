sunder

(ˈsʌndə ) archaic or literary

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to break or cause to break apart or in piecesnoun2. See in sunder

Derived Forms

sunderable (ˈsunderable) adjectivesunderance (ˈsunderance) or sunderment (ˈsunderment) nounsunderer (ˈsunderer) noun

Word Origin

Old English sundrian; related to Old Norse sundr asunder, Gothic sundrō apart, Old High German suntar, Latin sine without

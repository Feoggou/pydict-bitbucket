heady

(ˈhɛdɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: headier or headiest1. (of alcoholic drink) intoxicating2. strongly affecting the mind or senses; extremely exciting3. rash; impetuous
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

headily (ˈheadily) adverbheadiness (ˈheadiness) noun

(hedi ) for learners of English
Word forms: headier , headiest
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A heady drink, atmosphere, or experience strongly affects your senses, for example by making you feel drunk or excited. ⇒ ...in the heady days just after their marriage. ⇒ I felt heady and euphoric.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

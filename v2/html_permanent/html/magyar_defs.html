Magyar

(ˈmæɡjɑː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. plural -yars a member of the predominant ethnic group of Hungary, also found in NW Siberia2. the Hungarian languageadjective3. of or relating to the Magyars or their language4. sewing of or relating to a style of sleeve cut in one piece with the bodice

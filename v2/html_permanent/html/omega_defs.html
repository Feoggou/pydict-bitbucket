omega

(ˈəʊmɪɡə )
Definitions
noun1. the 24th and last letter of the Greek alphabet (Ω, ω), a long vowel, transliterated as o or ō2. the ending or last of a series
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Greek ō mega big o; see mega-, omicron

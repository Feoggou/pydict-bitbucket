propitiatory

(prəˈpɪʃɪətərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. designed or intended to propitiate; conciliatory; expiatorynoun2. the mercy seat

Derived Forms

propitiatorily (proˈpitiatorily) adverb
Example sentences containing 'propitiatory'
On isolated houses, propitiatory slogans had been painted, large in red paint: `LONG LIVE THE REPUBLIC was evidently the safest slogan. Aldiss, Brian SOMEWHERE EAST OF LIFE

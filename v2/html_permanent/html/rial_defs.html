rial

(ˈraɪəl )
Definitions
noun1. the standard monetary unit of Iran2. the standard monetary unit of Oman, divided into 1000 baizas3. another name for riyal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Persian, from Arabic riyāl riyal

innocently

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnəsəntli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]If you say that someone does or says something innocently, you mean that they are pretending not to know something about a situation. ⇒ I caught Chrissie's eye, but she only smiled back at me innocently. ⇒ 'Any chance you'd be heading out that way again, soon?' I asked innocently.2. → See also innocent

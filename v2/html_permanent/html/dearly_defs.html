dearly

Powered by COBUILD
(dɪəʳli )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]If you love someone dearly, you love them very much. [formal, emphasis] ⇒ She loved her father dearly.2. graded adverb & adverb [ADV before v]If you would dearly like to do or have something, you would very much like to do it or have it. [formal, emphasis] ⇒ I would dearly love to marry.3. →  pay dearly/cost dearly

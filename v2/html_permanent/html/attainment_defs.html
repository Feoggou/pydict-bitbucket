Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounThe attainment of an aim is the achieving of it. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ ...the attainment of independence.2. countable nounAn attainment is a skill you have learned or something you have achieved. [formal] ⇒ ...their educational attainments.

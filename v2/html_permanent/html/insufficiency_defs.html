insufficiency

(ˌɪnsəˈfɪʃənsɪ )
Definitions
noun1. Also called: insufficience the state of being insufficient2. pathology failure in the functioning of an organ, tissue, etc ⇒ cardiac insufficiency
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

He banged the receiver against the outer glass panel, and only caused scratches, which appalled him with his own insufficiency.

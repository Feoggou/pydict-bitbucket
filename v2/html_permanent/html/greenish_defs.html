greenish

Powered by COBUILD
(griːnɪʃ ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveGreenish means slightly green in colour. ⇒ ...his cold greenish eyes.Greenish is also a combining form. ⇒ ...greenish-yellow flowers.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

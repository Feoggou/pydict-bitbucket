merchandise

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (ˈmɜːtʃənˌdaɪs , -ˌdaɪz)1. commercial goods; commoditiesverb (ˈmɜːtʃənˌdaɪz ) also: merchandize2. to engage in the commercial purchase and sale of (goods or services); trade

Derived Forms

merchanˌdiser (ˈmerchanˌdiser) or merchanˌdizer (ˈmerchanˌdizer) noun

Word Origin

C13: from Old French. See merchant

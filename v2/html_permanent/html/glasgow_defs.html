Glasgow

(ˈɡlɑːzɡəʊ , ˈɡlæz-)
Definitions
noun1. a city in W central Scotland, in City of Glasgow council area on the River Clyde: the largest city in Scotland; centre of a major industrial region, formerly an important port; universities (1451, 1964, 1992). Pop: 598 830 (2011) ▶ Related adjective: Glaswegian2. See City of Glasgow

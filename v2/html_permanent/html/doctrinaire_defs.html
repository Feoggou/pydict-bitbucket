doctrinaire

(ˌdɒktrɪˈnɛə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. stubbornly insistent on the observation of the niceties of a theory, esp without regard to practicality, suitability, etc2. theoretical; impracticalnoun3. a person who stubbornly attempts to apply a theory without regard to practical difficulties
Derived forms
ˌdoctrinairism (ˌdoctriˈnairism) or ˌdoctrinarism (ˌdoctriˈnarism) nounˌdoctrinarian (ˌdoctriˈnarian) noun

occupancy

(ˈɒkjʊpənsɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -cies1. the act of occupying; possession of a property2. law the possession and use of property by or without agreement and without any claim to ownership3. law the act of taking possession of unowned property, esp land, with the intent of thus acquiring ownership4. the condition or fact of being an occupant, esp a tenant5. the period of time during which one is an occupant, esp of property

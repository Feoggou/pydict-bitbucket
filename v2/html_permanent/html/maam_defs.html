ma'am

Powered by COBUILD
(mæm , mɑːm )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounPeople sometimes say ma'am as a very formal and polite way of addressing a woman whose name they do not know or a woman of superior rank. [mainly US, politeness] ⇒ Would you repeat that please, ma'am?

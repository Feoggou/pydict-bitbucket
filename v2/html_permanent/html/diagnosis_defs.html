diagnosis

(ˌdaɪəɡˈnəʊsɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ses (-siːz)1. a. the identification of diseases by the examination of symptoms and signs and by other investigationsb. an opinion or conclusion so reached2. a. thorough analysis of facts or problems in order to gain understanding and aid future planningb. an opinion or conclusion reached through such analysis3. a detailed description of an organism, esp a plant, for the purpose of classification
Word origin
C17: New Latin, from Greek: a distinguishing, from diagignōskein to distinguish, from gignōskein to perceive, know

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounDiagnosis is the discovery and naming of what is wrong with someone who is ill or with something that is not working properly. ⇒ I need to have a second test to confirm the diagnosis. ⇒ Symptoms may not appear for some weeks, so diagnosis can be difficult.

Atropos

(ˈætrəˌpɒs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounGreek mythology the one of the three Fates who severs the thread of life
Word origin
Greek, from atropos that may not be turned, from a-1 + -tropos from trepein to turn
Example sentences containing 'Atropos'
Atropos , on station well away from Base Six, was now out of communication. Niven, Larry & Pournelle, Jerry THE MOAT AROUND MURCHESON'S EYE

terrain

(təˈreɪn , ˈtɛreɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. ground or a piece of ground, esp with reference to its physical character or military potential ⇒ radio reception can be difficult in mountainous terrain, ⇒ a rocky terrain2. a variant spelling of terrane

Word Origin

C18: from French, ultimately from Latin terrēnum ground, from terra earth

sandpit

(ˈsændˌpɪt )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a shallow pit or container holding sand for children to play in2. a pit from which sand is extracted3. an event organized by a governmental body, business enterprise, etc at which specialists from a wide range of disciplines are brought together to try to find a balanced solution to a given problem

avoidance

(əˈvɔɪdəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of keeping away from or preventing from happening2. lawa. the act of annulling or making voidb. the countering of an opponent's plea with fresh evidence3. ecclesiastical law the state of a benefice having no incumbent

eunuch

(ˈjuːnək )
Definitions
noun1. a man who has been castrated, esp (formerly) for some office such as a guard in a harem2. informal an ineffective man ⇒ a political eunuch
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: via Latin from Greek eunoukhos attendant of the bedchamber, from eunē bed + ekhein to have, keep

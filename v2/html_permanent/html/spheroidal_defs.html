spheroidal

(sfɪəˈrɔɪdəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. shaped like an ellipsoid of revolution; approximately spherical2. of or relating to an ellipsoid of revolution

Derived Forms

spheroidally (spheˈroidally) or spheroidically (spheˈroidically) adverb

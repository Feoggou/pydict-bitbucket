overt

(ˈəʊvɜːt , əʊˈvɜːt )
Definitions
adjective1. open to view; observable2. law open; deliberate. Criminal intent may be inferred from an overt act
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

overtly (ˈovertly) adverbovertness (ˈovertness) noun

Word Origin

C14: via Old French, from ovrir to open, from Latin aperīre

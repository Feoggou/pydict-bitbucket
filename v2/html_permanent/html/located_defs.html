located

Powered by COBUILD
(loʊkeɪtɪd , US loʊkeɪt- ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [adv ADJ]If something is located in a particular place, it is present or has been built there. [formal] ⇒ The restaurant is located near the cathedral. [+ within] ⇒ A boutique and beauty salon are conveniently located within the grounds. ⇒ ...well-located buildings.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

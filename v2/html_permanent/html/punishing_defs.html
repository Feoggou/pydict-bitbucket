Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A punishing schedule, activity, or experience requires a lot of physical effort and makes you very tired or weak. ⇒ He claimed his punishing work schedule had made him resort to taking the drug. ⇒ Besides diets, he devised punishing exercise routines.

A psychiatrist would say she is punishing me through her boyfriends for marrying Sophie.

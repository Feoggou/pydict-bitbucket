changer

(ˈtʃeɪndʒər )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  a person or thing that changes something2.  record changer3.  a device that automatically sets compact discs in position for playing in succession, as from a carousel tray or slotted magazine cartridge

Jehoshaphat

(dʒɪˈhɒʃəˌfæt , -ˈhɒs-)
Definitions
noun Old Testament1. the king of Judah (?873–?849 bc) (I Kings 22:41–50)2. the site of Jehovah's apocalyptic judgment upon the nations (Joel 4:14)

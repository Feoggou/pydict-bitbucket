Thessaly

(ˈθɛsəlɪ )
Definitions
nouna region of E Central Greece, on the Aegean: an extensive fertile plain, edged with mountains. Pop: 609 100 (2001). Area: 14 037 sq km (5418 sq miles). Modern Greek name: Thessalía (ˌθɛsaˈljia)

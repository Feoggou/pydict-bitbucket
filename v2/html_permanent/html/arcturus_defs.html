Arcturus

(ɑːkˈtjʊərəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe brightest star in the constellation Boötes: a red giant. Visual magnitude: –0.4; spectral type: K2III; distance: 37 light years
Derived forms
Arcturian (Arcˈturian) adjective
Word origin
C14: from Latin, from Greek Arktouros, from arktos bear + ouros guard, keeper

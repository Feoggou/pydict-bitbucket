unsatisfactory for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnsætɪsfæktəri )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as unsatisfactory, you mean that it is not as good as it should be, and cannot be considered acceptable. ⇒ The inspectors said just under a third of lessons were unsatisfactory. ⇒ He asked a few more questions, to which he received unsatisfactory answers.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

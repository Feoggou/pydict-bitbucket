whilom

(ˈwaɪləm ) archaic
Definitions
adverb1. formerly; onceadjective2. (prenominal) one-time; former
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English hwīlum, dative plural of hwīl while; related to Old High German hwīlōm, German weiland of old

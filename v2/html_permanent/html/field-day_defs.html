Word Frequency

field day

Definitions
noun1. a day spent in some special outdoor activity, such as nature study or sport2. a day-long competition between amateur radio operators using battery or generator power, the aim being to make the most contacts with other operators around the world3. military a day devoted to manoeuvres or exercises, esp before an audience4. informal a day or time of exciting or successful activity ⇒ the children had a field day with their new toys5. Australiana. a day or series of days devoted to the demonstration of farm machinery in country centresb. a combined open day and sale on a stud property

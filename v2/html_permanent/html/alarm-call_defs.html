alarm call

(əˈlɑːm kɔːl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna telephone call that someone arranges to have made in order to wake him or her up in the morning ⇒ I've arranged an alarm call for 6am.
Example sentences containing 'alarm call'
Nearby she heard the sharp alarm call of a small bird and she screwed up her eyes, trying to see it. Barbara Erskine SANDS OF TIMESomewhere outside a pheasant shrieked its alarm call into the pre-dawn darkness and fell silent again. Erskine, Barbara MIDNIGHT IS A LONELY PLACEBefore turning in, Raven checked that the ashes were still in the room safe, took a laxative, and put in an alarm call. Forbes, Bryan A SONG AT TWILIGHT

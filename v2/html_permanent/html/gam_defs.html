gam1

(ɡæm )
Definitions
noun1. a school of whales2. nautical an informal visit between crew members of whalers3. New Zealand a flock of large sea birdsverbWord forms: gams, gamming or gammed4. (intransitive) (of whales) to form a school5. nautical (of members of the crews of whalers) to visit (each other) informally6. (transitive) US to visit or exchange visits with
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: perhaps dialect variant of game1

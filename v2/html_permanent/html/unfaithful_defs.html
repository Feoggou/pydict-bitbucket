unfaithful

(ʌnˈfeɪθfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. not true to a promise, vow, etc2. not true to a wife, husband, lover, etc, esp in having sexual intercourse with someone else3. inaccurate; inexact; unreliable; untrustworthy ⇒ unfaithful copy4. obsolete not having religious faith; infidel5. obsolete not upright; dishonest
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unfaithfully (unˈfaithfully) adverbunfaithfulness (unˈfaithfulness) noun

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone is unfaithful to their lover or to the person they are married to, they have a sexual relationship with someone else. [+ to] ⇒ James had been unfaithful to Christine for the entire four years they'd been together. ⇒ ...her unfaithful husband.

flowery

(ˈflaʊərɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. abounding in flowers2. decorated with flowers or floral patterns3. like or suggestive of flowers ⇒ a flowery scent4. (of language or style) elaborate; ornate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

(flaʊəri ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA flowery smell is strong and sweet, like flowers. ⇒ Amy thought she caught the faintest drift of Isabel's flowery perfume.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Flowery cloth, paper, or china has a lot of flowers printed or painted on it. ⇒ The baby, dressed in a flowery jumpsuit, waved her rattle.3. graded adjective & adjectiveFlowery speech or writing contains long or literary words and expressions. ⇒ They were using uncommonly flowery language.

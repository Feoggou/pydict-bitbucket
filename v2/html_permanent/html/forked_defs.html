forked

(fɔːkt , ˈfɔːkɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. a. having a fork or forklike partsb. (in combination) ⇒ two-forked2. having sharp angles; zigzag3. insincere or equivocal (esp in the phrase forked tongue)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

forkedly (ˈfɔːkɪdlɪ ) adverbforkedness (ˈforkedness) noun

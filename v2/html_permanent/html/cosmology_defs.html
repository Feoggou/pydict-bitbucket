cosmology

(kɒzˈmɒlədʒɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the philosophical study of the origin and nature of the universe2. the branch of astronomy concerned with the evolution and structure of the universe3. a particular account of the origin or structure of the universe ⇒ Ptolemaic cosmology
Derived forms
cosmological (ˌkɒzməˈlɒdʒɪkəl ) or ˌcosmologic (ˌcosmoˈlogic) adjectiveˌcosmologically (ˌcosmoˈlogically) adverbcosmologist (cosˈmologist) noun

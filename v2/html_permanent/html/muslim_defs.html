Muslim

(ˈmʊzlɪm , ˈmʌz-) or Moslem
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -lims or -lim1. a follower of the religion of Islamadjective2. of or relating to Islam, its doctrines, culture, etc

Derived Forms

Muslimism (ˈMuslimism) or Moslemism (ˈMoslemism) noun old-fashioned, derogatory

Word Origin

C17: from Arabic, literally: one who surrenders

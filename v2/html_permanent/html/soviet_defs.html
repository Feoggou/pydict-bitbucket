soviet

(ˈsəʊvɪət , ˈsɒv-)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (in the former Soviet Union) an elected government council at the local, regional, and national levels, which culminated in the Supreme Soviet2. (in prerevolutionary Russia) a local revolutionary counciladjective3. of or relating to a soviet

Word Origin

C20: from Russian sovyet council, from Old Russian sŭvĕtŭ

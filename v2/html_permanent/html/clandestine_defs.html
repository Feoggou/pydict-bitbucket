Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivesecret and concealed, often for illicit reasons; furtive
Derived forms
clandestinely (clanˈdestinely) adverbclandestineness (clanˈdestineness) noun
Word origin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Something that is clandestine is hidden or kept secret, often because it is illegal. [formal] ⇒ ...their clandestine meetings.clandestinely adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ He left the country clandestinely.

destitute

(ˈdɛstɪˌtjuːt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. lacking the means of subsistence; totally impoverished2. (postpositive; foll by of) completely lacking; deprived or bereft (of) ⇒ destitute of words3. obsolete abandoned or deserted
Derived forms
destiˌtuteness (ˈdestiˌtuteness) noun
Word origin
C14: from Latin dēstitūtus forsaken, from dēstituere to leave alone, from statuere to place

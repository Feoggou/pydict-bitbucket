quay

(kiː )
Definitions
nouna wharf, typically one built parallel to the shoreline. Compare pier (sense 1)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14 keye, from Old French kai, of Celtic origin; compare Cornish kē hedge, fence, Old Breton cai fence

Definitions
countable nounA quay is a long platform beside the sea or a river where boats can be tied up and loaded or unloaded. ⇒ Jack and Stephen were waiting for them on the quay.

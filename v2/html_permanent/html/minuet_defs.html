minuet

(ˌmɪnjʊˈɛt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a stately court dance of the 17th and 18th centuries in triple time2. a piece of music composed for or in the rhythm of this dance, sometimes as a movement in a suite, sonata, or symphony. See also scherzo

Word Origin

C17: from French menuet dainty (referring to the dance steps), from menu small

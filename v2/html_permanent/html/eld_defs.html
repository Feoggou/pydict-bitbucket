eld

(ɛld ) or eild (eɪld)
Definitions
noun archaic1. old age2. olden days; antiquity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English eldu; related to Old Norse elli; see old
Example sentences containing 'eld'
From there, the rumours and grumbling wandered farther a. eld. Robin Hobb THE GOLDEN FOOL: BOOK TWO OF THE TAWNY MAN

concha

(ˈkɒŋkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -chae (-kiː)1. any bodily organ or part resembling a shell in shape, such as the external ear2. Also called: conch architecture the half dome of an apse
Derived forms
conchal (ˈconchal) adjective
Example sentences containing 'concha'
He held a battered black hat with a silver concha band in his lap and looked relaxed and comfortable. Tony Hillerman THE FALLEN MAN

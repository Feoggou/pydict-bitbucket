agriculture

(ˈæɡrɪˌkʌltʃə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe science or occupation of cultivating land and rearing crops and livestock; farming; husbandry ▶ Related adjective: geoponic
Derived forms
ˌagricultural (ˌagriˈcultural) adjectiveˌagriculturally (ˌagriˈculturally) adverbˌagriculturist (ˌagriˈculturist) or ˌagriculturalist (ˌagriˈculturalist) noun
Word origin
C17: from Latin agricultūra, from ager field, land + cultūra culture

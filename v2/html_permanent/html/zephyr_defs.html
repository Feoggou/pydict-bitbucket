zephyr

(ˈzɛfə )
Definitions
noun1. a soft or gentle breeze2. any of several delicate soft yarns, fabrics, or garments, usually of wool
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin zephyrus, from Greek zephuros the west wind; probably related to Greek zophos darkness, west

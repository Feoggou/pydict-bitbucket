works

(wɜːks )
Definitions
plural noun1. (often functioning as singular) a place where a number of people are employed, such as a factory2. the sum total of a writer's or artist's achievements, esp when considered together ⇒ the works of Shakespeare3. the deeds of a person, esp virtuous or moral deeds performed as religious acts ⇒ works of charity4. the interior parts of the mechanism of a machine, etc ⇒ the works of a clock5. See in the works6. See spanner in the works7. See the works8. slang a syringe9. (modifier) of or denoting a racing car, etc, that is officially entered by a manufacturer in an event ⇒ a works entry

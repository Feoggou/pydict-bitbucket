Moravia1

(məˈreɪvɪə , mɒ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna region of the Czech Republic around the Morava River, bounded by the Bohemian-Moravian Highlands, the Sudeten Mountains, and the W Carpathians: became a separate Austrian crownland in 1848; part of Czechoslovakia 1918–92; valuable mineral resources. Czech name: Morava. German name: Mähren

enactment

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnæktmənt ) for learners of English
Word forms: enactments
Definitions
1. variable nounThe enactment of a law is the process in a parliament or other law-making body by which the law is agreed upon and made official. [technical] [+ of] ⇒ We support the call for the enactment of a Bill of Rights.2. variable nounThe enactment of a play or story is the performance of it by an actor or group of actors. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ The main building was also used for the enactment of mystery plays.

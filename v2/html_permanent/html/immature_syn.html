Synonyms of immature

immature

adjective

  1. young, adolescent, undeveloped, green, raw, premature, unfinished, imperfect, untimely, unripe, unformed, unseasonable, unfledged
    • The birds were in immature plumage.
  2. childish, juvenile, infantile, puerile, callow, babyish, wet behind the ears (informal), jejune
    • You're just being childish and immature.
    OPPOSITES: developed, adult, mature, ripe, mellow, fully-fledged

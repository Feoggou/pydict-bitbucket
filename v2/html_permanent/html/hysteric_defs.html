hysteric

(hɪˈstɛrɪk )
Definitions
noun1. a hysterical personadjective2. hysterical
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin hystericus literally: of the womb, from Greek husterikos, from hustera the womb; from the belief that hysteria in women originated in disorders of the womb

Whatever she had said seemed to have led Downing to assume Mike was an incompetent hysteric ! Barbara Erskine HIDING FROM THE LIGHTShe was a neurotic hysteric with a pathological guilt complex. Stewart, Michael COMPULSIONIf she didn't make a report, then she was a silly hysteric who had acted on groundless fears. Laura Lippman EVERY SECRET THING

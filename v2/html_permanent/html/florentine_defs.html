Florentine

(ˈflɒrənˌtaɪn )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to Florence2. (usually postpositive) (of food) served or prepared with spinachnoun3. a native or inhabitant of Florence4. a biscuit containing nuts and dried fruit and coated with chocolate5. a type of domestic fancy pigeon somewhat resembling the Modena

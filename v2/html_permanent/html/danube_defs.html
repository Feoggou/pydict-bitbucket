Danube

(ˈdænjuːb )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna river in central and SE Europe, rising in the Black Forest in Germany and flowing to the Black Sea. Length: 2859 km (1776 miles). German name: Donau. Czech name: Dunaj. Hungarian name: Duna. Croatian and Serbian name: Dunav (ˈdunaf). Romanian name: Dunărea

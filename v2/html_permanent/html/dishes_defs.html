dishes

(ˈdɪʃɪz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounall the objects that have been used to cook, serve, and eat a meal ⇒ He'd cooked dinner and washed the dishes., ⇒ There were dirty dishes in the sink., ⇒ He never does the dishes., ⇒ I hate doing the dishes.

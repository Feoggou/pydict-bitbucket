swelling

(ˈswɛlɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of expansion or inflation2. the state of being or becoming swollen3. a swollen or inflated part or area4. an abnormal enlargement of a bodily structure or part, esp as the result of injurytumescent

(swelɪŋ )
Word forms: swellings
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounA swelling is a raised, curved shape on the surface of your body which appears as a result of an injury or an illness. ⇒ His eye was partly closed, and there was a swelling over his lid. ⇒ There is some swelling and he is being detained for observation.

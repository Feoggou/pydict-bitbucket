custodian

(kʌsˈtəʊdɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who has custody, as of a prisoner, ward, etc2. a guardian or keeper, as of an art collection, etc

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounThe custodian of an official building, a companies' assets, or something else valuable is the person who is officially in charge of it. [+ of] ⇒ ...the custodian of the holy shrines in Mecca and Medina.

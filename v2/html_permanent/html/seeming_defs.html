Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]Seeming means appearing to be the case, but not necessarily the case. For example, if you talk about someone's seeming ability to do something, you mean that they appear to be able to do it, but you are not certain. [formal, vagueness] ⇒ Wall Street analysts have been highly critical of the company's seeming inability to control costs. ⇒ Whatever troubles arise, we'll have peace of mind amidst seeming chaos.

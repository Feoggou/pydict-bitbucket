potato

(pəˈteɪtəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -toes1. Also called: Irish potato, white potatoa. a solanaceous plant, Solanum tuberosum, of South America: widely cultivated for its edible tubersb. the starchy oval tuber of this plant, which has a brown or red skin and is cooked and eaten as a vegetable2. any of various similar plants, esp the sweet potato3. See hot potato

Word Origin

C16: from Spanish patata white potato, from Taino batata sweet potato

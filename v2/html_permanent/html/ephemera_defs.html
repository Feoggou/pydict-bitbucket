ephemera

(ɪˈfɛmərə )
Definitions
noun plural -eras or -erae (-əˌriː)1. a mayfly, esp one of the genus Ephemera2. something transitory or short-lived3. (functioning as plural) a class of collectable items not originally intended to last for more than a short time, such as tickets, posters, postcards, or labels4. a plural of ephemeron
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16; see ephemeral

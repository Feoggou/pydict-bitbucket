precursor

(prɪˈkɜːsə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that precedes and shows or announces someone or something to come; harbinger2. a predecessor or forerunner3. a chemical substance that gives rise to another more important substance

Word Origin

C16: from Latin praecursor one who runs in front, from praecurrere, from prae in front + currere to run

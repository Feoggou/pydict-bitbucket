thumbnail

(ˈθʌmˌneɪl )
Definitions
noun1. the nail of the thumb2. (modifier) concise and brief ⇒ a thumbnail sketch3. computing a small image which can be expanded
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
1. countable nounYour thumbnail is the nail on your thumb.2. adjective [ADJ n]A thumbnail sketch or account is a very short description of an event, idea, or plan which gives only the main details.

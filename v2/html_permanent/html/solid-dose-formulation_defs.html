solid dose formulation

Powered by COBUILD
(sɒlɪd doʊs fɔrmyəleɪʃən)
Word forms: (regular plural) solid dose formulations
Definitions
COBUILD Key Words for the Pharmaceutical Industry
noun(Pharmaceutical: Dosage forms) A solid dose formation is a hard tablet made by compressing medicine in a powder form. ⇒ Patients who have difficulty swallowing tablets can change a solid dose formulation by crushing it into powder. ⇒ Some solid dose formulations can be dissolved with water in the mouth rather than swallowed whole. ⇒ A solid dose formation is a hard tablet made by compressing medicine in a powder form. ▶  dose, formulation

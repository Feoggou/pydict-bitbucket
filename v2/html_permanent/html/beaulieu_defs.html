Beaulieu

(ˈbjuːlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna village in S England, in Hampshire: site of Palace House, seat of Lord Montagu and once the gatehouse of the ruined 13th-century abbey; the National Motor Museum is in its grounds. Pop: 809 (2001)

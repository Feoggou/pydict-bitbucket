ravenous

(ˈrævənəs )
Definitions
adjective1. famished; starving2. rapacious; voracious
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ravenously (ˈravenously) adverbravenousness (ˈravenousness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Old French ravineux, from Latin rapīna plunder, from rapere to seize

Definitions
adjectiveIf you are ravenous, you are extremely hungry. ⇒ Amy realized that she had eaten nothing since leaving Bruton Street, and she was ravenous. ⇒ ...a pack of ravenous animals.ravenously adverb ⇒ She began to eat ravenously. ⇒ She emerged looking ravenously hungry.

outcrop

Definitions
noun (ˈaʊtˌkrɒp )1. part of a rock formation or mineral vein that appears at the surface of the earth2. an emergence; appearanceverb (ˌaʊtˈkrɒp ) -crops, -cropping or -cropped (intransitive)3. (of rock strata, mineral veins, etc) to protrude through the surface of the earth4. another word for crop out

eiderdown

(ˈaɪdəˌdaʊn )
Definitions
noun1. the breast down of the female eider duck, with which it lines the nest, used for stuffing pillows, quilts, etc2. a thick warm cover for a bed, made of two layers of material enclosing a soft filling3. US a warm cotton fabric having a woollen nap

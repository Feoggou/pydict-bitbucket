outer

(ˈaʊtə )
Definitions
adjective (prenominal)1. being or located on the outside; external2. further from the middle or central partnoun3. archerya. the white outermost ring on a targetb. a shot that hits this ring4. Australian the unsheltered part of the spectator area at a sports ground5. See on the outer

outer for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(aʊtəʳ )
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]The outer parts of something are the parts which contain or enclose the other parts, and which are furthest from the centre. ⇒ He heard a voice in the outer room. ⇒ ...the outer suburbs of the city.

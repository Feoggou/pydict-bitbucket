indelicate

(ɪnˈdɛlɪkɪt )
Definitions
adjective1. coarse, crude, or rough2. offensive, embarrassing, or tasteless
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

indelicacy (inˈdelicacy) or indelicateness (inˈdelicateness) nounindelicately (inˈdelicately) adverb

indelicate

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndelɪkət ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf something or someone is indelicate, they are rude or embarrassing. [formal] ⇒ She really could not touch upon such an indelicate subject.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone as conniving, you mean you dislike them because they make secret plans in order to get things for themselves or harm other people. [disapproval] ⇒ Edith was seen as a conniving, greedy woman.

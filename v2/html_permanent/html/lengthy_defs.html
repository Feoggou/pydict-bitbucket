(leŋθi ) for learners of English
Word forms: lengthier , lengthiest
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]You use lengthy to describe an event or process which lasts for a long time. ⇒ ...a lengthy meeting. ⇒ ...the lengthy process of filling out passport application forms.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A lengthy report, article, book, or document contains a lot of speech, writing, or other material. ⇒ ...a lengthy report from the Council of Ministers. ⇒ There is a lengthy article on Spike Milligan in the Observer newspaper.

crèche - French

(krɛʃ , kreɪʃ , krɛʃ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. mainly Britisha. a day nursery for very young childrenb. a supervised play area provided for young children for short periods2. a tableau of Christ's Nativity3. a foundling home or hospital
Word origin
C19: from Old French: manger, crib, ultimately of Germanic origin; compare Old High German kripja crib

regale

(rɪˈɡeɪl )
Definitions
verb (transitive; usually foll by with)1. to give delight or amusement to ⇒ he regaled them with stories of his youth2. to provide with choice or abundant food or drinknoun3. archaica. a feastb. a delicacy of food or drink
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

regalement (reˈgalement) noun

Word Origin

C17: from French régaler, from gale pleasure; related to Middle Dutch wale riches; see also gala

ill at ease

(ɪl ət iːz)
Definitions
adjectiveunable to relax; uncomfortable ⇒ Do people seem anxious and ill at ease around you?, ⇒ He looked ill at ease., ⇒ We don't want people to feel ill at ease when they visit., ⇒ She seemed ill at ease, preoccupied., ⇒ He was ill at ease with people whom he didn't understand., ⇒ She felt ill at ease with children.

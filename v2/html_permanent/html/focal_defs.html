focal

(ˈfəʊkəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to a focus2. situated at, passing through, or measured from the focus
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

focally (ˈfocally) adverb

focal

Powered by COBUILD
(foʊkəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Focal is used to describe something that relates to the point where a number of rays or lines meet. ⇒ ...the focal plane of the telescope.2. adjective [ADJ n]Focal is used to describe something that is very important. ⇒ ...one of the focal centres of the Far East.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

civil rights

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. the personal rights of the individual citizen, in most countries upheld by law, as in the US2. (modifier) of, relating to, or promoting equality in social, economic, and political rights

plural noun [oft N n]Civil rights are the rights that people have in a society to equal treatment and equal opportunities, whatever their race, sex, or religion. ⇒ ...the civil rights movement. ⇒ ...violations of civil rights.

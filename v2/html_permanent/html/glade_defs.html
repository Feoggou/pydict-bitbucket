glade

(ɡleɪd )
Definitions
nounan open place in a forest; clearing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gladelike (ˈgladeˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C16: of uncertain origin; perhaps related to glad1 (in obsolete sense: bright); see gleam

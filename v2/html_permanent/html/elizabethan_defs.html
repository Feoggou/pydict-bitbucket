Elizabethan

(ɪˌlɪzəˈbiːθən )
Definitions
adjective1. of, characteristic of, or relating to England or its culture in the age of Elizabeth I or to the United Kingdom or its culture in the age of Elizabeth II2. of, relating to, or designating a style of architecture used in England during the reign of Elizabeth I, characterized by moulded and sculptured ornament based on German and Flemish modelsnoun3. a person who lived in England during the reign of Elizabeth I

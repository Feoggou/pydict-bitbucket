shiny

(ˈʃaɪnɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: shinier or shiniest1. glossy or polished; bright2. (of clothes or material) worn to a smooth and glossy state, as by continual rubbing

Derived Forms

shininess (ˈshininess) noun

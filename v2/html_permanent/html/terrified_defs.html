terrified

(ˈtɛrɪfaɪd)
Definitions
adjectivevery frightened ⇒ I was absolutely terrified when I heard the door opening., ⇒ I'm terrified to think about the fact that my mother will die someday., ⇒ I was terrified!, ⇒ She was terrified of public appearances, ⇒ Blair is notoriously terrified of risk, ⇒ He was terrified of heights., ⇒ She was terrified that Ronnie would kidnap Sam.

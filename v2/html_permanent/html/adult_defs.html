adult

(ˈædʌlt , əˈdʌlt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having reached maturity; fully developed2. of or intended for mature people ⇒ adult education3. regarded as suitable only for adults, because of being pornographic ⇒ adult films and magazinesnoun4. a person who has attained maturity; a grownup5. a mature fully grown animal or plant6. law a person who has attained the age of legal majority (18 years for most purposes). Compare infant
Derived forms
ˈadulthood noun
Word origin
C16: from Latin adultus, from adolēscere to grow up, from alēscere to grow, from alēre to feed, nourish

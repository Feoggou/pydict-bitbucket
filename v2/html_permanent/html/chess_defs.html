chess1

(tʃɛs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna game of skill for two players using a chessboard on which chessmen are moved. Initially each player has one king, one queen, two rooks, two bishops, two knights, and eight pawns, which have different types of moves according to kind. The object is to checkmate the opponent's king
Word origin
C13: from Old French esches, plural of eschec check (at chess); see check

Cassini

(kæˈsiːnɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Giovanni Domenico. 1625–1712, French astronomer, born in Italy. He discovered (1675) Cassini's division, the gap that divides Saturn's rings into two parts, and four of Saturn's moons

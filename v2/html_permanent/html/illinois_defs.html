Illinois

(ˌɪlɪˈnɔɪ )
Definitions
noun1. a state of the N central US, in the Midwest: consists of level prairie crossed by the Illinois and Kaskaskia Rivers; mainly agricultural. Capital: Springfield. Pop: 12 653 544 (2003 est). Area: 144 858 sq km (55 930 sq miles). Abbreviation: Ill. or (with zip code) IL2. a river in Illinois, flowing SW to the Mississippi. Length: 439 km (273 miles)

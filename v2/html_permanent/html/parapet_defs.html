parapet

(ˈpærəpɪt , -ˌpɛt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a low wall or railing along the edge of a balcony, roof, etc2. Also called: breastwork a rampart, mound of sandbags, bank, etc, in front of a trench, giving protection from fire from the front

Word Origin

C16: from Italian parapetto, literally: chest-high wall, from para-2 + petto, from Latin pectus breast

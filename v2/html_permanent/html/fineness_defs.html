fineness

(ˈfaɪnnɪs )
Definitions
noun1. the state or quality of being fine2. a measurement of the purity of precious metal, expressed as the number of parts per thousand that is precious metal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Had I not been concentrating on other things, I too would have been rendered senseless by the fineness of the food. Edward Docx THE CALLIGRAPHER"Oh, no one will take fineness from a morning," says Clara. Gregory Maguire CONFESSIONS OF AN UGLY STEPSISTER

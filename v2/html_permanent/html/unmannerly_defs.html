unmannerly

(ʌnˈmænəlɪ ) or unmanneredly (ʌnˈmænədlɪ)
Definitions
adjective1. lacking manners; discourteousadverb2. archaic rudely; discourteously
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unmannerliness (unˈmannerliness) noun
Example sentences containing 'unmannerly'
To your English upper class, industry and trade are symbols of gross Philistinism, boring crassness, unmannerly usury, unacceptable. Malcolm, John GOTHIC PURSUIT

  2. wonderful, great (informal), excellent, brilliant, amazing, fantastic (informal), tremendous (informal), marvellous, sensational (informal), awesome (informal), booshit (Australian, slang), exo (Australian, slang), sik (Australian, slang), rad (informal), phat (slang), schmick (Australian, informal)

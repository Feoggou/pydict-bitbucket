elate

(ɪˈleɪt )
Definitions
verb(transitive) to fill with high spirits, exhilaration, pride or optimism
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin ēlāt- stem of past participle of efferre to bear away, from ferre to carry
Example sentences containing 'elate'
The fact that she was to be the wife of the Ambassador to France seemed to frighten her rather than elate her. Gaskin, Catherine THE AMBASSADOR'S WOMEN

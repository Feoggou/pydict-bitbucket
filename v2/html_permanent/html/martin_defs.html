martin

(ˈmɑːtɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany of various swallows of the genera Progne, Delichon, Riparia, etc, having a square or slightly forked tail. See also house martin

Word Origin

C15: perhaps from St Martin, because the birds were believed to migrate at the time of Martinmas

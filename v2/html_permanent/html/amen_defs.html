amen

(ˌeɪˈmɛn , ˌɑːˈmɛn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
exclamation1. so be it!: a term used at the end of a prayer or religious statementnoun2. the use of the word amen, as at the end of a prayer3. See say amen to
Word origin
C13: via Late Latin via Greek from Hebrew āmēn certainly

Amen

, Amon or Amūn (ˈɑːmən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounEgyptian mythology a local Theban god, having a ram's head and symbolizing life and fertility, identified by the Egyptians with the national deity Amen-Ra

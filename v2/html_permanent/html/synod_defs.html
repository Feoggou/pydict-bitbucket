synod

(ˈsɪnəd , ˈsɪnɒd )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna local or special ecclesiastical council, esp of a diocese, formally convened to discuss ecclesiastical affairs

Derived Forms

synodal (ˈsynodal) or rare synodical (synˈodical) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Late Latin synodus, from Greek sunodos, from syn- + hodos a way

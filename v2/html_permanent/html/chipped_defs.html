chipped

(tʃɪpt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. damaged by having a small piece broken off ⇒ A chipped bone in his ankle had required surgery., ⇒ They drank out of chipped mugs.2. flaking; in bad condition ⇒ The wagon's paint was badly chipped on the outside.3. electronics fitted with a microchip

Cavan

(ˈkævən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a county of N Republic of Ireland: hilly, with many small lakes and bogs. County town: Cavan. Pop: 56 546 (2002). Area: 1890 sq km (730 sq miles)2. a market town in N Republic of Ireland, county town of Co Cavan. Pop: 6098 (2002)

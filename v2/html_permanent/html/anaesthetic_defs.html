Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable noun [oft under N]Anaesthetic is a substance that doctors use to stop you feeling pain during an operation, either in the whole of your body when you are unconscious, or in a part of your body when you are awake. ⇒ The operation is carried out under a general anaesthetic.

vascular

(ˈvæskjʊlə )
Definitions
adjectivebiology, anatomy of, relating to, or having vessels that conduct and circulate liquids ⇒ a vascular bundle, ⇒ the blood vascular system
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vascularity (ˌvæskjʊˈlærɪtɪ ) nounvascularly (ˈvascularly) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from New Latin vāsculāris, from Latin: vasculum

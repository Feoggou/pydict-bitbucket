(driːmlænd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. singular nounIf you describe a place as a dreamland, you mean that it is so beautiful it is hard to believe that it is real. ⇒ ...a dreamland of snowy moonlit peaks and twinkling lights.2. uncount noun [oft a N]If you refer to a situation as dreamland, you mean that it represents what someone would like to happen, but that it is completely unrealistic. ⇒ In dreamland we play them in the final.

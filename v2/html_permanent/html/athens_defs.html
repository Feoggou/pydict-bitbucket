Athens

(ˈæθɪnz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe capital of Greece, in the southeast near the Saronic Gulf: became capital after independence in 1834; ancient city-state, most powerful in the 5th century bc; contains the hill citadel of the Acropolis. Pop: 3 238 000 (2005 est). Greek name: Athinai (aˈθinɛ), Athina (aˈθina)

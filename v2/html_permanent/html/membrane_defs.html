membrane

(ˈmɛmbreɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any thin pliable sheet of material2. a pliable sheetlike usually fibrous tissue that covers, lines, or connects plant and animal organs or cells3. biology a double layer of lipid, containing some proteins, that surrounds biological cells and some of their internal structures4. physics a two-dimensional entity postulated as a fundamental constituent of matter in superstring theories of particle physics5. a skin of parchment forming part of a roll

Word Origin

C16: from Latin membrāna skin covering a part of the body, from membrum member

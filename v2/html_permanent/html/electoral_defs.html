Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]Electoral is used to describe things that are connected with elections. ⇒ The Mongolian Democratic Party is campaigning for electoral reform. ⇒ ...Italy's electoral system of proportional representation.electorally adverb [ADV adj/-ed] [ADV after v] ⇒ He believed that the policies were both wrong and electorally disastrous.

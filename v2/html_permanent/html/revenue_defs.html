revenue

(ˈrɛvɪˌnjuː )
Definitions
noun1. the income accruing from taxation to a government during a specified period of time, usually a year2. a. a government department responsible for the collection of government revenueb. (as modifier) ⇒ revenue men3. the gross income from a business enterprise, investment, property, etc4. a particular item of income5. something that yields a regular financial return; source of income
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

revenued (ˈreveˌnued) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Old French, from revenir to return, from Latin revenīre; see revenant

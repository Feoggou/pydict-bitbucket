Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnweəri )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone as unwary, you mean that they are not cautious or experienced and are therefore likely to be harmed or deceived. [formal] ⇒ With its quicksands the river usually drowns a few unwary visitors every season.The unwary are people who are unwary. ⇒ Specialist subjects are full of pitfalls for the unwary.

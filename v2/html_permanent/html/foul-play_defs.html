foul play

Definitions
noun1. unfair or treacherous conduct esp with violence2. a violation of the rules in a game or sport

foul play

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounFoul play is criminal violence or activity that results in a person's death. ⇒ The report says it suspects foul play was involved in the deaths of two journalists.2. uncount nounFoul play is unfair or dishonest behaviour, especially during a sports game. ⇒ Players were warned twice for foul play.

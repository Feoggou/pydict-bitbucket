fust

(fʌst)
Definitions
noun1. obsolete a strong mouldy odour2. a type of cheese ⇒ Put a piece of fust in the middle of the dough.3. architecture dialect the ridge of a roofadjective4. a dialectal version of firstverb (intransitive)5. dialect to become mouldy; to become stale or stale-smellingadverb6. a dialectal version of first ⇒ Old master had abundance of books: sometimes would have twenty of 'em down on the floor at once; read fust one then tother.

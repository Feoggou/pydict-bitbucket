areola

(əˈrɪələ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -lae (-ˌliː) or -lasanatomy any small circular area, such as the pigmented ring around the human nipple or the inflamed area surrounding a pimple
Derived forms
aˈreolar or areolate (əˈrɪəlɪt , -ˌleɪt) adjectiveareolation (əˌrɪəˈleɪʃən ) noun
Word origin
C17: from Latin: diminutive of area

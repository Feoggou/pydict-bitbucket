dossier

(ˈdɒsɪˌeɪ , -sɪə, French dosje)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna collection of papers containing information on a particular subject or person
Word origin
C19: from French: a file with a label on the back, from dos back, from Latin dorsum

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA dossier is a collection of papers containing information on a particular event, or on a person such as a criminal or a spy. [+ of/on] ⇒ The company is compiling a dossier of evidence to back its allegations. ⇒ The government kept dossiers on thousands of its citizens.

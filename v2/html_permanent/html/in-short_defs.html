Definitions
phraseYou use in short when you have been giving a lot of details and you want to give a conclusion or summary. ⇒ Try tennis, badminton or windsurfing. In short, anything challenging. ⇒ She was surrounded by doctors, lawyers, housewives–people, in short, like herself. →  short
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

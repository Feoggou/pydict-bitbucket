prodigious

(prəˈdɪdʒəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. vast in size, extent, power, etc2. wonderful or amazing3. obsolete threatening

Derived Forms

prodigiously (proˈdigiously) adverbprodigiousness (proˈdigiousness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin prōdigiōsus marvellous, from prōdigium, see prodigy

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Something that is prodigious is very large or impressive. [literary] ⇒ This business generates cash in prodigious amounts. ⇒ He impressed all who met him with his prodigious memory.prodigiously graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ She ate prodigiously.

cyanide

(ˈsaɪəˌnaɪd ) or cyanid (ˈsaɪənɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any salt of hydrocyanic acid. Cyanides contain the ion CN– and are extremely poisonous2. another name (not in technical usage) for nitrile
Derived forms
ˌcyanidation (ˌcyaniˈdation) noun

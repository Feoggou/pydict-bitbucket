procedure

(prəˈsiːdʒə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a way of acting or progressing in a course of action, esp an established method2. the established mode or form of conducting the business of a legislature, the enforcement of a legal right, etc3. computing another name for subroutine

Derived Forms

procedural (proˈcedural) adjectiveprocedurally (proˈcedurally) adverb

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
variable nounA procedure is a way of doing something, especially the usual or correct way. ⇒ A biopsy is usually a minor surgical procedure. ⇒ Police insist that Michael did not follow the correct procedure in applying for a visa. ⇒ The White House said there would be no change in procedure.

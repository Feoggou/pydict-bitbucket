exterminate

(ɪkˈstɜːmɪˌneɪt ) or extermine (ɪkˈstɜːmɪn)
Definitions
verb(transitive) to destroy (living things, esp pests or vermin) completely; annihilate; eliminate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

exterminable (exˈterminable) adjectiveextermination (exˌtermiˈnation) nounexterminative (exˈterminative) or exterminatory (exˈterminatory) adjectiveexterminator (exˈtermiˌnator) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin extermināre to drive away, from terminus boundary

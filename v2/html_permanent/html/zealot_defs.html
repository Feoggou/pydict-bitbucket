zealot

(ˈzɛlət )
Definitions
nounan immoderate, fanatical, or extremely zealous adherent to a cause, esp a religious one
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Late Latin zēlōtēs, from Greek, from zēloun to be zealous, from zēlos zeal

Zealot

(ˈzɛlət )
Definitions
nounany of the members of an extreme Jewish sect or political party that resisted all aspects of Roman rule in Palestine in the 1st century ad
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
countable nounIf you describe someone as a zealot, you think that their views and actions are very extreme, especially in following a particular political or religious belief. [disapproval] ⇒ He was forceful, but by no means a zealot.

go in

Definitions
verb (intr, mainly adverb)1. to enter2. (preposition) See go into3. (of the sun) to become hidden behind a cloud4. to be assimilated or grasped ⇒ nothing much goes in if I try to read in the evenings5. cricket to begin an innings6. See go in for

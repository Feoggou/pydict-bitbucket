clanger

(ˈklæŋə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. informal a conspicuous mistake (esp in the phrase drop a clanger)2. something that clangs or causes a clang
Word origin
C20: from clang, referring to a mistake whose effects seem to clang

Bulgarian

(bʌlˈɡɛərɪən , bʊl-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Bulgaria, its people, or their languagenoun2. the official language of Bulgaria, belonging to the S Slavonic branch of the Indo-European family3. a native, inhabitant, or citizen of Bulgaria

(bʌlgeəriən )
Word forms: Bulgarians
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveBulgarian means belonging or relating to Bulgaria, or to its people, language, or culture.2. countable nounA Bulgarian is a person who comes from Bulgaria.3. uncount nounBulgarian is the main language spoken by people who live in Bulgaria.

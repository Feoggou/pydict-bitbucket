sublimation

(ˌsʌblɪˈmeɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (in Freudian psychology) the diversion of psychic energy derived from sexual impulses into nonsexual activity, esp of a creative nature2. the process or an instance of sublimating3. something sublimated4. chemistry the process or instance or subliming

greater

Powered by COBUILD
(greɪtəʳ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. Greater is the comparative of great.2. adjective [ADJ n]Greater is used with the name of a large city to refer to the city together with the surrounding urban and suburban area. ⇒ ...Greater London.3. adjective [ADJ n]Greater is used with the name of a country to refer to a larger area which includes that country and other land which used to belong to it, or which some people believe should belong to it. ⇒ ...greater Syria.

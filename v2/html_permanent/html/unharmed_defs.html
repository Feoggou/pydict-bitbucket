unharmed for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnhɑːʳmd )
Definitions
adjective [ADJ after v, v-link ADJ]If someone or something is unharmed after an accident or violent incident, they are not hurt or damaged in any way. ⇒ The car was a write-off, but everyone escaped unharmed. ⇒ His eleven-year-old daughter was unharmed in the attack. ⇒ The nine crew on the Boeing 747 were released unharmed.

genie

(ˈdʒiːnɪ )
Definitions
noun1. (in fairy tales and stories) a servant who appears by magic and fulfils a person's wishes2. another word for jinni
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from French génie, from Arabic jinni demon, influenced by Latin genius attendant spirit; see genius

aedes

(eɪˈiːdiːz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany mosquito of the genus Aedes (formerly Stegomyia) of tropical and subtropical regions, esp A. aegypti, which transmits yellow fever and dengue
Word origin
C20: New Latin, from Greek aēdēs unpleasant, from a-1 + ēdos pleasant

chariot

(ˈtʃærɪət )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a two-wheeled horse-drawn vehicle used in ancient Egypt, Greece, Rome, etc, in war, races, and processions2. a light four-wheeled horse-drawn ceremonial carriage3. poetic any stately vehicle
Word origin
C14: from Old French, augmentative of char car

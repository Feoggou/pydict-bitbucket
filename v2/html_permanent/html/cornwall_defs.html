Cornwall

(ˈkɔːnˌwɔːl , -wəl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna former administrative county of SW England; became a unitary authority in 2009: hilly, with a deeply indented coastline. Administrative centre: Truro. Pop: 513 500 (2003 est). Area: 3564 sq km (1376 sq miles). Cornish name: Kernow

extraction

(ɪkˈstrækʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act of extracting or the condition of being extracted2. something extracted; an extract3. a. the act or an instance of extracting a tooth or teethb. a tooth or teeth extracted4. origin, descent, lineage, or ancestry ⇒ of German extraction

Definitions

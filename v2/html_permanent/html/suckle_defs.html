suckle

(ˈsʌkəl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to give (a baby or young animal) milk from the breast or (of a baby, etc) to suck milk from the breast2. (transitive) to bring up; nurture

Derived Forms

suckler (ˈsuckler) noun

Word Origin

C15: probably back formation from suckling

waiver

(ˈweɪvə )
Definitions
noun1. the voluntary relinquishment, expressly or by implication, of some claim or right2. the act or an instance of relinquishing a claim or right3. a formal statement in writing of such relinquishment
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Old Northern French weyver to relinquish, waive

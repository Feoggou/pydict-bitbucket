Powered by COBUILD
(ɪgzæm ) for learners of English
Word forms: exams
Definitions
1. countable nounAn exam is a formal test that you take to show your knowledge or ability in a particular subject, or to obtain a qualification. ⇒ I don't want to take any more exams. ⇒ Kate's exam results were excellent.2. countable nounIf you have a medical exam, a doctor looks at your body, feels it, or does simple tests in order to check how healthy you are. [mainly US]

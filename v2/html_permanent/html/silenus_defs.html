Silenus

(saɪˈliːnəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Greek mythology1. chief of the satyrs and foster father to Dionysus: often depicted riding drunkenly on a donkey2. Word forms: plural Sileni (saɪˈliːnəɪ) (often not capital) one of a class of woodland deities, closely similar to the satyrs

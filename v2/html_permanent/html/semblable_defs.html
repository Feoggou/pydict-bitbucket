semblable

(ˈsɛmbləbəl ) archaic
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. resembling or similar2. apparent rather than realnoun3. something that resembles another thing4. a resemblance

Derived Forms

semblably (ˈsemblably) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Old French, from sembler to seem; see semblance

Caucasus

(ˈkɔːkəsəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun the Caucasus1. Also called: Caucasus Mountains a mountain range in SW Russia, running along the N borders of Georgia and Azerbaijan, between the Black Sea and the Caspian Sea: mostly over 2700 m (9000 ft). Highest peak: Mount Elbrus, 5642 m (18 510 ft)2. another name for Caucasia

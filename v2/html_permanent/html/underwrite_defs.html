underwrite

(ˈʊndəˌraɪt , ˌʌndəˈraɪt )
Definitions
verb -writes, -writing, -wrote or -written (transitive)1. finance to undertake to purchase at an agreed price any unsold portion of (a public issue of shares, etc)2. to accept financial responsibility for (a commercial project or enterprise)3. insurancea. to sign and issue (an insurance policy) thus accepting liability if specified losses occurb. to insure (a property or risk)c. to accept liability up to (a specified amount) in an insurance policy4. to write (words, a signature, etc) beneath (other written matter); subscribe5. to support or concur with (a decision, statement, etc) by or as if by signature

optical

(ˈɒptɪkəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, producing, or involving light2. of or relating to the eye or to the sense of sight; optic3. (esp of a lens) aiding vision or correcting a visual disorder
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

optically (ˈoptically) adverb

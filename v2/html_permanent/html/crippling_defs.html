Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]A crippling illness or disability is one that severely damages your health or your body. ⇒ Arthritis and rheumatism are prominent crippling diseases. ⇒ They both suffered from crippling pains in their hips.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]If you say that an action, policy, or situation has a crippling effect on something, you mean it has a very serious, harmful effect. ⇒ The high cost of capital has a crippling effect on many small American high-tech firms. ⇒ The American military presence on the islands had suffered a crippling blow.cripplingly adverb ⇒ ...cripplingly high interest rates.

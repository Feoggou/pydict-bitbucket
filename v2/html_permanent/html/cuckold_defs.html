cuckold

(ˈkʌkəld )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a man whose spouse has committed adultery, often regarded as an object of scornverb2. (transitive) to make a cuckold of
Derived forms
cuckoldry (ˈcuckoldry) noun
Word origin
C13 cukeweld, from Old French cucuault, from cucu cuckoo; perhaps an allusion to the parasitic cuckoos that lay their eggs in the nests of other birds

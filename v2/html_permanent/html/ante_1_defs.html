ante

(ˈæntɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the gaming stake put up before the deal in poker by the players2. informal a sum of money representing a person's share, as in a syndicate3. See up the anteverb -tes, -teing, -ted or -teed4. to place (one's stake) in poker5. (usually foll by up) informal, mainly US to pay

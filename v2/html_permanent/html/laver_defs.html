laver1

(ˈleɪvə )
Definitions
noun1. Old Testament a large basin of water used by the priests for ritual ablutions2. the font or the water of baptism
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French laveoir, from Late Latin lavātōrium washing place

laver2

(ˈlɑːvə )
Definitions
nounany of several seaweeds of the genus Porphyra and related genera, with edible fronds: phylum Rhodophyta (red algae)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Latin

Laver

(ˈleɪvə )
Definitions
noun Rod(ney) (George). born 1938, Australian tennis player: won eleven Grand Slam singles titles (1960–69), including all four in a single year twice (1962, 1969)

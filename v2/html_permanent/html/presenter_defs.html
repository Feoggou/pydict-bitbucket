presenter

(prɪˈzɛntə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who presents something or someone2. radio, television a person who introduces a show, links items, interviews guests, etc; compere

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA radio or television presenter is a person who introduces the items in a particular programme. [mainly British] ⇒ Most people think being a television presenter is exciting. ⇒ Marcel Berlins is the presenter of the BBC radio programme Law in Action.regional note:   in AM, usually use host, anchor

refugee

(ˌrɛfjʊˈdʒiː )
Definitions
nouna. a person who has fled from some danger or problem, esp political persecution ⇒ refugees from Rwandab. (as modifier) ⇒ a refugee camp, ⇒ a refugee problem
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

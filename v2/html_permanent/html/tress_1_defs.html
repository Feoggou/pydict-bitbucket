tress

(trɛs )
Definitions
noun1. (often plural) a lock of hair, esp a long lock of woman's hair2. a plait or braid of hairverb3. (transitive) to arrange in tresses
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tressy (ˈtressy) adjective

Word Origin

C13: from Old French trece, of uncertain origin

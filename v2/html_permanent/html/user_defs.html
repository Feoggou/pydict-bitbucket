user

(ˈjuːzə )
Definitions
noun1. lawa. the continued exercise, use, or enjoyment of a right, esp in propertyb. a presumptive right based on long-continued use ⇒ right of user2. (often in combination) a person or thing that uses ⇒ a road-user3. informal a drug addict

Definitions
countable nounA user is a person or thing that uses something such as a place, facility, product, or machine. ⇒ Beach users have complained about people walking their dogs on the sand. [+ of] ⇒ ...a regular user of Holland's health-care system. [+ of] ⇒ ...a user of electric current, such as an electric motor, a lamp, or a toaster.

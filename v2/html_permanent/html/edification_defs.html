edification

(ˌɛdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. improvement, instruction, or enlightenment, esp when morally or spiritually uplifting2. the act of edifying or state of being edified
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

edificatory (ˌedifiˈcatory) adjective

edification

Powered by COBUILD
(edɪfɪkeɪʃən ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [oft with poss]If something is done for your edification, it is done to benefit you in some way, for example by teaching you about something. [formal] ⇒ Demonstrations, films, and videotapes are shown for your edification.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

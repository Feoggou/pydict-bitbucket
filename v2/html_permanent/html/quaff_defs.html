quaff

(kwɒf , kwɑːf )
Definitions
verbto drink heartily or in one draught

Derived Forms

quaffable (ˈquaffable) adjectivequaffer (ˈquaffer) noun

Word Origin

C16: perhaps of imitative origin; compare Middle Low German quassen to eat or drink excessively

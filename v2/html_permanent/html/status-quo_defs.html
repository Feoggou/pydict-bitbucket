COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
singular nounThe status quo is the state of affairs that exists at a particular time, especially in contrast to a different possible state of affairs. ⇒ By 492 votes to 391, the federation voted to maintain the status quo. ⇒ They have no wish for any change in the status quo. ⇒ We must not return to the status quo.

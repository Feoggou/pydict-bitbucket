inhabited adj. (ɪnˈhæbɪtɪd). 1. having inhabitants; lived in. 2. (of a person, animal, or group) live in or occupy (a place or environment); colonised, colonized, settled, haunted, populous, populated, occupied, tenanted. Antonyms: uninhabited أغَنّ؛ آهِل؛ عامِر؛ مَأهُول؛ مَسْكُون؛ مَعْمُور

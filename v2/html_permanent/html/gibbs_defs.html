Gibbs

(ɡɪbz )
Definitions
noun1.  James. 1682–1754, British architect; his buildings include St Martin's-in-the-Fields, London (1722–26), and the Radcliffe Camera, Oxford (1737–49)2.  Josiah Willard. 1839–1903, US physicist and mathematician: founder of chemical thermodynamics

gull1

(ɡʌl )
Definitions
nounany aquatic bird of the genus Larus and related genera, such as L. canus (common gull or mew) having long pointed wings, short legs, and a mostly white plumage: family Laridae, order Charadriiformes ▶ Related adjective: larine
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gull-like (ˈgull-ˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C15: of Celtic origin; compare Welsh gwylan

gull2 - archaic

(ɡʌl )
Definitions
noun1. a person who is easily fooled or cheatedverb2. (transitive) to fool, cheat, or hoax
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: perhaps from dialect gull unfledged bird, probably from gul, from Old Norse gulr yellow

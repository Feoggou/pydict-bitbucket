vendor

(ˈvɛndɔː ) or vender (ˈvɛndə )
Definitions
noun1. mainly law a person who sells something, esp real property2. another name for vending machine

Definitions
1. countable nounA vendor is someone who sells things such as newspapers, cigarettes, or food from a small stall or cart. ⇒ ...ice-cream vendors.2. countable nounThe vendor of a house or piece of land is the person who owns it and is selling it.

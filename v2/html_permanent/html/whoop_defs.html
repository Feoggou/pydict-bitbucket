whoop

(wuːp )
Definitions
verb1. to utter (speech) with loud cries, as of enthusiasm or excitement2. medicine to cough convulsively with a crowing sound made at each inspiration3. (of certain birds) to utter (a hooting cry)4. (transitive) to urge on or call with or as if with whoops5. See whoop it upnoun6. a loud cry, esp one expressing enthusiasm or excitement7. medicine the convulsive crowing sound made during a paroxysm of whooping cough8. See not worth a whoop▷ See also whoops
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: of imitative origin

Definitions
1. verbIf you whoop, you shout loudly in a very happy or excited way. [written] [V] ⇒ She whoops with delight at a promise of money.Whoop is also a noun. ⇒ Scattered groans and whoops broke out in the crowd.2. → See also whoops

wonted

(ˈwəʊntɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. (postpositive) accustomed or habituated (to doing something)2. (prenominal) customary; usual ⇒ she is in her wonted place
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'wonted'
But before I left I said I wonted to go out into the garden. Clive Barker GALILEEWith Venus camped in my communal garden, what chance work, what chance sleep, what chance me doing any wonted thing at all? Edward Docx THE CALLIGRAPHER

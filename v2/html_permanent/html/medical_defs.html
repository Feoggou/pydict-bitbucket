medical

(ˈmɛdɪkəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to the science of medicine or to the treatment of patients by drugs, etc, as opposed to surgery2. a less common word for medicinalnoun3. informal a medical examination

Derived Forms

medically (ˈmedically) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Medieval Latin medicālis, from Latin medicus physician, surgeon, from medērī to heal

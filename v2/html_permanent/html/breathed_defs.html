breathed

(brɛθt , briːðd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivephonetics relating to or denoting a speech sound for whose articulation the vocal cords are not made to vibrate. Compare voiced

I stood there for a long time watching her as she breathed and snuffled and murmured occasionally.

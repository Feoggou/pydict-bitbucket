Synonyms of patch

patch

noun

  1. spot, bit, stretch, scrap, shred, small piece
    • a damp patch on the carpet
  2. plot, area, ground, land, tract
    • the little vegetable patch in her backyard
  3. reinforcement, piece of fabric, piece of cloth, piece of material, piece sewn on
    • jackets with patches on the elbows
  4. period, time, spell, phase, spot (British), stretch
    • His marriage is going through a rough patch.

verb

  1. (often with up) sew (up), mend, repair, reinforce, stitch (up)
    • elaborately patched blue jeans
  2. (often with up) mend, cover, fix, reinforce
    • They patched the barn roof.
  • See not be a patch on something
  • See patch someone up
  • See patch things up

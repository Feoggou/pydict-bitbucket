hateful

(ˈheɪtfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. causing or deserving hate; loathsome; detestable2. full of or showing hate
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hatefully (ˈhatefully) adverbhatefulness (ˈhatefulness) noun

hateful

Powered by COBUILD
(heɪtfʊl ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone or something that is hateful is extremely bad or unpleasant. [old-fashioned] ⇒ I'm sorry. That was a hateful thing to say.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is hateful hates someone else. ⇒ These are not necessarily hateful, malicious people. ⇒ ...a lying, hateful and racist campaign.

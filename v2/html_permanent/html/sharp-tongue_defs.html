sharp tongue

(ˌʃɑːp ˈtʌŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounif you say that someone has a sharp tongue, you are critical of the fact that they say things which are unkind or critical though often clever ⇒ Despite her sharp tongue, she inspires loyalty from her friends.

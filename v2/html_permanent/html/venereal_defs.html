venereal

(vɪˈnɪərɪəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or infected with venereal disease2. (of a disease) transmitted by sexual intercourse3. of, relating to, or involving the genitals4. of or relating to sexual intercourse or erotic desire; aphrodisiac
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

venereally (veˈnereally) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Latin venereus concerning sexual love, from venus sexual love, from Venus1

bane1

(beɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that causes misery or distress (esp in the phrase bane of one's life)2. something that causes death or destruction3. a. a fatal poisonb. (in combination) ⇒ ratsbane4. archaic ruin or distress
Word origin
Old English bana; related to Old Norse bani death, Old High German bano destruction, death

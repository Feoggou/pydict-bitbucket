infrared

(ˌɪnfrəˈrɛd )
Definitions
noun1. the part of the electromagnetic spectrum with a longer wavelength than light but a shorter wavelength than radio waves; radiation with wavelength between 0.8 micrometres and 1 millimetreadjective2. of, relating to, using, or consisting of radiation lying within the infrared ⇒ infrared radiation
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

infrared

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnfrə red ) also infra-red for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Infrared radiation is similar to light but has a longer wavelength, so we cannot see it without special equipment.2. adjective [ADJ n]Infrared equipment detects infrared radiation. ⇒ ...searching with infra-red scanners for weapons and artillery.

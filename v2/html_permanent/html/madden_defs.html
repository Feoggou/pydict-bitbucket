Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbTo madden a person or animal means to make them very angry. [V n] ⇒ He knew that what he was saying did not reach her. And the knowledge of it maddened him. [V n] ⇒ He is calmed by a lullaby, but maddened by any sudden loud noise. [V-ed] ⇒ ...the maddened dogs.

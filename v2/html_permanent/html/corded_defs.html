corded

(ˈkɔːdɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. bound or fastened with cord2. (of a fabric) ribbed3. (of muscles) standing out like cords
Example sentences containing 'corded'
The whole area was being corded off and a canvas tent placed over the body itself. Butler, Gwendoline COFFIN ON THE WATERMac's neck was corded , the tendons jumping out as he tried to lift his head up. Terman, Douglas CORMORANT

prudence

(ˈpruːdəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. caution in practical affairs; discretion or circumspection2. care taken in the management of one's resources3. consideration for one's own interests4. the condition or quality of being prudent

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounPrudence is care and good sense that someone shows when making a decision or taking action. [formal] OPPOSITE: rashness ⇒ Western businessmen are showing remarkable prudence in investing in the region. ⇒ A lack of prudence may lead to financial problems.

Powered by COBUILD
(lɪmɪtləs ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveIf you describe something as limitless, you mean that there is or appears to be so much of it that it will never be exhausted. ⇒ ...a cheap and potentially limitless supply of energy. ⇒ The opportunities are limitless.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

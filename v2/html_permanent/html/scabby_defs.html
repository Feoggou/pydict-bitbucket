scabby

(ˈskæbɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -bier or -biest1. pathology having an area of the skin covered with scabs2. pathology obsolete having scabies3. informal despicable

Derived Forms

scabbily (ˈscabbily) adverbscabbiness (ˈscabbiness) noun

graded adjective & adjectiveIf a person, an animal, or a part of their body is scabby, it has scabs on it. ⇒ He had short trousers and scabby knees.

suave

(swɑːv )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(esp of a man) displaying smoothness and sophistication in manner or attitude; urbane

Derived Forms

suavely (ˈsuavely) adverbsuavity (ˈswɑːvɪtɪ ) or suaveness (ˈsuaveness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin suāvis sweet

maize

(meɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: Indian corna. a tall annual grass, Zea mays, cultivated for its yellow edible grains, which develop on a spikeb. the grain of this plant, used for food, fodder, and as a source of oil. Usual US and Canadian name: corn See also sweetcorn2. a. a yellow colourb. (as modifier) ⇒ a maize gown

Word Origin

C16: from Spanish maiz, from Taino mahiz

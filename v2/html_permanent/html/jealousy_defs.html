jealousy

(ˈdʒɛləsɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ousiesthe state or quality of being jealous. Also called (obsolete): jealoushood
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

jealousy for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(dʒeləsi )
Word forms: jealousies
Definitions
1. uncount nounJealousy is the feeling of anger or bitterness which someone has when they think that another person is trying to take a lover or friend, or a possession, away from them. ⇒ At first his jealousy only showed in small ways–he didn't mind me talking to other guys.2. uncount nounJealousy is the feeling of anger or bitterness which someone has when they wish that they could have the qualities or possessions that another person has. ⇒ Her beauty causes envy and jealousy.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

sales

(seɪlz)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. a. the number of goods, products, or services that a company sells within a certain time period ⇒ The newspaper has sales of 1.72 million., ⇒ Car sales are 5 per cent down on a year ago., ⇒ Sales are looking very healthy this quarter., ⇒ Book sales have gone up.b. (as modifier) ⇒ sales figures2. a. the profession of trying to sell on behalf of a company ⇒ Until 1983 he worked in sales and marketing., ⇒ She works in sales.b. (as modifier) ⇒ a sales role3. a. the department of a company that is responsible for selling its products ⇒ This is our head of sales.b. (as modifier) ⇒ a sales manager

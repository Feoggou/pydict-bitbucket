Corneille

(French kɔrnɛj)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Pierre (pjɛr). 1606–84, French tragic dramatist often regarded as the founder of French classical drama. His plays include Médée (1635), Le Cid (1636), Horace (1640), and Polyeucte (1642)

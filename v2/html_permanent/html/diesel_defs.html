diesel

(ˈdiːzəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. See diesel engine2. a ship, locomotive, lorry, etc, driven by a diesel engine3. informal short for diesel oil4. South Africa slang any cola drink ⇒ spook and diesel5. See sucking diesel

Diesel

(ˈdiːzəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Rudolf (ˈruːdɔlf). 1858–1913, German engineer, who invented the diesel engine (1892)

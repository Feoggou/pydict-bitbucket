rolled-up

(ˌrəʊldˈʌp)
Definitions
adjective1. rolled into a cylinder shape ⇒ a rolled-up newspaper2. folded ⇒ an open-necked shirt, with rolled-up sleeves

rolled-up

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Rolled-up objects have been folded or wrapped into a cylindrical shape. ⇒ ...a rolled-up newspaper.2. adjective [ADJ n]Rolled-up sleeves or trouser legs have been made shorter by being folded over at the lower edge. ⇒ ...an open-necked shirt, with rolled-up sleeves.

postmortem

(pəʊstˈmɔːtəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (prenominal) occurring after deathnoun2. analysis or study of a recently completed event ⇒ a postmortem on a game of chess3. Also: postmortem examination dissection and examination of a dead body to determine the cause of death. Also called: autopsy, necropsy

Word Origin

C18: from Latin, literally: after death
Example sentences containing 'postmortem'
It was bad luck on Steve that her flight had been delayed and she'd got involved in this ugly postmortem. Robert Wilson INSTRUMENTS OF DARKNESSThe prosector did the postmortem , keeping track of the process... giving a play-by-play to the microphone. Jack Kerley THE HUNDREDTH MAN

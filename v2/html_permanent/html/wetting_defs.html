wetting

(ˈwɛtɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the act or an instance of making something wet ⇒ a wetting of the lips, ⇒ The wave gave us a wetting we weren't expecting., ⇒ I seldom wear a hat or an overcoat and do not in the least mind a wetting., ⇒ Without confidence you will never learn how seldom the bottom really changes enough to give you a wetting.2. the act or an instance of urinating (on something) ⇒ bed-wetting, ⇒ I knew he really needed to get the wetting under control.

starry

(ˈstɑːrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -rier or -riest1. filled, covered with, or illuminated by stars2. of, like, or relating to a star or stars

Derived Forms

starrily (ˈstarrily) adverbstarriness (ˈstarriness) noun

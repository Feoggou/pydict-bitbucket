amount

noun

  1. quantity, lot, measure, size, supply, mass, volume, capacity, extent, bulk, number, magnitude, expanse • I still do a certain amount of work for them.
  2. total, whole, mass, addition, sum, lot, extent, aggregate, entirety, totality, sum total • If you always pay the full amount, this won't affect you.
  3. importance, result, import, significance, full value, full effect

PROVERB:
 Many a mickle makes a muckle
  • See amount to something

usage note:   Although it is common to use a plural noun after amount of, for example in the amount of people and the amount of goods, this should be avoided. Preferred alternatives would be to use quantity, as in the quantity of people, or number, as in the number of goods.

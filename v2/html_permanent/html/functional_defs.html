functional

(ˈfʌŋkʃənəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, involving, or containing a function or functions2. practical rather than decorative; utilitarian ⇒ functional architecture3. capable of functioning; working4. medicine affecting a function of an organ without structural change5. psychologya. relating to the purpose or context of a behaviourb. denoting a psychosis such as schizophrenia assumed not to have a direct organic cause, like deterioration or poisoning of the brain. Compare organic psychosisnoun6. mathematics a function whose domain is a set of functions and whose range is a set of functions or a set of numbers

Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveFunctional things are useful rather than decorative. ⇒ ...modern, functional furniture. ⇒ The decor is functional.2. adjective [ADJ n]Functional means relating to the way in which something works or operates, or relating to how useful it is. ⇒ ...rules defining the territorial boundaries and functional limits of the local state.functionally adverb [ADV adj, ADV with v] ⇒ The Indian cavalry under Haig became a functionally efficient unit. ⇒ It satisfies the user's requirements both functionally and emotionally.3. adjectiveFunctional equipment works or operates in the way that it is supposed to. ⇒ We have fully functional smoke alarms on all staircases.

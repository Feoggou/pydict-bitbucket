workings

(ˈwɜːkɪŋz)
Definitions
plural noun1. functioning ⇒ computer systems which mimic the workings of the brain, ⇒ the workings of government, ⇒ information about the workings of government2. a part of a mine or quarry that is being or has been worked ⇒ housing which was built above old mine workings

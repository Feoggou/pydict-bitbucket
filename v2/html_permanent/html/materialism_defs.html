materialism

(məˈtɪərɪəˌlɪzəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. interest in and desire for money, possessions, etc, rather than spiritual or ethical values2. philosophy the monist doctrine that matter is the only reality and that the mind, the emotions, etc, are merely functions of it. Compare idealism (sense 3), dualism (sense 2). See also identity theory3. ethics the rejection of any religious or supernatural account of things

Derived Forms

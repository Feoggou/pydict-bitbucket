concentrated

(ˈkɒnsəntreɪtɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having had water removed to increase concentration ⇒ concentrated apple juice, ⇒ A little concentrated fruit juice can be used as a sweetener.2. focused and intense ⇒ a concentrated effort to stay in touch▶ USAGE The abbreviation for concentrated is conc.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA concentrated liquid has been increased in strength by having water removed from it. OPPOSITE: diluted ⇒ Sweeten dishes sparingly with honey, or concentrated apple or pear juice.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A concentrated activity is directed with great intensity in one place. ⇒ ...a more concentrated effort to reach out to troubled kids.

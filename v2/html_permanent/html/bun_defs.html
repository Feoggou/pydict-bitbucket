bun

(bʌn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small roll, similar to bread but usually containing sweetening, currants, spices, etc2. any of various types of small round sweet cakes3. a hairstyle in which long hair is gathered into a bun shape at the back of the head4. See have a bun in the oven
Word origin
C14: of unknown origin

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [oft n N]Buns are small bread rolls. They are sometimes sweet and may contain dried fruit or spices. ⇒ ...a currant bun.2. countable nounBuns are small sweet cakes. They often have icing on the top. [British]3. countable nounIf a woman has her hair in a bun, she has fastened it tightly on top of her head or at the back of her head in the shape of a ball.4. countable noun [usu pl]Your buns are your buttocks. [mainly US, informal] ⇒ I'd pinch his buns and kiss his neck.

cavalcade

(ˌkævəlˈkeɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a procession of people on horseback, in cars, etc2. any procession ⇒ a cavalcade of guests
Word origin
C16: from French, from Italian cavalcata, from cavalcare to ride on horseback, from Late Latin caballicāre, from caballus horse

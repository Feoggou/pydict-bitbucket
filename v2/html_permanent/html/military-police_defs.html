Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. singular noun [with sing or pl verb]The military police are the part of an army, navy, or air force that act as its police force. ⇒ The government has said it will reform the military police.2. plural nounMilitary police are men and women who are members of the part of an army, navy, or air force that act as its police force. ⇒ The camp is surrounded by razor-wire fences and guarded by military police.

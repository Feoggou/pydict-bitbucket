retinue

(ˈrɛtɪˌnjuː )
Definitions
nouna body of aides and retainers attending an important person, royalty, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

retinued (ˈretiˌnued) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Old French retenue, from retenir to retain

Definitions
countable nounAn important person's retinue is the group of servants, friends, or assistants who go with them and look after their needs. ⇒ Mind trainers are now part of a tennis star's retinue. [Also + of]

gritty

(ˈɡrɪtɪ )
Definitions
adjective -tier or -tiest1. courageous; hardy; resolute2. of, like, or containing grit
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

grittily (ˈgrittily) adverbgrittiness (ˈgrittiness) noun

gritty

Powered by COBUILD
(grɪti ) for learners of English
Word forms: grittier , grittiest
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is gritty contains grit, is covered with grit, or has a texture like that of grit. ⇒ The sheets fell on the gritty floor, and she just let them lie.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is gritty is brave and determined. ⇒ We have to prove how gritty we are. ⇒ ...a gritty determination to avoid humiliation.3. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]A gritty description of a tough or unpleasant situation shows it in a very realistic way. ⇒ ...gritty social comment. ⇒ His most celebrated work is the film classic, 'Woman in the Dunes,' a gritty look at survival in an extreme environment.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

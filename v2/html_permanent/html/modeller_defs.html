Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
regional note:   in AM, use modeler1. countable nounA modeller is someone who makes shapes or figures out of substances such as wood or clay.2. countable nounA modeller is someone who makes theoretical descriptions of systems or processes in order to understand them and be able to predict how they will develop. ⇒ ...climate modellers.

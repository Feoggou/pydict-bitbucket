relaxing

(rɪˈlæksɪŋ)
Definitions
adjectivehaving the effect of making a person feel calm ⇒ a relaxing day in the sun with a bottle of good wine, ⇒ We come here once a year expecting a quiet, relaxing holiday., ⇒ a quiet, relaxing holiday, ⇒ Create a relaxing atmosphere with soft music and candle light., ⇒ a relaxing atmosphere, ⇒ Enjoy a wonderfully relaxing body massage., ⇒ I find cooking relaxing.

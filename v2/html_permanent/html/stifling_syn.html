Synonyms of stifling

stifling

adjective

  1. suffocating, close, oppressive, airless, sticky, muggy
    • The stifling heat of the little room was beginning to make me nauseous.
  2. oppressive, severe, overwhelming, repressive, cruel, brutal, unjust, overbearing, tyrannical, despotic
    • a stifling bureaucracy

stifle

verb

  1. suppress, repress, prevent, stop, check, silence, curb, restrain, cover up, gag, hush, smother, extinguish, muffle, choke back
    • Critics have accused them of trying to stifle debate.
  2. restrain, suppress, repress, smother
    • She makes no attempt to stifle a yawn.
  3. suffocate, choke, strangle, smother, asphyxiate

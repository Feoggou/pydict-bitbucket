mullet1

(ˈmʌlɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of various teleost food fishes belonging to the families Mugilidae (grey mullet) or Mullidae (red mullet). See also grey mullet, red mullet2. the US name for grey mullet

Word Origin

C15: via Old French from Latin mullus, from Greek mullos

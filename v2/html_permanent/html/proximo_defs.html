proximo

(ˈprɒksɪməʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb(esp abbreviated in formal correspondence) in or during the next or coming month ⇒ a letter of the seventh proximo. Abbreviation: prox. Compare: instant, ultimo

Word Origin

C19: from Latin: in or on the next, from proximus next

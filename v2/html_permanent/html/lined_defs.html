Powered by COBUILD
(laɪnd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf someone's face or skin is lined, it has lines on it as a result of old age, tiredness, worry, or illness. ⇒ His lined face was that of an old man.2. adjectiveLined paper has lines printed across it to help you write neatly.3. → See also line

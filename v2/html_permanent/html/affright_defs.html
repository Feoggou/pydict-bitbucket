affright

(əˈfraɪt ) archaic or poetic
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (transitive) to frightennoun2. a sudden terror
Word origin
Old English āfyrhtan, from a-, a prefix indicating the beginning or end of an action + fyrhtan to fright

ere

(ɛə )
Definitions
conjunction, prepositiona poetic word for before
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English ǣr; related to Old Norse ār early, Gothic airis earlier, Old High German ēr earlier, Greek ēri early

ere

Powered by COBUILD
(eəʳ ) for learners of English
Definitions
subordinating conjunction & conjunctionEre means the same as 'before'. [literary, old-fashioned] ⇒ It was not long ere a call came from the house and recalled me from my reflections. ⇒ Take the water ere the clock strikes twelve.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

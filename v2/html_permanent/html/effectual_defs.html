effectual

(ɪˈfɛktjʊəl )
Definitions
adjective1. capable of or successful in producing an intended result; effective2. (of documents, agreements, etc) having legal force
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

effectuality (efˌfectuˈality) or effectualness (efˈfectualness) noun

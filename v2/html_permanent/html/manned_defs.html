manned

(mænd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. supplied or equipped with men, esp soldiers2. (of spacecraft, aircraft, etc) having a human crew

manned

(mænd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveA manned vehicle such as a spacecraft has people in it who are operating its controls. ⇒ In thirty years from now the United States should have a manned spacecraft on Mars. ⇒ ...the history of manned flight. ⇒ ...manned exploration of the solar system.2. → see also man

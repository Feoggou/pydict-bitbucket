usually for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(juːʒuəli )
Definitions
1. adverb [ADV before v]If something usually happens, it is the thing that most often happens in a particular situation. ⇒ The best information about hotels usually comes from friends and acquaintances who have been there. ⇒ They ate, as they usually did, in the kitchen. ⇒ Usually, the work is boring. ⇒ Offering only one loan, usually an installment loan, is part of the plan.2. →  more than usually

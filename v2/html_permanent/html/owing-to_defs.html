owing to

(ˈəʊɪŋ tʊ)
Definitions
prepositionbecause of or on account of ⇒ owing to bad weather, ⇒ I missed my flight owing to a traffic hold-up., ⇒ He was off work owing to a back injury., ⇒ Owing to staff shortages, there was no restaurant car on the train.

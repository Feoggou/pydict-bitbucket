aggravation

(ˌægrəˈveɪʃən )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  the act of aggravating, or making worse, or the condition of being aggravated2.  a thing or circumstance that aggravates, or makes worse3.  Informal exasperation; annoyance

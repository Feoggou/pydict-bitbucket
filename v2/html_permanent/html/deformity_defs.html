deformity

(dɪˈfɔːmɪtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. a deformed condition; disfigurement2. pathology an acquired or congenital distortion of an organ or part3. a deformed person or thing4. a defect, esp of the mind or morals; depravity

(dɪfɔːʳmɪti )
Word forms: deformities
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA deformity is a part of someone's body which is not the normal shape because of injury or illness, or because they were born this way. ⇒ ...facial deformities in babies.2. uncount nounDeformity is the condition of having a deformity. ⇒ The bones begin to grind against each other, leading to pain and deformity.

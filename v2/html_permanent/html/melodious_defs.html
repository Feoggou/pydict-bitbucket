melodious

(mɪˈləʊdɪəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having a tune that is pleasant to the ear2. of or relating to melody; melodic

Derived Forms

melodiously (meˈlodiously) adverbmelodiousness (meˈlodiousness) noun

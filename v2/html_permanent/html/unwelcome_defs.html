unwelcome

(ʌnˈwɛlkəm )
Definitions
adjective1. (of a person) not welcome2. causing dissatisfaction or displeasure
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

unwelcomely (unˈwelcomely) adverbunwelcomeness (unˈwelcomeness) noun

unwelcome for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnwelkəm )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveAn unwelcome experience is one that you do not like and did not want. ⇒ The media has brought more unwelcome attention to the Royal Family. ⇒ A colleague made unwelcome sexual advances towards her.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that a visitor is unwelcome, you mean that you did not want them to come. ⇒ ...an unwelcome guest. ⇒ She was, quite deliberately, making him feel unwelcome.

dilatory

(ˈdɪlətərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. tending or inclined to delay or waste time2. intended or designed to waste time or defer action
Derived forms
dilatorily (ˈdilatorily) adverbdilatoriness (ˈdilatoriness) noun
Word origin
C15: from Late Latin dīlātōrius inclined to delay, from differre to postpone; see differ

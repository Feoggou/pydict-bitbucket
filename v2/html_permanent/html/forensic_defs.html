forensic

Powered by COBUILD
(fərensɪk ) for learners of English
Word forms: forensics
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]Forensic is used to describe the work of scientists who examine evidence in order to help the police solve crimes. ⇒ They were convicted on forensic evidence alone. ⇒ Forensic experts searched the area for clues. ⇒ ...the use of genetic data for forensic science.2. adjective [ADJ n]Forensic means relating to the legal profession. ⇒ He won admiration for his forensic skills in cross-examining ministers. ⇒ ...a forensic psychiatrist.3. uncount nounForensics is the use of scientific techniques to solve crimes. ⇒ ...the newest advances in forensics. ⇒ ...federal forensics legislation.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

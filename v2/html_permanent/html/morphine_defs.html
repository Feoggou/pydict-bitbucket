morphine

(ˈmɔːfiːn ) or morphia (ˈmɔːfɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan alkaloid extracted from opium: used in medicine as an analgesic and sedative, although repeated use causes addiction. Formula: C17H19NO3

Word Origin

C19: from French, from Morpheus

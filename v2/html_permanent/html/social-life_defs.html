social life

(ˈsəʊʃəl laɪf)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. your social life involves spending time with your friends, for example at parties or in pubs or bars ⇒ I have a good social life., ⇒ How's your social life these days?2. the opportunities in a particular place for a person to socialize ⇒ The only source of social life was the public house.

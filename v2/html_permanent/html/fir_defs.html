fir

(fɜː )
Definitions
noun1. any pyramidal coniferous tree of the N temperate genus Abies, having single needle-like leaves and erect cones: family Pinaceae. See also red fir, silver fir, balsam fir2. any of various other trees of the family Pinaceae, such as the Douglas fir3. the wood of any of these trees
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English furh; related to Old Norse fura, Old High German foraha fir, Latin quercus oak

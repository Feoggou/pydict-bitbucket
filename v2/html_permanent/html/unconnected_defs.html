unconnected

(ˌʌnkəˈnɛktɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. not linked; separate or independent2. disconnected or incoherent

Derived Forms

(ʌnkənektɪd )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf one thing is unconnected with another or the two things are unconnected, the things are not related to each other in any way. [+ with] ⇒ She was known to have had personal problems unconnected with her marriage. ⇒ I can't believe that those two murders are unconnected. [Also + to]

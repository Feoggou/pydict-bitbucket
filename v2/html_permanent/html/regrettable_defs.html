regrettable

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪgretəbəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveYou describe something as regrettable when you think that it is bad and that it should not happen or have happened. [formal, feelings] ⇒ ...an investigation into what the army described as a regrettable incident. ⇒ It is regrettable that strike leaders seem intent on spoiling holidays.regrettably graded adverb & adverb [ADV adj] ⇒ Regrettably we could find no sign of the man and the search was terminated. ⇒ The incidents are regrettably true.

portly

(ˈpɔːtlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -lier or -liest1. stout or corpulent2. archaic stately; impressive

Derived Forms

portliness (ˈportliness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from port5 (in the sense: deportment, bearing)

Definitions
noun(Insurance: Sales and distribution) An insurance broker is someone who advises people on their insurance needs and negotiates insurance contracts on their behalf with insurers in return for a fee or commission. ⇒ We paid an insurance broker to look for insurance on our behalf. ⇒ Insurance brokers act as middlemen between firms that want to insure their property and casualty risks and the insurers that underwrite policies. ⇒ An insurance broker is someone who advises people on their insurance needs and negotiates insurance contracts on their behalf with insurers in return for a fee or commission. ▶  insurance1, insurance2, insurance3

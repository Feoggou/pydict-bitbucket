onerous

(ˈɒnərəs , ˈəʊ-)
Definitions
adjective1. laborious or oppressive2. law (of a contract, lease, etc) having or involving burdens or obligations that counterbalance or outweigh the advantages
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

onerously (ˈonerously) adverbonerousness (ˈonerousness) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Latin onerōsus burdensome, from onus load

reimburse

(ˌriːɪmˈbɜːs )
Definitions
verb(transitive) to repay or compensate (someone) for (money already spent, losses, damages, etc) ⇒ your fare will be reimbursed after your interview
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

reimbursable (ˌreimˈbursable) adjectivereimbursement (ˌreimˈbursement) nounreimburser (ˌreimˈburser) noun

Word Origin

C17: from re- + imburse, from Medieval Latin imbursāre to put in a moneybag, from bursa purse

apportionment

(əˈpɔːʃənmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of apportioning2. US government the proportional distribution of the seats in a legislative body, esp the House of Representatives, on the basis of population
Example sentences containing 'apportionment'
He had nothing to tell them regarding the preciousness of water and the apportionment of supplies... given they were in their sane minds. C.J. Cherryh HAMMERFALL

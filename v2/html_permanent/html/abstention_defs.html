abstention

(əbˈstɛnʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a voluntary decision not to act; the act of refraining or abstaining2. the act of withholding one's vote
Derived forms
abˈstentious adjective
Word origin
C16: from Late Latin abstentiōn-, from Latin abstinēre. See abstain

Definitions

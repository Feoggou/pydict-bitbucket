diadem

(ˈdaɪəˌdɛm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a royal crown, esp a light jewelled circlet2. royal dignity or powerverb3. (transitive) to adorn or crown with or as with a diadem
Word origin
C13: from Latin diadēma, from Greek: fillet, royal headdress, from diadein to bind around, from dia- + dein to bind

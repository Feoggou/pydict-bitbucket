argent

(ˈɑːdʒənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna. an archaic or poetic word for silverb. (as adjective; often postpositive, esp in heraldry) ⇒ a bend argent
Word origin
C15: from Old French, from Latin
Example sentences containing 'argent'

banker1

(ˈbæŋkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who owns or is an executive in a bank2. an official or player in charge of the bank in any of various games, esp gambling games3. a result that has been forecast identically in a series of entries on a football pool coupon4. a person or thing that appears certain to win or be successful

banker2

(ˈbæŋkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a fishing vessel of Newfoundland2. a fisherman in such a vessel3. Australian and New Zealand informal a stream almost overflowing its banks (esp in the phrase run a banker)4. Also called: bank engine British a locomotive that is used to help a heavy train up a steep gradient

banker3

(ˈbæŋkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a craftsman's workbench2. a timber board used as a base for mixing building materials

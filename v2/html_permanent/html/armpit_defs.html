armpit

(ˈɑːmˌpɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the small depression beneath the arm where it joins the shoulder. Technical name: axilla ▶ Related adjective: axillary2. slang an extremely unpleasant place ⇒ the armpit of the Mediterranean

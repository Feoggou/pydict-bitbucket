waxing

(ˈwæksɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. a gradual process of growth or development ⇒ their numbers rising with the waxing of the moon, ⇒ the waxing and ebbing of the seasons2. hairdressing hair-removal treatment ⇒ The business focuses on waxing, body piercing and permanent make-up.

northeastern in American English

(ˌnɔrθˈistərn ; Nautical ˌ nɔrˈistərn )
Definitions
adjective1.  in, of, to, toward, or facing the northeast2.  from the northeast ⇒ a northeastern wind3.  Us; [N-] of or characteristic of the Northeast or New England

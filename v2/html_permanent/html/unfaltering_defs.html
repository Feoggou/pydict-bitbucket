unfaltering

(ʌnˈfɔːltərɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. never failing or hesitating ⇒ the unfaltering attention my mother had paid him2. certain and assured ⇒ The knitters looked up from their stitches, hands unfaltering in their rhythm., ⇒ his calm, unfaltering voice, ⇒ his relentless, unfaltering stride

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are distracted, you are not concentrating on something because you are worried or are thinking about something else. ⇒ She had seemed curiously distracted. ⇒ She seemed less like a poetess than a distracted housewife.distractedly adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ He looked up distractedly. 'Be with you in a second.'

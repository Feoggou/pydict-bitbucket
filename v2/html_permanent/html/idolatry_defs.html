idolatry

(aɪˈdɒlətrɪ )
Definitions
noun1. the worship of idols2. great devotion or reverence
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

idolater (iˈdolater) or idolator (iˈdolator) nounidolatress (iˈdolatress) feminine nounidolatrous (iˈdolatrous) adjectiveidolatrously (iˈdolatrously) adverbidolatrousness (iˈdolatrousness) noun

idolatry

Powered by COBUILD
(aɪdɒlətri ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounSomeone who practises idolatry worships idols. [formal]2. uncount nounIf you refer to someone's admiration for a particular person as idolatry, you think it is too great and uncritical. [formal, disapproval] ⇒ Their affection for her soon increased almost to idolatry.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

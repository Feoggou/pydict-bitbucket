Synonyms of valid

valid

adjective

  1. sound, good, reasonable, just, telling, powerful, convincing, substantial, acceptable, sensible, rational, logical, viable, credible, sustainable, plausible, conclusive, weighty, well-founded, cogent, well-grounded
    • Both sides have made valid points.
    OPPOSITES:  weak, false, unacceptable, spurious, unrealistic, bogus, untrue, sham, unfounded, illogical, baseless, unrecognized, fallacious
  2. legal, official, legitimate, correct, genuine, proper, in effect, authentic, in force, lawful, bona fide, legally binding, signed and sealed
    • For foreign holidays you will need a valid passport.
    OPPOSITES:  illegal, unofficial, unlawful, invalid, inoperative

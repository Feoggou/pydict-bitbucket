Sistine

(ˈsɪstiːn)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveof or relating to the chapel of the pope in the Vatican at Rome, built for Sixtus IV and decorated with frescoes by Michelangelo and others ⇒ the Sistine ceiling
Example sentences containing 'Sistine'
A patchwork sky, the roof of the Sistine Chapel, a sparrow sailing through the trees. James Birrell THE MANANA MAN-- and my fingertips brush against his, like God's and Adam's in the Sistine Chapel, or God's and Lucifer's on the battlements of heaven. Anthony Masters CASCADES - THE DAY OF THE DEAD

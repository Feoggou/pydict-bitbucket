Salamis

(ˈsæləmɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan island in the Saronic Gulf, Greece: scene of the naval battle in 480 bc, in which the Greeks defeated the Persians. Pop (municipality): 28 423 (2001). Area: 95 sq km (37 sq miles). Modern Greek name: Salamina (salaˈmiːna)

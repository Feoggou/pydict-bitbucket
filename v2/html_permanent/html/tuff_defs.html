Word Frequency

tuff

(tʌf )
Definitions
nouna rock formed by the fusing together on the ground of small rock fragments (less than 2 mm across) ejected from a volcano
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tuffaceous (tʌˈfeɪʃəs ) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Old French tuf, from Italian tufo; see tufa

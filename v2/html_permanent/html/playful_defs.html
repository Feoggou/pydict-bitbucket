playful

(ˈpleɪfʊl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. full of high spirits and fun ⇒ a playful kitten2. good-natured and humorous ⇒ a playful remark

Derived Forms

playfully (ˈplayfully) adverbplayfulness (ˈplayfulness) noun

(pleɪfʊl )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA playful gesture or person is friendly or humorous. ⇒ ...a playful kiss on the tip of his nose. ⇒ ...a playful fight. ⇒ Her manner is playful and girlish.playfully graded adverb & adverb ⇒ She pushed him away playfully.playfulness uncount noun ⇒ ...the child's natural playfulness.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA playful animal is lively and cheerful. ⇒ ...a playful puppy.
Example sentences containing 'playful'

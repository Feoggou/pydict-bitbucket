masonry

(ˈmeɪsənrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ries1. the craft of a mason2. work that is built by a mason; stonework or brickwork3. (often capital) short for Freemasonry

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounMasonry is bricks or pieces of stone which have been stuck together with cement as part of a wall or building.

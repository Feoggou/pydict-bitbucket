ellipse

(ɪˈlɪps )
Definitions
nouna closed conic section shaped like a flattened circle and formed by an inclined plane that does not cut the base of the cone. Standard equation x2/a2 + y2/b2 = 1, where 2a and 2b are the lengths of the major and minor axes. Area: πab
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: back formation from ellipsis

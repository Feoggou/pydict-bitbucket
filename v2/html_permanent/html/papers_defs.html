papers

(ˈpeɪpəz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. documents for establishing the identity of the bearer; credentials ⇒ A young Moroccan stopped by police refused to show his papers., ⇒ They have arrested four people who were trying to leave the country with forged papers., ⇒ Each person travelled under a false name and papers.2. nautical official documents relating to the ownership, cargo, etc, of a ship3. collected diaries, letters, etc ⇒ Always file important papers as soon as they have been dealt with., ⇒ Her papers included unpublished articles and correspondence.4. See the papers

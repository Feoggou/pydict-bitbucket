vehement

(ˈviːɪmənt )
Definitions
adjective1. marked by intensity of feeling or conviction; emphatic2. (of actions, gestures, etc) characterized by great energy, vigour, or force; furious
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vehemence (ˈvehemence) or rare vehemency (ˈvehemency) nounvehemently (ˈvehemently) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Latin vehemēns ardent; related to vehere to carry

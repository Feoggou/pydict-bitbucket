changeable

(ˈtʃeɪndʒəbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. able to change or be changed; fickle ⇒ changeable weather2. varying in colour when viewed from different angles or in different lights
Derived forms
ˌchangeability (ˌchangeaˈbility) or changeableness (ˈchangeableness) nounchangeably (ˈchangeably) adverb

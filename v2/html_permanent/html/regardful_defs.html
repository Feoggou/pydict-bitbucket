regardful

(rɪˈɡɑːdfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. (often foll by of) showing regard (for); heedful (of)2. showing regard, respect, or consideration
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

regardfully (reˈgardfully) adverbregardfulness (reˈgardfulness) noun

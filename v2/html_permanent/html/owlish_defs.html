owlish

(ˈaʊlɪʃ )
Definitions
adjective1. like an owl2. solemn and wise in appearance

Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]An owlish person looks rather like an owl, especially because they wear glasses, and seems to be very serious and clever. ⇒ With his owlish face, it is easy to understand why he was called 'The Professor'.

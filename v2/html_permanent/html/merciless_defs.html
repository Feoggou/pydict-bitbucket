Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as merciless, you mean that they are very cruel or determined and do not show any concern for the effect their actions have on other people. ⇒ Whistler, as always, was merciless. ⇒ ...the merciless efficiency of a modern police state.mercilessly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v] [oft ADV adj] ⇒ We teased him mercilessly. ⇒ The sun beat down mercilessly.

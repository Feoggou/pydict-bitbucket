Spartan

(ˈspɑːtən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Sparta or its citizens2. (sometimes not capital) very strict or austere ⇒ a Spartan upbringing3. (sometimes not capital) possessing courage and resolvenoun4. a citizen of Sparta5. (sometimes not capital) a disciplined or brave person6. a Canadian variety of eating apple

Derived Forms

Spartanˌism (ˈSpartanˌism) noun

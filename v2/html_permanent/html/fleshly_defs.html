fleshly

(ˈflɛʃlɪ )
Definitions
adjective -lier or -liest1. relating to the body, esp its sensual nature; carnal ⇒ fleshly desire2. worldly as opposed to spiritual3. fleshy; fat
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fleshliness (ˈfleshliness) noun

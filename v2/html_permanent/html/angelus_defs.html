Angelus

(ˈændʒɪləs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun Roman Catholic Church1. a series of prayers recited in the morning, at midday, and in the evening, commemorating the Annunciation and Incarnation2. the bell (Angelus bell) signalling these prayers
Word origin
C17: Latin, from the phrase Angelus domini nuntiavit Mariae the angel of the Lord brought tidings to Mary

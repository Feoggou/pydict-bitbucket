belligerent

(bɪˈlɪdʒərənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. marked by readiness to fight or argue; aggressive ⇒ a belligerent tone2. relating to or engaged in a legally recognized war or warfarenoun3. a person or country engaged in fighting or war
Word origin
C16: from Latin belliger, from bellum war + gerere to wage

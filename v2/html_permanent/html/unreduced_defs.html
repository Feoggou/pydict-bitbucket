unreduced

(ˌʌnrɪˈdjuːst)
Definitions
adjective1. not reduced in size or amount2. not simplified or put into simple terms3. not dissolved or pulverized4. not conquered or subjugated by force5. Scots law not made invalid or revoked6. medicine not returned to a normal condition7. phonetics relating to a phonetic element, vowel sound, or syllable that is not reduced

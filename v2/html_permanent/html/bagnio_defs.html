bagnio

(ˈbɑːnjəʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ios1. a brothel2. obsolete an oriental prison for slaves3. obsolete an Italian or Turkish bathhouse
Word origin
C16: from Italian bagno, from Latin balneum bath, from Greek balaneion

bristling

(ˈbrɪslɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. containing stiff hairs ⇒ a bristling white moustache2. vigorous and energetic ⇒ His bristling determination has become a symbol of England's renaissance., ⇒ bristling, exuberant, rock'n'roll, ⇒ He is not the bristling villain he once was.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Bristling means thick, hairy, and rough. It is used to describe things such as moustaches, beards, or eyebrows. ⇒ ...a bristling white moustache.2. adjective [ADJ n]If you describe someone's attitude as bristling, you are emphasizing that it is full of energy and enthusiasm. [emphasis] ⇒ His bristling determination has become a symbol of England's renaissance. ⇒ ...bristling, exuberant, rock'n'roll.

ceramic

(sɪˈræmɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a hard brittle material made by firing clay and similar substances2. an object made from such a materialadjective3. of, relating to, or made from a ceramic ⇒ this vase is ceramic4. of or relating to ceramics ⇒ ceramic arts and crafts
Word origin
C19: from Greek keramikos, from keramos potter's clay, pottery

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable noun [usu N n]Ceramic is clay that has been heated to a very high temperature so that it becomes hard. ⇒ ...ceramic tiles. ⇒ ...items made from hand-painted ceramic.2. countable noun [usu pl]Ceramics are ceramic ornaments or objects. ⇒ ...a collection of Chinese ceramics.3. uncount nounCeramics is the art of making artistic objects out of clay.

imago

(ɪˈmeɪɡəʊ )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural imagoes or imagines (ɪˈmædʒəˌniːz )1. an adult sexually mature insect produced after metamorphosis2. psychoanalysis an idealized image of another person, usually a parent, acquired in childhood and carried in the unconscious in later life
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: New Latin, from Latin: likeness; see image

fateful

(ˈfeɪtfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. having important consequences; decisively important2. bringing death or disaster3. controlled by or as if by fate4. prophetic
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If an action or a time when something happened is described as fateful, it is considered to have an important, and often very bad, effect on future events. ⇒ It was a fateful decision, one which was to break the Government.

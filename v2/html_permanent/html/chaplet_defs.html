chaplet

(ˈtʃæplɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an ornamental wreath of flowers, beads, etc, worn on the head2. a string of beads or something similar3. Roman Catholic Churcha. a string of prayer beads constituting one third of the rosaryb. the prayers counted on this string4. a narrow convex moulding in the form of a string of beads; astragal5. a metal support for the core in a casting mould, esp for the core of a cylindrical pipe
Derived forms
chapleted (ˈchapleted) adjective
Word origin
C14: from Old French chapelet garland of roses, from chapel hat; see chapeau

Similar Words

garland, bouquet, wreath, coronal

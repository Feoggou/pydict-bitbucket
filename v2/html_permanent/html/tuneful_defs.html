tuneful

(ˈtjuːnfʊl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having a pleasant or catchy tune; melodious2. producing a melody or music ⇒ a tuneful blackbird

Derived Forms

tunefully (ˈtunefully) adverbtunefulness (ˈtunefulness) noun

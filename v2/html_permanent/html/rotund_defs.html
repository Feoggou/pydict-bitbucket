rotund

(rəʊˈtʌnd )
Definitions
adjective1. rounded or spherical in shape2. plump3. sonorous or grandiloquent; full in tone, style of speaking, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

rotundity (roˈtundity) or rotundness (roˈtundness) nounrotundly (roˈtundly) adverb

Word Origin

C18: from Latin rotundus wheel-shaped, round, from rota wheel

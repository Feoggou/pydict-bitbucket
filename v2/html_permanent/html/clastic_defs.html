clastic

(ˈklæstɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of sedimentary rock, etc) composed of fragments of pre-existing rock that have been transported some distance from their points of origin2. biology dividing into parts ⇒ a clastic cell3. able to be dismantled for study or observation ⇒ a clastic model of the brain
Word origin
C19: from Greek klastos shattered, from klan to break

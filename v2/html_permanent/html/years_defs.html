years

(ˈjɪəz)
Definitions
plural noun1. a long time ⇒ It took me years to recover., ⇒ I haven't laughed so much in years.2. See for years3. time; period ⇒ The joy turned to tragedy during his years in Cyprus., ⇒ his years as Director of the Manchester City Art Gallery4. See the years of sth5. age, esp old age ⇒ A young man of his years needed to have a separate room., ⇒ He was moving with surprising speed for a man of his years.6. See to put years on sb7. See to take years off sb

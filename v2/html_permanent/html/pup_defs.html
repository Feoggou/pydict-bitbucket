pup

(pʌp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a young dog, esp when under one year of age; puppyb. the young of various other animals, such as the seal2. See in pup3. informal, mainly British derogatory a conceited young man (esp in the phrase young pup)4. See sell someone a pup5. See the night's a pupverbWord forms: pups, pupping or pupped6. (of dogs, seals, etc) to give birth to (young)

Word Origin

C18: back formation from puppy

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA pup is a young dog. ⇒ I'll get you an Alsatian pup for Christmas.2. countable noun [oft n N]The young of some other animals, for example seals, are called pups. ⇒ Two thousand grey seal pups are born there every autumn.

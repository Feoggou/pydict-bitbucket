deputation

(ˌdɛpjʊˈteɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of appointing a person or body of people to represent or act on behalf of others2. a person or, more often, a body of people so appointed; delegation

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA deputation is a small group of people who have been asked to speak to someone on behalf of a larger group of people, especially in order to make a complaint. [+ of] ⇒ A deputation of elders from the village arrived headed by its chief.

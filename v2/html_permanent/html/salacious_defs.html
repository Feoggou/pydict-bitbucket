salacious

(səˈleɪʃəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having an excessive interest in sex2. (of books, magazines, etc) erotic, bawdy, or lewd
Derived forms
salaciously (saˈlaciously) adverbsalaciousness (saˈlaciousness) or salacity (səˈlæsɪtɪ ) noun
Word origin
C17: from Latin salax fond of leaping, from salīre to leap

Synonyms of loop

loop

noun

  1. curve, ring, circle, bend, twist, curl, spiral, hoop, coil, loophole, twirl, kink, noose, whorl, eyelet, convolution
    • She reached for a loop of garden hose.

verb

  1. twist, turn, join, roll, circle, connect, bend, fold, knot, curl, spiral, coil, braid, encircle, wind round, curve round
    • He looped the rope over the wood.
  2. turn, roll, circle, bend, twist, curve
    • The helicopter looped west, making for the hills.

oftentimes

(ˈɒfəntaɪmz)
Definitions
adverbUS many times or much of the time ⇒ Oftentimes, these fossils are not made available to science at all., ⇒ Oftentimes I wouldn't even return the calls., ⇒ It was oftentimes difficult to discuss certain issues while he was in the room.

oftentimes for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ɒfəntaɪmz , US ɔːf- )
Definitions
adverb [ADV with v]If something oftentimes happens, it happens many times or much of the time. [US] ⇒ Oftentimes, these fossils are not made available to science at all. ⇒ Oftentimes, I wouldn't even return the calls. ⇒ It was oftentimes difficult to discuss certain issues while he was in the room.

Circassian

(sɜːˈkæsɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a native of Circassia2. a language or languages spoken in Circassia, belonging to the North-West Caucasian family. See also Adygei, Kabardianadjective also: Circassic3. relating to Circassia, its people, or language

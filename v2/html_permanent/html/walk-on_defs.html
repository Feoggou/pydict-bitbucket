walk-on

Definitions
noun1. a. a small part in a play or theatrical entertainment, esp one without any linesb. (as modifier) ⇒ a walk-on partadjective2. (of an aircraft or air service) having seats to be booked immediately before departure rather than in advance

walk-on for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]A walk-on part in a play or film is a very small part which usually does not involve any speaking. ⇒ He and his family have walk-on parts in the latest film.

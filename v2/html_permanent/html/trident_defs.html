trident

(ˈtraɪdənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a three-pronged spear, originally from the East2. (in Greek and Roman mythology) the three-pronged spear that the sea god Poseidon (Neptune) is represented as carrying3. a three-pronged instrument, weapon, or symboladjective also: tridented4. having three prongs

Word Origin

C16: from Latin tridēns three-pronged, from tri- + dēns tooth

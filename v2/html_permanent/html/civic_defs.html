(ˈsɪvɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveof or relating to a city, citizens, or citizenship ⇒ civic duties
Derived forms
civically (ˈcivically) adverb
Word origin
C16: from Latin cīvicus, from cīvis citizen

civic

Powered by COBUILD
(sɪvɪk )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]You use civic to describe people or things that have an official status in a town or city. ⇒ ...the businessmen and civic leaders of Manchester. ⇒ ...Bromley Civic Centre.2. adjective [ADJ n]You use civic to describe the duties or feelings that people have because they belong to a particular community. ⇒ ...a sense of civic pride.

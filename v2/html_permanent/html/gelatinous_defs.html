gelatinous

(dʒɪˈlætɪnəs )
Definitions
adjective1. consisting of or resembling jelly; viscous2. of, containing, or resembling gelatine
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gelatinously (geˈlatinously) adverbgelatinousness (geˈlatinousness) noun

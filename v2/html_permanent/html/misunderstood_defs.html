(mɪsʌndəʳstʊd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. Misunderstood is the past tense and past participle of misunderstand.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as misunderstood, you mean that people do not understand them and have a wrong impression or idea of them. ⇒ Eric is very badly misunderstood. ⇒ The cost of capital is widely misunderstood. ⇒ ...a misunderstood genius.

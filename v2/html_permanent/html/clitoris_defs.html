clitoris

(ˈklɪtərɪs , ˈklaɪ-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna part of the female genitalia consisting of a small elongated highly sensitive erectile organ at the front of the vulva: homologous with the penis
Derived forms
clitoral (ˈclitoral) or clitoric (ˈclitoric) adjective
Word origin
C17: from New Latin, from Greek kleitoris; related to Greek kleiein to close

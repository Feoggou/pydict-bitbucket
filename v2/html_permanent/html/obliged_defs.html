obliged

(əˈblaɪdʒd)
Definitions
adjective1. See to feel obliged to do2. grateful ⇒ I'd be obliged for the other half., ⇒ I'm obliged to you for all the trouble you've taken.3. See much obliged4. See I would be obliged5. archaic used as a polite convention for opening or closing a letter; literally, indebted ⇒ The volume contained a dedication to John Johnston, with gratitude from his much obliged niece, Elizabeth Blackwell.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

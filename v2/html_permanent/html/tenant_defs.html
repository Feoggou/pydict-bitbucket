tenant

(ˈtɛnənt )
Definitions
noun1. a person who holds, occupies, or possesses land or property by any kind of right or title, esp from a landlord under a lease2. a person who has the use of a house, flat, etc, subject to the payment of rent3. any holder or occupantverb4. (transitive) to hold (land or property) as a tenant5. (intransitive; foll by in) rare to dwell
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tenantable (ˈtenantable) adjectivetenantless (ˈtenantless) adjectivetenant-like (ˈtenant-ˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C14: from Old French, literally: (one who is) holding, from tenir to hold, from Latin tenēre

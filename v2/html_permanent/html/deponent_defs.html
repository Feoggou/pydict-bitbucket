deponent

(dɪˈpəʊnənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. grammar (of a verb, esp in Latin) having the inflectional endings of a passive verb but the meaning of an active verbnoun2. grammar a deponent verb3. lawa. a person who makes an affidavitb. a person, esp a witness, who makes a deposition
Word origin
C16: from Latin dēpōnēns putting aside, putting down, from dēpōnere to put down, depone

paunch

(pɔːntʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the belly or abdomen, esp when protruding2. another name for rumen3. nautical a thick mat that prevents chafingverb (transitive)4. to stab in the stomach; disembowel

Word Origin

C14: from Anglo-Norman paunche, from Old French pance, from Latin panticēs (pl) bowels

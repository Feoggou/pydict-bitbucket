Ganymede1

(ˈɡænɪˌmiːd )
Definitions
nounclassical mythology a beautiful Trojan youth who was abducted by Zeus to Olympus and made the cupbearer of the gods

Ganymede2

(ˈɡænɪˌmiːd )
Definitions
nounthe brightest and largest of the four Galilean satellites of Jupiter, and the largest in the solar system. Diameter: 5262 km; orbital radius: 1 070 000 km

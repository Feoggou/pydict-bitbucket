selling

(ˈsɛlɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna. the act of offering something for saleb. (as modifier) ⇒ a selling price
Example sentences containing 'selling'
A row of milkmaids sat milking the cows near the Pall Mall, and selling cups of the warm white stuff. Fidelis Morgan THE RIVAL QUEENS: A COUNTESS ASHBY DE LA ZOUCHE MYSTERYThe rationing's bad enough without that, what with Salazar selling everything to Jerry. Robert Wilson THE COMPANY OF STRANGERSOn her hands and knees, she scrambled to her left, then rose to her feet by a stall selling kitchenware. Mark Burnell CHAMELEON

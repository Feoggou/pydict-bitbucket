pimp1

(pɪmp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a man who solicits for a prostitute or brothel and lives off the earnings2. a man who procures sexual gratification for another; procurer; panderverb3. (intransitive) to act as a pimp4. (transitive) slang to adapt or embellish in an ostentatious manner

Word Origin

C17: of unknown origin

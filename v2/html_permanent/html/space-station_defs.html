space station

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nounany large manned artificial satellite designed to orbit the earth during a long period of time thus providing a base for scientific and medical research in space and a construction site, launch pad, and docking arrangements for spacecraft. Also called: space platform, space laboratory

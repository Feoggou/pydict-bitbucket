expiry

(ɪkˈspaɪərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ries1. a. a coming to an end, esp of a contract period; termination ⇒ expiry of a leaseb. (as modifier) ⇒ the expiry date2. death

expiry

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪkspaɪəri ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [N n]The expiry of something such as a contract, deadline, or visa is the time that it comes to an end or stops being valid. [+ of] ⇒ ...the expiry of a fixed term contract. ⇒ Make a note of credit card numbers and check expiry dates.

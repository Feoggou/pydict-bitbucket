blessing

(ˈblɛsɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of invoking divine protection or aid2. the words or ceremony used for this3. a short prayer of thanksgiving before or after a meal; grace4. Also called: brachah, brocho Judaisma. a short prayer prescribed for a specific occasion and beginning "Blessed art thou, O Lord…"b. a section of the liturgy including a similar formula5. approval; good wishes ⇒ her father gave his blessing to the marriage6. the bestowal of a divine gift or favour7. a happy event or state of affairs ⇒ a blessing in disguise

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA blessing is something good that you are grateful for. [+ for] ⇒ Rivers are a blessing for an agricultural country. [+ of] ⇒ ...the blessings of prosperity.2. countable noun [usu sing, with poss]If something is done with someone's blessing, it is done with their approval and support. [+ of] ⇒ With the blessing of the White House, a group of Democrats in Congress is meeting to find additional budget cuts. ⇒ In April Thai and Indonesian leaders gave their formal blessing to the idea.3. countable nounA blessing is a prayer asking God to look kindly upon the people who are present or the event that is taking place.4. → see also bless5. →  to count your blessings6. →  a blessing in disguise7. →  a mixed blessing

administrator

(ədˈmɪnɪˌstreɪtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who administers the affairs of an organization, official body, etc2. property law a person authorized to manage an estate, esp when the owner has died intestate or without having appointed executors3. a person who manages a computer system
Derived forms
adˌministratrix (adˌminisˈtratrix) feminine noun

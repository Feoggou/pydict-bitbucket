humerus

(ˈhjuːmərəs )
Definitions
noun plural -meri (-məˌraɪ)1. the bone that extends from the shoulder to the elbow2. the corresponding bone in other vertebrates
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin umerus; related to Gothic ams shoulder, Greek ōmos

microscope

(ˈmaɪkrəˌskəʊp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an optical instrument that uses a lens or combination of lenses to produce a magnified image of a small, close object. Modern optical microscopes have magnifications of about 1500 to 2000. See also simple microscope, compound microscope, ultramicroscope2. any instrument, such as the electron microscope, for producing a magnified visual image of a small object

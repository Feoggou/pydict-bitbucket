haunted

(ˈhɔːntɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. frequented or visited by ghosts2. (postpositive) obsessed or worried

haunted

Powered by COBUILD
(hɔːntɪd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveA haunted building or other place is one where a ghost regularly appears. ⇒ Tracy said the cabin was haunted. ⇒ ...a haunted house.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who has a haunted expression looks very worried or troubled. ⇒ She looked so haunted, I almost didn't recognize her.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

widowed

(ˈwɪdəʊd)
Definitions
adjectivehaving become a widow or a widower ⇒ In 1921, his widowed mother moved to Sydney., ⇒ a mother who tries to build a relationship with her widowed daughter, ⇒ Imogen stayed with her widowed sister., ⇒ She spent 20 years looking after her widowed father., ⇒ Or a widowed man may strike up a friendship with his female neighbour.

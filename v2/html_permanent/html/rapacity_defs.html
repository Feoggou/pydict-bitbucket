rapacity

Powered by COBUILD
(rəpæsɪti ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount noun [oft with poss]Rapacity is very greedy or selfish behaviour. [formal, disapproval] ⇒ He argued that the overcrowded cities were the product of a system based on 'selfishness' and 'rapacity'. ⇒ ...the rapacity of landlords and the misery of tenants. ⇒ ...their sexual desire and rapacity.

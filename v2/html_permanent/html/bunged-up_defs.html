bunged up

(bʌŋd ʌp)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective British informal1. congested ⇒ She's not going to let anything so trivial as a bunged-up nose stop her going to the party., ⇒ This can help you feel better when you are bunged up., ⇒ My nose is bunged up., ⇒ You do sound quite bunged up.2. constipated ⇒ Being bunged up results in large amounts of gas being trapped and a spectacular explosion when your bowels eventually perform.3. blocked ⇒ The sink's got bunged up again.

clogged, stuffed-up, blocked up, jammed

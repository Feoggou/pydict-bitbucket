Definitions
reciprocal pronoun & pronounYou use each other when you are saying that each member of a group does something to the others or has a particular connection with the others. one another ⇒ We looked at each other in silence. ⇒ Both sides are willing to make allowances for each other's political sensitivities. ⇒ Uncle Paul and I hardly know each other. →  each

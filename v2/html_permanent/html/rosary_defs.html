rosary

(ˈrəʊzərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -saries1. Roman Catholic Churcha. a series of prayers counted on a string of beads, usually consisting of five or 15 decades of Aves, each decade beginning with a Paternoster and ending with a Gloriab. a string of 55 or 165 beads used to count these prayers as they are recited2. (in other religions) a similar string of beads used in praying3. a bed or garden of roses4. an archaic word for garland (sense 1)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin rosārium rose garden, from rosārius of roses, from rosa rose1

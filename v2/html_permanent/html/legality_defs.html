legality

(lɪˈɡælɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. the state or quality of being legal or lawful2. adherence to legal principles

legality

(ligælɪti ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIf you talk about the legality of an action or situation, you are talking about whether it is legal or not. [+ of] ⇒ The auditor has questioned the legality of the contracts.

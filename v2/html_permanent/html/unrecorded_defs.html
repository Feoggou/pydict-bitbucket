unrecorded for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnrɪkɔːʳdɪd )
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n, v-link ADJ, ADJ after v]You use unrecorded to describe something that has not been written down or recorded officially, especially when it should have been. ⇒ The statistics don't reveal of course unrecorded crime. ⇒ Much of Poland's private industry goes unrecorded. ⇒ Nothing is left unrecorded.

lie in

Definitions
verb (intr, adverb)1. to remain in bed late in the morning2. to be confined in childbirthnoun lie-in3. a long stay in bed in the morning

lie-in

(ˌlaɪˈɪn)
Definitions
nounBritish a long stay in bed in the morning ⇒ I have a lie-in on Sundays.

Word forms: lie-ins
Definitions
countable noun [usu sing]If you have a lie-in, you rest by staying in bed later than usual in the morning. [British, informal] ⇒ I have a lie-in on Sundays.

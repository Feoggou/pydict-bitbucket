Hindu

or Hindoo (ˈhɪnduː , hɪnˈduː )
Definitions
noun plural -dus or -doos1. a person who adheres to Hinduism2. an inhabitant or native of Hindustan or India, esp one adhering to Hinduismadjective3. relating to Hinduism, Hindus, or India
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Persian Hindū, from Hind India; see Hindi

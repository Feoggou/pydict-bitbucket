half-yearly

(ˌhɑːfˈjɪəlɪ)
Definitions
adjective1. happening in the middle of a calendar year or a financial year ⇒ This is the half-yearly meeting of the interim committee., ⇒ the Central Bank's half-yearly report on the state of the economy2. business (of a company's profits) made in six months ⇒ The company announced a half-yearly profit of just £2 million., ⇒ Their half-yearly results were published yesterday.3. happening twice a year, with six months between each event ⇒ half-yearly paymentsadverb4. twice a year ⇒ The bank pays interest half-yearly.

domesticate

(dəˈmɛstɪˌkeɪt ) or sometimes US domesticize (dəˈmɛstɪˌsaɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to bring or keep (wild animals or plants) under control or cultivation2. to accustom to home life3. to adapt to an environment ⇒ to domesticate foreign trees
Derived forms

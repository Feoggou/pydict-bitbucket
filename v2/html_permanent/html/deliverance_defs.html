deliverance

(dɪˈlɪvərəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a formal pronouncement or expression of opinion2. rescue from moral corruption or evil; salvation3. another word for delivery (sense 3), delivery (sense 4), delivery (sense 5)

hostilities

(hɒˈstɪlɪtɪz)
Definitions
plural noun1. formal fighting; warfare ⇒ The authorities have urged people to stock up on fuel in case hostilities break out., ⇒ Both sides wanted a cessation of hostilities.2. See hostility

overawed

(ˌəʊvərˈɔːd)
Definitions
adjectivesubdued, restrained, or overcome by affecting with a feeling of awe ⇒ Don't be overawed by what the experts say., ⇒ Don't be overawed by people in authority, however important they are.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

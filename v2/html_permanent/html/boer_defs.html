Boer

(bʊə , ˈbəʊə , bɔː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun, adjectivea. a descendant of any of the Dutch or Huguenot colonists who settled in South Africa, mainly in Cape Colony, the Orange Free State, and the Transvaalb. (as modifier) ⇒ a Boer farm
Word origin
C19: from Dutch Boer; see boor

instead

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnsted ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. →  instead of sth2. adverbIf you do not do something, but do something else instead, you do the second thing and not the first thing, as the result of a choice or a change of behaviour. ⇒ He reached for the wine but did not drink, pushed it, instead, across the table towards Joanna. ⇒ The kitchen might have been workable had Nicola kept it tidy; instead it was littered with pots and pans. ⇒ My husband asked why I couldn't just forget about dieting all the time and eat normally instead.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

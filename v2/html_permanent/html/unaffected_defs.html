unaffected for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnəfektɪd )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If someone or something is unaffected by an event or occurrence, they are not changed by it in any way. OPPOSITE: affected [+ by] ⇒ She seemed totally unaffected by what she'd drunk. ⇒ The strike shut down 50 airports, but most international flights were unaffected.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as unaffected, you mean that they are natural and genuine in their behaviour, and do not act as though they are more important than other people. [approval] OPPOSITE: affected ⇒ ...this unaffected, charming couple.

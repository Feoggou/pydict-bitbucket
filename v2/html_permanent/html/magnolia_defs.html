magnolia

(mæɡˈnəʊlɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any tree or shrub of the magnoliaceous genus Magnolia of Asia and North America: cultivated for their white, pink, purple, or yellow showy flowers2. the flower of any of these plants3. a very pale pinkish-white or purplish-white colour

Word Origin

C18: New Latin, named after Pierre Magnol (1638–1715), French botanist

col

(kɒl , French kɔl)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the lowest point of a ridge connecting two mountain peaks, often constituting a pass2. meteorology a pressure region between two anticyclones and two depressions, associated with variable weather
Word origin
C19: from French: neck, col, from Latin collum neck

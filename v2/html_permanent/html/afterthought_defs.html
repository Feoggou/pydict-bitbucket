afterthought

(ˈɑːftəˌθɔːt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a comment, reply, etc, that occurs to one after the opportunity to deliver it has passed2. an addition to something already completed

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [usu sing]If you do or say something as an afterthought, you do or say it after something else as an addition, perhaps without careful thought. ⇒ Almost as an afterthought he added that he missed her.

relaxation

(ˌriːlækˈseɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. rest or refreshment, as after work or effort; recreation2. a form of rest or recreation ⇒ his relaxation is cricket3. a partial lessening of a punishment, duty, etc4. the act of relaxing or state of being relaxed5. physics the return of a system to equilibrium after a displacement from this state6. mathematics a method by which errors resulting from an approximation are reduced by using new approximations

relaxation

(riːlækseɪʃən ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount noun [oft N n]Relaxation is a way of spending time in which you rest and feel comfortable. ⇒ You should be able to find the odd moment for relaxation. ⇒ Relaxation techniques are taught in hospitals in order to help all sorts of conditions.2. uncount nounIf there is relaxation of a rule or control, it is made less firm or strong. [+ of/in] ⇒ ...the relaxation of travel restrictions.

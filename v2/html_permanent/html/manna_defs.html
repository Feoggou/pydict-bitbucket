manna

(ˈmænə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Old Testament the miraculous food which sustained the Israelites in the wilderness (Exodus 16:14–36)2. any spiritual or divine nourishment3. a windfall; an unexpected gift (esp in the phrase manna from heaven)4. a sweet substance obtained from various plants, esp from an ash tree, Fraxinus ornus (manna or flowering ash) of S Europe, used as a mild laxative

Word Origin

Old English via Late Latin from Greek, from Hebrew mān

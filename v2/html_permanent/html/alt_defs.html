alt

(ælt ) music
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (esp of vocal music) high in pitch2. of or relating to the octave commencing with the G above the top line of the treble staffnoun3. See in alt
Word origin
C16: from Provençal, from Latin altus high, deep

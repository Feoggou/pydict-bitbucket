Powered by COBUILD
(hɔːltɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you speak or do something in a halting way, you speak or do it slowly and with a lot of hesitation, usually because you are uncertain about what to say or do next. ⇒ In a halting voice she said that she wished to make a statement. ⇒ The officer replied in halting German. ⇒ Russia's efforts to attract investment have been halting and confused.haltingly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ She spoke haltingly of her deep upset and hurt. ⇒ Kenya is moving haltingly towards democracy.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

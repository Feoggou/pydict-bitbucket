smoking

(ˈsməʊkɪŋ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or habit of smoking cigarettes, cigars, or a pipe ⇒ Smoking is now banned in many places of work., ⇒ Smoking is bad for you., ⇒ This is a no-smoking area., ⇒ I'm trying to give up smoking.adjective2. emitting smoke, sometimes excessively ⇒ a pile of smoking rubble3. intended for people who want to smoke cigarettes, etc ⇒ Would you like smoking or non-smoking?

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Clipped means neatly cut. ⇒ ...a quiet street of clipped hedges and flowering gardens. ⇒ ...a dapper man with a clipped moustache.2. adjectiveIf you say that someone has a clipped way of speaking, you mean they speak with quick, short sounds, and usually that they sound upper-class. ⇒ The Chief Constable's clipped tones crackled over the telephone line.

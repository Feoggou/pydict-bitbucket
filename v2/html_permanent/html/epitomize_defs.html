epitomize

or epitomise (ɪˈpɪtəˌmaɪz )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to be a personification of; typify2. to make an epitome of
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Definitions
regional note:   in BRIT, also use epitomiseverbIf you say that something or someone epitomizes a particular thing, you mean that they are a perfect example of it. [be V-ed + by] ⇒ Lyonnais cooking is epitomized by the so-called 'bouchons'. [V n] ⇒ ...the sleek lift that epitomized the hotel's glossy decor.

blazing

(ˈbleɪzɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. burning or shining very brightly ⇒ Quite a few people were eating outside in the blazing sun.2. British (of a row or argument) very intense and acrimonious ⇒ My husband has just had a blazing row with his boss.3. displaying a lot of emotion, esp anger ⇒ Miss Turner turned blazing eyes on the victim.adverb4. involving fire or great heat ⇒ freezing cold winters and blazing hot summers

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Blazing sun or blazing hot weather is very hot. ⇒ Quite a few people were eating outside in the blazing sun. ⇒ ...freezing cold winters and blazing hot summers.2. adjective [ADJ n]When people have a blazing row, they quarrel in a very noisy and excited way. [British] ⇒ My husband has just had a blazing row with his boss.

concurrence

(kənˈkʌrəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of concurring2. agreement in opinion; accord; assent3. cooperation or combination4. simultaneous occurrence; coincidence5. geometry a point at which three or more lines intersect

Word forms: concurrences
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable noun [oft with poss]Someone's concurrence is their agreement to something. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ Any change ought not to be made without the general concurrence of all concerned.2. variable nounIf there is a concurrence of two or more things, they happen at the same time. [+ of] ⇒ The concurrence of their disappearances had to be more than coincidental.

conflicted

(kənˈflɪktɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveunable to decide between opposing feelings or views ⇒ I honestly don't know what I think about going to war. I'm conflicted. Most of me says yes, some of me says no., ⇒ Some who oppose abortion admit they are conflicted in cases of rape and incest., ⇒ She tried to weave women's conflicted feelings about work and family into the life of her main character., ⇒ In the end, I think, we're conflicted about shopping., ⇒ she is conflicted about having a second baby, ⇒ Americans remain understandably conflicted about a war that has cost 1,519 American lives.

beagle

(ˈbiːɡəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a small sturdy breed of hound, having a smooth dense coat usually of white, tan, and black; often used (esp formerly) for hunting hares2. archaic a person who spies on othersverb3. (intransitive) to hunt with beagles, normally on foot
Word origin
C15: of uncertain origin

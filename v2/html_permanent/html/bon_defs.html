Bon1

(bɔːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: Feast of Lanterns, Festival of Lanterns an annual festival celebrated by Japanese Buddhists2. a. the pre-Buddhist priests of Tibet or one such priestb. their religion
Word origin
from Japanese bon, originally Urabon, from Sanskrit ullambana hanging upside down

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If someone is perished, they are extremely cold. [British, informal] ⇒ I was absolutely perished. ⇒ I was perished. No jacket, no torch, wet through, exhausted.2. → see also perish

oxidize

or oxidise (ˈɒksɪˌdaɪz )
Definitions
verb1. to undergo or cause to undergo a chemical reaction with oxygen, as in formation of an oxide2. to form or cause to form a layer of metal oxide, as in rusting3. to lose or cause to lose hydrogen atoms4. to undergo or cause to undergo a decrease in the number of electrons. Compare reduce (sense 12c)

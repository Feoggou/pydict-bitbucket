Satan

(ˈseɪtən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe devil, adversary of God, and tempter of mankind: sometimes identified with Lucifer (Luke 4:5–8)
Word origin
Old English, from Late Latin, from Greek, from Hebrew: plotter, from sātan to plot against

Satan

Powered by COBUILD
(seɪtən )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
proper nounIn the Christian religion, Satan is the Devil, a powerful evil being who is the chief opponent of God.

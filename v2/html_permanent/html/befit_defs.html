befit

(bɪˈfɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb -fits, -fitting or -fitted(transitive) to be appropriate to or suitable for
Derived forms
befitting (beˈfitting) adjectivebefittingly (beˈfittingly) adverb
Word origin
C15: from be- + fit1

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf something befits a person or thing, it is suitable or appropriate for them. [formal] [V n] ⇒ They offered him a post befitting his seniority and experience. [V n] ⇒ He writes beautifully, as befits a poet.

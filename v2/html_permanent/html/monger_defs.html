monger

(ˈmʌŋɡə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (in combination except in archaic use) a trader or dealer ⇒ ironmonger2. (in combination) a promoter of something unpleasant ⇒ warmonger

Derived Forms

mongering (ˈmongering) noun, adjective

Word Origin

Old English mangere, ultimately from Latin mangō dealer; compare Old High German mangari

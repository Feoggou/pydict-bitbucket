reclining

(rɪˈklaɪnɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. capable of being lowered in order to be more comfortable to sit in ⇒ a soft reclining chair2. art leaning or lying back in a resting position ⇒ Move to a reclining position on the mattress and rest for 15 minutes., ⇒ the giant reclining figure of the Buddha, ⇒ a painting of a reclining nude

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of a person) continuing to live, esp after a dangerous situation such as an accident or an illness ⇒ She wants to spend the remaining years of her life or France, where her surviving friends are., ⇒ William Shakespeare's last surviving descendant2. continuing to exist ⇒ Due to an overall lack of surviving correspondence, Jane Austen's biographers have an incomplete picture of what her day-to-day life was really like., ⇒ The railway line, its only surviving link with the north, had been blown up in several places., ⇒ surviving examples of 19th-century architecture in the Mid-West

affected1

(əˈfɛktɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective (usually postpositive)1. deeply moved, esp by sorrow or grief ⇒ he was greatly affected by her departure2. changed, esp detrimentally
Word origin
C17: from affect1 + -ed2

affected2

(əˈfɛktɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. behaving, speaking, etc, in an artificial or assumed way, esp in order to impress others2. feigned ⇒ affected indifference3. archaic inclined; disposed
Derived forms
affectedly (afˈfectedly) adverbaffectedness (afˈfectedness) noun
Word origin
C16: from affect2 + -ed2

affected

Powered by COBUILD
(əfektɪd )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe someone's behaviour as affected, you disapprove of the fact that they behave in an unnatural way that is intended to impress other people. [disapproval] ⇒ She had an affected air and a disdainful look.

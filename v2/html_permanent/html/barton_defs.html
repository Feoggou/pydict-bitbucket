barton

(ˈbɑːtən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounarchaic a farmyard
Word origin
Old English beretūn, from bere barley + tūn stockade; see town

Barton

(ˈbɑːtən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Sir Derek (Harold Richard). 1918–98, British organic chemist: shared the Nobel prize for chemistry (1969) for his work on conformational analysis2.  Sir Edmund. 1849–1920, Australian statesman; first prime minister of Australia (1901–03)3.  Elizabeth, known as the Maid of Kent. ?1506–34, English nun, who claimed the gift of prophecy. Her criticism of Henry VIII's attempt to annul his first marriage led to her execution4.  John (Bernard Adie). born 1928, British theatre director, noted esp for his productions of Shakespeare

muted

(ˈmjuːtɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of a sound or colour) softened ⇒ a muted pink shirt2. (of an emotion or action) subdued or restrained ⇒ his response was muted3. (of a musical instrument) being played while fitted with a mute ⇒ muted trumpet

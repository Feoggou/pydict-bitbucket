cote1

(kəʊt ) or cot
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. a small shelter for pigeons, sheep, etcb. (in combination) ⇒ dovecote2. dialect, mainly British a small cottage
Word origin
Old English cote; related to Low German Kote; see cot2

cote2

(kəʊt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(transitive) archaic to pass by, outstrip, or surpass
Word origin
C16: perhaps from Old French costoier to run alongside, from coste side; see coast

Southerner

(ˈsʌðənə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun(sometimes not capital) a native or inhabitant of the south of any specified region, esp the South of England or the Southern states of the US

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA southerner is a person who was born in or lives in the south of a place or country. ⇒ Bob Wilson is a Southerner, from Texas. ⇒ Southerners smoke less and drink less than those in other parts of the country.

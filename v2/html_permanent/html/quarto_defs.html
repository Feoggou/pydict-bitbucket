quarto

(ˈkwɔːtəʊ )
Definitions
noun plural -tos1. a book size resulting from folding a sheet of paper, usually crown or demy, into four leaves or eight pages, each one quarter the size of the sheet. Often written: 4to, 4°2. (formerly) a size of cut paper 10 in. by 8 in. (25.4 cm by 20.3 cm)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from New Latin phrase in quartō in quarter

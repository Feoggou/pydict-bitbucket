tableau

(ˈtæbləʊ )
Definitions
noun plural -leaux (-ləʊ, -ləʊz) or -leaus1. same as tableau vivant2. a pause during or at the end of a scene on stage when all the performers briefly freeze in position3. any dramatic group or scene4. logic short for semantic tableau
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from French, from Old French tablel a picture, diminutive of table

tableau

Powered by COBUILD
(tæbloʊ ) for learners of English
Word forms: tableaux
Definitions
1. countable nounA tableau is a scene, for example from the Bible, history, or mythology, that consists of a group of people in costumes who do not speak or move. The people are sometimes on a float in a procession. ⇒ ...a nativity tableau. ⇒ ...tableaux depicting the foundation of Barcelona.2. countable nounA tableau is a piece of art such as a sculpture or painting that shows a scene, especially one from the Bible, history, or mythology. ⇒ ...Gaudi's luxuriant stone tableau of the Nativity on the cathedral's east face.

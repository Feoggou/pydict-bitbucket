peter1

(ˈpiːtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(intr; foll by out or away) to fall (off) in volume, intensity, etc, and finally cease ⇒ the cash petered out in three months

Word Origin

C19: of unknown origin

peter2 - bridge, whist

(ˈpiːtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (intransitive)1. to play a high card before a low one in a suit, usually a conventional signal of a doubleton holding or of strength in that suitnoun2. the act of petering

Word Origin

C20: perhaps a special use of peter1 (to fall off in power)

Definitions

noun slang1. a safe, till, or cash box2. a prison cell3. the witness box in a courtroom4. mainly US a slang word for penis

Word Origin

C17 (meaning a case): from the name Peter

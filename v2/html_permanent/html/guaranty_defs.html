guaranty

(ˈɡærəntɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. a pledge of responsibility for fulfilling another person's obligations in case of that person's default2. a thing given or taken as security for a guaranty3. the act of providing security4. a person who acts as a guarantorverb -ties, -tying or -tied5. a variant of guarantee
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Old French garantie, variant of warantie, of Germanic origin; see warranty

Similar Words

pledge, word, promise, contract, agreement, insurance, bond, guarantee, undertaking, assurance, warrant, oath, warranty, covenant
security, bond, earnest, deposit, pledge, token, bail, collateral, pawn, gage

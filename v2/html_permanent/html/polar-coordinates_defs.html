Word Frequency

polar coordinates

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nouna pair of coordinates for locating a point in a plane by means of the length of a radius vector, r, which pivots about the origin to establish the angle, θ, that the position of the point makes with a fixed line. Usually written (r, θ). See also Cartesian coordinates, spherical coordinates

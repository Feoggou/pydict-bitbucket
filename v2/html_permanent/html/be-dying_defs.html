be dying

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
(foll by for or an infinitive) to be eager or desperate (for something or to do something) ⇒ I'm dying to see the new house▷ See die

Fred notices that the twisted branches of the medlar look as if they might be dying. Sara MacDonald SEA MUSICThey'll all be dying to know if we've heard anything when we go back.

magistracy

(ˈmædʒɪstrəsɪ ) or magistrature (ˈmædʒɪstrəˌtjʊə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cies or -tures1. the office or function of a magistrate2. magistrates collectively3. the district under the jurisdiction of a magistrate

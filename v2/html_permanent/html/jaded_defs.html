Definitions
adjective1. exhausted or dissipated2. satiated

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are jaded, you feel bored, tired, and not enthusiastic, for example because you have had too much of the same thing. ⇒ We had both become jaded, disinterested, and disillusioned. ⇒ ...his air of jaded cynicism.

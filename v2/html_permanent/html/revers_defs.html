revers

(rɪˈvɪə )
Definitions
noun plural -vers (-ˈvɪəz)(usually plural) the turned-back lining of part of a garment, esp of a lapel or cuff
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from French, literally: reverse

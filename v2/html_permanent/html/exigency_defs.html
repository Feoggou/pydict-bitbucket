exigency

(ˈɛksɪdʒənsɪ , ɪɡˈzɪdʒənsɪ ) or exigence (ˈɛksɪdʒəns )
Definitions
noun plural -gencies or -gences1. the state of being exigent; urgency2. (often plural) an urgent demand; pressing requirement3. an emergency
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
countable noun [usu pl]The exigencies of a situation or a job are the demands or difficulties that you have to deal with as part of it. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ ...the exigencies of a wartime economy.

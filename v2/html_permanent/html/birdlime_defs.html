birdlime

(ˈbɜːdˌlaɪm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a sticky substance, prepared from holly, mistletoe, or other plants, smeared on twigs to catch small birdsverb2. (transitive) to smear (twigs) with birdlime to catch (small birds)

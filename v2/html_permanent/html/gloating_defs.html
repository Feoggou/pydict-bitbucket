Word Frequency

gloating

(ˈɡləʊtɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the act or process of dwelling on something with malevolent smugness or exultationadjective2. dwelling on something with malevolent smugness or exultation; smug ⇒ the merest hint of a gloating smile
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

gloatingly (ˈgloatingly) adverb

I don't want those ghouls from the council gawping at his wounds and gloating over a victory they did nothing to achieve.

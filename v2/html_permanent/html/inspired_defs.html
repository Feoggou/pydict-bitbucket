inspired

(ɪnˈspaɪəd )
Definitions
adjective1. aroused or guided by or as if aroused or guided by divine inspiration ⇒ an inspired performance, ⇒ she was like one inspired2. extremely accurate or apt but based on intuition rather than knowledge or logical deduction ⇒ an inspired guess
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

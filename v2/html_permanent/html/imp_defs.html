imp

(ɪmp )
Definitions
noun1. a small demon or devil; mischievous sprite2. a mischievous childverb3. (transitive) falconry to insert (new feathers) into the stumps of broken feathers in order to repair the wing of a hawk or falcon
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English impa bud, graft, hence offspring, child, from impian to graft, ultimately from Greek emphutos implanted, from emphuein to implant, from phuein to plant

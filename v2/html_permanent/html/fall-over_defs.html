fall over

Definitions
verb (intr, adverb)1. to lose one's balance and collapse to the ground2. to fall from an upright position ⇒ the vase fell over3. See fall over oneself

fall over

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
phrasal verbIf a person or object that is standing falls over, they accidentally move from their upright position so that they are then lying on the ground or on the surface supporting them. [V P] ⇒ If he drinks more than two glasses of wine he falls over. →  fall

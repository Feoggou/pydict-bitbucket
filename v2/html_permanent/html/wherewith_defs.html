wherewith

(wɛəˈwɪθ , -ˈwɪð) archaic or formal
Definitions
pronoun1. (often foll by an infinitive) with or by which ⇒ the pen wherewith I am wont to write2. something with which ⇒ I have not wherewith to buy my breadadverb3. with what?sentence connector4. with or after that; whereupon
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'wherewith'
`He's coming," fourteen-year-old Stephen said in the new, deep voice wherewith he sometimes startled his family nowadays. Brent-Dyer, Elinor ADRIENNE AND THE CHALET SCHOOL

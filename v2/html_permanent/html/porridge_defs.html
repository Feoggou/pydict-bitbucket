porridge

(ˈpɒrɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a dish made from oatmeal or another cereal, cooked in water or milk to a thick consistency2. slang a term in prison (esp in the phrase do porridge)

Word Origin

C16: variant (influenced by Middle English porray pottage) of pottage

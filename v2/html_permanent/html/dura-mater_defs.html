dura mater

(ˈdjʊərə ˈmeɪtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe outermost and toughest of the three membranes covering the brain and spinal cord. Often shortened to: dura See also meninges
Word origin
C15: from Medieval Latin, hard mother

(dʌnˈkɜːk) or Dunkerque
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna port in N France, on the Strait of Dover: scene of the evacuation of British and other Allied troops after the fall of France in 1940; industrial centre with an oil refinery and naval shipbuilding yards. Pop: 70 850 (1999)

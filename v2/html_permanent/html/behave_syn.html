behave

verb

  1. act, react, conduct yourself, acquit yourself, comport yourself • He'd behaved badly.
  2. (often reflexive) be well-behaved, be good, be polite, mind your manners, keep your nose clean, act correctly, act politely, conduct yourself properly • You have to behave. • Sit down and behave yourself. OPPOSITES:  carry on (informal), be bad, act up (informal), misbehave, be naughty, muck about (British, slang), get up to mischief (informal), be insubordinate

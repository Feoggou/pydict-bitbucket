Word forms: one-pieces
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]A one-piece article of clothing consists of one piece only, rather than two or more separate parts. ⇒ ...a blue one-piece bathing suit.2. countable nounA one-piece is a type of woman's swimming costume that consists of one piece of material and which covers her chest. ⇒ A one-piece is more flattering than a bikini.

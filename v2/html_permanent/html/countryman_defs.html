countryman

(ˈkʌntrɪmən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -men1. a person who lives in the country2. a person from a particular country or from one's own country (esp in the phrase fellow countryman)
Derived forms
countryˌwoman (ˈcountryˌwoman) feminine noun gender-neutral form compatriot

(kʌntrimən )
Word forms: countrymen
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu poss N]Your countrymen are people from your own country. ⇒ He lost last year's final to fellow countryman Michael Stich.2. countable nounA countryman is a person who lives in the country rather than in a city or a town. ⇒ He had the red face of a countryman.

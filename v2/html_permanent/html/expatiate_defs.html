Word Frequency

expatiate

(ɪkˈspeɪʃɪˌeɪt )
Definitions
verb (intransitive)1. (foll by on or upon) to enlarge (on a theme, topic, etc) at length or in detail; elaborate (on)2. rare to wander about
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

expatiation (exˌpatiˈation) nounexpatiator (exˈpatiˌator) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin exspatiārī to digress, from spatiārī to walk about

Similar Words

go into detail about, develop on, elaborate on, enlarge on, dwell on, amplify on, embellish, expound on, dilate on, descant on

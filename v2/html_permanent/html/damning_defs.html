damning

(ˈdæmɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(of evidence or a report) suggesting very strongly that someone is guilty of a crime or has made a serious mistake ⇒ New and damning evidence has been uncovered., ⇒ a damning report on his handling of the affair, ⇒ He was most damning in his comments about the army.

Gomorrah

or Gomorrha (ɡəˈmɒrə )
Definitions
noun1. Old Testament one of two ancient cities near the Dead Sea, the other being Sodom, that were destroyed by God as a punishment for the wickedness of their inhabitants (Genesis 19:24)2. any place notorious for vice and depravity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

Gomorrean (Goˈmorrean) or Gomorrhean (Goˈmorrhean) adjective

solely

(ˈsəʊllɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb1. only; completely; entirely2. without another or others; singly; alone3. for one thing only

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverb [ADV with v]If something involves solely one thing, it involves only this thing and no others. ⇒ Too often we make decisions based solely upon what we see in the magazines. ⇒ This program is a production of NPR, which is solely responsible for its content.

inharmonious

(ˌɪnhɑːˈməʊnɪəs )
Definitions
adjective1. Also: inharmonic, inharmonical lacking harmony; discordant2. lacking accord or agreement
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inharmoniously (ˌinharˈmoniously) adverbinharmoniousness (ˌinharˈmoniousness) noun

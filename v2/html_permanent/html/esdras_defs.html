Esdras

(ˈɛzdræs )
Definitions
noun1. either of two books of the Apocrypha, I and II Esdras, called III and IV Esdras, in the Douay Bible2. either of two books of the Douay Bible Old Testament, I and II Esdras corresponding to the books of Ezra and Nehemiah in the Authorized Version

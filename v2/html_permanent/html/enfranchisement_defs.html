enfranchisement

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnfræntʃaɪzmənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounEnfranchisement is the condition of someone being enfranchised. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ ...the enfranchisement of the country's blacks.

evening dress

Definitions
nounattire for wearing at a formal occasion during the evening, esp (for men) a dinner jacket and black tie, or (less commonly, for women) a floor-length gown

Word forms: evening dresses
Definitions
1. uncount nounEvening dress consists of the formal clothes that people wear to formal occasions in the evening.2. countable nounAn evening dress is a special dress, usually a long one, that a woman wears to a formal occasion in the evening.

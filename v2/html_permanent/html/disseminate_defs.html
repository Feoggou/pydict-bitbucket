disseminate

(dɪˈsɛmɪˌneɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(transitive) to distribute or scatter about; diffuse
Derived forms
disˌsemination (disˌsemiˈnation) noundisseminative (disˈseminative) adjectivedissemiˌnator (disˈsemiˌnator) noun
Word origin
C17: from Latin dissēmināre, from dis-1 + sēmināre to sow, from sēmen seed

related

(rɪˈleɪtɪd )
Definitions
adjective1. connected; associated2. connected by kinship or marriage3. (in diatonic music) denoting or relating to a key that has notes in common with another key or keys

related

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪleɪtɪd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjectiveIf two or more things are related, there is a connection between them. ⇒ The philosophical problems of chance and of free will are closely related. ⇒ ...equipment and accessories for diving and related activities.2. adjective [v-link ADJ]People who are related belong to the same family. [+ to] ⇒ ...people in countries like Bangladesh who have been able to show they are related to a spouse or parent living in Britain.3. adjectiveIf you say that different types of things, such as languages, are related, you mean that they developed from the same language. [+ to] ⇒ Sanskrit is related very closely to Latin, Greek, and the Germanic and Celtic languages. ⇒ ...closely related species.

adjoin

(əˈdʒɔɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to be next to (an area of land, etc)2. (transitive; foll by to) to join; affix or attach
Word origin
C14: via Old French from Latin adjungere, from ad- to + jungere to join

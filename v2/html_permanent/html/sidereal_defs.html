sidereal

(saɪˈdɪərɪəl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or involving the stars2. determined with reference to one or more stars ⇒ the sidereal day

Derived Forms

sidereally (siˈdereally) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin sīdereus, from sīdus a star, a constellation

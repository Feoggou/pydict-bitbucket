miscarriage

(mɪsˈkærɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (also ˈmɪskær-) spontaneous expulsion of a fetus from the womb, esp prior to the 20th week of pregnancy2. an act of mismanagement or failure ⇒ a miscarriage of justice3. British the failure of freight to reach its destination

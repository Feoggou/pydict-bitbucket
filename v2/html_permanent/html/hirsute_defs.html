hirsute

(ˈhɜːsjuːt )
Definitions
adjective1. covered with hair2. (of plants or their parts) covered with long but not stiff hairs3. (of a person) having long, thick, or untrimmed hair
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hirsuteness (ˈhirsuteness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin hirsūtus shaggy; related to Latin horrēre to bristle, hirtus hairy; see horrid

joint account in Finance

Powered by COBUILD
(dʒɔɪnt əkaʊnt)
Word forms: (regular plural) joint accounts
Definitions
noun(Finance: Banking) A joint account is a bank account that is jointly owned by two people together, for example a married couple, or a parent and child. ⇒ We both used to have checking accounts, but now that my wife is retired, we use one joint account. ⇒ In the case of a joint account, each may make withdrawals from the account independently. ⇒ A joint account is a bank account that is jointly owned by two people together, for example a married couple, or a parent and child.

extol

or US extoll (ɪkˈstəʊl )
Definitions
verb -tols, -tolling, -tolled or US -tolls, -tolling or -tolled(transitive) to praise lavishly; exalt
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

extoller (exˈtoller) nounextollingly (exˈtollingly) adverbextolment (exˈtolment) noun

Word Origin

C15: from Latin extollere to elevate, from tollere to raise

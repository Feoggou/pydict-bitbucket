carcinoma

(ˌkɑːsɪˈnəʊmə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -mas or -mata (-mətə) pathology1. any malignant tumour derived from epithelial tissue2. another name for cancer (sense 1)
Derived forms
ˌcarcinomaˌtoid (ˌcarciˈnomaˌtoid) or ˌcarcinomatous (ˌcarciˈnomatous) adjective
Word origin
C18: from Latin, from Greek karkinōma, from karkinos cancer

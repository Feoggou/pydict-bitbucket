tetra

(ˈtɛtrə )
Definitions
noun plural -ra or -rasany of various brightly coloured tropical freshwater fishes of the genus Hemigrammus and related genera: family Characidae (characins)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C20: short for New Latin tetragonopterus (former genus name), from tetragon + -o- + -pterous, from Greek pteron wing

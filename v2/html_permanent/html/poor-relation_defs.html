Word forms: poor relations
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounIf you describe one thing as a poor relation of another, you mean that it is similar to or part of the other thing, but is considered to be inferior to it. [+ of] ⇒ Watercolour still seems somehow to be the poor relation of oil painting.
Example sentences containing 'poor relation'
I look like some poor relation up from the country, she thought. Pacter, Trudi YELLOW BIRDIt is a tricky moment when any author finds a cherished, hard-wrought work in the position of poor relation in a second-hand bookshop. Salley Vickers MR GOLIGHTLY'S HOLIDAY

unnamed

(ʌnˈneɪmd )
Definitions
adjective1. having no name2. not mentioned by name ⇒ the culprit shall remain unnamed
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

unnamed for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnneɪmd )
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Unnamed people or things are talked about but their names are not mentioned. ⇒ An unnamed man collapsed and died while he was walking near Dundonald. ⇒ The cash comes from an unnamed source.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Unnamed things have not been given a name. ⇒ ...unnamed comets and asteroids.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

satisfactory

(ˌsætɪsˈfæktərɪ , -trɪ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. adequate or suitable; acceptable ⇒ a satisfactory answer2. giving satisfaction3. constituting or involving atonement, recompense, or expiation for sin

Derived Forms

ˌsatisfactorily (ˌsatisˈfactorily) adverbˌsatisfactoriness (ˌsatisˈfactoriness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is satisfactory is acceptable to you or fulfils a particular need or purpose. ⇒ I never got a satisfactory answer. ⇒ It seemed a very satisfactory arrangement. ⇒ Neither solution seemed satisfactory.satisfactorily (sætɪsfæktərɪli ) graded adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Their motives have never been satisfactorily explained.

nutritious

(njuːˈtrɪʃəs )
Definitions
adjectivenourishing, sometimes to a high degree
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

nutritiously (nuˈtritiously) adverbnutritiousness (nuˈtritiousness) noun

Word Origin

C17: from Latin nūtrīcius nourishing, from nūtrix a nurse

nutritious for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(njuːtrɪʃəs , US nuː- )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveNutritious food contains substances which help your body to be healthy. ⇒ It is always important to choose enjoyable, nutritious foods. ⇒ Some ready made meals are nutritious and very easy to prepare.

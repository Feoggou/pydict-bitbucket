butter

(ˈbʌtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a. an edible fatty whitish-yellow solid made from cream by churning, for cooking and table useb. (as modifier) ⇒ butter icing ▶ Related adjective: butyraceous2. any substance with a butter-like consistency, such as peanut butter or vegetable butter3. See look as if butter wouldn't melt in one's mouthverb (transitive)4. to put butter on or in5. to flatter▷ See also butter up
Word origin
Old English butere, from Latin būtyrum, from Greek bouturon, from bous cow + turos cheese

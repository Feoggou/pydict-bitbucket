gravitate

(ˈɡrævɪˌteɪt )
Definitions
verb (intransitive)1. physics to move under the influence of gravity2. (usually foll by to or towards) to be influenced or drawn, as by strong impulses3. to sink or settle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

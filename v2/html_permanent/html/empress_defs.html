empress

(ˈɛmprɪs )
Definitions
noun1. the wife or widow of an emperor2. a woman who holds the rank of emperor in her own right3. a woman of great power and influence
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C12: from Old French empereriz, from Latin imperātrix feminine of imperātor emperor

(seɪf ˈkɒndʌkt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounanother name for safe passage ⇒ They were issued with certificates guaranteeing safe conduct., ⇒ Her family was given safe conduct to Britain when civil war broke out., ⇒ a guarantee of safe conduct signed personally by General Williams

safe-conduct

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a document giving official permission to travel through a region, esp in time of war2. the protection afforded by such a documentverb (transitive)3. to conduct (a person) in safety4. to give a safe-conduct to

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft a N]If you are given safe conduct, the authorities officially allow you to travel somewhere, guaranteeing that you will not be arrested or harmed while doing so. [+ to/from] ⇒ Her family was given safe conduct to Britain when civil war broke out. ⇒ ...a guarantee of safe conduct signed personally by General Williams.

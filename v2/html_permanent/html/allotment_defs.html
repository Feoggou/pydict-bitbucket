allotment

(əˈlɒtmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of allotting; apportionment2. a portion or amount allotted3. British a small piece of usually public land rented by an individual for cultivation

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounIn Britain, an allotment is a small area of land in a town which a person rents to grow plants and vegetables on.2. countable nounAn allotment of something is a share or amount of it that is given to someone. [+ of] ⇒ His meager allotment of gas had to be saved for emergencies.

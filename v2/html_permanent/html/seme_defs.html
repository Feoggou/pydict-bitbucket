Definitions

semé - French

or semée (ˈsɛmeɪ , səme)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective(postpositive; usually foll by of) heraldry dotted (with) ⇒ semé of fleurs-de-lys gules. Also: seméed

Word Origin

C16: from French, literally: sown, from semer to sow, from Latin sēmināre, from sēmen seed

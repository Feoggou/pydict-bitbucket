agonized

or agonised (ˈæɡənaɪzd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. showing that acute mental or physical pain is felt ⇒ the agonized screams of other prisoners under torture, ⇒ The agonized look on his face said he wouldn't be staying in the game much longer.2. feeling acute physical pain ⇒ his agonized body bathed in an icy malarial sweat

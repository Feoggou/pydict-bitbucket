Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are contented, you are satisfied with your life or the situation you are in. OPPOSITE: discontented ⇒ Whenever he returns to this place he is happy and contented. ⇒ She was gazing at him with a soft, contented smile on her face.contentedly graded adverb ⇒ The landlady sighed contentedly.

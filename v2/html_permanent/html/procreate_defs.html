procreate

(ˈprəʊkrɪˌeɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to beget or engender (offspring)2. (transitive) to bring into being

Derived Forms

procreant (ˈprocreant) or procreˌative (ˈprocreˌative) adjectiveˌprocreation (ˌprocreˈation) nounprocreˌator (ˈprocreˌator) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin prōcreāre, from pro-1 + creāre to create

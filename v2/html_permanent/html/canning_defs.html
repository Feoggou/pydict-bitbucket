(ˈkænɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Charles John, 1st Earl Canning. 1812–62, British statesman; governor general of India (1856–58) and first viceroy (1858–62)2.  his father, George. 1770–1827, British Tory statesman; foreign secretary (1822–27) and prime minister (1827)

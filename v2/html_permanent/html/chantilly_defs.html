Chantilly

(ʃænˈtɪlɪ , French ʃɑ̃tiji)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a town in N France, near the Forest of Chantilly formerly famous for lace and porcelain. Pop: 10 902 (1999)2. Also called: Tiffany a breed of medium-sized cat with silky semi-long hairadjective3. (of cream) lightly sweetened and whipped

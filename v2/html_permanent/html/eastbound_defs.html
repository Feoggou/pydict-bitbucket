(iːstbaʊnd ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjective [ADJ n]Eastbound roads or vehicles lead to or are travelling towards the east. [formal] ⇒ He caught an eastbound train to Tottenham Court Road.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'eastbound'
He was in high spirits, whistling under his breath as he drove out of the lot onto Grant and from there onto eastbound I-10. J.A. Jance KISS OF THE BEES

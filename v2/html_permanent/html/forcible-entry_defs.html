forcible entry

(ˈfɔːsɪbəl ˈɛntrɪ)
Definitions
nounlaw entry into a building by force, eg by forcing a lock ⇒ There were no signs of forcible entry and the home was locked from the inside., ⇒ A teen, arrested Friday, is charged with robbery and forcible entry., ⇒ At present forcible entry is allowed only on commercial premises.

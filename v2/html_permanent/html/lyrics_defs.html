lyrics

(ˈlɪrɪks)
Definitions
plural nounthe words of a popular song ⇒ Kurt Weill's Broadway opera with lyrics by Langston Hughes, ⇒ He wrote the lyrics for Abba's songs.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

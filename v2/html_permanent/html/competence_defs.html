competence

(ˈkɒmpɪtəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the condition of being capable; ability2. a sufficient income to live on3. the state of being legally competent or qualified4. embryology the ability of embryonic tissues to react to external conditions in a way that influences subsequent development5. linguistics (in transformational grammar) the form of the human language faculty, independent of its psychological embodiment in actual human beings. Compare performance (sense 7), langue, parole (sense 5)

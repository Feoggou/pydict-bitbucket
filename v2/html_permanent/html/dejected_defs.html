graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are dejected, you feel miserable or unhappy, especially because you have just been disappointed by something. ⇒ Everyone has days when they feel dejected or down.dejectedly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Passengers queued dejectedly for the increasingly dirty toilets.

prosper

(ˈprɒspə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(usually intr) to thrive, succeed, etc, or cause to thrive, succeed, etc in a healthy way

Word Origin

C15: from Latin prosperāre to succeed, from prosperus fortunate, from pro-1 + spēs hope

verbIf people or businesses prosper, they are successful and do well. [formal] [V] ⇒ The high street banks continue to prosper. [V] ⇒ His teams have always prospered in cup competitions.

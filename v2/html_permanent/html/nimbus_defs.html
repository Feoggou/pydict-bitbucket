nimbus

(ˈnɪmbəs )
Definitions
noun plural -bi (-baɪ) or -buses1. a. a dark grey rain-bearing cloudb. (in combination) ⇒ cumulonimbus clouds2. a. an emanation of light surrounding a saint or deityb. a representation of this emanation3. a surrounding aura or atmosphere
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

nimbused (ˈnimbused) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Latin: cloud, radiance

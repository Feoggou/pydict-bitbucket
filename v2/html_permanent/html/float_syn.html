Synonyms of float

float

verb

  1. glide, sail, drift, move gently, bob, coast, slide, be carried, slip along
    • barges floating quietly by the grassy river banks
  2. be buoyant, stay afloat, be or lie on the surface, rest on water, hang, hover, poise, displace water
    • Empty things float.
    OPPOSITES:  settle, sink, go down, founder, dip, drown, submerge
  3. waft, coast, drift
    • Sublime music floats on a scented summer breeze.
  4. suggest, present, propose, recommend, put forward, move
    • He floated the idea of a new alliance.
  5. launch, offer, sell, set up, promote, get going, push off
    • He floated his firm on the Stock Market.
    OPPOSITES:  cancel, abolish, dissolve, terminate, annul

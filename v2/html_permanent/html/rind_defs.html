rind

(raɪnd )
Definitions
noun1. a hard outer layer or skin on bacon, cheese, etc2. the outer layer of a fruit or of the spore-producing body of certain fungi3. the outer layer of the bark of a tree
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English rinde; Old High German rinta, German Rinde

Definitions
1. variable nounThe rind of a fruit such as a lemon or orange is its thick outer skin. ⇒ ...grated lemon rind.2. variable nounThe rind of cheese or bacon is the hard outer edge which you do not usually eat. ⇒ Discard the bacon rind and cut each rasher in half.

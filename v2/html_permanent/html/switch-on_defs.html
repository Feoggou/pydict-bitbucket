switch on

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (adverb)1. to cause (a device) to operate by or as if by moving a switch, knob, or lever; turn on2. (transitive) informal to produce (charm, tears, etc) suddenly or automatically3. (transitive) informal (now slightly dated) to make up-to-date, esp regarding outlook, dress, etc4. (transitive) slang to arouse emotionally or sexually5. (intransitive) slang to take or become intoxicated by drugs6. (transitive) slang to introduce (someone) to drugs

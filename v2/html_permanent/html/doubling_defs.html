doubling

(ˈdʌblɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe activity of multiplying by two or repeating ⇒ her doubling of her prayers for him, ⇒ A simple doubling of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere will take us to exactly that point: one degree warmer., ⇒ A ten-fold increase in dietary selenium causes a doubling of this enzyme in the body.

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as grasping, you are criticizing them for wanting to get and keep as much money as possible, and for being unwilling to spend it. [disapproval] ⇒ ...a greedy grasping drug-ridden individual. ⇒ They were grasping and manipulative.

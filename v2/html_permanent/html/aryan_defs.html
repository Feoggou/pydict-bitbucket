Aryan

or Arian (ˈɛərɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (in Nazi ideology) a Caucasian of non-Jewish descent, esp of the Nordic type2. a member of any of the peoples supposedly descended from the Indo-Europeans, esp a speaker of an Iranian or Indic language in ancient timesadjective3. of, relating to, or characteristic of an Aryan or Aryansadjective, noun4. archaic Indo-European
Word origin
C19: from Sanskrit ārya of noble birth

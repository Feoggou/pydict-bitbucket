Ephraim

(ˈiːfreɪɪm )
Definitions
noun Old Testament1. a. the younger son of Joseph, who received the principal blessing of his grandfather Jacob (Genesis 48:8–22)b. the tribe descended from himc. the territory of this tribe, west of the River Jordan2. the northern kingdom of Israel after the kingdom of Solomon had been divided into two

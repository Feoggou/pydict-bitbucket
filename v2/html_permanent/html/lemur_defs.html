lemur

(ˈliːmə )
Definitions
noun1. any Madagascan prosimian primate of the family Lemuridae, such as Lemur catta (the ring-tailed lemur). They are typically arboreal, having foxy faces and long tails2. any similar or closely related animal, such as a loris or indris
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lemur-like (ˈlemur-ˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C18: New Latin, adapted from Latin lemurēs ghosts; so named by Linnaeus for its ghost-like face and nocturnal habits

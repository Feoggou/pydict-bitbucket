rapids

Powered by COBUILD
(ræpɪdz ) for learners of English
Definitions
plural nounRapids are a section of a river where the water moves very fast, often over rocks. ⇒ His canoe was there, on the river below the rapids.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

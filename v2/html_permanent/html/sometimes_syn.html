sometimes

adverb

  1. occasionally, at times, now and then, from time to time, on occasion, now and again, once in a while, every now and then, every so often, off and on
    • During the summer, my skin sometimes gets greasy.
    OPPOSITES:  constantly, continually, always, ever, forever, consistently, invariably, eternally, perpetually, without exception, evermore, unceasingly, everlastingly, twenty-four-seven (informal)

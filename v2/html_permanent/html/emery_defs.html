emery

(ˈɛmərɪ )
Definitions
nouna. a hard greyish-black mineral consisting of corundum with either magnetite or haematite: used as an abrasive and polishing agent, esp as a coating on paper, cloth, etc. Formula: Al2O3b. (as modifier) ⇒ emery paper
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Old French esmeril, ultimately from Greek smuris powder for rubbing

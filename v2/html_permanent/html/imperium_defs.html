imperium

(ɪmˈpɪərɪəm )
Definitions
noun plural -ria (-rɪə)1. (in ancient Rome) the supreme power, held esp by consuls and emperors, to command and administer in military, judicial, and civil affairs2. the right to command; supreme power3. a less common word for empire
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin: command, empire, from imperāre to command; see emperor

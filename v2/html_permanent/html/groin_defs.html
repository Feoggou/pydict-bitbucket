groin

(ɡrɔɪn )
Definitions
noun1. the depression or fold where the legs join the abdomen ▶ Related adjective: inguinal2. euphemistic the genitals, esp the testicles3. a variant spelling (esp US) of groyne4. architecture a curved arris formed where two intersecting vaults meetverb5. (transitive) architecture to provide or construct with groins
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: perhaps from English grynde abyss; related to ground1

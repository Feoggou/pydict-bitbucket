sultry

(ˈsʌltrɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective -trier or -triest1. (of weather or climate) oppressively hot and humid2. characterized by or emitting oppressive heat3. displaying or suggesting passion; sensual ⇒ sultry eyes

Derived Forms

sultrily (ˈsultrily) adverbsultriness (ˈsultriness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from obsolete sulter to swelter + -y1

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]Sultry weather is hot and damp. [written] ⇒ The climax came one sultry August evening.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is sultry is attractive in a way that suggests hidden passion. [written] ⇒ ...a dark-haired sultry woman.

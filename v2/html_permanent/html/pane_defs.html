pane1

(peɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a sheet of glass in a window or door2. a panel of a window, door, wall, etc3. a flat section or face, as of a cut diamond4. philatelya. any of the rectangular marked divisions of a sheet of stamps made for convenience in sellingb. a single page in a stamp booklet. See also tête-bêche, se tenant

Word Origin

C13: from Old French pan portion, from Latin pannus rag

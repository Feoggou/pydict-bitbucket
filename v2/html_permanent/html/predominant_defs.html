predominant

(prɪˈdɒmɪnənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. having superiority in power, influence, etc, over others2. prevailing; prominent

Derived Forms

predominance (preˈdominance) or predominancy (preˈdominancy) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is predominant, it is more important or noticeable than anything else in a set of people or things. ⇒ Amanda's predominant emotion was that of confusion.

diver

(ˈdaɪvə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that dives2. a person who works or explores underwater3. Also called: loom any aquatic bird of the genus Gavia, family Gaviidae, and order Gaviiformes of northern oceans, having a straight pointed bill, small wings, and a long body: noted for swiftness and skill in swimming and diving. US and Canadian name: loon4. any of various other diving birds5. soccer slang a player who pretends to have been tripped or impeded by an opposing player in order to win a free kick or penalty

ribbons

(ˈrɪbənz)
Definitions
plural nounragged strips or shreds ⇒ He emerged from the smoke and dust with his suit in ribbons., ⇒ The streets were flooded 2ft deep in places, trees torn up or torn to ribbons., ⇒ a woman with cheeks cut to ribbons and with a dagger in her heart

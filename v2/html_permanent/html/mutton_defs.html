mutton

(ˈmʌtən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the flesh of sheep, esp of mature sheep, used as food2. See mutton dressed as lamb3. printing another word for em (sense 1). Compare nut (sense 12)

Derived Forms

muttony (ˈmuttony) adjective

Word Origin

C13 moton sheep, from Old French, from Medieval Latin multō, of Celtic origin; the term was adopted in printing to distinguish the pronunciation of em quad from en quad

momentous

adjective

  1. significant, important, serious, vital, critical, crucial, grave, historic, decisive, pivotal, fateful, weighty, consequential, of moment, earth-shaking (informal)
    • the momentous decision to send in the troops
    OPPOSITES:  trivial, trifling, insignificant, unimportant, inconsequential

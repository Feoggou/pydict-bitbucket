graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is arty seems very interested in drama, film, music, poetry, or painting. People often describe someone as arty when they want to suggest that the person is pretentious. [informal] ⇒ Didn't you find her a little bit too arty? ⇒ ...an arty French film.

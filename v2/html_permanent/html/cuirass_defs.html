cuirass

(kwɪˈræs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a piece of armour, of leather or metal covering the chest and back2. a hard outer protective covering of some animals, consisting of shell, plate, or scales3. any similar protective covering, as on a shipverb4. (transitive) to equip with a cuirass
Word origin
C15: from French cuirasse, from Late Latin coriacea, from coriaceus made of leather, from Latin corium leather

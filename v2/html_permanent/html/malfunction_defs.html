Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (intransitive) to function imperfectly or irregularly or fail to functionnoun2. failure to function or defective functioning

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
verbIf a machine or part of the body malfunctions, it fails to work properly. [formal] [V] ⇒ The radiation can damage microprocessors and computer memories, causing them to malfunction. [V-ing] ⇒ Early physicians removed the malfunctioning organs.Malfunction is also a noun. ⇒ There must have been a computer malfunction.

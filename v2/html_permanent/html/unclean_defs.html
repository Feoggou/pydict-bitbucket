Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is unclean is dirty and likely to cause disease. ⇒ ...the Western attitude to insects as being dirty and unclean. ⇒ By bathing in unclean water, they expose themselves to contamination.2. adjectiveIf you describe someone or something as unclean, you consider them to be spiritually or morally bad. ⇒ They felt as though they had done something discreditable and unclean. ⇒ ...unclean thoughts.

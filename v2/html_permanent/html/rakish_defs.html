rakish1

(ˈreɪkɪʃ )
Definitions
adjective1. dissolute; profligate2. dashing; jaunty ⇒ rakish charm, ⇒ a hat set at a rakish angle
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

rakishly (ˈrakishly) adverbrakishness (ˈrakishness) noun

Word Origin

C18: from rake2 + -ish

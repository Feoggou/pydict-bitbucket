Sitwell

(ˈsɪtwəl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Dame Edith. 1887–1964, English poet and critic, noted esp for her collection Façade (1922)2.  her brother, Sir Osbert. 1892–1969, English writer, best known for his five autobiographical books (1944–50)3.  his brother, Sir Sacheverell (səˈʃɛvərəl). 1897–1988, English poet and writer of books on art, architecture, music, and travel

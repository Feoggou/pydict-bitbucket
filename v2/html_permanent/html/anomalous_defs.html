anomalous

(əˈnɒmələs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectivedeviating from the normal or usual order, type, etc; irregular, abnormal, or incongruous
Derived forms
aˈnomalously adverbaˈnomalousness noun
Word origin
C17: from Late Latin anōmalus, from Greek anōmalos uneven, inconsistent, from an- + homalos even, from homos one and the same

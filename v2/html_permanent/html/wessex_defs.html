Wessex1

(ˈwɛsɪks )
Definitions
noun1. an Anglo-Saxon kingdom in S and SW England that became the most powerful English kingdom by the 10th century ad2. a. (in Thomas Hardy's works) the southwestern counties of England, esp Dorsetb. (as modifier) ⇒ Wessex Poems

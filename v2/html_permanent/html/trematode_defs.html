trematode

(ˈtrɛməˌtəʊd , ˈtriː-)
Definitions
nounany parasitic flatworm of the class Trematoda, which includes the flukes
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: from New Latin Trematoda, from Greek trēmatōdēs full of holes, from trēma a hole

dilatation

(ˌdɪləˈteɪʃən ; ˌ daɪləˈteɪʃən )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  dilation2.  Medicine the state of enlargement of an organ, cavity, duct, or opening of the body beyond normal size. see also dilation

Word origin

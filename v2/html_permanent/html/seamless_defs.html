seamless

(ˈsiːmlɪs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of a garment) having no seams2. continuous or flowing ⇒ seamless output, ⇒ a seamless performance

seamless

(siːmləs )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveYou use seamless to describe something that has no breaks or gaps in it or which continues without stopping. ⇒ It was a seamless procession of wonderful electronic music. ⇒ ...the seamless blue sky.seamlessly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ He has moved seamlessly from theory to practice.

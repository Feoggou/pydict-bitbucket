sprouts

(spraʊts)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. newly grown shoots or buds ⇒ After eleven days of growth the number of sprouts was counted.2. Brussels sprouts ⇒ Along with sprouts, Christmas pudding is so entrenched in our festive cuisine that we feel almost duty-bound to eat it.

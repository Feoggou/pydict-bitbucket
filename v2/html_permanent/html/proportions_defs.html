proportions

(prəˈpɔːʃənz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noundimensions or size ⇒ a fraud of breathtaking proportions, ⇒ In the tropics plants grow to huge proportions., ⇒ The proportions of the building made it look strange., ⇒ She had restored the rooms to their original proportions., ⇒ You can vary the relative proportions of things in a picture very simply., ⇒ Alcohol consumption has now reached alarming proportions.

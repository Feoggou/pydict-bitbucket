evocation

(ˌɛvəˈkeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act or an instance of evoking2. French law the transference of a case from an inferior court for adjudication by a higher tribunal3. another word for induction (sense 6)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

evocable (ˈɛvəkəbəl ) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Latin ēvocātiō a calling forth, from ēvocāre to evoke

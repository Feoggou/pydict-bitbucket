sinus

(ˈsaɪnəs )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -nuses1. anatomya. any bodily cavity or hollow spaceb. a large channel for venous blood, esp between the brain and the skullc. any of the air cavities in the cranial bones2. pathology a passage leading to a cavity containing pus3. botany a small rounded notch between two lobes of a leaf, petal, etc4. an irregularly shaped cavity

Word Origin

C16: from Latin: a curve, bay

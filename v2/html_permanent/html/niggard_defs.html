niggard

(ˈnɪɡəd )
Definitions
noun1. a stingy personadjective2. archaic miserly
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: perhaps of Scandinavian origin; related to Swedish dialect nygg and Old English hnēaw stingy
Example sentences containing 'niggard'
`No niggard are you, omer,' said Aragorn, `to give thus to Gondor the fairest thing in your realm! J.R.R. Tolkien THE LORD OF THE RINGS

worship

(ˈwɜːʃɪp )
Definitions
verb -ships, -shipping, -shipped or US -ships, -shiping or -shiped1. (transitive) to show profound religious devotion and respect to; adore or venerate (God or any person or thing considered divine)2. (transitive) to be devoted to and full of admiration for3. (intransitive) to have or express feelings of profound adoration4. (intransitive) to attend services for worship5. (transitive) obsolete to honournoun6. religious adoration or devotion7. the formal expression of religious adoration; rites, prayers, etc8. admiring love or devotion9. archaic dignity or standing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

worshipable (ˈworshipable) adjectiveworshipper (ˈworshipper) or US worshiper (ˈworshiper) noun

Word Origin

Old English weorthscipe, from worth1 + -ship

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA countrywoman is a woman who lives in the country rather than in a city or a town. ⇒ She had the slow, soft voice of a countrywoman.2. countable noun [usu poss N]Your countrywomen are women from your own country. ⇒ Britain's Martine Le Moignan defeated her countrywoman Suzanne Horner in four games.

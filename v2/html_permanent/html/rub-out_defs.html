rub out

Definitions
verb (tr, adverb)1. to remove or be removed with a rubber2. US slang to murder3. Australian Rules football to suspend (a player)

rub out

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. phrasal verbIf you rub out something that you have written on paper or a board, you remove it using a rubber or eraser. [V P n] ⇒ She began rubbing out the pencilled marks in the margin. [Also V n P]2. phrasal verbIf one person rubs out another, they kill them. [informal] [V P n (not pron)] ⇒ Nobody else believed that they had tried to rub out the pope. [Also V n P] →  rub

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone's behaviour as commendable, you approve of it or are praising it. [formal, approval] ⇒ Mr Sparrow has acted with commendable speed. ⇒ The tone of Mr Kinnock's speech was commendable.commendably (kəmendəbli ) graded adverb [ADV adj/adv, ADV with v, ADV with cl] ⇒ Her manner was commendably restrained. ⇒ They all behaved very commendably. ⇒ Exercise classes, commendably, are limited to groups of 5.

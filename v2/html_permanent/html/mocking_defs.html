Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveA mocking expression or mocking behaviour indicates that you think someone or something is stupid or inferior. ⇒ She gave a mocking smile. ⇒ Behind the mocking laughter lurks a growing sense of unease.mockingly graded adverb ⇒ 'Isn't that sweet?' he says mockingly.
Example sentences containing 'mocking'
The corners of Ilmari's eyes lifted a fraction in slightly mocking concern. Low, Ona TO HIS JUST DESSERTSThe housebreaker's unclean face was still cheerful and mocking , although a slow worry had begun gathering at its edges. Heller, Keith MAN'S LOVING FAMILYIt was her unsophisticated way of getting back at them for mocking her. Fraser, Christine Marion NOBLE BEGINNNINGS

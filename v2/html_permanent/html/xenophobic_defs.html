xenophobic

Powered by COBUILD
(zenəfoʊbɪk ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as xenophobic, you disapprove of them because they show strong dislike or fear of people from other countries. [formal, disapproval] ⇒ Xenophobic nationalism is on the rise in some West European countries. ⇒ Stalin was obsessively xenophobic.

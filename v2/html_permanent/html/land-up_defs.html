land up

Definitions
verb(adverb, usually intr) to arrive or cause to arrive at a final point ⇒ after a summer in Europe, he suddenly landed up at home

land up

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
phrasal verbIf you say that you land up in a place or situation, you mean that you arrive there after a long journey or at the end of a long series of events. [mainly British, informal] [V P prep/adv] ⇒ Half of those who went east seem to have landed up in southern India. [V P prep/adv] ⇒ We landed up at the Las Vegas at about 6.30. →  land

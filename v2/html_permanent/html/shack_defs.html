shack1

(ʃæk )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a roughly built hut2. South Africa temporary accommodation put together by squattersverb3. See shack up

Word Origin

C19: perhaps from dialect shackly ramshackle, from dialect shack to shake

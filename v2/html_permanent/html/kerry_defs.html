Kerry1

(ˈkɛrɪ )
Definitions

Kerry2

(ˈkɛrɪ )
Definitions
noun1. a county of SW Republic of Ireland, in W Munster province: mostly mountainous (including the highest peaks in Ireland), with a deeply indented coast and many offshore islands. County town: Tralee. Pop: 132 527 (2002). Area: 4701 sq km (1815 sq miles)2. a small black breed of dairy cattle, originally from Kerry

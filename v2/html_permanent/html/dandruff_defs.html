dandruff

(ˈdændrəf )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounloose scales of dry dead skin shed from the scalp. Also called (now rarely): dandriff
Derived forms
dandruffy (ˈdandruffy) adjective
Word origin
C16: dand-, of unknown origin + -ruff, probably from Middle English roufe scab, from Old Norse hrūfa

(dændrʌf )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounDandruff is small white pieces of dead skin in someone's hair, or fallen from someone's hair. ⇒ He has very bad dandruff.

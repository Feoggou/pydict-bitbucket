rendering

(ˈrɛndərɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. the act or an instance of performing a play, piece of music, etc2. a translation of a text from a foreign language3. Also called: rendering coat, render a coat of plaster or cement mortar applied to a surface4. a perspective drawing showing an architect's idea of a finished building, interior, etc

rendering

Powered by COBUILD
(rendərɪŋ ) for learners of English
Word forms: renderings
Definitions
1. countable nounA rendering of a play, poem, or piece of music is a performance of it. [+ of] ⇒ ...a rendering of Verdi's Requiem by the BBC Symphony Orchestra.2. countable nounA rendering of an expression or piece of writing or speech is a translation of it. translation [+ of] ⇒ This phrase may well have been a rendering of a popular Arabic expression.3. countable nounThe rendering on a wall is a layer of plaster or cement which covers and protects it. [mainly British]regional note:   in AM, usually use stucco

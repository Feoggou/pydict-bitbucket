zoo

(zuː )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural zoosa place where live animals are kept, studied, bred, and exhibited to the public. Formal term: zoological garden
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: shortened from zoological gardens (originally applied to those in London)

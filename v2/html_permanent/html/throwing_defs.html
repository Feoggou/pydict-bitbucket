throwing

(ˈθrəʊɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the act of projecting or casting (something) through the air, esp with a rapid motion of the arm and wrist ⇒ arrests, shootings and throwing of stones, ⇒ He didn't really know very much about javelin throwing.2. the act of projecting (the voice) so as to make it appear to come from somewhere other than its source3. the act of giving or holding (a party)4. the act of tipping (dice) out onto a flat surface

Synonyms of mint

mint

verb

  1. make, produce, strike, cast, stamp, punch, coin
    • the right to mint coins
  2. invent, produce, fashion, make up, construct, coin, devise, forge, fabricate, think up
    • The book comprises a lexicon of freshly minted descriptions.

noun

  1. fortune, million, bomb (British, slang), pile (informal), packet (slang), bundle (slang), heap (informal), top dollar (informal), King's ransom, top whack (informal)
    • They were worth a mint.

adjective

  1. perfect, excellent, first-class, brand-new, fresh, unmarked, undamaged, unblemished, untarnished
    • a set of Victorian stamps in mint condition

Rama

(ˈrɑːmə )
Definitions
noun(in Hindu mythology) any of Vishnu's three incarnations (the heroes Balarama, Parashurama, or Ramachandra)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

from Sanskrit Rāma black, dark

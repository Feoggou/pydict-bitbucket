supporting

(səˈpɔːtɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of a role) being a fairly important but not leading part, esp in a play or film2. (of an actor or actress) playing a supporting role

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]In a film or play, a supporting actor or actress is one who has an important part, but not the most important part. ⇒ ...the winner of the best supporting actress award. ⇒ ...acting the supporting role in a Harrison Ford-type movie.2. → see also support

mitten

(ˈmɪtən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a glove having one section for the thumb and a single section for the other fingers. Sometimes shortened to: mitt2. slang a boxing glove

Word Origin

C14: from Old French mitaine, of uncertain origin

countable noun [usu pl]Mittens are gloves which have one section that covers your thumb and another section that covers your four fingers together.

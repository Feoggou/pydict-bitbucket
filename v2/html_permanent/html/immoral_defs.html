immoral

(ɪˈmɒrəl )
Definitions
adjective1. transgressing accepted moral rules; corrupt2. sexually dissolute; profligate or promiscuous3. unscrupulous or unethical ⇒ immoral trading4. tending to corrupt or resulting from corruption ⇒ an immoral film, ⇒ immoral earnings
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

immorally (imˈmorally) adverb

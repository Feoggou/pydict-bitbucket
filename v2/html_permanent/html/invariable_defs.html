invariable

(ɪnˈvɛərɪəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. not subject to alteration; unchangingnoun2. a mathematical quantity having an unchanging value; a constant
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

invariability (inˌvariaˈbility) or invariableness (inˈvariableness) noun

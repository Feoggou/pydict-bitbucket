do a runner

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf someone does a runner, they leave a place in a hurry, for example in order to escape arrest or to avoid paying for something. [British, informal] ⇒ At this point, the accountant did a runner–with all my bank statements, expenses and receipts. →  runner

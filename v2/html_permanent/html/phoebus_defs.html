Phoebus

(ˈfiːbəs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: Phoebus Apollo Greek mythology Apollo as the sun god2. poetic a personification of the sun

Word Origin

C14: via Latin from Greek Phoibos bright; related to phaos light

burgeon

or bourgeon (ˈbɜːdʒən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (often foll by forth or out) (of a plant) to sprout (buds)2. (intr; often foll by forth or out) to develop or grow rapidly; flourishnoun3. a bud of a plant
Word origin
C13: from Old French burjon, perhaps ultimately from Late Latin burra shaggy cloth; from the downiness of certain buds

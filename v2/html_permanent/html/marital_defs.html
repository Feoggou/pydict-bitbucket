marital

(ˈmærɪtəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to marriage ⇒ marital status2. of or relating to a husband

Derived Forms

maritally (ˈmaritally) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin marītālis, from marītus married (adj), husband (n); related to mās male

alcohol-free

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of beer or wine) containing only a trace of alcohol. Compare low-alcohol2. (of a period of time) during which no alcoholic drink is consumed ⇒ there should be one or two alcohol-free days a week

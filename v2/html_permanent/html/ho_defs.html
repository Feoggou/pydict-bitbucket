ho1

(həʊ )
Definitions
exclamation1. Also: ho-ho an imitation or representation of the sound of a deep laugh2. an exclamation used to attract attention, announce a destination, etc ⇒ what ho!, ⇒ land ho!, ⇒ westward ho!
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: of imitative origin; compare Old Norse hó, Old French ho! halt!

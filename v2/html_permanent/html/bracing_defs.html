bracing

(ˈbreɪsɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. refreshing; stimulating; invigorating ⇒ the air here is bracingnoun2. a system of braces used to strengthen or support ⇒ the bracing supporting the building is perfectly adequate
Derived forms
bracingly (ˈbracingly) adverb

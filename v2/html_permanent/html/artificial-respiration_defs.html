artificial respiration

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. any of various methods of restarting breathing after it has stopped, by manual rhythmic pressure on the chest, mouth-to-mouth breathing, etc2. any method of maintaining respiration artificially, as by use of an iron lung

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounArtificial respiration is the forcing of air into the lungs of someone who has stopped breathing, usually by blowing through their mouth or nose, in order to keep them alive and to help them to start breathing again. ⇒ She was given artificial respiration and cardiac massage.

Synonyms of morning

morning

noun

  1. before noon, forenoon, morn (poetic), a.m.
    • On Sunday morning he was woken by the telephone.
  2. dawn, sunrise, morrow (archaic), first light, daybreak, break of day
    • I started to lose hope of ever seeing the morning.
related adjective:
matutinal

distributor

or distributer (dɪˈstrɪbjʊtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that distributes2. a wholesaler or middleman engaged in the distribution of a category of goods, esp to retailers in a specific area3. the device in a petrol engine that distributes the high-tension voltage to the sparking plugs in the sequence of the firing order

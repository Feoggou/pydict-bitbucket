(əbʌndəntli )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adverb [ADV adj]If something is abundantly clear, it is extremely obvious. ⇒ He made it abundantly clear that anybody who disagrees with his policies will not last long.2. graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj]Something that occurs abundantly is present in large quantities. ⇒ ...a plant that grows abundantly in the United States. ⇒ All the pages are abundantly illustrated with colour photographs.

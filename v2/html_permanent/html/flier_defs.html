flier

(ˈflaɪər ) in American English
Definitions
noun1.  a person or thing that flies; specif., an aviator2.  Us a bus, train, etc. that has a fast schedule3.  any step in a straight stairway4.  Us a small circular or handbill widely distributed5.  Us, Informal a risky, sometimes reckless, gamble or speculation, as in the stock marketoften in the phrase take a flier

veranda

or verandah (vəˈrændə )
Definitions
noun1. a porch or portico, sometimes partly enclosed, along the outside of a building2. New Zealand a canopy sheltering pedestrians in a shopping street
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

verandaed (veˈrandaed) or verandahed (veˈrandahed) adjective

Word Origin

C18: from Portuguese varanda railing; related to Hindi varandā railing

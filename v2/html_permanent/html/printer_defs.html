printer

(ˈprɪntə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or business engaged in printing2. a machine or device that prints3. computing an output device for printing results on paper

(prɪntəʳ )
Word forms: printers
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA printer is a machine that can be connected to a computer in order to make copies on paper of documents or other information held by the computer. → see also laser printer2. countable nounA printer is a person or company whose job is printing things such as books. ⇒ The manuscript had already been sent off to the printers.

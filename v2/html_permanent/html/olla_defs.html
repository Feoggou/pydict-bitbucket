olla

(ˈɒlə , Spanish ˈoʎa)
Definitions
noun1. a cooking pot2. short for olla podrida
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Spanish, from Latin olla, variant of aulla pot

abash

(əˈbæʃ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb(tr; usually passive) to cause to feel ill at ease, embarrassed, or confused; make ashamed
Derived forms
aˈbashment noun
Word origin
C14: via Norman French from Old French esbair to be astonished, from es- out + bair to gape, yawn

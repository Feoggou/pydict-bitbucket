reedy

(ˈriːdɪ )
Definitions
adjectiveWord forms: reedier or reediest1. (of a place, esp a marsh) abounding in reeds2. of or like a reed3. having a tone like a reed instrument; shrill or piping ⇒ a reedy voice
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you say that someone has a reedy voice, you think their voice is unpleasant because it is high and unclear. ⇒ The big man had a high-pitched reedy voice.

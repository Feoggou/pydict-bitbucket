(ˈruːfɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. material used to construct a roof2. the act of constructing a roof

roofing

Powered by COBUILD
(ruːfɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount noun [oft N n]Roofing is material used for making or covering roofs. ⇒ A gust of wind pried loose a section of sheet-metal roofing. ⇒ Stone began to be used as a roofing material.2. uncount noun [oft N n]Roofing is the work of putting new roofs on houses. ⇒ ...a roofing company.

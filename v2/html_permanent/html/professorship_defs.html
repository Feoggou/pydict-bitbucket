professorship

(prəˈfɛsəʃɪp)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun education1. the position or office of the principal lecturer or teacher in a field of learning at a university or college ⇒ He has accepted a research professorship at Cambridge University.2. mainly US and Canadian the position of any teacher in a university or college

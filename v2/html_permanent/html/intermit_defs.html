intermit

(ˌɪntəˈmɪt )
Definitions
verb -mits, -mitting or -mittedto suspend (activity) or (of activity) to be suspended temporarily or at intervals
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

intermittingly (ˌinterˈmittingly) adverbintermittor (ˌinterˈmittor) or intermitter (ˌinterˈmitter) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin intermittere to leave off, from inter- + mittere to send

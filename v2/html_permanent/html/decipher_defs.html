decipher

(dɪˈsaɪfə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to determine the meaning of (something obscure or illegible)2. to convert from code into plain text; decode
Derived forms
decipherable (deˈcipherable) adjectivedeˌcipherability (deˌcipheraˈbility) noundecipherer (deˈcipherer) noundecipherment (deˈcipherment) noun

Definitions

verbIf you decipher a piece of writing or a message, you work out what it says, even though it is very difficult to read or understand. [V n] ⇒ I'm still no closer to deciphering the code.

tabular

(ˈtæbjʊlə )
Definitions
adjective1. arranged in systematic or table form2. calculated from or by means of a table3. like a table in form; flat
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

tabularly (ˈtabularly) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin tabulāris concerning boards, from tabula a board

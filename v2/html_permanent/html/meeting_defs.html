meeting

(ˈmiːtɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an act of coming together; encounter2. an assembly or gathering3. a conjunction or union4. a sporting competition, as of athletes, or of horse racing

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA meeting is an event in which a group of people come together to discuss things or make decisions. ⇒ Can we have a meeting to discuss that? ⇒ ...business meetings.You can also refer to the people at a meeting as the meeting. ⇒ The meeting decided that further efforts were needed.2. countable noun [oft with poss]When you meet someone, either by chance or by arrangement, you can refer to this event as a meeting. ⇒ In January, 37 years after our first meeting, I was back in the studio with Denis. ⇒ Her life was changed by a chance meeting with her former art master a few years ago.

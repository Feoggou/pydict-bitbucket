hourly

(ˈaʊəlɪ )
Definitions
adjective1. of, occurring, or done every hour2. done in or measured by the hour ⇒ we are paid an hourly rate3. continual or frequentadverb4. every hour5. at any moment or time
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

hourly

Powered by COBUILD
(aʊəʳli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]An hourly event happens once every hour. ⇒ He flipped on the radio to get the hourly news broadcast.Hourly is also an adverb. ⇒ The hospital issued press releases hourly.2. adjective [ADJ n]Your hourly earnings are the money that you earn in one hour. ⇒ They have little prospect of finding new jobs with the same hourly pay.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

medieval

or mediaeval (ˌmɛdɪˈiːvəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or in the style of the Middle Ages2. informal old-fashioned; primitive

Derived Forms

ˌmedievally (ˌmediˈevally) or ˌmediaevally (ˌmediˈaevally) adverb

Word Origin

C19: from New Latin medium aevum the middle age. See medium, age

trembles

(ˈtrɛmbəlz )
Definitions
noun (functioning as singular)1. Also called: milk sickness a disease of cattle and sheep characterized by muscular incoordination and tremor, caused by ingestion of white snakeroot or rayless goldenrod2. a nontechnical name for Parkinson's disease

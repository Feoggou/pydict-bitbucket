scholasticism

(skəˈlæstɪˌsɪzəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (sometimes capital) the system of philosophy, theology, and teaching that dominated medieval western Europe and was based on the writings of the Church Fathers and (from the 12th century) Aristotle2. strict adherence to traditional doctrines

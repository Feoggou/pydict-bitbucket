Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If something is permissible, it is considered to be acceptable because it does not break any laws or rules. ⇒ Religious practices are permissible under the Constitution. ⇒ He said it was just not permissible to postpone the main issue by allowing business to carry on as usual.

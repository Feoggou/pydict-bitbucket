pigtail

(ˈpɪɡˌteɪl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a bunch of hair or one of two bunches on either side of the face, worn loose or plaited2. a twisted roll of tobacco

Derived Forms

pigˌtailed (ˈpigˌtailed) adjective

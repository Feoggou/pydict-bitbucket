arrant

(ˈærənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveutter; out-and-out ⇒ an arrant fool
Derived forms
ˈarrantly adverb
Word origin
C14: a variant of errant (wandering, vagabond); sense developed from its frequent use in phrases like arrant thief (hence: notorious)

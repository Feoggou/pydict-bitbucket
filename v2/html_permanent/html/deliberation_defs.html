deliberation

(dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. thoughtful, careful, or lengthy consideration2. (often plural) formal discussion and debate, as of a committee, jury, etc3. care, thoughtfulness, or absence of hurry, esp in movement or speech

Word forms: deliberations
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounDeliberation is the long and careful consideration of a subject. ⇒ After much deliberation, a decision was reached. ⇒ After five minutes of deliberation, he was found guilty of murdering the president.2. plural nounDeliberations are formal discussions where an issue is considered carefully. ⇒ ...the outcome of the deliberations. ⇒ Their deliberations were rather inconclusive.3. uncount noun [usu with N]If you say or do something with deliberation, you do it slowly and carefully. ⇒ Fred spoke with deliberation. ⇒ My mother folded her coat across the back of the chair with careful deliberation.

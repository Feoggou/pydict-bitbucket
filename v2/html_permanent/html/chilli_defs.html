Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural chillies or chiliesthe small red hot-tasting pod of a type of capsicum used for flavouring sauces, pickles, etc
Word origin
C17: from Spanish chile, from Nahuatl chilli

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounChillies are small red or green peppers. They have a very hot taste and are used in cooking.2. uncount nounChilli is the same as chilli con carne.

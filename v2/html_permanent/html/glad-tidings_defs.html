glad tidings

(ɡlæd ˈtaɪdɪŋz)
Definitions
plural nounliterary good news ⇒ the lawyer who brought the glad tidings, ⇒ Drake sent messengers to London to acquaint the Queen with the glad tidings that he had brought home some £600,000 in booty.

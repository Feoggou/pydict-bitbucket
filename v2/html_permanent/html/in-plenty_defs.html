Definitions
phrase [n PHR, PHR after v]If there are things in plenty, those things exist or happen in large amounts or numbers. ⇒ There were thrills in plenty on Saturday at Terrassa, where Germany won the gold medal. ⇒ School inspectors visit regularly, finding everything satisfactory, books in plenty and five computer terminals. →  plenty

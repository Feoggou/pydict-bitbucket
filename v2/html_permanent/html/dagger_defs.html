dagger

(ˈdæɡə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a short stabbing weapon with a pointed blade2. Also called: obelisk a character (†) used in printing to indicate a cross reference, esp to a footnote3. See at daggers drawn4. See look daggersverb (transitive)5. to mark with a dagger6. archaic to stab with a dagger
Word origin
C14: of uncertain origin

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna person who rescues another person or a thing from danger or harm

Word Origin

C13 saveour, from Old French, from Church Latin Salvātor the Saviour; see save1

regional note:   in AM, use savior1. countable nounA saviour is a person who saves someone or something from danger, ruin, or defeat. [+ of] ⇒ ...the saviour of his country. ⇒ ...the saviour of English football. ⇒ She regarded him as her saviour.2. proper noun [the N]In the Christian religion, the Saviour is Jesus Christ.

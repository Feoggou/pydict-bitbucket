conto

(ˈkɒntəʊ , Portuguese ˈkõːtu)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -tos (-təʊz, Portuguese -tuʃ)1. a former Portuguese monetary unit worth 1000 escudos2. an unofficial Brazilian monetary unit worth 1000 cruzeiros (now replaced by the real)
Word origin
C17: from Portuguese, from Late Latin computus calculation, from computāre to reckon, compute; see count1

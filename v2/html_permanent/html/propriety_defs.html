propriety

(prəˈpraɪətɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ties1. the quality or state of being appropriate or fitting2. conformity to the prevailing standard of behaviour, speech, etc3. See the proprieties

Word Origin

C15: from Old French propriété, from Latin proprietās a peculiarity, from proprius one's own

(ʌnˈtjuːtəd )
Definitions
adjective1. without formal instruction or education2. lacking sophistication or refinement

untutored for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌntjuːtəʳd , US -tuːt- )
Definitions
adjectiveIf someone is untutored, they have not been formally trained to do something, although they may be quite skilled at it. [formal] ⇒ This untutored mathematician had an obsession with numbers. [+ in] ⇒ They had left school at fifteen and were quite untutored in writing.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

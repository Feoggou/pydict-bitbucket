mailing

(ˈmeɪlɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the activity of sending things to people through the postal service ⇒ The newsletter was printed towards the end of June in readiness for mailing.2. something that is sent to people through the postal service ⇒ The seniors organizations sent out mailings to their constituencies.

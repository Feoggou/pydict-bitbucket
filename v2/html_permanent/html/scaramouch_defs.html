Scaramouch

or Scaramouche (ˈskærəˌmaʊtʃ , -ˌmuːtʃ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna stock character who appears as a boastful coward in commedia dell'arte and farce

Word Origin

C17: via French from Italian Scaramuccia, from scaramuccia a skirmish

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
pronunciation note:   The pronunciation (kɜːʳsɪd) is used for meaning [sense 3].1. adjective [v-link ADJ with n]If you are cursed with something, you are very unlucky in having it. [+ with] ⇒ Bulman was cursed with a poor memory for names.2. adjective [usu v-link ADJ]Someone or something that is cursed is suffering as the result of a curse. ⇒ The whole family seemed cursed. ⇒ The ground was cursed because of him.3. adjective [ADJ n]Cursed is used by some people to emphasize what they are saying, especially when they are angry. [old-fashioned, rude, emphasis] ⇒ I wish this cursed place was burned.

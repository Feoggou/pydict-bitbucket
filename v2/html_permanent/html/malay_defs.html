Malay

(məˈleɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a member of a people living chiefly in Malaysia and Indonesia who are descendants of Mongoloid immigrants2. the language of this people, belonging to the Malayo-Polynesian familyadjective3. of or relating to the Malays or their language

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Malay means belonging or relating to the people, language, or culture of the largest racial group in Malaysia. ⇒ ...the Malay community.2. countable nounA Malay is a member of the largest racial group in Malaysia. ⇒ ...the three main racial groups in Malaysia–the Malays, the Indians and the Chinese.3. uncount nounMalay is a language that is spoken in Malaysia and in parts of Indonesia.

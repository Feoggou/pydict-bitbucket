feathering

(ˈfɛðərɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. the plumage of a bird; feathers2. another word for feathers (sense 2)3. printinga. an imperfection in print caused by the spreading of inkb. the use of additional space between lines in typesetting in order to fill the page
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

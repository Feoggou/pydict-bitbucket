ewe

(juː )
Definitions
nouna. a female sheepb. (as modifier) ⇒ a ewe lamb
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English ēowu; related to Old Norse ǣr ewe, Old High German ou, Latin ovis sheep, Sanskrit avi

Ewe

(ˈɛwɛ )
Definitions
noun1. Word forms: plural Ewe or Ewes a member of a Negroid people of W Africa living chiefly in the forests of E Ghana, Togo, and Benin2. the language of this people, belonging to the Kwa branch of the Niger-Congo family
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

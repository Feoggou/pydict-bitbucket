go without

Definitions
verb (intransitive)1. mainly British to be denied or deprived of (something, esp food) ⇒ if you don't like your tea you can go without2. See that goes without saying

Definitions
phrasal verbIf you go without something that you need or usually have or do, you do not get it or do it. [V P n/v-ing] ⇒ I have known what it is like to go without food for days. [V P] ⇒ The embargo won't hurt us because we're used to going without. →  go

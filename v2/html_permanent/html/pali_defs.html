Pali

(ˈpɑːlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan ancient language of India derived from Sanskrit; the language of the Buddhist scriptures

Word Origin

C19: from Sanskrit pāli-bhāsa, from pāli canon + bhāsa language, of Dravidian origin

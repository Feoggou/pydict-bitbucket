repartee

(ˌrɛpɑːˈtiː )
Definitions
noun1. a sharp, witty, or aphoristic remark made as a reply2. terse rapid conversation consisting of such remarks3. skill in making sharp witty replies or conversation
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from French repartie, from repartir to retort, from re- + partir to go away

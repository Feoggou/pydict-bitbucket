Definitions
countable nounA weekday is any of the days of the week except Saturday and Sunday. ⇒ If you want to avoid the crowds, it's best to come on a weekday. ⇒ Visitor Centre and shop open 9 a.m.–5 p.m. weekdays; 10 a.m.–5 p.m. weekends.

athwart

(əˈθwɔːt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb1. transversely; from one side to anotherpreposition2. across the path or line of (esp a ship)3. in opposition to; against
Word origin
C15: from a-2 + thwart
Example sentences containing 'athwart'
It ran from north to south and thus lay squarely athwart their route. Robert Silverberg THE LONGEST WAY HOME

Western Isles

Definitions
noun (functioning as singular or plural)1. an island authority in W Scotland, consisting of the Outer Hebrides; created in 1975. Administrative centre: Stornoway. Pop: 26 100 (2003 est). Area: 2900 sq km (1120 sq miles). Gaelic name: Eilean Siar2. Also called: Western Islands another name for Hebrides

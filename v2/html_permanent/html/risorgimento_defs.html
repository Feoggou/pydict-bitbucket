Risorgimento

(rɪˌsɔːdʒɪˈmɛntəʊ )
Definitions
nounthe period of and the movement for the political unification of Italy in the 19th century
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Italian, from risorgere to rise again, from Latin resurgere, from re- + surgere to rise

clan

(klæn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a group of people interrelated by ancestry or marriage2. a group of families with a common surname and a common ancestor, acknowledging the same leader, esp among the Scots and the Irish3. a group of people united by common characteristics, aims, or interests
Word origin
C14: from Scottish Gaelic clann family, descendants, from Latin planta sprout, plant1

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA clan is a group which consists of families that are related to each other. ⇒ ...rival clans.2. countable nounYou can refer to a group of people with the same interests as a clan. [informal] [+ of] ⇒ ...a powerful clan of industrialists from Monterrey.

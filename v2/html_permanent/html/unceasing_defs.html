Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]If you describe something as unceasing, you are emphasizing that it continues without stopping. [emphasis] ⇒ ...the unceasing quest for more speed. ⇒ ...his unceasing labours.unceasingly adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ Paul talked unceasingly from dawn to dusk.

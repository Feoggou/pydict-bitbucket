unselfish for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnselfɪʃ )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as unselfish, you approve of the fact that they regard other people's wishes and interests as more important than their own. [approval] ⇒ She started to get a reputation as an unselfish girl with a heart of gold. ⇒ As a player he was unselfish, a true team man.unselfishly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ She has loyally and unselfishly spent every day at her husband's side.unselfishness uncount noun ⇒ ...acts of unselfishness and care.

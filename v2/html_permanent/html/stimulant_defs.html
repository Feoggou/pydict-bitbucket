stimulant

(ˈstɪmjʊlənt )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a drug or similar substance that increases physiological activity, esp of a particular organ2. any stimulating agent or thingadjective3. increasing physiological activity; stimulating

Word Origin

C18: from Latin stimulāns goading, from stimulāre to urge on; see stimulus

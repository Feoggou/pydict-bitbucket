felony

(ˈfɛlənɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -nies(formerly) a serious crime, such as murder or arson. All distinctions between felony and misdemeanour were abolished in England and Wales in 1967

felony

Powered by COBUILD
(feləni ) for learners of English
Word forms: felonies
Definitions
countable nounIn countries where the legal system distinguishes between very serious crimes and less serious ones, a felony is a very serious crime such as armed robbery. [law] ⇒ He pleaded guilty to six felonies.

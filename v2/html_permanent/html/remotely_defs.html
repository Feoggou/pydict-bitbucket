remotely

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪmoʊtli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adverb [oft ADV adj/-ed]You use remotely with a negative statement to emphasize the statement. [emphasis] ⇒ We had never seen anything remotely like it before. ⇒ Nobody was remotely interested. ⇒ ...a reluctance to say or do anything that might remotely provoke or offend.2. graded adverb & adverb [ADV -ed]If someone or something is remotely placed or situated, they are a long way from other people or places. ⇒ ...the remotely situated, five bedroom house.

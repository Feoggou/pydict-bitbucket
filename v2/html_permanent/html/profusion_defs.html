Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
singular noun [with sing or pl verb, oft in N]If there is a profusion of something or if it occurs in profusion, there is a very large quantity or variety of it. [formal] [+ of] ⇒ The Dart is a delightful river with a profusion of wild flowers along its banks. ⇒ Olive groves, grapes, and citrus fruits grow in profusion.

principles

(ˈprɪnsɪpəlz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nouna set of standards or rules of personal conduct ⇒ He'd stoop to anything; he has no principles., ⇒ moral principles, ⇒ It goes against my principles.
Example sentences containing 'principles'
It seems to me he's simply following sound Marxist-Leninist principles where urban revolution is concerned. Higgins, Jack CONFESSIONALIt must have been one of your guiding principles in your heyday: know your enemy. Hilton, John Buxton MOONDROP TO MURDER

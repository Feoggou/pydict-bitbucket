Roscommon

(rɒsˈkɒmən )
Definitions
noun1. an inland county of N central Republic of Ireland, in Connacht: economy based on cattle and sheep farming. County town: Roscommon. Pop: 53 774 (2002). Area: 2463 sq km (951 sq miles)2. a former name for Galway (sense 3)

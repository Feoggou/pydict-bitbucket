itinerant

(ɪˈtɪnərənt , aɪ-)
Definitions
adjective1. itinerating2. working for a short time in various places, esp as a casual labourernoun3. an itinerant worker or other person
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

itinerantly (iˈtinerantly) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Late Latin itinerārī to travel, from iter a journey

Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]An itinerant worker travels around a region, working for short periods in different places. [formal] ⇒ ...the author's experiences as an itinerant musician.2. countable nounAn itinerant is someone whose way of life involves travelling around, usually someone who is poor and homeless. [formal]

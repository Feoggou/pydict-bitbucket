drowsy

(ˈdraʊzɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: drowsier or drowsiest1. heavy with sleepiness; sleepy2. inducing sleep; soporific3. sluggish or lethargic; dull
Derived forms
drowsily (ˈdrowsily) adverbdrowsiness (ˈdrowsiness) noun

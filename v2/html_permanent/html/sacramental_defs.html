sacramental

(ˌsækrəˈmɛntəl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or having the nature of a sacrament2. bound by or as if by a sacramentnoun3. Roman Catholic Church a sacrament-like ritual action, such as the sign of the cross or the use of holy water

Derived Forms

ˌsacramentally (ˌsacraˈmentally) adverbsacramentality (ˌsækrəmɛnˈtælɪtɪ ) or ˌsacramentalness (ˌsacraˈmentalness) noun

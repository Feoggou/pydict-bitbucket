Powered by COBUILD
(gæmbləʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: gamblers
Definitions
1. countable nounA gambler is someone who gambles regularly, for example in card games or horse racing.2. countable nounIf you describe someone as a gambler, you mean that they are ready to take risks in order to gain advantages or success. risk-taker ⇒ He had never been afraid of failure: he was a gambler, ready to go off somewhere else and start all over again.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
singular nounThe sum total of a number of things is all the things added or considered together. You often use this expression to indicate that you are disappointed because the total amount is rather small. [+ of] ⇒ That small room contained the sum total of the family's possessions. ⇒ We have already seen the sum total of his attributes.

Kaiser1

(ˈkaɪzə )
Definitions
noun (sometimes not capital) history1. any German emperor, esp Wilhelm II (ruled 1888–1918)2. obsolete any Austro-Hungarian emperor
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

kaiserdom (ˈkaiserdom) or kaiserism (ˈkaiserism) noun

Word Origin

C16: from German, ultimately from Latin Caesar emperor, from the cognomen of Gaius Julius Caesar

Kaiser2 - German

( ˈkaizər)
Definitions
noun Georg (ˈɡeːɔrk). 1878–1945, German expressionist dramatist

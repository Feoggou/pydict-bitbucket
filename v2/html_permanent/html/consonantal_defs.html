consonantal

(ˌkɒnsəˈnæntəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. relating to, functioning as, or constituting a consonant, such as the semivowel w in English work2. consisting of or characterized by consonants ⇒ a consonantal cluster
Derived forms
ˌconsonantally (ˌconsoˈnantally) adverb

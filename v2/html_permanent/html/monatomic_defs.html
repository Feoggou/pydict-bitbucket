monatomic

(ˌmɒnəˈtɒmɪk ) or monoatomic (ˌmɒnəʊəˈtɒmɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective chemistry1. (of an element) having or consisting of single atoms ⇒ argon is a monatomic gas2. (of a compound or molecule) having only one atom or group that can be replaced in a chemical reaction3. a less common word for monovalent

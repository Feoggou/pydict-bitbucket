gratis

(ˈɡreɪtɪs , ˈɡrætɪs , ˈɡrɑːtɪs )
Definitions
adverb, adjective(postpositive) without payment; free of charge
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Latin: out of kindness, from grātiīs, ablative pl of grātia favour

gratis

Powered by COBUILD
(grætɪs , grɑːt-) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [ADV after v]If something is done or provided gratis, it does not have to be paid for. ⇒ David gives the first consultation gratis.Gratis is also an adjective. ⇒ What I did for you was free, gratis, you understand?
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

gratis

Powered by COBUILD
(grætɪs) in Retail
Definitions
adverb(Retail: Service) When something is offered gratis, it is free of charge. ⇒ Some services must be paid for but alterations to garments can be made gratis. ⇒ Catalogues are mailed gratis to all club members. ⇒ When something is offered gratis, it is free of charge.

tray

(treɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a thin flat board or plate of metal, plastic, etc, usually with a raised edge, on which things can be carried2. a shallow receptacle for papers, etc, sometimes forming a drawer in a cabinet or box

Word Origin

Old English trieg; related to Old Swedish trö corn measure, Old Norse treyja carrier, Greek driti tub, German Trog trough

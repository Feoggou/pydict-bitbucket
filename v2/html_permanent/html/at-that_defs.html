Definitions

at that

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseYou use at that after a statement which modifies or emphasizes what you have just said. [emphasis] ⇒ Success never seems to come but through hard work, often physically demanding work at that. ⇒ The café was popular with locals, and not with the more respectable locals at that. →  that

Zacharias

(ˌzækəˈraɪəs ), Zachariah (ˌzækəˈraɪə ) or Zachary (ˈzækərɪ )
Definitions
nounNew Testament John the Baptist's father, who underwent a temporary period of dumbness for his lack of faith (Luke 1)

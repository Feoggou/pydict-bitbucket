sticker

(ˈstɪkə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an adhesive label, poster, or paper2. a person or thing that sticks3. a persevering or industrious person4. something prickly, such as a thorn, that clings to one's clothing, etc5. informal something that perplexes6. informal a knife used for stabbing or piercing

Definitions

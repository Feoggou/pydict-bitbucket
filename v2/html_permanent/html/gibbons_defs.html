Gibbons

(ˈɡɪbənz )
Definitions
noun1.  Grinling. 1648–1721, English sculptor and woodcarver, noted for his delicate carvings of fruit, flowers, birds, etc2.  Orlando. 1583–1625, English organist and composer, esp of anthems, motets, and madrigals

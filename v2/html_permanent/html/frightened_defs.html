(fraɪtənd ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [ADJ to-inf]If you are frightened, you are anxious or afraid, often because of something that has just happened or that you think may happen. [+ of] ⇒ She was frightened of flying. ⇒ Miriam was too frightened to tell her family what had happened.

Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a period of time as uneventful, you mean that nothing interesting, exciting, or important happened during it. ⇒ The return journey was uneventful, the car running perfectly. ⇒ It was rare for her to have an opportunity to discuss her dull, uneventful life.uneventfully graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v] ⇒ The five years at that school passed fairly uneventfully.

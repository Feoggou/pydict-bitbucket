upper deck

(ˈʌpə dɛk)
Definitions
noun1. automobiles the top floor of a double-decker vehicle ⇒ I sat on the upper deck of the bus.2. nautical the highest deck on a ship ⇒ He examined the sea from both sides of the upper deck but all was dark and quiet and still.3. aeronautics the top deck on an aircraft ⇒ It was dark on the upper deck of the 747.

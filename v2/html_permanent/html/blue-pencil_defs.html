blue pencil

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. deletion, alteration, or censorship of the contents of a book or other workverb blue-pencil -cils, -cilling, -cilled or US -cils, -ciling or -ciled2. (transitive) to alter or delete parts of (a book, film, etc), esp to censor

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Statistical means relating to the use of statistics. ⇒ The report contains a great deal of statistical information. ⇒ We need to back that suspicion up with statistical proof.statistically (stətɪstɪkli ) adverb [ADV adj] ⇒ The results are not statistically significant. ⇒ Statistically, ninety-eight percent of all acute sunstroke cases are fatal.

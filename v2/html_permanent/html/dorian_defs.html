Dorian

(ˈdɔːrɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a member of a Hellenic people who invaded Greece around 1100 bc, overthrew the Mycenaean civilization, and settled chiefly in the Peloponneseadjective2. of or relating to this people or their dialect of Ancient Greek; Doric3. music of or relating to a mode represented by the ascending natural diatonic scale from D to D. See also Hypo-

hearse

(hɜːs )
Definitions
nouna vehicle, such as a specially designed car or carriage, used to carry a coffin to a place of worship and ultimately to a cemetery or crematorium
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French herce, from Latin hirpex harrow

attache

(əˈtæʃeɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna person attached to a diplomatic mission who serves a specific purpose. another name for attaché

attaché - French

(əˈtæʃeɪ , ataʃe)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a specialist attached to a diplomatic mission ⇒ military attaché2. British a junior member of the staff of an embassy or legation
Word origin
C19: from French: someone attached (to a mission), from attacher to attach

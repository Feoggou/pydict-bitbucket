Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjectiveAlgerian means belonging or relating to Algeria, or its people or culture. ⇒ ...the Algerian desert. ⇒ ...a young Algerian actor.2. countable nounAn Algerian is an Algerian citizen or a person of Algerian origin.

Portland cement

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna cement that hardens under water and is made by heating a slurry of clay and crushed chalk or limestone to clinker in a kiln

Word Origin

C19: named after the Isle of Portland, because its colour resembles that of the stone quarried there

(θɜrdz) in Retail
Definitions
noun(Retail: Products) Thirds are faulty or damaged goods of a standard lower than seconds. ⇒ Their products are worse than factory seconds: we call them factory thirds. ⇒ The crockery on clearance is categorized as seconds, with some thirds, which have more obvious cracks and flaws. ⇒ Thirds are faulty or damaged goods of a standard lower than seconds.

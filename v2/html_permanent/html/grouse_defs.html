Definitions

grouse

Powered by COBUILD
(graʊs ) for learners of English
Word forms: grouses , grousing , groused
Definitions
language note:   The form grouse is used as the plural for meaning [sense 1].1. countable noun [oft N n]A grouse is a wild bird with a round body. Grouse are often shot for sport and can be eaten. ⇒ The party had been to the grouse moors that morning.Grouse is the flesh of this bird eaten as food. ⇒ The menu included roast grouse.2. verbIf you grouse, you complain. [V with quote] ⇒ 'How come we never know what's going on?' he groused. [V + about] ⇒ When they groused about the parking regulations, they did it with good humor. [Also V that, V]3. countable nounA grouse is a complaint. ⇒ There have been grouses about the economy, interest rates and house prices.

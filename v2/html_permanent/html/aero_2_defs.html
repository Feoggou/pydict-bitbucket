aero-

, aeri- or before a vowel aer-
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
combining form1. denoting air, atmosphere, or gas ⇒ aerodynamics2. denoting aircraft ⇒ aeronautics
Word origin
ultimately from Greek aēr air

aero-

Powered by COBUILD
(eəroʊ- )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. prefixaero- is used at the beginning of words, especially nouns, that refer to things or activities connected with air or movement through the air.2. combining formaero- combines with nouns to form nouns relating to aeroplanes. ⇒ ...the British aero-engine maker, Rolls-Royce.

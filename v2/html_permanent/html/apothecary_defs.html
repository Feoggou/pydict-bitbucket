apothecary

(əˈpɒθɪkərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -caries1. an archaic word for pharmacist2. law a chemist licensed by the Society of Apothecaries of London to prescribe, prepare, and sell drugs
Word origin
C14: from Old French apotecaire, from Late Latin apothēcārius warehouseman, from apothēca, from Greek apothēkē storehouse

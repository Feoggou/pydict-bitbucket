timer

(ˈtaɪmə )
Definitions
noun1. a device for measuring, recording, or indicating time2. a switch or regulator that causes a mechanism to operate at a specific time or at predetermined intervals3. a person or thing that times

Definitions
1. countable nounA timer is a device that measures time, especially one that is part of a machine and causes it to start or stop working at specific times. ⇒ ...electronic timers that automatically switch on the lights when it gets dark.2. → See also egg timer

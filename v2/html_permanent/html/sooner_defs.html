sooner

(ˈsuːnə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adverb1. the comparative of soon ⇒ he came sooner than I thought2. rather; in preference ⇒ I'd sooner die than give up3. See no sooner…than4. See sooner or later▶ USAGE When is sometimes used instead of than after no sooner, but this use is generally regarded as incorrect: no sooner had he arrived than (not when) the telephone rang

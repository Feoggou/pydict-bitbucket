herewith

(ˌhɪəˈwɪð , -ˈwɪθ)
Definitions
adverb1. formal together with this ⇒ we send you herewith your statement of account2. a less common word for hereby (sense 1)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

herewith

Powered by COBUILD
(hɪəʳwɪð ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [usu ADV with v] [oft n ADV, ADV n]Herewith means with this document, text, or book. You can use herewith in a letter to say that you are enclosing something with it. [formal, written] ⇒ ...the 236 revolutionary prisoners whose names are listed herewith. ⇒ I return herewith your papers.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

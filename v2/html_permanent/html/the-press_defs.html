the press

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
a. news media and agencies collectively, esp newspapersb. (as modifier) ⇒ a press matter, ⇒ press relations▷ See press

the press

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
those who work in the news media, esp newspaper reporters and photographers▷ See press

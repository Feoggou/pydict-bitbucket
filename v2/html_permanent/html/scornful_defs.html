Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are scornful of someone or something, you show contempt for them. [+ of] ⇒ He is deeply scornful of politicians. ⇒ ...a scornful simile.scornfully graded adverb [usu ADV with v] contemptuously ⇒ 'I didn't think so,' the judge said scornfully.

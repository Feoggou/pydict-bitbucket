coulter

(ˈkəʊltə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna blade or sharp-edged disc attached to a plough so that it cuts through the soil vertically in advance of the ploughshare. Also (esp US): colter
Word origin
Old English culter, from Latin: ploughshare, knife

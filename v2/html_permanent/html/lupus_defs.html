lupus

(ˈluːpəs )
Definitions
nounany of various ulcerative skin diseases▶ USAGE In current usage the word lupus alone is generally understood to signify lupus vulgaris, lupus erythematosus being normally referred to in full or by the abbreviation LE
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: via Medieval Latin from Latin: wolf; said to be so called because it rapidly eats away the affected part

suffrage

(ˈsʌfrɪdʒ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the right to vote, esp in public elections; franchise2. the exercise of such a right; casting a vote3. a supporting vote4. a prayer, esp a short intercessory prayer

Word Origin

C14: from Latin suffrāgium

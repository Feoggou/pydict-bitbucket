overmuch

(ˌəʊvəˈmʌtʃ )
Definitions
adverb, adjective1. too much; very muchnoun2. an excessive amount

overmuch for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(oʊvəʳmʌtʃ )
Definitions
adverb [usu ADV after v, oft ADV -ed]If something happens overmuch, it happens too much or very much. [formal] ⇒ He was not a man who thought overmuch about clothes.

snooze

(snuːz ) informal

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (intransitive) to take a brief light sleepnoun2. a nap

Derived Forms

snoozer (ˈsnoozer) nounsnoozy (ˈsnoozy) adjective

Word Origin

C18: of unknown origin

(snuːz )
Word forms: snoozes , snoozing , snoozed
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA snooze is a short, light sleep, especially during the day. [informal] 2. verbIf you snooze, you sleep lightly for a short period of time. [informal] [V] ⇒ Mark snoozed in front of the television. ⇒ Patients are given an extra 15 minutes to snooze before being woken gently.

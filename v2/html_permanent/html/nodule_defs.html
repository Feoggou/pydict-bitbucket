nodule

(ˈnɒdjuːl )
Definitions
noun1. a small knot, lump, or node2. Also called: root nodule any of the knoblike outgrowths on the roots of clover and many other legumes: contain bacteria involved in nitrogen fixation3. anatomy any small node or knoblike protuberance4. a small rounded lump of rock or mineral substance, esp in a matrix of different rock material
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

nodular (ˈnodular), nodulose (ˈnodulose) or nodulous (ˈnodulous) adjective

Word Origin

C17: from Latin nōdulus, from nōdus knot

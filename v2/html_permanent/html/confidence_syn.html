confidence

noun

  1. trust, belief, faith, dependence, reliance, credence • I have every confidence in you. OPPOSITES:  doubt, disbelief, distrust, mistrust, misgiving
  2. self-assurance, courage, assurance, aplomb, boldness, self-reliance, self-possession, nerve • She always thinks the worst of herself and has no confidence whatsoever. OPPOSITES:  fear, uncertainty, apprehension, shyness, self-doubt
  3. secret • I'm not in the habit of exchanging confidences with her.

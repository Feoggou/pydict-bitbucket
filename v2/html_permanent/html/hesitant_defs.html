hesitant

Powered by COBUILD
(hezɪtənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [ADJ to-inf]If you are hesitant about doing something, you do not do it quickly or immediately, usually because you are uncertain, embarrassed, or worried. [+ about] ⇒ She was hesitant about coming forward with her story. ⇒ His advisers are rightfully hesitant to let the United States be sucked into the conflict. ⇒ ...a quiet hesitant voice.hesitancy (hezɪtənsi ) uncount noun ⇒ A trace of hesitancy showed in Dr. Stockton's eyes.hesitantly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ 'Would you do me a favour?' she asked hesitantly.

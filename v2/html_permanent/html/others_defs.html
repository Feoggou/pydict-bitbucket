others

(ˈʌðəz)
Definitions
pronoun1. used to indicate that people or things are not the ones already mentioned, but different ones ⇒ Some projects are shorter than others., ⇒ Some of these methods will work. Others will not.2. used at the end of a list or a group of examples, to refer generally to people or things like the ones just mentioned ⇒ Copernicus, Galileo, and others, ⇒ The issue affects the fast-food industry, the farming industry and some others.3. See the others4. people in general, as opposed to yourself or a person you have already mentioned ⇒ Working for others can be most fulfilling., ⇒ a brave man who died helping others5. See among others

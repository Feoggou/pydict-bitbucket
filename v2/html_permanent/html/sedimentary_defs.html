sedimentary

(ˌsɛdɪˈmɛntərɪ )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. characteristic of, resembling, or containing sediment2. (of rocks) formed by the accumulation and consolidation of mineral and organic fragments that have been deposited by water, ice, or wind. Compare igneous, metamorphic

Derived Forms

ˌsedimentarily (ˌsediˈmentarily) adverb

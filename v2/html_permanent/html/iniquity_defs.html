iniquity

(ɪˈnɪkwɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. lack of justice or righteousness; wickedness; injustice2. a wicked act; sin
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

iniquitous (inˈiquitous) adjectiveiniquitously (inˈiquitously) adverbiniquitousness (inˈiquitousness) noun

Word Origin

C14: from Latin inīquitās, from inīquus unfair, from in-1 + aequus even, level; see equal

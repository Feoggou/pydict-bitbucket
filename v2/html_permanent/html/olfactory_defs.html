olfactory

(ɒlˈfæktərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
adjective1. of or relating to the sense of smellnoun plural -ries2. (usually plural) an organ or nerve concerned with the sense of smell
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Latin olfactus, past participle of olfacere, from olere to smell + facere to make

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveprepared or flavoured with spices ⇒ Every dish was served heavily spiced., ⇒ delicately spiced sauces, ⇒ Tuscan spiced lamb, ⇒ a jug of spiced wine, ⇒ She had a penchant for spiced apple juice., ⇒ pork spiced with black pepper, ⇒ The dish consists of chicken breast fillets spiced with nutmeg, parsley and ginger.

Definitions
variable nounIf you refer to an activity as a frivolity, you think that it is amusing and rather silly, rather than serious and sensible. ⇒ There is a serious message at the core of all this frivolity. ⇒ He was one of my most able pupils, but far too easily distracted by frivolities.

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
language note:   The form seawards can also be used for meaning [sense 1].1. adverb [ADV after v]Something that moves or faces seaward or seawards moves or faces in the direction of the sea or further out to sea. ⇒ A barge was about a hundred yards away, waiting to return seaward. ⇒ It faced seawards to the north.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]The seaward side of something faces in the direction of the sea or further out to sea. ⇒ The houses on the seaward side of the road were all in ruins.

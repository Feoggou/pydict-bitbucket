stratagem

(ˈstrætədʒəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna plan or trick, esp one to deceive an enemy

Word Origin

C15: ultimately from Greek stratēgos a general, from stratos an army + agein to lead

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA stratagem is a plan that is intended to achieve a particular effect, often by deceiving people. [formal] ⇒ Trade discounts may be used as a competitive stratagem to secure customer loyalty.

physiological

(ˌfɪzɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ) or physiologic (ˌfɪzɪəˈlɒdʒɪk)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to physiology2. of or relating to normal healthful functioning; not pathological

Derived Forms

ˌphysiologically (ˌphysioˈlogically) adverb

counting

(ˈkaʊntɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the saying of numbers ⇒ reading and writing, painting, counting, and scientific endeavours2. totalling up; adding ⇒ Computers are good at counting., ⇒ The counting of votes is proceeding smoothly.

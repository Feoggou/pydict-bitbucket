lentil

(ˈlɛntɪl )
Definitions
noun1. a small annual leguminous plant, Lens culinaris, of the Mediterranean region and W Asia, having edible brownish convex seeds2. any of the seeds of this plant, which are cooked and eaten as a vegetable, in soups, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: from Old French lentille, from Latin lenticula, diminutive of lēns lentil

infinitesimal

(ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməl )
Definitions
adjective1. infinitely or immeasurably small2. mathematics of, relating to, or involving a small change in the value of a variable that approaches zero as a limitnoun3. mathematics an infinitesimal quantity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe something as disordered, you mean it is untidy and is not neatly arranged. ⇒ Moretti ran a hand through his disordered red hair. ⇒ ...a disordered heap of mossy branches.2. graded adjectiveSomeone who is mentally disordered or who has a disordered mind is mentally ill. disturbed ⇒ ...agencies working with mentally disordered offenders. ⇒ ...events that crystallized into a conspiracy in one disordered mind.

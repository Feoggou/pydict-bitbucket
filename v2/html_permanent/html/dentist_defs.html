Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA dentist is a person who is qualified to examine and treat people's teeth. ⇒ Visit your dentist twice a year for a check-up.The dentist or the dentist's is used to refer to the surgery or clinic where a dentist works. ⇒ It's worse than being at the dentist's.

(ˈflaʊərɪŋ )
Definitions

flowering

Powered by COBUILD
(flaʊərɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounThe flowering of something such as an idea or artistic style is the development of its popularity and success. blossoming ⇒ He may be happy with the flowering of new thinking, but he has yet to contribute much to it himself. ⇒ ...the flowering of creative genius.2. adjective [ADJ n]Flowering shrubs, trees, or plants are those which produce noticeable flowers.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

futile

(ˈfjuːtaɪl )
Definitions
adjective1. having no effective result; unsuccessful2. pointless; unimportant; trifling3. inane or foolish ⇒ don't be so futile!
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

futilely (ˈfutilely) adverbfutileness (ˈfutileness) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin futtilis pouring out easily, worthless, from fundere to pour out

futile

Powered by COBUILD
(fjuːtaɪl , US -təl ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that something is futile, you mean there is no point in doing it, usually because it has no chance of succeeding. ⇒ He brought his arm up in a futile attempt to ward off the blow. ⇒ It would be futile to sustain his life when there is no chance of any improvement.

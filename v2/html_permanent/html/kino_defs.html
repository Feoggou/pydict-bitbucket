kino

(ˈkiːnəʊ )
Definitions
nouna dark red resin obtained from various tropical plants, esp an Indian leguminous tree, Pterocarpus marsupium, used as an astringent and in tanning. Also called: kino gum
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: of West African origin; related to Mandingo keno

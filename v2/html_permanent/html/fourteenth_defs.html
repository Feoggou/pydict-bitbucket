fourteenth

(ˈfɔːˈtiːnθ )
Definitions
adjective1. a. coming after the thirteenth in order, position, time etc. Often written: 14thb. (as noun) ⇒ the fourteenth in successionnoun2. a. one of 14 equal or nearly equal parts of somethingb. (as modifier) ⇒ a fourteenth part3. the fraction equal to one divided by 14 (1⁄14)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

fourteenth

Powered by COBUILD
(fɔːʳtiːnθ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. ordinal numberThe fourteenth item in a series is the one that you count as number fourteen. ⇒ The Festival, now in its fourteenth year, has become a major international jazz event.2. fractionA fourteenth is one of fourteen equal parts of something.

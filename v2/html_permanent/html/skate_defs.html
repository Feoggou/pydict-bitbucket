skate1

(skeɪt )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. See roller skate, ice skate2. the steel blade or runner of an ice skate3. such a blade fitted with straps for fastening to a shoe4. a current collector on an electric railway train that collects its current from a third rail. Compare bow collector5. See get one's skates onverb (intransitive)6. to glide swiftly on skates7. to slide smoothly over a surface8. See skate on thin ice

Word Origin

C17: via Dutch from Old French éschasse stilt, probably of Germanic origin

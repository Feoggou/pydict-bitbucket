demolition

(ˌdɛməˈlɪʃən , ˌdiː-)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act of demolishing or state of being demolished2. mainly militarya. destruction by explosivesb. (as modifier) ⇒ a demolition charge
Derived forms
ˌdemolitionist (ˌdemoˈlitionist) noun, adjective

(deməlɪʃən )
Word forms: demolitions
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounThe demolition of a building is the act of deliberately destroying it, often in order to build something else in its place. ⇒ The project required the total demolition of the old bridge. ⇒ The High Court has granted permission for the demolition work to continue.2. uncount nounThe demolition of a team or opponent is their defeat by a great amount. [journalism] annihilation ⇒ ...Lazio's impressive 3-1 demolition of Inter Milan.

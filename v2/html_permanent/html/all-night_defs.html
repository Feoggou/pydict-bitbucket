all-night

(ˌɔːlˈnaɪt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. open, available or operating throughout the night ⇒ He stopped off at an all-night supermarket, ⇒ the all-night pharmacy2. lasting all night ⇒ an all-night sitting in Parliament, ⇒ an all-night candlelit prayer vigil
Example sentences containing 'all-night'
These all-night rock parties are obviously too much for you these days.' I scowled.

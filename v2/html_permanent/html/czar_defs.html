czar

(zɑr )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  an emperor: title of any of the former emperors of Russia and, at various times, the sovereigns of other Slavic nations2.  Us any person having great or unlimited power; autocrat ⇒ the President's energy czar

embarrassing

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪmbærəsɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is embarrassing makes you feel shy or ashamed. ⇒ That was an embarrassing situation for me. ⇒ Men find it embarrassing to be honest.embarrassingly graded adverb & adverb [usu ADV adj/adv] ⇒ The lyrics of the song are embarrassingly banal. ⇒ Stephens had beaten him embarrassingly easily.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is embarrassing to a public figure such as a politician or an organization such as a political party causes problems for them. ⇒ He has put the Bonn government in an embarrassing position. [+ to] ⇒ The speech was deeply embarrassing to Cabinet ministers.

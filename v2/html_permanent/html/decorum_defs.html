decorum

(dɪˈkɔːrəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. propriety, esp in behaviour or conduct2. a requirement of correct behaviour in polite society
Word origin
C16: from Latin: propriety

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounDecorum is behaviour that people consider to be correct, polite, and respectable. [formal] ⇒ I was treated with decorum and respect throughout the investigation.

possum

(ˈpɒsəm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an informal name for opossum (sense 1)2. Also called: phalanger Australian and New Zealand any of various Australasian arboreal marsupials, such as Trichosurus vulpecula (brush-tailed phalanger), having dense fur and a long tail: family Phalangeridae3. See play possum

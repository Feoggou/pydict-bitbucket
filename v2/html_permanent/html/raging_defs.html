raging

(ˈreɪdʒɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. feeling or exhibiting intense anger2. (esp of storms, fires, etc) moving or surging with great violence ⇒ The field trip involved crossing a raging torrent., ⇒ As he came closer he saw a gigantic wall of raging flame before him.3. extreme or intense ⇒ There may be the occasional criticism but it's clear there is no raging debate right now., ⇒ If raging inflation returns, then interest rates will shoot up., ⇒ a raging thirst

Definitions

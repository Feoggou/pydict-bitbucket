violation

(ˌvaɪəˈleɪʃən ) in American English
Definitions
noun a violating or being violated; specif.,a.  infringement or breach, as of a law, rule, right, etc.b.  sexual assault; rapec.  desecration of something sacred, as a churchd.  interruption; disturbance

Word Origin

ME violacion < L violatio

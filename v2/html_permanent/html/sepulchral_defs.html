sepulchral

(sɪˈpʌlkrəl )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. suggestive of a tomb; gloomy2. of or relating to a sepulchre

Derived Forms

sepulchrally (seˈpulchrally) adverb

sepulchral

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is sepulchral is serious or sad and rather frightening. [literary] ⇒ 'He's gone,' Rory whispered in sepulchral tones.2. graded adjective & adjectiveA sepulchral place is dark, quiet, and empty. [literary] ⇒ He made his way along the sepulchral corridors.

anointing

(əˈnɔɪntɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. theology the application of oil as a sign of consecration or sanctification in a sacred rite ⇒ healing with prayer and anointing, ⇒ an oath, administered immediately before the anointing, which sets out the sovereign's duty to uphold the Church of England2. formal the official choice of a person to do an important job ⇒ the anointing and rise of a new teen pop star3. formal the smearing or rubbing over with oil or an oily liquid

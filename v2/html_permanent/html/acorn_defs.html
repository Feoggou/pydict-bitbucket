acorn

(ˈeɪkɔːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe fruit of an oak tree, consisting of a smooth thick-walled nut in a woody scaly cuplike base
Word origin
C16: a variant (through influence of corn) of Old English æcern the fruit of a tree, acorn; related to Gothic akran fruit, yield

Britannia

(brɪˈtænɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a female warrior carrying a trident and wearing a helmet, personifying Great Britain or the British Empire2. (in the ancient Roman Empire) the S part of Great Britain3. short for Britannia coin

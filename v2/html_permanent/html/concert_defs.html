concert

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (ˈkɒnsɜːt , -sət)1. a. a performance of music by players or singers that does not involve theatrical staging. Compare recital (sense 1)b. (as modifier) ⇒ a concert version of an opera2. agreement in design, plan, or action3. See in concertverb (kənˈsɜːt )4. to arrange or contrive (a plan) by mutual agreement
Word origin
C16: from French concerter to bring into agreement, from Italian concertare, from Late Latin concertāre to work together, from Latin: to dispute, debate, from certāre to contend

nocturnal

(nɒkˈtɜːnəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, used during, occurring in, or relating to the night2. (of animals) active at night3. (of plants) having flowers that open at night and close by day▷ Compare diurnal
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

nocturnality (ˌnocturˈnality) nounnocturnally (nocˈturnally) adverb

Word Origin

C15: from Late Latin nocturnālis, from Latin nox night

Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Nocturnal means occurring at night. night-time ⇒ ...long nocturnal walks. ⇒ ...the immensity of the nocturnal sky.2. adjectiveNocturnal creatures are active mainly at night. ⇒ When there is a full moon, this nocturnal rodent is careful to stay in its burrow.

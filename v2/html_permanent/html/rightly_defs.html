rightly

(ˈraɪtlɪ )
Definitions
adverb1. in accordance with the facts; correctly2. in accordance with principles of justice or morality3. with good reason; justifiably ⇒ he was rightly annoyed with her4. properly or suitably; appropriately ⇒ rightly dressed for a wedding5. (used with a negative) informal with certainty; positively or precisely (usually in the phrases I don't rightly know, I can't rightly say)

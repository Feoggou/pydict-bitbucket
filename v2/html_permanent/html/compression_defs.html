compression

(kəmˈprɛʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Also called: compressure (kəmˈprɛʃə ) the act of compressing or the condition of being compressed2. an increase in pressure of the charge in an engine or compressor obtained by reducing its volume

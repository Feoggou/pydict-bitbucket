quizzical

Powered by COBUILD
(kwɪzɪkəl ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [usu ADJ n]If you give someone a quizzical look or smile, you look at them in a way that shows that you are surprised or amused by their behaviour. ⇒ He gave Robin a mildly quizzical glance.quizzically graded adverb & adverb [ADV after v] ⇒ She looked at him quizzically.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

quotidian

(kwəʊˈtɪdɪən )
Definitions
adjective1. (esp of attacks of malarial fever) recurring daily2. everyday; commonplacenoun3. a malarial fever characterized by attacks that recur daily
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin quotīdiānus, variant of cottīdiānus daily

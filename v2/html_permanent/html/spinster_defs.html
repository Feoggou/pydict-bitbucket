spinster

(ˈspɪnstə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an unmarried woman regarded as being beyond the age of marriage2. law (in legal documents) a woman who has never married. Compare feme sole3. (formerly) a woman who spins thread for her living

Derived Forms

spinsterˌhood (ˈspinsterˌhood) nounspinsterish (ˈspinsterish) adjective

Word Origin

C14 (in the sense: a person, esp a woman, whose occupation is spinning; C17: a woman still unmarried): from spin + -ster

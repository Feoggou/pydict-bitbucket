tarnished

(ˈtɑːnɪʃt)
Definitions
adjective1. having lost its shine, esp by exposure to air or moisture, resulting in surface oxidation ⇒ its brown surfaces of tarnished brass2. stained, tainted, or spoilt ⇒ He says he wants to improve the tarnished image of his country.

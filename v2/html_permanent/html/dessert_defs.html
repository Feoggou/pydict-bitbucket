dessert

(dɪˈzɜːt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the sweet, usually last course of a meal2. mainly British (esp formerly) fruit, dates, nuts, etc, served at the end of a meal
Word origin
C17: from French, from desservir to clear a table, from des- dis-1 + servir to serve

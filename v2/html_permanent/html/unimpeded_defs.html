(ʌnɪmpiːdɪd )
Definitions
adjective [ADJ after v, ADJ n, v-link ADJ]If something moves or happens unimpeded, it continues without being stopped or interrupted by anything. [formal] ⇒ We drove, unimpeded by anyone, to Arras. ⇒ He promised to allow justice to run its course unimpeded. ⇒ U.N. aid convoys have unimpeded access to the city.

deficiency

(dɪˈfɪʃənsɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -cies1. the state or quality of being deficient2. a lack or insufficiency; shortage3. another word for deficit4. biology the absence of a gene or a region of a chromosome normally present

deficiency

Powered by COBUILD
(dɪfɪʃənsi )
Word forms: deficiencies
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. variable nounDeficiency in something, especially something that your body needs, is not having enough of it. ⇒ They did blood tests on him for signs of vitamin deficiency. ⇒ There are serious deficiencies in the numbers of suitable aircraft.2. variable nounA deficiency that someone or something has is a weakness or imperfection in them. [formal] ⇒ ...a serious deficiency in our air defence.

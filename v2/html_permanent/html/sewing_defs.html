Powered by COBUILD
(soʊɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounSewing is the activity of making or mending clothes or other things using a needle and thread. ⇒ Her mother had always done all the sewing.2. uncount nounSewing is clothes or other things that are being sewn. ⇒ We all got out our own sewing and sat in front of the log fire.

bowed

(baʊd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. lowered; bent forward; curved ⇒ bowed head, ⇒ bowed back2. See bowed down

bowed

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
pronunciation note:   Pronounced (boʊd ) for meaning [sense 1], and (baʊd ) for meaning [sense 2].1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is bowed is curved. ⇒ ...an old lady with bowed legs.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf a person's body is bowed, it is bent forward. stooped, hunched ⇒ He walked aimlessly along street after street, head down and shoulders bowed.3. → see also bow1

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v]If you do something peaceably, you do it quietly or peacefully, without violence or anger. [written] ⇒ The rival guerrilla groups had agreed to stop fighting and settle their differences peaceably. ⇒ 'OK, whatever you say,' he had agreed peaceably.

jolting

(ˈdʒəʊltɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the action of moving suddenly and quite violently ⇒ His rattled brain still not recovered from the jolting.adjective2. causing or characterized by sudden jars or blows ⇒ a jolting journey3. causing surprise or shock ⇒ When they got back that evening Nancy got a nasty, jolting surprise: her piggy bank was empty.

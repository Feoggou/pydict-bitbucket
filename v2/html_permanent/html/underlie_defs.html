underlie

(ˌʌndəˈlaɪ )
Definitions
verb -lies, -lying, -lay or -lain (transitive)1. to lie or be placed under or beneath2. to be the foundation, cause, or basis of ⇒ careful planning underlies all our decisions3. finance to take priority over (another claim, liability, mortgage, etc) ⇒ a first mortgage underlies a second4. to be the root or stem from which (a word) is derived ⇒ "happy" underlies "happiest"

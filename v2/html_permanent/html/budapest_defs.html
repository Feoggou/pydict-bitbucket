Budapest

(ˌbjuːdəˈpɛst , Hungarian ˈbudɔpɛʃt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe capital of Hungary, on the River Danube: formed in 1873 from the towns of Buda and Pest. Traditionally Buda, the old Magyar capital, was the administrative and Pest the trade centre: suffered severely in the Russian siege of 1945 and in the unsuccessful revolt against the Communist regime (1956).

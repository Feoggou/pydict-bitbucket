affable

(ˈæfəbəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. showing warmth and friendliness; kindly; mild; benign2. easy to converse with; approachable; amicable
Derived forms
ˌaffaˈbility nounˈaffably adverb
Word origin
C16: from Latin affābilis easy to talk to, from affārī to talk to, from ad- to + fāri to speak; compare fable, fate

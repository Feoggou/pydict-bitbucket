capital

noun

  1. money, funds, stock, investment(s), property, cash, finance, finances, financing, resources, assets, wealth, principal, means, wherewithal, wonga (slang) • The company is having difficulties in raising capital.

adjective

  1. principal, leading, controlling, major, important, chief, main, prime, central, essential, primary, vital, prominent, cardinal, paramount, foremost, overruling, pre-eminent
  2. upper case, block • The name and address are written in capital letters.
  3. (old-fashioned) first-rate, fine, excellent, superb, sterling, splendid, world-class • They had a capital time in London.

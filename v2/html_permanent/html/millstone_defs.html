millstone

(ˈmɪlˌstəʊn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. one of a pair of heavy flat disc-shaped stones that are rotated one against the other to grind grain2. a heavy burden, such as a responsibility or obligation ⇒ his debts were a millstone round his neck

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA millstone is a large, flat, round stone which is one of a pair of stones used to grind grain into flour.2. →  a millstone/a millstone around one's neck

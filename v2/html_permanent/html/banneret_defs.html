banneret

(ˈbænərɪt , -əˌrɛt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun (in the Middle Ages)1. Also called: knight banneret a knight who was entitled to command other knights and men-at-arms under his own banner2. a title of knighthood conferred by the king for valour on the battlefield
Word origin
C14: from Old French banerete a small banner

joyful

(ˈdʒɔɪfʊl )
Definitions
adjective1. full of joy; elated2. expressing or producing joy ⇒ a joyful look, ⇒ a joyful occasion
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

joyfully (ˈjoyfully) adverbjoyfulness (ˈjoyfulness) noun

joyful for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(dʒɔɪfʊl )
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is joyful causes happiness and pleasure. [formal] ⇒ Giving birth to a child is both painful and joyful. ⇒ A wedding is a joyful celebration of love.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone who is joyful is extremely happy. [formal] ⇒ We're a very joyful people; we're very musical people and we love music.joyfully graded adverb & adverb ⇒ They greeted him joyfully.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

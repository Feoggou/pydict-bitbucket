sectarian

(sɛkˈtɛərɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, belonging or relating to, or characteristic of sects or sectaries2. adhering to a particular sect, faction, or doctrine3. narrow-minded, esp as a result of rigid adherence to a particular sectnoun4. a member of a sect or faction, esp one who is bigoted in his adherence to its doctrines or in his intolerance towards other sects, etc

Derived Forms

sectarianˌism (secˈtarianˌism) noun

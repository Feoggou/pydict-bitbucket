assignation

(ˌæsɪɡˈneɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a secret or forbidden arrangement to meet, esp one between lovers2. the act of assigning; assignment3. law, mainly Scottish another word for assignment
Word origin
C14: from Old French, from Latin assignātiō a marking out; see assign

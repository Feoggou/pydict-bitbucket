diurnal

(daɪˈɜːnəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. happening during the day or daily2. (of flowers) open during the day and closed at night3. (of animals) active during the day. Compare nocturnalnoun4. a service book containing all the canonical hours except matins
Derived forms
diurnally (diˈurnally) adverb
Word origin
C15: from Late Latin diurnālis, from Latin diurnus, from diēs day

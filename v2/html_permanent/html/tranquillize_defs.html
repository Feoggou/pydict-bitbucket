Definitions
language note:   The spellings tranquilize in American English, and tranquillise in British English, are also used.verbTo tranquillize a person or an animal means to make them become calm, sleepy, or unconscious by means of a drug. [V n] ⇒ This powerful drug is used to tranquilize patients undergoing surgery.

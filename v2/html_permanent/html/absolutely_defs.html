Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adverb [usu ADV adj/adv, oft ADV with v]Absolutely means totally and completely. [emphasis] ⇒ Jill is absolutely right. ⇒ I absolutely refuse to get married. ⇒ There is absolutely no difference!2. adverbSome people say absolutely as an emphatic way of saying yes or of agreeing with someone. They say absolutely not as an emphatic way of saying no or of disagreeing with someone. [emphasis] ⇒ 'It's worrying, isn't it?'—'Absolutely.' ⇒ 'Did they approach you?'—'No, absolutely not.'

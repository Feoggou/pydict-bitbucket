variously

Powered by COBUILD
(veəriəsli ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverb [usu ADV with v, oft ADV adj]You can use variously to introduce a number of different ways in which something can be described. ⇒ ...the crowds, which were variously estimated at two to several thousand. ⇒ The family has been described variously as crass, bigoted, racist and plain boring.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers
Example sentences containing 'variously'
The three dots, most usual in formal writing for a, were variously written in quicker styles, a form like a circumflex being often employed. J.R.R.

adorn

(əˈdɔːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to decorate ⇒ she adorned her hair with flowers2. to increase the beauty, distinction, etc, of
Derived forms
aˈdornment noun
Word origin
C14: via Old French from Latin adōrnāre, from ōrnāre to furnish, prepare

engraved

(ɪnˈɡreɪvd)
Definitions
adjective1. (of an object or substance, such as silver, crystal, etc) having had a design cut into it ⇒ an engraved crystal goblet2. having a permanent place (in one's emotions or memory) ⇒ her image is engraved upon my heart, ⇒ The drowning at Chappaquiddick is still engraved in the collective memory of America.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

engraved

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪngreɪvd ) for learners of English
Definitions
adjectiveIf you say that something is engraved on your mind or memory or on your heart, you are emphasizing that you will never forget it, because it has made a very strong impression on you. [emphasis] etched [+ in/on/upon] ⇒ Her image is engraved upon my heart. ⇒ The drowning at Chappaquiddick, now more than 20 years ago, is still engraved in the collective memory of America.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

schooner

(ˈskuːnə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a sailing vessel with at least two masts, with all lower sails rigged fore-and-aft, and with the main mast stepped aft2. British a large glass for sherry3. US, Canadian, Australian and New Zealand a large glass for beer

Word Origin

C18: origin uncertain

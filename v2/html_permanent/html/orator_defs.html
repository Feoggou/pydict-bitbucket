orator

(ˈɒrətə )
Definitions
noun1. a public speaker, esp one versed in rhetoric2. a person given to lengthy or pompous speeches3. obsolete the claimant in a cause of action in chancery
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

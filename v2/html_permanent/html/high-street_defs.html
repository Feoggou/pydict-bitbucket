High Street

often not capitals
Definitions
noun the High Street1. British the main street of a town, usually where the principal shops are situated2. the market constituted by the general public3. (modifier) geared to meet the requirements of, and readily available for purchase by, the general public ⇒ High-Street fashion

high-street

(ˈhaɪˌstriːt)
Definitions
adjective British1. (often capitals) geared to meet the requirements of, and readily available for purchase by, the general public ⇒ High-Street fashion2. referring to the typical shops and businesses found on the high street of towns ⇒ The scanners are available from high-street stores.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

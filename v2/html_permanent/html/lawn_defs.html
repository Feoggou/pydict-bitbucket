lawn1

(lɔːn )
Definitions
noun1. a flat and usually level area of mown and cultivated grass2. an archaic or dialect word for glade
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lawny (ˈlawny) adjective

Word Origin

C16: changed form of C14 launde, from Old French lande, of Celtic origin; compare Breton lann heath; related to land

lawn2

(lɔːn )
Definitions
nouna fine linen or cotton fabric, used for clothing
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

lawny (ˈlawny) adjective

Word Origin

C15: probably from Laon, a town in France where linen was made

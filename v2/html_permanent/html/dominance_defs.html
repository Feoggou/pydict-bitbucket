(dɒmɪnəns )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounThe dominance of a particular person or thing is the fact that they are more powerful, successful, or important than other people or things. [+ of/over] ⇒ ...an attempt by each group to establish dominance over the other. ⇒ These economies will no doubt maintain their dominance of financial markets. ⇒ Legislation is the only route to ending the car's dominance as a form of transport.

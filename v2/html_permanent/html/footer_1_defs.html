footer1

(ˈfʊtə )
Definitions
noun1. archaic a person who goes on foot; walker2. (in combination) a person or thing of a specified length or height in feet ⇒ a six-footer

footer2

Definitions

footer3

or fouter (ˈfuːtər, ˈfuːtə)
Definitions
Scottishverb (intransitive)1. to potter; occupy oneself trivially or to little effectnoun2. a person who footers

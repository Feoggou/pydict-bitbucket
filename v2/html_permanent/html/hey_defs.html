hey

(heɪ )
Definitions
exclamation1. an expression indicating surprise, dismay, discovery, etc, or calling for another's attention2. South Africa an exclamation used for emphasis at the end of a statement, or alone to seek repetition or confirmation of another person's statement3. See hey presto
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C13: compare Old French hay, German hei, Swedish hej

hey

Powered by COBUILD
(heɪ ) for learners of English
Definitions
conventionIn informal situations, you say or shout 'hey' to attract someone's attention, or to show surprise, interest, or annoyance. [feelings] ⇒ 'Hey! Look out!' shouted Patty. ⇒ Hey, can I ask you a question?

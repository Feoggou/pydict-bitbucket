Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is up-to-date, it is the newest thing of its kind. ⇒ ...Germany's most up to date electric power station. ⇒ ...enhancing the system and bringing it up to date. ⇒ This production is bang up-to-date.2. graded adjective & adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If you are up-to-date about something, you have the latest information about it. ⇒ We'll keep you up to date with any news. ⇒ I am very up to date on this sort of thing because I listen to the news.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

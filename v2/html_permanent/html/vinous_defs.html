vinous

(ˈvaɪnəs )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of wine2. indulging in or indicative of indulgence in wine ⇒ a vinous complexion
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

vinously (ˈvinously) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Latin vīnōsus, from vīnum wine

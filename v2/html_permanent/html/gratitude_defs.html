gratitude

(ˈɡrætɪˌtjuːd )
Definitions
nouna feeling of thankfulness or appreciation, as for gifts or favours
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from Medieval Latin grātitūdō, from Latin grātus grateful

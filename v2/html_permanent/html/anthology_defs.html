anthology

(ænˈθɒlədʒɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -gies1. a collection of literary passages or works, esp poems, by various authors2. any printed collection of literary pieces, songs, works of art, etc
Derived forms
anthological (ˌænθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ) adjectiveanˈthologist noun
Word origin
C17: from Medieval Latin anthologia, from Greek, literally: a flower gathering, from anthos flower + legein to collect

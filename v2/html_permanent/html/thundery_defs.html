Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveWhen the weather is thundery, there is a lot of thunder, or there are heavy clouds which make you think that there will be thunder soon. ⇒ Thundery weather is forecast. ⇒ Heavy thundery rain fell throughout Thursday.

amazement

(əˈmeɪzmənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. incredulity or great astonishment; complete wonder or surprise2. obsolete bewilderment or consternation

(əmeɪzmənt )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft in N]Amazement is the feeling you have when something surprises you very much. ⇒ I stared at her in amazement.
Example sentences containing 'amazement'
To her amazement she saw that only about half the girls were there. Brent-Dyer, Elinor CHALLENGE FOR THE CHALET SCHOOLHe greeted them and now amazement had been replaced by reserve. Ashford, Jeffrey A QUESTION OF PRINCIPLEI have found that people are often struck dumb with amazement at the quickness of my intellect. Elizabeth Peters LION IN THE VALLEY

goodness

(ˈɡʊdnɪs )
Definitions
noun1. the state or quality of being good2. generosity; kindness3. moral excellence; piety; virtueexclamation4. a euphemism for God ⇒ goodness knows!, ⇒ thank goodness!
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

goodness

Powered by COBUILD
(gʊdnəs ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. exclamationPeople sometimes say 'goodness' or 'my goodness' to express surprise. [feelings] ⇒ Goodness, I wonder if he knows. ⇒ My goodness, he's earned millions in his career.2. → for goodness sake3. → thank goodness4. uncount nounGoodness is the quality of being kind, helpful, and honest. ⇒ He retains a faith in human goodness.

dewy

(ˈdjuːɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveWord forms: dewier or dewiest1. moist with or as with dew ⇒ a dewy complexion2. of or resembling dew3. poetic suggesting, falling, or refreshing like dew ⇒ dewy sleep
Derived forms
dewily (ˈdewily) adverbdewiness (ˈdewiness) noun

(djuːi , US duːi )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is dewy is wet with dew. [literary] ⇒ The satin slippers tread daintily through the dewy grass.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf your skin looks dewy, it looks soft and glows healthily.

geology

(dʒɪˈɒlədʒɪ )
Definitions
noun1. the scientific study of the origin, history, structure, and composition of the earth2. the geological features of a district or country
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

geological (ˌdʒɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ) or geologic (ˌgeoˈlogic) adjectivegeologically (ˌgeoˈlogically) adverbgeologist (geˈologist) or geologer (geˈologer) noun

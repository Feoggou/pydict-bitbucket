warring

(ˈwɔːrɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. involved in a war; involved in fighting ⇒ The warring factions have not yet turned in all their heavy weapons.2. involved in a quarrel or dispute ⇒ warring husbands and wives3. conflicting ⇒ warring interests, ⇒ His years in Scotland had been devoted to reconciling warring interests.
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

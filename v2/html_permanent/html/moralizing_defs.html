moralizing

or moralising (ˈmɒrəlaɪzɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. serving as a moral lessonnoun2. the act or an instance of moralizing ⇒ We have tried to avoid any moralizing.
Example sentences containing 'moralizing'
`It seems to me that there is little basis in the modern world for an essentialist and moralizing language of lost or future purity. Appiganesi, Lisa DREAMS OF INNOCENCEThere was no point in judging or moralizing , because Richard was his own law. Thomas, Rosie THE WHITE DOVEI resented his moralizing tone, and couldn't resist a comeback, though I disguised it with a smile. Stuart Harrison BETTER THAN THIS

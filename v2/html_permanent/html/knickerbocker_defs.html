Knickerbocker

(ˈnɪkəˌbɒkə )
Definitions
noun US1. a descendant of the original Dutch settlers of New York2. an inhabitant of New York
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C19: named after Diedrich Knickerbocker, fictitious Dutchman alleged to be the author of Washington Irving's History of New York (1809)

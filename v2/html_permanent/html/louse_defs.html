louse

(laʊs )
Definitions
nounWord forms: plural lice (laɪs )1. any wingless bloodsucking insect of the order Anoplura: includes Pediculus capitis (head louse), Pediculus corporis (body louse), and the crab louse, all of which infest man ▶ Related adjective: pedicular2. See biting louse3. any of various similar but unrelated insects, such as the plant louse and book louse4. Word forms: plural louses slang an unpleasant or mean personverb (transitive)5. to remove lice from6. (foll by up) slang to ruin or spoil
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English lūs; related to Old High German, Old Norse lūs

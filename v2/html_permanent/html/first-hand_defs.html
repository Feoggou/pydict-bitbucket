first hand

Powered by COBUILD
also first-hand also firsthand for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]First hand information or experience is gained or learned directly, rather than from other people or from books. ⇒ School trips give children firsthand experience not available in the classroom.First-hand is also an adverb. ⇒ We've been through Germany and seen first-hand what's happening there.2. → at first hand
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

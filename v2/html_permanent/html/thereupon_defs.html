thereupon

(ˌðɛərəˈpɒn )
Definitions
adverb1. immediately after that; at that point ⇒ thereupon, the whole class applauded2. formal upon that thing, point, subject, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

thereupon

Powered by COBUILD
(ðeərəpɒn ) for learners of English
Definitions
adverbThereupon means happening immediately after something else has happened and usually as a result of it. [formal] ⇒ Some months ago angry demonstrators mounted a noisy demonstration beneath his window. His neighbours thereupon insisted upon more security.

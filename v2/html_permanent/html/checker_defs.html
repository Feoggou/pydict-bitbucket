checker1

(ˈtʃɛkə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun, verb1. the usual US spelling of chequernoun2. textiles a variant spelling of chequer (sense 2)3. US and Canadian any one of the 12 flat thick discs used by each player in the game of checkers. Also called (in Britain and certain other countries): draughtsman

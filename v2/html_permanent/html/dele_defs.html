dele

(ˈdiːlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural deles1. a sign (δ) indicating that typeset matter is to be deleted. Compare stetverbWord forms: deles, deleing or deled2. (transitive) to mark (matter to be deleted) with a dele
Word origin
C18: from Latin: delete (imperative), from dēlēre to destroy, obliterate; see delete

Synonyms of thrill

thrill

noun

  1. pleasure, charge (slang), kick (informal), glow, sensation, buzz (slang), high, stimulation, tingle, titillation, flush of excitement
    • I remember the thrill of opening presents on Christmas morning.
    OPPOSITES:  boredom, tedium, monotony, dullness, ennui, dreariness
  2. trembling, throb, shudder, flutter, fluttering, tremor, quiver, vibration
    • He felt a thrill of fear, of adrenaline.

verb

  1. excite, stimulate, arouse, move, send (slang), stir, flush, tingle, electrify, titillate, give someone a kick
    • The electric atmosphere both thrilled and terrified him.
  2. tremble, shake, quake, throb, shudder, flutter, quiver, vibrate

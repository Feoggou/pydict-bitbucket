footstep

(ˈfʊtˌstɛp )
Definitions
noun1. the action of taking a step in walking2. the sound made by stepping or walking3. the distance covered with a step; pace4. a footmark5. a single stair; step6. to continue the tradition or example of another
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

footstep

Powered by COBUILD
(fʊtstep ) for learners of English
Word forms: footsteps
Definitions
1. countable noun [usu pl]A footstep is the sound or mark that is made by someone walking each time their foot touches the ground. ⇒ I heard footsteps outside.2. →  to follow in someone's footsteps
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

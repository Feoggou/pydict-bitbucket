heating

Powered by COBUILD
(hiːtɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounHeating is the process of heating a building or room, considered especially from the point of view of how much this costs. ⇒ You can still find cottages for £350 a week, including heating. ⇒ ...heating bills.2. uncount nounHeating is the system and equipment that is used to heat a building. ⇒ There is no heating in the shed, so the dogs would be bitterly cold at night. ⇒ I wish I knew how to turn on the heating.3. → See also central heating

phlogiston

(flɒˈdʒɪstɒn , -tən)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounchemistry a hypothetical substance formerly thought to be present in all combustible materials and to be released during burning

Word Origin

C18: via New Latin from Greek, from phlogizein to set alight; related to phlegein to burn

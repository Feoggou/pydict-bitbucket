toddy

(ˈtɒdɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -dies1. a drink made from spirits, esp whisky, with hot water, sugar, and usually lemon juice2. a. the sap of various palm trees (toddy or wine palms), used as a beverageb. the liquor prepared from this sap3. (in Malaysia) a milky-white sour alcoholic drink made from fermented coconut milk, drunk chiefly by Indians
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from Hindi tārī juice of the palmyra palm, from tār palmyra palm, from Sanskrit tāra, probably of Dravidian origin

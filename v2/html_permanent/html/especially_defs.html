especially

(ɪˈspɛʃəlɪ )
Definitions
adverb1. in particular; specifically ⇒ for everyone's sake, especially your children's2. very much ⇒ especially useful for vegans

especially

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪspeʃəli ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adverbYou use especially to emphasize that what you are saying applies more to one person, thing, or area than to any others. [emphasis] ⇒ Millions of wild flowers colour the valleys, especially in April and May. ⇒ Re-apply sunscreen every two hours, especially if you have been swimming.2. adverb [ADV adj/adv]You use especially to emphasize a characteristic or quality. [emphasis] ⇒ Babies lose heat much faster than adults, and are especially vulnerable to the cold in their first month.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

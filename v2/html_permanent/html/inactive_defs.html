inactive

(ɪnˈæktɪv )
Definitions
adjective1. idle or inert; not active2. sluggish, passive, or indolent3. military of or relating to persons or equipment not in active service4. chemistry (of a substance) having little or no reactivity5. (of an element, isotope, etc) having little or no radioactivity
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inactively (inˈactively) adverbinactivity (ˌinacˈtivity) or inactiveness (inˈactiveness) noun

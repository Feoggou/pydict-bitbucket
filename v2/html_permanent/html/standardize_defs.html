standardize

or standardise (ˈstændəˌdaɪz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to make or become standard2. (transitive) to test by or compare with a standard

Derived Forms

ˌstandardization (ˌstandardiˈzation) or ˌstandardisation (ˌstandardiˈsation) nounstandardˌizer (ˈstandardˌizer) or standardˌiser (ˈstandardˌiser) noun

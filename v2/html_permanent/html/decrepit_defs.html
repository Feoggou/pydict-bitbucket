decrepit

(dɪˈkrɛpɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. enfeebled by old age; infirm2. broken down or worn out by hard or long use; dilapidated
Derived forms
decrepitly (deˈcrepitly) adverbdecrepiˌtude (deˈcrepiˌtude) or decrepitness (deˈcrepitness) noun
Word origin
C15: from Latin dēcrepitus, from crepāre to creak

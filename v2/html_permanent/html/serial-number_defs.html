Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [oft with poss]The serial number of an object is a number on that object which identifies it. ⇒ ...the gun's serial number. ⇒ ...your bike's serial number. ⇒ All the bills had the same serial number. The cash was counterfeit.2. countable nounThe serial number of a member of the United States military forces is a number which identifies them. ⇒ He could never ever give any responses to his captor other than name, rank, serial number and date of birth.

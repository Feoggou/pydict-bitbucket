puzzled

(ˈpʌzəld)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective(of a person) confused or bewildered ⇒ Norman looked puzzled., ⇒ He looked at his wife with a puzzled expression., ⇒ A puzzled look came across his face., ⇒ He gave a puzzled frown., ⇒ You look puzzled!, ⇒ Critics are puzzled by the election results., ⇒ I was puzzled about one thing., ⇒ I'm puzzled as to why he decided not to come., ⇒ He was puzzled as to why he had got such a strange result.

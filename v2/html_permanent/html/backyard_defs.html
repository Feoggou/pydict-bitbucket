Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA backyard is an area of land at the back of a house.2. countable noun [with poss]If you refer to a country's own backyard, you are referring to its own territory or to somewhere that is very close and where that country wants to influence events. ⇒ Economics will not stop Europe's politicians complaining when jobs are lost in their own backyard. ⇒ Cuba is the largest island in the Caribbean, an area that the United States has long regarded as its own backyard.

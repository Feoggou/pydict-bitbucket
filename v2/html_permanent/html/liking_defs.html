liking

(ˈlaɪkɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. the feeling of a person who likes; fondness2. a preference, inclination, or pleasure

liking

Powered by COBUILD
(laɪkɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. singular nounIf you have a liking for something or someone, you like them. [+ for] ⇒ She had a liking for good clothes. ⇒ He bought me records to encourage my liking for music. [+ to] ⇒ Mrs Jermyn took a great liking to him.2. →  too fast etc/not fast etc enough for sb's liking3. →  to sb's liking
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

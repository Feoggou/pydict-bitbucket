homeopathy

or homoeopathy (ˌhəʊmɪˈɒpəθɪ )
Definitions
nouna method of treating disease by the use of small amounts of a drug that, in healthy persons, produces symptoms similar to those of the disease being treated. Compare allopathy
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

homeopathic or homoeopathic (ˌhəʊmɪəˈpæθɪk ) adjectivehomeopathically (ˌhomeoˈpathically) or homoeopathically (ˌhomoeoˈpathically) adverbhomeopathist, homoeopathist (ˌhəʊmɪˈɒpəθɪst ) or homeopath or homoeopath (ˈhəʊmɪəˌpæθ ) noun

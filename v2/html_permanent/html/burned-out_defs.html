Definitions
COBUILD Advanced American English Dictionary
1. adjectiveBurned-out vehicles or buildings have been so badly damaged by fire that they can no longer be used. ⇒ ...a burned-out car.2. adjectiveIf someone is burned-out, they exhaust themselves at an early stage in their life or career because they have achieved too much too quickly. [informal] ⇒ Everyone I know who kept it up at that intensity is burned-out.

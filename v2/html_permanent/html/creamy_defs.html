Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveFood or drink that is creamy contains a lot of cream or milk. ⇒ ...rich, creamy coffee. ⇒ ...a creamy chocolate and nut candy bar.2. graded adjective & adjectiveFood that is creamy has a soft smooth texture and appearance. ⇒ ...creamy mashed potato. ⇒ Whisk the mixture until it is smooth and creamy. ⇒ ...a delicious soft blue cheese with a creamy texture.

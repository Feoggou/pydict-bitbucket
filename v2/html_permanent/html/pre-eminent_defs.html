Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
regional note:   in AM, usually use preeminentgraded adjective & adjectiveIf someone or something is pre-eminent in a group, they are more important, powerful, or capable than other people or things in the group. [formal] ⇒ ...his fifty years as the pre-eminent political figure in the country. ⇒ For a decade 'X' was the pre-eminent punk band in Los Angeles.pre-eminence uncount noun ⇒ ...London's continuing pre-eminence among European financial centres. ⇒ For those under 40 the pre-eminence of post-war U.S. literature goes unquestioned.

asinine

(ˈæsɪˌnaɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. obstinate or stupid2. resembling an ass
Derived forms
ˈasiˌninely adverbasininity (ˌæsɪˈnɪnɪtɪ ) noun
Word origin
C16: from Latin asinīnus, from asinus ass1

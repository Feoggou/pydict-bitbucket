billy1

(ˈbɪlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -liesUS and Canadian a wooden club esp a police officer's truncheon
Word origin
C19: special use of the name Billy, pet form of William

billy2

(ˈbɪlɪ ) or billycan (ˈbɪlɪˌkæn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -lies or -lycans1. a metal can or pot for boiling water, etc, over a campfire2. Australian and New Zealand (as modifier) ⇒ billy-tea3. Australian and New Zealand informal to make tea
Word origin
C19: from Scot billypot cooking vessel

Synonyms of tour

tour

noun

  1. circuit, course, round
    • the first official cricket tour of South Africa for 22 years
  2. journey, expedition, excursion, trip, progress, outing, jaunt, junket, peregrination
    • week five of my tour of European cities
  3. visit, inspection, ramble, walk round, walkabout
    • a guided tour of a ruined Scottish castle
  4. (of duty) stint, turn, shift, stretch, spell, period of service, period of enlistment
    • His tour of duty in Ireland changed him.

verb

  1. travel round, holiday in, travel through, journey round, trek round, go on a trip through
    • A few years ago they toured the country in a roadshow.
  2. visit, explore, go round, inspect, walk round, drive round, sightsee
    • You can tour the site in modern coaches fitted with videos.

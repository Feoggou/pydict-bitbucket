innings

(ˈɪnɪŋz )
Definitions
noun1. (functioning as singular) cricketa. the batting turn of a player or teamb. the runs scored during such a turn2. (sometimes singular) a period of opportunity or action3. (functioning as plural) land reclaimed from the sea

Definitions
uncount nounIf you say that someone's claims or promises are just hot air, you are criticizing them because they are made mainly to impress people and have no real value or meaning. [informal, disapproval] ⇒ His justification for the merger was just hot air. ⇒ I'd come to the conclusion by then that he was all hot air.

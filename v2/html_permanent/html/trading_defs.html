trading

(ˈtrædɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe act of buying and selling goods and services either on the domestic (wholesale and retail) markets or on the international (import, export, and entrepôt) markets ⇒ Trading on the stock exchange may be suspended., ⇒ Sunday trading laws will be reformed.

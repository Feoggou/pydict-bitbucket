liquidation

(ˌlɪkwɪˈdeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a. the process of terminating the affairs of a business firm, etc, by realizing its assets to discharge its liabilitiesb. the state of a business firm, etc, having its affairs so terminated (esp in the phrase to go into liquidation)2. destruction; elimination

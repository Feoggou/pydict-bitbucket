umbilical

(ʌmˈbɪlɪkəl , ˌʌmbɪˈlaɪkəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or resembling the umbilicus or the umbilical cord2. in the region of the umbilicus ⇒ an umbilical hernianoun3. short for umbilical cord
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

umbilically (umˈbilically) adverb

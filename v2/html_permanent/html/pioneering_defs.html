pioneering

(ˌpaɪəˈnɪərɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiverelating to, involving, or doing new and innovative work ⇒ The school has won awards for its pioneering work with the community., ⇒ a pioneering treatment for breast cancer, ⇒ The pioneering surgery was carried out at Queen Margaret hospital.

pioneering

(paɪənɪəʳɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]Pioneering work or a pioneering individual does something that has not been done before, for example by developing or using new methods or techniques. ⇒ The school has won awards for its pioneering work with the community. ⇒ America has always retained her pioneering spirit.

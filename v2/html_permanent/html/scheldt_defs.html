Scheldt

(ʃɛlt , skɛlt )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
nouna river in W Europe, rising in NE France and flowing north and northeast through W Belgium to Antwerp, then northwest to the North Sea in the SW Netherlands. Length: 435 km (270 miles). Flemish and Dutch name: Schelde (ˈsxɛldɛ ). French name: Escaut

leisure

(ˈlɛʒə , US English ˈliːʒər)
Definitions
noun1. a. time or opportunity for ease, relaxation, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ leisure activities2. ease or leisureliness3. See at leisure4. See at one's leisure
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French leisir; ultimately from Latin licēre to be allowed

leisure

Powered by COBUILD
(leʒəʳ , US liːʒ- ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount noun [usu N n]Leisure is the time when you are not working and you can relax and do things that you enjoy. ⇒ ...a relaxing way to fill my leisure time. ⇒ ...one of Britain's most popular leisure activities.2. →  at leisure/at sb's leisure
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

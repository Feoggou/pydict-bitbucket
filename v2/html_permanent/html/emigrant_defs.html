emigrant

(ˈɛmɪɡrənt )
Definitions
nouna. a person who leaves one place or country, esp a native country, to settle in another. Compare immigrantb. (as modifier) ⇒ an emigrant worker
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is manageable is of a size, quantity, or level of difficulty that people are able to deal with. ⇒ He will now try to cut down the task to a manageable size. ⇒ The present flow of refugees was manageable. ⇒ Keep your spending on luxuries down to manageable proportions.

filth

(fɪlθ )
Definitions
noun1. foul or disgusting dirt; refuse2. extreme physical or moral uncleanliness; pollution3. vulgarity or obscenity, as in language4. See the filth
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English fӯlth; related to Old Saxon, Old High German fūlitha; see foul, defile

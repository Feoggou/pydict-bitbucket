pharmaceutical

(ˌfɑːməˈsjuːtɪkəl ) or less commonly pharmaceutic
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveof or relating to drugs or pharmacy

Derived Forms

ˌpharmaceutically (ˌpharmaˈceutically) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin pharmaceuticus, from Greek pharmakeus purveyor of drugs; see pharmacy

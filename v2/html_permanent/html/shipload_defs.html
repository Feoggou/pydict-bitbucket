Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounA shipload of people or goods is as many people or goods as a ship can carry. [+ of] ⇒ ...a shipload of refugees. ⇒ A shipload of supplies arrived in Havana, Cuba, last night.

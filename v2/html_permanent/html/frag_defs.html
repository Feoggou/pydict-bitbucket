frag

(fræɡ )
Definitions
verbWord forms: frags, fragging or fragged(transitive) US military slang to kill or wound (a fellow soldier or superior officer) deliberately with an explosive device
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fragging (ˈfragging) noun

Word Origin

C20: short for fragmentation grenade, as used in Vietnam
Example sentences containing 'frag'
A blade across the throat, a bullet through the back of the neck, even a frag grenade in the gut and you died on the spot. Gregg Andrew Hurwitz MINUTES TO BURN

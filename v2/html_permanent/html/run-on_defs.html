run on

Definitions
verb (adverb)1. (intransitive) to continue without interruption2. to write with linked-up characters3. printing to compose text matter without indentation or paragraphingnoun run-on4. printinga. text matter composed without indentingb. (as modifier) ⇒ run-on text matter5. a. a word added at the end of a dictionary entry whose meaning can be easily inferred from the definition of the headwordb. (as modifier) ⇒ a run-on entry

run-on

(ˈrʌnˌɒn)
Definitions
noun1. printing text matter composed without indenting2. a word added at the end of a dictionary entry whose meaning can be easily inferred from the definition of the headwordmodifier3. printing designating text matter composed without indenting ⇒ run-on text matter4. designating a word added at the end of a dictionary entry whose meaning can be easily inferred from the definition of the headword ⇒ a run-on entry

bespoke

(bɪˈspəʊk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective mainly British1. (esp of clothing or a website, computer program, etc) made to the customer's specifications2. making or selling such clothing, websites, etc ⇒ a bespoke tailor, ⇒ a bespoke web designer

(bɪspoʊk )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]A bespoke craftsman such as a tailor makes and sells things that are specially made for the customer who ordered them. [British, formal] ⇒ ...suits made by a bespoke tailor.2. adjective [ADJ n]Bespoke things such as clothes have been specially made for the customer who ordered them. [British, formal] ⇒ ...bespoke coats.

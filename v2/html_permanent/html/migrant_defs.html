migrant

(ˈmaɪɡrənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or animal that moves from one region, place, or country to another2. an itinerant agricultural worker who travels from one district to another3. mainly Australiana. an immigrant, esp a recent oneb. (as modifier) ⇒ a migrant hosteladjective4. moving from one region, place, or country to another; migratory

Word Origin

C17: from Latin migrāre to change one's abode

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA migrant is a person who moves from one place to another, especially in order to find work. ⇒ The government divides asylum-seekers into economic migrants and genuine refugees. ⇒ ...migrant workers following harvests northward.2. countable noun [oft N n]Migrants are birds, fish, or animals that migrate from one part of the world to another. ⇒ Migrant birds shelter in the reeds.

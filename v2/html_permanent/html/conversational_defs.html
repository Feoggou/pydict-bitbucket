conversational

(ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃənəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, using, or in the manner of conversation2. inclined to or skilled in conversation; conversable
Derived forms
ˌconversationally (ˌconverˈsationally) adverb

Synonyms of knot

knot

noun

  1. connection, tie, bond, joint, bow, loop, braid, splice, rosette, ligature
    • One lace had broken and been tied in a knot.
  2. group, company, set, band, crowd, pack, squad, circle, crew (informal), gang, mob, clique, assemblage
    • A little knot of men stood clapping.
  3. cluster, collection, mass, pile, bunch, heap, clump, tuft, aggregation

verb

  1. tie, secure, bind, complicate, weave, loop, knit, tether, entangle
    • He knotted the bandanna around his neck.
  2. tighten, become tense, tauten, stretch
    • I felt my stomach knot with apprehension.
  • See tie the knot

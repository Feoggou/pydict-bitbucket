as against

Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf you discuss a particular set of facts or figures as against another set, you are comparing or contrasting the two sets of facts or figures. ⇒ Over 50% of divorced men regretted their divorce, as against 25% of women. →  against

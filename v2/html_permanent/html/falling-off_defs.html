falling-off

(ˌfɔːlɪŋˈɒf)
Definitions
nouna decrease in amount or quality ⇒ This is due to the economic downturn and a falling-off of interest in flotations., ⇒ There has been a falling-off in box-office income and other earnings., ⇒ I think there is a falling-off in the standards of playwriting today.

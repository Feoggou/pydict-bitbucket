stanza

(ˈstænzə )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. prosody a fixed number of verse lines arranged in a definite metrical pattern, forming a unit of a poem2. US and Australian a half or a quarter in a football match

Derived Forms

stanzaed (ˈstanzaed) adjectivestanzaic (stænˈzeɪɪk ) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Italian: halting place, from Vulgar Latin stantia (unattested) station, from Latin stāre to stand

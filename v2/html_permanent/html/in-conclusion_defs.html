Definitions
phraseYou say 'in conclusion' to indicate that what you are about to say is the last thing that you want to say. ⇒ In conclusion, walking is a cheap, safe, enjoyable and readily available form of exercise. →  conclusion
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

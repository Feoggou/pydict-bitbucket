Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu pl]The conquerors of a country or group of people are the people who have taken complete control of that country or group's land. ⇒ The people of an oppressed country obey their conquerors because they want to go on living.2. countable noun [usu with poss]The conqueror of a person or team is the person or team that beats them in a game or contest. [journalism] ⇒ He easily overcame Garcia, Saturday's conqueror of Ernie Els. ⇒ Her conqueror, Senator Pete Wilson, is a diffident, moderate man.

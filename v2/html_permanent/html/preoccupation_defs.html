Definitions

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounIf you have a preoccupation with something or someone, you keep thinking about them because they are important to you. ⇒ Karouzos's poetry shows a profound preoccupation with the Orthodox Church. [+ with] ⇒ In his preoccupation with Robyn, Crook had neglected everything.2. uncount nounPreoccupation is a state of mind in which you think about something so much that you do not consider other things to be important. ⇒ It was hard for him to be aware of her; he kept sinking back into black preoccupation.

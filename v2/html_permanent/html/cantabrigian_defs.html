Cantabrigian

(ˌkæntəˈbrɪdʒɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or characteristic of Cambridge or Cambridge University, or of Cambridge, Massachusetts, or Harvard Universitynoun2. a member or graduate of Cambridge University or Harvard University3. an inhabitant or native of Cambridge
Word origin
C17: from Medieval Latin Cantabrigia

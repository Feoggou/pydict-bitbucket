fold up

Definitions
verb (adverb)1. (transitive) to make smaller or more compact2. (intransitive) to collapse, as with laughter or pain

Definitions
1. phrasal verbIf you fold something up, you make it into a smaller, neater shape by folding it, usually several times. [V n P] ⇒ She folded it up, and tucked it into her purse. [V P n] ⇒ He folded up his paper and put it away.2. → See also fold [sense 4], fold-up →  fold

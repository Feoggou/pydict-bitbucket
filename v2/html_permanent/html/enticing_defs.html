Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is enticing is extremely attractive and makes you want to get it or to become involved with it. ⇒ A prospective premium of about 30 per cent on their initial investment is enticing. ⇒ ...many enticing illustrations.enticingly graded adverb & adverb ⇒ ...laying out their stall enticingly.

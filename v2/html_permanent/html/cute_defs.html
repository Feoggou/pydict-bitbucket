cute

(kjuːt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. appealing or attractive, esp in a pretty way2. mainly US informal sexually attractive3. informal affecting cleverness or prettiness4. clever; shrewd
Derived forms
cutely (ˈcutely) adverbcuteness (ˈcuteness) noun
Word origin
C18 (in the sense: clever): shortened from acute

Word forms: cuter , cutest
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething or someone that is cute is very pretty or attractive, or is intended to appear pretty or attractive. [informal] ⇒ Oh, look at that dog! He's so cute. ⇒ ...a cute little house. ⇒ ...a cute little baby.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as cute, you think they are sexually attractive. [mainly US, informal] ⇒ There was this girl, and I thought she was really cute.3. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe someone as cute, you mean that they deal with things cleverly. [US] ⇒ The President, trying to be cute, said his liberal arts education had not really prepared him for such complicated issues as this. ⇒ That's a cute trick.

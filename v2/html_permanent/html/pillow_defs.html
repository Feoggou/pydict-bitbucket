pillow

(ˈpɪləʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a cloth case stuffed with feathers, foam rubber, etc, used to support the head, esp during sleep2. Also called: cushion a padded cushion or board on which pillow lace is made3. anything like a pillow in shape or functionverb (transitive)4. to rest (one's head) on or as if on a pillow5. to serve as a pillow for

Word Origin

Old English pylwe, from Latin pulvīnus cushion; compare German Pfühl

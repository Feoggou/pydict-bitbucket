Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phraseIf you say that someone or something is past it, they are no longer able to do what they used to do. [informal, disapproval] ⇒ I suppose they're saying that I'm past it. ⇒ We could do with a new car. The one we've got is a bit past it. →  past

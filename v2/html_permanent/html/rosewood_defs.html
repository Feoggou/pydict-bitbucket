rosewood

(ˈrəʊzˌwʊd )
Definitions
noun1. the hard dark wood of any of various tropical and subtropical leguminous trees, esp of the genus Dalbergia. It has a roselike scent and is used in cabinetwork2. any of the trees yielding this wood

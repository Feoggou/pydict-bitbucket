championship

(ˈtʃæmpɪənˌʃɪp )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (sometimes plural) any of various contests held to determine a champion2. the title or status of being a champion3. support for or defence of a cause, person, etc

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA championship is a competition to find the best player or team in a particular sport. ⇒ ...the world chess championship.2. singular nounThe championship refers to the title or status of being a sports champion. ⇒ He went on to take the championship. ⇒ This season I expect us to retain the championship and win the European Cup.

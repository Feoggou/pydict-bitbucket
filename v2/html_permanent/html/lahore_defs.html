Lahore

(ləˈhɔː )
Definitions
noun1. a city in NE Pakistan: capital of the former province of West Pakistan (1955–70); University of the Punjab (1882). Pop: 6 373 000 (2005 est)2. a variety of large domestic fancy pigeon having a black-and-white plumage

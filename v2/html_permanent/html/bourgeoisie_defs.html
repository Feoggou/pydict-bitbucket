bourgeoisie

(ˌbʊəʒwɑːˈziː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun the bourgeoisie1. the middle classes2. (in Marxist thought) the ruling class of the two basic classes of capitalist society, consisting of capitalists, manufacturers, bankers, and other employers. The bourgeoisie owns the most important of the means of production, through which it exploits the working class

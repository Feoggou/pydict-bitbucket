Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounthe adult female of a horse or zebra

Word Origin

C12: from Old English, of Germanic origin; related to Old High German mariha, Old Norse merr mare

mare2

(ˈmɑːreɪ, -rɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural maria (ˈmɑːrɪə )1. (capital when part of a name) any of a large number of huge dry plains on the surface of the moon, visible as dark markings and once thought to be seas: Mare Imbrium (Sea of Showers)2. a similar area on the surface of Mars, such as Mare Sirenum

Word Origin

from Latin: sea

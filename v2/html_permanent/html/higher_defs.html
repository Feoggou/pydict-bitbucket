higher

(ˈhaɪə )
Definitions
adjective1. the comparative of highnoun (usually capital) (in Scotland)2. a. the advanced level of the Scottish Certificate of Educationb. (as modifier) ⇒ Higher Latin3. a pass in a particular subject at Higher level ⇒ she has four Highers

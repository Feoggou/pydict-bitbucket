prognostic

(prɒɡˈnɒstɪk )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, or serving as a prognosis2. foretelling or predictingnoun3. medicine any symptom or sign used in making a prognosis4. a sign or forecast of some future occurrence

Word Origin

C15: from Old French pronostique, from Latin prognōsticum, from Greek prognōstikon, from progignōskein to know in advance

Similar Words

predicting, indicating, diagnostic, prophetic, predictive, foretelling
sign, warning, forecast, symptom, indication, omen, portent, preindication

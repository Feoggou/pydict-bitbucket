Eumenides

(juːˈmɛnɪˌdiːz )
Definitions
plural nounanother name for the Furies, used by the Greeks as a euphemism
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

from Greek, literally: the benevolent ones, from eumenēs benevolent, from eu- + menos spirit
Example sentences containing 'Eumenides'
At that moment, had there been any way he could have got off Matlock Island, he would have fled from her like Orestes from the Eumenides. Colleen McCullough THE THORN BIRDS

aliquot

(ˈælɪˌkwɒt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. mathematics of, signifying, or relating to an exact divisor of a quantity or number ⇒ 3 is an aliquot part of 12. Compare aliquant2. consisting of equal quantities ⇒ the sample was divided into five aliquot partsnoun3. an aliquot part
Word origin
C16: from Latin: several, a few

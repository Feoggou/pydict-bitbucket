hourglass

(ˈaʊəˌɡlɑːs )
Definitions
noun1. a device consisting of two transparent chambers linked by a narrow channel, containing a quantity of sand that takes a specified time to trickle to one chamber from the other2. (modifier) well-proportioned with a small waist ⇒ an hourglass figure

(aʊəʳglɑːs ) also hour glass for learners of English
Word forms: hourglasses
Definitions
countable nounAn hourglass is a device that was used to measure the passing of an hour. It has two round glass sections linked by a narrow channel, and contains sand which takes an hour to flow from the top section into the lower one.

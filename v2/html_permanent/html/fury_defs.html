fury

(ˈfjʊərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ries1. violent or uncontrolled anger; wild rage2. an outburst of such anger3. uncontrolled violence ⇒ the fury of the storm4. a person, esp a woman, with a violent temper5. See Furies6. See like fury
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Latin furia rage, from furere to be furious

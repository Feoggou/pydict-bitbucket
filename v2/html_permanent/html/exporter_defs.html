(ekspɔːʳtəʳ , ɪkspɔːʳtəʳ) for learners of English
Word forms: exporters
Definitions
countable nounAn exporter is a country, firm, or person that sells and sends goods to another country. [+ of] ⇒ France is the world's second-biggest exporter of agricultural products.

Darwinian

(dɑːˈwɪnɪən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of or relating to Charles Darwin or his theory of evolution by natural selectionnoun2. a person who accepts, supports, or uses this theory
Example sentences containing 'Darwinian'
All his life, so far, Patrick has marvelled at the Darwinian logic of everything. Sean Thomas THE CHEEK PERFORATION DANCEThis change, he was fond of saying, was nothing less than Darwinian.

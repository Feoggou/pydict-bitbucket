Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adverbPer se means 'by itself' or 'in itself', and is used when you are talking about the qualities of one thing considered on its own, rather than in connection with other things. ⇒ The authors' argument is not with the free market per se but with the western society in which it works. ⇒ Global warming per se is not really the problem; the catastrophic climate changes which might be caused by it are.

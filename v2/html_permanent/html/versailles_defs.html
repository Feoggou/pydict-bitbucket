Versailles

(vɛəˈsaɪ , -ˈseɪlz, French vɛrsɑj)
Definitions
noun1. a city in N central France, near Paris: site of an elaborate royal residence built for Louis XIV; seat of the French kings (1682–1789). Pop: 86 477 (2009)2. See Treaty of Versailles

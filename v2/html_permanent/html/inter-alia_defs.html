Definitions
phraseYou use inter alia, meaning 'among other things', when you want to say that there are other things involved apart from the one you are mentioning. [formal] ⇒ ...a collector who had, inter alia, 900 engraved gems, 59 marble busts, and over 2,500 coins and medals. →  inter alia

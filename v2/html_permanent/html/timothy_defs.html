Timothy

(ˈtɪməθɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun New Testament1.  Saint. a disciple of Paul, who became leader of the Christian community at Ephesus. Feast day: Jan 26 or 222. either of the two books addressed to him (in full The First and Second Epistles of Paul the Apostle to Timothy), containing advice on pastoral matters

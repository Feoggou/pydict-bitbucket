hutch

(hʌtʃ )
Definitions
noun1. a cage, usually of wood and wire mesh, for small animals2. informal, derogatory a small house3. a cart for carrying ore4. a trough, esp one used for kneading dough or (in mining) for washing oreverb5. (transitive) to store or keep in or as if in a hutch
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14 hucche, from Old French huche, from Medieval Latin hutica, of obscure origin

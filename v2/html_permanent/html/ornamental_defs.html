ornamental

(ˌɔːnəˈmɛntəl )
Definitions
adjective1. of value as an ornament; decorative2. (of a plant) used to decorate houses, gardens, etcnoun3. a plant cultivated for show or decoration
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

ornamentally (ˌornaˈmentally) adverb

ornamental for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ɔːʳnəmentəl )
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Ornamental things have no practical function but are put in a place because they look attractive. ⇒ ...ornamental trees.2. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is ornamental is attractive and decorative. ⇒ ...ornamental plaster mouldings.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

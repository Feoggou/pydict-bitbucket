contretemps

(ˈkɒntrəˌtɑːn , French kɔ̃trətɑ̃)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -temps1. an awkward or difficult situation or mishap2. fencing a feint made with the purpose of producing a counterthrust from one's opponent3. a small disagreement that is rather embarrassing
Word origin
C17: from French, from contre against + temps time, from Latin tempus

Powered by COBUILD
(riːmæri ) for learners of English
Word forms: remarries , remarrying , remarried
Definitions
verbIf someone remarries, they marry again after they have obtained a divorce from their previous husband or wife, or after their previous husband or wife has died. [V] ⇒ Her mother had never remarried. [Also V n]

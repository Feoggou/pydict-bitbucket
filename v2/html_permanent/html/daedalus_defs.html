Daedalus

(ˈdiːdələs )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounGreek mythology an Athenian architect and inventor who built the labyrinth for Minos on Crete and fashioned wings for himself and his son Icarus to flee the island
Derived forms
Daedalian, Daedalean (dɪˈdeɪlɪən ) or Daedalic (dɪˈdælɪk ) adjective

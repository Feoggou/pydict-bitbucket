hiding

Powered by COBUILD
(haɪdɪŋ ) for learners of English
Word forms: hidings
Definitions
1. uncount nounIf someone is in hiding, they have secretly gone somewhere where they cannot be seen or found. ⇒ Gray is thought to be in hiding near the France/Italy border. ⇒ Hundreds of people are said to have gone into hiding to avoid arrest. ⇒ The duchess is expected to come out of hiding to attend the ceremony.2. countable nounIf you give someone a hiding, you punish them by hitting them many times. [informal] 3. →  on a hiding to nothing

cynically

Powered by COBUILD
(sɪnɪkli )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. graded adverb & adverbIf you say that someone is cynically doing something, you mean they are doing it to benefit themselves and they do not care that they are deceiving, harming, or using people. [disapproval] ⇒ He accused the mainstream political parties of cynically exploiting this situation. ⇒ I firmly believe our strategy is being deliberately, cynically manipulated.2. → see also cynical
Example sentences containing 'cynically'
He wants her back so he can play the lead in the parable of the prodigal son,' she complained cynically. Val McDermid DEAD BEAT

godfather

(ˈɡɒdˌfɑːðə )
Definitions
noun1. a male godparent2. the head of a Mafia family or other organized criminal ring3. an originator or leading exponent ⇒ the godfather of South African pop
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

godfather

Powered by COBUILD
(gɒdfɑːðəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: godfathers
Definitions
1. countable nounA godfather is a male godparent.2. countable nounA powerful man who is at the head of a criminal organization is sometimes referred to as a godfather. ⇒ ...the feared godfather of the Mafia.3. countable nounYou can refer to a man who started or developed something such as a style of music as the godfather of that thing. [journalism] [+ of] ⇒ ...the godfather of soul, James Brown.

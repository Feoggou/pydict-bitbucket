Word Frequency

individually

(ˌɪndəˈvɪdʒuəli ; ˌɪndəˈvɪdʒəli ) in American English
Definitions
adverb1.  as an individual or individuals rather than as a group; one at a time; separately; singly2.  as an individual with special characteristics; personally3.  in a way showing individual characteristics; distinctively

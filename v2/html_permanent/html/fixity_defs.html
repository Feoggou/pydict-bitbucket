fixity

(ˈfɪksɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. the state or quality of being fixed; stability2. something that is fixed; a fixture
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

fixity

Powered by COBUILD
(fɪksɪti ) for learners of English
Definitions
uncount nounIf you talk about the fixity of something, you talk about the fact that it does not change or weaken. [written] ⇒ I have been working on exploding the myth of fixity of meaning. ⇒ She believed in the fixity of the class system.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

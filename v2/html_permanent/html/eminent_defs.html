eminent

(ˈɛmɪnənt )
Definitions
adjective1. above others in rank, merit, or reputation; distinguished ⇒ an eminent scientist2. (prenominal) noteworthy, conspicuous, or outstanding ⇒ eminent good sense3. projecting or protruding; prominent
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Latin ēminēre to project, stand out, from minēre to stand

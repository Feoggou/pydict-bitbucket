examiner

(ɪɡˈzæmɪnə )
Definitions
noun1. one who examines or inspects2. a person who sets or marks an examination3. law (in the Republic of Ireland) a person appointed by a court to ensure the survival of an Irish public limited company
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

collegiate

(kəˈliːdʒɪɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. Also: collegial of or relating to a college or college students2. (of a university) composed of various colleges of equal standingnoun3. Canadian short for collegiate institute

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [ADJ n]Collegiate means belonging or relating to a college or to college students. [mainly US] ⇒ The 1933 national collegiate football championship was won by Michigan. ⇒ ...collegiate life.

hypnotic

(hɪpˈnɒtɪk )
Definitions
adjective1. of, relating to, or producing hypnosis or sleep2. (of a person) susceptible to hypnotismnoun3. a drug or agent that induces sleep4. a person susceptible to hypnosis
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hypnotically (hypˈnotically) adverb

Word Origin

C17: from Late Latin hypnōticus, from Greek hupnōtikos, from hupnoun to put to sleep, from hupnos sleep

sausage

(ˈsɒsɪdʒ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. finely minced meat, esp pork or beef, mixed with fat, cereal or bread, and seasonings (sausage meat), and packed into a tube-shaped animal intestine or synthetic casing2. an object shaped like a sausage3. aeronautics informal a captive balloon shaped like a sausage4. See not a sausage

Derived Forms

sausage-ˌlike (ˈsausage-ˌlike) adjective

Word Origin

C15: from Old Norman French saussiche, from Late Latin salsīcia, from Latin salsus salted; see sauce

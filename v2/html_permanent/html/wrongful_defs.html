(rɒŋfʊl , US rɔːŋ- )
Definitions
adjective [usu ADJ n]A wrongful act is one that is illegal, immoral, or unjust. ⇒ He is on hunger strike in protest at what he claims is his wrongful conviction for murder. ⇒ One of her employees sued her for wrongful dismissal.wrongfully adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ The criminal justice system is in need of urgent reform to prevent more people being wrongfully imprisoned.

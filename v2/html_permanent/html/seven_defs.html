seven

(ˈsɛvən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the cardinal number that is the sum of six and one and is a prime number. See also number (sense 1)2. a numeral, 7, VII, etc, representing this number3. the amount or quantity that is one greater than six4. anything representing, represented by, or consisting of seven units, such as a playing card with seven symbols on it5. Also called: seven o'clock seven hours after noon or midnightdeterminer6. a. amounting to seven ⇒ seven swans a-swimmingb. (as pronoun) ⇒ you've eaten seven already ▶ Related prefixes: hepta-, septi-

Word Origin

Old English seofon; related to Gothic sibun, German sieben, Old Norse sjau, Latin septem, Greek hepta, Sanskrit saptá

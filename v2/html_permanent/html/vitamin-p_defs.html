vitamin P

Definitions
nounWord forms: plural P vitaminsany of a group of water-soluble crystalline substances occurring mainly in citrus fruits, blackcurrants, and rosehips: they regulate the permeability of the blood capillaries. Also called: citrin, bioflavonoid

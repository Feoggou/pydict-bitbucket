poignancy

Powered by COBUILD
(pɔɪnjənsi )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount nounPoignancy is the quality that something has when it affects you deeply and makes you feel very sad. ⇒ The film contains moments of almost unbearable poignancy. ⇒ There is a certain poignancy about Eric.

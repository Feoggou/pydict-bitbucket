innovate

(ˈɪnəˌveɪt )
Definitions
verbto invent or begin to apply (methods, ideas, etc)
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

innovative or innovatory (ˈinnoˌvatory) adjectiveinnovator (ˈinnoˌvator) noun

Word Origin

C16: from Latin innovāre to renew, from in-2 + novāre to make new, from novus new

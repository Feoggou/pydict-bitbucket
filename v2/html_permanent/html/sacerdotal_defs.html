sacerdotal

(ˌsæsəˈdəʊtəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveof, relating to, or characteristic of priests

Derived Forms

ˌsacerdotally (ˌsacerˈdotally) adverb

Word Origin

C14: from Latin sacerdōtālis, from sacerdōs priest, from sacer sacred
Example sentences containing 'sacerdotal'
He would immerse himself in the sacerdotal labor of translation. Joyce Carol Oates THE TATTOOED GIRL

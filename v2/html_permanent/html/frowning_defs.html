frowning

(ˈfraʊnɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. drawing the brows together and wrinkling the forehead, esp in worry, anger, or concentration ⇒ a frowning man.2. disapproving or disliking
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

ding1

(dɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. to ring or cause to ring, esp with tedious repetition2. (transitive) another word for din1 (sense 2)noun3. an imitation or representation of the sound of a bell4. Australian informal a party or social event
Word origin
C13: probably of imitative origin, but influenced by din1 + ring2; compare Old Swedish diunga to beat

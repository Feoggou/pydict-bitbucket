harry

(ˈhærɪ ) or herry (ˈhɛrɪ)
Definitions
verb -ries, -rying or -ried1. (transitive) to harass; worry2. to ravage (a town, etc), esp in war
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English hergian; related to here army, Old Norse herja to lay waste, Old High German heriōn

resonant

(ˈrɛzənənt )
Definitions
adjective1. (of sound) resounding or re-echoing2. producing or enhancing resonance, as by sympathetic vibration3. characterized by resonance
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions

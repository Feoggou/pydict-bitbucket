gobble1

(ˈɡɒbəl )
Definitions
verb1. (when tr, often foll by up) to eat or swallow (food) hastily and in large mouthfuls2. (transitive; often foll by up) informal to snatch
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: probably from gob1

gobble2

(ˈɡɒbəl )
Definitions
noun1. the loud rapid gurgling sound made by male turkeysexclamation2. an imitation of this soundverb3. (intransitive) (of a turkey) to make this sound
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: probably of imitative origin

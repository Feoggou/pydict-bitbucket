create

(kriːˈeɪt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb1. (transitive) to cause to come into existence2. (transitive) to invest with a new honour, office, or title; appoint3. (transitive) to be the cause of ⇒ these circumstances created the revolution4. (transitive) to act (a role) in the first production of a play5. (intransitive) to be engaged in creative work6. (intransitive) British slang to make a fuss or uproar
Derived forms
creatable (creˈatable) adjective
Word origin
C14 creat created, from Latin creātus, from creāre to produce, make

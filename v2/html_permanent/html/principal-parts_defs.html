principal parts

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural noun1. grammar the main inflected forms of a verb, from which all other inflections may be deduced. In English they are generally considered to consist of the third person present singular, present participle, past tense, and past participle2. the sides and interior angles of a triangle

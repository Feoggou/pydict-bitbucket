Poseidon

(pɒˈsaɪdən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. Greek mythology the god of the sea and of earthquakes; brother of Zeus, Hades, and Hera. He is generally depicted in art wielding a trident. Roman counterpart: Neptune2. a US submarine-launched ballistic missile

redemption

(rɪˈdɛmpʃən )
Definitions
noun1. the act or process of redeeming2. the state of being redeemed3. Christianitya. deliverance from sin through the incarnation, sufferings, and death of Christb. atonement for guilt4. conversion of paper money into bullion or specie5. a. removal of a financial obligation by paying off a note, bond, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ redemption date
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

redemptional (reˈdemptional), redemptive (reˈdemptive) or redemptory (reˈdemptory) adjectiveredemptively (reˈdemptively) adverb

Word Origin

C14: via Old French from Latin redemptiō a buying back; see redeem

striving

(ˈstraɪvɪŋ)

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
noungreat and tenacious efforts to do something ⇒ this constant striving for perfection, ⇒ Realism is needed, and a constant striving to improve., ⇒ a politician consumed by his own passionate striving for leadership, ⇒ I believe we should value the old, and stop the striving after new things and new experiences.

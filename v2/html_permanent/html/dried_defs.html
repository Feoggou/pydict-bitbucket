Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Dried food or milk has had all the water removed from it so that it will last for a long time. dehydrated ⇒ ...an infusion which may be prepared from the fresh plant or the dried herb. ⇒ Thirty-six trucks were loaded with some 100 tons of dried milk and bandages.2. → see also dry

Kneeling at the corner closest to me, I saw a stain that was the rust colour of dried blood.

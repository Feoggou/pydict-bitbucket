top-heavy

Definitions
adjective1. unstable or unbalanced through being overloaded at the top2. finance (of an enterprise or its capital structure) characterized by or containing too much debt capital in relation to revenue or profit so that too little is left over for dividend distributions; overcapitalized3. (of a business enterprise) having too many executives

top-heavy

Powered by COBUILD
for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveSomething that is top-heavy is larger or heavier at the top than at the bottom, and might therefore fall over. ⇒ ...top-heavy flowers such as sunflowers.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a business or other organization as top-heavy, you mean that it has too many senior managers in relation to the number of junior managers or workers. [disapproval] ⇒ ...top-heavy bureaucratic structures.

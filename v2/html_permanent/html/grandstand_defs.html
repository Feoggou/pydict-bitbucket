grandstand

(ˈɡrænˌstænd , ˈɡrænd-)
Definitions
noun1. a. a terraced block of seats, usually under a roof, commanding the best view at racecourses, football pitches, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ grandstand tickets2. the spectators in a grandstand3. (modifier) as if from a grandstand; unimpeded (esp in the phrase grandstand view)verb4. (intransitive) informal, mainly US and Canadian to behave ostentatiously in an attempt to impress onlookers

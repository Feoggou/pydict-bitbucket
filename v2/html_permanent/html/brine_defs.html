brine

(braɪn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a strong solution of salt and water, used for salting and pickling meats, etc2. the sea or its water3. chemistrya. a concentrated solution of sodium chloride in waterb. any solution of a salt in water ⇒ a potassium chloride brineverb4. (transitive) to soak in or treat with brine
Derived forms
brinish (ˈbrinish) adjective
Word origin
Old English brīne; related to Middle Dutch brīne, Old Slavonic bridŭ bitter, Sanskrit bibhrāya burnt

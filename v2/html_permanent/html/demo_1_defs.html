demo

(ˈdɛməʊ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -os informal1. short for demonstration (sense 4)2. a. a demonstration record or tape, used for audition purposesb. a demonstration of a prototype system3. US short for demonstrator (sense 3)verb4. short for demonstrate (sense 4)5. to demonstrate a new or updated application, operating system, console, etc

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA demo is a demonstration by a group of people to show their opposition to something or their support for something. [British, informal] ⇒ ...an anti-racist demo.2. countable noun [oft N n]A demo is a CD or tape with a sample of someone's music recorded on it. [informal] ⇒ He listened to one of my demo tapes. ⇒ Send us a demo with one or two of your best songs.

high life

(haɪ laɪf)
Definitions
nounyou use the high life to refer to an exciting and luxurious way of living that involves a great deal of entertainment, going to parties, and eating good food ⇒ the Hollywood high life, ⇒ Most large cities are good for business and high life, but not for families on holiday., ⇒ The Beatles managed to combine living the high life with making great music.

fortis

(ˈfɔːtɪs ) phonetics
Definitions
adjective1. (of a consonant) articulated with considerable muscular tension of the speech organs or with a great deal of breath pressure or plosionnoun plural -tes (-tiːz)2. a consonant, such as English p or f, pronounced with considerable muscular force or breath pressure▷ Compare lenis

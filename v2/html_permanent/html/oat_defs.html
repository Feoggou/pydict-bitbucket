oat

(əʊt )
Definitions
noun1. an erect annual grass, Avena sativa, grown in temperate regions for its edible seed2. (usually plural) the seeds or fruits of this grass3. any of various other grasses of the genus Avena, such as the wild oat4. poetic a flute made from an oat straw5. See feel one's oats6. See get one's oats7. See sow one's oats
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English āte, of obscure origin

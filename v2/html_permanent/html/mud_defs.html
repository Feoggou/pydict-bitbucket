mud

(mʌd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a fine-grained soft wet deposit that occurs on the ground after rain, at the bottom of ponds, lakes, etc2. informal slander or defamation3. See clear as mud4. See drag someone's name in the mud5. See here's mud in your eye6. See someone's name is mud7. See throw mud atverbWord forms: muds, mudding or mudded8. (transitive) to soil or cover with mud

Word Origin

C14: probably from Middle Low German mudde; compare Middle High German mot swamp, mud, Swedish modd slush

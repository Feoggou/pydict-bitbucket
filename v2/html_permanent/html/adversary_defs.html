adversary

(ˈædvəsərɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -saries1. a person or group that is hostile to someone; enemy2. an opposing contestant in a game or sportadjective3. the US term for adversarial (sense 2)
Word origin
C14: from Latin adversārius, from adversus against. See adverse

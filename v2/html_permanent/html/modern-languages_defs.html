modern languages

(ˈmɒdən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒɪz)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounplural of modern language; often used to describe the educational department in a school or university which teaches the languages spoken in present-day Europe, with the exception of English ⇒ head of modern languages at a London grammar school

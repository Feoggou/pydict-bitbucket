cashew

(ˈkæʃuː , kæˈʃuː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a tropical American anacardiaceous evergreen tree, Anacardium occidentale, bearing kidney-shaped nuts that protrude from a fleshy receptacle2. Also called: cashew nut the edible nut of this tree
Word origin
C18: from Portuguese cajú, from Tupi acajú

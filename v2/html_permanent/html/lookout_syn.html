Synonyms of lookout

lookout

noun

  1. watchman, guard, sentry, sentinel, vedette (military)
    • One committed the burglary and the other acted as lookout.
  2. watch, guard, vigil, qui vive
    • He denied that he had failed to keep a proper lookout during the night.
  3. watchtower, post, tower, beacon, observatory, citadel, observation post
    • Troops tried to set up a lookout post inside a refugee camp.
  4. (informal) concern, business, worry, funeral (informal), pigeon (British, informal)
    • It was your lookout if you put your life in danger.
  5. (mainly British) prospect, view, future, outlook, likelihood
    • If this is true, it's a poor lookout for future generations.

sept

(sɛpt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. anthropology a clan or group that believes itself to be descended from a common ancestor2. a branch of a tribe or nation, esp in medieval Ireland or Scotland

Word Origin

C16: perhaps variant of sect

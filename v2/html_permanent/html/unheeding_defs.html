unheeding

(ʌnˈhiːdɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. having noticed or heard something but disregarding it ⇒ He'd bellowed his final entreaties to his unheeding players.adverb2. having noticed or heard something but disregarding it ⇒ They passed by unheeding., ⇒ She spoke to him all the time, unheeding of whether he replied.

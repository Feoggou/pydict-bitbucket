verity

(ˈvɛrɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. the quality or state of being true, real, or correct2. a true principle, statement, idea, etc; a truth or fact
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French vérité, from Latin vēritās, from vērus true

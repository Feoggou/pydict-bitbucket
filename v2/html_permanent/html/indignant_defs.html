indignant

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪndɪgnənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjective [ADJ that]If you are indignant, you are shocked and angry, because you think that something is unjust or unfair. [+ at/about] ⇒ He is indignant at suggestions that they were secret agents. ⇒ MPs were indignant that the government had not consulted them. ⇒ Sheena gave her an indignant look.indignantly graded adverb & adverb [ADV with v] ⇒ 'That is not true,' Erica said indignantly.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. verbTo counterbalance something means to balance or correct it with something that has an equal but opposite effect. [V n] ⇒ Add honey to counterbalance the acidity. [V n] ⇒ His patriarchal generosity is counterbalanced by his ruthlessness.2. countable nounSomething that is a counterbalance to something else counterbalances that thing. [+ to] ⇒ ...organisations set up as a counterbalance to groups allied to the ANC.

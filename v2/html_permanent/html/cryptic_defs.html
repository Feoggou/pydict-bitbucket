cryptic

(ˈkrɪptɪk ) or cryptical
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. hidden; secret; occult2. (esp of comments, sayings, etc) obscure in meaning3. (of the coloration of animals) tending to conceal by disguising or camouflaging the shape
Derived forms
cryptically (ˈcryptically) adverb
Word origin
C17: from Late Latin crypticus, from Greek kruptikos, from kruptos concealed; see crypt

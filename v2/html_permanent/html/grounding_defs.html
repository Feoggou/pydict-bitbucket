Powered by COBUILD
(graʊndɪŋ ) for learners of English
Definitions
singular nounIf you have a grounding in a subject, you know the basic facts or principles of that subject, especially as a result of a particular course of training or instruction. [+ in] ⇒ The degree provides a thorough grounding in both mathematics and statistics.

grounding

Powered by COBUILD
(graʊndɪŋ) or earthing in Electrical Engineering
Definitions
noun(Electrical engineering: Circuits, Electrical power) Grounding is the act of connecting a conductor, or exposed conductive parts of an installation, to the earth. ⇒ The grounding protects personnel from stray currents that could leak to the metallic enclosures. ⇒ A lightning rod and its associated grounding conductors provide protection because they divert the current from nonconducting parts of the structure. ⇒ Grounding is the act of connecting a conductor, or exposed conductive parts of an installation, to the earth. ▶  ground1, ground2

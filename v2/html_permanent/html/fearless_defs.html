Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you say that someone is fearless, you mean that they are not afraid at all, and you admire them for this. [approval] intrepid ⇒ ...his fearless campaigning for racial justice.fearlessly adverb [ADV adj, ADV with v] ⇒ ...an honest and fearlessly outspoken politician.

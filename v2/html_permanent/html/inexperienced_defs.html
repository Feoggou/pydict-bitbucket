inexperienced

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnɪkspɪəriənst ) for learners of English
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are inexperienced, you have little knowledge or experience of a particular situation or activity. ⇒ They are inexperienced when it comes to decorating. ⇒ Routine tasks are often delegated to inexperienced young doctors.

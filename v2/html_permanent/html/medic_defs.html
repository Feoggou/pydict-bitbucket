Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA medic is a doctor or medical student. [informal]2. countable nounA medic is a doctor who works with the armed forces, as part of a medical corps. [US] ⇒ A Navy medic was wounded by sniper fire.

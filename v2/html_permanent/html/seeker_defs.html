Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu pl, usu n N]A seeker is someone who is looking for or trying to get something. ⇒ I am a seeker after truth. ⇒ The beaches draw sun-seekers from all over Europe.2. → see also asylum seeker, job seeker

And it was never easy to tell when a seeker had left her path to become a godchild of Ringism who blindly followed another's way.

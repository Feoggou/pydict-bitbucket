beneficial

(ˌbɛnɪˈfɪʃəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (sometimes foll by to) causing a good result; advantageous2. law entitling a person to receive the profits or proceeds of property ⇒ a beneficial interest in land
Derived forms
ˌbeneficially (ˌbeneˈficially) adverb
Word origin
C15: from Late Latin beneficiālis, from Latin beneficium kindness

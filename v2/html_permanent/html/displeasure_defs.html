Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
uncount noun [oft poss N]Someone's displeasure is a feeling of annoyance that they have about something that has happened. [+ with/at] ⇒ The population has already begun to show its displeasure at the slow pace of change.
Example sentences containing 'displeasure'
Clients dislike making decisions in a hurry, and they had left Roos in no doubt of the displeasure he had caused. St. James, Ian FINAL RESORTWhen I looked up I `m sure my face revealed my displeasure.

showing

(ˈʃəʊɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a presentation, exhibition, or display2. manner of presentation; performance3. evidence

The upside of this was that she answered all Bowler's questions without showing the slightest curiosity about his reasons for asking them.

Friday

(ˈfraɪdɪ , -deɪ)
Definitions
noun1. the sixth day of the week; fifth day of the working week2. See girl Friday, man Friday
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English Frīgedæg, literally: Freya's day; related to Old Frisian frīadei, Old High German frīatag

Definitions
variable nounFriday is the day after Thursday and before Saturday. ⇒ Mr Cook is intending to go to the Middle East on Friday. ⇒ The weekly series starts next Friday. ⇒ I get home at half seven on Fridays. ⇒ He left Heathrow airport on Friday morning.

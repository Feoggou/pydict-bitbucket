well-meant

(ˌwɛlˈmɛnt)
Definitions
adjective(well meant when postpositive) arising from good or benevolent intentions, usually with unfortunate results ⇒ Any decision taken by them now, however well meant, could complicate the peace process., ⇒ a well-meant experiment gone wrong
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

folding

(ˈfəʊldɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. able to be folded ⇒ a folding chair/table/bednoun2. geology bends in stratified rocks that result from movements within the earth's crust and produce such structures as anticlines and synclines ⇒ The original strata and folding of the rocks can still be discerned.

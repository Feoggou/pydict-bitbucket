inflammatory

(ɪnˈflæmətərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
adjective1. characterized by or caused by inflammation2. tending to arouse violence, strong emotion, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

inflammatorily (inˈflammatorily) adverb

inflammatory

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnflæmətəri , US -tɔːri ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you accuse someone of saying or doing inflammatory things, you mean that what they say or do is likely to make people react very angrily. [disapproval] ⇒ ...nationalist policies that are too drastic and inflammatory. ⇒ She described his remarks as irresponsible, inflammatory and outrageous.2. adjective [ADJ n]An inflammatory condition or disease is one in which the patient suffers from inflammation. [formal] ⇒ ...the inflammatory reactions that occur in asthma.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

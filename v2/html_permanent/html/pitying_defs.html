(pɪtiɪŋ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjective [usu ADJ n]A pitying look shows that someone feels pity and perhaps slight contempt. ⇒ She gave him a pitying look; that was the sort of excuse her father would use.pityingly adverb [ADV after v] ⇒ Stasik looked at him pityingly and said nothing.
Example sentences containing 'pitying'
Carol concentrated on keeping the look of pitying horror on her face. Val McDermid THE LAST TEMPTATIONBut this would only invite a pitying smile, and a "She'll learn "comment.

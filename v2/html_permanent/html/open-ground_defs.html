open ground

(ˈəʊpən ɡraʊnd)
Definitions
noununcovered or unobstructed ground in a wide open space ⇒ the attacker advancing over open ground., ⇒ all plants were grown in the open ground
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

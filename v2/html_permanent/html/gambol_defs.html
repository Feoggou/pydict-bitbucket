gambol

(ˈɡæmbəl )
Definitions
verb -bols, -bolling, -bolled or US -bols, -boling or -boled1. (intransitive) to skip or jump about in a playful manner; frolicnoun2. a playful antic; frolic
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16: from French gambade; see gambado2, jamb

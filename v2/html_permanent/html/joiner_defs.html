joiner

(ˈdʒɔɪnə )
Definitions
noun1. mainly British a person trained and skilled in making finished woodwork, such as windows, doors, and stairs2. a person or thing that joins3. informal a person who joins many clubs, causes, etc
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

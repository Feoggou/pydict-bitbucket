hypertrophy

(haɪˈpɜːtrəfɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -phies1. enlargement of an organ or part resulting from an increase in the size of the cells. Compare atrophy, hyperplasiaverb -phies, -phying or -phied2. to undergo or cause to undergo this condition

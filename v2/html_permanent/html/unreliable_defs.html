unreliable for learners of English

Powered by COBUILD
(ʌnrɪlaɪəbəl )
Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveIf you describe a person, machine, or method as unreliable, you mean that you cannot trust them. ⇒ Diplomats can be a notoriously unreliable and misleading source of information. ⇒ His judgement was unreliable. ⇒ He had an unreliable car.unreliability (ʌnrɪlaɪəbɪlɪti ) uncount noun ⇒ ...his lateness and unreliability.

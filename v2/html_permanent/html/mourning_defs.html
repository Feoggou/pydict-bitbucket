mourning

(ˈmɔːnɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the act or feelings of one who mourns; grief2. the conventional symbols of grief, such as the wearing of black3. the period of time during which a death is officially mourned4. See in mourningadjective5. of or relating to mourning

Derived Forms

mourningly (ˈmourningly) adverb

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounMourning is behaviour in which you show sadness about a person's death. ⇒ The period of mourning and bereavement may be long. ⇒ Expect to feel angry, depressed and confused. It's all part of the mourning process. ⇒ Human rights groups declared what they called a day of mourning and protest.2. →  in mourning

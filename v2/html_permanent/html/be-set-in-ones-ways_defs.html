Powered by COBUILD
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
phrase [v-link PHR]If you say that someone is set in their ways, you mean that they have been behaving in the same way for many years and do not want to change. ⇒ He was too set in his ways to make any real changes. →  way

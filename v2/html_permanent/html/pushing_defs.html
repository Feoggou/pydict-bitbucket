pushing

(ˈpʊʃɪŋ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. enterprising, resourceful, or aggressively ambitious2. impertinently self-assertiveadverb3. almost or nearly (a certain age, speed, etc) ⇒ pushing fifty

Derived Forms

pushingly (ˈpushingly) adverbpushingness (ˈpushingness) noun

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
prepositionIf you say that someone is pushing a particular age, you mean that they are nearly that age. [informal] ⇒ Pushing 40, he was an ageing rock star.

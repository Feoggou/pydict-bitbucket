estranged

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪstreɪndʒd ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]An estranged wife or husband is no longer living with their husband or wife. [formal] ⇒ ...his estranged wife.2. graded adjective & adjectiveIf you are estranged from your family or friends, you have quarrelled with them and are not communicating with them. [formal] ⇒ He was laid to rest yesterday without a final goodbye from his estranged son. [+ from] ⇒ Joanna, 30, spent most of her twenties virtually estranged from her father.3. graded adjective & adjective [v-link ADJ]If you describe someone as estranged from something such as society or their profession, you mean that they no longer seem involved in it. [formal] [+ from] ⇒ Arran became increasingly estranged from the mainstream of Hollywood.

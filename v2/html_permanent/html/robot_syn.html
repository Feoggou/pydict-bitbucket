robot

noun

  1. machine, automaton, android, bot (informal), mechanical man

QUOTATION:
The three fundamental Rules of Robotics... One, a robot may not injure a human being, or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm... Two... a robot must obey the orders given it by human beings except where such orders would conflict with the First law... Three, a robot must protect its own existence as long as such protection does not conflict with the First or Second LawsIsaac Asimov – I, Robot

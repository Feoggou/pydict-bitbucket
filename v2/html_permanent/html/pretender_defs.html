pretender

(prɪˈtɛndə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person who pretends or makes false allegations2. a person who mounts a claim, as to a throne or title

pretender

(prɪtendəʳ )

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable noun [adj N]A pretender to a position is someone who claims the right to that position, and whose claim is disputed by others. [+ to] ⇒ ...the Comte de Paris, pretender to the French throne.

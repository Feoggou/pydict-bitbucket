petal

(ˈpɛtəl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany of the separate parts of the corolla of a flower: often brightly coloured

Derived Forms

petaline (ˈpetaline) adjectivepetal-ˌlike (ˈpetal-ˌlike) adjectivepetalled (ˈpetalled) adjective

Word Origin

C18: from New Latin petalum, from Greek petalon leaf; related to petannunai to lie open

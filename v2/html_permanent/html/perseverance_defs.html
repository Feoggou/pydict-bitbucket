perseverance

(ˌpɜːsɪˈvɪərəns )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. continued steady belief or efforts, withstanding discouragement or difficulty; persistence2. Christianity persistence in remaining in a state of grace until death

Derived Forms

ˌperseverant (ˌperseˈverant) adjective

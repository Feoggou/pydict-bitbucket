astern

(əˈstɜːn )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adverb, adjective (postpositive) nautical1. at or towards the stern2. with the stern first ⇒ full speed astern!3. aft of the stern of a vessel

astern

Powered by COBUILD
(əstɜːʳn )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adverb [be ADV]Something that is astern is at the back of a ship or behind the back part. [technical] ⇒ The captain was astern, pretending he was sleeping.2. adverb [ADV after v, from ADV]A ship that is moving astern is moving backwards. [technical] ⇒ At the crucial moment, the barge would not go astern.

revolving

(rɪˈvɒlvɪŋ )
Definitions
adjective1. moving around a central axis ⇒ revolving door2. (of a fund) constantly added to from income from its investments to offset outgoing payments3. (of a letter of credit, load, etc) available to be repeatedly drawn on by the beneficiary provided that a specified amount is never exceeded
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

revolvingly (reˈvolvingly) adverb

transmitting

(trænzˈmɪtɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. telecommunications passing or broadcasting signals or programmes (esp by using wires, radio waves, or satellites) ⇒ the transmitting stationnoun2. another word for transmissionpresent participle of verb3. See transmit

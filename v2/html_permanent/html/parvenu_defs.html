parvenu

(ˈpɑːvəˌnjuː )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person, esp a man, who, having risen socially or economically, is considered to be an upstart or to lack the appropriate refinement for his or her new positionadjective2. of or characteristic of a parvenu

Word Origin

C19: from French, from parvenir to attain, from Latin pervenīre, from per through + venīre to come

racking

(ˈrækɪŋ)
Definitions
adjective1. causing or indicating mental suffering ⇒ She was now shaking with long, racking sobs.2. causing or indicating physical suffering ⇒ His racking cough was gone.

Definitions
1. adjective [ADJ n]A racking pain or emotion is a distressing one which you feel very strongly. ⇒ She was now shaking with long, racking sobs.2. → See also nerve-racking

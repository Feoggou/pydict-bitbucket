frond

(frɒnd )
Definitions
noun1. a large compound leaf, esp of a fern2. the thallus of a seaweed or a lichen
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

fronded (ˈfronded) adjectivefrondless (ˈfrondless) adjective

Word Origin

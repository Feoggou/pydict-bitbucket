holly

(ˈhɒlɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -lies1. any tree or shrub of the genus Ilex, such as the Eurasian I. aquifolium, having bright red berries and shiny evergreen leaves with prickly edges2. branches of any of these trees, used for Christmas decorations3. See holly oak▷ See also sea holly
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

Old English holegn; related to Old Norse hulfr, Old High German hulis, German Hulst, Old Slavonic kolja prick

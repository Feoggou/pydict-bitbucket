negotiation

(nɪˌɡəʊʃɪˈeɪʃən )
Definitions
noun1. a discussion set up or intended to produce a settlement or agreement2. the act or process of negotiating

negotiation

Powered by COBUILD
(nɪgoʊʃieɪʃən ) for learners of English
Word forms: negotiations
Definitions
variable nounNegotiations are formal discussions between people who have different aims or intentions, especially in business or politics, during which they try to reach an agreement. ⇒ We have had meaningful negotiations and I believe we are very close to a deal. ⇒ After 10 years of negotiation, the Senate ratified the strategic arms reduction treaty.

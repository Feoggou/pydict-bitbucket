Synonyms of monster

monster

noun

  1. giant, mammoth, titan, colossus, monstrosity, leviathan, behemoth, Brobdingnagian
    • He said he'd hooked a real monster of a fish.
  2. brute, devil, savage, beast, demon, villain, barbarian, fiend, ogre, ghoul, bogeyman
    • You make me sound like an absolute monster!
  3. rascal, rogue, horror (informal), devil, monkey (informal), imp, tyke (informal), scallywag (informal), mischief-maker, scamp (informal), nointer (Australian, slang)
    • I don't think I could be as patient as they are with that little monster!
  4. freak, mutant, monstrosity, lusus naturae, miscreation, teratism
    • She keeps me hidden like some hideous monster she's ashamed of.

modifier

  1. huge, giant, massive, enormous, tremendous, immense, mega (slang), titanic, jumbo (informal), gigantic, monstrous, mammoth, colossal, stellar (informal), stupendous, gargantuan, elephantine, ginormous (informal), Brobdingnagian, humongous or humungous (US, slang)
    • The film will be a monster hit.

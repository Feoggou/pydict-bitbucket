thanksgiving

(ˈθæŋksˌɡɪvɪŋ , US English θæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ)
Definitions
noun1. the act of giving thanks2. a. an expression of thanks to Godb. a public act of religious observance or a celebration in acknowledgment of divine favours

Definitions
variable nounIn the United States, Thanksgiving or Thanksgiving Day is a public holiday on the fourth Thursday in November. It was originally a day when people celebrated the end of the harvest and thanked God for it. ⇒ No matter where his business took him, he always managed to be home for Thanksgiving. ⇒ It's going to be the most magnificent Thanksgiving dinner we ever had.

peninsula

(pɪˈnɪnsjʊlə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna narrow strip of land projecting into a sea or lake from the mainland▶ USAGE The noun peninsula is sometimes confused with the adjective peninsular: the Iberian peninsula (not peninsular)

Derived Forms

peninsular (penˈinsular) adjective

Word Origin

C16: from Latin, literally: almost an island, from paene pene- + insula island

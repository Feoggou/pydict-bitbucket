refectory

(rɪˈfɛktərɪ , -trɪ)
Definitions
noun plural -toriesa communal dining hall in a religious, academic, or other institution
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C15: from Late Latin refectōrium, from Latin refectus refreshed

reliant

Powered by COBUILD
(rɪlaɪənt ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. graded adjective & adjectiveA person or thing that is reliant on something needs it and often cannot live or work without it. [+ on/upon] ⇒ These people are not wholly reliant on Western charity. ⇒ Lithuania is heavily reliant on Moscow for almost all its oil.2. → See also self-reliant

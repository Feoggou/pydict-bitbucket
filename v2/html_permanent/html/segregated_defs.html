segregated

(ˈsɛɡrɪɡeɪtɪd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. intended for the use of individuals of a particular race, sex, or religion ⇒ It cut off federal funds to segregated institutions and programs., ⇒ Segregated education was developed for the bright children of Party members., ⇒ Studies show that blacks who attend desegregated schools achieve higher test scores than blacks who attend segregated schools., ⇒ racially segregated schools2. following a system in which specific groups are confined to specific areas or institutions ⇒ We grew up in a segregated society., ⇒ John grew up in Baltimore when that city was segregated., ⇒ She refused to move to the back of a segregated bus., ⇒ He had prohibited the use of segregated seating in his court.

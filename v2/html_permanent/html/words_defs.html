words

(wɜːdz )
Definitions
plural noun1. the text of a part of an actor, etc2. the text or lyrics of a song, as opposed to the music3. angry speech (esp in the phrase have words with someone)4. See eat one's words5. See for words6. See have no words for7. See in other words8. See in so many words9. See of few words10. See of many words11. See put into words12. See say a few words13. See take the words out of someone's mouth14. See words fail me

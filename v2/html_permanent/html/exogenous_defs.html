exogenous

(ɛkˈsɒdʒɪnəs )
Definitions
adjective1. having an external origin2. biologya. developing or originating outside an organism or part of an organismb. of or relating to external factors, such as light, that influence an organism3. psychiatry (of a mental illness) caused by external factors

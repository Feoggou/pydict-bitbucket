Moldavia

(mɒlˈdeɪvɪə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. another name for Moldova2. a former principality of E Europe, consisting of the basins of the Rivers Prut and Dniester: the E part (Bessarabia) became Moldova; the W part remains a province of Romania

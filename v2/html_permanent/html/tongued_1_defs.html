tongued

(tʌŋd )
Definitions
adjective1. a. having a tongue or tonguesb. (in combination) ⇒ long-tongued2. (in combination) having a manner of speech as specified ⇒ sharp-tongued
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Now, vexation at having missed the festivities made her sharp tongued. Zindell, David THE BROKEN GODMuch to her obvious delight, I'd tongued away the last vestiges of the toast before I dared answer. James Birrell THE MANANA MAN

teaching

(ˈtiːtʃɪŋ )
Definitions
noun1. the art or profession of a teacher2. (sometimes plural) something taught; precept3. (modifier) denoting a person or institution that teaches ⇒ a teaching hospital4. (modifier) used in teaching ⇒ teaching materials
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

teaching

Powered by COBUILD
(tiːtʃɪŋ ) for learners of English
Word forms: teachings
Definitions
1. uncount nounTeaching is the work that a teacher does in helping students to learn. ⇒ The Government funds university teaching. [+ of] ⇒ ...the teaching of English in schools.2. countable noun [usu pl, with poss]The teachings of a particular person, group of people, or religion are all the ideas and principles that they teach. ⇒ ...the teachings of Jesus. [+ on] ⇒ ...their teachings on sexuality and marriage.

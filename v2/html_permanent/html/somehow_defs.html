(sʌmhaʊ )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj]You use somehow to say that you do not know or cannot say how something was done or will be done. ⇒ We'll manage somehow, you and me. I know we will. ⇒ Channel 4 arrived and somehow created a different role for television. ⇒ Somehow Karin managed to cope with the demands of her career. ⇒ Somehow I knew he would tell me the truth. ⇒ He's been very quiet and withdrawn, sort of different, somehow. ⇒ This city is somehow different.2. → somehow or other

seldom

adverb

  1. rarely, occasionally, not often, once in a while, infrequently, sporadically, once in a blue moon (informal), hardly ever, only now and then, scarcely ever • They seldom speak. OPPOSITES:  often, frequently, much, again and again, over and over again, time and again, time after time, many a time

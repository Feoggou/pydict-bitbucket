cutter

(ˈkʌtə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a person or thing that cuts, esp a person who cuts cloth for clothing2. a sailing boat with its mast stepped further aft so as to have a larger foretriangle than that of a sloop3. a ship's boat, powered by oars or sail, for carrying passengers or light cargo4. a small lightly armed boat, as used in the enforcement of customs regulations5. a pig weighing between 68 and 82 kg, from which fillets and larger joints are cut

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu n N]A cutter is a tool that you use for cutting through something. ⇒ ...a pastry cutter. ⇒ ...wire cutters.2. countable nounA cutter is a person who cuts or reduces something. ⇒ ...a glass cutter. ⇒ He has been using every opportunity to boost his credibility as a budget cutter.3. countable nounA cutter is a type of boat.

graded adjective & adjectiveIf something is dangerous, it is able or likely to hurt or harm you. ⇒ It's a dangerous stretch of road. ⇒ ...dangerous drugs. ⇒ It's dangerous to jump to early conclusions.dangerously graded adverb & adverb [oft ADV after v] ⇒ He is dangerously ill. ⇒ He rushed downstairs dangerously fast. ⇒ The coach rocked dangerously.

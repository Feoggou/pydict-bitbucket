Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. uncount nounCooking is food which has been cooked. ⇒ The menu is based on classic French cooking. ⇒ ...Mom's home cooking.2. adjective [ADJ n]Cooking ingredients or equipment are used in cookery. ⇒ Finely slice the cooking apples. ⇒ ...cooking chocolate. ⇒ ...cooking pots.3. → see also cook

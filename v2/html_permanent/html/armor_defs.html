armor

(ˈɑrmər )
Definitions
Webster’s New World College Dictionary
noun1.  covering worn to protect the body against weapons2.  any defensive or protective covering, as on animals or plants, or the metal plating on warships, warplanes, etc.3.  the armored forces and vehicles of an army; tanks, reconnaissance cars, etc.4.  a quality or condition serving as a defense difficult to penetrate verb transitive, verb intransitive5.  to put armor or armor plate on

Word origin

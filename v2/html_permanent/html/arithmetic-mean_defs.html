arithmetic mean

Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounan average value of a set of integers, terms, or quantities, expressed as their sum divided by their number ⇒ the arithmetic mean of 3, 4, and 8 is 5. Often shortened to: mean. Also called: average Compare geometric mean

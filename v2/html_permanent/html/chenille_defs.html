chenille

(ʃəˈniːl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a thick soft tufty silk or worsted velvet cord or yarn used in embroidery and for trimmings, etc2. a fabric of such yarn3. a rich and hard-wearing carpet of such fabric
Word origin
C18: from French, literally: hairy caterpillar, from Latin canicula, diminutive of canis dog

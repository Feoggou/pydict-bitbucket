Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. (ˈdɛnɪˌeɪ, ˈdɛnjə) a unit of weight used to measure the fineness of silk and man-made fibres, esp when woven into women's tights, etc. It is equal to 1 gram per 9000 metres2. (dəˈnjeɪ, -ˈnɪə) any of several former European coins of various denominations
Word origin
C15: from Old French: coin, from Latin dēnārius denarius

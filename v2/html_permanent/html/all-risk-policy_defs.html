all-risk policy

Powered by COBUILD
(ɔl rɪsk pɒlɪsi)
Word forms: (plural) all-risk policies
Definitions
COBUILD Key Words for Insurance
noun(Insurance: Commercial insurance) An all-risk policy is an insurance policy that covers all loss or damage however it is caused, apart from any stated exceptions. ⇒ A named-peril policy covers the policyholder only for the risks named in the policy in contrast to an all-risk policy, which covers all causes of loss except those specifically excluded. ⇒ An all-risk policy is different from a peril-specific policy that covers losses from only those perils listed in the policy. ⇒ An all-risk policy is an insurance policy that covers all loss or damage however it is caused, apart from any stated exceptions. ▶  policy

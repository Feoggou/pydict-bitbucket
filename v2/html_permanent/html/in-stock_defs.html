in stock

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪn stɒk) in Retail
Definitions
phrase(Retail: Merchandising) When a physical or online store has an item in stock, it has that item in the store or in a warehouse and available for purchase now. ⇒ All showers and enclosures in the range are held in stock and are available for fast delivery. ⇒ The toy store's website indicated that there were 10 of that particular teddy bear in stock. ⇒ When a physical or online store has an item in stock, it has that item in the store or in a warehouse and available for purchase now. ▶  stockRelated wordsThe opposite of in stock is out of stock.

munificent

(mjuːˈnɪfɪsənt )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. (of a person) very generous; bountiful2. (of a gift) generous; liberal

Derived Forms

munificence (muˈnificence) or munificentness (muˈnificentness) nounmunificently (muˈnificently) adverb

Word Origin

C16: back formation from Latin mūnificentia liberality, from mūnificus, from mūnus gift + facere to make

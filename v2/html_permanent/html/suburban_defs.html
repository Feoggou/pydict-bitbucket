suburban

(səˈbɜːbən )

Definitions

Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. of, relating to, situated in, or inhabiting a suburb or the suburbs2. characteristic of or typifying a suburb or the suburbs3. pejorative narrow or unadventurous in outlooknoun4. another word for suburbanite

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [ADJ n]Suburban means relating to a suburb. ⇒ ...a comfortable suburban home. ⇒ ...a suburban shopping centre in Sydney.2. graded adjectiveIf you describe something as suburban, you mean that it is dull and conventional. ⇒ His clothes are conservative and suburban. ⇒ ...ghastly good taste and suburban gentility.

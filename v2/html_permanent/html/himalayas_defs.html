Himalayas

(ˌhɪməˈleɪəz ; hɪˈmɑljəz ) in American English
Definitions
mountain system of SC Asia, extending along the Indian-Tibetan border and through Pakistan, Nepal, & Bhutan: highest peak, Mt. Everest: also Himalaya Mountains

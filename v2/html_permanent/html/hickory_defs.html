hickory

(ˈhɪkərɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ries1. any juglandaceous tree of the chiefly North American genus Carya, having nuts with edible kernels and hard smooth shells. See also pecan, pignut (sense 1), bitternut (sense 1), shagbark2. the hard tough wood of any of these trees3. the nut of any of these trees4. a switch or cane made of hickory wood
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C17: from earlier pohickery, from Algonquian pawcohiccora food made from ground hickory nuts

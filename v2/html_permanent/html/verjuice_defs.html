verjuice

(ˈvɜːˌdʒuːs )
Definitions
noun1. a. the acid juice of unripe grapes, apples, or crab apples, formerly much used in making sauces, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ verjuice sauce2. rarea. sourness or sharpness of temper, looks, etcb. (as modifier) ⇒ a verjuice old wifeverb3. (transitive) rare to make sour; embitter
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French vert jus green (unripe) juice, from Old French vert green (from Latin viridis) + jus juice (from Latin jūs)

ablaze

adjective

  1. on fire, lighted, burning, flaming, blazing, fiery, ignited, alight, aflame, afire • Shops, houses and vehicles were ablaze.
  2. bright, brilliant, flashing, glowing, sparkling, illuminated, gleaming, radiant, luminous, incandescent, aglow • The chamber was ablaze with light.
  3. furious, angry, raging, fuming, incensed, incandescent, on the warpath, foaming at the mouth, fit to be tied (slang), off the air (Australian, slang) • His dark eyes were ablaze with anger.
  4. passionate, excited, stimulated, fierce, enthusiastic, aroused, animated, frenzied, fervent, impassioned, fervid • He was ablaze with enthusiasm.

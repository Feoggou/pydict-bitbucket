oxide

(ˈɒksaɪd )
Definitions
noun1. any compound of oxygen with another element2. any organic compound in which an oxygen atom is bound to two alkyl or aryl groups; an ether or epoxide
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C18: from French, from ox(ygène) + (ac)ide; see oxygen, acid

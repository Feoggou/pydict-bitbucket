strawberry

(ˈstrɔːbərɪ , -brɪ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun plural -ries1. a. any of various low-growing rosaceous plants of the genus Fragaria, such as F. vesca (wild strawberry) and F. ananassa (garden strawberry), which have white flowers and red edible fruits and spread by runnersb. (as modifier) ⇒ a strawberry patch2. a. the fruit of any of these plants, consisting of a sweet fleshy receptacle bearing small seedlike parts (the true fruits)b. (as modifier) ⇒ strawberry ice cream3. See barren strawberry4. a. a purplish-red colourb. (as adjective) ⇒ strawberry shoes5. another name for strawberry mark

Word Origin

Old English streawberige; perhaps from the strawlike appearance of the runners

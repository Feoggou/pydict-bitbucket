shocked

(ʃɒkt)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. very upset; in a state of shock ⇒ Leaving their victim bruised and severely shocked, the gang drove off in a small, dark coloured car., ⇒ This was a nasty attack and the woman is still very shocked.2. surprised and scandalized ⇒ Don't look so shocked., ⇒ He'll be shocked if you say that.

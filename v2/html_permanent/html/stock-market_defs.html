Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
countable nounThe stock market consists of the general activity of buying stocks and shares, and the people and institutions that organize it. [business] ⇒ He's been studying and playing the stock market since he was 14. ⇒ The company's shares promptly fell by 300 lire on the stock market.

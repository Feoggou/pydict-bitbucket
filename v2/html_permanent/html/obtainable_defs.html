(ɒbteɪnəbəl )
Definitions
adjective [usu v-link ADJ]If something is obtainable, it is possible to get or achieve it. [+ from] ⇒ The dried herb is obtainable from health shops. ⇒ ...delicious cheeses which are obtainable anywhere in France.

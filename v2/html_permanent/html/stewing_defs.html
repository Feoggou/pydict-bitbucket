stewing

(ˈstjuːɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna. the act or process of cooking by long slow simmering ⇒ Casseroling or stewing is an excellent way of cooking meat., ⇒ Meat for stewing tends to be less expensive., ⇒ Damson are excellent for stewing or jam making.b. (as modifier) ⇒ stewing beef, ⇒ I usually buy my stewing meat at Sainsbury's.

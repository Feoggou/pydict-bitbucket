meme

(miːm )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an idea or element of social behaviour passed on through generations in a culture, esp by imitation2. an image or video that is spread widely on the internet, often altered by internet users for humorous effect

Word Origin

C20: possibly from mimic, on the model of gene

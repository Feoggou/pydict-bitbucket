hidden

(ˈhɪdən )
Definitions
verb1. a past participle of hide1adjective2. concealed or obscured ⇒ a hidden cave, ⇒ a hidden meaning
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

hiddenly (ˈhiddenly) adverbhiddenness (ˈhiddenness) noun

hidden

Powered by COBUILD
(hɪdən ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. Hidden is the past participle of hide.2. adjectiveHidden facts, feelings, activities, or problems are not easy to notice or discover. ⇒ Under all the innocent fun, there are hidden dangers, especially for children. ⇒ The precise details of the origins of life remain hidden.3. adjectiveA hidden place is difficult to find. ⇒ As you descend, suddenly you see at last the hidden waterfall.
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

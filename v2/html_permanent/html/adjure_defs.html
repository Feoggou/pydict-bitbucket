adjure

(əˈdʒʊə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
verb (transitive)1. to command, often by exacting an oath; charge2. to appeal earnestly to
Derived forms
adjuration (ˌædʒʊəˈreɪʃən ) nounadˈjuratory adjectiveadˈjurer or adˈjuror noun
Word origin
C14: from Latin adjūrāre to swear to, from ad- to + jūrāre to swear, from jūs oath

Similar Words

beg, appeal to, pray, invoke, implore, beseech, entreat, supplicate
order, charge, direct, command, enjoin

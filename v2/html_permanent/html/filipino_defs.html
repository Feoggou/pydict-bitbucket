Filipino

(ˌfɪlɪˈpiːnəʊ )
Definitions
noun1. plural -nos a native or inhabitant of the Philippines. Also (feminine): Filipina2. another name for Tagalogadjective3. of or relating to the Philippines or their inhabitants
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Filipino

Powered by COBUILD
(fɪlɪpiːnoʊ ) for learners of English
Word forms: Filipinos
Definitions
1. adjectiveFilipino means belonging or relating to the Philippines, or to its people or culture. ⇒ ...a group of Filipino girls.2. countable nounA Filipino is a person who comes from the Philippines.

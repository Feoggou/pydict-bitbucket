Mills

(mɪlz )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1.  Hayley. born 1946, British actress. Her films include Pollyanna (1960) and The Parent Trap (1961)2.  her father, Sir John. 1908–2005, British actor. His films include This Happy Breed (1944), Great Expectations (1946), and Ryan's Daughter (1971)

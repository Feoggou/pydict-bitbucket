aura

(ˈɔːrə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounWord forms: plural auras or aurae (ˈɔːriː )1. a distinctive air or quality considered to be characteristic of a person or thing2. any invisible emanation, such as a scent or odour3. pathology strange sensations, such as noises in the ears or flashes of light, that immediately precede an attack, esp of epilepsy4. (in parapsychology) an invisible emanation produced by and surrounding a person or object: alleged to be discernible by individuals of supernormal sensibility
Word origin
C18: via Latin from Greek: breeze

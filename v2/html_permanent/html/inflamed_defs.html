inflamed

(ɪnˈfleɪmd)
Definitions
adjectiveswollen, red, and painful as a result of disease or injury ⇒ Red, inflamed gums and bleeding when brushing are signs of gum disease., ⇒ He had inflamed joints in his big toes., ⇒ If an acutely inflamed appendix is not removed it may perforate., ⇒ Symptoms include red, itchy and inflamed skin., ⇒ Her eyes were sore and inflamed., ⇒ The gums become inflamed and bleed easily.

inanity

(ɪˈnænɪtɪ )
Definitions
noun plural -ties1. Also: inaneness lack of intelligence or imagination; senselessness; silliness2. a senseless action, remark, etc3. archaic emptiness
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

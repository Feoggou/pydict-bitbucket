brazen

(ˈbreɪzən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. shameless and bold2. made of or resembling brass3. having a ringing metallic sound like that of a brass trumpetverb (transitive)4. (usually foll by out or through) to face and overcome boldly or shamelessly ⇒ the witness brazened out the prosecutor's questions5. to make (oneself, etc) bold or brash
Derived forms
brazenly (ˈbrazenly) adverbbrazenness (ˈbrazenness) noun
Word origin
Old English bræsen, from bræs brass

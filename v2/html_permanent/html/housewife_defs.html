housewife

(ˈhaʊsˌwaɪf )
Definitions
noun plural -wives1. a woman, typically a married woman, who keeps house, usually without having paid employment2. Also called: hussy, huswife (ˈhʌzɪf ), hussif (ˈhʌsɪf) mainly British a small sewing kit issued to soldiers
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

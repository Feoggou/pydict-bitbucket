exposed

(ɪkˈspəʊzd )
Definitions
adjective1. not concealed; displayed for viewing2. without shelter from the elements3. susceptible to attack or criticism; vulnerable4. mountaineering (of a climb, pitch, or move) performed on a high, sheer, and unsheltered rock face
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

makeover

(ˈmeɪkəʊvə)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a series of alterations, including beauty treatments and new clothes, intended to make a noticeable improvement in a person's appearance ⇒ She received a cosmetic makeover at a beauty salon as a birthday gift.2. a series of alterations intended to improve the appearance of a room ⇒ the chance to give your living-room a makeover3. a remodelling of an organization or system in which important changes are made in order to improve it ⇒ The biggest makeover has been in TV drama.

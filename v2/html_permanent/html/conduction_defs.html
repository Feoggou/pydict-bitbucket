conduction

(kənˈdʌkʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. the transfer of energy by a medium without bulk movement of the medium itself ⇒ heat conduction, ⇒ electrical conduction, ⇒ sound conduction. Compare convection (sense 1)2. the transmission of an electrical or chemical impulse along a nerve fibre3. the act of conveying or conducting, as through a pipe4. physics another name for conductivity (sense 1)
Derived forms
conductional (conˈductional) adjective

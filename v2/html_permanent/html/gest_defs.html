gest

or geste (dʒɛst )
Definitions
noun archaic1. a notable deed or exploit2. a tale of adventure or romance, esp in verse. See also chanson de geste
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French, from Latin gesta deeds, from gerere to carry out

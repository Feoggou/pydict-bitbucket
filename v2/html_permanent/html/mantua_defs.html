mantua

(ˈmæntjʊə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna loose gown of the 17th and 18th centuries, worn open in front to show the underskirt

Word Origin

C17: changed from manteau, through the influence of Mantua

Mantua

(ˈmæntjʊə )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna city in N Italy, in E Lombardy, surrounded by lakes: birthplace of Virgil. Pop: 47 790 (2001). Italian name: Mantova

disembodied

(ˌdɪsɪmˈbɒdɪd )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjective1. lacking a body or freed from the body; incorporeal2. lacking in substance, solidity, or any firm relation to reality

disembodied

(dɪsɪmbɒdid )
Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. adjective [usu ADJ n]Disembodied means seeming not to be attached to or to come from anyone. ⇒ A disembodied voice sounded from the back of the cabin.2. adjective [usu ADJ n]Disembodied means separated from or existing without a body. ⇒ ...a disembodied head.

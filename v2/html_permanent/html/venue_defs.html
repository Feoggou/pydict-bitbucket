venue

(ˈvɛnjuː )
Definitions
noun1. lawa. the place in which a cause of action arisesb. the place fixed for the trial of a causec. the locality from which the jurors must be summoned to try a particular cause2. a meeting place3. any place where an organized gathering, such as a rock concert or public meeting, is held4. mainly US a position in an argument
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C14: from Old French, from venir to come, from Latin venīre

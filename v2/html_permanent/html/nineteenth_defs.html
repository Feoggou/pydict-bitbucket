nineteenth

(ˌnaɪnˈtiːnθ )
Definitions
adjective1. (usually prenominal)a. coming after the eighteenth in numbering or counting order, position, time, etc, being the ordinal number of nineteen. Often written: 19thb. (as noun) ⇒ the nineteenth was rainynoun2. a. one of 19 approximately equal parts of somethingb. (as modifier) ⇒ a nineteenth part3. the fraction that is equal to one divided by 19 (1⁄19)

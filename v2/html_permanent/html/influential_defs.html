Definitions
graded adjective & adjectiveSomeone or something that is influential has a lot of influence over people or events. ⇒ It helps to have influential friends. ⇒ ...the influential position of president of the chamber. [+ in] ⇒ He had been influential in shaping economic policy. ⇒ ...one of the most influential books ever written.

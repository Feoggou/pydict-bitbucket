has-been

Definitions
nouninformal a person or thing that is no longer popular, successful, effective, etc

Word forms: has-beens
Definitions
countable nounIf you describe someone as a has-been, you are indicating in an unkind way that they were important or respected in the past, but they are not now. [disapproval] ⇒ ...the so-called experts and various has-beens who foist opinions on us.

inheritor

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnherɪtəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: inheritors
Definitions
1. countable noun [usu pl]The inheritors of something such as a tradition are the people who live or arrive after it has been established and are able to benefit from it. [+ of] ⇒ ...the proud inheritors of the Prussian military tradition.2. countable nounAn inheritor is someone who inherits money or property from someone who has died. [law] ⇒ Two-thirds of inheritors promptly sold the houses they were left.

Ionian

(aɪˈəʊnɪən )
Definitions
noun1. a member of a Hellenic people who settled in Attica in about 1100 bc and later colonized the islands and E coast of the Aegean Seaadjective2. of or relating to this people or their dialect of Ancient Greek; Ionic3. of or relating to Ionia4. music relating to or denoting an authentic mode represented by the ascending natural diatonic scale from C to C and forming the basis of the modern major key. See also Hypo-

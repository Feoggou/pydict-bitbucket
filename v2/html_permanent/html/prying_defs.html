prying

(ˈpraɪɪŋ)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
adjectiveintrusively curious ⇒ prying neighbours, ⇒ She thought she was safe from prying eyes.
Example sentences containing 'prying'
Her hands trembled as she lifted the box from its shelf, and she ripped a fingernail in prying the lid up. Bringle, Mary DEATH OF AN UNKNOWN MANRob helped me get the boxes into the cab on Jonathan's side, which was obscured from prying eyes.

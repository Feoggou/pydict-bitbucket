arrangements

(əˈreɪndʒmənts)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
plural nounpreparatory measures taken or plans made; preparations ⇒ security arrangements, ⇒ travel arrangements, ⇒ I am in charge of the travel arrangements., ⇒ Anne made arrangements to send Mary to New York., ⇒ They made arrangements to go out on Friday night., ⇒ She telephoned Ellen, but made no arrangements to see her., ⇒ I must make all the arrangements for the burial., ⇒ the final arrangements for the meeting, ⇒ I've made all the arrangements for the conference., ⇒ I'll make arrangements for you to be met.

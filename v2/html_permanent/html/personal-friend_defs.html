personal friend

(ˈpɜːsənəl frɛnd)
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nouna person who is a friend, rather than a colleague or acquaintance ⇒ He was a personal friend whom I've known for many years.

Her name is the Countess Ashby de la Zouche, and she is a personal friend of the King.

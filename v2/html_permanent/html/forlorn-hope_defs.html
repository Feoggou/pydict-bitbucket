Word Frequency

forlorn hope

Definitions
noun1. a hopeless or desperate enterprise2. a faint hope3. obsolete a group of soldiers assigned to an extremely dangerous duty
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Word Origin

C16 (in the obsolete sense): changed (by folk etymology) from Dutch verloren hoop lost troop, from verloren, past participle of verliezen to lose + hoop troop (literally: heap)
Example sentences containing 'forlorn hope'
But now that was a forlorn hope , and Danis knew it to be one. Tony Daniel METAPLANETARY

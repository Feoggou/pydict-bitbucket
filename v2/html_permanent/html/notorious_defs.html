notorious

(nəʊˈtɔːrɪəs )
Definitions
adjective1. well-known for some bad or unfavourable quality, deed, etc; infamous2. rare generally known or widely acknowledged
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

notoriety (ˌnəʊtəˈraɪɪtɪ ) or notoriousness (noˈtoriousness) nounnotoriously (noˈtoriously) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Medieval Latin notōrius well-known, from nōtus known, from noscere to know

confetti

(kənˈfɛtɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounsmall pieces of coloured paper thrown on festive occasions, esp at the bride and groom at weddings
Word origin
C19: from Italian, plural of confetto, originally, a bonbon; see comfit

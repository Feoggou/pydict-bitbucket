forcible

(ˈfɔːsəbəl )
Definitions
adjective1. done by, involving, or having force2. convincing or effective ⇒ a forcible argument
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

forcibleness (ˈforcibleness) or forcibility (ˌforciˈbility) nounforcibly (ˈforcibly) adverb

plantation

(plænˈteɪʃən )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. an estate, esp in tropical countries, where cash crops such as rubber, oil palm, etc, are grown on a large scale2. a group of cultivated trees or plants3. (formerly) a colony or group of settlers4. rare the planting of seeds, shoots, etc

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable nounA plantation is a large piece of land, especially in a tropical country, where crops such as rubber, coffee, tea, or sugar are grown. ⇒ ...banana plantations in Costa Rica.2. countable nounA plantation is a large number of trees that have been planted together. [+ of] ⇒ ...a plantation of almond trees.

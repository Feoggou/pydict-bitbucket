freemasonry

Powered by COBUILD
(friːmeɪsənri ) for learners of English
Definitions
1. uncount nounFreemasonry is the organization of the Freemasons and their beliefs and practices. ⇒ He was very active in Freemasonry.2. uncount noun [oft a N]Freemasonry is the friendly feeling that exists between people who are of the same kind or who have the same interests. ⇒ ...the freemasonry of sailors.

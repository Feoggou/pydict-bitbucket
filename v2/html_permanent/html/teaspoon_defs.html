teaspoon

(ˈtiːˌspuːn )
Definitions
noun1. a small spoon used for stirring tea, eating certain desserts, etc2. Also called: teaspoonful (ˈtiːˌspuːnfʊl ) the amount contained in such a spoon3. a unit of capacity used in cooking, medicine, etc, equal to about one fluid dram
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

teaspoon

Powered by COBUILD
(tiːspuːn ) for learners of English
Word forms: teaspoons
Definitions
1. countable nounA teaspoon is a small spoon that you use to put sugar into tea or coffee. ⇒ Drop the dough onto a baking sheet with a teaspoon.2. countable nounYou can refer to an amount of food resting on a teaspoon as a teaspoon of food. [+ of] ⇒ He wants three teaspoons of sugar in his coffee.

inhalation

Powered by COBUILD
(ɪnhəleɪʃən ) for learners of English
Word forms: inhalations
Definitions
1. variable nounInhalation is the process or act of breathing in, taking air and sometimes other substances into your lungs. [formal] ⇒ ...a complete cycle of inhalation and exhalation. ⇒ They were taken to hospital suffering from smoke inhalation. ⇒ Accidental inhalation of the powder can be harmful. ⇒ Take several deep inhalations.2. countable nounAn inhalation is a treatment for colds and other illnesses in which you dissolve substances in hot water and breathe in the vapour. ⇒ Inhalations can soothe and control the cough. ⇒ Inhalations of menthol may do the trick.

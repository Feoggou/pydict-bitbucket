Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
adjectiveSomething that is minimal is very small in quantity, value, or degree. ⇒ The co-operation between the two is minimal. ⇒ One aim of these reforms is effective defence with minimal expenditure.minimally adverb [ADV with v, ADV adj] ⇒ He was paid, but only minimally. ⇒ I was minimally successful.

(ˈdaʊnˌfɔːl )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
noun1. a sudden loss of position, health, or reputation2. a fall of rain, snow, etc, esp a sudden heavy one3. another word for deadfall

Definitions
COBUILD Advanced British English Dictionary
1. countable noun [usu with poss]The downfall of a successful or powerful person or institution is their loss of success or power. ⇒ His lack of experience had led to his downfall. ⇒ ...people wishing to see the downfall of the government.2. countable noun [usu with poss]The thing that was a person's downfall caused them to fail or lose power. ⇒ His honesty had been his downfall. ⇒ Alan's downfall was women.

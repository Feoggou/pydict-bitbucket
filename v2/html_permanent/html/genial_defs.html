genial1

(ˈdʒiːnjəl , -nɪəl)
Definitions
adjective1. cheerful, easy-going, and warm in manner or behaviour2. pleasantly warm, so as to give life, growth, or health ⇒ the genial sunshine
Collins English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

Derived Forms

geniality (ˌdʒiːnɪˈælɪtɪ ) or genialness (ˈgenialness) noungenially (ˈgenially) adverb

Word Origin

C16: from Latin geniālis relating to birth or marriage, from genius tutelary deity; see genius

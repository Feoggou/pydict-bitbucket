reassure

(ˌriːəˈʃʊə )
Definitions
verb (transitive)1. to relieve (someone) of anxieties; restore confidence to2. another term for reinsure

reassure

Powered by COBUILD
(riːəʃʊəʳ ) for learners of English
Word forms: reassures , reassuring , reassured
Definitions
verbIf you reassure someone, you say or do things to make them stop worrying about something. [V n] ⇒ I tried to reassure her, 'Don't worry about it. We won't let it happen again.'. [V n that] ⇒ She just reassured me that everything was fine. [Also V n + about]
COBUILD Advanced English Dictionary. Copyright © Harper Collins Publishers

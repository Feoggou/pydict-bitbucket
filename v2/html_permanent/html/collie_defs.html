collie

(ˈkɒlɪ )
Definitions
Collins English Dictionary
nounany of several silky-coated breeds of dog developed for herding sheep and cattle. See Border collie, rough collie, bearded collie
Word origin
C17: Scottish, probably from earlier colie black with coal dust, from cole coal

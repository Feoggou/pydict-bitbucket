over

preposition

  1. = on, above, on top of, upon
  2. = exceeding, above, in excess of, more than

adverb

  1. =  above, aloft, on high, overhead
  2. =  extra, beyond, in addition, in excess, left over

adjective

  1. =  finished, bygone, closed, completed, concluded, done, done with, ended, gone, past

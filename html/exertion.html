exertion (ɛgˈzɜrʃən ;  ɪgˈzɜrʃən )

Definitions

noun

  1. the act, fact, or process of exerting; active use of strength, power, etc.; exercise
  2. energetic activity; effort
ˈeffort

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= effort, elbow grease (facetious), endeavor, exercise, industry, strain, struggle, toil

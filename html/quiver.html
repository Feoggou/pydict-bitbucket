Definitions of quiver

quiver (ˈkwɪvər )

Definitions

intransitive verb

  1. to shake with a tremulous motion; tremble

noun

  1. the act or condition of quivering; tremor; tremble

Derived Forms

ˈquivery adjective

Word Origin

ME quiveren < OE cwifer-, eager, akin to MDu quiveren < IE base * gwei-, to live, lively: see bio-

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= shake, oscillate, quake, quaver, shiver, shudder, tremble, vibrate
= shake, oscillation, shiver, shudder, tremble, tremor, vibration

quiver (ˈkwɪvər )

Definitions

noun

  1. a case for holding arrows
  2. the arrows in it

Word Origin

ME quyuere < OFr coivre < Gmc * kukur (> OE cocer, quiver, sheath, Ger köcher, quiver), prob. a loanword from the Huns

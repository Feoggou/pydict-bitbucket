gaunt (gɔnt ;  gɑnt )

Definitions

adjective

  1. thin and bony; hollow-eyed and haggard, as from great hunger or age; emaciated
  2. looking grim, forbidding, or desolate

Derived Forms

ˈgauntly adverb
ˈgauntness noun

Word Origin

ME gawnte, earlier gant, slender, thin, gaunt < ?

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= emaciated, angular, anorexic, bony, cadaverous, lean, pinched, scrawny, skeletal, skinny, spare

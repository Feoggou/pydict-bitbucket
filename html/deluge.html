deluge (ˈdɛlˌjudʒ )

Definitions

noun

  1. a great flood
  2. a heavy rainfall
  3. an overwhelming, floodlike rush of anything   ⇒ a deluge of visitors

transitive verb

Word forms:  ˈdelˌuged,  ˈdelˌuging
  1. to flood; inundate
  2. to overwhelm as with a flood
  • See the Deluge

Word Origin

ME < OFr < L diluvium < dis, off, from + luere, var. of lavare, to lave1

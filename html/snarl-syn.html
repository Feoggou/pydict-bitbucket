Synonyms of snarl

snarl1

verb

  1. = growl, show one's teeth
  2. =  snap, bark, lash out, speak angrily, jump down someone's throat, yell at

snarl2

verb

  1. (often with up) =  tangle, entangle, entwine, muddle, ravel

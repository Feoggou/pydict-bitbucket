crash

noun

  1. =  collision, accident, bump, pile-up (informal), prang (informal), smash, wreck
  2. =  smash, bang, boom, clang, clash, clatter, din, racket, thunder
  3. =  collapse, debacle, depression, downfall, failure, ruin

verb

  1. =  collide, bump, bump into, crash-land [an aircraft], drive into, have an accident, hit, plow into, wreck
  2. =  collapse, be ruined, fail, fold, fold up, go belly up (informal), go bust (informal), go to the wall, go under
  3. =  hurtle, fall headlong, give way, lurch, overbalance, plunge, topple

treat

verb

  1. =  handle, act towards, behave towards, consider, deal with, look upon, manage, regard, use
  2. =  attend to, care for, nurse
  3. =  entertain, lay on, provide, regale, stand (informal)

noun

  1. =  entertainment, banquet, celebration, feast, gift, party, refreshment
  2. =  pleasure, delight, enjoyment, fun, joy, satisfaction, surprise, thrill

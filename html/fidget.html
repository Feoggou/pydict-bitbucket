fidget (ˈfɪdʒɪt )

Definitions

noun

  1. the state of being restless, nervous, or uneasy
  2. a fidgety person

intransitive verb

  1. to move about in a restless, nervous, or uneasy way

transitive verb

  1. to make restless or uneasy
  • See the fidgets

Word Origin

< obs. fidge, to fidget < ME fichen < ? or akin to ON fìkja, to fidget

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= move restlessly, fiddle (informal), fret, squirm, twitch

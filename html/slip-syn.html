slip

verb

  1. =  fall, skid
  2. =  slide, glide, skate, slither
  3. =  sneak, conceal, creep, hide, steal
  4. (sometimes with up) = make a mistake, blunder, err, miscalculate

  5. See let slip

noun

  1. =  mistake, blunder, error, failure, fault, lapse, omission, oversight

  2. See give (someone) the slip

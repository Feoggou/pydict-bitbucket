glow

verb

  1. =  shine, brighten, burn, gleam, glimmer, redden, smolder

noun

  1. =  light, burning, gleam, glimmer, luminosity, phosphorescence
  2. =  radiance, brightness, brilliance, effulgence, splendor, vividness

haggle (ˈhægəl )

Definitions

transitive verb

Word forms:  ˈhaggled,  ˈhaggling
  1. to chop or cut crudely; hack; mangle

intransitive verb

  1. to argue about terms, price, etc.; bargain; wrangle

noun

  1. the act of haggling

Derived Forms

ˈhaggler noun

Word Origin

freq. of hag2

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= bargain, barter, beat down

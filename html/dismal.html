dismal (ˈdɪzməl )

Definitions

adjective

  1. causing gloom or misery; depressing
  2. dark and gloomy; bleak; dreary
  3. depressed; miserable

Derived Forms

ˈdismally adverb

Word Origin

ME, orig. n., evil days (of the medieval calendar) < OFr dis mal < ML dies mali, evil days: see deity & , mal-

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= gloomy, bleak, cheerless, dark, depressing, discouraging, dreary, forlorn, somber, wretched

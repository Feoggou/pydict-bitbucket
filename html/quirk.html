quirk (kwɜrk )

Definitions

noun

    1. a sudden twist, turn, or stroke   ⇒ a quirk of fortune
    2. a flourish in writing
  2. an evasion, subterfuge, or quibble
  3. a peculiarity, peculiar trait, or mannerism
  4. (rare) a clever turn of speech; sally; quip
  5. (architecture) a groove running lengthwise in a molding

transitive verb

  1. (architecture) to form with quirks

Derived Forms

ˈquirkily adverb
ˈquirkiness noun
ˈquirky adjective
(ˈquirkier,  ˈquirkiest)

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry

nefarious (nəˈfɛriəs )

Definitions

adjective

  1. very wicked; villainous; iniquitous

Derived Forms

neˈfariously adverb
neˈfariousness noun

Word Origin

L nefarius < nefas, crime, sin < ne-, not + fas, (divine) law, lawful

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= wicked, criminal, depraved, evil, foul, heinous, infernal, villainous

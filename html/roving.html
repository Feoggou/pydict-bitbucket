Definitions of roving

roving (ˈroʊvɪŋ )

Definitions

noun

  1. the strand of twisted and drawn-out fibers of cotton, wool, silk, etc. from which yarns are made
  2. the process of preparing such a strand

Word Origin

< rove2

rove (roʊv )

Definitions

intransitive verb

Word forms:  roved,  ˈroving
  1. to wander about; go from place to place, esp. over an extensive area, with no particular course or destination; roam
  2. to look around (said of the eyes)

transitive verb

  1. to wander over; roam through   ⇒ to rove the woods

noun

  1. the act of roving; a ramble

Word Origin

ME roven, orig. an archery term as vt. < ?

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= wander, drift, ramble, range, roam, stray, traipse (informal)

bland (blænd )

Definitions

adjective

  1. pleasantly smooth in manner; suave
  2. mild and soothing; not sharp, harsh, etc.   ⇒ bland medicine
  3. tasteless, insipid, dull, etc.
soft, suave
  • See bland out

Derived Forms

ˈblandly adverb
ˈblandness noun

Word Origin

L blandus, mild, prob. < IE base * mḷdu-, soft, var. of * mel-: see mill1

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= dull, boring, flat, humdrum, insipid, tasteless, unexciting, uninspiring, vapid

paramount (ˈpærəˌmaʊnt )

Definitions

adjective

  1. ranking higher than any other, as in power or importance; chief; supreme

noun

  1. supreme ruler; overlord
ˈdominant

Derived Forms

ˈparaˌmountcy  (ˈpærəˌmaʊntsi )  noun
ˈparaˌmountly adverb

Word Origin

Anglo-Fr paramont < OFr par (L per), by + amont, à mont (< L ad montem, to the hill), uphill

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= principal, cardinal, chief, first, foremost, main, primary, prime, supreme

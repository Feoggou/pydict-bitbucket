bang

noun

  1. =  explosion, clang, clap, clash, pop, slam, thud, thump
  2. =  blow, bump, cuff, knock, punch, smack, stroke, whack

verb

  1. =  hit, belt (informal), clatter, knock, slam, strike, thump
  2. =  explode, boom, clang, resound, thump, thunder

adverb

  1. =  hard, abruptly, headlong, noisily, suddenly
  2. =  straight, precisely, slap, smack

confide (kənˈfaɪd )

Definitions

intransitive verb

Word forms:  conˈfided,  conˈfiding
  1. to trust (in someone), esp. by sharing secrets or discussing private affairs

transitive verb

  1. to tell or talk about as a secret   ⇒ to confide one's troubles to a friend
  2. to entrust (as a duty, object, or person) to someone
comˈmit

Derived Forms

conˈfider noun

Word Origin

L confidere < com-, intens. + fidere, to trust: see faith

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= tell, admit, confess, disclose, divulge, impart, reveal, whisper
= entrust, commend, commit, consign

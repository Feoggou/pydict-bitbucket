forlorn (fɔrˈlɔrn ;  fərˈlɔrn )

Definitions

adjective

  1. abandoned or deserted
    1. lonely and sad; unhappy and neglected
    2. without hope; desperate
  3. bereft or deprived (of)

Derived Forms

forˈlornly adverb
forˈlornness noun

Word Origin

ME forloren < OE, pp. of forleosan, to lose utterly: see for- & , lose

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= miserable, disconsolate, down in the dumps (informal), helpless, hopeless, pathetic, pitiful, unhappy, woebegone, wretched

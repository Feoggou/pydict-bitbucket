leisure (ˈliʒər ;  ˈlɛʒər )

Definitions

noun

  1. free, unoccupied time during which a person may indulge in rest, recreation, etc.

adjective

  1. free and unoccupied; spare   ⇒ leisure time
  2. having much leisure; not working for a living   ⇒ the leisure class
  3. done or used during one's leisure   ⇒ leisure activities, leisure wear
  • See at leisure
  • See at one's leisure

Derived Forms

ˈleisured adjective

Word Origin

ME leiser < OFr leisir, substantive use of inf., to be permitted < L licere < IE base * leik-, to offer for sale, bargain

Synonyms

= spare time, ease, freedom, free time, liberty, recreation, relaxation, rest

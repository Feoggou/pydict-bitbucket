droop (drup )

Definitions

intransitive verb

  1. to sink down; hang or bend down
  2. to lose vitality or strength; become weakened; languish
  3. to become dejected or dispirited

transitive verb

  1. to let sink or hang down

noun

  1. an act or instance of drooping

Word Origin

ME droupen < ON drūpa: for IE base see drip

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= sag, bend, dangle, drop, fall down, hang, hang down, sink

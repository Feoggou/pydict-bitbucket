aloud (əˈlaʊd )

Definitions

adverb

  1. loudly   ⇒ to cry aloud
  2. in an audible voice; not silently   ⇒ read the letter aloud

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= out loud, audibly, clearly, distinctly, intelligibly, plainly

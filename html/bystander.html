bystander (ˈbaɪˌstændər )

Definitions

noun

  1. a person who stands near but does not participate; mere onlooker

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= onlooker, eyewitness, looker-on, observer, passer-by, spectator, viewer, watcher, witness

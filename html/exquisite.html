exquisite (ˈɛkskwɪzɪt ;  ɛkˈskwɪzɪt )

Definitions

adjective

  1. carefully done or elaborately made   ⇒ an exquisite design
  2. very beautiful or lovely, esp. in a delicate or carefully wrought way   ⇒ exquisite lace
  3. of highest quality; consummate   ⇒ exquisite technique
  4. highly sensitive; keenly discriminating; fastidious   ⇒ an exquisite ear for music
  5. sharply intense; keen   ⇒ exquisite pain

noun

  1. a person who makes a great show of being refined and fastidious in matters of taste, etc.
ˈdelicate

Derived Forms

ˈexquisitely adverb
ˈexquisiteness noun

Word Origin

ME, carefully sought out < L exquisitus, pp. of exquirere, to search out < ex-, out + quaerere, to ask

Synonyms

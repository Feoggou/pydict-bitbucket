menial (ˈminiəl ;  ˈminjəl )

Definitions

adjective

  1. of or fit for servants; lowly; humble   ⇒ a menial task
  2. servile; low; mean

noun

  1. a domestic servant
  2. a servile, low person

Derived Forms

ˈmenially adverb

Word Origin

ME meynal < Anglo-Fr meignal < meiniee, a family retainer, servant < OFr meisniee, household < L mansio: see mansion

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= unskilled, boring, dull, humdrum, low-status, routine
= servant, attendant, dogsbody (informal), drudge, flunky, lackey, skivvy (mainly) (British), underling

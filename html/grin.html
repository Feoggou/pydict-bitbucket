grin (grɪn )

Definitions

intransitive verb

Word forms:  grinned,  ˈgrinning
  1. to smile broadly as in amusement or pleasure, or, sometimes, in embarrassment
  2. to draw back the lips and show the teeth in pain, scorn, etc.

transitive verb

  1. to express by grinning

noun

  1. the act or look of one who grins
  • See grin and bear it

Derived Forms

ˈgrinner noun
ˈgrinningly adverb

Word Origin

ME grennen < OE grennian, to gnash or bare the teeth, akin to OHG grennan, to mutter, Ger greinen, to weep

pour (pɔr )

Definitions

transitive verb

  1. to cause to flow in a continuous stream
  2. to emit, discharge, supply, utter, etc. profusely or steadily

intransitive verb

  1. to flow freely, continuously, or copiously
  2. to rain heavily
  3. to rush in a crowd; swarm
  4. (US) to serve as a hostess at a reception or the like by pouring the tea, coffee, etc. for the guests

noun

  1. an act of pouring
  2. a heavy rain or downpour
  • See pour it on

Derived Forms

ˈpourer noun

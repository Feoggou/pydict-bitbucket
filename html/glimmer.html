glimmer (ˈglɪmər )

Definitions

intransitive verb

  1. to give a faint, flickering light
  2. to appear or be seen faintly or dimly

noun

  1. a faint, flickering light
  2. a faint manifestation or dim perception

Word Origin

ME glimeren, to shine, freq. < base of OE glæm (see gleam), akin to Du glimmeren, Ger glimmern

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry

= trace, flicker, gleam, hint, inkling, suggestion

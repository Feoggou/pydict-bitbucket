wooden (ˈwʊdən )

Definitions

adjective

  1. made of or consisting of wood
  2. stiff, lifeless, expressionless, etc., as if made of wood
  3. dull; insensitive

Derived Forms

ˈwoodenly adverb
ˈwoodenness noun

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= woody, ligneous, timber
= expressionless, deadpan, lifeless, unresponsive

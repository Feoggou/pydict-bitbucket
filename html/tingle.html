tingle (ˈtɪŋgəl )

Definitions

intransitive verb

Word forms:  ˈtingled,  ˈtingling
  1. to have a prickling or stinging feeling, as from cold, a sharp slap, excitement, etc.
  2. to cause this feeling

transitive verb

  1. to cause to have this feeling

noun

  1. this feeling

Derived Forms

ˈtingler noun
ˈtinglingly adverb
ˈtingly adjective
(ˈtingglier,  ˈtinggliest)

Word Origin

ME tynglen, var. of tinklen, to tinkle

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= prickle, have goose pimples, itch, sting, tickle
= quiver, goose pimples, itch, pins and needles (informal), prickling, shiver, thrill

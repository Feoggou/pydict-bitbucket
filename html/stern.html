Definitions of stern

stern (stɜrn )

Definitions

adjective

  1. hard; severe; unyielding; strict   ⇒ stern measures
  2. grim; forbidding   ⇒ a stern face
  3. relentless; inexorable   ⇒ stern reality
  4. unshakable; firm   ⇒ stern determination
seˈvere

Derived Forms

ˈsternly adverb
ˈsternness noun

Word Origin

ME sterne < OE styrne < IE base * ster-, stiff, rigid > stare, , starve

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= severe, austere, forbidding, grim, hard, harsh, inflexible, rigid, serious, strict

stern (stɜrn )

Definitions

noun

  1. the rear end of a ship or boat
  2. the rear end of anything

Word Origin

ME steorne, stern, rudder < ON stjorn, steering < styra, to steer1

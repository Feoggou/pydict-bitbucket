Definitions of roan

roan (roʊn )

Definitions

adjective

  1. of a solid color, as reddish brown, brown, or black, with a thick sprinkling of white hairs (said chiefly of horses)

noun

  1. a roan color
  2. a roan horse or other animal

Word Origin

OFr < OSp roano < L ravidus, grayish < ravus, grayish-yellow, tawny

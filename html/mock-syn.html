mock

verb

  1. = laugh at, deride, jeer, make fun of, poke fun at, ridicule, scoff, scorn, sneer, taunt, tease
  2. =  mimic, ape, caricature, imitate, lampoon, parody, satirize, send up (British, informal)

adjective

  1. =  imitation, artificial, dummy, fake, false, feigned, phony (informal), pretended, sham, spurious

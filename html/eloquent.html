eloquent (ˈɛləkwənt )

Definitions

adjective

  1. having, or characterized by, eloquence; fluent, forceful, and persuasive
  2. vividly expressive   ⇒ an eloquent sigh

Derived Forms

ˈeloquently adverb

Word Origin

ME & OFr < L eloquens, prp. of eloqui, to speak out, utter < e-, out + loqui, to speak

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= silver-tongued, articulate, fluent, forceful, moving, persuasive, stirring, well-expressed
= expressive, meaningful, suggestive, telling, vivid

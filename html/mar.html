Definitions of mar

mar (mɑr )

Definitions

transitive verb

Word forms:  marred,  ˈmarring
  1. to injure or damage so as to make imperfect, less attractive, etc.; spoil; impair; disfigure

noun

  1. (rare) something that mars; an injury or blemish

Word Origin

ME marren < OE mierran, to hinder, spoil, akin to Goth marzjan, to offend < IE base * mer-, to disturb, anger > Sans mŕṣyate, (he) forgets, neglects

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= spoil, blemish, damage, detract from, disfigure, hurt, impair, ruin, scar, stain, taint, tarnish

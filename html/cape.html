Definitions of cape

cape (keɪp )

Definitions

noun

  1. a piece of land projecting into a body of water; promontory; headland (for Cape Cod, Cape Charles, Cape Hatteras, etc) see Cod, Charles3 (sense 2), Hatteras
  • See the Cape

Word Origin

ME & OFr < ML caput, headland < L, head

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= headland, head, peninsula, point, promontory

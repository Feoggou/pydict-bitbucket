Definitions of swat

swat (swɑt )

Definitions

transitive verb

Word forms:  ˈswatted,  ˈswatting
  1. to hit with a quick, sharp blow

noun

  1. a quick, sharp blow, as with a bat or swatter

Word Origin

echoic

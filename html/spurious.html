spurious (ˈspjʊriəs ;  ˈspɜriəs )

Definitions

adjective

  1. (rare) illegitimate; bastard
  2. not true or genuine; false; counterfeit
  3. (botany) like in appearance but unlike in structure or function
  4. (radio) designating or of an unwanted signal transmitted or received at other than the desired frequency
ˌartiˈficial

Derived Forms

ˈspuriously adverb
ˈspuriousness noun

Word Origin

L spurius, illegitimate (in LL, false), orig., a bastard < Etr

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= false, artificial, bogus, fake, phony (informal), pretended, sham, specious, unauthentic

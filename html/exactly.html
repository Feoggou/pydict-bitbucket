exactly (ɛgˈzæktli ;  ɪgˈzæktli )

Definitions

adverb

  1. in an exact manner; accurately; correctly; precisely (also used as an affirmative reply, equivalent to “I agree,” “quite true”)

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= precisely, accurately, correctly, explicitly, faithfully, scrupulously, truthfully, unerringly
= in every respect, absolutely, indeed, precisely, quite, specifically, to the letter

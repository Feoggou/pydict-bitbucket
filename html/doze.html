Definitions of doze

doze (doʊz )

Definitions

intransitive verb

Word forms:  dozed,  ˈdozing
  1. to sleep lightly or fitfully; nap; be half asleep

transitive verb

  1. to spend (time) in dozing (usually with away)

noun

  1. a light sleep; nap
  • See doze off

Derived Forms

ˈdozer noun

Word Origin

prob. < Scand, as in Ice (& Swed dial.) dusa: for IE base, see dizzy

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= nap, kip (British) (slang), nod off (informal), sleep, slumber, snooze (informal)
= nap, catnap, forty winks (informal), kip (British) (slang), shuteye (slang), siesta, snooze (informal)

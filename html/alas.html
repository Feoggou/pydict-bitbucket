Definitions of alas

alas (əˈlæs ;  əˈlɑs )

Definitions

exclamation

  1. used to express sorrow, pity, regret, or worry

Word Origin

ME < OFr a las, helas < a-, he-, ah + las, wretched < L lassus, weary

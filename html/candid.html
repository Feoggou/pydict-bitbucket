candid (ˈkændɪd )

Definitions

adjective

  1. free from prejudice or bias; fair; just; impartial
  2. very honest or frank in what one says or writes
  3. unposed and informal   ⇒ a candid photograph
  4. (archaic)
    1. white
    2. pure
frank

Derived Forms

ˈcandidly adverb

Word Origin

L candidus, white, pure, sincere < candere: see candescent

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= honest, blunt, forthright, frank, open, outspoken, plain, straightforward, truthful

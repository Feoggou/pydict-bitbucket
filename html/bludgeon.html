bludgeon (ˈblʌdʒən )

Definitions

noun

  1. a short club with a thick, heavy, or loaded end

transitive verb, intransitive verb

  1. to strike with or as with a bludgeon
  2. to bully or coerce

Word Origin

? altered < MFr bougeon, dim. of bouge, a club

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= club, cosh (British), cudgel, truncheon
= club, beat up, cosh (British), cudgel, knock down, strike
= bully, bulldoze (informal), coerce, force, railroad (informal), steamroller

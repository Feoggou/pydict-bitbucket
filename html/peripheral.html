peripheral (pəˈrɪfərəl )

Definitions

adjective

  1. of, belonging to, or forming a periphery
    1. lying at the outside or away from the central part; outer; external
    2. (anatomy) of, at, or near the surface of the body; specif., designating or of that part of a nervous system that branches out from a central nervous system see also central (sense 6), central (sense 6a)
  3. only slightly connected with what is essential or important; merely incidental

noun

  1. a piece of equipment that can be used with a computer to increase its functional range or efficiency, as a printer, scanner, disk, etc.

Derived Forms

peˈripherally adverb

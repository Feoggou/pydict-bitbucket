Definitions of waffle

waffle (ˈwɑfəl )

Definitions

intransitive verb

Word forms:  ˈwaffled,  ˈwaffling
  1. to speak or write in a wordy, vague, or indecisive manner

noun

  1. (mainly British) wordy, vague, or indecisive talk or writing

Word Origin

orig., to yelp < echoic waff, to yelp

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= prattle, blather, jabber, prate, rabbit, rabbit on (British) (informal), witter on (informal)
= verbosity, padding, prolixity, verbiage, wordiness

waffle (ˈwɑfəl )

Definitions

(US)

noun

  1. a batter cake like a pancake but crisper, baked in a waffle iron, which gives it a gridlike surface

adjective

  1. having a surface like that of a waffle (also ) ˈwaffled

Word Origin

Du wafel, akin to OHG waba, honeycomb, OE wefan, to weave

stratagem (ˈstrætədʒəm )

Definitions

noun

  1. a trick, scheme, or plan for deceiving an enemy in war
  2. any trick or scheme for achieving some purpose
trick

Word Origin

LME stratageme < L strategema < Gr stratēgēma, device or act of a general < stratēgos, a general < stratos, army (for IE base see strew) + agein, to lead: see , act1

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= trick, device, dodge, maneuver, plan, ploy, ruse, scheme, subterfuge

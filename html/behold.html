behold (biˈhoʊld ;  bɪˈhoʊld )

Definitions

transitive verb

Word forms:  beˈheld,  beˈholding
  1. to hold in view; look at; see; regard

exclamation

  1. look; see
see

Derived Forms

beˈholder noun

Word Origin

ME biholden < OE bihealdan, to hold, keep hold of: see be- & , hold1

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= look at, observe, perceive, regard, survey, view, watch, witness

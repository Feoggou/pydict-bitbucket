Synonyms of rank

rank1

noun

  1. =  status, caste, class, degree, division, grade, level, order, position, sort, type
  2. =  row, column, file, group, line, range, series, tier

verb

  1. =  arrange, align, array, dispose, line up, order, sort

rank2

adjective

  1. =  absolute, arrant, blatant, complete, downright, flagrant, gross, sheer, thorough, total, utter
  2. =  foul, bad, disgusting, funky (slang), noisome, noxious, offensive, rancid, revolting, smelly, stinking
  3. =  abundant, dense, lush, luxuriant, profuse

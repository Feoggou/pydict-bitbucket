roll

verb

  1. =  turn, go round, revolve, rotate, spin, swivel, trundle, twirl, wheel, whirl
  2. =  wind, bind, enfold, envelop, furl, swathe, wrap
  3. =  flow, run, undulate
  4. =  level, even, flatten, press, smooth
  5. =  tumble, lurch, reel, rock, sway, toss

noun

  1. =  turn, cycle, reel, revolution, rotation, spin, twirl, wheel, whirl
  2. =  register, census, index, list, record
  3. = rumble, boom, reverberation, roar, thunder

Definitions of laden

laden (ˈleɪdən )

Definitions

adjective

  1. loaded
  2. burdened; afflicted   ⇒ laden with sorrow

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= loaded, burdened, charged, encumbered, full, weighed down

laden (ˈleɪdən )

Definitions

transitive verb, intransitive verb

  1. (rare) lade

lade (leɪd )

Definitions

transitive verb, intransitive verb

Word forms:  ˈladed,  ˈladed,  ˈladen,  ˈlading
(archaic)
  1. to load
  2. to dip or draw out (water, etc.) with a ladle; bail; ladle

Word Origin

ME laden < OE hladan, akin to Ger laden < IE base * klā-, to set down, lay, place > ladle, OSlav klasti, to load

-laden (ˈleɪdən )

Definitions

  1. filled, covered, permeated, or burdened with   ⇒ flower-laden, metaphor -laden, doom -laden

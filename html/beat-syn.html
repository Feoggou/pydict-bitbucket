beat

verb

  1. =  hit, bang, batter, buffet, knock, pound, strike, thrash
  2. =  flap, flutter
  3. =  throb, palpitate, pound, pulsate, quake, thump, vibrate
  4. =  defeat, conquer, outdo, overcome, overwhelm, surpass, vanquish

noun

  1. =  throb, palpitation, pulsation, pulse
  2. =  route, circuit, course, path, rounds, way
  3. =  rhythm, accent, cadence, meter, stress, time

pretend (priˈtɛnd ;  prɪˈtɛnd )

Definitions

transitive verb

  1. to claim; profess; allege   ⇒ to pretend ignorance of the law
  2. to claim or profess falsely; feign; simulate   ⇒ to pretend anger
  3. to make believe, as in play   ⇒ to pretend to be astronauts

intransitive verb

  1. to lay claim   ⇒ to pretend to a throne
  2. to make believe in play or in an attempt to deceive; feign

adjective

  1. (informal) make-believe   ⇒ pretend jewelry
asˈsume

Word Origin

ME pretenden, to intend < MFr pretendre < L praetendere, to hold forth, allege < prae-, before + tendere, to stretch: see thin

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= feign, affect, allege, assume, fake, falsify, impersonate, profess, sham, simulate
= make believe, act, imagine, make up, suppose

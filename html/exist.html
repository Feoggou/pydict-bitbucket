exist (ɛgˈzɪst ;  ɪgˈzɪst )

Definitions

intransitive verb

  1. to have reality or actual being; be
  2. to occur or be present   ⇒ the qualities that exist in a person
  3. to continue being; live   ⇒ the refugees barely exist

Word Origin

Fr exister < L existere, exsistere, to come forth, stand forth < ex-, out + sistere, to cause to stand, set, place, caus. of stare, stand

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= be, be present, endure, live, occur, survive
= survive, eke out a living, get along, get by, keep one's head above water, stay alive, subsist

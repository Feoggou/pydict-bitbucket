heap (hip )

Definitions

noun

  1. a pile, mass, or mound of things jumbled together
  2. ([often pl.], informal) a great number or amount   ⇒ a heap of toys, heaps of money
  3. (US, slang) an automobile, esp. an old, dilapidated one

transitive verb

  1. to make a heap of; bring together into a pile
  2. to give or supply in large amounts; load   ⇒ to heap gifts upon someone
  3. to fill (a plate, dry measure, etc.) full or to overflowing

intransitive verb

  1. to accumulate or rise in a heap, or pile

Word Origin

ME hepe, a troop, heap < OE heap, a troop, band, multitude, akin to Ger hauf(en), Du hoop < IE * keub- < base * keu-, bend, arch > hop1, , hive

amiss (əˈmɪs )

Definitions

adverb

  1. in a wrong way; astray, wrongly, faultily, improperly, etc.

adjective

  1. wrong, faulty, improper, etc. (used only in the predicate)

Word Origin

ME amis, on-mis: see a-1 & , miss1

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= wrongly, erroneously, improperly, inappropriately, incorrectly, mistakenly, unsuitably
= wrong, awry, faulty, incorrect, mistaken, untoward

musty (ˈmʌsti )

Definitions

adjective

Word forms:  ˈmustier,  ˈmustiest
  1. having a stale, moldy smell or taste, as an unused room, food kept in a damp place, etc.
  2. stale or trite; worn-out; antiquated   ⇒ musty ideas
  3. dull; apathetic

Derived Forms

ˈmustily adverb
ˈmustiness noun

Word Origin

< ? earlier moisty < moist

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= stale, airless, dank, funky (slang), fusty, mildewed, moldy, old, smelly, stuffy

down and out

Definitions

(US)
  1. (boxing) knocked out
  2. lacking enough money, shelter, a job, etc.; destitute or impoverished
See down1

Translations for 'down and out'

  • American English: down-and-out If you describe someone as down-and-out, you mean that they have no job and nowhere to live, and they have no real hope of improving their situation....a short story about a down-and-out advertising copywriter.

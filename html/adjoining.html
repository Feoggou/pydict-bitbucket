Definitions of adjoining

adjoining (ædˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ ;  æˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ ;  əˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ )

Definitions

adjective

  1. touching at some point or along a line; contiguous
adˈjacent

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= connecting, abutting, adjacent, bordering, neighboring, next door, touching

adjoin (əˈdʒɔɪn )

Definitions

transitive verb

  1. to be next to; be contiguous to
  2. to unite or annex (to a person or thing)

intransitive verb

  1. to be next to each other; be in contact

Word Origin

ME ajoinen < OFr ajoindre < L adjungere < ad-, to (see ad-) + jungere: see , join

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= connect, border, join, link, touch

Example Sentences Including 'adjoining'

The keep was a massive cube of carefully cut rocks as was the adjoining great hall.
David Zindell THE LIGHTSTONE: Book One, Part One of the EA Cycle (2002)

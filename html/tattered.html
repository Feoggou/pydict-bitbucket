Definitions of tattered

tattered (ˈtætərd )

Definitions

adjective

  1. in tatters; torn and ragged
  2. wearing torn and ragged clothes

Word Origin

ME tatered

tatter (ˈtætər )

Definitions

noun

  1. a torn and hanging shred or piece, as of a garment
  2. a separate shred or scrap; rag
  3. ([pl.]) torn, ragged clothes

transitive verb

  1. to reduce to tatters; make ragged

intransitive verb

  1. to become ragged

Word Origin

ME, prob. < ON töturr, rags, tatters, akin to Ger zotte, tuft < IE base * dā(i)-, to cut out, divide > tide1

Translations for 'tattered'

  • American English: tattered If something such as clothing or a book is tattered, it is damaged or torn, especially because it has been used a lot over a long period of time.He fled wearing only a sarong and a tattered shirt.

  • Chinese: > 破旧的衣物、书籍等> 破旧舊的

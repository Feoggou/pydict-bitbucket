garb (gɑrb )

Definitions

noun

  1. clothing; manner or style of dress, esp. as characteristic of an occupation, profession, or rank   ⇒ clerical garb
  2. external form or appearance
  3. (obsolete) style; manner

transitive verb

  1. to clothe; dress; attire

Word Origin

OFr garbe, gracefulness < It garbo, elegance < ? Gmc * garwī > OHG gar(a)wen, to prepare, dress, ornament

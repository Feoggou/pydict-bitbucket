lukewarm (ˈlukˌwɔrm )

Definitions

adjective

  1. barely or moderately warm (said of liquids)
  2. not very warm or enthusiastic   ⇒ lukewarm praise

Derived Forms

ˈlukeˌwarmly adverb
ˈlukeˌwarmness noun

Word Origin

ME luke warme < luke, tepid (akin to LowG luk, Du leuk, tepid < IE * leu- < base * el-, warm > OE hleowe, tepid, L calere, to be warm) + warm, warm

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= tepid, warm
= half-hearted, apathetic, cool, indifferent, unenthusiastic, unresponsive

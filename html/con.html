Definitions of con

con (kɑn )

Definitions

(US)

adjective

  1. (slang) confidence   ⇒ a con man

transitive verb

Word forms:  conned,  ˈconning
(slang)
  1. to swindle (a victim) by first gaining the person's confidence
  2. to trick or fool, esp. by glib persuasion

noun

  1. (slang) the act or an instance of conning; swindle; trick

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= swindle, deception, fraud, scam (slang), sting (informal), trick
= swindle, cheat, deceive, defraud, double-cross (informal), dupe, hoodwink, rip off (slang), trick

deliberate

adjective

  1. =  intentional, calculated, conscious, planned, prearranged, premeditated, purposeful, willful
  2. =  unhurried, careful, cautious, circumspect, measured, methodical, ponderous, slow, thoughtful

verb

  1. =  consider, cogitate, consult, debate, discuss, meditate, ponder, reflect, think, weigh

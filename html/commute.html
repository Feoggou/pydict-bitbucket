commute (kəˈmjut )

Definitions

transitive verb

Word forms:  comˈmuted,  comˈmuting
  1. to change (one thing) for or into another; exchange; substitute
  2. to change (an obligation, punishment, etc.) to one that is less severe
  3. to substitute (payment in a lump sum) for payment in installments

intransitive verb

    1. to be a substitute
    2. to make up; compensate
  2. (US) to travel as a commuter

noun

(US)
  1. (informal) the trip of a commuter

Word Origin

ME commuten < L commutare, to change < com-, intens. + mutare, to change: see miss1

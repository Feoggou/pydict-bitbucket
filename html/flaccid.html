flaccid (ˈflæksɪd ;  (often) ˈflæsɪd )

Definitions

adjective

  1. hanging in loose folds or wrinkles; soft and limp; flabby   ⇒ flaccid muscles
  2. lacking force; weak; feeble

Derived Forms

flacˈcidity noun
ˈflaccidly adverb

Word Origin

L flaccidus < flaccus, flabby

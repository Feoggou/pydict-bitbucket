Definitions of ire

ire (aɪr )

Definitions

noun

  1. anger; wrath
ˈanger

Derived Forms

ˈireful adjective
ˈirefully adverb
ˈirefulness noun

Word Origin

OFr < L ira < IE base * eis-, to move quickly, violently > Gr oima, stormy attack, ON eisa, to rush on

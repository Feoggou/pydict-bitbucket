aloof (əˈluf )

Definitions

adverb

  1. at a distance but in view; apart

adjective

  1. at a distance; removed
  2. distant in sympathy, interest, etc.; reserved and cool   ⇒ her manner was aloof

Derived Forms

aˈloofly adverb
aˈloofness noun

Word Origin

a-, a-1 + loof < Du loef, , luff

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= distant, detached, haughty, remote, standoffish, supercilious, unapproachable, unfriendly

laborious (ləˈbɔriəs )

Definitions

adjective

  1. involving or calling for much hard work; difficult
  2. industrious; hardworking
  3. labored
hard

Derived Forms

laˈboriously adverb
laˈboriousness noun

Word Origin

ME < OFr laborios < L laboriosus < labor, labor

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= hard, arduous, backbreaking, exhausting, onerous, strenuous, tiring, tough, wearisome

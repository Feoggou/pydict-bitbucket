Definitions of hip

hip (hɪp )

Definitions

noun

    1. the part of the human body surrounding and including the hip joint; esp., the fleshy part between the waist and the upper thigh; haunch
    2. hip joint
  2. the corresponding part of an animal's body
  3. (architecture) the angle formed by the meeting of two sloping sides of a roof

transitive verb

Word forms:  hipped,  ˈhipping
  1. to make (a roof) with such an angle or angles
  • See on the hip
  • See smite hip and thigh

Word Origin

ME hipe < OE hype, akin to Ger hüfte (OHG huf) < IE * keub-, to bend, as at a joint (< base * keu-, to bend, bend over) > L cubare, to lie, Gr kybos, hollow above the hips of cattle

purvey (pərˈveɪ )

Definitions

transitive verb

  1. to furnish or supply (esp. food or provisions)

Derived Forms

purˈveyor noun

Word Origin

ME pourveien < Anglo-Fr purveier < OFr porveir < L providere: see provide

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= supply, cater, deal in, furnish, provide, sell, trade in

stark (stɑrk )

Definitions

adjective

    1. stiff or rigid, as a corpse
    2. rigorous; harsh; severe   ⇒ stark discipline
  2. sharply outlined or prominent   ⇒ one stark tree
  3. bleak; desolate; barren   ⇒ stark wasteland
    1. emptied; stripped   ⇒ stark shelves
    2. totally naked; bare
  5. grimly blunt; unsoftened, unembellished, etc.   ⇒ stark realism
  6. sheer; utter; downright; unrelieved   ⇒ stark terror
  7. (archaic) strong; powerful

adverb

  1. in a stark manner; esp., utterly; wholly   ⇒ stark mad

Derived Forms

ˈstarkly adverb
ˈstarkness noun

Word Origin

ME starc < OE stearc: see stare

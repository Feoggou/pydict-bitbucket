greenhouse (ˈgrinˌhaʊs )

Definitions

noun

  1. a building made mainly of glass, in which the temperature and humidity can be regulated for the cultivation of delicate or out-of-season plants

adjective

  1. of or contributing to the greenhouse effect   ⇒ greenhouse gases

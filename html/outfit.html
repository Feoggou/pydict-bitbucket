outfit (ˈaʊtˌfɪt )

Definitions

noun

    1. a set of articles for fitting out, or equipping
    2. the equipment used in any craft or activity; paraphernalia   ⇒ a mason's outfit, camping outfit
  2. (US) articles of clothing worn together; ensemble   ⇒ a fall outfit
  3. (US) a group of people associated in some undertaking or activity, as a military unit, business, ranch, etc.
  4. a fitting out; equipping

transitive verb

Word forms:  ˈoutˌfitted,  ˈoutˌfitting
  1. to furnish or equip, as with an outfit; fit out

intransitive verb

  1. to obtain an outfit
ˈfurnish

Synonyms

= costume, clothes, ensemble, garb, get-up (informal), kit, suit

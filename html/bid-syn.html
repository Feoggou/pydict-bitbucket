bid

verb

  1. =  offer, proffer, propose, submit, tender
  2. =  say, call, greet, tell, wish
  3. =  tell, ask, command, direct, instruct, order, require

noun

  1. =  offer, advance, amount, price, proposal, sum, tender
  2. =  attempt, crack (informal), effort, go (informal), stab (informal), try

fury (ˈfjʊri )

Definitions

noun

Word forms:   plural ˈfuries
    1. violent anger; wild rage
    2. a fit of this
  2. violence; vehemence; fierceness   ⇒ the fury of a storm
  3. a violent or vengeful person; esp., such a woman
  4. ([F-]) any of the Furies
  5. (archaic) inspired frenzy
ˈanger
  • See like fury

Word Origin

ME furie < OFr < L furia < furere, to rage, prob. < IE * dhus-, to rage, storm, dust-colored < base * dheu-, to blow: see dull

Synonyms

haughty (ˈhɔti )

Definitions

adjective

Word forms:  ˈhaughtier,  ˈhaughtiest
  1. having or showing great pride in oneself and disdain, contempt, or scorn for others; proud; arrogant; supercilious
  2. (archaic) lofty; noble
proud

Derived Forms

ˈhaughtily adverb
ˈhaughtiness noun

Word Origin

ME haut, high, haughty < OFr, high < altus (with h- after Frank * hoh, high) + -y3: gh prob. inserted by analogy with , naughty

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= proud, arrogant, conceited, contemptuous, disdainful, imperious, scornful, snooty (informal), stuck-up (informal), supercilious

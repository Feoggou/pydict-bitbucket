dingy (ˈdɪndʒi )

Definitions

adjective

Word forms:  ˈdingier,  ˈdingiest
  1. dirty-colored; not bright or clean; grimy
  2. dismal; shabby

Derived Forms

ˈdingily adverb
ˈdinginess noun

Word Origin

orig. dial. var. of dungy

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= dull, dark, dim, drab, dreary, gloomy, murky, obscure, somber

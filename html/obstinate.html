obstinate (ˈɑbstənət )

Definitions

adjective

  1. unreasonably determined to have one's own way; not yielding to reason or plea; stubborn; dogged; mulish
  2. resisting remedy or treatment   ⇒ an obstinate fever
  3. not easily subdued, ended, etc.
ˈstubborn

Derived Forms

ˈobstinately adverb
ˈobstinateness noun

Word Origin

ME < L obstinatus, pp. of obstinare, to resolve on < obstare, to stand against, oppose < ob- (see ob-) + stare, to , stand

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= stubborn, determined, dogged, inflexible, intractable, intransigent, pig-headed, refractory, self-willed, strong-minded, willful

cursory (ˈkɜrsəri )

Definitions

adjective

  1. hastily, often superficially, done; performed rapidly with little attention to detail
ˌsuperˈficial

Derived Forms

ˈcursorily adverb
ˈcursoriness noun

Word Origin

LL cursorius < cursor, runner < cursus: see course

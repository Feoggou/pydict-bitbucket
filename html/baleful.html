baleful (ˈbeɪlfəl )

Definitions

adjective

  1. harmful or threatening harm or evil; ominous; deadly
  2. (archaic) sorrowful; wretched
ˈsinister

Derived Forms

ˈbalefully adverb
ˈbalefulness noun

Word Origin

ME < OE bealoful < bealu, bale2 + -ful, full

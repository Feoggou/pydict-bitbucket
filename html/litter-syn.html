litter

noun

  1. =  rubbish, debris, detritus, garbage (mainly, US), muck, refuse, trash
  2. =  brood, offspring, progeny, young

verb

  1. =  clutter, derange, disarrange, disorder, mess up
  2. =  scatter, strew

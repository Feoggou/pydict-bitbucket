scanty (ˈskænti )

Definitions

adjective

Word forms:  ˈscantier,  ˈscantiest
  1. barely sufficient; not ample; meager
  2. insufficient; not enough
  3. narrow; small; close
ˈmeager

Derived Forms

ˈscantily adverb
ˈscantiness noun

Word Origin

scant + , -y2

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= meager, bare, deficient, inadequate, insufficient, lousy (slang), poor, scant, short, skimpy, sparse, thin

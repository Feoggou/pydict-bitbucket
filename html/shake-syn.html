shake

verb

  1. =  vibrate, bump, jar, jolt, quake, rock, shiver, totter, tremble
  2. =  wave, brandish, flourish
  3. =  upset, distress, disturb, frighten, rattle (informal), shock, unnerve

noun

  1. =  vibration, agitation, convulsion, jerk, jolt, quaking, shiver, shudder, trembling, tremor

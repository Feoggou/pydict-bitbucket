lid (lɪd )

Definitions

noun

  1. a movable cover, hinged or unattached, as for a box, trunk, pot, etc.; top
  2. eyelid
  3. (US, informal) a curb or restraint   ⇒ to put a lid on gambling
  4. (slang) a cap, hat, etc.
  5. (slang) a small package of marijuana, usually about an ounce
  6. (botany, loosely) an operculum

Word Origin

ME < OE hlid (akin to Ger -lid in augenlid, eyelid) < base seen in OE hlidan, to cover < IE base * lei-, to lean1

un- (ʌn )

Definitions

  1. not, lack of, the opposite of   ⇒ unconcern, unreason, unwonted
    see also non-
  2. back; the reverse or removal of (added to verbs to indicate a reversal of the action and to nouns to indicate a removal or release of the thing mentioned or from the condition, place, etc. indicated, and sometimes used merely as an intensive [unbind, unfold; unbonnet, unbosom; unloose])

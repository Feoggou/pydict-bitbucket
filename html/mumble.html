mumble (ˈmʌmbəl )

Definitions

transitive verb, intransitive verb

Word forms:  ˈmumbled,  ˈmumbling
  1. to speak or say indistinctly and in a low voice, as with the mouth partly closed; mutter
  2. (rare) to chew gently and ineffectively, as with toothless gums

noun

  1. a mumbled sound or utterance
ˈmurmur

Derived Forms

ˈmumbler noun
ˈmumblingly adverb

Word Origin

ME momelen, like Ger mummeln, Du mommelen, of echoic orig.

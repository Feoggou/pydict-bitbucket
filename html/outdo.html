outdo (ˌaʊtˈdu )

Definitions

transitive verb

Word forms:  ˌoutˈdid,  ˌoutˈdone,  ˌoutˈdoing
  1. to exceed or surpass
exˈcel
  • See outdo oneself

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= surpass, beat, best, eclipse, exceed, get the better of, outclass, outmaneuver, overcome, top, transcend

Translations for 'outdo'

  • American English: outdo If you outdo someone, you are a lot more successful than they are at a particular activity. outdid, outdoes, outdoing, outdoneIt was important for me to outdo them, to feel better than they were.

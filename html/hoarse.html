hoarse (hɔrs )

Definitions

adjective

Word forms:  ˈhoarser,  ˈhoarsest
  1. harsh and grating in sound; sounding rough and husky
  2. having a rough, husky voice

Derived Forms

ˈhoarsely adverb
ˈhoarseness noun

Word Origin

ME hors, hase < OE has, akin to ON hāss, OS hēs, OHG heisi < IE base * kai-, heat > hot, , heat

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= raucous, croaky, grating, gravelly, gruff, guttural, husky, rasping, rough, throaty

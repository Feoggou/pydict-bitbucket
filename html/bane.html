bane (beɪn )

Definitions

noun

  1. (old-fashioned, poetic) deadly harm; ruin; death
  2. the cause of distress, death, or ruin
  3. deadly poison (now obsolete except in ratsbane, etc.)

Word Origin

ME < OE bana < IE base * bhen-, to strike, wound > Avestan banta-, sick, weak, Goth banja, wound, blow

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= plague, bête noire, curse, nuisance, pest, ruin, scourge, torment

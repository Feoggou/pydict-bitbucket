ghastly (ˈgæstli ;  ˈgɑstli )

Definitions

adjective

Word forms:  ˈghastlier,  ˈghastliest
  1. horrible; frightful
  2. ghostlike; pale; haggard
  3. (informal) very bad or unpleasant

Derived Forms

ˈghastliness noun

Word Origin

ME gastli < gast, frightened, pp. of gasten, to frighten < OE gæstan < gæst, var. of gast (see ghost): meaning infl. in ME by gostlich, , ghostly

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= horrible, dreadful, frightful, gruesome, hideous, horrendous, loathsome, shocking, terrible, terrifying

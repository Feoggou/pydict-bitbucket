concede (kənˈsid )

Definitions

transitive verb

Word forms:  conˈceded,  conˈceding
  1. to admit as true or valid; acknowledge   ⇒ to concede a point in argument
  2. to admit as certain or proper   ⇒ to concede victory to an opponent
  3. to grant as a right or privilege   ⇒ to concede autonomy to local governments

intransitive verb

  1. to make a concession
  2. (US) to acknowledge defeat in an election

Derived Forms

conˈceder noun

Word Origin

L concedere < com-, with + cedere, to go, grant, cede

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= admit, accept, acknowledge, allow, confess, grant, own
= give up, cede, hand over, relinquish, surrender, yield

gaze (geɪz )

Definitions

intransitive verb

Word forms:  gazed,  ˈgazing
  1. to look intently and steadily; stare, as in wonder or expectancy

noun

  1. a steady look

Derived Forms

ˈgazer noun

Word Origin

ME gazen < Scand, as in Norw & Swed dial. gasa, to stare < ON gas, goose

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= stare, gape, look, regard, view, watch, wonder
= stare, fixed look, look

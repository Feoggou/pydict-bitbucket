cripple (ˈkrɪpəl )

Definitions

noun

  1. a person or animal that is lame or otherwise disabled in a way that prevents normal motion of the limbs or body (somewhat offensive when used to refer to a person)
  2. (US, dialect) thicketed, swampy or low, wet land

transitive verb

Word forms:  ˈcrippled,  ˈcrippling
  1. to make cripple of; lame
  2. to make unable or unfit to act, function effectively, etc.; disable
maim

Derived Forms

ˈcrippler noun

Word Origin

ME cripel < OE crypel (akin to Ger krüppel) < base of creopan: see creep

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= disable, hamstring, incapacitate, lame, maim, paralyze, weaken
= damage, destroy, impair, put out of action, put paid to, ruin, spoil

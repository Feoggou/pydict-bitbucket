Synonyms of bound

bound1

verb noun

  1. =  leap, bob, bounce, gambol, hurdle, jump, skip, spring, vault

bound2

adjective

  1. = tied, cased, fastened, fixed, pinioned, secured, tied up
  2. =  certain, destined, doomed, fated, sure
  3. =  obliged, beholden, committed, compelled, constrained, duty-bound, forced, pledged, required

bound3

verb

  1. =  limit, confine, demarcate, encircle, enclose, hem in, restrain, restrict, surround

bind

verb

  1. =  secure, fasten, hitch, lash, stick, strap, tie, wrap
  2. =  oblige, compel, constrain, engage, force, necessitate, require

noun

  1. =  nuisance, bore, drag (informal), pain in the neck (informal)
  2. =  difficulty, dilemma, quandary, spot (informal)

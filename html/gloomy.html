gloomy (ˈglumi )

Definitions

adjective

Word forms:  ˈgloomier,  ˈgloomiest
  1. overspread with or enveloped in darkness or dimness
    1. very sad or dejected; hopeless; melancholy
    2. morose or sullen
  3. causing gloom; dismal; depressing
dark

Derived Forms

ˈgloomily adverb
ˈgloominess noun

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry

= depressing, bad, cheerless, disheartening, dispiriting, dreary, sad, somber
= miserable, crestfallen, dejected, dispirited, downcast, downhearted, glum, melancholy, morose, pessimistic, sad

frantic (ˈfræntɪk )

Definitions

adjective

  1. wild with anger, pain, worry, etc.; frenzied
  2. marked by frenzy; resulting from wild emotion
  3. (archaic) insane

Derived Forms

ˈfrantically adverb
(rare) ˈfranticly

Word Origin

ME frantik, frenetik: see phrenetic

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= furious, at the end of one's tether, berserk, beside oneself, distracted, distraught, wild
= hectic, desperate, fraught (informal), frenetic, frenzied

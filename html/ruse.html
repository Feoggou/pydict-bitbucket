Definitions of ruse

ruse (ruz ;  rus )

Definitions

noun

  1. a stratagem, trick, or artifice
trick

Word Origin

Fr < MFr < OFr reuser, to deceive < L recusare, to be reluctant, refuse: see recusant

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= trick, device, dodge, hoax, maneuver, ploy, stratagem, subterfuge

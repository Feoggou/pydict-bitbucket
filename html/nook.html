nook (nʊk )

Definitions

noun

  1. a corner of a room, or a part of a room cut off from the main part   ⇒ a breakfast nook
  2. a small recess or secluded spot; retreat

Word Origin

ME (chiefly Northern) nok, akin to Norw nakke, a hook, ON hnekkja, to hem in, drive back, OE hnecca, the neck

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= niche, alcove, corner, cubbyhole, hideout, opening, recess, retreat

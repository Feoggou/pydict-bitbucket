ascertain (ˌæsərˈteɪn )

Definitions

transitive verb

  1. (archaic) to make certain or definite
  2. to find out with certainty
learn

Derived Forms

ˌascerˈtainable adjective
ˌascerˈtainment noun

Word Origin

ME acertainen < OFr acertainer < a-, to + certain, certain

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= find out, confirm, determine, discover, establish, learn

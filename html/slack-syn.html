slack

adjective

  1. =  loose, baggy, lax, limp, relaxed
  2. =  negligent, idle, inactive, lax, lazy, neglectful, remiss, slapdash, slipshod
  3. =  slow, dull, inactive, quiet, slow-moving, sluggish

noun

  1. =  room, excess, give (informal), leeway

verb

  1. =  shirk, dodge, idle, skive (British, slang)

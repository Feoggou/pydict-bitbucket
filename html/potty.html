Definitions of potty

potty (ˈpɑti )

Definitions

noun

Word forms:   plural ˈpotties
  1. a small chamber pot for a child
  2. a child's chair used for toilet training, consisting of an open seat beneath which a pot is attached (in full ) potty chair
  3. a toilet (a child's word)

Word Origin

dim. of pot1

potty (ˈpɑti )

Definitions

adjective

Word forms:  ˈpottier,  ˈpottiest
(informal)
  1. (British) trivial; petty
  2. (British) slightly crazy

Derived Forms

ˈpottiness noun

Word Origin

< (sense pot1 (sense 4)) + , -y2

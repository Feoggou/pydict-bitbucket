spiteful (ˈspaɪtfəl )

Definitions

adjective

  1. full of or showing spite; purposefully annoying; malicious
vinˈdictive

Derived Forms

ˈspitefully adverb
ˈspitefulness noun

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= malicious, bitchy (informal), ill-natured, malevolent, nasty, vindictive

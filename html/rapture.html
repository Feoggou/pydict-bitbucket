rapture (ˈræptʃər )

Definitions

noun

  1. the state of being carried away with joy, love, etc.; ecstasy
  2. an expression of great joy, pleasure, etc.
  3. a carrying away or being carried away in body or spirit (now rare except in theological usage)

transitive verb

Word forms:  ˈraptured,  ˈrapturing
  1. (rare) to enrapture; fill with ecstasy
ˈecstasy
  • See the rapture

Derived Forms

ˈrapturous adjective
ˈrapturously adverb

Word Origin

ML raptura: see rapt & , -ure

Synonyms

View thesaurus entry
= ecstasy, bliss, delight, euphoria, joy, rhapsody, seventh heaven, transport
